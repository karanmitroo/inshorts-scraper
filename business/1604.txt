Markets regulator SEBI has attached all bank, demat and mutual fund accounts held by Vijay Mallya-owned United Breweries Holdings (UBHL). The regulator said UBHL has defaulted on the payment of a penalty imposed by it in 2015 for disclosure lapses. The company is liable to pay the pending dues totalling ₹18.5 lakh which includes interest of ₹3.5 lakh, it added.
