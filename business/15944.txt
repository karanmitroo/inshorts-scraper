January CPI inflation rose to 5.11% measured on new base year of 2012 as against 4.28% in December, government data showed on Thursday. Also, the Index of Industrial Production (IIP) for the month of December was reported at 1.7% as against 3.8% in November. On a cumulative basis, the IIP grew 2.1% between April-December, when compared to the year-ago period.
