BSE-listed companies lost ₹1.54 lakh crore in market capitalisation (m-cap) on Monday, as the benchmark Sensex plunged by 537.55 points (2.05%) to 25,659.67. Investor wealth fell below the ₹100 lakh crore mark that the BSE had first hit in November 2014. Broader markets also witnessed a heavy sell-off, with the mid-cap and small-cap indices falling by 1.20% and 1.11% respectively.
