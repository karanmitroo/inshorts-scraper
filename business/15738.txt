The BSE Sensex Thursday went down 152.45 points (0.5 percent) to 28,469.67 and Nifty declined 51.25 points (0.6 percent) to 8,634.65. While 1,123 shares advanced, 1,714 shares declined and 195 shares remained unchanged. GAIL, TCS, NTPC, ONGC and Tata Steel were the major gainers, while Axis Bank, BHEL, SBI, Reliance and ICICI Bank were the major losers.
