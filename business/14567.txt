The Bombay Stock Exchange's benchmark Sensex on Tuesday gained about 424 points or 1.7 percent to close at 25,317.87. The 50-share Nifty index also ended in the green, gaining 129.45 points to finish the day at 7,688.25. Twenty six of 30 Sensex stocks ended with gains, with GAIL being the top gainer. Hindustan Unilever was the day's biggest loser.
