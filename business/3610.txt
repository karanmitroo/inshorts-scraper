The BSE Sensex hit a record closing high of 32,245.87 after rising 216 points. The NSE Nifty also surged 51 points to touch a life-time closing high of 9,966.40. The rally in technology stocks and HDFC Bank after earnings helped the market end at fresh record closing high. Wipro, Reliance Industries, HDFC Bank and ITC were among the major gainers.
