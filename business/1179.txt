Azim Premji Trust, along with other promoter entities, has sold 2.73% stake in Wipro for over ₹5,700 crore in the share buyback that closed on December 13. The price for the buyback offer was fixed at ₹320 per share. A total of 34.37 lakh shares were bought back under the offer with a total amount of ₹11,000 crore being utilised.
