Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPIs) have invested ₹2,543 crore in the Indian equity markets in May, remaining net buyers in equities for the third straight month. However, they withdrew ₹4,409 crore from the debt markets during the month, leading to a net outflow of ₹1,866 crore. Forecasts of above-normal monsoon and positive quarterly results enthused investor sentiment, analysts said.
