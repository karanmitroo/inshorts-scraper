Hardcastle Restaurants, the franchisee of McDonald’s for its West and South India operations, plans to invest ₹750 crore in the next 5-7 years to double the chain's outlets to 500 in these regions. Hardcastle will also double the number of employees to 15,000. Around 60-70% of the new openings will be in cities like Mumbai, Chennai, and Hyderabad, among others.
