Gold prices in the domestic bullion market on Saturday slipped amid weak local buying, meanwhile, silver prices marginally surged. Standard gold (99.5 purity) fell by ₹30 to end the day ₹26,865 per 10 grams and Pure gold (99.9 purity) also fell to ₹27,015 per 10 grams. Silver (.999 fineness) price however, increased by ₹50 to end at ₹37,670 per kg.
