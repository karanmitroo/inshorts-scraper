Foreign Portfolio Investments (FPIs) in the July-September period reached over ₹11,314 crore, highest in any quarter in the last 1.5 years. The FPIs in 2016 have totalled to around ₹46,722 crore, twice the amount that flowed in the corresponding period last year. This reportedly makes India the third biggest destination for FPIs in Asia after Taiwan and South Korea respectively.
