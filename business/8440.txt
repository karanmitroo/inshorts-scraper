India’s largest liquor maker United Spirits' profit rose 15.8% from a year earlier to ₹82.54 crore in the quarter ended September. Net sales in its Prestige & Above premium segment grew 12% in the quarter, while liquor sales of its mass-market segment declined 3%, the company said. Its total sales rose 12.5% from the year-ago period to ₹6,038.8 crore.
