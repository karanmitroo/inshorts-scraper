After ending a 26-year old arrangement with Mumbai-based Khorakiwala family over using the brand 'Monginis' in May, the Rs 400-crore company -Switz Foods Pvt Ltd (SFPL)- will foray into new market segments. The company, with prominence in eastern states of India, will rebrand itself in a new avatar 'Mio Amore' with a new brand logo and new colour scheme.
