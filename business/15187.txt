India's largest multiplex chain, PVR Ltd., on Tuesday announced the acquisition of DLF's multiplex subsidiary DT Cinemas for Rs 500 crore. DT Cinemas currently operates 29 screens with approximately 6,000 seats across eight properties in the NCR and Chandigarh. After the acquisition, "PVR will have a presence in 44 cities with 115 multiplexes and 506 screens," the company said.
