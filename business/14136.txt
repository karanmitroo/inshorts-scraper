According to a report jointly published by the Boston Consulting Group and the Confederation of Indian Industry, the Indian Media Industry is forecasted to touch ₹1.15 lakh crore by 2015-end and ₹3.8-5 lakh crore by 2025. TV industry is projected to reach ₹1.28 lakh crore from ₹60,000 crore and film industry would reach ₹24,000 crore from ₹13,000 crore by 2020.
