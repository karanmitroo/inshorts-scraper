The Foreign Investment Promotion Board (FIPB) on Tuesday approved four FDI proposals, worth around ₹1,810 crore. The biggest among these involves a 9% increase in foreign stake in insurance joint venture HDFC Standard Life for ₹1,700 crore. The Economic Affairs Secretary-led FIPB is an inter-ministerial committee with the authority to approve FDI proposals of up to ₹5,000 crore.
