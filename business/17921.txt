Sensex and Nifty fell Friday to mark their biggest weekly decline of 1.6% in eight weeks as profit-taking hit blue chips such as Reliance Industries for a third consecutive day. Sensex has fallen in each of the three trading sessions of the New Year. While Sensex fell 0.18% to end at 20,851.33, Nifty fell 0.16%  to close at 6,211.15.
