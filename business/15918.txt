Mahindra & Mahindra on Friday posted a consolidated net profit of Rs 930.32 crore for the quarter ended December 31, the PTI reported. The Indian auto major had posted a net profit of Rs 1,230 crore in the year-ago period. The company reported a revenue at Rs 18,371.91 crore in Q3, as against Rs 20,679.92 crore in the year-ago period.
