The Indian Navy has reportedly signed a ₹5,000 crore contract with Russian shipbuilder Zvezdochka, for the extension of its submarines' service life to 35 years. INS Sindhukesari would be the first submarine under the deal to be sent for refitment and modernisation of combat potential. Zvezdochka is also partnering Reliance Defence for upgradation of India’s submarines at Gujarat's Pipavav yard.
