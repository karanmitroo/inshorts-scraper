Baba Ramdev-promoted Patanjali Ayurved may announce an investment worth ₹1,600 crore for setting up an international herbal food park in Noida in poll-bound Uttar Pradesh to meet domestic and global demands, an official said. The plant is expected to produce goods worth ₹25,000 crore annually, another official claimed, adding that around 10,000 jobs could be generated, benefiting 50,000 families.
