Nearly 400 stocks on the BSE plunged to their respective one-year lows on Thursday as the Sensex dropped by 807.07 points to a 21-month low of 22,951.83. The benchmark index has lost around 23% of its value since touching an all-time high of over 30,000 in March 2015, with investors’ wealth decreasing by ₹20 lakh crore to ₹86 lakh crore.
