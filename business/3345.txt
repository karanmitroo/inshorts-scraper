A woman in Northern Ireland has been jailed after using same Tesco vouchers numerous times, exploiting a failure in the supermarket's system, to accumulate goods worth $1,313 (₹84,000). She was ordered to pay a $522 (₹33,000) fine, and was jailed for three months. The woman redeemed vouchers, meant for one-time use, several times for items like cosmetics and washing powder.
