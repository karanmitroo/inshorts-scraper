The Shipping Ministry is targeting a cumulative profit of ₹8,000 crore from all major ports and flagship organisations in the current fiscal against a record-high profit of ₹6,000 crore in 2015-16, Shipping Minister Nitin Gadkari said on Saturday. Meanwhile, 27 industrial clusters envisaging investment worth ₹8 lakh crore are being developed near port-led development zones, he added.
