The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty extended their rally with gains of over 0.6% in the week ended September 9. While the Sensex advanced 265.14 points to close at 28,797.25, the Nifty gained 57.05 points to settle at 8,866.70. Investors remained upbeat as slower US jobs data lowered expectations of an interest rate hike by the US Federal Reserve.
