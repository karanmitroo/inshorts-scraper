The number of wilful defaulters rose to 7,129, as of December 2015, compared to 6,458 in June that year, according to credit information firm Cibil. Maharashtra topped the list with 1,366 defaulting entities followed by West Bengal (1,059) and Andhra Pradesh (679). Nationalised banks are owed the highest of ₹39,218 crore followed by SBI and its associate banks (₹18,576 crore).
