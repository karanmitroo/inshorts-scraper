Yes Bank on Thursday posted a net profit of ₹610.41 crore for the second quarter (Q2), a 26.5% increase from ₹482.54 crore in the same period last year. Net interest income (bank's core income from lending business) surged 29.4% to ₹1,108.47 crore. However, gross non-performing assets (NPAs) rose to 0.61% of the total loans, compared to 0.36% last year.
