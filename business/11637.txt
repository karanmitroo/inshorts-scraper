Oil companies on Saturday hiked the price of petrol by ₹1.06/litre and the price of diesel by ₹2.94/litre, effective midnight. Petrol and diesel in Delhi will now cost ₹62.19/litre and ₹50.95/litre respectively. "The current level of international product prices of Petrol & Diesel and INR-USD exchange rate" warrants increase in the price of petrol and diesel, state-owned IOCL said.
