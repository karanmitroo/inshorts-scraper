Chinese internet giant Tencent is giving its employees over $220 million (₹1,486 crore) worth of company shares to help celebrate its 18th anniversary. The company, which was founded on November 11, 1998, will give 300 shares to each of its 31,500 employees. Tencent is Asia's most valuable listed company with a market capitalisation of over $240 billion (₹16.2 lakh crore).
