IT giant Infosys has reported a 38.3% year-on-year increase in net profit to ₹5,129 crore for the December quarter, compared to ₹3,708 crore in the same period last fiscal. The revenue of the company also increased by 3% to ₹17,794 crore. This is the first quarterly result since Salil Parekh took charge as Infosys' CEO and MD on January 2.
