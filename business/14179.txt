NIIT Technology reported a net profit of ₹68.2 crore the previous quarter, a jump by 70.3% against the corresponding period previous year. Meanwhile, the revenues rose by 15.2% to ₹677.9 crore against ₹588.3 crore previous year. "Growth in all international geographies and industry segments of focus resulted in 8% sequential growth in international business", said NIIT CEO Arvind Thakur.
