The Bombay Stock Exchange's benchmark Sensex on Monday lost about 109 points to close at 26,283.09, ahead of the GDP data, which was released later during the day. The broader 50-share Nifty closed at 7,971.30, losing 30.65 points. The Sensex's top gainers were Lupin, Cipla, and Dr. Reddy's Labs. The day's major losers included BHEL, Bharti Airtel, and Hindalco.
