The BSE Sensex crashed more than 500 points and Nifty over 150 points in intra-day trade on Wednesday. At 12:58 pm, the Sensex was trading at 27,693.01 points (478.68 points down) and Nifty at 8,362.10 (148.70 points down). Tata Motors and Yes Bank went down by about 7 percent and 6 percent, respectively.
