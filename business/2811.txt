IT giant Infosys on Friday said that the newly appointed Non-Executive Chairman Nandan Nilekani will not receive any remuneration for his current post. "His last drawn remuneration for the fiscal 2010 in which he ceased to be a director was ₹34 lakh," Infosys said. Notably, Nilekani has a net worth of over $1.73 billion (over ₹11,000 crore), according to Forbes.
