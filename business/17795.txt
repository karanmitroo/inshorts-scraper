Global smartphone sales rose by 36% (crossed 282 million units) in Q4 of 2013, propelled by two-fold growth in India. With 166.8% increase in the Q4 of 2013, India exhibited the highest sales expansion among countries, research firm Gartner said on Thursday. In 2013, global smartphone shipments totalled 968 million units, registering a growth of 42.3% from 2012.
