Cabinet Committee on Investment headed by Prime Minister may consider investing Rs 1.6 lakh crore on seven stalled mega infrastructure projects (with average size of Rs.25,000 crore) on September-17. "Expeditious" clearance will be sought for projects like JSW Steel (Rs 35,000 crore) and Indian Oil Corporation (Rs 29,777 crore). Capital expenditure for PSUs this fiscal is Rs 1.41 lakh crore.
