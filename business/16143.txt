The Sensex and the Nifty on Wednesday hit their new all-time peaks of 28,958.10 and 8,741.85, respectively on sustained capital inflows amid pre-budget optimism among market players. The 30-share index finally ended at a fresh record of 28,888.86, rising 104.19 points. The Nifty ended above the 8,700 level for the first time, surging 33.90 points at new peak of 8,729.50.
