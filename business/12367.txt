Following the 2016 Union Budget, car manufacturers Hyundai and Nissan have hiked prices across their entire line-ups. The hike passes on the additional infrastructure cess of 1%-4% levied on automobiles onto the end consumer. While Hyundai's price hike ranges from ₹2,889 to ₹82,906, Nissan has raised prices of all vehicles, including the Datsun Range, by 1% to 3.5%.
