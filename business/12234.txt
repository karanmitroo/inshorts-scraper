India has received 424 FDI proposals worth ₹48,902 crore, till January, in the ongoing fiscal, in sectors where FDI is permitted under government approval route, Commerce Minister Nirmala Sitharaman said on Wednesday. In comparison, 344 FDI proposals worth ₹44,005 crore were received in 2014-15, she added. FDI in India comes through the automatic route and through government approval.
