Bharti Group has announced that legal aid service 'Nyaya Bharti', the group's corporate social responsibility initiative, will be operational from April 1, 2016. In the ₹10-crore initiative, the group will hire lawyers to represent undertrials and provide bail or surety. Chairman Sunil Mittal promised to contribute ₹5 crore to the initiative from his salary, which was ₹27.17 crore in 2014-15.
