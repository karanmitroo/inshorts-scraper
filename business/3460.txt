Tata Motors cut the salaries of its top executives last fiscal amid mounting losses in the company’s India business. The sharpest drop in remuneration was that of Ravindra Pisharody, former commercial vehicles head, who quit in June and received around ₹2.31 crore, a 17% decrease from the previous year. COO Satish Borwankar saw a 12% cut to ₹2.15 crore.
