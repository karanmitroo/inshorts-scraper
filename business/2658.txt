Infosys interim CEO UB Pravin Rao has said the company had an addition of around 6,000 recruits in 2016-17, and expects similar kind of additions over the next 1 or 2 years. Rao said Infosys is recruiting about 10,000 people in the next few years in the US. Notably, US accounted for over 61% of Infosys' revenue in June quarter.
