Mining company Vedanta and Aditya Birla Group's Hindalco will exit the BSE Sensex on December 21 and will be replaced by Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone and Asian Paints. Three companies- NHPC, Jaiprakash Associates, and Unitech- will be dropped from the BSE 100 index and S and P BSE Carbonex , while Britannia will be added to BSE 100.
