Logging its first weekly loss in seven weeks, the benchmark Sensex on Friday closed nearly 105 points down at 28,458.10. The 30-share index came under pressure due to fag-end profit booking in IT, oil & gas and healthcare shares. On a weekly basis, the Sensex fell by about 236 points. The 50-share Nifty index ended 26.10 points lower at 8,538.30.
