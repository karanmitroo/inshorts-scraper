According to Bloomberg, the foreign capital inflow in India, which currently stands at $4.32 billion in 2015, is the seventh highest in the world. This is the third largest inflow among the Asian markets and the emerging markets. However, foreign portfolio investors (FPIs) invested around $16.16 billion in 2014, $19.7 billion in 2013 and a record $24.54 billion in 2012.
