The Employees' Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) settled 1.18 crore claims including provident fund withdrawals in 2015-16, the retirement fund body said in a statement. While 96% of these claims were settled within the stipulated time of 20 days, 79% were resolved within 10 days, it added. Further, over 2.2 lakh grievances were addressed by EPFO during the fiscal.
