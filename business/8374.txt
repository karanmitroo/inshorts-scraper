The 30-share Sensex ended Samvat 2072, which came to a close on Diwali, with gains of 2,198.25 points or 8.53%. Meanwhile, the broader 50-share NSE Nifty surged 854.65 points or 10.98% during the Samvat year. However, the Sensex dropped 135.67 points while the Nifty lost 55.05 points during the week ended October 28.
