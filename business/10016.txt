The benchmark indices on Tuesday ended on a flat note as the BSE Sensex gained 4.67 points to settle at 27,990.21, and the NSE Nifty rose by 3.45 points to close at 8,632.60 amid mixed global cues. Infosys, TCS, and Asian Paints were the major gainers while NTPC, Bajaj Auto, and Larsen & Toubro were the major losers.
