The BSE Sensex plunged over 335 points to hit a fresh 19-month low and the NSE Nifty crashed below the 7,500-mark in early trade on Monday due to heavy selling and investors tracking weak Asian markets amid persistent China fears. The Sensex was down by 335.43 points to 24,598.90 and the Nifty dropped by 105.70 points to 7,495.65.
