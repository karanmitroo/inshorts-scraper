After the Tata Group replaced Cyrus Mistry as Chairman, three other members, who were part of Mistry's advisory council, have also quit. The three members are Tata Group's Chief of Human Resources NS Rajan, business development officer Madhu Kannan, and strategist Nirmalya Kumar. Earlier, Tatas reportedly discontinued the Group Executive Council (GEC) of Tata Sons set up by Mistry.
