State-owned Central Bank of India registered a rise of 7.3% in its net profit to Rs. 174.29 crore for the quarter ended March 31. In a filing submitted to BSE, the Central Bank said its total income in the quarter ended March 31, 2015 increased to Rs. 7,321.50 crore from Rs. 6,961.67 crore in the quarter ended March 31, 2014.
