The benchmark indices on Wednesday ended the day with losses with the Sensex plunging by 66.51 points to end at 27,984.37. Meanwhile, the broader Nifty lost 18.80 points to close at 8,659.10. Wipro, Lupin, and Adani Ports SEZ emerged as the day's biggest gainers while ITC, ICICI Bank, and Hindustan Unilever were the major losers.
