The benchmark Sensex and the broader Nifty extended their losses on Tuesday as they fell 207.27 and 58.90 points to settle at 25,229.70 and 7,747.00 respectively amid weak global cues. Bharti Airtel, Asian Paints, Adani Ports, HDFC and Tata Motors made the maximum gains while CIL, BHEL, ICICI Bank, Lupin and TCS emerged as the biggest losers.
