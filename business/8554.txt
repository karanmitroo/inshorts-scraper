The benchmark indices on Monday ended with gains, with the BSE Sensex rising by 101.90 points to close at 28,179.08, while the NSE Nifty gained 15.90 points to settle at 8,708.95. ONGC, Tata Motors and ICICI Bank emerged as the day's biggest gainers while Wipro, Asian Paints and Larsen & Toubro were the major losers.
