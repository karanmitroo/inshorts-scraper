State-owned Coal India Limited on Tuesday reported a 14.8% year-on-year decline in its consolidated net profit at ₹3,065.28 crore for the quarter ended June. The fall was attributed to a decline in net sales which dropped by 6.1% to ₹17,796.05 crore during the period under review. Meanwhile, coal production during the period grew by 3.56% to 125.67 million tonnes (MT).
