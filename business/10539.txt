Multi-business conglomerate ITC on Thursday posted a net profit of ₹2384.67 crore in the quarter ended June, a rise of 10.1% against ₹2,166.09 crore in the corresponding period last year. The company's total income from operations registered a growth of 8.3% at ₹13,253.06 crore. Further, gross revenue from cigarettes rose 6.4% from a year earlier to ₹8,230.6 crore, ITC stated.
