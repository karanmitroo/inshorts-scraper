India's foreign exchange (forex) reserves decreased by $1.08 billion to $277.72 billion for the week ended August-23, according to data released by RBI. The foreign currency assets (FCA) - the biggest component of the forex reserves - fell by $1.07 billion to $250.48 billion. Gold reserves remained stagnant at $20.74 billion after plunging $1.28 billion in the week ended July-5.
