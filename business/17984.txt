To counter the Unsolicited Commercial Communications(UCC) from persons not registered as telemarketers, sectoral regulator TRAI has imposed a total fine of Rs 5.04 crore on 13 telecom operators for non-compliance with regulations relating to UCCs. The menace of telemarketers masquerading themselves as "normal subscribers" persists in-spite of blacklisting of 1,45,696 unregistered telemarketers in last 2 years.
