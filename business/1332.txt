Infosys' ex-CFO Mohandas Pai said Infosys must apologise to Co-founder Narayana Murthy for abusing him and damaging his reputation. He said this asking Infosys to withdraw the letter issued to stock exchange accusing Murthy of interfering when he raised issues of corporate governance. He added some former Infosys directors, who made wrong allegations against Murthy, must also apologise.
