Finance Minister Arun Jaitley said on Friday that there is no official estimation of black money either before or after November 8, the day demonetisation was announced. Income Tax department searches on 1,356 groups of assessees between April 2014 and November 2016 led to admission of ₹31,277 crore undisclosed income and seizure of undisclosed assets worth ₹2,164 crore, Jaitley added.
