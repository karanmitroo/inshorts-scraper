Continuing its gain on the third consecutive day, the benchmark Sensex on Monday surged by 329.95 points to close at 27701.79 points. Tracking the momentum, the NSE Nifty gained 98.80 points to end at 8324.00 points. M&M, Coal India, BHEL and HDFC twins were among the gainers, while Hindalco, L&T, Infosys and Tata Steel were among the losers.
