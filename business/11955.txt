The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty fell by over 2% in the week ended April 8. While the Sensex lost 595.80 points to close at 24,673.84, the Nifty dropped 157.85 points to settle at 7,555.20. Sector-wise, the BSE Bankex plunged the most at 4.07%, while the BSE Auto and BSE FMCG indices also slid by 2.35% and 2.15% respectively.
