BMW on Thursday launched X5 M and X6 M in India for Rs 1.5 crore and Rs 1.6 crore (ex-showroom price) respectively. The four-wheel drive vehicles are powered by a V8 petrol engine which produces a maximum power of 575 hp. X6 M can reach 0-100 kmph in 4.2 seconds, and can achieve a top speed of 250 kmph.
