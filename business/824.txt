FMCG major Hindustan Unilever (HUL) on Wednesday posted a nearly 28% year-on-year increase in net profit to ₹1,326 crore for the December quarter, compared to ₹1,038 crore in the same period last fiscal. The company's revenue grew 3.3% to ₹8,590 crore. Revenue from the company's personal care segment, which includes brands like Vaseline and Dove, rose 2.8% to ₹4,090 crore.
