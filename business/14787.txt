Yamaha India launched the new 321cc-engine sports bike ‘YZF-R3', priced at Rs 3.25 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi) on Tuesday. The bike has an all-new engine and chassis design, in a combination of “class-leading power and a lightweight body”. The company makes about 1,000 bikes a month in the Supersports segment (250cc-600cc) and plans to attain a 20% market share in it.
