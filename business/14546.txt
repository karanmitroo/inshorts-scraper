The Wholesale Price Index (WPI)-based inflation, which has been in the negative zone since November 2014, fell to a historic low of (-)4.95% year-on-year in August as fuel costs decreased. The number was (-)4.05% in July. The corresponding figure for August 2014 stood at 3.85%. However, according to official data, onions and pulses were costlier in August 2015.
