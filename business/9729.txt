India's stock, bond, and currency markets will remain closed on Monday for the 'Ganesh Chaturthi' festival and trading will resume on Tuesday. The NSE Nifty gained 0.4%, while the BSE Sensex rose by 0.38% on Friday. Further, the benchmark 10-year bond yield ended unchanged at 7.12%, while the rupee strengthened to 66.82/66.83 per dollar against the previous close of 66.95/66.96.
