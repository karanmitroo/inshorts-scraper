Reliance Power Saturday posted a 4.8% decline in net profit at Rs 254.44 crore for the third quarter ended December 31, 2014, the PTI reported. The company had posted a net profit of Rs 267.22 crore in the year-ago period. Total income rose to Rs 1,827 crore for the quarter under review from Rs 1,484 crore in the year-ago period.
