Union Transport Minister Nitin Gadkari on Friday said that ₹2 lakh crore will be spent on expanding and building roads in Maharashtra in the next five years, including ₹20,000 crore for the Konkan region alone. Gadkari further promised the promotion of water transport in Goa and the Konkan, and announced a ₹10-12 lakh subsidy for small fisherman to buy trawlers.
