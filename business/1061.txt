The government on Friday said over 25,800 fraud cases involving about ₹179 crore related to credit/debit cards and internet banking were reported in 2017. It added that 10,220 cases of such frauds were reported in the December quarter. Notably, Maharashtra had topped list of credit/debit card and online banking-related frauds in the fiscal 2016-17 with 380 cases involving ₹12.10 crore.
