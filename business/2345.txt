Paytm Founder Vijay Shekhar Sharma, and Flipkart Founders Binny Bansal and Sachin Bansal have made it to "Self-made under 40" category in 'Hurun India Rich List 2017'. Divyank Turakhia of Directi Group is ranked one in the category with ₹11,500 crore. The list which has a $153 million (₹1,000 crore) cut-off also included edtech startup Byju's Founder, Byju Raveendran.
