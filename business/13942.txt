Foreign investors in October pumped in around ₹22,350 crore, thereby registering a seven-month high after the capital markets saw an inflow of ₹20,723 crore in March. As per depositories data, equities market saw an inflow of ₹6,650 crore, whereas, the debt market saw ₹15,700 crore inflow after the capital market suffered a net ₹23,000 crore outflow in August and September.
