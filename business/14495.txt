Gold imports increased by 140% in August 2015 to $4.95 billion against $2.06 billion in August 2014, according to the data from Ministry of Commerce. However, oil imports dropped this August, by 42.59% to $7.35 billion (year on year). The exporting sectors of petroleum, engineering products and leather saw a decline of 47.88%, 29% and 12.78% respectively (year on year).
