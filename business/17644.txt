With a strong growth of 22% in asset base, ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund has joined the Rs.1 lakh crore club, while HDFC MF  retained its top slot (Rs.1.13 lakh cr). ICICI (Rs.1.07 lakh cr) is followed by Reliance MF (Rs.1.03 lakh cr), Birla Sunlife (Rs.89k cr). Total asset base of the entire MF industry grew by Rs.40k cr during FY14.
