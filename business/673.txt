Finance Minister Arun Jaitley on Monday tabled the Economic Survey 2017-18 in the Parliament, which predicts India's GDP growth at 7-7.5% in 2018-19. It says that major reforms over the past year will allow growth to reach 6.75% this fiscal. The survey added that retail inflation during 2017-18 averaged to the lowest in the last 6 years at 3.3%.
