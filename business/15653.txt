Indian markets completed their best financial year since 2009-10 on Tuesday as the Sensex posted an annual gain of 24.89% to end at 27,957.49. The 50-share NSE also gained 26.65%, despite losing 4.6% and the Sensex dropping 4.8% in Feb 2015. Analysts expect the bullish run to continue next year after markets hit record highs of 30024.74 in March.
