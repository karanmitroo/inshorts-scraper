Benchmark indices BSE Sensex and Nifty 50 closed at fresh lifetime highs on Wednesday powered by India's jump on World Bank's ease of doing business list. Sensex rose over 387 points to close at 33,600 and Nifty closed at 10,440 after crossing the 10,450-mark for the first time. Bharti Airtel, SBI, ICICI Bank and HDFC were the top gainers.
