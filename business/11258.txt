India's largest realty firm DLF on Friday reported a 23% year-on-year decline in net profit for the quarter ended March to ₹132 crore. However, income from operations rose by 20% to ₹2,335 crore. For the entire fiscal 2015-16, net profit increased marginally by 1.7% to ₹549 crore while income from operations surged 21% to ₹9,260 crore.
