As many as 12 key domestic institutional investors (DIIs) have written to Infosys asking for Co-founder Nandan Nilekani's appointment to the board, and expressed their concerns over Vishal Sikka's resignation as CEO last week. The investors further said, "In our opinion, he enjoys the confidence of...customers, stakeholders and employees". Notably, Nilekani served as Infosys CEO from 2002 to 2007.
