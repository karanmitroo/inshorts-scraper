The benchmark indices ended flat in the week ended August 19, with the Sensex losing 75.40 points to settle at 28,077.00, while the NSE Nifty closed 5.25 points down at 8,666.90. Market sentiment dampened following the wholesale price index (WPI) inflation climbing to a near two-year high at 3.55% and retail inflation also rising to 6.07% in July, analysts said.
