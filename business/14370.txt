The small and medium cap companies have outperformed the large companies of the benchmark Sensex this fiscal amid negative global cues and weak domestic growth. The MidCap index surged 2.31% this fiscal to 10818.68 and SmallCap index gained 1.39% to 11042.60, against the 30-stock benchmark Sensex which lost 6.21% from April 1 to 26,220.95 (as on October 1).
