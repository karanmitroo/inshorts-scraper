TCS has topped the Forbes list of country's most admired companies replacing Tata Steel of the same conglomerate, which slipped to 7th position. Also, ten PSUs made it to the list with SBI(Rank-5) topping among them. The rankings have considered corporate governance, innovativeness, CSR and leadership as parameters. Hindustan Unilever(Rank-2), ITC, Infosys(Both Rank-3), L&T(Rank-6), ONGC(Rank-8), Maruti(Rank-9), ICICI(Rank-10) are among Top-10.
