Following reports that Mukesh Ambani-led Reliance Jio is planning to create its own cryptocurrency called JioCoin, over 30 fake JioCoin apps have appeared on the Google Play Store. Some of these apps offer JioCoins in exchange for doing "tasks". An app named "Jio Coin" lists its publisher's name as "Rellance Digital" and claims that 'all prices listed include GST'.
