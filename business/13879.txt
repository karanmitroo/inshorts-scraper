Punjab National Bank (PNB) on Friday reported a net profit of ₹621.03 crore for the quarter ended September, an 8% jump over ₹575.34 crore in the same period last year. The bank’s net interest income grew 4.1% year-on-year to ₹4,322 crore from ₹4,151.2 crore, while provisions for bad loans rose 6.4% to ₹1,882 crore from ₹1,768 crore.
