As per Wealth-X, Bill and Melinda Gates are the world's wealthiest couple, having a combined net worth of $85.7 billion. Spanish retail tycoon Amancio Ortega Gaona and his wife, Flora Perez, with a combined net worth $70.7 billion, took the second spot. Ranked 9th, Mark Zuckerberg (31) and wife Priscilla Chan (30) were the youngest couple on the list.
