As a consequence of the benchmark BSE Sensex tanking 541.14 points (2.07%) on Tuesday, over Rs 1 lakh crore of investor wealth was wiped out on the day. Twenty-six of the 30 BSE-listed companies ended the day in red with the market capitalisation (m-cap) of the companies decreasing by Rs 1.36 lakh crore to Rs 93.33 lakh crore.
