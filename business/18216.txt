Slowdown in homebuilding and infrastructure projects has led to a  52% dip in net profit, the biggest since 2010, for Aditya Birla Group firm Ultratech Cement for the July-September quarter. The company reported a Rs.2.6-billion net profit for the quarter, as opposed to the market estimates of Rs.4.1-billion, according to Thomson Reuters. Net sales also fell 4% to Rs.45-billion.
