The market capitalisation of BSE-listed companies surged by over ₹2.51 lakh crore to nearly ₹88.35 lakh crore on Tuesday. The gains were registered as the benchmark Sensex rose by 777.35 points (3.38%) to close at 23,779.35, marking its biggest post-Budget day rally ever and also its highest single-day gain in points value since May 2009.
