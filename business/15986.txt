Government will soon infuse Rs 6,990 crore in nine public sector banks. SBI leads the pack with a capitalisation of Rs 2,970 crore, followed by BoB Rs 1,260 crore, PNB Rs 870 crore and Canara Bank Rs 570 crore. This is the first tranche of capital infusion out of the total Rs 11,200 crore, allocated in the Budget for 2014-15.
