The share prices of Mandhana Industries and Eros International Wednesday plunged after the court sentenced Salman Khan for 5 years. Mandhana Industries, which has an exclusive global license to design, manufacture, retail and distribute Being Human Clothing, fell 11.7%. Also, Eros International, which has global rights of Salman Khan's movies Bajrangi Bhaijaan and Hero, plunged up to 24.4%.
