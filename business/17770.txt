Indian banks lost Rs.17,284 crore during 2012-13 due to fraud- a four-fold jump over the previous fiscal- an RTI query has revealed. 62 banks filed 26,598 cases, with PNB being the worst-hit among public sector banks and ICICI in private sector. Although the state-run banks account for 25% of the total cases, these lenders lost 94% of the total money.
