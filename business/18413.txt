India's industrial production contracted 2.2% in June, more-than-expected from a year earlier, government data showed on Monday. Analysts polled by Reuters had expected output to shrink 1.2% annually. Manufacturing sector, which constitutes about 76% of industrial production, shrank 2.2% and Capital goods production, a barometer for investments in the economy, contracted an annual 6.6% in June from a year earlier.
