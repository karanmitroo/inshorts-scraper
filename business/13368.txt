Oil and Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC) on Tuesday got the Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs' (CCEA) approval for converting ₹5,000 crore of its ₹6,000 crore loan to overseas investment subsidiary ONGC Videsh (OVL), into equity. The move would reportedly raise the equity base of OVL to ₹15,000 crore, while boosting ONGC’s overseas Exploration and Production (E&P) business prospects.
