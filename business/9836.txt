The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty on Wednesday extended their gains after touching a 13-month high and 16-month high on Tuesday respectively. Sensex rose 109.16 points to settle at 28,452.17 points, while the Nifty gained 41.85 points to close at 8,786.20 points. L&T and Hero MotoCorp were the top gainers, while Tata Steel and ONGC emerged as the biggest losers.
