Fifty-four years and a day after American investor Warren Buffett bought his firm Berkshire Hathaway's first shares, its A-class shares crossed $250,000 (around ₹1.7 crore) in the morning trade on Tuesday. Buffett bought his first shares at $7.50, which would be worth $60.03 (₹4,000) in 2016. Notably, Berkshire Hathaway has jumped about 8% since Donald Trump won the US election.
