The Directorate of Revenue Intelligence on Sunday recovered ₹15.75 crore in old ₹1,000 and ₹500 notes in New Delhi. As per law, a person found having old demonetised notes can be fined ₹10,000 or five times the amount of the face value of notes seized, whichever is higher. Thus, the penalty in this case can go up to ₹78.75 crore.
