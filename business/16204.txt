The Department of Telecom issued a notification late Friday night that the next round of spectrum auction for 2G and 3G networks will be held from February 25. The government aims to collect at least Rs 64,840 crore from the spectrum auction, which will be held for frequencies in four bands of 800 Mhz, 900 Mhz, 1800 Mhz and 2100 Mhz.
