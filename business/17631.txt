The BSE benchmark sensex on Wednesday surpassed the 22,500 level to set a new closing high for the sixth day in succession. The barometer spurted to its all-time record of 22,592 intra-day, before settling down at life-time closing high of 22,551. Also, the NSE Nifty gained 31.50 points to finish at 6,752 after registering an all-time high of 6,763.50 intra-day.
