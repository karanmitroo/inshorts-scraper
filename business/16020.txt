Nagpur-based snack manufacturer Haldiram's dominated the Indian snack market in 2013-2014, beating McDonald's and Domino's. Haldiram's revenues, at Rs 3,500 cr, is more than the combined revenue of Domino's (Rs 1,733 cr) and McDonald's (Rs 1,390 cr). Also, it stands ahead of Maggi, which has a Rs 1,200-cr revenue. Haldiram's commands 40% of India's traditional snacks business.
