E-commerce major Snapdeal has acquired Hyderabad-based mobile technology startup, MartMobi for an undisclosed amount. MartMobi which creates mobile sites and apps for e-commerce stores, will help Snapdeal strengthen its mobility platform for merchant partners. "We are focusing on mobile platform as about 75% of orders are coming from mobiles-based devices," Snapdeal co-founder Rohit Bansal said.
