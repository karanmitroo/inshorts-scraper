 After the United Bank of India, now the State Bank of India has declared Kingfisher Airlines promoter Vijay Mallya and three other directors as wilful defaulters. According to SBI Chairperson Arundhati Bhattacharya, "There is a mandatory time that needs to be given to them to respond and that time is currently on." Kingfisher Airlines owes Rs.7,600 crores to 17 banks.
