Condiments and sauce maker Veeba Food Services has reportedly raised over $6 million (₹40 crore) in funding led by existing investor Verlinvest, a Belgium-based investment holding company. Verlinvest manages €1.8 billion (over ₹13,800 crore) in assets across the globe. In October 2016, Veeba Food had raised $6 million in a Series B round of fundraising from Saama Capital and Verlinvest.
