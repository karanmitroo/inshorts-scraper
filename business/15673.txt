The Foreign Investment Promotion Board has given its nod to 10 foreign direct investment (FDI) proposals worth Rs 2,858 crore. It referred the Holcim-Ambuja deal to the Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA) and deferred 18 proposals, including that of Sharekhan Ltd. FDI proposals worth more than Rs 1,200 crore are given final go-ahead by the CCEA.
