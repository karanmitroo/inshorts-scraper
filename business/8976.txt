The 30-share BSE Sensex rebounded from last week's tumble and finished the week ended October 7 with a gain of 195.18 points to close at 28,061.14. Meanwhile, the broader Nifty rose 86.45 points to settle at 8,697.60. The gains were seen after the Monetary Policy Committee slashed the repo rate by 25 basis points to a six-year low of 6.25%.
