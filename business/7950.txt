Snapchat has reportedly filed for an IPO in March 2017 that could value the company between $20 billion to $25 billion. Snapchat's IPO could be the largest technology IPO since Chinese e-commerce giant Alibaba went public two years ago, valued at $170.9 billion. It would also be the biggest US technology IPO since Facebook's 2012 debut at $81.2 billion valuation.
