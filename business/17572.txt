Riding on the so-called 'Modi wave', shares of various Gujarat-based companies, including the Adani group, have gained up to three times since the state's CM, Narendra Modi, was declared the BJP's PM candidate seven months ago. Adani Enterprises has seen its share price surge over three-fold from Rs. 141.20 on September 13, 2013 to Rs. 437.5 on April 11, 2014.
