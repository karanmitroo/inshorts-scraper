The combined market capitalisation of the top five BSE-listed firms surged by ₹25,388 crore in the week ended May 13, with the benchmark Sensex rising 261.07 points (1.03%) to close at 25,489.57. TCS witnessed the highest rise in m-cap at ₹10,098 crore followed by Infosys and HDFC Bank, while HUL saw the highest fall in market valuation at ₹4,728 crore.
