The Income Tax department issued 2.10 crore refunds totalling over ₹1.22 lakh crore in 2015-16, compared to ₹1.12 lakh crore in 2014-15 and ₹89,664 crore in 2013-14, a finance ministry statement has said. Further, in the 2015-16 fiscal, over 94% of income tax returns were filed online and 4.14 crore returns were processed without any human intervention, the statement informed.
