After tanking by almost 300 points in morning trade, the benchmark Sensex rallied after the Reserve Bank of India announced the policy rate cut of 50 basis points to settle 161.82 points (0.63%) up at 25,778.66. The broader Nifty surged 47.60 points to end the day with 7,843.30 points. Bank Nifty rallied 1% and HDFC was Sensex's biggest gainer.
