Indian stocks (SENSEX) climbed to a one-month high, as software makers rallied to offset a report showing faster-than-estimated inflation. At the close, the S&P BSE Sensex added 0.4% to 20,607.54 and a gauge of 223 mid-cap companies rose to its highest level since July 23. The Sensex rallied 3.1% last week and Wholesale prices rose 6.46%.
