Bombay Stock Exchange, India's second-biggest exchange, plans to file a draft prospectus for an up to $150 million initial public offering this week, reports claimed. Axis, Edelweiss, Jefferies, and Nomura are the global coordinators, while Deutsche Bank, Motilal Oswal, and SBI Capital Markets are the bookrunners for the IPO. Earlier, the NSE had also decided to make its shares public.
