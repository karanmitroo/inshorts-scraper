The Sensex closed 604.17 points (2 percent) down at 28,844.78 on Monday and Nifty too declined 181 points (2 percent) to 8,756.75 to post their biggest single-day fall in two months. Asian markets suffered similar losses on increased expectation of a rate hike by the U.S. Federal Reserve, with the Asia-Pacific shares index outside Japan down 1.17%.
