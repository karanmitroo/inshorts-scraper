Private lender ICICI Bank on Wednesday bought 19.58 lakh shares in Vijay Mallya-headed Indian conglomerate United Breweries (UB) Ltd worth around ₹186 crore. The shares which were bought at ₹ 950.1 apiece translate to a 0.74% stake in the company. United Breweries which is the manufacturer of Kingfisher beer, is India's largest brewer.
