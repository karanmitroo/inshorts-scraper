Siblings Shravan(14) and Sanjay Kumaran(12) have become probably India's youngest entrepreneurs having founded Go Dimensions, an app-development unit, almost 2-years ago. The two have developed 11-apps for iOS and Android, and have received over 35,000 downloads. The duo, who recently presented at an SAP-event in Bangalore, aim to get at least 50% of Indian smartphone users to use their apps.
