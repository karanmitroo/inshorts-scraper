The budgetary allocation of over ₹3 lakh crore for the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) sector can lead to a direct benefit of ₹2 lakh crore to the sector, according to SME Rating Agency of India (SMERA). The implementation of public welfare schemes like Swachh Bharat Abhiyan and MGNREGA will create demand for the sector's services, it added.
