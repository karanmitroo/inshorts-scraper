Vineet Taneja, Chief Executive Officer of domestic handset maker Micromax has resigned from his position. Taneja had joined Micromax in July 2014 after heading the cellphone business at Samsung India and Bharti Airtel. Reports suggest that Taneja had clashes with Micromax promoters including Rahul Sharma, Rajesh Agarwal, Vikas Jai and Sumeet Kumar for non-compliance on implementing his ideas.
