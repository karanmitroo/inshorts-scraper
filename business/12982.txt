India, with 2.36 lakh millionaires, has the fourth largest number of High Net Worth Individuals (HNWIs) in the Asia-Pacific region, according to the Asia-Pacific 2016 Wealth Report. Japan topped the list with 12.60 lakh millionaires, followed by China (6.54 lakh) and Australia (2.9 lakh). HNWIs are designated as people with a net worth of $1 million or more.
