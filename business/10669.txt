The combined market capitalisation (m-cap) of four of the top 10 companies listed on the BSE fell by ₹19,173.4 crore for the week ended July 8, with the Sensex losing 18.01 points to close at 27,126.90. TCS was the biggest loser, after shedding ₹15,034.4 crore in m-cap, followed by Infosys and HDFC Bank. RIL, HUL, and HDFC registered gains.
