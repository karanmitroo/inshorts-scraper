FMCG major Dabur India announced on Friday that it is set to adopt five villages in Uttar Pradesh under Prime Minister Narendra Modi's 'Swachh Bharat Abhiyan'. The company said that it will make the adopted villages (Nandpur, Nagla Gajju, Naraina, Nidhawali and Domatikri) 'Open-Defecation Free' within the current fiscal by ensuring that all households have toilets.
