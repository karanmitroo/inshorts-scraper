Billionaire investor Warren Buffett's annual letter has revealed his firm Berkshire Hathaway’s gain from Apple stands at $1.6 billion (over ₹10,600 crore), after the latter's shares surged. Berkshire's stake in Apple, as of December, was at $6.75 billion. On Friday, value of Berkshire's Apple holding surged to over $8.3 billion. Berkshire has become one of the top 10 Apple investors.
