BSNL has launched a promotional offer under which prepaid customers get double benefit on four new data Special Tariff Vouchers. Under the offer, ₹1,498 gives 18 GB data instead of 9 GB, ₹2,798 gives 36 GB instead of 18 GB, ₹3,998 gives 60 GB instead of 30 GB and ₹4,498 gives 80 GB data instead of 40 GB.
