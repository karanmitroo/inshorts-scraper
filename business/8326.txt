Mukesh Ambani-led Reliance Jio will install about 45,000 mobile towers in the next six months amid attempts to boost its 4G network. This is part of Jio's plans to invest ₹1 lakh crore over a 4-year period. Further, Jio claims it invested ₹1.6 lakh crore and installed 2.82 lakh base stations across 18,000 cities and 2 lakh villages, officials said.
