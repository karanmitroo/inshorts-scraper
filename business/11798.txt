India needs to grow at 10% to become a $10 trillion economy and eliminate poverty by 2032, Niti Aayog CEO Amitabh Kant on Thursday said. He added that a 10% growth rate would also help in the creation of 17.5 crore jobs by 2032. Notably, the country's growth rate was 7.6% in 2015-16 with an economy worth $1.7 trillion.
