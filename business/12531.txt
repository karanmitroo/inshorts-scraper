The combined market capitalisation (m-cap) of nine of the 10 most valued companies increased by ₹75,585.19 crore in the week ended February 19, as the benchmark Sensex surged 723.03 points to 23,709.15. TCS was the biggest gainer as its m-cap rose by ₹18,462.91 crore, while financial services firm HDFC was the only loser, as its valuation fell by ₹3,615.64 crore.
