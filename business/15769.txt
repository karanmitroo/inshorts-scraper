Ending the three-day losing streak, the BSE Sensex closed 271.24 points (0.9 percent) higher at 28,930.41 and Nifty went up 76.05 points (0.9 percent) to 8,776.00 on Thursday. Stocks were bullish on hopes that PM Narendra Modi would be able to steer a bill seeking to raise foreign investment limits in the insurance sector through the Rajya Sabha.
