According to Inshorts Pulse of the Nation Poll, 82% people support the government’s decision to invalidate the ₹500, ₹1000 notes, while 10% are unsupportive and 8% responded with ‘Can’t Say’. Conducted on November 8 and 9, in association with research firm IPSOS, the poll garnered over 5 lakh responses from over 2.5 lakh users on 3 questions.
