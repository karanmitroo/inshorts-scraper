Nifty Next 50 index, the next rung of liquid securities after Nifty 50, gained 118.20 points to settle at 19679.95 on Monday. The Nifty 50 and Nifty Next 50 which are filtered for liquidity, constitute the 100 most liquid stocks in India. Motherson Sumi, Aurobindo Pharma were the major gainers, while, Eicher Motors, Idea Cellular were major losers.
