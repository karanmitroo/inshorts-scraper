Out of the ₹13,000 crore black money declared under the Income Declaration Scheme (IDS) in Hyderabad, ₹10,000 crore belonged to a single individual, Andhra Pradesh CM Chandrababu Naidu said on Wednesday. Hinting towards his political rivals, Naidu added politics has become a "shelter for black money earners". Around ₹65,000 crore black money was disclosed under the scheme across the country.
