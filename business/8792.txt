The combined market capitalisation of seven of the top 10 BSE-listed firms fell by ₹46,107.96 crore in the week ended October 14, with the benchmark Sensex falling 387.54 points to close at 27,673.60. HDFC witnessed the highest decline in m-cap at ₹13,685.57 crore followed by Reliance Industries, while ONGC saw the biggest gain in m-cap at ₹7,571.61 crore.
