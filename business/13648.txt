After witnessing a seven-month high inflow by the Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPIs) in October, the FPIs have pulled out ₹8,278 crore (around $1.26 billion) from the Indian capital markets in November. According to depositories data, net outflow from equities market stood at ₹5,713 crore this month, while, ₹2,565 crore was pulled out of the debt market.
