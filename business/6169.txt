Telecom operators Bharti Airtel, Vodafone, and Idea, on Tuesday responded to rival Reliance Jio's 'Valentine's Day' wish on Twitter. Idea in its post said, "Same to you! Nice to know that love is in the air today", while Vodafone posted, "Let's all #BeSuper this Valentine's day". This comes after Jio earlier on Tuesday wished the operators a "Happy Valentine's Day".
