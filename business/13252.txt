Shares of Jet Airways, SpiceJet, and InterGlobe Aviation (IndiGo's parent company) surged over 10% in intraday trading on Friday, with those of Jet and SpiceJet touching fresh five-year highs. This comes after oil marketing companies slashed aviation turbine fuel (ATF) prices by 10% on Thursday. ATF costs account for over 30% of the total expenditure of Jet Airways and SpiceJet.
