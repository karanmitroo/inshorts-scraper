Rating agency Crisil has said the government will garner an estimated cumulative of ₹5,000 crore from the Phase III of the FM radio auctions. It is estimated to earn ₹1,900-2,000 crore through migration of 266 frequencies to phase III from phase II and ₹2,000-2,300 crore from licence fees. It earned ₹1,150 crore from 97 frequencies auctioned in the first batch.
