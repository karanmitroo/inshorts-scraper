Austrian energy drinks firm Red Bull has created 13 billionaires, the most by any company globally, according to the Hurun Global Rich List 2018. Red Bull Founder Dietrich Mateschitz has a net worth of $15 billion and is ranked 87 globally in the rich list. Chilean retailer SACI Falabella and Thai conglomerate Charoen Pokphand have created 12 billionaires each.
