Mercedes-Benz on Tuesday launched its flagship Mercedes-AMG S63 at Rs 2.53 crore. A 5.5-litre V8 Bi-Turbo petrol engine which churns out 567bhp helps the vehicle touch the 100km/h mark from standstill in 4.4 seconds with a top speed of 250km/h. With this launch, the company has rolled out 10 of the 15 new products promised for this year.
