Triggered by moderation in vegetable and fruit prices, the retail inflation, that was in double digits in Oct and Nov, eased to three-month low of 9.87% in Dec. Fruit prices rose 14.64% compared to 15% in Nov, pulses were dearer by 2.15% and cereals by 12.14%. Inflation in the food and beverages segment was 12.16% as against 14.72% in Nov.
