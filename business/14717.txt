Cab aggregator Ola on Friday said it had partnered with the Directorate General Resettlement (DGR), under Ministry of Defence, to enrol 10,000 ex-servicemen as entrepreneurs by 2020. The programme, Ola Sainik, aims to train ex-servicemen to grow as skilled micro-entrepreneurs on the Ola platform. Earlier on Thursday, e-commerce company Amazon announced a similar programme for ex-service personnel.
