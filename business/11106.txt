Four Indians have featured in Forbes' 13th ‘World’s 100 Most Powerful Women’ list announced Monday. They include SBI Chair-MD Arundhati Bhattacharya (ranked 25), ICICI Bank CEO Chanda Kochhar (40), Biocon Founder-Chair Kiran Mazumdar Shaw (77), and HT Media Chair Shobhana Bhartia (93). Further, German Chancellor Angela Merkel topped the list for the sixth consecutive year and 11 times in total.
