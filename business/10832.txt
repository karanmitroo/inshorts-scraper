I-T authorities have reportedly found undeclared income of around ₹13,000 crore from two sets of information they had received. In 2011, France gave information about at least​ 400 Indians having accounts in HSBC, Geneva, which led to the discovery of ₹8,186 crore. ICIJ, in 2013, released information about 700 Indians having alleged undisclosed income of ₹5,000 crores in foreign banks.
