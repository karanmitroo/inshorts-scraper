The benchmark indices on Friday ended on a flat note with the Sensex losing 45.07 points to settle at 28,061.14, and the Nifty dropping 11.95 points to close at 8,697.60. Market sentiments were dragged amid rise in crude oil prices. Tata Steel and Tata Motors emerged as the biggest gainers while Asian Paints and Infosys ended as the major losers.
