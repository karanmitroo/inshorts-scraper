The combined market valuation of top nine companies enlisted in the BSE Sensex tanked by ₹81,812 crore in the week ended October 30, after the index dropped by 2.96% to 26,656.83 last week. ITC was the major loser with a ₹18,863.88 crore hit in m-cap, followed by Coal India, which lost ₹13,580.18 crore, and SBI, which slumped by ₹12,148.75 crore.
