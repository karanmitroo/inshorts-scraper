Qantas Airways CEO Alan Joyce's compensation almost doubled in the last financial year, making him Asia-Pacific's highest-paid airline CEO. Joyce's total package surged to $19.7 million (₹126.23 crore) in the year through June 30, from $10.4 million (₹66.63 crore) a year earlier. Notably, under Joyce, the airline reported its second highest annual profit in August.
