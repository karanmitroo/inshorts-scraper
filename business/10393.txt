IT services firm HCL Technologies on Wednesday reported a 14.8% year-on-year rise in net profit for the quarter ended June to ₹2,047 crore. Revenue grew 15.9% to ₹11,336 crore. In dollar terms, however, the company's net profit rose 9.5% to $305.2 million, while revenue grew 10% to $1.69 billion compared to the year-ago period.
