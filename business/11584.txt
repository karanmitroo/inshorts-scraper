Minister of State of Finance Jayant Sinha said the amount owed by wilful defaulters to Public Sector Banks (PSB) till December 2015 increased to ₹66,190 crore from ₹27,749 crore in three years. The number of wilful defaulters rose from 5,554 to 7,686 in the period. Further, top 50 defaulters had an exposure of ₹1.21 trillion as on December 2015.
