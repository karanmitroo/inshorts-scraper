Fruit juice concentrate maker Rasna is eyeing a revenue of ₹250 crore in the next three years from the snack category, Chairman Piruz Khambatta said. Rasna, which has presence in the sweet snack category with its brand Vitos, is planning to introduce ethnic and western snacks. Notably, Rasna enjoys an 85% market share in the ₹1,000 crore powdered drink space.
