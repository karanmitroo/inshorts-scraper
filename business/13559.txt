Indian IT trade association Nasscom has projected an addition of 2.75 lakh jobs in the IT sector in 2015-16, compared to earlier estimates of 2.30 lakh. The industry would be able to add $20 billion to the previous year’s $148 billion when it grew by $16 billion, said Nasscom Chairman BVR Mohan Reddy. “Business will grow by 12-13%,” he added.
