The BSE Sensex on Tuesday rose by 123.43 points to close at 25,145.59, while the NSE Nifty gained 37.55 points to settle at 7,708.95, on account of buying in blue-chip stocks amid positive global cues. GAIL, Maruti Suzuki, and Tata Motors emerged as the major gainers while Coal India, Tata Steel, and HDFC Bank ended among the major losers.
