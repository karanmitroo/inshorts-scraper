Amid a strong stock market rally last week, 10 most valued companies listed in the Sensex added Rs 74,521.57 crore in market valuation. Sun Pharma emerged as the biggest gainer, as its market cap increased by Rs 14,318.55 crore to Rs 2,17,806.55 crore, followed by Reliance Industries (RIL) which added 11,026.64 crore and HDFC, which surged by Rs 10,257.25 crore.
