India’s Gross Domestic Product growth slowed to 5.3% in the July-September quarter, as against 5.7% in the previous quarter, a government data showed on Friday. The growth was, however, better  than 5.2%, posted during the same period last year. Meanwhile, RBI governor Raghuram Rajan may meet Finance Minister Arun Jaitley on Monday ahead of the central bank’s monetary policy review Tuesday.
