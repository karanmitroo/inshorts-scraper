RBI has infused new currency notes worth ₹5,000 crore in Odisha, RBI General Manager said. Around 50% of the unbanked Gram Panchayats in Odisha have been provided with the banking services. The state government suggested SBI to provide sufficient cash to branch post offices which would allow deposits up to ₹25,000 and withdrawals up to ₹5,000 per day.
