General Motors India Tuesday hiked car prices by up to Rs.61k due to rising input costs and expiry of reduced excise duty concessions. Spark and Beat hatchback's prices were raised in the range of Rs.15,739 to Rs.18,300, now costing between Rs 3.6 lakh-Rs 4.18 lakh and Rs 4.2 lakh-Rs 6.28 lakh respectively. Sail, Cruze and Tavera's prices were also hiked.
