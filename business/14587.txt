The maximum insurance cover under the Employees' Provident Fund Organisation's (EPFO) Employees' Deposit Linked Insurance Scheme (EDLI) is likely to be increased from Rs 3.6 lakh to Rs 5.5 lakh. Around 6 crore subscribers of the EPFO are likely to benefit from the scheme if it is approved by the Pension and EDLI Implementation Committee meeting on September 9.
