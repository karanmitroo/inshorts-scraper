Bharat Aluminium Company (Balco) bagged one more coal block in Chhattisgarh on the eighth day of the auction on Saturday, taking the total number of mines sold so far to 18. "Gare Palma IV-1 mine closed at Rs 1,585 (per tonne)," a coal ministry official said. Earlier, Balco had bagged Chotia mine in the same state.
