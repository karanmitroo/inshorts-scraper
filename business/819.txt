The government has announced that it has reduced additional borrowing target for this fiscal by 60% to ₹20,000 crore from the earlier announced ₹50,000 crore. The government had pegged its aggregate gross market borrowing at ₹5.8 lakh crore in the budget. Notably, the government's fiscal deficit was 112% of the ₹5.47 lakh crore target for 2017-18 from April to November.
