With Nestle’s Maggi being pulled out of stores, the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India is scanning popular brands Heinz and Kellogg’s. Authorities have reportedly written to the companies with labelling and branding queries of packaged food products like Complan Pista Badam, Heinz Easy-to-Cook Baked Beans and Kellogg's Oats and Honey, alleging mismatch in their marketing and contents.
