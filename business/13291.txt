Oil exploration and production firm Essar Oil on Wednesday announced the completion of its delisting from stock exchanges after a ₹3,745 crore payout to its shareholders, the largest to privatise a publicly-listed company in India’s history. The company’s market capitalisation, which stood at ₹2,000 crore when it got listed in 1995, was over ₹38,000 crore at the time of delisting.
