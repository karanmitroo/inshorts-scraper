Tata Steel on Tuesday reported 90.51% growth in net-profit with Rs.1,139-crore for the quarter(Q1) ended June-30, compared to last year's Rs.597.88-crore. The growth is an outcome of reduced tax outgo and robust performances by Indian and European operations, said the officials. However, company's net-sales decreased by 3% to Rs.32,550.21-crore, compared to last Q1's Rs.33,547.73-crore, due to subdued global steel demand.
