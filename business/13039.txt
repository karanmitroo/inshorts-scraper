Saddled with debt worth ₹40,000 crore, Air India has reportedly sought nearly ₹4,300 crore from the government, as part of the Budget wishlist for the 2016-17 fiscal. The airline is surviving on a ₹30,231 crore bailout package extended by the UPA government in 2012. Under the Turnaround Plan (TAP), which runs till March 2021, it has already received ₹22,280 crore.
