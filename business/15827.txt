As per the Economic Survey 2014-15, which was tabled by finance minister Arun Jaitley in Parliament Friday, the Indian economy is expected to grow 8.1-8.5% in the fiscal 2015-16. The survey estimated 257.07 million tonnes foodgrain production for the fiscal 2014-15, exceeding that of last fiscal by 8.5 million tonnes. It further added that inflation was showing a declining trend.
