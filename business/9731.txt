Petronet LNG has posted a 54.7% jump in its net profit at ₹377.86 crore from the year-ago period for the April-June quarter. However, its total income from operations during the period fell to ₹5,337.31 crore as against ₹8,377.71 crore a year ago. Petronet was formed as a joint venture by the Centre to import LNG and set up LNG terminals.
