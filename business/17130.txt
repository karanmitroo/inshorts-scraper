The government has slapped an additional penalty of USD 579 million (Rs 3,500 crore) on Reliance Industries for producing less than targeted natural gas from its KG-D6 block, Oil Minister Dharmendra Pradhan said on Monday. With this, the total penalty on RIL for missing the target in four fiscal years now stands at a cumulative $2.376 billion (Rs 14,300 crore).
