Snapchat Co-founders Evan Spiegel and Bobby Murphy are worth ₹27,000 crore ($4 billion) each, according to Forbes. Each of them owns a 22.4% stake in Snap, the parent company of Snapchat, which is currently valued at ₹1.21 lakh crore ($18 billion). Snapchat has filed for IPO and hopes to hit ₹1.70 lakh crore ($25 billion) in valuation.
