Torrent Pharmaceuticals, a pharma giant based in Ahmedabad, has completed acquisition of Zyg Pharma, a company that manufactures various dermatological formulations like ointments and lotions. Torrent Pharma had entered into an agreement to purchase 100% stake in Zyg Pharma, whose entire equity was transferred on July 17. The deal size is being pegged at Rs 350 crore.
