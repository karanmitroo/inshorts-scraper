Two Indian-origin women have been featured on Forbes' annual list of ‘America's Richest Self-Made Women’. IT consultancy Syntel Co-founder Neerja Sethi is ranked 16th with a networth of $1.1 billion, while computer networking firm Arista Networks CEO Jayshree Ullal is ranked 30th with a $470 million networth. The list has been topped by ABC Supply owner Diane Hendricks ($4.9 billion).
