Nifty FMCG, which tracks the performance of the top 15 Nifty companies manufacturing FMCG (Fast Moving Consumer Goods) products, gained 482.55 points (2.41%) to close at 20469.10 points on Tuesday. Tata Global Beverages, ITC, Jubilant Foodworks, and United Breweries emerged among the top gainers while Colgate-Palmolive and Godrej Industries were the top losers.
