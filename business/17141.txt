Sunil Kakkad, chairman of Ahmedabad-based Sai InfoSystems (India), who had been on the run after allegedly cheating 1,400 employees as well as defaulting on bank loans to the tune of Rs 1,000-1,200 crore, was arrested in Liberia. He was brought to Ahmedabad from Liberia early on Monday by the Crime Branch. Notably, SIS was the largest IT company in Gujarat.
