Indian exports rose by 9.59% year-on-year in dollar terms in October, which were valued at $23,512.70 million (₹1.5 lakh crore) for the month. Further, the country's imports during the month stood at $33,673.53 million (₹2.2 lakh crore), 8.11% higher than the corresponding month a year back. India's oil imports also surged 3.98% from the year-ago period to $7,141.48 million.
