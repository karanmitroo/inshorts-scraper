UB Group chairman Vijay Mallya's son Sidhartha Mallya has resigned as a non-executive, non-independent director of United Breweries (Holdings), effective from March 31, the company said on Friday. Earlier, Vijay Mallya had appealed to people on Twitter not to attack his son in the ongoing Kingfisher Airlines' loan default case. Notably, Mallya owns a majority 52.34% stake in the company.
