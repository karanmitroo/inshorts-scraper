Asia’s largest manufacturer of irrigation equipment, Jain Irrigation Systems on Friday announced its plan to raise ₹792 crore by issuing equity shares in the company and its food subsidiary Jain Farm Fresh Foods (JFFFL) to agri-business investment firm Mandala Capital and promoters. Mandala would acquire a 14.08% or 17.13% equity stake in JFFFL, depending on the company’s performance.
