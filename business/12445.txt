The Union Budget announced today suggested that the income tax slabs for next fiscal will remain unchanged. The limit on tax rebate for people earning below ₹5 lakh was raised to ₹5,000 from ₹2,000. Further, a tax relief of ₹50,000 for first time home buyers was announced for up to ₹35 lakh loan, provided the house costs under ₹50 lakh.
