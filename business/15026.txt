The Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers (SIAM) has released the automobile sales figures for June 2015. The data showed a rise of 1.53% in domestic passenger car sales from 1,60,232 units in June 2014 to 1,62,677 units in June 2015. Further, two-wheeler sales surged by 3.55% to 13,07,710 units while commercial vehicles sale increased marginally to 51,446 units.
