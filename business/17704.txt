The BSE Sensex and Nifty rallied to record highs on Tuesday. The broader Nifty ended up 0.19% after earlier gaining as much as 1.1% to hit a record high of 6,574.95 points, surpassing its previous record high hit on Mar-11. The BSE Sensex added 0.1% after earlier rising as much as 1.1% to an all-time high of 22,040.72 points.
