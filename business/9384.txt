The benchmark indices on Wednesday ended the trade on a flat note with the Sensex falling 15.78 points to settle at 28,507.42. Meanwhile, the broader Nifty gained 1.25 points to close at 8,777.15. Tata Steel, Hindustan Unilever, and Axis Bank emerged as the top gainers of the day while PowerGrid, ITC, and SBI ended as the major losers.
