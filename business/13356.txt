Indian technology companies “paid $22.5 billion in taxes in the US during FY2011-15”, quoting a report by IT trade body Nasscom, Commerce Minister Nirmala Sitharaman on Monday informed the Parliament. The report also said that the Indian tech industry "invested more than $2 billion in USA during FY2011-13… and supported 4,11,000 jobs in FY2015 directly or indirectly”.
