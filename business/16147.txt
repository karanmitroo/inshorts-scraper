ITC on Wednesday reported an over 10% rise in net profit at Rs 2,635 crore for the third quarter ended December 31, 2014. The diversified conglomerate had posted a profit of Rs 2,385.34 crore in the corresponding period a year earlier. However, cigarettes revenue rose only 0.6% year-on-year to Rs 4,142 crore, dented by price hikes and excise duty.
