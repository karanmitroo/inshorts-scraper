Rising for the fourth straight session, the BSE benchmark Sensex on Friday rose 129.36 points to hit a new high of 21,293.88 on sustained foreign fund inflows in banking, auto, metal, and realty sectors and the RBI easing liquidity situation by cutting marginal standing facility (MSF) to 8.75%. The NSE index Nifty also rose by 33.45 points to 6,332.60.
