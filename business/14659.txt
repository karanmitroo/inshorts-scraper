The BSE Sensex fell 1000 points to 26,359.53 during intra-day trade on Monday and Nifty declined 244 points to 8055.95, owing to heavy selloff in China. Shanghai shares too tanked 7.7 percent, hitting a five-month low. The Indian rupee hit a 2-year low of 66.47 against the US dollar. Tata Motors, Axis Bank, Vedanta and GAIL were the major losers.
