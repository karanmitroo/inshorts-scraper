India imported more than 528 tonnes of gold (worth ₹1.12 lakh crore) and exported 76 tonnes in April-September 2015, Minister of State for Finance Jayant Sinha said on Wednesday. Replying to a question in the Rajya Sabha, he added that gold imports and exports stood at 915 tonnes (worth ₹2.10 lakh crore) and 70 tonnes respectively, in the year-ago period.
