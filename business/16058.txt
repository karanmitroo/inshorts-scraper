The Sensex plunged by 498.82 points to close at 29,182.95 on Friday, pulled lower by losses in shares of banking sector. The 30-share index touched an all-time high of 29,844.16 and a low of 29,070.48 in trade. Tracking the momentum, the 50-share Nifty ended at 8,808.90, down 143 points from its previous close.
