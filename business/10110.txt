The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty on Thursday ended the trade with gains, with the Sensex rising 118.07 points to settle at 28,123.44, and the Nifty gaining 49.20 points to close at 8,673.25, amid mixed global cues. Adani Ports, PowerGrid, and ICICI Bank emerged as the biggest gainers, while L&T, Tata Steel, and Coal India ended as the major losers.
