T Suvarna Raju has taken the charge as Chairman of Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) from RK Tyagi, who retired on Saturday. 56-year-old Raju, who hails from Andhra Pradesh, joined Indian state-owned aerospace and defence company HAL in June 1980 as management trainee after graduating from IIT in Chennai. Raju had also drafted HAL's first research and development (R&D) policy.
