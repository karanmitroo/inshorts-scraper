Shares of Mukesh Ambani-led Reliance Industries surged over 3% during intraday trade on Friday to hit a nine-year high and added as much as ₹16,104 crore to its market valuation. Reliance's scrip was the top performer among the 30-Sensex components with the company's valuation hitting ₹4,84,743.42 crore. Notably, the company’s stock has gained around 38% so far this year.
