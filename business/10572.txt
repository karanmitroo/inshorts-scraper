The benchmark indices on Tuesday ended with marginal gains with the Sensex rising 40.96 points to end at 27,787.62, and the broader Nifty gaining 19.85 points to settle at 8,528.55. ICICI Bank, PowerGrid, and Lupin ended as the biggest gainers, while HDFC Bank, Hindustan Unilever, and Axis Bank were amongst the major losers.
