Foreign institutional investors (FIIs) have pumped in nearly Rs.7,000 crore ($1.12 billion) in the Indian equity market this month till now. The total foreign investment in the country's stock market has reached to Rs 80,174 crore ($14.77 billion) so far in 2013. FIIs have boosted the benchmark S&P BSE Sensex by 1,503 points(7.75%) this month, to settle at 20,882.89 points.
