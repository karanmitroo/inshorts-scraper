Consumer goods company Hindustan Unilever on Monday reported a 7% year-on-year rise in net profit for the quarter ended March to ₹1,089 crore. Revenue from operations rose 3.52% to ₹7,945 crore. The profit rise was attributed to better sales in the company's hair care, skin care and packaged food products. Further, the company announced a dividend of ₹9.50 per share.
