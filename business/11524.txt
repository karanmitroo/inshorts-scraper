Domestic passenger car sales rose 1.87% to 1.62 lakh units in April, from 1.59 lakh units in the corresponding period last year, according to data released by the Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers (SIAM). During the same period, sales of two-wheelers rose by 21.23%, commercial vehicles grew 17.36% and vehicles across all categories increased by 20.04%, SIAM said.
