The government on Saturday hiked the excise duty on Petrol by 37 paise, and on Diesel by ₹2 per litre. With this, the total levy on petrol increased to ₹19.73 per litre and on diesel surged to ₹13.83 per litre. The government is reportedly expected to garner ₹4,300 crore with this present hike in excise duty.
