The BSE Sensex on Wednesday went down by 213.68 points (0.8%) to close at 27,039.76 points and the Nifty fell 61.70 points (0.7%) to 8,171.20. As many as 1161 shares advanced, 1489 declined and 186 remained unchanged. Cipla, Bharti, ONGC, Tata Motors and HUL were the top gainers while Axis Bank, ICICI Bank, SBI, NTPC and Lupin were major losers.
