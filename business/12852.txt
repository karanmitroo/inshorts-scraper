The combined market capitalisation (m-cap) of the top ten companies listed on the BSE Sensex increased by over ₹1.02 lakh crore in the week ended January 29, with the index rising by 435 points to 24,870.69. Sun Pharmaceutical Industries and Tata Consultancy Services witnessed the maximum gains, with boosts of ₹19,962 crore and ₹18,906 crore to their market capitalisation respectively.
