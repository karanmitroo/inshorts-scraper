The benchmark indices on Tuesday ended with gains as the Sensex rose by 520.91 points to close at 28,050.88, while the broader Nifty gained 157.50 points to close at 8,677.90. Adani Ports SEZ, ICICI Bank, and HDFC emerged as the day's biggest gainers while ONGC, Asian Paints, and Hero MotoCorp were the major losers.
