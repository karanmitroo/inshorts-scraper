Nearly ₹180 crore was lost in 2,632 cases of robbery, theft, dacoity, and burglaries at India's 51 banks in the last three financial years, Minister of State for Finance Santosh Gangwar said. Among these, India's largest lender SBI lost ₹30 crore in 344 incidents. Notably, RBI earlier said banks need not compensate customers for theft of articles from bank lockers.
