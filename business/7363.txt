The Chief Executive Officer of a Jaipur co-operative bank, Keshav Badaya, went missing after officials of the Income Tax department raided several Jaipur-based branches of the bank. As much as ₹1.56 crore in cash and jewellery worth ₹50 lakh have been seized from the bank's premises. Some bank officials said Badaya was missing since the raids began on Friday night.
