The BSE Sensex on Tuesday fell by 111.89 points to close at 27,166.87, while the NSE Nifty lost 34.75 points to settle at 8,335.95, on account of profit-booking by investors amid mixed global cues. Coal India, Tata Steel and Reliance Industries ended as the major gainers while GAIL, Bharti Airtel and NTPC ended among the major losers.
