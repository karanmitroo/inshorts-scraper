Sumit D Chowdhury, president of Telecom operator Reliance Jio Infocomm, has resigned from his position in the company, in order to start his personal venture. Chowdhury said that the venture is "in the field of smart cities". His resignation marks the third high-profile exit from Reliance Jio. Chowdhury joined RJio in 2012 and was responsible for several noteworthy strategies.
