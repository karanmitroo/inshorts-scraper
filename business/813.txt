Private sector lender Yes Bank on Thursday posted a 22% year-on-year rise in profit at ₹1,076.8 crore for the December quarter, compared to ₹882.6 crore a year earlier. The bank's net interest income rose 26.8% to ₹1,888.8 crore. As a percentage of total loans, gross bad loans decreased to 1.72% as compared to 1.82% in the previous quarter.
