In yet another real-estate deal, Sahara Group has acquired Britain's renowned Oakley Court, a gothic heritage country house, for 27-million pounds. Popularly called the Dracula's Den since featuring in 'Dracula' and other films, it will be used as the group's UK headquarters, said Sahara chief Subrata Roy. Earlier, he made headlines after buying Grosvenor House Hotel for 470-million pounds.
