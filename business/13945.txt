Rakesh Jhunjhunwala’s RARE Enterprises on Friday bought 11,95,000 shares or 1.05% stake in Jet Airways for ₹50.52 crore at an average price of ₹422.76 through an open market transaction. Following the transaction, the shares of the company rose 11.28% to close at ₹445.40 on the NSE. Previously Jhunjhunwala also picked up Indigo shares from InterGlobe Aviation Initial Public Offer (IPO).
