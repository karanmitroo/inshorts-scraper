The government raised ₹4,177 crore during the third day of the biggest-ever spectrum auction, taking the total bids to ₹60,969 crore. The telecom operators stayed away from the high-cost 700 Mhz and 900 Mhz bands again on Tuesday. Airwaves, valued at ₹5.63 lakh crore at base price, have been put up for auction in this round.
