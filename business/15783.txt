Nifty rose by 39.50 points on Tuesday, closing at a record high of 8,996.25, while Sensex gained 134.59 points, ending at 29,593.73. As many as 1,667 shares advanced and 1,188 went down. TCS and Reliance were top contributors to Nifty. Cipla, Bajaj Auto and HDFC were the main gainers on Sensex, while losers included Coal India, M&M and Axis Bank.
