Foreign investors pumped in Rs 15,500 crore ($2.5 billion) in Indian equities this month, ahead of the tapering of US Federal Reserve's stimulus programme starting in January. So far in 2013, foreign institutional investors (FIIs) have invested Rs.1.13 lakh crore ($20 billion) in the domestic stock market. As of Dec-27, the number of registered FIIs in India stood at 1,742.
