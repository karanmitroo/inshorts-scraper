InterGlobe Aviation, owner of IndiGo airlines on Monday raised ₹3,010 crore via initial public offering at an issue price of ₹765, upper end of the ₹700-₹765 price band. The biggest IPO in three years witnessed an over-subscription of 6.15 times, including 17.80 times over-subscription by qualified institutional buyers. The company will make its market debut later this month.
