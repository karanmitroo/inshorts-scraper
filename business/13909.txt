IT services firm Tech Mahindra reported a net profit of ₹785.63 crore in the quarter ended September, a 9.1% rise against ₹719.64 crore the company posted in the corresponding period the previous fiscal. The company's revenue increased by 20.54% to ₹6,615.54 crore in the quarter under review, against ₹5,487.90 crore revenue last year.
