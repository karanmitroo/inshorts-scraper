The shares of Tata Steel and Tata Global fell by over 2% to drag down the Sensex from a three-week high, following the removal of Tata Sons Chairman Cyrus Mistry, reports said. "[G]iven its (Tata's) size, it's pulling down the index," an expert said. Notably, Tata is India's largest conglomerate with over $100 billion combined capitalisation.
