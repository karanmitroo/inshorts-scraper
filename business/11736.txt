According to media reports citing the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), the central bank's Governor Raghuram Rajan, with a monthly compensation of ₹1,98,700 during June-July 2015, was not the highest paid person in the organisation. His salary was exceeded by at least 3 officials in the organisation who earned ₹4 lakh, ₹2.2 lakh and ₹2.2 lakh in remuneration.
