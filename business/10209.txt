Prime Minister Narendra Modi has been featured on professional networking site LinkedIn's list of 'Most Viewed CEOs in India for 2016'. Further, Biocon Chairman and Managing Director Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw has also been featured on the list, along with Tata Power's Anil Sardana, and Accenture in India's Rekha M Menon. Notably, Modi has also become the most followed Indian on Twitter.
