The BSE Sensex on Friday closed on a flat note at 26,160.90 points, up by 43.36  points, in the absence of any major cues. Meanwhile, the NSE Nifty gained 16.85 points to settle at 7,963.20. Coal India, Tata Motors, HDFC and Bharti Airtel emerged among the major gainers, while Axis Bank, Hero MotoCorp and NTPC ended among the major losers.
