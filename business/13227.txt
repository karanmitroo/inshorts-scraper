According to a report by VCCEdge, India Inc's Merger and Acquisition (M&A) deals in 2015 dipped 31% to $22.9 billion as against 2014. However, the total number of transactions in 2015 stood at 938 deals, as compared to a total of 892 deals in 2014. Healthcare, financials, telecommunications, information technology and materials witnessed the highest M&A activity.
