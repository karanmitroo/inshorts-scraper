The benchmark indices on Friday ended with marginal gains as the Sensex rose 38.43 points to settle at 27,865.96, while the broader Nifty gained 19.90 points to close at 8,611.15 amid weak global cues. GAIL, M&M, and ONGC were the top gainers, whereas Cipla, ITC, and Coal India were among the major losers of the day.
