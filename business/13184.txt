The BSE Sensex on Thursday plunged 554.50 points to close at a 19-month low of 24,851.83. Meanwhile, the NSE Nifty dropped to a four-month low of 7,568.30, down by 172.70 points. The slump was triggered by a massive sell-off in Chinese stock markets, a drop in Brent Crude prices to a fresh 11-year low and subsequent negative global cues.
