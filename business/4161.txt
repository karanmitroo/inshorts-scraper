HDFC Bank MD Aditya Puri saw his remuneration rise to ₹10 crore in 2016-17, while Yes Bank MD Rana Kapoor took home ₹6.8 crore. ICICI Bank’s Chanda Kochhar received a total remuneration of ₹6.09 crore in the fiscal. In comparison, India's largest lender SBI's Chief Arundhati Bhattacharya made only ₹28.96 lakh during the period.
