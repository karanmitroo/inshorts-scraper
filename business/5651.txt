The Managing Director of Idea, Himanshu Kapania, has said that integrating operations of Vodafone and Idea will cost the combined entity around ₹13,400 crore. However, he said the synergies leading to cost saving will result in a net benefit of ₹65,000 crore for the merged entity. Idea and Vodafone confirmed their merger on Monday to form India's largest telecom firm.
