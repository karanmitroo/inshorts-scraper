According to a study by Assocham, the revenue drawn by the E-commerce industry in 2016 is likely to hit $38 billion, as against a $23 billion revenue in 2015. The study, which revealed that Mumbai is ranked first in online shopping (followed by Delhi, Ahmedabad, Bengaluru and Kolkata), suggested 33% customers in Tier-1 and Tier-2 cities make transactions through mobiles.
