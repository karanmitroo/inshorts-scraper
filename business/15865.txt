Foreign direct investment (FDI) in India almost doubled to $2.16 billion in December 2014, as against $1.10 billion in the year-ago period. During the April-December period of current fiscal, FDI rose by 27% to $21.04 billion, compared to $16.56 billion in the year-ago period. Amongst the top 10 sectors, telecom received the maximum FDI of $2.67 billion during April-December.
