With a majority of 99.52% votes,  Hindustan Unilever's shareholders have approved the appointment of Sanjiv Mehta as its managing director and chief executive for a period of five years, with effect from 10 October 2013. In July, Sanjiv Mehta was appointed as MD  by HUL. Mehta replaced Nitin Paranjpe who will take up a global role at parent Unilever.
