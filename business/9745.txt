The Enforcement Directorate on Saturday attached Vijay Mallya’s assets worth ₹6,630 crore in connection to the money laundering case against him and his associates. The agency has so far attached assets worth over ₹8,000 crore. ED claimed that Mallya "criminally conspired" with Kingfisher Airlines and United Breweries to obtain funds from banks, of which ₹4,930.34 crore "still remains unpaid."
