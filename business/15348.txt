After a 195% surge in sales compared to last year, Toyota Corolla reclaimed the top spot in executive sedan segment in India from Hyundai Elantra for 2014-15. The world's most selling car, Toyota Corolla had launched a new model last year which enhanced its sales in 2014-15 to 9,137 units compared to Hyundai Elantra's 2,938 units in the same period.
