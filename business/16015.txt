German industrial group Siemens plans to cut over 7,000 jobs worldwide as part of a reorganization under Chief Executive Joe Kaeser, the Reuters reported on Thursday. About 3,000 of those jobs will be in Germany alone, a source said. Siemens had been expected to announce 10,000 job cuts Friday. Siemens employs 343,000 people worldwide, of whom 115,000 are in Germany.
