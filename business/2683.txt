Leading FMCG companies, including Hindustan Unilever, Coca-Cola, Emami, and PepsiCo, have been pulled up by Advertising Standards Council for misleading advertisements in June. It pulled up Coca-Cola India for its campaigns for Maaza, Fanta, Sprite, and Thums Up, saying disclaimers in ads "were not legible". Similarly, ASCI found HUL's ad for its ayurvedic shampoo 'Ayush' to be misleading.
