Indian automobile company Mahindra & Mahindra on Thursday launched a new version of its lifestyle off-road vehicle Thar at Rs 8.03 lakh. It is powered by a 2500cc CRDe engine with a high ground clearance of 200mm and a torque of 274Nm. It has re-designed A/C vents, windshield demister, new floor console and new door trims among other features.
