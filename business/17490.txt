Budget carrier SpiceJet on Wednesday announced another sale offer with all inclusive fares as low as Rs 1,899 (specifically for eastern cities such as Agartala, Bagdogra, Guwahati and Kolkata). For cities such as Ahmedabad, Varanasi, Chandigarh and Coimbatore, fares start at Rs 1,999. Booking dates are till May 2 for travel between July 1 and September 30.
