HDFC Bank on Monday slashed its lending rate by 0.25% to 9.65%, with effect from Tuesday. The lending rate for women would be 9.60%. The reduction will also be applicable on loans to Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/ PIO card holders, HDFC said in a statement. Meanwhile, Corporation bank reduced its base rate to 9.70% from 9.90%, with effect from Thursday.
