Hindustan Zinc (HZL) on Wednesday announced that it will pay ₹10,141 crore as dividend to its shareholders for the 2015-16 fiscal. “This is the highest ever dividend announced by a private company,” HZL CEO Sunil Duggal said. He further informed that the firm’s board has declared a special golden jubilee dividend at 1,200% – ₹24 per equity share of ₹2.
