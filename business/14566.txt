With a brand value of $12.6 billion, HDFC Bank has topped the list of 50 most valuable brands in India, according to the WPP-Millward Brown's BrandZ rankings released on Wednesday. HDFC Bank is followed by Airtel ($11 billion), State Bank of India ($9.4 billion) and ICICI Bank ($5.1 billion). Thirteen of the total brands are banks and financial services firms.
