Consulting and IT company Infosys has become third most valued company in terms of market value, outperforming banking and financial services company, HDFC Bank. Market valuation of Infosys touched Rs 2,69,489.03 crore on Thursday, which is Rs 432.68 crore more than HDFC Bank's Rs 2,69,056.35 crore. Also, shares of Infosys touched one-year-high mark at Rs 1,197 per share on Thursday.
