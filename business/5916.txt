On the 178th birth anniversary of Tata Group Founder Jamsetji Nusserwanji Tata, Ratan Tata in a letter to employees, thanked them for being a part of "the House of Tata". "You bring your zeal, your vigor, your integrity, and most importantly your hard work," he wrote. "As employees...you are the inheritors and custodians of the trusts," Tata added.
