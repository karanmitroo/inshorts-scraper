Enforcement Directorate (ED) on Saturday searched various premises linked to Karti Chidambaram, son of Congress leader P Chidambaram, in relation to the Aircel-Maxis money laundering case. The case pertains to the then Finance Minister P Chidambaram approving the Foreign Direct Investment of over ₹3,500 crore in Aircel-Maxis while having the authority to grant approval up to ₹600 crore only.
