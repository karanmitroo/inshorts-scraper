Maruti Suzuki India and Hyundai Motor India reported their best-ever monthly sales in October. Maruti posted domestic sales of 1,21,063 units, a 24.7% increase over 97,069 units sold in October 2014. Hyundai witnessed sales of 47,015 units, 23.7% higher than 38,010 units last year. Honda, Ford, Mahindra & Mahindra and Tata Motors also reported a rise in car sales.
