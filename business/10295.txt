The benchmark indices on Tuesday fell after the RBI kept its policy rates unchanged and retained the 7.6% growth forecast. The BSE Sensex lost 97.41 points to end at 28,085.16 while the NSE Nifty fell by 33.10 points to close at 8,678.25. Coal India and ONGC were the major gainers while Lupin and HDFC were the biggest losers.
