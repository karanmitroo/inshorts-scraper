BSE Sensex on Friday surged over 467 points, nearly a three-year high, on across the board buying in bluechips, on easing concerns over the US tapering and a pick-up in China's economic growth. The 30-share BSE index rose to 20,932.23 and ended 467.38 points or 2.29% to 20,882.89. This is the highest closing level since 20,932.48 on November 9, 2010.
