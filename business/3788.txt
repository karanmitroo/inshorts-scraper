IT major Infosys is going to relocate its CFO MD Ranganath to the US, its largest market, and also revise his compensation package. Ranganath’s salary will comprise a fixed pay of ₹4.44 crore, up from ₹2.84 crore last fiscal. He will also receive variable compensation of up to ₹3.6 crore and would be eligible for stock incentives based on performance.
