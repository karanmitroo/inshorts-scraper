Investor wealth soared by over Rs 16 lakh crore to Rs 90.19 lakh crore in the first quarter of 2014-15 because of rising stock prices. Experts said the BJP's landslide victory in elections coupled with expectations of strong economic reforms have propelled the stock market ahead of the next week's Budget. It was Rs 74,15,000 crore as on Mar-31, 2014.
