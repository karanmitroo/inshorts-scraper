Total investor wealth, measured in terms of cumulative market valuation of all listed stocks, has tanked by ₹4,45,395 crore to ₹97,03,605 crore this fiscal so far. The benchmark BSE Sensex has fallen 7.47% this fiscal to 25,866.95 as on November 11. Total market capitalisation of the BSE-listed firms stood at ₹101.49 lakh crore as on March 31.
