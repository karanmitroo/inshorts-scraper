The BSE Sensex on Thursday fell 134.09 points (0.5%) to close at 28,370.84 and Nifty went down by 43.70 points (0.5%) to 8,589.80. As many as 1,525 shares increased, 1316 shares declined and 167 share remained unchanged. Tata Motors, Dr Reddy's Labs and M&M were major gainers, while Lupin, Bajaj Auto, Tata Steel and Bharti Airtel were major losers.
