Securities brokerage firm Indiabulls Ventures stock has gained 890% this year, becoming world’s best-performing stock among companies that have a market value of at least $1 billion. The company's profit for the June quarter more than doubled to about ₹50.3 crore ($7.84 million) from a year ago. Indiabulls Ventures now has market value of ₹8,980 crore.
