Beverage giant Coca-Cola on Monday announced that it will launch milk-based beverages under the brand name 'Vio' starting January 26. Initially, the drinks will exclusively be available in Reliance Retail's stores and will make their debut across the country next month. Vio, which was launched in the US in 2009, will be available in 'Kesar Treat' and 'Almond Delight' variants.
