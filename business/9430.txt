India's largest operator Airtel on Sunday said it has rolled out over 20,000 units of Aadhaar based e-know your customer (KYC) solution across India and also plans to have the facility deployed in over 5,00,000 retail outlets, including rural pockets. On a daily basis, over 50,000 subscribers are activating their Airtel mobile SIMs using Aadhaar-based digital verification, the company stated.
