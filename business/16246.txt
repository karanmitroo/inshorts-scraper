Beverage giant Coca-Cola is cutting up to 1,600 to 1,800 jobs globally as part of its cost trimming strategy, the company said Thursday. The Atlanta-based company begun notifying workers in the US and some international locations on Thursday. According to FactSet, Coca-Cola, which manufactures Sprite, Powerade, VitaminWater and other drinks, has about 130,600 employees around the world.
