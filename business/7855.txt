India's forex reserves declined $1.19 billion to stand at $367.04 billion during the week ended November 11, following a fall in foreign currency assets (FCA), the RBI data showed. FCA, which constitute the biggest share, dropped $1.15 billion to $342.77 billion. Meanwhile, gold reserves remained steady at $20.46 billion and the special drawing rights with the IMF fell $13.4 million.
