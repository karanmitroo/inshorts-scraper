Department of Disinvestments reportedly wants the budgeted target of the government of raising ₹69,500 crore via disinvestments this fiscal to be brought down to ₹30,000 crore amid volatility in markets. The government currently has been able to sell its stake in four companies amounting to ₹12,600 crore. Notably, for five consecutive years, the government has missed its disinvestments targets.
