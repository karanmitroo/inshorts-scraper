JK Tyre is set to acquire Cavendish Industries, a Haridwar-based tyre manufacturing unit owned by BK Birla’s Kesoram Industries, for ₹2,200 crore. JK Tyre will invest ₹450 crore in the deal and draw the remaining funds from other entities of the JK group. Post this, the company is expected to enter into the two- and three-wheeler tyre market.
