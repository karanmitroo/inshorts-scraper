Airtel has said that due to 'tsunami of calls' coming from Jio, Airtel loses 21 paisa every minute amounting to ₹550 crore per quarter. Jio is seeking an end to interconnection usage charge to transfer its own costs of ₹20,000 crore a year to other operators, Airtel said. Airtel further accused Jio of using “sinister design” to create a monopoly.
