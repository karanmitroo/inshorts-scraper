Harley Davidson India opened 'Tiger Harley-Davidson' dealership in Indore, its tenth-dealership in the country and first in the Central India, which will offer the entire Harley-Davidson India line-up of 12-motorcycle models in the prices range of Rs 5,83,409-25,09,431. In India, Harley Davidson sold around 2,000 premium segment bikes during 2010-2012 and  holds more than 50% market-share in above 500cc segment.
