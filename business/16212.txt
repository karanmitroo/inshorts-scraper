Yoga guru Baba Ramdev's 'Patanjali Ayurved', the company that manufactures products ranging from soaps and mustard oil to cornflakes, is expected to clock a turnover of Rs 2,000 crore in the current fiscal, according to sources. The company had clocked a turnover of about Rs 1,200 crore in 2014 fiscal year, up from about Rs 850 crore a year earlier.
