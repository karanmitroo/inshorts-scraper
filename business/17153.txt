Reliance Group chairman, Anil Dhirubhai Ambani, Monday called on Telangana CM, K Chandrasekhara Rao and showed interest in investing in the newly-created state, said officials. Ambani expressed his willingness to invest in electricity generation, infrastructure, industrialisation and media sectors. This was the first meeting by any key industrialist with Rao since he took over as the first CM of Telangana.
