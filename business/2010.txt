Software services giant HCL Technologies on Tuesday posted an 8.6% year-on-year rise in profit to ₹2,188 crore for the quarter ended September 30. The revenue of the company also increased by 7.9% to ₹12,434 crore. The company's verticals like manufacturing and financial services, which together contribute to 60% of their revenues, grew at 21.9% and 14.2% respectively.
