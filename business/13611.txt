According to a study by Global Mobile operator association GSMA, the contribution of mobile services sector is expected to increase to 8.2% of India's GDP or ₹14 lakh crore by 2020 compared to 6.1% (₹7.7 lakh crore) contributed in 2014. Notably, the industry provided around 4 million jobs in 2014, which is expected to grow to 5 million in 2020.
