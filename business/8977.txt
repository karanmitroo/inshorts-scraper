The benchmark indices on Monday ended flat as the Sensex rose 21.20 points to settle at 28,082.34, while the Nifty gained 11.20 points to close at 8,708.80. Markets remained upbeat following NITI Aayog Vice-Chairman's statement that India can become a $10 trillion economy in 15 years. Tata Steel was the top gainer, while NTPC was the major loser.
