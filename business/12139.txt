The government earned a total of ₹190.23 crore from the entrance fee of the monuments managed by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) during 2013-15. Taj Mahal earned the highest with ₹43.1 crore. Further, 16 monuments from Uttar Pradesh contributed ₹83.5 crore while 10 monuments from Delhi contributed ₹48.15 crore. The ASI manages 116 different monuments in 19 states.
