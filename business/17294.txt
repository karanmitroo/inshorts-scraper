Ernst & Young has named Uday Kotak, MD of Kotak Mahindra Bank, as the ‘world entrepreneur of the year’ for 2014. Kotak was chosen from among 60 finalists from 51 countries, each of whom were named ‘entrepreneur of the year’ in home country. The 55-yr-old is the second Indian to win the award, 1st was NR Narayana Murthy in 2003.
