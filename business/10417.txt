Maruti Suzuki India (MSI) has hiked prices of several models including the new compact SUV Vitara Brezza by up to ₹20,000, starting Monday. MSI said the price hike will be between ₹1,500 and ₹5,000 for a selected range. "This price revision is based on factors like segment-wise demand, forex movements and strategic objectives of the company," MSI added.
