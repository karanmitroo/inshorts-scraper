The CBI on Tuesday detained Vipul Chitalia, Vice-President (Banking Operations) of Mehul Choksi-owned Gitanjali Group, at Mumbai Airport for questioning over the $2-billion PNB fraud case. The CBI is reportedly checking if Chitalia was a signatory authority at any stage while obtaining the Letters of Undertakings fraudulently. So far, at least 18 arrests have been made in the case.
