Hero MotoCorp (HMC) will acquire 49.2% equity in US-based motorcycle company Erik Buell Racing for $25-million (about Rs. 148-crore).  "This is the first time that HMC is buying a stake in a company outside India," HMC MD and CEO Pawan Munjal told. He also said HMC would soon enter the African market, starting with Kenya, Burkinafso and Ivory Coast.
