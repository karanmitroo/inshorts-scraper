CBI on Wednesday arrested the co-founder and MD of Micromax, Rajesh Aggarwal, and another senior executive of the company named Manish Tuli, for allegedly bribing 4-engineers of NDMC and DDA to obtain clearance for making a banquet hall in Wazirpur. The 4-engineers have also been detained along with 2-beldars  who were reportedly given the task of receiving the kickback.
