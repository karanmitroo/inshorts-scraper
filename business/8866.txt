According to Hurun's report on the richest people in China, 95% of the country's billionaires are self-made, with only 5% inheriting their wealth. This year's rich list rose by 179 to 2056 individuals, double that from three years ago and up ten-fold from ten years ago, the report added. Dalian Wanda head Wang Jianlin is China's richest person in 2016.
