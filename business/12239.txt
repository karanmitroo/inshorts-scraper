The government on Wednesday hiked the petrol price by ₹3.07/litre and increased the diesel price by ₹1.90/litre, effective midnight. Petrol in Delhi will now cost ₹59.68/litre while diesel will cost ₹48.33/litre. "The current level of international product prices of petrol & diesel and INR-USD exchange rate warrant increase in price of petrol and diesel", state-owned Indian Oil Corporation said.
