Riding on the strategy of focusing on first time buyers and rural markets, country's largest carmaker Maruti Suzuki sold 2,41,812 units of passenger cars and utility vehicles out of an industry total of 5,73,038 units in the April-June period, garnering 42.19% market share, according to latest SIAM data. In June, the company's market share rose to 45% with 91,226 cars.
