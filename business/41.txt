Infosys has granted 1.13 lakh Restricted Stock Units (RSUs) worth ₹13 crore to its new CEO, Salil Parekh. Others who have been given RSUs include CFO MD Ranganath, Presidents Ravi Kumar S and Mohit Joshi. For the next fiscal, Parekh will receive a fixed pay of ₹6.5 crore and will be eligible for variable pay of up to ₹12.18 crore.
