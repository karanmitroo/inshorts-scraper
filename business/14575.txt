India's biggest airline, IndiGo, has posted a record profit of Rs 1,304 crore for 2014-15 fiscal year, which is over four times higher than 2013-14 fiscal's profit of Rs 317 crore. IndiGo's revenue increased 25%, from Rs 11,447 crores in FY 2014 to Rs 14,320 crores in FY 2015, courtesy lower fuel cost, increased fleet size and passengers.
