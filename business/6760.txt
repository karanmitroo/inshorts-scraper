There are a total of 84 billionaires in India, with a combined wealth of $248 billion (around ₹17 lakh crore), according to a report by Oxfam. Of these, Reliance Industries Chairman Mukesh Ambani had the highest wealth, followed by Sun Pharmaceuticals' Dilip Shanghvi. Further, the richest 1% of Indians now hold as much as 58% of the country’s total wealth.
