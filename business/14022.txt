Maruti Suzuki on Tuesday reported a net profit of ₹1,225.56 crore in the quarter that ended September, a 42.1% surge against ₹862.54 crore in the corresponding period last year. Net sales jumped by 13.2% to ₹13,574.8 crore against ₹11,996.33 the previous year. The company's sales grew by 9.8% to 3,53,335 units over 3,21,898 units the previous fiscal.
