Investors pumped in ₹1.35 lakh crore into mutual funds in October, according to Association of Mutual Funds in India (AMFI). Net inflow in MF schemes in April-October crossed ₹2.15 lakh crore, compared to ₹1.55 lakh crore net outflow in the year-ago period. The asset base of India’s fund houses grew to a historic high of ₹13.24 lakh crore last month.
