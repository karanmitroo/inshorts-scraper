Low-cost domestic carrier SpiceJet, on Saturday, posted the highest load factor for the month of September, aided chiefly by the numerous discounts offered by the airline in the past few months. The airline reported a load factor of 85.9%, followed by JetLite (82.7%), GoAir (82.3%), Air India (76.7%), Jet Airways (77.2%) and IndiGo (77.9%).
