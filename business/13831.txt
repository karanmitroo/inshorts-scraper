After the benchmark Sensex dipped by 391.59 points in the week ended November 6 to close at 26,265.24, three of the top 10 blue chip companies lost ₹32,447.1 crore combined in market capitalisation (m-cap). Sun Pharma took the steepest hit of ₹20,526.9 crore in m-cap, followed by HDFC Ltd, which lost ₹8,101.38 crore and HDFC Bank, which lost ₹3,818.82 crore.
