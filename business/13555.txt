Replying to a question in the Rajya Sabha, Oil Minister Dharmendra Pradhan on Wednesday acknowledged that excise duty on petrol and diesel has more than doubled under BJP-led NDA rule. Following five rounds of hikes, excise duty on petrol has increased from ₹9.48/litre in April 2014 to ₹19.06 at present while that on diesel has risen from ₹3.65/litre to ₹10.66.
