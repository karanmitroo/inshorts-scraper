Forbes’ ‘100 Richest People in Tech’ list has two Indians in the top 20 slots with Wipro Chairman Azim Premji and HCL founder Shiv Nadar on the 13th and 14th rank respectively. Premji’s net worth is marked at $17.4 billion and Nadar’s at $14.4 billion. Premji is among Asia’s most generous tycoons, having donated over $4 billion of his fortune.
