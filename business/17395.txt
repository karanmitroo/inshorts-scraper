Padma Bushan awardee, Rustomji Hormusji (aka Russy Mody), who was known for his man management while he headed Tata Steel, died aged 97, at his home in Kolkata on Friday. Even after he had formally retired from Tatagroup in 1993, the then PM, Narasimha Rao, asked him to take charge of Indian Airlines and Air-India to revive the carriers.
