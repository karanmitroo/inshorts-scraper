Budget carrier AirAsia on Monday announced its 'Big Sale' scheme, offering tickets at an all-inclusive price of Rs 799. Under the promotional offer, Bengaluru-Kochi fares are priced from Rs 799 while Bengaluru-Pune, Bengaluru-Goa, and Bengaluru-Visakhapatnam from Rs 999. The offer is applicable on travel period between February 15, 2016 and August 31, 2016. The bookings are open till June 28.
