IDFC Ltd posted a net loss of ₹1,468.83 crore in the quarter ended September, against ₹421.4 crore net profit in the corresponding period last year. However, net income rose  to ₹2,512.90 crore from ₹2,487.46 crore reported last year. RBI earlier granted approval to the company to use the balance in statutory reserves to allocate capital against identified stressed assets.
