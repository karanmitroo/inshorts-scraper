Oil companies on Monday reduced the price of petrol by ₹2.16/litre and the price of diesel by ₹2.10/litre. Non-branded petrol and diesel in Delhi will now cost ₹65.32/litre and ₹54.90/litre, respectively. "The current level of international product prices of Petrol & Diesel and INR-USD exchange rate warrant decrease in selling price of Petrol and Diesel", state-owned IOCL said.
