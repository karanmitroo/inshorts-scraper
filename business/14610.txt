As per Bloomberg data, CMD of Reliance Industries Limited Mukesh Ambani has overtaken Sun Pharma founder Dilip Shanghvi to regain the spot of the wealthiest Indian, with a net worth of $18.2 billion. Shanghvi's net worth stands at $17.80 billion. Chairman of Shapoorji Pallonji Group, Pallonji Shapoorji Mistry, came third on the list with a net worth of $14.60 billion.
