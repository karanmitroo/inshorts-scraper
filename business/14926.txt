Premium-service airline Jet Airways has rolled out a special ‘Monsoon’ offer on ‘Premier’ fares in its domestic network for travel from July 20-October 15, 2015. The all-inclusive ticket prices in the offer start at Rs 6,999 for distances less than 750km and Rs 8,999 for distances between 750km and 1,000km. For distances over 1,000km, it is giving a 20% discount.
