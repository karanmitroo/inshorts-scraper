Kerala has slapped a fine of Rs 53.63 crore on four online trading companies including Flipkart and Jabong for allegedly doing illegal business in the state. The fine was imposed for 2012-13 and 2013-14. Flipkart has been slapped a fine of Rs 47.15 crore, Jabong.Com Rs 3.89 crore, Vector e-commerce Rs 2.23 crore and Robemall Apparels Rs 36 lakh.
