In a week when the benchmark sensex fell 1.25%, six top sensex companies lost a combined Rs 18,699 crore in market valuation last week, with energy major Reliance Industries taking the biggest hit. TCS stood at No.1 position in the domestic market capitalisation chart, followed by RIL, ONGC, ITC, Coal India, Infosys, HDFC Bank, SBI, ICICI Bank and HDFC.
