Mukesh Ambani-led Reliance Industries on Tuesday said it will buy a 5% stake in NYSE-listed Eros International for $48.75 million (over ₹316 crore). Separately, the companies also announced a ₹1,000-crore fund to produce and acquire Indian films and digital content. Last year, Reliance had purchased 25% stake in media company Balaji Telefilms for ₹413 crore.
