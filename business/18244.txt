TCS (Tata Consultancy Services) attained a market valuation of Rs 4 lakh crore, the highest for the company since its listing in 2004. TCS is the second company to cross this limit after Reliance (October 2007). Now, TCS  leads RIL by more than Rs 1.35 lakh crore. Also, the shares of TCS have risen by 65% so far this year.
