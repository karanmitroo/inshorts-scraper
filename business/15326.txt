As many as 113 executives drew over Rs 1 crore in total compensation at Infosys in 2014-15, up from just 18 in 2013-14. Also, 202 executives earned Rs 60 lakh, compared to 72 in 2013-14. The move is seen as an effort from CEO Vishal Sikka to align salaries with the market to stem the spate of top-level exits.
