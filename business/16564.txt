Kerala and Goa were declared first states in India with every household having at least one bank account under Pradhan Mantri Jan-Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) on Nov-11, the finance ministry has said. Also, Chandigarh, Puducherry and Lakshadweep and 3 districts of Gujarat (Porbandar, Mehasana, Gandhi Nagar) have covered all households under PMJDY, giving a boost to the Centre's financial inclusion agenda.
