Transactions through credit and debit cards at point-of-sale (PoS) terminals rose to ₹40,160 crore in December from ₹35,240 crore in November, for four banks, according to RBI data. This comes amid a rise in digital transactions following the demonetisation announcement. Earlier, reports stated that State Bank of India (SBI) plans to install 2.5 lakh PoS terminals by March.
