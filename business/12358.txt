The aggregate hiring by India's top five software exporters- TCS, Infosys, Wipro, HCL and Cognizant- in 2015 had gone down by 24% year-on-year to 77,265, owing to their focus on automation, brokerage firm Centrum Broking has said. Cognizant Technologies recorded the highest plunge of 74.6% followed by HCL Technologies (71%) while hiring by Infosys had gone up by 111.4%.
