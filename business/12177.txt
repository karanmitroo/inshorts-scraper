Jindal Steel and Power Limited (LSPL) has slapped a ₹123 crore notice on state-owned power equipment maker BHEL, citing delays in execution of projects at JSPL's coal-based plant at Tamnar, Chhattisgarh. The total contract value of the projects stands at ₹600 crore. In reply, BHEL reportedly said that such claims often arise due to delays from the client as well.
