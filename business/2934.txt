Infosys Co-founder Nandan Nilekani on Friday tweeted "Joined @Infosys at 26, re-joined it at 62. Life does turn full circle!". He was appointed the non-executive Chairman of Infosys board on Thursday after the Chairman R Seshasayee and Co-Chairman Ravi Venkatesan resigned. Nilekani said that he has come in as an "execution guy" and will "move on" when tasks are completed.
