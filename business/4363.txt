As many as 1,800 overseas employees at IT giant Infosys earn over ₹1 crore, according to reports. In fiscal 2017, Infosys paid over ₹1 crore to only 50 employees based in India, from a total of over 1.51 lakh it employs here. Notably, 700 of the 1,800 high-earning overseas employees were hired after CEO Vishal Sikka took over in 2014.
