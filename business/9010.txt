Telecom operator Vodafone spent the maximum, about ₹20,000 crore, in the recently concluded spectrum auction to gain 4G access in 17 circles, reports said. It was followed by Bharti Airtel, which reportedly spent around ₹14,000 crore and now has 4G and 3G spectrum in all 22 circles. Idea Cellular gained the maximum 349.2 MHz spectrum for ₹12,798 crore.
