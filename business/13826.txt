Bank of India (BOI) reported a net loss of ₹1,126.24 crore for the quarter ended September, compared to a net profit of ₹786 crore in the corresponding period last year. Net interest income (core income from lending business) reduced by 0.36% to ₹3,019.74 crore. Provisions for bad loans jumped 283.3% to ₹3,036 crore from ₹792 crore in the year-ago period.
