Infosys' move to hire 10,000 Americans was an act to 'appease' US President Donald Trump's administration amid tightening of H-1B visa rules, Head Hunters India said. Local hiring in US is costly and would lead to offshore job cuts in India, Chairman K Lakshmikanth added. Infosys' move came after US accused that it unfairly cornered major part of H-1B visas.
