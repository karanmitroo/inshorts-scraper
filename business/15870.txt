Hindalco Industries has bagged one more mine in the ongoing coal auction, the PTI reported on Saturday. This takes the total number of blocks won by the firm to three that includes two in Chhattisgarh and one in Jharkhand. "Hindalco is the highest bidder at Rs 3,001 (per tonne) for Gare Palma 4/4," Coal Secretary Anil Swarup tweeted.
