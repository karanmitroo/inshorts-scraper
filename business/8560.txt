Baba Ramdev-led Patanjali Ayurved is now the third major advertiser in the nation, according to a report by AdExIndia. The company, that claims to be the most organic, beat Cadbury India with TV advertisements totalling to 4,524 hours. Notably, India-based Hindustan Unilever and UK-based Reckitt Benckiser were named the top two advertisers in the country.
