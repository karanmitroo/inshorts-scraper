India's third-largest lender by assets, ICICI Bank, reported a 34% fall in profit to ₹2,058 crore for the quarter ended September, as against ₹3,102 crore in the year-ago period. However, its net interest income grew by 8.7% to ₹5,709 crore. Its gross bad loans as a percentage of total loans stood at 7.87% as against 7.99% in the previous quarter.
