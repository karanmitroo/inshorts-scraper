Gold prices on Wednesday advanced by ₹15 to ₹26,250 per ten grams during the special ‘Diwali Muhurat’ trading, thereby marking the beginning of Hindu Samvat Year 2072. Meanwhile, Silver also gained ₹25 to ₹34,900 per kg. The bullion market extended its gains for the third straight day, majorly steered by the ongoing festive season.
