The market capitalisation (m-cap) of the top seven BSE-listed companies increased by ₹38,623 crore in the holiday-shortened week ended March 23, with the benchmark Sensex gaining 384.82 points to close at 25,337.56. TCS emerged as the biggest gainer, adding ₹9,349 crore to its valuation, followed by Sun Pharma and HDFC, while the valuation of ITC and Coal India dipped.
