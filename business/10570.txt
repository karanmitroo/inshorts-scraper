The total market valuation of the BSE-listed firms surged to an all-time high of ₹107 lakh crore on Thursday. The previous record market valuation for the BSE-listed firms was ₹106.85 lakh crore, which was recorded after trading on April 13, 2015. BSE, which has over 2,400 companies listed, is among the world’s 10 largest exchanges in terms of market value.
