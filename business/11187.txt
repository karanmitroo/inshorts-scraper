India's largest car-maker Maruti Suzuki India on Wednesday posted 7.1% year-on-year increase in total sales at 1,23,034 units in May. Auto major Mahindra & Mahindra reported an 11% year-on-year rise in total sales to 40,656 units in May. Meanwhile, commercial vehicles maker Ashok Leyland reported a 6% year-on-year increase in total sales at 9,875 units in May.
