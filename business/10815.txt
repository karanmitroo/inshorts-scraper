The benchmark indices on Tuesday ended in the green after the BSE Sensex and the NSE Nifty gained 121.59 and 33.15 points to close at 26,524.55 and 8,127.85 respectively, amid firm global cues. Lupin, Hindustan Unilever, ITC, and Bharti Airtel were among the top gainers while Tata Motors, TCS, Wipro and Sun Pharma were among the major losers.
