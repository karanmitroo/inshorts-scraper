The BSE Sensex fell for a third straight day, losing 118 points to end on 26,599.11 on Thursday. Having lost 700 points on Wednesday, this is the lowest closing in over 6 months, with the Sensex having lost 11% since its all-time high of 30,024 attained in March. The NIFTY too fell to 8,057, its lowest since December'14.
