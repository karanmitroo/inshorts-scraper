The BSE Sensex and Nifty jumped nearly 1.5% on Monday to a record, surpassing their previous all-time highs hit just last week. The Nifty ended 1.34% higher (at 6,583.50) after marking a lifetime high of 6,591.50, while the Sensex finished 1.38% up (at 22,055) after touching a record high of 22,074.34, surpassing their previous milestones hit on Mar-18.
