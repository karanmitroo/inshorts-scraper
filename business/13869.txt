The country’s largest bank, the State Bank of India (SBI), on Friday posted a net profit of ₹3,879.07 crore for the quarter ended September, a 25% jump over ₹3,100.41 crore in the corresponding period last year. The bank’s net interest income rose 7.4% year-on-year to ₹14,252.55 crore from ₹13,274.6 crore, while provisions for bad loans fell 6.7% to ₹3,841.77 crore.
