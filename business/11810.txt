The benchmark Sensex and the broader Nifty on Wednesday ended on a flat note, up by 27.82 and 0.05 points to settle at 25,844.18 and 7,914.75 respectively amid mixed global cues. Tata Steel, Axis Bank, GAIL, Wipro and NTPC emerged as the biggest gainers while TCS, RIL, Maruti Suzuki, Sun Pharma and Mahindra & Mahindra ended as the major losers.
