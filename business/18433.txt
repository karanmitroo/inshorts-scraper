BMW launched the first production series of its all-electric 'i3' city car simultaneously in three continents -- Europe (Price - 35,000 Euro), US (41,350 USD) and Asia (Not decided) on Monday. The four-seater 'i3', with its zero emissions all-electric engine, has a range of 130-300 kilometres. The BMW created the separate 'i' sub-brand to market electric vehicles.
