Shares in Tata Motors Ltd gained as much as 3.1% to a record high of Rs.434.30 after Citigroup Inc. raised its price target on the stock to Rs.486 from Rs.421, citing the outlook for TATA’s luxury unit Jaguar Land Rover. Jaguar is poised to become the world’s 4th biggest luxury car maker in the next 15-18 months, according to Citi.
