Indian markets on Thursday logged their best daily gain since May 12 with Sensex jumping 481 points to 27,112.21 and Nifty soaring over 139 points to close at 8,114.75. The gain came amid rising optimism over trade ties with China. Major Sensex gainers were Hero Motocorp (5.67 per cent), HDFC (3.73 per cent) and Tata Motors (3.70 per cent).
