The ongoing 'Make in India' week in Mumbai has so far attracted investment commitments worth ₹15.2 lakh crore, the government reported on Thursday. Around 2,400 MoUs worth around ₹8 lakh crore were signed by the Maharashtra government, while the Jharkhand government attracted MoUs worth ₹62,000 crore. The six-day event, which started on February 13, will conclude today.
