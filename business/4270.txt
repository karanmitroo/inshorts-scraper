As credit-deposit ratio at May end was 72% according to RBI data, out of every ₹100 deposited, ₹72 went towards lending and the rest was used to purchase government bonds. Out of ₹72 lent, roughly ₹17 went to services and personal loans each, while ₹28 went to building and running factories. Only ₹10 out of ₹100 was lent to agriculture.
