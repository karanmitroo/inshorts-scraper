Hewlett Packard Enterprise's Chief Executive Officer Meg Whitman earned as much as $35.6 million (₹240 crore) in compensation for fiscal 2016. The total value of her pay package more than doubled from the previous year, when she ran Hewlett-Packard Company and initiated the split from HP Inc. Further, Executive Vice President Michael Nefkens made $14.6 million (₹98 crore).
