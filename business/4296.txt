Hero MotoCorp Chairman Pawan Munjal took home a salary of nearly ₹60 crore in 2016-17, an increase of 3.94% from the previous fiscal. Munjal's salary was 731 times the median salary of employees, which was reported ₹8.16 lakh during the year. The company said that the average percentage increase in the salary of employees other than managerial personnel was 10.8%.
