Flipkart aims to double its sales to $8 billion in the next three years, the Reuters reported on Thursday. With this target, Flipkart looks to widen the gap with rivals including Snapdeal and Amazon.com's India unit. Flipkart is currently clocking $4 billion, while Snapdeal and Amazon's India arm notch up gross merchandising values of $3 billion and $1 billion, respectively.
