More than ₹4 lakh crore worth of cash was deposited in banks in the first five days after demonetisation of old ₹500 and ₹1000 notes. Further, about 5.12 crore people exchanged old notes in the last five days at banks and ATMs. Notably, the limit for exchanging old notes has been lowered from ₹4,500 to ₹2,000 from November 18.
