Airbus has agreed to sell 40 aircraft to Vietnamese airlines in deals worth an estimated $6.5 billion, the European planemaker said on Tuesday. Airbus will sell 20 A321 aircraft to VietJet, 10 A320 planes to Jetstar Pacific, and 10 A350 jets to Vietnam Airlines. The deals were signed during French President Francois Hollande's two-day visit to the Southeast Asian nation.
