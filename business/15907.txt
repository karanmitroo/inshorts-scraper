The Sensex gained for the seventh straight session on Thursday, led by gains in shares of metals and mining companies. The 30-share index closed at 29,462.27, up 142.01 points or 0.48%. The 50-share Nifty followed suit and ended at 8,895.30, up 27.70 points or 0.30%. Jindal Steel (up 28.61%) and Sesa Sterlite (up 7.12%) were among the top Nifty gainers.
