Telecom regulator TRAI has imposed over ₹11 crore as penalty on telecom operators, including BSNL, Airtel, and Vodafone, for poor service quality including call drops, Telecom Minister Manoj Sinha said on Friday. Of this, the highest penalty has been imposed on Aircel of ₹3 crore for not meeting quality parameters for 2G service and ₹1.56 crore for poor 3G service.
