IT services firm HCL Technologies posted 59% increase in net profit at Rs 1,624 crore for the quarter ended Mar-31, 2014. The firm had posted a net profit of Rs 1,021 crore in the corresponding quarter of last fiscal (2012-13). In dollar terms, the company's net profit grew 39.9% to $264.2 million in Q3 of FY'14 from year-ago period.
