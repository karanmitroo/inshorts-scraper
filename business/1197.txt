Benchmark indices Sensex and Nifty closed at new lifetime highs on Tuesday, a day after Bharatiya Janata Party's (BJP) victory in Gujarat and Himachal Pradesh Assembly polls. Sensex gained 235 points to hit 33,836, while Nifty rose 74 points to reach 10,463. Maruti Suzuki and Hero Motocorp were the top gainers, while Infosys and HCL Technologies were the top losers.
