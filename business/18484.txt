CEO Mark Zuckerberg's fortune soared $3.8-billion on Thursday, bringing his net worth to $16.8-billion, as shares of Facebook rallied 30% to the highest level since May-2012. Zuckerberg(29) is at No-42, ahead of Microsoft CEO Steve Ballmer and Dell Chairman Michael Dell, on the Bloomberg Billionaires Index. Bill Gates(57), with net worth of $72.2-billion, remains the richest person in the world.
