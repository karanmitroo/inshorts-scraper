Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) engaged in a sell-off amounting to $2.5 billion from equity markets in the quarter ended September, thereby registering the highest quarterly outflow since 2009. FIIs also reportedly reduced their exposure in 35 out of the 50 NSE-Nifty stocks. However, net holdings of FIIs in Nifty remained flat at 28.6% in the September quarter.
