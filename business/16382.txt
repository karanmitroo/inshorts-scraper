Micromax on Thursday launched its first smartphone under the Yureka brand at a price of Rs 8,999. The phone runs on CyanogenMod OS, and has a 5.5 inch HD screen, 2GB RAM, A 53 cortex, Qualcomm Snapdragon processor, 615 octa core 64 bit chipset and 16 GB expandable memory. The device will be available on Amazon India from January 8.
