In less than six years after starting Flipkart, it's founders Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal (IIT Delhi grads) took home a handsome remuneration of Rs 10.25 crore each in the six months between September 2011 and March 2012. The colossal numbers even trumped the $290,000 (Rs 1.74 crore) paycheck figures of co-founder and CEO of US-based shopping portal, Zulily.
