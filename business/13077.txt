The government on Friday hiked excise duty on petrol by ₹0.75 per litre and on diesel by ₹2 per litre. With this, basic excise duty on unbranded or 'normal' petrol has increased to ₹8.48 per litre while that on unbranded diesel has reached ₹9.83 per litre. The increase is expected to yield ₹3,700 crore of additional revenue to the government.
