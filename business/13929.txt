Reliance Communications (R-Com) and Sistema Shyam Teleservices Ltd (SSTL) on Monday signed a definitive agreement for the latter’s merger with the Anil Ambani-led company. Under the all-stock deal, SSTL, which operates the MTS brand, would get a 10% equity stake in the combined entity. Further, R-Com would acquire SSTL’s 800/850 MHz band spectrum suitable for 4G services.
