The benchmark indices on Monday ended with gains as the BSE Sensex rose 120.41 points to settle at 27,902.66 points, while the broader Nifty gained 34.90 points to close at 8,607.45 points. Tata Motors, RIL, and Hero MotoCorp were the top gainers, while Wipro, Lupin, and HDFC Bank emerged as the biggest losers.
