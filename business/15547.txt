The Trade deficit, the difference of value of imports and exports, has further widened to $11.79 bn in March from $6.85 bn in February. The trade deficit for the full financial year increased by $1.21 bn to $137.01 bn. For FY15, Imports ($447.5 bn) decreased by 0.6% from FY14 and exports ($310.5 bn) reduced by 1.2% in the same period.
