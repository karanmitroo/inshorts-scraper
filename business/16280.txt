Ending its six-day winning streak, the Sensex on Monday closed 45.58 points lower at 27,842.32. The losses in technology, banks and healthcare sectors offset the gains in auto and capital goods. The 30-share Sensex touched a high of 28,064.49 and a low of 27,786.85 intraday. The 50-share Nifty also declined in the session, falling by 18.50 points at 8,376.95.
