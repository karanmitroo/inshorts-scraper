The largest seller on e-commerce platform Flipkart, WS Retail, nearly tripled its turnover to ₹10,163 crore this fiscal, after reporting sales of ₹3,135 crore last year. WS Retail's sales were reportedly more than the combined sales of Pantaloons, Shoppers Stop, Lifestyle and Trent. Notably, WS Retails' sales numbers last fiscal were higher than that of ₹2,846 crore revenue of Flipkart.
