The benchmark Sensex and the broader Nifty on Wednesday closed on a flat note, up 17.04 and 11.15 points to close at 24,900.63 and 7,614.35 respectively, amid mixed global cues. Tata Steel, Tata Motors, Lupin, Bharti Airtel and Larsen & Toubro emerged as the major gainers while Axis Bank, Infosys, ICICI Bank, and NTPC ended among the major losers.
