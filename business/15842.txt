The government plans to build one smart city each at the country’s 12 major ports, at an estimated investment of Rs 50,000 crore. “Each city will be built with an expenditure of about Rs 3,000-4,000 crore,” Union Minister Nitin Gadkari told PTI. The 12 ports include Kandla, Mumbai, Marmugao, New Managlore, Cochin, Chennai, Ennore, Visakhapatnam, Paradip and Kolkata, among others.
