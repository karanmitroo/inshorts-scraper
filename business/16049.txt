HDFC Ltd’s net profit rose by 11.6% to Rs 1,425.49 crore in the third quarter ended December 2014-15, the PTI reported Thursday. HDFC had reported a net profit of Rs 1,277.71 crore in the corresponding quarter last year. Company’s income rose to Rs 6,870.95 crore during the quarter, up from Rs 6,019.80 crore in the same period a year ago.
