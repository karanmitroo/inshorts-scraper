Punjab Government on Friday signed Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) worth ₹1.15 lakh crore with 378 companies, Deputy Chief Minister Sukhbir Singh Badal said. The investment projects included ₹1,200 crore jewellery park by Geetanjali jewellers and a ₹500 crore manufacturing facility by Videocon. Further, HDFC bank would generate 5,000 jobs by shifting its entire back end operations to Mohali.
