Electrical equipment company Havells India on Thursday announced that it will divest 80% stake each in two of its overseas subsidiary units Havells Sylvania Malta Ltd and Hong Kong-based Havells Exim Ltd. The combined 100% equity value for both companies is ₹1,340 crore. The announcement saw the rise of Havell India's stock price in intra-day trading by 8.60% to ₹307.40.
