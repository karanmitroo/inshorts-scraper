Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s radio address 'Mann Ki Baat' has generated a revenue of nearly ₹10 crore for the All India Radio (AIR) over last two fiscals. The revenue was ₹5.19 crore in 2016-17, while it was ₹4.78 crore in 2015-16. Notably, only the radio broadcast of 'Mann Ki Baat' is monetised and other platforms like live streaming, are not.
