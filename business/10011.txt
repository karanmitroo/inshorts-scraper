Jindal Steel and Power Ltd (JSPL) on Monday said that it has secured a long-term coal linkage of 1.18 million tonnes per annum (MTPA) for its "state-of-the-art captive power plants" in Chhattisgarh. The five-year linkage was secured during the recent coal linkage auctions and will enhance the fuel security for the power plants, a JSPL statement added.
