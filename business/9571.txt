The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty on Wednesday posted marginal gains with the Sensex rising 18.69 points to settle at 28,372.23, and the Nifty gaining 11.00 points to close at 8,726.60 amid mixed global cues. Adani Ports SEZ and SBI emerged as the biggest gainers while Coal India and ONGC ended as the day's major losers.
