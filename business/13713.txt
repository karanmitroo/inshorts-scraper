The Foreign Investment Promotion Board (FIPB) on Wednesday cleared six FDI proposals amounting to ₹1,810.25 crore. The proposals included increasing foreign equity from 50.16% to 80% in non-banking finance company IIFL Holdings resulting in ₹1,800 crore investment. Further, restructuring proposals of three companies along with ₹10.25 crore (combined) foreign inflow in two others were approved.
