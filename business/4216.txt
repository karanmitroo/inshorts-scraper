HDFC Bank’s Managing Director Aditya Puri saw his remuneration rise marginally to ₹10 crore and exercised stock options worth over ₹57 crore during 2016-17. The stock options included those granted and vested over several previous years, but exercised during the last financial year. The increase in remuneration followed HDFC Bank’s net profit increasing by over 18% in the last fiscal.
