According to the RBI data released on Friday, India's  foreign exchange reserves fell by $643.3mn for the week ending Aug-8 to $319.35 bn. Foreign currency assets, a key component of reserves, fell by $646.7 mn to $292.05 bn. The gold reserves remained unchanged during the week at $21.17 billion. India's reserve position with the IMF was up $0.9 million to $1.70 billion.
