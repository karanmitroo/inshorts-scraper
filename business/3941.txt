Sports Direct Founder Mike Ashley has described himself as a "power drinker" and dismissed his £2.2 billion (over ₹18,000 crore) fortune as "wallpaper" at the high court. Ashley was defending claims that he agreed to pay a banker £15 million (₹125 crore) while binge drinking in a London pub. "I like to get drunk, I’m a power drinker," Ashley stated.
