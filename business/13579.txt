With the Himachal Pradesh government granting permission to Nestle India for restarting the production of Maggi noodles at its Tahliwal factory, the company on Monday announced that it has resumed production at all five factories in India. The company now has production going on in Nanjangud in Karnataka, Moga in Punjab, Bicholim in Goa, and Pantnagar in Uttarakhand and Tahliwal.
