Rising for the sixth straight session, the Sensex on Friday closed nearly 400 points higher, buoyed by hopes of reforms in banking sector. The 30-share index ended higher by 380.36 points  at 27,887.90. The Nifty also ended on a positive note, closing shy of the 8,400 mark. The 50-share index rose 111.45 points to end at 8,395.45.
