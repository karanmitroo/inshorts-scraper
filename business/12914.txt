The benchmark Sensex and the broader Nifty both ended on a flat note on Wednesday ahead of US Federal Reserve policy statement. Sensex closed 6.44 points up at 24,492.39, while Nifty settled 1.60 points up at 7,437.75. China’s Shanghai Composite Index shed 2.4%, adding to Tuesday’s 6.4% loss, thereby giving up almost all the gains made since December 2014.
