State-run telecom company BSNL is investing over Rs 11,000 crore to improve its all-round services, Telecom Minister Ravi Shankar Prasad informed the Parliament in a written reply on Wednesday. The minister said that BSNL is augmenting its mobile network by installing additional 14,421 2G mobile sites and 10,605 3G sites with an estimated investment of Rs 4,804 crore.
