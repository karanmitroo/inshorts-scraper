Baba Ramdev-led Patanjali Ayurveda will set up a ₹500 crore food processing unit in Madhya Pradesh's Dhar district. "We have demarcated 400 acres of land to Patanjali Ayurveda... and have got an advance of ₹1 crore," a senior official said. The food processing project, to start production in three years, will provide direct employment to around 700 people, he added.
