Realty firm Unitech has sold 74 acres of land in Gurugram, Chennai, and Hyderabad for ₹260 crore to boost cash flow and complete ongoing projects. Earlier, Unitech announced it'll surrender land in Noida as it was unable to clear dues of about ₹4,000 crore. The company's Managing Director Sanjay Chandra was held in April for duping Greater Noida flat buyers.
