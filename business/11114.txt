The benchmark indices on Wednesday ended on a flat note as the BSE Sensex gained 10.99 points to settle at 27,020.66, and the NSE Nifty rose by 6.60 points to close at 8,273.05. BHEL, L&T, ICICI Bank, Hero and ONGC were the major Sensex gainers while Infosys, HDFC Bank, Asian Paints, Dr Reddy's Labs and TCS were the major losers.
