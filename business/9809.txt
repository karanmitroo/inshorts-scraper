State-run petroleum firm BPCL on Wednesday reported a rise of 11% year-on-year in its net profit to stand at ₹2,620.5 crore for the quarter ended June. Its sales registered an 8% increase against a drop of 1.10% a year ago. However, the refiner's total income fell to ₹57,015.75 crore, down 3%, against ₹58,818.37 in the year-ago period.
