After Congress alleged the government's Rafale aircraft deal promoted the interest of Anil Ambani-owned Reliance, party Vice-President Rahul Gandhi tweeted, "Self 'Reliance' is obviously a critical aspect of Make in India." Congress had claimed the deal with Dassault Reliance Aerospace, a joint venture between Reliance and Dassault Aviation, was 'overpriced' and a huge loss to national exchequer.
