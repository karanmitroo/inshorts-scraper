Months after its launch in Delhi, Swedish retailer H&M on Thursday announced its plans to expand to Noida, Bengaluru, Gurgaon and Mumbai in 2016. The Noida store, spread over 37,000 sq ft (four floors), will open at DLF’s Mall of India in Spring 2016. The company plans to open 50 more stores in the country, investing ₹700 crore.
