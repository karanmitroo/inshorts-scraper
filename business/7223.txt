On the day Prime Minister Narendra Modi announced demonetisation, the RBI had only ₹4.94 lakh crore in 2,000 rupee notes, which was approximately one-fourth of what it had in demonetised notes. An RTI response to a Mumbai-based activist further revealed that on November 8, the RBI had ₹9.13 lakh crore in ₹1,000 notes and ₹11.38 lakh crore in ₹500 notes.
