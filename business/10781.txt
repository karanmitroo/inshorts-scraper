The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty on Friday ended on a high for the fifth consecutive session, with the Sensex gaining 145.19 points to settle at 27,144.91, amid firm global cues. Meanwhile, the Nifty gained 40.60 points to close at 8,328.35. ONGC, BHEL, and ITC were amongst the top gainers while TCS and Adani ports ended up as the losers.
