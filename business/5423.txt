Reliance Industries has added ₹1 lakh crore in market capitalisation in the last six weeks amid expectations of revenue generation from Reliance Jio, which has started charging its users. The company's market capitalisation is up at ₹4.44 lakh crore. Earlier, Jio announced that 7.2 crore Jio customers signed up for Jio Prime and also extended its membership till April 15.
