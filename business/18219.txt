The International Monetary Fund(IMF) has forecasted in its World Economic Outlook released on Tuesday that economic growth for India would dip to 4.25%(lowest since 2002-03) in the year to 31 March. At market price, India's economy will expand slower than that of sub-Saharan Africa this fiscal. However, it also said that India's growth in 2018-19 will bounce back to 6.7%.
