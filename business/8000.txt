Gujarat-based businessman Laljibhai Patel, famous for gifting cars and flats to his employees on Diwali, has surrendered ₹6,000 crore cash following the government's move to demonetise ₹500 and ₹1,000 notes, reports stated. He had also made headlines when he broke the Guinness World Record for buying PM Narendra Modi's suit-jacket for ₹4.3 crore with his name embroidered on it.
