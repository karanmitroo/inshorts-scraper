Kalanithi Maran, Chairman & MD of Sun Group, was the highest paid executive in India in 2015-16 with a salary of ₹71.47 crore. Maran's wife Kavery Kalanithi retained the second spot with a salary of ₹71.46 crore. A M Naik, Group Executive Chairman of Larsen & Toubro, who occupied the third spot took home a salary of ₹66.14 crore.
