ONGC Videsh Ltd. Tuesday reported a 13.1% jump in 2013-14 net profit on the back of higher output. Net profit in the fiscal year ending, Mar-31 2014 rose to Rs 4,445 crore. Company MD said, OVL was aiming to produce 3.6% more oil and gas during the current fiscal at 8.66 million tonnes of oil and its equivalent gas.
