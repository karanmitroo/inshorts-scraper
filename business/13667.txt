After witnessing a loss of around 7% in three consecutive weeks, the benchmark Sensex and broader Nifty gained around 1% in the week ended November 20. The BSE Sensex gained 257.96 points to settle at 25,868.49 while the NSE Nifty surged 94.30 points to close at 7,856.55. GAIL gained 23.55% in the 50-stock index, while, Axis Bank slipped by 5.10%.
