The country's car exports remained almost flat at 5.50 lakh units in 2013-14, compared to 5.47 lakh units in 2012-13. Hyundai Motors and Maruti Suzuki posted a decline in shipments, while overseas sales by Nissan, Toyota and Volkswagen India increased. As reported earlier, domestic car sales in FY14 fell to 17.86 lakh units from 18.74 lakh units in 2012-13.
