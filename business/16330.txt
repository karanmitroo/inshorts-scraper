Japanese largest financial institution, Sumitomo Mitsui Trust Bank (SMTB), may take up to 10% stake in the Reliance Capital's proposed banking venture. SMTB was roped in last week by Reliance Capital as a strategic partner to collaborate on various businesses. SMTB will take an initial stake of 2.77% in the Reliance Capital at an investment of Rs 371 crore.
