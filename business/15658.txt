Led by banking & financials, FMCG, capital goods and IT stocks, the BSE Sensex hit 28000-mark in intra-day trade on Monday. The Sensex increased 517.22 points (1.88 percent) to 27975.86 and Nifty surged 150.90 points (1.81 percent) to 8492.30. UltraTech Cement, Asian Paints, HDFC and ONGC were major gainers, while Hindalco, Tech Mahindra and Cairn India were major losers.
