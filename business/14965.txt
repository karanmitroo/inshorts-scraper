Italian luxury car-maker Maserati on Wednesday announced its re-entry into the Indian market with dealerships in Delhi, Mumbai and Bangalore. The company started selling in India in 2011 for the first time, but discontinued due to trouble with its dealer partner. It plans to launch 4 models, Quattroporte, Ghibli, Gran Turismo and GranCabrio, all priced between Rs 1.14- 2.2 crore.
