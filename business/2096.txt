The BSE Sensex rose by 200.95 points to close at 32,633.64, while Nifty 50 rallied 63.4 points to close at 10,230.85 on Monday. Rising for the third session in a row, both indices also hit lifetime highs. The surge comes after investor optimism climbed following IMF Chief Christine Lagarde's comment that Indian economy is on a "very solid track".
