Bharti Airtel on Tuesday reported a 40% jump in its consolidated net profit, to Rs 1,554.3 crore in the current financial year's first quarter. It had posted a net profit of Rs 1,108.5 crore in last fiscal's April-June quarter. Its consolidated total sales grew by 2.9% to Rs 23,680.8 crore, as against Rs 23,005.5 crore in last fiscal's first quarter.
