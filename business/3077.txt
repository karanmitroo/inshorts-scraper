Infosys in a letter to the media has said that it "categorically rejects any speculation or allegation of discord between the Infosys board and Dr Vishal Sikka." In its letter, Infosys blamed Narayan Murthy's criticism for Sikka's exit. It said, "Murthy's continuous assault...is the primary reason that the CEO, Vishal Sikka, has resigned despite strong board support."
