Snapping its five-day fall, the Sensex on Thursday surged by 416 points to close above 27,000 mark, aided by value buying and firm global markets. The Sensex ended at 27,126.57 by rising 1.56%, its biggest single-session gain since October 31. The 50-share Nifty also climbed in the trade, reclaiming 8,100 level by rising 129.50 points to end at 8,159.30.
