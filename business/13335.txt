The BSE Sensex on Thursday ended flat at 25,838.71 points, down by 11.59 points, while the NSE Nifty lost 4.90 points to close at 7,861.05. Analysts attributed the low trading volumes to the holiday season. Bharti Airtel, GAIL and Tata Motors emerged as the major gainers while ICICI Bank, Maruti Suzuki and ONGC ended as the major losers.
