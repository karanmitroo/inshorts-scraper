UK Prime Minister Theresa May has inducted Rishi Sunak, an Indian-origin lawmaker and the son-in-law of Infosys Co-founder Narayana Murthy, into her government. The 37-year-old has been appointed as the Parliamentary Under Secretary of State at Ministry of Housing, Communities and Local Government. Sunak had married Akshata Murthy, who was his classmate at the Stanford Business School, in 2009.
