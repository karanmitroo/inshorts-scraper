Wipro on Friday reported a consolidated net profit of Rs 2,198 crore, up 5.4% QoQ for the third quarter ended December 31, 2014. Wipro had posted a net profit of Rs 2,085 crore in the previous quarter. Recently, TCS and Infosys, recorded tepid topline growth in the seasonally weak quarter, growing at 0.8% and flat top line growth respectively.
