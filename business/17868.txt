With 22% drop in sales from a year ago, German carmaker BMW India lost its luxury car sales crown to Audi (No. 1) and Mercedes-Benz (No. 2) to be ranked at Number 3. Last year, Mercedes sold 9,003 units (with 32% growth) and market leader Audi sold a thousand more (with 11% growth), while BMW sold 7,327 vehicles only.
