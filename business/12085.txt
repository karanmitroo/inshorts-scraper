BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty on Tuesday extended their losses for the second day after they lost 65.94 and 18.10 points to settle at 24,900.46 and 7,597.00 respectively, amid weak Asian markets. Tata Motors, Maruti Suzuki, Axis Bank, RIL and Tata Steel were the major gainers while Lupin, Cipla, Dr Reddy's Labs, GAIL and Sun Pharma were the major losers.
