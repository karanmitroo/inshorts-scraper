The benchmark Sensex plummeted 537.55 points, and the broader Nifty slumped 171.90 on Monday points after a sell-off was triggered by a weak Indian manufacturing data, Chinese stock market tumbling by around 7% and rising tensions in the middle-east. The BSE Sensex ended at 25,623.35 while the NSE Nifty closed at 7,791.30 points. Most Asian markets witnessed a downfall.
