Low cost carrier IndiGo has placed the largest global order for narrowbody aircraft by signing a memorandum of understanding with Airbus to buy 250 A-320neo planes. The list price for each of these planes is $102 million, putting the collective list price of IndiGo order at $25.5 billion. IndiGo has previously placed orders for 100 A320s and 180 A320neo planes.
