The 39-year-old Founder of mobile payments startup Paytm, Vijay Shekhar Sharma, is the youngest Indian on the Forbes 2018 Billionaires List. Sharma ranked 1,394 globally with an estimated net worth of $1.7 billion. Further, 92-year-old Chairman Emeritus of pharmaceutical company Alkem Laboratories, Samprada Singh, is the oldest billionaire in India with a net worth of $1.2 billion.
