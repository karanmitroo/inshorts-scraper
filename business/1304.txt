Union Steel Minister Chaudhary Birender Singh on Friday said that that the joint venture (JV) between Steel Authority of India (SAIL) and ArcelorMittal will be signed soon. “They (ArcelorMittal) have the technology and they are keen to have a JV with SAIL," he added. He further said the proposed ₹15,000 crore automotive steel plant will come up in Andhra Pradesh.
