Food and beverages major Pepsico India on Monday announced the launch of 150 ml mini cans of Pepsi priced at ₹15. This is "part of the strategy to create more affordable options", Vice President (Beverages), Pepsico India, said. Notably, Coca-Cola India has also rolled out 180 ml cans of Coca-Cola and Thums Up priced at ₹20 in select outlets.
