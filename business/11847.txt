The top 9 BSE-listed companies added ₹68,023 crore in market capitalisation (m-cap) in the week ended April 13, with the benchmark Sensex surging 952.91 points to close at 25,626.75. TCS emerged as the biggest gainer, adding ₹18,610 crore to its m-cap followed by HUL and RIL, while CIL was the only loser having lost ₹2,242 crore in valuation.
