According to a report by advisory firm Grant Thornton, India Inc's Merger and Acquisition (M&A) deals stood at $31.16 billion between January-November. “Cross-border deal values increased by 26% year-on-year, driven by 11 deals worth over $500 million and 27 deals worth over $100 million”, said the report. Sectorally​, telecom players led as they accounted for 40% of the total value.
