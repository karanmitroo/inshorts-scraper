Warren Buffet's Berkshire Hathaway Inc, an American conglomerate holding company, on Monday announced that it had reduced its stake in Goldman Sachs by 13% during the quarter ended September. Berkshire said it owned 10.96 million Goldman Sachs shares worth $1.9 billion down from 12.63 million shares. The stake in Walmart was also reduced from 60.4 million shares to 56.2 million.
