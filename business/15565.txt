GSM group Cellular Operators Association of India (COAI) said on Tuesday that Airtel has added maximum new subscribers at 2.90 million in Mar'15 making its total consumer base of 226.02 million. Airtel is followed by Idea Cellular (2.35 million) and Vodafone India (1.36 million) in the same period, taking India's GSM base to 705.20 million.
