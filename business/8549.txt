Reliance Capital on Monday reported a consolidated net profit of ₹253 crore for the second quarter, up 1% year-on-year. The company’s total income increased to ₹4,926 crore in the July-September quarter of the current fiscal, more than double from ₹2,372 crore in the year-ago period. As on September 30, the company's net worth stood at ₹15,913 crore.
