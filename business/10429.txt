Larsen & Toubro, the country's largest engineering and construction major, on Friday reported a net profit of ₹610 crore for the quarter ended June, up by 46% from the corresponding period a year ago. The gross revenue was at ₹21,874 crore, up 9.1% on yearly basis. Fresh orders increased by 14% to ₹29,702 crore from the year-ago period.
