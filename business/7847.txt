India's largest lender State Bank of India Chairperson Arundhati Bhattacharya has said that around ₹1.4 lakh crore has been deposited in the bank since the demonetisation of old ₹500 and ₹1,000 notes. Further, she said that normalcy was returning to banks. At the moment, the  ₹500 notes are short in supply, she added.
