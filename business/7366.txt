The Central Industrial Security Force (CISF) has seized over ₹60 crore cash and gold weighing 245 kg from 10 major airports across the country since the demonetisation move. All of the currency seized is in the scrapped notes of ₹500 and ₹1,000 denomination. The seizures were made at Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Guwahati, Chennai, and Patna airports, among others.
