The BSE Sensex and the NSE Nifty rose over 5% in the week ended May 27, the highest weekly gain in nearly three months. While the Sensex rose 1,351.70 points to close at 26,653.60, the Nifty surged 406.95 points to settle at 8,156.65. Investors remained buoyant amid firm global markets and the government approving the Capital Goods Policy, analysts said.
