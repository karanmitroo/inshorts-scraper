Carmaker Aston Martin is building its first apartment block in United States' Miami, with condos' prices ranging from $600,000 (₹3.9 crore) to $50 million (₹325 crore). The 66-storey waterfront tower will feature a total of 391 condos, two movie theatres, a spa, a virtual golf room and an infinity pool. It is expected to be completed by 2021.
