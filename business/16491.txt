The Sensex on Thursday bounced back to close with a gain of about 120 points, led by FMCG, capital goods and banks. The 30-share index ended at 28,562.82, up 120.11 points, after hitting a day's high of 28,808.78. The 50-share Nifty ended at 8,564.40, up 26.75 points, after hitting an all-time high of 8,626.95 in early trade.
