IT firm Tata Consultancy Services on Tuesday announced results for the quarter ended December, posting a net profit of ₹6,083.39 crore, an increase of 14.18% from its corresponding figure of ₹5,327.55 crore last year. The company posted a revenue of ₹27,364 crore, an increase of 11.7% from last year. It also declared an interim dividend of ₹5.50 per equity share.
