According to a report released by The Financial Express, investments into real estate sector has reached nearly ₹53,000 crore ($8 billion) in 2015, and are set to hit a seven-year high. Around ₹30,500 crore ($4.5 billion) has been invested through non-convertible debentures (NCD) till November. Private equity players invested around ₹18,700 crore ($2.8 billion) in the given market till September.
