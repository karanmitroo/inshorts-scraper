India's food services industry is expected to touch about ₹4.98 lakh crore ($77 billion) by 2021, growing at an annual rate of 10%, a National Restaurant Association report stated. The organised food market, including chain outlets, is expected to reach ₹2.04 lakh crore by 2021. In regard with 2016, the food services market could employ 5.8 million people, it added.
