Overtaking Bajaj Auto in both domestic and overseas sales, Honda motorcycle and scooter India private limited(HMSI) grabbed 2nd position in 2-wheeler market of India in July. HMSI sold 287,177 units in July, highest ever in its 13 years in India. Gripping a market-share of 24%, the company showed a growth of 35% and 8% in motorcycle and scooter segments respectively.
