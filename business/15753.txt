The BSE Sensex declined by 134.91 points to 28,709.87 and Nifty fell 44.70 points to 8,712.05 on Tuesday. During the day, 1,261 shares advanced, 1,584 shares declined and 200 shares remained unchanged. Coal India, Mahindra & Mahindra, BHEL and Hero were the top gainers, while HDFC, Hindalco, HUL, Sun Pharma and Tata Steel were the top losers.
