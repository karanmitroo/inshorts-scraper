Equity mutual funds saw a net withdrawal of ₹1,370 crore in March – the first instance of monthly net outflow since March 2014 – amid profit booking. However, a net inflow of ₹74,024 crore was registered in 2015-16, up by 4.2% from ₹71,029 crore in 2014-15, according to data with the Association of Mutual Funds in India (AMFI).
