On the back of demand from Africa, Asean and Far East regions, exports grew by 11.64% to $25.83 billion in July, snapping two consecutive months of decline. Moreover, imports declined by 6.2% to $38.1 billion during the month. During April-July this fiscal, exports grew by 1.72% to $98.2 billion. Imports also increased by 2.82% to $160.7 billion during the period.
