The Enforcement Directorate (ED) on Friday froze bank accounts with ₹30 crore balance, shares worth ₹13.86 crore held in a company of jeweller Nirav Modi. During the search, ED seized 176 steel almirahs and 60 plastic containers containing imported watches. Earlier, the ED had frozen shares and mutual funds worth ₹94.52 crore belonging to Nirav Modi and Mehul Choksi's firms.
