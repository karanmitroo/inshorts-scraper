FDI in the services sector in India declined 54% to $2.18 billion during April-Feb of 2013-14 from a year earlier. Furthermore, the overall foreign inflows into the country declined to $20.76 billion during the first 11 months of 2013-14 from $20.89 billion in April-Feb 2013. Remarkably, the services sector contributes over 60% to India's gross domestic product (GDP).
