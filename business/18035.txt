With an estimated personal fortune of $15.3 billion, 60-year old Microsoft co-founder Paul Allen is the world's wealthiest bachelor, followed by 79-year old Italian fashion designer Giorgio Armani ($10.9 billion), Wealth-X reported. The other three wealthiest bachelors (in top 5) include Mikhail Prokhorov (48), Xavier Niel(46), David Lawrence Geffen(70).
