SpiceJet on Thursday said its promoter Kalanithi Maran would transfer his entire stake in the carrier to original founder Ajay Singh. The proposal of the principal shareholder, Maran and KAL Airways Pvt Ltd, to transfer the ownership, management and control to Singh was approved by the SpiceJet board Thursday. Maran and KaL Airways hold 53.48% stake in the BSE-listed SpiceJet.
