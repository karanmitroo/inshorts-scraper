Indian e-tailer Snapdeal is planning to raise $600 to $650 million from existing investors to expand its operations, sources said, adding that the amount will likely be announced next week. Snapdeal’s investors include Japan’s SoftBank Corp, eBay Inc, Temasek Holdings and Ratan Tata. Notably, another Indian e-tailer and snapdeal's rival Flipkart had raised $1 billion in July.
