HDFC bank's net profit increased 20.5% to ₹2,869 crore from ₹2,381 year-on-year basis for its fiscal second quarter to September 30, in alignment with analysts' estimates. Gross non-performing assets were at 0.91% as against 1.02% y-o-y. Net interest income, which is the difference between the interest earned on loans and that paid on deposits, increased 21.2% to ₹6,681 crore y-o-y.
