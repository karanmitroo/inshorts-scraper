E-Commerce major Snapdeal will allow its users to pay through their credit and debit cards when they opt for the pay-on-delivery option in another 120 cities. The solution has been developed in partnership with GoJavas. The service is already available across metros and in cities like in Agra, Ahmedabad, Ajmer, Aligarh, Bareilly, Chandigarh, Darjeeling, Gorakhpur, Hisar, Kanyakumari and Ooty.
