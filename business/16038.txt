Two-wheeler maker Royal Enfield Sunday reported a 43% jump in total sales in January this year at 28,927 units. The company had sold 20,232 units in the year-ago period. Domestic sales surged by 42% at 28,157 units last month as against 19,808 units in January 2014. Exports during the month stood at 770 units as against 424 units last year.
