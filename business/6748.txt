Mukesh Ambani-led Reliance Jio said it has garnered 7.24 crore subscribers in less than four months of commencement of services. Jio's comprehensive ecosystem has enabled millions of Indians to lead a richer life, Ambani said. Jio crossed the 5 crore subscriber-mark in 83 days, adding 6 lakh users daily on average, and surpassing subscriber addition rates of Facebook and WhatsApp.
