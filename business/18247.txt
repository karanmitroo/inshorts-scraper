IRCTC(Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation) has earned Rs. 29 crore, Rs.18 crore more compared to previous year from its 80 non-railway catering outlets across the country in 2012-13. The net worth of the corporation has also increased to Rs. 291.77 crore(with gross profit Rs. 92.41 crore) from last year's Rs. 246.70 crore(with gross profit of Rs. 76.54 crore).
