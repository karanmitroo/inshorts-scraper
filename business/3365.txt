Venezuela's currency bolivar, is worth less than fake gold in online role-playing game 'World of Warcraft'. A Twitter user made the observation last month, noting that on Venezuela's black market, one can get $1 for 8493.97 bolivars while a "WoW" token is worth 8385 gold per dollar. According to DolarToday, the currency has since slipped further to 12,197 per dollar.
