Sensex on Monday made a rally of 300 points and closed at 31,222, the highest since June 22. Nifty broke through key 9,600 mark and ended 94 points higher at 9,615. ITC, HeroMotoCorp and Coal India are the top gainers while Sun Pharma and Tata Motors were the biggest losers. Notably, this is the first market close after GST rollout.
