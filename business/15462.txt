Vodafone India has announced to reduce its national roaming price by 75% effective from May-1. The price of local text on roaming is reduced to 25 paise from Re 1 and 38 paise from Rs 1.5 for STD text. Roaming incoming calls will now cost 45 paise/min and outgoing local and STD will cost 80 paise/min and Rs 1.15/min respectively.
