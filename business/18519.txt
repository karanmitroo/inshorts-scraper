Oil companies raised petrol rates the third time in a month by Rs 1.82 a litre, excluding taxes, effective from yesterday. The prices will depend on local taxes of the cities. Both depreciating rupee and rising international prices have warranted the rise. Diesel rates likely to increase early next week.
Revised Prices: Delhi - Rs 68.58, Chennai - Rs 71.72, Kolkata -  Rs 76.10, Mumbai - Rs 76.90.
