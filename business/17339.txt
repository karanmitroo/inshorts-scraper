Declining for a third session in four, the Nifty posted its biggest daily fall in nearly four months as Infosys slumped after its president resigned, while profit-taking continued to hit shares from record highs earlier this month. The Sensex provisionally closed 1.31% lower at 24,234.15, while the broader Nifty ended 1.14% down at 7,246.20. Infosys' Shares provisionally ended 7.94% lower.
