The Prime Minister Narendra Modi-chaired Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA) on Wednesday gave its approval to the initial public offering (IPO) of Cochin Shipyard Limited (CSL), India’s largest ship-building and repair facility. CSL, whose turnover grew five times to ₹1,859 crore in 2014-15  from ₹373 crore in 2005-06, would be allowed to offload a 10% stake.
