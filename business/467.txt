National carrier Air India's net loss widened to ₹5,765.16 crore in FY17 compared to ₹3,836 crore in FY16, the government informed the parliament on Thursday. However, its operating profit more than doubled to ₹298 crore. Further, Finance Minister Arun Jaitley has said that it is better to disinvest the airline as it currently has ₹58,000 crore debt.
