The Comptroller and Auditor General has flagged "glaring lapses" on the part of railway accounting officials, leading to cases of misclassification and mistakes in accounting. Mistakes include non-accounting of transactions, and belated adjustments in accounts. The CAG pointed out 64 cases of miscalculations of expenditure worth ₹53 crore, and 66 cases of irregular adjustments of ₹1,431 crore.
