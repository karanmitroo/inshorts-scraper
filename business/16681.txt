Taxi service aggregator Olacabs has entered the premium billion dollar club, following $210 million investment from Japan's SoftBank Corp (at a valuation of nearly $1 billion), ET reported. Olacabs declined to comment. With this, the company, which was founded in January 2011 by IIT Bombay alumni Bhavish Aggarwal and Ankit Bhati, joins the league of India's most valuable startups.
