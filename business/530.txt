The net worth of the world's 500-richest people dropped by $68.5 billion on Friday, with billionaire Warren Buffett leading the decline as his fortune fell by $3.3 billion. Google Co-founders Larry Page and Sergey Brin lost about $4.7 billion combined. However, the world's richest person Jeff Bezos led the billionaires whose wealth increased and added $3.2 billion to his fortune.
