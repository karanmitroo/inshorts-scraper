Glassware Company Borosil bought a 10th-floor, sea-facing, 1,706 sqft (2,625 sqft, with servant quarters), three-bedroom apartment of the marquee Samudra-Mahal, Worli (Mumbai) for Rs 21 crore, at Rs 1.23 lakh/sqft., the highest in the country. The deal overrode the Rs 1.2 lakh/sqft. paid for an apartment in Tahnee Heights on Nepean Sea Road by Jindal Drugs in June-2012.
