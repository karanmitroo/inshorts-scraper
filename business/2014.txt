Private sector lender HDFC Bank on Tuesday reported a 20.1% year-on-year increase in its September quarter profit to ₹4,151 crore. The bank's net interest income during the quarter rose by 22% year-on-year to ₹9,752 crore. As a percentage of total loans, gross bad loans rose to 1.26% as compared to 1.24% in the previous quarter.
