HDFC bank, India's second-biggest private lender by assets, on Monday reported a net profit of ₹3,356.8 crore for the quarter ended December, registering a year-on-year increase of 20.1%. The total income also rose to ₹18,282 crore against ₹14,931 crore during the period under review. Net interest income, the difference between interest earned and interest expended, grew 24%.
