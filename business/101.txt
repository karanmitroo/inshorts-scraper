Adani Group Founder Gautam Adani, whose infrastructure group is India's largest coal trader, has doubled his wealth to $14 billion over the last year, according to Hurun Global Rich List 2018. The 55-year-old tycoon's wealth surged 109%, the most in percentage terms among the Indian billionaires in the list. Adani is also among the 19 new entrants in top 100.
