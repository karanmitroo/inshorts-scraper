Infosys promoters, including Co-founders Narayana Murthy and Nandan Nilekani, have offered to sell around 1.77 crore shares worth up to ₹2,038 crore in the company's ₹13,000 crore buyback offer. Nilekani and family have offered to tender 58 lakh shares, while Murthy and family have offered over 54 lakh shares. Notably, this is Infosys' first buyback offer in its 36-year history.
