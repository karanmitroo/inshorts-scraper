As many as 1,463 entities have bad loans worth ₹100 crore or more in 21 public sector banks, Finance Ministry data showed. At the end of September 2017, SBI alone had 265 accounts with bad loans of over ₹100 crore each, totalling ₹77,538 crore. Punjab National Bank had 143 such bad loan accounts, collectively owing the bank ₹45,973 crore.
