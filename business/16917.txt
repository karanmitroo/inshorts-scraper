Apollo Hospitals plans to invest Rs. 2,032.7 crore to open 12 hospitals in the next 3 years, it said in its Corporate Review and Business Update 2014. "Total funding requirement is Rs 2,032.7 crore. Already Rs 590.1 crore have been invested," it added. Most of the hospitals will be super-speciality, except one multi-speciality and one reach hospital.
