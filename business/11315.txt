The domestic life insurance industry registered a 22.12% rise in new business premium in 2015-16, on the back of high growth in group insurance policies. New business premium stood at ₹1.38 lakh crore in the fiscal, compared to ₹1.13 lakh crore in 2014-15. Private insurers witnessed 17.63% growth, while state-run Life Insurance Corporation of India saw a 24.74% jump.
