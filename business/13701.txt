The benchmark Sensex on Wednesday plummeted by 381.95 points (1.48%) to close at 25,482.52 points, while the broader Nifty slipped by 105.75 points (1.35%) to settle at 7,731.80 points. According to analysts, the dip is majorly attributed to rising concerns over weak earnings of Indian companies, falling commodity prices, and dampening global markets weighing down investor sentiments.
