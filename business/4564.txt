Former TCS CEO and current Tata Sons Chairman N Chandrasekaran earned over ₹30 crore in FY17, around 83% of which was commission. The rest included basic salary of ₹2.4 crore and benefits of ₹2.7 crore. While Chandrasekaran's salary as Tata Sons Chairman wasn't revealed, reports said he will get ₹4.8 crore as basic salary along with variable payout of 200%.
