The number of launches in affordable housing doubled in the first half of 2016 year-on-year, with the top eight cities seeing launches of 17,000 units (a 95% rise), a Cushman & Wakefield report stated. The total launches across all segments were 60,000 units, a 17% increase year-on-year. The highest number of launches were in Pune at 4,170 units, it added.
