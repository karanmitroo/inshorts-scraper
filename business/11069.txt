Domestic passenger car sales dipped marginally by 0.86% to 1.59 lakh units in May, from 1.60 lakh units in the corresponding period last year, according to data released by the Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers (SIAM). During the same period, sales of two-wheelers rose 9.75%, commercial vehicles grew 16.89% and vehicles across all categories increased by 9.89%, SIAM said.
