After Cyrus Mistry's exit, Tata Sons current Interim Chairman Ratan Tata's half-brother Noel Tata has reportedly emerged as one of the top contenders for the group's Chairman post. Tata Consultancy Services CEO N Chandrasekaran and Jaguar Land Rover CEO Ralf Speth are among the other contenders. A five-member committee is looking for a new Chairman for Tata Sons.
