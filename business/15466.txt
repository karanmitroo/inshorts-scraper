Cement manufacturer JK Laxmi Cement Friday tied up with e-commerce portal Snapdeal to sell its entire product range online. Customers can order cement, POP (plaster of Paris), RMC (Ready Mix Concrete), AAC (Autoclaved Aerated Concrete) blocks and gypsum plaster from the site. JK Lakshmi is the first company of its kind to make a foray into the online space.
