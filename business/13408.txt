According to data released by the Commerce Ministry, exports from India in November fell for the 12th straight month as they plunged by 24.43% year-on-year to $20.01 billion. Imports also slumped 30.26% to $29.79 billion compared to November last year. However, the trade deficit fell to $9.78 billion against $16.23 billion in November 2014 owing to a fall in imports.
