Flipkart Co-founders Sachin and Binny Bansal together own around 14% of the company while Paytm founder and CEO Vijay Shekhar Sharma controls roughly 20% of the payments platform. Ola Co-founders Bhavish Aggarwal and Ankit Bhati own little over 10% of the company. On the other hand, Snapdeal Co-founders Kunal Bahl and Rohit Bansal together own only 6.5% of the company.
