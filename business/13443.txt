Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) was India's biggest wealth creator between 2010-15, generating ₹3.46 lakh crore, according to a study by financial services firm Motilal Oswal. ITC (₹1.56 lakh crore) and HDFC Bank (₹1.54 lakh crore) were the next two biggest contributors. Ajanta Pharma boosted shareholder wealth at the highest pace while consumer/retail was the top wealth creator, sector-wise.
