Andhra Bank net profit surged 74% to ₹251 crore in the quarter ended September, against  ₹144 crore in the corresponding period last year. The bank's revenue jumped by 7.24% to ₹4,744.16 crore in the second quarter from ₹3,750 crore last year. Net non-performing assets (NPAs) fell to 2.95% from 3.86% last year.
