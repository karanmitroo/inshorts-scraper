India Cements on Saturday reported a 62.10% jump in standalone net profit, which stood at ₹62.41 crore for the quarter ended September. Its performance was aided by lower finance cost and tax expenditures. Its total income from operations grew to ₹1,314.44 crore during the quarter, up 6.94% from ₹1,229.1 crore a year earlier, the company said in a filing.
