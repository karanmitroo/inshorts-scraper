Tata Motors-owned Jaguar Land Rover sold a record 425,006 vehicles worldwide in 2013, up 19% from the previous year, as demand for its luxury vehicles increased in all major markets. Jaguar posted its strongest full-year sales performance since 2005 of 76,668 vehicles. Moreover, Jaguar also set new annual sales records in 17 markets, including, Brazil, India, Russia and China.
