India's largest paint company Asian Paints topped the list of Indian companies in the Forbes' 'World's Most Innovative Companies' list of 2016. According to Forbes, Asian Paints recorded a 12-month sales growth of 7.6% and scored 59.8% on its innovation index. Only four other Indian companies including Hindustan Unilever and Tata Consultancy Services were on the 100 innovative companies list.
