The combined market capitalisation (m-cap) of seven of the top ten companies listed on the BSE increased by over ₹1.14 lakh crore in the week ended March 4, with the Sensex gaining 6.44% to close at 24,646.48 points. Tata Consultancy Services and FMCG major ITC emerged as the biggest gainers, adding ₹28,443 crore and ₹20,101 crore to their m-cap respectively.
