India’s first GST collection hit ₹92,283 crore in July, surpassing the target of ₹91,000 crore, Finance Minister Arun Jaitley said on Tuesday. He further said that out of 59.57 lakh registered taxpayers, 38.38 lakh have filed GST returns so far. Late filing penalty of ₹100 a day would be levied for CGST and IGST, he added.
