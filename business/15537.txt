Microsoft's Indian-origin CEO, Satya Nadella has topped the list of the top-paid CEOs in the US, with annual pay package of $ 84.3 million (Rs 525 crore). Nadella is followed by Oracle's Larry Ellison, who was at the top last year. PepsiCo's Indian-origin CEO, Indra Nooyi stands at the 19th place, while Warren Buffet is ranked 100th.
