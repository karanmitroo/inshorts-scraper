Industrialist Ratan Tata has been nominated as a member of board of trustees of the Carnegie Endowment for International Peace, US's oldest think-tank. Welcoming Tata, Carnegie president JT Mathews expressed hope that he would be helpful in establishing Carnrgie's new South-Asia Centre in New Delhi. Tata is also the Chairman of two of the largest private-sector-promoted philanthropic-trusts in India.
