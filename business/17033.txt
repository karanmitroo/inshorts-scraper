The co-founders of Flipkart now have a combined worth of approx Rs 6000 crore, as the company was recently valued (reportedly) at around $7 billion (Rs 42000 crores) in the recent $1 billion funding round. Notably, Bangalore's iconic tech billionaires Narayana Murthy and Nandan Nilekani of Infosys have net worths of around Rs 8,700 crore and Rs 6,500 crore, respectively.
