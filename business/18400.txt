Rupee slumped 1.6% to a record low of 64.13 against dollar in early trade Tuesday, closing at 63.25 and bond yields hit another five-year high. Sensex fell 1.2% to an 11-month low. "India's problems are nowhere near resolution because it has not done anything-there is no focus on improving productivity, infrastructure or getting FDI back," said Nomura credit-analyst Pradeep Mohinani.
