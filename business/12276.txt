State-owned Oil and Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC) Ltd plans to invest $5 billion in the Krishna-Godavari (KG) basin by March-end, the company chairman DK Sarraf has said. The announcement follows the government decision to allow a higher pricing policy for undeveloped gas discoveries in India. Reportedly, the investment can boost ONGC's gas output by around 25% in next 4-5 years.
