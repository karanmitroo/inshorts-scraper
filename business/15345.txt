The BSE Sensex declined by 310.58 points (1 percent) to 27646.92 and Nifty went down 91.30 points (1 percent) to 8367.65 on Monday. As many as 1163 shares advanced, 1521 shares declined and 163 shares remained unchanged. Vedanta, ITC, Tata Steel, HDFC and GAIL were the major losers, while ONGC, Bharti Airtel and Wipro were the major gainers.
