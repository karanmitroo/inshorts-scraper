The S&P BSE Greenex index, which includes top 25 of the BSE-100 companies according to their performance based on carbon emissions, turnover and free-float market capitalisation, lost 18.18 points on Tuesday. Tata Motors and Bajaj Auto were the major gainers, while, DLF and GAIL were the major laggards. The index is re-balanced bi-annually, based on fresh carbon emission numbers.
