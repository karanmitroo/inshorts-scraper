Four months before Cyrus Mistry was sacked from the Tata Sons Chairman post, the firm's remuneration panel had lauded Mistry's performance and recommended a salary hike, reports said. This comes amid allegations that Mistry mishandled Tata's dispute with Japanese telecom major NTT DoCoMo. Meanwhile, interim Chairman Ratan Tata said Mistry's removal was "absolutely necessary" for Tata's future.
