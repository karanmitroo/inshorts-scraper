India-born brothers Srichand and Gopichand Hinduja of the Hinduja Group have topped the Sunday Times Rich List 2017 for the UK with a combined fortune of $21 billion. David and Simon Reuben, of Iraqi Jewish origin but Mumbai-born, have been ranked the third-richest with an $18 billion fortune. Steel magnate Lakshmi N Mittal is UK's fourth-richest with $17 billion.
