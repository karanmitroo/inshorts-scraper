The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty on Friday closed on a flat note at 25,301.70 and 7,718.05, down by 40.16 and 20.35 points respectively, on profit booking by investors amid weak global cues. BHEL, ITC, Larsen & Toubro, and Axis Bank emerged as the biggest gainers, while GAIL, TCS, ONGC, and Bharti Airtel ended as the major losers.
