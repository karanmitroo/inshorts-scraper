Due to a heavy selling in the shares of RIL and TCS, the combined market capitalisation of eight most-valued Sensex companies including RIL, TCS, CIL and HDFC Bank, fell by Rs. 43,430 crore in the past week. The market-caps of RIL and TCS dropped by Rs. 13015 crore and Rs. 12888 crore respectively making them the biggest losers.
