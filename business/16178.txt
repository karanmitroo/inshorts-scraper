Audi on Thursday launched its limited edition sports car Audi R8 LMX at Rs 2.97 crore (ex-showroom Delhi). Audi R8 LMX is the first production car with laser high beam lighting in India, the German luxury car maker claimed. The car can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/hr in 3.4 seconds and reach a top speed of 320 km/hr.
