The BSE benchmark Sensex is likely to cross the level of 21,000 this Diwali, driven by robust foreign fund inflow and good quarterly earnings from corporates so far. The 30-stock index is 323.88 points away from its all-time peak of 21,206.77 hit on January 10, 2008. Sensex has gained 1,365.74 (6.99%) so far this month to reach 20,882.89.
