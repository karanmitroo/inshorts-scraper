The combined market capitalisation of six of the top ten BSE-listed firms surged by ₹16,445.27 crore in the week ended August 5, with the benchmark Sensex rising 26.49 points to close at 28,078.35. TCS witnessed the highest rise in m-cap at ₹5,980.25 crore followed by ONGC and Sun Pharma, while HDFC saw the biggest fall at ₹5,363.14 crore.
