Sunil Mittal, who has been re-appointed as Bharti Airtel’s Chairman for another five years, will get an annual pay package of ₹30 crore. The package includes a fixed pay of ₹21 crore and a performance-linked variable pay of ₹9 crore. According to Forbes, Sunil Mittal is India's 9th richest person with a net worth of $5.7 billion (over ₹38,200 crore).
