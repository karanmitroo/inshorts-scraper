The world's richest person Amazon CEO Jeff Bezos' net worth dropped by $5.3 billion to $113 billion on Thursday. Berkshire Hathaway Chairman Warren Buffett's wealth fell $3.5 billion and Facebook CEO Mark Zuckerberg lost $3.4 billion. The world's 500 richest people lost $93 billion in net worth, and 20 of them lost at least $1 billion each.
