Former SpiceJet Chief Operating Officer (COO) Sanjiv Kapoor today joined TATA-Singapore Airlines run Vistara as its Chief Strategy and Commercial Officer. Kapoor succeeds Giam Ming Toh, who is returning to Singapore Airlines on completion of his deputation with Vistara. Kapoor, who joined SpiceJet in 2013, has over 19 years of experience in the airline industry.
