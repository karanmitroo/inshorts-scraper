India's biggest listed real estate developer, DLF Ltd reported a 21% increase in the net profit year-on-year to ₹132 crore for the quarter ended September. The company's revenue decreased 6.5% to ₹1,997 crore y-o-y in the reporting period. "Low consumer interest, coupled with infrastructural constraints, delayed approval and high cost of capital afflict the industry," DLF said in a statement.
