Tata Sons' ousted Chairman Cyrus Mistry has claimed the company was spending nearly ₹100 crore yearly on Ratan Tata to bear his office expenses and public relations of Tata. Mistry said that while Ratan Tata decided to spend ₹60 crore on Public Relations (PR) just before Mistry joined, more than ₹30 crore was accounted for office expenses, including corporate jets.
