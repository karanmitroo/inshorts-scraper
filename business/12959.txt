ITC on Friday reported a net profit of ₹2,652.82 crore for the third quarter ended December,  a marginal increase against the net profit of ₹2,635.00 crore during the corresponding period last fiscal. Net sales of the company rose 3.43% to ₹9,102.66 crore for the quarter under review against the year-ago period. The company's shares rose 0.69% on BSE during trade.
