The Haryana government utilised 80% of its borrowed funds of ₹18,859 crore in 2014-15 on repayment of principal and interest on public debt of previous years, the Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG) has said. Further, the public debt and liabilities of Haryana have increased from ₹19,588 crore in 2005-06 to ₹70,925 crore in 2014-15, CAG added.
