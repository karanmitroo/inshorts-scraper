The benchmark indices on Tuesday ended the trade in losses after the BSE Sensex and the NSE Nifty fell by 70.58 and 16.65 points to close at 28,223.70 and 8,706.40 respectively. TCS, Lupin, and Asian Paints emerged as the biggest gainers, while Adani Ports SEZ, Bharti Airtel, and Larsen and Toubro were the day's major losers.
