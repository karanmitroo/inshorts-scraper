A total of about 3-lakh telephone connections of unregistered telemarketers have been disconnected and over 25000 unregistered entities have been blacklisted for making pesky calls and SMSes who will not get a phone connection for next 2-years, telecom minister Kapil Sibal said on Friday. As per TRAI guidelines, only registered telemarketers with numbers starting with 140 can make promotional calls.
