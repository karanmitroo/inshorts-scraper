The BSE Sensex and the NSE Nifty slipped for the second consecutive week amid mixed quarterly results and weak rupee among other reasons. Sensex plunged 391 points to settle at 26,265.24, while Nifty slipped by 111.50 points to close at 7954.30. Major losers – Dr Reddy’s Laboratories and Tata Steel – fell by 14.99% and 10.80% respectively.
