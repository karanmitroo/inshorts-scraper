Japan’s second largest life insurance company Nippon Life Insurance on Tuesday signed a definitive agreement to raise its stake in Anil Ambani-controlled Reliance Life Insurance to 49% from the existing 26%. Nippon's payment of ₹2,265 crore ($341 million) for the additional 23% in the company, being renamed as Reliance Nippon Life Insurance, pegs the latter's valuation at nearly ₹10,000 crore.
