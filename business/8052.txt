Finance Minister Arun Jaitley post an emergency meeting with banks said the State Bank of India alone has made 2.28 crore transactions in the two days following the demonetisation of the ₹500 and ₹1,000 notes. Further, 20-25% of the total banking operations in India were by SBI and its organisations, he said. Notably, SBI saw ₹54,370 crores worth monetary transactions.
