The combined market capitalisation (m-cap) of three of the top ten companies (Reliance Industries, Sun Pharma and ONGC) listed on the BSE Sensex fell by ₹29,464 crore in the week ended February 5, with the index dropping around 1% to 24,616.97 points. In contrast, the other seven of the top ten firms recorded a cumulative gain of ₹25,639 crore.
