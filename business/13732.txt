Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPIs) pulled out around ₹2,818 crore between November 2-13 from the capital markets, reportedly triggered by weak quarterly earnings reports and fears of an interest rate hike by the US. Equities markets witnessed an outflow of ₹2,505 crore while debt markets saw outflows worth ₹313 crore. Earlier, FPIs registered a net investment of ₹22,350 crore in October.
