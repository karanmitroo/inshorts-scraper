To expand their manufacturing capacity in Maharashtra, several carmakers including Tata Motors Ltd and Volkswagen AG, have agreed to invest 115 billion rupees ($1.9 billion), a statement said Thursday. While Tata will invest 40 billion, Volkswagen agreed to invest 15 billion rupees. The rest will be invested by Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd (40 billion) and Bajaj Auto Ltd (20 billion).
