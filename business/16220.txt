Mercedes-Benz will increase prices of its products in India by 4% across models from January 22 following withdrawal of excise duty cuts. Mercedes-Benz sells A-Class at Rs 26 lakh and sedan S 500 at Rs 1.44 crore. It sells the AMG range - starting with CLA 45 AMG at Rs 68.5 lakh to G63 AMG priced at Rs 1.8 crore.
