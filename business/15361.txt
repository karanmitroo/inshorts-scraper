The BSE Sensex rose for a second straight day, gaining 363 points on Monday to end at an over three-week high of 27,687.30. The NSE Nifty gained 111.30 points to regain the 8,300 level, ending at 8,373.65. The volatility index dropped 9% to 17.90. The government's declaration of containing fiscal deficit at 4% of the GDP boosted market sentiment.
