The Income Tax Department has sent notices to 1.16 lakh people who made cash deposits of more than ₹25 lakh after demonetisation but failed to file returns. They have been asked to file I-T returns within 30 days, CBDT Chairman Sushil Chandra said. He added that 2.4 lakh people made cash deposits of ₹10-25 lakh who have not filed returns.
