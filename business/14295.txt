Domestic passenger car sales increased by 9.5% to 1.69 lakh units in September 2015 from 1.54 lakh units in September 2014, according to the data released by the Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers (SIAM). During the same period, sales of motorcycles fell by 2.87%, commercial vehicles rose by 12.07% and vehicles across all categories fell by 0.45%, SIAM said.
