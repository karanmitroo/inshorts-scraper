Tech Mahindra CEO CP Gurnani received ₹165.6 crore in FY15 and ₹45.3 crore in FY16. Further, Vineet Nayyar, who was Executive Vice Chairman of the company till August 2015, made ₹299.4 crore in total in the two fiscals. In FY17, Infosys CEO Vishal Sikka earned ₹45.1 crore, while former TCS CEO and current Tata Chairman N Chandrasekaran made ₹30 crore.
