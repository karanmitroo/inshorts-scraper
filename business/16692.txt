Two-wheeler majors Hero MotoCorp and Honda raked in massive sales on Dhanteras selling 2-lakh and 1.65-lakh units respectively. “For the first time, our total sales to end-customers across the country in a single day far surpassed the 2-lakh mark, which is a growth of over 80% over Dhanteras day last year,” Hero MotoCorp Head(National Sales) said in a statement.
