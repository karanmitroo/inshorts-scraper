The benchmark Sensex on Monday slipped by 108 points (0.42%) to settle at 25,530.11, while, the broader Nifty fell 16.50 points (0.21%) to close at 7,765.40. ITC declined by over 6% and registered its biggest slump in nine months after a government-appointed panel on Friday suggested a 'sin' tax of 40% on tobacco and tobacco products.
