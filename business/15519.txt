Led by banks and capital goods stocks, the BSE Sensex Wednesday increased 214.09 points (0.8%) to 27890.13 and Nifty went up 51.95 points (0.6%) to 8429.70. As many as 1379 shares advanced, 1379 shares declined and 180 shares remained unchanged. HUL, Axis Bank, L&T and Hero were the top gainers, while Wipro, ONGC, SBI and Hindalco were the major losers.
