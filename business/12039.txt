The price of non-subsidised LPG was on Friday reduced by ₹4 per cylinder on global cues. A 14.2-kg cylinder will now cost ₹509.50 in Delhi. This is the third consecutive price cut after a reduction of ₹82.5 in February and ₹61.5 in March. Meanwhile, the price of Aviation Turbine Fuel (ATF), or jet fuel, was hiked by 8.7%.
