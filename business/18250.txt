The combined market valuation of 7 top Sensex firms dropped by Rs 59,572 crore last week. The companies that made gain were  CIL, Infosys and NTPC whereas TCS, ITC, RIL, ONGC, HDFC Bank, HUL and Bharti Airtel saw a downfall. In the top 10 list, TCS remained in the 1st position followed by ITC and RIL.
