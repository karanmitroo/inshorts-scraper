Entertainment Company Viacom on Friday acquired a 50 percent stake in Prism TV, a regional television player in India, which owns and operates ETV Bangla, ETV Marathi, ETV Kannada among others, for Rs 940 crore. The deal received clearance from India's Foreign Investment Promotion Board earlier in July. Viacom operates 10 channels in India, including MTV, Comedy Central, and Colors.
