The government on Friday hiked the excise duty on petrol by ₹1.6 per litre and on diesel by 40 paisa per litre. With this, the total excise duty on petrol increased to ₹19.06 per litre against the present ₹17.46 per litre. Meanwhile, total levy on diesel increased to ₹10.66 per litre against the present ₹10.26 per litre.
