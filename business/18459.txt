With strong Ramzan demand and tight supply, Wholesale Onion prices jumped to two-and-a-half-year high of Rs 24-per-kg at Maharashtra's Lasalgoan, Asia's biggest wholesale market for onion. Moreover, the rate is almost five times higher than levels at the same time last year. In Delhi, retail prices of onion are ruling at Rs 30-35-per-kg and prices are expected to rise further.
