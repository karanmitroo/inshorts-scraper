The Income Tax department is set to issue over 64,000 refunds of about ₹1,148 crore to small taxpayers by January-end as part of government’s directives to address grievances of the taxpayers in claiming their bonafide dues. The refunds pertain to taxpayers where the individual refunds in a case are below ₹5,000 and belong to Assessment Years of 2013-14 and 2014-15.
