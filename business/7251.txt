The RBI has supplied ₹5.5 lakh crore in fresh currency to the banking system since demonetisation, Economic Affairs Secretary Shaktikanta Das said. Further, “45-50% notes in value terms (of demonetised currency) have been supplied,” Das stated. “[B]y December 31, we should be able to supply ₹6 lakh crore to ₹6.5 lakh crore in currency value in the market,” he added.
