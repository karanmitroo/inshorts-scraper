Maruti Suzuki, the country's largest car maker, has launched the mild hybrid diesel version of Ciaz, called the Maruti Suzuki Ciaz SHVS. With a 1.3 litre diesel engine, the vehicle is priced in the range of Rs. 8.23 lakh to Rs. 10.17 lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi). The car claims to offer a fuel efficiency of 28 km/litre.
