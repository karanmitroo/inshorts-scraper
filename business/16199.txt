Bajaj Auto Ltd Thursday reported a 4.78% decline in its net profit to Rs 861.24 crore for the third quarter ended December 31,2014. Bajaj had posted a net profit of Rs 904.55 crore during the same quarter in the preceding year. Net sales during the quarter were at Rs 5,520 crore, as against Rs 5,294.55 crore in the year-ago quarter.
