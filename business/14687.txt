The BSE Sensex went up by 100.10 points (0.4 percent) to close at 27,931.64 and Nifty increased 28.60 points (0.3 percent) to 8,495.15. Sun Pharma, Lupin, Wipro, Hero and Cipla were the major gainers, while Hindalco, SBI, Coal India, Axis Bank and NTPC were the major losers. Notably, shares of Amtek Auto declined 36.36 percent.
