Apple's Senior Vice President of Retail, Angela Ahrendts, received a payout of $22.9 million (₹156 crore) in 2016, which is over $14 million (₹96 crore) more than what CEO Tim Cook received. Tim Cook's compensation was reduced 15% from $10.3 million (₹70 crore) in 2015 to $8.7 million (₹60 crore) in 2016 after the company failed to reach annual targets.
