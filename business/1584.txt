Tata Sons, the holding company of Tata group firms, has elevated group CFO Saurabh Agrawal and MD of Titan Bhaskar Bhat to the company's board. Agarwal was hired this year as Group CFO from the Aditya Birla Group, where he was the head of strategy. Bhat, who is also a director at Tata Chemicals, has been running Titan since 2002.
