Independent Director Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw has said everyone has confidence that Non-Executive Chairman Nandan Nilekani would lead Infosys out of its troubled times. "The unanimous decision of the board reflects the confidence every one of us had in his credible leadership," she stated. Earlier, ex-CFO Mohandas Pai said, "Nilekani coming back is good, he knows every body, he is approachable."
