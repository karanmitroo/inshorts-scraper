IndiGo's parent company, Interglobe Aviation on Monday raised ₹832 crores from anchor investors such as Goldman Sachs, Fidelity Investments, Merrill Lynch and 40 other companies a day before its IPO on Tuesday. Offered at a price band of ₹700-765 per share, this is set to be India's biggest IPO in three years, after Bharti Infratel's ₹4,155 crore offer.
