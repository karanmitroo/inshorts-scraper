According to RBI data, deposits by non-resident Indians (NRIs) in Indian banks in the first half of the ongoing fiscal jumped by 55% year-on-year. NRIs deposited $10 billion in foreign currency non-resident-bank (FCNR-B), non-resident (external) rupee account (NRERA) and non-resident ordinary (NRO) accounts in April-September. As of September 30, NRIs had deposited $121.8 billion in local banks.
