Markets on Wednesday logged their worst loss in over a month with Sensex falling 208 points to end at 27,057.41 hit by losses in bluechips. While, the National Stock Exchange index, Nifty fell below the crucial 8,100 mark and closed 58.85 points, down at 8,094.10 Wednesday. In addition, the rupee plunged to 61-mark against the US dollar intra-day.
