Nissan Motor on Wednesday launched the Datsun Go, an entry-level hatchback having a price range of Rs.3.12 lakh to Rs.3.69 lakh. The Go sports a 1,200cc petrol engine and has a fuel economy (ARAI) figure of 20.63 km. It is expected to give stiff competition to its rivals - Hyundai Eon (Rs 2.83-3.85 lakh) and Maruti Alto (Rs 2.72-3.25 lakh).
