The combined market capitalisation (m-cap) of the top seven companies listed on the BSE Sensex fell by ₹32,188 crore for the week ended April 1, with the index losing 67.92 points to close at 25,269.64. HDFC was the major loser after shedding ₹8,144 crore in m-cap, followed by Sun Pharma and CIL. RIL, ITC and HDFC Bank have registered gains.
