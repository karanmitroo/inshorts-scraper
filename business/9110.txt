The benchmark indices on Tuesday extended gains as the Sensex rose 91.26 points to settle at 28,334.55, while the broader Nifty gained 31.05 points to close at 8,769.15. This followed the Monetary Policy Committee cutting the repo rate by 25 basis points to a six-year low of 6.25%. ONGC was the top gainer while Coal India was the major loser.
