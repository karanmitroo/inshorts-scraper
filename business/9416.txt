Following Reliance Jio accusing Airtel of providing insufficient interconnection points, the latter stated that Jio is trying to cover up technical issues in its own network, which is causing call failures, by "blaming others". However, the Mukesh Ambani-led Jio responded by saying that there were zero Jio-to-Jio call failures, against 2 crore call failures between Jio and Airtel networks.
