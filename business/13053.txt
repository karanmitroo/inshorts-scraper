The benchmark Sensex and broader Nifty lost 2% during the week ended January 15 to end at their respective 19-month lows. Analysts attributed the losses to a global sell-off, weak corporate earnings, soft rupee, disappointing macro-economic data and downfall in crude oil prices. Sensex lost 479.29 points to close at 24,455.04, while Nifty fell 163.60 points to settle at 7,437.80.
