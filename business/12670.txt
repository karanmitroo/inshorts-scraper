Mahindra & Mahindra (M&M) on Friday reported a 14.13% year-on-year decline in its net profit to ₹808 crore for the quarter ended December. However, net sales increased by 17.1% to ₹10,900.39 crore. M&M’s tractor sales grew by 5% to 59,883 units, while combined sales of utility vehicles, trucks and light and commercial vehicles, rose by 15% to 1,30,888 units.
