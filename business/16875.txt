In the new flagship Jan Dhan Yojana, State Bank of India has taken the lead by opening over 30 lakh accounts so far, followed by Punjab National Bank. PNB opened about 20.24 lakh accounts, Canara Bank 16.21 lakh, Central Bank of India 15.98 lakh and Bank of Baroda 14.22 lakh. However, private banks taken together opened just 5.8 lakh accounts.
