Grounded carrier Kingfisher Airlines has paid Employee Provident Fund (EPF) for just two employees between January 2013 and September 2015, the Labour Ministry said on Tuesday. The airlines had 6,185 employees in March 2012 and it dropped to 3,339 in December 2012. Notably, the company's licence was revoked in October 2012 while the flight operations stopped from December 2012.
