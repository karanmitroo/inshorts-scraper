After the government's pep talk, the rupee on Friday made a stunning comeback, snapping a six-session losing streak to rise by 135-paise and close at 63.20, its second-biggest rise in a decade in absolute terms. Sensex gained 206.50 points (1.13%) after yesterday's 2.27% rise. Foreign institutional investors pulled out Rs 1,277.64-crore on Thursday, as per provisional data with stock exchanges.
