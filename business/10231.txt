India's fuel demand rose 3.9% in July when compared with the same month last year, data from the Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell of the Oil Ministry showed. Sales of petrol were 14.7% higher from a year earlier at 1.92 million tonnes. Liquefied petroleum gas (LPG) sales increased 8.8% to 1.71 million tonnes, while naphtha sales surged 6.3%.
