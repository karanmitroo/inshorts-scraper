According to Hurun Global Rich List 2016, India now has 111 billionaires, up from 97 in 2015. Reliance Industries chairman Mukesh Ambani leads the rankings for India, occupying the 21st spot with a $26 billion net worth. Globally, the number of billionaires increased by 99 to 2,188, with Bill Gates as the richest person with a $80 billion net worth.
