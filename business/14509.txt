The Benchmark Sensex recorded its highest close in over two weeks and surged 258.04 points (1%) to settle at 25,963.97 on Wednesday. The broader Nifty gained 70.05 points (0.89%) to stabilize at 7,899.15. Axis Bank and Bhart Airtel were among the top gainers on the BSE benchmark, ending with gains of 2.5% each.
