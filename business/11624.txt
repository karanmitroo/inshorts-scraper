The BSE Sensex on Monday lost 169.65 points to close at 25,436.97 while the NSE Nifty fell 43.90 points to settle at 7,805.90 amid weak Asian markets. BHEL, GAIL, Maruti Suzuki, Lupin and CIL emerged as the biggest gainers while ICICI Bank, Dr Reddys Labs, Adani Ports, Bharti Airtel and HDFC Bank registered the biggest losses.
