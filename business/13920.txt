Activision Blizzard, the gaming company behind ‘World of Warcraft’ and ‘Call of Duty’ franchises, on Monday announced that it would acquire Candy Crush maker King Digital for $5.9 billion. The purchase will be made through ABS Partners, a subsidiary of Activision Blizzard at a price of $18 per share, which is a 20% premium over King's October 30th closing price.
