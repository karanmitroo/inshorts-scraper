Yusuf Bashir, considered as a top-notch innovator and software product developer, will join Infosys to oversee the group's $500 mn startup fund. Aspiring for $20 bn revenue by 2020, Infosys CEO Vishal Sikka hired Yusuf to oversee the company's early-stage investments and head the newly-created incubator programme. Yusuf previously was part of Sikka's core team at German software company SAP.
