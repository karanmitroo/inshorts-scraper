Mercedes Benz launched its AMG C 63 S on Thursday in New Delhi at a price of Rs 1.3 crore onwards (ex-showroom Delhi). The sports car consumes 32% less fuel compared with its predecessors and speeds up to 100 km/h in four seconds. The new AMG 4.0-litre V8 bi-turbo engine gives a power output of 375kW (510 hp).
