Indian stocks retreated, led by lenders and industrials, as the BSE Sensex lost 0.2% to close at 20,854.92, falling from a one-month high. Sensex has risen 7.4% this year, the top performer among the four largest emerging markets. The rupee lost 0.1% to 62.3700 per dollar, as US manufacturing data boosted speculation the Federal Reserve will pare stimulus next month.
