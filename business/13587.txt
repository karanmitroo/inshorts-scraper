The BSE Sensex on Friday climbed 169.57 points (0.65%) to close at 26,128.20 while the NSE Nifty gained 58.90 points (0.75%) to settle at 7,942.70. Logistics companies traded higher on hopes of progress on the Goods and Services Tax (GST) Bill, even as the rupee's plunge to a 2-year low weighed on market sentiment, according to analysts.
