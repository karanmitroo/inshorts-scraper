Taxi service Ola cabs response to a customer Seelam Veerapa Naidu, a Hyderabad resident, demanding for a 'driver of Hindu faith only' has gone viral. In reply to Naidu's tweet, the cab service tweeted, "@SVeerapaNaidu Sorry, we do not discriminate against our drivers on the basis of their religion." Ola's tweet has been retweeted over 800 times since then.
