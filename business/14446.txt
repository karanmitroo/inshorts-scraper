With a net worth of $18.9 billion, Reliance Industries' chairman Mukesh Ambani has topped Forbes India Rich list for the ninth consecutive year. Sun Pharmaceuticals’ Dilip Shanghvi remained second with a net worth of $18 billion, followed by Wipro’s Azim Premji with a net worth of $15.9 billion. The combined net worth of India’s 100 richest stood at $345 billion.
