Euro Disney, which owns Disneyland Paris, faced record annual losses during the 2016 fiscal year as its group turnover fell by 6.91% to €1.27 billion (around ₹9,300 crore). Overall net loss stood at €858 million (around ₹6,200 crore) for the year and the number of visitors dropped by 10%. Notably, Paris has witnessed a fall in tourism after terror attacks.
