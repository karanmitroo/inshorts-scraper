IT firm Tech Mahindra on Wednesday said its net profit increased 30.44% to about ₹839 crore for the quarter ended September. The company's revenue increased 6.1% to about ₹7,606 crore in this quarter, from ₹7,167 crore in the same quarter last fiscal. Tech Mahindra said it added 21 clients during the quarter, taking the total active clients count to 885.
