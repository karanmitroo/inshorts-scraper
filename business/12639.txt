The combined market capitalisation (m-cap) of the top ten companies listed on the BSE Sensex fell by over ₹1.83 lakh crore in the week ended February 12, with the index dropping by 6.62% to a 21-month low of 22,986.12 points. Tata Consultancy Services and ONGC took the biggest hit, losing ₹39,280 crore and ₹22,586 crore from their market valuation respectively.
