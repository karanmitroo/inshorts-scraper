ILoveGin, a West London-based online gin and tonic club, hires a 'gintern' to test gins from around the world. The part-time position, which pays up to £20,000 (₹17.4 lakh), requires the 'gintern' to travel across Europe visiting one or two gin companies every week. According to ILoveGin's website, over 5,000 applicants had sent their resumes for the position.
