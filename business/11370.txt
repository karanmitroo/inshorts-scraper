The BSE Sensex on Friday fell by 97.82 points to close at 25,301.90, while the NSE Nifty dropped 33.70 points to settle at 7,749.70. Adani Ports SEZ, ITC, ONGC, NTPC, and Bajaj Auto emerged as the major gainers, while Lupin, ICICI Bank, Reliance Industries, Mahindra & Mahindra, and Cipla ended among the major losers.
