Snapping its eight-day-long winning streak, the Sensex on Wednesday ended down 23.41 points at 29,547.63. The 30-share index, which touched an all-time high of 29,786.32 and an intra-day low of 29,417.67 in day trade, closed lower ahead of the January F&O (Futures  & Options) series expiry. Tracking BSE, the 50-share Nifty also settled lower at 8,907.90, down 2.60 points.
