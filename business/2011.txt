Shares of Punjab National Bank (PNB) gained nearly 50% on Wednesday after the government announced a ₹2.11 lakh crore capital infusion plan for public sector banks. PNB was the biggest gainer among the banking stocks and its shares hit their 52-week high. Further, SBI gained around 26% adding ₹60,000 crore in market value, and Canara Bank surged nearly 40%.
