Infosys Co-founder Narayana Murthy on Tuesday said he was pleased that Co-founder Nandan Nilekani had returned to the company as Non-Executive Chairman. "I am confident the management team will rally behind Nilekani and bring back glory to Infosys," Murthy stated. "I wish Nandan the best of everything in his effort to bring back 'acche din' to Infosys," he further added.
