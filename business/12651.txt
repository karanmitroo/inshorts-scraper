India’s largest two-wheeler manufacturer Hero MotoCorp on Thursday reported a net profit of ₹795.81 crore for the quarter ended December, a rise of 36.5% over the corresponding period a year ago. Revenue increased by 6.7% to ₹7,295 crore. Total sales during the quarter witnessed a growth of 2.53% to 16.9 lakh units, with a boost during the festive season.
