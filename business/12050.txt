The BSE Sensex closed the 2015-16 fiscal at 25,341.86, recording a fall of 2,615.63 points or 9.36%, its worst performance since 2011-12. Meanwhile, the NSE Nifty lost 752.60 points or 9.72% to settle the fiscal at 7,738.40. Crash in commodity prices, the US Federal Reserve's rate hike, global slowdown, and slower pace of key domestic reforms weighed on market sentiment.
