The price of petrol was slashed by ₹2.25/litre while the price of diesel was cut by ₹0.42/litre on Friday. Petrol and diesel in Delhi will now cost ₹62.51/litre and ₹54.28/litre respectively. "The current level of international product prices of Petrol & Diesel and INR-USD exchange rate" warrants decrease in the price of petrol and diesel, state-owned IOCL said.
