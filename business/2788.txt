Investors have filed 13 FIRs against real estate majors such as Supertech and Amrapali Group. Eight FIRs were lodged against Amrapali Group, and one each against Supertech, Today Home Builder, Alpine group, JNC, and BRUY Limited. Earlier, the National Company Law Tribunal admitted IDBI Bank's insolvency plea against Jaypee Infratech, one of the 12 companies referred by the RBI.
