Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) has released a blacklist of 500 rejected products. The list includes about 32 Tata Starbucks products (mainly syrups), Kellog’s Special K Red Berries cereal, Ranbaxy Revital tablets and Amway’s Nutrilite mixed drink flavour for children. Most of these products are found to have excess sugar, caramel, iron filings, salt or heavy metals.
