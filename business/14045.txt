Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPIs) put in ₹19,383 crore in the Indian capital market in October so far, the highest investment by FPIs since April when it stood at ₹15,333 crore. A total of ₹13,838 was invested in the debt market while ₹5,545 crore was put in the stock market. Earlier, outflows of ₹23,000 crore were registered in September and August.
