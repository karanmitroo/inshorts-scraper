The benchmark Sensex on Tuesday plummeted 378.14 points to settle at 25,743.26, after the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) cut its global GDP forecast for 2015 to 2.9% on Monday from previously estimated 3.7% in November 2014. Meanwhile, the broader Nifty slipped 131.85 points to close at 7,783.35 points.
