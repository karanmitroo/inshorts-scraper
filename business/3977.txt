Russian luxury jewellery firm Caviar has made a 'limited edition' fidget spinner, which is about to go on sale for £13,000 (nearly ₹11 lakh). The exclusive gold-plated Caviar Spinner Full Gold is believed to be the most expensive of its kind in the world. The firm also offers fidget spinners made out of diamonds for £1,290 (over ₹1 lakh).
