The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty on Friday closed on a flat note, with the Sensex gaining 3.52 points to 25,606.62, and the Nifty rising 2.55 points to 7,849.80, amid weak global cues. Lupin, Cipla, HDFC Bank, and Maruti Suzuki emerged as the biggest gainers while Bharti Airtel, SBI, ICICI Bank, and Reliance Industries ended as the major losers.
