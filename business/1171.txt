Mukesh Ambani-led Reliance Jio has emerged as the front-runner to acquire the assets of Anil Ambani-led Reliance Communications (RCom), according to reports. Reportedly, Reliance Jio is interested in acquiring a range of RCom's spectrum, which will be valued at about ₹19,000 crore. RCom, which has a debt of about ₹45,000 crore, is in a standstill period till December 2018.
