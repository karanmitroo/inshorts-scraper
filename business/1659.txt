Anil Ambani-led Reliance Communications on Saturday posted a loss of ₹2,709 crore for the September quarter, marking the company's fourth straight quarter of loss. The company had registered a profit of ₹62 crore in the same period a year ago. The revenue of the company, which is reeling under ₹45,000-crore debt, also fell 48% to ₹2,667 crore.
