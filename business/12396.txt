The BSE Sensex and the NSE Nifty continued to register gains following the Union Budget, as the indices climbed 39.49 points and 9.75 points to settle at 24,646.48 and 7,485.35 respectively. Investor sentiment remained upbeat after a report suggested that oil prices are likely to recover after hitting a 12-year low, from the current $34/barrel to $55 in 12 months.
