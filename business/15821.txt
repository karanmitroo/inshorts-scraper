A day before the Union Budget, the BSE Sensex on Friday surged 473.47 points, ending at 29,220.12. The 50-share Nifty too went up by 160.75 points, closing at 8,844.60. HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank and Axis Bank were the top gainers, while ITC, GAIL, Wipro and HUL were the top losers. Markets will remain open on Saturday too.
