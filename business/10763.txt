Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPIs) pumped in ₹3,713 crore in India's capital markets in June, taking the year's total to ₹20,648 crore. Positive macroeconomic data and forecasts of a good monsoon resulted in an increased inflow, analysts said. However, FPIs withdrew ₹6,220 crore from the debt market during the month, taking the total outflow for the year to ₹12,105 crore.
