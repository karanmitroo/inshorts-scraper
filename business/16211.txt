Honda Cars India Tuesday launched new variants of its compact sedan Amaze, with the petrol variant priced at Rs. 7.32 lakh and the diesel grade priced at Rs. 8.2 lakh. The company also expanded the Brio hatchback range by adding two new variants. The manual model is priced at Rs. 5.99 lakh, and the automatic variant at Rs. 6.78 lakh.
