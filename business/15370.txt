Low-cost airline Spicejet launched a 3-day sale on Tuesday to commemorate its 10th birthday on May 23. During the sale, the tickets can be booked till Midnight, May 21 for travel period between 1 Jul'15 to 15 Oct'15, both dates inclusive. The limited inventory offer is available on all domestic routes, and seats will be available on the First-come-First-Served basis.
