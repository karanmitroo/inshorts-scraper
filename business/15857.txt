Amul will invest Rs 5,000 crore over the next three years to ramp up milk production and new processing capacities. "The new investments will help attaining Rs 50,000 crore turnover in 2-3 years from Rs 18,000 crore as on March 2014," managing director RS Sodhi said. Amul will be setting up 10 new milk processing plants across India, he added.
