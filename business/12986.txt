Tracking weak cues from the Asian markets, the benchmark Sensex on Wednesday slipped by around 400 points during early trade to hit a 52-week low of 24101.50. Meanwhile, the broader Nifty tanked by over 130 points to 7,315.70, its lowest level since June 2014. Further, the rupee tumbled 30 paise to trade over 28-month low at 67.95 against the dollar.
