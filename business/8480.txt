Tata Group stocks reportedly fell by up to 3% on Tuesday as its key firms collectively lost about ₹10,700 crore in market capitalisation after Tata Sons Chairman Cyrus Mistry was removed from his post. The market valuation of Tata Consultancy Services declined by ₹5,753 crore, and the dip in Tata dragged down the Sensex from a three-week high, reports stated.
