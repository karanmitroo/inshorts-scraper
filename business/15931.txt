HDFC Bank Saturday reported a 20% rise in its net profit at Rs 2,794.51 crore for the third quarter ended December. The bank had reported a net profit of Rs 2,325.70 crore in the year-ago period. Total income of HDFC rose to Rs 14,930.74 crore in Q3, as against Rs 12,738.95 crore in the corresponding quarter of last financial year.
