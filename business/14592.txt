The Bombay Stock Exchange's 30-share Sensex on Thursday broke its three-day losing streak as it gained about 311 points to close at 25,764.78. The National Stock Exchange's Nifty index also posted gains, finishing at 7,823 points, a gain of 106. Of the 30 Sensex stocks, 27 posted gains. Tata Steel, Vedanta, and Axis Bank were the day's top gainers.
