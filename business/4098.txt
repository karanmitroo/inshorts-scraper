ICICI Bank on Thursday said a lenders' consortium led by it has concluded the biggest asset resolution in India with consummation of the ₹16,189 crore takeover of Jaypee Cement by Ultratech. The lenders could reportedly receive about ₹4,000 crore from the sale. "I hope this landmark transaction will pave the way for more such resolutions,” MD Chanda Kochhar said.
