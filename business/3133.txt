Actor Kamaal R Khan (KRK) in a series of tweets has slammed Gautam Singhania for harassing his father Raymond Group's Chairman Emeritus Vijaypat Singhania, who was once among India's richest men. "I have seen many Ghatiya people in life but I haven't seen bigger Ghatiya than you who is harassing 78 years old father," tweeted KRK.
