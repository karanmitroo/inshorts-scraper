Maruti Suzuki India Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) Kenichi Ayukawa took home a total remuneration of ₹4.2 crore in 2016-17, an increase of 6.32% from the previous fiscal. This included a performance-linked bonus of ₹1.5 crore. Further, the company's Chairman RC Bhargava received a total remuneration of ₹99 lakh in 2016-17, compared to ₹87.5 lakh in previous fiscal.
