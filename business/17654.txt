The beverages and snacks giant, PepsiCo, has given its India-born chief Indra Nooyi (58) a pay package of $18.6 million (about Rs 113 crore) for 2013, a 7% hike from the previous year. PepsiCo said that the company has delivered strong performance in 2013 under the leadership of Nooyi, who has been chief executive of the company since 2006.
