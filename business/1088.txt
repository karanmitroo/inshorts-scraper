Benchmark indices BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty closed the calendar year 2017 higher by 28% and 29% respectively. Sensex closed at a record high of 34,056.83 points compared to last year's close at 26,626.46. The Nifty 50 index closed at 10,530.70 points compared with its close in 2016 at 8,185.80 points.
