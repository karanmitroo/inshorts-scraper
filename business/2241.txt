The richest newcomer on Forbes India Rich List 2017 is Wadia Group Chairman Nusli Wadia, ranked 25th with a net worth of $5.6 billion. Other newcomers include Dinesh Nandwana of e-governance services firm Vakrangee (88th spot, $1.72 billion), Vijay Shekhar Sharma of mobile wallet Paytm (99th spot, $1.47 billion), and Rana Kapoor of Yes Bank (100th spot, $1.46 billion).
