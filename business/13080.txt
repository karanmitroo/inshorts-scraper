According to data released by market regulator Sebi, Assets Under Management (AUM) of portfolio managers increased to ₹10.41 lakh crore at the end of December 2015, as against ₹8.68 lakh crore in December 2014. Further, of the total assets managed by portfolio mangers, about ₹7.08 lakh crore was contributed by employee provident fund organisation (EPFO) or provident fund (PF).
