The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has sent blocks of demonetised ₹500 and ₹1,000 notes weighing 200 kg to the students of the National Institute of Design (NID) for recycling. The institute will hold an all-India competition in May to recycle these blocks into some useful items. Winning ideas will be awarded prize money of ₹50,000, ₹75,000 and ₹1 lakh.
