State-owned banks installed a total of 13,935 automatic teller machines (ATMs) during the 2015-16 fiscal but missed their cumulative target of 15,249, according to data disclosed by 27 banks. With this addition, the total number of ATMs of PSU banks rose to over 1.42 lakh at the end of March, with the country's biggest lender SBI having 49,724 ATMs.
