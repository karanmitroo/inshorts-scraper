Eleven Indians have lost their billionaire status mainly due to demonetisation, as per Hurun's Global Rich List released on Tuesday. With a total of 100 billionaires, India has dropped to the 4th position among nations with most billionaires. Reliance Industries Chairman Mukesh Ambani continues to be India's richest person, followed by SP Hinduja and family, and Sun Pharma's Dilip Shanghvi.
