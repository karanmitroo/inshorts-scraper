Bharti Airtel said on Monday that it will invest ₹60,000 crore in India to improve quality of its services over three years. Over 70,000 base stations will be deployed as part of its 'Project Leap', this fiscal and over the three years, more than 1,60,000 base stations will be set up countrywide. Airtel has already invested ₹1,60,000 crore till date.
