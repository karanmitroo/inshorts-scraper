A total of 133 companies with outstanding dues of ₹500 crore or more under direct and indirect taxes, owed over ₹3.39 lakh crore approximately to the exchequer, Finance Minister Arun Jaitley stated. This included one case of service tax in which amount due is ₹1,606.26 crore. He added out of the outstanding corporate dues, over ₹2.45 lakh crore weren't realisable.
