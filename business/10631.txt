The combined market capitalisation (m-cap) of eight of the top 10 BSE-listed firms rose by ₹57,965.56 crore in the week ended July 15, with the benchmark Sensex rising 709.60 points to close at 27,836.50. The m-cap of HDFC Bank surged the highest by ₹13,030 crore, followed by HDFC and RIL, while Infosys slumped the most by ₹19,868 crore.
