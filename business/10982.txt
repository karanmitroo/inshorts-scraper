Oil companies on Wednesday hiked the price of petrol by ₹0.05/litre and the price of diesel by ₹1.26/litre, effective midnight. Petrol and diesel in Delhi will now cost ₹65.65/litre and ₹55.19/litre respectively. "The current level of international product prices of Petrol & Diesel and INR-USD exchange rate" warrants increase in the price of petrol and diesel, state-owned IOCL said.
