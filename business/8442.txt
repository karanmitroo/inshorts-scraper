Cairn India, which operates Rajasthan's Barmer gas fields, is planning to invest $100 million to increase gas production to 40-45 million standard cubic feet per day (mscfd) by 2017 and to 100 mscfd by 2018-19. The investment is estimated for FY17 with a focus on developing the Raageshwari Deep Gas project and enhanced recovery from Mangala block, an official said.
