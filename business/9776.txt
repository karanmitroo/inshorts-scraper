The benchmark indices on Friday posted gains as the Sensex rose 108.63 points to settle at 28,532.11 points, while Nifty gained 35.00 points to close at 8,809.65 points. Market sentiment remained firm on Chief Economic Advisor Arvind Subramanian stating GDP growth rate could be sustained at 8-10%. Adani Ports was the biggest gainer while Coal India was the major loser.
