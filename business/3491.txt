Hui Ka Yan, Chairman of property developer Evergrande Group, saw his fortune jump by $8 billion (₹51,300 crore) to $33.5 billion in just two days. The rise in Hui's wealth followed a surge in his firm's shares, which rose by 34%. Notably, the firm's stock has increased 393% this year, giving Hui year-to-date boost of $24.5 billion (₹1.57 lakh crore).
