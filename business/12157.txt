Finance Minister Arun Jaitley on Tuesday launched the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) and Unified Package Insurance Scheme in Mumbai. Terming agriculture as "absolutely critical to the country's economy", Jaitley said the revamped insurance schemes would be rolled out from April 1 to cover Kharif crops. Earlier, the Union Cabinet had in January approved the launch of PMFBY.
