The benchmark Sensex on Tuesday crashed 538.12 points, its biggest single-day drop in the year 2014, to close at 26,781.44 points. Also, the NSE Nifty ended down 152 points at 8,067.60. Both the indices dropped amid weak trade deficit data, sharp falls in global markets and weakening rupee which slumped to a 13-month low of 63.58 against the dollar (intra-day).
