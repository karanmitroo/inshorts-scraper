With a disinvestment target of INR 16,027 crore, the government will sell 5% stake in Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd to Life Insurance Corporation of India, fetching it about INR 2046 crore. Earlier on Friday, the EGoM had cleared a 10% stake sale in Indian Oil Corporation to ONGC and Oil India, which would fetch the exchequer about INR 5300 crore.
