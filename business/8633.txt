India's second-largest developer by market value Oberoi Realty's net profit rose 12.45% year-on-year to stand at ₹83.72 crore for the quarter ended September. Further, it posted net sales of ₹250.89 crore for the quarter, a 32.6% jump from the year-ago period. On a standalone basis, Oberoi's second quarter net profit stood at ₹70.8 crore while net sales were ₹194 crore.
