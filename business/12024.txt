Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPIs) have registered a net inflow of ₹19,667 crore (around $2.93 billion) in March, after pulling out funds in the preceding four months. While FPIs invested ₹21,143 crore in the equity markets, ₹1,476 crore was pulled out from debt markets during the period. Analysts attributed the inflows to continued hopes of a rate cut by RBI.
