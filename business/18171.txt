Reliance Industries Chairman Mukesh Ambani remains India's richest man for the second consecutive year, with personal assets of $18.9 billion, followed by LN Mittal ($15.9 billion) and Dilip Shanghvi ($14.1 billion), said a report by China-based Hurun India Rich List. Savitri Jindal, the non-executive chairperson of OP Jindal Group, is the richest Indian woman with assets of $5.1 billion.
