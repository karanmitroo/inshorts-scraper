An anonymous philanthropist has donated $5 million worth of Bitcoin to US-based non-profit organisation Open Medicine Foundation. The donor, who goes by the nickname 'Pine', created 'The Pineapple Fund' in December to give away 5,057 Bitcoins, then worth $86 million. The fund has so far donated $37 million to 42 charities, with 23 of them getting $1 million or more.
