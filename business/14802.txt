The central government has invested Rs 4.85 lakh crore in infrastructure projects, costing over Rs 150 crore each, as of June 1 in the current fiscal. The projects fall under petroleum, power, urban development, rail and road transport departments among others. The total investment figure was around Rs 5.44 lakh crore in 2014-15 and Rs 5.02 lakh crore in 2013-14.
