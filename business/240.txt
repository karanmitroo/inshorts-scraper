Kangana Ranaut has accused Mehul Choksi-owned Gitanjali Gems of not paying her for advertisement campaigns that she featured in for the company's brand 'Nakshatra'. The actress was appointed the brand ambassador of Nakshatra diamonds in 2016. Further, actress Bipasha Basu has accused Gitanjali Group of using her pictures to endorse 'Gili', even after her contract with the brand expired.
