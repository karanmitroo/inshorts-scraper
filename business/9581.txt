Around 500 Domino's outlets across India will turn 'all-vegetarian' during the Navratri festive period starting October 1, Domino's Pizza India President Dev Amritesh has said. Further, the pizza chain will not use onion and garlic in their preparation and make the pizza base with 'singhara atta', reports said. Domino's leads the organised pizza market in India with over 70% market-share.
