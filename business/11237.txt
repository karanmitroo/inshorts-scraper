The combined market capitalisation (m-cap) of the top 10 BSE-listed firms surged by ₹98,598 crore in the week ended May 27, with the benchmark Sensex rising 1,351.70 points (5.34%) to close at 26,653.60. ITC witnessed the highest gain in market valuation at ₹23,739 crore followed by RIL (₹12,495 crore), HDFC (₹12,227 crore), HDFC Bank (₹11,900 crore) and HUL (₹10,581 crore).
