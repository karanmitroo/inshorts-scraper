Rising for the sixth straight session, the benchmark indices Wednesday hit their highest in nearly 2-1/2 weeks. The BSE Sensex closed 162.96 points higher at 29,298.84, buoyed by gains in shares of capital goods and consumer durables. The Sensex touched a high of 29,411.32 and a low of 29,126.91 intra-day. The 50-share Nifty rose 56.95 points to close at 8,866.30.
