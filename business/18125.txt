On Thursday, Infosys board member V Balakrishnan sold 1-lakh equity shares (held by his two daughters) in the company for Rs.33-crore via the open market. Balakrishnan, who heads Infosys BPO and the India business, is among those in line to succeed as CEO in 2015. When contacted, Balakrishnan said that he'd sold the shares for personal requirements.
