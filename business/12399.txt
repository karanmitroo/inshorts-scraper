The government on Friday appointed the CEO of Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) N Chandrasekaran and two others as part-time non-official directors to the board of RBI. Former Chief Secretary of Gujarat Sudhir Mankad and Mahindra & Mahindra non-executive chairman BN Doshi were the other two appointed members. The government can nominate up to ten non-official directors to the RBI board.
