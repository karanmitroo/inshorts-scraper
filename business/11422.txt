Loan recovery cases worth ₹4.5 lakh crore filed by banks were pending with government body Debt Recovery Tribunal (DRT) by December 31, 2015, according to Finance Ministry data. Notably, this was more than the gross non-performing assets of banks in the period. Further, during April-December 2015, 35% of the ₹88,974 crore worth of cases filed before the DRT were settled.
