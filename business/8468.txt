Vijaya Bank has reported a 34.05% year-on-year rise in its net profit for the second quarter, which stood at ₹154.55 crore. The bank's net interest income, or the core income a bank earns by giving loans, rose 19.5% from the year-ago period to ₹827.82 crore during the quarter. Gross NPAs were 7.07% of the total loans at the quarter-end.
