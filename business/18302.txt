Union Finance-minister Chidambaram on Monday launched the IRDA's insurance repository system(IRS), which will provide policy-holders the facility to keep insurance-policies in electronic-form. IRS will also have digitised non-life insurance-policies soon. Repositories licence, valid till July-31,2014 has been given to five-companies -NSDL Database Management Limited, Central Insurance Repository Limited, SHCIL Projects Limited, CAMS Repository Services Limited and Karvy Insurance Repository Limited.
