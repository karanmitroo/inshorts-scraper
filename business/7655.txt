Telangana government's estimated revenue loss following the Centre's demonetisation move is between ₹1,000 to ₹1,500 crore per month, State Finance Minister Etela Rajender has said. "We don't know for how many months this will continue... It could be three months or six months," he said. Further, businesses like real estate have come to a standstill following demonetisation, an official said.
