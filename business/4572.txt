Mahindra Group's Anand Mahindra on Monday tweeted Rajinikanth's upcoming movie Kaala's poster, which has the actor sitting atop a Mahindra Thar SUV. "When the legend @superstarrajini uses a car as a throne, it becomes a legend," Mahindra tweeted, adding he'd like to acquire the car. Later, the movie's production house Wunderbar Films and Dhanush confirmed Mahindra could have the vehicle.
