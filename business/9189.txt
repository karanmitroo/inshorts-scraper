The BSE Sensex on Thursday plunged 465.28 points to close at 27,827.53 while the NSE Nifty fell by 153.90 points to close at 8,591.25. The fall came amid the Indian Army confirming that it conducted "surgical strikes" in the Pakistani territory on Wednesday night. TCS was the top gainer while Adani Ports SEZ was the major loser of the day.
