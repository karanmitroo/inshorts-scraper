The BSE Sensex on Thursday went up by 75.05 points (0.3%) to close at 28,298.13 points and Nifty increased by 20.70 points (0.2%) to 8,588.65. As many as 1402 shares advanced, 1525 declined, and 145 remained unchanged. L&T, Dr Reddy's Labs, Lupin and Tata Motors were major gainers, while ITC, Coal India, Vedanta, Reliance and Wipro were major losers.
