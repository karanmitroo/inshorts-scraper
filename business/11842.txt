Mondelez India, the makers of Cadbury chocolates, on Tuesday launched the Cadbury Bournvita biscuits in India, the company's second offering in the biscuit segment after Oreo. The biscuit will complement the beverage Bournvita, Chandramouli Venkatesan, Managing Director, Mondelez India said. It will be available in packs of ₹10 and ₹25 and is expected to be available from May.
