Mutual Funds lost over 13 lakh investors in terms of individual accounts (foilios) in last 4-months of current fiscal, owing to profit booking and various merger schemes, despite the number rising by 2.20 lakh by the July-end (according to Sebi) . Last financial year marked 4th consequetive year of loss by the industry, losing  about 36 lakh investors in 2012-13.
