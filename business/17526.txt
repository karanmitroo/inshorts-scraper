The sensex and NSE rallied to record high levels during late trading on Monday riding on across the board buying, mainly in banks. The BSE benchmark sensex closed up by 135.99 points at all-time high of 22,764.83 before rising to all time intra-day high of 22,795.58. Nifty, which touched a new high of 6,823, spurted by 38.25 points to 6,817.65.
