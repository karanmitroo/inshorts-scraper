A plunge in US stocks on Monday cut the fortunes of the world's 500 richest people by $114 billion, Bloomberg reported. Berkshire Hathaway CEO and world's third-richest person Warren Buffett, who lost $5.1 billion, was the worst hit. Facebook CEO Mark Zuckerberg saw the second-biggest decline of $3.6 billion followed by world's richest person Jeff Bezos, who lost $3.2 billion.
