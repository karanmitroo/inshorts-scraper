NTPC reported a net profit of ₹2,898 crore in the quarter that ended September, a 39.9% surge against ₹2071.63 crore profit in the corresponding period last year. The net income increased to ₹18,173.9 crore against ₹17,267.32 crore the previous fiscal. Further, Operating profits surged to ₹4,030 crore, a 24.3% jump over last year.
