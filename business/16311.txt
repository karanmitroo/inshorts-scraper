Honda is set for record car sales in India in 2014, as the Japanese auto major is likely to register 62% growth in the year at 1.8 lakh units. The company sold 1.65 lakh units during the January-November period, up 61.8% year-on-year. “2014 was a very successful year for Honda…,” Honda’s Senior Vice President, Sales & Marketing, Jnaneswar Sen said.
