BSE Sensex and Nifty rose to record highs for the second consecutive day on Tuesday. While Sensex rose by 82.78 points to reach 28,260.66, Nifty rose by 16.65 points to a new high of 8,447.40. Strong foreign inflows and higher global markets buoyed the sentiments, brokers said, adding these factors have raised hopes of a rate cut by the RBI.
