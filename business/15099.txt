Government data on Tuesday revealed India's fiscal deficit for the duration of April-May had reached Rs.208,624 crore ($32.8 billion), or 37.5% of the target for the 2015/16 fiscal year. The figure was 45.3% during April-May last year. The government collected taxes amounting to Rs.19,889 crore or 2.2 percent of the estimate during the same period.
