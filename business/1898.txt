In reply to an RTI query, the RBI has said that demonetised notes are still being "processed in all earnest" through Sophisticated Currency Verification and Processing Systems (CVPS). It said the combined value of the processed notes is ₹10.91 lakh crore approximately. Notably, ₹15.28 lakh crore out of the ₹15.44 lakh crore in demonetised currency came back into the system.
