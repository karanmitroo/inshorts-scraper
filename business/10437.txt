The combined market capitalisation of eight of the top 10 BSE-listed firms surged by ₹46,505.25 crore in the week ended July 29, with the benchmark Sensex rising 248.62 points to close at 28,051.86. TCS witnessed the highest rise in m-cap at ₹21,556.48 crore followed by Sun Pharma and HUL, while ONGC saw the highest fall in m-cap at ₹2,138.87 crore.
