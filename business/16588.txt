The Benchmark BSE Sensex on Wednesday gained 98.84 points to close above 28k for the first time at record high of 28008.90 points, while the Nifty climbed 20.65 points up to end at 8383.30 points. Around 1517 shares advanced, 1528 shares declined, and 105 shares were unchanged. Banks and auto companies were among the gainers in the trade on Wednesday.
