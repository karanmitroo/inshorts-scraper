The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty on Thursday ended on a flat note, with the Sensex losing 28.69 points to settle at 28,423.48, and the Nifty falling 11.55 points to close at 8,774.65. GAIL, Coal India, and ICICI Bank emerged as the biggest gainers while Bharti Airtel, RIL, and Adani Ports SEZ ended the day as major losers.
