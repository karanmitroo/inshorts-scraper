The benchmark Sensex on Wednesday slipped 174.01 points to settle at 25,406.33 while the broader Nifty lost 43.65 points to close at 7,741.00. Investor sentiments remained sluggish amid global crude prices slipping to around 11-year-lows, tensions rising in the Middle-East and weak Asian markets. Shares of HMT gained 9.11% after the government decided the closure of its three units.
