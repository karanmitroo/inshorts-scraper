Maruti Suzuki on Saturday launched new Vxi variants of its WagonR and Stingray cars with auto gear shift (AGS) technology starting from ₹4.64 lakh and ₹4.86 lakh respectively (ex-showroom, New Delhi). Further, airbags and anti-lock braking system are incorporated as optional features across all variants of both cars. The company already offers AGS transmission in Celerio and Alto K10.
