Union Minister PP Chaudhary has said penal action will be initiated against 160 companies under the Companies Act for failing to comply with Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) norms during 2014-15. Earlier, show-cause notices were served to 1,018 defaulting entities. Notably, a minimum fine of ₹50,000 can be imposed for non-compliance which may go up to ₹25 lakh for certain violations.
