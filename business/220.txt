Jeweller Mehul Choksi, who is an accused in the ₹11,400 crore PNB fraud, has also defrauded the Jammu and Kashmir Bank of ₹152 crore. A Mumbai branch of the bank had sanctioned a loan of ₹121 crore to Mehul Choksi for Gitanjali Gems in 2012. An official said that no effort was made to recover the loan in 6 years.
