Indian multinational conglomerate Larsen and Toubro (L&T) on Friday reported a revenue of ₹23,393 crore for the quarter that ended September, a 10.6% rise over ₹21,159 crore in the same period last year. L&T’s net profit grew 15.56% to ₹995.9 crore from ₹861.75 crore. However, fresh orders fell by 28% to ₹28,620 crore from ₹39,797 crore in the previous fiscal.
