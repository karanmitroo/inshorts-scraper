India's largest taxi aggregator Ola has raised $400 million (approx. Rs 2500 crore) in its recent round of funding. The Series E funding was from investors DST Global, Singapore's GIC and Falcon Edge Capital along with the existing investors. With this deal,  Ola, valued at $2.5 bn, has become one of the most valued internet company after Flipkart and Snapdeal.
