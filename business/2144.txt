Mukesh Ambani-led Reliance Industries (RIL) on Friday posted a 12.5% increase in its net profit to ₹8,109 crore for the September quarter. Its revenue also increased by 23.9% to ₹1.01 lakh crore. Notably, Reliance Jio posted a revenue of ₹6,147 crore whereas its net loss widened to ₹271 crore as against ₹21 crore in the previous quarter.
