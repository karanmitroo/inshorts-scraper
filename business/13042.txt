State Bank of India (SBI) on Friday announced the launch of ‘SBI e-Smart SME’ to provide easy working capital to e-commerce players. The bank has initially tied up with Snapdeal to offer loans of up to ₹25 lakh to its sellers, while it is also planning a similar partnership with Flipkart, Paytm and Amazon, according to SBI’s chairperson Arundhati Bhattacharya.
