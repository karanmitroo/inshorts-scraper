Flipkart founders, Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal, have debuted on the Forbes India Rich List 2015. With a net worth of $1.3 billion each, Bansals are at rank 86. Notably, there are 12 new faces on the list of top 100 richest Indians. Ranking at 48, Dubai-based Sunil Vaswani is the richest among the newcomers with assets worth $2 billion.
