Western Coalfields (WCL), a subsidiary of Coal India Limited has reportedly finalised a ₹6,280 crore capital investment plan to be executed by 2019-20 in a phased manner. WCL plans to open 36 mines in the next three years, while spending ₹3,486 crore on land acquisition, ₹2,032 crore on installation of plant and machinery, and ₹242 crore on exploration.
