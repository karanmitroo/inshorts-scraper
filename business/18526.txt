IIM, Kozhikode's will have a record 54.29% of female students, a first for any IIM history in its 2015-batch. Explaining the reason Debashis Chatterjee, Director, IIM Kozhikode said, "Companies are looking at more women employees. Gender diversity brings in a different perspective to the class." The admission process in many IIM's was strategically tweaked this year to erase gender inequality.
