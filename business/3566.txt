The benchmark index NSE Nifty closed above the 10,000-mark for the first time ever on Wednesday after gaining 56.10 points to end at 10,020.65. This comes a day after Nifty breached the milestone during intraday trade for the first time in its 21-year history. BSE Sensex also climbed 154 points to end at an all-time high of 32,382.46.
