The benchmark indices on Wednesday ended the day with losses with the Sensex plunging by 254.91 points to end at 27,836.51. Meanwhile, the broader Nifty lost 76.05 points to close at 8,615.25. Hero MotoCorp, Bharti Airtel, and Maruti Suzuki emerged as the day's biggest gainers while Axis Bank, Tata Steel and Tata Motors were the major losers.
