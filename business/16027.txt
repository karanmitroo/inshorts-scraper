The Reserve Bank of India has estimated the Current Account Deficit to have come down to 1.3% of the GDP in 2014-15, helped by moderation in petroleum and gold imports. The CAD, which is the difference between the inflow and outflow of foreign exchange was 1.7% ($32.4 billion) in 2013-14 and 4.7 per cent ($88 billion) in 2012-13.
