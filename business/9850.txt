Haryana's Gross State Domestic Product grew 8.2% to stand at ₹3.96 lakh crore during 2015-16 at constant prices of 2011-12, as against 8% during 2014-15, state Finance Minister Captain Abhimanyu said on Tuesday, citing advance estimates. Further, CM Manohar Lal Khattar said the outstanding amount of electricity bills for the 2015-16 was ₹6,431 crore, up from ₹5,692 crore for 2014-15.
