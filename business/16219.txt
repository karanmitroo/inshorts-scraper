PVR Cinemas, the largest cinema exhibitor in India, is set to buy Chennai-based movie exhibition company Sathyam Cinemas. The deal may close for approximately Rs 750-1,000 crore for 40 odd screens, located predominantly in the Southern metro. The south-based Cinemas currently operate in Chennai and Coimbatore under 5 categories- Sathyam, Escape, thecinema, Luxe and S2 Cinemas.
