The Initial Public Offering (IPO) of Bengaluru-based Healthcare Global Enterprises has opened for subscription on Wednesday, with the shares priced at ₹205-218 per share. The issue, which closes on March 18, is expected to raise between ₹611 crore to ₹650 crore. Notably, the issue has already raised ₹292 crore from 11 anchor investors including Citi and Reliance Life.
