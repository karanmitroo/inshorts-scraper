The Sensex Tuesday surged 522 points to close at a new peak of 28,784.67, led by rally in metal and banking sector. The Sensex surpassed its previous record of 28,693.99, touched November 28. The Nifty too breached the 8,700-mark for the first time, but it pared some gains to close at 8,695.60, still a new high, up by 144.90 points.
