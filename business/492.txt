Nearly ₹10 lakh crore was wiped out from the market capitalisation of BSE-listed companies in the last 6 days, bringing the total market cap to ₹145 lakh crore. As much as ₹2.7 lakh crore was wiped off the companies' value on Tuesday. Extending its falling streak for the sixth straight session, the Sensex fell 561 points to settle at 34,195.
