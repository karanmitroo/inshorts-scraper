Global fast food giant McDonald's pays up to $192,602 (₹1.24 crore) annually to its high-skill employees who develop and execute the company’s strategy, according to a Glassdoor survey. The survey further revealed that cooks, crew workers, and cashiers, earn around $8.5 (₹546) per hour. Despite this, 72% of the low-pay employees gave a vote of approval to CEO Steve Easterbrook.
