Oil companies on Friday reduced petrol price by Rs 2.41/litre and diesel price by Rs 2.25/litre with effect from Friday midnight. With this price revision, petrol rate in Delhi will be reduced to Rs 64.24/litre and diesel rate will be reduced to Rs 53.35/litre. Petrol prices were last revised on Oct-14, while Diesel prices were last deregulated on Oct-19.
