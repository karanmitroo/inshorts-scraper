About Rs.2,280-crore unaccounted income is being allegedly routed through Private Placement Programme(PPP), which offers securities to select individuals/groups, has been detected by the Central Economic Intelligence Bureau(CEIB). The Central Board of Direct Taxes(CBDT) and Enforcement-Directorate(ED) are carrying out investigations to check the misuse of PPP, which has emerged as a new method for converting black money into white.
