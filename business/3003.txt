Infosys interim CEO UB Pravin Rao and his team face a major challenge of controlling higher attrition rate from senior management departures and a drop in employee morale, Macquarie Research said. "[R]ise in attrition could hurt Infosys performance, if not addressed by Pravin and his team," it added. Further, Nomura said recent developments could push back the company's "turnaround hopes".
