Maruti Suzuki has surpassed Infosys and ONGC in market capitalisation (m-cap) as its stock rose by 3% on Friday, adding ₹6,563 crore to the value. Following the gains, Maruti Suzuki's market capitalisation was at over ₹2.25 lakh crore. With this the company stood at the eighth position among the top 10 Indian companies in terms of m-cap.
