For the second quarter ended September 30, Infosys announced revenues of ₹15,635 crore, a growth of 8.9% quarter-on-quarter and 17.2% year-on-year. Further, the company reported a net profit of ₹3,398 crore, at a q-o-q growth of 12.1% and a y-o-y growth of 9.8%. Following the quarterly result's announcement, its shares declined by around 2.5% during intra-day trade.
