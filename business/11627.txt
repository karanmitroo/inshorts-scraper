The Maharashtra government plans to develop the Nandgaon industrial estate in Vidarbha's Amravati as a 'textile park' and promote Amravati as a 'textiles city', CM Devendra Fadnavis has said. "The state government is promoting textile industries in the suicide-prone area of Vidarbha to give... alternate market linkage to the cotton growers of the region... to prevent farmer suicides," he added.
