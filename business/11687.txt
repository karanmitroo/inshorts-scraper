Telecommunications firm Bharti Airtel on Wednesday reported a 2.8% year-on-year rise in net profit for the quarter ended March to ₹1,290 crore. Revenue for the quarter rose 8.4% to ₹24,960 crore led by a 44.5% rise in mobile data revenues. For the entire fiscal, the company's net profit increased to ₹5,484 crore compared to ₹5,183 crore in 2014-15.
