Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPIs) pulled out nearly ₹5,487 crore from the equity markets between December 1-11 as market sentiment was reportedly dampened by worries on interest rate hike by the US Federal Reserve. Meanwhile, FPIs pulled out nearly ₹368 crore from the debt markets. In 2015, equity markets have witnessed a net inflow of ₹15,136 crore by FPIs.
