According to a data released by the Commerce Ministry, India's exports have plunged for the ninth-straight month to $21.26 billion in August 2015, from $26.8 billion in August 2014 (20.66% decline). Imports too plummeted to $33.74 billion, a decrease by 9.95%, widening the trade deficit to $12.47 billion in August, from $10.66 billion in August 2014 (16.97% decline).
