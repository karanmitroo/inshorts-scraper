Telecommunications provider Idea Cellular on Thursday reported a 0.4% year-on-year fall in profits for the quarter ended December to ₹764 crore. However, the revenue rose 12.4% year-on-year to ₹9,001 crore. Idea has raised its expenditure estimate for the current fiscal by over ₹1,000 crore to ₹7,500 crore against its previous estimate owing to the recent launch of its 4G services.
