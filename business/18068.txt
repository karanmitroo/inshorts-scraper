RBI's 'Data on banks' on Thursday revealed that over 1.33 crore accounts of all kinds are inoperative, resulting in Rs 3,652.30 crore lying unclaimed in the commercial banks across the country. Most of these funds are lying in 'savings' accounts in rural areas. SBI and its associates top the list with Rs.714 crore, followed by Canara Bank at Rs.525.8 crore.
