Cairn India's outgoing interim CEO P Elango was paid a bonus of Rs 2.51 crore for 2013-14. Together with Rs 2.51 crore bonus along with performance incentives and retirement benefits, his total compensation in 2013-14 came to Rs 5.11 crore, according to the company's latest annual report. Elango resigned on May-2 from the post citing "personal reasons".
