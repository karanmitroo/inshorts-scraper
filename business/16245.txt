Vistara Airlines, a Tata-SIA joint venture, will start its operations from Friday, with its 1st flight from Delhi to Mumbai. After Air India and Jet Airways, it will be the 3rd full-service carrier in the country. The airline plans to operate 87 flights from Delhi to 9 different cities including Goa, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Ahmedabad and others, in its 1st year.
