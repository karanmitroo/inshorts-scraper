Bank of America Merrill Lynch, in a report, has estimated India’s “4G smartphone base to jump from 5 million in FY 2015 to 180 million (18 crore) by FY 2018". It has further predicted the growth of 4G subscribers "from almost zero in FY 2016 to 90 million (9 crore) by FY 2018”, along with a fall in 4G tariffs.
