The Supreme Court has granted Sahara chief Subrata Roy ten more working days to deposit ₹709.82 crore, out of the promised ₹1,500 crore, with the Sahara-SEBI refund account. The court further extended its interim order granting bail to Roy till July 5. Sahara has been asked to pay back investors from whom it illegally raised funds in 2008 and 2009.
