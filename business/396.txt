The fraud amount of ₹11,360 crore detected by PNB on Wednesday at a Mumbai branch is equivalent to 8.5 times the bank's FY17 net profit of ₹1,325 crore. The amount is also 49 times its December quarter profit of ₹230 crore. It is also nearly one-third of the bank's total market capitalisation of ₹35,365 crore.
