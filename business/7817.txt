State-run lender Punjab National Bank (PNB) on Saturday said it has got deposits worth ₹47,000 crore since the demonetisation of old ₹500 and ₹1,000 notes. A total of 2,000 out of 9,000 of the bank's cash vending machines have been recalibrated. Further, 200 PNB branches have been working in double shifts, CEO Usha Ananthasubramanian stated.
