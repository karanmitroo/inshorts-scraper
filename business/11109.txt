State-owned power generating firm NTPC on Monday said it plans to raise ₹20,000 crore during the 2016-17 fiscal. Of this, ₹6,000 crore would be raised from overseas bond market and ₹14,000 crore from the domestic market, including bank loans. Meanwhile, the company has a total installed capacity of 47,178 MW, and contributes to 24% of country’s generation, NTPC Chairman said.
