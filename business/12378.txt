Various banks have so far disbursed over ₹1.15 lakh crore under the Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana (PMMY), Financial Services Secretary Anjuly Chib Duggal said today. Under the scheme, which was launched in April 2015, loans between ₹50,000 and ₹10 lakh are provided to non-corporate small businesses. PMMY runs under government body Micro Units Development and Refinance Agency Bank (MUDRA).
