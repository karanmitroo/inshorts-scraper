The benchmark Sensex and the broader Nifty on Monday ended in losses, with the indices falling 65.58 and 19.75 points to close at 26,777.45 and 8,201.05 respectively. Investors remained cautious ahead of the RBI monetary policy review on Tuesday, analysts said. Tata Motors and SBI were the major gainers while Bharti Airtel and Lupin ended as the biggest losers.
