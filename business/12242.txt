The interest rate on Public Provident Fund (PPF) was cut from 8.7% to 8.1% on Friday. The government also slashed interest rates on several other small savings schemes, including that of the Kisan Vikas Patra from 8.7% to 7.8% and the 5-year senior citizen scheme from 9.3% to 8.6%. Further, several post office deposit interest rates were also slashed.
