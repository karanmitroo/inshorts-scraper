The GST Council has lowered tax rates on 178 mass consumption products like shampoos, chocolates, chewing gums, deodorants, and beauty products to 18%. GST on 13 items including condensed milk, pasta, spectacle frames, and refined sugar has been reduced from 18% to 12%. Tax rate on cattle feed, bangles of lac/shellac, and unbranded frozen/dried fish has been reduced to nil.
