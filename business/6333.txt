Minister of State for Finance Santosh Gangwar has said that employees from 14 bank branches were involved in illegally exchanging demonetised high-value notes worth about ₹71.5 crore from November 9 to December 30. The highest amount of ₹46.29 crore was transacted through three branches of Axis Bank, followed by ₹22.67 crore exchanged by eight employees of Dhanlaxmi Bank, he added.
