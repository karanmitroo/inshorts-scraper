Beating estimates, YES Bank - the fourth largest private sector bank - reported fourth quarter (ending Mar-31) net profit of Rs 430.20 crore, which was higher YoY by 18.8% from that of last year's Q4. For FY14, the bank's net profit rose by 24.4% to Rs 1,617.8 crore. Also, the non-interest income was up YoY 17.4% to Rs 445.50 crore.
