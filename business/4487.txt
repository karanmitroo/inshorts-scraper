Patanjali Ayurved spokesman SK Tijarawala on Monday rejected reports that yoga guru Baba Ramdev is in talks to buy media company NDTV. Moreover, NDTV Executive Editor Nidhi Razdan tweeted, "Hello people, Ramdev is not buying NDTV. Thank you." Earlier reports claimed NDTV was in preliminary talks with some parties to sell the channel and Ramdev was among the interested parties.
