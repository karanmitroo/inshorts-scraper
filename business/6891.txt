Amazon's Founder and CEO Jeff Bezos added ₹8,860 crore ($1.3 billion) to his net worth on Friday after his company's stock price rose by 2%. The surge added ₹26,000 crore ($3.8 billion) to his fortune this week. Bezos, who owns over 80 million Amazon shares, is currently the world's fourth richest person with net worth of ₹4.6 lakh-crore ($68.6 billion).
