Alibaba founder Jack Ma is the biggest billionaire gainer of 2014, as his assets rose by a whopping 173% this year to $29.2 billion, a report says. His fortune has surged largely due to Alibaba's blockbuster IPO in September, according to private wealth consultancy, Wealth-X. Warren Buffett is at second position with his assets increasing by 23% to $75.2 billion.
