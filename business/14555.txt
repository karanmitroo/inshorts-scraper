The 30-share Sensex on Wednesday posted gains for the second consecutive day, increasing by about 402 points to finish at 25,719.58. The broader 50-share Nifty index gained by about 130 points, closing at 7,818.60. Twenty seven of 30 Sensex stocks ended the day in green. In Wednesday's early trade, the rupee strengthened by 21 paise to 66.34 against the dollar.
