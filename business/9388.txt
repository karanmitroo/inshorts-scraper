Domestic air passenger traffic rose nearly 24% year-on-year in August to 83.81 lakh passengers, which was the highest across aviation markets worldwide, Civil Aviation Minister Ashok Gajapathi Raju said. Further, domestic air travel demand during January-August this year surged 23.14% to 6.45 crore. IndiGo carried the most number of passengers at 33.41 lakh with a market share touching 40%.
