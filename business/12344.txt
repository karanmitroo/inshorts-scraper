According to the Inshorts Youth of the Nation Poll (YOTN), 68% of the 42,958 respondents who voted for the question, said that in 2016 they would prefer to work with a startup than a large corporation, while 11% disagreed. However, 21% voted they were not informed enough.  As per a NASSCOM study, 80,000 jobs were created by startups in 2014-15.
