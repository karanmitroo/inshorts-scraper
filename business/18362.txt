Grounded for almost a year now, Kingfisher Airlines has given its CEO Sanjay Aggarwal a pay package of nearly Rs 4 crore for the last fiscal, during which its headcount nearly halved to 2,851 employees. Kingfisher had accumulated losses of over Rs 16,000 crore as on 31-March-2013, while it had a negative net worth of close to Rs 13,000 crore.
