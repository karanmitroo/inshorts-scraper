Market sentiments picked up on Monday after finance minister Arun Jaitley said that India's economy is expected to grow over 7.3% this fiscal. The benchmark Sensex surged 149.57 points (0.58%) to settle at 25,760.10, while, the broader Nifty gained 44.35 points (0.57%) to close at 7,806.60. Blue-chip stocks posted gains after witnessing heavy sell-offs in the week ended November 13.
