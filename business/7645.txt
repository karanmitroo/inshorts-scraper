New notes worth only ₹1.5 lakh crore have come into circulation since demonetisation, financial services major Credit Suisse report said. This is against ₹14.18 lakh crore worth old currency taken out of circulation. Further, it could be “several months” before RBI is able to plug the hole left behind by withdrawal of old ₹500 and ₹1,000 notes, it added.
