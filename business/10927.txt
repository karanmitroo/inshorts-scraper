Several Indian industrialists expressed their disappointment after RBI Governor Raghuram Rajan decided not to take a second term, and said this would be the "nation's loss" as he brought economic stability and increased India's credibility on the world stage. The personalities included Infosys founder Narayana Murthy, SBI chair Arundhati Bhattacharya, and Mahindra & Mahindra head Anand Mahindra.
