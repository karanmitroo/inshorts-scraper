Etihad, along with its equity partners, has raised $500 million. Of this amount, 20% will be given to Etihad Airways, Etihad Airport Services, airberlin and Alitalia each, 16% to Jet Airways and the remainder to Air Serbia and Air Seychelles. Jet Airways, whose 24% stake is with Etihad, was in a debt of Rs 11,920 crore last fiscal.
