India's President Pranab Mukherjee and Jordan's King Abdullah II on Saturday jointly inaugurated the world's largest sulphuric acid plant in Eshidiya, 325 km from Jordan's capital Amman. The plant is a joint venture between the  Indian Farmers Fertiliser Cooperative Limited (IFFCO) and the Jordanian Phosphate Mines Company (JPMC).  IFFCO has a 52 percent stake in the plant.
