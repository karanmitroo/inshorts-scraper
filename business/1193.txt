The contribution of direct taxes in the total tax collection in 2016-17 has fallen below 50% to 49.66% for the first time since 2006-07. CBDT data shows that direct tax collection in 2016-17 was ₹8.5 lakh crore while the indirect tax collection was ₹8.6 lakh crore. The direct tax collection stood at ₹7.42 lakh crore in the previous year.
