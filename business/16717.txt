Amid investor concern over growth prospects, Tata Consultancy Services and HCL Technologies each declined by about 9% on Friday, the worst daily fall in at least five years. TCS reported revenue of $3,929 million for the Sept quarter, compared with expectations of about $3,975 million. HCL Tech's revenue grew 1.9% to $1,433 million, lower than the analysts' estimate of over 3.5%.
