Tata Motors Ltd hit its lifetime high of Rs.364.70, rallying 5.7% in morning-trade on Friday on BSE, crossing Rs.1-lakh-crore market cap mark. Analysts at top global brokerage firms have largely maintained their 'BUY' rating on Tata Motors citing improvement in global macros and robust sales from its UK-subsidiary. Deutsche Bank, the global investment-bank has also upgraded the stock to 'BUY'.
