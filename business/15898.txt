Becoming the first person of Indian-origin to head a 'Big-Four' audit firm, Punit Renjen was appointed the CEO of US-based Deloitte Global, on Monday, and would begin on the first day of Deloitte's new fiscal, from June-1. Present across 150 countries, Deloitte is one of the 4 largest audit firms worldwide, and competes with PwC, KPMG and EY.
