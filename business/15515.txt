Red chilli and turmeric prices fell by Rs 100 per quintal in Delhi NCR on Thursday, concluding at Rs 6,500-12,500 for Red chilli and Rs 8,900-12,700 for turmeric per quintal. Traders attributed the price fall to subdued demand from retailers and stockists against sufficient supply. Price of Ajwain was Rs. 150-185/kg, black pepper was 560-740/kg and betel-nut was Rs 300-350/kg.
