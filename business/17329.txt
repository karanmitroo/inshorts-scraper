Toyota Kirloskar Motor (TKM) Tuesday launched an all new version of its premium sedan Corolla Altis priced between Rs 11.99 lakh and Rs 16.89 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi). The vehicle was unveiled in February at the Auto Expo 2014. "It is the world's No 1 car with more than 40 million cars sold across the globe," TKM MD Naomi Ishii said.
