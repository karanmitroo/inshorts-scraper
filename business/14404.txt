A day after the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) cut the repo rate by 50 basis points, the BSE Sensex went up by 376.17 points (1.46 percent) to close at 26,154.83. The Nifty also increased by 105.60 points (1.35 percent) to 7,948.90. As many as 1,668 shares advanced, 988 declined and 116 remained unchanged.
