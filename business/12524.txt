NSE's Nifty50 index will see the exit of three securities– Cairn India, Vedanta and Punjab National Bank, and will include four new securities– Aurobindo Pharma, Bharti Infratel, Eicher Motors and Tata Motors (DVR). This will take the number of stocks in the index to 51. Further, a new structure of Nifty broad market indices, including five new indices, was announced.
