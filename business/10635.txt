The BSE Sensex on Friday lost 105.61 points to close at 27,836.50, while the NSE Nifty fell by 23.60 points to settle at 8,541.40. Markets were dragged down by Infosys' lower-than-expected quarterly results, analysts said. Tata Steel, Bharti Airtel, HDFC and Tata Motors ended among the major gainers while Infosys, TCS, Wipro, and Coal India ended as the major losers.
