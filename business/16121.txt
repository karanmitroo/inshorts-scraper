The Sensex on Friday surged by 272.82 points to settle at all-time closing high of 29,278.84, boosted by European Central Bank's stimulus package. The 30-share index rose for the seventh straight session, and touched a fresh lifetime high of 29,408.73 intra-day. The Nifty also closed above the 8,800 level for the first time, up 74.20 points at 8,835.60.
