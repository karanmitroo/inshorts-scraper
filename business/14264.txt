Index of Industrial Production for the month of August hit a three-year high of 6.4% in as against 4.1% in July this year. It was also 6.4% higher year-on-year basis. The indices for Mining, Manufacturing and Electricity sectors for August grew at 3.8%, 6.9% and 5.6% respectively year-on-year basis. IIP grew 4.1% in the April-August period.
