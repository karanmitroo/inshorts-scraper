The market capitalisation (m-cap) of four of the top ten BSE-listed companies lost ₹40,779.97 crore in the week ended September 9, with the benchmark Sensex gaining 265.14 points to close at 28,797.25. TCS saw the steepest plunge at ₹31,723.89 crore in m-cap followed by ITC and HDFC, while ONGC surged the most at ₹13,303.79 crore.
