Nissan has overtaken Maruti Suzuki to become India's second largest car exporter with a 17% increase in shipments in 2013-14, with Hyundai continuing to retain the top spot. Maruti recorded a 16% decline in exports to 99,832 units in FY14, compared to FY13. Nissan began its export in 2010 and currently ships 85% of its production to overseas.
