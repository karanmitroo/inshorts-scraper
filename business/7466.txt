Chaudhary Group Chairman Binod Chaudhary has said the group's instant noodles Wai Wai will become a market leader in India over a period of time. Chaudhary, with net worth of $1.14 billion, said he believes India is where his next billion will come from. Earlier, when food regulator FSSAI banned Maggi noodles, Wai Wai became India's second largest noodle brand.
