Honda Cars India on Friday reported nearly three-fold increase in domestic sales at 14,428 units in December. The company had sold 5,493 units in December 2013. During the month, the company sold 1,120 units of small car Brio, 5,176 units of compact sedan Amaze, 2,098 units of Mobilio and 6,012 units of the mid-sized sedan City.
