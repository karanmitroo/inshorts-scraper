The benchmark Sensex on Wednesday surged by 171.15 points (0.67%) to settle at 25822.99, and the broader Nifty gained 33.95 points (0.43%) to end the day at 7,845.95 points. Lupin, SESA Goa, HDFC Bank and Mahindra & Mahindra were the major gainers, while TATA Motors, Bharti Airtel, BHEL and GAIL were the major losers.
