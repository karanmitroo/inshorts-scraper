Wipro chief Azim Hashim Premji topped the Hurun India Philanthropy List 2013 with a donation of Rs 8,000 crore in 2012, followed by HCL group chairman Shiv Nadar (Rs 3,000 crore) and GMR Varalakshmi Foundation's GM Rao (Rs 740 crore). Education sector received highest total contribution of Rs 12,200 crore from Indian philanthropists, followed by social development (Rs 1,210 crore).
