The BSE Sensex on Thursday gained 126.93 points to settle at 27,942.11 while the NSE Nifty breached the 8,550 mark for the first time in ten months to close at 8,565.00, gaining 45.50 points. ICICI bank, Maruti Suzuki, and SBI were among the top gainers while ONGC, Infosys, and Mahindra & Mahindra were among the major losers.
