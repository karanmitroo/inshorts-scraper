With the growing number of high net worth individuals, Lamborghini is aiming to almost double its sales in India in the coming years. The first-generation entrepreneurs aged 30-40 and second-generation businessmen are the major customers of its two models, Gallardo and Aventador(priced between Rs. 2.8-6.30 crore). The sports-car maker is targeting to sell over 20-cars this year(sold 17 last year).
