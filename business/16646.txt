Alleging involvement in the multi-crore-chit-fund scam by the Artha Tatwa (AT) group, the CBI on Friday made the 11th arrest, detaining ruling Biju Janata Dal (BJD) legislator and former Odisha chief whip Prabhat Tripathy. He was charged of criminal conspiracy and of having received huge sums from the AT group. He was suspended from the BJD soon after his arrest.
