Maharashtra and the National Capital Region (NCR) have together accounted for 49% of the total FDI inflows into the country since April 2000. Maharashtra attracted maximum foreign inflows at $70.41 billion, about 30% of the total FDI inflows during April 2000–November 2014. The NCR received $45.77 billion FDI during the period, accounting for 19% of the country’s total FDI.
