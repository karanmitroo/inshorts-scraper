Delhi-NCR has emerged as the biggest job creator among eight major cities in India in the January-March quarter, according to a report by trade body Assocham. A total of 8.5 lakh jobs were created in the cities, with 2.6 lakh generated in Delhi-NCR followed by Bengaluru (1.9 lakh), Mumbai (1.5 lakh), Chennai (82,200) and Hyderabad (60,000).
