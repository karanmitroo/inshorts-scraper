Mahindra & Mahindra’s (M&M) agricultural division on Monday entered the branded pulses market with ‘NuPro Tur Dal’. A one kg pack would cost ₹210, while the commodity is trading between ₹190-220 per kg. M&M’s agricultural business, with branded products in the vegetable, fruit, and edible oil categories, has witnessed an eight-fold growth in the last four years.
