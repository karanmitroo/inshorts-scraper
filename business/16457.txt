The benchmark Sensex fell by 322.39 points to reach a 1-month closing low of 27,797.01 on Tuesday, amid persistent selling pressure due to a sell-off in China and weak cues in other global markets. Owing to investors booking profits for the third straight session, the NSE barometer Nifty also slipped 97.55 points to end below 8,400-mark at 8,340.70.
