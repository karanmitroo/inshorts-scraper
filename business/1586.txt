Matrimony.com, the owner of online matchmaking firm BharatMatrimony has reported a 61.1% year-on-year increase in consolidated net profit at ₹18.8 crore for the quarter ended September. The company's consolidated revenue also rose 13.7% to ₹83.7 crore. The matchmaking segment accounted for over 90% of the company's revenue at ₹79 crore during the quarter.
