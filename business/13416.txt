Japan’s SoftBank, through its joint venture SB Energy with India’s Bharti Enterprises and Taiwan’s Foxconn Technology, has won its first solar power project in India, following its pledge to invest $20 billion on the same. SB Energy won the 350 megawatt solar project in Andhra Pradesh, after matching a record-low bid of ₹4.63 per kilowatt-hour made in the previous round.
