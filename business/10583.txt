HDFC Bank on Thursday reported a net profit of ₹3,238.91 crore for the quarter ended June, registering a year-on-year increase of 20.15%. The bank's gross non-performing assets surged 34.7% to ₹4,920.89 crore as against ₹3,652.23 crore in the corresponding period a year ago. Further, total income increased from ₹16,502.9 crore last year to ₹19,322.6 crore during the period under review.
