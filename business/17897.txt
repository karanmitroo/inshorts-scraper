Tracking the momentum provided to the IT market by Infosys' Q3 performance, TCS, HCL Technologies and Tech Mahindra rallied in a rangebound market to touch their respective fresh 52-week high on the Bombay Stock Exchange on Friday. TCS surged 0.95% to Rs 2265 and HCL Technologies rallied 1.08% to Rs 1299, while Tech Mahindra rose 2.6% to touch Rs 1875.50.
