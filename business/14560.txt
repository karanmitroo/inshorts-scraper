Mahindra & Mahindra on Thursday launched the compact SUV TUV300 at a starting price of Rs 6.9 lakh (ex-showroom Pune). TUV300, which comes only with a diesel engine, is priced up to Rs 9.12 lakh for the top-end variant. It is equipped with a 1.5 litre engine and claims to offer an average of 18.49 km per litre.
