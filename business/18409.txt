In a bid to promote high-speed wired broadband service, Tata Docomo said - "Every subscriber to the 100 Mbps annual plan (Rs.6,666/month) will get a tablet worth Rs.40,000 free whereas subscribers opting for other annual plan of Rs.1,099/month or above, will get a free tablet worth Rs.5,000." It added the offer will be applicable till September-30, 2013.
