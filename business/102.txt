The CBI has charged former CMD of United Bank of India Archana Bhargava for amassing assets worth over ₹3.6 crore, 133.23% more than her known sources of income. The agency alleged that during her tenure at various banks between 2004-2014, Bhargava amassed assets worth ₹4.89 crore and incurred an additional expenditure of ₹1.47 crore against the income of ₹2.73 crore.
