Jewellery and watch maker Titan Company posted a 67.44% year-on-year rise in consolidated net profit to ₹277.93 crore for the quarter ended September 30. The company's revenue also increased 28.4% to ₹3,473 crore, primarily driven by a 36.9% increase in revenue of the jewellery segment. Titan added 18 stores across all the segments, taking its total store count to 1,415.
