Financial services firm Edelweiss on Monday announced a long-term partnership with Canada-based Caisse de dépôt et placement du Québec (CDPQ), which will include investing ₹5,000 crore over the next four years. Further, CDPQ will acquire a 20% stake in Edelweiss Asset Reconstruction Company. The partnership creates one of the "largest and most diversified" credit investing platforms in India.
