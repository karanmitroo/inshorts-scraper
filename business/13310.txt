Chennai-based micro, small and medium enterprises (MSMEs) have incurred losses worth over ₹1,700 crore due to last month’s floods in the region, SME Rating Agency of India (SMERA) has said. The leather sector suffered the most with ₹380 crore in losses. Analysts including trade and commerce association Assocham, have estimated overall loss to industries in Chennai at over ₹15,000 crore.
