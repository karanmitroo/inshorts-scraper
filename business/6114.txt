Unilever, the maker of Lipton tea and Dove soap, added nearly $19 billion to its market capitalisation in 80 minutes after food company Kraft Heinz made a $143 billion takeover approach. Unilever shares ended the day up by 13.5%, hitting an all-time high. Notably, the offer, which could be one of the biggest in history, has been rejected by Unilever.
