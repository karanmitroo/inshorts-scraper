Oil firms on Sunday raised the price of non-subsidised LPG by ₹18 per cylinder, in line with global trends. A 14.2-kg cylinder will now cost ₹527.50 in Delhi. This comes after three straight monthly reductions in non-subsidised LPG by the firms. Meanwhile, the cost of Aviation Turbine Fuel (ATF), or jet fuel, was hiked by 1.5%.
