Coal imports declined by 27.16% to 12.6 million tonnes (MT) in September 2015 against 17.3 MT in September 2014. “With unprecedented increase in coal production by Coal India, import of coal comes down for the third successive month", tweeted Coal Secretary Anil Swarup. In value terms, there was a 30% decrease in imports, from ₹8,598 crore to ₹6,027 crore.
