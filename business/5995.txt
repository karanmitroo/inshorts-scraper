Addressing the nation in the 29th edition of radio programme 'Mann ki Baat', PM Narendra Modi on Sunday said that under the Digi Dhan Yojana, 10 lakh people were rewarded. "Under this Yojana, over 100 winners have received ₹1 lakh as reward," PM Modi said. "More than 4,000 businessmen have received ₹50,000 as reward," he added.
