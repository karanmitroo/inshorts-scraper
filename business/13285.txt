IDBI Bank, a public sector lender, on Tuesday said it received a capital infusion of ₹2,229 crore from the government in return for preferential share allotment. The bank said in a regulatory filing that 29.6 crore equity shares were preferentially allotted for ₹75.28 per unit. The government in August had announced ₹20,088 crore capital infusion in 13 public sector banks.
