Bengaluru-based techno-logistics startup ‘Letstransport’, which envisions to simplify the logistics space, has raised $1.3mn in a funding round led by Rebright Partners of Japan. Angel investors and mentors Ankush Nijhawan, Gaurav Bhatnagar and Manish Dhingra also participated in the funding. The firm was founded by IIT-Kharagpur alumni Pushkar Singh, Sudarshan Ravi and Ankit Parasher in Jan 2015.
