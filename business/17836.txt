Aiming to bolster its stand in the compact car segment, country's largest car maker Maruti Suzuki India Thursday launched 'Celerio', priced between Rs 3.9 lakh and Rs 4.96 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi). Pitched against the likes of Hyundai's Grandi10 and Honda's Brio, Celerio is expected to cater to a growing demand for vehicles with automatic gear shift at an affordable price.
