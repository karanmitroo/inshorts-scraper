After Snapchat went for IPO on Thursday, its American investor Benchmark Capital made ₹6,042 crore from its stake in the photo chat app startup. Snapchat's another investor Lightspeed Venture Partners made ₹4,097 crore from its IPO. Snapchat founders, Evan Spiegel and Bobby Murphy, have each made ₹10,700 crore from the IPO.
