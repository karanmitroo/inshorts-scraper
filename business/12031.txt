According to a Finance Ministry report, India's total external debt stood at $480.2 billion at December-end. While the government (sovereign) external debt stood at $90.7 billion, non-government debt amounted to $389.5 billion. US dollar denominated debt accounted for 57.6% of the total external debt, followed by Indian rupee (28.7%), Japanese Yen (4.1%) and Euro (2.3%).
