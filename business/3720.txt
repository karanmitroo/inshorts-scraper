Air India Chairman Ashwani Lohani has written to employees, stating that a stake sale of the debt-ridden carrier would be a win-win solution. In the first formal communication with employees over government’s disinvestment decision, Lohani assured that employees' interests would be "safeguarded". Referring to Air India's debt of ₹50,000 crore, Lohani said disinvestment was in its best interest.
