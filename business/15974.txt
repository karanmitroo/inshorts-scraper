The number of telephone subscribers in India reached all-time high of 970.97 million at the end of December 2014, a report by regulator TRAI showed. Telecom subscriber base was at 964.20 million at the end of November. This breaches the previous record of about 965.50 million in June 2012. Teledensity in the country, which means connection per 100, reached 77.58.
