Fortis Healthcare has denied reports that its promoters, Malvinder Singh and his brother Shivinder, took ₹500 crore out of the company without board approval about a year ago. Fortis clarified that the billionaire brothers took "loans" amounting to about ₹473 crore. Fortis also denied claims that auditor Deloitte refused to certify Fortis' quarterly results until the funds were accounted for.
