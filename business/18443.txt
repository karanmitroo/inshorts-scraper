Airtel reported a 9.6% fall in net-profit at Rs.688.9-crore for the first quarter (April-June), as compared to last year's Rs.762.2-crore, marking 14th consecutive fall in company's quarterly profits. However, company's total income was up 9.3% at Rs.20299.5 crore, compared to last year's Rs.18570.3 crore. The net debt of the company has been reduced to Rs.58,380.6 crore as on June 30, 2013.
