India's largest car maker Maruti Suzuki on Tuesday reported a net profit at ₹1,486 crore for the quarter ended June, a 23% jump year-on-year, amid favourable Rupee-Yen rate and a higher non-operating income. Further, the company's revenue rose 11.6% to ₹14,927 crore during the period under​ review. Net sales for the quarter rose by 12.1% to ₹14,654 crore.
