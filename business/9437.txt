The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty on Monday posted marginal gains with the Sensex rising 35.47 points to settle at 28,634.50, and the Nifty gaining 28.55 points to close at 8,808.40 amid firm global cues. TCS, ICICI Bank, and Adani Ports SEZ emerged as the biggest gainers while Maruti Suzuki, Bajaj Auto, and Axis Bank ended as the major losers.
