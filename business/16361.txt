India’s largest car maker Maruti Suzuki India is all set for its highest ever sales in 2014, with over 11.48 lakh units during the year, said Maruti Suzuki India Chairman RC Bhargava. Maruti’s last best selling year was 2010 when it sold around 10.6 lakh units. Maruti also grew its market share to 44.8% in the April-November period in 2014.
