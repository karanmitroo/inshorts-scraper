The benchmark indices on Monday ended the trade in losses after the BSE Sensex and the NSE Nifty fell by 91.46 and 37.75 points to close at 27,985.54 and 8,629.15 respectively. Sentiments remained downbeat following the appointment of Urjit Patel as the 24th RBI Governor on Saturday. Hindustan Unilever emerged as the biggest gainer, while Lupin was the major loser.
