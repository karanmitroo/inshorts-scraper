Mukesh Ambani-led Reliance Jio today announced the launch of its 4G feature phone called 'JioPhone', terming it as "India ka smartphone". The phone will effectively cost ₹0 with one-time security deposit of ₹1,500, which will be refundable after three years on returning it. The phone will be available for beta testing from August 15 and for pre-booking from August 24.
