Maruti Suzuki Celerio, which was launched at the Auto Expo on Feb-6, is registering 1,000 bookings per day, the company's chairman RC Bhargava said on Wednesday. The car comes with the Automatic Manual Transmission (AMT), a technology introduced for the first time in the mass segment; and the AMT variant starts at Rs. 4.29 lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi).
