Karnataka received ₹16,469.43 crore in 2015-16, making it the top beneficiary of loans disbursed under Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana, a scheme for giving credit to micro units, as per government data. Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra were second and third respectively. Overall, banks and financial institutions disbursed ₹1.33 lakh crore under PMMY, exceeding the ₹1.22 lakh crore target, data added.
