Yoga guru Baba Ramdev-promoted Patanjali on Thursday reported a turnover of ₹10,561 crore in the previous fiscal year. Of these, the company reported that Patanjali cow desi ghee's turnover was ₹1,467 crore, while Patanjali Honey's turnover was around ₹350 crore. During a press briefing, Ramdev added that Patanjali's promoter will never be a businessman, it will always be a 'sanyasi'.
