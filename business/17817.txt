Facebook CEO Mark Zuckerberg and his wife, Priscilla Chan, were 2013's most generous American philanthropists, with a donation of 18 million Facebook shares, valued at over $970 million, reported the Chronicle of Philanthropy magazine. Businessman George Mitchell and Nike's chairman Philip Knight stood 2nd and 3rd. The top 50 made donations worth $7.7 billion, plus pledges of 2.9 billion.
