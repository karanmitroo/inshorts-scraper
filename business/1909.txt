IndiGo's parent InterGlobe Aviation on Tuesday reported a 294% year-on-year increase in its net profit to ₹551.55 crore for the quarter ended September 30. The company's revenue for the quarter rose 27.2% to ₹5,505.56 crore. IndiGo has about 38% of the domestic aviation market with about 141 planes and 913 daily flights.
