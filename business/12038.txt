Investor wealth plunged by nearly ₹7 lakh crore in 2015-16 as the benchmark Sensex lost 2,615.63 points (9.36%), its worst performance in four fiscals. Further, foreign investors pulled out an estimated ₹18,000 crore from the capital markets, registering their first net outflow in seven years. Meanwhile, the rupee, at 66.26, lost 3.96 (6.36%) against the US dollar during the fiscal.
