"India's economic growth is expected to rise to 7.5% in 2015-16, followed by further acceleration to 7.9% in 2016-17 and 8% in 2017-18," the World Bank stated in its India Development Update on Tuesday. The growth was conditional of an 11% investment growth rate during FY16-18. The Government had earlier projected an 8.5% GDP growth in the current fiscal.
