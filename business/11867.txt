Maharashtra saw the highest worth of new projects announced in the quarter ended March at ₹96,889 crore, according to economic think-tank Centre for Monitoring Indian Economy (CMIE). Overall, projects worth ₹3.18 lakh crore were announced compared to ₹1.28 lakh crore in the previous quarter. Notably, Maharashtra accounted for 30% of the projects followed by Andhra Pradesh (20%).
