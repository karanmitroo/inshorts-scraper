The benchmark Sensex Monday slipped 490.52 points to close at 28,227.39 points, while 50-share Nifty closed 134.70 points down at 8526.35 points. The market fell for seventh consecutive day as exit polls showed that AAP would form the govt in New Delhi. L&T also disappointed street with the Q3 profit falling 14.6% year-on-year to Rs 1,060 crore on standalone basis.
