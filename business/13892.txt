The Nifty Midcap 50 Index, which captures the movement of stocks (in Nifty) with average free float market capitalisation between ₹1,000 crore to ₹5,000 crore at the time of selection, fell by 0.18% to 3300.55 points on Wednesday. Reliance Power, Ashok Leyland and Sun TV were among the major gainers, while Voltas, MRF and Apollo Tyres were among the losers.
