The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty on Tuesday closed on a flat note at 25,330.49 and 7,714.90 points, up by 45.12 and 10.65 points respectively. Buying in the last half hour of trade in realty, metal, and consumer durables supported the indices. BHEL and Tata Steel emerged as the major gainers while ITC and Wipro ended among the major losers.
