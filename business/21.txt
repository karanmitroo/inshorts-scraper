Reliance Industries Chairman Mukesh Ambani has been ranked India's richest person on Forbes 2018 Billionaires List after he added $16.9 billion to his wealth over the last year. Ambani has a net worth of $40.1 billion and is the 19th richest person globally. Wipro Chairman Azim Premji has been ranked second richest Indian with a fortune of $18.8 billion.
