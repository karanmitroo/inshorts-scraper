Arnab Goswami's Republic TV is part of media venture ARG Outlier Media, where Rajeev Chandrasekhar, the Vice-Chairman of NDA in Kerala, is one of the largest investors. SARG Media Holding, a company in which Goswami and his wife are stakeholders, is also an investor. SARG's investors include Infosys' former CFO Mohandas Pai, and Sameer Manchanda, who helped set up CNN-IBN.
