The benchmark indices on Tuesday ended the trade in losses after the BSE Sensex and the NSE Nifty fell 54.14 and 18.60 points to close at 26,812.78 and 8,219.90 respectively. Investors remained hesitant ahead of the UK referendum on European Union status on Thursday, analysts said. ONGC was the major gainer while Adani Ports emerged as the biggest loser.
