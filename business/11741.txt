The combined market capitalisation (m-cap) of five of the top ten BSE-listed companies declined by ₹38,969 crore last week despite the benchmark Sensex gaining 211.39 points to close at 25,838.14. TCS witnessed the biggest dip in valuation at ₹20,876 crore followed by RIL (₹8,651 crore) and ITC (₹4,224 crore) while Infosys emerged the biggest gainer, adding ₹9,601 crore in m-cap.
