American Tower Corporation (ATC) on Wednesday announced to buy 51% stake in Viom Networks for ₹7,635 crore by mid of 2016. The deal is expected to value Viom at about ₹22,000, including debt. Current majority stakeholder, Tata Teleservices will reduce its 54% stake to 32%, while Srei Group's entire 18.5% stake will be sold to ATC, after the deal.
