The benchmark Sensex on Thursday slumped by 201.62 points (0.75%) to end the day at 26,838.14 points, meanwhile, the broader Nifty lost 59.45 points (0.73%) to settle at 8,111.75 points. Among sectors, BSE Power index, which constitutes heavy electrical equipment & electric utilities sector companies in S&P BSE 500, slipped the most, as it suffered a 1.1% decline.
