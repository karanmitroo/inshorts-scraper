The telephone subscriber base in India grew marginally by 0.66% in March to 105.88 crore from 105.18 crore in February, telecom regulator TRAI said on Wednesday. Further, wireless subscribers grew 0.68% to 103.36 crore. Meanwhile, the tele-density (number of telephone connections per person) increased to 83.36% for the country, with 154.01% in urban areas and 51.37% in rural areas.
