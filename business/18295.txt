A survey by GMAC, which conducts the GMAT, shows that the growth of full-time MBA programmes worldwide is powered by young graduates from India and China. The survey shows that 31% MBA programmes (of 328 B-schools) received the second-largest number of applications from India. Globally, the full-time two-year and one-year Programmes received about 4.1 and 2.7 applications respectively for every-spot.
