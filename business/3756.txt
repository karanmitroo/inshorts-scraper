Chitra Ramkrishna, former MD and CEO of National Stock Exchange (NSE), had drawn nearly ₹23 crore as total remuneration for the last eight months of her tenure. This was 3-fold more than ₹7.87 crore that she earned in 2015-16. Ramkrishna, who has been with NSE since its inception, quit on December 2 last year, citing personal reasons.
