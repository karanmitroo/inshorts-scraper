Online payments company PayPal has said users donated $7.3 billion (nearly ₹50,000 crore) to charity in 2016 through its platform. The company said it processed the highest amount of daily donations on New Year's Eve. The largest amount given on New Year's Eve was over $230,000 (₹1.57 crore), while the year-long average was $89 (approximately ₹6,000).
