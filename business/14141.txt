German luxury carmaker Audi on Tuesday launched its ‘S5 Sportback’ model in India for ₹62.95 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi). With a 333 horsepower engine, the S5 can go from 0 to 100 kmph in 5.1 seconds and hit a top speed of 250 kmph. The car is also fitted with advanced infotainment systems, eight airbags and anti-theft wheel bolts.
