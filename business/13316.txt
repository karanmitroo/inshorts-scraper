Top 10 blue-chip firms listed on the Sensex gained around ₹28,382.58 crore in their market capitalisation (combined) after the benchmark index surged 319.49 points to 25,838.71 in the week ended December 24. ONGC emerged as the biggest gainer, as its valuation jumped by ₹9,154.37 crore, and was followed by ITC, Coal India (CIL), TCS and Reliance Industries (RIL).
