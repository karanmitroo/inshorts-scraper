The Sensex Friday climbed higher by 183 points, led by gains in IT stocks as India's second largest software giant Infosys beat market expectations with a robust set of quarterly results. The 30-share index closed 27,458.38 in a volatile trade, with shares of Infosys rising about 5%. The Nifty also ended higher and rose 49.90 points to close at 8,284.50.
