Mahindra and Mahindra (M&M) on Friday unveiled two vehicles- Supro Van and Supro Maxitruck for ₹4.38 lakhs and ₹4.25 lakhs (ex-showroom Thane) respectively. Supro Van is "an 8-seater diesel vehicle suitable for large families and travel segment, and Supro Maxitruck, a load carrier with a payload capacity of 1-tonne suitable for intra and inter-city transportation of goods", M&M president said.
