The production of currency by the government and RBI press was between ₹12,000 crore and ₹15,000 crore per day after demonetisation, a top official has said. According to this, new currency worth up to ₹2.85 lakh crore could be printed from December 12 till December 30. Notably, the government has introduced about ₹5 lakh crore in new notes since demonetisation.
