The Enforcement Directorate on Wednesday attached assets worth ₹1.67 crore in connection with its money laundering probe in the coal blocks allocation scam case in Maharashtra. A Mercedes Benz car worth ₹35.85 lakh, land measuring 854 square metre, and cash worth ₹14.50 lakh were seized, the agency said. The attachment order was issued against the ex-Director of Gondwana Ispat Limited.
