The GDP of the national capital territory of Delhi in 2015-16 is estimated to grow by 13% to ₹5.58 lakh crore from ₹4.94 lakh crore in the previous fiscal, Delhi Finance Minister Manish Sisodia said on Friday. Further, Delhi's contribution in the national GDP (at current prices) is 4.12%, as compared to 1.43% share in the country's population, Sisodia added.
