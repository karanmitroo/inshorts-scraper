For the second consecutive year, China's Hurun Research Institute has named Wipro chairman Azim Premji the "Most Generous Indian", with a donation of Rs 12,316 crore to the Azim Premji Foundation between April 2013 and October 2014. Premji is followed by Anil Agarwal of London-listed Vedanta Resources (Rs 1,796 crore) and HCL chairman Shiv Nadar (Rs 1,316 crore).
