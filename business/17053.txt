As a result of frequent discounts, SpiceJet registered the largest increase in market share in the domestic sector in June, adding 1.1% to its market share over May to 19%, ahead of Air India(18.5%). The only other gainer was GoAir, which increased its share to 10%, according to DGCA data. With 31.6%, IndiGo remained the leader, followed by Jet Airways(19.6%).
