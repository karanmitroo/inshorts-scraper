Patanjali Ayurved Managing Director and primary stakeholder Acharya Balkrishna has debuted on the Forbes' 'India's 100 Richest People' list, with a net worth of $2.5 billion. Balkrishna holds 97% of Patanjali Ayurved, which he Co-founded with Yoga guru Baba Ramdev in 2006, according to Forbes. Patanjali generated $780 million revenue selling products like cosmetics, noodles, and jams.
