The BSE Sensex shed 213 points to close at 29,380.73 on Wednesday after hitting a record high of 30,024.74 during intra-day trade. Nifty too fell 73.60 points to close at 8,922.65 after reaching a new high of 9,119.20. Sesa Sterlite, Hindalco Industries and Axis Bank were the prominent losers, while Sun Pharma, ITC and Bharti Airtel were the prominent gainers.
