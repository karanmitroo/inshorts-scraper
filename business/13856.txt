Mahindra & Mahindra (M&M) reported a net profit of ₹923.56 crore for the quarter ended September, a 2.45% fall from ₹946.63 crore in the corresponding period last year. The revenue declined 1.8% to ₹9,245 crore from ₹9,410 crore, majorly due to a 10.2% drop in sales volume triggered by a 26% fall in sales of farm equipment including tractors.
