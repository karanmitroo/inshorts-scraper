Oil companies on Saturday hiked the price of petrol by 89 paise/litre and diesel by 86 paise/litre, effective midnight. Petrol and diesel in Delhi will now cost ₹66.70/litre and ₹55.90/litre respectively. "The current level of international product prices of Petrol & Diesel and INR-USD exchange rate" warrants the change in prices of petrol and diesel, state-owned IOCL said.
