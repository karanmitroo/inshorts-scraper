Rajasthan Government has reportedly signed memoranda of understanding (MoUs) worth over ₹1.5 lakh crore with companies, ahead of the Resurgent Rajasthan Partnership Summit to be held in Jaipur on November 19 and 20. “A major chunk of the investments promised... are in solar energy sector, apart from petroleum and mines," Veenu Gupta, Principal Secretary, Industries, Rajasthan Government said.
