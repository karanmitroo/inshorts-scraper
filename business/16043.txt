Gurgaon-based handset maker Micromax has beaten Samsung to become India's biggest smartphone vendor, research firm Canalys said Wednesday. As per Q4 2014 figures, Micromax controlled 22% market share in smartphones shipments in India, while Samsung controlled 20%. With products like Canvas Nitro and Canvas Hue, Micromax has effectively targeted the Rs 9,000- Rs 12,000 segment of the market.
