Led by losses in shares of software companies, the Sensex plunged by 298 points on Wednesday, as traders remained cautious due to the expiry of December month F&O contract. The 30-share index closed at  27,208.61.  The Nifty also declined in the trade, falling below the 8,200 level. The 50-share index shed 92.9 points to end at 8,174.10.
