The government on Wednesday approved seven proposals of foreign direct investment (FDI) amounting Rs 9.81 billion based on the recommendations of Foreign Investment Promotion Board. The proposals, that got the approval, were Hathway Cable and Datacom (Rs 9.63 bn), Celon Laboratories, Keppel Puravankara  Development, Cheetah Communications, Destimoney Enterprises, Kotak Mahindra Bank and Lalea Trading.
