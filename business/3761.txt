The telecom subscriber base in India crossed the 1.2 billion mark in May, according to telecom regulator TRAI. Total wireless subscribers increased by 0.53% to 1,180.82 million at the end of May. While Reliance Jio added 4.7 million new customers, the most in the mobile services segment during the period, Tata Teleservices lost the most at 2.1 million customers.
