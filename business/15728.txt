The BSE Sensex declined 427.11 points (1.5 percent) to 28,503.30 and Nifty went down 128.25 points (1.5 percent) to 8,647.75 on Friday. As many as 925 shares went up, 1,949 shares declined and 176 shares remained unchanged. Apprehensions about US Federal Reserve Bank's policy-setting committee meeting and selling pressure are stated to be the main reasons behind the decline.
