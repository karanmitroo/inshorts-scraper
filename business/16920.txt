The BSE Sensex broke from life-time highs on Thursday due to profit booking by investors in bluechips after a nine-day record high. The Sensex fell 54.01 points to end at 27,085.93 after hitting intra-day high of 27,225.85. The 30-share Sensex dipped below the 27,000-mark to hit the day's low of 26,972.39 after oil, banking, metal and IT stocks fell.
