The operational losses of e-commerce company Snapdeal for the financial year ended March 31, 2015 have increased five times to ₹1,319.29 crore compared to ₹264.62 crore in FY14. With valuations of $5 billion each, Snapdeal and Ola are the second highest valued Indian startups behind Flipkart ($15 billion). The company has raised $500 million in its latest round of funding.
