Around 4,000 companies submitted H-1B visa applications in the 2016 fiscal and the top 20 sponsors account for 37% of all visa approvals. Of these, IT giant Cognizant saw the most petitions approved at over 21,000, followed by Infosys at around 12,700 and TCS at over 11,000. Notably, Apple offered the highest average salary to its H-1B visa employees.
