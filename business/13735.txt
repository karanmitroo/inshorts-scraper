According to data released by the Commerce Ministry on Monday, India's merchandise exports fell for the 11th-straight month in October as it witnessed a 17.53% slump to $21.35 billion from $25.89 billion last year amid weak global demand. Imports fell by 21.15% annually to $31.12 billion, thereby narrowing the trade deficit to $9.76 billion from $13.57 billion last year.
