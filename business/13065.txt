Telecom company Idea has announced its expansion plans and launched 4G services across four states including Chhattisgarh, Haryana, Madhya Pradesh and Punjab. The 4G service is expected to be available in 183 cities across seven telecom service areas by January 31. "By June 2016, Idea’s 4G footprint will cover 750 cities across 10 telecom circles,” Idea's Deputy Managing Director said.
