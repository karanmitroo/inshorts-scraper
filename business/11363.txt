The benchmark Sensex and the broader Nifty on Thursday plunged by 304.89 and 86.75 points to settle at 25,399.72 and 7,783.40 respectively amid weak global cues. Investors remained anxious amid the declaration of Assembly poll results, analysts said. Lupin, Wipro and Hero MotoCorp emerged as the biggest gainers while Adani Ports, SBI and L&T ended as major losers.
