E-commerce major Amazon's Founder and CEO Jeff Bezos net worth dropped by $2.8 billion (around ₹19,000 crore) on Thursday as his company’s stock fell more than 4%. The fall in the stock came after Amazon’s fourth-quarter revenue didn't meet analysts' expectations. Bezos has now become the world's fourth richest person with a fortune of $69.4 billion (nearly ₹4.6 lakh crore).
