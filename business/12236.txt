The World Economic Forum (WEF) has named four Indians in its list of 121 young global leaders aged under 40. Kanika Dewan, President of Bahrain-based Bramco Group, Dhivya Suryadevara, CEO of US-based GM Asset Management, Sheetal Amte-Karajgi, CEO of Baba Amte-founded Maharogi Sewa Samiti and Shaurya Veer Himatsingka, Deputy MD, India Carbon, are the Indians featured in the list.
