The CEOs of some of the world's largest corporations earned large sums in 2016, including HP Enterprise's Meg Whitman who earned $32.9 million (₹211 crore). PepsiCo's Indra Nooyi earned over $25 million (₹160 crore), while Apple CEO Tim Cook took home $8.74 million (₹56 crore). Warren Buffett, the world's fourth richest person, earned only $487,881 (₹3 crore).
