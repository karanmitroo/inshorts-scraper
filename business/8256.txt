Industrialist Ajay Piramal, who leads the Piramal group, will head the UK-India CEO Forum, replacing ousted Tata Sons chairman Cyrus Mistry, reports stated. The forum is slated to meet during the UK PM Theresa May's visit from November 6 to 8. Piramal was among the Tata Sons directors who had reportedly voted in favour of Mistry's removal as the Chairman.
