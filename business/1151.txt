IT services major Infosys has completed its ₹13,000 crore buyback programme that saw participation from Infosys' largest promoter shareholder Sudha Gopalakrishnan, Narayana Murthy's son Rohan Murty and LIC among others. This was the first share buyback in the company's 36-year history. Nearly 11.3 crore equity shares were bought back at a price of ₹1,150 per equity share.
