India's largest private lender ICICI Bank on Friday reported a net profit of ₹2,232 crore in the quarter ended June, a 25% drop from the year-ago period. Net interest income, the difference between interest earned on loans and that spent on deposits, grew marginally by 0.85% ₹5,158 crore. Gross non-performing assets rose 3.71% over the previous quarter to ₹27,193 crore.
