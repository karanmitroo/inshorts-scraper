Surpassing Monday's high of 22,796, the Sensex touched a record high of 22,853.03 on Tuesday for the second straight day, on healthy buying in capital goods, consumer durables, and bank sectors. However, it closed at 22,736.81 (provisional), down 0.12% from the previous day. NSE Nifty too touched a new record high of 6,838 points and closed 0.01% up at 6,818.20.
