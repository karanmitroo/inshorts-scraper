Retreating from one-month highs, the benchmark Sensex on Monday, plunged 98.15 points in its first drop in six sessions to close at 26,752.90 points. Tracking the momentum, the NSE Nifty also dropped by 22.85 points to end at 7,991.70 points. The 30-share Sensex dropped on heavy selling in realty, oil & gas and FMCG stocks amid firm European cues.
