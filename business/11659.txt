The BSE Sensex and the NSE Nifty on Thursday plunged 461.02 and 132.65 points to settle at 25,603.10 and 7,847.25 respectively on account of selling in blue chip stocks amid derivatives contracts' expiry. Market sentiment remained under pressure after the Bank of Japan left interest rates unchanged. Meanwhile, the US Federal Reserve also left key policy rates unchanged at 0.25-0.50%.
