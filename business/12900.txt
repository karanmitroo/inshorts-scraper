Social media behemoth Facebook reported quarterly revenue of $5.84 billion for the quarter that ended in December - up 52% over the year-ago period. The company's net income surged 49% year-on-year to $2.26 billion. Mobile advertising witnessed a 69% year-on-year surge, and it accounted for around 80% of total advertisement revenue. 1.04 billion people now use Facebook every day.
