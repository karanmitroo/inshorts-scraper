Hyundai on Tuesday launched its Sports Utility Vehicle (SUV) Creta at a starting price of Rs 8.59 lakh. It will be offered in three engine variants- 1.6-litre diesel and petrol engines along with a 1.4-litre diesel engine. Creta, whose high-end model is priced at Rs 13.60 lakh, will compete with compact SUVs Ford EcoSport, Renault Duster and Nissan Terrano.
