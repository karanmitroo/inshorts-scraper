Bank of Baroda reported an 88.73% decline in its net profit for the quarter ended September to ₹124.48 crore from ₹1,104.22 crore in the year-ago period. Net interest income dropped 4.61% to ₹3,244.47 crore while provisions for bad loans rose 113.02% to ₹1,891.70 crore. The bank was also hit by a scam involving alleged illegal foreign remittances worth ₹3,500 crore.
