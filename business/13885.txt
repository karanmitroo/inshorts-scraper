The benchmark Sensex closed 37.67 points down (0.14%) at 26,552.92 points after rallying by over 200 points in early trade, while, the broader Nifty settled 20.50 points (0.25%) down at 8,040.20 points, thereby closing at their respective one-month lows. Tata Motors and Mahindra & Mahindra emerged as the major gainers, while, ICICI and GAIL  were the major laggards.
