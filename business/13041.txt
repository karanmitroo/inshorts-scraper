Seven of the top 10 blue-chip firms listed on the Sensex lost ₹48,763 crore in their combined market capitalisation (m-cap) after the benchmark index slipped 479.29 points to 24,455.04 in the week ended January 15. TCS emerged as the biggest loser, as its valuation slumped by ₹26,354.48 crore, and was followed by ONGC and HDFC Bank.
