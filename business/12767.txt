The Gujarat-based Adani Group on Wednesday announced it will invest ₹21,000 crore in energy, ports and renewable-energy sectors in Karnataka. Around ₹11,500 crore will be used to increase Udupi Power Company’s capacity, while ₹7000 crore will be used in setting up a 1,000 MW solar plant. The group would also invest ₹2,000 crore to develop a seaport in Karnataka.
