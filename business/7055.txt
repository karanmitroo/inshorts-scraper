Reliance Industries Chairman and India's richest person Mukesh Ambani is the only billionaire among the country's top five who didn't lose money and gained $0.7 billion during 2016. Sun Pharma's Dilip Shanghvi lost $4 billion and Wipro's Azim Premji lost $3 billion of his net worth. HCL's Shiv Nadar and Shapoorji and Pallonji Group's Pallonji Mistry lost $0.5 billion each.
