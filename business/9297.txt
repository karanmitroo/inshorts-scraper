A special court on Saturday issued arrest warrants against two Malaysian nationals, T Ananda Krishnan and Augustus Ralph Marshall, who have been accused in the Aircel-Maxis case. Former Telecom Minister Dayanidhi Maran and media baron Kalanithi Maran are co-accused in the case. The case pertains to alleged irregularities in selling Aircel's stake to Malaysian firm Maxis in 2006.
