Extending losses for the fourth straight session, the Sensex on Wednesday fell over 117 points to slip below the 29,000-mark. The 30-share index ended lower at 28,883.11 
as funds and retail investors indulged in profit-booking amid mixed global cues. Tracking the momentum, the 50-share Nifty also declined in the trade, and slipped by 32.85 points to close at 8,723.70.
