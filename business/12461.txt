The combined market capitalisation (m-cap) of six of the top ten companies listed on the BSE fell by ₹41,672.7 crore in the week ended February 26, with the benchmark Sensex dropping over 2% to 23,154.30 points. Tata Consultancy Services and FMCG major ITC took the biggest hit, losing ₹20,788 crore and ₹11,061.67 crore from their market valuation respectively.
