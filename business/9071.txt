Indian investor Rakesh Jhunjhunwala, while speaking of the importance of charity, committed that he would donate 25% of his portfolio or ₹5,000 crore, whichever is less, on July 5, 2021.  Incidentally, Jhunjhunwala turns 60 on this date. "I am still not a philanthropist. I hope to be one," he said adding that "giving is not giving, it’s a duty".
