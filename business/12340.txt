Domestic passenger car sales decreased by 4.21% to 1.64 lakh units in February, from 1.71 lakh units in the corresponding period last year, according to data released by the Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers (SIAM). During the same period, sales of two-wheelers rose by 12.76%, commercial vehicles grew 19.93% and vehicles across all categories increased by 11.76%, SIAM said.
