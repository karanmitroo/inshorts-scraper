Honda Motorcycle & Scooter India Pvt. Ltd. Thursday launched new CB Unicorn 160 with a starting price of Rs 69,350 (Ex-showroom, Delhi). There are two variants of the bike, Standard and CBS. Honda CB Unicorn 160 is powered by the latest 163cc mid-sized advanced Honda engine. It is equipped with Honda Eco Technology, and delivers a mileage of 62 km/litre.
