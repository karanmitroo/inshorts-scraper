The BSE Sensex on Tuesday ended flat at 26,079.48 points, up by 45.35 points, while the NSE Nifty gained 3.80 points to close at 7,928.95. Analysts attributed the low trading volumes to the year-end holiday season, even as some volatility was witnessed in the afternoon session. Bajaj Auto emerged as the top gainer while BHEL ended as the major loser.
