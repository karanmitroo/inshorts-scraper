Oil companies on Friday slashed the prices of non-subsidised LPG cylinders, which consumers buy after exhausting their subsidised quota of 12, by ₹11. A 14.2 kg LPG cylinder will now cost ₹537.50 in Delhi as against ₹548.50 per cylinder previously. The reduction comes after two successive hikes, first by ₹18 on May 1 and then by ₹21 on June 1.
