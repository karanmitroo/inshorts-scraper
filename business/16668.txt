The benchmark Sensex on Tuesday regained 127.92 points to end at over one-month high at 26,880.82 points. Also, the NSE Nifty climbed 35.90 points to close above 8,000-mark at 8,027.60 points on the back of good earnings and rising hopes of a cut in interest rates. Pharma, banking and power shares led the rise, amid positive cues from global markets.
