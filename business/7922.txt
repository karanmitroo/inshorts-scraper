Finance Minister Arun Jaitley has said that 22,500 ATMs will be re-calibrated on Thursday to allow withdrawal of money, including the new ₹2,000 notes. This will be out of the nearly 2 lakh ATMs that exist in India as of now. Further, Jaitley said that the government has no plans of reintroducing new notes of ₹1,000 denomination into the market.
