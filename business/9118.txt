Fashion brand Zara's Founder and Europe's richest man, Amancio Ortega has reportedly bought a $551-million skyscraper (about ₹3,667 crore) in Spain's capital Madrid. This marks another purchase by the Ortega's property investment firm, reports said. According to Forbes, Ortega, who turned 80 this year, is also the world's second-richest man with a net worth of $72.7 billion (₹4.83 lakh crore).
