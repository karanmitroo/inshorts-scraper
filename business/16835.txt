In a third consecutive fall on Thursday, the 30-share BSE index Sensex slipped 276.33 points down to close at 26,468.36 and the 50-share NSE index Nifty plunged 90.55 points to close at 7,911.85. The BSE Sensex and Nifty fell as investors remained circumspect ahead of F&O expiry.  Realty, oil & gas, metal and PSU stocks also slumped on Thursday.
