Rising for the fifth straight day, the BSE benchmark Sensex on Tuesday closed at over 30-month high at 20,302.13 gaining 143 points as markets cheered RBI's fresh curbs on gold imports. Investors became richer by Rs 40,000-crore with market capitalization standing at Rs 66.32-lakh-crore. The NSE Nifty also rose 46 points to end at an almost two-month high of 6,078.
