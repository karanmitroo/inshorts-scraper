The government on Friday said it has no plans to come out with ₹5,000 and ₹10,000 banknotes. "The matter was examined in consultation with the Reserve Bank of India and it has not been found suitable to introduce ₹5,000 and ₹10,000 note,” Union Minister Arjun Ram Meghwal said. Earlier, government said it had no plans to re-introduce ₹1,000 notes.
