The Sensex plunged by 251.33 points to end at 27,350.68 Friday, logging its worst weekly drop in three years. The 30-share Nifty also slumped, and fell for a fifth session in six to post its worst weekly loss since July. The Nifty closed down 0.83% at 8,224.1. For the week it lost 3.7% for its worst weekly performance.
