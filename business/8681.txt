The benchmark indices on Thursday ended with gains, with the BSE Sensex rising by 145.47 points to close at 28,129.84, while the NSE Nifty gained 40.30 points to settle at 8,699.40. This came amid strong global cues following positive US earnings and rally in oil prices. Adani Ports SEZ was the top gainer while Lupin ended as the biggest loser.
