The benchmark Sensex on Monday rallied by 150.32 points (0.55%) to end the day at 27,364.92 points, meanwhile, the NSE Nifty gained by 36.90 points (0.45%) to settle at 8,275.05 points. Reliance Industries (RIL) posted the biggest gain of 5.61%, followed by Bharti Airtel, which posted a 3.96% gain. ONGC and Tata Motors dropped by 2.42% and 1.99% respectively.
