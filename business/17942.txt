Reflecting the general weakness in economy, mergers & acquisitions (M&As) involving domestic companies were down by 11.5% to $31.5 billion in 2013, the lowest since 2009 when it stood at $21.5 billion. Number of deals also declined by 12.6% to 967 from 1,107 in 2012. However, the average M&A size climbed to $76.1 million, compared to $73.5 million in 2012.
