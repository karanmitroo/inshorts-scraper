On the fourth day of the ongoing auction of coal mines, the government garnered Rs 5,164 crore from the sale of two mines to Jaypee Cement and Ambuja Cement, taking the total amount generated to Rs 1.57 lakh crore. Ambuja Cement bagged Gare-Palma Sector-IV/8 block in Chhattisgarh, while Jaypee Cement clinched Mandla-South block in Madhya Pradesh.
