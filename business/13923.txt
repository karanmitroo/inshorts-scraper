India’s biggest liquor company, United Spirits Limited reported a net profit of ₹929.3 crore for the quarter ended September, following a one-time gain from the divestment of its shares worth ₹870 crore in United Breweries. In comparison, the company posted a ₹26 crore loss in the year-ago period. Further, net sales grew by 5.7% to ₹2,122.17 crore.
