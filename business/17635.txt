The government will borrow Rs 3.68 lakh crore upto September in 2014-15, 61.5% of the total budgeted borrowing for the year. This (market borrowing plan) is in line with fiscal correction by the government, economic affairs secretary Arvind Mayaram said. The net borrowing has been pegged at Rs.4.57 lakh crore in the interim budget presented by finance minister P Chidambaram.
