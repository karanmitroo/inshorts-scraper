In another low fare offer, AirAsia India has offered a reduction in advance fares, starting from Rs 699. The offer, which started on Monday, is valid till Wednesday for travel between August 3 and March 26, 2016. The offer prices list a one-way Bengaluru- Kochi fare at Rs 899, Bengaluru-Goa at Rs 1,099 and Bengaluru-Pune at Rs 1,499.
