State-owned telecom operator BSNL has made an offer of less than ₹1 per GB stating that from September 9 it will give “unlimited” wireline broadband services priced at a monthly pack of ₹249. The 'Experience Unlimited BB 249' plan will enhance customer experience and attract new users, BSNL stated. Notably, this offer follows Reliance Jio's launch announcement earlier this week.
