According to regulatory filings, Wipro CEO Abidali Neemuchwala received ₹12 crore in salary for the year ended March 2016, his first year at the company. However, TK Kurien, Wipro’s last CEO and current Executive Vice Chairman, earned ₹14 crore. Wipro rivals TCS and Infosys CEOs N Chandrasekaran and Vishal Sikka received ₹25 crore and ₹49 crore in salaries respectively.
