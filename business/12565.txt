As many as 304 projects in the country involving investments worth ₹12.76 lakh crore remain stalled with the Project Management Group (PMG) of the Prime Minister's Cabinet Secretariat as of February 1, 2016, PMG said. Notably, the top 100 stalled projects, which are mostly in the power, steel, railways and petroleum sectors, account for ₹10.41 lakh crore of the investments.
