The BSE Sensex fell 237.98 points (0.8%) on Tuesday to close at 28,182.14 and Nifty slipped 74 points (0.9%) to 8,529.45. IT major Infosys reported a 5 percent growth in net profit for the April-June quarter and its shares surged by over 11 percent. Whereas, Sun Pharma tanked 15 percent, and Lupin, Vedanta and ONGC were the other major losers.
