The combined market capitalisation (m-cap) of the top nine companies listed on the BSE Sensex fell by ₹44,123 crore for the week ended April 8, with the index falling nearly 2% to close at 24,673.84. ITC was the biggest loser after shedding ₹11,789.15 crore in m-cap, followed by Infosys and TCS. However, Reliance Industries (RIL) added ₹1,360.96 crore in m-cap.
