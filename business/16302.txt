Nissan on Friday announced the start of pre-bookings of its soon-to-be-launched multi purpose vehicle Datsun GO+ in India. The Datsun GO+ will be available for pre-booking between January 3-14. "Prospective customers can now pre-book their Datsun GO+ with a fully refundable amount of Rs 11,000 at any Nissan dealership across India," Nissan India said in a statement.
