Oil companies on Saturday hiked the price of petrol by ₹1.34/litre and diesel by ₹2.37/litre, effective midnight. Petrol and diesel in Delhi will now cost ₹65.81/litre and ₹55.04/litre respectively. "The current level of international product prices of Petrol & Diesel and INR-USD exchange rate" warrants the change in prices of petrol and diesel, state-owned IOCL said.
