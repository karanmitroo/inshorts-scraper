Snapdeal on Friday signed an agreement with the Uttarakhand Handloom and Handicraft Development Council, for marketing artefacts manufactured by the state’s artisans, handloom weavers and small entrepreneurs, on its e-commerce portal. About 100 handicraft and handloom products unique to Uttarakhand have been selected, 64 of which are currently available online under the brand name ‘Himadri’.
