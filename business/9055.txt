The benchmark indices on Thursday ended the day with losses, with the BSE Sensex losing 114.77 points to settle at 28,106.21, while the NSE Nifty fell by 34.40 points to close at 8,709.55. GAIL, RIL, and Hindustan Unilever were among the top gainers while Cipla, NTPC, and PowerGrid ended among the day's biggest losers.
