The benchmark Sensex and broader Nifty recorded gains for the third straight week in the week ended January 1. The Sensex gained 322.19 points (1.25%) to close at 26,160.90 while the Nifty rose by 102.15 points (1.3%) to settle at 7,963.20, its highest level since November 4. Public sector banks and aviation companies emerged as the biggest gainers.
