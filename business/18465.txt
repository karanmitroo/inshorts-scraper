Samsung Electronics reported a record high net-profit of $6.9-billion, 50% up over a year, in the April-June quarter. Samsung shipped a record 76-million smartphones, more than double Apple's 31.2-million, thereby making more money selling handsets than Apple for the first time. Samsung's global market share soared to 33.1%, from last year's 31.1%, while Apple's share fell to 13.6% from 16.6%.
