Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPIs) have pumped in nearly ₹6,800 crore in the Indian capital markets so far in September, driven by strong global and domestic cues. According to depositors’ data, net investments stood at ₹3,178 crore in equities, while that for debt markets was at ₹3,617 crore. Good monsoon and positive quarterly results enthused investor sentiment, analysts said.
