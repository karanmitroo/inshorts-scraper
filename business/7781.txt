Nearly ₹1.15 crore worth of cash in old ₹500 and ₹1,000 currency notes has been looted from a bank in Odisha's Dhenkanal area, police said on Monday. "We strongly suspect the involvement of some insiders behind the crime. We are surprised to find nearly ₹7 crore cash intact in the strong room... We're examining the CCTV footages," the police added.
