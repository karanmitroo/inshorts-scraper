IT major Tech Mahindra on Monday reported a profit of ₹799 crore, a nearly 36% increase in comparison to the last quarter, and a 6.6% growth as against the same period of the 2016-17 fiscal. It also reported a 6% year-on-year increase in revenue to ₹7,336 crore. The company further added 21 active clients as compared to the last quarter.
