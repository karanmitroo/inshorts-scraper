The government has granted environmental clearance to 190 projects worth an estimated Rs 6.31 lakh crore during June-December 2014. Of the total projects, the Environment Ministry has cleared 37 non-coal mining projects worth Rs 4.49 lakh crore, and 48 infrastructure projects worth Rs 95,000 crore. Another 69 industrial projects worth Rs 40,600 crore were also cleared, among others.
