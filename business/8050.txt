Finance Minister Arun Jaitley has said around ₹2 lakh crore has been deposited in banks across the country in the two days following the demonetisation of ₹500 and ₹1,000 notes. Further, SBI alone had received ₹47,868 crore deposits in the past two days, he added. Notably, an estimated 10 crore monetary transactions with banks have taken place during this  time.
