A bottle of discontinued McDonald's Szechuan sauce, named on American adult animated sitcom 'Rick and Morty', was sold for $15,350 (₹9.6 lakh) on eBay. McDonald’s brought back three limited edition 64 oz bottles of the sauce after an online campaign. The auction attracted over 100 bids, including bids from DJ Deadmau5, escalating price from a starting $99 to $15,350.
