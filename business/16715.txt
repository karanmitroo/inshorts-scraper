The benchmark BSE Sensex fell for a second consecutive session on Thursday, closing 350 points lower at 25,983.05 points.  The Sensex plunged as companies seen most exposed to the global economy such as Hindalco Industries and Sesa Sterlite declined tracking a downturn in global markets. Also, the 50-share NSE index slipped 115 points to close at 7,748.20 points.
