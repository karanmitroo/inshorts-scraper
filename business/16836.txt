Chinese e-commerce giant Alibaba has made its founder and CEO, Jack Ma, the second richest man in Asia. With a personal wealth of $26.5 billion, Ma (50) has overtaken Mukesh Ambani and Hong Kong's Lee Shau Kee to become China's richest and Asia's second wealthiest. According to Bloomberg Billionaires Index, Ma trails Hong Kong's Li Ka-Shing among Asia's richest.
