Force Motors posted a net profit of ₹42.48 crore in the quarter that ended September, a 65.35% rise against ₹25.69 crore net profit in the corresponding period the previous year. The company's net sales surged by 27.61% to ₹755.97 crore in the second quarter against ₹592.37 crore last year. Force Motors sells agricultural tractors and multi-utility vehicles among others.
