Brothers Jai Hari and Yadu Hari Dalmia, Managing Directors of cement company Dalmia Bharat, are the richest Indian newcomers on Forbes 2018 Billionaires List with a fortune of $2.4 billion. They have inherited the business from their father Jaidayal Dalmia. Other Indian newcomers include RSPL Group's Murli Dhar and Bimal Gyanchandani ($2.3 billion) and Mankind Pharma's Ramesh Juneja ($2 billion).
