Boosted by growth in its refining and oil and gas business, retail-to-energy group Reliance Industries on Saturday posted a 13.7% jump year-on-year in its first quarter net profit to almost Rs.6,000 crore ($1-billion), the highest quarterly profit by a private firm. While the net profit in April-June rose to Rs.5,957 crore, consolidated revenues rose 7.2% y-o-y to Rs.1.07 trillion.
