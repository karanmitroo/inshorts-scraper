With 78 aircrafts and 496 daily flights in the Indian circuit, IndiGo has cemented it's position as India's largest domestic airline, leaving Jet Airways at second position with 21.8% share. 
IndiGo garnered 29.8% market share by April 2013 and recently secured a flight occupancy of 76.9%.
"IndiGo is leading on all key benchmarks", said Kapil Kaul, CEO of Asia-Pacific Aviation.
