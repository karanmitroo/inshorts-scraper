Mukesh Ambani-led Reliance Industries surpassed IT giant Tata Consultancy Services in intraday trade on Tuesday to reclaim its tag of India's most valued company. Reliance's market capitalisation touched ₹4.57 lakh crore, while TCS' valuation was at ₹4.56 lakh crore in early trade. Notably, Reliance has rallied 30% so far in the year following telecom venture Reliance Jio's launch in September.
