Digital currency Bitcoin has risen 320% in 2017, reaching another record high of over $4,300 (over ₹2.75 lakh) on Monday. With this increase, $1 (₹64) worth of Bitcoin in 2010 is now worth over $1.4 million (₹9 crore). Bitcoin, which was priced at just $0.003 in May 2010, saw its market capitalisation surge by $15 billion in the last week.
