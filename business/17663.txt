With 40 stores in four cities (in 17 months) and nearly 1,000 partners, India is the fastest growing market in Starbucks history, Tata-Starbucks chief executive Avani Davda said. The US-based coffee chain, Starbucks, which has an equal joint venture with Tata Global Beverages, opened its first outlet in Mumbai in Oct-2012. Globally it operates over 20,000 stores across 64 countries.
