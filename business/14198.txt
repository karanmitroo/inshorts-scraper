The BSE Sensex on Friday surged 204.46 points (0.76%) to settle at 27,214.60 points, meanwhile, the NSE Nifty gained 58.65 points (0.72%) to end the day 8,238.15 points. Larsen & Toubro, SBI, Maruti Suzuki, ONGC were the major gainers, whereas, Lupin, Tata Steel, Coal India and Hindustan Unilever were the major losers. Nestle India shares ended 5.6% higher.
