The market capitalisation (m-cap) of seven of the top 10 BSE-listed companies increased by ₹45,024.83 crore in the week ended August 12, with the benchmark Sensex gaining 74.05 points to settle at 28,152.4. TCS emerged as the biggest gainer, adding ₹16,443.22 crore to its valuation, followed by Reliance Industries and ONGC. Sun Pharma's m-cap dipped the most by ₹9,759.14 crore.
