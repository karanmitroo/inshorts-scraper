A day after Budget, Sensex ended 97.64 points higher at 29459.14 and Nifty closed 54.90 points higher at 8956.75. Axis Bank, Cipla, L&T, BHEL and HUL were the top gainers, while ITC, Bajaj Auto, Bharti Airtel, Hero Motocorp and Tata Motors were the top losers. Stocks like Muthoot Finance and Relaxo Footwear gained 5-7 percent in the intra-day trade.
