Tata Group’s retail arm Trent's flagship store Westside has opened its 100th store in Bhubaneswar, Odisha. The firm is ahead of its peers like Shoppers Stop, which has 83 stores and Lifestyle International which has 56 stores in the country. Currently, Westside has 18.6 lakh square feet of space in the country with net sales of ₹8,623 per square feet.
