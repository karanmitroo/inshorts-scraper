Four Indians have been named in Fortune's 2014 '40 under 40' list that has been topped by Uber's 38-year-old co-founder Travis Kalanick and Airbnb's 33-year-old CEO Brian Chesky. The indians on the list are Harvard professor Raj Chetty (ranked 16), Micromax co-founder Rahul Sharma (ranked 21), Snapdeal Co-Founder  Kunal Bahl (ranked 25) and Twitter's General counsel Vijaya Gadde (ranked 28).
