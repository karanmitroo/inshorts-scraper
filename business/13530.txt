According to data by the Association of Mutual Funds in India (AMFI), equity mutual fund schemes saw a net inflow of ₹6,379 crore in November, thereby taking the total inflow to around ₹87,000 crore this year so far. In comparison, 2014 saw an infusion of ₹49,000 crore in equity mutual funds while 2013 witnessed an outflow of ₹8,700 crore.
