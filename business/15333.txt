Mahindra & Mahindra (M&M) have signed definitive agreements with the Japanese conglomerate Mitsubishi Heavy Industries to acquire a 33.33% stake in Mitsubishi Agricultural Machinery. M&M has invested $25 million in the company reportedly to expand their global footprint. M&M has had a decade-long association with Mitsubishi U.S., where it has been supplying 6,000 OEM tractors to Mahindra USA.
