Mahindra Group Chairman Anand Mahindra took home a salary of ₹7.67 crore during the 2016-17 fiscal, marking an increase of 16.38% from the previous fiscal. Further, Managing Director Pawan Goenka received ₹7.39 crore as remuneration, an increase of 15.86%. Notably, the median remuneration of employees of the company during the fiscal stood at ₹7.08 lakh.
