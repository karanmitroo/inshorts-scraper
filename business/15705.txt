Malaysian carrier AirAsia has joined the low-fare war putting as many as 30 lakh seats on discounted prices, starting as low as Rs 1,390. The highest fare in the offer is for travel from Kolkata and Bengaluru to Kuala Lumpur (Rs 6,999). The offer, with a 7-day window, commenced Monday, and is valid for travel between Sep-1 to May-31 2016.
