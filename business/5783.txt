Martin Sorrell, CEO of the world's largest advertising and public relations company WPP, has been awarded a payout of more than $48 million (over ₹320 crore) in 2016. This took Sorrell's total payout in the past five years to more than $243 million (over ₹1,600 crore). Sorrell has consistently been ranked the highest-paid CEO in FTSE 100 since 2012.
