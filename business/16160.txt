Reliance Industries (RIL) Friday reported a 4.5% decline in its net profit at Rs 5,256 crore for the third quarter ended December 31, pricing in tumbling oil prices. RIL had posted a net profit of Rs 5,502 crore in the same period last year. During the period, RIL's turnover declined by 20.4% to Rs 96,330 crore from Rs 1,21,077 crore.
