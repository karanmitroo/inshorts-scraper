The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty snapped a three-day losing streak to post gains on Tuesday. The benchmark Sensex gained 291.47 points to close at 24,479.84, while, the broader Nifty surged 84.10 points, to settle at 7,435.10. Among sectors, BSE Capital Goods index gained the most, 2.85%, followed by BSE Bankex index, which gained 1.68%.
