Reliance Industries (RIL) profits surged by 12.5% to ₹6,720 crore this quarter from ₹5,972 crore, majorly triggered by a downfall in global crude prices. Meanwhile, the revenue declined by 33.8% to ₹75,117 crore as against ₹1,13,396 crore. However, the company's outstanding debts increased by 7.4% to ₹1,72,765 crore during second quarter this fiscal, from 1,60,860 crore in March 2015.
