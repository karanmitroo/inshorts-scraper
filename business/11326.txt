The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty on Tuesday snapped a four-day losing streak, gaining 75.11 points to close at 25,305.47, and 17.80 points to settle at 7,748.85 respectively. NTPC, Tata Motors, ICICI Bank, Tata Steel, and HDFC emerged as the major gainers while Sun Pharma, Bajaj Auto, TCS, BHEL, and ONGC ended as the major losers.
