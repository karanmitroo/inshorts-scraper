Maruti Suzuki India has recalled around 70k units of Swift, Dzire and Ritz models manufactured between 8-Mar 2010 and 11-Aug 2013, the company said Tuesday.  Maruti will inspect the wiring harness fitment condition of the 69,555 diesel vehicles and carry out the necessary repairs, free of cost. Maruti Suzuki dealers will contact owners of all the affected vehicles.
