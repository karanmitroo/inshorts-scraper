Jubilant FoodWorks Limited, formerly known as Domino's Pizza India Private Ltd, on Monday said its CEO Ajay Kaul has resigned after a service of 11 years. Kaul will continue his current post till March 31, 2017, while a search for the successor has been initiated, it added. Domino's Pizza and Dunkin' Donuts outlets in India are operated by Jubilant FoodWorks.
