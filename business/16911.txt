Microsoft launched 3 new Lumia smartphones, Lumia 830, Lumia 735 and Lumia 730, on Thursday, priced at around Rs. 26,190, Rs.17,370 and Rs.15,800, respectively. The company is also about to launch a software update called ‘Lumia Denim’ that adds functionality exclusively to Lumia phones running Windows Phone 8. The handsets will begin rolling out globally this month.
