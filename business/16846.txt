LD Mittal, chairman of Sonalika Group, has paid Rs 8.5 lakh for a fancy number plate. The 0001 number of the newly launched CH-01-AZ series was auctioned at the Registering and Licensing Authority (RLA) office in Chandigarh. The special number plate is for a new Mercedes S series car owned by Sonalika Group's flagship company International Tractors Ltd.
