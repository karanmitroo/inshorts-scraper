Tata has emerged as India’s biggest brand for the third consecutive year, according to brand consultancy Interbrand India’s annual survey of the top 30 brands in the country. Tata’s brand value has grown 12% year-on-year to ₹66,940 crore in 2015. Reliance Industries, Bharti Airtel, Life Insurance Corporation and State Bank of India, are the other brands in the top five.
