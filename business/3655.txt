Reliance Industries Chairman Mukesh Ambani, while speaking at the company's 40th annual general meeting, said that ₹1,000 invested in Reliance shares in 1977 is today worth over ₹16.5 lakh. This means that the company's stock is now over 1,600 times more valuable. Notably, the company's market cap has grown from ₹10 crore in 1977 to over ₹5 lakh crore now.
