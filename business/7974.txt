Cyrus Mistry on Tuesday alleged that Tata Sons made wrong investments on the orders of now Interim Chairman Ratan Tata, which adversely impacted its finances. Mistry’s office said there were investments of "questionable nature", such as the ₹400 crore put in Nagarjuna Refineries. Further, the statement also claimed that Tata Sons was bearing the entire office costs of Ratan Tata.
