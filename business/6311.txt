Facebook CEO Mark Zuckerberg and his wife Priscilla Chan's medical research organisation, Chan Zuckerberg Biohub, has announced an investment of ₹330 crore in "high-risk" research. The initiative has chosen 47 investigators from 700 applications to work towards "curing, preventing or managing every disease." Each investigator will receive a five-year appointment and up to ₹10 crore in funding.
