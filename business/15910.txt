Software exports from Hyderabad may touch Rs 63,000-64,000 crore during the current fiscal, Telangana IT, Electronics and Communications Secretary Harpreet Singh said. In the previous fiscal, the software exports from Hyderabad stood at Rs 57,000 crore, he said. An addition of 20,000 employes is expected in the IT sector to reach figures of 3.4 lakh during this fiscal, he added.
