Eight of the top 10 blue-chip firms listed on the Sensex gained ₹38,355.66 crore in their combined market capitalisation (m-cap) after the benchmark index surged 322.19 points to 26,160.90 in the week ended January 1. Coal India emerged as the biggest gainer, as its valuation jumped by ₹7,263.82 crore, and was followed by ONGC, Sun Pharma and Reliance Industries.
