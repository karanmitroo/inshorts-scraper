The BSE Sensex fell 483.97 points (1.7%) on Wednesday to close at 27,687.72 and Nifty decreased by 147.75 points (1.7%) to end at 8,363.05 points. Vedanta, Tata Motors, Hindalco, Tata Steel and HDFC were the major losers, while Wipro and HUL were the major gainers. The crises in China and Greece are said to be the reason behind the fall.
