Indian stocks on Friday notched up their best gain in 3 weeks, as the BSE Sensex climbed 267 points to close at fresh record high of 28,334.63, after touching a lifetime high of 28,360.66 on intra-day basis. Tracking the momentum, the Nifty surged 75.45 points to hit new high of 8,477.35, after rising lifetime high of 8,489.80 in intra-day trade.
