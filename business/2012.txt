Bengaluru Police arrested a 22-year-old man for using fake documents to get a job at JPMorgan and allegedly siphoning off ₹12.15 crore from the company. Police recovered ₹8.14 crore lodged in a bank account opened by an accomplice, who was arrested earlier this month. Police also recovered property, and ₹31 lakh-worth jewellery that was bought with the stolen money.
