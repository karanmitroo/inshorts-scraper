Maruti Suzuki on Monday launched the premium hatchback Baleno in India starting at a price of ₹4.99 lakh. The diesel versions of the car are priced between ₹6.16 lakh to ₹8.11 lakh. With a fuel efficiency of 21.4 kmpl for the petrol variant and 27.39 kmpl for the diesel range, the cars will be sold through Nexa dealerships.
