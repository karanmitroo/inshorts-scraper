The BSE Sensex on Tuesday fell by 58.09 points (0.21%) to settle at 27,306 points while the NSE Nifty lost 13.4 points to end the day at 8,261.65 points. TCS, Tata Motors, Infosys and Maruti Suzuki were the major gainers, whereas SESA Goa, Tata Steel, Hindalco and Mahindra & Mahindra were the major laggards.
