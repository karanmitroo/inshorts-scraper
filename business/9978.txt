The benchmark indices on Thursday posted losses as the BSE Sensex fell 224.03 points to settle at 27,835.91, while the NSE Nifty lost 58.10 points to close at 8,592.20 amid weak global cues. GAIL, ITC, and Dr Reddy's Labs emerged as the biggest gainers, while Adani Ports SEZ, Wipro, and Tata Steel were the biggest losers.
