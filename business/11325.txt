The BSE Sensex on Monday fell 71.54 points to settle at 25,230.36 while the NSE Nifty lost 18.65 points to close at 7,731.05 amid mixed global cues. ITC, Adani Ports, Bharti Airtel, Hindustan Lever and Coal India emerged as the biggest gainers while Tata Steel, Lupin, ONGC, Cipla and Bajaj Auto ended as the biggest losers.
