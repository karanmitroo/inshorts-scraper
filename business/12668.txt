Domestic passenger car sales dropped after recording gains for 14 consecutive months, witnessing a slight dip of 0.72% year-on-year to 1.68 lakh units in January, according to data released by the Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers (SIAM). During the same period, sales of two-wheelers rose by 2.63%, commercial vehicles grew by 17.53% and vehicles across all categories increased by 3.03%.
