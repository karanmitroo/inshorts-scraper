Infosys has said the feedback from its clients and stakeholders has been “positive and reassuring”. Interim CEO UB Pravin Rao said Nandan Nilekani's return has brought a sense of stability at the board. Referring to Vishal Sikka's exit, Rao said many clients were "sad" but, "[O]nce we gave them reassurance of continuity in strategy and stability, they were fairly comfortable."
