Chris Howard, an executive of Royal Mint that produces coins for the UK, claimed £45,000 (₹38 lakh) in expenses last year, including £10 cocktails in Las Vegas. Howard also claimed a 50p packet of chewing gum and a £1.50 bottle of water. He also spent £15,000 (₹12.7 lakh) of public money staying in some of the world's most luxurious hotels.
