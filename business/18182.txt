With a decrease of 38% year-on-year, FDI witnessed an 8-month low figure of $1.4 billion in August. FDI inflows declined from $36.50 billion in 2011-12 to $22.42 billion in 2012-13. It grew by a meagre 4% during the April-August period of 2013-14 fiscal. As per industry experts, there is a need to improve business environment in the country.
