India’s largest and most profitable airline IndiGo has posted a record profit of ₹640.43 crore, on a revenue of ₹4,317.19 crore, for the first quarter (Q1) of this fiscal year. However, it also reported a negative net worth of ₹139.38 crore as of June 30. IndiGo is set to launch a ₹2,500 crore initial public offering from October 27-29.
