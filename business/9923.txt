The top three Indian telecom players Bharti Airtel, Vodafone, and Idea continued to dominate market share, contributing 75.2% of industry revenues in June quarter. Bharti Airtel, India's largest telecom operator, saw the biggest rise in revenue market share to 32.7% from the last year period, a BNP Paribas report said. Overall, industry revenue slowed amid weak consumer spending, analysts said.
