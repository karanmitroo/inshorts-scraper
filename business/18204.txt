Kumar Mangalam Birla, head of the Aditya Birla Group has been charged by CBI with conspiracy and cheating to acquire two coal-blocks in Odisha in 2005 for his firm Hindalco; along with PC Parekh, who was Coal Secretary when Hindalco landed the coal-licenses. Sources said that Hindalco's offices were raided in different cities and Mr. Birla would be questioned soon.
