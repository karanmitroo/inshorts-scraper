The benchmark Sensex on Tuesday snapped a six-day losing streak and gained 31.44 points (0.12%) to settle at 26,590.59 points, while, the broader Nifty gained 9.90 points (0.12%) to close at 8060.70 points. NTPC, Hindalco, ONGC and Mahindra & Mahindra were the major gainers, while, Tata Motors, Tata Steel, Lupin and L&T were the major laggards.
