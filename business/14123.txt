India's life insurance industry witnessed an 18.5% growth in new business during the first half of the present fiscal year. The sector, comprising 24 players, recorded cumulative earnings of ₹58,286.40 crore in April-September, up from ₹49,178.17 crore in the same period last year. India’s largest insurer LIC, having a 67% market share, recorded 9.2% growth in new premium collection.
