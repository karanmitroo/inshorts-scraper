The Andhra Pradesh government on Monday inked MoUs involving investments worth ₹1.70 lakh crore on the second day of the three-day CII Partnership Summit in Visakhapatnam. Australia-based Queensland Coal Corp agreed to set up a coal-based power plant in AP with an investment of over ₹30,000 crore. A total of 32 MoUs worth ₹1.95 lakh crore were signed on Sunday.
