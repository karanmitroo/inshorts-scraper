Sun TV promoter Kalanithi Maran and his wife Kavery were the top paid corporates in India in the last fiscal, earning Rs 56.25 crore each. They sent Jindal Steel's Naveen Jindal (Rs 54.98 cr) from 1st to 3rd-position. Dominating the list for the last 4 years now, the three however saw a marginal cut in packages compared to earlier salaries.
