Aditya Birla Fashion and Retail, formerly known as Pantaloons Fashion, on Wednesday reported a net loss of ₹20.82 crore for the quarter ended June 30, as against a net loss of ₹67.88 crore in the same period last year. However, its total income from operations rose 8.28% year-on-year to ₹1,415.12 crore during the period under review.
