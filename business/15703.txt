HDFC Bank MD, Aditya Puri, has been named in the list of 30 best CEOs, published by America's financial magazine Barron's. He was named for "making HDFC Bank India’s second-largest private-sector bank, due to the company’s focus on consumer banking. Their stock has gained about 25% annually in the past 20 years, more than double the Indian market", wrote Barron's.
