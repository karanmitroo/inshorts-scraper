Eight companies co-founded and headed by Indian-origin entrepreneurs are among Forbes magazine's 100 most-promising companies in America, according to an annual ranking of privately-held companies with under $250-million in annual revenues. The list includes Maryland-based WeddingWire (No.27), co-founded by Sonny Ganguly, and Pennsylvania-based Prepay Nation (No.52), co-founded by Anurag Jain and Ajay Goyal. The list is topped by California-based Fuhu.
