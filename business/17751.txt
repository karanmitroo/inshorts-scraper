According to Chinese research firm Hurun's 2014 global rich list, India is home to the 5th largest group of billionaires in the world and Mukesh Ambani, is the country's richest man. India has 70 billionaires and their combined wealth comes to a staggering $390 billion. Other noted Indians in the list include Lakshmi Mittal, Azim Premji and Pallonji Mistry.
