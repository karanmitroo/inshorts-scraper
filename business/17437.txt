Mukesh Ambani-led Reliance Industries leads the pack of 54 Indian companies in Forbes' annual list of the world's 2000 largest and most powerful public companies. Reliance Industries is ranked 135, with a market value of $50.9 billion and $72.8 billion in sales as on May-2014. The US retains its dominance as the country with most Global 2000 companies at 564.
