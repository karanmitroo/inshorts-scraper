Warren Buffett’s Berkshire Hathaway posted a record net profit of $9.43 billion in the quarter ended September, from $4.62 billion in the corresponding period last year. the company's revenue grew by 15% to around $59 billion, majorly boosted by $4.4 billion gain from Kraft-Heinz merger. Meanwhile, Buffet's other major holdings– American Express and IBM have tumbled this year.
