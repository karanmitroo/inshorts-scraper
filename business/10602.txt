FMCG major Hindustan Unilever (HUL) on Monday posted an increase of 9.79% in standalone net profit to ₹1,173.90 crore for the quarter ended June 30 against ₹1,069.16 crore the preceding fiscal. Net sales were up by 3.56% at ₹7,987.74 crore during the quarter under review and total expenses of the company increased 2.8% to ₹6,585.58 crore.
