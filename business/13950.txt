Indian Overseas Bank posted a net loss of ₹550.83 crore in the quarter ended September, against ₹245.51 crore net loss in the corresponding period last year. The bank posted a net loss of ₹536.07 crore in this fiscal's first half against ₹26.21 crore net profit last year. However, net income rose to ₹6,769.94 crore from ₹6,440.77 crore last year.
