As many as 82,500 ATMs, which account for about 40% of cash vending machines in the country, have been recalibrated to dispense new high security currency notes of ₹500 and ₹2,000 rupees. As per reports, about 12,000-14,000 ATMs are getting recalibrated daily. Further, ATMs are able to stock ₹50-60 lakh cash after  recalibration.
