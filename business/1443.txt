Benchmark indices BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty saw their biggest single-day fall in over two months on Thursday ahead of the second quarter GDP data. Sensex fell 453 points to 33,149 and Nifty slumped 135 points to 10,226. Kotak Mahindra Bank, SBI and Reliance Industries were the major losers, whereas shares of GAIL, Bosch and Dr Reddy's Labs rose.
