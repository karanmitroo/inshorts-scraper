Singapore has replaced Mauritius as the top source of foreign direct investment into India, accounting for about 25% of FDI inflows in 2013-14. During the period, India attracted $5.98 billion in FDI from Singapore, whereas it was $4.85 billion from Mauritius. Overall FDI into India grew by 8 per cent year-on-year to $24.3 billion in 2013-14.
