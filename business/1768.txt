With 2.19 lakh millionaires at the end of 2016, India has the fourth largest number of millionaires in the Asia Pacific region, according to Asia-Pacific Wealth Report by Capgemini. India's millionaires have a combined wealth of $877 billion, it added. Japan has the highest number of millionaires at 28.9 lakh, followed by China with 11.29 lakh millionaires.
