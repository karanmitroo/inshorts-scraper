Axis Bank posted a net profit of ₹1,915.64 crore in the quarter that ended September, a 18.93% surge against ₹1,610.71 crore in the corresponding period the previous fiscal. Total income rose to ₹12,001.01 crore from ₹10,549.97 crore earned a year ago. However, Non-Performing Assets (NPAs) rose to 1.38% of gross advances, against 1.34% the previous year.
