The BSE Sensex plunged by 338.61 points on Wednesday to end at 27,252.53 while the Nifty lost 111.55 points to close at 8,432. This comes after the Centre announced the scrapping of ₹500 and ₹1,000 notes from November 9, and Donald Trump's victory in the US Presidential elections. Dr Reddy's was the biggest gainer while TCS was the top loser.
