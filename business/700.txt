American rapper 50 Cent, who has earned nearly $8 million in Bitcoin, has admitted he forgot about earning 700 Bitcoins in 2014 via sales of his album 'Animal Ambition'. The 700 Bitcoins were worth just $462,000 in 2014. In 2015, 50 Cent had filed for bankruptcy after declaring debts of $36 million and assets of less than $20 million.
