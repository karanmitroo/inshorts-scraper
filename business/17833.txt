As per the official estimates released on Friday, India’s gross domestic product (GDP) is anticipated to grow at 4.9% during 2013-14. Last year it had grown by 4.5%, the lowest since 2002-03. Despite the deceleration, India’s average GDP growth between 2004-05 and 2013-14 is 7.6%; second only to China and far higher than the USA and European countries.
