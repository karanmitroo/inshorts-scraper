Procter & Gamble India (P&G) has appointed Al Rajwani as its new managing director and CEO. The current MD Shantanu Khosla, who has been heading the company for last 13 years, will leave on June 30, 2015. Rajwani, who is a US citizen of Indian origin, has been credited for making P&G the leading FMCG company in the Middle East.
