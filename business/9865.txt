Future Group-owned online platform to buy Big Bazaar products, 'Big Bazaar Direct' is shutting down next week following losses, the Group's Founder and CEO Kishore Biyani has confirmed. "I attempted e-commerce four times in my life. We started FutureBazaar way before Flipkart. We lost ₹300-350 crore in business. Then, we created Big Bazaar Direct...We lost there as well,” said Biyani.
