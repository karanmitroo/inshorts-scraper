The BSE Sensex tanked 550.93 points (1.9 percent) on Monday to close at 27,561.38 points and the Nifty fell 160.55 points (1.9 percent) to 8,361.00. As many as 1083 shares advanced, 1741 declined and 142 remained unchanged. The SIT investigation on Participatory Notes and weak growth in China are said to be the reasons behind the fall.
