India's second-largest software services exporter, beat estimates with a 21.6% rise in consolidated net profit to Rs 2,886 crore for the quarter ended Jun-30. The profit was higher than the Rs 2,672 crore average of analyst estimates, according to Thomson Reuters data. Revenue for the quarter was up 13.3% to Rs 12,770 crore from Rs.11,267 crore in the year-ago period.
