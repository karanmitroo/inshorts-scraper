Natarajan Chandrasekaran, who was on Thursday appointed as the new Tata Sons Chairman, has been the CEO and Managing Director of Tata Consultancy Services since 2009. He has also been a non-official director of RBI since March 2016. Born in a Tamil agricultural family, Chandrasekaran joined TCS in 1987 after completing his MCA from National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli.
