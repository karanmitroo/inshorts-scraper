Infosys Co-founder Narayana Murthy has said that "all is well" at Infosys under the chairmanship of Nandan Nilekani. He added that "there were complexities" but we can now "sleep well" as it's Nilekani's strength to make complex things simple. On being questioned about new CEO's search, Murthy said, "Nandan himself has been a CEO and he knows what he requires."
