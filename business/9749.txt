The combined market capitalisation of the seven of the top 10 BSE-listed firms surged by ₹41,687 crore in the week ended September 2, with the benchmark Sensex rising 749.86 points to close at 28,532.11. HDFC witnessed the highest rise in m-cap at ₹11,309 crore followed by ITC and HDFC Bank, while Reliance Industries saw the highest fall at ₹4,800 crore.
