AirAsia said in an official release on Monday that it will launch flights originating from New Delhi, which will be the north India hub for the no-frills carrier. The flights, connecting Delhi to Bengaluru, Guwahati and Goa, will start from May-21. The airline has offered all-inclusive fares of Rs 1,500 for Delhi-Guwahati and Rs 1,700 for Delhi-Goa and Delhi-Bengaluru.
