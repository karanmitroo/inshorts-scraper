Five of the top 10 blue-chip firms listed in the Sensex took a combined hit of ₹47,445.38 crore in their market capitalisation (m-cap) after the markets slipped by 593.68 points (2.31%) to 25,044.43 in the week ended December 11. ITC's m-cap tumbled by ₹11,807.7 crore, followed by ONGC which lost ₹10,352.15 crore. However, TCS’ valuation jumped ₹11,251.14 crore.
