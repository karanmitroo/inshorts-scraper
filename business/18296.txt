Parle-Agro, the company which sold its popular soft-drink brands Thums-Up, Limca etc., to Coca-Cola in 1993, has announced its re-entry into the Rs.15000 crore cola-market with ‘Cafe Cuba’, a coffee-flavoured carbonated-drink. The drink, which would hit the market in January/Februaray-2014, took 10-years to develop, the company said. Cafe Cuba will be available in three different sizes 250-ml, 500-ml and 1-litre.
