Tata Motors-owned Jaguar Land Rover has launched the two-seater convertible Jaguar F-Type in India on Monday. It was rolled out in two variants - the F-Type S, which costs Rs. 1.37 crore, and the F-Type V8S, which costs Rs. 1.61 crore. Jaguar India has registered sales growth of 68% in the first quarter of this fiscal year.
