Liquor firm United Breweries said it has withheld payment of dividends, amounting to ₹2.4 crore, to its beleaguered chairman Vijay Mallya for the financial year 2015-16, following directions from government body Debt Recovery Tribunal. The company has announced a dividend of ₹1.15 per equity share of ₹1. Notably, Mallya directly and indirectly holds about 32.45% stake in the firm.
