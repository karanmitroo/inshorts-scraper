The BSE Sensex Tuesday climbed up 145.80 points to close at 26,575.65, while the 50-share NSE index Nifty moved up by 48.35 points to end at 7,927.75. The Sensex and the Nifty rose on continued buying by funds and retail investors amid firm European cues. Except oil & gas and healthcare, all other BSE sectoral indices ended in the green.
