Private sector lender HDFC Bank has reported a 20.1% year-on-year increase in profit to ₹4,642 crore for the December quarter. The bank's net interest income during the quarter rose by 24.1% year-on-year to ₹10,314.34 crore. As a percentage of total loans, gross bad loans rose to 1.29% as compared to 1.26% in the previous quarter.
