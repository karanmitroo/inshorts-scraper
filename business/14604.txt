The benchmark 30-share Sensex on Tuesday fell by 586.65 points, a decrease of more than 2 percent to close at 25,696.44. The National Stock Exchange's Nifty index closed at 7,785.85, losing 185.45 points. The fall was attributed to weak GDP data, which was released on Monday evening, and weak global cues. All sectors at the Bombay Stock Exchange lost points.
