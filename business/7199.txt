Tamil Nadu Chief Secretary P Rama Mohana Rao has been sacked after Income Tax officials recovered ₹30 lakh in cash and 5 kilograms gold in a 24-hour raid conducted at his and his son Vivek Rao's office and residences. Undisclosed assets worth ₹5 crore were also unearthed. Girija Vaidyanathan has been appointed as the new Chief Secretary of the state.
