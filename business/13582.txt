The benchmark BSE Sensex on Monday ended flat, gaining 17.47 points to close at 26,145.67. The broader 50-share Nifty index lost 7.45 points, finishing the day at 7,935.25 points. Infosys, ICICI Bank, Mahindra and Mahindra were the Sensex's top gainers. Bharti Airtel, Vedanta, and Coal India led the losses. The BSE's consumer durables and information technology sectors saw substantial gains.
