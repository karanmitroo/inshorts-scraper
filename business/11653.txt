The benchmark Sensex and the broader Nifty snapped their two-week gaining streak for the week ended April 29, losing 231.52 (0.9%) and 49.50 (0.6%) points to settle at 25,606.62 and 7,849.80 respectively. Quarterly results' announcements and the monthly derivatives expiry in the week along with the US Fed Reserve and the Bank of Japan meetings impacted the sentiment, analysts said.
