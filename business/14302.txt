In an on-road emissions testing programme conducted by Emissions Analytics, diesel cars manufactured by Mercedes-Benz, Honda,  Mitsubishi and Mazda were found to violate emission standards. Mercedes-Benz's diesel cars were found to emit noxious gases five times the permissible Euro 6 levels, Honda's cars at 2.6-6 times, Mitsubishi 1.5-3.4 times and Mazda's cars at 1.6-3.6 times the test levels.
