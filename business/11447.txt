State-run UCO Bank on Friday reported a net loss of ₹1,715 crore for the quarter ended March against a profit of ₹209 crore in the year-ago period. The loss was attributed to a 104% year-on-year rise in non-performing assets (NPAs) to ₹20,907 crore. Further, bad loans as percentage of total loans rose to 15.43% from 10.98% in the previous quarter.
