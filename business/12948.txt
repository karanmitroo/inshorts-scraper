Seven of the top 10 blue-chip firms listed on the BSE lost ₹51,647.43 crore in their combined market capitalisation (m-cap) after the benchmark index lost 19.38 points to 24,435.66 in the week ended January 22. Reliance Industries emerged as the biggest loser, as its valuation slumped by ₹22,396.27 crore, and was followed by Coal India and Hindustan Unilever.
