Stock markets ended in green for the third consecutive week. Improvement in rupee and US's uncertainty on attacking Syria led markets to jump up with BSE ending the week at 19,732.76 points, registering a hike of 6.55% (1,213.32 points) in three weeks. NSE also escalated to 5,850.60 points till closing, with a hike of 6.92% (378.85 points) in three weeks.
