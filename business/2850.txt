The Reserve Bank of India on Wednesday revealed in its annual report that ₹15.28 lakh crore, or nearly 99%, of ₹15.44 lakh crore scrapped currency notes have been brought back into the system. The RBI report further revealed that about 8.9 crore units out of the around 630 crore demonetised ₹1,000 notes failed to make it back to banks.
