Tata Motors has reportedly cut its workforce at the Pantnagar plant by almost 21%  in Q1-2013 following slowdown in automobile sector. Report says that the company fired around 1,208 employees at the Pantnagar plant between April-June 2013 and has also sacked many employees from Sanand(Gujarat) and Hubli(Karnataka) units. Earlier, Maruti and Mahindra have also adopted similar strategies for production cutbacks.
