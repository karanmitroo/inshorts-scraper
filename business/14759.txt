The BSE Sensex on Wednesday went down by 353.83 points (1.3 percent) to close at 27,512.26 and Nifty declined by 108.55 points (1.3 percent) to 8,353.80 points. Infosys, Sun Pharma, TCS, Wipro and Lupin were the major gainers, while Vedanta, Hindalco, Coal India, SBI and Tata Motors were the major losers. Vedanta and Hindalco declined by over 7 percent each.
