NR Narayana Murthy of Infosys regained top spot in 'India Inc's Most Powerful CEOs' listing this year by dislodging Mukesh Ambani of Reliance Industries. More than 500 executives evaluated CEOs where Murthy topped the ranking followed by Mukesh Ambani, Kumar Birla, Azim Premji and Anil Ambani. This listing is based on a survey conducted by IMRB International.
