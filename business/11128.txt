The market capitalisation (m-cap) of four of the top ten BSE-listed firms rose by ₹38,703 crore in the week ended June 3, with the benchmark Sensex gaining 189.43 points to close at 26,843.03. CIL witnessed the highest gain in m-cap at ₹17,433 crore followed by TCS and HUL while Sun Pharma saw the biggest dip at ₹20,998 crore.
