Anil Ambani-led Reliance Commercial Finance (RCF) has tied up with e-tailer Snapdeal to offer financing to over 80,000 Snapdeal vendors across India. The company is also in talks for similar tie-ups with e-commerce players including Flipkart and Amazon India. The new e-commerce centric product will offer customised options to the vendors of e-commerce companies, RCF said.
