The Sensex Thursday surged 728.73 points to close at 28,075.55 after the RBI cut interest rate by 25 basis points. With this rise, the Sensex reclaimed the 28,000-mark for the first time since December 9. The Nifty also rose 216.60 points to end at 8,494.15. Meanwhile, the total market valuation of all BSE-listed firms Thursday regained the Rs 100-trillion mark.
