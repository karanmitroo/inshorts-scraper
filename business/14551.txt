Maruti Suzuki India on Thursday launched a limited edition version of WagonR called Avance at a starting price of Rs 4.30 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi). The model is being introduced in two variants LXi Petrol and LXi CNG priced at Rs 4.30 lakh and Rs 4.84 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi) respectively. This model will be offered for a period of three months.
