Luxury car-maker Audi Tuesday introduced the RS 7 Sportback in India, priced at Rs 1.28 crore (ex-showroom Mumbai). The company sold the first new car to Bollywood actor Salman Khan. The German car-maker, which sold a record 10,002 units in 2013, sells 12 models in India now, including the popular Q3 and A4 sedans.
