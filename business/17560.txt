Infosys senior VP and general counsel Nithyanandan Radhakrishnan has quit the company to start his own legal venture. Company secretary Parvatheesam Kanchinadam replaces Radhakrishnan as the chief compliance officer effective Apr-15, an Infosys statement said. Radhakrishnan is the 10th senior executive to leave the company since Narayana Murthy returned as executive chairman in June 2013.
