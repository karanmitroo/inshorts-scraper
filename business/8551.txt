India's largest private power producer Adani Power on Monday reported that its consolidated net loss for the second quarter narrowed to ₹114.40 crore from ₹410.92 crore a year earlier. The company's consolidated net sales rose 0.4% year-on-year to ₹5,764.29 crore. Its finance costs for the quarter fell 13% from the year-ago period to ₹1,434 crore due to refinancing of loans.
