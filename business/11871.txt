Job generation in eight sectors (textiles, leather, metal, automobiles, gems and jewellery, transport, IT and handloom) fell to a six-year low of 1.35 lakh in 2015, from 4.21 lakh in 2014. Contractual employment decreased by 32,000 against an increase of 1.36 lakh in 2014, while direct employment increased by 1.67 lakh, down from an increase of 2.85 lakh in 2014.
