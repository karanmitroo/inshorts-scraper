Howard Schultz, Chairman and CEO of coffee giant Starbucks, has bought a duplex penthouse worth $40 million (around ₹270 crore) in New York City, according to reports. At $7,159 (₹4.8 lakh) per square foot, the penthouse is among the priciest residential sales in Greenwich Village. Notably, the deal comes two months before Schultz is set to step down as CEO.
