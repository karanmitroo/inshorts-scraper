Nine off the top ten companies listed on the benchmark BSE Sensex have added ₹80,086 crores in market capitalisation after the Sensex surged by 858 points last week to 27,079.51. ONGC registered the highest gain as its m-cap surged by ₹26,393.68 crore to ₹2,25,137.72 crore. HDFC and ITC were the second and third biggest gainers respectively.
