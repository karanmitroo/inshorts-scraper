Japanese manufacturer, Toshiba, has launched the world's first Ultra HD (4K) display laptop in the Indian market, priced at  Rs 86,000, which will be available for sale by the end of June. The Satellite P50t-B Y3110 laptop has a 15.6-inch IPS display with 3840x2160p resolution, four times higher than that of the standard Full HD (1080p) screen.
