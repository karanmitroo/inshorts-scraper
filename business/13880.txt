The BSE Sensex on Friday fell by 38.96 points (0.15%) to close at 26,265.24 points while the NSE Nifty ended nearly flat at 7,954.30, losing 1.15 points (0.01%). SBI, Reliance, Infosys, ONGC and Larsen & Toubro were the major gainers while Dr Reddy's Labs, Mahindra & Mahindra, BHEL, Tata Steel and Vedanta were among the major losers.
