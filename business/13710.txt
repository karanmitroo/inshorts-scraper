Videocon on Tuesday launched the issue of five year Foreign Currency Convertible Bonds (FCCBs) for raising ₹641 crore ($97.2 million) by 2020. The FCCBs (bonds issued in a currency different from the issuer's domestic currency) are “convertible into ordinary equity shares, in exchange of a part of outstanding amount of existing” $200 million at an interest rate of 6.75%.
