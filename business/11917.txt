Shopping is the main reason for Indians to access internet, followed by social networking and banking, according to a survey by American Express and market research firm Nielsen. Further, the average monthly online spending in metros and Tier-I cities was estimated at ₹10,900 and ₹8,000 respectively. The survey was held across six cities– Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Jaipur, Ahmedabad and Hyderabad.
