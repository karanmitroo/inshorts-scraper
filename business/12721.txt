Online retailer Amazon has invested another ₹1,980 crore in its Indian business, bringing the total investment in the country to ₹7,679 crore since July 2014. Earlier, Amazon CEO Jeff Bezos had promised a $2 billion investment (around ₹13,600 crore) in the company's India business. Amazon's last round of fund infusion in India was ₹1,696 crore in December.
