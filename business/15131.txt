India stood 9th in the list of top 10 foreign direct investment (FDI) recipients globally with total FDI inflows of $34 bn in 2014, according to the World Investment Report 2015. India, the only BRIC nation to not cross $50 billion-a-year mark, was ranked 15th in 2012 and 2013. China topped the list with $129 billion FDI inflows in 2014.
