Telecom giant Bharti Airtel has re-appointed Sunil Mittal as its Chairman for another 5 years, with effect from October 1, 2016. The 58-year-old founded the Bharti Airtel's parent company, Indian business conglomerate Bharti Enterprises, in 1976. Airtel operates in 20 countries across Asia and Africa, and ranks amongst the top four mobile service providers globally in terms of subscribers.
