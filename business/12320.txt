The National Stock Exchange (NSE) will begin disseminating the value of three indices-- Nifty Private Bank, Nifty Quality 30 and Nifty Midcap Liquid 15-- online on its terminal from March 14. Nifty Private Bank tracks ten largest private banks, Nifty Quality 30 reflects performance of top 30 quality companies and Nifty Midcap Liquid 15 shows liquid midcap stocks' performance.
