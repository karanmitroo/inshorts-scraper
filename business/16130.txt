India's crude oil production fell 1.4% at 3.21 million tons in December, owing to decline in output from oilfields operated by private companies. Crude oil production was at 3.25 million tons in December 2013. ONGC reported a 0.7% rise in output at 1.9 million tons. However, privately operated fields saw a 5.2% drop in production at 1.02 million tons.
