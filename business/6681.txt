American banking and financial services company JPMorgan's CEO Jamie Dimon received an annual pay package of ₹190 crore in 2016, a 3.7% hike than a year ago. Dimon received a ₹7-crore raise after the bank’s stock climbed 31% last year. Under Dimon, JPMorgan became the world's most valuable bank by market capitalisation last year, taking the crown from Wells Fargo.
