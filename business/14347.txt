The investor wealth in BSE on Monday surged ₹1,84,664.63 crore to ₹98,39,357 crore 
as the Sensex rallied over 560 points. "The weaker-than-expected US employment data provided the Asian and European stock markets a reason to cheer, which led market participants building in the assumption of the US interest lift-off getting postponed probably beyond December", a Reliance Securities analyst said.
