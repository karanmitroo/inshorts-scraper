Mukesh Ambani-owned Reliance Jio Infocomm on Monday said it will raise ₹15,000 crore through rights issue to its existing shareholders. The company will issue 15 billion equity shares of ₹10 each to its existing shareholders. It also announced a spectrum sharing and trading agreement with Anil Ambani-owned Reliance Communications, to bolster its indoor coverage and seamless voice services.
