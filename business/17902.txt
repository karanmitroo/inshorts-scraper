Snapping the double-digit growth rate for the second time, merchandise exports rose 3.5% in Dec-2013 to $26.3 billion from $25.4 billion a year-ago. Exports had been growing in double-digits since Jul-2013. However, in Nov-2013, growth in shipments came down to 5.9%. Imports dropped 15.25% to $36.5 billion. Thus, trade deficit  narrowed to $10.1 billion, compared to $17.2 billion a year-ago.
