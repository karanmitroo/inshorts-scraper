The BSE Sensex on Thursday went up by 283.17 points (1 percent) to 27115.83 and the Nifty rose 83.05 points (1 percent) to 8174.60. As many as 1634 shares advanced, 1099 declined and 157 remained unchanged. Reliance, ONGC, Tata Motors and Sun Pharma were the top gainers, while Coal India, Cipla, NTPC and Tata Steel were the major losers.
