
R Venkataramanan, Managing Trustee of Tata Trusts, has filed a criminal defamation case against former Tata Sons chairman Cyrus Mistry for ''causing irreparable damage to his reputation". Venkataramanan has claimed ₹500 crore in damages and alleged that certain emails leaked to the media caused damage to his reputation. Mistry’s office has said they are not intimidated by these tactics.
