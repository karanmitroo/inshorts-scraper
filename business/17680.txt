E-commerce giants Flipkart, Snapdeal, Myntra and LensKart, which all have Bansals as their founders, account for nearly Rs 10,000 crore of the total online retail market of Rs 12,000 crore (i.e. 85% of India's entire e-tailing industry). Leading them all is Flipkart, co-founded by Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal, that generates Rs 6,100 crore in sales, half the industry total.
