Billionaire Warren Buffet's investment company Berkshire Hathway has agreed to buy United States aircraft parts manufacturer Precision Castparts for $37.2 billion (approximately Rs 2,376 billion) including debt. Buffet agreed to pay $235 per share in cash for the company, which is a 21 percent increase on Precision's closing share price on Friday. The deal is Buffet's latest manufacturing foray.
