Saugata Gupta of Marico, which owns brands like Saffola, was the highest paid CEO of a consumer goods company with a pay of ₹16.5 crore in 2016-17. Gupta’s salary was 188 times the median pay at the company. India's largest consumer goods maker Hindustan Unilever's Sanjiv Mehta earned ₹14.2 crore. Emami's SK Goenka earned the lowest among his peers.
