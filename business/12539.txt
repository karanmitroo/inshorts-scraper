According to SocialCops analysis of data from the Export Promotion Council for Handicrafts, the value of Indian handicraft exports (in rupees) increased 9.8% from 2014-15 to 2015-16. The highest growth occurred in zari and zari goods, shawls as artwares and handprinted textiles and scarves. The total value of handicraft exports was 186.4 billion rupees in 2014-15.
