The government's ambitious disinvestment program started out promisingly, with the first disinvestment of 5% stake in SAIL fetching a whopping Rs.1715 crore. "Since the stock market is hitting fresh highs, it is an appropriate time to push the divestment program," said D K Josh, a prominent economist. The government hopes to raise Rs 43,425 crore from disinvestment in state-run enterprises.
