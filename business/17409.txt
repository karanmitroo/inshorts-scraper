Amid hope that the BJP will come in power, the benchmark sensex rose as much as 2.52% to a record intraday high of 23,572. The Nifty also rose 2.35% to a life high of 7,020, surpassing its previous record hit on Friday. The Sensex finally ended up at 23,551, while the Nifty closed at 7,014, their all time closing high.
