Domestic passenger car sales rose by 9.62% to 1.77 lakh units in July, from 1.62 lakh units in the corresponding period last year, according to data released by the Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers (SIAM). During the same period, sales of two-wheelers rose by 13.52%, commercial vehicles grew marginally by 0.1% and vehicles across all categories increased by 13.22%.
