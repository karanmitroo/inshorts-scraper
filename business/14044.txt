Seven of the top ten companies listed on the benchmark Sensex added  ₹54,619 crore in market capitalisation (m-cap) in the past week with RIL, Infosys and TCS registering biggest gains. RIL gained ₹14,135.74 crore in m-cap, Infosys’ m-cap soared by ₹12,587.26 crore and TCS's valuation jumped by ₹11,871.82 crore. On other hand, ONGC, Sun Pharma and SBI registered biggest losses.
