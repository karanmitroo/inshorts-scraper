Puerto Rico-born Omar de Frias left his $200,000 (nearly ₹1.3 crore) NASA project analyst job to build a cigar company named Fratello Cigars. The 38-year-old's firm would be generating a $1 million (nearly ₹6.5 crore) revenue after a recent cigar deal. Further, his firm paid a tribute to NASA by launching 'Navetta' (Italian for shuttle) cigars named after NASA spacecrafts.
