According to a data released by the Commerce Ministry, Gold imports in December 2015 more than doubled to $3.80 billion as compared to $1.36 billion in the year-ago period. Meanwhile, Oil imports declined 33.19% to $6.65 billion in December. Imports between April-December contracted 15.87% to $295.8 billion against the year-ago period, leaving a trade deficit of $99.2 billion.
