IT services firm Wipro Wednesday reported a 7.9% growth (Y-o-Y) in consolidated net profit at Rs 2,084 crore for the 2nd quarter ended Sept 30, helped by infrastructure services. Net sales of the country's 3rd largest software services exporter rose by 7.5% to Rs 11,816 crore during the period. However, on Q-o-Q basis, Wipro's net profit declined marginally by 0.9%.
