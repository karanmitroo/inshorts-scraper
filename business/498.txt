Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT) Chairman Sushil Chandra has said that salaried employees are the best taxpayers and deserve better treatment. "So, we put standard deduction of ₹40,000 to all salaried persons," he said. He further added that 1.89 crore salaried taxpayers paid ₹1.48 lakh crore in taxes and 1.88 crore business people gave ₹44,000 crore.
