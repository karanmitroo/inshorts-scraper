Allahabad Bank, State Bank of Bikaner and Jaipur, Yes Bank and Kotak Mahindra Bank on Thursday announced a 0.25% cut in the base rate. The decision comes after RBI announced a 0.50% cut in the repo rate. The rates, effective from October 5, for Yes Bank, SBBJ, Allahabad Bank and Kotak Mahindra will be 10.25%, 9.7%, 9.7%, and 9.5% respectively.
