The BSE Sensex on Thursday closed on a flat note at 24,469.57 points, down by 22.82 points, while the NSE Nifty lost 13.10 points to settle at 7,424.65. Hindustan Unilever, ITC, Mahindra & Mahindra, and Reliance Industries emerged as the major gainers, while Larsen & Toubro, Bharti Airtel, Axis Bank, and BHEL ended among the major losers.
