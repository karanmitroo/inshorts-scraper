Sumitomo Mitsui Trust Bank of Japan (SMTB) on Wednesday sold its entire 2.77% stake in Reliance Capital for about ₹456 crore through open market transactions. Sumitomo Mitsui had purchased 70 lakh shares when Reliance Capital had applied for a banking licence. Meanwhile, Royal Bank of Scotland picked up 17.2 lakh shares in the company for ₹112 crore.
