The Sensex plunged by over 229 points on Thursday, with shares of energy, banking and capital goods emerging the weakest among all. The 30-share index came under pressure due to weak Chinese equities, and lingering political uncertainty in Greece.The  Sensex closed 229.09 points, lower at 27,602.01. The 50-share Nifty also fell 62.75 points, to end at 8,292.90.
