The BSE Sensex on Monday fell 175.40 points or 0.65% to end at 26904.11 while Nifty slipped 46.10 points or 0.6% at 8143.60, mainly due to a decline in the software stocks. Infosys and Lupin were the top losers on the BSE benchmark index. The September quarter results of Infosys was the key highlight of the market.
