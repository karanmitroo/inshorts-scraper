The benchmark indices gained around 2% in the week ended September 2 to post their biggest weekly gains in over two months. The Sensex gained 749.86 points to close at 28,532.11, hitting a 16-month high on Friday. The Nifty soared 237.10 points to settle at 8,809.65. This followed RBI's annual report stating the economy could expand at 7.6% in 2016-17.
