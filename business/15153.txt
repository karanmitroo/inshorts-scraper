Drug manufacturer Sun Pharma has reportedly sacked 18 of its high-ranking executives who earlier used to work for Ranbaxy Labs, which was acquired by Sun Pharma in 2014. Those who have been asked to resign include Indrajit Banerjee, president and CFO of Ranbaxy and Yugal Sikri, country head (India) Ranbaxy. All 18 executives have been given a severance package.
