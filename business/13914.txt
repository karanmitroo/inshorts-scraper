In an auction of debt securities worth ₹852 crore for Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPIs) on Monday, bids worth ₹1,678 crore were witnessed during the two-hour auction held at the NSE's eBid platform. According to depository data, investments in government securities via auction route for FPIs till October 29 stood at ₹1,29,048 crore against the permitted ₹1,29,900 crore.
