In the first 5-months of this financial-year(April-August 2013), the fiscal-deficit, which is the difference between government receipts and spending, increased from last year's 65.7% to 74.6%(Rs.4.04-lakh-crore) of the budget-estimate, without accounting for a substantial portion of food and oil-subsidies. It came down during 2012-13 from last-to-last year's 5.8% to 4.9%. Government plans to lower it to 4.8% of the GDP.
