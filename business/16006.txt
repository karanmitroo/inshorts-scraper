Tata Motors Thursday posted a 25.47% decline in its consolidated net profit at Rs 3,580.72 crore for the third quarter ended December 31, the PTI reported. Tata Motors had registered consolidated net profit of Rs 4,804.8 crore in the year-ago period. Consolidated net sales during Q3 rose 8.83% to Rs 69,121.61 crore from Rs 63,512.51 crore in the year-ago period
