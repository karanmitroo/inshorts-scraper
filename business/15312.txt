Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) took a massive 61.6% jump from $21.6 billion in FY 2014 to $34.9 billion in FY 2015, according to a Nomura report. The report says the FY 2015 inflows are 1.7% of GDP, up from 1.1% in the previous year. The higher FDI was attributed to the growing investor confidence in the country.
