The government’s indirect tax revenue rose 34.3% year-on-year to ₹4.38 lakh crore in the first eight months of the ongoing fiscal, according to a Finance Ministry statement. Collections from central excise jumped 67.1% in the April-November period to ₹1.70 lakh crore, service tax collections grew 25% to ₹1.27 lakh crore and customs increased by 14.7% to ₹1.40 lakh crore.
