India's third largest outsourcing firm Wipro, surged to its 52-week high (at Rs. 577.70) Monday after it met Street estimates in its December quarter. With over 4% rise, Wipro's stock was the top gainer on the 50-share Nifty benchmark. Also, it outperformed the broader BSE IT benchmark. Wipro's dollar sales grew 3% sequentially in the quarter.
