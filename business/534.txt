Drugmaker GlaxoSmithKline (GSK) has reported a 69% year-on-year rise in standalone net profit to ₹89.7 crore for December quarter, compared to ₹52.95 crore in the same period last fiscal. The company's revenue stood at ₹703.9 crore during the quarter. GSK MD A Vaidheesh said, "Overall underlying performance...supported by high single to double-digit volume growth on key brands and therapy areas."
