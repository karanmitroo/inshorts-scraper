Reckitt Benckiser, which makes Dettol cleaning products and Durex condoms, said the "NotPetya" ransomware cyber attack could cost the company $129 million (over ₹800 crore) in revenue. The company said the attack had disrupted production and deliveries of goods in several countries. The attack affected some of the world’s biggest firms including advertising group WPP and delivery service FedEx.
