The BSE Sensex and the NSE Nifty rose over 1% in the week ended May 13. The announcements of revised tax treaties with Mauritius, macroeconomic data and quarterly results in the week directed the course of the markets, analysts said. While the Sensex rose 261.07 points to close at 25,489.57, the Nifty surged 81.45 points to settle at 7,814.90.
