The Sensex and Nifty surged on Monday to record highs on hopes of wide-ranging reforms by the newly formed Narendra Modi govt. The Sensex rose 0.98% to hit a record high of 25,644, before closing down at record 25,580. The Nifty gained 1.2% to hit an all-time high of 7,673, and finally closed at all time high of 7,654.
