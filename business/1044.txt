Union Minister Rajen Gohain has said that the Indian Railways earned ₹2,354 crore more through the sale of tickets in 2016-17 as compared to 2015-16. He revealed that the Indian Railways earned about ₹19,209 crore in online bookings and about ₹28,468 crore in offline bookings in 2016-17. The revenue generated through tickets in 2015-16 was about ₹45,324 crore.
