The government will sell an 11.36% stake in state-owned hydropower firm NHPC through a two-day offer for sale (OFS) that begins on Wednesday. A floor price of ₹21.75 has been set for the sale which will help the government raise around ₹2,700 crore, NHPC said on Tuesday. Post the sale, the government's stake in NHPC will fall to 74.6%.
