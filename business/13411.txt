The Nifty Commodities Index, which tracks the performance of the top 30 NSE listed companies representing the commodities segment, gained 25.05 points on Wednesday to close at 2337.90. The sectors include cement, oil, chemical, power, sugar, metals and mining. ONGC, Cairn India and Reliance Infrastructure emerged among the major gainers while Hindalco and Jindal Steel ended among major losers.
