Continuing with their bullish stance on India for the second month, Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPIs) have pumped in ₹7,625 crore into India's capital market in April (till Friday), buoyed by a rate cut by the RBI. According to the depositories' data, the FPIs invested ₹3,469 crore in equities, while they poured ₹4,152 crore in the debt markets.
