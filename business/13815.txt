Domestic passenger car sales increased by 21.8% to 1.94 lakh units in October from 1.59 lakh units in the corresponding period last year, according to data released by the Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers (SIAM). During the same period, sales of two-wheelers rose by 13.31%, commercial vehicles grew 12.73% and vehicles across all categories increased by 13.91%, SIAM said.
