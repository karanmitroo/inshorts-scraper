Cars sales in India rose by 17.47% in July, highest in three months, to 1,62,022 units, from 1,37,922 units in July 2014, according to Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers. The rise came in the backdrop of a lower base and aggressive pricing by manufacturers. In June, the car sales grew 1.53% to 1,62,677 units, from 1,60,232 units in June 2014.
