SBI reported an increase of 3.3% in net profit to Rs 3,349.08 crore for the June 30 quarter. Also, the total income increased to Rs 40,739.21 crore during the quarter against Rs 36,192.62 crore for the same period a year ago. Shares of SBI were down to a 0.95% down at Rs 2,413.95 during afternoon trade on the BSE.
