The Managing Director of HDFC bank, Aditya Puri is the highest paid banker in India with a total package of ₹31.53 crore, according to VCCircle's analysis. Chanda Kochhar, MD and CEO of ICICI bank, came next with a ₹18.59-crore total package. Notably, top private banks, except Kotak Mahindra, offered 20-30% pay hike on cash compensation to CEOs in 2015-16.
