Assam Chief Minister Sarbananda Sonowal has said the state's first-ever global investors summit 'Advantage Assam' has received investment commitments worth nearly ₹70,000 crore. State-owned oil major ONGC committed ₹13,000 crore investment while Oil India pledged an investment of ₹10,000 crore. Among private companies, Mukesh Ambani's Reliance Industries announced an investment of ₹2,500 crore.
