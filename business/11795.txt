The BSE Sensex on Monday rose 36.20 points to close at 25,880.38 while the NSE Nifty lost 2.70 points to settle at 7,912.05. ICICI Bank and SBI emerged as the major gainers amid reports that the RBI has trimmed the list of companies whose loans need to be provisioned against default risk. Wipro and BHEL ended as the major losers.
