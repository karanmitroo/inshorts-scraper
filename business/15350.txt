Pinky Mehta will become the first woman Chief Financial Officer of the Aditya Birla group when she takes over in Aditya Birla Group's holding, Nuvo in July. She was the first woman officer hired by Aditya Birla Group's Indian Rayon 24 years ago. She has the versatile ability and willingly accepts new challenges, Aditya Birla Group Chairman said about Mehta.
