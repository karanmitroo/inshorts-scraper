Over 8.5 lakh new jobs are expected to be created in 2014 across various sectors, according to a survey (conducted among 5,600 firms) by MyHiringClub.com. FMCG would lead the chart with 1.5 lakh jobs, followed by healthcare (1.33-lakh), IT and ITeS (1.21-lakh), retail (86,700) and hospitality (83,400). An estimated 7.9 lakh jobs were created this year.
