Lava has launched a budget range smartphone, the Lava 3G 415 with a price tag of Rs 5,249 for the Indian market. The announcement comes a day after the firm launched its new KitKat based Iris X1 smartphone at INR 7,999. Unlike the Iris X1, which runs Android KitKat, the new device is embedded with Android 4.2.2 Jelly Bean.
