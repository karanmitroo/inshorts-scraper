The benchmark indices on Monday posted gains after the BSE Sensex and the NSE Nifty rose by 104.22 and 28.20 points to close at 28,182.57 and 8,711.35 respectively. Investors remained upbeat following a positive US jobs report, analysts said. Adani Ports SEZ and HDFC were the major gainers, while Sun Pharma and Bharti Airtel were the biggest losers.
