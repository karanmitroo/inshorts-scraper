The BSE Sensex plunged by 514.19 points on Tuesday to end at 26,304.63, it's lowest level in over 5 months. Further, the Nifty lost 187.85 points to close at 8,108.45. This comes after the Centre announced the scrapping of the ₹500 and ₹1,000 notes. SBI was the biggest gainer while Tata Motors was the top loser.
