Ford India launched a refreshed version of Ford Figo starting from Rs.3.87 lakh on Monday. The refreshed Ford Figo will be available at Rs.3.87 lakh to Rs.5.14 lakh for petrol variant and Rs.4.83 lakh to Rs.6.09 lakh (ex-Showroom Delhi) for the diesel option, Ford India said. The new Ford Figo will have smoky stealth finish exterior styling elements.
