Fuelled by strong foreign inflows, Sensex on Thursday gained 130.55 points to close at an all-time high of 21,164.52, while touching an intra-day high of 21,205.44. The Nifty closed at a 3-year-high of 6,299.15, up 47.45 points. The market today recorded the highest ever turnover at Rs 5.33 lakh crore.
