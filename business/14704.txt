After much speculation, Snapdeal on Tuesday confirmed $500 million funding from investors including Alibaba, Foxconn and SoftBank, valuing the firm at $5 billion. Existing investors Temasek, BlackRock, Myriad and Premji Invest also participated in the round. The investment will help the company increase the number of merchants on its site to 1 million, from around 1.5 lakh today.
