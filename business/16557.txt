The benchmark Sensex on Wednesday closed 130.44 points down at 28,032.85, after hitting new lifetime high of 28,294.01 in intra-day trade. Also, the 50-share Nifty slipped 44 points to end at 8,382.30 after logging fresh peak of 8,455.65 on intra-day basis. Wednesday was the second consecutive day when indices have closed with a loss after hitting new highs.
