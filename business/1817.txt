The government on Friday said investments worth ₹68,000 crore have been committed by 13 domestic and global firms, including ITC, PepsiCo, Patanjali and Coca-Cola, in India's food and agriculture sector. While ITC and Patanjali signed deals to invest ₹10,000 crore each, PepsiCo will invest ₹13,300 crore over five years. Yes Bank will invest ₹1,000 crore in 100 food processing projects.
