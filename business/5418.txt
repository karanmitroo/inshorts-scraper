The basic pay of RBI Governor has been retrospectively hiked to ₹2.5 lakh from ₹90,000 per month, with effect from January 1, 2016. RBI Governor Urjit Patel, who assumed office in September 2016, will now receive around ₹3.7 lakh in total per month. The basic pay of RBI Deputy Governors was also increased from ₹80,000 to ₹2.25 lakh per month.
