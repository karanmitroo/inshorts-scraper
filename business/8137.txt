Following demonetisation of the current ₹500, ₹1000 notes, the RBI said all ATMs and cash-dispensing machines will have to be re-configured to disburse ₹100 and ₹50 notes from November 11. Further, new notes of ₹2,000 and ₹500 will also be available at the ATMs from November 11. Notably, Prime Minister Narendra Modi has declared the move to curb black money.
