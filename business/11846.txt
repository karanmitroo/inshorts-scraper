Beleaguered businessman Vijay Mallya diverted ₹430 crore from the ₹950 crore IDBI loan for Kingfisher Airlines to purchase a property abroad, the Enforcement Directorate (ED) alleged before a special court on Saturday. Further, the special Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA) court reserved its order on the agency's plea for a non-bailable warrant against Mallya till April 18.
