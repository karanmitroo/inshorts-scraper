Over 2 lakh crore of total investor wealth was wiped out over a sharp sell-off in the stock market, as the benchmark Sensex plunged by 586.65 points (2.23 percent) on Tuesday. Total investor wealth of BSE-listed companies collapsed by Rs 2.02 lakh crore to Rs 96.25 lakh crore. At BSE, a total of 2093 stocks declined on Tuesday.
