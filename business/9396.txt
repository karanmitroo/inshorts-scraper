India's crude oil imports in August touched a seven-year high at 18.81 million tonnes, a 9.1% year-on-year increment, Oil Ministry said. However, India paid ₹39,030 crore for crude oil imports, as opposed to ₹39,024 crore in August 2015. Amid an increase in domestic demand, India's overall fuel consumption rose by 11.4% and the petrol demand grew by a record 25%.
