As many as 73 listed companies made open offers worth 11,795 crore in 2015-16, a slump of 32% from the preceding fiscal. Further, shareholders tendered ₹2,971 crore or 25% of the amount on offer, compared to ₹13,009 crore or 75% in 2014-15. An open offer allows shareholders to purchase a stock at a price lower than the existing market price.
