Naveen Jindal-led Jindal Steel and Power (JSPL) on Thursday said its consolidated net loss doubled to ₹1,082.15 crore for the June quarter on account of decline in demand as well as higher fuel and finance costs. The firm's total consolidated income was higher at ₹4,655.45 crore in the April-June quarter this fiscal as against ₹4,405.73 crore in the year-ago period.
