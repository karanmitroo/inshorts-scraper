As of September 30, as many as 1,624 FIRs were filed against wilful defaulters owing over ₹16,600 crore to Public Sector Banks (PSBs). At the end of first half of the current fiscal, 7,265 wilful defaulters owed more than ₹64,330 to PSBs. While Canara Bank filed highest number of FIRs (225), lowest (4) FIRs were filed by the Corporation Bank.
