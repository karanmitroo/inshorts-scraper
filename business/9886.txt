Delhi-based Oswal group has sold its 6.285 million shares or 9.75% stake in NDTV, for around ₹51 crore. The shares were picked up by a Mauritius-based foreign portfolio investor, LTS Investment fund, according to bulk deal data on BSE. One of the group's officials said that the firm booked a profit of about ₹33.8 crore from the deal.
