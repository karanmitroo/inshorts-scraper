Domestic air traffic witnessed a 24.27% year-on-year growth in February with various carriers flying 74.76 lakh passengers in the month, according to data by Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA). IndiGo continued to lead in the domestic segment with a 36.8% market share, followed by Jet Airways at 18.4%. Further, the January-February period saw a 23% increase in air traffic.
