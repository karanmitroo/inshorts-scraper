India's largest two-wheeler maker Hero MotoCorp, on Monday, posted an 18.13% year-on-year rise in net profits to ₹883.1 crore in the quarter ended June. Further, the revenue increased to ₹8,010.66 crore up 7.7% from the corresponding period of last fiscal. The company said they had sold 17.45 lakh units during the June-ended quarter against 16.45 lakh units in year-ago period.
