The benchmark indices on Wednesday ended with marginal gains with the Sensex rising 128.27 points to end at 27,915.89, and the broader Nifty gaining 37.30 points to settle at 8,565.85. Coal India Ltd, GAIL India Ltd, and Larsen & Toubro ended as the biggest gainers, while Wipro, Axis Bank, and Tata Motors were amongst the major losers.
