Extending its gain for the second consecutive day on Thursday, the BSE Sensex climbed to 3-month high of 28,446.12, up by 247.83 points. Closing above 8,600-mark for the first time since April 20, the 50-share Nifty also surged 84.25 points to close at 8,608.05. The surge in the market came after the government simplified foreign investment norms.
