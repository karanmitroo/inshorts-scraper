Continuing the decline for fifth consecutive day on Friday, the BSE Sensex went down by 181.31 points (0.7 percent) to close at 26,656.83 points and Nifty fell by 45.95 points (0.6 percent) to 8,065.80. NTPC, ICICI Bank, Axis Bank, Dr Reddy and Sun Pharma were the top gainers, while Vedanta, Larsen and Toubro, ITC and BHEL were the major losers.
