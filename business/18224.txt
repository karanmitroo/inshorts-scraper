US-based Harley-Davidson has launched the new 2014 edition of its motorcycle 'Street Glide' in India (under its 'Project Rushmore'), which will be available at all eleven Harley-Davidson dealerships across India. "Priced at Rs 29 lakh, Street Glide offers stunning design and rider-focused engineering to reach a perfect balance between custom touring style and long haul functionality," said Harley-Davidson India MD.
