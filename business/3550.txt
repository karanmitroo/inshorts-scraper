The Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI) spent ₹9,055 crore over the last eight years towards issuing Aadhaar numbers. This includes an expenditure of nearly ₹3,820 crore towards enrolment and ₹1,171 crore towards logistics like printing and dispatching of Aadhaar letters. Notably, a total of about 116.09 crore Aadhaar numbers have been generated up to July 21, 2017.
