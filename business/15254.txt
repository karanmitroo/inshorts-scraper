Maruti Suzuki India on Wednesday launched the country's most fuel-efficient car, Celerio diesel, with an ARAI-certified mileage of 27.62 km per litre. The car has a 793cc DDiS 125 diesel engine which will deliver a maximum power of 47bhp and 125Nm of peak torque. The price of the hatchback ranges between Rs 4.65 lakh and Rs 5.71 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi).
