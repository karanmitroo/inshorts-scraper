Male-grooming is now a Rs.3800-crore market in India and is expected to touch Rs.5,270-crore in the next 3-years, estimates global market-research firm Euromonitor. Beauty-salons have also seen a huge spike in male-clientele. The market is expected to grow annually by 21% on the back of skin-whitening creams. Brands like Garnier-Men and Amway-India are the top-gainers in the men's grooming market.
