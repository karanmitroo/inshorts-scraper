Gold prices hit its lowest levels in 1-1/2 month on Friday due to sluggish demand from jewellery stockists as well as poor local buying sentiment. Standard Gold (99.5 purity) declined by Rs 95 to end at Rs 26,625 per 10 grams. However, price of silver (.999 fineness) edged up by Rs 25 to settle at Rs 37,685 per kg.
