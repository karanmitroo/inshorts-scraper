The Nifty PSU Bank Index, which reflects the performance of the top 12 public sector banks listed on the NSE, on Friday gained 109.40 points (3.52%) to close at 3217.05, after falling 2.74% over the last month. All 12 stocks surged, with Oriental Bank and Bank of Baroda as the top gainers, climbing 7.33% and 5.62% respectively.
