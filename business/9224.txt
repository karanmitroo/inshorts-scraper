India's second largest telecom operator Vodafone on Monday slashed data prices for new 4G and 3G users to ₹25 per GB. The offer, open to those who buy a new 4G handset, couples 9 GB free data for every 1 GB purchased at ₹250 for a 90-day period and is valid in 16 circles. Earlier, Airtel also cut data prices.
