The BSE Sensex on Friday rose by 363.98 points to close at 28,078.35, while the NSE Nifty gained 132.05 points to settle at 8,683.15, on account of buying in blue-chip stocks amid firm global cues. The markets also got a boost with the government’s plan to set a target of Apr​il 2017 for the rollout of the GST, analysts said.
