Rising for the second straight session, the Sensex surged 153 points on Monday, led by gains in metals, auto and healthcare sectors. The 30-share index ended at 27,395.73, after touching a high of 27,507.25 and a low of 27,266.49. The 50-share Nifty also gained in the trading session, and ended at 8,246.30, up 45.60 points from the previous close.
