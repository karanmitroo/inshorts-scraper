Presenting the annual budget, Delhi Finance Minister Manish Sisodia on Monday announced a reduction in VAT on readymade garments and footwear from 12.5% to 5%. Further, the allocation for municipal corporations was increased by around ₹1,000 crore to ₹6,919 crore. Drinking water will be provided for every colony by December 2017, with ₹676 crore allocated for the same, Sisodia added.
