The BSE Sensex and the NSE Nifty on Friday plunged 604.51 and 181.85 points to close at 26,397.71 and 8,088.60 respectively on account of the UK's decision to exit the European Union. The benchmark Sensex and Nifty fell over 1,000 points and 330 points respectively during intraday trade before paring their losses in the second half.
