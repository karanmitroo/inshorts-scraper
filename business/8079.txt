State Bank of India Chairperson Arundhati Bhattacharya has said that after the government demonetised old ₹500 and ₹1,000 currency notes, SBI banks have received deposits worth ₹53,000 crore till Friday afternoon. Further, SBI has reportedly replaced nearly ₹750 crore worth of notes with the junked ones on Thursday, while till mid-day on Friday it exchanged currency amounting to ₹723 crore.
