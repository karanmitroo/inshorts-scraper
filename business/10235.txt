According to data released on Friday, India's exports contracted 6.84% year-on-year to $21.68 billion in July, after rising for the first time in 18 months in June. The trade deficit declined to $7.76 billion against $13 billion in the previous year period. Further, imports fell 19.03% at $29.45 billion, as gold imports declined 63.65% during the month to $1.07 billion.
