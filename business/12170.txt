Low-income states like Bihar, Jharkhand and Odisha saw higher retail inflation in February than higher income states like Haryana, Uttarakhand, Maharashtra and Gujarat, according to global credit rating firm Crisil. While Punjab registered the lowest inflation of 2.7%, Odisha recorded the highest of 8.04%. The retail inflation in India stood at 5.18% in February, down from 5.69% in January.
