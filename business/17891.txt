TCS on Thursday posted a growth of 50.3% (Y-o-Y) in consolidated net profit (Rs 5,333 crore) for the third quarter ended Dec-31. Growth in this quarter was aided by industries like Life Science & Healthcare, Media, Travel & Hospitality, Telecom and manufacturing & improved business in Europe. Quarter on Quarter (Q-o-Q) in rupee terms, TCS net profit rose by 15.1%.
