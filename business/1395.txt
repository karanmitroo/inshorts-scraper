GST Network Chairman Ajay Bhushan Pandey has said he hopes new leadership at Infosys will continue to further strengthen the GSTN portal. His statement comes after Infosys on Saturday appointed Salil Parekh as its new CEO and MD. Pandey said, "In one month, there was a jump of 4.5 lakh businesses filing GST returns...It shows that GSTN system is stabilising."
