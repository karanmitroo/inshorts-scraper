The benchmark indices on Friday ended the trade in losses after the BSE Sensex and the NSE Nifty fell 156.76 and 27.80 points to close at 28,051.86 and 8,638.50 respectively on account of profit booking amid weak global cues. Adani Ports SEZ and Lupin were the major gainers while Bharti Airtel and ICICI Bank emerged as the biggest losers.
