Average CEO salary at top-listed Indian companies in the private sector is reportedly around ₹20 crore on average, double the ₹10-crore average two years ago. The highest-paid Indian CEOs in 2015-16 included L&T's AM Naik (₹66.14 crore) and Infosys' Vishal Sikka (₹48.73 crore). Notably, the average CEO salaries at top-listed US companies was $20 million (about ₹130 crore) in 2015.
