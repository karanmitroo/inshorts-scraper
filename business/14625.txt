The 30-share benchmark Sensex on Wednesday continued its downward spiral as it lost about 243 points to close at 25,453.56. The broader 50-share Nifty index fell 68.85 points, or 0.88%, to close at 7,717. BHEL was the top Sensex loser, down about 5%. In intraday trade, stocks of sugar companies rose up to 15%.
