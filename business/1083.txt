Reliance Industries Founder Dhirubhai Ambani, born on December 28, 1932, used to sell 'bhajias' to pilgrims in Gujarat at the age of 15. Ambani returned to India in 1957 from Yemen and started a yarn trading business from a 500 sq ft office in Mumbai. Currently, the combined net worth of his sons, Mukesh and Anil, is over $44 billion.
