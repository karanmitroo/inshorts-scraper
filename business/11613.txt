As per data from the Director General of Civil Aviation, 10 out of around 310 routes accounted for 31% of the 4.48 crore-strong domestic air traffic between October 2015 and March 2016. Over 55% of the routes had under 10,000 monthly passengers, with at least 10 routes having under 100 passengers. Further, Mumbai-Delhi was the busiest route, followed by Bengaluru-Delhi.
