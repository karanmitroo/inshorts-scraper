Several high-profile executives left their jobs in 2016, including TV journalist Arnab Goswami who resigned as the Editor-in-Chief of Times Now and ET Now in November. The year also saw the removal of Cyrus Mistry as Tata Chairman, and Nikesh Arora resign as Softbank President and COO. Further, Chitra Ramkrishna also stepped down as the MD and CEO of NSE.
