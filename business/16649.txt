The Sensex on Thursday closed at an all-time-high of 27,346, up 0.92 per cent or 248 points. The market also recorded its highest ever turnover at Rs 10.5 lakh crore. Reliance (up 2.94 per cent), Hindalco (up 2.39 per cent), TCS (up 2.21 per cent), GAIL (up 1.93 per cent) and Infosys (up 1.72 per cent) were among the gainers.
