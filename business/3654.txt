Reliance Industries' Mukesh Ambani, while speaking at its 40th annual general meeting, said net profit has grown 10,000 times from ₹3 crore to ₹30,000 crore. The company's market cap has risen from ₹10 crore in 1977 to ₹5 lakh crore in 2017. In 4 decades, Reliance has grown from a startup to one of the world's largest companies, he added.
