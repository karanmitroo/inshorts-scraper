The Indian media and entertainment industry is expected to grow at a 14.3% compound annual growth rate to touch ₹2.26 lakh crore by 2020 from ₹1.15 lakh crore in 2015, a report by consulting firm KPMG and industry body Ficci said. Further, advertising revenues are expected to grow at 15.9% between 2015 and 2020, led by digital advertising at 33.5%.
