Six of the top ten blue-chip firms listed in the Sensex gained ₹31,967.21 crore combined, while the remaining four lost ₹16,963.72 crore in their market capitalisation (m-cap) in the week ended November 20. Reliance Industries gained ₹5,101.52 crore and HDFC Bank surged ₹4,486.88 crore. Infosys was the biggest loser with a loss of ₹10,922.70 crore from its m-cap.
