India's largest IT services provider Tata Consultancy Services reportedly paid salaries of over ₹1 crore to 91 employees in India in 2016-17. Of these 91 employees, 22 of them have spent their entire careers at TCS. In comparison, Infosys has 51 employees earning in the range of  ₹1 crore while Wipro has 61 employees earning in the same range.
