Honda Cars India Monday raised its product prices by up to Rs 60,000 following the expiry of reduced excise duty concessions and on rising input costs. Prices of Honda Brio were increased by Rs 15,000-Rs 18,000, now costing between Rs 4.21 lakh-Rs 6.30 lakh. Honda CR-V's prices were hiked by Rs 60,000, now costing between Rs 20.85 lakh-Rs 24.96 lakh.
