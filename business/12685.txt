Under the Congress-led UPA government, the number of jobs in different industries rose by 4.4% year-on-year, whereas wages increased by 14.05% in 2013-14, according to a government survey. Further, the gross capital formation, an indicator of investments, declined by 5.62%, while the profitability of factories increased by 2.02%. The Indian economy had grown at 6.6% in 2013-14.
