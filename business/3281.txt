Tata Motors, on Wednesday, posted a 41.6% year-on-year rise in its profit to ₹3,200 crore for the June quarter. The profit beat analysts' estimates due to a one-time gain of ₹3,609 crore on account of changes made to Jaguar-Land Rover's pension plans. The company saw a 10% year-on-year decline in its revenue to ₹58,651 crore.
