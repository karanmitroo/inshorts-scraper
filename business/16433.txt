The three-day Great Online Shopping Festival saw almost 80 lakh visitors, Google said Saturday. "Compared to last year, the user engagement is up by 7 times," said Nitin Bawankule, Industry Director, eCommerce Google India. Around 40% people visited the Rs 299 corner. People from around 220 Indian cities, including smaller cities like Ranchi and Hubli also participated in the sale.
