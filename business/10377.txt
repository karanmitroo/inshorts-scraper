The benchmark indices on Thursday ended the trade on a flat note, with the BSE Sensex rising 16.86 points to close at 27,714.37, and the NSE Nifty rising 6.25 points to settle at 8,551.10. Tata Steel, Tata Motors, and Bharti Airtel ended as the biggest gainers, while Infosys, Lupin, and Asian Paints were the biggest losers.
