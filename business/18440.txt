With transactions of over Rs 132.6-billion during Jan-Jun 2013, Indians became the top foreign investors in Dubai's real estate market. Within only six-months, Indians spent Rs 132.6-billion while only Rs 149-billion were invested in the entire 2012. The other top foreign investors include the nationals from Arab (Rs 82.7-billion), Britain (Rs 66.3-billion) and Pakistan (Rs 49.7-billion).
