Minister of State for Finance, Jayant Sinha, informed the Lok Sabha Friday
that the government expected to raise Rs 22,574 crore by selling stakes in four state-run companies - ONGC, NALCO, BHEL and NMDC. The Cabinet had given its nod to the sale of five percent stake in ONGC and BHEL and 10 percent in NALCO and NMDC, he added.
