SpiceJet on Thursday reported nearly 16% jump in consolidated profit at ₹172.67 crore for the June quarter. The airline had posted a profit of ₹149.03 crore in the same period a year ago. Its revenue also increased 22.4% to ₹1,889.7 crore. CMD Ajay Singh said the airline had an "eventful quarter" and stated it's the airline's tenth successive profitable quarter.
