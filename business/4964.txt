The rate of non-subsidised cooking gas, LPG, has been cut by ₹92 per cylinder in line with international trends. Also, the price of subsidised LPG cylinder has been hiked by ₹1.87 and kerosene by 26 paise. A 14.2-kg non-subsidised LPG cylinder will now cost ₹631 as against ₹723 earlier while a subsidised cylinder will cost ₹442.77 in Delhi.
