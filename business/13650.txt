Average daily volumes in the derivatives market plunged to 3.37 million contracts in November from 8.78 million in October on the NSE, and to 0.09 million from 0.41 million on the BSE. This came after SEBI, in the equity derivatives segment, raised the minimum contract size from ₹2 lakh to ₹5 lakh, effective after October F&O (futures & options) expiry.
