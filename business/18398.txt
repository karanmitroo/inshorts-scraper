Indian railways ticketing arm, IRCTC -which has nearly 2-crore registered users, twice that of India's largest online retailer Flipkart- has launched its e-commerce site, shop.irctc.co.in, in partnership with Yebhi.com. The e-commerce company, Yebhi will build and manage the platform of the Rs.1000-crore project. In 2011, Infibeam partnered with Gujarat government to enable their rural e-commerce initiative "Vishwagram Bazaar".
