The benchmark indices on Monday ended the trade in losses after the BSE Sensex and the NSE Nifty fell 89.84 and 32.70 points to close at 27,746.66 and 8,508.70 respectively.  Bajaj Auto, Infosys, and Sun Pharma were the major gainers while Hindustan Unilever, Bharti Airtel, and ONGC emerged as the biggest losers.
