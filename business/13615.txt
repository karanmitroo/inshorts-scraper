The BSE Sensex on Tuesday lost 43.60 points (0.17%) to settle at 25,775.74 points while the NSE Nifty fell by 17.65 points (0.22%) to close at 7,831.60. Hindustan Unilever, HDFC, Reliance Industries and Lupin emerged as the major gainers while Larsen & Toubro, Maruti Suzuki, Sun Pharma and Bajaj Auto were among the major losers.
