Shares of incumbent telecom operators plunged on Tuesday after Reliance Jio Chairman Mukesh Ambani announced new offers. Airtel was the biggest loser after its shares declined by over 4% in intraday trade, while Idea Cellular dipped by nearly 2%. Notably, Ambani announced that Jio crossed the 100 million customers mark in 170 days and also announced the Jio Prime offer.
