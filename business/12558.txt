State-owned IDBI Bank on Saturday said that it plans to raise ₹1,500 crore by selling stake to Life Insurance Corporation (LIC) through preferential issuance of shares. The insurance major and various other schemes currently hold 7.25% stake in IDBI Bank. Last week, the bank also got board approval for raising up to $500 million (around ₹3,400 crore) through tier-I bonds.
