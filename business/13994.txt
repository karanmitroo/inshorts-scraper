Dabur India Limited on Wednesday announced a net profit of ₹341.1 crore for the second quarter (Q2), an 18.7% rise from ₹287.5 crore in the same period last year. Net sales increased by 8.7% to ₹2,092.1 crore. Dabur’s oral care, hair oil, home care and over-the-counter & ethical drug business segments grew 19%, 14%, 12% and 11% respectively.
