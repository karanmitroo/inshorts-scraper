Speaking at the launch of Larsen & Toubro Chairman AM Naik's biography, Reliance Industries Chairman Mukesh Ambani said Naik is the first and "original Make-in-India man". He said in his 35 years of association with Naik, their basic parameter was to make in India but without compromising on quality. Naik's biography titled 'The Nationalist' details his 53-year journey at L&T.
