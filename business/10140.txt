Investors pumped in ₹1.02 lakh crore into mutual fund schemes in July, as per data from the Association of Mutual Funds in India. In comparison, June had witnessed a ₹21,535 crore outflow. The net inflow in mutual funds in the April-July period stood at ₹1.93 lakh crore, as against an inflow of ₹2.05 lakh crore in the year-ago period.
