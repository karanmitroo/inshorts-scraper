State-run oil firms on Saturday raised the price of non-subsidised Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) by ₹1 per cylinder, based on global oil prices. A 14.2 kg cylinder will now cost ₹585 in Delhi. The rise comes on the back of a ₹54.5 hike on December 1. Further, a subsidised LPG cylinder will also become expensive by ₹2 to ₹434.71.
