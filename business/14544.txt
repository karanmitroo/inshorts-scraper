Ola founders Ankit Bhati, 28, and Bhavish Aggarwal, 29, are youngest in the list of super-rich Indians, according to Hurun India Rich list for 2015. The debutant duo, with estimated fortunes of Rs 2,385 crore each, was ranked at 238 amongst the 296 in the list, which also includes tech entrepreneurs Flipkart's Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal (jointly at 85th).
