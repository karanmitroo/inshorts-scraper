Fortune's global list of top-50 women business leaders features four Indians led by ICICI Bank CEO Chanda Kochhar at fourth position, followed by NSE chief Chitra Ramkrishna(17), Axis Bank's Shikha Sharma(32) and HSBC's NL Kidwai(42). This list is topped by Brazil's Petrobras CEO MDG Foster. Also, PepsiCo's Indian-origin CEO Indra Nooyi is ranked second in Fortune's similar list for US.
