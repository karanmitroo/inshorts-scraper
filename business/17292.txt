India's second-largest IT services exporter, Infosys Ltd, Thursday appointed former SAP AG executive board member Vishal Sikka as its first non-founder CEO, the company said. Sikka will take over from current CEO SD Shibulal, one of the engineers who founded Infosys, on Aug-1. Moreover, Executive Chairman NR Narayana Murthy and Executive Vice Chairman S Gopalakrishnan would step down on Jun-14.
