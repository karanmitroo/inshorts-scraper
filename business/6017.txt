IT major Infosys has said that its board has approved the pay hike of its Chief Operating Officer UB Pravin Rao. His fixed salary per annum has been raised to ₹4.62 crore while the variable component of his package will stand at ₹3.87 crore, payable on achievement of targets. Earlier, Infosys Co-founders raised concerns over CEO Vishal Sikka's pay hike.
