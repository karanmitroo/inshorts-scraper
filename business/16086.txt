Bangalore-based Axiom Research Labs Pvt Ltd, the company behind aviation technology venture Team Indus has won $1 million from Google Lunar XPRIZE, a global competition to develop low-cost methods of robotic space exploration. The company was founded in February 2011 by an IIT-ians Rahul Narayan and Indranil Chakraborty. They were selected to compete for both Landing and Imaging sub-categories.
