The combined market capitalisation (m-cap) of eight of the top ten companies listed on the BSE increased by ₹21,296 crore in the week ended March 11, with the Sensex gaining 71.51 points to close at 24,717.99. HDFC and FMCG major ITC emerged as the biggest gainers, adding ₹4,777.47 crore and ₹4,100.76 crore to their m-cap respectively.
