The Enforcement Directorate (ED) on Sunday "provisionally" attached assets worth ₹12.52 crore of four of the accused in the ongoing Bank of Baroda (BoB) money-laundering probe. The probe pertains to alleged illegal forex transactions conducted through the bank, worth around ₹6,000 crore, from fake companies' accounts to Hong Kong. The four accused were alleged middlemen for a few fake companies.
