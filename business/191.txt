India's wilful defaulters owe over ₹1.1 trillion to banks and the major defaulters include Jatin Mehta-promoted Winsome Diamonds and Jewellery and Forever Precious Jewellery, which owe around ₹5,500 crore. Vijay Mallya's Kingfisher Airlines has to pay back over ₹3,000 crore to banks. Kolkata-based FMCG firm REI Agro, which is owned by Sandip Jhunjhunwala, owes about ₹2,730 crore to banks.
