Nestlé India has changed the packaging of Maggi noodles, Nescafé, and KitKat to support girl child education in association with the NGO 'Nanhi Kali'. Maggi’s tagline has changed from ‘2 minute noodles’ to ‘2 minutes for education’, KitKat changed to ‘No break from education’, while Nescafé switched from ‘It all starts with a NESCAFÉ’ to ‘It all starts with education’.
