Reliance Infrastructure (RInfra) on Monday agreed to sell 49% equity stake in its Mumbai's electricity generation and distribution business to PSP Investments – a Canadian pension fund – for an enterprise value of ₹15,000 crore. The deal, which is reportedly expected to close in March 2016 would bring down the debt level of RInfra from ₹16,000 crore to ₹5,000 crore.
