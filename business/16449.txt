Four Infosys founders- NR Narayana Murthy, SD Shibulal, Nandan Nilekani and K Dinesh- Monday sold 32.6 million shares in the company in a bulk deal, thus, raising $1.1 billion. The move pulled down the share price by 3.3% to Rs 2002 in early trade on the BSE. The founders currently hold 15.92% in the company.
