The price of non-subsidised Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) was on Wednesday hiked by ₹21 per cylinder. A 14.2 kg domestic cylinder will now cost ₹548.50 in Delhi. This is the second straight price hike after an increase of ₹18 per cylinder in May. Meanwhile, the price of Aviation Turbine Fuel (ATF), or jet fuel, was hiked by 9.2%.
