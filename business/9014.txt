India's mergers and acquisitions (M&A) activity has grown by 36.2% from the whole of 2015, to reach its highest value of ₹3.07 lakh crore ($46 billion) from 278 deals in 2016, according to  a global financial report. The report said 98 deals worth ₹1.84 lakh crore were finalised during Q3 of 2016, amounting to the highest quarterly M&A activity ever.
