HDFC Bank on Friday reported a 20% year-on-year jump in net profit to ₹3,374 crore in the quarter ended March. Total income increased by 21% to ₹18,862 crore. Further, net interest income, which is the difference between the interest earned on loans and that paid on deposits, rose by 23.9% to ₹7,453 crore, while gross non-performing assets stood at 0.94%.
