Commenting on the Union Budget 2018, Yoga guru Ramdev said it would've been better to increase the non-taxable income limit to ₹5 lakh per annum. The non-taxable income limit remains at ₹2.5 lakh per annum as Finance Minister Arun Jaitley announced that personal income tax laws will not be changed this year. "This was a nation-building budget', Ramdev added.
