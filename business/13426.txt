India Inc has garnered ₹6.3 lakh crore through capital markets in 2015, with bonds emerging as the preferred route for investors. The capital raised from debt securities this year was around ₹5 lakh crore while equity markets raked in around ₹1.3 lakh crore. Around ₹5.75 lakh crore was gathered from domestic markets while ₹58,900 crore was raised from overseas.
