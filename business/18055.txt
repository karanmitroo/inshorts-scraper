Telecom regulator TRAI has relaxed penalty norms under which there will be no penalty on telecom operators up to 50 complaints of pesky calls and SMSes, received against them in a week. The new rule says that for complaints in the range of 50-300, 301-700 and beyond 700, a fine of Rs 1,000, 2,000 and 5,000 will be levied respectively.
