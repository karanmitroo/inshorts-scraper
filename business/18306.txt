With the continued depreciation in rupee, losses on diesel sale have widened to Rs 14.50 per litre this week from Rs 12.12 loss in September, while oil firms are losing Rs 36.83/litre on kerosene sold through ration shops and Rs 470.38 per LPG cylinder. IOC, HPCL and BPCL are losing Rs 486 crore per day to keep inflation under check.
