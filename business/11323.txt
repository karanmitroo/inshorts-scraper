Bajaj Auto on Wednesday reported a 29.1% year-on-year growth in profit after tax to ₹803.06 crore for the quarter ended March, on the back of strong domestic sales of three wheelers and motorcycles. Net sales for the quarter grew by 14.7% ₹5,303.89 crore. Sales volumes, including exports, rose by 11.5% on a 26% jump in domestic volumes.
