India's largest e-tailer - Flipkart - has raised $210 million (Rs. 1,228 crore) in a fresh round of funding from a group of four investors, led by internet-focused investment group DST Global. Overall, Flipkart has raised between $540-$560 million, according to industry estimates which valued the company at about $1.6 billion (Rs. 9,360 crore) at the end of 2013.
