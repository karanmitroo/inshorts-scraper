Cash worth nearly ₹73 lakh in old demonetised ₹1,000 and ₹500 currency notes was seized from two cars in the Nashik district of Maharashtra, police said on Thursday. One of these cars was on its way from Nashik to Kopargaon in Maharashtra, while the other carrying ₹40 lakh in cash was going from Gujarat to Maharashtra's Vaijapur area.
