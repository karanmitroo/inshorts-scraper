The BSE Sensex on Wednesday lost 19.17 points (0.07%) to close at 27,287.66 while the NSE Nifty fell 9.95 points (0.12%) to settle at 8,251.70 points. Bajaj Auto, Bharti Airtel, Tata Steel and NTPC emerged as the top gainers while Dr Reddy's Labs, SBI, Sun Pharma, ICICI Bank and GAIL were the major losers.
