IDBI Bank on Thursday posted a 78.35% year-on-year rise in its net profit at ₹241.1 crore in the quarter ended June. The bank's gross non-performing assets jumped 93.3% to ₹27,275.15 crore against the previous year period. Further, net interest income, the core income a bank earns by giving loans, rose by 14.2% to ₹1,706.5 crore.
