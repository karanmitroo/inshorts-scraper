Domestic air traffic witnessed a 25% year-on-year growth in November, according to data by Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA). Eleven Indian carriers flew 73.2 lakh passengers, compared to 58.2 lakh in the year-ago period. IndiGo continued to lead the domestic carrier segment with a 35.5% market share. Further, domestic air traffic has grown by 20.4% in the January-November period.
