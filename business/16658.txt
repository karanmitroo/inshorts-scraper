The chairman and managing director of the Panchshil chain of luxury hotels, Ajay Chordia, was, Monday, found dead in a room of a hotel he owned, in the township of Pimpri-Chinchwad, Pune. The motive of the death, which apparently was a suicide, has not be ascertained yet. Investigations for the same are on, said B. Mudiraj, inspector, Pimpri police station.
