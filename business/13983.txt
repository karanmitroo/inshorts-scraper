India's largest private sector bank, ICICI reported a 12% increase in its net profit to ₹3,418.53 crore year-on-year for the quarter ended September. Its net revenue in the September quarter rose 13.5% to ₹25,137.61 crore year-on-year. The net interest income, which is the interest earned minus the interest paid, was up 13% to ₹5,251 crore from a year ago.
