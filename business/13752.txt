Vodafone announced plans to invest ₹13,000 crore in India for capacity augmentation and new business initiatives on Thursday. It said ₹8,000 crore will be used for expansion and capacity building and ₹4,000 crore is earmarked for the 'Make in India' initiative. Vodafone, slated to receive in-principle approval for setting up a payments bank, will allocate ₹1,000 crore towards financial inclusion.
