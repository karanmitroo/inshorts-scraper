RBL Bank made a debut on the stock markets on Wednesday with the stocks listing 22% higher at ₹274.20 on NSE against the issue price of ₹225. RBL shares closed 33% higher on the debut at Rs 299.40 on NSE. Established in 1943 as a regional Maharashtra bank, RBL's IPO was subscribed over 69 times on August 23 this year.
