With continued heavy foreign buying for the 17th consecutive session, the benchmark indices Friday hit record highs backed by improving macroeconomic data. Sensex touched a lifetime intra-day high of 21,960.89 before settling at 21,919.79, a rise of 405.92 points. Nifty also rose by 125.50 to end at a new closing high of 6,526.65, after hitting a lifetime high of 6,537.80.
