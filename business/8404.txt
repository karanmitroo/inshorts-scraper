Gold prices increased by ₹160 to ₹30,750 per 10 grams at the bullion market on Saturday, due to increased buying by jewellers to meet the festive demand. Silver also recaptured the ₹43,000 per kg-mark by gaining ₹300 on increased offtake by coin makers and industrial units. The yellow metal also rose 0.52% to $1,274.70 an ounce in New York.
