Indian shares recorded highs for a fifth consecutive session on Tuesday, as the Sensex closed up 292.20 points (1 percent) at 29571.04. This comes after a deal that could open the door for US companies to build nuclear reactors in the country. Further, the 50-share Nifty was up 74.90 points (0.8 percent) to end at an all-time high of 8910.50.
