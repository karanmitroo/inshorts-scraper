Moving a step forward in encouraging companies' participation in the 'Make in India' programme, PM Narendra Modi will launch the Pt. Deendayal Upadhyay Shramev Jayate Karyakram, Thursday. Various initiatives to protect workforce interests will be launched during the event. These include the Shram Suvidha Portal, Labour Inspection Scheme and Universal Account Number for Employees Provident Fund.
