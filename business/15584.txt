Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) inflows in the service sector grew about 47% to $2.64 bn in Apr'14-Jan'15 from $1.8 bn during the same period of 2013-14. The service sector which includes banking, insurance, outsourcing, R&D, courier and technology testing, contributes over 60% to India's GDP. Overall foreign inflows in the country rose by 36% to $25.52 bn during Apr'14-Jan'15.
