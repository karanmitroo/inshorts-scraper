Mukesh Ambani-led Reliance Jio has clarified that they haven't launched any app called JioCoin. The company said it would take legal action against people who solicit to make investments under Jio's name and misguide the public. Notably, following reports that Jio is planning to create its own cryptocurrency 'JioCoin', over 30 fake JioCoin apps appeared on the Google Play Store.
