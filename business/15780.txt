The government on Saturday raised Rs 12,591 crore with the sale of three coal mines to Hindalco, Jindal Power and Indrajit Power. Hindalco Industries won Dumri mine in Jharkhand, Jindal Power grabbed Tara block in Chhattisgarh and Indrajit Power clinched Nerad Malegaon block in Maharashtra. Three more coal blocks will be put up for auction on Sunday.
