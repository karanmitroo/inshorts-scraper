The government's total GST collection for the month of October has dropped by over ₹11,000 crore to ₹83,346 crore till November 27. The revenue collected for September was ₹95,131 crore. Finance Ministry in a statement also said that 95.9 lakh taxpayers have been registered under GST so far and 50.1 lakh returns have been filed for the month of October.
