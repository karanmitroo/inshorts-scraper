The government’s indirect tax revenue rose 35.9% year-on-year to ₹3.82 lakh crore in the first seven months of this fiscal year, according to a Finance Ministry press release. Collections from central excise jumped 68.6% in the April-October period to ₹1.47 lakh crore, service tax collections grew 26.1% to ₹1.12 lakh crore and customs increased by 16.8% to ₹1.22 lakh crore.
