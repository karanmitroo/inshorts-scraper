The Nifty FMCG index, which tracks the performance of 15 NSE listed companies manufacturing FMCG (Fast Moving Consumer Goods) or non-durable, mass consumption, off-the-shelf products, gained 321.40 points to close at 20,134.85 on Monday. ITC, Jubilant Foods and Britannia emerged as the top gainers while Hindustan Unilever and Godrej Consumer Products ended among the top losers.
