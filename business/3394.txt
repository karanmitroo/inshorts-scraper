Jewellery and watch maker Titan Company saw its net profit rise 96.84% year-on-year to ₹237.97 crore in the June quarter. The company's revenue also rose 42% to ₹4,049 crore. This was primarily driven by a 56% increase in revenue of the jewellery segment. Titan added 31 stores across all its segments, taking its total store count to 1,397.
