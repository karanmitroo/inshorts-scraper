After Tata Group appointed Ratan Tata as its Interim Chairman, replacing Cyrus Mistry, former Infosys board member Mohandas Pai said bringing back Tata is not a positive step and Mistry's resignation was worrisome. “I really don’t know why Cyrus Mistry resigned,” Pai said. “It is worrisome for many investors who are following Tata Sons,” he added.
