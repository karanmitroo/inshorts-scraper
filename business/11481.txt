The Supreme Court on Wednesday allowed the Income Tax Department to reopen Amitabh Bachchan's 2001 tax case over his earnings from 'Kaun Banega Crorepati'. Bachchan was previously given relief by the Bombay HC in the case. He paid ₹1.6 crore tax in 2001 over ₹3.23 crore income, however, an assessment revealed his earnings were ₹26 crore, according to IT department.
