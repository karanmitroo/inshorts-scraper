Forbes' annual "Global 2000" has listed 56 Indian companies among the world's 2000 largest and most powerful public companies. Mukesh Ambani's Reliance, at 142nd rank, leads the pack which includes State Bank of India (152nd) and ONGC (183rd). The list is topped by the Chinese bank ICBC, placing China second with 232 companies behind the US with 579 companies.
