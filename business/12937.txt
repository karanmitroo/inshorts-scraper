Telecom Secretary Rakesh Garg on Friday said that the government is likely to hold the next round of spectrum auction in May-June this year. The last spectrum auction conducted across 800, 900, 1800 and 2100 MHz frequencies in March 2015 collected around ₹1.1 lakh crore. Further, the government pegged a revenue of ₹42,865 crore from telecommunications for the current fiscal.
