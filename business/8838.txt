IT major Infosys on Friday said it has revised the pay packages of its top leadership, including Chief Operating Officer Pravin Rao and Chief Financial Officer MD Ranganath. Rao's pay includes fixed compensation of ₹4.62 crore per annum and a variable compensation of up to ₹3.88 crore per annum, Infosys said. This will be effective from November 1, it added.
