Three of the top 10 companies in the Indian equity market- TCS, Infosys and Sun Pharma took a collective hit of ₹51,472.27 crore from their market cap in the week that ended October 16. TCS saw a slump of ₹30,324.88 crore in m-cap, followed by Infosys which fell by ₹17,020.36 crore and Sun Pharma which lost ₹4,127.03 crore.
