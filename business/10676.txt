The benchmark Sensex and broader Nifty ended on fresh 11-month highs on Tuesday to close 181.45 and 50.80 points up at 27,808.14 and 8,518.45 respectively on account of firm global cues, ahead of CPI and factory output data. Tata Steel and ICICI Bank were the top gainers while Cipla and Coal India emerged as the major losers.
