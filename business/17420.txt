With the polls having ended, diesel prices were on Monday hiked by Rs. 1.09/litre, excluding state levies, effective from Monday midnight. Diesel price in Delhi will be hiked by Rs. 1.22/litre including taxes to Rs. 56.71/litre, while it will cost Rs. 65.21/litre in Mumbai as against 63.86 at present. State-owned oil companies will lose Rs. 5.71/litre even after the hike.
