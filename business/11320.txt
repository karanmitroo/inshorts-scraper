Yoga guru Baba Ramdev is reportedly going to launch an online portal called 'Chikitsalaya' that will provide Ayurvedic consultation and treatment. Ramdev has also set an Ayurveda hospital and a medical college at his Patanjali Yogpeeth campus in Haridwar, Uttarakhand. Ramdev also runs retail portal Patanjali Ayurveda for Ayurvedic products.
