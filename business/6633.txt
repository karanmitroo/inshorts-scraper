Infosys has invested over $62 million in more than 12 startups from its $500 million Innovation fund operating in spaces such as IoT, automation, and drones. The funded startups include  Stellaris Venture Partners, Unsilo, Trifacta, Cloudyn, and TidalScale. Set up with an initial corpus of $100 million in 2013, the fund was expanded to $500 million in 2015.
