India's top business tycoons Monday had to wait in a queue to meet US President Barack Obama at the US-Indo CEO Forum. Reliance Industries' chairman Mukesh Ambani, who is also India's richest person, was seen standing in a line along with Ratan Tata, the Tata Groups' CEO. Anil Ambani, Gautam Adani and Anand Mahindra were also among the queue members.
