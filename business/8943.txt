Tata Motors on Monday reported a 5.35% increase year-on-year in its global sales at 1.02 lakh units for September. The sales of Tata-owned luxury brand Jaguar Land Rover were up by 3.6% from the year-ago period to 52,914 units during the month. Further, the automaker's commercial vehicles and passenger vehicles sales grew 1.85% and 7.2% respectively.
