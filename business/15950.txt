Announcing the cheapest offer of the year so far, budget-carrier SpiceJet is offering tickets for as low as Rs 599 (all-inclusive) for domestic flights and Rs 3,499 for international flights. The offer is applicable for international flights, except Colombo, Kabul and Dubai-Ahmedabad-Dubai sectors. The offer is available for booking during Feb 11-13  and for travel from July 1- Oct 24.
