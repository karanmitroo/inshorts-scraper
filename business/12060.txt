The benchmark Sensex on Wednesday regained the 25,000-mark after it rose 438.12 points to close at 25,338.58 while the broader Nifty reclaimed its 7,700-mark as it surged 138.20 points to settle at 7,735.20 amid firm global trends. Market sentiment remained upbeat after US Federal Reserve Chair Janet Yellen signalled that a rate hike was not likely to come before June.
