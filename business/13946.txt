Equity Markets were bearish in the week ended October 30, as the benchmark indices lost around 3%. BSE Sensex lost 814 points, thereby registering its worst weekly performance in two months, to settle at 26,656.83 on Friday. NSE Nifty fell 230 points to close at 8,065.80. Among sectors, BSE Capital goods index was the major loser with 4.40% dip.
