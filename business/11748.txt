Mumbai-born property magnates David and Simon Reuben have topped the annual Britain rich list, 'The Sunday Times Rich List 2016', with a fortune of £13.1 billion (approximately $18.9 billion). The Hinduja brothers, at £13 billion, occupy the second spot. Notably, Lakshmi Mittal, whose wealth dropped from £27.7 billion in 2008 to £7.12 billion this year, is 11th on the list.
