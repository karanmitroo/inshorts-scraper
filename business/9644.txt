Mukesh Ambani-led Reliance Industries has been ranked eighth, up from 14 last year, on the 'Top 250 Global Energy Company Rankings' list for 2016 released by S&P Global Platts on Thursday. Other Indian energy firms, including Coal India, ONGC, and Adani Power, have also been named in the list. Further, US-based Exxon Mobil has ranked on top of the list.
