Nike CEO Mark Parker was named Businessperson of 2015 according to Fortune's list of top 50 global business leaders. Facebook's Mark Zuckerberg, Electronic Arts CEO Andrew Wilson, Apple's Tim Cook and Mastercard CEO Ajay Banga completed the top five. Alphabet's Larry Page (11th), Microsoft's Satya Nadella (47th) and Alibaba's Chairman Jack Ma (25th) also featured on the list.
