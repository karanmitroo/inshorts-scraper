India's largest lender SBI has the highest amount of wilful defaults amounting to ₹27,716 crore till December, 2017. PNB, which is under the scanner by top probing agencies for $1.77 billion alleged fraud by firms of jeweller Nirav Modi, has wilful defaults of ₹12,574 crore. Bank of India has 314 wilful defaulters who owe ₹6,104 crore to the bank.
