PepsiCo has announced the appointment of Sanjeev Chadha to the position of Chief Executive Officer, Asia, Middle-East and Africa (AMEA), effective immediately. In this role, Chadha,an IIM-A graduate and 23-year veteran of PepsiCo, will report to Chairman and CEO Indra Nooyi and oversee all aspects of PepsiCo's AMEA business. Chadha most recently served as President, PepsiCo Middle East and Africa.
