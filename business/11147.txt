The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty on Friday closed on a flat note with the Sensex losing 0.11 points and the Nifty gaining 1.85 points to settle at 26,843.03 and 8,220.80 respectively. The key indices pared their initial gains on buying by funds and retail investors. Earlier, the Sensex breached the 27,000-mark for the first time since October 2015.
