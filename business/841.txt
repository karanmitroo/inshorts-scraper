Benchmark indices Sensex and Nifty ended at record closing highs for the third straight session on Monday with Nifty closing above 10,700 for the first time. The Sensex ended 251 points higher at 34,844, while Nifty gained 60 points to close at 10,742. The markets were influenced by industrial growth that surged 8.4%, a 17-month high, in November 2017.
