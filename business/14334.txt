The benchmark Sensex on Tuesday surged 147.33 points (0.55%) to settle at 26,932.88, meanwhile, the broader Nifty gained 33.60 points (0.41%) to end the day at 8,152.90.  Tata Motors, ITC, Coal India and Cipla were the major gainers, whereas BHEL, Infosys, Maruti Suzuki and NTPC were the major losers.
