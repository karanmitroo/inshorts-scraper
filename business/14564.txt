Automobile maker Land Rover has unveiled its first luxury armoured vehicle, the Range Rover Sentinel, which is capable of withstanding gunfire and grenades. The Sentinel will be available at a cost of 360,000 pounds sterling (about Rs 3.68 crore). Billed a "mobile fortress", the Sentinel has multi-laminated bulletproof windows and can withstand 15kg TNT blasts and DM51 grenade explosions.
