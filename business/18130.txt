In order to derive more data usage and grow subscriber portfolio, IDEA Cellular, after Vodafone and Airtel, slashed 2G and 3G data-tariffs by upto 90% and 33% respectively in its 22 circles for six months starting Nov-15. The current tariffs which are 2 paise/KB for 2G and 3 paise/10KB for 3G will now be 2 paise/10KB for both(2G and 3G).
