The benchmark Sensex on Thursday dropped 190 points, breaking a six-day gaining streak, to close at 26,845.81. The 50-share Nifty also lost points, decreasing by 48 to finish at 8,129.35. The BSE's midcap and smallcap indices also lost points, reflecting the broader sentiment. Reliance, GAIL, and ITC were the Sensex's top losers. Vedanta and Tata Steel managed to post gains.
