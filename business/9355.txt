The BSE Sensex on Thursday gained 265.71 points to settle at 28,773.13 while the NSE Nifty rose by 90.30 points to close at 8,867.45. Investors remained upbeat after the US Fed decided to keep the interest rates unchanged. SBI and Hero MotoCorp were among the top gainers while TCS and Lupin were among the major losers of the day.
