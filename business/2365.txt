Prime Minister Narendra Modi has constituted an Economic Advisory Council headed by NITI Aayog member Bibek Debroy. The council will analyse and advise on policy matters referred by PM Modi. The 5-member body will comprise Surjit Bhalla, Rathin Roy, and Ashima Goyal as part-time members, and includes Ratan Watal, the Principal Advisor of NITI Aayog as Member-Secretary of the panel.
