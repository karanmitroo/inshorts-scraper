Jet Airways posted the biggest ever quarterly loss of Rs 2,153.6 crore for the quarter ending March 31, 2014, up from the loss of Rs 495.5 crore in the same quarter previous fiscal. Also, the Jet Airways lost Rs 4,129.7 crore in FY 2013-14 (its seventh straight annual loss), as opposed to Rs 779.8 crore lost in the previous fiscal.
