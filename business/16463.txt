Led by losses in software giants, Infosys and TCS,  the Sensex on Monday plunged by over 338 points as investors continued to book profits for the second straight session. The 30-share index closed 338.70 points lower at 28,119.40. The 50-share Nifty index also fell in the session, ending below its crucial psychological support level of 8500 at 8,438.25.
