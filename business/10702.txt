The benchmark indices on Friday ended the trade in losses after the BSE Sensex and the NSE Nifty fell by 74.59 and 14.70 points to close at 27,126.90 and 8,323.20 respectively, on account of weak global cues. Asian Paints and Tata Motors emerged as the major gainers while GAIL and Bharti Airtel emerged as the biggest losers.
