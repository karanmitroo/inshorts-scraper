Top 10 most valued companies listed in the Sensex took a combined hit of ₹94,560.96 crore in their market capitalisation (m-cap), after the markets slipped by 654.71 points (2.49%) to 25,610.53 in the week ended November 13. TCS'  m-cap tumbled by ₹21,369.29 crore, followed by ONGC which lost ₹19,763.19 crore and Sun Pharma, whose valuation plunged by ₹14,907.88 crore.
