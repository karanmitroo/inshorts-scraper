The BSE Sensex on Thursday fell 248.72 points (0.94%) to settle at a one-month low of 26,304.20 points after US Federal Reserve Chairperson Janet Yellen hinted at the possibility of a rate hike in December. Meanwhile, the NSE Nifty lost 84.75 points (1.05%) to close at 7,955.45, below its psychological mark of 8,000 for the first time since October 1.
