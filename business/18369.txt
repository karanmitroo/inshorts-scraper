Oil Minister M Veerappa Moily has said that without corrective steps, losses on sale of subsidised-fuel will rise to an unprecedented Rs.1,80,000-crore. Financial health of Oil Marketing Companies has been deteriorating over the years. During 2012-13 even after getting almost 100% compensation, OMCs reported a combined profit after tax of mere Rs.8,552-crore (0.95% of their combined turnover of Rs. 892,653-crore).
