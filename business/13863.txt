Toyota Motors' net profit surged 13.5% to 611.7 billion yen ($5 billion) in the quarter ended September against 539 billion yen posted in the corresponding period last year. The company's quarterly sales rose 8% to 7.1 trillion yen ($58.5 billion). However, the company cut its global sales forecast for full-year by 150,000 vehicles to 10 million vehicles.
