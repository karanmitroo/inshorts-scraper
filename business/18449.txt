Rohini Nilekani, wife of Infosys founder Nandan Nilekani, has sold 5.77 lakh Infosys shares worth Rs 163.51 crore. The filing in Bombay Stock Exchange  stated that the proceeds of the share sale would be utilised for her initiatives in "multiple sectors" of philanthropy. Following the sale, Ms. Nilekani's stake in the company declined from 1.41 %(80,78,174 shares)  to 1.31%(75,01,174 shares).
