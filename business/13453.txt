Domestic passenger car sales increased by 10.39% to 1.73 lakh units in November from 1.57 lakh units in the corresponding period last year, according to data released by the Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers (SIAM). During the same period, sales of two-wheelers rose by 1.47%, commercial vehicles grew 8.56% and vehicles across all categories increased by 3.18%, SIAM said.
