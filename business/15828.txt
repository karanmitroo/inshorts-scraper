The benchmark BSE Sensex on Saturday saw the first rise on a Budget day in four years, as it surged 141.38 points up, to close at 29,361.50. Also, the 50-share Nifty rose 57.25 points to end at 8,901.85. Among the sectoral indices, the Bankex was the top gainer, up 3.27%, while the FMCG index lost the most, 4.09%.
