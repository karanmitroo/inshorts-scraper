Auto major Tata Motors on Friday reported a consolidated net loss of ₹427.79 crore for the quarter ended September as against a profit ₹3,290.86 crore in the same period last year. The revenue for the reporting period increased 0.31% year-on-year to ₹49,391.43 crore. The loss was attributed to Jaguar Land Rover's weaker China sales and foreign exchange revaluation among others.
