Microsoft on Thursday posted results for the quarter that ended September 2015. Revenue dropped by 12% to $20.37 billion in 2015 September quarter against 2014's $23.20 billion (y-o-y, except per share amounts). Net income surged 2% to $4.62 billion against $4.54 billion in 2014 September quarter. Intelligent cloud revenue grew 8% to $5.9 billion whereas Personal Computing revenue dropped 17%.
