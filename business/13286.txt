Reliance Capital on Monday acquired 6.82% stake in music publishing company Saregama India for ₹43.17 crore through the open market. According to data available with BSE and NSE, Reliance Capital acquired 11,88,000 shares out of which 7,65,000 shares were bought at an average price of ₹364 on BSE and 4,23,000 shares were bought at an average price of ₹362.37.
