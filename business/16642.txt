On Friday, silver slumped to hit a four-year low and closed below the Rs 37,000 mark under the impact of massive unwinding from speculators and savvy traders. Silver (0.999 fineness) declined by Rs 65 to finish at Rs 36,970 per kg. Standard gold (99.5 purity) closed at Rs 26,065 per 10 grams, up Rs 105 from last closing level.
