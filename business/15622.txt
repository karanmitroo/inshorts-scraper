The Sensex surged 302.65 points (1.08 percent) to 28260.14 and Nifty went up 95.25 points (1.12 percent) to 8586.25 on the first day of the new fiscal. Bank stocks led the surge as RBI's policy review is due next week. Sun Pharma, SBI, ICICI Bank, Hindalco Industries, PNB, Yes Bank and Kotak Mahindra Bank surged in the range of 2-5%.
