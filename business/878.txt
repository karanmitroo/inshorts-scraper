The Walt Disney Company CEO Bob Iger received $36.3 million in compensation during fiscal 2017, down by 17% from the fiscal 2016. Iger had received a remuneration of $43.9 million in fiscal 2016. Iger's base salary remained steady at $2.5 million, but his non-equity compensation fell from $20 million in 2016 to $15.2 million in 2017.
