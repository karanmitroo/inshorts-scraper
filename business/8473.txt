The benchmark indices on Thursday ended in green, after the BSE Sensex rose by 79.39 points to close at 27,915.90, while the NSE Nifty remained unchanged at 8,615.25. HDFC, Dr Reddys Labs, and ICICI Bank emerged as the day's biggest gainers while Asian Paints, Hero MotoCorp, and Wipro were among the top losers.
