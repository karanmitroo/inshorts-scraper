Kochi has been named the top real estate destination among 19 tier-2 cities, according to a study by Gurgaon-based online real estate data and analytics platform, PropEquity. The assessment was done keeping in view 10 parameters including price, supply, sales, unsold inventory and new launch trends. Nashik, Vishakapatnam, Vadodara and Trivandrum completed the top 5 in that order.
