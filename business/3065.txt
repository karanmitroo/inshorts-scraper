Vishal Sikka, who resigned as Infosys MD and CEO today and was appointed as the Executive Vice Chairman, will receive an annual salary of $1 (₹64) in his new role. Sikka received an annual salary of ₹48.73 crore in 2015-16, which dropped to ₹16.01 crore in 2016-17. Notably, Co-founder Narayana Murthy had earlier raised concerns over Sikka's salary.
