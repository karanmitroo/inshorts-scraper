Bharat Heavy Electricals (BHEL) on Thursday said that it recorded its highest-ever commissioning of projects (15,059 MW of power generating equipment) and highest order booking (₹43,727 crore) in five years in 2015-16. Announcing its flash results, the company also reported a net loss of ₹877 crore (provisional) in 2015-16 against a net profit of ₹1,419 crore the previous fiscal.
