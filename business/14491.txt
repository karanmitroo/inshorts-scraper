Volkswagen AG lost around €15 billion (approximately Rs 1.1 lakh crore) off its market value on Monday, following the revelations on Friday of emissions data being rigged by the German manufacturer. Volkswagen's share price crashed by 19.3% to a three-year low of €130.2 in Frankfurt by mid-day. The US Environmental Protection Agency had previously ordered to recall 4,82,000 Volkswagen cars.
