US President Donald Trump's company has earned $1.97 million (₹12.6 crore) profit at its Trump International Hotel so far in 2017. The hotel, located near the White House, is charging more for rooms than most others in the city. Notably, guests paid $652.98 (₹42,000) a night on average, and spent $8.2 million (₹52.6 crore) on food and drinks this year.
