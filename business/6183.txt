Industrialist Anil Ambani on Wednesday flew a sortie in the Rafale fighter jet at the Aero India 2017 expo in Bengaluru. Notably, Ambani's Reliance Defence has set up a joint venture with Dassault for the execution of obligations in the India-France deal for 36 Rafale jets. "I look forward to... make these aircraft... and serve our nation," Ambani said.
