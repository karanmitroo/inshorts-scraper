Finance Minister Arun Jaitley announced that Long-Term Capital Gains on equities exceeding ₹1 lakh will be taxed at a rate of 10% without any indexation. However, all gains till January 31, 2018, will be exempted. The total exempted capital gains from listed shares and units is ₹3.67 trillion as per returns filed for the assessment year 2017-2018, the minister added.
