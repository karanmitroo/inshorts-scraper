The BSE Sensex on Monday trimmed early losses to close 143.84 points (0.55%) down at 26,121.40 while the NSE Nifty lost 39.10 points (0.49%) to settle at 7,915.20 points. Earlier, the Sensex and Nifty fell by 579.45 and 171 points respectively during intra-day trading, due to a selloff reportedly triggered by the BJP's defeat in Bihar elections.
