R Venkataramanan, a trustee at Tata Trusts, has filed a ₹500 crore criminal defamation suit against ousted Tata Sons Chairman Cyrus Mistry and his family investment firms. Venkataramanan filed the case in his personal capacity on 7 June, citing allegations leveled by Mistry against him. The allegations pertained to transactions at AirAsia India, Tata Sons’ joint venture with AirAsia.
