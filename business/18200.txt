According to data released by Cellular Operators Association of India, GSM mobile companies have added over 44-lakh new customers in September, taking their cumulative base to 67.88-crore. While Idea topped the list with 12.07-lakh new customers, Vodafone and Airtel added 12.05-lakh and 11.60-lakh new customers respectively. The data doesn't include Reliance and Tata Teleservices customers as they aren't its members.
