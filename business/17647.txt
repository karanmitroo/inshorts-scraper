The barometer index, the BSE Sensex, and the NSE Nifty, both, hit record high on intraday basis as well as on closing high on Tuesday. The Sensex garnered 60.17 points to settle at 22,446, after hitting an all time high of 22,485; while the Nifty garnered 16.85 points to settle at 6,721, after recording the intraday high of 6,732.
