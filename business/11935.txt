The BSE Sensex on Monday reclaimed the 25,000-mark as it rose 348.32 points to close at 25,022.16 while the NSE Nifty rose 116.20 points to settle at 7,671.40 on account of buying in frontline blue chip stocks. Market sentiment remained upbeat amid Chinese shares posting strong gains after China reported an unchanged inflation rate of 2.3% for March.
