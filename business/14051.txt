Gold prices extended its losses for the second straight day as it dropped by around ₹110 to ₹27,090 per 10 grams (99.9% purity) in Delhi on Saturday. Previously, it lost ₹135 on Friday. Silver also lost its value by ₹235 to ₹37,050 per kg, amid weak demands. Gold on Friday fell by 0.3% to $1,162.80 an ounce in New York.
