Amid Infosys Co-founders questioning CEO Vishal Sikka's pay hike, board member and Biocon Chairperson Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw on Friday said the firm's board is firmly behind Sikka. "There have been...questions...about remuneration to Sikka. But these have been deliberated upon," she said. "Whatever issues the founders have raised are not governance issues but differences of opinion," Shaw added.
