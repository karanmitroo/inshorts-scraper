Bajaj Auto on Thursday reported a net profit of ₹901.49 crore for the quarter ended December, registering a marginal increase of 4.7% against the corresponding period last year. Net sales for the quarter stood at ₹5,463.20 crore against ₹5,520.01 crore during the corresponding period last year. The company's other income jumped 109.6% year-on-year to ₹199.68 crore.
