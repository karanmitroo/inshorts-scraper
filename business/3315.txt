Shares of Jaiprakash Associates on Monday gained around 20% during intra-day trade, hitting a nearly three-year high, after the company reported a net profit of ₹764.99 crore in the June quarter. It had reported a loss of ₹602.95 crore in the corresponding quarter of the previous year. Its group companies, Jaypee Infratech, and Jaiprakash Power Ventures also rose 5% each.
