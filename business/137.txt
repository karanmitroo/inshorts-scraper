Mehul Choksi-owned Gitanjali Group has reportedly not repaid working capital loans worth nearly ₹5,280 crore to a consortium of 31 banks. Working capital loans are used to finance the everyday operations of a company. While ICICI Bank, which is the "lead bank" in the consortium, gave ₹405 crore, PNB has given the highest loan amount of ₹587 crore.
