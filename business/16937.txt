NSE Nifty breached the 8,000 mark for the first time ever, while Sensex set a new record high of 26,812.69 in opening TRADE on Monday. Nifty surpassed its previous record of 7,968.25 by shooting up by 64.30 points, or 0.80%. Sensex, on the other hand rose 174.58 points, or 0.65 per cent, breaking the previous record of 26,674.38.
