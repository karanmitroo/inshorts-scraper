IT services company Wipro on Monday inaugurated Saudi Arabia's first all women business and technology park (WBP) at the Princess Nourah Bint Abdulrahman University in Riyadh. The WBP is a joint venture between Wipro and the university, with oil firm Saudi Aramco acting as the strategic advisor. The initiative is expected to create nearly 21,000 jobs by 2025, Wipro said.
