Snapdeal's largest investor SoftBank's CEO Masayoshi Son on Monday said he respects Snapdeal founders Kunal Bahl and Rohit Bansal's decision of calling off merger talks with rival Flipkart. He, however, confirmed that SoftBank is still in talks to invest in Flipkart, but did not specify the details. Reports claim SoftBank is looking at investing up to $2 billion in Flipkart.
