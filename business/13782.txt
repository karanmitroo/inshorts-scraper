The benchmark Sensex on Wednesday gained 123.69 points to close at 25,866.95 during the first session of Mahurat Trading, conducted on the occasion of Diwali on Wednesday. Meanwhile, the broader Nifty also gained 41.65 points to close at 7,825.00. The trading was conducted between 5:30 pm and 6:45 pm to mark the beginning of New Year as per Hindu calendar.
