India's highest paid executive in 2016-17 was Tech Mahindra CEO CP Gurnani, who took home ₹150 crore, according to available data. Reliance Industries executive directors Nikhil and Hital Meswani drew ₹80.76 crore each, higher than Mukesh Ambani who has capped his pay at ₹15 crore. In contrast, ITC's Nakul Anand saw the sharpest pay cut of 82% to ₹2.7 crore.
