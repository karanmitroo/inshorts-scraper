Audi has launched its 2016 A8 L 4.0T Sport model for $90,500 in the US market. The sedan supports a 4.0-litre twin turbo charged eight-cylinder engine with a horse power of 450, a 15 hp increase from its previous non-sport 4.0T model. The car also has driver assistance functions including a pre-sense rear and a head-up display with navigation information.
