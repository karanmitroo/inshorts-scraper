Finance Minister Arun Jaitley on Monday launched a portal to electronically collect over ₹2 lakh crore annually in non-tax receipts. The sources mainly include dividends, interest receipts, spectrum charges, royalty, licence fee, sale of forms and RTI application fees. As per the Budget, the government aims to collect over ₹2.21 lakh crore as non-tax receipts during 2015-16.
