Siemens India on Thursday announced bagging an order worth almost Rs 123 crore from Power Grid Company of Bangladesh at 2 site-locations in Dhaka. The project's scope included design, procurement, supply, installation, testing, commissioning of 230/132kV GIS Substation at Shyampur and 132/33kV GIS Substation at Dhamrai. The company's shares traded 0.78% higher than its previous close on the BSE.
