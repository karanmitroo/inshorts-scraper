The e-verification facility for filing I-T returns has been used by over 75.3 lakh taxpayers, growing more than twice, as compared to 32.95 lakh taxpayers in 2015-16, a Finance Ministry release stated. Further, over 226.98 lakh e-returns were filed in the current assessment year till August 5, an over 9.8% growth against 206.55 lakh filed last year.
