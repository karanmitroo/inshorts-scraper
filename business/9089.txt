Market regulator SEBI on Monday raised the limit for Foreign Portfolio Investment in government securities to ₹1.48 lakh crore from the existing cap of ₹1.44 lakh crore. This will be further increased to ₹1.52 lakh crore from January. SEBI also increased the investment limit for long-term FPIs (like Insurance Funds and Pension Funds) in government securities to ₹62,000 crore.
