The Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) registered a growth of 32% with a turnover of ₹1,506 crore in 2015-16 as against ₹1,141 crore in 2014-15. The net worth of IRCTC during the fiscal was ₹542.07 crore as compared to ₹444.25 crore in the previous year period. The growth was attributed to the robust performance of all its verticals.
