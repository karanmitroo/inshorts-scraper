The BSE Sensex on Friday went up 321.73 points (1.1 percent) to 27828.44 and Nifty increased by 114.65 points (1.4 percent) to 8433.65. Mahindra & Mahindra, Bharti Airtel, Coal India, Maruti and ITC were the top gainers while Hindalco, GAIL and Dr Reddy's Labs were the major losers. The surge was led by pharma, infra, auto and bank sectors.
