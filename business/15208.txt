The BSE Sensex declined by 245.40 points on Monday to end at 26,523.09 (lowest since October 20, 2014) and the Nifty went down by 70.55 points to close at 8,044.15, its lowest level in 2015. Tata Power, Bajaj Auto, Axis Bank, M&M and TCS were top gainers, while Vedanta, Tata Steel, Sun Pharma, Reliance and Hero MotoCorp were top losers.
