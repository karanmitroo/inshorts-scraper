Nine of the top 10 blue-chip firms listed in the Sensex took a combined hit of ₹47,139.45 crore in their market capitalisation (m-cap) after the markets slipped by 490.09 points (1.87%) to ₹25,638.11 in the week ended December 4. HDFC' m-cap tumbled by ₹9,176.8  crore, followed by SBI which lost ₹6,675.99 crore and ITC, whose valuation plunged by ₹6,583.12 crore.
