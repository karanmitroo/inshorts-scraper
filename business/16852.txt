A sharp 152% jump in his wealth saw Adani Group chairman Gautam Adani break into the list of 10 richest Indians, said the Hurun Report tracking the uber rich. Adani piped Reliance Group Chairman Anil Ambani for the 10th spot with a net worth of Rs.44k crore. Mukesh Ambani retained the top position with a fortune of Rs.1.65 lakh crore.
