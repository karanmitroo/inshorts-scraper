InterGlobe Aviation, which runs IndiGo, India's largest airline by passenger volume, has posted a 53% year-on-year growth in net profit for the 2015-16 fiscal to ₹1,989 crore. For the quarter ended March, net profit rose marginally by 0.3% to ₹579 crore. Further, the carrier ferried 85 lakh people during the quarter and had a market share of 37%.
