According to media reports, the Employee Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) is "likely" to announce the interest rates on Provident Fund (PF) investments for 2015-16 at its trustees meeting on November 24. EPFO offered an interest rate of 8.75% on PF deposits for 2013-14 and 2014-15. Finance Ministry earlier said that it would also review interest rates on small savings scheme.
