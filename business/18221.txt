Telecom operator Aircel on Friday launched two new internet tariff plans, providing 2G/3G services in the same pack. The first tariff is an Rs.28 pack providing unlimited 2G/3G data with 7 days validity for customers using mobile-Internet for social-networking, browsing, etc; and the second is Rs.129 pack offering 1.5GB data with 30-days validity for customers with high data usage.
