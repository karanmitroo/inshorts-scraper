Huawei has tied-up with online retailer Flipkart to sell its ‘Honor’ range of devices, announced the company on Monday. The flagship device, Honor 6, is a 4G phone which comes with 3GB RAM and 13 megapixel rear camera, and will be available for Rs. 19,999. A total of 2 smartphones and 2 tablets will be available for sale from Oct-6.
