Petrol price in Delhi was on Tuesday hiked by 96 paise per litre and diesel by 53 paise after the state government on Monday raised VAT on the two fuels. VAT on petrol was raised to 27% from 25%, and on diesel was increased to 18% from 16.6%. Petrol now costs ₹59.99 per litre and diesel costs ₹44.71 per litre.
