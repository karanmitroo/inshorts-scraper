Indian government-owned United Bank of India on Tuesday reported a net profit of ₹38.32 crore for the first quarter ended June, a 26.6% fall from the year-ago period. Gross non-performing assets rose by 14.29% over the previous quarter, up from 9.57% in the year-ago period. Total income grew marginally by 2.4% to ₹2,819.37 crore from the previous quarter.
