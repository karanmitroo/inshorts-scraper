A year after it commenced operations in the country, budget carrier AirAsia will start operations from the Indira Gandhi International Airport in New Delhi from Thursday. As of now the airline, AirAsia flies to Chennai, Kochi, Goa, Chandigarh, Jaipur, Pune from its Bengaluru base. The airline is set to launch flights to Bengaluru, Goa and Guwahati from New Delhi.
