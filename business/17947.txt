The BSE Sensex closed at 21,170.68 on Tuesday, sending the index to an 8.9% annual gain, compared with the 25.7% gain last year. However, in dollar terms, the index rose only 3.2% for the year due to an 11% plummet in the rupee. The Nifty held 0.21% on the day to end at 6,304, advancing 6.8% this year.
