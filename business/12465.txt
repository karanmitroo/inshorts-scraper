The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty dropped by over 2% in the week ended February 26. According to analysts, overall market sentiment remained cautious on account of F&O expiry, weak rupee, Railway Budget and Economic Survey. While the benchmark Sensex lost 554.85 points to close at 23,154.30, the broader Nifty fell by 181.00 points to settle at 7029.75.
