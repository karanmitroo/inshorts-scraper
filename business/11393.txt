Telecommunications firm Vodafone India on Tuesday reported a 5% year-on-year rise in total revenue for the financial year 2015-16 to ₹44,490 crore. Further, revenue from internet data rose by 45% to ₹8,263 crore as data usage went up by 63%. Meanwhile, the subscriber base of the company stood at 19.8 crore at the end of 2015-16, it added.
