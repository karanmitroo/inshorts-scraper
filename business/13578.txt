Eight of the top 10 blue-chip Sensex firms gained ₹31,922 crore in their market capitalisation (total market value of the outstanding shares) in the week ended November 27, after the benchmark Sensex rose 259.71 points to close at 26,128.20. RIL’s m-cap gained ₹10,721.28 crore, followed by SBI which surged ₹5,084.62 crore and Sun Pharma, which climbed ₹5,084.62 crore.
