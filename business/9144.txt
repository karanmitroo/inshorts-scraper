Oil companies on Friday hiked the price of petrol by ₹0.28/litre and cut the price of diesel by ₹0.06/litre, effective midnight. Petrol and diesel in Delhi will now cost ₹64.33/litre and ₹52.57/litre respectively. "The current level of international product prices of Petrol & Diesel and INR-USD exchange rate" warrants change in the price of petrol and diesel, state-owned IOCL said.
