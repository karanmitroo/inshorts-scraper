With a sale of 2.64 lakh units in 2014-15, Maruti Alto has retained its position of top selling car in India for the tenth consecutive year in a row. Interestingly, Maruti’s models Alto, Swift, Dzire and WagonR retained the top four positions. Hyundai Motor India’s Grand i10 was on the fifth spot followed by Eon, Elite i20 and Honda City.
