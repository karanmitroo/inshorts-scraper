Wipro's chairman Azim Premji, the first Indian to sign the Giving Pledge, has allocated an additional 18% stake in the company to fund philanthropy. With this, Premji has donated total 39% stake (approximately Rs 53,284 crore) which is more than half of his wealth. Premji owns 73.39% stake in the $15.7 billion (approximately Rs 99,500 crore) worth company.
