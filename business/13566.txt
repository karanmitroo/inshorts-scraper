Chennai-based Indian motorcycle manufacturing company Royal Enfield on Tuesday reported a 48% jump in total sales in November at 40,769 units, as against 27,542 units in the corresponding period last year. Domestic sales increased 48.1% to 40,312 units, compared to 27,198 in the year-ago period, while exports grew 32.8% to 457 units from 344 in November 2014.
