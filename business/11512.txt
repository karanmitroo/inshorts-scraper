As per data given by the government in the parliament, India's per capita electricity consumption in 2015-16 is approximately 1,075 Kilo Watt Hours (kWh). This is the lowest among BRICS nations. The world average per capita electricity consumption in 2013 was 3,026 kWh while India's per capita in 2013-14 was just 957, less than 1/3rd of the world average.
