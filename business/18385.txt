Rupee on Thursday soared by 225-paise (3.27%) -the biggest single-day rise in 15 years -to end at 66.55 against the dollar and Sensex jumped by over 400 points on the back of RBI allowing three state-owned oil companies to buy dollars directly. Gold prices in the national capital fell from record high, plunging Rs 1,575 to Rs 32,325 per ten-gram.
