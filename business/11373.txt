ITC on Friday reported a 5.67% year-on-year rise in net profit to ₹2,495 crore for the quarter ended March. Revenue grew by 5% to ₹9,770 crore, while net sales jumped 9.51% to ₹10,062 crore. Further, the company announced a dividend of ₹8.50 per share, in addition to a bonus issue of one share for two existing shares.
