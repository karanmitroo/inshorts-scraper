The Bombay Stock Exchange's benchmark Sensex on Friday lost about 242 points, finishing at 27,366 points to end the week on a negative note. The Nifty lost about 73 points to close at 8,300 points. Hindalco, Infosys, and Hindustan Unilever were the Sensex's top gainers and Vedanta, Bajaj Auto, and Hero Motocorp ended the day as the top losers.
