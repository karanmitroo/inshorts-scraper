Tata sons Chairman Emeritus, Ratan Tata, has invested an undisclosed amount and equity in a women's fashion portal Kaaryah. This is his latest personal investment in technology ventures besides Snapdeal, Urban Ladder, Bluestone among others. Kaaryah founder Nidhi Agarwal said, "This validation from Tata is an endorsement, of the highest order, of our vision of creating a world-class brand".
