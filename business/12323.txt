Officials from the Finance Ministry and the RBI will hold a meeting on March 18 to decide the government’s market borrowing calendar for April-September 2016. In Union Budget 2016-17, the government set its gross borrowing at ₹6 lakh crore, out of which ₹10,000 crore will be raised through gold bonds. The net borrowing has been targeted at ₹4.25 lakh crore.
