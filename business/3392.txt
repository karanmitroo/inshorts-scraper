Telecom Minister Manoj Sinha has said that there is no proposal to merge loss making public sector telecom operator MTNL with BSNL. MTNL's net loss for 2016-17 widened to ₹2,963 crore compared to ₹1,946 crore the year before. He also said that Department of Telecommunications has initiated formulating the "revival/ restructuring/ closure road map" for MTNL.
