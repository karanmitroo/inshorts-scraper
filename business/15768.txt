As per the Knight Frank Wealth Report 2015, India's billionaire count is expected to double in 10 years. India will have 136 billionaires by 2024, as against 68 presently. Wealth creation in India is expected to accelerate, Shishir Baijal, chairman at Knight Frank India, said. Interestingly, India was ranked fourth in billionaire count by the Forbes rich list 2015.
