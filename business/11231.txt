State-owned Coal India on Sunday reported a marginal 0.2% year-on-year rise in its net profit for the quarter ended March to ₹4,248 crore. For the entire fiscal 2015-16, the profit increased 3.99% to ₹14,274 crore while income from operations rose 5.04% to ₹75,644 crore. Meanwhile, coal production during the fiscal grew 9% to 538.75 million tonnes.
