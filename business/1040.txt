Salil Parekh taking over as Infosys CEO is hugely positive, ex-CFO Mohandas Pai has said. Being Bengaluru-based, Parekh would make a huge difference as most clients visit the campuses before they sign deals, he added. Taking a dig at ex-CEO Vishal Sikka who mostly operated out of US, Pai said it's important for the CEO to be at the headquarters.
