The fiscal deficit target of 3.9% of GDP for 2015-16 has been met after reducing the Plan expenditure from the revised FY16 estimate by ₹7,197 crore, the Finance Ministry said on Friday. Further, the estimated tax revenue of ₹9.47 lakh crore is "expected to be fully met" while the revised disinvestment target of ₹25,000 crore has been achieved, it added.
