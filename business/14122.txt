The consolidated net profit of India's third largest IT Services company, Wipro rose 7.2% to ₹2,235.40 crore year-on-year basis for the quarter ended September, from ₹2,084.80 a year ago. It also posted a 2.18% rise in profit quarter-on-quarter basis. The growth was majorly powered by better IT spending by global clients along with an increase in high margin digital projects.
