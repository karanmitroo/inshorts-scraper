Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPIs) have invested ₹1,795 crore in the equity markets so far in May (till May 20). However, they have pulled out ₹3,496 crore from the debt markets during the period, leading to a net outflow of ₹1,701 crore. The declaration of the Assembly election results, with BJP claiming victory in Assam, has boosted investor sentiment, analysts said.
