The benchmark Sensex on Sunday lost 11.30 points to close at 27,930.21 during the 60-minute 'Muhurat' trading session, conducted on the occasion of Diwali. Meanwhile, the broader Nifty also lost 12.30 points to close at 8,625.70. The trading was conducted between 6:30 pm and 7:30 pm to mark the beginning of New Year as per the Hindu calendar.
