India's largest port developer Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone on Tuesday reported a 38% year-on-year rise in consolidated profit for the quarter ended March to ₹914 crore. Revenue for the quarter rose 18% to ₹2,162 crore. For the entire fiscal 2015-16, the company reported a net profit ₹2,867 crore, up 24% from 2014-15.
