The CBI in Chennai on Tuesday arrested registrar of companies, Tamil Nadu, Manuneethi Cholan on bribery charges and questioned chairman of Chettinad Group MAM Ramaswamy in this connection. Inquiries indicate that, Manuneethi Cholan had allegedly accepted Rs 10 lakh as bribe from Ramaswamy, to declare the annual general body meeting of the conglomerate as null and void.
