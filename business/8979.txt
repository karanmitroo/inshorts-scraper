The market capitalisation (m-cap) of six of the top ten BSE-listed companies rose by ₹30,968 crore in the week ended October 7, with the benchmark Sensex gaining 195.18 points to close at 28,061.14. ONGC was the highest gainer, rising by ₹9,411.04 crore in m-cap followed by Reliance Industries and SBI, while TCS plunged the most by ₹11,615.67 crore.
