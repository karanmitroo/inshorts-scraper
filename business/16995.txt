Tigerair, Singapore's leading budget-carrier, has announced a 'Fly to Singapore for Free' offer, for   the debit, credit and prepaid card customers of HDFC Bank, on Wednesday. An onward ticket from India to Singapore will be given complimentary on booking a round-trip fare during the offer period, that is from Aug-18 to Sep-7. The round-trip fares start at Rs. 7,499.
