HDFC Bank on Tuesday surpassed Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) to become the country's second most valued firm. The market capitalisation of HDFC Bank on Tuesday morning stood at ₹4.735 lakh crore, which was ₹797.4 crore more than TCS' ₹4.727 lakh crore valuation. Notably, shares of HDFC Bank have surged nearly 53% so far this year.
