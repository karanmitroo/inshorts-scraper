State-owned Oil and Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC) on Saturday approved a ₹3,104 crore investment in Tripura for the production of natural gas from the state's fields. Earlier, an investment of ₹1,946 crore was approved for the same, taking the amount to 5,050 crore. So far, 11 gas fields have been discovered by ONGC in Tripura and seven are on production.
