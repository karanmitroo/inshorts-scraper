Maruti Suzuki India (MSI) on Friday launched a facelifted version of its multi-purpose vehicle (MPV) Ertiga, with starting prices of ₹5.99 lakh (petrol) and ₹7.55 lakh (diesel). “Ertiga diesel is the first smart hybrid MPV in India” with fuel efficiency of 24.52 km/l, MSI said. The new Ertiga comes with Maruti's SmartPlay infotainment system and a 7-inch touchscreen display.
