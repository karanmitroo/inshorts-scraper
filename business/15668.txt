The BSE Sensex fell for the fifth successive day Tuesday as it declined 30.30 points to 28,161.72 and Nifty decreased 7.95 points to 8,542.95, mainly due to the downfall in banks' shares. As many as 1,111 shares increased, 1,701 shares declined and 182 shares remained unchanged. HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank and State Bank of India decreased about one percent each.
