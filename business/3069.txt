Five Indian-origin people have featured in Fortune's 2017 40 Under 40 list of most influential young people in business. Irish Prime Minister Leo Varadkar was ranked 5th in the list while Apple executive Divya Nag came in at 26. The list also includes Leila Janah, Founder of non-profit firm Samasource, and Co-founders of Outcome Health, Rishi Shah and Shradha Agarwal.
