State-owned Indian Overseas Bank (IOB) on Saturday announced that it is planning to raise up to ₹1,570 crore through a public offer at the current market price. Equity shares "not exceeding 517.9 million" will be issued with a face value of ₹10 at an "appropriate premium", IOB said. IOB's shares closed at ₹30.45 apiece on the BSE on Friday.
