The BSE's benchmark 30-share Sensex on Thursday gained 230.48 points, closing at 27,010.14. The broader 50-share Nifty index also posted gains, increasing by 71.60 points to end the day at 8,179.50. The BSE's midcap and smallcap indices also reflected broader sentiment to post gains. The Sensex's top gainers were Tata Motors, BHEL, and Maruti Suzuki. The auto sector gained 2.33%.
