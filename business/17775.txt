Nearly 77 of the top 155 BSE-200 companies (excluding banking and financial ones), have destroyed shareholder value since the 2008 Lehman crisis. In all, since 2007-08, those 155 companies have together destroyed over Rs 22 lakh crore of shareholder wealth, or nearly 46% of their combined market value in Jan-2014. Topping the list is Reliance Industries (RIL), followed by NTPC.
