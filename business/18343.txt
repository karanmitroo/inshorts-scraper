The rupee gained 142-paise to close at 63.83/$ up Rs.5 since hitting a historic low of 68.85/$ on Aug-28 and the Sensex jumped 727-points, the highest one-day rise in 4-years, to close at 19,997. The rupee has gained in the last four-consecutive trading sessions, since the start of new RBI governor Raghuram Rajan's tenure, who announced a host of measures.
