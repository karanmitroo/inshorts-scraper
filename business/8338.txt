The benchmark indices on Wednesday ended the day with losses after the Sensex plunging by 349.39 points to end at 27,527.22. Meanwhile, the broader Nifty lost 112.25 points to close at 8,514. M&M, Hindustan Unilever, and NTPC emerged as the day's biggest gainers while ONGC, Tata Motors and SBI were the major losers.
