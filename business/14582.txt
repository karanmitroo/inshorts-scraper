The benchmark 30-share Sensex on Monday lost about 308 points or 1.22% to close below 25,000 at 24,893.81. The broader 50-share Nifty Index also lost points, finishing at 7,558.80, a loss of 96.25 points. Despite opening on a positive note, the Sensex lost gains as the day's trade progressed. Axis bank, Vedanta, and ICICI bank were the Sensex's top losers.
