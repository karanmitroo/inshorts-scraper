Luxury SUV maker Jaguar Land Rover India on Thursday launched the new variant of Range Rover Evoque priced between ₹47.1 lakh and ₹63.2 lakh. The 2016 Evoque facelift sports redesigned bumpers and new alloy wheels, among others. It comes with a 2.2-litre, four-cylinder turbo-charged diesel engine which delivers 140 kW at 3,500 rpm.
