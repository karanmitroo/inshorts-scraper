The BSE Sensex on Wednesday surged by 575.70 points to close at 25,881.17, while the NSE Nifty gained 186.05 points to settle at 7,934.90 amid firm global cues. Analysts attributed the rise to Skymet’s raised outlook for the 2016 monsoon, Citigroup’s prediction of 7.8% GDP growth in the fourth quarter of 2015-16, and Morgan Stanley’s upgraded outlook on Indian equities.
