Anil Ambani-led Reliance Cement bagged the first mine in the maiden auction of coal mines on Saturday through e-bidding for Rs 798 crore. Reliance Cement beat Hindustan Zinc Ltd and OCL Iron & Steel to win the Sial Ghoghri coal mine in Chhidwara district of MP. The company bid Rs 1,402 per tonne to get the mine.
