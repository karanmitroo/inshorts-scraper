State-owned oil firms, Tuesday, reduced the petrol price by Re.1/litre (excluding tax) with effect from Tuesday midnight. With this price revision petrol rate in Delhi was reduced by Rs.1.21/litre (including tax) to Rs.66.65/litre, while in Mumbai, petrol price was cut from Rs.75.73 to Rs.74.46/litre. Last time, the oil firms had cut petrol price by 54 paisa on Oct-1.
