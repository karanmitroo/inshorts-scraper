After receiving overwhelming response for Celerio, car market leader Maruti Suzuki on Monday introduced Celerio Green - the CNG powered Celerio - priced at Rs 4.68 lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi). Powered by the factory fitted iGPI technology (intelligent Gas Port Injection) the Bi-fuel Celerio Green delivers a fuel efficiency of 31.79 km/kg in CNG mode.
