Prices of non-subsidised cooking gas (LPG) were on Tuesday raised by ₹61.50 per cylinder. Following the hike – the second straight one after ₹27.50 in November – a 14.2 kg cylinder would now cost ₹606.50 in Delhi. Further, aviation turbine fuel (ATF) or jet fuel prices were reduced by ₹526.20 per kilolitre to cost ₹44,320.32 per kilolitre in Delhi.
