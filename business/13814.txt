SBI Chairperson Arundhati Bhattacharya has been again named as the most powerful Indian businesswoman for 2015 by the magazine Fortune India. The list of 50 most powerful Indian businesswomen ranked ICICI Bank Managing Director (MD) Chanda Kochhar second, followed by Axis Bank MD Shikha Sharma. Among debutants, Portea MD Meena Ganesh and Eros International MD Jyoti Deshpande entered the list.
