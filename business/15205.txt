The BSE Sensex on Wednesday went up 359.25 points (1.4%) to close at 26840.50 points and the Nifty, which ended its seven-day losing streak, rose by 102.05 points (1.3%) to 8124.45. As many as 1705 shares advanced, 949 shares declined and 151 remained unchanged. BHEL, Wipro, Bajaj Auto, Reliance and L&T were the top gainers in the Sensex.
