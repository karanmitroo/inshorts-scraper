Anil Ambani-led Reliance Group on Thursday announced to invest ₹46,000 crore in Madhya Pradesh in sectors including defence, IT, electronics and energy. It plans to invest ₹5,000 crore in setting up an aircraft maintenance centre in Bhopal and nearly ₹27,000 crore for manufacturing ingots and poly-silicons. This is expected to generate nearly 70,000 direct and indirect employments.
