The BSE Sensex on Friday rose by 200.34 points (0.74%) to 27316.17 and the Nifty increased by 50.35 points (0.62%) to 8224.95. As many as 1426 shares advanced, 1236 declined and 197 remained unchanged. M&M, Maruti Suzuki, HDFC, Vedanta, ONGC and Bank of Baroda surged 2-5%, while Tata Power, GAIL and Sun Pharma declined 1-2%.
