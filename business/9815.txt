Oil companies on Wednesday hiked the price of petrol by ₹3.38/litre and the price of diesel by ₹2.67/litre, effective midnight. Petrol and diesel in Delhi will now cost ₹63.47/litre and ₹52.94/litre respectively. "The current level of international product prices of Petrol & Diesel and INR-USD exchange rate" warrants increase in the price of petrol and diesel, state-owned IOCL said.
