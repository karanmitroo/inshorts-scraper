Telecom player Vodafone on Friday said that it will launch its 4G services by the end of 2015. “Important data markets, including Mumbai, Delhi, Kolkata, Bengaluru and Kochi, will be part of the first wave of the rollout,” the company said. It will also introduce its 3G networks in 7 circles --Assam, North-East, Uttar Pradesh-West, Rajasthan, Karnataka, Kerala and Odisha.
