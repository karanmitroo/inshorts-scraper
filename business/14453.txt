Designer Dilip Chhabria has launched India's first sports car, the DC Avanti, in Pune at a price of about Rs 36 lakh. The car is powered by a Renault-sourced 2.0-Litre, 4-cylinder turbocharged petrol engine. The sports car runs 0-100 kmph in 6 seconds and has an electronically-limited top speed of 200 kmph.
