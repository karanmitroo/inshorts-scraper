Indian companies reportedly raised ₹2,90,470 crore from capital markets in the first-half (H1) of the ongoing fiscal. Debt instruments accounted for over ₹2.44 lakh crore while ₹46,197 crore were garnered from equity, including ₹4,904 crore from IPOs. In comparison, Indian companies had raised around ₹4.80 lakh crore from equity and debt in 2014-15 and ₹3.92 lakh crore in 2013-14.
