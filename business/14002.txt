ONGC and RIL, two of India’s largest oil and gas companies, improved their positions in the benchmark Platts Top 250 Global Energy Company Rankings 2015. RIL was ranked 14th, up from 22nd in 2014 while ONGC moved from the 21st to the 17th spot. This year’s rankings witnessed the highest representation of Indian companies (14). Exxon Mobil topped the charts.
