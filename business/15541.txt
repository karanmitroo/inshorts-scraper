Reliance Industries (RIL) posted a record net income of Rs 6,381 Cr in Q4 2014-15, thus re-establishing itself as the country's most profitable company. RIL clocked an 8.5% year-on-year rise in net profit even after the sales dropped 33.3% to Rs 70,863 crore. The company had lost its top position to Tata Consultancy Services during the preceding quarter (Q3).
