India's 8-core infrastructure industries, weighing about 38% in the Index of Industrial Production, posted a 7-month high growth rate of 3.7% in August'13; however, lower as compared to 6.1% in August'12. Power generation; cement, steel and fertilizer production grew by 6.7, 5.5, 4.3 and 1.7 percent in August'13 as compared to 1.9, 0.4, 2.9 and -2.1 percent respectively in August'12.
