The BSE Sensex on Thursday went up by 166.30 points (0.6 percent) to close at 27895.97 and Nifty increased by 37.15 points (0.4 percent) to 8398. As many as 1353 shares advanced, 1316 shares declined and 159 remained unchanged. Bajaj Auto, L&T and GAIL were the major gainers, while Wipro, HUL, ONGC, Cipla and TCS were the major losers.
