The benchmark Sensex on Thursday rallied 359.40 points (1.41%) to close at 25,841.92, while, the broader Nifty gained 110.95 points (1.43%) to end the day at 7,842.75 points. Market sentiments remained upbeat after Chief Economic Adviser Arvind Subramanian said that the inflation is under control and is within the target set by RBI. Retail inflation stood at 5% in October.
