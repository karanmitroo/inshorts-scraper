A recent development in the alleged ₹6,000 crore Bank of Baroda scandal uncovered that low-income citizens like rickshaw pullers, household workers were reportedly made 'Directors' in 59 fake companies. These people were given ₹10,000-₹15,000 per month in return for their IDs, which were used to open fake company accounts in BoB through which black money was sent to Hong Kong.
