IndiGo slashed its air-fare by over 50% on tickets bought above 90-days in advance between Delhi and cities like Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, Bhubaneshwar and Patna, which is being suspected as a move to maintain its supremacy after AirAsia-India declared starting its services by October. All airlines except AI have cut their rates by 15-40% for a week advance bookings.
