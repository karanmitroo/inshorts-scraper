The BSE Sensex on Tuesday reclaimed the 27,000-mark for the first time since October 2015 after it rose 232.22 points to close at 27,009.67, while the NSE Nifty gained 65.40 points to settle at 8,266.45 on account of firm global markets. Market sentiment remained upbeat amid the RBI leaving the key interest rates unchanged in its bi-monthly review.
