The benchmark Sensex on Friday surged 183.15 points (0.67%) to near a two-month high at 27,470.81 points, while, the broader Nifty gained 43.75 points (0.53%) to settle at 8,295.45 points. "Global markets are positive and the ECB signal was positive. That's a key trigger", said the head of research at Geojit BNP Paribas.
