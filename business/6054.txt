Westin Hotels & Resorts has opened its seventh Indian property in Pushkar, Rajasthan. The resort has nearly 100 guestrooms, of which 44 feature private pools, a spa, a mini-theatre and a swimming pool. "The opening of The Westin Pushkar Resort & Spa brings the first internationally-branded resort to the city of Pushkar," said Paradise Properties Managing Partner Pankaj Prabhashankar Saboo.
