The biennial Auto Expo at Greater Noida starting from Feb-7 will see 70 new car and bike launches, including 26 global unveilings. Mass-market players like Maruti, Hyundai and Honda and luxury automakers Audi, BMW and Mercedes alike will be trotting out debutants. Legendary bikemaker Harley Davidson and homegrown Hero Moto will also showcase a variety of new models and concepts.
