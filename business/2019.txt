India's total currency in circulation has reached 90% of the level before demonetisation on November 8, 2016, RBI data has revealed. As of October 13, the currency in circulation was ₹16.18 trillion, compared to ₹17.97 trillion on  November 4, 2016. According to RBI's annual report, 98.96% of the demonetised notes by value were returned by the end of June.
