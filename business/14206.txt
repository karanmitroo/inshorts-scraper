Tata Housing on Wednesday launched a first-of-its-kind initiative in India called ‘Social Sell’ in collaboration with Facebook, for selling properties in Goa online. The initiative would enable prospective buyers to book limited homes at Tata’s upcoming project ‘Codename: Goa Paradise’, through Facebook. The residential project offers a range of homes from 1BHK to 3BHK, starting at ₹29 lakh.
