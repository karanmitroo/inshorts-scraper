The benchmark Sensex and Nifty hit a third consecutive record high on Monday but slipped down later in the session, with both indexes ending flat. The 30-share Sensex closed 5 points down at 27,860.38 points after hitting new life high of 27,969.82 points. Similarly, the Nifty hit yet another high of 8,350.60 and closed 1.95 points down at 8,324.15 points.
