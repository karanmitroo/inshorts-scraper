As per a report by anti-corruption watchdog Transparency International, Indian firms performed best among 75 companies from BRICS(Brazil, Russia, India, China, South Africa) in terms of transparency in corporate reporting, with Tata Communications at the top, followed by Tata Global Beverages and Tata Steel(both at 2nd position) and Bharti Airtel. While Indian firms scored highest (54%), Chinese scored the lowest(20%).
