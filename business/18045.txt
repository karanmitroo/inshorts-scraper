Current Account Deficit for the second quarter of current financial year plunged to $5.2 billion versus $21 billion year-on-year, on the back of turnaround in exports and decline in gold imports. The figure implies that CAD was at 1.2% of GDP versus 5% of GDP year-on-year. Moreover, capital and financial account surplus stood at $5 billion versus $20.8 billion year-on-year.
