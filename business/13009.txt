Anil Ambani's Reliance Communications (R-Com) and Mukesh Ambani's Reliance Jio Infocomm (R-Jio) on Monday signed a spectrum-sharing deal to enable faster rollout of 4G services. Under the strategic collaboration, the two companies said they will enter into reciprocal Intra Circle Roaming (ICR) arrangements. R-Jio is expected to commercially launch 4G services in India in the year's first quarter.
