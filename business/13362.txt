The combined market valuation of nine of the top ten companies, barring ITC, listed on the BSE Sensex increased by ₹69,415 crore in the week ended December 18, with the index rising nearly 1.9% to 25,519 points. Mukesh Ambani-led Reliance Industries (RIL) emerged as the biggest gainer, with a ₹12,892 crore boost to its market capitalisation (m-cap).
