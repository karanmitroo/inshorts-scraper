The Supreme Court (SC) on Friday restrained the promoters of real estate major Amrapali Group from leaving the country without its permission. The Amrapali Group has liabilities of about ₹3,000 crore towards authorities and owes over ₹1,000 crore to about 10 banks. Amrapali had launched 11,000 flats in Noida, of which 8,500 have been delivered and 2,500 are pending.
