Oil companies on Monday hiked the price of petrol by ₹0.83/litre and the price of diesel by ₹1.26/litre, effective midnight. Petrol and diesel in Delhi will now cost ₹63.02/litre and ₹51.67/litre respectively. "The current level of international product prices of Petrol & Diesel and INR-USD exchange rate" warrants increase in the price of petrol and diesel, state-owned IOCL said.
