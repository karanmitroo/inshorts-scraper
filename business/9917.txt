Service tax collection from the Mumbai zone has surged 22.9% year-on-year to stand at ₹23,811 crore in the April-July period, as per official data. With this, the zone also surpassed its government-mandated target for the duration by ₹1,447 crore or 6.5%. The net revenue collected in the month of July alone was ₹6,228 crore, an official said.
