Amid a late sell-off, the benchmark Sensex on Monday plunged by 246.66 points (0.95%) to settle at 25,616.84, meanwhile, the broader Nifty ended 72.80 points (0.93%) below to end the day at 7,795.70. According to experts, the Reserve Bank of India governor Raghuram Rajan is expected to cut the policy rates on Tuesday by at least 25 basis points.
