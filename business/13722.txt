Employees’ Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) invested around ₹2,322.1 crore in exchange traded funds (ETF) between August and October as a part of its planned ₹5,000 crore worth of investments in equities this fiscal. Around ₹758 crore was invested from Pension Scheme and ₹1,564.1 crore from Provident Fund Scheme. Meanwhile, ₹588.03 crore was invested in Sensex and ₹1,734.08 crore in Nifty.
