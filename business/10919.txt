The market capitalisation (m-cap) of six of the top ten BSE-listed companies surged ₹22,459 crore in the week ended June 17, with the benchmark Sensex falling marginally by 9.84 points to settle at 26,625.91. TCS emerged the biggest gainer, adding ₹8,384 crore in m-cap followed by ITC and CIL, while ONGC saw the biggest fall in m-cap at ₹6,373 crore.
