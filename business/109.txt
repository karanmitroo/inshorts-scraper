The Enforcement Directorate has attached 41 properties worth ₹1,217.2 crore belonging to fraud-accused Gitanjali Gems' owner Mehul Choksi and his companies. A 170-acre park in Hyderabad worth over ₹500 crore has also been attached. Other assets include 15 flats and 17 office premises, a shopping mall, a farmhouse in Alibaug, and 231 acres of land in Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu.
