With a net profit of Rs 102.21 crore, Adani Enterprises posted a six fold jump in net profit to Rs 2847.8 crore in Q4 of the financial year 2013-14, as against Rs 473.7 crore in Q4FY13. However, its total income, has decreased from Rs 3586.8 crore in Q4FY13 to Rs 3056.8 crore in the quarter ended March, down almost 15%.
