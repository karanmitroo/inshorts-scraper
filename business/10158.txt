The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty on Tuesday registered losses after they fell 87.79 and 29.60 points to close at 28,064.61 and 8,642.55 respectively amid weak global cues. Investors remained cautious amid the wholesale price index (WPI)-based inflation posting a two-year high at 3.55% in July. Adani Ports SEZ was the major gainer, while Infosys emerged as the biggest loser.
