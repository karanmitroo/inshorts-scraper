The Benchmark BSE Sensex gained 246.49 points (0.96%) to settle at 25,856.7, and NSE's Nifty gained 82.95 points (1.06%) to climb over 7,800 points to reach 7,872.25 on Monday. NTPC, Hindalco, Tata Steel and Axis Bank were among the top gainers. Most Asian markets, including China's Shanghai Composite, Japan's Nikkei and South Korea's Kospi saw a downfall.
