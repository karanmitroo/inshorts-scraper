The Unique Identification Authority of India has deactivated close to 81 lakh Aadhaar numbers till date, Minister of State for Electronics and Information Technology, PP Chaudhary said on Saturday. The Aadhaar numbers were deactivated by the regional offices of the UIDAI, for a number of reasons stated in Section 27 and 28 of Aadhaar (Enrolment and Update) Regulations, 2016.
