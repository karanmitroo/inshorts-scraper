Union Minister Nitin Gadkari has said 24 companies have offered to invest over ₹60,000 crore in a special economic zone adjoining India's largest container port, Jawaharlal Nehru Port in Mumbai.  The investment will create employment for 1.25-1.50 lakh people. Gadkari said one firm has said in an affidavit that it will invest ₹6,000 crore and create employment for 40,000 people.
