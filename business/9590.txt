The benchmark indices on Monday posted their biggest one-day loss in percentage terms since the ‘Brexit’ vote on June 24. The BSE Sensex tanked 443.71 points to close at 28,353.54 while the NSE Nifty fell by 151.10 points to settle at 8,715.60 on account of weak global cues. Traders stood cautious ahead of the factory output and inflation data.
