Nestle India on Friday reported a 23.26% year-on-year increase in profit to ₹343 crore for the quarter ended September 30. The company's total sales rose to ₹2,500.67 crore, of which the domestic market accounted for nearly 94% of sales at ₹2,340.75 crore during the quarter. Nestle India said growth rates were impacted by the "change in structure of indirect taxes".
