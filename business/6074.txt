India needs to "celebrate" success stories of "heroes" like Dhirubhai Ambani and Narayana Murthy, who have created wealth for the nation and jobs for millions, former Infosys executive Mohandas Pai said. "We have to create a good narrative... Our narrative is broken up into very negative matters," he stated. "Why can't an Ambani, Murthy get a Bharat Ratna?" he added.
