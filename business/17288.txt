The Sensex and Nifty ended flat on Tuesday after hitting record highs for the 3rd consecutive session. The Sensex rose as much as 0.51% to hit a record high of 25,711, surpassing the highest level of 25,644 hit on Monday. The Nifty rose 0.37% to hit a fresh all-time high of 7,683. The indices finally ended at 25,583.69 and 7,656.
