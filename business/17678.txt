Seven women have made it to the Fortune India's first '40 Under 40' compilation that lists (but doesn't rank) individuals who have managed to grow an idea into a profitable business. Richa Kar (Zivame.com), Kavita Bhupathi Chadda (Globosport), Simeran Bhasin (Fastrack & New Brands, Titan Industries), AS Devda (Tata Starbucks) and Neha Kirpal (India Art Fair) are some of them.
