The benchmark indices on Wednesday ended the day with gains with the BSE Sensex gaining 69.11 points to settle at 28,292.81 points. Meanwhile, the NSE Nifty rose by 38.75 points to close at 8,745.15 points. Tata Steel and Bharti Airtel were among the top gainers while Reliance Industries Limited and Coal India Limited ended the day as major losers.
