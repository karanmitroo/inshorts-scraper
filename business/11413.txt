As many as 2.8 lakh income tax dispute appeals involving ₹5.67 lakh crore were pending before various Commissioners of Income Tax (Appeals) in India, till October-end last year, according to government data. In comparison, 2.32 lakh appeals involving ₹3.84 lakh crore were pending by March-end 2015. Notably, this is only the first step in resolving an income tax dispute.
