IndiGo's parent InterGlobe Aviation on Tuesday reported a 56.4% year-on-year increase in its net profit to ₹762.03 crore for the December quarter, compared to ₹487.25 crore a year ago. The company's revenue for the quarter rose 23.89% to ₹6,177.88 crore. IndiGo has about 39.4% of the domestic aviation market with nearly 153 planes and operates over 1,000 daily flights.
