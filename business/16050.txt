Beleaguered low-cost carrier SpiceJet has accepted the resignations of Kalanithi Maran, Kavery Kalanithi and S Natrajhen from the Board. The Airlines also plans to raise Rs. 1,500 crore, by issuing securities and allotting non-convertible preference shares to the earlier promoters, the Marans. The Board approved the entire 58.46 per cent stake sale to Ajay Singh.
