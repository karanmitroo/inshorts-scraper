Gold prices Friday surged Rs 565 to hit over two-month highs at Rs 27,885 per ten grams, aided by wedding season demand and strong global cues. Gold prices in New York, which set the domestic trend, jumped up by $33.50 to $1,262.60 an ounce amid weak dollar. Domestic silver prices also soared by Rs 620 to Rs 37,900 per kg.
