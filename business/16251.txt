Led by gains in banks, FMCG, auto and metals, the Sensex on Thursday bounced back to rise over 365 points in a pull-back rally. The 30-share index closed at 27,274.71, after touching a high of 27,316.41 and a low of 27,101.94 intraday. The 50-share Nifty also rose in the trade, closing at 8,234.60, up 132.50 points.
