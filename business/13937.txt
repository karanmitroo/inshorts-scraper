Founder and Chairman of Hero MotoCorp Brijmohan Lall Munjal (92) passed away on Sunday in Delhi after a brief illness. Munjal, born in 1923 in Punjab's Kamalia (now in Pakistan), shifted to Amritsar in 1943 with his three brothers and then to Ludhiana where they started Hero Cycles Limited in 1956. He is survived by three sons and a daughter.
