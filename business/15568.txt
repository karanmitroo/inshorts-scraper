To mark the occasion of its 121st Foundation Day, state-owned Punjab National Bank (PNB) opened 121 ATMs across the country, Sunday. The total number of ATMs of the bank is now 6,421. Also, 121 Offsite E-Lobbys were opened, taking their number to 621. The government's saving scheme for the girl child, the Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana was also made available.
