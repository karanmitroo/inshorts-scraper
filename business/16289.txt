Tata Motors Friday reported a 10% rise in total sales at 41,734 units in December. The company had sold 37,836 units in December 2013. In the domestic sales, the company reported an 8% increase at 37,776 units, as against 35,010 units in December 2013. Exports during the month were at 3,958 units, up 40% from 2,826 units in December 2013.
