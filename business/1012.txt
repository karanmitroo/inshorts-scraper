Indian-origin Ravi Menon, the MD of Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS), has been named the best central bank governor in Asia-Pacific for 2018, according to a UK-based magazine, The Banker. Singapore's Finance Minister Heng Swee Keat had won the same award in 2011 when he had been the MD of MAS. Raghuram Rajan was awarded the honour in 2016.
