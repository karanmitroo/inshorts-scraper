The benchmark Sensex and broader Nifty continued to trade flat on Tuesday to close 21.41 and 13.65 points down at 27,981.71 and 8,622.90 respectively on account of profit booking ahead of tabling of the GST Bill in Rajya Sabha on Wednesday. ITC and Maruti Suzuki emerged as the biggest gainers, while HDFC and Tata Motors ended as the major losers.
