Seven telecom operators on Wednesday submitted applications to participate in the country’s largest spectrum auctions slated to be held in October. These operators included Bharti Airtel, Vodafone, Idea, Aircel, Reliance Communications, Reliance Jio Infocomm, and Tata Teleservices, reports claimed. Airwaves worth ₹5.56 lakh crore at base prices will be put up for sale in this round.
