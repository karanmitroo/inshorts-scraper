Petrol price was on Thursday cut by Rs 1.15 a litre, the second reduction in a month, whereas diesel prices saw a hike of 50 paise/litre, excluding local sales tax or VAT. It is effective from Thursday midnight. Petrol price in Delhi will be cut by Rs 1.38 to Rs.71.02/litre, with  diesel price hiked by 56 paise to Rs.53.10/litre.
