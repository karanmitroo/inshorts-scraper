Penalties amounting to ₹258 crore were slapped on airline carriers Jet Airways, IndiGo and SpiceJet on Tuesday by the Competition Commission of India (CCI) for allegedly "overcharging cargo freight in the garb of fuel surcharge". Jet Airways, IndiGo and SpiceJet have been fined ₹151.7 crore, ₹63.7 crore and ₹42.5 crore respectively.
