The monthly Goods and Services Tax (GST) collection declined to its lowest since its rollout in June this year. The government collected ₹80,808 crore for November as on December 25, compared with ₹83,346 crore in October, and ₹92,150 crore in September. The GST Council had lowered rates on over 200 items, most of which were brought to 18% from 28%.
