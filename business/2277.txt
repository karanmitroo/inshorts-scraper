Jet fuel price was hiked by 6% on Sunday and will now cost ₹53,045/kilolitre in Delhi. This is the third consecutive monthly hike in jet fuel price. Further, the price of subsidised LPG cylinders has been hiked by ₹1.50 to ₹488.68 per cylinder and the price of non-subsidised LPG cylinders was hiked by ₹1.50 per cylinder to ₹599 in Delhi.
