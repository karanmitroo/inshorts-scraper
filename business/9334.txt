Tech billionaire brothers, Divyank and Bhavin Turakhia, with an estimated net worth of $1.3 billion have debuted on the Forbes' 'India's 100 Richest People' list. The brothers, who started their first business in 1996, recently sold their tech-advertising firm Media.net for $900 million in August this year. The brothers have made web hosting, cloud infrastructure, and payments companies till date.
