Maruti Suzuki on Friday said it is recalling 75,419 Baleno cars manufactured between August 3, 2015 and May 17, 2016 to upgrade the hatchback's airbag controller software. Further, 1,961 units of Swift Dzire are also being recalled for the inspection and replacement of a faulty fuel filter. Maruti Suzuki said dealers will begin contacting car owners starting from May 31.
