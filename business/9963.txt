The benchmark indices on Friday posted losses as the Sensex fell 53.66 points to settle at 27,782.25 points, while the broader Nifty dropped 19.65 points to close at 8,572.55 points amid mixed global cues. Investors remained cautious ahead of US Federal Reserve Chair Janet Yellen's speech later today. Tata Motors was the top gainer, whereas Wipro was the major loser.
