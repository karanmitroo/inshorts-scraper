The combined market capitalisation (m-cap) of nine of the top ten companies, barring Reliance Industries, listed on the BSE Sensex dropped by ₹78,511.56 crore in the week ended January 8, with the index plunging 4.68% to 24,934.33 points. ONGC suffered the maximum erosion, losing ₹11,250 crore of its market valuation, followed by ITC, which lost ₹11,211 crore.
