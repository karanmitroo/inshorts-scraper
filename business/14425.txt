The market valuation of top six Sensex companies fell by Rs 53,886 crore last week, with Reliance Industries and Coal India taking the biggest hits. The m-cap of RIL plunged Rs 18,523.8 crore and that of CIL fell Rs 15,443.51 crore, followed by ONGC, HDFC, Sun Pharma and SBI. The Sensex lost 355.41 points last week to settle at 25,863.50.
