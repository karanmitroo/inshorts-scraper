Mukesh Ambani-led Reliance Jio has reportedly deposited the highest guarantee of around ₹6,500 crore as earnest money deposit with the Department of Telecom (DoT) for the spectrum auction starting on October 1. Vodafone deposited the second highest guarantee of around ₹2,745 crore, amongst​ seven telecom companies. Notably, Jio is the "biggest investment in the history" of Reliance Industries.
