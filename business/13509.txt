The benchmark Sensex and broader Nifty lost around 2% during the week ended December 4, on negative sentiment triggered by floods in Chennai, concerns over a US Federal Reserve rate hike, continued sell-off by foreign investors and a weak rupee. The Sensex lost 490.09 points to close at 25,638.11, while the Nifty fell by 160.80 points to settle at 7,781.90.
