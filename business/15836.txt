Falling for the second straight session, the Sensex on Monday ended over 256 points lower at 28,975.11. Losses were outsized in shares of oil & gas, metals and banks. The 30-share index touched a high of 29,362.96 and a low of 28,913.16 in trade. The 50-share Nifty followed suit, and closed at 8,739.30, down 94.30 points.
