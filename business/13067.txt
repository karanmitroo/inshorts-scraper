A report by The Financial Express states that Kerala-based private sector bank Federal Bank sold loans to Essar Steel worth ₹70 crore to Edelweiss Asset Reconstruction Company (ARC) in December. This comes after HDFC Bank offloaded Essar Steel loans of ₹550 crore last year. The steel producer's gross debt stood at ₹32,050 crore at the end of March 2015.
