Foreign Portfolio Investors' (FPIs) net inflows in the Indian stock markets from October 1 to 9, 2015, stood at ₹1,607 crore and the net inflow in the debt market was ₹406 crore. FPIs withdrew ₹17,524 crore from Indian capital markets in August. August's withdrawal was the highest net outflow in a single month by FPIs since 1997.
