Indian Oil Corporation on Tuesday hiked the price of petrol by ₹0.14/litre and diesel by ₹0.10/litre, effective midnight. Petrol and diesel in Delhi will now cost ₹64.47/litre and ₹52.67/litre respectively. The hike in the price of Petrol and Diesel has been warranted by the change in the Dealer Commission, state-owned IOCL said.
