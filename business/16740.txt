S ‘Kris’ Gopalakrishnan, the last of Infosys’ seven co-founders, received what company termed as an “informal” farewell on Wednesday. Gopalakrishnan will step down from the board on Friday. His departure will snap the company’s last connection with its founders, although many of them remain shareholders in the company. Infosys Consultants Pvt. Ltd was incorporated on 2nd July 1981.
