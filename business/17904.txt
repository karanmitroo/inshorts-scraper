Following the return of NR Narayana Murthy as Infosys' executive chairman, the Indian IT major has bounced back with double-digit (12% in dollar terms) revenue forecast this fiscal (2013-14). In rupee terms, revenue forecast has been revised to 25% from 22% estimated in July and 17% in April. Moreover, the company reported net profit of Rs.2,875 crore for third quarter.
