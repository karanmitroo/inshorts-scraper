India's largest state-owned telecommunications service provider BSNL has decided to increase the landline and broadband monthly tariff from March-1 across the country. Monthly plan for landline will be revised from Rs 180 to Rs 195 while in case of broadband, the revision is between Rs 24 and Rs 350 depending on the scheme, BSNL CGM (Kolkata) Gautam Chakraborty said.
