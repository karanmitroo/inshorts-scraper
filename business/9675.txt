The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty on Wednesday ended with marginal losses, with the Sensex losing 51.66 points to settle at 28,926.36, and the Nifty falling by 25.05 points to close at 8,917.95. SBI, ONGC, and ICICI Bank emerged as the biggest gainers while Asian Paints, HDFC, and Axis Bank ended the day as major losers.
