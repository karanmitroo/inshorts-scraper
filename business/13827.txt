Tata Power reported a net profit of ₹247.31 crore for the quarter ended September, compared to a net loss of ₹77.75 crore in the year-ago period. Total income from operations grew 11% to ₹9,541.19 crore. Revenue from the power business increased by 10.4% to ₹6903.89 crore while that from the coal business declined by 13.4% to ₹1,800.17 crore.
