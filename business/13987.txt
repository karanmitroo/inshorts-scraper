ITC's net profit surged to ₹2,431.25 crore in the quarter that ended September, a marginal rise of 0.25% against ₹2,425.16 crore posted in the corresponding period last year. However, the company's net sales dropped to ₹8,804.70 crore, a 1.4% decline against ₹8,930.32 crore in the previous fiscal. Revenue also fell by 1.4% to ₹8,904 crore against ₹9,024 crore last year.
