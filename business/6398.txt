The Enforcement Directorate on Thursday attached two flats worth ₹100 crore belonging to former Union Minister Matang Sinh in connection with its probe in Saradha chit fund ponzi case. The properties, worth ₹63 crore in 2013, are worth now over ₹100 crore as per present market value. Notably, the ED has now attached properties worth ₹700 crore in this case.
