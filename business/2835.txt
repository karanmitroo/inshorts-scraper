The Income Tax Department has said that about 14,000 properties worth over ₹1 crore each are under scrutiny as their owners have not filed returns. Further, in the first phase of Operation Clean Money, 13.33 lakh accounts of 9.72 lakh persons with unusual cash deposits of around ₹2.89 lakh crore were identified. Moreover, ₹15,496 crore was admitted as undisclosed income.
