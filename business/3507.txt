Liquor firm Diageo has asked Vijay Mallya to return $40 million (over ₹256 crore) paid to him as part of the $75 million (₹480 crore) deal for his exit from United Spirits. It has sought compensation for losses incurred due to breaches committed by Mallya. Citing violation of the agreement by Mallya, Diageo has also stopped future instalments to him.
