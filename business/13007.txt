According to a data released by the Commerce Ministry, merchandise exports contracted for 13th straight month in December 2015, as outward shipments shrank 14.75% against December 2014 to $22.2 billion, amid a global demand slowdown. Imports dipped 3.88% to $33.9 billion in December against the year-ago period, thereby widening the trade deficit to $11.6 billion for the month under review.
