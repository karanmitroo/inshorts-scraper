Car sales witnessed a decline in January after 14 consecutive months of growth, as per sales data released by individual automobile companies on Monday. Sales of Maruti Suzuki, Hyundai, Honda Cars and Toyota Kirloskar Motor fell by 2.6%, 1.23%, 6.52% and 33.31% respectively against the year-ago period. However, Mahindra & Mahindra recorded a 9.6% jump in sales.
