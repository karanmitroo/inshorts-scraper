The BSE Sensex and Nifty ended higher Wednesday, with hopes of sustaining the momentum in 2015. While Nifty rose 0.42% to end at 8,282.70, Sensex gained 0.35% to reach 27,499.42. The Sensex and Nifty gained nearly 30% this year, their best gain since 2009. Foreign investors were key to the rally, buying a net $16.1 billion in shares this year.
