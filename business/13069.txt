Hindustan Unilever (HUL) on Friday reported a net profit of ₹971.4 crore in the quarter ended December, a 22.4% drop from the year-ago period. Revenue rose 2.7% to ₹7,981 crore. The company said that growth was impacted by "the phasing out of excise duty incentives and price de-growth, as the benefit of lower commodity costs was passed on to consumers."
