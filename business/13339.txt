The Nifty Midcap 50 Index, which reflects performance of the market's medium segment through NSE-listed companies with average free float market capitalisation between ₹1,000 crore and ₹5,000 crore, gained 29.60 points to close at 3,366.70 on Thursday. Reliance Infrastructure and Biocon emerged as the major gainers, while TVS Motor and IDBI Bank ended among the major losers.
