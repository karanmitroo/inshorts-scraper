IT services firm HCL Technologies on Thursday reported a 14.4% year-on-year rise in net profit for the quarter ended March to ₹1,926 crore, the highest growth in 4 years. Revenue grew 15.4% to ₹10,698 crore. For the entire fiscal 2015-16, the net profit increased by 0.7% to ₹7,354 crore. Further, the company has announced a dividend of ₹6 per share.
