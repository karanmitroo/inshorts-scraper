Oil companies on Friday hiked the price of petrol by ₹2.21/litre and the price of diesel by ₹1.79/litre, effective midnight. Petrol and diesel in Delhi will now cost ₹67.58/litre and ₹56.04/litre respectively. "The current level of international product prices of Petrol & Diesel and INR-USD exchange rate" warrants change in the price of petrol and diesel, state-owned IOCL said.
