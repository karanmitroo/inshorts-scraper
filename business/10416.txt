The benchmark indices on Monday ended the trade in losses after the BSE Sensex and the NSE Nifty fell marginally by 48.74 and 1.95 points to close at 28,003.12 and 8,636.55 respectively. TCS, Tata Steel, and Wipro emerged as the major gainers while Adani Ports SEZ, Larsen & Toubro, and ICICI Bank were the biggest losers.
