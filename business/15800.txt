Taking cue from RBI's move to cut repo rate by 25 basis points to 7.5 percent, markets hit a record high in the morning trade on Wednesday. Sensex went up 407.43 points (1.4 percent) at 30,001.16 and Nifty rose 112.90 points (1 percent) at 9109.15. As many as 533 shares advanced, 94 shares declined and 93 shares remained unchanged.
