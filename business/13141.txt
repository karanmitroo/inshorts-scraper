The benchmark Sensex and broader Nifty snapped a three-week gaining streak to witness massive losses triggered by the Chinese sell-off and subsequent negative global cues in the week ended January 8. The Sensex, which touched a 19-month low this week, dropped by 1,226.57 points to close at 24,934.33, while the Nifty lost 361.85 points to settle at 7,601.35.
