Mukesh Ambani-owned Reliance Industries Limited (RIL) posted a record ₹7,290 crore net profit in the quarter ended December, registering a 38.69% rise year-on-year. However, the company's revenue from operations fell by 27% to ₹68,261 crore. The fall in revenue was attributed to a 42.7% fall in the benchmark oil prices and a 37.5% fall in the company's exports from India.
