The Central Railway has collected over ₹11.35 crore in fines from ticketless travellers between October 1 to 21, registering a 61.48% increase against ₹7.03 crore in the corresponding period in 2016. These fines were recovered from 2.20 lakh commuters who were ticketless or had excess luggage. The Central Railway had also launched a drive against touts and unauthorised ticket vendors.
