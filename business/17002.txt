Whirlpool India reported a standalone net profit of Rs. 83 crore, up 67.37% as compared to the Rs 49.59 crore standalone net profit in the year-ago period. The net sales of the consumer durables firm went up by 19.18% to Rs 1,020.10 crore during the April-June quarter. The company is planning to launch over 200 new models in 2014.
