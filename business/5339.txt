The Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) on Thursday ordered Mukesh Ambani-led Reliance Jio to withdraw the 15-day extension for subscribing to its Jio Prime service. Further, the regulator has asked Jio to withdraw the 3-month complimentary benefits of its Jio Summer Surprise offer. In response, the company has said it will fully comply with the regulator's direction.
