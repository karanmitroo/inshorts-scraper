Six Indian businesswomen, including SBI head Arundhati Bhattacharya and ICICI CEO Chanda Kochchar have featured in Forbes' 50 'Power Businesswomen' from Asia list. Other Indians on the list are MD/Non-Executive Director of Shriram Life Insurance Akhila Srinivasan, Biocon Founder, Chairman and MD Kiran Mazumdar Shaw, Axis Bank CEO and MD Shikha Sharma and LIC India MD Usha Sangwan.
