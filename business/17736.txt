Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (Hyderabad) has retained its 2nd position in the world for 2013 in the Airport Service Quality survey conducted by the Airport Council International. The ranking is in the 5-15 million passengers per annum (MPPA) category. RGIA was ranked as the best airport in 2009 and 2010, third-best in 2011, and second-best in 2012.
