In a welcome new report, Deutsche Bank said that Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) have recouped around 25% of the past outflows worth $1 billion following Raghuram Rajan's recent announcements on assuaging currency markets and FCNR-B swap announcements. Further, it claims that investor sentiments were boosted by supportive trade data, easing investment in debt markets and tempering of the Syria risk.
