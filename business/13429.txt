The benchmark Sensex and broader Nifty lost over 2% during the week ended December 11, triggered by sustained selling by foreign investors, slump in oil prices, and concerns over a US Federal Reserve rate hike and the GST bill's passage. The Sensex lost 593.68 points to close at 25,044.43, while the Nifty fell by 171.45 points to settle at 7,610.45.
