After the $1.77-billion PNB fraud, the CBI has now booked the manager of a PNB branch in Barmer, Rajasthan for allegedly cheating the bank of over ₹2 crore. The accused, Inder Chand Chundawat, allegedly transferred money from government schemes worth ₹1.57 crore and Mudra loans worth ₹62 lakh to private accounts. The CBI has registered two FIRs in the case.
