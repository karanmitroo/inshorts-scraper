According to data released by the Commerce and Industry Ministry, the core sector grew at a four-month high of 3.2% in September, against 2.6% in September 2014. Electricity generation rose by 10.8% and fertiliser output grew by 18.1% (year-on-year) in the month under review. However, steel output and crude oil production dropped by 2.5% and 0.1% respectively over last year.
