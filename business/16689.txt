SpiceJet has announced another low fare special offer ranging from all-inclusive fares of Rs.899 to Rs.2,499. The all-in fares, starting at Rs.899, are available for Bangalore-Chennai- Kochi, while a one-way ticket price would start at Rs.1,599 for Bangalore-Goa sector. The bookings of the offer are open till Oct-26, which is valid on travel period between Nov-1 and Dec-15.
