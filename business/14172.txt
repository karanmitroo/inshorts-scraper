Foreign portfolio investors (FPIs) invested ₹16,990 crore between October 1-16, with ₹3,295 crore in the stock market and  ₹13,695 crore in debts, majorly triggered by RBI's decision to increase the cap for FPIs in government securities. This investment came after the capital markets saw outflows of ₹5,784 crore in September and ₹17,524 crore in August.
