The benchmark indices on Thursday ended with gains with the Sensex rising 84.72 points to end at 27,859.60, and the broader Nifty gaining 16.85 points to settle at 8,592.15. ITC, Lupin, and ICICI Bank ended as the biggest gainers, while Mahindra & Mahindra, Adani Ports SEZ, and SBI were amongst the major losers.
