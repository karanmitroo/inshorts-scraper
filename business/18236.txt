According to Credit Suisse Research Institute's Annual Global Wealth Report-2013, the number of millionaires in the country is expected to jump over 66% at 3.02 lakh by 2018, even as 94% of India's population has wealth below $10,000. Wealth per adult has risen by 135% from $2000 in 2000 to $4,700 in 2013 at an average annual rate of 8%.
