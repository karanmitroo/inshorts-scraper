The BSE Sensex on Friday lost 207.89 points (0.82%) to close at 25,044.43 while the NSE Nifty fell 72.85 points (0.95%) to settle at 7,610.45. Banking shares plunged after the RBI said it would review how the sector is utilising provisions for dealing with debt burden. Overall sentiment remained cautious ahead of the US Federal Reserve's meet, according to analysts.
