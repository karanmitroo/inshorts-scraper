India's first women's bank, Bharatiya Mahila Bank will be inaugurated on Indira Gandhi's birth anniversary (Nov-19) by PM Manmohan Singh and UPA Chairperson Sonia Gandhi in Mumbai. Usha Ananthasubramanian has been appointed as CMD of the Bank. Headquartered in Delhi, seven branches will be operational post inauguration and the bank is expected to have network of 25 branches by March.
