Cafe Coffee Day Founder-Owner and former Karnataka CM SM Krishna's son-in-law VG Siddhartha's premises have been raided by Income-Tax Department on Thursday. Over 20 locations in Bengaluru, Mumbai, Chennai, and Chikmagalur have been raided apart from some CCD outlets. Reportedly, raids were conducted in relation to a new case to verify if there was any undisclosed income or not.
