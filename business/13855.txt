The country’s largest steelmaker Steel Authority of India (SAIL) posted a net loss of ₹1,055.96 crore for the quarter ended September, as compared to a profit of ₹649.49 crore in the corresponding period last year. Total income fell by 20.7% to ₹9,387.55 crore “mainly due to lower sales realisation which dropped 24%... and a decline in sales by 6.7%”.
