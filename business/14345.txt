State-run National Mineral Development Corporation slashed the price of iron ore (fines) by over 52% to Rs 1,460 per tonne for October, against Rs 3,060 per tonne in January amid global over-supply and subdued demand. Higher grade iron ore (lumps) price was also slashed by 41% to Rs 2,500 per tonne for October against Rs 4,200 a tonne in January.
