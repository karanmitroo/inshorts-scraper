Bad loans by state-run banks rose to Rs 2.67 Lakh crores at the end of March 2015, from Rs 2.16 lakh crore a year ago. The Non-performing asset ratio of public sector banks also surged to 5.43% as compared to 4.72% the previous fiscal. However, Minister of State for Finance Jayant Sinha noted that recovery by banks had increased 22.3%.
