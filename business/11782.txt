The benchmark Sensex and broader Nifty continued to trade flat on Friday to close 42.24 and 12.75 points down at 25,838.14 and 7,899.30 respectively amid weak cues from global markets. Maruti Suzuki, SBI, ONGC, Axis Bank, and NTPC emerged as the biggest gainers while Hindustan Unilever, Asian Paints, HDFC, Sun Pharma, and ITC ended as the major losers.
