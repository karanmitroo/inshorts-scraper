Two Indian-origin women, Neerja Sethi (60) and Jayshree Ullal (54), have made it to the Forbes list of Most Successful, Self-Made Women in the US. The co-founder of Syntel, India-born Sethi is ranked 14th with a net worth of $1.1 billion. President and CEO of Arista Networks, London-born Ullal is ranked 30th with a net worth of $470 million.
