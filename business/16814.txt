Five Indian-Americans are among the 400 richest people in the US as per Forbes. Synthel founder Bharat Desai, entrepreneur John Kapoor, Symphony Technology founder Romesh Wadhwani, Silicon Valley investor Kavitark Ram Shriram and venture capitalist Vinod Khosla feature in the list. Mr Desai ranks 255 on the list, followed by Mr. Kapoor (261), Mr Wadhwani (264) and Mr Khosla (381).
