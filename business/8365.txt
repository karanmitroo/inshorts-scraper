The BSE Sensex and NSE Nifty on Tuesday posted marginal changes with the Sensex losing 53.60 points to settle at 27,876.61, and the Nifty gaining 0.55 points to close at 8,626.25. Tata Steel, HDFC, and NTPC emerged as the biggest gainers while Axis Bank, TCS, and Sun Pharma ended as the major losers.
