
The BSE Sensex on Friday surged by 286.92 points to close at 26,653.60, while the NSE Nifty gained 87.00 points to settle at 8,156.65 as foreign inflows continued amid mixed Asian cues. SBI, Sun Pharma, Adani Ports SEZ, Reliance Industries, and Bajaj Auto emerged as the major gainers while ONGC, Axis Bank, ITC, and BHEL ended among the major losers.
