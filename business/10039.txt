National Thermal Power Corporation (NTPC) reported a net profit of over ₹2,369 crore in the quarter ended June, a​ 4% surge against the corresponding period last year. The net income increased 11.5% to ₹19,062.91 crore year-on-year. Further, profit from operations surged to ₹3,815.35 crore, a 62.69% jump over last year.
