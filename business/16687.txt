The benchmark BSE Sensex and Nifty rose by nearly 1.2% during mid-morning trade on Monday as BJP won the Maharashtra and Haryana assembly elections and also due to oil and gas reforms announced by the govt on Saturday. At 2.35 pm, the Sensex surged 321.29 points to trade at 26,429.82 and the Nifty rose 90.90 points to trade at 7,870.60.
