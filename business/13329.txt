The benchmark Sensex and broader Nifty gained 1.25% in the holiday-shortened week ended December 24. Analysts attributed the gains to value buying and net inflow by foreign institutional investors. All sectoral indices, barring the BSE Consumer Durables, recorded gains during the week. Sensex gained 319.49 points to close at 25,838.71 while Nifty rose by 99.10 points to settle at 7,861.05.
