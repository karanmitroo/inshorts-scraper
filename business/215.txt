The Enforcement Directorate (ED) has seized 9 cars of PNB fraud accused Nirav Modi in its money laundering probe. The cars include a Rolls Royce Ghost, a Porsche Panamera and two Mercedes-Benz SUVs among others. ED has also frozen ₹94.52 crore worth mutual funds and shares of the Nirav Modi and Mehul Choksi groups in connection with the ₹11,400-crore scam.
