Tata Consultancy Services launched a 10,000 seat campus at Garima Park in Gandhinagar, Gujarat on Saturday. The software development facility was inaugurated by Gujarat CM Narendra Modi. The company's CEO, N Chandrasekaran said, "This will help expand the IT industry in Gujarat."  The Campus  is spread over 25.5 acres of land with a built-up area of 1.6 million square feet.
