The benchmark Sensex on Monday lost 108.85 points (0.40%) to end the day at 27,361.96 points, meanwhile, the broader Nifty went down by 34.90 points (0.42%) to settle at 8,260.55 points. BHEL, Sesa Goa, Bajaj Auto and Tata Steel were the major gainers, while, Coal India, HDFC, Bharti Airtel and RIL were the major laggards.
