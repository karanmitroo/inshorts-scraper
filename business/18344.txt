According to the latest wealth report, India has the highest proportion (16%) of Ultra High Net worth (UHNW) Women in the world. India, which is home to 103-billionaires, has over 1,250-UHNW individuals: those with net assets worth $30-millions or above-with a combine fortune of $95-billions. Bangalore has recorded the highest percentage increase in the number of UHNW followed by Chennai.
