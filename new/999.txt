Automaker Mahindra Two Wheelers and e-commerce platform Paytm have entered into a partnership to make the Mahindra MOJO motorcycle available online. Mahindra has opened the first two-wheeler brand store on Paytm, listing the Mahindra MOJO. The automobile manufacturer has also launched an introductory benefit of a Magnetic Tank Bag on every purchase of MOJO made through Paytm.
