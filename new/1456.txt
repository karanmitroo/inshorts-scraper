British luxury car maker Rolls-Royce on Friday launched Dawn in India at a starting price of ₹6.25 crore (ex-showroom​, Mumbai). The soft-top convertible has a 6.6-litre twin turbocharged V12 petrol engine capable of churning out 563 bhp of power. The car can go from 0-100 kmph in five seconds and has an electronically limited top speed of 250 kmph.
