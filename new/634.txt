Italian luxury sports car maker Lamborghini on Wednesday launched Huracán RWD Spyder in India at ₹3.45 crore (ex-showroom, New Delhi). The drop-top version of Hurácan RWD coupe can accelerate from 0 to 100 kmph in just 3.6 seconds and reach a maximum speed of 319 kmph. The 5.2-litre engine of the car produces 572 hp and generates 540 Nm torque.
