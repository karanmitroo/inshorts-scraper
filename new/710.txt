American automaker Chevrolet has unveiled a life-size model of Batmobile made of Lego pieces at the ongoing Detroit Auto Show. The 17-feet long and nearly 7-feet tall Batmobile was made using 3,44,187 individual Lego bricks of 17 different colours. It took 222 hours to design the car and 1,833 hours to put the Lego pieces together.
