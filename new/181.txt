Mercedes-Benz, as part of its #AMG50Years celebration in India, has launched the 'Beast of the Green Hell'. The Mercedes-AMG GT R offers a power of 585 HP at 6250 rpm and can go from 0-100 km/hr in 3.6 seconds and can generate a top speed of 318 km/hr. It comes with active rear-wheel steering and nine-way adjustable traction control system.
