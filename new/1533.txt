Japanese auto-giant Honda has launched its BR-V compact SUV, with prices starting at ₹8.75 lakh (petrol) and ₹9.90 lakh (diesel). The BR-V is a seven-seater that gets goodies such as projector headlamps, 16-inch alloy wheels, automatic climate control, rear AC and an integrated music system. The diesel motor delivers 100 PS of power, whereas the petrol variant produces 119 PS.
