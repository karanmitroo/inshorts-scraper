Maruti Suzuki's Vitara Brezza has become the highest selling Sports Utility Vehicle (SUV) in India in the month of July 2016. Brezza took the top spot by overtaking South Korean automaker Hyundai's Creta by selling 10,232 units compared to Creta's 7,981 units. Launched in March this year, July marked as the best month for sales of the SUV so far.
