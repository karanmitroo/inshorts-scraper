American automaker Ford has launched a Black Edition of its compact SUV, the Ford EcoSport at ₹8.58 lakh (ex-showroom). The edition is available from the 'Trend+' variant onwards, with both the petrol and diesel engine option. It includes blacked out elements such as the grille, alloy wheels, fog lamp bezels and more. Mechanically, the EcoSport remains unchanged.  ​
