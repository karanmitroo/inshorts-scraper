Mahindra has launched a more powerful variant ​of its TUV300 compact SUV called the mHAWK100 with prices starting at ₹8.87 lakh (ex-showroom). It is available in the top-spec T8 variant only with both manual and automatic transmissions. Power is bumped up to 101PS and torque now stands at 240Nm. Other than this, the mHAWK100 is identical to the standard TUV.
