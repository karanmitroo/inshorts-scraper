Mahindra has revealed that their upcoming compact SUV will be known as the KUV100. The small SUV boasts of a youthful design philosophy and will be introduced with a choice of 1.2 litre petrol and diesel engines. These all-new engines belong to the mFalcon engine family. The KUV will launch on 15 January 2016. Bookings start from 19th December 2015.
