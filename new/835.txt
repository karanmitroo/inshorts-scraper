A number of cars including electric vehicles were showcased at the annual Los Angeles Auto Show, which took place from November 18-27. While Bentley, Bugatti, Ferrari, McLaren, and Rolls-Royce skipped the show, Jaguar, Alfa Romeo, Lamborghini, and Mercedes participated in the show. A Chinese startup unveiled electric car 'Nio EP9', which accelerates from 0-100 kmph in 2.7 seconds.
