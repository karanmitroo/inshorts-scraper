Tata Motors today launched its latest hatchback, the Tiago (earlier called Zica) with prices starting at ₹3.20 lakh for the petrol and ₹3.94 lakh for the diesel variant. The 1.2-litre petrol motor delivers a mileage of 23.84 km/l, whereas the 1.05-litre diesel engine offers 27.28 km/l mileage. Features include an 8-speaker Harman sound system and 14-inch alloy wheels, among others.
