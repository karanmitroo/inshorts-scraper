India’s first all-electric motorcycle 'Tork T6X' was launched by Pune-based startup Tork Motorcycles at ₹1.25 lakh on Friday. To be sold in Pune, Bengaluru and Delhi initially, the bike has a top speed of 100 kmph and can travel up to 100 kilometres on single charge. The startup has already set up six charging stations in Pune and Lonavala.
