Motorscribes has released a list of the biggest stories of the automotive industry of 2015. Mercedes-Benz launching fifteen new models and the sales figures of Renault Kwid and Hyundai Creta were on the list. Tech Mahindra acquiring controlling stake in Pininfarina, Maruti Suzuki launching a luxury experience and demise of industry stalwart Brijmohan Lall Munjal were also on the list.
