British luxury cars maker Bentley has unveiled the updated 2017 Continental GT Speed coupe with a top speed of 332 km/h. It has a twin-turbo W12 633 hp engine, which can go from 0 to 96 km/h in 3.9 seconds. The starting price for the model is around ₹1.6 crore, while a black-coloured convertible model costs around ₹1.8 crore.
