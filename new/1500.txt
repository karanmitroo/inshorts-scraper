General Motors (GM) will reportedly spend $100 million to compensate 1,35,000 customers who bought 2016 Chevrolet Traverse, GMC Acadia and Buick Enclave SUVs. The company reportedly overstated the fuel economy by 1 to 2 miles per gallon for these cars. GM will give the customers a debit card or a 48-month or a 60,000-mile protection plan in compensation.
