Bengaluru-based startup Workbench Projects has partnered with Hyperloop India to build a pod prototype for Elon Musk-led SpaceX's Hyperloop competition. Hyperloop India, which is one of the five teams shortlisted for the global competition, is building the 'OrcaPod' to reach speeds of up to 460 kmph. Hyperloop India was founded in 2015 by BITS Pilani student Sibesh Kar.
