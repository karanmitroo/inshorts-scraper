A Ferrari F430 F1 Coupe formerly owned by the US President Donald Trump was bought for a final sale price of ₹1.75 crore at an auction on Saturday, while the maximum price was expected to be about ₹2.3 crore. Trump's Cadillac limousine sold for ₹44 lakh last month, and his Lamborghini Diablo fetched nearly ₹3 crore last year.
