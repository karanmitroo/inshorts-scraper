Automobile manufacturer Maruti Suzuki India on Saturday increased the price of their cars by up to ₹12,000. The hike is in the range of ₹1,000 to ₹4,000 across models and ₹5,000 to ₹12,000 for recently launched premium hatchback Baleno, the company said. Previously, Honda, Skoda, Toyota and Tata Motors, all had hiked the prices of their vehicles.
