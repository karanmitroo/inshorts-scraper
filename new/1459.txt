Chennai-based Indian motorcycle manufacturing company Royal Enfield on Wednesday reported a 37.47% rise in total sales in May at 48,604 units, as against 35,354 units in the corresponding period last year. Domestic sales grew by 36.4% to 47,232 units as against 34,615 units in the year-ago period, while exports surged 85.6% from 739 to 1,372 units.
