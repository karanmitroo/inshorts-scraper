British manufacturer of luxury vehicles, Jaguar has announced to launch its F-Pace SUV in India on October 20 at a starting price of ₹68.40 lakh, which goes up to ₹1.12 crore (ex-showroom, Delhi). The SUV's 2.0-litre 4-cylinder turbocharged Ingenium engine generates 179.5PS of power. It will be competing against the likes of Range Rover Evoque, BMW X5 and Mercedes-Benz GLE.
