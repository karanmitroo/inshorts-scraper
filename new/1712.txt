Suzuki Motorcycle launched two All Terrain Vehicles (ATVs) on Saturday– Quadsport Z400 and Ozark 250–priced at ₹8.50 lakh and ₹5.45 lakh respectively (ex-showroom Delhi). The Ozark 250 features a 245cc four-stroke engine with twin front hydraulic disc brakes and fuel capacity of 9.7 litres. The Quadsport Z400, weighing 193kg, has a 398cc four-stroke engine and can be used for off-roading.
