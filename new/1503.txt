Maruti Suzuki today launched the 2016 Alto 800 with prices starting at ₹2.49 lakh. It claims to deliver a fuel efficiency of 24.7 km/l of petrol, nearly a 9% improvement over the earlier version. The car comes with revamped interiors, remote keyless entry and two new colours. A CNG variant is also available at a starting price of ₹3.70 lakh.
