Car manufacturer Volkswagen Group, which translates to 'People's Automobile' in German, was founded on May 28, 1937. The world's second largest car manufacturer Volkswagen has three cars namely Golf, Beetle and Passat in the top 10 list of best-selling cars of all time. The Volkswagen Group comprises 12 vehicle manufacturers including Audi, Lamborghini, Bugatti, Bentley and Skoda.
