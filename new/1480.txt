German auto major Volkswagen on Sunday launched its cheapest Sedan ‘Ameo’ in India, starting at ₹5.13 lakh (ex-showroom Mumbai). The 1.2-litre petrol variant of the vehicle would be available across Volkswagen showrooms starting July 2016, while the diesel version of the car will come later. The made in India car is the company's first ever sub 4-metre sedan.
