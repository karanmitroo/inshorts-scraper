Rio Olympics bronze medal-winning wrestler Sakshi Malik will get a customised Thar SUV from automaker Mahindra. The company's Chairman and MD, Anand Mahindra ran a Twitter campaign where he asked users to tweet with #TharForSakshi in support of gifting the vehicle to Malik. "Overwhelming support for #TharForSakshi...let's check with her what colour & customization she wants," he tweeted later.
