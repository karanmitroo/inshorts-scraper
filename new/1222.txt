French automobile manufacturer Renault on Monday launched a new version of Kwid with 1.0-litre engine in India at a starting price of ₹3.82 lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi). Its 3-cylinder petrol engine produces 68 PS of power and delivers 23.01 km/l fuel efficiency. The new Kwid has been launched at a premium of ₹22,000 over the standard model.
