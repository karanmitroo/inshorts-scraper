India Kawasaki Motors has introduced two new bikes for the market, namely the Versys 650 and the KLX110. The Versys 650 is a CKD and comes with a parallel-twin engine, while the KLX110 is a 112cc off-roader. The Versys 650 is priced at ₹660,000 and the KLX 110 will retail for ₹265,000 (ex-Showroom Delhi).
