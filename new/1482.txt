Japanese automaker Nissan's subsidiary Datsun, has launched its most affordable hatchback, the redi-GO. Priced between ₹2.38 lakh and ₹3.34 lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi), it will compete against the Nano and the Renault Kwid. Powered by a 54 PS, 799 cc engine, it claims to deliver 25.17 kmpl mileage. It has features including driver airbag, power windows and an integrated music system.
