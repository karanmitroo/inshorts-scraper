British car manufacturer Jaguar on Wednesday launched 2016 version of its Jaguar XF sedan in India priced between ₹49.5 lakh – ₹61.85 lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi). Its 2.0-litre turbocharged petrol engine generates 237hp. Jaguar XF accelerates from 0-100 kmph in 8.7 seconds and has a top speed of 229 kmph. The sedan's deliveries are scheduled to begin from September 2016 end.
