British manufacturer of luxury cars Aston Martin on Friday unveiled a new convertible open-top coupe named 'Vanquish Zagato Volante' that achieves 0-100 kmph of speed in 3.7 seconds. Powered by a V12 powertrain engine, the coupe delivers 592 horsepower. The company will make only 99 units of the car and begin deliveries in 2017.
