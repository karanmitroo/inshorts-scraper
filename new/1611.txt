Ford India on Friday slashed the prices of its compact SUV EcoSport by upto ₹1.12 lakh. Applicable with immediate effect, the petrol variants of the EcoSport will be available from ₹6.68 lakh onwards and the diesel variants will start from ₹7.28 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi). Notably, the price reduction comes in three days after Maruti launched Vitara Brezza at ₹6.99 lakh.
