Mercedes-Benz has launched its first-ever SUV Coupé in India, GLE 450 AMG Coupé, at ₹86.40 lakh (ex-showroom Mumbai). With a 362 horsepower engine, the sports car can go from 0 to 100 kmph in 5.6 seconds and hit a top speed of 250 kmph. It has a fuel capacity of 93 litres and is fitted with a 360° camera system.
