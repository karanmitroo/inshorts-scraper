Mahindra & Mahindra on Monday launched​ its new SUV Bolero Power+ in India at a starting price of ₹6.59 lakh (ex-showroom, Mumbai). Its 1.5-litre mHawk-D70 engine claims to deliver 13% more power and 5% more mileage than the existing Boleros. The new Bolero shuts down the engine if any key other than the original is used to start the vehicle.
