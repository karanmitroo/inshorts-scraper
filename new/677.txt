The most expensive house listed in the US for ₹1,703 crore ($250 million) includes 10 motorcycles, 12 cars and a helicopter. The cars in the mansion include Rolls-Royce Dawn, Ferrari 488, and two of the most expensive cars in the collection, a Pagani Huayra, and a 1936 Mercedes-Benz 540K. While Pagani costs around ₹13 crore, Mercedes costs over ₹102 crore.
