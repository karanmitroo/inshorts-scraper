British luxury sports car manufacturer Aston Martin on Wednesday announced that it will make only 99 units of its Vanquish Zagato Coupe. It comes with a six-litre V12 engine which accelerates from 0-100 km/h in 3.5 seconds. The car's delivery will begin in the first quarter of 2017. Aston Martin's upcoming 'Project Nebula' hypercar will also have only 99 units.
