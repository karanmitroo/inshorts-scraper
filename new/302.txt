A green 1994 Audi A4 driven by Princess Diana has been auctioned for about ₹48 lakh, ₹18.3 lakh less than expected. Audi loaned the car to the Princess in 1994, and she drove it for about 6,400 km. The auction house called it 'one of the most photographed cars of its generation' as she rode it soon after her divorce.
