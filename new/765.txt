The most expensive car launched in India this year is the bullet-proof Mercedes-Maybach S600 Guard. Called the "safest luxury spot on earth" by Mercedes, the car is priced at ₹10.5 crore (ex-showroom Delhi). Other cars on the most expensive list include the Audi A8 L Security limousine and soft-top Rolls-Royce Dawn at ₹9.15 crore and ₹6.25 crore respectively.
