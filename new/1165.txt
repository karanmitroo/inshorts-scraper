American SUV maker Jeep on Tuesday launched the Wrangler Unlimited at ₹71.59 lakh in India. Jeep also launched Grand Cherokee Limited at ₹93 lakh, Grand Cherokee Summit at ₹1.03 crore and the Grand Cherokee SRT at ₹1.12 crore (all prices ex-showroom, Delhi). The company announced to open 10 distribution locations across India.
