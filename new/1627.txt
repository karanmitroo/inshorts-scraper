Bugatti Chiron, the world's fastest production car which can go from 0 to 100km/h in under 2.5 seconds, debuted at the Geneva Motor Show on Tuesday. The car, with an 8.0-litre W16 engine capable of producing 1500bhp and 1600Nm of torque, has a top speed of 420km/h. Only 500 units of the car, priced at $2.6 million, will be made.
