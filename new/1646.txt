Bentley has launched the Grand Limousine, based on its flagship luxury saloon - the Mulsanne. The Limousine is one meter longer and 79 mm taller than the standard version. Handcrafted by Bentley's coachbuilding division Mulliner, the Limousine gets six seats, 21-inch wheels and it's own intercom system. Prices were not revealed since the limousine is made to order.
