Tata Motors has introduced a new variant of the Safari Storme. The upgraded SUV comes with a new 2.2 litre VARICOR diesel engine that makes 156PS of power and 400Nm of torque. It also gets Tata’s new CONNECTNEXT integrated system. Prices range from ₹13.3– ₹14.6 lakh (ex-showroom, New Delhi). The new Storme comes with a 3 year/100,000km warranty.
