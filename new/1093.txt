Italian motorcycle manufacturer Ducati on Thursday launched its XDiavel and XDiavel S cruiser motorcycles in India at ₹15.87 lakh and ₹18.47 lakh respectively (all prices ex-showroom, Delhi). Powered by an L-Twin liquid cooled engine, the 1262 cc XDiavel delivers 156 hp at 9,000 rpm. XDiavel S delivers 162 hp and also has a Bluetooth-enabled infotainment system on the dashboard.
