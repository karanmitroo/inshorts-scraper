German automaker Mercedes-Benz has launched the S 500 Cabriolet in India at a price of ₹2.25 crore (ex-showroom, New Delhi). The luxury car, equipped with a 4.7-litre, V8 bi-turbo petrol engine, can go from 0-100 kmph in 4.6 seconds and has a top speed of 250 kmph. The company also presented its C-300 Cabriolet, which is priced at ₹60 lakh.
