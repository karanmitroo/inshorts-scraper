Automobile major Nissan India has launched the new Micra hatchback with a new ‘Sunshine Orange’ colour and European-style all-black sporty interior, ahead of the festive season. The car has a starting price of ₹4.55 lakh (Ex-showroom, New Delhi) and its CVT version, priced at ₹5.99 lakh, is India’s most affordable premium automatic car equipped with a continuously variable transmission (CVT).
