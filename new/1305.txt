Italian luxury automaker Lamborghini has released a video of its limited edition Centenario model which it launched to celebrate the hundredth birth anniversary of its Founder Ferruccio Lamborghini. Powered by a 759-horsepower V12 engine, the most powerful Lamborghini engine till date, the Centenario reaches 0-100 kmph in 2.8 seconds with a top speed of 350 kmph.
