Mahindra has launched a new compact SUV christened as the KUV100. It boasts of new 1.2-litre petrol and diesel engines, bold SUV styling, flexi seating and a host of innovative features. The KUV100 is priced aggressively with the range starting from ₹4.42 lakh for the petrol variant and ₹5.22 lakh for the diesel variant (ex-showroom Pune).
