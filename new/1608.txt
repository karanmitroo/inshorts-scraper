Italian supercar manufacturer, Lamborghini announced that its global sales exceeded 3,000 units for the first time in history, in FY15. Lamborghini sold a total of 3,245 units that includes 2,242 units of the Huracan range and 1,003 units of the Aventador series. The sales saw a growth of 28% while turnover increased by 39% to €872 million (approximately ₹6,425 crore).
