Mercedes-Benz on Tuesday unveiled a new luxury electric 'Style Edition Garia Golf Cart' made jointly with Scandinavian golf cart manufacturer Garia. The cart comes with a carbon fibre body, wooden decor and includes a 10.1-inch touchscreen infotainment system and an in-built refrigerator. It has a top speed of 30 kmph and travels up to 80 kilometres on a single charge.
