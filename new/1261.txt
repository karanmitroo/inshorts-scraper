Mahindra & Mahindra (M&M) on Wednesday reported a 12.36% year-on-year increment in its net profit to ₹955 crore for the quarter ended June. The firm's total revenue from operations rose by 14.05% from the year-ago period and stood at ₹11,943 crore. M&M's automobile sales grew by 10.9% to 1.21 lakh units in the quarter.
