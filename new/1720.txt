Mahindra & Mahindra (M&M) and its subsidiary Tech Mahindra on Monday agreed to acquire a 76.06% stake in Italian car designer Pininfarina for €25.3 million (₹187 crore). The firm has designed cars for Ferrari, Rolls-Royce, Maserati, and Cadillac. M&M earlier acquired South-Korean automaker SsangYong in 2011 and French PSA Peugeot Citroen’s motorcycle division this year.
