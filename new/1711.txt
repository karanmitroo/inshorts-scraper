Hyundai Motor's SUV Creta has won the 'Indian Car of the Year 2016' award. The second and third position was bagged by Maruti Suzuki Baleno and Renault Kwid respectively. This is Hyundai’s third consecutive win after Grand i10 in 2014 and Elite i20 in 2015. Creta, launched in July this year, has reportedly received over 74,000 bookings and 400,000 enquiries globally.
