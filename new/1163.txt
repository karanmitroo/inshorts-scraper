Tata Motors-owned Jaguar Land Rover on Wednesday reported a 26% increase in its retail sales with 36,926 units in August. The month's performance was driven by strong sales of the Land Rover Discovery Sport and the Jaguar F-PACE. Jaguar sales rose by 104% year-on-year to 10,868 units during the month while Land Rover sold 26,058 units, up by 9%.
