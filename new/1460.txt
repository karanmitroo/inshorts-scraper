Automakers recalled 22.4 lakh vehicles in India over safety concerns between July 2012 and May 2016, according to an analysis of Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers (SIAM) data by Mint. The highest number of recalls were made by Honda at 5,13,768, followed by Volkswagen (4,02,007), Ford (3,70,054) and Maruti Suzuki (2,82,875). Notably, 10.1 lakh cars were recalled in 2015 alone.
