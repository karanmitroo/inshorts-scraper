Silicon Valley startup Atieva has released a video of its electric van 'Edna' outrunning a BMW i8 and a Dodge Viper in a 400-metre drag race. 'Edna' completed the race in 11.3 seconds, going 0-100 kmph in 2.94 seconds, reaching a top speed of 188 kmph. In July, Atieva had released a video of 'Edna' outrunning a Tesla and Ferrari.
