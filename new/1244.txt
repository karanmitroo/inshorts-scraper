Italian luxury automaker Lamborghini unveiled its latest Centenario Roadster on Friday and it was sold out even before its debut. Based on the previous Centenario coupe, the roofless version sports a top speed of over 350 kmph with a 0-100 kmph acceleration in 2.9 seconds. Only 20 Roadsters will reportedly be built, and will be priced at $2.3 million.
