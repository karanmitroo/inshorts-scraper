German carmaker Porsche on Wednesday launched the updated 2017 Porsche 911 models Carrera and Carrera S in India at a starting price of ₹1.39 crore and ₹1.42 crore respectively. The new models feature styling updates and three-litre turbocharged flat-six engines which deliver 370 hp and 420 hp in the Carrera and Carrera S respectively.
