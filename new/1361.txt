Mahindra & Mahindra on Wednesday launched its Scorpio SUV with 'Intelli-Hybrid’ technology at ₹12.84 lakh (ex-showroom, Navi Mumbai) for the top-end S10-2WD model. The Intelli-Hybrid technology claims to reduce the fuel consumption by up to 7% by assisting the engine with electric power during acceleration. It comes with 2.2-litre mHAWK engine that delivers 120PS of power.
