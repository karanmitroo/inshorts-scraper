Britain-based race series for driverless electric cars Roborace has unveiled its first self-driving race car 'Robocar' which has a top speed of 320 kmph. The Robocar is bigger than a standard Formula 1 car and measures 4.5 metres long and 2 metres wide. It has two radars, five LiDARs, 18 ultrasonic sensors, two optical speed sensors and six AI-driven cameras.
