The Renault-Nissan Alliance plant in India rolled out its millionth vehicle on Friday. The landmark vehicle was a Nissan Micra that rolled off the line at 12:15pm. This facility, set up in Oragadam near Chennai, is the largest Alliance facility in the world. It commenced production in 2010 and manufactures over 32 models across the Renault, Nissan and Datsun brands.
