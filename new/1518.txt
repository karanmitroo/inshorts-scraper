Luxury automobile-manufacturer Rolls-Royce on Friday unveiled a six-piece luggage set for Rolls-Royce owners. The $45,854 set of the Wraith Luggage Collection - three "Long Weekender" bags, two "Grand Tourer" valises and a "Garment Carrier", was designed to complement the company's cars. Every piece of the set can be customized to mimic the design features found in the actual Rolls-Royce Wraith.
