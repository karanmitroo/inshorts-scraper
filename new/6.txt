The new Mercedes-Benz S-Class has been launched in India, with the diesel S 350 d priced at ₹1.33 crore and the petrol S 450 at ₹1.37 crore. The S-Class 350 d is India’s first ‘BS VI compliant–Made in India, for India’ vehicle. Touted as the world's best car driven by intelligence, its features include latest radar-based driving assistance systems and class-leading Rear Seat Comfort Package.
