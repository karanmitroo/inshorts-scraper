Japanese auto-giant Toyota has launched the Innova Crysta, with prices ranging between ₹13.84 lakh and ₹20.78 lakh. The Crysta debuts two all-new diesel engines, that produce 150 and 174 PS of power respectively. Available as a 7-seater or an 8-seater, it gets new features such as projector headlamps, automatic climate control, seven airbags, touchscreen audio system among others.
