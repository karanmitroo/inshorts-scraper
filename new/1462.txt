Tata Motors on Monday reported a three-fold jump in consolidated net profit to ₹5,177 crore for the quarter ended March. The rise was attributed to strong sales by Jaguar Land Rover and higher domestic demand for trucks. Further, for the entire fiscal 2015-16, the company posted a consolidated net profit of ₹11,024 crore, down 21.18% from ₹13,986 crore in 2014-15.
