A custom-made Pagani Zonda supercar reportedly crashed with a Maybach 62S in Tokyo, Japan earlier this week. The purple Pagani Zonda Zozo was commissioned by Japanese billionaire and Founder of online fashion retail website Zozotown, Yusaku Maezawa. Powered by a 7.3-litre V12 engine, the model delivers 760 horsepower with a reported top speed of 345 kmph.
