Rolls-Royce has unveiled picnic baskets especially designed for its Phantom and Drophead Coupes. Each of the baskets is crafted from walnut wood and leather matching its accompanying car's exterior colour. The baskets are equipped with fine dining ware including a cutting board, Rolls-Royce embroidered napkins, and stainless steel cutlery. Only 50 units of such baskets will be made by Rolls-Royce.
