Sports-car Bugatti Chiron can consume over 10 litres of petrol in under a minute, while running at a top speed of nearly 420 km per hour. The car, with an 8.0-litre W16 engine, is capable of producing 1500 bhp and 1600 Nm of torque. The automaker's founder, Ettore Bugatti was born 135 years ago on September 15, 1881.
