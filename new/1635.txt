The latest supercar from British marque Aston Martin - the DB11, debuted at the ongoing Geneva Motor Show. The DB11, houses a 5.2-litre twin-turbocharged V12 engine. The engine produces 600 bhp and 700 Nm of torque, making the DB11 the most powerful production DB model ever. The top speed is rated at 322 km/h, while 0-100 km/h takes 3.9 seconds.
