German luxury automaker BMW on Tuesday launched its 5 Series variant, the M Sport sedan in India at a starting price of ₹54 lakh (ex-showroom, pan India). Powered by a 2.0-litre four-cylinder diesel engine, the M Sport produces 190 horsepower. The sedan can go from 0-100 kmph in 7.7 seconds and has a top speed of 233 kmph.
