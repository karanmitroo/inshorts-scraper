The most expensive car launched in India this year is Lamborghini's Aventador S Roadster, priced at ₹5.79 crore. Italian sports car manufacturer Ferrari also launched the GTC4 Lusso and its V8-engined model, GTC4 Lusso T, this year at ₹5.2 crore and ₹4.2 crore respectively. Other cars include Lamborghini's Huracan Performante at ₹3.97 crore and Porsche 911 GT3 at ₹2.31 crore.
