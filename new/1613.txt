Prices of the sports bike Suzuki Hayabusa were slashed by ₹2.38 lakh in India after Suzuki Motor Corporation started assembly of the bike at its plant in Gurgaon, Haryana. The Hayabusa will now be imported as CKD (Completely Knocked Down) Kits. It will be available at Suzuki Big Bike Dealerships for ₹13.57 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi) by the end March.
