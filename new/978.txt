The newly launched Audi A4 is the only car in its segment to offer a class leading ‘Audi Virtual Cockpit - a 12.3-inch LCD screen replacing the traditional mechanical speedometer and other dials’. The other unique features of the all-new A4 are the Next-Generation MMI and Audi Smartphone interface. The new A4 was launched last month starting at ₹38.1 lakh.
