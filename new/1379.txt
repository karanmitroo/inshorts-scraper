British luxury sports car manufacturer Aston Martin is recalling 6,076 cars to fix an issue with the vehicles' door locks. The recalled models include Aston Martin DB9, DBS, Rapide, V8 Vantage, Virage, Vanquish, V12 Vantage and V12 Zagato. The door unlocking components of these cars were reportedly not functioning properly, which may trap occupants inside the cars.
