India Yamaha Motor on Friday produced its one-millionth scooter in the country. The one-millionth scooter, a 'Fascino', was made at the company's Surajpur plant situated at Greater Noida. The company entered the country's scooter market in 2012 with the launch of Cygnus Ray scooter aimed at females. The company has set a target to produce a million vehicles this year.
