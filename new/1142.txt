Olympic medalists PV Sindhu and Sakshi Malik were handed over customised Mahindra Thar vehicles for their performance in Rio, as promised by Chairman Anand Mahindra. Sakshi’s red-coloured Thar has been customised with a bronze coloured front fascia and matching stripes which symbolise her bronze medal, while Sindhu’s Thar has been finished in a silver colour with matching stripes.
