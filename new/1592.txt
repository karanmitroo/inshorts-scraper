Indian auto giant, Tata Motors will launch the Tiago hatchback on April 6, 2016. The official launch was originally slated for January 20, 2016 and was later postponed to March 28. The hatchback can be booked across Tata dealerships for a token amount of Rs. 10,000. The Tiago will compete with the Maruti Celerio, Hyundai Grand i10 and Chevrolet Beat.
