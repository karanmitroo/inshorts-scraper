Hindustan Times has compiled a list of cars “that turned heads” in 2015. The list includes Renault's low-cost Kwid and Maruti Suzuki’s premium hatchback Baleno in the economical segment with Hyundai Creta among SUVs. Suzuki's crossover S-Cross has also been mentioned. In the luxury segment, the newspaper lists Mercedes’ AMG GT-S, Maybach S500 and S600 and the BMW i8.
