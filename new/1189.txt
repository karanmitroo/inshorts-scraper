Maruti Suzuki's domestic sales rose 12.2% to 1,19,931 units in August compared to the year-ago period, the automaker said in a statement on Thursday. The utility vehicle segment boosted the sales, as the number of such units sold more than doubled to 16,806 as against 7,836 last year. Its exports during the month also rose 10.8% to 12,220 units.
