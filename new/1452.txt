To celebrate 50 years of its super sports car Miura, Lamborghini unveiled the 'Aventador Miura Homage', a special edition of the current Aventador coupe. Lamborghini said that it will produce only 50 units of the car. Powered by a V12 engine, the coupe can accelerate from 0-100 km/h in 2.9 seconds and has a top speed of 350 km/h.
