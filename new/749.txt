Royal Enfield has launched its Classic 350 motorcycle in three Redditch series variants – Red, Green and Blue, in India at ₹1.46 lakh (on-road, Delhi). The series is inspired by 1950’s motorcycles manufactured in Redditch, a UK town known as the birthplace of Royal Enfield motorcycles. Powered by a 346cc Twin spark single cylinder engine, the motorcycle produces 19.8 bhp.
