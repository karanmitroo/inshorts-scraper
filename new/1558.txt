Hennessey Venom GT Spyder has become the world's fastest convertible car after reaching a top speed of 265.6 mph (427.4 km/h), beating the previous record set by Bugatti Veyron Super Sport Vitesse at 254.04 mph (408.8 km/h). The car can do 0 to 60 mph in less than 2.4 seconds. Priced $1.3 million, Hennessey will build three Venom GT Spyders.
