South Korean automaker Hyundai on Friday unveiled India-bound 2017 Verna model at the Chengdu Motor Show in China. The sedan will be available in China in 1.4 or 1.6-litre petrol engine delivering 100 hp and 123 hp respectively. It features new body panels, 8-inch touchscreen infotainment system and LED tail lights. It's expected to be launched in India in 2017.
