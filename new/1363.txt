Honda Motorcycle and Scooter India has said that its Activa scooter has surpassed Hero Splendor to become India's largest selling two-wheeler in January-June 2016. This is the first time in 17 years that a scooter topped volume sales in the domestic two-wheeler market "ending...monopoly of motorcycles". HMSI further said Activa "added 1,97,295 units over last year in just six months".
