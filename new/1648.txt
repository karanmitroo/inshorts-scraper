Jaguar launched its new sports sedan, the XE at the Auto Expo today. The XE will be powered by a 2-litre petrol engine available in two versions, a 197bhp and the other 237bhp. They are priced at ₹39.9 lakh and ₹47.5 lakh (ex Delhi) respectively, and come with 8-speed automatic transmission. Sale commences by the end of the month.
