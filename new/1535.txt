German automaker BMW has launched the petrol variant of the 3-Series sedan. Called the 320i, it is available in two trims viz. Prestige - ₹36.9 lakh and Luxury - ₹42.7 lakh. It is powered by a 2.0-litre turbocharged petrol motor developing 184PS of power and 270Nm of torque. Features include a sunroof, 17-inch alloy wheels and LED headlamps. ​
