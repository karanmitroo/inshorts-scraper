To celebrate the first anniversary of its Creta SUV, Korean automaker Hyundai on Friday launched three new Creta variants in India. The variants include the Anniversary Edition (SE) priced at ₹13.76 lakh, the Executive variant at ₹9.99 lakh and the S+ diesel variant at ₹13.58 lakh (all prices ex-showroom, Delhi). A new Earth Brown colour option has also been introduced.
