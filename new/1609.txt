Mahindra owned Ssangyong unveiled its latest SUV concept called the SIV-2, at the Geneva Motor Show. The concept borrows design cues from the Ssangyong Tivoli, that was showcased at the 2016 Auto Expo. The SIV-2 is a hybrid, powered by a 1.5-litre turbo petrol engine aided by a small electric motor. The production version will go on sale in 2018.
