Ride-hailing app Uber has partnered with German sports car maker Porsche to allow its Mumbai users to book the car between 2 pm to 4 pm on October 16, 2016. Porsche rides will be available under a 'Supercar' option in the app. Selected riders will also get a chance to experience Porsche's Boxster roadster and win Uber's 'Swag Baskets'.
