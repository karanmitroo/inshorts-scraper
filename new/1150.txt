Maruti Suzuki's Vitara Brezza has topped sales in the SUV segment with 31,930 units sold, and the company's Ciaz topped sales in the Sedan segment in the April-July period. Ciaz sold 18,852 units, overtaking Honda City, which sold 16,996 units in the same period. According to reports, Maruti Suzuki's market share has grown to 44.4% in the top 10 cities.
