Nissan-owned brand Datsun on Thursday launched special editions of its GO hatchback and GO+ multi-purpose vehicle in India, priced at ₹4.07 lakh and ₹4.78 lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi) respectively. The cars, in addition to featuring a limited-edition 'Style' moniker, will come in a new colour option called ‘New Blue’, and will be on sale from August through October 2016.
