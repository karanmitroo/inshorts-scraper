Two-wheeler manufacturer Bajaj Auto has reported a 6.68% year-on-year growth in the second quarter profit, which stood at ₹1,122 crore. Bajaj’s income from operations was up marginally by 0.38% from the corresponding period a year ago to ₹6,432 crore. However, Bajaj Auto sold 10.3 lakh units in the quarter, compared to 10.56 lakh units sold in the year-ago period.
