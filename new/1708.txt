Mercedes-Benz India launched two new variants of the A-class– A180 petrol and A200d diesel– for ₹24.95 lakh and ₹25.95 lakh (ex-showroom, Mumbai) respectively. The new features include updated headlamps, new bumpers and revamped interiors. The A180 features 1.6-litre, four-cylinder turbo engine which churns out 120bhp while A200D has 2.1-litre, four-cylinder engine with a maximum power of 136bhp.
