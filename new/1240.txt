Japanese automaker Nissan will celebrate August 21, 2016 as 'Happy Bolt Day' in honour of athlete Usain Bolt's 30th birthday. Fans have been invited to imitate Bolt's famous lightning bolt pose on social media with the hashtag #HappyBoltDay. Interestingly, Bolt was named Nissan's Global Director of Excitement in 2012, serving as an ambassador for the sports car GT-R.
