Rupert Stadler, the CEO of German automobile manufacturer Audi, has announced that the company will introduce a new electrified car every year starting 2018. It will kick-off the plan with its all-electric crossover utility vehicle based on the E-Tron Quattro Concept, unveiled at the 2015 Frankfurt Motor Show. Stadler also confirmed that the next-generation Audi A8 will debut in 2017.
