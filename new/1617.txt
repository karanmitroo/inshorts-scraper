The Lazareth LM 847 is a V8 powered Quad motorcycle. It is propelled by a 4.7-liter  32-valve V-8 engine made by Maserati. This engine churns out 470hp at 7,000rpm and a torque of 620Nm at 4,750rpm. It gets dual 420mm disc brakes with Nissin calipers up front and dual 255mm discs with Brembo calipers at the rear.
