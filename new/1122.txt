Japanese automaker Toyota today launched its hatchback Etios Liva and C-segment sedan Etios in India at a starting price of ₹5.24 lakh and ₹6.43 lakh (ex-showroom, Mumbai) respectively. Both vehicles carry forward their existing 1.2-litre and 1.5-litre petrol engines and deliver 80PS and 90PS respectively. Also available in 1.4-litre diesel variant, the car delivers 68PS.
