Jaguar Land Rover's luxury arm Jaguar on Monday unveiled the 'I-PACE' SUV, the company's first ever all-electric vehicle. Powered by a 90 kilowatt-hour lithium-ion battery pack, it generates 400 horsepower and accelerates from 0-100 kmph in 4 seconds. Aimed to travel about 354 kilometres on one charge, the car is scheduled to launch in 2018.
