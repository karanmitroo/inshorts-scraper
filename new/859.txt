Lexus, the luxury vehicle division of Japanese automaker Toyota, has unveiled its design for a spaceship called 'Skyjet' for the upcoming science fiction movie 'Valerian and the City of a Thousand Planets' starring Cara Delevingne. The Skyjet is a 28th-century single-seat pursuit   craft and features Lexus' "spindle" grille with the headlight design of the 2018 Lexus LC coupe.
