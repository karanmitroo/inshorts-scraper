The Bugatti Chiron, which can go from 0 to 100km/h in under 2.5 seconds, is the world's fastest production car priced at $2.6 million. The car, with an 8.0-litre W16 engine capable of producing 1500 bhp, has a top speed of 420 km/h. The company's founder Ettore Arco Isidoro Bugatti passed away 69 years ago on August 21, 1947.
