Mercedes-Benz part-owned Italian motorcycle brand MV Agusta, in partnership with Pune-based Kinetic group, has launched five luxury bikes in India priced between ₹16.78 lakh and ₹35.71 lakh (ex-showroom Pune). The company also plans to launch F4 RC model in India later in the year priced around ₹50 lakh, which would make it the most expensive two-wheeler in the Indian market.
