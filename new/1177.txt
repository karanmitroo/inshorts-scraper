Maruti Suzuki launched its first light commercial vehicle (LCV) named 'Super Carry' on Thursday at ₹4.03 lakh (ex-showroom, Ahmedabad). The LCV comes with a 793-cc engine that delivers a mileage of 22.07 kmpl. It has a loading capacity of 740 kg and a 3.25-square metre loading deck. The company has invested about ₹300 crore for the LCV's development.
