American SUV maker Jeep is reportedly working on locally-made compact SUVs in India that will be priced between ₹10-20 lakh. Jeep localised SUVs will compete against Maruti Suzuki Vitara Brezza, Hyundai Creta and Ford EcoSport. The brand entered the Indian market on Tuesday with its Wrangler and Grand Cherokee models at a starting price of ₹71.59 lakh.
