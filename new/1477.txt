Celebrating the 2016 UEFA Euro Championship, Mercedes-Benz India has launched 'Sport Editions' of the A-Class, GLA and CLA. While the Sport Edition A-Class starts from ₹25.95 lakh, the Sport Editions of CLA and GLA start from ₹33.4 lakh and ₹34.23 lakh respectively. The versions include different bumpers, side skirts and mirror covers, available in limited numbers until July 10.
