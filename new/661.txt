Mahindra Marine has unveiled the Mahindra Odyssea 55, its new flagship yacht. The 55-feet-long luxury yacht, which features a saloon and master cabin with a personal bar area, can travel at speeds of up to 48 kmph. "Our flagship Mahindra Odyssea 55 series, built to suit Indian conditions, embodies 'Make in India' in its true form", CEO Hiten Ghelani said.
