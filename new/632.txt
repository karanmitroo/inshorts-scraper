India’s leading luxury carmaker Mercedes-Benz launched the A-Class and B-Class ‘Night Edition’ cars starting at ₹27.31 lakh (ex-showroom, Pune). “The trendy and fashionable ‘Night Edition’ are a synthesis of youthful design, modern luxury and fantastic performance, designed for the ‘go getters’ who believe in arriving in style wherever they go,” said Roland Folger, MD & CEO, Mercedes-Benz India.
