Automobile major Fiat has launched the ‘The New Urban Cross’ at an introductory starting price of ₹6.85 lakh (Ex-Showroom Delhi) with powerful engines – 145PS Abarth & 93PS Powertech. The car, which is available in both diesel & petrol, comes up with 12.7cm touch-screen navigation system as standard, dual-tone interior with optional designer package, alloy-wheels, airbags & ABS with EBD.
