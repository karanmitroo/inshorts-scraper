German automaker Mercedes-Benz on Thursday launched an Indian-assembled version of GLC-Class SUV in India at ₹47.90 lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi). The 220d Sport has been priced at ₹51.50 lakh and the GLC 300 Sport petrol variant costs ₹51.90 lakh. The SUV's 2.2-litre diesel engine generates 170PS of power. The vehicles will be assembled at the automaker's manufacturing facility in Chakan, Pune.
