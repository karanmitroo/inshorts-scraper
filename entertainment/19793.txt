Padma Bhushan awardee Actor Pran(93), who received the 44th Dadasaheb Phalke Award this year, passed away Friday at the Lilavati hospital, Mumbai. Pran made his debut from "Yamla Jat" and acted in over 400 films during a career span of almost six decades. Among them, the notable ones are Ziddi, Madhumati, Ram Aur Shyam, Devdas, Aadmi, Zanjeer and Amardeep.
