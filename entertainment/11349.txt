The Ranbir Kapoor, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Anushka Sharma starrer 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' (ADHM) has earned ₹35.60 crore in its opening weekend. The film earned ₹13.30 crore on Friday, ₹13.10 crore on Saturday and ₹9.20 crore on Sunday. ADHM earned ₹41.05 crore overseas, which is the highest opening weekend internationally for director Karan Johar and Ranbir.
