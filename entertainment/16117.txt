Priyanka Chopra, while wishing her fans a happy Diwali on Sunday, released a poster of her character Kashibai from Sanjay Leela Bhansali's forthcoming production 'Bajirao Mastani'. Priyanka plays the wife of Maratha Peshwa Bajirao I in the film. While Bajirao's role is played by Ranveer Singh, Deepika Padukone features as Mastani. 'Bajirao Mastani' is scheduled to release on December 18.
