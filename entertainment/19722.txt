Salman Khan, who charged Rs 2.5-crore/episode for 'Bigg Boss-6', is allegedly playing the host of 'Bigg Boss-7' for a whooping Rs 5-crore/episode. The actor, who will appear in 26 episodes, will get a total of Rs 130-crore, thereby becoming the highest paid actor on the television. TOI sources report that he will shoot two episodes in a day per week.
