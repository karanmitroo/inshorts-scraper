The teaser for Prabhudeva, Sonu Sood and Tamannaah starrer horror comedy Hindi-language film 'Tutak Tutak Tutiya' was released on Monday. "What better way to start the campaign with the blessings of Lord Ganesha... the team decided to break a teaser to capture the vibe of the film," said producer Vashu Bhagnani. The film is scheduled to release on October 7.
