The Salman Khan and Anushka Sharma starrer 'Sultan', which released on Wednesday, is a "thorough crowd-pleaser", wrote India Today. The film is "a cocktail of sportsmanship, drama, romance, patriotism", it added. Meanwhile, NDTV wrote, "Sultan remains a Salman Khan vehicle, pure and simple". The Ali Abbas Zafar directorial is rated 3.5/5 (India Today, Zee News) and 3/5 (HT, Indian Express).
