Actor Salman Khan was seen attending the wedding reception of his driver Ashok Singh's son Shekhar. Ashok is one of Salman's oldest drivers. The actor's father Salim Khan, brother Sohail Khan, sisters Arpita and Alvira, brother-in-law Atul Agnihotri and nephew Aahil also attended the function. Actor Suraj Pancholi and music composer Himesh Reshammiya were also seen at the reception.
