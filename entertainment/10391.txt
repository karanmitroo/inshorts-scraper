Late Bollywood actor-director Raj Kapoor's 1964 film 'Sangam' had two intervals. The film, which was 3 hours and 58 minutes long, earned Kapoor the Filmfare Award for 'Best Director'. It also marked his first colour film. Kapoor's 1970 film 'Mera Naam Joker' also had two intervals. December 14, 2016, marked the late actor's 92nd birth anniversary.
