The first look of Riteish Deshmukh from the upcoming musical drama ‘Banjo’ was revealed on Tuesday. Ritesh, who plays a band member in the movie, can be seen sitting next to drums sporting long hair. Ravi Jadhav’s directorial debut ‘Banjo’, also features Nargis Fakhri as an American DJ. The music for the movie has been composed by Vishal and Shekhar.
