Television actors Gurmeet Choudhary and wife Debina Bonnerjee have announced that they'll adopt two girls, Pooja (6) and her first cousin Lata (9) based in Bihar. The couple decided to adopt them when they realised that the girls would've to work as domestic helps to sustain themselves after Lata's father passed away. Gurmeet revealed, "They'll shift with us by 2018."
