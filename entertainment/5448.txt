Tubelight has become Salman Khan's eleventh film to earn ₹100 crore in India, after posting earnings of ₹106.86 crore within a week of its release. With this, the actor continues to hold the record of having the maximum number of Bollywood movies in the ₹100 crore club. Sultan and Dabangg are among his other films which earned ₹100 crore.
