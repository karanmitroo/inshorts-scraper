Karni Sena convenor Lokendra Singh Kalvi has said that they will never allow the screening of Sanjay Leela Bhansali's 'Padmavati' anywhere in India or abroad. He added, "Let there be a complete ban on the release of Padmavati." Talking about the song 'Ghoomar', Kalvi said that they do not want any changes but a complete ban on the song.
