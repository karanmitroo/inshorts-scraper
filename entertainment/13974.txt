'Jag Ghoomeya', the new song from the Salman Khan and Anushka Sharma starrer 'Sultan', was released on Tuesday. It has been composed by the music duo 'Vishal & Shekhar' and sung by Rahat Fateh Ali Khan. Notably, reports state that this song was initially sung by Arijit Singh but his version was removed by Salman owing to an alleged dispute.
