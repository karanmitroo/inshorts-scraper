A picture which shows actors Ranbir Kapoor and Katrina Kaif celebrating filmmaker Anurag Basu's birthday on the sets of a promotional shoot for the upcoming film 'Jagga Jasoos' has surfaced online. Ranbir and Katrina reportedly dated each other for six years before splitting last year. They have worked together in films including 'Ajab Prem Ki Ghazab Kahani' and 'Raajneeti'.
