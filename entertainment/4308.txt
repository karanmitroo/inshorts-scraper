Bollywood celebrities including Riteish Deshmukh, Abhishek Bachchan, Genelia Deshmukh, Sonu Sood and Anil Kapoor took to Twitter to wish actor Suniel Shetty on his 56th birthday on Friday. Riteish wrote, "A man who defies age...his fitness would put any young actor to shame," while Abhishek wrote, "To someone who has been like an elder brother to me...Happy Birthday anna."
