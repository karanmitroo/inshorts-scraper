Choreographer Remo D'Souza, who has directed two films under the 'ABCD' franchise, has debunked the rumours claiming actor Tiger Shroff has been finalised for 'ABCD 3'. "I'm doing an action film with Tiger... I haven't even thought about 'ABCD 3', how can Tiger be a part of it?", Remo clarified at the promo shoot of his upcoming dance reality show.
