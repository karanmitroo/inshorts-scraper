Actor Randeep Hooda on Wednesday launched the official motion poster of his upcoming film 'Main Aur Charles'. Hooda will play the role of serial killer Charles Shobhraj in the Prawaal Raman directorial. In the poster, Randeep can be seen holding a cigarette, sporting a look from the 1970s -- wearing a two-piece suit, tie, flat cap and bell-bottomed trousers.
