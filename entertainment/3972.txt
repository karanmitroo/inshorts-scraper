Emma Stone, who was named World's Highest-Paid Actress by Forbes, earned less than Shah Rukh Khan, who is India's highest paid actor. While Emma earned ₹166 crore ($26 million) during the 12-month scoring period, Shah Rukh had total earnings of ₹243.7 crore ($38 million). Notably, 14 actors, including Shah Rukh, earned more than Emma Stone during the 12-month scoring period.
