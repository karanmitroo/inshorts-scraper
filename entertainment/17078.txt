Vishal Bharadwaj has reportedly signed Kangana Ranaut, Shahid Kapoor and Saif Ali Khan for his upcoming project 'Rangoon'. To a query on Twitter asking about the cast of the film apart from Saif and Shahid, Bharadwaj's assistant Gillani Shahrukh replied, "The Queen!" (a film Kangana won National Award for). The shoot for the film will begin soon, he added.
