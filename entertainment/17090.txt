Actor Nawazuddin Siddiqui will be seen portraying the role of a Pakistani journalist in Salman Khan-starrer 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan', which will be released on July 17. Siddiqui and Salman were last seen together in 'Kick'. “The makers are very excited about their coming together again... the duo share a very special relationship in the film,” a spokesperson of the film said.
