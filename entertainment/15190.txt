Celebrities including Priyanka Chopra, Lady Gaga and Charlize Theron wore designer wear, ranging from Zuhair Murad to Dior, for the 88th Academy Awards. Priyanka Chopra opted for a translucent white couture gown from Zuhair Murad while Charlize Theron wore a red Dior gown with a plunging neckline. Meanwhile, Lady Gaga wore a Brandon Maxwell jumpsuit with a gown-like silhouette.
