The trailer of 'Kyaa Kool Hain Hum 3', dubbed as India's first porn-com, crossed the mark of 10 million views on YouTube. Breaking the news on Twitter, Aftab Shivdasani said, "10MILLION views!! Thankyou to all for showing this love!! Get ready for Jan 22,2016." The film, also starring Tusshar Kapoor, is produced by Ekta Kapoor and directed by Umesh Ghadge.
