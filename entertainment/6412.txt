Actor Akshay Kumar on Twitter condoled the demise of NRI businessman Mathunny Mathews, who inspired his role of Ranjit Katyal in 2016 film 'Airlift'. Mathews played a key role in evacuating Indians based in Kuwait during the Iraqi invasion in 1990. Notably, Akshay won the National Film Award for best actor for his performance in 'Airlift' along with 'Rustom'.
