Actor Anupam Kher film 'The Headhunter’s Calling', which also stars actors Gerard Butler and William Dafoe, is set to premiere at the Toronto International Film Festival (TIFF) in September. "I came to... Mumbai on June 3, 1981, with ₹37 in my pocket... and I can say that I have done fairly well," said Kher while sharing the news on Twitter.
