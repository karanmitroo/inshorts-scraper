Shah Rukh Khan starrer 'Fan' which was scheduled to release on Aug 14 has been postponed to complete 'never-seen-before' VFX, announced Yash Raj Films on Twitter. The film's new release date has not been decided so far. Instead of 'Fan', Karan Johar produced 'Brothers', starring Akshay Kumar, Siddharth Malhotra and Jacqueline Fernandez will release on Aug 14.
