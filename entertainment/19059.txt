Actor-director Satish Kaushik's horror-drama flick Gang of Ghosts, featuring Sharman Joshi, Rajpal Yadav, Anupam Kher, Mahie Gill, Meera Chopra, Parambrata Chatterjee and Jackie Shroff, released this Friday. The movie has been rated 1 (koimoi.com), 1.5 (Indian Express; DNA; NDTV), 2 (Mumbai Mirror), and 2.5 (Times of India). The movie is the remake of Bengali hit 'Bhooter Bhabishyat'.
