Veteran actor Anupam Kher on Monday revealed on Twitter that he will be playing the role of MS Dhoni's father, Paan Singh, in the upcoming biopic of the cricketer. Titled 'MS Dhoni: The Untold Story', the film features Sushant Singh essaying the title role. The film, being directed by Neeraj Pandey, also stars Kiara Advani as Dhoni's wife, Sakshi.
