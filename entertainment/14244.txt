The teaser for the Mohanlal starrer Malayalam film 'Pulimurugan' was released on Friday. The 56-year-old actor plays the titular role in the action adventure film. "Hold your breath and watch your step. Murugan is out for a short hunt," tweeted Mohanlal while sharing the teaser. Directed by Vysakh, the film also stars Kamalini Mukherjee. It will release on July 7.
