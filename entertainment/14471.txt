'Chitta Ve', the first song from the Shahid Kapoor starrer 'Udta Punjab' was released on Wednesday. Composed by Amit Trivedi, the song has been sung by Babu Haabi, Shahid Mallya and Bhanu Prtap. Directed by Abhishek Chaubey, the film also stars Kareena Kapoor Khan, Alia Bhatt and Diljit Dosanjh. The film is scheduled to release on June 17.
