'Just Go To Hell Dil', a new song from Shah Rukh Khan and Alia Bhatt's upcoming film 'Dear Zindagi' has been released. It has been composed by Amit Trivedi, sung by Sunidhi Chauhan, with lyrics by Kausar Munir. Also starring actors Kunal Kapoor and Angad Bedi, the film, directed by Gauri Shinde, will release on November 25.
