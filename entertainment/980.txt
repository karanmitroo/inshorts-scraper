Bollywood stars Amitabh Bachchan, Aishwarya Rai and Karan Johar, among others posed for a selfie with the Israeli PM Benjamin Netanyahu at the event 'Shalom Bollywood'. While addressing the event, Netanyahu said, "Amitabh Bachchan has 30 million more Twitter followers than I do." He shared that selfie on Twitter, captioned, "Will my Bollywood selfie beat Hollywood selfie at the Oscars?"
