Zee TV, along with the team of forthcoming Bollywood entertainer "Happy New Year", has launched a new reality show - "Dil Se Naachein Indiawaale". "If 'Dance India Dance' is about celebrating dance as art form, Dil Se Naachein...' is about celebrating it as a 'heart' form," said Punit Goenka, ZEE CEO. The show will go on air Oct 11.
