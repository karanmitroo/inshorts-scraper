The first song of the Amitabh Bachchan, Farhan Akhtar and Aditi Rao Hydari starrer 'Wazir' was unveiled on Friday. Titled 'Tere Bin', the song traces the love story of Farhan and Aditi. It has been sung by Sonu Nigam and Shreya Ghoshal while its lyrics have been written by producer Vidhu Vinod Chopra. 'Wazir' will release on January 8, 2016.
