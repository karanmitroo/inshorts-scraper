Megastar Amitabh Bachchan, Salman Khan, Lata Mangeshkar, AR Rahman and other Bollywood celebrities will come together for an event to raise funds for the Uttarakhand flood victims.  Big B will lend his voice to recite 'Mera Desh', an anthem created by Prasoon Joshi, set to Shankar-Ehsaan-Loy's music during the performance titled 'Saath Hain Hum Uttarakhand'.
