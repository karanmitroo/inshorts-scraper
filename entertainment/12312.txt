Actor Akshay Kumar has shared a video on Twitter of flying a kite from the banks of the river Ganga. "Impromptu kite flying session on the banks of River Ganga... BEST EXPERIENCE EVER!!! Couldn't be happier #simplejoys," tweeted the 49-year-old actor. He is currently shooting in Varanasi for his upcoming film 'Jolly LLB 2'.
