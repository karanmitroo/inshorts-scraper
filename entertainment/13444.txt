The Hindi trailer for Disney's fantasy adventure film 'The BFG', featuring the voices of actors Amitabh Bachchan, Parineeti Chopra and Gulshan Grover, has been released. While Bachchan lends his voice to the 'Big Friendly Giant' or the 'BFG', Parineeti has voiced the character 'Sophie'. Directed by Steven Spielberg, the film will release in India in English, Telugu and Tamil also.
