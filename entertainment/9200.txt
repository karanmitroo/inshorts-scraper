The Shah Rukh Khan, Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Mahira Khan starrer 'Raees' entered the ₹100 crore club on Monday, the sixth day of its release. Film trade analyst Ramesh Bala tweeted that the film approximately collected ₹7.5 crore early on Monday, bringing the total earnings to ₹100.74 crore. Notably, 'Raees' is Shah Rukh's seventh film to enter the ₹100 crore club.
