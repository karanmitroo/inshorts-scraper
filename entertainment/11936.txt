Actress Anushka Sharma has shared a video of her dancing to live music on the streets of Lisbon, Portugal. The actress wrote alongside the image, "[M]aking most of it #Lisbon #OffDay #DontMissTheOpportunityTobeFree." She is currently in the city along with Shah Rukh Khan to shoot for their upcoming film ''The Ring', which is being directed by Imtiaz Ali.
