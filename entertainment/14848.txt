The Kareena and Arjun Kapoor-starrer 'Ki & Ka' has collected ₹7.30 crore at the box office on the first day. According to reports, this makes it the second highest opening grosser of 2016, after Akshay Kumar-starrer Airlift's ₹12.53 crore opening. The film, directed by R Balki, also features Rajat Kapoor and Swaroop Sampat.
