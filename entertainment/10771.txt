Salman Khan and Vidya Balan will be seen recreating a scene from the 1995 film 'Dilwale Dulhaniya Le Jayenge' on the reality show 'Bigg Boss 10'. While Salman will act as Shah Rukh Khan, Vidya will be seen acting as Katrina Kaif. They will also recreate the train sequence where Salman Khan will enact Amrish Puri's dialogues like Aamir Khan.
