Neeraj Ghaywan's Masaan has won the FIPRESCI award at the Cannes film festival, 2015. The film starring Richa Chadha, Shweta Tripathi, Vicky Kaushal and Sanjay Mishra in pivotal roles deals with the journey of four small-town individuals. The FIPRESCI awards are given out by the International Federation of Film Critics for the promotion and development of film culture.
