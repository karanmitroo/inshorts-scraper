Actors including Nawazuddin Siddiqui, Amy Jackson and Daisy Shah attended the Ganpati visarjan at actor Salman Khan's residence in Mumbai. While Salman missed the celebrations due to his shooting schedule for 'Tubelight', his sister Arpita Khan Sharma and brother Sohail Khan were part of the festivities. The eco-friendly Ganesha idol was immersed in a temporary pond in their garden.
