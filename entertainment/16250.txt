Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol, who are shooting for Rohit Shetty's upcoming film 'Dilwale', on Monday created a special video to mark 20 years of their hit film 'Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge'. In the video, the duo can be seen sharing jokes about the film. They have also recreated a scene from DDLJ, which was released on October 19, 1995.
