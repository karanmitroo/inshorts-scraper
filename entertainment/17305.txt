Megastar Amitabh Bachchan has recorded a video message for veteran actor Shashi Kapoor which will be screened when the latter would be conferred the Dadasaheb Phalke Award next month. Amitabh said that he was left speechless when asked to narrate his experience of working with Kapoor. The duo had acted together in numerous films like 'Trishul', 'Silsila' and 'Deewar'.
