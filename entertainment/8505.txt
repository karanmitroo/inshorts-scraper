A musical tribute was paid to Indian actor Om Puri, who passed away on January 6 this year, at Oscars 2017 during the 'In Memorium' segment. Om Puri has starred in Hollywood films like 'The Hundred-Foot Journey'. Singer Sara Bareilles sang 'Both Sides, Now' to honour celebrities and icons who passed away, which included Carrie Fisher, Gene Wilder and Prince.
