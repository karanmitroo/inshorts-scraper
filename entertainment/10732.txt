Superhero film 'Doctor Strange', with earnings of $616 million (over ₹4,200 crore) worldwide, has become Marvel Studio's highest-grossing single character film. It surpassed the 2008 film 'Iron Man', starring Robert Downey Jr, which recorded earnings of $585 million (about ₹4,000 crore). The film, which starred Benedict Cumberbatch as the titular character, released on November 4.
