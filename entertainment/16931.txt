Bollywood actor Rajpal Yadav on Wednesday said that he doesn't want any tag of a comedian. Yadav, who was at the trailer launch event of his upcoming film 'Thoda Lutf Thoda Ishq', added, "I never wanted to become a comedian and I’m not a comedian either." He has played serious roles in films like ‘Main, Meri Patni Aur Woh’.
