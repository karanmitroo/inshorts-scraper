Denying the reports of Salman Khan dropping Arijit Singh's version of the song 'Ishtehaar' from 'Welcome To New York', producer Vashu Bhagnani said, "Arijit Singh never sang for Welcome to New York in any capacity." Bhagnani added, "Composer of the song and I were interested in a certain texture of voice and Rahat suited it perfectly...so we asked him."
