Hemant, the son of playback singer Asha Bhosle, passed away on September 28, 2015, in Scotland. Hemant (66) was suffering from cancer for long. Hemant, a music composer by profession, was living in Scotland for the past five years. He is accredited with music in films like 'Damad'. Notably, Bhosle's daughter Varsha had also committed suicide in 2012.
