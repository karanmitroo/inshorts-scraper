Censor Board chief Leela Samson has decided to resign following reports that, controversial film "Messenger of God" featuring Dera Saccha Sauda chief Gurmeet Ram Rahim Singh in lead role, has been cleared by Film Certification Appellate Tribunal (FCAT). "It is a mockery of Central Board of Film Certification. My resignation is final. Have informed the (I&B) Secretary," she said.
