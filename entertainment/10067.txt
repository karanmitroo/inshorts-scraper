Actress Deepika Padukone's upcoming Hollywood debut 'xXx: The Return of Xander Cage' will release in India on January 14, 2017, a week before its global release. "Thrilled to announce that #XxX:TheReturnofXanderCage will release in India first! Before anywhere else in the world!," Deepika tweeted. The film stars actor Vin Diesel in the lead role.
