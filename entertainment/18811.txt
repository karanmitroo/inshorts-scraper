Along with the poster, the first trailer of superstar Salman Khan's much-awaited film Kick is finally out. The trailer shows that Salman is no hero in the film, but a Devil. 
The movie also features Randeep Hooda, Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Jacqueline Fernandes. Shot in Delhi, Dubai, Poland and Mumbai among other places, Kick is set to hit theatres this Eid.
