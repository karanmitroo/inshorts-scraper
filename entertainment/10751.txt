The first look of actor Arjun Rampal, from the upcoming biopic 'Daddy', has been revealed. Arjun will play the role of the gangster-turned-politician Arun Gawli in the film. The biopic, which will reportedly cover Gawli's life from his 20s to his 60s, will also feature Farhan Akhtar. The film is scheduled to release in 2017.
