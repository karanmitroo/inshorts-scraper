On Tamil actor Vikram's 50th birthday, #HBDChiyaanVikram was a top Indian Twitter trend on Sunday. Users wished the actor with tweets like, "#ChiyaanVikram =Hardwork+Versatile, Age is just A number for Him! #HBDChiyaanVikram", and "If Hollywood has Christian Bale, INDIA has Chiyaan Vikram". Users also shared photographs of the actor and listed their favourite films featuring Vikram.
