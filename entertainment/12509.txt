The Sonakshi Sinha starrer 'Akira', which released on Friday is "a watered-down female version of Ghajini", wrote The Indian express. "'Akira' is a dumb commercial potboiler," said India Today while NDTV wrote that "'Akira' isn't blockbuster material". The film was rated 1.5/5 (The Indian Express), 2/5 (India Today) and 2.5/5 by NDTV.
