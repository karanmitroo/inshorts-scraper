Actor Benedict Cumberbatch joined former Pink Floyd singer-guitarist David Gilmour to sing the band's iconic song 'Comfortably Numb'. Gilmour was performing at the Royal Albert Hall in London as part of a series of shows for his 2015 album 'Rattle The Lock'. Meanwhile, Cumberbatch recorded the song 'Can't Keep It Inside' with Bon Iver and Kings of Leon in 2013.
