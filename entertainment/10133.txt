Akshay Kumar became the only Bollywood actor who had three films in the ₹100 crore club in 2016. Akshay's 'Airlift' earned ₹128.1 crore, which was also the third highest Bollywood grosser of 2016. This was followed by the multi-starrer 'Housefull 3', which minted ₹109.14 crore while 'Rustom', Akshay's last release in 2016, earned ₹127.42 crore.
