Actor Rajkummar Rao performed with an injured leg on choreographer-turned-director Farah Khan's show 'Lip Sing Battle'. In pictures that have surfaced online from the sets of the show, the actor can be seen dressed as Hrithik Roshan's character from the song 'It's Magic' in 'Koi Mil Gaya'. Rajkummar was accompanied by Hrithik and Kriti Sanon on the show.
