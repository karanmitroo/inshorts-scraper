The Jamie Dornan and Dakota Johnson-starrer 'Fifty Shades of Grey' was named 'Worst Picture' at the 2016 Golden Raspberry Award (Razzies) on Saturday, alongside 'Fantastic Four'. Dornan was named Worst Actor while Johnson received the Worst Actress award. The duo was also named Worst Onscreen Duo. Meanwhile, Josh Trank got the Worst Director Award for 'Fantastic Four'.
