Megastar Amitabh Bachchan has shared his intense look for his upcoming film Shamitabh. The movie will be Bachchan's third collaboration with director Balki after hits like Cheeni Kum and Paa, which also earned him his third National award. The film, shot in Goa and Tamil Nadu, also stars Dhanush and Kamal Haasan's younger daughter Akshara.
