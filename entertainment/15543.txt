The Uttar Pradesh government on Wednesday declared Akshay Kumar and Nimrat Kaur starrer 'Airlift' tax-free across the state. Akshay took to Twitter to thank Chief Minister Akhilesh Yadav for the same. "Just been informed the Uttar Pradesh Govt has declared #Airlift as Tax Free. Thank you to the young & progressive team led by @yadavakhilesh," he posted.
