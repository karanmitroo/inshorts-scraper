“Priyamanasam”, the third film ever to be made in Sanskrit, will open the Indian Panorama at the International Film Festival of India (IFFI) which will be held from November 20-30 in Goa. The Vinod Mankara directorial is the tale of 17th century Malayalam poet, Unnayi Varrier, and the creative process he went through while writing the romantic Kathakali play “Nalacharitham”.
