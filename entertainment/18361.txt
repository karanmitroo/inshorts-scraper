Days after Tanishaa Mukherjee and Armaan Kohli confirmed their break up, model-turned-actress Gauahar Khan and TV actor Kushal Tandon have called off their 10-month long relationship. A friend of the couple says that their religious beliefs were the cause. Kushal revealed the news of the split on Twitter. Their relationship had blossomed in the house of 'Bigg Boss 7'.
