Two dusky beauties of Bollywood, Priyanka Chopra and Deepika Padukone, who are known to share a great bond off-screen, will make a joint appearance on Karan Johar's popular chat show 'Koffee with Karan 4'. Priyanka shared her pic with Deepika from the show on Instagram. The show will be premiered on Dec-1. The first episode will feature Salman Khan.
