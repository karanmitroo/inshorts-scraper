The official trailer of 'X-Men: Apocalypse' was released on YouTube on Friday. The film stars many actors including James McAvoy, Michael Fassbender, Jennifer Lawrence and Oscar Isaac. Notably, this is the eighth film in the X-Men franchise, with the previous one being 'X-Men: Days of Future Past' (2014). The Bryan Singer directorial is scheduled to release on May 27, 2016.
