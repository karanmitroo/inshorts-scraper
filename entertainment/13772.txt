Producer Deepak Mukut has confirmed that a sequel for Anil Kapoor's film 'Nayak: The Real Hero' is being made, 15 years after the film's release in 2001. The sequel will be produced along with 'Eros International' while Deepak added that it will be a political satire. The original film starred Anil as Shivaji Rao, who becomes CM for a day.
