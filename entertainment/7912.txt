Actress Alia Bhatt's sister Shaheen Bhatt has slammed the media for taking pictures of Aishwarya Rai Bachchan at her father Krishnaraj Rai's funeral. "I have never understood the media showing up to high-profile funerals to take pictures of grieving family members," she wrote in a post. Shaheen further wrote that the insensitivity of it is "mind-boggling".
