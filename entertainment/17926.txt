Bollywood superstar Salman Khan paid a surprise visit to a local girls school in Mandawa, Rajasthan. Salman is currently shooting for Kabir Khan's upcoming movie 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' in Rajasthan. The actor was also seen playing with Rajasthan Tourism Minister Bina Kak's grandson. Bina Kak played the role of Salman's mother in the movie 'Maine Pyaar Kyun Kiya'.
