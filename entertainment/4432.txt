Akshay Kumar on Sunday tweeted a picture with his female martial artist friends to wish them on the occasion of Friendship Day. "All you ladies make me prouder than you can imagine," tweeted Akshay. Tweeting on the occasion, Anupam Kher wrote, "If you have a friend then #FriendshipDay is Everyday." Actors Dhanush and Farhan Akhtar also tweeted Friendship Day wishes.
