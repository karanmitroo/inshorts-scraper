Actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan completed 20 years in Bollywood on Tuesday. Her debut Hindi film 'Aur Pyaar Ho Gaya', which also co-starred Bobby Deol, released on this date in 1997. The 43-year-old actress is known for starring in films like 'Taal', 'Hum Dil De Chuke Sanam', 'Devdas', 'Guru' and 'Jodhaa Akbar'.
