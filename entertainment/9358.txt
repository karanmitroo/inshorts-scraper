The Hrithik Roshan and Yami Gautam starrer film 'Kaabil' is "failed by the obvious plot", wrote The Indian Express. "Kaabil is a thorough crowd-pleaser," said India Today. The Times of India wrote, "The highlight of the film is Hrithik's bravura performance". 'Kaabil' has been rated 1.5/5 (The Indian Express), 2.5/5 (India Today) and 4/5 (The Times of India).
