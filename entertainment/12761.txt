The trailer of the new web series 'Tripling' by online digital entertainment channel 'The Viral Fever' has been released. The series revolves around three siblings who go on a road trip together. It stars Sumeet Vyas, Maanvi Gagroo, Amol Parashar and Kunaal Roy Kapur among others. The first episode of 'Tripling' will release on August 28 on TVFPlay.
