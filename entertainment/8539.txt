Actress Fatima Sana Shaikh, who portrayed wrestler Geeta Phogat in 'Dangal', has featured on the cover of the March issue of 'Femina Wedding Times' magazine. She can be seen in an outfit by couture label SVA and jewellery by Euremme. The cover story featuring her is titled 'Fatima Sana Shaikh: From 2016's Biggest Blockbuster To The Road Ahead'.
