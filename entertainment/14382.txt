Yash Raj Films on Wednesday took to YouTube to reveal the look of actor Ayushmann Khurrana in the upcoming romantic drama film 'Meri Pyaari Bindu'. Ayushmann plays the role of a Bengali author named Abhimanyu Roy in the film, which features Parineeti Chopra as Bindu. The film, directed by debutant director Akshay Roy, also marks the singing debut of Parineeti.
