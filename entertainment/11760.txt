The Deepika Padukone, Ranveer Singh and Shahid Kapoor starrer film 'Padmavati' will release on November 17, 2017 as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. While Deepika will play the titular role, Ranveer will portray 'Alauddin Khilji'. Shahid, who will be seen as Deepika's husband will portray the character 'Rawal Ratan Singh'. The film will be directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali.
