TV show 'Balika Vadhu' Monday became the longest running daily Hindi fiction show on Indian TV, breaking the record of 1,833 episodes, previously set by Kyunki... Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi. The show crossed this number without taking a generation leap.  "We are extremely happy about our show breaking this record...", Sunjoy Waddhwa, CMD, Sphereorigins Multivision said.
