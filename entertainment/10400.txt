Rapper and singer Baba Sehgal has released a song called 'ATM', where he raps about the effects of the policy of demonetisation. In the song, Sehgal raps, "Modi ji ne kar diya demonetisation, Opposition boley naya law very funny, ATM mein boley toh no money money." Notably, Indian banknotes of ₹500 and ₹1,000 were rendered invalid starting from November 9.
