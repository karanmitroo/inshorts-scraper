A recreated version of the 'Ghungta' song, originally composed by RD Burman for the 1979 film 'Dhongee', has been released as part of the Nawazuddin Siddiqui starrer 'Babumoshai Bandookbaaz'. The new version has been composed by Gaurav Dagaonkar, sung by Neha Kakkar with lyrics by Ghalib Asad Bhopali. Directed by Kushan Nandy, the film will release on August 25.
