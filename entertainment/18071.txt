For the first time in Bollywood, R Balki, the director of "Shamitabh" has released the "audio poster" of the film. The audio poster features a voice clip of a journalist asking Shamitabh for the release date of his film. The Amitabh Bachchan starrer also features Dhanush and Kamal Haasan's daughter Akshara, who will make her Bollywood debut with Shamitabh.
