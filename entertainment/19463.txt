The number of followers of Amitabh Bachchan on Twitter has crossed the 7-million mark. "Thank you AALLLLLLLL...Its 7 MILLION plus!! On twitter."tweeted Amitabh.  Amitabh is credited with starting the celebrity-blogging trend in India with his blog and twitter account which became live on May-19, 2010. Also, in August, BigB crossed 5-million like on his Facebook page in just one year.
