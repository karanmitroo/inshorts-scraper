A tentative list of contestants for the ninth season of the celebrity reality show Bigg Boss surfaced on Monday. As per the list, celebrities who have been approached for Bigg Boss 9 include controversial godwoman Radhe Maa and former CEO of Housing.com, Rahul Yadav. Other celebrities that feature in the list are Rashami Desai, Sana Saeed and Shweta Prasad Basu.
