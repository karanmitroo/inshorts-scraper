Tejasswi Prakash, lead actress of the controversial serial 'Pehredaar Piya Ki' which was taken off air, said, "We ended Pehredaar Piya Ki abruptly, so we wanted something (as good) to take its place." Tejasswi said that in the upcoming serial which is replacing the show, she plays a modern character in a story set in the traditional backdrop of Rajasthan.
