Actress Deepika Padukone has said that it is not fair to make comparisons between her and actress Priyanka Chopra. "Let me just say everyone has a different path and journey," she added. Notably, Deepika's Hollywood debut film 'xXx: Return of Xander Cage' released in January while Priyanka's film 'Baywatch', which will mark her Hollywood debut, will release on May 26.
