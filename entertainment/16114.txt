Salman Khan and Sonam Kapoor's Prem Ratan Dhan Payo, that released on Thursday, has an abundance of songs which "is a major hindrance to the pace of the film”, wrote India Today. Hindustan Times wrote, “It has everything a quintessential Hindi ‘masala’ family film would crave for”. It has been rated 3/5 (India Today), 2/5 (HT) and 1.5/5 (Indian Express).
