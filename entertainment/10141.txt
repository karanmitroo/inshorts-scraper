Amitabh Bachchan, Shahid Kapoor, Ranbir Kapoor, Sushant Singh Rajput and Diljit Dosanjh will appear on this year's edition of film critic Rajeev Masand's show 'The Actors Roundtable'. The actors have talked about their insecurities and the work they wish they could improve on. While Masand has shared the show's excerpt, the full episode first aired on CNN News18.
