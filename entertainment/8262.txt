Actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan has won the Best Actress award for her role in 'Sarbjit' at the International Film Festival and Awards of Australia. Based on the life of Sarabjit Singh, an Indian prisoner in Pakistan, the film had Aishwarya portraying his sister 'Dalbir Kaur'. She had also been nominated in the Best Actress category for 'Sarbjit' at Filmfare Awards.
