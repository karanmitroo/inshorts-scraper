Red Chillies VFX and Autodesk India have created Shah Rukh Khan's first life size 3D printed model as the birthday present. The art-work stands in SRK's signature pose. SRK, the owner of Red Chillies VFX, tweeted, "How fantastic is my Team Red Chillies v/fx. Fully made in India the first 3D lifesize printout. My best B'Day present."
