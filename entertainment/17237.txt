After revealing its official poster, the film-makers have released the first trailer of 'Hamari Adhuri Kahani' starring Emraan Hashmi and Vidya Balan. The film, a romantic drama, will witness actor Rajkumar Rao in a negative role playing Vidya's possessive husband. After 'The Dirty Picture' and 'Ghanchakkar', Emraan is sharing onscreen space with Vidya for the third time.
