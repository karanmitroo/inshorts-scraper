'Koi Ishaara', a new song from the John Abraham and Sonakshi Sinha starrer upcoming action thriller film 'Force 2' has been released. It has been composed by Amaal Malik, with vocals by Armaan Malik and lyrics by Rashmi Virag. The film is a sequel to the 2011 film 'Force'. Directed by Abhinay Deo, the film will release on November 18.
