Bollywood actress Kajol, the female lead of Dilwale Dulhaniya Le Jayenge, doesn't want the film to be remade. DDLJ has completed its 1000 weeks. The film, directed by Aditya Chopra, was released on Oct 20, 1995 and is still running in Mumbai's Maratha Mandir. According to Kajol, "You can never remake a phenomenon."
