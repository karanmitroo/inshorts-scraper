Bollywood's on-screen and off-screen couple Genelia D'Souza and Riteish Deshmukh turned parents on Tuesday, as they were blessed with a baby boy, their first child. The actor took to Twitter to express his happiness and tweeted, "It's a BBBOOOOYYYYYY!!!!!!!!". The duo, who had been dating since 2003, tied the knot in February, 2012.
