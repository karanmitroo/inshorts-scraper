Comedian Kapil Sharma, whose show 'Comedy Nights With Kapil' is climbing the popularity charts with each episode, has been listed at 93rd position in Forbes India's 'celebrity 100' list --above other popular TV stars like Ram Kapoor. Moreover, Sharma has also been nominated alongside Shahrukh Khan, Hrithik Roshan, Farhan Akhtar and Deepika Padukone for CNN-IBN's Indian of the Year (Entertainment).
