A new poster of the Rajinikanth starrer upcoming Tamil film 'Kabali' was unveiled by its makers on Saturday. The crime drama film, written and directed by Pa Ranjith, stars Radhika Apte as Rajinikanth's wife. Scheduled to release in May this year, 'Kabali' also features Kishore, Dhansika, Dinesh Ravi and John Vijay in pivotal roles.
