Zee TV is all set to launch a new comedy show, 'Gangs of Haseepur', which will go on air Apr-26. Being judged by actress Mandira Bedi and Tanishaa Mukherjee, the show will be hosted by Ragini Khanna. The show is produced by Essel Vision Productions and will feature several popular stand-up comedians like Raju Srivastav, Bharti Singh and Krushna Abhishek.
