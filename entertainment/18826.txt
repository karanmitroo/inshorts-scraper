Akshay Kumar's ‘Holiday’ has been a hit with movie goers in the UAE as it has earned $332,000 (Rs 1.96 crore) on its first day. "Highest ever single day gross for Akshay starrer in UAE-GCC," trade analyst Taran Adarsh tweeted. The movie, which has been produced by Vipul Shah and also features Sonakshi Sinha, focuses on terrorism and sleeper cells.
