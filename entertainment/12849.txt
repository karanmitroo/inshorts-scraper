The Hrithik Roshan starrer film 'Mohenjo Daro', which hit the theatres on Friday, "just cannot be taken seriously", India Today wrote. The film "is a massive mess that the loose-limbed Hrithik Roshan cannot yank away from its mediocrity", NDTV said. The Ashutosh Gowarikar directorial has been rated 2/5 (India Today, NDTV) and 3/5 (Zee News).
