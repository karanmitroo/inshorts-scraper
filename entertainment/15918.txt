The trailer of the science fiction film 'Independence Day: Resurgence' was released on Sunday. Directed by Roland Emmerich, it is a sequel of  'Independence Day' (1996) and features Jeff Goldblum, Bill Pullman and Judd Hirsch reprising their roles from the first film. Liam Hemsworth and Jessie Usher also star in the film, due to release on June 24, 2016.
