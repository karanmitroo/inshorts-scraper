Actor Amitabh Bachchan on Twitter shared his 'real' look from the upcoming film '102 Not Out', while claiming that the film's first look was leaked by the media. "The ethics of confidentiality and secrecy have never been borne to discipline and trust," wrote Bachchan about the look's leak. Bachchan will play a 102-year-old in the film, also starring Rishi Kapoor.
