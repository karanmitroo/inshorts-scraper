Choreographer and filmmaker Farah Khan has shared old pictures of Priyanka Chopra and Rani Mukerji dancing at Farah's sangeet ceremony in 2004. Farah also shared pictures of her brother Sajid Khan, Shah Rukh Khan, his wife Gauri Khan and Karan Johar from the ceremony. Farah celebrated her 12th wedding anniversary with her husband Shirish Kunder on December 9.
