Actors Shah Rukh Khan (SRK), Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif were among those attending the annual Iftaar party hosted by former MLA Baba Siddique on Sunday. Filmmaker Kabir Khan, Bipasha Basu, Karan Singh Grover and Huma Qureshi also attended the party. "This Iftar dinner is the only one to have women break their fast along with the men," said Siddique.
