A video shows Ranbir Kapoor's niece Samara Sahni dancing to his song Galti Se Mistake from Jagga Jasoos. Samara is the daughter of Ranbir's elder sister Riddhima Kapoor and her husband Bharat Sahni. Sharing the video on Instagram, Ranbir's mother Neetu Singh wrote, "Sam slays Jagga." Directed by Anurag Basu, Jagga Jasoos is scheduled to release on July 14.
