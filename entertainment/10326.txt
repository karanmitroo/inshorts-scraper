The teaser of '(Secret) Superstar', which features actor Aamir Khan in a cameo appearance, released on Friday. The film stars Zaira Wasim as the female lead 'Insia', a 14 year old girl from Vadodara, whose dream of becoming a singer changes her life and of everyone around her. Directed by Advait Chandan, the film will release on August 4, 2017.
