Sonam Kapoor has acquired the film adaptation rights for Krishna Udayasankar's 'Govinda', the first novel in the three book series which is a modern retelling of the Hindu epic 'Mahabharata'. Sonam said, "I don't know which character I will essay, but it's Indian mythology, which...has a lot of potential." She also acquired the rights for the novel 'Battle for Bittora'.
