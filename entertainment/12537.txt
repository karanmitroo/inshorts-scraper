The first look of Raj Kummar Rao from his upcoming film 'Omerta' was revealed by filmmaker Hansal Mehta on the occasion of Rao's 32nd birthday on August 31. This will be Rao and Mehta's fourth film together after 'Shahid' (2013), 'CityLights' (2014) and 'Aligarh' (2016). Notably, Rao won the National Film Award in the Best Actor category for 'Shahid'.
