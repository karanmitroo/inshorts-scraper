Actor Randeep Hooda has shared his first look from the upcoming film 'Battle of Saragirhi'. Randeep is playing the character Havaldar Ishar Singh in the film, which is based on the Battle of Saragarhi, that took place in 1897 between the British Indian Army and Afghan Orakzai tribesmen. Notably, Ajay Devgn's next production is also about the same battle.
