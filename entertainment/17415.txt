Bollywood actress Sonakshi Sinha will soon be seen on the small screen, judging the music based reality show 'Indian Idol Juniors', the actress revealed Friday in a series of tweets. "Enough guessing!! HAPPY to announce #ShotgunJunior is going to be a part of Indian Idol Junior", she tweeted. The show will be hosted by Hussain Kuwajerwala and Asha Negi.
