Actor Hrithik Roshan has become the highest advance taxpaying actor in Bollywood till mid-December. He has paid an advance tax of ₹80 crore till December 15, 2016. Aamir Khan came second by paying ₹74 crore. Ranbir Kapoor, who paid ₹37 crore, came third followed by Salman Khan and Akshay Kumar, who paid ₹14 crore and ₹10 crore respectively.
