Maharashtra Navnirman Sena (MNS) leader Ameya Khopkar has threatened singer Mika Singh over his "Hamara Pakistan" remark. In a video, Mika had urged fans to attend a concert to jointly celebrate the independence days of India and Pakistan. Ameya tweeted, "Mika Singh is doing Hamara Pakistan concert in USA. Open challenge to him, try holding a mic in Maharashtra now."
