The title song of the Pulkit Samrat-starrer film 'Sanam Re' was released on Monday. While the romantic song has been composed and written by Mithoon, it has been sung by Mithoon and Arijit Singh. 'Sanam Re' is a romantic drama, which also features Yami Gautam, Rishi Kapoor and Urvashi Rautela. The Divya Kumar Khosla-directorial will release on February 12, 2016.
