As per reports, actress Sonakshi Sinha will get engaged to rumoured boyfriend Bunty Sajdeh in February 2017. Rumours of the two dating have been circulating since 2012 when Bunty started managing Sonakshi's endorsement deals. Notably, Bunty, who is the CEO of agency 'Cornerstone', has previously been linked to actresses Sushmita Sen, Dia Mirza and Neha Dhupia.
