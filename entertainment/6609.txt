Tamil actor Rajinikanth has got a legal notice from Sundar Shaekhar, who claims to be the adopted son of 'underworld don' Haji Mastan Mirza. In the notice, Sundar has asked Rajinikanth not to depict his father as a 'smuggler and an underworld don'. It is speculated that Rajinikanth will play the role of Haji Mastan in his next film.
