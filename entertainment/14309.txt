Aishwarya Rai Bachchan has featured on the cover of the magazine 'Filmfare' for its June issue. The 42-year-old actress has worn a floral print off-shoulder dress from Bambah Boutique and matched it with pink lipstick and loose curls for the cover shot. The cover story titled 'The Queen is Back' features Aishwarya speaking about her journey in the showbiz.
