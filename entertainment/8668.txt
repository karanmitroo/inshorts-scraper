A new song titled 'Police Wala Don' from Govinda's upcoming film 'Aa Gaya Hero' has been released. The song has been composed by Shamir, sung by Ahan and Poorvi Koutish while Govinda has penned the lyrics for it. Also starring Ashutosh Rana, Makarand Deshpande and Poonam Pandey, the film is scheduled to release on March 3.
