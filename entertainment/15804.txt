Filmmaker Karan Johar on Thursday tweeted that actor Aditya Roy Kapoor and Shraddha Kapoor will star together in his production company Dharma Productions' upcoming film. The untitled film will be a Hindi remake of Mani Ratnam's Tamil film 'O Kadhal Kanmani' which released in April 2015. The Shaad Ali directorial will have AR Rahman's music and Gulzar's lyrics.
