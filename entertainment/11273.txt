Actress Priyanka Chopra's debut Marathi production 'Ventilator' "is a study in human emotions", wrote Indian Express. Hindustan Times called the film "a delightful, well-written family drama" while Filmfare wrote that the film "is one of the most honest and emotional films of the year." It has been rated 3/5 (Indian Express), 3.5/5 (Hindustan Times) and 4/5 (Filmfare).
