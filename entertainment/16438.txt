The first official song of Shahid Kapoor and Alia Bhatt starrer 'Shaandaar' was realeased on Thursday. The song, titled 'Gulaabo', features Shahid as wedding planner Jagjinder Joginder, Alia as Alia, and the bride-to-be, played by Shahid's real-life sister Sanah. The music for the Vikas Bahl-directorial has been composed by Amit Trivedi. The film is scheduled to release on October 22.
