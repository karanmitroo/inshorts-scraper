As per reports, actor Akshay Kumar will star in filmmaker Neeraj Pandey's upcoming film which bears the tentative title 'Toilet: Ek Prem Katha'. The film is reportedly inspired by Prime Minister Narendra Modi's Swachh Bharat campaign. Earlier, Akshay and Neeraj have worked together on the films 'Special 26' and 'Baby', where Akshay starred in the lead roles.
