Rapper Yo Yo Honey Singh is set to make his solo acting debut in a Punjabi film titled 'Zorawar'. The film is being directed by Vinnil Markan who also directed Singh's maiden song video. Notably, Singh first appeared in Punjabi film 'Mirza' (2012) and debuted as a lead in 'Tu Mera 22 Main Tera 22' (2013) alongside Amrinder Gill.
