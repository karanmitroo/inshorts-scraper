Aishwarya Rai Bachchan has overplayed emotional parts in her film 'Jazbaa' which released on Friday, wrote Indian Express, India Today and Mid-Day. In the Sanjay Gupta directorial, "Irrfan Khan is the most entertaining thing", wrote Hindustan Times. The film, based on Korean thriller ‘Seven Days’, has been rated 1.5/5 (Indian Express), 2.5/5 (India Today) and 3/5 (Mid-Day and HT).
