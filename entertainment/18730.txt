Director Shashank Khaitan’s Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania, starring Alia Bhatt and Varun Dhawan, released this Friday. The movie has been rated 2.5 (NDTV, Hindustan Times), 3 (Times of India), 3.5 (India Today). “Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania is going to be worth your time and money! It's an entertainer in the true sense,” writes India Today.
