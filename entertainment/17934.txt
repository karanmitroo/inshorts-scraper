Richard Linklater's film 'Boyhood' won top honours at the 72nd Golden Globe Awards 2015 announced Monday. The movie won Best Film (Drama), with Linklater named best director and Patricia Arquette Best Supporting Actress. British actor Eddie Redmayne won Best Actor (Drama) for the movie 'The Theory of Everything'. Julianne Moore received Best Actress (Drama) for 'Still Alice'.
