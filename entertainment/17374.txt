Actress Alia Bhatt has said that her upcoming film 'Udta Punjab' is the "biggest risk" she has taken in her career. "I still can't talk about the film but it is definitely the hardest film, hardest character that I have played till date," Alia said. Directed by Abhishek Chaubey, 'Udta Punjab' also features, Shahid Kapoor, Kareena Kapoor and Diljit Dosanjh.
