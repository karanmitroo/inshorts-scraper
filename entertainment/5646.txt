Shah Rukh Khan has compared filmmaker Imtiaz Ali to late director Yash Chopra and said, "Imtiaz is like a modern-day Yash Chopra". He added that he wishes to work with Imtiaz every year. SRK, who is working with Imtiaz for the first time in Jab Harry Met Sejal (JHMS), said that the film is not edgy but funny and happy.
