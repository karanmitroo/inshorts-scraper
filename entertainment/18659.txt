With Kick set to enter the Rs.100 crore club, Salman Khan will become the first Bollywood actor to have seven Rs.100 crore movies to his credit: Ek Tha Tiger, Dabangg 2, Bodyguard, Dabangg, Ready, and Jai Ho. According to trade analyst Taran Adarsh, Kick collected Rs. 98.14 crore until Monday, with experts expecting Rs.30 crore revenue on Tuesday (Eid holiday).
