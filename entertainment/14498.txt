'Baaghi', which released on Friday, collected ₹38.58 crore in its opening weekend, getting the third biggest opening weekend of 2016 for a Bollywood film. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film collected ₹11.94 crore on Friday, ₹11.13 crore on Saturday and ₹15.51 crore on Sunday. Directed by Sabbir Khan, the film stars Tiger Shroff and Shraddha Kapoor.
