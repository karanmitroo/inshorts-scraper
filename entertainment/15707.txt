Harrison Ford has become the highest-grossing actor in Hollywood history, as his 41 movies have grossed $4.7 billion in total, according to BoxOffice Mojo. Ford replaced Samuel Jackson, whose 68 movies have grossed $4.6 billion. Ford starrer 'Star Wars: The Force Awakens' earlier grossed $770 million. Tom Hanks, Morgan Freeman and Eddie Murphy are also featured in the top five.
