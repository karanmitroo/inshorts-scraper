The 1st trailer of Bhootnath Returns is out, starring Amitabh Bachchan, Boman Irani and child actor Parth in pivotal roles. The trailer begins with Amitabh, the ghost, trying to convince people that he can scare kids away and soon, he is sent to earth to 'prove himself'! For reasons unrevealed in the trailer, Amitabh decides to fight election against Boman.
