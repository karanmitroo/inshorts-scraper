'Behooda', the new song from the Nawazuddin Siddiqui starrer 'Raman Raghav 2.0' was released on Friday. The song has been composed by Ram Sampath and sung by Nayantara Bhatkal. It is picturised on Nawazuddin's character 'Raman', who is described as "God's own CCTV camera" in the film. Directed by Anurag Kashyap, the film is scheduled to release on June 24.
