The Randeep Hooda and Kajal Aggarwal starrer 'Do Lafzon Ki Kahani' has earned ₹3.74 crore in its opening weekend in India. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film collected ₹1.03 crore on Friday, ₹1.26 crore on Saturday and ₹1.45 crore on Sunday. Directed by Deepak Tijori, the film is a remake of the 2011 South Korean film 'Always'.
