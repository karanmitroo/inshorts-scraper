'Bulleya', a new song from the Ranbir Kapoor, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Anushka Sharma starrer 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' has been released. The sufi love song has been composed by Pritam with vocals by Amit Mishra and Shilpa Rao. The lyrics have been written by Amitabh Bhattacharya. Directed by Karan Johar, the film will release on October 28, 2016.
