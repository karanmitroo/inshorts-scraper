Actress Sonam Kapoor wore a golden shimmery gown by Elie Saab for her second appearance at the 70th Cannes Film Festival's red carpet. The gown featured a plunging neckline and was cinched at the waist with a belt. She accessorised her look with diamond jewellery from Chopard. Sonam sported golden eyeshadow and carried a golden clutch to complete her look.
