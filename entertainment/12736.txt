The Ranveer Singh and Tamannaah Bhatia starrer advertisement for 'Ching's Secret', an Indian brand of Chinese cuisine ingredients, has been released. Directed by Rohit Shetty, it features the actor as 'Ranveer Ching', the "king" who can save the world from starvation. The advertisement's makers have said that it is a tribute to the film 'Mad Max' with a "Bollywood twist".
