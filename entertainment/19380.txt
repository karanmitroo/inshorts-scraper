Director Tigmanshu Dhulia's action film 'Bullett Raja' starring Saif Ali Khan, Sonakshi Sinha, Jimmy Shergill and Vidyut Jamval released this Friday. This is the story of simple Raja Mishra (Saif) who turns into a gangster due to violent, caste-ridden UP politics. The movie has been rated 2 (India Today and Indian Express), 3 (DNA), 3.5 (TOI) and 4 (NDTV).
