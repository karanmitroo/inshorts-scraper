Actor Arjun Rampal has revealed that among the young actors, he likes watching Ranveer Singh. "He is fun and a good actor," Arjun said, further adding that he also liked Alia Bhatt, Shraddha Kapoor and Varun Dhawan. The 43-year-old actor will be next seen in the musical drama film, 'Rock On 2', also starring actress Shraddha Kapoor.
