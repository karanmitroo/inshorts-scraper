Actress Alia Bhatt on Friday took to Twitter to share her first look in the upcoming movie 'Udta Punjab'. She plays the role of a Bihari migrant in the film, which also stars Shahid Kapoor, Kareena Kapoor Khan and Diljit Dosanjh. Directed by Abhishek Chaubey of 'Ishqiya' fame, the film is scheduled to release on June 17.
