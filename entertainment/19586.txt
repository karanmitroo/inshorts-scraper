Joining the likes of SRK, Salman and Aamir, Ranbir Kappor has bagged a Rs 100-crore deal with Disney UTV for his upcoming film 'Jagga Jasoos'. The studio is also expected to spend another Rs 20-crore on print and publicity, making this a Rs 120 crore project. Riding high on his success, Kapoor charges an acting fee of around Rs 15-crore.
