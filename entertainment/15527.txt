R Madhavan and Ritika Singh starrer 'Saala Khadoos' which hit the screens on Friday "works because of the performances by its cast", said India Today. "Apart from the acting, the comic dialogues and sequences, though sparse, keep you entertained," wrote Hindustan Times. The Sudha Kongara directorial has been rated 3/5 (India Today), 2.5/5 (NDTV) and 2/5 (HT).
