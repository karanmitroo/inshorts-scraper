Director Abhishek Choubey’s ‘Dedh Ishqiya’, the sequel to the zany 2010 black comedy ‘Ishqiya’, released Friday. The movie features Naseeruddin Shah, Madhuri Dixit, Arshad Warsi, Huma Qureshi and Vijay Raaz. The movie has been rated 2.5 (Financial Express), 3 (DNA), 3.5 (Times of India, Headlines Today) and 4 (IBNLive, NDTV).
