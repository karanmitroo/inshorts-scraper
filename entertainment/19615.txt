Almost after 3-years, Rajnikanth will be seen again on silver screen in "Kochadaiiyaan", a period film directed by his daughter, Soundarya Ashwin. The movie, reportedly made over a budget of 100-crore, will hit theatres this December. The film's first teaser was released today with a tagline "There are heroes, there are superheroes but there is only one Rajinikanth."
