Aashiqui 2 girl Shraddha Kapoor has replaced Priyanka Chopra in Tigmanshu Dhulia's Milan Talkies. Tigmanshu and his producer Ekta Kapoor were keen to cast a fresh pair in Milan talkies, and eventually have decided to bring Shahid Kapoor and Shraddha for the first time together on screen, after Imran khan (who was their first choice) opted out of the project.
