The video of actor-singer Ayushmann Khurrana's new single 'Ik Vaari' has been released. The song has been composed by Apaarshakti Khurrana and Ayushmann Khurrana, who have also written the lyrics. It incorporates a few lines from the track 'Tu Meri Zindagi Hai' from the 1990 film 'Aashiqui'. Directed by Pradeep Sarkar, the video also features Aisha Sharma.
