Sixty-two-year-old actor Rowan Atkinson, known for playing the character Mr Bean, is expecting his first child with his 33-year-old partner Louise Ford. Rowan already has a 23-year-old son named Benjamin and a 21-year-old daughter named Lily from his marriage to Sunetra Sastry. A source said, "Rowan and Louise are over the moon and can't wait for their new arrival."
