In a letter to Union minister Smriti Irani and Sanjay Leela Bhansali over the 'Padmavati' row, Haryana minister Vipul Goel wrote, "To glamourise Alauddin Khilji's character is akin to praising...those people who carry out acid attacks on girls." He added that doing so might ensure success at the box-office but will fail to do justice to the country's rich history.
