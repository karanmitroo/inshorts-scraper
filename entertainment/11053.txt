The Farhan Akhtar, Shraddha Kapoor and Arjun Rampal starrer 'Rock On 2', which released on Friday, has earned ₹7.01 crore in its opening weekend. The film has minted ₹2.02 crore on Friday, ₹2.41 crore on Saturday and ₹2.58 crore on Sunday, as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. Rampal admitted that the demonetisation policy affected the film's collection at theatres.
