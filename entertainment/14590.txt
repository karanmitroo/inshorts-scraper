'The Jungle Book' has become the highest grossing film of 2016 in India, having minted ₹132.96 crore in 3 weeks. It surpassed the lifetime collections of Akshay Kumar's 'Airlift', which stand at ₹128.10 crore. 'The Jungle Book' also recorded the second biggest April opening in the US and Canada by grossing $103.6 million in its first weekend.
