Radhika Apte, who shot to fame after the release of 'Badlapur', has been signed in a Tamil film opposite superstar Rajnikanth. The film will be directed by Pa. Ranjith who is known for his work in 'Madras' (2014) and 'Attakathi' (2012). In a recent interview, Apte said that she was very excited to be Rajnikanth's new heroine.
