Singer Kailash Kher released his fifth album 'Ishq Anokha' on Monday after a gap of four years. He shared the music video of the album's titular song, which stars actor Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Miss India Earth 2013 Sobhita Dhulipala. The song has been written, composed and sung by the 42-year-old singer. Kher had released his fourth album 'Rangeele' in 2012.
