Actress Jacqueline Fernandez has featured on the cover of Femina for its August edition. The 30-year-old actress is seen wearing a striped bodice top paired with high waist shorts in the picture. "She's cool. She's hot, she's all that!" wrote the magazine while sharing the picture on Instagram. Jacqueline features in the cover story titled, 'Jacqueline Fernandez, Full steam ahead'.
