Dairy cooperative Amul has released a new poster on the recent fire at RK Studios. The poster has been captioned, "Jaane Kahan Gaye Woh Din?" and shows the Amul girl holding the doll which featured in Raj Kapoor's film 'Mera Naam Joker'. Amul's official Twitter handle while sharing the poster wrote, "Amul Topical: Precious memorabilia lost in RK Studios fire!"
