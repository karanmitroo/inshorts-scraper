Actresses Priyanka Chopra and Deepika Padukone have featured in the 'Hot 100 2017' list by international men's magazine Maxim. The list has been topped by American model Hailey Baldwin, who has also featured on the magazine's latest cover. Other personalities who have featured in the list include Kendall Jenner, Gigi Hadid, Alexandra Daddario, Ashley Graham and Chrissy Teigen.
