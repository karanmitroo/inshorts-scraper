Actor Shahid Kapoor has shared a boomerang video on Instagram wherein he is seen imitating wife Mira Rajput as she talks on the phone. The video has been captioned "#marriedtothephone". The post has received over 9.7 lakh views since it was shared by the actor. Shahid and Mira got married in a low-key gurudwara wedding on July 7, 2015.
