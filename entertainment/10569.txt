Bollywood celebrities and members of Tamil film industry expressed their grief on AIADMK supremo Jayalalithaa's demise on Twitter. Amitabh Bachchan tweeted, "Deeply grieved at passing of Jayalalithaa ji, a strong woman." "Sad to hear of passing away of Jayalalithaaji," tweeted Shah Rukh. "The end of THE most inspirational era of Tamilnadu Politics. You leave behind a vast vacuum," tweeted Dhanush.
