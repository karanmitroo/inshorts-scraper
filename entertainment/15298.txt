On the day after Bollywood actress Priyanka Chopra announced on Twitter that she will star in Hollywood movie Baywatch alongside Dwayne Johnson, 'Baywatch' was among the most trending Google searches in India on Wednesday. It was most looked up by Goa, Odisha and Uttarakhand. 'Rene Laennec', 'Neerja Bhanot' and 'AB de Villiers' were also among the most trending searches.
