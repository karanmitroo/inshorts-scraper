The team of 'Finding Fanny' has released an exclusive video showing Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh's wedding from the film. Ranveer has made a cameo in the film, and plays Deepika's dead husband Gabo. The highly anticipated film starring Deepika Padukone, Arjun Kapoor, Naseeruddin Shah, Dimple Kapadia and Pankaj Kapur is set to release on Sep-12.
