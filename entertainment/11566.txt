Devendra Fadnavis, the Chief Minister of Maharashtra has extended his support for Karan Johar's upcoming film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil'. "Police has taken very strict action on Maharashtra Navnirman Sena (MNS) today. Twelve members of the MNS who had gone to Metro theatre to threaten them have been sent to judicial custody till November 4," said Fadnavis in a statement.
