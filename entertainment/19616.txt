Facing each other opposite for the first time, movies of Chopra sisters premiered together on friday at the box office where Parineeti starrer shuddh desi romance managed to open at 6.45 crore,while Priyanka's Zanjeer remake collected 3.58 crore at the first day. With Parineeti's flick beating the PC's Zanjeer at the box office, PC denied any clash with her sister.
