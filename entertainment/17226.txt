The first poster of Emraan Hashmi and Vidya Balan-starrer 'Hamari Adhuri Kahani', a Mohit Suri directorial, was released Monday by film-maker Vishesh Bhatt on Twitter. The duo was also seen together in 'The Dirty Picture'. The Mahesh Bhatt production, scheduled to release on June 12, is an intense love story based on the life of his parents and stepmother.
