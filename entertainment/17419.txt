Bollywood stars like Hrithik Roshan, Anushka Sharma and Shahid Kapoor are all set to glam up the IPL opening ceremony in Kolkata on April 7. Music director Pritam and actor-director Farhan Akhtar will also perform at the event. Furthermore, "...the evening's proceedings will be hosted by Saif Ali Khan," BCCI Secretary Anurag Thakur said in a statement.
