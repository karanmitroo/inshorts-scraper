Gori Tu Latth Maar, the new song from Akshay Kumar and Bhumi Pednekar starrer Toilet- Ek Prem Katha, has been released. The song has been sung by Sonu Nigam and Palak Muchhal with music by Manas-Shikhar and lyrics by Garima Wahal and Siddharth Singh. Directed by Shree Narayan Singh, the film is scheduled to release on August 11.
