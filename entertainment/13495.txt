'Tere Sang Yaara', the first song from the Akshay Kumar and Ileana D'cruz starrer film 'Rustom' has been released. Composed by Arko and sung by Atif Aslam, the song features Akshay and Ileana, the lead couple of the film. 'Rustom' has been directed by Tinu Suresh Desai and also features Esha Gupta. It is scheduled to release on August 12.
