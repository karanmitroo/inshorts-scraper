The trailer for the upcoming science fiction-action film 'Transformers: The Last Knight' has been released. It is the fifth instalment in the 'Transformers' franchise and sequel to 2014 film 'Transformers: Age of Extinction'. The film will feature Mark Wahlberg, Josh Duhamel and Isabela Moner. Directed by Michael Bay, it is scheduled to release on June 23, 2017.
