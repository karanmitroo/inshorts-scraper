Actor Ranveer Singh has said that he thinks Ranbir Kapoor should 'hook up' with actress Kangana Ranaut. "There's a lot of fireworks. He loves it," added Ranveer. He made these comments on the talk show 'Koffee with Karan', when he was asked who he would want Ranbir to "hook up" with. The two actors appeared on the show's fourth episode.
