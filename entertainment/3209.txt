A biopic on Padma Shri awardee Shahnaz Husain, known for her line of herbal and Ayurveda products, has been announced. The film will be based on Husain's daughter Nelofar Currimbhoy's biography on her titled 'Flame: The Inspiring Life Of My Mother Shahnaz Husain'. Kamlesh Pandey, who is credited for the story of 'Rang De Basanti', will write the film's script.
