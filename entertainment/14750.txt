A new trailer of the upcoming  science fiction action-comedy film 'Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Out of the Shadows' has been released. The film, featuring Megan Fox, Stephen Amell and Will Arnett, is a sequel to the 2014 film 'Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles'. Directed by Dave Green, the film is scheduled to release on June 3 in 3D and IMAX 3D.
