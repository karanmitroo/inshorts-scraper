Veteran music composer and lyricist Ravindra Jain passed away at 71 in Mumbai on Friday after suffering from a urinary infection. Jain, who was visually-challenged, had been unwell from sometime and was being treated at Mumbai's Lilavati Hospital. He had composed music for various hit Bollywood films including 'Chor Machaye Shor', 'Geet Gaata Chal', 'Chitchor' and 'Ankhiyon Ke Jharokhon Se'.
