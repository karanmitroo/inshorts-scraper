Minting Rs 46.35 crore, Shraddha Kapoor and Varun Dhawan-starrer 'ABCD 2' has become Bollywood's highest opening weekend grosser of the year 2015. The record is followed by Akshay Kumar's ‘Gabbar is Back’ and Kangana Ranaut’s ‘Tanu Weds Manu Returns’ which made Rs 39.41 crore and Rs 38.15 crore respectively. 'ABCD 2' is also the highest opening day grosser of 2015.
