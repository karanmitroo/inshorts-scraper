'Rehnuma', another song from the upcoming film 'Rocky Handsome', starring John Abraham and Shruti Haasan, was released on YouTube today. Sung by Shreya Ghoshal and Inder Bawra, the song has been written by Manoj Muntashir and Sagar Lahauri. Inspired from 2010 release Korean film 'The Man From Nowhere', 'Rocky Handsome' is scheduled to release on March 25.
