The Amitabh Bachchan and Taapsee Pannu starrer Bollywood film 'Pink' has ranked first in the list of 'Top 10 Indian Movies in 2016' by IMDb, an online database for films, television programs and video games. Akshay Kumar's 'Airlift' ranks second on the list while 'Dear Zindagi' comes in third. The list has been curated based on ratings by IMDb users.
