Doordarshan Kisan channel, which is said to be reeling under losses, reportedly paid Rs 6.31 crore to actor Amitabh Bachchan for endorsing the channel. As per reports, the public-funded channel had earlier set Rs 4.02 crore budget for its celebrity endorsement. Notably, the channel's total budget is Rs 45 crore. This is also Amitabh's most expensive endorsement till date.
