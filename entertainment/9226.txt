The Hrithik Roshan and Yami Gautam starrer film 'Kaabil' earned ₹67.46 crore in its extended five-day weekend. The film grossed ₹15.05 crore on Sunday with 40% screen shares, according to the film's makers. Both Hrithik and Yami portray visually-challenged people in the film. Notably, Shah Rukh Khan's 'Raees', which released on the same day, has earned ₹93 crore so far.
