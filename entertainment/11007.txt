Actor Ranveer Singh has said that he is not Emraan Hashmi 'Part 2' when asked about the kissing scenes in the film 'Befikre'. Earlier, Emraan was referred to as the 'serial kisser' owing to the numerous kissing scenes he did. Notably, 'Befikre', which has almost 40 kissing scenes, has been passed by the Censor Board without any cuts.
