'Let's Talk About Love', the second song from the Tiger Shroff and Shraddha Kapoor starrer film 'Baaghi' was released on Friday. The song, composed by Manj Musik, has been sung by Manj, along with Raftaar and Neha Kakkar. Directed by Sabbir Khan, 'Baaghi' also features Sudheer Babu and is scheduled to release on April 29.
