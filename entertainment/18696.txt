Tamil-star Dhanush has got his magnum opus, with his recently released action-film 'Vella Illa Pattatharia' (VIP) making Rs. 5.18 crore on the opening day. "I don't know how 2 thank my fans 4 giving VIP d biggest opening of my career n making it a huge blockbuster. I dedicate my tears of joy 2 u all," he posted on Twitter.
