Actor Amitabh Bachchan has confirmed that he will be playing the role of a villain in the film 'Aankhen 2', a sequel to the 2002 film 'Aankhen'. The 73-year-old-actor said that he will once again team up with filmmaker Gaurang Doshi for the sequel. Notably, Bachchan had also played a negative character in 'Aankhen', which was directed by Doshi.
