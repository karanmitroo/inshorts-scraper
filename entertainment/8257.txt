Actor Ryan Reynolds trolled Hugh Jackman on Twitter after the latter posted a photo of himself with his fans at the premiere of his film 'Logan' in Beijing, China. Sharing the photo, Reynolds jokingly commented, "Pretty sure those are protesters". Responding to this, Jackman tweeted, "Really...Guy ... Really?!". Reynolds and Jackman worked together in the 2009 film 'X-Men Origins: Wolverine'.
