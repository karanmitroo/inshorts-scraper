Megastar Amitabh Bachchan recently took to Twitter to praise Kangana Ranaut, calling her "talented and wonderful". "At work at last with the wonder called Kangana Ranaut .. she be so good .. and talented and wonderful", the 72-yr-old actor tweeted, along with a picture with the actress. Big B and Ranaut will be seen together in a Rajkumar Hirani-directed commercial.
