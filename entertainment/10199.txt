A photograph of actress Kareena Kapoor Khan, reportedly with her new-born baby Taimur Ali Khan Pataudi being circulated online has been termed fake by Kareena and Saif's representatives. The picture has been morphed and is not real at all, the representatives added. Notably, Kareena gave birth to Taimur, her first child with husband Saif Ali Khan, on Tuesday in Mumbai.
