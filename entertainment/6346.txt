Filmmaker Anil Sharma, who directed 'Gadar: Ek Prem Katha', has claimed that 'Baahubali 2: The Conclusion' has not set any record yet. He added, "Gadar had done a business of ₹265 crore in 2001 when the ticket rates were ₹25 only. As per valuation, it is ₹5,000 crore today and Bahubali 2 has just reached some ₹1,500 crore."
