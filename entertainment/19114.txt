A still from Emraan Hashmi's next flick 'Mr X' was posted by filmmaker Mahesh Bhatt on Twitter, which shows Emraan sporting a scary bald look. Announcing the commencement of the principal photography of the movie, Bhatt tweeted, "Principal photography of 'MR X' begins today (Sunday)". The film is being directed by Vikram Bhatt and has been written by Shagufta Rafique.
