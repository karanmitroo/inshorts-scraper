With a collection of Rs 36.22 crores on Friday, Aamir Khan starrer Dhoom-3 has broken all previous opening day records at the box office. The movie was screened at approximately 4500 screens across India and reportedly 820 screens overseas. Earlier, the holiday and non-holiday records were held by Chennai Express (Rs 33.12 crore) and Krrish-3 ( Rs.25 crore) respectively.
