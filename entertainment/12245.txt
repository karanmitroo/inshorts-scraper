The Emraan Hashmi starrer 'Raaz Reboot' which released on Friday is an "unintentionally hilarious" film, wrote India Today. "Raaz Reboot is a tough watch for close to 140 minutes," said Hindustan Times. The Indian Express wrote, "This horror film will leave you shaking, with laughter." The film has been rated 0.5/5(India Today), 1/5(Hindustan Times) and 1.5/5( The Indian Express).
