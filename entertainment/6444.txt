Actress Kangana Ranaut has denied allegations by Apurva Asrani, the writer of 'Simran', that she lied about co-writing the film's script. She revealed that she has introduced key elements in 'Simran' including the main character being a divorced woman, feminist undercurrents and various subplots. "Apurva is not fighting for his credits... He's hellbent on not giving me mine," added Kangana.
