The Kalki Koechlin and Sumeet Vyas starrer 'Ribbon', which released today, "is an insightful take on urban relationships," wrote India Today. 'Ribbon' is a "laudable effort on unplanned parenthood," wrote The Times of India (TOI) while The Quint called it "a film throbbing with realism". The film has been rated 3/5 (India Today, TOI) and 3.5/5 (The Quint).
