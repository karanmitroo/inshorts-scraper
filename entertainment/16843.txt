Actress Parineeti Chopra has reportedly been approached to play the role of Indian cricket captain MS Dhoni's wife Sakshi Rawat in the cricketer's biopic. "The makers are keen to sign Pari as she is as bubbly as Dhoni's wife", media reports said. Titled 'MS Dhoni: The Untold Story', the bipoic will star Sushant Singh Rajput in the lead role.
