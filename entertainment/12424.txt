According to Inshorts Youth of the Nation Poll (YOTN) 2, 50% of the 53,758 respondents said they believe comedians should be allowed to mock eminent personalities. Further, 39% respondents were against the same while 11% were undecided. Comedian Tanmay Bhat this year made headlines when he was condemned for mocking former cricketer Sachin Tendulkar and singer Lata Mangeshkar on Snapchat.
