SS Rajamouli's Telugu film 'Bahubali' has reportedly collected Rs 500 crore globally, becoming the first non-Hindi film from India to enter the club. The film is being screened on more than 2,000 screens worldwide. Other than 'Bahubali', 'PK' and 'Dhoom 3', both starring Aamir Khan, are the only Indian films to have collected over Rs 500 crore globally.
