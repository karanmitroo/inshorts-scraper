Actor Arjun Rampal, while sharing a selfie with composer Bappi Lahiri, wrote, "So awesome, selfie with the man himself #Bappida. Was nervous to show him our rendition of 'Zindagi Meri Dance Dance', so happy he loved it." Arjun has used Lahiri's song 'Zindagi Meri Dance Dance' from the 1987 film 'Dance Dance' in his upcoming biopic 'Daddy'.
