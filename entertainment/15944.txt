Bollywood actor Shah Rukh Khan has topped 'Forbes India Celebrity 100' list this year, based on earnings and fame. Further, Salman Khan ranked second on the list, Amitabh Bachchan third and Aamir Khan fifth. Ranking at number four, Mahendra Singh Dhoni is the only non-actor in top five ranks. Also, Deepika Padukone (rank-9) is the only female in top ten.
