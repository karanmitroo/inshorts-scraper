Action Jackson, Prabhudheva's fifth directorial venture in Bollywood, which hit the screens on Friday has been described as a 'mindless story with gravity-defying action, cliched dialogues and funny-looking villains' by Hindustan Times. The Ajay Devgn and Sonakshi Sinha starrer film has been rated 0.5/5 (Indian Express), 1/5 (India Today), 1.5/5 (Mid-day, HT).
