TV and film producer Ekta Kapoor shared a picture with Union Minister of Textiles Smriti Irani on the occasion of their show 'Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi' completing 17 years on Monday. Smriti played the serial's female lead character Tulsi Virani. The show, which aired for around eight years, is among the longest-running daily soaps on Indian television.
