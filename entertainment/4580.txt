A picture of actors Saif Ali Khan and Kareena Kapoor with son Taimur from their vacation in Gstaad, Switzerland has surfaced online. In the picture, Taimur can be seen sitting in the lap of his father, while Kareena can be seen sitting next to Saif. Taimur, who was born in December last year, is Saif and Kareena's first child together.
