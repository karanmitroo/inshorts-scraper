Bhaag Milkha Bhaag, Bollywood's ode to star athlete Milkha Singh, was made tax free by the Bihar government on Tuesday. "The move is to provide an opportunity to people to watch this movie, which is about one of our greatest athletes," an official said. Directed by Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra, BMB stars Farhan Akhtar, who plays Milkha Singh, and Sonam Kapoor.
