Celebrity sisters Karisma and Kareena Kapoor have not starred together in a movie as yet. As per Kareena, only the 'right script' can bring them together on screen. "People have offered us roles, but we need to get the right script," she said. Karisma, who quit the industry in 2003 after her marriage, dubbed for Kareena's voice in 'Bodyguard'.
