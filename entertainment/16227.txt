Comedian Jerry Seinfeld has topped Forbes' list of highest paid comedians in the  world. As per reports, he has earned $36 million in one year between June 2014 and May 2015. Seinfeld is followed by Kevin Hart ($28.5 million) and Terry Fator ($21.5 million) on the list. With $9.5 million earnings, Indian-American comedian Aziz Ansari ranked sixth on the chart.
