The trailer for the Priyanshu Chatterjee starrer 'Majaz- Ae Gham-e-Dil Kya Karun' has been released. The film is a biopic based on the life of the Indian Urdu poet Asrar ul Haq Majaz Lakhnawi. Directed by Ravindra Singh, the film also stars Rashmi Mishra, Anas Khan, Kajal Raghwani and Neelima Azeem.
