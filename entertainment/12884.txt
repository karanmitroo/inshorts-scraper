Actress Anushka Sharma has become the only Bollywood actress to have two films in the ₹300 crore club. 'Sultan' is Anushka's second film to mint over ₹300 crore at the box-office after the 2014 film 'PK'. Anushka, who made her debut with the 2008 film 'Rab Ne Bana Di Jodi', has appeared in 12 films since then.
