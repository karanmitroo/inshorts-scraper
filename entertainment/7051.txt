A picture of actor Farhan Akhtar from the sets of his upcoming film 'Lucknow Central' has surfaced online. He can be seen with co-actor Gippy Grewal in the picture. Farhan plays a prisoner who forms a band along with other jail inmates in 'Lucknow Central'. The film will also star Diana Penty, Deepak Dobriyal and Ronit Roy, among others.
