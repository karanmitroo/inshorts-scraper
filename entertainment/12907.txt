The teaser for 'Sau Aasmaan', a new song from the Katrina Kaif and Sidharth Malhotra starrer 'Baar Baar Dekho', has been released. It has been sung by Armaan Malik and Neeti Mohan while it has been composed by Armaan's brother Amaal Mallik. The song, which was shot in Thailand, has been picturised on the lead pair of Katrina and Sidharth.
