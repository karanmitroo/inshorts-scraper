Salman Khan has said he is entering the 'Bigg Boss' house as a 'padosi', as part of a format introduced in the reality show's new season. Other 'padosis' include designer-TV personality Sabyasachi Satpathy, actress-model Lucinda Nicholas and two commoners. While 'padosis' are supposed to keep an eye on the other contestants, Salman will keep an eye on all the contestants.
