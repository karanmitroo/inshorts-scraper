The official trailer of Abhishek Bachchan-starrer ‘All Is Well’, an Umesh Shukla directorial, was launched by the film’s actors in Mumbai on Wednesday. The film, also starring Asin along with veteran actors Rishi Kapoor and Supriya Pathak, is about a dysfunctional family who doesn't quite get along with each other. The film is scheduled to release on August 21, 2015.
