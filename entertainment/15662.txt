The song 'Pashmina' from the Aditya Roy Kapur and Katrina Kaif starrer film 'Fitoor' was YouTube's second most viewed video in India on Saturday. The song has been composed and sung by Amit Trivedi, while it has been written by Swanand Kirkire. Posted on the video sharing platform on Wednesday, the video has garnered over 24.88 lakh views so far.
