Actor Shah Rukh Khan has responded to Uber CEO Dara Khosrowshahi's tweet by saying, "You are cooler than the 'King' my man." Earlier, Khosrowshahi posted a picture with SRK while tweeting, "This is the part where the fancy CEO tries to look cool by posing with Bollywood superstar @iamsrk, the King Khan himself." Khosrowshahi was on his first India tour.
