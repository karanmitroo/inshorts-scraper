Social activist Anna Hazare will promote his biopic ‘Anna: Kisan Baburao Hazare’ on ‘The Kapil Sharma Show’ hosted by comedian Kapil Sharma. The biopic has been directed by Shashank Udapurkar who is also playing Anna in the film. It also stars Rajit Kapoor, Sharat Saxena, Govind Namdeo and Tanishaa Mukerji. The film will release on October 14.
