Actress Priyanka Chopra penned a note after the Supreme Court upheld the death sentence awarded to the four convicts in the 2012 Nirbhaya gang-rape case. In the note, Priyanka wrote that she is proud of the verdict. Other Bollywood celebrities including Rishi Kapoor, Taapsee Pannu, Varun Dhawan and Dia Mirza also praised the Supreme Court's decision.
