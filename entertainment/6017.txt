Google marked the 81st birth anniversary of late actress Nutan with a doodle on Sunday. Nutan is best known for her work in films like 'Bandini' (1963), 'Sujata' (1960). She holds the record for maximum Filmfare Award wins for Best Actress, along with niece Kajol. Nutan acted in over 65 Hindi films in a career spanning more than 40 years.
