Bollywood celebrities took to social media to wish actor Hrithik Roshan on the occasion of his 43rd birthday on January 10, 2017. "May #Kaabil and everything else in your life touch super-heroic heights!," tweeted Akshay Kumar.  "Happy birthday to one of the most amazing dancers," wrote Govinda. Jacqueline Fernandez tweeted, "Happy Birthday @iHrithik! Break a leg like only you can!"
