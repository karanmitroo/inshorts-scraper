'Sab Tera', the first song from the Tiger Shroff and Shraddha Kapoor starrer film 'Baaghi' has been released. Composed by Amaal Mallik, the song has been sung by Armaan Malik and Shraddha herself. 'Baaghi' has been directed by Sabbir Khan and marks the debut of Telugu actor Sudheer Babu in Bollywood. It is scheduled to release on April 29.
