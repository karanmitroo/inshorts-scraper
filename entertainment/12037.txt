Screenplay writer Gulzar has revealed that late actor Rajesh Khanna worked in the 1971 film 'Anand' at a nominal fee as he "liked" the story. "[Khanna] threw a big party after the success of Anand," Gulzar was quoted as saying. Gulzar wrote the screenplay and dialogues for 'Anand', which won the National Award for the Best Feature Film in Hindi.
