Actor Rishi Kapoor has tweeted that the Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor starrer upcoming film 'Befikre' is an "adult version" of his 1989 romantic drama film 'Chandni', co-starring actress Sridevi. "An outstanding hot trailer! Chandni ka 2016 updated "adult version". Exotic locale, natural actors, naughty, witty, funny, romantic!" wrote Kapoor.
