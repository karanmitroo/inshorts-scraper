A new trailer of the upcoming science fiction disaster film 'Independence Day: Resurgence' has been released. Directed by Roland Emmerich, the film is a sequel to the 1996 film 'Independence Day'. It features Jeff Goldblum, Bill Pullman and Brent Spiner reprising their roles, alongside newcomers Liam Hemsworth, Jessie Usher and Maika Monroe. The film will release on June 24.
