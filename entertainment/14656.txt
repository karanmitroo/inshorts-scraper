The Shah Rukh Khan starrer film 'Fan', which released on Friday, collected ₹52.35 crore in its first weekend in India, becoming the highest opening weekend grosser of 2016. The film surpassed 'Airlift', which collected ₹44.30 crore. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, 'Fan' collected ₹19.20 crore on Friday, ₹15.40 crore on Saturday and ₹17.75 crore on Sunday.
