The Michael Keaton starrer 'The Founder' is "a fast food take on McDonald's story", wrote Indian Express. Hindustan Times wrote, it "effectively indicts unsavoury business ethics, corporate greed and the predatory nature of capitalist enterprise." Times of India wrote, "This business drama falls short of being a powerful biopic." It was rated 2.5/5 (Indian Express), 3/5 (HT) and 3.5/5 (TOI).
