Actress Jacqueline Fernandes, who turned 30 on Tuesday, celebrated the day by visiting the BSF Chhawla Camp in New Delhi, along with her 'Brothers' co-stars Akshay Kumar and Siddharth Malhotra. Jacqueline posted a picture of the celebration on Instagram and wrote, "Awesome start to a birthday!!! with #brothers@akshaykumar @s1dofficial #bordersecurityforce". 'Brothers' will release on Aug 14.
