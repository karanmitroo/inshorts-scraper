As per reports, veteran actress Rekha will be playing the role of Salman Khan's mother in his upcoming film 'Sultan'. Interestingly, Salman had made his debut in Rekha-starrer 'Biwi Ho Toh Aisi', 30 years ago. Rekha had played Salman's sister-in-law in the film. The actress was in news recently after she decided to opt out of Abhishek Kapoor's 'Fitoor'.
