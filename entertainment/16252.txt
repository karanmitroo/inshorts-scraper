Diganth, Anushka Ranjan and Karan Grover starrer 'Wedding Pullav' which released on Friday is more like an overcooked ‘khhichdi’, wrote Indian Express. "It's loaded with mandatory party/ wedding songs, honeymoon jokes, friendly banter, mummiji's drama etc," said TOI. The Binod Pradhan directorial has been rated 0.5/5 (Indian Express), 1/5 (NDTV) and 1.5/5 (TOI).
