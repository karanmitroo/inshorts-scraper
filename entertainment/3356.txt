YouTube personality Dhinchak Pooja has released the video for her song 'Baapu Dede Thoda Cash'. This was the first song she had released following the removal of several of her music videos from YouTube over copyright issues. Dhinchak Pooja is known for her songs like 'Dilon Ka Shooter', 'Swag Meri Topi' and 'Selfie Maine Leli Aaj'.
