Technology giant Google paid tribute to late singer Mohammed Rafi with a doodle on his 93rd birth anniversary on Sunday. Born in Amritsar, Punjab, Rafi is known for songs like 'Kya Hua Tera Wada' and 'Chura Liya Hai Tumne Jo Dil Ko' among others. The National Award-winning singer died at the age of 55 due to a heart attack.
