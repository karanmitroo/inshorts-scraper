The teaser of Rajinikanth's film 'Kabali' has become the most viewed Indian film teaser on YouTube with over 17.15 million views, the makers of the film claimed on Friday. Released on May 1, it broke the record held by the teaser of 'Dhoom 3', with 17.05 million views. Salman Khan's 'Sultan' stands third with 17.02 million views for its teaser.
