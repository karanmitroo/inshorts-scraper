Anil Sharma's action-drama movie 'Singh Saab the Great' featuring Sunny Deol, Urvashi Rautela, and Amrita Rao released this Friday. The movie is about a collector who becomes a victim of the law and on his return from jail, vows to eradicate corruption. The movie has been rated 2 by DNA, 3 by TOI and 3.5 by NDTV and Zee News.
