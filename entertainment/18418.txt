Populist writer Chetan Bhagat's next novel, 'Half Girlfriend' will have a film adaptation, and Kriti Sanon (of 'Heropanti' fame) is being considered for the lead, reveals a source. However, Alia Bhatt, who was the lead in one of Bhagat's novel's film adaptation, '2 States',  tops the list of actresses who are being considered. 'Half Girlfriend' will be launched on Oct-1.
