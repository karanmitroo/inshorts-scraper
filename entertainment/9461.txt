Karan Johar, when asked by Alia Bhatt whom he would 'Kill, Marry and Hook Up' in an episode of Koffee with Karan, said he'd marry Deepika Padukone, hook up with Katrina Kaif and kill Aishwarya Rai Bachchan. When asked about killing Aishwarya, Karan said, "Abhishek and I grew up together...so will have to kill her because of my family connections."
