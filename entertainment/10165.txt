There were 14 newcomers who featured on the '2016 Celebrity 100' list by Forbes India. Rapper Badshah, who was part of songs including 'Kaala Chashma' from 'Baar Baar Dekho' and 'Baby Ko Bass Pasand Hai' from 'Sultan', featured at the 81st rank. Celebrity chef Sanjeev Kapoor took the 73rd spot while badminton player PV Sindhu ranked 62nd on the list.
