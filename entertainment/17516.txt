Actor Shashi Kapoor, who recently celebrated his 77th birthday, will receive this year's Dadasaheb Phalke Award, the highest honour in Indian Cinema. Kapoor starred in a number of hit movies like 'Deewar' and 'Junoon'. He also featured in a number of English movies produced by Merchant Ivory. He won the National Award thrice and received the Padma Bhushan in 2011.
