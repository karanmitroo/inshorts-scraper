Akshay Kumar took to social media to share a video of himself dancing on the recreated version of song 'Tu Cheez Badi Hai Mast Mast' from his 1994 film 'Mohra'. He captioned the video, "Still got those mast mast moves, What say?" The song, originally featuring Raveena Tandon, has been recreated for the Mustafa Burmawala and Kiara Advani starrer 'Machine'.
