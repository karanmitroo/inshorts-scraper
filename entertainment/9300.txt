'The Humma Song' from the Shraddha Kapoor and Aditya Roy Kapur starrer 'OK Jaanu' has become the fastest among Bollywood tracks to get over 100 million views on YouTube. It's a recreation of the original 'Humma Humma' song from the 1995 film 'Bombay'. The new version was sung by Jubin Nautiyal and Shashaa Tirupati and features a rap by Badshah.
