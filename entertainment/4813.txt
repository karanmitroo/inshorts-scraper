Actor Amitabh Bachchan tweeted on comedian Mehmood's 13th death anniversary on Sunday. He wrote, "An amazing artist comedian singer producer director .. and a kind soul...they don't make them like him anymore!" while sharing pictures of Mehmood. He worked in over 300 films and was best known for his roles in 'Padosan' (1968), 'Bhoot Bungla' (1965), among others.
