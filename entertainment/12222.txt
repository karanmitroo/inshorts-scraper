The release date of the Varun Dhawan starrer 'Judwaa 2' has been announced as September 29, 2017. Varun's father David Dhawan will be returning as the director for the film, which is a sequel to the 1997 film 'Judwaa'. Earlier, reports emerged that Alia Bhatt and Shraddha Kapoor would star opposite Varun, who will be seen in a double role.
