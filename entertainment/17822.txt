Inspiring biopic 'Bhaag Milkha Bhaag', based on Indian athlete Milkha Singh, was released in Japan after gaining much applauses by Indian audiences. Actor Farhan Akhtar, who played the role of Milkha Singh, took to Twitter to announce "Bhaag Milkha Bhaag released in Japan this week. Thank you to all there for showing their love to our film."
