Varun Dhawan-starrer Badlapur did steady business at the domestic box office as it collected Rs.30.90 crore in five days, despite ongoing cricket World Cup. The film, on the other hand, minted approx Rs.6.23 crore in the international market in the opening weekend. The Sriram Raghavan directorial is a revenge drama that also stars Huma Qureshi, Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Yami Gautam.
