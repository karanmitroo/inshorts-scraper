Model-actor Milind Soman walked the runway with 101-year-old marathon runner Mann Kaur at the Amazon India Fashion Week. They walked for the show by designer Nida Mahmood, who collaborated with Milind's active lifestyle brand 'Deivee'. Other personalities who walked for the show included plus size yoga teacher Dolly Singh and Anshu Jamsenpa, who climbed Mt Everest twice within 5 days.
