Actor Salman Khan has revealed that his brother-in-law Aayush Sharma's Bollywood acting debut will be titled 'Loveratri'. Salman's production house Salman Khan Films will be launching Aayush in the film, which will also mark the directorial debut of Abhiraj Minawala. 'Loveratri' is a love story based in Gujarat and is scheduled to release in 2018.
