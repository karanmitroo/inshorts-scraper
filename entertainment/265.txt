Rapper Badshah, along with singer Aastha Gill, has sung a song titled 'Happy Happy' for Irrfan Khan starrer 'Blackmail'. Badshah has also composed the song and written its lyrics. Directed by Abhinay Deo and also starring Kirti Kulhari, Arunoday Singh, Divya Dutta and Omi Vaidya, the film is scheduled to release on April 6.
