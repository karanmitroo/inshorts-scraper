After 12-year gap, Hrithik Roshan and Kareena Kapoor will be seen together in 'Shuddhi' - a love-story with reincarnation theme, to be produced and directed by Karan Johar and Karan Malhotra, the team behind the Rs 100 crore film, 'Agneepath' (2012). The duo was last seen together in 2001, in Subhash Ghai's Yaadein and in Karan's Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham.
