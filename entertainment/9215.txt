Actor Jackie Shroff has said that he would choose actors Ranbir Kapoor and Ranveer Singh to play the brothers, if a remake of his 1989 film 'Ram Lakhan' was made. Jackie played inspector Ram Pratap Singh while Anil Kapoor played inspector Lakhan Pratap Singh in the Subhash Ghai directorial. Jackie gave this response during his appearance on 'Koffee With Karan'.
