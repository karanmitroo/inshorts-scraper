The trailer of the Govinda starrer film 'Aa Gaya Hero' has been released. The action comedy film will also feature Ashutosh Rana, Makarand Deshpande and Harish Kumar, among others. Actor Salman Khan, in his tweet about the trailer, wrote, "Aa gaya mera Hero. Mera Partner. Superb Trailer." Produced by Govinda, the film will release on February 24, 2017.
