The International Premier Tennis League, which kickstarted Monday, was graced by Bollywood and sport celebrities like Deepika Padukone, Akshay Kumar, Riteish Deshmukh, Roger Federer, Novak Djokovic, Sania Mirza and Sunil Gavaskar. Bollywood actress Deepika Padukone even picked up a racquet to show off her tennis skills. She played a doubles match with Roger Federer, against Novak Djokovic.
