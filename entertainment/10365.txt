'The Humma Song', a new song from the Shraddha Kapoor-Aditya Roy Kapur starrer film 'OK Jaanu' has been released. It is a recreated version of AR Rahman's song 'Humma Humma' from 1998 film 'Bombay'. Recreated by Badshah, it has been sung by Jubin Nautiyal, Shashaa Tirupati and Badshah. Directed by Shaad Ali, 'OK Jaanu' will release on January 13, 2017.
