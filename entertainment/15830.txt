'MS Dhoni: The Untold Story', a film based on Indian cricketer MS Dhoni's life, starring Sushant Singh Rajput, will release on September 2, 2016. The release date of the film was announced by the actor himself on Twitter on Monday. The film is being directed by Neeraj Pandey, who has been accredited with 'A Wednesday', 'Special 26' and 'Baby'.
