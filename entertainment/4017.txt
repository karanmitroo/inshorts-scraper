As per reports, actor Ranveer Singh will host the first episode of yoga guru Ramdev's upcoming bhajan reality show 'Om Shanti Om'. A source said, "Ranveer has been roped in for the Shubhaarambh episode, after which Aparshakti Khurana will take over as host." Ranveer will reportedly open the show by performing on the 'Gajanana' song from 'Bajirao Mastani'.
