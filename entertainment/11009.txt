The music video of song 'Alizeh', from the Ranbir Kapoor, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Anushka Sharma-starrer 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' has been released. The song, named after Anushka's character in the film, has been composed by Pritam, sung by Arijit Singh, Ash King and Shashwat Singh, and lyrics by Amitabh Bhattacharya. The film, directed by Karan Johar, released in October.
