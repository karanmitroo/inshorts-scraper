Actress Deepika Padukone has referred to Aishwarya Rai Bachchan as "the torch bearer for putting India on the global map". She added, "[E]veryone who has managed to break through into the western market, whether it is Anil, Irfan, Priyanka, or myself... we have all done a great job." Deepika will make her Hollywood debut with 'xXx: Return of Xander Cage'.
