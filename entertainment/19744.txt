"Shuddh Desi Romance", starring National award winner Parineeti and "Kai Po Che" fame Sushant, alongwith "Lunchbox", featuring Irrfan Khan and Nawazuddin Siddiqui, have been selected for gala premieres at the 38th Toronto International Film Festival being held this September. Director Ritesh Batra's "Lunchbox" already had a successful world premiere at Cannes' Critics Week where it bagged the viewer's choice award.
