The Prabhu Deva, Sonu Sood and Tamannaah Bhatia starrer 'Tutak Tutak Tutiya' has "only horror" in it and "no comedy," wrote The Indian Express. Hindustan Times wrote, "[T]he dance numbers and comic timing of Prabhudheva alone make it work." "[T]he horror comedy turns into a drama," said The Times of India. It was rated 0.5/5(Indian Express), 2/5(HT) and 3/5(TOI).
