'Baahubali- The Beginning' was named the Best Film at the 63rd National Film Awards announced on Monday. Baahubali is an epic historical fiction film directed by SS Rajamouli. It was made in Telugu and Tamil simultaneously and was dubbed into Hindi, Malayalam and French. The movie features Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Tamannaah and Anushka Shetty in lead roles.
