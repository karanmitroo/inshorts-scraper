Shah Rukh Khan's 'Fan' and Salman Khan's 'Sultan' will be screened at the 21st Busan International Film Festival, which will be held from October 6-15. While the Maneesh Sharma-directorial 'Fan' will be screened in the 'A Window on Asian Cinema' section, director Ali Abbas Zafar's 'Sultan' will be screened in the 'Open Cinema' section.
