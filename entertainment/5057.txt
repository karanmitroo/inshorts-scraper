Actors Salman Khan, Katrina Kaif, Alia Bhatt and Varun Dhawan were among the celebrities who attended the IIFA Rocks event in New York. Katrina wore a ruffled saree and golden tassel blouse by Abu Jani Sandeep Khosla. Alia chose a cream coloured lehenga by Manish Malhotra. Meanwhile, Taapsee Pannu paired her red bridal gown by Komal Sood with a bindi.
