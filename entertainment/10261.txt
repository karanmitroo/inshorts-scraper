The trailer of the Ranbir Kapoor and Katrina Kaif starrer 'Jagga Jasoos' has been released. Ranbir will portray the role of detective Jagga, who tries to find the whereabouts of his father. Interestingly, the trailer has no dialogues. Also starring Adah Sharma and Saurabh Shukla, and directed by Anurag Basu, the film is scheduled to release on April 7, 2017.
