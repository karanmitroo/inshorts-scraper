Director-producer Arbaaz Khan on Tuesday posted a Dubsmash video on Instagram over reports of his separation with wife Malaika Arora Khan. He dubsmashed on the popular song ‘Kuch toh log kahenge’ from the 1972 film Amar Prem. "Some people need to mind their business, stop talking and writing bullshit and concentrate on their own miserable lives," he captioned the post.
