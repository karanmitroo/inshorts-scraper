'Meer-E-Kaarwan', a new song from the Farhan Akhtar and Diana Penty starrer 'Lucknow Central', has been released. The track has been composed by Rochak Kohli, sung by Amit Mishra and Neeti Mohan with lyrics by Adheesh Verma. Directed by Ranjit Tiwari, the film is scheduled to released on September 15.
