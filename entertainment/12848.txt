Eros Now has announced India's first ever celebrity expedition to the Siachen glacier, the world's highest battleground. Titled 'Salute Siachen', the initiative is a tribute to the Indian Army for making history by traversing the glacier in 1984. Nine celebrities, including Arjun Rampal, Rannvijay Singha and Sonnalli Seygall will come together for the 20-day expedition this Independence Day.
