The teaser of the upcoming Telugu film 'Sardaar Gabbar Singh' was YouTube's most shared video on Facebook and Twitter in India on Friday. The action film, starring Pawan Kalyan, Kajal Aggarwal and Sharad Kelkar, has been directed by KS Ravindra. The teaser was posted on the video-sharing platform on Thursday and has garnered over 8.42 lakh views so far.
