'X-Men: Apocalypse' director Bryan Singer has confirmed that a 'X-Force' film script is being developed while reportedly hinting at the possibility of the film featuring 'X-23', a female clone of 'Wolverine'. 'X-23' had first appeared in season 3 of the animated series 'X-Men: Evolution'  (2003). Notably, 'X-Men: Apocalypse', the ninth film in the 'X-Men' series, will release on May 27.
