A new song titled 'Tere Mere Dil' from the Farhan Akhtar and Arjun Rampal-starrer musical drama film 'Rock On 2' has been released. The song has been composed by music composer trio Shankar-Ehsaan-Loy with vocals by Shraddha Kapoor and lyrics by the legendary Javed Akhtar. Directed by Shujaat Saudagar, 'Rock On 2' will release on November 11.
