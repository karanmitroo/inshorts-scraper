Actress Anushka Sharma has said she was mesmerised by Aishwarya's beauty and not just her persona while working with her in the forthcoming film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil'. "I have one scene with her but it's very impactful. For me, sharing screen space with her feels great," the 28-year-old actress said. The Karan Johar directorial will release on October 28.
