A Facebook page called 'Jaaton Ka Adda' live streamed Sanjay Leela Bhansali's film 'Padmaavat' from a movie theatre screening the film. Over 15,000 people had shared the link and the video on Facebook received over 3.5 lakh views by the afternoon of the film's release. Meanwhile, protests against the film's screening were led by Karni Sena in several cities.
