Actress Rani Mukerji is set to make her comeback with Yash Raj Film's upcoming project titled 'Hichki'. Directed by Siddharth P Malhotra, the film will be a positive story about a woman who turns her biggest weakness into her strength. Notably, Rani last featured in the 2014 film 'Mardaani', wherein she played the role of a police officer.
