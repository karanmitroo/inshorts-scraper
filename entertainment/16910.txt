Actor Randeep Hooda has been roped in to play the lead role in the Sarabjit Singh biopic, director Omung Kumar disclosed the news. “Aishwarya and @RandeepHooda in my next film SARBJIT coming soon", he tweeted. Earlier, Kumar had confirmed that Aishwarya Rai will be playing Sarabjit's sister, Dalbir, who fought for her brother’s release from the Lahore jail.
