In celebration of the "golden jubilee" of the movie, 'MSG:2 The Messenger' reportedly had 777 screenings across 30 Rajasthani cities on Sunday. The movie has earned Rs 395.15 crore in the 50 days since its release this September, said its spokesperson Ajay Dhameeja. Similar screenings were held in Delhi, Maharastra, Punjab, UP and Haryana over the last few days.
