Television actress Jaya Bhattacharya, known for playing 'Payal' in the Smriti Irani starrer serial 'Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi', has said that she is badly in need of work as her mother is in the ICU. "My mother has been admitted from 26 November. I'm in a deep fix as all my finances are down the drain," added Jaya.
