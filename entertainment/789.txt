According to reports, Sridevi's daughter Janhvi Kapoor will star as the lead actress in Ranveer Singh's upcoming film 'Simmba'. The film, which will feature Ranveer in the role of a police officer, is a remake of the Telugu action movie 'Temper'. Directed by Rohit Shetty and produced by Karan Johar, 'Simmba' is scheduled to release on December 28.
