Anushka Sharma along with rumoured boyfriend Virat Kohli, Shah Rukh Khan and Kareena Kapoor were among the celebrities attending fashion designer Manish Malhotra's 50th birthday party, hosted by Karan Johar on Monday. Aishwarya Rai, Sonam Kapoor, Alia Bhatt and Katrina Kaif were also present. Earlier, Manish celebrated his birthday at his residence, where he cut a 50 kg cake.
