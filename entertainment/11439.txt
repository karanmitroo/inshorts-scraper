Singer Justin Bieber has rented a mansion valued at £4.75 million (around ₹38 crore) in England. The mansion features a helipad, a football pitch, a wine cellar, a swimming pool and a sports bar. The seven-bedroom property is built into a hillside. Known as 'Steppingstone,' it reportedly costs over £17,000 (around ₹13 lakh) a month to rent.
