Actress Priyanka Chopra posted a picture of herself on Instagram to wish people on Holi. "Do me a favour let's play Holi! Here is wishing everyone a safe beautiful wonder filled Holi," wrote Priyanka alongside the picture. 'Do Me A Favor Lets Play Holi' was a song from the 2005 Priyanka Chopra starrer 'Waqt: The Race Against Time'.
