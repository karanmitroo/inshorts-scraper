Singer Justin Timberlake has released a new track, 'Can’t Stop the Feeling', three years after the release of his last album 'The 20/20 Experience – 2 of 2' in 2013. The new track is part of the upcoming animated film 'Trolls'. The song's video features Timberlake's co-stars from the film including Gwen Stefani, Anna Kendrick, James Corden and Kunal Nayyar.
