Actress-singer Priyanka Chopra's track "I Can't Make You Love Me" has bagged the 7th position in the list of 100 international songs and albums, released by Hungama.com. Topped by Jessie J, Ariana Grande and Nicki Minaj's song Bang Bang, the list also features Oscar-winning composer AR Rahman's tracks like "My Mind is a Stranger Without You" and "New Beginnings."
