The first poster of Kangana Ranaut and Imran Khan starrer 'Katti Batti' was revealed on Friday by film's director Nikhil Advani. The film is scheduled to release on September 18 and its trailer will be out on June 14. Imran, who was last seen in 'Gori Tere Pyar Mein', will be seen on the silver screen after two years.
