As per reports, Priyanka Chopra will star opposite Aamir Khan in the biopic on Rakesh Sharma, who was the first Indian to travel to space. Titled 'Salute', it is said to be directed by Mahesh Mathai. Aamir will also co-produce the film with Siddharth Roy Kapur and Ronnie Screwvala. Priyanka's mother however said that she's unaware of any such development.
