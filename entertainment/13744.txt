Following the death of the 27-year-old actor Anton Yelchin who portrayed the role of 'Pavel Chekov' in the 'Star Trek' reboot series, 'Anton Yelchin' was the second most trending Google search in India on Monday. It was most looked up in Mizoram, Goa and Kerala. 'Yoga Day', 'Baba Siddique' and 'Brexit' were some of the other trending searches.
