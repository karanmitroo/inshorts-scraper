Actor Salman Khan has revealed television actress Shilpa Shinde, who earlier played the lead character in 'Bhabi Ji Ghar Par Hai', as the first celebrity contestant on Bigg Boss 11. In a short promo, Salman is shown introducing her while saying, "Ye rahi India ki sabse chaheti." Dawood Ibrahim's relative and Haryanvi singer Sapna Choudhary are also participants this season.
