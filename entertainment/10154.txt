Actor Sidharth Malhotra and actress Jacqueline Fernandes will appear in an upcoming episode of the talk show 'Koffee With Karan'. "Most fun I have had on a cup of koffee," tweeted Sidharth, while sharing a picture with Jacqueline and the show's host Karan Johar. Notably, Jacqueline and Sidharth are starring together in 'Reload', which releases on August 25, 2017.
