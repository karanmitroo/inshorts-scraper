Actress Aishwarya Rai was named 'Vogue Influencer Of The Decade' while Shah Rukh Khan won the award for 'Vogue Entertainer of the Decade' at this year's Vogue Women Of The Year Awards. Anushka won 'Game Changer of The Year Award' while Karan Johar was named 'Vogue Man of the Year'. Further, Anita Dongre was named 'Designer Of The Year'.
