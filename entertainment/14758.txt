Shraddha Kapoor has revealed that she will be singing all her songs in her upcoming film 'Rock On 2'. Earlier, the actress recorded songs for her films including 'Ek Villain' and 'Haider'. 'Rock On 2', starring Farhan Akhtar, Arjun Rampal, Prachi Desai and Purab Kohli, is a sequel to the 2008 film 'Rock On'. It will release on November 11.
