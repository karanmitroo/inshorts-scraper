Director Siddharth Anand has said he will cast either actress Deepika Padukone or Priyanka Chopra for his next Indo-China project, 'Love In Beijing'. Anand, who previously directed 'Bang Bang', further said, "It's a cross-cultural romance set in Beijing. I'm excited to showcase another culture through this film." Reportedly, Chinese actor Deng Chao is being considered for the lead actor's role.
