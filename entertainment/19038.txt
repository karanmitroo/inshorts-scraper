Actors Sonakshi Sinha and Ajay Devgn were announced the worst actors of 2013 at the 6th Golden Kela Awards on Saturday for 'R...Rajkumar' and 'Himmatwala', which was also named the worst film. Sanjay Leela Bhansali got the worst director award for 'Ram Leela', while the ‘Baawra Ho Gaya Hai Ke’ award went to Aamir Khan for ‘Dhoom 3’.
