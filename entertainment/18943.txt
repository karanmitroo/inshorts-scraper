Mishti Mukherjee (real name Indrani Chakraborty) is the central showpiece of ‘Kaanchi’, Subhash Ghai’s – the maker of Pardes and Taal - first film since Yuvraaj (2008). The movie has received mixed critics’ response, with the script and music facing the most criticism. It has been rated 1 (Indian Express), 2 (Zee), 2.5 (Times of India; koimoi.com) and 3 (OneIndia).
