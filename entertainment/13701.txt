The first promo of the ninth season of celebrity dance reality show 'Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa' has been released. The promo features Bollywood actress Jacqueline Fernandes, who will be a judge in the show, alongside Karan Johar and Ganesh Hegde. The new season, to be hosted by Manish Paul, will reportedly have contestants including Karishma Tanna and Surveen Chawla, among others.
