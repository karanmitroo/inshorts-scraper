Actor Ajay Devgn has said that it is better for his film 'Shivaay' and filmmaker Karan Johar's 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' to avoid a clash at the box-office as both the films are scheduled to release on October 28. However, Ajay clairifed, "There is no pressure... Both films are of different genres. [W]e have space for both to do well."
