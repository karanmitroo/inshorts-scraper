The official trailer of superstar Salman Khan's upcoming film 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' was released on YouTube on Thursday. Salman, who is playing a Hanuman devotee in the film, is seen chanting Hanuman Chalisa in the trailer. The Kabir Khan directorial, also starring Kareena Kapoor Khan as Salman's love interest and Nawazuddin Siddiqui as a journalist, is slated to release on Eid.
