Film producer Sajid Nadiadwala will reportedly be launching actress Kriti Sanon's sister Nupur Sanon in Bollywood. Kriti also made her Bollywood debut with Sajid's 2014 film 'Heropanti'. Earlier, Kriti mentioned that Nupur was keen on joining Bollywood, while adding, "When I look back, I am proud of my journey, and I'm glad that she wants to do it her way."
