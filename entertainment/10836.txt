Ali Haji, who was a child actor in Salman Khan's 2007 film 'Partner', has shared a recent picture with Salman alongside an old picture from the film. Haji captioned the picture, "It's been a while." Notably, Haji also starred in Aamir Khan's 2006 'Fanaa' and the 2007 film 'Ta Ra Rum Pum' among others.
