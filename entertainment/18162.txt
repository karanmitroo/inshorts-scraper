'Balika Vadhu' star Siddharth Shukla, who made his Bollywood debut with 'Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania', has signed a three-film deal with Karan Johar. When asked to comment on Karan's acting skills in 'Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge', Siddharth said, "Every director is a very good actor. I would judge his acting skills on the basis of his performance in 'Bombay Velvet'."
