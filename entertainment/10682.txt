Cricketer Yuvraj Singh got married to actress Hazel Keech on Wednesday at the Fatehgarh Sahib gurudwara in Punjab. Outfits of both, the bride and groom were designed by JJ Valaya, with Yuvraj wearing a maroon sherwani and Hazel a full-length anarkali in the same shade. The wedding was preceded by a mehendi and sangeet ceremony on Tuesday.
