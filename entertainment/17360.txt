Bollywood actor Randeep Hooda has been roped in a social thriller 'Yeh Laal Rang', a Syed Ahman Afzal directorial. The film, set in a small town of Haryana, is based on the issue of theft from blood banks. "Randeep loved the story when he heard it," said Afzal. The Krian Media production is set to go on floors this July.
