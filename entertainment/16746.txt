The official trailer of Akshay Kumar and Sidharth Malhotra's upcoming film 'Brothers' has got over 8 million views on YouTube. The trailer was uploaded on the video-sharing website on June 10. The film's director Karan Johar took to Twitter to make the announcement and posted, "The #Brothers trailer at 8 million!!! #BrothersAreComing." The film will release on August 14.
