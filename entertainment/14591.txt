The official trailer for the Akshay Kumar starrer 'Housefull 3' was released on Sunday. Directed by Sajid Samji and Farhad Samji, the film also stars Riteish Deshmukh and Abhishek Bachchan along with Jacqueline Fernandez, Nargis Fakhri and Lisa Haydon. The film, which is the third instalment in the 'Housefull' franchise, is scheduled to release on June 3.
