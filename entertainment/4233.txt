Sanjay Dutt's daughter Trishala Dutt took to Instagram to thank her father for dedicating his comeback film 'Bhoomi' to her. Sanjay chose Trishala's birthday to release the trailer of his film. Trishala further wrote, "Thank you for gifting & dedicating your hard work to me! I'm so proud of you dad. For every setback, God has a major comeback."
