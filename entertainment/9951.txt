The box office in USA and Canada earned $11.4 billion (₹77,600 crore) in 2016, making it the highest-earning year in Hollywood ever, according to box-office analyst ComScore. 'Finding Dory' was the highest grosser with earnings of ₹3,300 crore, while 'Rogue One: A Star Wars Story' stood second. Interestingly, six out of the ten highest grossing films were produced by Disney.
