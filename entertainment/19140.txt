As per trade analyst Taran Adarsh, Vinil Mathew’s ‘Hasee Toh Phasee’ collected Rs.18.5 crore on its opening weekend in India. The romantic comedy, starring Parineeti Chopra and Sidharth Malhotra was made on an approximate budget of Rs.25 crore. "Thank you for the amazing response guys! We sure are happy," Parineeti tweeted.
