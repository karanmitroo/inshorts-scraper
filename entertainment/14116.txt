Actress Huma Qureshi has featured on the cover of the magazine 'Femina Wedding Times' for its June issue. The 29-year-old actress can be seen wearing a blue embroidered blouse by designer Payal Singhal paired with a sheer pleated skirt from the label 'Pinnacle' by Shruti Sancheti. Her look has been accessorised with a nose accessory, bracelets and rings.
