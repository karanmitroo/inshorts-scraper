The Dev Patel starrer 'The Man Who Knew Infinity', which released on Friday, is "more exotic than real", wrote Hindustan Times. Times of India (TOI) called the film "both enjoyable and gripping", while IANS said that it "unravels the mystique of the unschooled maestro (Ramanujan). It has been rated 2.5/5 (HT), 3.5/5 (TOI) and 5/5 (IANS).
