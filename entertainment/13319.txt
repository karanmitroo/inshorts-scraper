Actress Parineeti Chopra has shared a childhood picture with her cousin actress Priyanka Chopra to wish Priyanka, who turned 34 on Monday. "To the most inspiring sister in the world-HAPPPY BDAY!! wish u all the happiness! (love you) mimi didi," tweeted Parineeti. Actors Alia Bhatt, Sonam Kapoor, Farhan Akhtar and boxing champion Mary Kom also tweeted birthday wishes for Priyanka.
