'Dimpi De Naal Bhaage Bunty', a new song from the Taapsee Pannu and Amit Sadh starrer film 'RunningShaadi.com' has been released. The song has been composed by Abhishek-Akshay, with vocals by late singer Labh Janjua and lyrics by Shellee. Directed by Amit Roy and produced by Shoojit Sircar, the romantic comedy film is scheduled to release on February 3.
