Deepika Padukone, who has risen to the top of the box-office charts with four back-to- back hits in 2013 - Race 2, Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani, Chennai Express, and Goliyon Ki Raasleela Ram-Leela - will reportedly star with Aamir Khan in a film, which will be produced by Excel Entertainment. The shooting is likely to commence from July or August.
