A Chandigarh court Friday issued a legal  notice to actor Aamir Khan. The petition, filed by advocate Mandeep Kaur, alleges that the actor, in his show 'Satyamev Jyate', violated Supreme Court's order on homosexuality. An episode aired on Oct 19 had discussed the lifestyle and rights of eunuchs and gays/lesbians. Khan has been given time till Dec 19 to respond.
