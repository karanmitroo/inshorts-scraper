The Shah Rukh Khan-starrer film 'Fan', which released on Friday, "is not devoid of loopholes. But it still makes for a thrilling watch", wrote Hindustan Times. "Khan does a fabulous job", India Today said. "Fan is a not a great film, it is a brave film", said DNA. The film has been rated 3.5/5 (India Today, HT) and 3/5 (NDTV).
