Actress Parineeti Chopra has made her Bollywood singing debut with the song 'Maana Ke Hum Yaar Nahin' from her upcoming film 'Meri Pyaari Bindu'. The song has been composed by Sachin-Jigar with lyrics by Kausar Munir. Also featuring actor Ayushmann Khurrana, the film has been produced by Yash Raj Films. It is scheduled to release on May 12.
