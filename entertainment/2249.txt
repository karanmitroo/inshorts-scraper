The posters of actress Sridevi's daughter Janhvi Kapoor's Bollywood acting debut titled 'Dhadak' have been unveiled. Janhvi will star opposite Shahid Kapoor's half-brother Ishaan Khatter in the Shashank Khaitan directorial. Co-produced by Karan Johar, 'Dhadak' is reportedly a remake of the 2016 Marathi film 'Sairat', and is scheduled to release on July 6, 2018.
