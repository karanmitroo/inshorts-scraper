According to sources, filmmaker Sajid Nadiadwala has roped in Akshay Kumar and Abhishek Bachchan, after a gap of nearly 12 years, for the next film Housefull 3 in his super-successful franchise. The film will be writer duo Sajid-Farhad's second directorial venture. Earlier, both the actors were last seen in the romantic drama - Haan Maine Bhi Pyaar Kiya (2002).
