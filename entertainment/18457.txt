When asked whose real life was interesting enough to watch on-screen, actor and Bigg Boss host Salman Khan said he would like to see Sanjay Dutt inside the 'Big Boss' house. "I would like Sanjay Dutt to be transfered from the jail to the 'Bigg Boss' house," he said. Dutt would prefer jail than the 'Bigg Boss' house, Salman added.
