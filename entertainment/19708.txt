Akshay Kumar starrer 'Once Upon A Time in Mumbai Dobaara!' has reportedly been sold for Rs15-18 crores for distribution in the Delhi-UP-Punjab territories to a start-up company by distributor Anil Agarwal and Film-financier Bharat Shah. Tanuj Garg, CEO Balaji Motion Pictures confirmed the deal but didn't disclose the amount. The Milan Luthria directed movie is set to release on August-15.
