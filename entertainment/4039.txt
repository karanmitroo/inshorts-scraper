According to reports, actor R Madhavan will play the lead opposite Aishwarya Rai Bachchan in 'Fanney Khan'. Talking about Aishwarya's character, the film's co-producer Prerna Arora had said, "In our film she is the spunky, enchanting female protagonist." The musical comedy also stars Anil Kapoor and is said to be inspired by Dominique Deruddere's Oscar-nominated 'Everybody's Famous!' (2000).
