Actress Katrina Kaif has shared clips from her dance performance to the song 'Kala Chashma' at the Dream Team concert in the US. The video shows her dancing with her 'Baar Baar Dekho' co-star Sidharth Malhotra. The tour, which also featured Varun Dhawan, Alia Bhatt, Parineeti Chopra, Aditya Roy Kapur, Badshah and Karan Johar, concluded in New Jersey on Monday.
