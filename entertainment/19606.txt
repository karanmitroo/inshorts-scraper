With 16-million views, Hrithik Roshan starrer Krrish-3 has become the most-watched Bollywood trailer online, beating Chennai Express (10.2 million). The other top watched movies trailer are Jab Tak Hai Jaan (8.2 million), Race-2 (7-million) and Dabangg-2 (6-million). More often than not, films with the most-viewed trailers are also some of the highest-grossing films.
