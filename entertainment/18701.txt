After winning hearts through his performance as the junior Aamir Khan in Dhoom 3, Siddharth Nigam will be seen on a TV series. According to the information, Siddharth will play the younger male lead in the show 'Maha Kumbh', which stars Gautam Rode and Seema Biswas. Maha Kumbh is set to go on air in Oct 2014, on Life Ok.
