The ninth season of the popular reality show Bigg Boss is expected to air from Oct 11. ''The moment you've been waiting for! #BB9 Premieres 11th Oct, Sun 9PM! @BeingSalmanKhan @oppomobileindia @snapdeal." wrote Colors TV on Twitter. The show will reportedly introduce a new concept 'Double Trouble,' and will see actor Salman Khan hosting it for the sixth time.
