Bollywood actress Kareena Kapoor Khan has started shooting for her upcoming film 'Udta Punjab', directed by Abhishek Chaubey. The 34-year-old actress is shooting in Amritsar with Punjabi star Diljit Dosanjh, who took to Twitter to post pictures from the set. The film, which tells the story of substance abuse in Punjab, also stars Shahid Kapoor and Alia Bhatt.
