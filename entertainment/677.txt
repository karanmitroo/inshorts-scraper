The first look poster of Richa Chadha, Aditi Rao Hydari and Rahul Bhat starrer 'Daas Dev' has been released. The film, which will also star Anurag Kashyap in a guest appearance, is a modern take on Sarat Chandra Chattopadhyay's novel 'Devdas'. Directed by Sudhir Mishra, the film is scheduled to release on March 9.
