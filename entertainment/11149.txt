Actress Kareena Kapoor and her husband Saif Ali Khan have featured on the cover of the November issue of 'Harper's Bazaar Bride' magazine. Kareena wore a lehenga by designer Sabyasachi Mukherjee and a silver cape by designer Anamika Khanna. Saif can be seen in a kurta from Shantanu & Nikhil, paired with pants by designer Rohit Bal.
