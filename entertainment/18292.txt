Shah Rukh Khan and Deepika Padukone's "Happy New Year" domestic collection hit Rs. 200 crore mark on Tuesday. It is Khan and Deepika's second movie to have entered the Rs 200 crore mark after 'Chennai Express'. "Happy New Year" is the sixth Bollywood film to enter the elite club after "Dhoom 3", "Krrish 3", "Kick", "Chennai Express" and "3 Idiots".
