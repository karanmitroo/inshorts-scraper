The Sushant Singh Rajput-starrer 'MS Dhoni: The Untold Story' "stays true to every bit of highs in Dhoni's life", wrote India Today. "The film... is not Dhoni's biography, it is his hagiography", Indian Express wrote. Sushant "is every ounce Dhoni in this 'box-office oriented' biopic", said Hindustan Times. It has been rated 3/5 (HT, India Today) and 2/5 (Indian Express).
