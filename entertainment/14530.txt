The 150th film of Telugu actor-politician and Padma Bhushan awardee Chiranjeevi was launched at the office of the Konidela Production Company in Hyderabad on Friday. The film is the debut venture of the production house owned by Chiranjeevi's son and wife. The film, which is expected to release during Pongal 2017, is a Telugu remake of the Tamil film 'Kaththi'.
