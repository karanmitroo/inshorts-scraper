Walt Disney Studios on Monday confirmed that it is planning sequels for 'The Jungle Book', 'Maleficent' and 'Mary Poppins'. Angelina Jolie will return as the titular character in 'Maleficent 2'. Disney also announced 'Cruella' starring Emma Stone, a 'Tinker Bell' film project with Reese Witherspoon and a modern retelling of the 1941 film 'Dumbo', to be directed by Tim Burton.
