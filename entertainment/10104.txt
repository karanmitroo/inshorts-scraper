HBO series 'Game of Thrones' (GoT) was the most pirated television show in 2016, as per 'TorrentFreak', a blog which reports news and trends on BitTorrent protocol and file sharing. GoT was the most-downloaded TV show through BitTorrent for the fifth year in a row. 'The Walking Dead' came in second while 'Westworld' was the third most pirated TV show.
