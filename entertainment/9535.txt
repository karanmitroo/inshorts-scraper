Singer Alanis Morissette's ex-manager Jonathan Schwartz has admitted stealing approximately ₹32.7 crore ($4.8 million) from her and around ₹13.6 crore from other celebrity clients. When confronted about the theft earlier, Schwartz had said he invested the money in a marijuana business to conceal his crime. If convicted on criminal charges, he can face jail sentence of up to six years.
