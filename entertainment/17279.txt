As many as thirty Indian films, including documentaries, will be showcased at the annual New York Indian Film Festival this year. The six-day film festival will begin on May 4 with Kalki Koechlin-starrer 'Margarita with a Straw'. Director Vishal Bhardwaj's 'Maqbool', 'Omkara', 'Haider' and Ayushmann Khurrana-starrer 'Dum Laga Ke Haisha' will also be screened at the festival.
