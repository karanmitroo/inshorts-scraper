Actors Aditi Rao Hydari and Randeep Hooda walked the ramp on Day 3 of the Amazon Fashion Week (AIFW) Autumn-Winter 2016 in New Delhi. Aditi was the showstopper for Shruti Sancheti in an off-shoulder crop top and a high waist slit skirt. Randeep walked the ramp for designer Rohit Kamra in a modern ethnic sherwani jacket and rust orange pants.
