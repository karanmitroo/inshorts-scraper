Author JK Rowling has shared another little detail to tease fans who are eagerly awaiting the “Harry Potter” series spinoff, “Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them”. Her tweet read, “Well, I think it’s widely known that characters of (Porpen)tina and Queenie are sisters, but not that their surname is Goldstein”. Porpentina is the wife of the magizoologist Newt Scamander.
