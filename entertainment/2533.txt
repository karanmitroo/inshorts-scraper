Indo-Canadian YouTube star Lilly Singh, also known as Superwoman, paid tribute to Shah Rukh Khan on his 52nd birthday by reenacting his songs. In the one-minute video, Lilly can be seen lip syncing and dancing to numerous SRK songs like 'Tujhe Dekha Toh', 'Chaiyya Chaiyya', 'Suraj Hua Maddham' and 'Kuch Kuch Hota Hai' among others.
