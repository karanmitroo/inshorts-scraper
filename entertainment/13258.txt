Actor Amitabh Bachchan has shared birthday wishes for Naseeruddin Shah, who turned 67 on Wednesday. "Wishing one of the finest in the Industry... Naseeruddin Shah a happy birthday," tweeted Amitabh. Shabana Azmi, Naseeruddin's co-star in several films, wished him tweeting, "May u continue to enthrall us with your incredible performances." Tisca Chopra and Harsha Bhogle also shared their wishes.
