People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals (PETA) has named Bollywood actors Aamir Khan and Anushka Sharma as the hottest vegetarians of 2015. The duo left behind actors such as Amitabh Bachchan, Alia Bhatt, Kangana Ranaut and Shahid Kapoor, to top the list, which was compiled after conducting an online poll on PETA India's website.
