A new trailer of the Priyanka Chopra starrer action drama film 'Jai Gangaajal' was released on Thursday. The film, directed by Prakash Jha, is a sequel to the Ajay Devgn-starrer 'Gangaajal' (2003). It features Priyanka as SP Abha Mathur. The film, which also marks the acting debut of filmmaker Prakash Jha, is scheduled to release on March 4.
