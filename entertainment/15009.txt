'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' was named the Best Film at the Times of India Film Awards (TOIFA) 2016, held in Dubai on Friday. Sanjay Leela Bhansali bagged the Best Director award and Ranveer Singh won the Best Actor award for 'Bajirao Mastani'. Kangana Ranaut won Best Actor (Female) award for 'Tanu Weds Manu Returns'. Meanwhile, Amitabh Bachchan won the Lifetime Achievement Award.
