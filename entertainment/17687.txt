Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol's iconic “Dilwale Dulhaniya Le Jayenge” was taken down by Mumbai’s Maratha Mandir on Thursday, after the movie completed 1009 weeks at the Box Office. DDLJ had been running uninterrupted at this single-theatre cinema hall since its release in Oct 1995. Thursday’s morning show was the last show of DDLJ at Maratha Mandir.
