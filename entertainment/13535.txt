The first poster of the Emraan Hashmi starrer 'Raaz Reboot' has been released by director Vikram Bhatt on Twitter. The poster features actor Emraan Hashmi, who is reportedly playing the role of a vampire in the film. 'Raaz Reboot' is the fourth installment of the 'Raaz' series. Also starring Kriti Kharbanda, the film will release on September 16.
