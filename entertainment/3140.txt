Samuel Irving 'Si' Newhouse Jr, Chairman Emeritus of Condé Nast media company, passed away on Sunday aged 89, his family spokesperson said. Newhouse ran the company's magazine division, Condé Nast Publications, which publishes some of the world's leading magazines including Vogue, GQ, Vanity Fair, and the New Yorker. Bloomberg had estimated Newhouse's net worth to be $12.7 billion.
