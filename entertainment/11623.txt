The first dialogue promotional video from the Ranbir Kapoor, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Anushka Sharma starrer 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' has been released. The video, titled 'Boyfriend Filmo Ki Tarah', features Anushka Sharma and Ranbir Kapoor. Directed by Karan Johar, the film, also starring Fawad Khan, is scheduled to release on October 28.
