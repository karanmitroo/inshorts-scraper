An episode promo, featuring actors Parineeti Chopra and Aditya Roy Kapoor, from the fifth episode of Koffee with Karan season 5 has been released. In the promo, host Karan Johar is seen asking Aditya about the person most likely to be caught hooking up in a public place. Aditya is seen answering, "Pari", referring to Parineeti Chopra.
