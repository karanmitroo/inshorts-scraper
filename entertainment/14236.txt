'Haq Hai', the first song from the Amitabh Bachchan and Nawazuddin Siddiqui starrer 'TE3N' was released on Friday. The song has been composed and sung by Clinton Cerejo. It has been picturised on the lead actors Bachchan, Siddiqui and Vidya Balan. The film, which is an emotional thriller, is scheduled to release on June 10.
