'Pyaar Ka Test', sung by Bappi Lahiri for the Taapsee Pannu, Amit Sadh and Arsh Bajwa starrer 'Runningshaadi.com', has been released. The song has also been sung by Kalpana Patowary and composed by Abhishek-Akshay with lyrics by Manoj Yadav. Directed by Amit Roy, the film is scheduled to release on February 17.
