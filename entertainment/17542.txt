Indian batsman Virat Kohli recently watched Bollywood's latest thriller 'NH10', featuring his girlfriend Anushka Sharma in the lead role. Kohli was all praises for the movie and took to Twitter to appreciate Anushka's performance. "Just watched #NH10 and i am blown away. What a brilliant film and specially an outstanding performance by my love @AnushkaSharma. SO PROUD:)", he tweeted.
