The Maharashtra Navnirman Sena (MNS) on Tuesday demanded an "unconditional public apology" from all the participants of AIB Roast event. The MNS Chitrapat Sena chief Ameya Khopkar contended the content of the programme was totally contrary to Indian culture and decency. Further, his party threatened to ban the release of Karan Johar, Ranveer Singh and Arjun Kapoor's films.
