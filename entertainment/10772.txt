Filmmaker Farah Khan has confirmed that she is working on the script of the sequel to the 2004 Shah Rukh Khan starrer film 'Main Hoon Na'. She confirmed this on Zarine Khan's food and lifestyle show 'Spices and Secrets'. The film, which also stars Zayed Khan, Sushmita Sen and Amrita Rao, marked Farah's directorial debut in Bollywood.
