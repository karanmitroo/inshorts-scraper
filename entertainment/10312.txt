A video of the cast of the 'DuckTales' reboot singing the original theme song of the cartoon series has been released. David Tennant, who played Barty Crouch Jr in Harry Potter films, is voicing 'Scrooge McDuck' while Danny Pudi will voice 'Huey', Ben Schwartz will voice 'Duey' and Bobby Moynihan will voice 'Louie'. The reboot will premiere in summer 2017.
