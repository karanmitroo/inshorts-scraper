The teaser of the upcoming horror film 'Annabelle 2', a follow up to the 2014 film 'Annabelle', has been released. The film is the fourth instalment in 'The Conjuring' film series as 'Annabelle' was a spinoff of the 2013 film 'The Conjuring'. Directed by David Sandberg, the film is scheduled to release on May 19, 2017 in the US.
