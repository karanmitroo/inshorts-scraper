Celebrity couple Amruta Khanvilkar and Himmanshoo Malhotra were on Sunday declared as the winner of dance reality show "Nach Baliye 7". Along with the winner's trophy, the couple were presented with a car and prize money worth Rs 30 lakh. Rashmi Desai and Nandish Sandhu were placed at second while Upen Patel and Karishma Tanna came in third.
