Actress Anushka Sharma has featured on the October cover of Elle India. She has been styled by designer Sabyasachi Mukherjee for the cover story 'How to succeed on your own terms'. She has worn a black velvet coat by Sabyasachi paired with faux fur by Dhruv Kapoor, 'Temperley London' high waist suede pants, a 'Stella McCartney' top and 'Gucci' loafers.
