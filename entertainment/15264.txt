'High Heels', the song from the Arjun Kapoor and Kareena Kapoor-starrer 'Ki & Ka', was YouTube's most shared video on Facebook and Twitter in India on Monday. The song is a newer version of rapper Yo Yo Honey Singh's 2012 song by the same name. Posted on YouTube on Saturday, the song has garnered over 12.74 lakh views so far.
