As per reports, the Ranbir Kapoor, Anushka Sharma and Aishwarya Rai-starrer film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' has been passed by the Censor Board without any cuts. It was earlier reported that three intimate scenes between Ranbir and Aishwarya have been deleted. "Fawad is not being replaced," reports added, quashing rumours of the Pakistani actor being replaced due to MNS' threats.
