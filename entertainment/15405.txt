Actress Priyanka Chopra has lent her voice to a superhero character, Ms Marvel, in the new mobile game Marvel Avengers Academy. Announcing the news on Twitter on Friday, Priyanka said, "Say hello to Ms. Marvel-I’m her voice and she’s my alter ego in MARVEL Avengers Academy!". Priyanka had earlier lent her voice to Ishani's character in Disney's movie 'Planes' (2013).
