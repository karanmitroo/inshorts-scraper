The teaser of the Vidyut Jammwal and Esha Gupta starrer upcoming action film 'Commando 2' has been released. The film is a sequel to the 2013 film 'Commando'. Vidyut will be seen reprising his role as 'Captain Karanvir Singh Dogra'. Also featuring Adah Sharma, the film, directed by Deven Bhojani,' is scheduled to release on March 3.
