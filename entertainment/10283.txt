The poster of the Ranbir Kapoor and Katrina Kaif starrer 'Jagga Jasoos' was revealed on Monday by UTV Motion Pictures alongside the tweet, "Sometimes, the fastest getaway is an Ostrich!" In the film, Ranbir plays the detective Jagga, who tries to find the whereabouts of his father. Directed by Anurag Basu, it is scheduled to release on April 7, 2017.
