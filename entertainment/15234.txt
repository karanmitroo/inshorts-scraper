Manoj Bajpayee and Rajkummar Rao-starrer 'Aligarh' which released on Friday "is a film both timely and important, because of what it is about", wrote Indian Express. "It's supported by the immense acting prowess of Bajpayee," said HT. The Hansal Mehta directorial, based on real life story of a homosexual professor, has been rated 3.5/5 (Indian Express) and 4.5/5 (NDTV, HT).
