Former Victoria's Secret model Miranda Kerr on Wednesday announced her engagement with 26-year-old Snapchat Co-founder and CEO Evan Spiegel. Kerr and Spiegel first met at a Louis Vuitton dinner in New York in 2014 and have been dating for one year. This will be 33-year-old Kerr's second marriage after previously being wed for three years to British actor Orlando Bloom.
