Actor Ranveer Singh has shared a video in which he can be seen recreating the iconic climax scene from the Shah Rukh Khan starrer 1995 film 'Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge'. He reenacted the scene with choreographer Ganesh Hegde. "This stunt is performed by trained Filmys. Do not try this at home. Or at the train station," Ranveer captioned the video.
