As per reports, after watching the teaser of 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil', actor Salman Khan said, "She's so beautiful", while commenting on actress Aishwarya Rai. Earlier, reports suggested that the 50-year-old actor denied watching the teaser and counter-questioned journalists on how the teaser was. Notably, Aishwarya and Salman were reportedly in a relationship before their breakup in 2002.
