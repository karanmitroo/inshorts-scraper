Amitabh Bachchan and Dhanush-starrer "Shamitabh" has an unconventional story which will entertain you throughout the film, writes India Today. The R Balki directorial traces the story of how a mute actor becomes a superstar when a drunkard provides him his voice. The movie, which also features debutant Akshara Haasan, has been rated 3.5/5 (India Today, TOI) and 3/5 (HT).
