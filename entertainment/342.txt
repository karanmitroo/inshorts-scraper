The poster of actor Tiger Shroff starrer 'Baaghi 2' has been revealed. "Ronnie kal aa raha hai," tweeted Tiger while sharing the poster. The film is a sequel to the 2016 action film 'Baaghi' and will be directed by choreographer-turned-filmmaker Ahmed Khan. 'Baaghi 2' will also star Tiger's rumoured girlfriend actress Disha Patani as the female lead.
