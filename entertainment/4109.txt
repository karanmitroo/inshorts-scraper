'Bareilly Ki Barfi', starring Kriti Sanon, Ayushmann Khurrana and Rajkummar Rao which released on Friday, is a "light-hearted comedy... (that) slowly weaves its charm," wrote Hindustan Times. Koimoi wrote that Rajkummar "simply owns the film," while Times of India called the film "a hooray moment for Hindi cinema". It was rated 3/5 (HT), 2.5 (Koimoi) and 4/5 (TOI).
