The first look of 'Housefull 3', a Sajid-Farhad directorial, was unveiled on social media on Tuesday. The Sajid Nadiadwala production has retained Akshay Kumar, Jacqueline Fernandez and Riteish Deshmukh from Housefull 2. Other stars in 'Housefull 3' are Lisa Haydon, Abhishek Bachchan and Nargis Fakhri. The comedy genre film is slated to release on June 3, 2016.
