Cricketer Yuvraj Singh recently called actress Anushka Sharma 'bhabhi' on photo sharing app Instagram. Anushka, who is dating cricketer Virat Kohli, posted a picture of herself from her upcoming film 'Bombay Velvet'. On this picture, Yuvraj left the comment, "Oye Hoye Rosie bhabhi! Looking Awesome." However, the comment was later removed.
