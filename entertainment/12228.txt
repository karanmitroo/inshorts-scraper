A new behind-the-scenes video shows actor Sushant Singh Rajput's transformation into cricketer Mahendra Singh Dhoni in the upcoming biopic 'M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story'. Titled 'Dhoni Through The Years', the video shows footage of the film's make-up, hair and costume team working on Sushant to make him resemble Dhoni. Directed by Neeraj Pandey, the film will release on September 30.
