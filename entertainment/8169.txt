Govinda and Raveena Tandon danced to a medley of songs from the 1990s at Zee Cine Awards 2017. A video shows them performing to 'Kisi Disco Mein Jaayein' from their 1998 film 'Bade Miyan Chote Miyan'. The other songs to which they performed include 'Kya Lagti Hai' from 'Dulhe Raja' (1998) and 'Kurta Phaad Ke' from 'Anari No. 1' (1999).
