Ranveer Singh has won the Best Actor in Male Category for 'Bajirao Mastani' at the 17th edition of the International Indian Film Academy (IIFA) Awards in Spain. While Deepika Padukone won the Best Actor in Female Category for 'Piku', 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' was named the Best Picture. Meanwhile, Sanjay Leela Bhansali won the Best Direction Award for 'Bajirao Mastani'.
