The Diana Penty starrer 'Happy Bhag Jayegi' has earned ₹10.71 crore in its opening weekend. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film earned ₹2.32 crore on Friday, ₹3.81 crore on Saturday and ₹4.58 crore on Sunday. Directed by Mudassar Aziz, the romantic comedy film also stars Abhay Deol, Jimmy Sheirgill, Ali Fazal and Pakistani actress Momal Sheikh.
