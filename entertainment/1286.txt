In less than three weeks of its release, 'Star Wars: The Last Jedi' has earned $1.04 billion (₹6602 crore) worldwide. It became 2017's third highest grossing film worldwide after 'Beauty and the Beast' and 'The Fate of the Furious'. Earlier, the film minted ₹2890 crore worldwide to set the record for the fifth-biggest global weekend opening for a film ever.
