The Shah Rukh Khan starrer 'Raees', which released on Wednesday, is "a mish-mash of things we’ve seen before," wrote The Indian Express. Hindustan Times said, "the writing is shoddy and unoriginal", while The Times of India wrote, "for once, Shah Rukh Khan isn’t spreading his arms, but breaking others". It has been rated 2.5/5 (IE), 2/5 (HT) and 3.5/5 (TOI).
