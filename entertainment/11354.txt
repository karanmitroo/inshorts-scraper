The Ajay Devgn starrer 'Shivaay' has earned ₹28.56 crore in its opening weekend. The film minted ₹10.24 crore on Friday, ₹10.06 crore on Saturday and ₹8.26 crore on Sunday, as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. The film, which has been directed and produced by Devgn, also stars Sayyeshaa Saigal and Polish actress Erika Kaar.
