Actress Shraddha Kapoor has featured on the cover of the magazine 'Filmfare' for its June issue. The actress has worn a fringe cape from the label 'Adivitiya' by Esha Sethi Thirani paired with a bralet by Farah Sanjana and leather tights with cutouts. Her look has been accessorised with 'Forever 21' rings and neckpieces by Jamini Ahluwalia and 'Rara Avis'.
