Akshay Kumar has shared a video of himself where he can be seen singing a 'Thank You' song for the celebrities who promoted his film 'Rustom' while hula hooping. He also wished the actors, including Varun Dhawan, Sonakshi Sinha and Salman Khan, luck for their upcoming films. "Can't Thank You'll enough...There's a lot of Love inside this Hoop!", Akshay tweeted.
