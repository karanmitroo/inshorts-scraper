Bollywood actor John Abraham is all set to play a blind man in a sequel to the hit 2002 movie “Aankhen”. “Part two will start from where Part one left off- Amitabh Bachchan getting released from jail...", director Anees Bazmee said. Anil Kapoor has also been roped in for an important role. 'Aankhen 2' will go on floors in May.
