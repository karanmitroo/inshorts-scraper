Shahid Kapoor on Tuesday took to Twitter to share his first look from 'Udta Punjab'. The actor plays Punjabi rock star Tommy Singh in the film, which also features Alia Bhatt and Kareena Kapoor. Directed by Abhishek Chaubey of 'Ishqiya' fame, 'Udta Punjab' marks the Bollywood debut of Punjabi actor Diljit Dosanjh and is scheduled to release on June 17.
