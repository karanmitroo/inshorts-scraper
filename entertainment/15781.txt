The teaser of the Telugu film 'Brahmotsavam' was Youtube's most shared video on Facebook and Twitter in India on Friday. The film, starring Mahesh Babu, Kajal Aggarwal and Samantha Ruth Prabhu, is being shot in Tamil simultaneously. It has been directed by Srikanth Addala. Posted on YouTube on Thursday, the video has garnered over 3.67 lakh views so far.
