Actress Sonam Kapoor has revealed that she has been molested when she was younger while referring to the incident as "traumatising". She revealed this during an interview with film critic Rajeev Masand as part of 'The Bollywood Roundtable 2016 - The Actresses'. Alia Bhatt, Anushka Sharma, Vidya Balan and Radhika Apte also featured in the interview.
