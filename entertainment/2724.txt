Actress Sonam Kapoor took to social media to reveal the teaser poster of the upcoming film 'Veere Di Wedding'. The film also stars actresses Kareena Kapoor, Swara Bhasker and Shikha Talsania. Produced by Sonam's sister Rhea Kapoor and directed by Shashanka Ghosh, 'Veere Di Wedding' is scheduled to release in 2018.
