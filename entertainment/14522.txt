'Tung Lak', the new song from the upcoming film 'Sarbjit' was released on Thursday. Sung by Sukhwinder Singh, Sunidhi Chauhan, Shail Hada and Kalpana Gandharv, the song has been composed by Shail- Pritesh. It features Randeep Hooda, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Richa Chadha. Directed by Omung Kumar, 'Sarbjit' is scheduled to release on May 20.
