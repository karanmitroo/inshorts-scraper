Ayushmann Khurrana and Bhumi Pednekar, who shared screen in 'Dum Laga Ke Haisha', will be seen together in another film, the title of which has not been finalised yet. The film will be produced by Anand L Rai, known for 'Tanu Weds Manu', and directed by Sameer Sharma. Confirming the news, Bhumi said, "It is a wonderful script".
