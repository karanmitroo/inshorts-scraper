The first look of actor Sanjay Dutt in the upcoming film 'The Good Maharaja' has been revealed. In the film, Sanjay will play the role of Maharaja Jam Sahib Digvijaysinhji Ranjitsinhji, the ruler of Nawanagar, a princely state in British India. The film is an English-Polish production directed by Omung Kumar, whose Bollywood directorial debut was 'Mary Kom'.
