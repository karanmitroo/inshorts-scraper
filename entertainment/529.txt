As per reports, Aamir Khan has offered his nephew Imran Khan a comeback role in his upcoming film 'Mahabharata'. Imran was last seen in the Nikkhil Advani's 2015 film 'Katti Batti' opposite Kangana Ranaut. There have also been reports that SS Rajamouli, the director of 'Baahubali' film franchise, is planning to cast actors Rajinikanth and Mohanlal as well in 'Mahabharata'.
