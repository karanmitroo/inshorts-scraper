The first look of Marathi film 'Ek Albela', based on Bhagwan Dada or Master Bhagwan, "the first dancing superstar of Indian Cinema", has been released. The film stars Mangesh Desai in the lead role and marks the debut of actress Vidya Balan in Marathi films. Vidya will be seen making a special appearance as Geeta Bali in the film.
