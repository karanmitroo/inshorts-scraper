Actor Sidharth Malhotra will make his TV debut as the host of ‘Ek Villain Ek Daastan’, where he’ll take viewers through the lives of some of Bollywood's most loved villains. The show, which will be aired on Zoom channel starting Jun-23, will have journeys of actors like Amrish Puri, Amjad Khan, Pran, Prem Chopra, Ajit among several others.
