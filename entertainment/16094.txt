Priyanka Chopra and Deepika Padukone's face-off song 'Pinga' from their upcoming film 'Bajirao Mastani' was released on Sunday. In the song, both the actresses can be seen wearing navari sarees. The duo has performed lavani in the song, that has been choreographed by Remo D'Souza. Directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali, 'Bajirao Mastani' is scheduled to release on December 18.
