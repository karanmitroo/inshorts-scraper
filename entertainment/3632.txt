Actors including Randeep Hooda and Dia Mirza helped clean Mumbai's Juhu beach post Ganesh Visarjan. Vivek Oberoi, Keith Sequeira and his girlfriend Rochelle Rao were also a part of the cleanliness drive, which was organised by an NGO. Nearly 1,200 people gathered on the beach the morning after Ganesh Visarjan for the cleanliness drive.
