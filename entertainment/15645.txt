Kabir Bedi on Monday slammed daughter Pooja Bedi's comment on his fourth wife Parveen Dusanj wherein she had compared her with a fairy tale's wicked witch. "Deeply disappointed by venomous comments by my daughter Pooja against @parveendusanj just after we married. No excuse for bad behaviour," the 70-year-old actor posted on Twitter. Notably, Pooja had later retracted her tweet.
