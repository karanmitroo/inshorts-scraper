Vidya Balan was on Saturday felicitated with the 'Pride of Kerala' award by the World Malayalee Council and media broadcasting channel Kairali TV. The actress who hails from Palakkad in Kerala took to Twitter to express her joy after being presented the award. The 38-year-old actress will next be seen in Sujoy Ghosh’s ‘TE3N’, alongside Amitabh Bachchan and Nawazuddin Siddiqui.
