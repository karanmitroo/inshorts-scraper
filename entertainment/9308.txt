Shah Rukh Khan has shared a video where he sings his own version of the song 'Ikk Kudi' for Diljit Dosanjh. Diljit sang the song's reprised version for the film 'Udta Punjab'. Sharing the video, SRK tweeted, "Sorry for trying to sing paaji but had to tell u how much I love u. Please watch Raees in Punjab with friends."
