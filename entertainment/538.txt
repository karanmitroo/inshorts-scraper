'Padmaavat', which has earned ₹236 crore in the domestic market, has become Deepika Padukone's highest grossing film by beating the earnings of her film 'Chennai Express', which earned ₹227.13 crore in India. 'Padmaavat' was also Deepika's seventh film to earn over ₹100 crore. Further, the film was both Ranveer Singh and Shahid Kapoor first film in the ₹200 crore club.
