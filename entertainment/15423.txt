The first official trailer of 'M.A. Pass' has been released on YouTube by Indian media and entertainment company FilmyBOX. The Shadab Khan directorial is being produced by Narendra Singh and Aastha Naad, the producers of 2013's critically acclaimed film 'B.A. Pass'. The film, starring Kritika Sachdeva in the lead, also features Sasho, Indraneil Sengupta and Aarav Choudhary among others.
