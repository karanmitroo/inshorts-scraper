Yesteryear actress Neetu Singh shared a picture of her husband Rishi Kapoor with their son Ranbir Kapoor from the shooting of the 1996 film 'Prem Granth'. "Hope in future Son will direct the Kapoor!!!" wrote Neetu alongside the picture which shows a young Ranbir holding a clapboard. Rishi had played the lead role in 'Prem Granth' opposite Madhuri Dixit.
