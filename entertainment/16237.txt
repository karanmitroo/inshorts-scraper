The theatrical trailer of Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol starrer 'Dilwale' will be out on Diwali this year. Notably, the trailer will be attached to Salman Khan and Sonam Kapoor starrer 'Prem Ratan Dhan Payo'. While Salman's film is the solo release on Diwali next month, SRK's 'Dilwale' is scheduled to release on Christmas 2015.
