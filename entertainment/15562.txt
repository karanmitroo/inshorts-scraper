Actor Manoj Bajpayee on Monday shared the official poster of his upcoming film 'Aligarh' on Facebook. The poster shows Bajpayee and co-star Rajkummar Rao alongside the film's tagline '#Comeout'. Bajpayee plays the role of Dr. Shrinivas Ramchandra Siras, a professor suspended from Aligarh University, allegedly owing to his sexual orientation. The film is scheduled to release on February 26.
