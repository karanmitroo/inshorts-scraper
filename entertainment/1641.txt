A video which shows Ranveer Singh rapping has been shared by rapper Divine on Instagram. Ranveer, who'll play a rapper in 'Gully Boy', was attending a rapping workshop to prepare for his role. Zoya Akhtar directorial 'Gully Boy' is reportedly inspired by the lives of rappers Vivian Fernandes, also known as Divine and Naved Shaikh, also known as Naezy.
