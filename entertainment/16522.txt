Saif Ali Khan and Katrina Kaif-starrer 'Phantom', which released today, is an over-simplified thriller, wrote TOI. "Phantom begins fine, and then loses way so badly," said India Today. The Kabir Khan directorial knows where it is going, but has no clue how to get there, stated NDTV. The film has been rated 3.5/5 (TOI), 2.5/5 (NDTV) and 2/5 (India Today).
