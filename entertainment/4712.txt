Actor-turned-politician Shatrughan Sinha took to Twitter to congratulate the Ambani family on the launch of Reliance Jio's 4G feature phone. Mukesh Ambani announced the JioPhone at an effective cost of ₹0 with refundable security deposit of ₹1,500. The launch was during the company's 40th annual general meeting, which also saw Ambani's children Akash and Isha debut before Reliance shareholders.
