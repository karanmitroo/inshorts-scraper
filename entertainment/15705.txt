The first official motion poster of Kareena Kapoor and Arjun Kapoor's upcoming film 'Ki and Ka' was revealed on Facebook by Amitabh Bachchan on Monday. In the one-minute-long motion poster, Kareena can be seen putting a 'mangalsutra' on Arjun's neck. The film, in which Arjun is playing Kareena's house-husband, will release on April 1, 2016.
