Science fiction film 'War for the Planet of the Apes' which released on Friday, "is the rare blockbuster that arrives once, maybe twice every year", wrote Hindustan Times. Times of India said, "[It's] a thought-provoking and emotionally resonant film that balances action and special effects", while Mid-Day described it as "brilliantly-scripted". It was rated 4.5/5 (HT), 4/5 (TOI), 3.5/5 (Mid-Day).
