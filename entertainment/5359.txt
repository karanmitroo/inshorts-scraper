Filmmakers Hansal Mehta and Qaushiq Mukherjee (Q) will team up for the upcoming film Garbage. Hansal Mehta tweeted, "A new journey begins. Strange bedfellows Q, Karma Features and Odd Joint come together for Garbage." Hansal Mehta is known for films like Aligarh and CityLights while Q is known for his Bengali film Gandu.
