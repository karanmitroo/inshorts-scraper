Several Bollywood actors including Shah Rukh Khan, Ranveer Singh, Anushka Sharma, Shraddha Kapoor, John Abraham and Rekha cast their votes in the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation elections. "Voting is our duty towards our country and its progress. Please go out and vote," wrote Anushka while sharing a photo. "Please exercise your right and go vote!," wrote Shraddha Kapoor.
