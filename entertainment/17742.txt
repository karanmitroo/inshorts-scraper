A court in Mumbai has ordered an FIR against 14 people and others involved in the controversial AIB Roast show held January 20 including Karan Johar, Ranvir Singh and Arjun Kapoor. The names of actresses Deepika Padukone, and Alia Bhatt,  and stand up comedians, have also been included. The orders came on a complaint filed by social activist, Santosh Daundkar.
