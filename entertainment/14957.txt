As per reports, Bollywood actress Kareena Kapoor Khan is not doing Ajay Devgn starrer 'Baadshaho', as was previously rumoured. However, Kareena was reportedly approached for the film but had date issues and hence, could not commit to the project. Further, other names cropping up for playing the female lead in the film include Priyanka Chopra, Shruti Haasan and Lisa Haydon.
