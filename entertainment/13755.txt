Diljit Dosanjh, who made his Bollywood debut with 'Udta Punjab', has been replaced by Emraan Hashmi in Ajay Devgn's upcoming film 'Baadshaho'. Emraan will reunite with Ajay, 6 years after the film 'Once Upon a Time in Mumbaai'. Diljit was replaced as "he quoted heavy sum as his fee", a report claimed.
