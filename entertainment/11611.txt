Actress-turned-author Twinkle Khanna, while sharing her thoughts on the occasion of 'Karwa Chauth' has jokingly said that "men do not need to last that long anymore". "These days by 40 u could be on the way to your 2nd marriage so what's the point of fasting," tweeted Twinkle. She added that she has checked "longevity records" of various countries.
