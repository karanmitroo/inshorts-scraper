Actress Kalki Koechlin on Thursday said she is crowdfunding her latest project, a documentary called 'Azmaish: Trials of Life,' and tweeted, "Help if you can and want!" Kalki is collaborating with Pakistani filmmaker Sabiha Sumar, and the two will travel through India and Pakistan to explore lifestyles and religious dynamics. They have raised ₹14.7 lakh of their ₹20 lakh target.
