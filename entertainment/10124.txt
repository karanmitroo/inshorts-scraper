Anushka Sharma and Katrina Kaif said that Shah Rukh Khan is both witty and energetic, Salman Khan is grumpy and cranky while Aamir Khan is quiet and disciplined. The actresses were responding to host Karan Johar asking them to describe the three actors during an episode of 'Koffee With Karan'. Notably, Anushka and Katrina have worked with all three actors.
