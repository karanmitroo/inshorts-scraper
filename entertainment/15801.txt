The first poster of Aditya Roy Kapur and Katrina Kaif's upcoming film 'Fitoor' has been released, after a teaser poster was unveiled earlier this week. Directed by Abhishek Kapoor, the film is a romantic drama based on Charles Dickens' novel 'Great Expectations'. Aditya stars as Noor and Katrina as Firdaus in the film which also features Tabu.
