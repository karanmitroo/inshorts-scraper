India's most expensive animated film, Mahabharat, has collected only Rs.1.5 crore since its release on Friday. Remarkably, the Amaan Khan directorial has a starry list of voice-over artists like Amitabh Bachchan, Vidya Balan and Ajay Devgn. The movie, which was made on a budget of Rs 50 crore, was released on about 150 screens.
