'Tamma Tamma Again', a new song from the Alia Bhatt and Varun Dhawan starrer film 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania' has been released. It is a recreated version of 'Tamma Tamma', originally picturised on Madhuri Dixit and Sanjay Dutt in 1990 film 'Thanedaar'. The song has been recreated by Tanishk Bagchi with vocals by Bappi Lahiri and Anuradha Paudwal.
