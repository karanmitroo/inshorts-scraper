'Bol Do Na Zara', the first song from the Emraan Hashmi starrer film 'Azhar' has been released. The song, featuring Emraan with Nargis Fakhri, has been composed by Amaal Mallik, while it has been sung by Armaan Malik. Also starring Prachi Desai, 'Azhar' is a biopic of former Indian cricketer Mohammed Azharuddin. It will release on May 13.
