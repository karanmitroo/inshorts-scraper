Salman Khan and Sonam Kapoor starrer 'Prem Ratan Dhan Payo' (2015) was the most watched Bollywood film on television in 2016, according to Broadcast Audience Research Council of India (BARC). The Tiger Shroff and Shraddha Kapoor-starrer 'Baaghi' took the second spot. 'Baaghi' was reportedly also one of the most downloaded films of 2016 on BitTorrent.
