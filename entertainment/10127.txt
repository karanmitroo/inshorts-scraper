The making of the song 'Haseeno Ka Deewana' from Hrithik Roshan's upcoming film 'Kaabil' has been released. It is a recreation of the song by the same name from the 1981 film 'Yaarana'. Actress Urvashi Rautela, on whom the song is picturised, jokingly says that she is wearing an outfit weighing 150 kilograms in the video.
