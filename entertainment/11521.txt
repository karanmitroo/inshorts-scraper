Hanan Yasir and Hanna Yasir, a brother-sister duo from Malappuram, Kerala have created a mash-up of Justin Bieber's single 'Cold Water' with a Malayalam song titled 'Nenjodu Cherthu'. While Hanan takes Bieber's lines, Hanna voices the Malayalam song, which is from an album named 'Yuvvh'. The YouTube video has garnered over 26,000 views so far.
