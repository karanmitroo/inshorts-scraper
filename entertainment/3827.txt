Actress Esha Deol's sister Ahana Deol organised a surprise baby shower for Esha, who is expecting her first child with husband Bharat Takhtani. The baby shower included lavender-themed balloons, floral arrangements and a lavender-based chocolate Belgium cake. "Thanks to Bharat for sneakily getting me the measurements of Esha for the gown and managing to keep it a surprise," said Ahana.
