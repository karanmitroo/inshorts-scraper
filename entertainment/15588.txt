Actors Priyanka Chopra, Ajay Devgn and film director and producer Madhur Bhandarkar were on Monday announced to be honoured with Padma Shri award this year, the fourth highest civilian award in India. The list also includes lawyer Ujjwal Nikam, classical dancer Prathibha Prahlad, folk singer Mamata Chandrakar, classical vocalist Soma Ghosh and folk dancer Gulabi Sapera among others.
