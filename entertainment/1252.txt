Actors Sushant Singh Rajput and Bhumi Pednekar will be playing dacoits in their upcoming film 'Sone Chidaiya', their first film together. Directed by 'Udta Punjab' director Abhishek Chaubey, the film is set in the 1970s and narrates the tale of Chambal dacoits. Sushant will reportedly spend time with a few former dacoits to prepare for the role.
