The first look of the Irrfan Khan starrer 'Hindi Medium', which also marks the Bollywood debut for Pakistani actress Saba Qamar, has been revealed by Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. The film is said to be a romantic-comedy about a couple from Delhi's Chandni Chowk. Directed by Saket Chaudhary, the film will reportedly begin its production in August this year.
