Actor Sushant Singh Rajput's upcoming science fiction film 'Chandamama Door Ke' will release on January 26, 2018. Sushant will be seen playing an astronaut in the film. It will be directed by Sanjay Puran Singh Chauhan and will also feature Nawazuddin Siddiqui. Sushant will spend a month at US space agency NASA to prepare for the film.
