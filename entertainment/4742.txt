According to reports, actor Abhishek Bachchan will portray poet Sahir Ludhianvi in an upcoming biopic, which will be directed by debutante Jasmeet Reen. The actor has reportedly lost 12 kilogram to play the role of the poet in the film, which will be produced by Sanjay Leela Bhansali. The film will mark the first collaboration between Bachchan and Bhansali.
