Actress Sonam Kapoor won the Best Actress award for 'Neerja' while actor Nawazuddin Siddiqui was named the Best Actor for 'Raman Raghav 2.0' at the Indian Film Festival of Melbourne (IFFM) 2016. 'Kapoor and Sons' won the Best Film while 'Parched' won the Best Indie Film award. Further, actor Rishi Kapoor was honoured with the IFFM Excellence in Cinema Award.
