Addressing people at the JNU campus on Friday, veteran Bollywood actor Anupam Kher said, "One who talks ill of the country, how can he be celebrated as hero?". Notably, his comments came when he was at the campus for the screening of his film 'Buddha in a Traffic Jam', followed Kanhaiya Kumar, Umar Khalid and Anirban Bhattacharya's release on bail.
