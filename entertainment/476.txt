Actor Riteish Deshmukh took to Twitter to share a video in which he and his wife Genelia D'Souza can be seen imitating a scene from Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol starrer 1998 film 'Kuch Kuch Hota Hai'. "One of our favourite movies," he wrote in the caption. Responding to their video, Kajol replied with laughing emojis.
