Prakash Jha's socio-political drama 'Satyagraha' starring Amitabh Bachchan, Ajay Devgan, Kareena Kapoor, Manoj Bajpayee, Arjun Rampal and Amrita Rao, released on Friday and has been rated 4, 1.5, 2 and 2.5 by India Today, The Indian Express, Zee News and DNA respectively. "The movie is based on Anna Hazare's movement and it's all been-here-seen-this-and-that before," reports The Indian Express.
