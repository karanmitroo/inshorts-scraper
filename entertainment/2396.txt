Filmmaker Farah Khan took to Twitter to share a picture from the sets of Shah Rukh Khan and Deepika Padukone starrer 'Om Shanti Om', which completed 10 years today and wrote, "Feels like yesterday." "Thank you for the love it still gets," she added. 'Om Shanti Om' also starred Arjun Rampal, Shreyas Talpade and Kirron Kher.
