The Haryana police on Thursday informed the Punjab and Haryana High Court that no case had been made against TV artist and comedian Raghvendra Amarnath Sharda alias Kiku Sharda. Earlier, two FIRs were registered against Sharda at Fatehabad and Kaithal Police station for allegedly ‘hurting religious sentiments’ by mimicking spiritual organisation Dera Sacha Sauda head Gurmeet Ram Rahim Singh.
