The Michael Fassbender and Jennifer Lawrence starrer 'X-Men: Apocalypse', which released on Friday, "is overstuffed with characters, plot and... (has) inconsistent effects work", wrote Hindustan Times. The film has a "climax... devoid of suspense", wrote Times of India, while Mid-Day wrote that the film is "bigger... more populated". It was rated 2.5/5 (Hindustan Times) and 3/5 (TOI, Mid-Day).
