Actress Vaani Kapoor has featured on the December cover of 'Vogue India'. She wore a floral print sequinned dress by Burberry along with jewellery by Cartier. The 28-year-old actress, who will be seen opposite Ranveer Singh in the upcoming film 'Befikre', has featured in the cover story titled, 'Vaani Kapoor, expect the unexpected'.
