Actor Matt LeBlanc, who turns 49 on Monday, started his television career with a Heinz Ketchup commercial in 1987. LeBlanc is known for playing the role of Joey Tribbiani on American television sitcom 'Friends'. The ketchup commercial, which used the slogan ‘good things come to those who wait’, won the Gold Lion Award at the 1987 Cannes International Advertising Festival.
