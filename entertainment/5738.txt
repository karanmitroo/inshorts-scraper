Actress Taapsee Pannu has shared a video where she is trying to teach Punjabi phrases to Jacqueline Fernandez. Taapsee captioned the video, "On public demand that's her entry in our punjabi gang on #Judwaa2 sets! A very sporty student I must say!" Taapsee, Jacqueline and Varun Dhawan have been shooting for Judwaa 2, a sequel to the 1997 film Judwaa.
