Actor Hrithik Roshan has said that his upcoming film 'Mohenjo Daro' will be one of the best films in his career. "For 'Mohenjo Daro', I am travelling back in time. It has been a great journey, great experience," said Hrithik, who made his debut in the 2000 film 'Kaho Na Pyaar Hai '. 'Mohenjo Daro' will release on August 12.
