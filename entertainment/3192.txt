Tom Cruise starrer 'American Made' has the actor "at his charming best in this incredible real-life tale", wrote Hindustan Times. Firstpost called the film a "memorable movie from the team of Cruise and his Edge of Tomorrow director Doug Liman" while Times of India called it "a breath of fresh air". It was rated 4/5 (HT) and 3.5/5 (Firstpost, TOI).
