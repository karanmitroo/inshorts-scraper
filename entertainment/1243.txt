Manisha Koirala's look as Sanjay Dutt's mother late actress Nargis Dutt from the upcoming biopic on Sanjay has surfaced online. Actor Ranbir Kapoor will be seen portraying Sanjay Dutt in the film, which has been directed by Rajkumar Hirani. Earlier, talking about her role in the film, Manisha had said it will be a special appearance.
