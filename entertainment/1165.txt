The release date of Karan Johar, Riteish Deshmukh, Diljit Dosanjh, Sonakshi Sinha and Lara Dutta starrer 'Boom Boom in New York' has been announced as February 23. The comedy film has been directed by Indian American filmmaker Chakri Toleti. The film, in which Johar will be seen playing himself, will reportedly revolve around the IIFA Awards.
