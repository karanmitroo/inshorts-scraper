The poster of the Kangana Ranaut starrer Manikarnika -The Queen Of Jhansi has been unveiled. Talking about her character, Kangana said, "She had a great sense of right and wrong... That's a quality I identify with her. The British did ask her to choose between life and death." Directed by Krish Jagarlamudi, Manikarnika will release on April 27, 2018.
