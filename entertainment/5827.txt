PM Narendra Modi has called Akshay Kumar's film Toilet: Ek Prem Katha a good effort to further the message of cleanliness, as per Swachh Bharat Abhiyan. Modi further wrote, "125 crore Indians have to continue working together to create a Swachh Bharat." Sharing the trailer, Akshay had tagged Modi while tweeting, "Here is my humble contribution towards a Swachh Bharat."
