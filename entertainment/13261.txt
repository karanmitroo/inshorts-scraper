Pankaj Jha, a PhD scholar in Sanskrit grammar, has released a Sanskrit cover of the Hrithik Roshan and Sonam Kapoor starrer Honey Singh song 'Dheere Dheere'. "I'm getting offers from Mumbai, Delhi and my hometown to record Sanskrit albums," said Pankaj. He has also translated and recorded the songs 'Manma Emotion Jaage' and 'Piya Tu Ab To Aaja' in Sanskrit.
