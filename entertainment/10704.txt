Arjun Kapoor has called a Hindi daily's headline "insensitive". The headline read, "To isliye Arjun Kapoor ko chhor kar chali gai unki 'maa'." Arjun tweeted, "I don't normally react to what's written about me but for a man who's lost his mother I find this headline highly distasteful." The publication clarified that the headline referenced Arjun's onscreen mother Amrita Singh.
