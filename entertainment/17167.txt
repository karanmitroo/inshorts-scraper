MoS for Information & Broadcasting, Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore inaugurated the India Pavilion at the 68th Cannes Films Festival in France on Thursday. Neeraj Ghaywan’s “Masaan” and Gurvinder Singh’s “Chauthi Koot”, which will screen in the Un Certain Regard section, will represent India at the festival. Bollywood Actresses including Sonam Kapoor and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan will also appear during the event.
