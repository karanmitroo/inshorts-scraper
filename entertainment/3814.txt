Celebrities including Priyanka Chopra, Deepika Padukone, Dia Mirza and Alia Bhatt tweeted helpline numbers amid heavy rain in Mumbai. "Alerts showing up on my phone about the rains in Mumbai. Please stay safe and take care everyone," tweeted Deepika. Dia's tweet read, "If you can offer shelter to strays and people stuck in the rain please do...MCGM helpline number 1916."
