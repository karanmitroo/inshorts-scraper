According to reports, Priyanka Chopra has opted out of Sanjay Leela Bhansali's biopic on poet-lyricist Sahir Ludhianvi titled 'Gustakhiyan'. Priyanka, who was said to be co-producing the film with Bhansali, reportedly did not want to work with Abhishek Bachchan in the film. A source claimed Priyanka had wanted to work with an actor who hasn't done a period film before.
