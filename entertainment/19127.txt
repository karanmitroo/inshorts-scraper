Recently Superstar SRK gifted his ‘Happy New Year’ co-star Abhishek Bachchan a coveted model of a bike which costs approximately Rs 12.5 lakh. The bike was delivered to Abhishek Bachchan’s Juhu bungalow leaving the actor pleasantly surprised. Reportedly, their rapport had hit a rough patch when SRK was not invited to the Abhi-Ash wedding back in 2007.
