The poster for the Tom Hanks and Irrfan Khan starrer 'Inferno' has been unveiled. The film is based on the 2013 book 'Inferno' by Dan Brown and is a sequel to the 2006 film 'Da Vinci Code'. Hanks will be seen reprising his role as the character Robert Langdon while Irrfan will portray the character Harry 'The Provost' Sims.
