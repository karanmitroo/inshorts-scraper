The trailer of 'P.O.W. - Bandi Yuddh Ke', a new television serial which will premiere on Star Plus, has been released. Directed by Nikkhil Advani, the show is an Indian adaptation of Israeli TV show 'Hatufim'. It revolves around two Indian soldiers, played by Purab Kohli and Satyadeep Misra, who return to India after 17 years as prisoners in Pakistan.
