Actress Diana Penty, who made her Bollywood debut with the 2012 film 'Cocktail', will be seen opposite Abhay Deol in a romantic comedy titled 'Happy Bhaag Jaegi'. Notably, this will be Diana's second film. "I was waiting for the right kind of role and script", she said. The film, directed by Mudassar Aziz, also features Jimmy Shergill and Ali Fazal.
