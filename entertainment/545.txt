Aamir Khan and Zaira Wasim starrer 'Secret Superstar' has become the second Bollywood film after 'Dangal' to earn over $100 million (over ₹643 crore) in China. Earlier, 'Dangal' had earned $113.76 million within 16 days of its release in China. Released on January 19, 'Secret Superstar' had beaten the opening day collections of 'Dangal' in China by earning ₹43.35 crore.
