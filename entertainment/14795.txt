On the day before the release of American fantasy adventure film 'Jungle Book', it was among the most trending Google searches in India on Thursday. It was most looked up in Maharashtra, Goa, Chhattisgarh and Gujarat. 'AIPMT admit card', 'Real Madrid', 'Gudi Padwa' and 'Bipasha Basu marriage' were some of the other trending Google searches on the same day.
