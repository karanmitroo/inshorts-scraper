Actress Nimrat Kaur of 'The Lunchbox' fame has walked out of filmmaker Tony D'Souza's 'Azhar', a biopic based on former Indian cricketer Mohammad Azharuddin's life. Nimrat was supposed to play the role of Azhar's lawyer in the film. The movie, starring Emraan Hashmi in the lead role, is scheduled to go on floors in July this year.
