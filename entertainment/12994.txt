The Arshad Warsi, Boman Irani and Aditi Rao Hydari starrer 'The Legend Of Michael Mishra', which released on Friday, "is a shambolic mess", wrote India Today. "This comedy will make you cry for the wasted talent", wrote Indian Express while Times of India called it "absolutely absurd". It has been rated 0.5/5 (India Today), 1/5 (Indian Express) and 1.5/5 (TOI).
