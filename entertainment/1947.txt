Sonam Kapoor was trolled on social media for wishing Pakistani actor Fawad Khan on his 36th birthday on Wednesday. A user wrote, "F**k terroristani," while another comment read, "Go (to) Pakistan and wish him." Meanwhile, a user wrote, "Hope situation will be soon favourable for him to work there again." Notably, Sonam worked with him in the 2014 film 'Khoobsurat'.
