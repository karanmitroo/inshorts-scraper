Music composer Aadesh Shrivastava died of cancer in Kokilaben hospital (Mumbai) on Saturday. According to his family members, he was not responding to chemotherapy in the last few days, so the treatment was stopped. His work includes musical compositions for films like ‘Chalte Chalte’, ‘Baabul’, ‘Baghban’ and ‘Kabhie Khushi Kabhie Gham'.
