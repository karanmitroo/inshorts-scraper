The first look of Karan Johar's Gori Tere Pyar Mein, a romantic comedy starring Imran Khan and Kareena Kapoor, was unveiled on Sunday. The duo, Imran and Kareena, was last seen together in "Ek main aur ek tu". The movie, written and directed by Punit Malhotra, will release on November-22 while the trailer releases on the September-10.
