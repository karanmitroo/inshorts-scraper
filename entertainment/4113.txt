Kishore Kumar and Asha Bhosle's song 'Kehdoon Tumhen' from the 1975 film 'Deewaar' has been recreated as 'Socha Hai' for the Ajay Devgn and Emraan Hashmi starrer 'Baadshaho'. Tanishk Bagchi has recreated the music for the new song with vocals by Jubin Nautiyal and Neeti Mohan, and additional lyrics by Manoj Muntashir. 'Baadshaho' also stars Esha Gupta and Ileana D'Cruz.
