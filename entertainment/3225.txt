Actor Salman Khan has revealed that his first salary was ₹75 which he got for dancing behind in some show in Taj Hotel. Further talking about his pay scale in the industry, Salman added, "It went up to ₹750 for a soft drink brand...then it was ₹1,500 for the longest time. Then I got paid ₹31,000 for 'Maine Pyar Kiya'."
