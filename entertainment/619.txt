Actress Bhumi Pednekar and actor Vicky Kaushal have featured in Forbes India's '30 under 30' list. Mithila Palkar, known for her roles in web series, and singer Jubin Nautiyal are part of the list. Fashion designer Alan Alexander Kaleekal and jewellery designer Suhani Parekh were also included in the list, which focuses on young achievers under the age of 30.
