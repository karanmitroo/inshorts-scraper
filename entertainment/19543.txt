After the domestic assault allegation, Om Puri is compelled to provide financial support of amount close to Rs 3 lakh to his estranged wife, Nandita. This includes monthly interim maintenance of Rs 1.25 lakh to Nandita and Rs 50,000 towards their son. The court also directed Puri to continue to pay Rs.1.15 lakh, which he was voluntarily paying every month.
