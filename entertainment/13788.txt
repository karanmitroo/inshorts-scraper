The Jesse Eisenberg, Daniel Radcliffe and Mark Ruffalo starrer 'Now You See Me 2', which released on Friday, is "all style and glamour, no substance", wrote India Today. "It's... the sort of sequel everyone dreads," wrote Hindustan Times while Times of India called it "more of an emotional saga". It was rated 1.5/5 (India Today), 2/5 (HT) and 3/5 (TOI).
