Actors Harshvardhan Kapoor & Saiyami Kher have featured on the cover of the film magazine 'Filmfare' for its latest edition. Saiyami has worn a sheer lace ensemble while Harshavrdhan has worn a white t-shirt for the cover shot. This is the first magazine cover for the duo, who will make their Bollywood debut in the upcoming film 'Mirzya'.
