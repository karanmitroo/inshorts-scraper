Bollywood celebrities including Shah Rukh Khan, Akshay Kumar and Abhishek Bachchan on social media wished the Indian women's cricket team for the ICC World Cup final which was held on Sunday. Akshay tweeted, "Come on India, let our #WomenInBlue feel the support of a billion fans!". "Word!!! C'mon girls!!! Full power!! Jai Hind!," wrote Abhishek Bachchan.
