Marathi and Bollywood actor Sadashiv Amrapurkar passed away at Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani hospital Monday morning after battling with lung infection for over a fortnight. Amrapurkar had won two Filmfare awards- best supporting actor award for "Ardh Satya" and the best villain trophy for his portrayal of a ruthless eunuch in "Sadak". His other works include "Aankhen", "Ishq" and "Coolie No.1"
