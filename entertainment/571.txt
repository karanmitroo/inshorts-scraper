Ekta Kapoor has announced her production house Balaji Motion Pictures will be producing a film on Amul founder Verghese Kurien, who is known as 'Milkman of India'. The film will be directed by 'Toilet- Ek Prem Katha' director Shree Narayan Singh. It will be an adaptation of the book 'I Too Had A Dream' which chronicles the life of Kurien.
