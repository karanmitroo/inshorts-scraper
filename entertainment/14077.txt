A teaser of the Riteish Deshmukh and Nargis Fakhri starrer upcoming musical drama features 'Banjo' was released on Monday. The teaser features narration by actor Abhishek Bachchan introducing Riteish's character of a banjo player. Meanwhile, Nargis portrays the role of a DJ in the film. Directed by Ravi Jadhav, the film will release on September 23.
