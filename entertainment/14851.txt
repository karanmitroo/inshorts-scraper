A new promo of stand-up comedian Kapil Sharma's upcoming show 'The Kapil Sharma Show' has been released. The promo features Kapil with Ali Asgar, known for playing 'Dadi' in 'Comedy Nights With Kapil'. The show, also featuring Sumona Chakravarti, Sunil Grover and Kiku Sharda will premiere on Sony channel on April 23.
