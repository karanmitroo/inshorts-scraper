Indian actor Kunal Kapoor, of "Rang De Basanti" fame, has tied the knot with his long time lover Naina, who also happens to be the niece of Bollywood superstar Amitabh Bachchan. The nuptials took place on Monday in a close, private ceremony on the picturesque island of Seychelles. The couple was introduced to each other by Shweta Nanda, Amitabh's daughter.
