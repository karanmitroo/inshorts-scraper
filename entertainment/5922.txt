Singer Diljit Dosanjh has said he doesn't want to sing for any superstar, and if given a chance, he'd love to sing religious numbers. He added, "I've always wanted to be a singer and not an actor." Diljit, who made his Bollywood debut with 2016 film 'Udta Punjab', is known for songs like 'Do You Know', 'Patiala Peg', among others.
