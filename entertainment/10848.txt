'Khulke Dulke' a new song from the Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor starrer 'Befikre' was released on Wednesday. Composed by the music director duo Vishal and Shekhar, the song has been sung by Gippy Grewal and Harshdeep Kaur with lyrics by Jaideep Sahni. Yash Raj Films while sharing the video on Twitter wrote, "Ready to do the bhangra in Paris?"
