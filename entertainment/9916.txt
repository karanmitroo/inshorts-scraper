'Go Pagal', the first song from the Akshay Kumar and Huma Qureshi starrer upcoming courtroom comedy drama film 'Jolly LLB 2' has been released. It has been composed by Manj Musik with vocals by Raftaar and Nindy Kaur. The lyrics have been penned by Manj Musik and Raftaar. Directed by Subhash Kapoor, 'Jolly LLB 2' will release on February 10.
