Actress Sonam Kapoor will be awarded the Icon of India award at the upcoming 11th Asiavision Movie Awards. She will also receive the Best Actress Award for her performance in the film 'Neerja'. Actress Radhika Apte will be awarded for her performances in the films 'Parched', 'Phobia' and 'Kabali'. The award ceremony will take place on November 18 in UAE.
