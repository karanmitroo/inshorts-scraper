The trailer of 'Fifty Shades Darker' has become the most viewed trailer in 24 hours, getting 114 million views across all digital platforms, according to Universal. Previously, the title was held by 'Star Wars: The Force Awakens', with 112 million views online in 24 hours. Featuring Jamie Dornan and Dakota Johnson, 'Fifty Shades Darker' will release on February 10, 2017.
