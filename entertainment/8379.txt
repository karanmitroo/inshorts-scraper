A video by fashion magazine 'Vogue' shows comedian Mallika Dua interviewing actress Anushka Sharma. In the video, Mallika can be seen addressing Anushka as 'didi' and jokingly asks her what her name is and what she does. In the rapid fire round, Dua asks Anushka what her favourite snack is, to which Anushka replies "ragi chips".
