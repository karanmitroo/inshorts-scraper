Actor Akshay Kumar took to Twitter to share a scene which was deleted from his recently released film 'Jolly LLB 2'. Set during lunch break in a courtroom, the scene features Akshay, Saurabh Shukla and Annu Kapoor. Sequel to 2013 National Award-winning film 'Jolly LLB', the film, directed by Subhash Kapoor, also features Huma Qureshi.
