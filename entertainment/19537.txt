Bollywood movie buffs are in for a treat this November with 6-hyped releases spread throughout the month; Krishh-3 on November-1, 'Ramleela' and 'Rajjo' on (November-15), 'Gori Tere Pyaar Mein' and 'Singh Saab The Great'(November-22) and 'Bullett Raja'(November-29). A whopping Rs.310-crore rides on these releases although Krishh-3 is believed to have an edge over the others because of its release date.
