'Mon Amour', a new song from the Hrithik Roshan and Yami Gautam starrer film 'Kaabil' has been released. 'Mon Amour' is a French term which translates to 'my love' in English. Composed by Rajesh Roshan, the song has been sung by Vishal Dadlani, with lyrics by Manoj Muntashir. Directed by Rakesh Roshan, 'Kaabil' will release on January 25, 2017.
