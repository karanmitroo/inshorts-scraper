The first song of Salman Khan's upcoming film 'Prem Ratan Dhan Payo' was released on YouTube by T-Series on Wednesday. It is the title song of the film and has been composed by Himesh Reshammiya. The lyrics of the song are written by Irshad Kamil who is accredited with songs of the film 'Rockstar'. The video has over 44,000 views.
