Actress Richa Chadha and actor Vicky Kaushal have featured in a video by AIB, which traces how Bollywood "has enabled harassment" over the years. A satirical take on songs including 'Khud Ko Kya Samajhti Hai' and 'O Laal Dupatte Wali' have been featured in the video. The video also stars actress and YouTuber Mallika Dua.
