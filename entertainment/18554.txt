The much-awaited romantic comedy, "Happy Ending", starring Saif Ali Khan and Ileana D'cruz is set to release on Nov-28, said a press release. Earlier, actor Ranbir Kapoor's Bombay Velvet was scheduled for a Nov-29 release, but now it has been postponed to May 2015. Directed by Raj Nidimoru and Krishna DK, "Happpy Ending" is produced by Saif and Dinesh Vijan.
