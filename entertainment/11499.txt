Filmmaker Hansal Mehta has shared a photo of himself, along with actress Kangana Ranaut from the sets of their upcoming film 'Simran' on Twitter. The photo, captioned, "#PartnerInCrime - the one and only #KanganaRanaut #Simran #USA", shows Kangana with salt-and-pepper hair. The actress will play the role of 'Praful Patel', a Gujarati NRI in the film.
