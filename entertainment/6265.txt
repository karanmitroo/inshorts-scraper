Filmmaker Kunal Kohli revealed that Aamir Khan said, "I'll give you three names and that is Kajol, Kajol and Kajol," when asked who should star opposite him in the film Fanaa. Kunal's directorial Fanaa completed eleven years on Friday. Speaking on the occasion, he added, "Having them (Aamir and Kajol) on board... remains my best memory of Fanaa."
