Worried by the 'ghar wapsi' programme, the NDA government has roped in veteran actor Amitabh Bachchan for its secular advertisement.  The ad film, which is a brainchild of PM Narendra Modi, stresses the need to rise above religion and caste for inclusive development. Also, MoS for Minority Affairs Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi will tour areas which have a high minority population.
