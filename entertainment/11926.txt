According to reports, former badminton champion Pullela Gopichand's biopic will be made in three languages, namely Telugu, Hindi and English. The movie, to be directed by Telugu filmmaker Praveen Sattaru, will see Sudheer Babu, who had trained under Gopichand, playing the role of his teacher. Gopichand has coached Saina Nehwal and PV Sindhu, India's only two Olympic medalists in badminton.
