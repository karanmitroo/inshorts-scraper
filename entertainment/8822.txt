Actor Shah Rukh Khan's 1989 show 'Circus' will be aired on national television after 28 years, as announced by Doordarshan on its Twitter handle. The show will be aired on the channel DD National from February 19 at 8 pm. Shah Rukh Khan had acted in several other television shows including 'Fauji', 'Ummeed' and 'Dil Dariya' before entering into films.
