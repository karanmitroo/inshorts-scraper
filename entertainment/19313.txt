With 5.9 million downloads via BitTorrent, 'Game of Thrones' has been the most illegally downloaded TV show for the second year in a row, according to piracy site TorrentFreak. The show reportedly went past other TV shows like 'Breaking Bad' (4.2 mn downloads), 'The Walking Dead' (3.6 mn downloads), 'The Big Bang Theory' and 'How I Met Your Mother'.
