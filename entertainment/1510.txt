A photo gallery shows the birthday celebrations of Saif Ali Khan and Kareena Kapoor's son Taimur Ali Khan, who turned 1 on Wednesday. The celebrations were attended by Kareena's parents Randhir Kapoor and Babita Kapoor, Saif's mother Sharmila Tagore, Amrita Arora and Karisma Kapoor among others. Taimur's birthday was celebrated at the Pataudi Palace.
