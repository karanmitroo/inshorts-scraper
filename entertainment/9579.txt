Actor Ayushmann Khurrana took to Twitter to announce his upcoming film with actress Bhumi Pednekar titled 'Shubh Mangal Saavdhan'. The romantic comedy film is a remake of Tamil film 'Kalyana Samayal Saadham'. It will be directed by RS Prasanna, who directed the original film. 'Shubh Mangal Saavdhan' will be jointly produced by Aanand L Rai and Eros.
