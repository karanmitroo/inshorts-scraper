Bengali star Jisshu Sengupta will be seen in the romantic lead opposite Deepika Padukone in Shoojit Sircar's next film ‘Piku’. The film already has a formidable cast including Amitabh Bachchan and Irrfan Khan. Jisshu made his Bollywood debut opposite Ileana D'Cruz in a guest appearance in Anurag Basu's Barfi! Piku would take Jisshu to Mumbai in August.
