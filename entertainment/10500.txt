Actress Rani Mukerji Chopra shared a picture of Adira, her first child with husband Aditya Chopra, on her daughter's first birthday on Friday. In a note with the picture, she wrote, "I hope I can bring up Adira without any fears, well-mannered, wise. Encourage her to follow her heart." Notably, Aditya Chopra's film 'Befikre' hits the theatres on Friday.
