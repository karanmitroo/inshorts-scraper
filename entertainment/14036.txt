The Russell Crowe and Ryan Gosling starrer 'The Nice Guys', which released on Friday, "is a failed attempt", wrote Indian Express. The film "will have to make do with being a cult classic," wrote Hindustan Times. Mid-Day wrote that the film has "speedy action and... wild goose chases". It has been rated 2/5 (Indian Express), 3/5 (Mid-Day) and 4/5 (HT).
