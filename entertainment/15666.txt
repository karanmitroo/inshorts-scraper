The trailer of the film '10 Cloverfield Lane', a sequel to 'Cloverfield' (2008), was released on Friday. The thriller film has been directed by  Dan Trachtenberg and stars Mary Elizabeth Winstead, John Goodman and John Gallagher Jr in lead roles. Produced by the director of 'Star Wars: The Force Awakens', JJ Abrams, it is scheduled to release on March 11.
