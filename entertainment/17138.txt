Actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, who is currently at the ongoing 68th Cannes Film Festival, on Wednesday shared her first posts on Twitter. Under the handle '@LOrealParisIn', the actress posted a picture of her and captioned it "Good morning world! Rise and shine! Can't wait to share my day with you! #AishwaryaTakesOver". Aishwarya also shared a video with her fans.
