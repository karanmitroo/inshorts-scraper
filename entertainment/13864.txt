The Nithiin Reddy starrer Telugu film 'A Aa' has become the third-highest grosser among Telugu films in the US having minted over $2 million. "So far only ‘Baahubali’ and ‘Srimanthudu’ have collected over $2 million in the US,” said trade analyst Trinath. The film, which also stars Samantha Ruth Prabhu and Anupama Parameswaran, released on June 1 in the US.
