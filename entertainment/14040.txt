'Housefull 3', which released on Friday, minted ₹15.21 crore on its opening day, making it the second-highest opener of 2016 in India. Starring Akshay Kumar, Jacqueline Fernandes and Riteish Deshmukh among others, the film trails behind Shah Rukh Khan's 'Fan' which earned ₹19.20 crore on its first day. With an opening of ₹12.35 crore, 'Airlift' comes third in the list.
