The Maharashtra government today declared Sonam Kapoor starrer 'Neerja' tax-free in the state. The film, directed by Ram Madhvani, released in India on Friday. The biopic revolves around the 1986 hijacking of Pan Am Flight 73 in Karachi and its crew member Neerja Bhanot, who received the Ashok Chakra for her bravery and saving passengers' life on the flight.
