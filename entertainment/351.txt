Haryanvi singer Vikas Kumar has sent a legal notice to the makers of 'Veerey Ki Wedding' suing them for ₹7 crore while alleging that his song 'Hat Ja Tau' has been used without his permission. The film's version titled 'Hatt Ja Tau' features dancer Sapna Choudhary. "We have also sent a complaint to CFBC Chairman Prasoon Joshi,” said Kumar's lawyer.
