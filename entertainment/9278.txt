Filmmaker Hansal Mehta, while reacting to the attack on Padmavati's director Sanjay Leela Bhansali during the film's shoot in Jaipur, tweeted, "I will not rise for the national anthem before a film." He blamed India's political environment, which is encouraging for fringe elements and the attack on cultural freedom. Hansal further tweeted, "F**k you Karni sena & your ilk."
