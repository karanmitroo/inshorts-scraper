Actress Kareena Kapoor has said that her son Taimur is the most gorgeous man on this side of the equator. Responding to Priyanka Chopra's comment that Taimur has his mother's pout, Kareena said, "I take it as a compliment because his lips are amazing." Kareena gave birth to Taimur, her first child with husband Saif Ali Khan in December 2016.
