Sonam Kapoor, Shahid Kapoor, Alia Bhatt and Diljit Dosanjh have featured on the cover of 'Filmfare' magazine as winners at Filmfare Awards 2017. Sonam is seen in an Elie Saab gown while Alia has worn a Prabal Gurung gown. Other honourees of the award, including Shabana Azmi, Manoj Bajpayee, Rishi Kapoor and Nitesh Tiwari, have featured in the extended cover.
