Makers of the recently released film 'Tara- The journey Of Love And Passion' have dedicated it to Nirbhaya - the Delhi gang-rape victim. However Awnindra Pratap Pandey, Nirbhaya's friend, who was with her on the fateful night accused the makers for the misleading dedication. The Director slammed Pandey's allegations and accused him of demanding money to extend support to the film.
