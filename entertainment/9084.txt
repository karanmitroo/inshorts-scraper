Actress Urmila Matondkar featured in the song 'Lakdi Ki Kaathi' from the 1983 film 'Masoom'. The actress made her Bollywood debut as a child artist in the movie 'Kalyug' in 1981. Urmila, who turned 43 on Saturday, is best known for her movies 'Rangeela' (1995), 'Pyaar Tune Kya Kiya' (2001), and 'Ek Hasina Thi' (2004).
