The trailer of the all-female starrer heist film 'Ocean's 8', which stars Anne Hathaway, Sandra Bullock and Rihanna, has been released. The film, which is a spin-off of the Ocean's trilogy, also stars Cate Blanchett, Helena Bonham Carter, Sarah Paulson, Mindy Kaling, and Awkwafina. Directed by Gary Ross, 'Ocean's 8' is scheduled to release on June 8, 2018.
