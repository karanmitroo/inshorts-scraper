Creating a positive impact on critics and audience alike, Damien Chazelle's sophomore directorial, 'Whiplash' has been given a thumbs-up by most reviewers. Revolving around the complexities between a sadist jazz teacher and a student, the film has only "greatness" in its sights, writes Indian Express. It has been rated 4.5/5 (Indian Express, TOI), 4/5 (IBN Live) and 3.5/5 (Zee News).
