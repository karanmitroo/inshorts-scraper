Megastar Amitabh Bachchan Saturday took to his official blog to praise 'Furious 7', the latest installment in the 'Fast and Furious' franchise. "Watching 'Fast & Furious 7' is so inspiring... you can actually survive in its presentation and its moments of action...", Bachchan wrote. Talking about Paul Walker's demise, he wrote, "No one gets removed permanently, no one passes away...".
