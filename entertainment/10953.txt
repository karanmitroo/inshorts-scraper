The Neha Sharma, Aditya Seal and Aashim Gulati starrer 'Tum Bin 2' has "an over-cooked plot garnished with lots of contrivances", wrote Indian Express. NDTV called the film a "bland sequel" while Times of India wrote, "is an earnest remake of the original with a few minor twists". The film was rated 1.5/5 (Indian Express), 2.5/5 (NDTV) and 3/5 (TOI).
