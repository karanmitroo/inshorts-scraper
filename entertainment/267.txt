Malayalam actress Priya Prakash Varrier, who shot into limelight after the clip of her wink from a song's video went viral, met cricket legend Sachin Tendulkar at an Indian Super League match on Friday. The 18-year-old shared a picture of herself with a co-actor and Sachin Tendulkar on social media, captioned, "With- Master Blaster Sachin Sir...God Of Cricket."
