Haryanvi singer and dancer Sapna Chaudhary will mark her first Bollywood appearance with the song 'Love Bite' from the upcoming film 'Journey of Bhangover'. Sapna is currently seen in the reality show 'Bigg Boss 11'. Written and directed by Mahinder Singh Saniwal, 'Journey of Bhangover' will also mark the production debut of actress Bhumi Pednekar's mother Sumitra Hooda Pednekar.
