Ayushmann Khurrana and Kriti Sanon gatecrashed a wedding to promote their upcoming film 'Bareilly Ki Barfi'. The actors also danced to the song 'Sweety Tera Drama' from the film. Sharing her experience of gatecrashing the wedding, Kriti tweeted, "This was soooo much fun! Had never done it as a teenager- banta tha yaar!"
