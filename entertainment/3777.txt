According to reports, Abhishek Bachchan's stalker Jhanvi Kapoor will be a part of 'Bigg Boss 11'. Jhanvi, a Bhojpuri actress, had slit her wrist and claimed that she was married to Abhishek on the eve of his wedding to Aishwarya Rai. "The makers want to bring in controversial figures who can create a storm," a source was quoted as saying.
