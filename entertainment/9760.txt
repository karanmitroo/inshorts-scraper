A new poster of 'The Ghazi Attack', starring Taapsee Pannu and Rana Daggubati,
has been unveiled. The film is reportedly based on the sinking of PNS Ghazi, a Pakistani submarine during the Indo-Pak 1971 war and how a naval officer and his team remained underwater for 18 days. Also starring Kay Kay Menon, the film will release on February 17.
