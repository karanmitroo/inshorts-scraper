'A Billion Colour Story', co-produced by Bollywood actor Satish Kaushik and directed by Padmakumar Narasimhamurthy won the Audience Award at the 2017 London Indian Film Festival. "[The award] vindicates our faith that people and their hearts are the same across cultures," said the film's makers. It revolves around 11-year-old Hari Aziz, born to a Hindu mother and a Muslim father.
