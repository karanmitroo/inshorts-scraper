Late Hollywood actress Audrey Hepburn's personal shooting script for her 1961 film 'Breakfast at Tiffany's' was sold for £632,750 (over ₹5.5 crore) at an auction in London. A Tiffany bangle that was gifted by director Steven Spielberg to the actress sold for ₹2.9 crore. The auction fetched a total of $6.2 million (over ₹40 crore) by selling Audrey's memorabilia.
