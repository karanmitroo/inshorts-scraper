Actor Ayushmann Khurrana, while speaking about late singer-composer Kishore Kumar's biopic said, "I'm a legit actor who can play the legend on-screen." "I'd love to do the Kishore Kumar biopic... I can sing, too," he added. Earlier, Anurag Basu, who is planning to make the biopic, had said that he would like to cast Ranbir Kapoor in the film.
