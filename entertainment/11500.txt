A new song titled 'Cutiepie', featuring Ranbir Kapoor and Anushka Sharma, from the upcoming film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' has been released. Composed by Pritam, it has been sung by Pardeep Singh Sran and Nakash Aziz, with lyrics by Amitabh Bhattacharya. Also starring Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Fawad Khan, the Karan Johar directorial is scheduled to release on October 28.
