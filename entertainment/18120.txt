Lata Mangeshkar launched her new album, 'Main Hawa', the announcement of which she made on her Twitter handle, on Wednesday. Apart form her, Asha Bhonsle and Shaan have also contributed to the album, and the music is given by Rohan Shah. "Mein Hawaa. Sung by Asha Bhosle and Shaan. Music by Rohan Shah. Produced by LM MUSIC", she wrote.
