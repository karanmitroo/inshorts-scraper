'You And Me', a new song from the Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor starrer 'Befikre' was released on Tuesday. Composed by the music director duo Vishal and Shekhar, the song has been sung by Nikhil D'Souza and Rachel Varghese with lyrics by Jaideep Sahni. Written, produced and directed by Aditya Chopra, the film is scheduled to release on December 9.
