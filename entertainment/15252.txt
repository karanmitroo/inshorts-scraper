'Bolna', another song from Sidharth Malhotra, Alia Bhatt and Fawad Khan-starrer upcoming film, 'Kapoor & Sons', has been released. The song, composed by Tanishk Bagchi and written by Dr Devender Kafir, has been sung by Arijit Singh and Asees Kaur. Directed by Shakun Batra, 'Kapoor & Sons' is scheduled to release on March 18, 2016.
