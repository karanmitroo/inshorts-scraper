'Tiger Zinda Hai' has entered the ₹200 crore club by earning ₹206.04 crore within a week of its release, as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. It is Salman Khan's fifth film to earn over ₹200 crore. The film crossed the lifetime earnings of 'Golmaal Again' and has become the second highest grosser of 2017 after 'Baahubali 2: The Conclusion'.
