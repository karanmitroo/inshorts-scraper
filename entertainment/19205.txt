The official trailer ‘Main Tera Hero’, starring Varun Dhawan, Ileana D’Cruz and Nargis Fakhri, has been released. The movie, directed by David Dhawan and produced by Shobha Kapoor and Ekta Kapoor, is Varun’s second venture after ‘Student of the Year.’ The film, a typical David Dhawan style with lavish sets and witty dialogues, will release on April 4.
