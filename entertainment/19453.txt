With a bold slogan of 'Go digital or miss Dhoom3', YRF's scion Aditya Chopra has proclaimed that D3 would be screened exclusively on digital cinema and not reel. Although Chopra would incur setbacks from non-digital single theatres, he is confident to make up by viewer count. Digitalization eliminates piracy by preventing print duplication and the cost-saving content encryption protects copyrights.
