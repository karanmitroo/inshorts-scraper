Bollywood superstar Shah Rukh Khan starrer 'Raees', a Rahul Dholakia directorial, has been scheduled to release on Eid next year. The film also stars Pakistani actress Mahira Khan whose telly show 'Humsafar' with co-actor Fawad Khan became very popular in India. In the movie, also featuring Nawazuddin Siddiqui, SRK will be seen playing the role of a bootlegger.
