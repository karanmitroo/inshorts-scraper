Bollywood actors and actresses including Amitabh Bachchan, Shah Rukh Khan and Salman Khan celebrated Diwali on Sunday. While Salman marked the occasion with his family, Katrina celebrated the festival with Kabir Khan's family. Amitabh along with Abhishek Bachchan and  Aishwarya Rai hosted a Diwali party. Aamir Khan also hosted a party, which SRK, Shraddha Kapoor and Tiger Shroff attended.
