The Irrfan Khan and Saba Qamar starrer film 'Hindi Medium' "strongly drives its point home," wrote Hindustan Times (HT). The film has "remarkable quality of acting," said NDTV. The Times of India (TOI) wrote that the film exposes "the inadequacies and loopholes in our education system". It has been rated 4/5 by TOI, 3.5/5 by HT and 2.5/5 by NDTV.
