Rohit Shetty, who is all set to bring back the hit onscreen couple Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol together in his upcoming film 'Dilwale', has said that the movie will release this Christmas. Like Golmaal’ and ‘Singham’, most of the film will be shot in Goa too, Shetty said. The film also stars Varun Dhawan and Kriti Sanon.
