Actress-singer Priyanka Chopra has been nominated at the Europe Music Awards (EMA) 2015. Her nomination is under the best India act category, a subcategory of the worldwide act. Indus Creed, Your Chin, The Ska Vengers and Monica Dogra have also been nominated under the same category. The winners of EMA will be announced on VH1 on October 25, 2015.
