The official trailer of actor Chiranjeevi's upcoming Telugu action drama film 'Khaidi No 150' has been released. Remake of 2014 Tamil film 'Kaththi', the film also stars actress Kajal Aggarwal. It will be the 150th film of Chiranjeevi, wherein he will reportedly play a double role. 'Khaidi No 150' is scheduled to release on January 11, 2017.
