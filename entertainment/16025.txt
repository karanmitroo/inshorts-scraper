Sushant Singh Rajput and Kriti Sanon have been roped in by Dinesh Vijan for his debut directorial venture. Co-founder of Illuminati Films with actor Saif Ali Khan, Vijan has produced films like 'Being Cyrus' and 'Love Aaj Kal'. Sushant took to Twitter to share the first look of the yet unnamed film which will go on floors in early 2016.
