John Abraham appeared in the music video of Pankaj Udhas' 1999 song 'Chupke Chupke' from the album 'Mahek', before making his debut in Bollywood. The actor, who turned 44 on Saturday, uses the inverted version of his father Abraham John's name. John, who made his Bollywood debut with 2003 film 'Jism', also won modelling competition 'Gladrags Manhunt India' in 1999.
