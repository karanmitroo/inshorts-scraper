Sushant Singh Rajput will be seen 27-times kissing his co-stars Parineeti Chopra and Vaani Kapoor in the upcoming film 'Shuddh Desi Romance', Lehren (a video news magazine) has reported. After his impressive debut in 'Kai Po Che', Sushant(27) is in demand by industry's biggest banner, Yash Raj Films and has signed films with stars like Aamir Khan and Katrina Kaif.
