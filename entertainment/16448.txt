On the occasion of Teachers' Day, Bollywood superstar Shah Rukh Khan on Saturday posted on Twitter that his kids are like his teachers. “I learn Patience, Gentleness, New Songs & Trends, Unconditional love, Impudence & to Laugh without reason from them,” he tweeted. The 49-year-old actor has three kids namely Aryan (17), Suhana (15) and AbRam (2).
