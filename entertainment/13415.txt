'Jaaneman Aah’, the song featuring Parineeti Chopra, from the Varun Dhawan and John Abraham starrer ‘Dishoom' was released on Tuesday. Picturised on Parineeti and Varun, the song has been composed by Pritam and sung by Antara Mitra and Aman Trikha. The film, which also stars Jacqueline Fernandez, Akshaye Khanna and Saqib Saleem, is scheduled to release on July 29.
