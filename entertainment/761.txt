The Economic Survey 2017-18 has quoted Bollywood actor Sunny Deol's dialogue 'tarikh par tarikh' to highlight frequent delays in the judicial process. It also featured a line from a Bollywood song, "Mere desh ki dharti sona ugle, ugle heerey moti (My country's soil where crops grow like gold, diamonds, and pearls)," to underline threats to agriculture due to climate change.
