Actress Sonam Kapoor in a video for 'Vogue India' showcased her personal wardrobe and discussed her favourite outfits, shoes and accessories. The video was part of the 'Vogue All Access Series' where Sonam revealed customised 'Dolce & Gabbana' shoes and a studded 'Burberry' jacket among other favourites. She especially mentioned a 'Chanel' suit which she wore in the film 'Aisha'.
