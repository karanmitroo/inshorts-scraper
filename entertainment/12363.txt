Indian cricketer Mahendra Singh Dhoni and actor Sushant Singh Rajput have featured in a promotional video for the upcoming biopic 'M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story'. Titled 'Captain Curious', the video shows Dhoni asking Sushant what the film will be about, while Sushant refuses to give him any details. Directed by Neeraj Pandey, the film will release on September 30.
