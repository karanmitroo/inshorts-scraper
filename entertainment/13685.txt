Hollywood movie 'Independence Day: Resurgence', which is a sequel to the 1996 film 'Independence Day', was released on Friday following which 'Independence Day' was the second most googled term in India. It was most looked up in Pondicherry, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu and Meghalaya. 'SENSEX', 'Harshvardhan Kapoor', 'Dollar Rate' and 'Lionel Messi' were some of the other trending searches.
