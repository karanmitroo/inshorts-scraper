The release date of 'Ek Ladki Ko Dekha To Aisa Laga', which will star Sonam Kapoor and her father Anil Kapoor together for the first time, has been announced as October 12. The film will also star Juhi Chawla and Rajkummar Rao. It will be co-produced by Vidhu Vinod Chopra and also mark the directorial debut of Shelly Chopra Dhar.
