Actress and politician Hema Malini's first look from the upcoming Telugu film 'Gautamiputra Satakarni' has been revealed. She is set to play the role of Gautami Balasri in the film, which is based on the life of ruler Gautamiputra Satakarni, portrayed by Nandamuri Balakrishna. Directed by National award-winning director Krish Jagarlamudi, the film also stars Shriya Saran and Kabir Bedi.
