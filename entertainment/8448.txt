In a recent episode of Koffee with Karan, filmmakers Imtiaz Ali, Zoya Akhtar and Kabir Khan named actors Shah Rukh Khan and Salman Khan respectively, when asked by host Karan to name an actor who reports late to a film's sets. While Zoya and Kabir named Amitabh Bachchan as the most punctual actor, Imtiaz named Ranbir Kapoor.
