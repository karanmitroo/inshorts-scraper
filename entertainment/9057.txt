The Hrithik Roshan and Yami Gautam starrer 'Kaabil' has entered the ₹100 crore club on the eleventh day of its release, as per the film's makers. The film collected ₹9.22 crore on February 4, taking the total to ₹106.25 crore. Notably, 'Kaabil' released on January 25, sharing its release date with the Shah Rukh Khan starrer 'Raees'.
