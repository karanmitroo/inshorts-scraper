Alia Bhatt featured as a child actor in the 1999 film 'Sangharsh', starring Akshay Kumar, Preity Zinta, and Ashutosh Rana. Alia, who turned 24 on Wednesday, later made her Bollywood debut in a leading role in the 2012 movie 'Student of the Year'. She is also known to have sung for her movies including 'Dear Zindagi' (2016) and 'Highway' (2014).
