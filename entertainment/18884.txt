Sabbir Khan's 'Heropanti', starring Tiger shroff and Kriti sanon released this Friday. The movie has been rated 1/2 (Indian Express), 2.5 (DNA), 3 (TOI critics review), 3.5 (NDTV, Subhash K Jha). 'Heropanti' is an unabashedly straight and sentimental launchpad for Tiger Shroff. And he excels in every department, writes NDTV.
