Tiger Zinda Hai's director Ali Abbas Zafar has shared a picture of Katrina Kaif and Salman Khan from the film's sets. Zafar captioned the picture, "A very serious Katrina Kaif and super focused @BeingSalmanKhan on the set of @TigerZindaHai #behind the scenes. Friday Feelings." The film is a sequel to the 2012 film Ek Tha Tiger.
