Actress Vidya Balan recently met with her co-stars from the television serial 'Hum Paanch' at Zee's silver jubilee. Vidya played the character 'Radhika', who suffered from a hearing impairment, in the serial. The actress met Shoma Anand, who played the mother 'Bina Mathur', Bhairavi Raichura, who portrayed 'Kajal Bhai', and Vandana Pathak, who played the eldest sister 'Meenakshi Mathur'.
