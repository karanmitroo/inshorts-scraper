The title song of the Hrithik Roshan and Pooja Hegde starrer 'Mohenjo Daro' was released on Wednesday. The song has been composed by AR Rahman and sung by Rahman along with Arijit Singh, Bela Shende and Sanah Moidutty. The track has been penned by Javed Akhtar. Directed by Ashutosh Gowariker, the film is scheduled to release on August 12.
