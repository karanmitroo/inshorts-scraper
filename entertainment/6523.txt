Actress Madhuri Dixit-Nene has a celestial star, located in the constellation Orion, named after her. She was presented with the star certificate by her fans in 2012. Madhuri, who turned 50 on Monday, made her Bollywood debut with the 1984 film Abodh. Madhuri is known for films Hum Aapke Hain Koun...! (1994), Dil To Pagal Hai (1997) and Devdas (2002).
