A teaser introducing actor Varun Dhawan's character from the upcoming romantic comedy film 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania' has been released. The film marks the second instalment of a franchise that began with the 2014 film 'Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania'. Directed by Shashank Khaitan and also starring Alia Bhatt, the film is scheduled to release on March 10.
