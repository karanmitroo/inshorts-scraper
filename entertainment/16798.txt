Sanjay Leela Bhansali has released the first teaser of ‘Bajirao Mastani’, starring Ranveer Singh in Maratha Peshwa Bajirao-I's role. The teaser gives a glimpse of epic battle scenes and grandeur in a love-saga of Bajirao and his second wife, Mastani, played by Deepika Padukone. The teaser shows Padukone skilled in weaponry, while Bajirao's first wife is played by Priyanka Chopra.
