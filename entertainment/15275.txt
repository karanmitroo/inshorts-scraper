Renowned Odia singer Bhubaneswari Mishra passed away today, aged 67, at her residence in New Delhi following a cardiac arrest. The classical singer is survived by two sons and a daughter. She began her career with the song Tiki Mora Naa Ti for the mythological Odia movie Krushna Sudama in the 1970s. CM Naveen Patnaik has condoled her sudden demise.
