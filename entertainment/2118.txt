Actor Ranveer Singh has said that he has been asked not to say anything on the ongoing row over his upcoming film 'Padmavati'. "Whatever (the) official stand...is, you will get it from producers. It's a very sensitive issue," added Ranveer. He had earlier said that he is 200% with the film 'Padmavati' and his director Sanjay Leela Bhansali.
