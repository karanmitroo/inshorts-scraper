Amitabh Bachchan confused Ranbir Kapoor with Sanjay Dutt in the teaser of the biopic on Dutt, revealed film critic Rajeev Masand. After watching a clip, Bachchan asked the film's makers, "So you guys got Sanju to do that shot?" Masand added it was a shocking discovery for Bachchan to know it was Ranbir and not Dutt in the clip.
