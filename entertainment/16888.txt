Actors Amitabh Bachchan, Salman Khan and Akshay Kumar, and cricketer MS Dhoni have featured in Forbes' 100 Highest-paid Celebrities list 2015. With earnings of $33.5 million each, Bachchan and Salman share the 71st rank. While Akshay ($32.5 million) is ranked 76th, Dhoni is placed at 82nd position ($31 million). American boxer Floyd Mayweather tops the list with $300 million earnings.
