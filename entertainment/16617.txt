The first trailer of Shahid Kapoor and Alia Bhatt starrer 'Shaandaar', which was released on Tuesday, has already got 2 million views on YouTube in just two days. Alia took to Twitter to share the news. "More than 2 million views in less than 48 hours !!! Feel like giving you all a shaandaar kiss ;) hehehe #Shaandaar," she tweeted.
