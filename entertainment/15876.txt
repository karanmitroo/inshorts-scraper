In just three days of its release, the Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol starrer film 'Dilwale' has minted  ₹121.47 crore globally. As per trade analyst Taran Adarsh, till Sunday, the film earned ₹65.09 crore in India, while it minted ₹56.38 crore worldwide. Directed by Rohit Shetty, the film reunited SRK and Kajol after five years.
