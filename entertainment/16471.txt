Amid speculations whether Salman Khan will host 'Bigg Boss 9' or not, it has now been confirmed that the actor will return to the upcoming season of the show. The news was confirmed on Friday by Colors channel CEO Raj Nayak who tweeted, "Colors welcomes #BhajrangiBhaijaan @BeingSalmanKhan to #biggboss9". The show is scheduled to begin in October this year.
