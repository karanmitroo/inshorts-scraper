'Kya Tujhe Ab Ye Dil Bataye', the song from the Pulkit Samrat and Yami Gautam starrer film 'Sanam Re', was YouTube's ninth-most viewed video in India on Monday. The song has been composed by Amaal Mallik, while it has been sung by Falak Shabir. Posted on YouTube on Saturday, the video has garnered over 4.52 lakh views so far.
