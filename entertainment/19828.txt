Director Anurag Basu`s 'Barfi' won multiple awards at the 14th IIFA Awards, Macau(hosted by Shahrukh Khan and Shahid Kapoor) including the best film, best director and best actor(Ranbir Kapoor). The other major award winners are:
BEST ACTOR(F): Vidya Balan (Kahaani),
BEST DEBUT(M): Ayushmaan Khurana (Vicky Donor),
BEST DEBUT(F): Yami Gautam (Vicky Donor),
BEST SINGER(M): Sonu Nigam(Agneepath),
BEST SINGER(F): Shreya Ghoshal(Agneepath)
