A Hyderabad-based animation developer, is in the process of producing a new movie based on India’s first animated television serial character – Chhota Bheem – with a budget of Rs 8 crore. The new 3D movie, releasing in 2015, titled ‘Chhota Bheem Kung-Fu Dhamaka,’ is 100 minutes long; and is aimed at audience in the age group of 6-18.
