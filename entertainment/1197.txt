As per reports, 'Padmavati' is likely to release on January 26 or on the same date as Akshay Kumar's 'Pad Man', which is scheduled to release on January 25. Producers of 'Pad Man' have said that they will not shift the release of their film. "Padmavati is a very important film...it should release soon," said Pad Man's co-producer Prernaa Arora.
