Actress Deepika Padukone has featured on the cover of fashion magazine 'Elle India' for its December edition. She can be seen wearing a red, strapless gown by fashion designer Bibhu Mohapatra and earrings by Louis Vuitton with her hair tied in a messy bun. The cover story on the actress is titled, 'Actor Crusader Superstar Icon Deepika Padukone The Originals'.
