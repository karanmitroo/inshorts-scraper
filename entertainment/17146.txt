Actor Kamal Haasan on Saturday revealed that the name of his upcoming project was 'Thoongaavanam', which means a jungle that doesn't sleep. The film will be shot in Tamil and Telugu simultaneously, he added. Reportedly, Manisha Koirala will play the role of Haasan's wife in Thoongaavanam. Furthermore, Trisha Krishnan of 'Khatta Meetha' fame will also be seen in the film.
