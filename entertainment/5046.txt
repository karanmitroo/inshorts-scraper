The Tamil Nadu government has announced the State Film awards for six consecutive years from 2009-2014. In the Best Picture category, films Pasanga (2009), Myna (2010), Vaagai Sooda Vaa (2011), Vazhakku En 18/9 (2012), Ramanujan (2013) and Kutram Kadithal (2014) received the top honours. The awards were last announced in the year 2008.
