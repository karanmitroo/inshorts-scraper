Columbian singer-dancer Shakira turned 39 years old on Tuesday. Born as Shakira Isabel Mebarak Ripoll, she is the only child of her parents. Shakira recorded her debut album 'Magia' with Sony Music Colombia in 1990 when she was 13. Her official song for the 2010 FIFA World Cup 'Waka Waka' is the biggest-selling World Cup song of all time.
