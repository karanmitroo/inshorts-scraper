Sachin Tendulkar, on the occasion of Amitabh Bachchan's 75th birthday on Wednesday, narrated an incident between the two involving his son Arjun which left him "embarrassed". Sachin revealed he was shooting a commercial with Amitabh when his then a-year-and-a-half-old son Arjun "coolly" wiped his hands on Amitabh's kurta after finishing an orange. Sachin said he "didn't know where to look".
