The Facebook Page of Salman Khan crossed the massive figure of 12 million likes on Thursday. Notably, the actor, who made his Facebook entry in September 2012, is the most popular Bollywood star in terms of number of followers. The other popular stars are Aamir Khan (9.9 million), Amitabh Bachchan (8.3 million), Akshay Kumar (6.4 million) and SRK (6.1 million).
