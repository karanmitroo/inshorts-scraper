West Bengal CM Mamata Banerjee Monday said that megastar Amitabh Bachchan, who was Sunday conferred the Padma Vibhushan, deserves the Bharat Ratna. "The Padma Vibhushan to Amitabh Bachchan is not enough. He is a legend in his lifetime. He deserves a Bharat Ratna," Banerjee posted on her official Facebook and Twitter handles.
