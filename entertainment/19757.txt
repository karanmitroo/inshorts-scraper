After news of Sonam Kapoor charging Rs.11 as a "shagun" in 'Bhaag Milkha Bhaag', it is now reported that filmmaker Karan Johar has decided to charge Rs.11 as token money in "Bombay Velvet". KJo, who will play a negative character opposite Ranbir Kapoor said, "How can I charge for this? Anurag is a friend. I am doing this for experience."
