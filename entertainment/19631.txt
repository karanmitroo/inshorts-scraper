Bagging Rs.1 crore, a history teacher from Udaipur named Taj Mohammed has become the first contestant to walk away with the title of "Crorepati" on the show, Kaun Banega Crorepati-7, being hosted by Amitabh Bachchan. With the money, he wishes to get his partially blind 7-year-old daughter treated, buy a house, educate 3-underprivileged girls and get 2-orphans married.
