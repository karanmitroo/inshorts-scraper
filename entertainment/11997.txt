Actress-singer Lea Michele, known for playing 'Rachel' on the TV series 'Glee', has said that she does not want to date actor Tom Hiddleston as 'he loves Priyanka Chopra' and 'they were flirting'. The 30-year-old actress was playing a game called 'Who'd You Rather' on 'The Ellen Show' when Tom's photograph was shown to her as a potential date.
