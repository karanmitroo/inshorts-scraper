As per reports, actress Deepika Padukone has auditioned for a role in the 2017 reboot of 'The Mummy' franchise which stars Tom Cruise and Sofia Boutella. Deepika is reportedly yet to hear from the casting directors. Earlier, Huma Qureshi auditioned for the main lead opposite Cruise. Notably, Deepika will make her Hollywood debut with 'xXx: The Return of Xander Cage'.
