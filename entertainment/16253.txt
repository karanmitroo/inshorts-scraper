Actor Vishal Thakkar, who has made small appearances in movies like 'Tango Charlie' and 'Chandni Bar', was on Saturday booked for the alleged rape of a TV actress. Thakkar has been booked under sections 376 (rape), 420 (cheating), 323, 509 (insulting the modesty of a woman) and 506 (criminal intimidation). He has not been arrested as yet.
