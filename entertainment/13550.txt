A new poster for the Aamir Khan starrer 'Dangal' was revealed on Monday at an event attended by Aamir along with director Nitesh Tiwari and co-producer Siddharth Roy Kapur. The poster features Aamir as the character 'Mahavir Singh Phogat' along with his onscreen daughters and the caption "Are my daughters any less than sons?" 'Dangal' will release on December 23.
