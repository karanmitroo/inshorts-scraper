Bollywood actor Salman Khan, along with his 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' co-star Kareena Kapoor, director Kabir Khan and music composer Himesh Reshammiya, and rest of the film's crew, recently painted village Hatluni (Maharashtra). They also distributed saris to the ladies of the village. Known to be an artist, Salman also made a painting on one of the houses.
