Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, who is in France to attend the 68th Cannes Film Festival, announced on Thursday that she will be working with filmmaker Sujoy Ghosh in an upcoming project. Although she didn't reveal the film's name, some industry reports suggest the project is an adaptation of ‘The Devotion of Suspect X’. Ghosh is credited for directing Vidya Balan-starrer 'Kahaani'.
