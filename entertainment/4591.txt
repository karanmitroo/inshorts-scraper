Alia Bhatt won the Best Actress award and Shahid Kapoor was named Best Actor for 'Udta Punjab' at the Big Zee Entertainment Awards. Amitabh Bachchan was named Most Entertaining Actor in Drama for 'Pink', Aishwarya Rai Bachchan won Most Entertaining Actress award for 'Sarbjit' while Abhishek Bachchan bagged the award for Most Entertaining Actor in Comedy for 'Housefull 3'.
