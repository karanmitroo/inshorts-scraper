Ranveer Singh, while talking about his advertisement 'Ranveer Ching Returns', said that he "always wanted to do (the) role of a mother". Directed by Rohit Shetty, the advertisement, where Ranveer is portraying the character 'Ranveer Ching', also stars the actor as 'Maa Ching', the titular character's mother. The actor added, "[T]hanks to Rohit, I finally got to play this part."
