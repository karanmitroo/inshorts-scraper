A promotional video featuring actor Shahid Kapoor and wife Mira Rajput on the talk show 'Koffee with Karan' has been released. In the video, when Karan asks Mira, "What is the last thing you say to Shahid before going to bed," Mira jokingly replies, "Kuch Kuch Hota Hai". Notably, the episode will mark the couple's first appearance on television together.
