The official trailer of the John Abraham, Varun Dhawan and Jacqueline Fernandes starrer film 'Dishoom' was released on Wednesday. Directed by Rohit Dhawan, the action-adventure film is a remake of the 2013 Malayalam film 'Mumbai Police'. The film also features Saqib Saleem and Akshaye Khanna in pivotal roles, and Nargis Fakhri in a cameo. It will release on July 29.
