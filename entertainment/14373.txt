Fatima Sana Shaikh, who played the role of Kamal Haasan's daughter in the 1997 film 'Chachi 420', has been roped in to play Aamir Khan's daughter in the upcoming film 'Dangal'. The 24-year-old actress earlier appeared in Bollywood films including 'One 2 Ka 4', 'Bittoo Boss' and 'Akaash Vani'. In 'Dangal', Fatima will portray the role of Geeta Phogat.
