Akshay Kumar starrer Holiday, which was released on Jun-6, on Friday crossed the ‘100 crore’ mark at the box office. The movie grossed approx 69 crore in the first week, and 31 crore in the second. Holiday is the 29th movie of Bollywood to join the 100 crore club; Dhoom 3, Chennai Express, and Krrish 3 top the list.
