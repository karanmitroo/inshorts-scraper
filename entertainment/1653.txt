Actress Nargis Fakhri and American rapper Snoop Dogg sang in Punjabi singer Dr Zeus' new song titled 'Woofer'. Talking about the project, Nargis had said, "I grew up listening to Snoop...so when this came my way, I was completely thrilled." Zora Randhawa is also a part of the collaboration which is the first single from Zeus' album Global Injection.
