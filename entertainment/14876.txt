A video where actor Arjun Kapoor was shown slapping a Radio Mirchi RJ, was revealed as an April Fools' Day prank. Initially, the 30-year-old actor is shown reacting to the RJ's query on his choice of a 'girly' role for his film, 'Ki & Ka'. The film is scheduled to release on April 1, which coincides with April Fools' Day.
