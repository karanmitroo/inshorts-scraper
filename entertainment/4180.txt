'Aala Re Aala Ganesha', a new song from the Arjun Rampal starrer 'Daddy' has been released ahead of Ganesh Chaturthi, which begins on August 25. The song has been composed by Sajid Wajid with vocals by Wajid and lyrics by Prashant Ingole and Sajid. Based on the life of gangster-turned-politician Arun Gawli, 'Daddy' is scheduled to release on September 8.
