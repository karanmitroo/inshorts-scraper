Actor Farhan Akhtar has shared a picture with the original 'Magik' band from the 2008 film 'Rock On!!' featuring Arjun Rampal, Purab Kohli and Luke Kenny. Luke had played the character 'Rob Nancy', who dies at the end of the first film. The other three will be part of the upcoming film, 'Rock On 2', which also stars Shraddha Kapoor.
