The first look of Akshay Kumar and Siddharth Malhotra starrer film 'Brothers' is out. "Here’s the first look of my next film @Brothers2015 exclusively for you guys...", tweeted Akshay Kumar as he revealed the poster. Directed by Karan Malhotra, the film is an official remake of the 2011 Hollywood movie ‘Warrior’. The film also features Jacqueline Fernandez and Jackie Shroff.
