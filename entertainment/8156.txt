The trailer of the film 'Mukti Bhawan', which stars Adil Hussain and Lalit Behl, has been released. The film premiered at the Venice Film Festival under its English title 'Hotel Salvation' on September 2, 2016 and won the UNESCO XXIIIrd Prix 'Enrico Fulchignoni' at the fest. The film marks the feature film debut of director Shubhashish Bhutiani.
