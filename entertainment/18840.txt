Bollywood superstar Amitabh Bachchan has been announced as the leading man in R Balki's next ambitious project titled - 'Shamitabh'. "We are happy to announce that the film is titled "Shamitabh". As in SH before AMITABH," Balki said. Reportedly, the title combines the last alphabets from  Amitabh Bachchan's name with that of Dhanush, who also features in the movie.
