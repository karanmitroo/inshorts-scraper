The trailer for Ajay Devgn starrer 'Shivaay' was released on Sunday. Devgn plays the titular character described as "a Himalayan mountaineer who is an innocent everyman and yet is capable of transforming into a mean destroyer when he needs to protect his family". Directed by Devgn, 'Shivaay' also stars Sayyeshaa Saigal and Erika Kaar and will release on October 28.
