Lyricist-director Gulzar's unreleased film 'Libaas' is set to hit the theatres by the end of this year, 29 years after being made. The film, which was part of Directorate of Film Festivals archives till now, features actors Naseeruddin Shah and Shabana Azmi. The announcement was made by the film's distributor Zee Studios on the occasion of Gulzar's 83rd birthday.
