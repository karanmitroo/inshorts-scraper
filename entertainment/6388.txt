SS Rajamouli's 'Baahubali 2: The Conclusion' will be screened at the Cannes Film Festival on Sunday. 'Baahubali: The Beginning', the first instalment in the franchise, was screened at the festival on May 20. Baahubali 2 is the first Indian film to earn ₹1,500 crore worldwide. It is also the highest-ever grossing film in India, with earnings of over ₹900 crore.
