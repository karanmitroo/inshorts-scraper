Saif Ali Khan, Shahid Kapoor and Kangana Ranaut starrer 'Rangoon' will release on September 30, 2016, the filmmakers announced on Thursday. The Vishal Bhardwaj directorial, portraying a love triangle, is set against the backdrop of the Second World War. Notably, Bharadwaj's previous two released films, 'Haider' and 'Talvar', also hit the screens in the Gandhi Jayanti week.
