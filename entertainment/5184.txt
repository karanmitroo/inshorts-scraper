Singer Shreya Ghoshal released her first ever fully independent single Dhadkane Azad Hain on Monday. The song has been composed by Deepak Pandit with lyrics by Manoj Muntashir. Ghoshal, who has marked over 15 years in her music career in Bollywood, said, "I'm kind of re-discovering this whole process of making music out of the boundaries of a film."
