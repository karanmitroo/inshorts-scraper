Irrfan Khan-starrer 'Talvar' has managed to mint ₹9.25 crore at the box office in its opening weekend. The film earned ₹2.50 crore on Friday, ₹2.75 crore on Saturday and ₹3.50 crore on Sunday, tweeted trade analyst Taran Adarsh. Notably, 'Talvar' hit the screens alongside Akshay Kumar-starrer 'Singh is Bliing' which has earned ₹54.44 crore in its opening weekend.
