The trailer of filmmaker Vikram Bhatt's adult web series 'Maaya' has been released. The series will feature actors Shama Sikander, Vipul Gupta and Veer Aryan in lead roles. Shama will be seen playing the role of Maaya, who suffers from memory loss while Veer Aryan will portray her husband. The web series will release on YouTube on January 27.
