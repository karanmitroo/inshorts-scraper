'Disco Disco', the first song from the upcoming film 'A Gentleman- Sundar, Susheel, Risky', starring Sidharth Malhotra and Jacqueline Fernandez, has been released. It has been sung by Benny Dayal, Shirley Setia with lyrics by Vayu and its music composed by Sachin-Jigar. Directed by Krishna DK and Raj Nidimoru, the film is scheduled to release on August 25.
