Farhan Akhtar and his co-stars launched the new song 'Teen Kabootar' from their upcoming film 'Lucknow Central' before 4,000 inmates at the Yerwada Central Jail in Pune. The song's music has been made using natural sounds with the mouth and body, sounds from drums, plates and other objects. It's been composed by Arjunna Harjaie with rap verses by Raftaar.
