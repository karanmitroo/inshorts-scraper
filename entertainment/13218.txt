Dairy cooperative 'Amul' on Friday released a poster featuring actor Rajinikanth as the character from his recently released film 'Kabali'. The poster has been captioned “He makes onions cry, tomatoes blush & butter fry... Amul Utterly Kabali Delicious!” The film, which also stars actress Radhika Apte, was reportedly released in 12,000 screens across the world.
