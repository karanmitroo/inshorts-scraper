The trailer of the upcoming film Bareilly Ki Barfi, starring Kriti Sanon, Ayushmann Khurrana, and Rajkummar Rao, was released on Wednesday. It is reportedly based on a French book titled Ingredients of Love and is set in the town of Bareilly, UP. Directed by Ashwiny Iyer Tiwari, the film is scheduled to release on August 18.
