With the release of the film 'Annabelle: Creation', 'The Conjuring' series has earned over ₹6,400 crore ($1 billion) globally. 'Annabelle: Creation' has earned more than ₹700 crore ($110 million) in its first week at the global box-office. Directed by David Sandberg, it is the fourth instalment in 'The Conjuring' franchise.
