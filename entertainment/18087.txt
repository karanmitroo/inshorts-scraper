Evicted Bigg Boss 8 contestant Diandra Soares, who was rumoured to be pregnant, said, "I am not pregnant." Rubbishing the pregnancy rumours, the 35-yr-old model added, "Kissing does not lead to pregnancy." She was seen kissing co-contestant Gautam Gulati on the show. Her pregnancy rumours surfaced on social media after she was taken to the hospital from Bigg Boss house.
