Actress Shraddha Kapoor took to Twitter to share a picture of herself as the young and old version of Haseena Parkar, Dawood Ibrahim's late sister from the upcoming film 'Haseena'. The film will trace Haseena's life from her teenage to her 40s. Shraddha's brother Siddhant Kapoor will be seen playing the role of Dawood Ibrahim in the film.
