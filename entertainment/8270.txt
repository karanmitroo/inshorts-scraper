A dialogue promo titled 'Sorry Bhaiya' from the Taapsee Pannu starrer 'Naam Shabana' was released on Tuesday. The film is a prequel to the 2015 film 'Baby' with Taapsee reprising her role as 'Shabana' while Akshay Kumar features in an extended cameo appearance. Directed by Shivam Nair, the film is scheduled to release on March 31.
