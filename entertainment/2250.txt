'Mehbooba', the first song from Pulkit Samrat, Ali Fazal and Richa Chadha starrer upcoming film 'Fukrey Returns', has been released. It is a recreated version of Mohammed Rafi's 'O Meri Mehbooba' from the 1977 film 'Dharam Veer'. The new version has been sung by Neha Kakkar and Yasser Desai with music composed by Prem and Hardeep and rap by Raftaar.
