The official trailer of the Farhan Akhtar, Shraddha Kapoor and Arjun Rampal starrer film 'Rock On 2' has been released. Also starring Purab Kohli, Prachi Desai and Shashank Arora, the musical drama film is a sequel to the 2008 film 'Rock On'. Directed by Shujaat Saudagar, the film is scheduled to release on November 11.
