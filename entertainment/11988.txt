A new promotional video for the much awaited film 'M.S.Dhoni: The Untold Story' has been released. Titled 'Sushant Follows Mahi', the video features the film's actor Sushant Singh Rajput following cricketer Mahendra Singh Dhoni everywhere and imitating his actions. Directed by Neeraj Pandey, 'M.S.Dhoni: The Untold Story' will release on September 30.
