A video which explains how the visual effects (VFX) in SS Rajamouli's blockbuster film 'Baahubali' were created has gone viral on the internet. The video, created by Makuta Visual Effects, shares the VFX breakdown of some of the pivotal scenes of the movie. 'Baahubali' was released on July 10 and has minted over Rs 594 crores globally since then.
