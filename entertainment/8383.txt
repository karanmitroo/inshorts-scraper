'Roke Na Ruke Naina', a new song from the Alia Bhatt and Varun Dhawan starrer film 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania' has been released. The song has been composed by Amaal Mallik, with vocals by Arijit Singh, and lyrics penned by Kumaar. Directed by Shashank Khaitan, the romantic comedy film is scheduled to release on March 10.
