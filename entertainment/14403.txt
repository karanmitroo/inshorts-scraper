As per reports, actress Swara Bhaskar of 'Nil Battey Sannata' fame will star in the Sonam Kapoor starrer yet-to-be titled chick flick, a directorial venture of Sonam's sister Rhea Kapoor. The film has been tentatively titled 'Veeray Di Shaadi'. Meanwhile, Katrina Kaif and Huma Qureshi, who were both said to be part of the film, have reportedly opted out.
