Two Bollywood aspirants from Sweden, Hampus Bergqvist and Johan Bartoli, have recreated the iconic climax scene from the movie 'Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge' starring Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol. The duo, commonly known as '2 Foreigners In Bollywood', created the video using Snapchat's face-swap filter. Notably, they have also acted in movies like 'Banjo', 'Dheet Patangey', and 'Rangoon'.
