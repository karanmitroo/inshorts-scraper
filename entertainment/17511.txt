Actress Priyanka Chopra has congratulated Kangana Ranaut for winning the National Award for her work in 'Queen'. "I hate losing. But if I had to take a back seat to anyone this year it would be U #KanganaRanaut ! Congratulations!yay Queen!#SmallTownGirls," Priyanka tweeted. The former Miss World, was nominated in the same category for her role in 'Mary Kom'.
