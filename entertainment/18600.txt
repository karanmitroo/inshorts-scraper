Ahead of the trailer release on Aug-15, the makers of 'Happy New Year' have released a motion poster of the Shah Rukh Khan and Deepika Padukone starrer movie. Directed by Farah Khan, the movie, which is a musical heist, releases this Diwali. It also stars Abhishek Bachchan, Boman Irani, Sonu Sood and Vivaan Shah.
