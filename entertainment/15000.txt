Cricket Association of Bengal President Sourav Ganguly said that actor Amitabh Bachchan did not charge any money for his rendition of the national anthem ahead of the India-Pakistan cricket match on Saturday in Kolkata. Ganguly dismissed rumours that Bachchan had charged ₹4 crore for the performance. He added that Bachchan himself spent ₹30 lakh on bills and tickets to Kolkata.
