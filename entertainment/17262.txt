Upcoming movie 'Bombay Velvet' director Anurag Kashyap snapped at Kamal R Khan (KRK) after series of tweets where KRK said that he was offered Rs. 25 lakh for doing a good review for the movie and he has stopped reviewing as the film-makers of Bombay Velvet have threatened him. “You are just insignificant Mr KRK.”, Anurag tweeted as a reply.
