Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal on Monday took to Twitter to appreciate actor Ajay Devgn's latest film 'Drishyam'. "Watched "Drishyam". A must watch", Kejriwal tweeted. An emotional thriller, the film is about the life of a cable operator (played by Ajay Devgn) whose life revolves around his family. 'Drishyam' also stars actress Tabu as a cop.
