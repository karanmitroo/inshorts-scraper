Actor Hugh Jackman has confirmed that ‘The Wolverine 3’ would be his final appearance as the character he played in the ‘X-Men’ film franchise. ‘The Wolverine 3’ will be released in the US on March 3, 2017. However, it is still unclear whether he will make an appearance in ‘X-Men: Apocalypse’ which is scheduled for a May 27, 2016 release.
