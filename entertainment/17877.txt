Actress Raveena Tandon is no longer a part of Anurag Kashyap's much awaited film 'Bombay Velvet'. “I am no longer a part of ‘Bombay Velvet’... The whole scenario of movie has changed now. I had finished shooting my part too but there were a lot of changes in the sequences…", she said. Raveena added she is not angry with Anurag.
