Disney Pixar has released the teaser for 'Finding Dory', the sequel to its 2003 animated movie, 'Finding Nemo'. The sequel, set for a June 17, 2016 release, will centre around Dory (voiced by Ellen DeGeneres), the forgetful but helpful fish and the search for her family. In the original movie, Dory helps clownfish Marlin search for his son Nemo.
