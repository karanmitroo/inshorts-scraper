PM Narendra Modi has lauded Bollywood actor Hrithik Roshan for joining the "Swachh Bharat" mission. "Am sure you all will get inspired by Hrithik's efforts! He has made a significant effort towards creating a Swachh Bharat. MyCleanIndia," tweeted Modi. "Swachhbharat I started cleaning my own surroundings and learnt so much. Started with my lanes in Juhu", Hrithik had earlier tweeted.
