The upcoming musical drama, 'Banjo', is set to get a theatrical release on September 23 this year, as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. Director by Ravi Jadhav, the film stars Riteish Deshmukh and Nargis Fakhri in lead roles. While Deshmukh will portray the role of a Banjo player, Fakhri will be playing the role of a DJ in 'Banjo'.
