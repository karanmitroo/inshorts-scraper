Filmmaker Ram Gopal Varma has confirmed that actors Abhishek Bachchan and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan will not be a part of the third installment of the 'Sarkar' film franchise. Both of them had earlier starred in 'Sarkar Raj', the sequel to the 2005 film 'Sarkar'. The first look of the new film will be unveiled on August 26.
