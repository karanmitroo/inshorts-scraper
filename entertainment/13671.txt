Tennis player Serena Williams has appeared in the video for Beyoncé's new single 'Sorry'. Serena is seen twerking in the video, which has been directed by Beyoncé along with Kahlil Joseph. 'Sorry' is from Beyoncé's latest album 'Lemonade' and features the line "He better call Becky with the good hair", which reportedly refers to her husband Jay-Z cheating on her.
