The Chris Hemsworth starrer superhero film 'Thor: Ragnarok' is "one of Marvel's best movies," wrote Hindustan Times. The Indian Express wrote, "(The film) is primarily a comedy with some great action sequences sprinkled in at just the right moments," while Firstpost called it "an incredibly entertaining movie." The film was rated 4.5/5 (HT) and 4/5 (Indian Express, Firstpost).
