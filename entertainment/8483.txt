A video shared by Shilpa Shetty's husband Raj Kundra on Instagram shows their four-year-old-son Viaan enacting an acceptance speech holding the Oscars' trophy in his hand. Kundra captioned the video, "My son preparing...his oscar speech for #oscars2017 weekend." In the video, Viaan is heard saying, "I want to give this to my mum and dad so let me give it."
