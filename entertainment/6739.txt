SS Rajamouli-directed movie 'Baahubali 2: The Conclusion' has become the first ever Indian movie to achieve ₹1,000 crore earnings milestone. The movie, which earlier became India's biggest opener, has grossed more than  ₹800 crore in the country. It has beaten earning records of its own franchise movie 'Baahubali: The Beginning', and other box office hits like 'PK', 'Sultan' and 'Dangal'.
