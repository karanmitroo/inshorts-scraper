Aamir Khan's son Junaid will make his professional acting debut with Quasar Thakore Padamsee's play 'Mother Courage and Her Children', an adaptation of Bertolt Brecht's play. "Junaid is a very giving actor...He cares a lot about the whole production and not just his own part," said Padamsee. The play will be staged in Mumbai on August 19-20 and September 9-10.
