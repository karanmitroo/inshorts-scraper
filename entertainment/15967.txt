The first look of the film 'Tere Bin Laden: Dead or Alive' was released on Twitter on Wednesday. Directed by Abhishek Sharma, the film is a sequel to the 2010 film 'Tere Bin Laden', which featured Ali Zafar in the lead role. The sequel features Manish Paul along with Pradhuman Singh and will release on February 19, 2016.
