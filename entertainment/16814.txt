Bollywood superstar Shah Rukh Khan on Thursday shared the first teaser of his upcoming film 'Raees' on Twitter. In the film, SRK can be seen donning a pathani suit with glasses, kohl-rimmed eyes and a beard. The film, directed by Rahul Dholakia, also stars Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Pakistani actress Mahira Khan. 'Raees' is scheduled to release on Eid 2016.
