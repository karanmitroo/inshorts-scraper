Prior to the premiere of the popular television series Game of Thrones's sixth season, 'Game of Thrones Season 6' was one of the most trending Google searches in India on Sunday. It was most looked up in Mizoram, Goa and Uttarakhand. 'Sachin Tendulkar', 'Varun Dhawan' and 'Barcelona' were some of the other trending searches on the same day.
