Actor Bobby Deol made his debut as a child actor in the 1977 film 'Dharam Veer', where he played the younger version of his father Dharmendra. He went on to make his debut as a lead actor along with Twinkle Khanna in the 1995 film 'Barsaat'. Bobby, who turned 50 on Friday, won the Filmfare best debut award for 'Barsaat'.
