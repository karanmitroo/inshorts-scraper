As per reports, Anushka Sharma's father has gifted his son-in-law Indian cricket team captain Virat Kohli the book 'Smokes And Whiskey' by Tejaswini Divya Naik, which is a collection of 42 poems based on relationships. Anushka and Virat are reportedly lovers of poetry, and guests at their wedding had received an anthology of Rumi's poems as gifts.
