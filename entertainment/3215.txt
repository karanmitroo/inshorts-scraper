The original 'Judwaa' actors Salman Khan and Karisma Kapoor have been promoting the Varun Dhawan, Jacqueline Fernandez and Taapsee Pannu starrer sequel 'Judwaa 2'. Salman danced with Varun to the recreated version of 'Tan Tana Tan' song at IIFA Awards 2017. Varun also shared a video on social media, dancing with Karisma to the same song.
