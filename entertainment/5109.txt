Actor Tiger Shroff pays tribute to late singer Michael Jackson in 'Beparwah', a new song from the upcoming film 'Munna Michael'. The song has been composed by Gourov- Roshin, with vocals by Siddharth Basrur, Nandini Deb, and lyrics by Kumaar. Also starring Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Nidhhi Agerwal, the action dance film is scheduled to release on July 21.
