Akshay Kumar took to Twitter to share a fan's rap video on his upcoming film 'Toilet- Ek Prem Katha' and praised him for the video. The fan, named Abhishek Bhatt, wrote that he has made the video to "thank Mr Khiladi for always inspiring us". The film's plot is aligned with the principles of PM Narendra Modi's Swachh Bharat Abhiyaan.
