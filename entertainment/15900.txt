'Maula', the new song from Amitabh Bachchan and Farhan Akhtar starrer film 'Wazir' was released on YouTube on Friday. The devotional song has been composed by Shantanu Moitra and sung by Javed Ali. Directed by Bejoy Nambiar, 'Wazir' is an action thriller film also featuring Aditi Rao Hydari and Neil Nitin Mukesh. It is scheduled to release on January 8.
