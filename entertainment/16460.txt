Veteran actor Anupam Kher, who is visiting the US and Canada for his play 'Mera Woh Matlab Nahin Tha', has been honoured with a ‘Senate Proclamation’ by the state of Texas. Kher's play has been running successfully in Houston and 14 other US and Canadian cities. "I feel happy and humbled for such an amazing crowd and appreciation", Kher said.
