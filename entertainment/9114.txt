'Resident Evil: The Final Chapter', the sixth and final installment in the 'Resident Evil' film series, is "like being eaten to death by zombies", wrote Hindustan Times. "The movie feels confined to just a few intense sequences," wrote Times Of India while Mid-Day wrote, "Pace is relentless... action is never-ending." It was rated 1/5 (HT), 3/5 (TOI) and 2.5/5 (Mid-Day).
