The title track of Tiger Shroff's upcoming superhero film 'A Flying Jatt' has been released. The track has been composed by music composer duo Sachin-Jigar and sung by singers Raftaar, Mansheel Gujaral and Tanishkaa. The lyrics have been written by Vaayu and Raftaar. Also starring Jacqueline Fernandez in a lead role, the film will release on August 25.
