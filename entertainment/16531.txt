Praising Nawazuddin Siddiqui's latest film 'Manjhi- The Mountain Man', actor-politician Shatrughan Sinha has called Siddiqui "the discovery of the century". "The earthy "dynamite" @Nawazuddin_S is the discovery of the century. @radhika_apte is lovely and laudable. Wish roaring success," Sinha tweeted. Further, he called the film "a dynamic, realistic and timely film of Bihari pride".
