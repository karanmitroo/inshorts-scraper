Actor Shahid Kapoor turned showstopper for designer Gaurav Gupta, who launched his inaugural luxury menswear line, on the second day of Van Heusen + GQ Fashion Nights. Titled 'Diamond Demons', the designer's line was inspired by constellations, stars and space. Actresses Deepika Padukone and Saiyami Kher were among the attendees at Gaurav's show.
