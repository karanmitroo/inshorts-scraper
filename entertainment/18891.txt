Mahesh Bhatt, who has earned huge acclaim in Bollywood as a director, scriptwriter and producer, is all set to make a debut as an actor in Mukul Misra's 'Siddhartha'. Mahesh, who plays Lama, took to Twitter to give a sneak peek into his new look. As reports suggest, the film also features actor Shivam Bhargav and Shazahn Padamsee.
