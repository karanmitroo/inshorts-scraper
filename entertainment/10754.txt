Fashion at Big Bazaar (FBB) has set the Guinness World Record for the World's Largest Photobook through 'The Denim Dance' campaign. 'The Denim Dance' photobook, which is 18 feet tall and 27 feet wide, contains over 8,000 pictures of people posing in Denim Dance's signature step. Notably, choreographer Terence Lewis had created 'The Denim Dance' anthem with Radiocity and Bindaas.
