Police personnel have been posted outside Deepika Padukone's parents' house in Bengaluru following threats over her film 'Padmavati'. Karnataka's CM Siddaramaiah had earlier said the state stands by her amid the controversy. The state's Home Minister Ramalinga Reddy also tweeted, "I have personally written to the DG-IGP of Karnataka to provide maximum security to @deepikapadukone and her family."
