The hospitality manager of IPL team KXIP Tara Sharma has confirmed the spat between actress Preity Zinta against her ex-boyfriend Ness Wadia to the Mumbai Police. Police have so far recorded statements of 12 witnesses. In her complaint on Jun-12, Zinta had accused Wadia of molesting, abusing and threatening her during the IPL encounter between KXIP and CSK on May-30.
