Bollywood actor Shahid Kapoor (35) tied the knot with Delhi girl Mira Rajput (21) on Tuesday at a farmhouse in Chhatarpur, Delhi. As per reports, the wedding ceremony took place at 11am and was attended by 30-40 people that included family members and close friends. The wedding will reportedly be followed by an evening reception funtion at The Oberoi, Gurgaon.
