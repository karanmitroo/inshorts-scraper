The Sushant Singh Rajput starrer 'MS Dhoni: The Untold Story', by earning ₹66 crore, has become the second highest opening weekend grosser of 2016 after Salman Khan's 'Sultan', as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. The film, also the biggest opener in the biopic genre, collected ₹21.30 crore on Friday, ₹20.60 crore on Saturday and ₹24.10 crore on Sunday.
