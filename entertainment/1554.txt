The Mark Hamill, late actress Carrie Fisher and Daisy Ridley starrer 'Star Wars: The Last Jedi' has minted $450 million (over ₹2890 crore) worldwide to set the record for the fifth-biggest global weekend opening for a film ever. By earning $220 million (over ₹1400 crore) in North America, the film also became the second-highest weekend grosser in the domestic market.
