'Chal Tu Apna Kaam Kar', the first song from the Rajkummar Rao starrer 'Newton' has been released. The makers have described the song as "a shout out to India to get going". The track has been sung by Amit Trivedi, composed by Rachita Arora with lyrics by Irshad Kamil. Directed by Amit Masurkar, the film will release on September 22.
