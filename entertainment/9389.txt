The first poster of 'Irada', starring Naseeruddin Shah and Arshad Warsi in lead roles has been unveiled. Also starring Divya Dutta, the film has been directed by Aparnaa Singh. Arshad Warsi tweeted his poster from the film with the caption, "#Irada accha ho yeh zaruri nahi bus saccha hona chahiye!" The film is scheduled to release on February 17.
