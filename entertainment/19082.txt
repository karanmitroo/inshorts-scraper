Filmmaker Sanjay Leela bhansali has planned to revive his dream project "Bajirao Mastani" by signing Deepika Padukone for the role of 'Mastani' and is considering Hrithik Roshan for the role of 'Bajirao'. BM is based on the love story of the Maratha general Peshwa Bajirao and his Muslim-origin wife Mastani, who faced the wrath of the society on their marriage.
