As per reports, Deepika Padukone will play poetess Amrita Pritam opposite Abhishek Bachchan, who will reportedly play poet-lyricist Sahir Ludhianvi, in an upcoming biopic on the poet. The biopic, tentatively titled 'Gustakhiyan', is said to revolve around the love story of Ludhianvi and Pritam. The film will be produced by Sanjay Leela Bhansali and directed by debutante Jasmeet Reen.
