The trailer of the Ricky Gervais starrer 'David Brent: Life on the Road', was released on Friday.  In this mockumentary comedy, Gervais reprises his character 'David Brent', from the television series 'The Office', who is travelling with a film crew to record his self-financed UK tour with his band, 'Foregone Conclusion'. The film is scheduled to release on August 19.
