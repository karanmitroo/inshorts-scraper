Actor Sushant Singh Rajput is set to star in a space film called 'Chanda Mama Door Ke'. "I have never seen a Bollywood space film before," said Sushant. He added that his other upcoming projects include 'Raabta', a Dharma film with Jacqueline Fernandes, a biopic on an athlete and a film with Homi Adajania, also featuring Parineeti Chopra, titled 'Takadum'.
