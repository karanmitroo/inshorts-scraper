The trailer of Ranbir Kapoor and Deepika Padukone starrer 'Tamasha' was released on Tuesday. Set in France, the trailer gives a glimpse of how Ved Sahni (Ranbir) meets Tara Maheshwari (Deepika). Directed by Imtiaz Ali, the music of the film has been composed by AR Rahman. A nomadic love-story, 'Tamasha' is scheduled to release on November 27.
