Actress Katrina Kaif, in a pink off-shoulder gown by Oscar de la Renta from Le Mill, was among the celebrities who attended the Absolut Elyx Filmfare Glamour & Style Awards 2016. Alia Bhatt chose a gown by Ulyana Sergeenko while Aishwarya Rai Bachchan chose a gown from LaBourjoisie. Meanwhile, Kajol wore a black and white gown by designer Nikhil Thampi.
