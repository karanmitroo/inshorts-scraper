The Hindi version of SS Rajamouli's 'Baahubali 2: The Conclusion' has become the first film in Hindi to earn over ₹400 crore at the Indian box office. According to trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film posted earnings of ₹432.8 crore since its release. Previously, Aamir Khan's 'Dangal' was the highest grossing Hindi film, with earnings of ₹387.38 crore.
