As per reports, actor Hrithik Roshan will be starring as the male lead opposite actress Deepika Padukone in filmmaker Sanjay Leela Bhansali's 'Padmavati'. Bhansali is said to have been 'hurt' when his first choice Ranveer Singh asked for the film's narration. The filmmaker also reportedly approached Shah Rukh Khan, who could not commit 200 days for Padmavati's shoot.
