Actors starring in television series 'The Big Bang Theory' and 'Game of Thrones' were among the highest paid actors in television in the US, as per Variety magazine. 'The Big Bang Theory' lead stars, including Kaley Cuoco and Jim Parsons, earned $900,000 per episode. Five actors from 'Game of Thrones', including Emilia Clarke and Kit Harington, earned $500,000 per episode.
