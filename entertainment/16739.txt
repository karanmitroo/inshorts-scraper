Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol, who are currently shooting for Rohit Shetty's upcoming film "Dilwale", recreated a scene of their 1995 blockbuster "Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge" on the Dilwale film set. The photograph of the scene was shared by SRK on Twitter late on Wednesday and captioned "Dilwale on Black Sea...revisited". The film is scheduled to release in December.
