The Akshay Kumar starrer 'Housefull 3' has collected ₹53.31 crore in its opening weekend in India, making it the highest opening weekend grosser of 2016, Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh revealed. The film overtook the Shah Rukh Khan starrer 'Fan', which minted ₹52.35 crore, and Akshay Kumar's 'Airlift' which collected ₹44.30 crore in the opening weekend.
