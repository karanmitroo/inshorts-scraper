The 'Victoria-Jungfrau Grand Hotel & Spa' in the Interlaken town of Switzerland has a suite named after late film director Yash Chopra, who served as the Ambassador of Interlaken. The cinema-themed 'Yash Chopra Suite' has posters of his film 'Veer-Zaara' and a Ganesha sculpture. A Jungfrau Railways mountain train in Switzerland is also named after Chopra.
