An instrumental rendition of the patriotic song 'Saare Jahaan Se Accha' by veteran flute player Pandit Hariprasad Chaurasia has been released. "[T]his song perfectly encapsulates... what it means to be an Indian," said Chaurasia while adding that it is perfect for Independence​ Day. Singer Shaan’s younger son Shubh has also sung a few lines from the song towards​ the end.
