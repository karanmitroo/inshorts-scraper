The new poster for the Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor starrer 'Befikre' was revealed on Saturday. "Befikre vibes on Cannes beach!", tweeted Yash Raj Films while unveiling the fourth poster for the film. Directed by Aditya Chopra, the film is scheduled to release on December 9, 2016, which marks the first birthday of Adira, Aditya's daughter with wife Rani Mukerji.
