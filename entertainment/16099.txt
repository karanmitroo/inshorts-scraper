Veteran actor Saeed Jaffrey has passed away at the age of 86 after struggling with cancer for months, his niece announced on Sunday. Jaffrey is known for his roles in Hindi and British films which include Satyajit Ray’s "Shatranj Ke Khiladi", "Masoom" and Richard Attenborough’s Oscar-winning “Gandhi”. He had also founded the Unity Theatre, which toured Shakespeare’s play across India.
