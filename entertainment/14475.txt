Sonakshi Sinha will be the female lead in 'Namastey England', as per filmmaker Vipul Shah. The 28-year-old actress will be starring opposite Akshay Kumar in what Shah calls "a never-seen-before avatar" of Sonakshi. Shah clarified that the film is not a sequel or continuation of his 2007 film 'Namastey London', which starred Akshay Kumar and Katrina Kaif.
