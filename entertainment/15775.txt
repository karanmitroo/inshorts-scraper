'Ghazab Ka Hai Ye Din' song from the Pulkit Samrat and Yami Gautam starrer film 'Sanam Re' was YouTube's fourth most shared video on Facebook and Twitter in India on Saturday. It has been sung by Arijit Singh and Amaal Mallik. Released on the video-sharing website on Friday, the song has garnered over 6.38 lakh views so far.
