Actresses Jacqueline Fernandez, Deepika Padukone, Rani Mukerji and Sonakshi Sinha were listed as ration card holders in Uttar Pradesh's Sahabgang village. This came to light after the villagers filed a complaint against the distributor and a probe was ordered. Fernandez, Padukone, Mukerji and Sinha's husbands as listed on the cards were Sadhu Lal, Rakesh Chand, Ram Swaroop and Ramesh Chand.
