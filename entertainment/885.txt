Lokendra Singh Kalvi, convenor of Rajput group Karni Sena, has said that they are ready to watch 'Padmaavat'. He added that they will decide who watches the film for it to be approved for an all-India release. Talking about Sanjay Leela Bhansali's invitation letter, Kalvi said, "Bhansali may be expecting that we will refuse, but we are accepting his offer."
