Friday's release light hearted rom-com Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania, which received mixed reviews, saw a very good response and collected Rs 9.02 crore nett. This collection for Varun Dhawan's film is the best as his Main Tera Hero was Rs 6.60 crore on the opening day. The film, which  released on 1500 screens, was the single release with no competitor.
