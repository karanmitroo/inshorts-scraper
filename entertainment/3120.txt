Varun Dhawan and Anushka Sharma, who will star in the upcoming film 'Sui Dhaaga - Made In India', shared a video paying tribute to Mahatma Gandhi on his 148th birth anniversary on the occasion of Gandhi Jayanti. Describing Gandhi, they said, "Ek aisa insaan jo bana lauh se, aag se, desh ke liye bana, desh se bana."
