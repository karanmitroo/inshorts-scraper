Comedy group AIB has released a new song 'The 2017 Song #PeekeMatChala'. Sharing the new song, AIB tweeted, "2016 maybe the worst, but we predict 2017 will be awesome." In the video, AIB has used the tune of the song 'Badtameez Dil' while talking about trending topics of 2016 like Donald Trump as US President, demonetisation and 'Sonam Gupta bewafa'.
