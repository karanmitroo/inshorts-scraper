The trailer of the Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor starrer 'Befikre' will be launched at an event at the Eiffel Tower in Paris on October 10.  "For the first time, a film event will be held there," tweeted Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. Yash Raj Films stated that this first of its kind evening, salutes "a fresh... youthful love story".
