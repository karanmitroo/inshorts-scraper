Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol starrer 'Dilwale' has left behind Ranveer Singh, Priyanka Chopra and Deepika Padukone starrer 'Bajirao Mastani' at the box office in the opening weekend. While 'Dilwale' earned ₹65.09 crore within three days of its release, 'Bajirao Mastani' grossed ₹46.77 crore, according to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh.
