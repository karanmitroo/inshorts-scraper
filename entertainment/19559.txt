Kareena Kapoor Khan will be the first Indian actress to build a six pack for her next film  'Shuddhi', which is a romantic saga  opposite Hritik Roshan. She will be training in martial arts for the same after she is done promoting 'Gori Tere Pyaar Mein'. The film 'Shuddhi', directed by Karan Malhotra, goes on floor in December.
