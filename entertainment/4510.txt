'Mr. Kabaadi', said to be the last film of veteran actor Om Puri, who passed away in January this year, will release on August 25. The satirical comedy, which also stars Annu Kapoor, Vinay Pathak and Sarika, has been directed by Om Puri's first wife Seema Kapoor. The veteran actor was last seen onscreen posthumously in Salman Khan's 'Tubelight'.
