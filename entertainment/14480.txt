Actress Sonakshi Sinha has featured on the cover of Harper's Bazaar Bride for its May issue as a "boho bride". Posing with British model Stephen James, Sonakshi is seen in an Anita Dongre ensemble. Her look was accessorised using unconventional headgear, gold cuffs and a nose ring. "I think the look is very contemporary," said Sonakshi about her look.
