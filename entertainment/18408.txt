The official trailer of ‘Ungli’ movie, produced by Hiroo Yash Johar & Karan Johar, has been released. Starring Emraan Hashmi, Kangana Ranaut, Randeep Hooda, Neil Bhoopalam, Angad Bedi and Sanjay Dutt, the film is a story of five friends who start a fight against corruption. The film is directed by Rensil D'Silva and is slated to release on Nov 28.
