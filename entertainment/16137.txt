An acoustic guitar used by 'Beatles' musician, late John Lennon was sold on Saturday to an undisclosed buyer for $2.41mn in Beverly Hills, California. 'The 1962 J-160E Gibson acoustic guitar', once stolen from Lennon in 1963 was used by him in writing and recording songs like 'Love Me Do', 'I Want to Hold Your Hand', 'All My Loving' among others.
