Bollywood actress Deepika Padukone has emerged as the most followed Indian on photo-sharing application Instagram, with 4.5 million followers. Stars Alia Bhatt and Priyanka Chopra are a close second and third with 4.1 million and 4 million followers respectively. Shraddha Kapoor ranks fourth with 3.6 million followers, while Sonam Kapoor ranks fifth with 3.3 million followers.
