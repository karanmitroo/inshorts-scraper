Veteran film-maker Dev Benegal's next film, Bombay Samurai, might see actor Hrithik Roshan in a cameo. The production house, Excel Entertainment has shown keen interest in having him in the movie and have reportedly asked Benegal to create a role for him. The film, otherwise, has some big stars like Kareena Kapoor, Farhan Akhtar, Abhay Deol and Kalki Koechlin.
