Jr NTR and Mohanlal-starrer Telugu action-drama film ‘Janatha Garage’, which released on Thursday, has earned around ₹21 crore from Andhra Pradesh and Telangana on its opening day, as per trade analyst Trinath. The film, which also stars Nithya Menen and Samantha Ruth Prabhu, was reportedly made on a budget of ₹50 crore.
