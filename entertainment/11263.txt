Toy company Lego has launched a 550-piece 'The Beatles' Yellow Submarine' set, inspired by a 1968 musical fantasy film of the same name. The pack includes models of Beatles' band members John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison and Ringo Starr, along with Jeremy Hillary, a character from the film 'Yellow Submarine'. The set is priced at £49.99 (approximately ₹4,100).
