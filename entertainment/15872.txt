Maharashtra Minister Pankaja Munde on Sunday expressed objection to a Marathi biopic 'Sangharshyatra', which is based on the life of late Gopinath Munde, her father. Munde claimed no permission was taken from the family by the Producer, Sanjay Singh Suryawanshi, before making the biopic. However, Munde said she has no objection over the movie's release.
