'Raag Desh', a film based on the 1945-1946 Red Fort trials, "is a heavy dose of patriotism...totally worth your time", wrote Hindustan Times. NDTV wrote the film "reminds about the sacrifices of brave men and women" while Times of India said that the film "educates about the socio-political climate of that time". It was rated 3.5/5 (HT), 3/5 (NDTV, TOI).
