Actor Amitabh Bachchan, who took to Twitter to praise Shah Rukh Khan's performance in 'Raees', said that he loved the actor's anger in the film. "Congratulations Shahrukh... RAEES... loved your anger in it!!," wrote Amitabh. Responding to this, SRK tweeted, "Aap se hi seekha hai sir". Bachchan further wrote that some moments in the film were "electric".
