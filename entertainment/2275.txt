Television series 'Khichdi' is set to return with its third season and will star the original members of the cast including Anang Desai, Rajeev Mehta, Supriya Pathak, Vandana Pathak and JD Majethia. Actress Renuka Shahane will also make a cameo appearance in the new season. 'Khichdi', which first aired in 2002, revolved around a Gujarati joint family.
