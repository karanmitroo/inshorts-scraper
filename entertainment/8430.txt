The trailer of the Amitabh Bachchan, Jackie Shroff, Yami Gautam and Manoj Bajpayee starrer 'Sarkar 3' was released on Wednesday. Bachchan reprises his role as 'Subhash Nagre'. Also starring Ronit Roy and Amit Sadh, the film is the third instalment in the 'Sarkar' film franchise. Directed by Ram Gopal Varma, the film is scheduled to release on April 7.
