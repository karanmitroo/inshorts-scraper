The Aamir Khan and Asin-starrer 2008 film 'Ghajini' was the first Bollywood film to mint ₹100 crore in India. The action psychological thriller went on to collect around ₹160 crore in its lifetime. Furthermore, Aamir's '3 Idiots' (2009) was the first Bollywood film to enter the ₹200 crore club, while his 'PK' (2014) was the first to mint ₹300 crore.
