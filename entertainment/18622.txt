Upcoming YRF banner movie 'Daawat-e-Ishq', which was earlier scheduled to release on Sep-5, wil now hit the screens on Sep-19. The date has been revised to allot more time to a unique marketing idea that is being adopted by the production house. The film is directed by Aditya Chopra, and has Parineeti Chopra and Aditya Roy Kapur in the lead.
