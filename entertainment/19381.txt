Actress Madhuri Dixit is back on screen with enthralling dance moves in her latest song, "Hamari Atariya", from 'Dedh Ishqiya'. The song is penned down by legendary lyricist Gulzar, and is sung by Rekha Bharadwaj. Dedh Ishqiya, which is sequel to 2010 film Ishqiya, stars Madhuri Dixit Nene, Huma Qureshi, Arshad Warsi, and Naseeruddin Shah in the lead roles.
