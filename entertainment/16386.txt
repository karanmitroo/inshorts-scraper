Rajat Bansal, a Lucknow lawyer, has filed an FIR against Ranbir Kapoor and Farhan Akhtar for criminal breach of trust and forgery for promoting the online shopping site 'askmebazaar'. Bansal has accused the website of "duping" customers after it failed to deliver him the product he had ordered. The directors of the e-commerce site were also named in the FIR.
