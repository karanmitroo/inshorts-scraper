Italian fashion house Dolce & Gabbana launched its maiden collection of hijabs and abayas on fashion website Style.com's Arabia edition. Targeted mainly at its Middle East customers, the collection features 14 garments in black and sandy hues. Jewel-encrusted lemons, black lace trim, oversized daisies add to the sheer georgette and satin weave charmeuse fabrics that make up this unique collection.
