Yash Raj Films has introduced 'Shyra Gill', the character that Vaani Kapoor portrays in the upcoming film 'Befikre', in a series of new promotional videos called, 'Shyra From Paris'. The fourth episode in the promos shows Shyra, who is a professional tour guide in Paris, dancing at various tourist locations in Paris. 'Befikre' is scheduled to release on December 9.
