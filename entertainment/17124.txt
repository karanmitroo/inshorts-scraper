Sudha Shivpuri, who played the role of 'Baa' in the popular Indian daily soap 'Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi', passed away on Wednesday morning at the age of 78. As per reports, Shivpuri was not keeping well ever since she suffered a heart stroke on December 2, 2013. She made her film debut with Basu Chatterjee's Swami in 1977.
