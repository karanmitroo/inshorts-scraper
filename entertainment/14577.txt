Rashtriya Janata Dal (RJD) chief Lalu Prasad Yadav's eldest son, Tej Pratap Yadav, will be seen in a Bhojpuri film titled 'Apharan Udyog'. As per reports, the 27-year-old, who is also the health minister in the current Bihar government, will be playing the role of a chief minister in the film wherein Bhojpuri actor Chhotu Chhalia plays the lead.
