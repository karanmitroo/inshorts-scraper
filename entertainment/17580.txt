Taking cues here-and-there from JC Chandor's All Is Lost, Ang Lee's Life of Pi, Angelina Jolie's Unbroken and Alfred Hitchcock's Life Boat, Brian Falk's Against the Sun is a 34-day ordeal of survival and endurance, writes NDTV. The movie is based on a true incident about three US Naval Air Force men. It has got 2.5/5 (NDTV) and 3.5/5 (TOI).
