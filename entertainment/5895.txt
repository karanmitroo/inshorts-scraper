Actor Shah Rukh Khan responded to a Twitter user called Sejal Badala, after she "thanked" him for making her a meme. This happened after the posters and title of Shah Rukh's upcoming film 'Jab Harry Met Sejal' were unveiled. Shah Rukh apologised to her and tweeted, "Hope u like my next film title better..it's called Badala oops Badle ki Aag."
