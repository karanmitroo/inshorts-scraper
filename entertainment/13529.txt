Bollywood actor Ranveer Singh and South Indian actor Dulquer Salmaan have featured on GQ's list of '50 Most Influential Young Indians: Next Gen'. TVF's Arunabh Kumar and Biswapati Sarkar, singer Benny Dayal and political strategist Prashant Kishor have also featured in the list. "These are men who have a new vision for India", GQ Editor Che Kurrien said.
