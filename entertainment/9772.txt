Actress Sanya Malhotra, who featured in 'Dangal', has shared a video wherein she imitates Shah Rukh Khan's signature arms-spread pose in the song 'Mitwa' from 'Kabhi Alvida Naa Kehna'. The video was captioned, "Shot by our very own human jib Mr @niteshtiwari22 and of course world-class editing by me." Sanya played wrestler Babita Phogat in the Nitesh Tiwari directed 'Dangal'.
