Amitabh Bachchan sent a handwritten note to Rajkummar Rao telling him that he has been an admirer of his work for a long time while praising him for his performance in 'Bareilly Ki Barfi'. Sharing a picture of the note and bouquet on Twitter, Rajkummar wrote, "When...legend sends you this handwritten note...You've made my day. Much regards & charan sparsh."
