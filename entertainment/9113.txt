Television actress Kritika Kamra's look for 'Chandrakanta- Prem ya Paheli', a reboot of the 90s television serial by the same name, has been revealed. The serial, based on Devaki Nandan Khatri's novel, will air on the channel 'Life OK' starting from the first week of March. Notably, 'Colors' channel is also planning a serial produced by Balaji Telefilms on 'Chandrakanta'.
