Amitabh Bachchan and Farhan Akhtar starrer 'Wazir' collected a total of ₹12.73 crore across the nation in its first two days at the box office. The film reportedly grossed ₹7.36 crore overseas, bringing up the overall collection to ₹20.09 crore. Trade analyst Taran Adarsh said that the film "picked up at premium plexes." Wazir has mostly garnered positive reviews.
