'Shola Jo Bhadke', the first song from Vidya Balan's debut Marathi film 'Ekk Albela' has been released. The song, also featuring Marathi actor Mangesh Desai is a revamped version of the song by the same name in the 1951 film 'Albela'. 'Ekk Albela' is a biopic of Indian actor Bhagwan Dada and is scheduled to release on June 24.
