As per reports, Income-tax Appellate Tribunal (ITAT) has asked actress Priyanka Chopra to include a ₹40 lakh watch and a ₹27 lakh Toyota Prius car she received as gifts as her taxable income. Priyanka had reportedly shown the watch and car as gifts by companies. The ITAT order is said to cover four years from the financial year 2006-07 onwards.
