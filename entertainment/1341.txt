Vidya Balan has said that she has no time for a baby while adding, "Each film I do is a new baby; (so) I have 20-odd babies." She further said, "Right now, I'm focused on doing more and good work." The actress got married in 2012 to Siddharth Roy Kapur, known for co-producing films like 'Jagga Jasoos' and 'Dangal'.
