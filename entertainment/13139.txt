The fantasy adventure film 'The BFG', directed by Steven Spielberg, which released today, is "very much a minor Spielberg film", wrote Hindustan Times. India Today called it "all very forgettable" while Mid-Day wrote that "giants, dreams and sweeping landscape make it (the film) engaging". The film has been rated 2.5/5 (India Today, Mid-Day) and 3/5 (Hindustan Times).
