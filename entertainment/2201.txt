'An Insignificant Man', a documentary on Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal, "does not show any intra-party squabbling that we haven't watched on news channels," wrote Hindustan Times (HT). "'An Insignificant Man' is invaluable and fascinating," said Firstpost while Times Now called it "an engaging watch for new-age documentary lovers". It was rated 3/5 (HT), 3.5/5 (Times Now) and 4/5 (Firstpost).
