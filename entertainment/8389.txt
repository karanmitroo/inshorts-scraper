The Hugh Jackman starrer superhero film 'Logan' "changes the face of superhero movies forever", wrote Hindustan Times. "[T]he highlight of the film is Jackman's compelling performance," said The Times of India. Mid-Day wrote that "this is Jackman's best performance to date". The film has been rated 3.5/5 (Mid-Day), 4/5 (TOI) and 4.5/5 (HT).
