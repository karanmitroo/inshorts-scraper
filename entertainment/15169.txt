Priyanka Chopra's first look from upcoming Hollywood film 'Baywatch' was on Wednesday revealed on Twitter by the movie's screenwriters, Damian Shannon and Mark Swift. "Love this pic of @priyankachopra. Can't wait to see her bring Victoria to life. And those shoes are fire! #baywatch," they captioned the photograph. 'Baywatch' is a film adaptation of the 1990s American TV series.
