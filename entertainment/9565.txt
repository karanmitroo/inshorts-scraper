Actress Priyanka Chopra, in a peach-coloured outfit by designer Sally LaPointe, was among the celebrities who attended the People's Choice Awards 2017. Jennifer Lopez was seen in a black dress by Reem Acra. Blake Lively wore a black frilly dress by Lebanese fashion designer Elie Saab. Meanwhile, Sofia Vergara chose a gold and white knee-length outfit from design house Marchesa.
