Jharkhand police have made a movie on Maoists titled 'Pratyavartan-The Homecoming' featuring more than a dozen real-life policemen. Directed by Nimu Bhaumik, the movie "aims to showcase the true picture of the Maoist movement". The over 2 hours-long Hindi feature film, which is the brainchild of former DGP Rajiv Kumar, is set to release on Friday.
