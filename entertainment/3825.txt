Ajay Devgn has said nobody from the film industry has boycotted Kapil Sharma's show. He said this following reports of many actors including himself, Shah Rukh Khan and Anil Kapoor leaving Kapil's show without shooting. "We ('Baadshaho' cast) left as he had not reached...sets till then....When I'll talk to him next, we'll get to know what was wrong," added Ajay.
