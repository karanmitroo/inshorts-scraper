'Jolly Good Fellow', a new song from the Akshay Kumar starrer film 'Jolly LLB 2' has been released. The song has been sung and composed by Meet Brothers- Manmeet Singh and Harmeet Singh, with lyrics by Shabbir Ahmed. Also starring Huma Qureshi and Saurabh Shukla, the court drama film is scheduled to release on February 10, 2017.
