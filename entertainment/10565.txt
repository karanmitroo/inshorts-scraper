Veteran actor Dharmendra, who worked with late Tamil Nadu Chief Minister Jayalalithaa in the 1968 film 'Izzat', has said that she was "very dignified, graceful and quiet" on the film's sets. "She would bring delicious South Indian food for all of us," he added. Dharmendra further said that he was "very proud" to see how far Jayalalithaa went in life.
