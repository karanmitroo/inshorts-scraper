Actress Alia Bhatt will dance on the song 'Balam Pichkari' from Deepika Padukone and Ranbir Kapoor's 2013 film 'Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani' at Zee Cine Awards. The song is a part of a medley to which Alia will perform as a tribute to filmmaker Karan Johar. A video of Alia rehearsing on the song has been shared on social media.
