Actor Irrfan Khan, born as Sahabzade Irrfan Ali Khan in Rajasthan, turned 48 years old on Thursday. Beginning his career with television serials like 'Chandrakanta', Irrfan's Bollywood debut as a lead was 'Rog' (2005). Critically acclaimed for his versatile acting skills, some of Padma Shri Irrfan's best films are 'Paan Singh Tomar', 'Maqbool', 'Life of Pi' and 'The Namesake'.
