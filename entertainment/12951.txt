Actor Farhan Akhtar, on the occasion of 'Friendship Day' on Sunday, posted a video of him singing to wish his fans and friends. The actor chose to sing an acoustic version of the song 'Atrangi Yaari' from his film 'Wazir'. Farhan will be performing at the multi-city music festival 'NH7 Weekender', starting from Shillong on October 21-22.
