Actor Salman Khan has become Bollywood's highest advance tax payer for the financial year 2015-2016, according to Income Tax department's data. Salman, who has paid ₹20 crore as advance tax, is followed by Akshay Kumar (₹16 crore), Ranbir Kapoor (₹15 crore) and Shah Rukh Khan (₹14 crore). Notably, Akshay was the highest advance tax payer in the previous fiscal year.
