The new poster of the upcoming film 'PadMan' shows Akshay Kumar's on-screen wife Radhika Apte. While sharing the poster on his social media, Akshay wrote, "My wife also asking when trailer coming?" The R Balki directorial is based on the life of Arunachalam Muruganantham who invented a low-cost sanitary pad-making machine, and is scheduled to release on January 26, 2018.
