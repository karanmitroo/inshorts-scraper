The official trailer of '1982 A Love Marriage', a Prashant M Gorey directorial, was released on YouTube on Thursday. The film features newcomers Amitkumar Sharma and Omna Harjani among others. Produced under the banner 'Shuddh Paarivarik Chalchitra', it is a comedy film with the backdrop of arranged marriage versus love marriage. It is scheduled to release on February 26.
