Actress Shraddha Kapoor shared her brother Siddhant Kapoor's look as Dawood Ibrahim from the upcoming film 'Haseena: The Queen of Mumbai'. "SO proud to introduce my real AND reel life brother," read Shraddha's caption. The picture shows Siddhant as the young and old version of Dawood Ibrahim. Shraddha will be seen playing Dawood's late sister Haseena Parkar in the film.
