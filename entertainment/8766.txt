The Taapsee Pannu and Amit Sadh starrer 'Running Shaadi', which released on Friday, "is great in patches...", wrote India Today. Hindustan Times wrote, "The film has its moments, but they're too few to keep you engrossed", while Times of India said, "The story is funny...the performances up to the mark." It was rated 3/5 (India Today, TOI) and 2/5 (HT).
