On Tuesday, Chennai Express got a massive advantage of the Raksha Bandhan and surpassed Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani's total to become the third highest grosser ever in the history of Bollywood, only behind 3 Idiots and Ek Tha Tiger. With the film's domestic total at Rs 192 Crore, the film is even expected to surpass 3 Idiots' Rs.202 Crore mark.
