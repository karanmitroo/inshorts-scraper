The trailer of the upcoming Rajkummar Rao and Kriti Kharbanda starrer 'Shaadi Mein Zaroor Aana' has been released. Talking about the film, Rajkummar said, "It isn't exactly like the earlier romcom, but as the story progresses, something happens and this guy I'm playing transforms completely." Directed by Ratnaa Sinha, the film is scheduled to release on November 10.
