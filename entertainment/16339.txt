Director Prakash Jha has confirmed that he will be seen acting in his upcoming directorial 'Jai Gangaajal', which stars Priyanka Chopra in the role of a police officer. “I have done a role in the film. It’s the role of a cop. In the film, I’m one of Priyanka’s subordinates,” Jha said. The film will release on March 4, 2016.
