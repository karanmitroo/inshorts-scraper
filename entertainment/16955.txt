Shraddha Kapoor and Varun Dhawan-starrer 'ABCD 2', based on a real-life dance group from Nala Sopara who represented India on International level, has done a business of Rs 28.81 crore in just two days of its release. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film minted Rs 14.30 crore on Friday and Rs 14.51 crore on Saturday.
