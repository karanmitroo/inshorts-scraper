Aditya Chopra on Sunday announced that he will be returning to film direction after seven years with his new venture 'Befikre'. Breaking the news on his father Late Yash Chopra's 83rd birthday, he said, “It’s my happiest film. My youngest film. My riskiest film". Aditya's last film was 'Rab Ne Bana Di Jodi' in 2008.
