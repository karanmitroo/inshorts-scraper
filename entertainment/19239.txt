The watchmen outside actress Anushka Sharma's residence have disclosed that cricketer Virat Kohli has been living in Anushka's pad since he came back from South Africa. "Virat saab kal raat Anushka madam ke ghar par hi the. Paanch din se toh yahin par hain," said one of the watchmen Saturday. They had first met while shooting for a shampoo commercial.
