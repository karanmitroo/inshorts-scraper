Numerous Bollywood celebrities, including actor Amitabh Bachchan, Rishi Kapoor, Priyanka Chopra and Shahid Kapoor shared wishes on the occasion of Eid on Monday. Sharing Eid wishes, actress Shabana Azmi tweeted, "Bring on the biryani and the sheer qorma", while Swara Bhasker wrote, "Save me some sevaiin". Anushka Sharma, Varun Dhawan, Suniel Shetty also posted Eid wishes.
