Director Rohit Shetty, known to have good camaraderie with Ajay Devgn and SRK, says he is happy working with them in his career. "I don't have a wish list of actors. I am happy working with Ajay and Shah Rukh only..." he said. Notably, Rohit started as an assistant director with 'Phool Aur Kaante' that marked actor Ajay Devgn's debut.
