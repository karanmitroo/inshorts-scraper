'Padmaavat' has become Deepika Padukone's seventh film to gross over ₹100 crore, making her the actress with the highest number of Bollywood films in ₹100 crore club. Two of her other films with director Sanjay Leela Bhansali 'Bajirao Mastani' and 'Goliyon Ki Rasleela Ram-Leela' earned over ₹100 crore. 'Chennai Express' and 'Race 2' are also among her ₹100 crore films.
