Singer Sonu Nigam, while travelling on a flight from Jodhpur to Mumbai recently, surprised all the passengers with an impromptu performance. The 42-year-old singer went up to the instructor's microphone on the plane and started humming the song 'Do Pal' from the film 'Veer Zaara' (2004). A video of his performance has garnered over 7,000 likes on Facebook.
