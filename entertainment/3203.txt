The first poster of the upcoming Rajkummar Rao and Kriti Kharbanda starrer 'Shaadi Mein Zaroor Aana' has been shared by Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh on Twitter. Directed by Ratnaa Sinha, the film is scheduled to release on November 10. Notably, Kriti made her Bollywood debut opposite Emraan Hashmi in 'Raaz: Reboot'.
