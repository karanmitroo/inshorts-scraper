According to reports, the release date of Rohit Shetty's upcoming film 'Golmaal 4', starring Ajay Devgn, has been postponed to avoid a clash with actor Rajinikanth's upcoming film '2.0'. Rajinikanth's film, which also stars Akshay Kumar, is a spiritual successor to the 2010 film 'Robot'. The two films were reportedly set to release on October 19, 2017.
