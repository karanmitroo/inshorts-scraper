As per reports, actresses Alia Bhatt and Shraddha Kapoor have been cast as female leads opposite actor Varun Dhawan in 'Judwaa 2', which stars Varun in a double role. The film, a sequel to the 1997 film 'Judwaa', will be directed by David Dhawan. 'Judwaa' featured Salman Khan, Karisma Kapoor and Rambha in lead roles.
