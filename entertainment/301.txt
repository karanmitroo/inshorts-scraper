Vishal Bhardwaj has announced that the shooting of his upcoming directorial starring Irrfan Khan and Deepika Padukone has been postponed by a few months owing to the actors' health issues. "Irrfan down with jaundice and Deepika with resurfaced back issue caused during 'Padmaavat'," tweeted Bhardwaj. The film's shooting was scheduled to start in the first week of March in Nepal.
