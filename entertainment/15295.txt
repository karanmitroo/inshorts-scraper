'High Heels', the first song from the Arjun Kapoor and Kareena Kapoor-starrer upcoming film, 'Ki & Ka', has been released. Sung by Meet Bros featuring Jaz Dhami, Aditi Singh Sharma and rapper Yo Yo Honey Singh, the song is a newer version of the 2012 song 'High Heels'. Directed by R Balki, 'Ki & Ka' will release on April 1.
