The Aamir Khan-starrer film 'Dangal', with earnings of ₹304.38 crore within 13 days, has become the fastest Bollywood film to earn ₹300 crore at the Indian box office. Further, it is also the highest grossing film of 2016, beating Salman Khan's 'Sultan'. 'Dangal' is also Aamir's second film to enter the ₹300 crore club, after the 2014 film 'PK'.
