Soundarya Rajnikanth directorial, Kochadaiiyaan, featuring Rajinikanth in the lead role and Deepika Padukone, Jackie Shroff in supporting roles, released this Friday. The movie has been rated 2.5 (CNN IBN, Rajeev Masand), 3.5 (TOI, Bollywood hungama, Taran Adarsh). For the legion of Rajinikanth fans, this film is definitely worth a watch, says a review by Taran Adarsh, Bollywood Hungama.
