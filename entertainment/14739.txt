Actor Shahid Kapoor on Monday took to Twitter to share the motion logo of his upcoming film 'Udta Punjab'. The film, which also features Alia Bhatt, Kareena Kapoor Khan and Diljit Dosanjh, is a neo-noir thriller drama directed by Abhishek Chaubey. It revolves around the issue of drug abuse in Punjab. The film is scheduled to release on June 17.
