Director Shaad Ali's action-thriller 'Kill Dil' hasn't been received well with the critics. The film "simply doesn't have enough soul and substance to be able to go all the way to the finish line" writes NDTV. Govinda is lauded for his performance. The movie has got 2/5 stars (NDTV), 2.5/5 (IBN Live), 3/5 (HT, Zee News) and 0.5/5 (Indian Express).
