The Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol starrer 'Dilwale' was on Saturday named the worst film at the 8th annual Golden Kela Awards. 'Shaandaar', starring Shahid Kapoor and Alia Bhatt, won the ‘Baawra Ho Gaya Hai Ke’ Award, while 'MSG-2' bagged award for the ‘Most Pointless Sequel/Remake’. Sanjay Leela Bhansali’s 'Bajirao Mastani' won the ‘Manoj Kumar Award for Historical Accuracy’.
