Actress Priyanka Chopra wore a white Ralph & Russo gown to Oscars 2017. Best Actress winner Emma Stone was seen in a golden Givenchy dress while Best Supporting Actress winner Viola Davis wore a red Armani Privé gown. Oscar nominee Janelle Monáe wore a black gown by Elie Saab while Fifty Shades Darker actress Dakota Johnson wore a Gucci dress.
