Filmmaker Karan Johar's Dharma Productions unveiled a poster for the Rana Daggubati and Taapsee Pannu starrer 'The Ghazi Attack' on Sunday on the occasion of 'Navy Day'. Pannu tweeted, "Proud to be a small part of this BIG film #TheGhaziAttack India's first underwater war film!" The film is scheduled to release on February 17, 2017.
