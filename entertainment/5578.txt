Actress Sridevi has said that she can't control what's written about her elder daughter Jhanvi Kapoor as she's a public figure now. Sridevi also revealed that Jhanvi was very upset when reports stated that at a recent party, she was following her rumoured celebrity crush, Ranbir Kapoor. "I was like, 'Welcome to this world'," said Sridevi to Jhanvi.
