Actor Rajinikanth’s upcoming Tamil gangster drama 'Kabali' will be dubbed and released in Malay, a language spoken in Malaysia, Indonesia, Singapore and Thailand. 'Kabali' is reportedly the first Indian film to be dubbed in Malay. The film’s Malaysian distributor will release 'Kabali' in Malaysia, Indonesia and Singapore. Notably, Malaysian actor Rosyam Nor has played a villain in the film.
