The trailer of the Arjun Rampal starrer 'Daddy' was released on Tuesday. Rampal has played the role of gangster-turned-politician Arun Gawli in the biopic, which will reportedly cover Gawli's life from his 20s to his 60s. Directed by Ashim Ahluwalia and co-written by Rampal, the film is scheduled to release on July 21.
