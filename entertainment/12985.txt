'Jason Bourne', the fifth installment of the 'Bourne' franchise, "does not really break new ground with plot, action", wrote India Today. "Matt Damon is back but the thrill is missing," wrote Indian Express while Hindustan Times wrote that it "will likely impress diehard fans of the long-running series". It was rated 3.5/5 (India Today), 1/5 (Indian Express) and 3/5 (HT).
