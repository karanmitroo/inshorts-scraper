Fashion designer Manish Malhotra has shared a video of actress Alia Bhatt in Kareena Kapoor's look from the 2007 film 'Jab We Met'. Alia is seen wearing a white full sleeve top, a black corset and a red skirt, similar to what Kareena wore for the song 'Yeh Ishq Hai'. Alia paid tribute to Kareena at the recent Filmfare Awards.
