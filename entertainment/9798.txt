Farah Khan had a cameo appearance in the Shah Rukh Khan, Kajol and Rani Mukerji starrer film 'Kuch Kuch Hota Hai'. She features in two scenes in the film, including the one where Kajol comes wearing an orange and pink dress. Farah, who turned 52 on Monday, started her career as a choreographer with 1992 film 'Jo Jeeta Wohi Sikandar'.
