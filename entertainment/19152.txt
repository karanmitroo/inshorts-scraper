Rapper Yo Yo Honey Singh, who has given tracks in films like ‘Cocktail’, ‘Chennai Express’, ‘Boss’, and ‘Yaariyaan’, is all set to sing in Amitabh Bachchan’s ‘Bhootnath Returns’. The rapper has also been signed for a track in ‘Ragini MMS 2′. Honey Singh’s songs have been pictured on actors like Shah Rukh Khan, Akshay Kumar and Arshad Warsi.
