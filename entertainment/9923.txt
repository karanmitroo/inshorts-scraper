Television actress Kamya Punjabi, in an Instagram post on New Year, bid farewell to the year 2016, which took away her friend the late television actress Pratyusha Banerjee. The post featured a picture of Kamya and Pratyusha together. Kamya wrote alongside the picture, "Bas yaad saath hai.... aate hi jo tujhe le gaya uss 2016 ko mera alvida!!!"
