A second poster of 'Sachin: A Billion Dreams', the biopic of Sachin Tendulkar, has been released. Celebrities including Harbhajan Singh, Yuvraj Singh and Ranveer Singh also shared the poster. "Whata privilege 2 hav lived in 'The Sachin Era'!", Ranveer wrote. "One Man... Multiple Generations... Countless Fans", tweeted Yuvraj. The film featuring Sachin himself, will release this year.
