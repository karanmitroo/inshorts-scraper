Actor Shahid Kapoor's younger half-brother Ishaan Khattar, and Sridevi and Boney Kapoor's daughter Jhanvi Kapoor will reportedly make their Bollywood debut in the remake of Marathi film 'Sairat'. The official Hindi remake rights for 'Sairat' have been purchased by filmmaker Karan Johar. Notably, Boney Kapoor had recently confirmed that Jhanvi will make her debut in a Karan Johar film.
