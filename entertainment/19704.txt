Varun Dhawan and Alia Bhatt, who made their debut with Karan Johar's hit film 'Student Of The Year', will again be seen together in KJo's next 'Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania' along with debutant Shashank Khaitan. While the film is yet to go on the floor, July 27, 2014 has been finalized as the film's release date.
