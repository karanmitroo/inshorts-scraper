The Amitabh Bachchan and Nawazuddin Siddiqui starrer 'TE3N' has collected ₹11.13 crore in its opening weekend in India. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, 'TE3N' collected ₹2.62 crore on Friday, ₹3.96 crore on Saturday and ₹4.55 crore on Sunday. Directed by 	Ribhu Dasgupta, the film is a remake of the South Korean film 'Montage' and also stars Vidya Balan.
