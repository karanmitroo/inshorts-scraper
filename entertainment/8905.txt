The teaser of Swara Bhaskar's upcoming film 'Anaarkali Of Aaraah' has been released. The film is about an erotic singer played by Swara. Sonam Kapoor, who released the film's teaser said, "To see female empowerment at its most entertaining and heart-wrenching, see the teaser." Also featuring actors Sanjay Mishra and Pankaj Tripathi, it is scheduled to release on March 24.
