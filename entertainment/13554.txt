Actor Ajay Devgn on Friday released a new poster of his upcoming film 'Shivaay', wherein female lead Erika Kaar, a native of Poland, is seen with him for the first time. The action adventure film, releasing on October 28 this year, also stars 18-year-old Sayyeshaa Saigal in the lead. The Devgn directorial is the Bollywood debut of both the actresses.
