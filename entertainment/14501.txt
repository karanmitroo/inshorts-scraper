Shraddha Kapoor and Tiger Shroff starrer 'Baaghi' which released on Friday has become the third highest opener of the year 2016, after Shah Rukh Khan's 'Fan' and Akshay Kumar starrer 'Airlift'. While 'Baaghi' earned ₹11.87 crore on its opening day, 'Fan' had grossed ₹19.20 crore and 'Airlift' had made a business of ₹12.35 crore, Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh tweeted.
