Aamir Khan, Ranveer Singh, Anushka Sharma and Sidharth Malhotra were among the celebrities who attended the 2017 GQ Men of the Year Awards. While Anushka was named the Woman of the Year, Ranveer Singh won the award for Entertainer of the Year. Rajkummar Rao was named the Actor of the Year. Meanwhile, Aamir won the award for Creative Maverick.
