American reality television personality Kim Kardashian has worn a saree by designer Sabyasachi Mukerji in a photoshoot for the March edition of Vogue India magazine. Kim, who has also featured on the magazine's March cover, has been styled by Anaita Shroff Adajania. In some of the other pictures, she can be seen in outfits by Anamika Khanna and Anita Dongre.
