Former Union Minister Kapil Sibal has penned five-songs including one item-number for the movie "Zainab". Sibal had made his debut as a lyricist in Bollywood with a song 'Tu bata de' from the movie 'Bandook'. He had also worked with A.R.Rahman on his album 'Raunaq'. The politician has said he is open to writing for Bollywood-films if he is approached.
