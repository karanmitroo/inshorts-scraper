Actor Shah Rukh Khan took to social media to share a picture of himself with actress Rani Mukerji on the occasion of his film 'Paheli' completing 12 years of its release on Saturday. In the caption, Shah Rukh thanked the film's director Amol Palekar, and its cast including actors Amitabh Bachchan, Juhi Chawla, Anupam Kher, among others.
