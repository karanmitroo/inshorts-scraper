Zac Efron-starrer 'We Are Your Friends', which hit the screens across India this week, "will appeal only to the Disco and musically inclined buffs," said Zee News. The film's "characters never feel even remotely authentic or interesting", wrote IBN Live. The Max Joseph directorial has been rated 2.5/5 (TOI), 2/5 (IBN Live) and 1.5/5 (Zee News, ET and Indian Express).
