South Indian superstar Rajinikanth thanked PM Narendra Modi, people from the entertainment industry and his fans for their heartfelt wishes on his 64th birthday, Friday. "My heartfelt thanks to you dear @narendramodi ji, for your thoughtful birthday wishes amidst your busy schedule, Warm regards", he tweeted. He also thanked Amitabh Bachchan, Kamal Hassan and his fans in other tweets.
