Celebrities including Emma Watson, Lupita Nyong'o, Beyoncé and Taylor Swift on Monday attended 2016's Met Gala, which had the theme 'Manus x Machina: Fashion in an Age of Technology'. Watson and Lupita chose Calvin Klein ensembles, with the latter sporting a towering four-tier hairdo. Beyoncé wore a latex Givenchy dress, while Swift was seen wearing a metallic Louis Vuitton dress.
