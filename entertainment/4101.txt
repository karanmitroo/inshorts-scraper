Actress Sridevi took to Instagram to share a picture with Rekha, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Rani Mukerji and Vidya Balan, among others. The picture was taken at a party hosted by fashion designer Manish Malhotra to mark Sridevi's 54th birthday. "Thank you Manish for making me feel so special and all my friends for giving me so much love," wrote Sridevi.
