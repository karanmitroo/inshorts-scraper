The motion poster of the upcoming thriller film 'Aksar 2', which is a sequel to the Emraan Hashmi, Dino Morea and Udita Goswami starrer 2006 film 'Aksar', has been released. The sequel stars actors Zarine Khan and Gautam Rode in lead roles. It has been directed by Ananth Narayan Mahadevan, who also directed the first film.
