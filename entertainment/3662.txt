Former 'Bhabi Ji Ghar Par Hai' serial actress Shilpa Shinde has featured in the song 'Maro Line' from the upcoming Rishi Kapoor and Paresh Rawal starrer 'Patel Ki Punjabi Shaadi'. The song has been composed by Lalit Pandit with vocals by Neha Kakkar and Aishwarya Nigam, and lyrics by Sanjay Chhel. The film is scheduled to release on September 15.
