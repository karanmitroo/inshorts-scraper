Amitabh Bachchan praised 'Kaabil', starring Hrithik Roshan and Yami Gautam, calling it a very convincing film. He tweeted, "Endearing... performances, and dexterously handled by Sanjay Gupta, director! Congrats!" Responding to this, Hrithik tweeted, "Amit uncle! Can't express the joy. Thank you so much for taking the effort to encourage us!" Director Sanjay Gupta also thanked Bachchan on Twitter.
