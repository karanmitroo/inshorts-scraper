Kabir Khan, the director of 'Tubelight', has said that actor Salman Khan's performance in the film is five times better than in his 2015 film 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan'. He added, "Salman will be seen in a completely different way in the film." Also featuring Sohail Khan and late actor Om Puri, 'Tubelight' will have a cameo role by Shah Rukh Khan.
