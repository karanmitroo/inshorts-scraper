On actor Ajay Devgn's 47th birthday, #HappyBirthdayAjayDevgn was a top Indian Twitter trend on Saturday. Users wished the actor with tweets like, "Your acting is fab n ur sheer presence make movie(s) more thrilling. Thank you @ajaydevgn  for entertaining us" . 'Happy Birthday Ajay Devgn' and 'मुबारक अजय देवगन' were also trending on the actor's birthday.
