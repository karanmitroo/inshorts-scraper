Reacting to Censor Board reportedly asking for 89 cuts in the film 'Udta Punjab', Anurag Kashyap tweeted, "any person or party opposing it is... GUILTY of promoting drugs". Karan Johar tweeted, "Censoring reality amounts to delusion... the fraternity has to stand by what's right!" Meanwhile, Ram Gopal Varma tweeted, "Punjab Government should ban Udta drugs (or)... should ban themselves".
