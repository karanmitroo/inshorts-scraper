As per reports, actress Nargis Fakhri of 'Rockstar' fame has been roped in to play the role of Sangeeta Bijlani in an upcoming biopic on former Indian cricket captain Mohammed Azharuddin. Reportedly, Bijlani's role was initially offered to Kareena Kapoor Khan. The Anthony D'Souza directorial features Emraan Hashmi in the lead role and Prachi Desai as his first wife, Naureen.
