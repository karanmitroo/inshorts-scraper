Madhuri Dixit, Sushmita Sen, Alia Bhatt, Parineeti Chopra, Kareena Kapoor, and Nandita Das will star in 'Girl Rising – Woh Padhegi, Woh Udegi,' a film produced by Priyanka Chopra and Freida Pinto. The initiative is a collaboration between the ‘Beti Bachao Beti Padhao’ campaign and Star TV. It will be aired on Star Plus on Raksha Bandhan, August 29.
