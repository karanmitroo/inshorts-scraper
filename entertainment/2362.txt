Shah Rukh Khan, Amitabh Bachchan, Kamal Haasan and Kajol were among those present on the opening day of the 23rd Kolkata International Film Festival (KIFF) on Friday. Further, West Bengal CM Mamata Banerjee, British filmmaker Michael Winterbottom, Shah Rukh, Bachchan and Haasan were part of the inaugural function. The film festival began with the screening of the Iranian film 'Yellow'.
