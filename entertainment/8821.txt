The trailer for the Jason Momoa, Keanu Reeves and Suki Waterhouse starrer 'The Bad Batch' has been released. The film also features Jim Carrey as a 'Hermit'. The film revolves around a dystopian love story in a Texas wasteland and is set in a community of cannibals. Directed by Ana Lily Amirpour, it is scheduled to release on June 23.
