'Pashmina', the new song from Aditya Roy Kapur and Katrina Kaif starrer film 'Fitoor' was released on YouTube on Wednesday. The romantic song, penned by lyricist Swanand Kirkire, has been composed and sung by Amit Trivedi. Directed by Abhishek Kapoor, 'Fitoor' is based on Charles Dickens' novel 'Great Expectations' and is scheduled to release on February 12.
