Ashim Ahluwalia’s ‘Miss Lovely’, which premiered at the 2012 Cannes Film Festival and screened at the Toronto and Rotterdam International Film Festivals, released Friday. The movie, set in the criminal depths of Mumbai's C-grade (horror and porn film) industry, features Nawazuddin Siddiqui, Anil George and Niharika Singh. It has been rated 3 (IBNLive, TOI), 3.5 (India Today) and 4 (NDTV).
