As per reports, Salman Khan has chosen his brother Sohail Khan and Mithun Chakraborty's son Mahaakshay Chakraborty to star in the first TV series that he is producing. While Sohail will reportedly portray the role of the Indian wrestler Ghulam Mohammed also known as The Great Gama, Mahaakshay will is said to be playing the wrestler's brother in the series.
