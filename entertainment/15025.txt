Actor Akshay Kumar has featured on the cover of the luxury magazine, 'Man's World', for its March issue, a special edition to mark the 16th anniversary of the magazine. In the cover shot, Akshay is dressed in a suit by the Italian luxury fashion house, 'Ermenegildo Zegna'. The magazine terms the 48-year-old actor as "The Age-Defier" in the cover feature.
