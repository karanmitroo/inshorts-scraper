Padma Shri award-winning Indian-American actor Tom Alter is battling stage four cancer. The 67-year-old actor has had a relapse of a squamous cell carcinoma and is under treatment at Saifee Hospital, Mumbai. Alter has acted in films like 'Kranti', 'Veer-Zara', 'Karma' and 'Gandhi'. He was also seen in the Hindi television series 'Zabaan Sambhalke'.
