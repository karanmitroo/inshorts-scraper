Katrina Kaif has won the world's sexiest Asian woman title for the fourth consecutive year in the '50 Sexiest Asian Women List 2013' by the UK-based Eastern Eye, with Priyanka Chopra coming second, TV star Drashti Dhami third and current Box Office queen Deepika Padukone fourth. The biggest surprise of 2013 list was the sheer dominance of Indian television actresses.
