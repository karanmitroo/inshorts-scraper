The Akshay Kumar, Ileana D'Cruz and Esha Gupta starrer 'Rustom' has collected ₹50.42 crore in its opening weekend, making it the fourth highest opening weekend grosser in 2016. 'Rustom' surpassed the earnings of 'Airlift', which minted ₹44.30 crore. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, it earned ₹14.11 crore on Friday, ₹16.43 crore on Saturday and ₹19.88 crore on Sunday.
