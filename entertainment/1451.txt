Actresses Kareena Kapoor Khan, Alia Bhatt, Sridevi and Sonam Kapoor have featured on the cover of Filmfare magazine for its January issue. The extended cover also features Deepika Padukone, Katrina Kaif, Shahid Kapoor and Hrithik Roshan among others. Describing it as a "blockbuster cover", Filmfare tweeted that it features the winners of Filmfare Glamour & Style Awards 2017.
