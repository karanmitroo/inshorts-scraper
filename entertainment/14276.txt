The Randeep Hooda and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan starrer film 'Sarbjit', which released on Friday, "is a screechy, mawkish melodrama", wrote India Today. Mid-Day wrote the film has "a memorable performance by Randeep", while NDTV called the film "a well-meaning outing that fails to do justice to its subject". It has been rated 1.5/5 (India Today), 3/5 (Mid-Day) and 2/5 (NDTV).
