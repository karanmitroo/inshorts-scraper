Joshua Michael Stern directorial 'Jobs' - biopic of Apple co-founder Steve Jobs - starring Ashton Kutcher in the lead role,  has been rated 2.5 by Times Of India critics and DNA, and 2 by IBNLive. "Jobs is an ambitious but an incomplete effort," quotes TOI about the movie. "Kutcher is earnest. But the film is a snore", says IBNLive.
