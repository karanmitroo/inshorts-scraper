Actress Deepika Padukone has recently debunked the rumours that stated she would be a part of the third film in the 'Aashiqui' franchise. She clarified that she has not signed any new project and is only working on 'Tamasha' and 'Bajirao Mastani'. Notably, it was recently revealed that actor Hrithik Roshan will play the male lead in 'Aashiqui 3'.
