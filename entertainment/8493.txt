Actors Amitabh Bachchan, Varun Dhawan and Alia Bhatt walked the ramp for fashion designer duo Abu Jani and Sandeep Khosla to raise money for children suffering from cancer. While Bachchan was seen in a black kurta with white floral designs, Varun and Alia wore red outfits. They also danced on the runway at the end of the show.
