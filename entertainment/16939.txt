Stand-up comedian Kapil Sharma's debut film 'Kis Kisko Pyaar Karoon', an Abbas-Mustan directorial, will hit the screens on September 25. Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh took to Twitter to confirm the news on Friday. Besides Kapil, the movie also stars Manjari Fadnis, Simran Kaur Mundi, Elli Avram, Sai Lokur, Varun Sharma and Arbaaz Khan.
