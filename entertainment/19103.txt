Singer-composer Shankar Mahadevan's song -  "Mujhse hogi shurvaat" - has been nominated in the best activist anthem category of Honesty Oscars 2014. Apart from Mahadevan, the almost four minutes-long song has been sung by Sukriti Kakar, Prakriti Kakar and Chetan Naik and co-composed by Soumil and Siddharth. The winners will be announced on March 2.
