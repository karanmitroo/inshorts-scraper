As per trade analyst Taran Adarsh, 'Dhoom 3' on Monday crossed the Rs 200 crore mark (worldwide), after only 4 days of its release. "So here's *Fri to Mon* total of #Dhoom3... India 129.32 cr + Overseas 73.17 cr. Worldwide total: 202.49 cr. MIND-BOGGLING!" Taran tweeted. The worldwide collection till Tuesday stands at 233.57 crore.
