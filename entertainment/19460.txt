Rakesh Roshan's Krrish 3, starring Hrithik Roshan, Vivek Oberoi, Priyanka Chopra and Kangana Ranaut, released this Friday. The sci-fi movie, made on a budget of Rs.90 crore, has been awarded 2 star by Indian Express, 2.5 (NDTV), 3.5 (DNA) and 4.5 (TOI). Krrish 3 sees Hrithik take on evil mutants and save the world from destruction in his superhero avatar.
