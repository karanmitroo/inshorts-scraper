Comedian Mallika Dua's father Vinod Dua has slammed Akshay Kumar for making the remark 'Mallika ji aap bell bajao, main aapko bajata hoon' on the show 'The Great Indian Laughter Challenge'. "I am going to screw this cretin Akshay Kumar," Vinod wrote in a Facebook post, which was later removed. Akshay Kumar's remark was edited out of the show's episode.
