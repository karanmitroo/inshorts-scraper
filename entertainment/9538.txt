A new promo for the Shah Rukh Khan starrer film 'Raees' titled 'Sheron Ka Zamaana'  has been released. Shah Rukh, who stars as the titular character 'Raees', and Nawazuddin Siddiqui, who will be seen portraying the role of a police officer, feature in the promo. Directed by Rahul Dholakia, the film is scheduled to release on January 25.
