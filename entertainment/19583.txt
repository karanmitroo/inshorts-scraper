Bachchan-bahu  Aishwarya will make a comeback opposite husband Abhishek Bachchan in ad-man Prahlad Kakkad's maiden directorial venture 'Happy Anniversary', which explores anatomy of marriage." It also marks the return of producer Gaurang Doshi. "They are also planning to cast Amitabh Bachchan in the film," a source said.  The film will go on floors in the first month of 2014.
