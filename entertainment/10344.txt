'Rogue One: A Star Wars Story', which released on Friday, has "all the action and the soaring, near-deafening background track that we want from inter-galactic battles," wrote Indian Express. Hindustan Times called it "our generation's Star Wars story" while Times of India wrote, "[T]he Force is strong with this one." It was rated 3/5 (Indian Express) and 4/5 (HT, TOI).
