Filmmaker Tushar Tyagi's film 'Kaashi' which is based on the issue of open defecation and 'Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao' campaign will compete at Sundance Film Festival. The story revolves around a teenage girl who is forced to opt for open defecation because of the lack of a toilet. 'Kaashi' will also participate in Atlanta Film Festival and Florida Film Festival.
