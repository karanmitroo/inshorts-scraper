The release date of the upcoming Varun Dhawan starrer 'October', his first film with filmmaker Shoojit Sircar, has been shifted from June 1, 2018, to April 13, 2018. Sharing the news on his social media, Varun wrote, "As #October ends, it arrives earlier next year." Talking about the film, Shoojit had said, "It's an unconventional...story in the slice-of-life...romance space."
