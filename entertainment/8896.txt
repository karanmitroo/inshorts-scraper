A behind-the-scenes video from the upcoming superhero film 'Avengers: Infinity War' has been released. It features actors Robert Downey Jr from 'Iron Man', Tom Holland from 'Spider-Man: Homecoming' and Chris Pratt from 'Guardians of the Galaxy' discussing about being a part of the Marvel Universe. The last scene shows villain Thanos, played by Josh Brolin, preparing for battle.
