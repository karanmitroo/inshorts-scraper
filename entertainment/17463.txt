AskMen published a list of world's top 99 outstanding women, giving the coveted spot to Emma Watson. Emma was followed by MMA fighter Ronda Rousey and Kim Kardashian. Watson was selected as she is “rich, successful, famous, stylish, beautiful, intelligent, personable, kind", according to publisher James Bassil. He also praised her work for womanhood through the United Nations’ HeforShe campaign.
