First poster of Zoya Akhtar's much-awaited 'Dil Dhadakne Do' is out. Starring Priyanka Chopra, Farhan Akhtar, Ranveer Singh, Anil Kapoor and Anushka Sharma, the movie, which releases in 2015, is about a dysfunctional Punjabi family on a cruise trip. What is interesting is that Priyanka and Ranveer who were romancing each other in 'Gunday' will be playing siblings in it.
