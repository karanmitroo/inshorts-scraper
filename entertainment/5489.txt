Actors Arjun Kapoor and Athiya Shetty have featured on the July cover of fashion magazine 'Vogue' India. While Athiya can be seen in a shimmering red outfit from American luxury label Rodarte and earrings from Cartier, Arjun is wearing a black suit by British luxury fashion house Burberry. The two actors will be seen together in the upcoming film 'Mubarakan'.
