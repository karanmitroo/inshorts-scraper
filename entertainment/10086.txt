Actor Salman Khan, who turned 51 on Tuesday, celebrated his birthday with his parents, siblings Arbaaz, Sohail, Arpita, Alvira and nephew Ahil at his Panvel farmhouse in Maharashtra. Bollywood celebrities including Bipasha Basu, Preity Zinta and Neil Nitin Mukesh attended his birthday party. Salman's rumoured girlfriend Iulia Vantur was also spotted at the party.
