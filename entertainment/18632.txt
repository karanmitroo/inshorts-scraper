Actress Priyanka Chopra will be seen as one of the celebrity contestants on the 8th season of the game-show Kaun Banega Crorepati, which is hosted by superstar Amitabh Bachchan. Priyanka will be on the hot-seat to promote her upcoming biopic 'Mary Kom', which is based on the boxer's life. KBC season-8 will go on air from Aug-17, on Sony.
