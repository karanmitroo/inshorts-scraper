The official trailer of the Manoj Bajpai and Rajkummar Rao starrer film 'Aligarh' was released on YouTube on Friday. Directed by Hansal Mehta, the film is based on the life of Dr Shrinivas Ramchandra Siras, an Aligarh Muslim University professor who was suspended from his job owing to his sexual orientation. The film is scheduled to release on February 26.
