A Facebook live video shows actors Alia Bhatt, Varun Dhawan and television host-comedian Kapil Sharma singing the song 'Tere Bina Zindagi Se Koi Shikwa' from 1975 film 'Aandhi'. Alia and Varun were on the sets of 'The Kapil Sharma Show' to promote their upcoming film 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania'. They also sang the song 'Humsafar' from the film on the show.
