Kaun Banega Crorepati season nine featured a question on Nobel Prizes for its final ₹7-crore jackpot. The contestant Anamika Majumdar had to choose an option that does not represent a Nobel-winning parent-child pair. Anamika decided to quit as she didn't know the correct answer was Herman Emil Fischer, Hans Fischer but became the first to win ₹1 crore this season.
