Salman Khan and Bhagyashree starrer Maine Pyar Kiya completed 26 years on Tuesday as a result of which #26YearsOfEvergreenMPK was trending on Twitter in India. One tweet read, "Even after #26YearsOfEvergreenMPK, @BeingSalmanKhan's classic fills our hearts with romance". Rani Mukerji tweeted, "26 yrs ago Salman said 'Maine Pyar Kiya', and now the whole world loves him. One of my favs!"
