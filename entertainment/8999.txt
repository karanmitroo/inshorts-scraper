Actress Kangana Ranaut has featured on the cover of the third-anniversary issue of 'Harper's Bazaar Bride' magazine. She can be seen wearing a top with a plunging neckline and a pleated empire line skirt from Roberto Cavalli. She accessorised her look with jewellery by PN Gadgil Jewellers. The cover story on her is titled 'Kangana On Men, Movies and Marriage'.
