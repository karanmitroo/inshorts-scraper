Choreographer-filmmaker Remo D'souza has confirmed that actor Varun Dhawan will star in his upcoming directorial 'ABCD 3'. Remo added that choreographers and actors Dharmesh, Punit and Raghav would also star in the film. Varun, Dharmesh, Punit and Raghav were part of the cast in 'ABCD 2', which was also directed by Remo.
