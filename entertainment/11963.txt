Actress Alia Bhatt has featured on the October cover of 'Verve' magazine. The cover story is titled 'Alia Bhatt: Urban Sophisticate' and is a part of Verve's special issue on 'The Best Dressed Hall of Fame 2016'. Alia is seen in a dress by Delhi-based fashion designer Dolly J with jewellery by 'Misho' and 'Prerto' and a handbag from 'Dior'.
