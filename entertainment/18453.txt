Bollywood megastar Amitabh Bachchan was announced as ambassador of a nationwide campaign 'Swachh Banega India' on Thursday. Launched by NDTV and Dettol, the five-year programme is aimed at creating awareness about rising need for hygiene and sanitation in the country. The 71-year-old actor said that if his face and voice helps the cause, he will be "honoured".
