Late actor Paul Walker made his Hollywood debut with the horror-comedy film 'Monster in the Closet' which released in 1986, when Paul was 13. However, he bagged his first lead role in the 1998 film 'Meet the Deedles'. Born on September 12, 1973, Walker was best known for his role of 'Brian O'Conner' in the 'Fast & Furious' film franchise.
