British supermodel Naomi Campbell, while condoling late actress Sridevi's demise, shared two of her pictures on Instagram stories and wrote, "RIP Sridevi". Indian-origin American author Padma Lakshmi also condoled her demise in an Instagram story. Several Pakistani artistes including Mahira Khan, Rahat Fateh Ali Khan and Ali Zafar had earlier tweeted condolence messages following her demise.
