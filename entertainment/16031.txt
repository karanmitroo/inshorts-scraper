Salman Khan starrer 'Prem Ratan Dhan Payo' has earned ₹200 crore at the box office in India within 14 days of its release. According to Fox Star, PRDP has become the second Hindi film to enter the ₹200 crore club this year. Notably, the first film to cross the mark this year was also Salman Khan starrer 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan'.
