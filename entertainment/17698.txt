Veteran lawyer Ram Jethmalani is trending again on social media, after a picture of him locking lips with yesteryear actress Leena Chandavarkar went viral. After sharing warm greetings and a hug at an awards event, Jethmalani kissed Chandavarkar, late singer Kishore Kumar's wife. The lawyer's picture kissing actor Dharmendra had been the talk of the town last year.
