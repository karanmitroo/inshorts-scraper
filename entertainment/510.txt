The release date of the Rajinikanth starrer Tamil film 'Kaala' has been announced as April 27. "Mark the date! The don of dons is back," tweeted Rajinikanth's son-in-law Dhanush, who is also the film's producer. Written and directed by Pa Ranjith, the film is also said to star Huma Qureshi and Nana Patekar among others.
