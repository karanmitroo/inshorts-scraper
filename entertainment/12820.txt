According to reports, actor Shah Rukh Khan will star in a cameo role in filmmaker Karan Johar's upcoming film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil'. The 50-year old actor will reportedly play the love interest of the character portrayed by Aishwarya Rai Bachchan. Also starring Ranbir Kapoor, Anushka Sharma and Lisa Haydon, the film is scheduled to release on October 28, 2016.
