Actress Kangana Ranaut has said that Ranveer Singh is her buddy and he is "very curious" about her love life. Speaking about Alia Bhatt, Kangana said, "I love Alia, she is so talented and such a lovely girl." Kangana made these comments when asked to react to remarks made by Alia and Ranveer during the talk show 'Koffee with Karan'.
