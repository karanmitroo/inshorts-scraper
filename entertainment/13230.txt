The Rajinikanth and Radhika Apte starrer 'Kabali', which released on Friday, "lacks in pace and mass", wrote Indian Express. The film "is easily Rajinikanth's best performance in recent years", India Today said. "Though Rajinikanth shines, he has a long career in which Kabali doesn’t quite fit", wrote Hindustan Times. It has been rated 3/5 (India Today, HT) and 3.5 (Indian Express).
