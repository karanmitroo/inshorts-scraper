New posters of the John Abraham and Sonakshi Sinha starrer 'Force 2', featuring the characters, have been revealed. The film is a sequel to the 2011 film 'Force'. While John reprises his role as 'ACP Yashvardhan', Sonakshi plays the character 'KK' and Tahir Bhasin portrays the character 'Shiv'. Directed by Abhinay Deo, the film will release on November 18.
