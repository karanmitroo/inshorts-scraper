A video showing actor Rishi Kapoor singing 'Main Shayar Toh Nahi' from his 1973 film 'Bobby' at Neil Nitin Mukesh's sangeet ceremony has surfaced online. Pamela Chopra, late filmmaker Yash Chopra's wife was also seen singing a song from 1995 film 'Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge'. Neil, who got engaged to Rukmini Sahay in 2016, got married in Udaipur on Thursday.
