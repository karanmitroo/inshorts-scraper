After a long break, actress Bhagyashree Patwardhan of 'Maine Pyar Kiya' fame, is all set to make a comeback to the small screen after bagging a lead role in 24 Frames Productions' untitled show, according to sources. Earlier also Bhagyashree had acted in TV shows like Kacchi Dhoop, Jaan, Kaagaz Ki Kashti and a few more.
