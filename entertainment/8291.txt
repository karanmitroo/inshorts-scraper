The official trailer of the Sonakshi Sinha starrer upcoming film 'Noor' has been released.  It is based on Pakistani author Saba Imtiaz's novel 'Karachi, You're Killing Me!' The film revolves around journalist Noor, played by Sonakshi, and her misadventures and love life as she navigates through Mumbai. Also starring Purab Kohli and Shibani Dandekar, 'Noor' will release on April 21.
