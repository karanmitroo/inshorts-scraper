The teaser of the song titled 'Koi Fariyaad' from the upcoming film 'Tum Bin 2' has been released. The film is the sequel to the 2001 romantic drama 'Tum Bin'. Directed by Anubhav Sinha and starring Neha Sharma, Aditya Seal and Aashim Gulati in lead roles, 'Tum Bin 2' is scheduled to release on November 18.
