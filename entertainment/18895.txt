Actress Pallavi Sharda, who was last seen in 'Beshram', is playing a tamasha dancer in her next project 'Bambai Fairytale', a biopic on popular Maharashtrian scientist Shivkar Bapuji Talpade. Directed by debut filmmaker Vibhu Puri, the film narrates the journey of the legendary scientist who constructed and flew India's first unmanned airplane in 1895.
