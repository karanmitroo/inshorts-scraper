Actress Deepika Padukone shared a video message for her 'xXx: Return of Xander Cage' co-star Vin Diesel, which was played during his interview on film critic Rajeev Masand's television show. Deepika called him her 'teddy' and said that she loves him and misses him. "I love her so much and we have so much natural chemistry," said Vin in response.
