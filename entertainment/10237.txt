A video of actor Ranveer Singh dancing to the song 'Bole Bole Dil Mera Bole' from the 1992 film 'Shola Aur Shabnam' starring Govinda has been shared online. "Chi Chi I love you," Ranveer said in the video. He also revealed that his caller tune is a song from Govinda's 1994 film 'Raja Babu'. Govinda turned 53 on Wednesday.
