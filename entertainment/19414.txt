Surpassing SRK starrer ‘Chennai Express’, ‘Krrish 3’ has become the highest Bollywood grosser of all time. The movie, which was released on November-1, has already collected over Rs 231 crore in the domestic market. Earlier, ‘Chennai Express’ (Rs 227 crore) had taken over ‘3 Idiots’ (Rs 202 crore), which enjoyed the undisputed top position for three years in a row.
