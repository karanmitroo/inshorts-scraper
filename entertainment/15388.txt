Actress Vidya Balan is set to star in 'Kahaani 2' as confirmed by director Sujoy Ghosh in a recent interview. “Yes, ‘Kahaani 2’ is happening. It is in pre-production stage... Major parts of the film will be shot in Kolkata,” said Ghosh. The film will be a sequel to the 2012 film, 'Kahaani', which starred Balan in the lead role.
