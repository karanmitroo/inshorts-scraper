Actress Alia Bhatt, while referring to the earnings of 'Baahubali 2', jokingly said, "After Baahubali 2, I don't think we have the right to talk about box office." She added, "After Baahubali 2, everybody just wants to keep quiet and do good work." However, she further said she is not going to negate the positive response received by her films.
