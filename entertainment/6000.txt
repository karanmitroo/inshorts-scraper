According to reports, actress Alia Bhatt will be seen playing the role of a Kashmiri in Meghna Gulzar's upcoming film. The film, set against the 1971 Indo-Pak war, revolves around a Kashmiri woman who marries a Pakistani Army officer to provide Indian intelligence with information. 'Masaan' actor Vicky Kaushal is reportedly in talks to play the Army officer's role.
