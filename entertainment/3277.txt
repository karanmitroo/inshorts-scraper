Comedian Kapil Sharma, while replying to a tweet asking him when he plans to return with his talk show 'The Kapil Sharma Show', wrote, "Jaldi awaange paji...thanks for your love." Meanwhile, replying to another user's question if the show will return after Kapil finishes his film 'Firangi', he tweeted, "Hanji...aur kahan jayenge." Kapil's show will reportedly return in October.
