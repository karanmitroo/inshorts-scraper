Fashion designers like Ennio Capasa of Costume National and Peter Dundas of Roberto Cavalli paid tribute to singer and fashion icon, late David Bowie in their shows at the Milan Fashion Week menswear preview. Meanwhile, Donatella Versace played Bowie's 'Space Oddity' towards the end of her show. Other top labels like Dolce & Gabbana and Marni also showcased their collections.
