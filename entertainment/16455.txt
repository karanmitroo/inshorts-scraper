Multi starrer Bollywood film 'Welcome Back', which released this Friday, has a great pace and is funny in parts, according to HT. The Anees Bazmee directorial has unlimited ambitions but limited fun, HT added. It has jagged narrative and heavy-handed manner of delivering dialogue, wrote Indian Express. The film has been rated 2.5/5 (HT), 2/5 (NDTV) and 1.5/5 (Indian Express).
