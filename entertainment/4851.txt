The upcoming Deepika Padukone and Irrfan Khan starrer film directed by Vishal Bhardwaj will release on October 2, 2018. The film is reportedly based on the story ‘Femme Fatale’, which is part of S Hussain Zaidi's novel, 'Mafia Queens of Mumbai'. Deepika will reportedly play the role of mafia queen Rahima Khan and Irrfan will play a local gangster.
