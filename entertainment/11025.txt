Actress Katrina Kaif was named the 'Most Desirable Diva' at the recently held Lux Golden Rose Awards. Kareena Kapoor won the 'Glamour Diva of the Year' award, while Deepika Padukone received the 'Iconic Look of the Year' award. Priyanka Chopra won the 'International Face of the Year' title. Meanwhile, yesteryear actress Sharmila Tagore was honoured with 'The Legend Award'.
