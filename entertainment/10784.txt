According to reports, actress Shraddha Kapoor lost out on filmmaker Rohit Shetty's upcoming film 'Golmaal 4' as she had quoted a fee higher than the makers were willing to pay. Shraddha reportedly asked for ₹1.5 crore while the makers were not willing to pay more than ₹1 crore. Parineeti Chopra has been signed as the lead actress in the film.
