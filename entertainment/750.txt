Following Swara Bhasker's open letter to 'Padmaavat' director Sanjay Leela Bhansali, 'Golmaal' series director Rohit Shetty said, "If we...create problem then what would be the point...Let the film breathe now, for God's sake." He added, "After so much troubles...the film has released...let the audience watch it." Earlier, Swara wrote that she felt "reduced to a vagina" after watching 'Padmaavat'.
