Bollywood celebrities including Amitabh Bachchan, Dharmendra and Lata Mangeshkar took to social media to wish veteran actor Dilip Kumar on his 95th birthday on Monday. While sharing a picture of Dilip with Raj Kapoor, Amitabh wrote, "Two maestros." Singer Lata wished Dilip a long and healthy life while referring to him as her "rakhi brother".
