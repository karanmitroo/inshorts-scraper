Radhika Apte has won the Best Actress in an International Narrative Feature at the Tribeca 2016 Awards for 'Madly', becoming the first Indian actress to win the award. Apte won the award for 'Clean Shaven', a 20-minute segment directed by Anurag Kashyap in the anthology 'Madly'. Notably, the semi-nude clip featuring Apte which was leaked online is from the film.
