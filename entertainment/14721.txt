The official teaser of 'Sachin: A Billion Dreams', a biopic on Sachin Tendulkar, was released on Thursday following which 'Sachin movie' was among the most trending Google searches in India. It was most searched in Maharashtra, Kerala, Odisha and Goa. 'Barcelona', 'Sarabjit Trailer', 'Shilpa Shinde', 'Disha Patani' and 'Kobe Bryant' were the other trending searches on the same day.
