Actor Rajkummar Rao was named the Best Actor, and Mayank Tewari and Amit V Masurkar won the award for Best Screenplay at the 11th Asia Pacific Screen Awards (APSA) for the black comedy drama 'Newton'. Sharing the news on Twitter, Rao wrote, "Thank you Maa. Thank u team #Newton. Don't stop chasing your dreams because they really do come true."
