Former actress Mamta Kulkarni's husband Vicky Goswami has been alleged of being involved in a ₹2,000 crore narcotics racket which was unearthed after a drug bust in Thane, Mumbai. "We shall probe her (Kulkarni's) role in the matter,” said Thane police commissioner Parambir Singh. Meanwhile, Mamta denied Vicky's involvement in the drug dealings and claimed he was innocent.
