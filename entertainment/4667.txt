Actress Huma Qureshi, who turned 31 on Friday, said that the best birthday gift she ever got was her first car gifted by her father when she was in college. Huma made her acting debut with Anurag Kashyap's 'Gangs of Wasseypur' opposite Nawazuddin Siddiqui. Known for films like 'Dedh Ishqiya' and 'Badlapur', her latest Bollywood release was 'Jolly LLB 2'.
