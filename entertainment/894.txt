Actress Neetu Chandra has slammed Sidharth Malhotra for allegedly making fun of the Bhojpuri language on Bigg Boss 11. Neetu shared a video clip of Sidharth saying 'Latrine wali feel aayi bolte hue but accha laga' after delivering a dialogue in Bhojpuri. "Someone who's so fortunate to work with the best...For you to use your words so loosely...Shocked!" tweeted Neetu.
