Actor Sooraj Pancholi's family has filed a case of contempt against deceased actress Jiah Khan's mother Rabia Khan. Aditya Pancholi, his wife Zarina Wahab and daughter Sana accused that Rabia defamed them by running a Twitter account, where she passes lewd remarks and uses abusive language against the Pancholi family. They'd also filed a defamation case in 2014 against Rabia.
