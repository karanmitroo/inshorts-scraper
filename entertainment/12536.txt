As per reports, actor Dhanush, who is the son-in-law of actor Rajinikanth, will be producing​ 'Kabali 2'. Dhanush had tweeted that he will be collaborating with Pa Ranjith, who directed 'Kabali', and Rajinikanth on a new project. Dhanush is said​ to have taken permission from producer Kalaippuli S Thanu to continue the 'Kabali' film franchise.
