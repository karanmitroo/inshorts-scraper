The Goggle Song, a new song from the Arjun Kapoor and Anil Kapoor starrer Mubarakan has been released. The song has been sung by Sonu Nigam, Armaan Malik, Amaal Mallik, Tulsi Kumar, Neeti Mohan, with music by Amaal Mallik and lyrics by Kumaar. Directed by Anees Bazmee, Mubarakan is scheduled to release on July 28.
