Filmmaker Rohit Shetty has gifted a bicycle to Bollywood superstar Shah Rukh Khan. “Good day at work. Hotel Lobby heard Piano man and at shoot Rohit presented me a cycle to get my knee in shape. Yay!,” SRK, who is shooting in Bulgaria for Shetty's 'Dilwale', tweeted. This is the duo's second film together after 'Chennai Express'.
