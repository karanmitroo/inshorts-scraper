'Salamat', the first song from the Randeep Hooda and Aishwarya Rai starrer film 'Sarbjit', has been released. The song has been composed by Amaal Mallik, while it has been sung by Arijit Singh and Tulsi Kumar. 'Sarbjit', also starring Richa Chadda, has been directed by Omung Kumar and is scheduled to release on May 20.
