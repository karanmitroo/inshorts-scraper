Actor Shah Rukh Khan, who turned 51 on Wednesday, has starred in 16 films where his character dies. These films include Baazigar (1993), Darr (1993), Dil Se (1998), Devdas (2002) and Kal Ho Naa Ho (2003). However, in some films like Karan Arjun (1995), Paheli (2005) and Ra.One (2011), SRK's character is shown as getting a second life.
