The Ranveer Singh-Vaani Kapoor starrer film 'Befikre' "offers a romance that never really ignites", wrote The Indian Express. "The sparkling chemistry between the leads pulls you through... the movie," said The Times of India. NDTV wrote, "If it had more story and less love, it might've been a better film." It was rated 1.5(The Indian Express), 3/5(TOI) and 2.5(NDTV).
