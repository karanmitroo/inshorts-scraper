Actor Narendra Jha played the role of underworld don 'Musa Bhai' in the Shah Rukh Khan starrer 'Raees', while he portrayed police officer 'Chaubey' in the Hrithik Roshan starrer film 'Kaabil'. Both the films released on the same day. Jha also starred in the Shahid Kapoor starrer 2014 film 'Haider', where he portrayed the character 'Dr Hilal Meer'.
