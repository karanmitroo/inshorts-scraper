The teaser of Rajkumar Hirani's highly anticipated film 'PK' was released on the eve of Diwali. PK's posters have already generated a lot curiosity. The film, which will release in theaters on 19th December, features Aamir Khan and Anushka Sharma in the lead role, along with Sushant Singh Rajput, Boman Irani and Sanjay Dutt in key roles.
