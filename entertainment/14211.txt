'X-Men: Apocalypse', which released on Friday, collected ₹16.21 crore in its opening weekend. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film collected ₹4.51 crore on Friday, ₹5.65 crore on Saturday and ₹6.05 crore on Sunday. Directed by Bryan Singer, the Jennifer Lawrence and Michael Fassbender starrer is the latest film in the 'X-Men' franchise, based on Marvel superhero characters.
