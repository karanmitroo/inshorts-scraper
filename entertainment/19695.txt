Shah Rukh Khan and Deepika Padukone starrer 'Chennai Express' released on August-9  has collected an adorable average of Rs. 33.12-crore on the first day of its opening, culminating ahead of Salman Khan's 'Ek tha Tiger' that earned Rs. 31.25-crore on day-one. Even before the release, Rohit Shetty's this romantic-comedy-flick made a record collection of Rs. 6.75-crore from paid-review across India.
