Actors Arjun Kapoor and Jacqueline Fernandez walked the ramp for designer Manish Malhotra, who inaugurated the Lakme Fashion Week Summer/Resort 2016 on Tuesday. While Jacqueline was dressed in a crepe pink ensemble with a sheer floor-length cape, Arjun wore a dark blue sherwani with a plain silver jacket and a dupatta. The outfits were part of Malhotra's latest collection, 'Elements'.
