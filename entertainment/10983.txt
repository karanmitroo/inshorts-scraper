A new trailer for the Tom Hiddleston, Samuel Jackson and Brie Larson starrer film 'Kong: Skull Island' has been released. The film is a reboot of the 'King Kong' franchise and the second instalment in the 'Godzilla' and 'King Kong' film series by Legendary Pictures and Warner Bros. Directed by Jordan Vogt-Roberts, the film will release on March 10, 2017.
