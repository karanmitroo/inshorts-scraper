As per piracy-tracking firm Excipio, Leornado Di Caprio starrer "The Wolf of Wall Street" is the most pirated movie of 2014 with 30.035 million downloads by Torrent users since its release last Christmas. It is followed by popular animated film “Frozen” with 29.919 million downloads. “RoboCop”, starring Peter Weller, stands at the third position with 29.879 million downloads.
