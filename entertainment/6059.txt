Actor Amitabh Bachchan shared a picture with wife Jaya Bachchan on the occasion of their 44th marriage anniversary on Saturday. "Thank you all for the wishes you send..grateful and filled with love," read the tweet along with the picture. Amitabh and Jaya started dating in the early 1970s and have worked together in numerous films including 'Silsila', 'Guddi', 'Chupke Chupke'.
