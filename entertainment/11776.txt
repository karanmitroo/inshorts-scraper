The Sushant Singh Rajput starrer biopic 'M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story' has entered the ₹100 crore club within 9 days of its release. The Hindi language film, along with its dubbed versions in Tamil and Telugu, had earned ₹103.40 crore till Saturday. The biopic is the fifth Bollywood film in 2016 to have crossed the ₹100 crore mark.
