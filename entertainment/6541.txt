According to reports, real-life couple Amitabh Bachchan and Jaya Bachchan will be seen playing a married couple in an upcoming film. The film will be directed by Shoojit Sircar. Amitabh and Jaya have previously worked together in various films together including Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham... (2001), Silsila (1981), Chupke Chupke (1975), Abhimaan (1973), among others.
