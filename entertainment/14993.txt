Actor Sushant Singh Rajput has shared a picture with cricketer Mahendra Singh Dhoni, post India's win over Pakistan in a World Cup Twenty20 match on Saturday. Sushant was spotted with Sachin Tendulkar, Amitabh Bachchan, Abhishek Bachchan and Aftab Shivdasani cheering on the Indian cricket team. Notably, he is playing the titular role in the biopic, 'MS Dhoni: The Untold Story'.
