'Udi Udi Jaye', a new song from the Shah Rukh Khan and Mahira Khan starrer upcoming action crime thriller film 'Raees' has been released. The song has been composed by Ram Sampath with vocals by Sukhwinder Singh, Bhoomi Trivedi and Karsan Sagathia. Javed Akhtar has penned the lyrics. Directed by Rahul Dholakia, 'Raees' is scheduled to release on January 25.
