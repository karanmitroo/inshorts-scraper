Actor Fawad Khan co-founded the Lahore-based rock band 'Entity Paradigm' in 2000. The actor, who turned 35 on Tuesday, also sang on the music show 'Coke Studio' as part of the band. Fawad made his debut in films with a role in the Pakistani movie 'Khuda Kay Liye' in 2007 and later made his Bollywood debut with 'Khoobsurat' in 2014.
