Actor Rajkumar Rao has been receiving lot of film offers, after bagging the national award for the best actor for ‘Shahid’. "I am glad that I am getting to work with many people," Rajkumar said. He started his career with ‘Love Sex aur Dhokha’ and has been praised for his performances in movies like ‘Kai Po Che’, ‘Shahid’ and ‘Queen’.
