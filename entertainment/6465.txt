Celebrities including Rishi Kapoor, Kajol, Mahesh Bhatt and Aamir Khan with his wife Kiran Rao attended actress Reema Lagoo's funeral on Thursday in Mumbai. "My first film that I did had Reemaji in it. She was a brilliant actress, and she was pure at heart," said Aamir. Fatima Sana Shaikh, Sanya Malhotra and Shreyas Talpade were among other attendees.
