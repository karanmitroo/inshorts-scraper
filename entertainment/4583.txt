In a video shared on social media, actor Shah Rukh Khan can be seen singing the Bhojpuri song 'Lollypop Lagelu' for Anushka Sharma. The video also shows Bhojpuri actor Manoj Tiwari helping Shah Rukh with the song. This took place in Varanasi during the promotion of Anushka and Shah Rukh's upcoming romantic comedy 'Jab Harry Met Sejal'.
