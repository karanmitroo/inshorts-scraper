Priyanka Chopra, who turned 34 on Monday, began her acting career with a lead role in the Tamil movie 'Thamizhan' in 2002. She also sang her first song in the film, called ‘Ullathai Killathe’. The 2003 film ‘The Hero: Love Story of a Spy’ marked her debut in Bollywood. However, ‘Andaaz’ (2003) became her first Bollywood film to be released.
