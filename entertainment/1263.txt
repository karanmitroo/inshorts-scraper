A new song titled 'Hu Ba Hu' from Akshay Kumar, Sonam Kapoor and Radhika Apte starrer 'PadMan' has been released. It has been composed and sung by Amit Trivedi and the lyrics have been penned by Kausar Munir. Directed by R Balki and produced by Twinkle Khanna, 'PadMan' is scheduled to release on January 26.
