Debutant director Abhishek Varman's adaptation of Chetan Bhagat's book, '2 States' - a cross-culture love story starring Arjun Kapoor and Alia Bhatt- released this Friday. The Karan Johar-Sajid Nadiadwala production has been rated 2 (Suhani Singh, India Today), 2.5 (NDTV; Indian Express), 3 (koimoi.com) and 4 (Times of India; Saurabh Dwivedi, India Today).
