Filmmaker Indra Kumar announced the casting of Madhuri Dixit and Anil Kapoor in his film 'Total Dhamaal' while adding, "This film is...a comedy so don't expect any 'Dhak Dhak' but I promise total dhamaal." The film is the third instalment in 'Dhamaal' franchise. It also stars Ajay Devgn along with original cast members Riteish Deshmukh, Arshad Warsi and Jaaved Jaaferi.
