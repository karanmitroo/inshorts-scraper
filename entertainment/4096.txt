Simi Garewal tweeted, "Another takeover," while referring to an incident with Karan Johar when she finished hosting the Indian Film Festival of Melbourne (IFFM) Awards 2017. She said that Karan told the Festival Director that he wanted to host the event next year. Notably, Simi hosted a talk show where she interviewed celebrities, similar to the filmmaker's 'Koffee WIth Karan'.
