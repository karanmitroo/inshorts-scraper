As per reports, actor Suniel Shetty's son Ahan Shetty will make his debut in Bollywood with a film where he will star opposite Sara Ali Khan, the daughter of Saif Ali Khan and his first wife Amrita Singh. The film is said to be a co-production between Karan Johar's Dharma Productions and Sajid Nadiadwala's Nadiadwala Grandson Entertainment.
