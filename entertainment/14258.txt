The Sonakshi Sinha and John Abraham starrer 'Force 2' will release on November 18 this year, as per a tweet by Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. John will be reprising his character as a police officer in the film, which is a sequel to the 2011 film 'Force'. Directed by Abhinay Deo, the film also stars Tahir Bhasin.
