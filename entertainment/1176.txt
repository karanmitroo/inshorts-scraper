Actress Radhika Apte has said Sushant Singh Rajput is an overrated actor. She said this when Neha Dhupia asked her who she thinks is the most overrated actor in Bollywood during her appearance on the talk show 'BFFs with Vogue'. When asked to name one actor who needs acting classes rather than gym sessions, Radhika said, "Sooraj Pancholi."
