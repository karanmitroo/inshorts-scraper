'Gori Tere Pyaar Mein' that released this Friday is a romantic comedy film written and directed by Punit Malhotra. Produced by Karan Johar, the film features Imran Khan, Kareena Kapoor and Shraddha Kapoor in pivotal roles. Music for the film is provided by Vishal-Shekhar. The movie has got 0.5 (Indian Express), 1.5 (India Today), 3 (Zee News) and 3.5 (TOI).
