Actress Sonam Kapoor, who will be seen with Hrithik Roshan in a video of the song 'Dheere Dheere Se' from the film 'Aashiqui', on Thursday shared the first look of the video on her Instagram. The video will be released on September 1, to mark the 25th anniversary of 'Aashiqui'. Singer Yo Yo Honey Singh has recreated the song.
