Following Jalandhar-based Balraj Singh Khehra being crowned as the winner of the reality show MTV Roadies X4, 'Roadies X4 Winner' was the fifth most trending Google search in India on Sunday. It was most looked up in Goa, Uttarakhand and Himachal Pradesh. 'rajasthan board 10th Result', 'Argentina vs Venezuela' and 'JNTUH results' were some of the other trending searches.
