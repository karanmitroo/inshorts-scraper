In an interview to a daily, Arjun Kapoor said that he really likes kissing Alia Bhatt. Ahead of the release of their film '2 States', Arjun was posed with a question as to who was the better kisser between Alia and Parinneti. Arjun said, “Alia is the best kisser...It would be unfair to compare, but yeah, she is very good.”
