According to reports, Salman Khan and Govinda will return to star in the sequel of their 2007 film 'Partner'. The sequel will be directed by Sohail Khan, who has reportedly registered two titles 'Carry On Partner' and 'Partner 2' for the film. As per a source, "There were...reports of a fallout between Salman and Govinda. But that's...(in) the past now."
