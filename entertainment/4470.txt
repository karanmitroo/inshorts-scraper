Actor Prabhas, known for playing the titular character in the 'Baahubali' film franchise, has said that he is not even thinking of marriage right now. "At present, my [female] fans don't need to worry. I'm not getting married now," he jokingly added. The actor is rumoured to be dating actress Anushka Shetty, who co-starred with him in the 'Baahubali' films.
