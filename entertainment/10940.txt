The first look of Tamil film 'Kolaiyuthir Kaalam,' featuring Nayanthara, was revealed on Friday. Directed by Chakri Toleti and co-produced by Vashu Bhagnani and Deepshikha Deshmukh, the film will reportedly be shot in the UK. Notably, 'Kolaiyuthir Kaalam' will be Vashu Bhagnani's first production venture in Tamil.
