Actor Riteish Deshmukh has debuted as a singer with the song 'Thank God Bappa'. The 37-year old actor can be seen rapping in the video. The song has been composed by Amar Magrulkar and Kapil Sawant has penned the lyrics in Marathi. The music video has been directed by Kapil Sawant and produced by Riteish's wife Genelia Deshmukh.
