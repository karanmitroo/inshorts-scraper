Salman Khan starrer 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' has earned Rs 240.72 crore in India within 10 days of its release, becoming the biggest Bollywood grosser of 2015. It has surpassed the record of ‘Tanu Weds Manu’ that grossed Rs 150 crore. The Kabir Khan directorial, also starring Kareena Kapoor Khan and Nawazuddin Siddiqui, has also grossed Rs 83.29 crore outside India.
