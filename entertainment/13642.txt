Bollywood actor Aamir Khan made his first on screen appearance as a child actor in his uncle Nasir Hussain’s 1973 film ‘Yaadon Ki Baaraat’. His first feature film debut came in the experimental film ‘Holi’ in 1984. However, Aamir began a full-time acting career with a leading role in the tragic romance film ‘Qayamat Se Qayamat Tak’ in 1988.
