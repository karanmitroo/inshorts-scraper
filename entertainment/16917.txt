As per reports, Shahid Kapoor has been replaced by Arjun Kapoor in filmmakers Raj Nidimoru and Krishna DK's upcoming film called 'Farzi'. Reportedly, the directors had to take the decision mainly because of Shahid's date issues. The film will feature Kriti Sanon in the female lead opposite Arjun Kapoor. It will also star critically acclaimed actor Nawazuddin Siddiqui.
