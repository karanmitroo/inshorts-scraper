The official trailer of Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Radhika Apte starrer 'Manjhi - The Mountain Man' has been released on YouTube. The Ketan Mehta directorial is based on a true story and is set in early 1950's. In the film, releasing on August 21, Siddiqui plays Manjhi's character who decided to break a mountain and accomplishes the mission after 22 years.
