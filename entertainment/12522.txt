A new version of the 2003 track 'Where is the Love?' by The Black Eyed Peas has been released. The music video features the band members Fergie, will.i.am, apl.de.ap and Taboo along with celebrities including Justin Timberlake, Usher, Kendall Jenner, Jaden Smith and Diddy. The video highlights issues including the war in Syria, migrant crisis and 'Black Lives Matter' protests.
