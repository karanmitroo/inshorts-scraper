During a cabinet meeting chaired by CM Akhilesh, the UP government decided to exempt Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra's "Bhaag Milkha Bhaag" from entertainment tax in two installments or for a maximum period of two months. The movie, starring Farhan Akhtar and Sonam Kapoor, is already tax free in Delhi, Mumbai, Goa and Haryana.
