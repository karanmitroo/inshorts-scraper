The trailer of Rakesh Roshan's Krrish-3, starring Hrithik Roshan (in double role) and Priyanka Chopra, is all set to release on August-5. Produced by FilmKraft, the movie also features Vivek Oberoi as Kaal (a villain avatar). The music has been composed by Rajesh Roshan and Salim-Sulaiman and it is slated to release around Diwali this year.
