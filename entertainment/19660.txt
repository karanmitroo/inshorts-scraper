After shattering an array of records, Chennai Express has become the biggest grosser of Hindi cinema, surpassing 3 Idiots which had earned Rs 202.47crore at the domestic Box Office. Trade analyst Taran Adarsh tweeted, "Rohit Shetty becomes most successful director of Hindi cinema: 3 centuries and 1 double century. 4 films that feature in 100 cr+ grossers!"
