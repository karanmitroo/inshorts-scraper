'Trippy Trippy', the first song from Sanjay Dutt's comeback film 'Bhoomi', has been released. Starring Sunny Leone, the song has been composed by Sachin-Jigar with vocals by Neha Kakkar, Benny Dayal, Brijesh Shandilya and Badshah. The lyrics have been penned by Priya Saraiya and Badshah. Directed by Omung Kumar, 'Bhoomi' is scheduled to release on September 22.
