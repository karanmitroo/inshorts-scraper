Actor Salman Khan took to Twitter to share his picture from the first day of shoot of the upcoming film 'Race 3'. The third instalment in the 'Race' series also stars Jacqueline Fernandez, Bobby Deol, Daisy Shah and Saqib Saleem. Directed by Remo D'Souza, 'Race 3' is scheduled to release on the occasion of Eid next year.
