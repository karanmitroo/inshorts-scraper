Actress Twinkle Khanna has questioned if the Art of Living is "practising Art of intimidation" after the organisation's International Director Darshak Hathi tweeted against her comments on Sri Sri Ravi Shankar. Hathi tweeted that millions of followers would boycott 'Housefull 3' due to Twinkle's "inappropriate comments". Earlier, Twinkle had commented about Sri Sri's statement on the Nobel prize.
