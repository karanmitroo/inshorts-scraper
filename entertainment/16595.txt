In a recent interview, megastar Amitabh Bachchan, who played Jai of the Jai-Veeru duo in the iconic film 'Sholay', has revealed that in real life, his son Abhishek is his 'Veeru'. Actor Dharmendra had played the character of Veeru in 'Sholay', which released 40 years ago. It was directed by Ramesh Sippy and also featured Hema Malini and Jaya Bhaduri.
