Piko Taro, who gained fame for the 'Pen Pineapple Apple Pen' (PPAP) song, has released another single named 'I Like OJ'. The new song, which features Taro professing his love for orange juice, has been viewed nearly 22 lakh times. Interestingly, PPAP had won the Guinness World Record for the shortest song to make it to the Billboard Hot 100.
