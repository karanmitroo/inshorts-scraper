Filmmaker Anurag Kashyap, while reacting to the attack on director Sanjay Leela Bhansali by Rajput organisation Karni Sena, tweeted that Hindu terrorism is not a myth anymore. "Hindu extremists have stepped out of Twitter into the real world now," wrote Kashyap. Further, filmmaker Ashutosh Gowariker tweeted, "Shocking! Appalling!! Despairing!! And still, we shall not stop making what we want to!!"
