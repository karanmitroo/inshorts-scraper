The homes and offices of Baahubali's producers Shobu Yarlagadda and Prasad Devineni in Hyderabad were raided by Income Tax Department officials on Friday. The IT officials are said to have received an anonymous tip-off that the producers were hoarding large amounts of cash in ₹500 and ₹1000 notes. Notably, the 2015 film had earned over ₹650 crore worldwide.
