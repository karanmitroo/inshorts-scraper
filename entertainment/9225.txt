The Shah Rukh Khan starrer film 'Raees' has earned ₹93.24 crore in its extended five-day weekend, as revealed by Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. The film earned ₹17.80 crore on Sunday. Directed by Rahul Dholakia, the film also stars Pakistani actress Mahira Khan and Nawazuddin Siddiqui. Shah Rukh portrays the role of a bootlegger in the film.
