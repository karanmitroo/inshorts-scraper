Fox Star Studios and Phantom Films have finally decided to release Ranbir Kapoor starrer Bombay Velvet on 15th May, 2015, after postponing the film three times.  Directed by Anurag Kashyap, the period drama also stars Anushka Sharma and Karan Johar in key roles. Anurag has said that his film team is going to promote the movie in a unique way.
