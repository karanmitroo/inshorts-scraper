The trailer of Shahid Kapoor and Alia Bhatt starrer film 'Shaandaar' was revealed on Tuesday. The trailer portrays how a wedding planner (Shahid) falls in love with the bride's sister (Alia) at a destination wedding. The trailer also gives a glimpse of Pankaj Kapoor as Alia's father in the film. A Vikas Bahl-directorial, the film will release on Oct 22.
