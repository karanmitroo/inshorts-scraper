Actress Parineeti Chopra's first look from the upcoming film 'Sandeep Aur Pinky Faraar' has been unveiled. Talking about her character, Parineeti said, "I'm playing a fiercely ambitious girl from the corporate world. She's based in Delhi and she's extremely clear (about) what she wants from life." Directed by Dibakar Banerjee, 'Sandeep Aur Pinky Faraar' will release on August 3, 2018.
