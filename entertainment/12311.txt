'Chakora', the new song from the Harshvardhan Kapoor and Saiyami Kher starrer 'Mirzya' has been released. The song has been sung by Mame Khan, Shuchismita Das, Akhtar Chinnal and composed by the music director trio Shankar, Ehsaan and Loy, while Gulzar has written the lyrics. Directed by Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra, the film will release on October 7.
