Sunny Deol starrer 'Ghayal Once Again', which released on Friday, has grossed ₹23.25 crore in its opening weekend in India. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film earned ₹7.20 crore on Friday, ₹8.40 crore on Saturday and ₹8.40 crore on Sunday. The film, also starring Soha Ali Khan, has been directed by Sunny Deol.
