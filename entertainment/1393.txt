Salman Khan, who turned 52 on Wednesday, has played the character named 'Prem' in fifteen films till now. 'Maine Pyar Kiya', his first Bollywood film in a leading role, was also the first film where he played the character 'Prem'. He also portrayed 'Prem' in films like 'Hum Aapke Hain Koun..!', 'Judwaa', 'Partner', 'No Entry' and 'Prem Ratan Dhan Payo'.
