Bollywood newcomer Sunny Leone is the most searched personality this year, followed by Salman Khan, Katrina Kaif and Deepika Padukone, according to Google India's annual Zeitgeist. Shah Rukh Khan was fifth in the list, followed by Honey Singh, Kajal Agarwal, Kareena Kapoor, Sachin Tendulkar and Poonam Pandey. 'Chennai Express', 'IPL 2013', and 'Aashiqui 2' were the top trending words.
