Actor Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor danced on the ramp at a fashion show in Delhi for the clothing label 'Diva'ni', which is owned by Yash Raj Films. The duo were the show-stoppers for the label's Spring-Summer 2017 couture collection 'The Ivory Kiss'. Notably, Diva'ni is the official costume designer for the film 'Befikre', which stars Ranveer and Vaani.
