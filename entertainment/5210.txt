According to reports, Bajrangi Bhaijaan actress Alka Kaushal has been sentenced to two years in jail over a cheque-bouncing case. Alka and her mother had borrowed ₹50 lakh and returned it as two cheques of ₹25 lakh each which bounced. Reports suggest that Alka was given a jail sentence in 2015, but she filed a petition against it in court.
