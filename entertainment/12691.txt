Actor Amitabh Bachchan on Saturday reached 22 million followers on the micro-blogging social media site Twitter. The 73-year-old actor tweeted, "BAAADUUUUMMMBAAAA! Twitter followers reaches 22 million #AB22Million... Thank you all... Your love made it." While Bachchan remained the most followed Indian celebrity, PM Narendra Modi has the maximum number of followers on Twitter in India presently.
