Hollywood film 'Hitman: Agent 47' which hit the screens across India this week has almost all action in its first part, wrote Zee News. The film, starring Rupert Friend and Hannah Ware, has repetitive music and little to engage the audience, said Deccan Herald. The Aleksander Bach directorial has been rated 3/5 (TOI), 2/5 (Deccan Herald) and 1.5/5 (Zee News).
