'The Revenant' won the Best Motion Picture – Drama award and 'The Martian' won the Best Motion Picture – Musical or Comedy at the Golden Globes 2016 on Monday. 'The Revenant' beat 'Mad Max: Fury Road', 'Room' among others to win the Drama award. 'The Martian' beat 'Spy', 'The Big Short' among others to win the Musical/Comedy award.
