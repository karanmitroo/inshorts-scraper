The official teaser trailer of romantic drama film 'Mirzya' was the third most viewed YouTube video in India on Thursday. The video has garnered over 5.28 lakh views so far. Directed by Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra, it marks the Bollywood debut of Anil Kapoor's son Harshvardhan Kapoor and is inspired by the folk tale of the tragic romance of 'Mirza Sahiban'.
