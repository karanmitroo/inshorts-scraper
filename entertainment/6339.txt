Sanjay Dutt will star as a gangster in Tigmanshu Dhulia's upcoming film 'Saheb Biwi Aur Gangster 3'. Producer Rahul Mittra said, "With Sanju coming on board, we are hopeful the third part is going to be a treat for the audience." He added that the audience has been waiting to know what's happening in life of 'Saheb Biwi Aur Gangster'.
