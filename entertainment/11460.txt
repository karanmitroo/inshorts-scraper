The trailer of 'Kaabil', which stars Hrithik Roshan and Yami Gautam as visually impaired people, has been released. The film has been described as the story of a blind man, played by Hrithik, who is "driven by the fire of vengeance" following a terrible tragedy. Directed by Sanjay Gupta, the film is scheduled to release on January 26, 2017.
