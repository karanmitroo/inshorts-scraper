The Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol starrer 'Dilwale Dulhaniya Le Jayenge', which released in 1995 is the longest running Bollywood film, spanning a period of over 1,000 weeks. The movie continues to be screened at the Maratha Mandir in Mumbai. SRK, who turned 51 on Wednesday, won the Filmfare Award for Best Actor for his role in the film.
