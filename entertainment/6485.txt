According to reports, actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan may replace father-in-law Amitabh Bachchan as the host in the ninth season of television game show 'Kaun Banega Crorepati' (KBC). Reports suggest that actress Madhuri Dixit has also been approached to host the show. If either Aishwarya or Madhuri agree, this will be KBC's first season to be hosted by a female celebrity.
