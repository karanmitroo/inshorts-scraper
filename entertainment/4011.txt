Sidharth Malhotra has said that his family made fun of him when they saw him copying Shah Rukh Khan after he watched 'Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge' (DDLJ). "They would... (say), 'Tujhe hero banna hai kya?'," he added. He further said that he never thought he could be an actor but there was a subtle spark when he saw DDLJ.
