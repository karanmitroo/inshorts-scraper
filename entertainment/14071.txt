Indian television serial 'Balika Vadhu' has entered the Limca Books of Records as the 'Longest Running Daily Soap' in Hindi, with a run of over 2,000 episodes. Launched on July 21, 2008, the show initially addressed the issue of child marriage. It has two seasons, titled 'Balika Vadhu: Kachchi Umar Ke Pakke Rishte' and 'Balika Vadhu: Lamhe Pyaar Ke'.
