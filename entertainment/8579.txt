A new poster of the upcoming epic historical fiction film 'Baahubali: The Conclusion' featuring actor Prabhas has been released. The film is the continuation of 'Baahubali: The Beginning'. The poster shows Prabhas standing on top of an elephant. He will reprise the titular character of 'Baahubali'. Directed by SS Rajamouli, the film is scheduled to release on April 28.
