Bollywood actor Ranveer Singh will play the male lead in Aditya Chopra's upcoming directorial 'Befikre'. Yash Raj Films made the announcement on Twitter and also posted Ranveer's video, wherein he could be seen explaining his experiences at the Yash Raj office. Notably, Ranveer had started his career in the industry under Yash Raj banner's film 'Band Baaja Baaraat' (2010).
