Actor-producer Akshay Kumar Saturday unveiled the trailer of "The Shaukeens", directed by Abhishek Sharma and written by Tigmanshu Dhulia. The film is a remake of cult classic film "Shaukeen" and features Annu Kapoor, Akshay, Piyush Mishra, Anupam Kher and Lisa Haydon. "This film is a tribute to the original film," said Anupam Kher.
