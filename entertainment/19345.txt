Vijay Krishna Acharya's 'Dhoom 3' starring Aamir Khan, Katrina Kaif, Abhishek Bachchan and Uday Chopra released today. The movie has been rated 2 (Indian Express, India Today), 3 (NDTV) and 4 (Koimoi.com). The movie has got off to a fabulous start, witnessing 95-100% occupancy nationwide. As per reports, it is likely to enter the elite Rs.100 crore club by Sunday.
