Actor Shah Rukh Khan (SRK) has tweeted a poster for his film 'Raees', where the actor's face has been made entirely out of alcohol bottles in different colours. The digital poster has been made by film publicity designer Rajesh Ghadigaonkar. The 50-year-old actor plays the role of a bootlegger based on a real-life Gujarat gangster Abdul Latif in 'Raees'.
