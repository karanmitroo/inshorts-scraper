Aishwarya Rai Bachchan on Saturday confirmed that she has signed two films with filmmaker Sujoy Ghosh. "I’ve agreed to do Sujoy’s 'Durga Rani' and the screen adaptation of Keigo Huganshino’s Japanese novel 'The Devotion Of Suspect X'. I don’t know which one he will start first. We are working on that," Aishwarya said.
