After Tollywood Director Puri Jagannath announced his third movie with Telugu actor Mahesh Babu titled 'Jana Gana Mana' , #JanaGanaMana was a top Indian Twitter trend on Friday. Users trolled the trend with tweets like, "Feeling a bit uncomfortable because I am standing at ATTENTION while tweeting. #JanaGanaMana is trending". Fans also expressed their excitement for the upcoming movie.
