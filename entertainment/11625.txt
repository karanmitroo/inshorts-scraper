Filmmaker Ram Gopal Varma has shared the first look of the main cast from his upcoming film 'Sarkar 3' on Twitter. While actor Amitabh Bachchan will reprise his role of Subhash Nagre, Jackie Shroff will play one of the main antagonists. Also starring Manoj Bajpayee and Yami Gautam, the film will be the third installment in the 'Sarkar' film franchise.
