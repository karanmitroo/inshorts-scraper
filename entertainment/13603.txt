Singers Papon, Lucky Ali and Sona Mohapatra will be performing live at the concert 'Red Live Unwind' in Leisure Valley, Gurgaon on July 24. The concert is in collaboration with 'Red FM' and starts from 5:00 pm. The tickets have been priced from ₹1000 to ₹3000. The concert is open only to people aged above 8 years.
