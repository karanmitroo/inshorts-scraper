Late Indian filmmaker Yash Chopra has a mountain train named after him by the Jungfrau Railways in Switzerland. Chopra, who is known to have contributed to the renown of Switzerland by productions like the 1995 film 'Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge', was honoured with the feat in 2011. Notably, Yash Chopra's 84th birth anniversary was observed on September 27, 2016.
