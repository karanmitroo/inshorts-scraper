Celebrity chef Gordon Ramsay compared a photograph of khichdi and chicken soup shared by Priyanka Chopra to dog's dinner. Ramsay said this on a chat show while 'roasting' food pictures posted by celebrities. The picture which Priyanka reposted was originally shared by her Quantico co-star, captioned, "Lov u @priyankachopra...Good friends drop soup at your house when u're sick."
