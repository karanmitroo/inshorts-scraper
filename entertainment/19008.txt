Sanjay Leela Bhansali's dream project 'Bajirao Mastani', a historical love story that has been close to Sanjay's heart for the past 10 years, has finally found its Bajirao and Mastani. The hit jodi - Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone - will play Bajirao and Mastani, respectively. The movie is expected to release on Dec-25, 2015.
