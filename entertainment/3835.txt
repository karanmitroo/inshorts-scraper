As per reports, Deepika Padukone who plays the titular role in Sanjay Leela Bhansali's  'Padmavati' is being paid more than her male co-stars Ranveer Singh and Shahid Kapoor. A source said, "Deepika has taken home...₹13 crore, while Ranveer and Shahid share equal digits...close to ₹10 crore respectively." The film revolves around Alauddin Khilji's infatuation with the Rajput queen Padmavati.
