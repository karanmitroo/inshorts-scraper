The teaser of the Vidya Balan and Arjun Rampal starrer upcoming thriller film 'Kahaani 2' has been released. Vidya will be seen portraying the character 'Durga Rani Singh', a 36-year-old fugitive wanted by the police for kidnapping and murder. Directed by Sujoy Ghosh, the film is scheduled to release on December 2.
