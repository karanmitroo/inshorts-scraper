Actor Sidharth Malhotra has said that he envies his 'Baar Baar Dekho' co-star Katrina Kaif's "talent" of taking a nap during the "hectic" promotions for the film. Sidharth shared a video of Katrina sleeping in the car on Instagram. The actors have already travelled to Jaipur, Kolkata, Ahmedabad and Indore to promote the film, which releases on September 9.
