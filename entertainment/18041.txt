"Ugly", which hit the screens on Friday, is a film that should not be missed, says India Today. The Anurag Kashyap directorial is a thriller-drama that features Ronit Roy and Surveen Chawla of "Hate Story 2" fame among others. The film has been rated 4/5 by India Today and Times of India, 3/5 by NDTV and 2/5 by Indian Express.
