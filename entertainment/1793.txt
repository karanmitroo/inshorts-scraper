Twitter has released a list of Top Hashtag Trends in India in 2017 and the entertainment section of the list features the fantasy drama 'Baahubali 2: The Conclusion' and the television reality show 'Bigg Boss 11'. The Tamil film 'Mersal', which reportedly generated over 1.7 million tweets in three days, also featured on the microblogging site's Top Hashtag Trends list.
