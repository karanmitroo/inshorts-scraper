'Kaari Kaari', the new song from the Amitabh Bachchan and Taapsee Pannu starrer 'Pink' was released on Tuesday. The song has been sung by Pakistani singer Quratulain Balouch and composed by Shantanu Moitra with lyrics penned by Tanveer Ghazi. Directed by Aniruddha Roy Chowdhury, the film, which also stars Kirti Kulhari and Andrea Tariang, is scheduled to release on September 16.
