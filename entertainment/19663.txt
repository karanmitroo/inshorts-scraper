Salman Khan, who started his career with Biwi Ho Toh Aisi, released on August-22, 1988, has completed 25 years in Hindi film industry on Thursday. The first movie, in which Salman's voice was dubbed, was a flop. In December-1989, Sooraj Barjatya's Maine Pyar Kiya was released which became a blockbuster hit and propelled Salman to
where he is today.
