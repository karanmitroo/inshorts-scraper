Former cricketer Sachin Tendulkar is reportedly not charging any fee for his upcoming biopic 'Sachin: A Billion Dreams'. The film is being produced by the cricketer's friend Ravi Bhagchandka's production house 200 Not Out, and directed by Emmy award winner James Erskine. Tendulkar will play himself in the movie, while his son Arjun will play the cricketer's younger version.
