Actors Sidharth Malhotra and Varun Dhawan, who made their debut together in 'Student of the Year', have reportedly been finalised to play the lead roles in the 'Ram Lakhan' remake. Sidharth will play the role of Ram, originally played by Jackie Shroff, and Varun will be Lakhan, originally portrayed by Anil Kapoor. The film will be directed by Rohit Shetty.
