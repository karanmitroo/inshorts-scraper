The first teaser for the upcoming biopic, 'MS Dhoni: The Untold Story', was released on Tuesday. The teaser shows actor Sushant Singh Rajput portraying the early life of cricketer MS Dhoni as an Indian Railways TTE (Train Ticket Examiner). Directed by Neeraj Pandey, the film also stars Kiara Advani and Anupam Kher and is scheduled to release on September 2.
