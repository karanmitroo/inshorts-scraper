The first song titled 'Bappa' from Riteish Deshmukh's upcoming film 'Banjo' has been released. The song has been composed by music composer duo Vishal-Shekhar and sung by Vishal Dadlani, while Amitabh Bhattacharya has penned the lyrics for it. Directed by Ravi Jadhav, the film also stars Nargis Fakhri as the female lead and is scheduled to release on September 23.
