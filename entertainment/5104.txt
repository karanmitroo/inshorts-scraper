According to reports, Bollywood actor Siddharth Gupta has been signed to play the lead role in Disney's upcoming live-action Aladdin remake. Siddharth made his Bollywood debut with 2014 film 'Kuku Mathur Ki Jhand Ho Gayi'. Meanwhile, singer Salma Agha's daughter Sasha Agha and Tara Sutaria have reportedly been shortlisted for the role of Aladdin's love interest Jasmine.
