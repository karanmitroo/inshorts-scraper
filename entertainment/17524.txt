Director Harshvardhan Kulkarni’s “Hunterrr”, starring Gulshan Devaiah and Radhika Apte released this Friday. The story revolves around a guy named Mandar who believes that sex is an act of 'necessity' much like going to the loo daily. The movie has been rated 1.5 (India Today), 2 (Indian Express) and 3 (Times of India).
