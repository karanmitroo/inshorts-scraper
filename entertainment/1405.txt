Former cricketer Sachin Tendulkar and actors including Shah Rukh Khan, Ranbir Kapoor, Priyanka Chopra, Kangana Ranaut, Aishwarya Rai and Amitabh Bachchan attended the wedding reception of Virat Kohli and Anushka Sharma in Mumbai. Others who attended the reception included Sridevi, Madhuri Dixit, Karan Johar, Sidharth Malhotra, Rekha, Varun Dhawan, Katrina Kaif and her sister Isabelle.
