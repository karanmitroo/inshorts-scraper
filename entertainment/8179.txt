A new poster of 'Baahubali 2', featuring the characters Baahubali as a child and Kattappa, has been unveiled. The new poster shows Kattappa, portrayed by Sathyaraj, stabbing the grown up Baahubali, played by Prabhas. "The boy he raised, the man he killed," tweeted the film's director SS Rajamouli while sharing the poster.
