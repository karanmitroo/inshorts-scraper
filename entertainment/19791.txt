The Iron Man star Robert Downey Jr. is Hollywood's highest paid actor with an estimated $75-million in earnings in the last 12-months, followed by Channing Tatum($60-million) at No.2 and Hugh Jackman($55-million) at No.3, according to Forbes annual list of entertainers. Six of Roberts's recent films have grossed over $500-million worldwide, with "The Avengers" and "Iron Man-3" each earning over $1-billion.
