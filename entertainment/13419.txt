A new poster featuring actor Ali Fazal for the upcoming film 'Happy Bhag Jayegi' has been revealed. Fazal will be seen as the character 'Guddu' in the film while actress Diana Penty portrays the titular character 'Happy' and actor Abhay Deol plays the role of 'Bilal'. Directed by Mudassar Aziz, the film is scheduled to release on August 19.
