The romantic science fiction film 'The Space Between Us' is "cotton-candy fluff squarely aimed at the teenage crowd". "The initial setup is engrossing as it portrays the life of the first 'genuine' Martian," wrote Times of India while Indian Express called it, "This Fault in Our Stars in zero gravity". It was rated 1.5/5 (HT, Indian Express) and 2.5/5 (TOI).
