Salman Khan took to Twitter to announce the new release date of his brother-in-law, Aayush Sharma's debut film 'LoveRatri' as October 5. "#Loveratri releasing 224 days from today... batao kya date hai release ki," tweeted Salman. Directed by Abhiraj Minawala and produced by Salman Khan's production house, the film stars Warina Hussain as the lead female actress.
