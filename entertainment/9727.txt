As per reports, actress Rani Mukerji will play a differently-abled character in her next film. Reportedly, the film will be produced by Yash Raj Films and directed by Siddharth Malhotra, who last directed the 2010 film 'We Are Family'. As per sources, the story was initially about a differently-abled man, but Aditya Chopra suggested the change to a female character.
