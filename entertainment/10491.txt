The title song from Aamir Khan's upcoming biographical sports drama film 'Dangal' has been released. Composed by Pritam, the song has been sung by Daler Mehndi, with lyrics by Amitabh Bhattacharya. Aamir will be seen portraying wrestler Mahavir Singh Phogat, who taught wrestling to daughters Babita Kumari and Geeta Phogat. Directed by Nitesh Tiwari, 'Dangal' will release on December 23.
