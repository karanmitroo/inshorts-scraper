According to reports, the sequel to the 2012 film 'Ek Tha Tiger' will release in 2018. The film's original lead pair, Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif will reportedly reunite for the sequel. Tentatively titled 'Tiger Zinda Hai', the sequel will be directed by Ali Abbas Zafar, who directed 'Sultan'. The first instalment of the series was directed by Kabir Khan.
