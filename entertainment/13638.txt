After reports that Bollywood actor Tusshar Kapoor fathered a baby boy using surrogacy, 'Tusshar Kapoor' was the second most trending Google search in India on Monday. It was most looked up in Goa, Maharashtra, and Haryana. 'R D Burman', 'Wimbledon 2016', and 'Italy vs Spain' were some of the other trending searches on the same day.
