Actress Sonam Kapoor launched star-chef Vikas Khanna's new cookbook “Utsav–A Culinary Epic of Indian Festivals” at the 68th Cannes Film Festival on Sunday. Worth Rs 2 lakh, the 16kg book is about food in Indian festivals and is the world's most expensive cookbook. Chef Vikas Khanna owns the Manhattan restaurant Junoon and has Madonna and Tom Cruise for fans.
