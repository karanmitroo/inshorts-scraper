The trailer of the John Abraham and Sonakshi Sinha-starrer action thriller film 'Force 2' has been released. The film is a sequel to the 2011 film 'Force'. John will reprise his role as 'ACP Yashvardhan' while Sonakshi will portray the character of 'KK'. Directed by Abhinay Deo, the film is scheduled to release on November 18.
