The Salman Khan and Anushka Sharma starrer 'Sultan' was the most talked about film in 2016 on Twitter in India. The Ranbir Kapoor, Anushka Sharma and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan starrer 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' was the second most talked about film. This was followed by 'Shivaay' while 'Udta Punjab' ranked fourth on the list of the most talked about films.
