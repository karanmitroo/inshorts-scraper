As per reports, actor Salman Khan has asked filmmaker Puri Jagannadh to cast Sooraj Pancholi in a role written originally for Salman. The actor is said to consider Sooraj like part of his own family. Jagannadh is known for writing the story and screenplay of Salman's 2009 super-hit film 'Wanted', the Hindi adaptation of the Telugu film 'Pokiri'.
