The Sonam Kapoor starrer 'Neerja' was named the Best Picture at the 18th edition of IIFA Awards. For their performances in 'Neerja', actor Jim Sarbh won the Best Performance in a Negative Role award and actress Shabana Azmi bagged the Best Performance in a Supporting Role (Female) award. Meanwhile, filmmaker Aniruddha Roy Chowdhury won the Best Director award for 'Pink'.
