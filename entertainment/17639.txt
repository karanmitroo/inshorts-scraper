Ayushmann Khurrana and debutant Bhumi Pednekar-starrer 'Dum Laga Ke Haisha' has minted Rs 3.20 crore at the domestic box office in two days. The Yash Raj Film's latest offering managed to earn just Rs 1.11 crore on Friday. However, its collection almost doubled to Rs 2.09 crore on Saturday after receiving appreciation by critics as well as audiences.
