The teaser for 'All Eyez on Me', the biopic on rapper Tupac Shakur, has been released. Starring Demetrius Shipp Jr as Tupac, it revolves around his life up to his death at the age of 25 when he was killed in a shooting incident. Notably, the teaser was released on June 16, 2016, which would have been Tupac's 45th birthday.
