Actor Salman Khan, considered to be one of the most eligible bachelors in Bollywood, is the most searched celebrity online in India, a survey by Vuclip revealed. Moreover, the actor was also featured as the second most searched celebrity after Ayu Azhari, an Indonesian actress. The other top celebrities are Katrina Kaif(2), Sachin Tendulkar(3), Kim Kardashian(5) and Taylor Swift(8).
