Actress Anushka Sharma has tweeted a picture of Shashi, her 'friendly ghost' character from 'Phillauri', with the characters of the 1998 film 'Kuch Kuch Hota Hai'. "Koi mujhe bataye yeh teeno mujh pe ungli kyun utha rahein the," tweeted Anushka. Earlier, Anushka had also tweeted a photoshopped picture of her character on stage at Oscars 2017.
