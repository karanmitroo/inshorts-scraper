Emraan Hashmi's 'Raja Natwarlal', which released on Friday, portrays him as a small-time con-man Raja, seeking revenge on big-time swindler Vardha (Kay Kay Menon). "With its twisting story, good-looking frames and zingy acting, Raja Natwarlal keeps you entertained," according to TOI. The movie has been rated 3.5 (TOI), 1.5 (Indian Express), 2 (NDTV), 2 (HT) and 2.5 (India TV).
