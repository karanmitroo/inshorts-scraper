Actress Shabana Azmi has revealed her look from the upcoming film 'Idgah' on Twitter. The 65-year-old actress added that the film is being directed by Piyush Panjuani. The film is based on the short story 'Idgah' by Munshi Premchand, who is regarded as one of the greatest writers in Hindi Literature and is credited with writing around 250 short stories.
