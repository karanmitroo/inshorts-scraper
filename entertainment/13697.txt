A new poster for the Ajay Devgn starrer 'Shivaay' featuring the Polish actress Erika Kaar was revealed on Thursday. Directed and produced by Devgn under his banner 'Ajay Devgn FFilms', 'Shivaay' will be Kaar's debut in Bollywood where she portrays the character 'Olga'. Starring Sayyeshaa Saigal as the lead actress, the film is scheduled to release on October 28.
