Yash Raj Films and filmmaker Dibakar Banerjee have bought the whole library (30 stories) of the Bengali writer Sharadindu Bandyopadhyay's stories of famous fictional detective, Byomkesh Bakshi. The deal, worth Rs.8-10 crore, forbids the use of any of Byomkesh's stories in any language except Bengali. Film named Detective Byomkesh Bakshi, starring Sushant Singh Rajput, will release in December next year.
