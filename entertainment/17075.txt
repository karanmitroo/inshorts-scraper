Riteish Deshmukh on Tuesday shared the first picture of his son Riaan on social media platforms. In the picture captioned 'Riaan completes us', Riteish is seen cuddling him while Genelia kisses the actor from behind. The duo got married in 2012 and Riaan was born in Nov 2014. Ever since his birth, this is the first full glimpse of Riaan.
