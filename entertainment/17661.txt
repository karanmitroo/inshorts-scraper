Pakistani actor-singer Ali Zafar and his wife Ayesha Fazli became proud parents for the second time after they were blessed with a baby girl on Tuesday. The couple, who got married in 2009, also has a 4-year-old boy Azaan. Zafar, who made his Bollywood debut in 2010 with 'Tere Bin Laden', was last seen in 'Kill Dil'.
