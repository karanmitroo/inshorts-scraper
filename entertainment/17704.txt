Irrfan Khan and Tisca Chopra-starrer 'Qissa' is one of the best films in recent times whose experience cannot be forgotten easily, writes Hindustan Times. The Anup Singh directorial, which has Punjabi dialogues, is on gender politics and has been described as 'masterpiece' by critics. The movie has been rated 3.5/5 (Indian Express), 4/5 (NDTV) and 4.5/5 (HT, Zee News).
