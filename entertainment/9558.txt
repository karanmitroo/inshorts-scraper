Actress Aditi Rao Hydari will play the titular role in Sanjay Dutt's comeback film 'Bhoomi'. Dutt will portray the role of her father. "Aditi fits the role like a glove. She is what I want to see in my Bhoomi," said producer Bhushan Kumar.  The film will be directed by Omung Kumar. The shooting for 'Bhoomi' will begin in February.
