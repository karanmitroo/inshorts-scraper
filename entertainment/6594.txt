Actor Salman took to Twitter to react to actress Katrina Kaif being cast in the Aamir Khan starrer film 'Thugs of Hindostan'. Salman also made a reference to his upcoming film 'Tubelight' in the caption of his tweet. 'Thugs of Hindostan' will be the first film in which actors Aamir Khan and Amitabh Bachchan will feature together onscreen.
