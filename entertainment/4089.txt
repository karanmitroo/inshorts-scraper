Comedian Kapil Sharma has denied reports of actress Archana Puran Singh replacing Navjot Singh Sidhu on 'The Kapil Sharma Show' and said that he can never think of replacing Sidhu. "Whenever I plan to shoot, I...plan it along with Sidhuji...I feel he is lucky for me," added Kapil. Archana will shoot for a few episodes as Sidhu is ill.
