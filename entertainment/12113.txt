American band 'Maroon 5' vocalist Adam Levine's wife, model Behati Prinsloo has given birth to a baby girl, the couple's first child, in Los Angeles. The newborn child has been named Dusty Rose Levine. Adam, who married Behati in 2014, had earlier said, "I'm ready to be a dad. You're born to be a parent, that's what we're here for."
