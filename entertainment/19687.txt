Milan Luthria's 'Once Upon A Time in Mumbai Dobaara', which hit the theatres on the eve of Independence Day, has been given a rating of 1.5, 1.5 and 2.5 by The Indian Express, IBN Live and Mumbai Mirror respectively. The cast of the movie includes Akshay Kumar, Imran Khan, Sonakshi Sinha, Pitobash Tripathy, Mahesh Manjrekar and Sonali Bendre.
