The trailer of the Priyanka Chopra starrer film 'Jai Gangaajal' was the sixth most-viewed video on YouTube in India on Monday. The video, which was released on the video-sharing platform on December 22, has garnered over 41 lakh views so far. The film is a sequel to the 2003 film 'Gangaajal' and is scheduled to release on March 4.
