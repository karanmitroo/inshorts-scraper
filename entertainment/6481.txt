As per reports, actress Aishwarya Rai and her husband Abhishek Bachchan have refused to star in  Anurag Kashyap's film Gulab Jamun. It was said that the film's script did not excite them enough to come on-screen together. Aishwarya and Abhishek have worked together in films like Kuch Naa Kaho, Guru and Sarkar Raj.
