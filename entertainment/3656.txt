Actor Ssharad Malhotra, while talking about people who troll celebrities on social media, said, "Usko do log toh jaante hain. Aapko toh kutta bhi nahi jaanta, sorry to say." Ssharad added, "Even if it's a newcomer (celebrity) who is made fun of... saying that he cannot emote, can't act, can you (trolls) do what he is doing?"
