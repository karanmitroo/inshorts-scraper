The Sushant Singh Rajput starrer biopic 'MS Dhoni: The Untold Story' and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan's film 'Sarbjit' have featured in the Oscars' long list for the Best Picture. Notably, these are the only 2 Hindi films on the list. The list, featuring 336 films, also include 'Doctor Strange', 'Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them', and 'Captain America: Civil War'.
