Osho Jain, a student at Mumbai's True School of Music, has recreated the 'Kabira' song from movie 'Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani' to 'India's Startup Anthem' titled 'Mera Bhi Karobar'. The song is about the India's startup culture coming into existence and attracting the youth. The 3-minute and 30-seconds song also explains how an entrepreneur thinks about his business.
