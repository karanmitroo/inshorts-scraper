The trailer of Nagesh Kukunoor's upcoming film 'Dhanak' has been released on YouTube. The film revolves around a brother-sister duo, aged 8 and 10, who travels across Rajasthan to meet Bollywood actor Shah Rukh Khan. The film features Hetal Gada and Krrish Chhabria in lead roles. It was honoured at the 65th Berlin International Film Festival in 2015.
