Rapper Honey Singh, who collaborated with Shah Rukh Khan for the song 'Thalaivar Tribute-Lungi Dance', said he would work with SRK, whenever the actor asked. 'Thalaivar Tribute' is dedicated to veteran actor Rajinikanth, whom his fans gave the tiltle of 'Thalaivar' (supreme leader). He also praised Shah Rukh saying he was a source of inspiration to all outsiders in Bollywood.
