Following the launch of its teaser, director Rajkumar Hirani has decided to launch the first song of Aamir Khan-starrer 'PK' on Saturday in Delhi, where the movie has been extensively shot. The song - "Tharki chokro" - is a dedication from Sanjay Dutt to Aamir. Rajasthani singer Swaroop Khan has been roped to be the voice of the song.
