Following India captain Virat Kohli and actress Anushka Sharma's wedding reception in Mumbai on Tuesday, India all-rounder Yuvraj Singh took to Twitter to share a picture of himself with the actress and called her 'Rosie phabie'. Yuvraj captioned the picture, "Saadi rosie phabie officially now wishing you both a partnership of lifetime @AnushkaSharma @imVkohli."
