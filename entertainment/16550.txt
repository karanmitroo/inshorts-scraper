After sparking controversy in 'MSG: The Messenger', Sirsa Dera Sacha Sauda chief Gurmeet Ram Rahim Singh Insan is back with the film's sequel. The official teaser of 'MSG 2' has been released on YouTube. The film will release on 18th September 2015. It revolves around the reformation of tribals from their alienated lives in jungles to the mainstream social life.
