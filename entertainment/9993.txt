As per reports, actress Kajol will be playing a negative role in the Tamil film 'VIP 2', which also stars Dhanush and is the sequel to 'Velaiyilla Pattathari'. Notably, Kajol became the first female actor to be nominated and win the Filmfare Award for 'Best Performance in a Negative Role'. This was for her role in the 1997 film 'Gupt'.
