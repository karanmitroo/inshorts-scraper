The first look of actor Tiger Shroff from his upcoming film 'Baaghi' was shared on Twitter on Wednesday by the film's actress Shraddha Kapoor, as the former turned 26 years old. "Presenting... MY FAVORITE REBEL! #BAAGHI HAPPY BIRTHDAY @iTIGERSHROFF <3 #BeARebel," Shraddha captioned the picture. The Sabbir Khan directorial is scheduled to release on April 29 this year.
