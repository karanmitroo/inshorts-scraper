Starring Saif Ali Khan in the lead, 'Happy Ending' is a "predictable romantic comedy disguised as a cheeky comment on the predictable nature of romantic comedies", writes IBN Live. After being acclaimed for films like 'Shor in the City', Raj Nidimoru and Krishna DK's new film is given 2/5 (IBN Live), 2.5/5 (HT, India Today, NDTV) and 1.5 (Indian Express).
