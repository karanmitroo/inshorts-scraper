'Tukur Tukur', the new song from Rohit Shetty's upcoming film 'Dilwale' was released on Sunday. The song features the lead actors of the film, namely, Shah Rukh Khan, Kajol, Varun Dhawan and Kriti Sanon. It has been composed by Pritam and sung by Arijit Singh, Kanika Kapoor, Neha kakkar, Nakash Aziz and Siddharth Madhvan. 'Dilwale' will release on December 18.
