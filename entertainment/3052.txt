Kangana Ranaut's sister Rangoli Chandel, while responding to Hrithik's allegation that Kangana suffers from a mental disorder, claimed that Hrithik was in a suicidal state of mind after his brain surgery in 2014. She further claimed that Hrithik was also battling depression. Rangoli added that Hrithik has no medical proof that Kangana was suffering from a mental disorder.
