Farhan Akhtar and Arjun Rampal have been finalised for the sequel of 2008 cult musical ‘Rock On’ that will go on floors in Jan-2015. While producer Ritesh Sidhwani has retained Farhan, Arjun and Purab Kohli, search for rest of the cast is on. Abhishek Kapoor, who had directed the original, is learnt to be replaced by ad filmmaker Shujaat Saudagar.
