The trailer of the Kirti Kulhari, Neil Nitin Mukesh and Anupam Kher starrer Indu Sarkar, set in the backdrop of the 1975 emergency in India, was released on Friday. While Neil Nitin plays the role of Sanjay Gandhi in the film, Supriya Vinod has portrayed former PM Indira Gandhi. Directed by Madhur Bhandarkar, the film will release on July 28.
