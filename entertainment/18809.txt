Bollywood actress Sunny Leone will now be seen as a princess in her next film, tentatively titled Leela. The movie will be produced by Bhushan Kumar, Ahmed Khan and Shaira Khan and written-directed by Bobby Khan. This will be Sunny's 6th Hindi film as lead actress, after 'Jism-2', 'Jackpot' and 'Ragini MMS 2', and unreleased 'Mastizaade' and 'Tina and Lolo'.
