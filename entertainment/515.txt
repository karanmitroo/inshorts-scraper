With earnings of ₹10.26 crore on its first day, 'Pad Man' has become Akshay Kumar's lowest opening day grosser in the last three years. His previous release 'Toilet- Ek Prem Katha' earned ₹13.1 crore on its first day. Meanwhile, the 2016 film 'Rustom' minted ₹14.11 crore. His other major releases in the last three years include 'Brothers' and 'Airlift'.
