The male star cast of 'Housefull 3', namely Akshay Kumar, Riteish Deshmukh and Abhishek Bachchan, on Tuesday released a spoof picture of Deepika Padukone and Vin Diesel's photograph that went viral recently. Sharing the recreation of the original photograph on Twitter, Akshay Kumar posted, "Since Vin Diesel is busy with @deepikapadukone, we at #Housefull3 are making do with Vin Petrol."
