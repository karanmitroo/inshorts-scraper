The teaser of 'Salute Siachen', which will show the journey of 9 celebrities trekking to the Siachen glacier as a tribute to the Indian army, has been released. Actors including Arjun Rampal, Arunoday Singh, cricketer Rudra Pratap Singh and VJ Rannvijay Singha participated in the 20-day expedition. The initiative was undertaken to mark the 35th anniversary of the Siachen expedition.
