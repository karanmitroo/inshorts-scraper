'Bajirao Mastani' was named Best Film at the 61st Britannia Filmfare Awards 2016, held in Mumbai on Friday. Sanjay Leela Bhansali bagged the Best Director award and Ranveer Singh was named Best Actor for 'Bajirao Mastani'. Deepika Padukone bagged the Best Actress award for her role in 'Piku'. Neeraj Ghaywan won the Best Debut Director award for 'Masaan'.
