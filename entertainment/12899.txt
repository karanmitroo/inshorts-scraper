The trailer for 'Resident Evil: The Final Chapter', the sixth and final instalment in the 'Resident Evil'  film franchise, has been released. Actress Milla Jovovich reprises her role as 'Alice' in the film, which is a sequel to the 2012 film 'Resident Evil: Retribution'. Directed by Paul WS Anderson, the film is scheduled to release on January 27, 2017.
