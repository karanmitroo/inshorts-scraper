Actor Aamir Khan has said he is not interested in the box office collections of his film 'Dangal', which released on Friday. "I am concerned about the audience's reaction... just want the film to touch their hearts," the 51-year-old actor added. Aamir portrays wrestler Mahavir Singh Phogat in the film, who taught wrestling to daughters Babita Kumari and Geeta Phogat.
