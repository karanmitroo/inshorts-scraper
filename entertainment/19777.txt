Kamal Haasan's ambitious project Vishwaroop-2's first-look is out, showing Haasan in Muslim attire. The film also stars Rahul Bose, Shekhar Kapoor, Pooja Kumar, Andrea Jeremiah and Jaideep Ahlawat. The actor, who is also the writer and director, said he had written the story in two parts and doesn't feel too hurried about it, considering the controversies over the first part.
