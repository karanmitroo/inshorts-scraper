Bollywood actress Priyanka Chopra will be seen playing the role of a female cop in Prakash Jha's 'Gangaajal 2'. Confirming the news at a recent pre-award bash,  Priyanka said, "In Gangaajal I'm playing cop - Ajay Devgn's female counterpart." According to Prakash Jha, the sequel of 'Gangaajal' is an action flick about today's dictum of society-police relationship.
