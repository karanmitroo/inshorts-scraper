Bhaag Milkha Bhaag, starring Farhan Akhtar, has entered the Rs.100 Crore milestone at the Indian box office in its fourth weekend. The film added approximately Rs.5 Crore over the weekend, taking its 24-day-total to Rs.101 Crore. The film's worldwide numbers currently stand at Rs.115 Crore. With this, BMB has beaten Race 2, to become this year's second highest grosser.
