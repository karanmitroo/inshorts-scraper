Veteran actress Neetu Kapoor took to Instagram to share a picture from her 59th birthday celebration with son Ranbir Kapoor and husband Rishi Kapoor. Neetu's daughter Riddhima Kapoor Sahni and her six-year-old granddaughter Samara Sahni can also be seen in the picture. Actress Kareena Kapoor's mother Babita also attended the party.
