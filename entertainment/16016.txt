Deepika Padukone and Ranbir Kapoor starrer 'Tamasha' which released on Friday has managed to earn ₹38.23 crore in India in its opening weekend. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the Imtiaz Ali directorial earned ₹10.94 crore on Friday, ₹13.17 crore on Saturday and ₹14.12 crore on Sunday. Further, the film's worldwide collection has reportedly crossed ₹50 crore mark.
