A video shows Fatima Sana Shaikh and Sanya Malhotra, who starred as the Phogat sisters 'Geeta' and 'Babita' in 'Dangal', dancing to the song 'Do Mastane Chale' from the 1994 film 'Andaz Apna Apna', which featured Aamir Khan. While some of the clips show them dancing, there are also some clips of them with Dangal's director Nitesh Tiwari.
