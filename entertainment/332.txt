Talking about her song 'Nain Phisal Gaye' from the upcoming film 'Welcome To New York', Sonakshi Sinha said, "The combination of Sajid-Wajid, Salman Khan and my 'nain' is always a superhit." Salman has made a cameo appearance in the song. Sajid-Wajid were also the music directors of Salman and Sonakshi's song 'Tere Mast Mast Do Nain' from 2010 film 'Dabangg'.
