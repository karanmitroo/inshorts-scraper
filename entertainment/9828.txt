'Pehli Dafa', a new song by Pakistani singer Atif Aslam has been released. The music video of the song features Bollywood actress Ileana D'Cruz. The romantic song has been composed by Shiraz Uppal with lyrics penned by Shakeel Sohail. Atif has done playback singing for numerous Bollywood films, and is known for songs like 'Aadat' and 'Wo Lamhey', among others.
