According to reports, actors Anil Kapoor and Madhuri Dixit have been approached to star in 'Total Dhamaal', the third instalment in the 'Dhamaal' film series. Directed by Indra Kumar, the film will also star Ajay Devgn. The previous films starred Sanjay Dutt, Arshad Warsi, Riteish Deshmukh, Jaaved Jaaferi and Ashish Chowdhry.
