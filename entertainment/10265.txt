The making of 'The Humma Song' from the Aditya Roy Kapur and Shraddha Kapoor starrer 'OK Jaanu' has been released. The song is a recreation of the 'Hamma Hamma' song from the  1995 film 'Bombay'. "I love both the versions," said Aditya and Shraddha also agreed that she liked both while adding, "They have some similarities and many differences."
