With the trend of sequels getting popular in Bollywood, producer Bhushan Kumar has expressed his desire to take ahead 3-projects as a franchise, including 'Aashiqui' - which recently had a hit sequel 'Aashiqui-2', ghost comedy 'Bhootnath' and the 3D horror movie 'Creature'. "These days franchises are a click with the audience. This pattern is followed in Hollywood," said Kumar.
