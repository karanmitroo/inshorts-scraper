While talking about Miss World 2017 and Mr World 2016 titles, which have been won by India's Manushi Chhillar and Rohit Khandelwal respectively, Mr India 2017 Jitesh Singh Deo said the bar has been set by them. Deo, who will compete at Mr World 2020, added, "I'll take proper guidance from Rohit and Manushi, and work smart accordingly."
