Actor Amitabh Bachchan has completed 17 years as the host of the television game show 'Kaun Banega Crorepati'. Amitabh took to Twitter to share a picture from the show's set. The caption read, "Just back from work! and a day filled with reliving 17 years of KBC and my term with it...17 years !! Goodness that is a lifetime".
