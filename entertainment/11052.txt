The Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor starrer 'Befikre' will have its world premiere on December 8 at the Dubai International Film Festival (DIFF). "This refreshing new film will appeal... to the expatriate community... to Arabs who have a strong affinity for Bollywood films," said DIFF chairman Abdulhamid Juma. Yash Raj Films previously showcased films like 'Kabul Express' at DIFF.
