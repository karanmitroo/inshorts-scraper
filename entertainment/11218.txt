Actress Priyanka Chopra has done a scene, where she has spoken dialogues in Hindi for an episode in the American TV series 'Quantico'. "Loved doing a scene in Hindi on #Quantico #homeawayfromhome #misshome," tweeted Priyanka. In the scene, the actress' character 'Alex Parrish' is shown in captivity when a stranger, who starts conversing with her in Hindi, helps her escape.
