Director-producer Ram Gopal Varma has landed into legal trouble for posting offensive tweet about Saint Gurmeet Ram Rahim Singh Insaan. A hurt devotee of Gurmeet Ram Rahim Singh has filed a police complaint against RGV for calling the 'Godman' an 'ass' on Twitter. The FIR has been filed under Section 298 (uttering words to hurt religious sentiments) of the IPC.
