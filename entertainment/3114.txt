Varun Dhawan, Jacqueline Fernandez and Taapsee Pannu starrer 'Judwaa 2', with earnings of over ₹59 crore, has become the second highest opening weekend grosser for the lead actor. Varun's highest opening weekend grosser remains the 2015 film 'Dilwale', which earned ₹65.09 crore in its first weekend. 'Judwaa 2' also became the fifth highest grosser among Hindi language films this year.
