Actress Kriti Sanon wore a pink lehenga choli made of organic cotton derived from milk and soybean, for designer duo Sukriti and Akriti's show at the India Beach Fashion Week (IBFW) 2017. The lehenga featured golden floral detailing. The collection, titled 'Unicorn Tribe', included lehengas, shararas, deconstructed sarees, kurtas and palazzos in colours like pink, white, blue, green and pastels.
