The poster of Ajay Devgn's upcoming production 'Sons Of Sardaar' has been unveiled. Devgn, while sharing the poster, tweeted about the film, "My tribute to Warriors of Saragarhi: A tale of Rage, of Love, of Bravery." The film revolves around the story of how 21 Sikhs fought to death against 10,000 Afghans in the Battle of Saragarhi in 1897.
