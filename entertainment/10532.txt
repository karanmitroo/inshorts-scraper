'Kaabil Hoon', the new song from the Hrithik Roshan and Yami Gautam starrer upcoming film 'Kaabil' has been released. The song has been composed by Rajesh Roshan, with vocals by Jubin Nautiyal and Palak Muchhal, and lyrics by Nasir Faraaz. Produced by Rakesh Roshan, the film is scheduled to release on January 25, 2017.
