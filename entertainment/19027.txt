After Rowdy Rathore (2012), Akshay Kumar and Prabhu Dheva will unite once again as Akshay is essaying the lead in this untitled venture under his banner, Grazing Goat Pictures, co-founded with Ashvini Yardi. "Akshay is excited to be teaming up again with Prabhu. The venture will be one of the biggest so far from our production house," Ashvini says.
