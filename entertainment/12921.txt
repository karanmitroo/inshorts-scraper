The Salman Khan and Anushka Sharma-starrer 'Sultan' has minted over ₹300 crore in India, becoming the third Bollywood film to do so. Achieving the feat in 35 days, 'Sultan' also became Salman's second film to collect ₹300 crore. Aamir Khan's 'PK' (2014) was the first film to mint ₹300 crore in India, followed by Salman's 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' (2015).
