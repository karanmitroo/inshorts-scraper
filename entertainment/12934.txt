The Ranbir Kapoor and Katrina Kaif starrer 'Jagga Jasoos' is scheduled to release on April 7, 2017. Directed by Anurag Basu and with music composed by Pritam, the film revolves around Ranbir as 'Jagga', a teenage detective who is searching for his missing father. The film has been shot in various locations including Darjeeling, Cape Town and Thailand.
