Actress Priyanka Chopra is set to be honoured by the American fashion magazine 'InStyle' as the 'Breakthrough Style Star'. The 34-year-old actress will be honoured on October 24 at the second annual 'InStyle Awards', which honours actors, actresses and artists whose style defines the red carpet. Actresses Nicole Kidman and Shailene Woodley and designer Tom Ford are among other honourees.
