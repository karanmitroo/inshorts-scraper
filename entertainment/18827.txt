Actress Jacqueline Fernandez shared the first look of Salman Khan's 'Kick' with her fans on Twitter. 'Oh my god!!! It's happening!!!! So excited for this," she tweeted while unveiling the much awaited look. 'Kick', which also features Jacqueline, is Sajid Nadiadwala's debut directorial venture  and is set to release on Eid this year.
