Tourism Minister KJ Alphons has said the government might rope in Hollywood stars like Richard Gere, Julia Roberts, and Angelina Jolie as brand ambassadors for Incredible India 2 campaign. "Actually, we are looking for celebrities who have got global appeal," Alphons added. Earlier Bollywood actors Aamir Khan, Amitabh Bachchan, and Priyanka Chopra endorsed and promoted the campaign.
