Singer Justin Timberlake's song 'Can't Stop The Feeling' has been named the 'worst song' of 2016 by 'Time' magazine. The song, part of 2016 animated film 'Trolls', has a video featuring Gwen Stefani, Anna Kendrick, James Corden and Kunal Nayyar. Meghan Trainor's song 'Mom' and Iggy Azalea's 'Team' were named the second and third worst songs of the year respectively.
