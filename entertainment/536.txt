The Akshay Kumar starrer 'Pad Man', which released today, "scores high on content as well as emotions," wrote Bollywood Hungama. "Akshay delivers a film that India needed, period," wrote Hindustan Times while Times of India wrote that it "makes for an important film that needs to be watched." The film was rated 4/5 (Bollywood Hungama), 3/5 (HT) and 3.5/5 (TOI).
