The adult star-turned-Bollywood actress Sunny Leone, who was searched 3.5 crore times in June-2013, is the most-searched Indian celebrity on Google. No other Indian celebrity has ever been searched so many times. Katrina Kaif ranks second with 1.15 crore searches. While Salman Khan is the only man in the top-5, King of Bollywood Shah Rukh Khan doesn't feature in top-10.
