The poster of the upcoming film 'Mubarakan', starring Arjun Kapoor for the first time with his uncle Anil Kapoor, has been revealed. Sharing the poster, Arjun tweeted, "When @AnilKapoor & me combine there's bound to be double the madness double the fun!!! It's time to celebrate, its time for #Mubarakan!!!" The film is scheduled to release on July 28.
