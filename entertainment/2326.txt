Actress Amrita Puri, who made her Bollywood debut with the Sonam Kapoor starrer 'Aisha', married her long-time boyfriend and hotelier Imrun Sethi in Bangkok as per Sikh rituals. The ceremony, which took place on Saturday, was attended by actresses Sandhya Mridul, Eisha Chopra and Nauheed Cyrusi. Amrita also featured in Sushant Singh Rajput and Rajkummar Rao starrer 'Kai Po Che!'.
