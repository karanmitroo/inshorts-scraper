'Rustom Vahi', the title track from the Akshay Kumar starrer 'Rustom' has been released. Picturised on the lead stars Akshay, Ileana D'Cruz and Esha Gupta, the song has been composed by Raghav Sachar and sung by Sukriti Kakar. Directed by Tinu Suresh Desai, the film also stars Arjan Bajwa and is scheduled to release on August 12.
