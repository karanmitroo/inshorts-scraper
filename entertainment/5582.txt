Salman Khan's 'Tubelight' has collected ₹21.15 crore on its opening day in India, making it the second-highest opening day Hindi grosser of 2017. With earnings of ₹41 crore, the Hindi version of 'Baahubali 2' currently holds the record of being 2017's highest opening day grosser. Produced on a ₹100 crore budget, 'Tubelight' reportedly released in over 5000 screens across India.
