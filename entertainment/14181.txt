A new trailer of the Salman Khan and Anushka Sharma starrer film 'Sultan' was released on Tuesday.  The trailer, besides featuring Salman and Anushka, also introduces the characters played by Randeep Hooda and Amit Sadh in the film. Directed by Ali Abbas Zafar, the sports-drama has been produced by Aditya Chopra and is slated for an Eid release this year.
