Ranveer Singh has been approached to play the lead role in the film 'Sher Singh', a sequel to the 2008 Akshay Kumar starrer 'Singh is Kinng'. Talking about the project, producer Shailendra Singh said, "He (character Sher Singh) doesn't have super powers, but will be an extraordinary human being." Shailendra added Ranveer has all the characteristics to play a sardar.
