Actress Fatima Sana Shaikh, known for starring in 'Dangal', celebrated the occasion of Eid al-Adha with Aamir Khan and his family. She shared a story on Instagram, which shows her with Aamir's daughter Ira, and captioned it 'Eid!'. Fatima will be next seen in the film 'Thugs of Hindostan', which will also star Aamir Khan.
