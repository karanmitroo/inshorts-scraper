'Kaara Fankaara', a new song from the Aditya Roy Kapur and Shraddha Kapoor starrer upcoming film 'OK Jaanu' has been released. The song has been composed by AR Rahman with vocals by Shashaa Tirupati, Ashima Mahajan, Paroma Dasgupta. Navneet Virk, Kaly, Hard Kaur and ADK have penned the lyrics. Directed by Shaad Ali, 'OK Jaanu' will release on January 13.
