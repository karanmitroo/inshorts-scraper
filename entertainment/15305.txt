The Sonam Kapoor-starrer film 'Neerja', which released today, is a "milestone in Sonam's career", Hindustan Times wrote. "Ram Madhvani has crafted a story that will move the coldest of hearts", wrote India Today. "The atmosphere of dread and violence is created well", The Indian Express said. The film has been rated 4/5 (HT, India Today, NDTV) and 3/5 (Indian Express).
