With earnings of ₹232.83 crore, actor Salman Khan has topped the Forbes India Celebrity 100 list for the second consecutive year. While Shah Rukh Khan featured in the second spot with earnings of ₹170.50 crore, Indian cricket team captain Virat Kohli came third with ₹100.72 crore earnings. Akshay Kumar and Sachin Tendulkar featured in the fourth and fifth spots respectively.
