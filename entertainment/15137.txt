Vicky Kaushal, Manish Chaudhari and Sarah Jane Dias starrer 'Zubaan' which hit the screens on Friday has "music and choreography which gives the movie an extra edge over other musicals", wrote HT. The film's plot dips and its tone drops in some parts, said Indian Express. The Mozez Singh directorial has been rated 2.5/5 (Indian Express) and 3.5/5 (HT, NDTV).
