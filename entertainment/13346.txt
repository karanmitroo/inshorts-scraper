Actor Salman Khan has confirmed​ that he will be starring in the Marathi film 'Shivaji' alongside Riteish Deshmukh. "I have five-six days work. I have a good role,” said Salman. Riteish will play the titular role of the Indian warrior king 'Chhatrapati Shivaji' in the film. Earlier, Salman also made a guest appearance in the Marathi film 'Lai Bhaari'.
