Actress Mallika Sherawat made her debut in the 2002 film 'Jeena Sirf Merre Liye', featuring Kareena Kapoor and Tusshar Kapoor. Mallika, who turned 40 on Monday, played the role of Tusshar Kapoor's girlfriend in the film. She was credited as Reema Lamba, her real name, which she changed before 'Khwahish' (2003), her first film as a leading actress.
