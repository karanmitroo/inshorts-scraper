The official trailer of 'Kapoor & Sons', a Shakun Batra directorial, was released on YouTube on Wednesday. The Sidharth Malhotra, Alia Bhatt and Fawad Khan starrer is scheduled to release on March 18, 2016. In the film showing love triangle between the three, Sidharth and Fawad are playing brothers. It is being produced under Karan Johar's Dharma Productions banner.
