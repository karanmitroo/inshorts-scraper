Amitabh Bachchan has tweeted birthday wishes for Hollywood actor Leonardo DiCaprio, as he turned 42 on Friday. He referred to DiCaprio as a "colleague", "gentleman" and a "concerned climate change actor" while sharing the wishes. Bachchan made a cameo appearance as the character 'Meyer Wolfsheim' in the 2013 Hollywood film 'The Great Gatsby', which starred DiCaprio in a lead role.
