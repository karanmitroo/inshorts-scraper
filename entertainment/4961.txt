Actress Bhumi Pednekar, who turned 28 on Tuesday, has said that she got a confirmation call for her debut film Dum Laga Ke Haisha on her 25th birthday. Bhumi added, "I didn't know how to react because I remember going back to sleep again. I was in shock I guess." Dum Laga Ke Haisha also starred Ayushmann Khurrana.
