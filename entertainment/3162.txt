Varun Dhawan, Jacqueline Fernandez and Taapsee Pannu's 'Judwaa 2' is "a decent paisa-vasool entertainer", wrote Bollywood Hungama. Times of India wrote, "Varun doesn't let you down...Whether he is playing a geek or a goon, he is absolutely endearing," while Desimartini called it "a refreshing, honest, entertaining potboiler". The film was rated 3.5/5 (Bollywood Hungama, Desimartini) and 3/5 (TOI).
