Director Ali Abbas Zafar on Saturday took to Twitter to share actress Anushka Sharma's look in his upcoming film, 'Sultan'. Zafar captioned the picture, 'Haryane ki Sherni dhobi pachad master'. The 27-year-old actress will be starring alongside Salman Khan in the film which is set to release on July 8. 'Sultan' also features Randeep Hooda and Amit Sadh.
