Varun Dhawan, while sharing a picture with Salman Khan and referring to the double role in 'Judwaa', tweeted, "Thank you for being my Raja and Prem." He further wrote, "I'm not influenced from any Hollywood actor but influenced by Salman Khan." While Salman played the double role in 'Judwaa', Varun played the characters Raja and Prem in 'Judwaa 2'.
