Bigg Boss 11 contestant Sapna Chaudhary features in the recreation of Haryanavi song 'Hatt Ja Tau' for the film 'Veerey Ki Wedding'. The song has been sung by Sunidhi Chauhan and remixed by Bhanu Thakur. Starring Pulkit Samrat, Jimmy Sheirgill, Kriti Kharbanda, Yuvika Choudhary and Satish Kaushik, the film is scheduled to release on March 2.
