Dharma Productions on Monday tweeted that the Shraddha Kapoor and Aditya Roy Kapur starrer 'OK Jaanu' will release on January 13, 2017. Directed by Shaad Ali, the film is a remake of Mani Ratnam's Tamil film ‘Ok Kanmani’. This will be the second time that Shraddha and Aditya will be paired together after the 2013 film ‘Aashiqui 2’.
