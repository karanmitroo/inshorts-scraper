Filmmaker Karan Johar has revealed that Aishwarya Rai plays a poetess in his upcoming directorial venture 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil'. Speaking about Aishwarya and actress Anushka Sharma's characters, Johar added, "They are both incredibly strong and complex women characters... and they are immensely progressive characters." He further revealed that everyone in the film has a broken-hearted situation.
