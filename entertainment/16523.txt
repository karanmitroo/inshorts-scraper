Hollywood film 'Ricki And The Flash', which released this week, "is worth a watch", said Zee News. The Meryl Streep-starrer has very powerful performances, wrote TOI. In the film, Streep plays a mother of three who leaves her family behind to follow her dreams of becoming a rockstar. It has been rated 4/5 (HT), 3/5 (TOI) and 2.5/5 (Zee News).
