The Kangana Ranaut, Shahid Kapoor and Saif Ali Khan starrer 'Rangoon' is "gorgeous-looking with great cinematography, fantastic sound design and marvellous choreography," wrote India Today. "[O]ddly written characters don't let it become an engaging watch," said Hindustan Times. The Times of India wrote that "some scenes feel tedious". It has been rated 2/5 (HT) and 3.5/5 (TOI, India Today).
