Aimed to boost its market share in India, brand Coca-Cola has reportedly roped in actress Deepika Padukone as its face. Deepika, who was the face of Coca-Cola's rival Pepsi till 2008-09, would be paid about Rs.4 crore annually in fees. Aishwarya Rai was the previous top woman celebrity associated with Coca-Cola, endorsing the brand for about six years ending 2005.
