Deepika Padukone, Ranveer Singh and Shahid Kapoor took to social media to share the official logo of their upcoming film 'Padmavati' and wrote, "Rani Padmavati padhar rahi hai... kal suryoday ke saath." Earlier, it was reported that the film's teaser will be released on the onset of Navratri. The film has been directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali.
