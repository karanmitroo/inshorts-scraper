Actors Daisy Shah and Saqib Saleem will feature in the Salman Khan starrer upcoming film 'Race 3', confirmed the film's producer Ramesh Taurani. The film also stars Bobby Deol and Jacqueline Fernandez. While Salman will be paired opposite Jacqueline, Bobby will be seen paired opposite Daisy. It was also reported that Aditya Pancholi will play the villain in 'Race 3'.
