Despite the pre-Diwali fever, Hritik Roshan starrer 'Krrish-3', which released on Friday, collected Rs25crore on its opening day and Rs 72.8crore in its opening weekend. As the movie is running with packed houses in the morning itself, Monday is also expected to be humungous. Made at a budget of about Rs100-150crore, this superhero-drama is directed and produced by Rakesh Roshan.
