Debutant director Abhishek Dogra's "Dolly Ki Doli", which features Sonam Kapoor in the lead role, was released on Friday. If there is anything that makes Dolly Ki Doli worth a watch, it is the performances, writes Hindustan Times. The movie that also stars Rajkumar Rao has been rated 3/5 (TOI), 2.5/5 (NDTV), 2/5 (HT) and 1.5/5 (Indian Express).
