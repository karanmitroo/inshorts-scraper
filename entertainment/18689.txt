Allu Arjun and Shruthi Haasan starrer ‘Race Gurram’, which released on Apr-11, completed 100 days at the box-office Saturday in 26 centers across Andhra Pradesh. Touted to be the biggest blockbuster in Arjun's career, the film has collected Rs 60.82 crore nett worldwide, making it to the list of top 5 all-time grossers in the history of Telugu cinema.
