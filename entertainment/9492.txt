The Sunil Grover starrer 'Coffee With D', which released on Friday, "is beyond awful", wrote Indian Express. Hindustan Times wrote, "Grover's film is a satire meant to offend no one." "A story that has potential is ruined by poor execution that distracts you from the plot," wrote Times of India. It was rated 1/5 (Indian Express) and 2/5 (HT, TOI).
