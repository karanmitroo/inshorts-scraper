Mubarak Eid Mubarak, the first song from the Arjun Rampal starrer Daddy, was released on Monday on the occasion of Eid. The qawwali has been sung by Shabab Sabri and Tanvir Hussain with music by Sajid Wajid and lyrics by Danish Sabri. Based on the life of gangster-turned-politician Arun Gawli, Daddy is scheduled to release on July 21.
