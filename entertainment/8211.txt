The first look of science fiction adventure film 'Jurassic World 2' was unveiled on Twitter by the film's director JA Bayona. The film will be a sequel to the 2015 film 'Jurassic World', and the fifth instalment in the 'Jurassic Park' film series. Starring Chris Pratt, Bryce Howard and Toby Jones among others, it will release on June 22, 2018.
