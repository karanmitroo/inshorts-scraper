The release date of Salman Khan starrer 'Kick 2', which will be a sequel to the 2014 film 'Kick', has been announced as Christmas 2019. The sequel will be directed by Sajid Nadiadwala, who also directed the first film. It has also been reported that Deepika Padukone will replace Jacqueline Fernandez as the lead actress in 'Kick 2'.
