Google has named the Aamir Khan starrer 'PK' as the most searched Indian film in the last 10 years. Other films among the most searched include 'Kahaani', 'Baahubali', 'Aashiqui 2', 'Dhoom 3' and 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan'. Further, filmmaker Prabhu Deva was the most searched director while Karan Johar, Farhan Akhtar, Raj Kapoor and Farah Khan were also among most searched directors.
