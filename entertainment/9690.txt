Amul on Friday released a poster to mark Deepika Padukone's Hollywood debut with the film 'xXx: Return of Xander Cage'. The poster was captioned, "Deepika's AdVinture", referring to actor Vin Diesel, Deepika's co-star in the action film. It further reads, "Amul Lipxxxxxmacking!". Meanwhile, Diesel is currently in India to promote the film, which is scheduled to release on January 14.
