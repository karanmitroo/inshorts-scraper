As per reports, actor Varun Dhawan will portray Indian hockey legend Dhyan Chand in an upcoming biopic. Producers Pooja Shetty and Aarti Shetty, who have the rights of making the biopic on Chand, have reportedly approached filmmaker Karan Johar for the project. Notably, Johar is said to have initially approached Ranbir Kapoor for the role but Ranbir turned it down.
