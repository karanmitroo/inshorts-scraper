Amitabh Bachchan, Deepika Padukone and Irrfan Khan starrer, Piku, has minted Rs 14.02 crore in first two days of its release, announced industry trade analyst Taran Adarsh on Sunday. The film has grown via 'word of mouth' and earned Rs 5.32 crore at the box office on the opening day and Rs 8.70 crore on Saturday.
