Veteran Tamil actor SS Rajendran (85) passed away Friday morning due to issues related to lung infection. He was admitted to a private Hospital in critical condition on Thursday in Chennai. Rajendran, who made his cinematic debut with 1952 Tamil film "Parasakthi", acted in over 50 films like "Ratha Kanner", "Rangoon Radha" and "Sivagangai Seemai".
