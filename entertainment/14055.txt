Riteish Deshmukh and wife Genelia D'Souza were blessed with a baby boy on Wednesday. The couple got married in 2012 and had their first baby, Riaan, in 2014. Announcing the news on Twitter, Riteish shared a picture of Riaan with the caption, "[M]y Aai & Baba just gifted me a little brother. Now all my toys are his...- Love Riaan".
