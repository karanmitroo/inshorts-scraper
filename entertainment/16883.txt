John Abraham-starrer 'Welcome Back' will hit the screens on September 4 this year, the actor announced on Twitter on Tuesday. The multi-starrer film also features Anil Kapoor, Paresh Rawal, Nana Patekar and Shruti Haasan. The Anees Bazmee directorial is a sequel to the 2007 hit comedy film 'Welcome' which starred Akshay Kumar, Katrina Kaif along with Kapoor, Patekar and Rawal.
