In the recently released first look poster of 'Padmavati', actress Deepika Padukone can be seen sporting a unibrow. Reacting to this a user tweeted, "Deepika's unibrow in Padmavati makes me look back at my seventh grade photos with pride." Meanwhile, another user wrote, "If only Indians were outraged by censorship, lynching and systemic oppression as much as Deepika's unibrow."
