Colombian drug lord Pablo Escobar’s son Sebastian Marroquin has slammed Netflix original series 'Narcos' by pointing out 28 inaccuracies in the show while also calling it ‘insulting’. Marroquin denied that Escobar attacked rival drug lord Gilberto Rodriquez’s daughter at her wedding as was shown in the show. However, the show’s creators have maintained that they never depicted the entire truth.
