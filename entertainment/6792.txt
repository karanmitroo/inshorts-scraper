The song 'Nashe Si Chadh Gayi' from the Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor starrer romantic comedy-drama film 'Befikre' has become the most viewed Hindi song on YouTube. The song, which was released in October last year, has gained over 23 crore views. Composed by Vishal and Shekhar, the song has been sung by Arijit Singh.
