VJ-actor Gaurav Kapur Sunday tied the knot with Delhi based girl Kirat Bhattal in an intimate ceremony in Delhi. The actor, last seen in Bollywood movie 'Bad Luck Govind', is currently seen hosting sports shows. The wedding was attended by his friends from sports as well as the film industry including Neha Dhupia, Sandhya Mridul, Yuvraj Singh and Mini Mathur.
