The trailer of 'Padmavati' was launched at 13:03 hours on Monday and the specific time was said to be chosen as it coincides with 1303 AD. This is said to be the year in which the Delhi Sultanate ruler Alauddin Khilji captured the Chittor Fort from king Ratan Singh. Ranveer Singh plays Alauddin while Shahid Kapoor portrays Ratan Singh.
