Rapper Baba Sehgal has released a rap song titled 'O Ellen! Share some watermelon', which he dedicated to American TV show host Ellen DeGeneres. Sehgal, who composed, sung and penned the song, said that he has rhymed watermelon with Ellen because "watermelon is sweetness". Sehgal is known for songs like 'Aaja Meri Gaadi Mein Baith Jaa' and 'Aloo Ka Paratha'.
