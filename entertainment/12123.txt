Singer Fergie performed while Paris Hilton walked the ramp for Philipp Plein Women's Spring/Summer 2017 runway show at Milan Fashion Week, which was titled 'Alice in Ghettoland'. The show's setup included a flying swing ride which the models rode on post their walk. Fergie sang the songs 'M.I.L.F. $' and 'Gettin Over You' for the show.
