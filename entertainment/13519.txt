Actor Sushant Singh Rajput has confirmed that there will be a sequel to the 2015 film 'Detective Byomkesh Bakshy!', where Sushant had played the titular character. The actor confirmed this in a recent Q&A session on Twitter. The film was based on the fictional detective 'Byomkesh Bakshi' created by the Bengali writer Sharadindu Bandyopadhyay.
