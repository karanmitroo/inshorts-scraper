Varun Dhawan has shared a video on social media in which he can be seen singing 'Oonchi Hai Building' with music director Anu Malik. The song, which first appeared in the 1997 Salman Khan and Karisma Kapoor starrer 'Judwaa', was sung by Malik, who will also sing the recreated version. Titled 'Oonchi Hai Building 2.0', it'll release on September 7.
