The political journalist turned filmmaker, Subhash Kapoor, who made Phas Gaye Re Obama and Jolly LLB, is now ready with 'Guddu Rangeela'. Reportedly, the movie will be a comedy-drama that will have Amit Sadh play Guddu and Arshad Warsi Rangeela. The film, produced by Sangeeta Ahir and Fox, will go on the floor in the first half of 2014.
