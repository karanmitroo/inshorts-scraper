The official trailer of the Benedict Cumberbatch starrer superhero film 'Doctor Strange' was released on Wednesday following which it was among the most trending Google searches in India. It was most searched in Goa, Uttarakhand, Kerala and Odisha. 'Earthquake', 'RPSC', 'Tamil new year 2016' and 'Ambedkar Jayanti' were some of the other trending searches on the same day.
