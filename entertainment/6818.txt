Akshay Kumar on Wednesday received his first National Award after being presented the Best Actor Award for 'Rustom' at the 64th National Film Awards. Surbhi CM was given the Best Actress Award for Malayalam film 'Minnaminungu' while Sonam Kapoor accepted the Special Mention Award for 'Neerja'. Zaira Wasim received the Best Supporting Actor award for her role in 'Dangal'.
