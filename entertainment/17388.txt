Megastar Amitabh Bachchan has sent a hand-written note to actor Ayushmann Khurrana praising him for his performance in the recently released critically acclaimed film 'Dum Laga Ke Haisha'. Ayushmann posted the note on Instagram with the caption, "Will cherish this for life." The note, dated March-28, read, "The tone of the language, the nuanced gestures of the region were immaculate!"
