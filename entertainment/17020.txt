Aamir Khan starrer 'PK' that released in China on May 22 has minted over Rs 100 crore in just 16 days, becoming Bollywood's highest-grosser in a foreign market. Industry trade analyst Taran Adarsh tweeted, "#PK creates HISTORY in China. Crosses Rs 100 cr on Sat [6 June; 16th day]. Total: $ 15.88 million [Rs 101.81 cr]. ALL TIME BLOCKBUSTER."
