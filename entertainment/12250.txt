Amitabh Bachchan and Hema Malini along with singers Shankar Mahadevan, Kailash Kher, Shreya Ghoshal, Shaan, Arijit Singh and Sonu Nigam are among those who sang the title track of the upcoming television show 'Baba Kedar'. An initiative of the Uttarakhand government, the show is a 12-part series that highlights the damage caused by the 2013 floods at the Kedarnath temple.
