The trailer of the Yami Gautam and Pulkit Samrat starrer 'Junooniyat' was released on Saturday. The film is a love story between an army captain and a Punjabi girl. 'Junooniyat' will be the second time that Pulkit and Yami will star together after 'Sanam Re'. Directed by Vivek Agnihotri, the film is scheduled to release on June 24.
