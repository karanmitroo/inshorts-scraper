Amrik Singh Shera, a Punjab police head constable has claimed that he wrote the song 'Kala Chashma', which released in 1990 and later featured in 'Baar Baar Dekho'. A Jalandhar-based record company​ reportedly approached him four months ago for his other songs and paid him ₹11,000. "No one told me that the song would feature in a movie," said Shera.
