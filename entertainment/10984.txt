'Tu Hi Hai,' a new song from the Shah Rukh Khan and Alia Bhatt starrer upcoming film 'Dear Zindagi' has been released. Composed by Amit Trivedi, the song has been sung by Arijit Singh with lyrics by Kausar Munir. Written and directed by Gauri Shinde, 'Dear Zindagi' is scheduled to release on November 25.
