Actress Parineeti Chopra is set to make her Tollywood debut in a Telugu film opposite actor Mahesh Babu. The yet untitled film, to be directed by AR Murugadoss, will reportedly be  Mahesh's costliest film, having a budget of around ₹90 crore. Reports further claimed that Parineeti has charged ₹3.5 crore for the film, which will be shot in Tamil simultaneously.
