Akshay Kumar's brother-in-law Karan Kapadia will make his Bollywood debut with Tony D'Souza's production. Karan said that Akshay and his aunt Dimple Kapadia were surprised but very supportive when, as a 14-year-old, he told them about his plans to join Bollywood. While talking about bagging the role, Karan added, "I do as much as I can on my own."
