Filmmaker Anurag Kashyap confirmed on Twitter that his next film 'Raman Raghav' will premiere at the upcoming 69th Cannes International Film Festival. The film revolves around a serial killer who murders people in the dead of the night and is set in 1960s Mumbai. The film stars Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Vicky Kaushal, who is known for his role in 'Masaan'.
