The first teaser poster of Ranveer Singh’s much-awaited ‘Kill Dil’ has been released, where the actor is featuring his typical ‘chhichora’ look. Directed by Shaad Ali, the film also stars Parineeti Chopra, Govinda and Ali Zafar. The story of the film revolves around the lives of two killers Ranveer and Ali who play Dev and Tutu in the film.
