The first cast photo of 'Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald', the sequel to the 2016 film 'Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them' has been released. While lead actor Eddie Redmayne reprises the role of Newt Scamander, Jude Law has joined the cast as Professor Dumbledore. The film is scheduled to release on November 16, 2018.
