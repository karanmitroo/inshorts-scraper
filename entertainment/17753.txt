After the huge box-office success of 'Chennai Express', filmmaker Rohit Shetty and superstar Shah Rukh Khan are set to work together again. According to reports, the untitled film is similar to the plot of Satyen Bose's 'Chalti Ka Naam Gaadi' and Mukul Anand's 'Hum'. It also features Varun Dhawan as SRK's younger brother and Kriti Sanon playing Varun's love interest.
