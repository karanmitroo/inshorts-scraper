The Ryan Gosling and Emma Stone starrer romantic musical comedy-drama film 'La La Land' will have its premiere in India on November 23 at the Jio MAMI Film Club. It will be screened at the film club before it's official release date, December 9. 'La La Land' is directed by Damien Chazelle, whose 2014 film 'Whiplash' won three Academy Awards.
