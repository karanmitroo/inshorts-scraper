'I Wanna Tera Ishq', a new song from the Vivek Oberoi, Riteish Deshmukh and Aftab Shivdasani-starrer 'Great Grand Masti' has been released. The song has been composed by Sanjeev-Darshan and sung by Shivranjani Singh and Shivangi Bhayana. It has been picturised on Urvashi Rautela, who plays a pivotal role in the film. 'Great Grand Masti' will release on July 15.
