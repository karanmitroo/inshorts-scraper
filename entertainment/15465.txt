Veteran Bollywood actor Rishi Kapoor on Tuesday unveiled his first look from the upcoming film 'Kapoor & Sons' on micro-blogging website Twitter. Kapoor is playing the role of a grandfather in the Dharma Productions film, starring Alia Bhatt, Sidharth Malhotra and Fawad Khan. The trailer of the Shakun Batra directorial will be out on February 10, tweeted filmmaker Karan Johar.
