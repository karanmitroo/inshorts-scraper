As per a Mid-Day report, Saif Ali Khan’s daughter Sara along with Shahid Kapoor’s half brother Ishaan Khattar are being groomed for the sequel to Karan Johar’s film 'Student of the Year' (SOTY). Johar had recently confirmed a sequel to the 2012 film, which marked the debut of actors Alia Bhatt, Varun Dhawan and Sidharth Malhotra.
