The teaser of the Dhanush and Kajol starrer Tamil film Velai Illa Pattadhaari 2, also known as VIP 2, has been released. The film is a sequel to the 2014 film Velai Illa Pattadhari (VIP). Dhanush has penned the story and is also co-producing the film while his sister-in-law Soundarya Rajinikanth is directing it.
