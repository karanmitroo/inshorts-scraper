Manoj Bajpayee starrer 'Taandav', a short film directed by Devashish Makhija, was released on YouTube on Friday. Bajpayee shared the link of the same on his social media accounts today with the caption, "Time to do the ‪#‎Taandav‬! Naach! Watch and share!". The film's story revolves around Head constable Tambe, played by Bajpayee, and shows his daily job-related struggle.
