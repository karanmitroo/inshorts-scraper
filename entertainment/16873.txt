'Second Hand Husband', the acting debut film of Govinda's daughter Tina Ahuja, "is so ridiculous that you want to believe it's a poor joke", says India Today. The film, also starring Geeta Basra, Gippy Grewal and Dharmendra, has a cameo by cricketer Harbhajan Singh. The Smeep Kang directorial has been rated 0.5/5 (Indian Express), 1.5/5 (India Today) and 2/5 (TOI).
