Zee Entertainment Enterprises Limited's new channel "&TV" is set to launch on March 2. The channel, with the tagline 'Jashan Jeene Ka,' will showcase a diverse mix of relatable fiction, high voltage non-fiction, marquee events and blockbuster movies. It will also air 'India Poochega- Sabse Shaana Kaun', which will be hosted by Shah Rukh Khan, on weekdays at 9 pm.
