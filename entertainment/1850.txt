A new poster of the upcoming Akshay Kumar starrer 'PadMan' has been unveiled. Sharing the poster on his social media, Akshay wrote, "#PadManTalks: Mad only become famous!" The film also stars Radhika Apte and Sonam Kapoor. The R Balki directorial is based on the life of Arunachalam Muruganantham, the inventor of a low-cost sanitary pad-making machine.
