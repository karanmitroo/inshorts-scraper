Jennifer Lawrence(23) has been named the most bankable Hollywood star of 2013, followed by 'Gravity' star Sandra Bullock and Lawrence's 'American Hustle' co-star Bradley Cooper. Lawrence was voted the top money-making star of 2013 in Quigley Publishing Company's annual poll. Lawrence appeared in 'The Hunger Games' sequel 'Catching Fire' after 'Silver Linings Playbook' that bagged her the best actress Oscar.
