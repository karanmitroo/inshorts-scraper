The Sonam Kapoor and Shabana Azmi starrer film 'Neerja', which released on Friday, has minted ₹22.01 crore in its opening weekend in India. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film which released across 700 screens in India, collected ₹4.70 crore on Friday, ₹7.60 crore on Saturday and ₹9.71 crore on Sunday.
