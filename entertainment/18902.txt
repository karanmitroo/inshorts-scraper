Arjun Kapoor and Alia Bhatt-starrer '2 States' - which was released four weeks ago - has finally made its entry into the prestigious 100 crore club. “And #2States hits a ton. Crosses 100 cr mark. Week 4: Fri 70 lacs, Sat 1.06 cr, Sun 1.42 cr. Grand total: 102.13 cr nett. SUPER HIT.” tweeted Trade analyst Taran Adarsh.
