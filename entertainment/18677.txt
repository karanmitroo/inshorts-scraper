Abhishek Bachchan, Riteish Deshmukh along with Akshay Kumar have been signed on for Housefull-3, which will be directed by Farhad-Sajid, who have directed Akshay's Entertainment. Housefull is a Sajid Nadiadwala comedy franchise that was originally made in 2010 with Akshay, Riteish and Arjun Rampal. The film was directed by Sajid Khan, who also directed Housefull-2, replacing Arjun with John Abraham.
