As per reports, Kriti Sanon will play the female lead opposite Tiger Shroff in Baaghi 2. The film's producer Sajid Nadiadwala was reportedly keen to get Kriti and Tiger back together onscreen as they had earlier made their Hindi film debut in his 2014 production Heropanti. Earlier, reports of Tiger's rumoured girlfriend Disha Patani starring in Baaghi 2 had emerged.
