Amitabh Bachchan tweeted that he was feeling uneasy before the news of actress Sridevi's demise was reported, causing Twitter users to think he had a premonition about her death. Users reacted with tweets like, "Talk About Sixth Sense" and "That's some weirdly correct tweet". Sridevi's brother-in-law Sanjay Kapoor confirmed that she passed away late on Saturday night in Dubai.
