Filmmaker Karan Johar took to Instagram to share an old picture on his father Yash Johar's 88th birth anniversary on Wednesday. Karan captioned the picture, "He gives me strength....he completes me in every way....my guiding light...my god for life." Notably, Yash founded Dharma Productions in 1976 and is known for producing films like 'Agneepath', 'Kuch Kuch Hota Hai' and 'Gumrah'.
