With earnings of ₹33.75 crore on its first day, Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif's 'Tiger Zinda Hai' has become the second-highest Hindi opening grosser of 2017. It beat the record of 'Golmaal Again', which earned ₹30.14 crore on its first day. Meanwhile, the Hindi version of 'Baahubali 2', with opening day collection of ₹41 crore, remains 2017's highest opening grosser.
