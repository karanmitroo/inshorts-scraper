A video showing the making of the Sonakshi Sinha starrer 'Akira' was released on Monday. The behind-the-scenes video shows the actress undergoing rigorous training to perfect a kick, referred to as 'The Akira Kick'. Directed by AR Murugadoss, the film, which also stars Konkana Sen Sharma and Anurag Kashyap, is scheduled to release on September 2.
