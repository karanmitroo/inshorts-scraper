The fourth episode of Koffee with Karan will feature actors Ranveer Singh and Ranbir Kapoor. In the episode promo, Ranveer has responded to actor Shah Rukh Khan's comment on him wearing "padded underwear" in 'Befikre'. Ranveer tells host Karan Johar, "I was just saving them the post-production costs. Otherwise, they will have to do VFX on my red chillies."
