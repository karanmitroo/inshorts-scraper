The Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Vicky Kaushal starrer 'Raman Raghav 2.0', which released on Friday, has earned ₹3.98 crore in its opening weekend. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film collected ₹1.10 crore on Friday, ₹1.40 crore on Saturday and ₹1.48 crore on Sunday. Directed by Anurag Kashyap, the film is based on the serial killer Raman Raghav.
