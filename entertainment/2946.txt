A video, which has surfaced online, shows Kangana Ranaut in a training session to learn sword-fighting for her upcoming film 'Manikarnika -The Queen Of Jhansi'. The actress is training under Hollywood stunt director Nick Powell for the Krish Jagarlamudi directorial. Also starring Sonu Sood, Aankita Lokhaande and Vaibhav Tatwawaadi, the film is scheduled to release on April 27, 2018.
