Deepika Padukone's Hollywood debut film 'xXx: Return of Xander Cage' has earned over ₹1,000 crore ($155 million) at the worldwide box office since its release. The action film collected about ₹53 crore in India. Significantly, the film, made on a budget of approximately ₹568 crore ($85 million), is yet to release in China, reportedly its biggest market.
