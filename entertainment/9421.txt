Shah Rukh Khan, Salman Khan and Sunny Leone enacted a scene from 1975 film 'Deewar' on 'Bigg Boss 10'. Salman played Shashi Kapoor's role, SRK portrayed Amitabh Bachchan while Sunny played their mother Nirupa Roy. They enacted the scene in Bigg Boss's context wherein Sunny has been selected to participate in the show while the brothers argue about her participation.
