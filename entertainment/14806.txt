The trailer of Steven Spielberg's upcoming fantasy adventure film 'The BFG' has been released. The film, starring  Mark Rylance, Ruby Barnhill and Penelope Wilton, is based on Roald Dahl's novel by the same name. It revolves around a young girl, Sophie, who befriends a big friendly giant (BFG), played by Rylance. The film is scheduled to release on July 1.
