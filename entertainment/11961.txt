Actress Kareena Kapoor and her sister Karisma Kapoor have featured on the cover of the October issue of entertainment magazine 'Hello!'. They can be seen in ensembles by fashion designer Manish Malhotra for the 'festive edition' of the magazine. The cover story on Karisma and Kareena is titled 'Soulmates, Besties or Rivals?: The 'K' Sisters'.
