The remake of AR Murugadoss's action drama 'Ramana', titled as 'Gabbar', will have  Shraddha Kapoor and Akshay Kumar in lead roles. Kareena Kapoor also has a special role in the movie. The film is a fast-paced action thriller, where Shraddha plays a new-age lawyer. It's produced by Sanjay Leela Bhansali and is expected to go on floors early 2014.
