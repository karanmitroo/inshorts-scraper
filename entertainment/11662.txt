Singer Abhijeet Bhattacharya on Monday slammed director Anurag Kashyap after the latter asked Prime Minister Narendra Modi to apologise for his December trip wherein he met Pakistani Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif. Tagging Karan Johar and Mahesh Bhatt, Bhattacharya tweeted to Kashyap, "@narendramodi_in ne pehle baaton se manane Ki koshish Ki, ab unhe laat maara, aap kataar me hain".
