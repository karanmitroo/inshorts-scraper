Deepika Padukone beat Priyanka Chopra in terms of ranking on the '2016 Celebrity 100' list by Forbes India. While Deepika ranked 6th with earnings of ₹69.75 crore and a fame rank of '8', Priyanka stood eight with earnings of ₹76 crore and a fame rank of '9'. Notably, they were the only women who featured in the list's top ten.
