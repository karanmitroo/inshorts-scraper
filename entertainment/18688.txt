Sajid Nadiadwala's action-thriller Kick starring Salman Khan, Jacqueline Fernandez, Randeep Hooda and Nawazuddin Siddiqui has been rated 1.5(India Today), 2(Indian Express), 2.5(NDTV, HT), 3(Koimoi) and 3.5(TOI). While Firstpost writes: "No paisa vasool in Salman's spectacularly dull film", IE has termed it as "Unjhelable". "Go with an open mind, don't expect intellectual pontification and you'll have a great time," advises IBN-Live.
