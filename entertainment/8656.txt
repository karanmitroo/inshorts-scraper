Filmmakers Imtiaz Ali, Kabir Khan and Zoya Akhtar will feature in the next episode of 'Koffee With Karan'. In the episode's teaser, Kabir reveals, "It was a struggle to get a producer because I was a documentary filmmaker, which was bit of a bad word." Further, when Karan asks Imtiaz if he's Imtiaz's "kind of director", he jokingly says no.
