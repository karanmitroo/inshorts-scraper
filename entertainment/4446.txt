Shah Rukh Khan, while speaking about his achievements, said he never thought his name would become an adjective. "[P]eople...use [my name] to describe things. 'Shah Rukh kind of boy', 'Shah Rukh kind of romance'," he added. SRK further said, "I'm not showing off, but whatever I've achieved...in all these years, I don't think anybody can think about that in dreams".
