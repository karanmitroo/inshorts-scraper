Superstar Shah Rukh Khan has crossed the mark of 12 million followers on popular micro-blogging website Twitter, surpassing Aamir Khan and Salman Khan. SRK fans are celebrating the moment by trending hashtag '#SRK12Million'. While Aamir has over 11.9 Twitter followers, Salman has 11.2 million followers. However, megastar Amitabh Bachchan is still leading the chart with 13.9 million followers.
