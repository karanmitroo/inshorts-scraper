Actor Hugh Jackman gave commissioned prints from 'X-Men' comic book artist Joe Quesada to director Bryan Singer as a parting gift. The commissioned print features Jackman as 'Wolverine' standing behind Singer as 'Professor Xavier'. Jackman had earlier announced that his final appearance as Wolverine will be in James Mangold's sequel to 'The Wolverine', scheduled for release on March 3, 2017.
