Actress Genelia Deshmukh, while wishing her husband Riteish Deshmukh on his 39th birthday on Sunday, shared a picture on Twitter and wrote, "You will forever be my always." She further wrote, "Thank you for being my everything... For making Life about Loving you." Notably, Riteish and Genelia made their acting debut together with the 2003 film 'Tujhe Meri Kasam'.
