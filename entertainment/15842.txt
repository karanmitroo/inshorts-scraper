Sanjay Leela Bhansali's film 'Bajirao Mastani' dominated the Sony Guild Awards 2015 on Tuesday, winning 9 awards. While Bhansali was named the Best Director, Ranveer Singh won the Best Actor (Male) award. Remo D'Souza was named the Best Choreographer for the song 'Pinga'. Deepika Padukone won the Best Actress (Female) title for 'Piku'. 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' was named the Best Film.
