Superstar Shah Rukh Khan-starrer 'Chennai Express', which earned over Rs 150-crore in its opening week, crossed the Rs 200-crore-mark in the second week, making Bollywood's speediest double century. The action-comedy, co-starring Deepika Padukone, directed by Rohit Shetty and produced by UTV Motion Pictures along with Shah Rukh's Red Chillies Entertainment, was made at a budget of about Rs 75-crore.
