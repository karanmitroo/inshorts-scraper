Superstar Salman Khan, who turned 48 on Friday, threw a birthday bash at his Panvel farmhouse, where a host of Bollywood A-listers joined the party. Besides family members, actors Aamir Khan, Anil Kapoor, Suniel Shetty, Sonakshi Sinha, and Varun Dhawan were present. Bigg Boss-7 contestants like Elli Avram and Armaan Kohli, Boney Kapoor and Sajid Nadiadwala, too, joined the party.
