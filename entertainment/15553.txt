The trailer of Himesh Reshammiya starrer 'Teraa Surroor' was released on YouTube on Wednesday. The film, also featuring Farah Karimi, Kabir Bedi and Naseeruddin Shah, is a sequel to 2007 film Aap Kaa Surroor. The Shawn Arranha directorial, a joint production of T-Series and HR Musik Limited, is scheduled to release on March 11, 2016.
