A new song titled 'Tippa' from the Kangana Ranaut, Shahid Kapoor and Saif Ali Khan starrer upcoming film 'Rangoon' has been released. It has been composed by Vishal Bhardwaj with vocals by Sukhwinder Singh, Rekha Bhardwaj, Sunidhi Chauhan and OS Arun. Gulzar has penned the lyrics of the song. Directed by Vishal Bhardwaj, 'Rangoon' will release on February 24.
