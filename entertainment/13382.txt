Yoga guru Baba Ramdev has been named the brand ambassador for a charity football match in New Delhi betwe​en Bollywood celebrities and parliamentarians. Actors Ranbir Kapoor and Arjun Kapoor will be part of the celebrity team while MPs Babul Supriyo and Manoj Tiwari will represent the parliamentarians​. The match aims to raise funds for 'Swachh Bharat' and 'Beti Bachao...' campaigns.
