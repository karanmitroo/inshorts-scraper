As megastar Amitabh Bachchan-starrer blockbuster 'Don' on Tuesday completed 37 years since its release, the nostalgic actor shared the news on twitter, and also his blog. "37 years of 'DON' today (Tuesday). A film made by that gentle giant Nariman Irani, the DOP of extreme excellence," he wrote in his blog, also sharing anecdotes from the set of the film.
