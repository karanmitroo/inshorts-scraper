The trailer of the Taapsee Pannu starrer 'Naam Shabana' was released on Friday. Taapsee stars as a special agent in the film, which is a prequel to the 2015 film 'Baby'. Directed by Shivam Nair and also starring Akshay Kumar, Manoj Bajpayee, Anupam Kher and Danny Denzongpa, the film is scheduled to release on March 31.
