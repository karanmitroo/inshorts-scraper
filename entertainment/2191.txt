A video shared by TV actor Nakuul Mehta shows singer Ed Sheeran dancing to the song 'Badri Ki Dulhania' at a party hosted for him by filmmaker Farah Khan. Joining Sheeran were Shah Rukh Khan, Shahid Kapoor and his wife Mira Rajput, Karan Johar,  Sara Ali Khan and Janhvi Kapoor among others. Sheeran will perform in Mumbai on Sunday.
