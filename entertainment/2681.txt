Sridevi starrer song 'Hawa Hawai' from the 1987 film 'Mr India' has been recreated for Vidya Balan's film 'Tumhari Sulu'. The vocals of Kavita Krishnamurthy, who sang the original song, have been retained and the new version has been recreated and programmed by Tanishk Bagchi. Directed by Suresh Triveni, 'Tumhari Sulu' is scheduled to release on November 17.
