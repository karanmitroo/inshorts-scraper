Actor Harshvardhan Kapoor has apologised to Diljit Dosanjh for his comment that debutant awards should be for people relatively new to films. "Love you... sir, have lots of respect for you and your body of work sorry if I said anything that came out wrong @diljitdosanjh," he tweeted. Earlier, Diljit said that he is not hurt by Harshvardhan's comment.
