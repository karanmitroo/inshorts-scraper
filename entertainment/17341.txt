Portraying a young girl suffering from cerebral palsy, Kalki Koechlin "takes your breath away with a superlative performance" in Friday's release 'Margarita with a Straw', writes mid-day. The film deals with the "frustrations and helplessness attached to the unfulfilled sexual desires of a disabled person". It has been rated 4/5 (TOI), 4.5/5 (mid-day) and 5/5 (Economic Times).
