Recently released Priyanka Chopra-starrer sports biopic 'Mary Kom', minted Rs.28.32 crore in its opening weekend. The film, which is the directorial debut of Omung Kumar, raked in Rs. 8.75 crore on the first day of its release, Rs. 9.5 crore on the second and Rs. 10 crore on the third. The film is being lauded for Priyanka's performance.
