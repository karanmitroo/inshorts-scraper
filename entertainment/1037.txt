Ali Abbas Zafar, while talking about his directorial with Salman Khan 'Bharat', said, "The idea is to make Salman look the way he appeared in 'Maine Pyar Kiya'." Ali added 'Bharat' will show Salman's journey from the age of 18 to 70, for which discussions are on with the prosthetics and VFX team of 'The Curious Case of Benjamin Button'.
