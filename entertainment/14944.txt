Actress Priyanka Chopra has featured in Victoria's Secret 2016 'What is Sexy' list, winning the tag of 'Sexiest Eyes'. Furthermore, the cast of the 33-year-old actress' ABC thriller 'Quantico' was named 'Sexiest TV Cast'. American actress Kerry Washington was named 'Sexiest Actress' while actress-singer Jennifer Lopez bagged the 'Sexiest Forever' tag in the list.
