Aamir Khan and Zaira Wasim starrer Secret Superstar "has everything you ask for, but it isn't exceptional storytelling," wrote Hindustan Times (HT). The Times of India (TOI) said, "What makes this movie stand out is [its] adventurous narrative." Firstpost called the film a "thoroughly rewarding cinematic experience." It has been rated 3/5 (HT), 3.5/5 (Firstpost) and 4/5 (TOI).
