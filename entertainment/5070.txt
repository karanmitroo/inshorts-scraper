According to reports, actor Hrithik Roshan has been approached to play Shiva's role in the film adaptation of author Amish Tripathi's 'The Immortals of Meluha'. It is being said that the film will be directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali. 'The Immortals of Meluha' is the first book in Amish's Shiva trilogy and is set in the land of Meluha.
