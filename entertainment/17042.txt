In just 11 days of its release, Kangana Ranaut and R Madhavan starrer 'Tanu Weds Manu Returns' has collected Rs 100 crore globally, becoming the first Bollywood film of 2015 to cross the 100 crore mark. Trade analyst Taran Adarsh tweeted, "#TanuWedsManuReturns crosses Rs 100 cr mark! Worldwide [India + Overseas] total: Rs 101.67 cr. AWESOME!".
