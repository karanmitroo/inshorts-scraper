The trailer of the Radhika Apte, Tannishtha Chatterjee and Surveen Chawla-starrer film 'Parched' was released on Monday. Produced by Ajay Devgn and directed by Leena Yadav, it highlights women-related issues, while revolving around the lives of four women from a Rajasthan village. 'Parched' premiered at the 2015 Toronto Film Festival and is slated for an official release on September 23.
