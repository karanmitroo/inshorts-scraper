Actress Taapsee Pannu has featured on the January cover of 'Cosmopolitan' magazine. The 29-year-old actress can be seen wearing an Aztec print cut out top,  paired with a fitted striped skirt. She has accessorised her look with a bracelet. The story on her has been captioned, 'Taapsee Pannu: Don't call me an up-and-comer'.
