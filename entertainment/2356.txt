A new song titled 'Ek Dil Ek Jaan' from Deepika Padukone, Shahid Kapoor and Ranveer Singh starrer 'Padmavati' has been released. It has been composed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali with vocals by singer Shivam Pathak. AM Turaz has written the song's lyrics. Directed by Bhansali, 'Padmavati' is scheduled to release on December 1.
