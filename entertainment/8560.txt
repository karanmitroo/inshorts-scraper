Actors Amitabh Bachchan and Taapsee Pannu have been invited by President Pranab Mukherjee for the screening of their film 'Pink' at the Rashtrapati Bhavan. "Guess who has movie and dinner plans with The Honourable President today?" tweeted Taapsee. Directed by Aniruddha Roy Chowdhury, 'Pink' is a courtroom drama which also stars Kirti Kulhari and Andrea Tariang.
