As per reports, actor Salman Khan and filmmaker Kabir Khan will be working together in a film based on India's 1983 World Cup victory. Salman has worked with Kabir in 'Ek Tha Tiger' and 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' while he is still shooting for their upcoming film 'Tubelight'. Kabir is reportedly considered like a family member by Salman Khan's family.
