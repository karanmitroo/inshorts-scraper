The first look of actress Vidya Balan from her upcoming film 'Kahaani 2' has been revealed. Vidya is playing the character 'Durga Rani Singh', a 36-year-old fugitive wanted by the police for kidnapping and murder. The film also stars Arjun Rampal and Jugal Hansraj. Directed by Sujoy Ghosh, the film is scheduled to release on December 2.
