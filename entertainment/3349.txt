The first look of Deepika Padukone from the upcoming film 'Padmavati' was revealed on Thursday. Rani Padmavati has been referred to as "The Goddess Queen" in the film's first look poster. Also starring Ranveer Singh and Shahid Kapoor, the film revolves around Alauddin Khilji's infatuation with the Rajput queen Padmavati. Directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali, it'll release on December 1.
