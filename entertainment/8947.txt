Filmmaker Meghna Gulzar, who directed the 2015 film 'Talvar' will make a film on acid attack survivor Laxmi Agarwal. "It's an inspiring story with social and legal implication... I'm open to both newcomers and established actors to play Laxmi," she said. Laxmi, who became a victim of acid attack in 2005, currently runs an NGO named Chhanv Foundation.
