Actor Sonam Kapoor has been signed to play the lead in the upcoming woman-centric film, 'Dolly Ki Doli'. The movie, made under the 'Arbaaz Khan Productions' banner, will be directed by debutant Abhishek Dogra. The film will go on floors in January 2014 and if all goes well, it will be released by the end of 2014, said Arbaaz.
