According to reports, actor Rajinikanth's elder daughter and filmmaker Aishwaryaa R Dhanush will be acting with him in the sequel to the 2016 film 'Kabali'. She will reportedly play a pivotal role in the film, which will be produced by Aishwaryaa's husband Dhanush's home production. Aishwaryaa is currently directing her documentary film on stunt choreographers titled 'Cinema Veeran'.
