The John Abraham and Sonakshi Sinha starrer film 'Force 2' "ends up feeling overlong and stretched", wrote The Indian Express. Hindustan Times said that 'Force 2' is "mostly about muscles and very less about intelligence". "The absence of songs does the film a huge favour," wrote India Today. It has been rated 1.5/5 (TIE), 2/5 (HT) and 3/5 (IT).
