UP CM Akhilesh Yadav announced a gift of Rs.1 crore each for Madhuri Dixit starrer movie ‘Dedh Ishqiya’ and Saif Ali Khan’s ‘Bullett Raja’ at the Saifai Mahotsav, as their major portion was shot in UP. Over two dozen artists, including Salman Khan, Madhuri Dixit, Deepika Padukone and Ranvir Singh performed on the last day of the fortnight-long gala.
