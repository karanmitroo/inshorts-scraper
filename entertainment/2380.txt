Actress Deepika Padukone has denied reports that she is portraying poetess Amrita Pritam in an upcoming biopic on poet Sahir Ludhianvi, which is being produced by Sanjay Leela Bhansali. Deepika has currently signed only Vishal Bhardwaj's next production, where she will be essaying the role of gangster Rahima Khan who was popularly known as Sapna Didi.
