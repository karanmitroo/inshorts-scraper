The first song of the movie 'Shaadi Ke Side Effects' titled as "Harry Is Not Bhramchari" was released on YouTube Saturday. Penned by Amitabh Bhattacharya, the song is sung by Jazzy B and Ishq Bector while the music is composed by Pritam. Directed by Saket Chaudhary, the movie features Farhan Akhtar and Vidya Balan and will release on February 28.
