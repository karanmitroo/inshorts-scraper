Manasi Moghe was crowned Miss Diva 2013 at a star-studded event at Westin Mumbai Garden City on Thursday while Gurleen Grewal and Srishti Rana were the first and second runner-ups, respectively. The contest was adjudged by many renowned personalities like Zeenat Aman, Raveena Tandon, Malaika Arora Khan, Jacqueline Fernandez, Kunal Kapoor, Atul Kasbekar and Former Miss Universe Canada Sahar Biniaz.
