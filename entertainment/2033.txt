TV actor Piyush Sahdev, who played the character of Lord Rama in 'Devon Ke Dev...Mahadev', was arrested on Wednesday on charges of rape filed by a woman at Mumbai's Versova Police Station. The actor has been sent to police custody till November 27. Piyush is also known for his roles in 'Meet Mila De Rabba', 'Geet' and 'Beyhadh'.
