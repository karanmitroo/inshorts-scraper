Actress Parineeti Chopra has lent her voice to ‘Sophie’, the female protagonist in the Hindi version of Steven Spielberg’s fantasy adventure film ‘The BFG’. The film is based on the 1982 novel of the same name by Roald Dahl. Amitabh Bachchan and Gulshan Grover have also lent their voice to the film which will release in India on July 15.
