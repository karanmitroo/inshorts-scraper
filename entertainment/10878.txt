A recreated version of the song 'Ae Zindagi Gale Laga Le' from the upcoming Shah Rukh Khan and Alia Bhatt starrer film 'Dear Zindagi' has been released. The new version was composed by Amit Trivedi with vocals by Arijit Singh. The original song from the 1983 film 'Sadma' was sung by Suresh Wadkar, composed by Ilaiyaraaja, with lyrics by Gulzar.
