Actress Richa Chadha, who is critically acclaimed for her work in 'Gangs of Wasseypur', has signed a biopic on Sarabjit Singh who died in a Pakistani jail. While Randeep Hooda will play Sarabjit, Richa will portray his wife onscreen. The film, also starring Aishwarya Rai Bachchan as Sarabjit's sister, will be directed by Omung Kumar, the maker of 'Mary Kom'.
