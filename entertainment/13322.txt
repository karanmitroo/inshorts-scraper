Actor Akshay Kumar on Monday revealed his look from the upcoming film 'Jolly LLB 2'. He tweeted, "New day with a new look for a new film, let the mayhem begin! #JollyLLB2 it is!" The film is a sequel to Arshad Warsi starrer 2013 court drama 'Jolly LLB'. Also starring Saurabh Shukla, the sequel will release on February 10, 2017.
