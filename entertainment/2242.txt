Arbaaz Khan has confirmed that 'Dabangg 3', the third instalment in the 'Dabangg' franchise, will be directed by Prabhu Deva. "We're likely to start shooting in April 2018, and need some time to work on the script and the pre-production of the project," said Arbaaz. Earlier, he had also said he'd like to cast Sunny Leone in the film.
