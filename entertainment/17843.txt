Legendary singer Lata Mangeshkar Saturday praised PM Narendra Modi's 'Beti Bachao-Beti Padhao' campaign for saving the girl child. "Greetings, brother Narendra Modi. Vasant Panchami is a festival devoted to the goddess of knowledge Saraswati... on this auspicious occasion, I would like to extend my greetings for your 'Beti Bachao-Beti Padhao' campaign. I pray for your success", she tweeted.
