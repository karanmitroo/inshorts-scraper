Actor Vivek Oberoi has confirmed that he is working with director Ram Gopal Varma (RGV) on 'Company 2', a sequel to the 2002 film 'Company', which marked Vivek's Bollywood debut. "He (Varma) is a genius when it comes to dealing with gangster and underworld films," said Vivek. The 39-year-old actor added that he will be turning producer for the film.
