Kareena Kapoor Khan will reportedly play the lead role in Rhea Kapoor's directorial debut 'Veera Di Shaadi', also starring Rhea's sister Sonam Kapoor. Sonam had to pass on her lead role to Kareena, who refused to play a supporting role, reports added. The film is said to be loosely based on the Hollywood film 'The Sisterhood of the Travelling Pants'.
