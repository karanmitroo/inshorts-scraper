Superstar Salman Khan's younger sister Arpita Khan is pregnant, confirmed producer-actor Arbaaz Khan. The child is due early next year. The reports of Arpita's pregnancy had started fuelling after she was spotted with a baby bump at the Ganesh Chaturthi celebrations with entire Khan family. Arpita had tied the knot with aspiring actor and model Aayush Sharma in November 2014.
