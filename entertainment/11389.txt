'O Janiya', a new song from the John Abraham and Sonakshi Sinha starrer film 'Force 2' has been released. The song has been composed by Gourov Roshin with vocals by Neha Kakkar and lyrics by Kumaar. It is a remixed version of 'Kaate Nahin Katte' from 'Mr India' (1987). Directed by Abhinay Deo, 'Force 2' will release on November 18.
