Amitabh Bachchan and Deepika Padukone-starrer 'Piku' was screened for President Pranab Mukherjee at the Rashtrapati Bhavan on Sunday. Big B attended the screening along with film's director Shoojit Sircar and son Abhishek Bachchan. "(H)e seemed to have enjoyed the 'perfect' Bengali-Hindi accent, which he felt was flawless. He later invited us over for dinner...," Amitabh wrote on his blog.
