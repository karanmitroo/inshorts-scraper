With a collection of Rs 182.58 crore in the first week, Aamir Khan-starrer 'PK' has broken the record of 'Dhoom: 3', which had earned Rs 179 crore in one week in India. PK's international gross box office collection is $13.36 million (Rs 85 crore). The film may even cross Rs.300 crore ultimately, trade experts have predicted.
