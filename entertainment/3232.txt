Deepika Padukone asked filmmaker Karan Johar what his biography 'An Unsuitable Boy' is called in Hindi, to which he responded, "Ek Anokha Ladka." Deepika shared this in an Instagram story taken at fashion designer Manish Malhotra's place. Manish hosted a get-together at his place which Deepika, Siddharth Malhotra and Johar attended.
