The John Abraham, Varun Dhawan and Jacqueline Fernandez starrer 'Dishoom' has earned ₹37.32 crore in its opening weekend. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, it collected ₹11.05 crore on Friday, ₹12.02 crore on Saturday and ₹14.25 crore on Sunday. Directed by debutant director and Varun's brother Rohit Dhawan, 'Dishoom' has become Bollywood's sixth highest opening weekend grosser of 2016.
