Days after the 'My Choice' video on women empowerment featuring Deepika Padukone generated a heated debate, cricketers Virat Kohli, Suresh Raina and Ambati Rayudu along with Team Director Ravi Shastri featured in a video on women empowerment. The video, by Hyderabad-based NGO, My choices, is called “RespectToProtect”. It represents the male voices of our society pledging to protect their women.
