Ayushmann Khurrana-starrer 'Hawaizaada' is a masterpiece about a dreamer, reports Hindustan Times. The film tells the story of an Indian scientist, who supposedly built the world's first unmanned aircraft, years before the Wright brothers. 'Hawaizaada', which also features Mithun Chakraborty and Pallavi Sharda of 'Besharam' fame, has been rated 4.5/5 (HT, Zee News), 2.5/5 (TOI) and 2/5 (NDTV).
