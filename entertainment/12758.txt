Indian poet and director Gulzar, who turned 82 on Thursday, penned the lyrics for 'Jungle Jungle Baat Chali Hai' from the animated show, 'The Jungle Book'. The Indian TV adaptation of Rudyard Kipling's collection of stories premiered on Doordarshan in 1993. Gulzar was also the lyricist for the song's latest version which released with the movie adaptation in 2016.
