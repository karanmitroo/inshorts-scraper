The first look of romantic-comedy 'Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhaniya' starring Alia Bhatt and Varun Dhawan, is out. The poster shows Varun and Alia posing for a selfie in colourful sunglasses. The first trailer will come out on May-26, and the movie is scheduled to release on Jul-11. It has been directed by debutant Shashank Khaitan and produced by Karan Johar.
