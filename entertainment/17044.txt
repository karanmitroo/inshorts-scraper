Megastar Amitabh Bachchan has become the first Bollywood celebrity to reach the 15 million follower mark on Twitter. "T 1880-  TWITTER ... 15 MILLION !!! YEEEAAAEAEAEAAAHHHHHH!! BADDOOOOOOOMMMBAAAAA !!", the 72-yr-old actor tweeted. Bachchan, who joined Twitter in May 2010, has left Shah Rukh Khan (13.3 million), Salman Khan (12.2 million) and Aamir Khan (12.9 million) far behind.
