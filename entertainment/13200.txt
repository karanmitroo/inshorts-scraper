Saiyami Kher, the lead actress in filmmaker Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra's upcoming film 'Mirzya', made her debut on the ramp for designer Gaurav Gupta on the fourth day of the ongoing India Couture Week. Saiyami wore a pastel blue gown for the show. Gupta, on his collection 'Scape Song' said, "It's a statement collection of contemporary, almost futuristic new Indian wear."
