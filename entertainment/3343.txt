According to reports, the members of Rajput organisation Karni Sena set the posters of Sanjay Leela Bhansali's upcoming film 'Padmavati' on fire in Jaipur. They have also sought a pre-screening of the film for Hindu groups. The members recently also warned the film's makers over Rajput queen Padmavati's portrayal in the film, while responding to a promotional tweet.
