Randeep Hooda starrer 'Rang Rasiya', which released Friday, has received an average review by critics. Indian Express has described the movie as a choppy costume drama marred by false notes and static 'acting'. Directed by Ketan Mehta, the film is a biopic of painter Raja Ravi Varma. It has been rated 2/5 (Indian Express, NDTV), 3.5/5 (HT) and 4/5 (TOI).
