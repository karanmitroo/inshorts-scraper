Sidharth Malhotra, Kriti Sanon and Karan Johar turned showstoppers for designer Manish Malhotra, who showcased his bridal collection in Dubai. The collection featured garments in pastel shades embellished with pearls, tassels and sequins. "Dubai is Manish's sasural and he has made it his own... Manish Malhotra is shining and sparkling like his garments," said Karan.
