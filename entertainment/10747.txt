Actor Amitabh Bachchan thanked PM Narendra Modi on Twitter for acknowledging his contribution to 'Swachh Bharat Abhiyaan'. Modi shared an audio clip on Twitter from his radio programme 'Mann Ki Baat', where he's heard lauding the actor's dedication for taking the initiative forward. Modi also read a slogan by Bachchan, inspired from a poem by his father Harivansh Rai Bachchan.
