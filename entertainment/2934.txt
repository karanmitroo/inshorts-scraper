Maharashtra Navnirman Sena (MNS) chief Raj Thackeray took to social media to share sketches he made of Amitabh Bachchan to mark the actor's 75th birthday. Wishing Bachchan, he wrote, "Amitabh (has) altered the style and content of Hindi Cinema, and set a new agenda for filmdom." Thackeray added, "His roles re-defined the post 1970 Indian cinema and his legacy continues."
