Arunachalam Muruganantham has said that he is proud that Akshay Kumar, a mainstream male superstar, will talk about menstruation and sanitary napkins in the Bollywood film 'Pad Man'. He added that Twinkle Khanna, who is producing the film, tracked his every move to get the rights for making the film. Muruganantham admitted he didn't even know who Twinkle was initially.
