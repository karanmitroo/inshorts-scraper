Nana Patekar-starrer 'Ab Tak Chhappan 2' will hit the screens on February 27 this year, the filmmakers have announced. The Aejaz Gulab directorial also features Ashutosh Rana, Vikram Gokhale and Gul Panag among others. The film, an action thriller, is based on the life of Mumbai Police officer Daya Nayak who is known as an encounter specialist.
