Priyanka Chopra was named Best Dressed (Female) while Shahid Kapoor and Ranbir Kapoor tied for Best Dressed (Male) at the Koffee Awards as part of the television show 'Koffee With Karan'. Priyanka had appeared on the show wearing a Fendi gown. The winners were decided by a special jury comprising Malaika Arora, Neha Dhupia, Ayan Mukerji and AIB's Rohan Joshi.
