On the day a video of Amitabh Bachchan's granddaughter Navya Naveli Nanda dancing in a swimsuit emerged online, 'Navya Naveli' was the fifth most trending Google search in India on Monday. It was most looked up most in Goa, Kerala and Odisha. 'NIOS result 2016', 'Ramadan Kareem' and 'Thodari' were some of the other top Google searches.
