Bollywood singer Labh Janjua was found dead at his residence in Goregaon, Mumbai on Thursday morning. His body was sent for postmortem even though officials of the Bangurnagar police station said that prima facie no foul play was found. The 57-year-old singer was known for his songs 'Mundiyaan Toh Bach Ke', 'London Thumakda' and 'Jee Karda' among others.
