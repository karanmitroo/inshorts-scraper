'Tu Zaroorat Nahi Tu Zaroori Hai', a new song featuring Sharman Joshi and Sunny Leone from the upcoming Sunil Subramani-directorial 'Fuddu' has been released. The song has been composed by Sumeet Bellary and sung by Gandharv Sachdev and Shreya Ghoshal, with voiceover by Ranbir Kapoor and lyrics by Satya Khare. The film stars Shubham and Swati Kapoor in lead roles.
