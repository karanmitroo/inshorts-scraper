Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra’s ‘Bhaag Milkha Bhaag’ was chosen as the best film of 2013 at the 59th Idea Filmfare Awards 2013, held on Friday. Also, Rakeysh was conferred with the ‘Best Director’ award at the ceremony. Lunchbox’s director Ritesh Batra was given the ‘Best Debut Director’ award, whereas Lunchbox itself won the Critics Award for Best Film.
