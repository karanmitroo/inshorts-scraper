The trailer of the Tom Hiddleston, Samuel L Jackson and Brie Larson starrer 'Kong: Skull Island' has been released. Directed by Jordan Vogt-Roberts, the film is a reboot of the 'King Kong' franchise and is the second installment in the 'Godzilla' and 'King Kong' film series by Legendary Pictures and Warner Bros. The film will release on March 10, 2017.
