Nobel Peace Prize winner Malala Yousafzai, while talking about 'Pad Man' said, "I'm really excited to see the film... because the message behind the film is truly inspiring." Yousafzai met 'Pad Man' producer Twinkle Khanna at a session at the Oxford Union, UK. The film is based on the life of Arunachalam Muruganantham, inventor of a low-cost sanitary pad-making machine.
