Actress Ileana D'Cruz has shared a video on Instagram in which she can be seen dancing with Ranveer Singh to the song Hawa Hawa from her upcoming film Mubarakan. She captioned the video, "Ranveer Singh trying to patao (woo) me." The film, which also stars Arjun Kapoor, Athiya Shetty and Anil Kapoor, is scheduled to release on July 28.
