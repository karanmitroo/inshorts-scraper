Actor Salman Khan has featured in a lead role in ten consecutive ₹100 crore films, including 'Sultan' (2016), 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' (2015), 'Dabangg 2' (2012) and 'Dabangg' (2010). The actor, who turned 51 on Tuesday, has the maximum number of Bollywood movies in the ₹100 crore club. 'Dabangg' was Salman's first film to collect ₹100 crore at the Indian box-office.
