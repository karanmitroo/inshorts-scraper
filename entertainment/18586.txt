Actor Amitabh Bachchan created a buzz in the industry by revealing the 'Shamitabh' looks of his co-stars Dhanush and Akshara, on Twitter. Bachchan posted the pictures a day after he revealed his own look from the film. 'Shamitabh' is the debut film of Akshara (Kamal Hassan's youngest daughter), and is directed by R. Balki.
