Social networking site Twitter has created a special emoji for the Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor starrer film 'Befikre'. The emoji, which resembles the Eiffel Tower in Paris, will be activated by using hashtags such as #UdeDilBefikre, #Befikre and #BeBefikre. It will remain active until the film's release. Notably, the film's story is set in the French capital of Paris.
