Actress Kareena Kapoor Khan, in a red gown by Gauri & Nainika, was among the celebrities who attended the Lux Golden Rose Awards 2016. Deepika Padukone wore a gown from British fashion house Ralph & Russo, while Katrina Kaif chose a blue gown by designer Ziad Nakad. Anushka Sharma was seen in a lace top and skirt by Shehla Khan.
