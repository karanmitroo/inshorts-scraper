The trailer of the Jimmy Shergill and Ashutosh Rana starrer film 'Shorgul' was released on Thursday. The political drama, also featuring Sanjay Suri, Eijaz Khan and Hiten Tejwani, has been directed by Jitendra Tiwari and P Singh. Set in UP, it explores religious clashes, political rivalry and power games amid high-profile characters. It is scheduled to release on June 24.
