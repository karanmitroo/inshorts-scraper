Deepika Padukone, while speaking about her 'xXx: Return of Xander Cage' co-star Vin Diesel, said, "In my head I think... we are together and... we have these amazing babies!" She made these comments during her debut appearance on 'The Ellen DeGeneres Show'. She was responding to the host Ellen DeGeneres, who said Deepika and Vin had "a romance going on".
