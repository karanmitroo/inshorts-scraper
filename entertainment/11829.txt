Actress Shweta Basu Prasad is set to return to the small screen with the upcoming historical drama TV series 'Chandra Nandni'. The actress will play the role of Nandni, Chandragupta Maurya's wife, while actor Rajat Tokas will be seen as emperor Chandragupta Maurya. Produced by Balaji Telefilms, the show will be aired on Star Plus from October 10.
