The teaser of the first look of Shahid Kapoor and Alia Bhatt starrer film 'Shaandaar' was on Tuesday revealed on Twitter. "And they wake up tomorrow.....@aliaa08 @shahidkapoor #ShaandaarFirstLookTomorrow", producer Karan Johar tweeted along with a picture. In the picture, Shahid and Alia can be seen sleeping hand-in-hand on a bench. The film is directed by Vikas Bahl of 'Queen' fame.
