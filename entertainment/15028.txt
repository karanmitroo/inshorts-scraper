Bollywood actor Varun Dhawan has said that filmmaker Karan Johar's production 'Shuddhi' is not happening at the moment. ‘Shuddhi’ is not happening now but I am doing another project with Dharma (productions), which will be announced soon, the actor added. Further, he talked about his upcoming films 'Judwaa 2' and 'Dishoom', adding,"There is another surprise coming out soon".
