Sajid Nadiadwala's film 'Heropanti,' starring debutants Tiger Shroff and Kriti Sanon, has earned Rs 21 crore at the domestic box office during the first weekend, a Koimoi report said. The opening day collection of the film stands at Rs 6.63 crores. Heropanti presented by UTV Motion Pictures and Nadiadwala Grandsons Entertainment Pvt. Ltd. released on May-23.
