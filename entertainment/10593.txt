Trevor Noah, in an episode of American talk show 'The Daily Show', mocked  demonetisation and Supreme Court's ruling to play National Anthem before film screenings. In the section 'India's Unusual New Policies', Noah called ₹500, ₹1,000 notes "as worthless as Hillary inauguration ticket". Referring to the SC ruling, he said, “That's gonna make it especially awkward during erotic films."
