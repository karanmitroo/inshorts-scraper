The first poster of the Kajol and Dhanush starrer Tamil film 'Velaiyilla Pattathari 2' or 'VIP 2' was revealed on Sunday. "As the year comes to an end and a #NewYear begins... here's my gift to all #Dhanush fans," tweeted the film's director Soundarya Rajinikanth. The film is a  sequel to the 2014 film 'Velaiyilla Pattathari'.
