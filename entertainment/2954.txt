Hrithik Roshan has said his divorce with Sussanne Khan is not owing to infidelity. "When people get divorced, it is not always because of infidelity by the man...We are great friends...The reason we decided to live separately may have been an inspiring reason," added Hrithik. Earlier, Kangana Ranaut's sister Rangoli said she read rumours about Sussanne's affair with Hrithik's friend.
