The trailer of Sonam Kapoor starrer 'Neerja' was the third most viewed video on YouTube in India on Saturday. The video, which was posted on the video-sharing website on Thursday, has garnered over 17 lakh views so far. 'Neerja' is a biopic on flight attendant Neerja Bhanot who was killed by terrorists who hijacked a Mumbai-New York flight in 1986.
