Ranveer Singh and Shaid Kapoor have slammed the attack on Sanjay Leela Bhansali during the shoot of 'Padmavati' in Jaipur. The film's male lead Ranveer, who plays Alauddin Khilji, tweeted, "Whats happened is very unfortunate... Sanjay Sir is one of the most accomplished & authentic filmmakers we have in India."  "Violence is unacceptable... This incident has shocked me," tweeted Shahid.
