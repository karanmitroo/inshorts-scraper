Model-actor Milind Soman has completed the 42.2 km Berlin Marathon in 4 hours and 32 minutes. Milind, while sharing a picture on Facebook, wrote, "Beautiful course, great weather and 42000 runners... Looking forward to more World Marathon Majors, so much fun!!!" Soman, a fitness enthusiast, is also the brand ambassador of 'Pinkathon', India's biggest 'women only' marathon.
