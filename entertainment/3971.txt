Deepwater Horizon actor Mark Wahlberg has topped Forbes 2017 list of World's Highest Paid Actors, earning $68 million during the 12-month scoring period. The 46-year-old was followed by Dwayne Johnson, who earned $65 million, and Vin Diesel, who earned $54.5 million. The top 10 actors earned a cumulative $488.5 million, whereas the top 10 women had banked $172.5 million.
