The Irrfan Khan starrer 'Madaari', which released on Friday, is a film where "Irrfan makes for a compelling aam aadmi”, wrote India Today. Times of India called the film “poignant, provocative and politically incorrect” while NDTV wrote that 'Madaari' is worth a watch due to “Irrfan's flawless one-man show”. It was rated 2/5 (India Today), 3.5/5 (TOI) and 2.5/5 (NDTV).
