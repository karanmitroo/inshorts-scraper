Superstar Rajinikanth's daughter Soundarya R Ashwin was on Wednesday blessed with a baby boy in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Soundarya, who is a filmmaker and works in the Tamil film industry, had married industrialist Ashwin Ramkumar in 2010. This is the first child of the couple. Soundarya has directed India’s first motion capture animated film “Kochadaiyaan”, featuring her father.
