Actress Karisma Kapoor has featured on the January cover of 'Femina' magazine. The 42-year-old actress can be seen wearing a black waist cut-out pantsuit as she poses on a chair for the cover shot. Her look has been accessorised with a watch and a bracelet. The story on Karisma has been captioned, 'The timeless elegance of Karisma Kapoor'.
