Television actress Shilpa Shinde has filed an FIR alleging sexual harassment by Sanjay Kohli, husband of Binaifer Kohli, who is the producer of the serial 'Bhabi Ji Ghar Par Hai!'. Shilpa, who portrayed 'Angoori Bhabhi' earlier on the show, alleged that Kohli made remarks like, "You are very sexy," and would hug her forcefully and touch her waist and breasts.
