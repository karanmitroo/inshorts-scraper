Actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan recently met BSF soldiers at Attari border in Punjab's Amritsar district. Reportedly, when the soldiers came to know that Aishwarya was shooting nearby for her upcoming film 'Sarabjit', they expressed their desire to meet her. After Aishwarya fulfilled their wish, several pictures of her interacting with the jawans also surfaced on various social media platforms.
