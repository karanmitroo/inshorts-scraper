Dairy cooperative Amul has released a poster on filmmaker Karan Johar becoming a father to twins via surrogacy while captioning it 'Kabhi beti, kabhie son', a word play on Johar's film 'Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham'. The poster further read, "Amul... Johar se cheer karo!" The filmmaker recently announced his plan of becoming a single father to twins Roohi and Yash.
