A photo album created by movie merchandise company 'Fully Filmy', titled 'What if these biopics were actually made?' features popular actors playing roles based on real-life personalities. A poster shows Shah Rukh Khan playing filmmaker Satyajit Ray while R Madhavan is seen as journalist Arnab Goswami in another poster. Actors Ajith, Ramya Krishnan, among others, also feature on the posters.
