A new trailer of the Disney-Pixar animated film 'Cars 3' has been released. It will feature the voices of Owen Wilson as 'Lightning McQueen', Armie Hammer as 'Jackson Storm' and Cristela Alonzo as 'Cruz Ramirez'. The film, directed by Brian Fee, is the third instalment in the 'Cars' franchise. It is scheduled to release on June 16, 2017.
