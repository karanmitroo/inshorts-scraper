As per reports, actor Hrithik Roshan is all set to star opposite actress Sonam Kapoor in the third film of the Aashiqui franchise. 'Aashiqui 3' will be directed by Amit Sharma. Notably, the duo was last seen together in Yo Yo Honey Singh's video which had Aashiqui's popular song track 'Dheere Dheere'.
