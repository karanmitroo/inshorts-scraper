Actor Aamir Khan, who was offered the role of Sunil Dutt in the biopic on actor Sanjay Dutt, said he's not interested in playing the role of Sanjay's father. "I thought you were coming to narrate the role of Sanjay Dutt," Aamir told the film's makers. Meanwhile, Paresh Rawal has been finalised to play Sunil Dutt's role in the biopic.
