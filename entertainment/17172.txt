I&B Minister Arun Jaitley Sunday conferred the Dadasaheb Phalke award, India's highest award in cinema, on veteran actor-filmmaker Shashi Kapoor at the Prithvi Theatre in Mumbai. Due to ill health, the 77-year-old couldn't attend the award ceremony held at Delhi on May-3. He is the third from his family to receive the award, after Prithvi Raj Kapoor and Raj Kapoor.
