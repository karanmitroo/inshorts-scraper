Actor Shah Rukh Khan on Saturday launched the Marathi film 'Hrudayantar', which marks the directorial debut of fashion designer Vikram Phadnis. "Every film is like a first child. We all know Vikram is very hard-working and very sorted. We wish good luck to him and his team," said Shah Rukh. Arjun Kapoor and Malaika Arora Khan also attended the launch.
