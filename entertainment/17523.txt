As per reports, actor Ajay Devgn will do a cameo in Rohit Shetty's upcoming movie 'Dilwale', starring Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol. Considered Rohit's close friend, Devgn has appeared in his films like ‘Bol Bachchan’, ‘Golmaal’ and ‘Singham’ series. Interestingly, there have always been rumours of cold vibes between SRK and Ajay Devgn.
