Actress Kangana Ranaut will be seen sporting 10 different hairstyles in her upcoming film 'Katti Batti'. Reportedly, her look in the film is loosely inspired from Kate Winslet's 2004 film, 'Eternal Sunshine of a Spotless Mind'. Directed by Nikhil Advani, 'Katti Batti' is a romantic comedy film and also features Imran Khan in the lead role.
