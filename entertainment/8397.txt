Actress Shraddha Kapoor made her Bollywood debut with the 2010 film 'Teen Patti', starring Amitabh Bachchan and Ben Kingsley. The actress, who turned 30 on Friday, is best known for her films 'Aashiqui 2', 'Ek Villain' and 'Haider'. She is also known to have sung for her films including 'Rock On 2' and 'Ek Villain'.
