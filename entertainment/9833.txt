According to reports, Priyanka Chopra will produce a Sikkimese-Nepalese film titled 'Paua', which translates to 'Guest', under her production house 'Purple Pebble Pictures'. The film will reportedly revolve around two young children who get separated from their parents at the time of an unrest at the Indian border. Priyanka is currently searching for local talent from Sikkim and Nepal.
