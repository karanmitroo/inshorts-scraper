Veteran actor Shrivallabh Vyas, known for acting in films like 'Lagaan' and 'Sarfarosh', passed away on Sunday at the age of 60 in Jaipur. The actor had reportedly been bedridden for the past few years after suffering from a brain stroke. Aamir Khan, Manoj Bajpayee and Imran Khan were said to have helped Vyas financially for his treatment.
