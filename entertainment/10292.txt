The official trailer of the Akshay Kumar starrer court drama film 'Jolly LLB 2' has been released. Akshay will be seen playing lawyer Jagdishwar Mishra or Jolly in the film, which is a sequel to 2013 film 'Jolly LLB'. Also featuring Huma Qureshi, Annu Kapoor and Saurabh Shukla, the film, directed by Subhash Kapoor, will release on February 10, 2017.
