The poster of the web series 'Ragini MMS Returns', which stars Karishma Sharma and Siddharth Gupta in lead roles, has been released. Sharing the poster on social media, the production house wrote, "Sexier than before...Scarier than before!" 'Ragini MMS Returns' is the third instalment in the 'Ragini MMS' series, which previously released two films as part of the franchise.
