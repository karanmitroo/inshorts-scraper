Full video of Krrish 3's party song "Raghupati Raghav Raja Ram" has finally been released. The song, which features Priyanka Chopra and Hritik Roshan grooving, is sung by Neeraj Shridhara and Monali Thakur and is penned by lyricist Sameer Anjan. The film, also starring Vivek Oberoi and Kangana Renaut, and directed by Rakesh Roshan, is set to release on November-4th.
