Amitabh Bachchan and Aamir Khan will next month launch legendary actor Dilip Kumar's biography -  'Substance and Shadow' - authored by Uday Tara Nayar. The biography was earlier scheduled to release on Kumar's 91st birthday last year, but will be now unveiled on Jun-9. In a career spanning over six decades, the actor gave hits like 'Madhumati, 'Devdas' and 'Mughal-e-Azam'.
