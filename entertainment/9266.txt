Lyricist Gulzar and Sarod maestro Ustad Amjad Ali Khan will be participating in the third edition of Jashn-e-Rekhta, a festival celebrating the language Urdu. The event is scheduled to take place from February 17-19 at the Indira Gandhi International Centre for the Arts in the Capital. The festival will also host a Sufi evening and a qawwali performance.
