Renowned Telugu producer-director Daggubati Ramanaidu passed away at 78 in Hyderabad on Wednesday, after a long battle with prostrate cancer. Ramanaidu had won the Dada Saheb Phalke Award in 2009, and the Padma Bhushan in 2013. He also had the Guinness record for the most number of feature films by an individual. He made films in 13 languages.
