The makers of 'Padmaavat' have issued full-page newspaper advertisements where seven disclaimers have been listed. The makers clarified that it is based on a work of fiction and never had a dream sequence featuring Alauddin Khilji and Rani Padmavati. They reiterated that it does not tarnish Rani Padmavati's image while adding that every Indian will be proud of the film.
