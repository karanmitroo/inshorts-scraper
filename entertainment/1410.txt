Actor Ranveer Singh has said that filmmaker Aditya Chopra calls him "nalayak" (useless) and tells him to learn something from Anushka Sharma. Ranveer added, "It's wonderful to see how far she (Anushka) has come. She was always amazing as an actor, but her craft has gotten more solid." He further said that he shares a great camaraderie with Anushka.
