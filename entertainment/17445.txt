Bollywood actress Deepika Padukone has featured in a new video, directed by Homi Adjania, for Vogue's initiative on women's empowerment. The 2-minute-34-second video featuring 99 women, including Padukone, from Mumbai is titled 'My Choice'. In the black-and-white video, women are calling for change in 'caged' mindset and voicing their choice for equality - social, political, economic and sexual.
