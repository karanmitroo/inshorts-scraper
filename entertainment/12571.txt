Actor Sushant Singh Rajput has said that he has witnessed closely the story of Mahi (cricketer Mahendra Singh Dhoni's nickname) and that has changed him as a human being. "I want everyone to witness it," said Sushant referring to 'M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story', the upcoming biopic where he portrays the cricketer onscreen. The film will release on September 30.
