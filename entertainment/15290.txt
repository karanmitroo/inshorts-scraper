Bajrangi Bhaijaan won the Best Film award at the Zee Cine Awards on Saturday. Salman Khan won the Best Actor in Male Category for 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' and Deepika Padukone won the Best Actor in Female Category for 'Bajirao Mastani'. Neeraj Ghaywan won the Best Debutant Director award for 'Masaan'. Bhumi Pednekar, Harshaali Malhotra and Vicky Kaushal were adjudged best debutants.
