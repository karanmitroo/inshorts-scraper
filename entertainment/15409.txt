The first teaser of the Matt Damon-starrer film 'Jason Bourne' was released on Sunday. Directed by Paul Greengrass, the film is the fifth instalment in the 'Bourne' franchise. The action spy thriller film also features Alicia Vikander, Vincent Cassel and Tommy Lee Jones, with Julia Stiles reprising her role in the series. It is scheduled to release on July 29.
