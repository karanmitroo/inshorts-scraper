Ranbir Kapoor has said Karan Johar is obsessed with Pakistani actor Fawad Khan. Talking about the filming of Ae Dil Hai Mushkil, Ranbir added, "When he (Fawad) was on set, me and Anushka were pushed aside. Karan was giving him direction...we were just told to go act." Ranbir also said he feels like the less hotter person in Fawad's presence.
