The trailer for Amitabh Bachchan's first outing on a TV series, Yudh, was released online on May-31. The show, which boasts of acting talents such as Kay Kay Menon, Sarika and Nawazuddin Siddiqui, will start airing on Sony soon. Anurag Kashyap is the creative director of the show, while Shoojit Sircar has directed some parts of the series.
