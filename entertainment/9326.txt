A new poster of upcoming Indian epic historical fiction film 'Baahubali: The Conclusion' has been released. The film is the continuation of 'Baahubali: The Beginning'. The poster features actors Prabhas and Anushka Shetty. While Prabhas will reprise the titular character of 'Baahubali', Anushka will portray 'Devasena'. Directed by SS Rajamouli, the film is scheduled to release on April 28.
