Actor Amitabh Bachchan's film 'Pink' has been invited for a special screening at the United Nations headquarters in New York. Bachchan tweeted that he felt honoured that the film was invited by the UN Assistant Secretary-General Movses Abelian. The film, which starred Taapsee Pannu, Andrea Tariang and Kirti Kulhari in lead roles, released in India on September 16.
