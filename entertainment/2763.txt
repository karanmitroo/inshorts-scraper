The Ajay Devgn and Parineeti Chopra starrer film 'Golmaal Again' "mainly relies on the supernatural elements to make things look funny", wrote Hindustan Times (HT). "Ajay Devgn almost makes this film watchable," said NDTV. Meanwhile, The Times of India (TOI) wrote, "[The film] has no logic, only magic." It has been rated 2/5 (NDTV), 2.5/5 (HT) and 3.5/5 (TOI).
