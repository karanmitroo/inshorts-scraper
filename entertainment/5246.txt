Monali Thakur has sung 'Ye Awaaz Hai', a new song from the upcoming film 'Indu Sarkar'. The song has been composed by Anu Malik, with lyrics by Puneet Sharma. Directed by Madhur Bhandarkar, the film is based on Emergency, the 21-month-long period declared by then Prime Minister Indira Gandhi during 1975-1977. The film is scheduled to release on July 28.
