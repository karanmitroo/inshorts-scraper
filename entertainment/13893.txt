Cristiano Ronaldo has appeared in music producer RedOne’s recently-released music video ‘Don’t You Need Somebody’, alongside tennis star Rafael Nadal and actress Priyanka Chopra. The video features a number of sports and movie stars, including footballers Mesut Özil, James Rodríguez and Blaise Matuidi. Musicians Jennifer Lopez, Shaggy and Akon have also featured in the video.
