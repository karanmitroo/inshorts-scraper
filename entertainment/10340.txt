A video claiming to be a compilation of deleted scenes from the film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' has emerged online. In the video, a scene featuring Fawad Khan and Anushka Sharma is initially shown while other scenes show Ranbir Kapoor with either Anushka or Aishwarya Rai. The film marked Karan Johar's return as director after the 2013 film 'Bombay Talkies'.
