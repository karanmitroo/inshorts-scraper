The first show of the 'Dream Team' tour, featuring performances by Katrina Kaif, Sidharth Malhotra, Aditya Roy Kapur, Parineeti Chopra, Alia Bhatt, Varun Dhawan, Badshah and Karan Johar, started in Houston, Texas on Friday. For the final performance, Karan and Katrina danced to ‘Shava Shava’ and were joined by the other team members. The tour will last for a month.
