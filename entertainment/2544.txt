Actress Priyanka Chopra and Hindustan Times Group Chairperson Shobhana Bhartia have featured in 15 Most Powerful Women In Entertainment And Media 2017 list by Forbes. British-American journalist and editor Anna Wintour, who is the Artistic Director of media company Condé Nast, topped the list. Singers Beyoncé Knowles and Taylor Swift, and author J K Rowling also featured on the list.
