A teaser poster of the Riteish Deshmukh and Nargis Fakhri starrer film 'Banjo' has been released. The musical drama, directed by Ravi Jadhav, features Riteish in  the role of a Banjo player, while Nargis Fakhri plays a DJ. The film focusses on how this art has been neglected despite numerous youngsters being involved in it.
