Shamita Shetty, the sister of actress Shilpa Shetty Kundra, will be seen as a contestant on the 8th season of celebrity dance reality show 'Jhalak Dikhla Jaa'. While the official final list of contestants is not announced yet, the participation of two contestants- Shamita and TV actor Vivian Dsena- is confirmed. The show will go on air from July 11.
