Sussanne Khan on social media shared a photograph of herself with her ex-husband Hrithik Roshan, after attending the screening of his upcoming film 'Kaabil'. She captioned the post, "Eternal sunshine of the spotless mind... so so so proud of you." Further, Sussanne tweeted, "The most hearted performance in history of Indian cinema @iHrithik," referring to Hrithik's performance in 'Kaabil'.
