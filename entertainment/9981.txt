The Aamir Khan starrer film 'Dangal' has entered the ₹200 crore club by earning ₹216.12 crore within eight days of its release. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film earned ₹18.59 crore on Friday. 'Dangal' has become the second highest opening day grosser in Bollywood this year, after Salman Khan's 'Sultan'.
