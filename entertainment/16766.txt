UP Government proclaimed Bajrangi Bhaijaan tax-free in the state after the film's director Kabir Khan approached the CM with the request. Salman Khan, who plays lead in the movie, took to microblogging website Twitter to express his gratitude. “Bajrangi Bhaijaan is tax free in UP. We thank the Govt of Uttar Pradesh. @yadavakhilesh,” Salman tweeted.
