The release date of the Hrithik Roshan-Yami Gautam starrer film 'Kaabil' has been shifted to January 25, 2017, an evening before its scheduled release. It will be released across theatres from 6pm onwards. Earlier, 'Kaabil' and Shah Rukh Khan's 'Raees' were slated to release on the same date, January 26, 2017.
