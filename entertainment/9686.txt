The Aditya Roy Kapur and Shraddha Kapoor starrer 'OK Jaanu' "is a progressive love story," wrote Hindustan Times. "Bollywood needs to get more adept at depicting young love," said The Indian Express. The Times of India wrote, "The thing that works most in OK Jaanu's favour is its screenplay". It has been rated 1.5/5 (TIE), 3/5 (HT) and 3.5/5 (TOI).
