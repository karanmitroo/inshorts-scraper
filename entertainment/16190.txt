The Viral Fever (TVF) on Tuesday released a video on Bollywood's most iconic clichés on its official YouTube channel. The video features actor Ayushmann Khurrana as Bollywood cliché specialist Dr Rahul Raj Clichéwala. In the comic video, Gajraj Rao (last seen in 'Talvar') plays a reporter from Bollywood Science Journal and interviews Khurrana about the dying out tradition of clichés.
