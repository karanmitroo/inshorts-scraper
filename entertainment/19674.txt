'Barfi' lead actor Ranbir Kapoor has recorded the introductory song titled Pyar ki ghanti for his upcoming movie 'Besharam' by Abhinav Kashyap in which he is playing the lead role. The song reflecting the spirit of Ranbir's on-screen character has been directed by Lalit Pandit. The actor has collaborated with newcomers Sujeet Shetty and Amitosh Nagpal on the song.
