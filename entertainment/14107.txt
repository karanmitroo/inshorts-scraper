Actors Riteish Deshmukh and Celina Jaitly have criticised Tanmay Bhat, stand-up comedian associated with 'AIB', for allegedly insulting Lata Mangeshkar and Sachin Tendulkar. Bhat uploaded a video titled 'Sachin v/s Lata Civil War' in which he allegedly used insult humour against them. “Disrespect is not cool and neither is it funny,” tweeted Riteish, while Celina asked Bhat to apologise.
