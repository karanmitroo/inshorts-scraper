Actress Priyanka Chopra's Sikkimese production 'Pahuna: The Little Visitors' will have its world premiere at the Toronto International Film Festival (TIFF) 2017. Directed by Paakhi A Tyrewala, the film is about children from Nepal who get separated from their parents, while fleeing the Maoist agitation. Priyanka is producing it under her production house Purple Pebble Pictures.
