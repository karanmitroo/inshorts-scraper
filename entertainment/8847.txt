Actress Alia Bhatt said that she can go to jail for filmmaker Karan Johar and rumoured boyfriend Sidharth Malhotra. She gave this response in the 'Rapid Fire' segment of Koffee with Karan, when asked by Johar to name two people in Bollywood she would go to jail for. Alia featured with Varun Dhawan for the second time on the show.
