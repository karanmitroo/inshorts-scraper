The trailer launch of Gurmeet Ram Rahim Singh's 'Hind Ka Napak Ko Jawab - MSG Lion Heart 2' was attended by 2.75 crore people, as per film trade analyst Taran Adarsh. The film revolves around the country 'NaPak' and how India leads a mission against it. Directed by Gurmeet and his daughter Honeypreet Insan, it will release on February 10.
