Actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan wore a red multi-layered ruffled gown by Ralph & Russo for her second appearance at the 70th Cannes Film Festival's red carpet. The off-shoulder gown featured a fitted corset-styled bodice, which was embellished with stones in a floral pattern. The actress accessorised her look with a pair of chandelier diamond earrings decorated with rubies.
