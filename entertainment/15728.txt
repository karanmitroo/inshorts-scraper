The title song of upcoming film 'Fitoor', featuring Katrina Kaif and Aditya Roy Kapur, was released on YouTube on Wednesday. The song 'Yeh Fitoor Mera', sung by Arijit Singh, has been composed by Amit Trivedi and written by Swanand Kirkire. The film, also featuring Tabu and based on Charles Dickens' novel, is scheduled to release on February 12, 2016.
