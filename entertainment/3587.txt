As per reports, the Central Board of Film Certification (CBFC) has ordered for cuss words, including b***h and bast**d, to be removed from the Kangana Ranaut starrer 'Simran'. The CBFC reportedly demanded a total of ten cuts. A source said, "Derogating women by gesture or language will never be permissible under any guideline, be it Pahlaj (Nihalani) or Prasoon (Joshi)."
