'Rom Rom Romantic', the first song of Sunny Leone, Tusshar Kapoor and Vir Das starrer film 'Mastizaade' was released on YouTube on Monday. The song has been composed by Amaal Mallik, while it has been sung by Mika Singh along with Amaal and Arman Mallik. Directed by Milap Zaveri, 'Mastizaade' is a sex comedy and will release on January 29.
