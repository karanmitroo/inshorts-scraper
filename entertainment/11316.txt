Actress Priyanka Chopra has sung her first Marathi song titled, 'Baba' for her debut film as a producer, 'Ventilator'. Priyanka shared the song on Twitter, with the caption, "My first Marathi song.. Forgive me if there are any mistakes... this one is all heart." Starring Ashutosh Gowariker, the film is scheduled to release on November 4.
