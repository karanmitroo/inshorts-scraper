Sanjay Bairagi, the 40-year-old supervising producer of TV show 'Ishqbaaaz', committed suicide on Friday by jumping off the 16th floor of a building in Mumbai, as per the police. A suicide note has also been recovered where Bairagi cited depression and financial issues as the reason for committing suicide. The show stars Nakuul Mehta and Surbhi Chandna among others.
