Actress Tejasswi Prakash, while comparing the storyline of her TV show 'Pehredaar Piya Ki' to 'Game of Thrones' said, "People love 'Game Of Thrones' and if the same thing happens in 'Pehredaar Piya Ki', then it's an issue". Talking about the show, she added, "I think it's really progressive... But... people believe in judging a book by its cover".
