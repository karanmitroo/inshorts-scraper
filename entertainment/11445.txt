According to reports, actress Aditi Rao Hydari will play the role of 'Kamala Devi', one of the wives of Alauddin Khilji, who will be portrayed by Ranveer Singh in the upcoming film 'Padmavati'. The film's director Sanjay Leela Bhansali reportedly decided on Aditi over four other choices. 'Padmavati' will also star Deepika Padukone and Shahid Kapoor.
