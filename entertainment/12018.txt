Fashion magazine Vogue India, which will complete 9 years this October, shared a picture of its first cover featuring actresses Priyanka Chopra, Bipasha Basu and Preity Zinta. They had featured along with Australian model Gemma Ward and Indian models Laksmi Menon and Monikangana Dutta. The international magazine's maiden Indian edition was titled, 'The First Issue, Vogue arrives in India'.
