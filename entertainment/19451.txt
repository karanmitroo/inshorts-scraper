The Indian version of Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet "Goliyon Ki Rasleela Ram-leela", starring Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone, has received 2 (NDTV), 2.5 (Indian Express), 3 (India Today), and 5 (TOI). Directed and produced by Sanjay Leela Bhansali, the movie that released this Friday embarked upon an impressive start across the country with 70-75% occupancy in Delhi, UP and elsewhere.
