Neetu Chandra has written an open letter to 'Udta Punjab' director Abhishek Chaubey and actress Alia Bhatt for stereotyping Biharis in the film. "Abhishek Chaubey is guilty of strengthening the stereotype of Biharis by creating Bhatt's character the way it is", she wrote. Neetu further added, "[A]lmost all the films made by Prakash Jha... have always stereotyped Biharis".
