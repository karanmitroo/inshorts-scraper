The teaser of 'The Fate of the Furious', the upcoming eighth instalment in the 'Fast & Furious' action film franchise has been released. Actor Vin Diesel will portray the role of Dominic Toretto while Dwayne Johnson will play Luke Hobbs. Also starring 
Tyrese Gibson, the film, directed by F Gary Gray, is scheduled to release on April 14, 2017.
