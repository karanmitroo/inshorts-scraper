The trailer of the horror film Dobaara - See Your Evil, starring real life siblings Huma Qureshi and Saqib Saleem as onscreen sister-brother duo, has been released. It is a Hindi adaptation of the Hollywood film Oculus. Scheduled to release on June 2, Dobaara has been directed by Prawaal Raman and also stars Adil Hussain, Lisa Ray and Rhea Chakraborty.
