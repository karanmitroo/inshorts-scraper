UTV Motion Pictures on Twitter revealed Ranbir Kapoor's look in his upcoming film 'Jagga Jasoos'. Ranbir, who plays a geeky detective in the film, is seen sporting a clean-shaven look, a pair of spectacles and carries a backpack in the picture. Directed by Anurag Basu, the film also features Katrina Kaif and Govinda and is scheduled to release August 28.
