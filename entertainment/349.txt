Karan Johar has announced his upcoming production 'Rannbhoomi', a war drama starring Varun Dhawan and being directed by Shashank Khaitan. This is the third time that the trio will be working together after 'Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania' and 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania'. While the remaining cast is yet to be announced, the film is scheduled for a Diwali release in 2020.
