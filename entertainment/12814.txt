The trailer for the Sushant Singh Rajput starrer 'M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story' has garnered over 11 million views within three days of its release. "#DhoniTrailer is the most liked trailer in the history of Hindi cinema. Thanks everyone," tweeted Sushant. The trailer of the biopic on cricketer MS Dhoni currently has over 270,000 likes on YouTube.
