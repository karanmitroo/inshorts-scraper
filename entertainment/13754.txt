Actress Kareena Kapoor Khan has called her upcoming film 'Veere Di Wedding', "India's first real chick flick". "You watch it all the time in the west, but nobody really has the courage to really make a chick flick here," added Kareena. Produced by Rhea Kapoor and Ekta Kapoor, the film stars Kareena as the titular character 'Veere'.
