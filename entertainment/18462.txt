"Finding Fanny is a funny, dark, yet bright, a shimmering ride through Goa...Its drama keeps surprising", according to the Times of India. The much anticipated film released on Friday, and has got good reviews by critics. The movie has been rated 3.5/5 (TOI), 4/5 (NDTV), 3.5/5 (Hindustan Times), 3/5 (India Today) and 2.5/5 (Indian Express).
