The Aamir Khan starrer "PK" has landed in legal trouble as an FIR has been lodged against the film for having disapproving comments on Hindu rituals and hurting the religious sentiments of people. The FIR was lodged by Secretary of Hindu Legal Cell Prashant Patel against director Rajkumar Hirani, actor Aamir Khan, producer Vidhu Vinod Chopra and Sidharth Roy Kapur.
