'Judwaa 2' has become the fourth film in Varun Dhawan's acting career to enter the ₹100 crore club. The film, directed by Varun's father David Dhawan, earned ₹100 crore within 7 days of its release. 'Judwaa 2' has also become Taapsee Pannu's first ₹100 crore Bollywood film while it is the fifth film starring Jacqueline Fernandez to earn ₹100 crore.
