Actor Akshay Kumar, while talking about his wife Twinkle Khanna, said, "She thinks in English, I think in Hindi." He added, "We're complete opposites. I don't read; she's very well-read... She's outspoken... I'm a diplomat." Revealing what they fight most about, he said, "I like to pamper my family when we travel on holidays, but...she prefers to keep things simple."
