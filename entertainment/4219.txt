Filmmaker Anurag Kashyap, while talking about Pahlaj Nihalani getting fired as the Central Board of Film Certification (CBFC) Chairman, said, "Something you've been waiting for, and it suddenly happens, it's like wow!" Talking about the new Chairman Prasoon Joshi, Kashyap said, "I believe there should be no censorship and only certification...I believe Prasoon... (also) believes in that."
