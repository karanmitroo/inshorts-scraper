The trailer of the upcoming comedy film 'Wiener-Dog' has been released. The film has been directed by Todd Solondz of 'Welcome to the Dollhouse' and 'Happiness' fame. Starring Greta Gerwig, Julie Delpy, Kieran Culkin, Zosia Mamet and Danny DeVito, the film revolves around four stories that are connected by a dog. It is scheduled to release on June 24.
