The 29th Surajkund International Crafts Mela will be beginning on Sunday, Feb-1, with Chhattisgarh as the theme state this year. The 'Partner Nation' for this year, Lebanon, and theme state Chhatisgarh will put up cultural shows. A mobile application 'Surajkund Mela' has also been launched for smartphones for online ticketing. Tickets will also be available on 22 metro stations.
