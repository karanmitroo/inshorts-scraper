Actress Alia Bhatt, while refuting rumours of Pakistani actor Ali Zafar being replaced in the upcoming film 'Dear Zindagi', said that the film is "releasing as it is". Earlier, there were rumours that Ali Zafar had been replaced by actor Tahir Raj Bhasin. Directed by Gauri Shinde and also starring Shah Rukh Khan, the film will release on November 25.
