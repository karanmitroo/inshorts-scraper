The Mohanlal starrer 'Pulimurugan' has become the first Malayalam film to enter the ₹100 crore club with its worldwide earnings, a month after its release on October 7. The 56-year-old actor tweeted about the milestone while extending his gratitude to the film's crew and the audience. Directed by Vysakh, the film was reportedly made on a budget of ₹25 crore.
