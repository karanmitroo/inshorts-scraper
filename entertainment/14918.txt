Actor-singer Ayushmann Khurrana on Monday shared a video of himself singing 'Moh Moh Ke Dhage' from his film 'Dum Laga Ke Haisha' after the film and the song won various titles at the 63rd National Film Awards. "Never imagined our journey with Dum Laga Ke Haisha will go this far!... Thank you all for the love," he captioned the video.
