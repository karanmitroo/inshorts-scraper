Actor Amitabh Bachchan on Monday shared a video where he reads out a letter he wrote for his granddaughters Aaradhya Bachchan and Navya Naveli Nanda. "[Y]ou are also girls… because you are women people will force their thinking, their boundaries on you. Don’t live in the shadows of people’s judgement," wrote Bachchan. He tweeted the letter was for all granddaughters.
