The Viral Fever (TVF) has released a new video 'He's Not Chotu', which is a satire on the issue of child labour, on the occasion of Children's Day on November 14. The video has been made in collaboration with Nobel Peace Prize winner Kailash Satyarthi and his 'Kailash Satyarthi Children's Foundation' for the '100 Million for 100 Million' campaign.
