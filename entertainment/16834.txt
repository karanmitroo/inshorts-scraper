Megastar Amitabh Bachchan has lent his voice to a promotional song, 'Le Panga', for the upcoming Pro Kabaddi League. The 72-year-old actor shared the news about the new track on his official blog. The Kabaddi tournament that will begin on July 18 will witness its first match between U Mumba (Mumbai) and Jaipur Pink Panthers, owned by Abhishek Bachchan.
