Actress Huma Qureshi's debut film in Hollywood 'Viceroy's House' is set to premiere at the Berlinale 2017. In the film, Huma has portrayed the character 'Aalia', a Muslim interpreter to the last Viceroy of India, played by Hugh Bonneville, and his wife, played by Gillian Anderson. Huma's film 'Dedh Ishqiya' also had a special screening at the Berlinale in 2014.
