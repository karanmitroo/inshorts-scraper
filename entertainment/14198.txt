Actress Rani Mukerji and her daughter Adira will reportedly make a cameo appearance in the upcoming film 'Befikre', which is being directed by Rani's husband Aditya Chopra. Further, Pamela Chopra, wife of late filmmaker Yash Raj Chopra and Aditya's mother, will also feature in a cameo. The film, starring Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor, will release on December 9.
