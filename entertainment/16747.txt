The first look of 'Tamasha', starring Ranbir Kapoor and Deepika Padukone, has been revealed. In the film, directed by Imtiaz Ali, Ranbir plays "a free-spirited nomad who likes to spread happiness, and wanders from place to place," and he bumps into Deepika along the way. Set to release on Nov 27, the film has been shot in France's Corsica.
