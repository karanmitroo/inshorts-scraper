'Let's Nacho', another song from the film 'Kapoor & Sons', was released on YouTube on Tuesday. The song, written by Kumaar and Christopher Pradeep, has been sung by Badshah and Benny Dayal. The party song is composed by Nucleya and Benny Dayal. The Sidharth Malhotra, Alia Bhatt and Fawad Khan-starrer is scheduled to release on March 18, 2016.
