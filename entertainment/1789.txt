Actress Ayesha Jhulka, known for her role in Aamir Khan starrer 'Jo Jeeta Wohi Sikandar' which featured the song 'Pehla Nasha', will make her comeback with 'Gadar' director Anil Sharma's film. She will be seen playing the role of a mother. "It's not the typical, conventional mother, which is what made me say 'yes' to it," said Ayesha.
