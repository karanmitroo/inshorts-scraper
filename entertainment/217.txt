Actress Sridevi, who passed away in Dubai on Saturday, was 13 years old when she played the role of Rajinikanth's stepmother in 1976 Tamil film 'Moondru Mudichu'. Sridevi was portrayed as the wife of an ageing widower in the film. Sridevi began her career as a child artiste in the South Indian film industry in the late 1960s.
