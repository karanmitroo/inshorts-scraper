A video showing the making of the song 'Bloody Hell' from the Kangana Ranaut, Shahid Kapoor and Saif Ali Khan starrer 'Rangoon' has been released. In the video, Kangana reveals she underwent training in 'Kalaripayattu', a form of martial art, and ballet dancing for the song. She further describes how Shahid did the song's signature step without learning it.
