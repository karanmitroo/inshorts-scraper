The trailer of Ranveer Shorey-starrer film 'Titli' was released on Tuesday. The film is about a young man named Titli, played by Shashank Arora, who aspires to escape his family business of car-jacking. The film, critically acclaimed at the 2014 Cannes Film Festival, has been directed by Kanu Behl and co-produced by Dibakar Banerjee. It will release on October 30.
