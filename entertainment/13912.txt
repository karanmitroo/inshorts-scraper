The Shahid Kapoor-starrer film 'Udta Punjab' has been passed by the Central Board of Film Certification (CBFC) with an ‘A’ certificate, CBFC Chief Pahlaj Nihalani said on Sunday. Nihalani added that the Censor Board did not demand the removal of the term 'Punjab' from the title. Meanwhile, the Bombay High Court has reserved its verdict on the case for Monday.
