Actor Shah Rukh Khan has been invited by Alan Rusbridger, the Principal of the Lady Margeret Hall College at the Oxford University. "Can we tempt to Oxford University to talk to our students at @lmhoxford? They love you," tweeted Rusbridger to the actor. In the past, SRK was honoured with the 'Chubb Fellowship' at USA's Yale University in 2012.
