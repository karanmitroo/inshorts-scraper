The trailer of the Ranbir Kapoor, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Anushka Sharma starrer romantic drama film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' has been released. The film also stars Fawad Khan in a special role and Shah Rukh Khan in a cameo appearance. Written and directed by Karan Johar, the film is scheduled to release on October 28.
