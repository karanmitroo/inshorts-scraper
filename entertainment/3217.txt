Amitabh Bachchan and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan submitted documents of their financial dealings with the Enforcement Directorate (ED) in connection with the Panama Papers probe. Earlier, Amitabh denied the allegation that he was the director in at least four offshore shipping companies. Further, the Bachchans and Ajay Devgn will reportedly be summoned by the ED for interrogation in the ongoing case.
