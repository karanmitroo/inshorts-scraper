The makers of the Shah Rukh Khan and Alia Bhatt starrer 2016 film 'Dear Zindagi' have released a scene titled 'Kahani Kya Hai', which was deleted from the film. In the scene, psychologist Jehangir Khan, played by Shah Rukh, narrates the story of a Portuguese woman Dona Maria to Kaira, Alia's character. Alia plays a cinematographer in the film.
