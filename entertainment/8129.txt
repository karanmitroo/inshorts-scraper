Singer Ed Sheeran will guest star in the seventh season of the HBO series 'Game of Thrones'. "Guess the cats out the bag," tweeted the 26-year-old singer while confirming the news. "For years we tried to get Ed Sheeran on the show to surprise Maisie," said the show's co-creator David Benioff. Maisie Williams, who portrays Arya Stark, is Sheeran's fan.
