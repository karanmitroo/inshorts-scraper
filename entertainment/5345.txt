Ex-Bigg Boss contestant Mandana Karimi has filed a case of domestic violence against husband Gaurav Gupta, whom she married in January 2017. Mandana claimed she was asked to convert to Hinduism, quit acting, and accused her mother-in-law of harassment. She has asked for a maintenance of ₹10 lakh per month and a compensation of ₹2 crore for loss of career.
