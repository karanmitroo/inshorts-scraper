Actress Priyanka Chopra along with Academy Award-winning actress Jodie Foster have attempted to recreate 'Toxic', the 2003 song by Britney Spears in a new episode of 'Lyrical Improv with Lynn Hirschberg' by W magazine. While Priyanka sings the lyrics, Foster chooses to read out the lines in the song, which include, "You are dangerous... I am loving it."
