Actors Deepika Padukone and Fawad Khan walked the ramp at the FDCI India Couture Week 2016 for designer Manish Malhotra. Malhotra's opening show, inspired from Persian architecture, was titled 'The Persian Story'. The event, which started on Wednesday in New Delhi, will see the participation of 11 designers including Rohit Bal, Tarun Tahiliani and Anita Dongre.
