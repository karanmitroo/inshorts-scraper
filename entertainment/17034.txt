Choreographer-director Remo D'Souza, whose film 'ABCD 2' will hit the theatres soon, plans to do a full-fledged film with Bollywood's dancing diva Madhuri Dixit. "There are some ideas in mind right now, but let’s see how it shapes up", he said. "...I don’t want to cast her (Madhuri) for a small role", Remo added.
