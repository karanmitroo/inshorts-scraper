The Akshay Kumar and Huma Qureshi starrer 'Jolly LLB 2' has earned ₹50.46 crore in its opening weekend. The film minted ₹13.20 crore on Friday, ₹17.31 crore on Saturday and ₹19.95 crore on Sunday, as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. The film registered the third highest opening weekend of 2017, after the Shah Rukh starrer 'Raees' and Hrithik's 'Kaabil'.
