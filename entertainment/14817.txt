The Arjun Kapoor and Kareena Kapoor starrer film 'Ki and Ka' has emerged as the third highest weekend opening grosser of 2016, having minted ₹25.23 crore in its first weekend in India. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film collected ₹7.30 crore on Friday, ₹8.41 crore on Saturday and ₹9.52 crore on Sunday.
