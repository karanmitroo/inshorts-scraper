The first look of actor Akshay Kumar from the upcoming court drama film 'The State vs Jolly LLB 2' has been revealed. Starring actors Huma Qureshi, Arshad Warsi and Saurabh Shukla, the film is a sequel to the National Award-winning 2013 film 'Jolly LLB'. Written and directed by Subhash Kapoor, the film is scheduled to release on February 10, 2017.
