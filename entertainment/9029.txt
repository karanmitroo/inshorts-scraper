According to reports, actress Manisha Koirala is set to play Sanjay Dutt's late mother Nargis Dutt in the upcoming biopic on Sanjay. Ranbir Kapoor will be portraying Sanjay's role in the film, which will be directed by Rajkumar Hirani. The role of Sanjay's late father Sunil Dutt will be portrayed by Paresh Rawal.
