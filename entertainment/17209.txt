'Sponge Out of Water' that released across India today, is a bizarre, clever and fun animated film, said Hindustan Times. The sequel to the film 'The SpongeBob SquarePants' (2004), is chaotic but enjoyable, wrote IANS while mid-day claimed it can be seen by both kids and adults. The film has been rated 3/5 (TOI), 4/5 (HT, mid-day) and 2.5/5 (IANS).
