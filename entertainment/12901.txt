A new poster for Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor starrer 'Befikre' was revealed on Tuesday. The poster was shared with the caption, "Guess who's making waves in the middle of the sea! @Vaaniofficial & @RanveerOfficial sizzle it up!" To promote the film, which releases on December 9, Yash Raj Films has been unveiling posters on the ninth of every month.
