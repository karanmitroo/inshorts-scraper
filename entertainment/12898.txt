According to reports, actors Fawad Khan, Sooraj Pancholi and Shraddha Kapoor will star in the sequel to the 2000 film 'Dhadkan'. Fawad and Sooraj have reportedly been approached for  the characters played by Suniel Shetty and Akshay Kumar respectively. Shraddha will reportedly play Shilpa Shetty's character, who was the love interest for both the heroes.
