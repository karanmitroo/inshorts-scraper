'Independence Day: Resurgence', the sequel to the 1996 film 'Independence Day', which released on Friday, "relies... heavily on cliches", wrote India Today. Mid-Day called it "bigger, louder, showier than the first one" while HT called it "a devastatingly dumb film... more disaster porn to rob your money". It has been rated 1/5 (India Today), 2.5/5 (Mid-Day) and 2/5 (Hindustan Times).
