The teaser for the upcoming British black comedy film 'T2', which is a sequel to the 1996 film 'Trainspotting', has been released. Director Danny Boyle has returned for the sequel along with actors Ewan McGregor, Ewen Bremner, Jonny Lee Miller and Robert Carlyle, who will reprise their roles. The film is scheduled to release on January 27, 2017.
