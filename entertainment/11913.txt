Veteran singer Asha Bhosle has tweeted that she deleted "abusive" followers from her Twitter handle. "Didn't know so many abusive kutte were following me," she tweeted. Earlier, Bhosle faced abuse for tweeting 'Jai Hind' and her fans assumed Pakistani users were abusing her. She clarified that she did not mean to imply Pakistanis when she used the term "abusive kutte".
