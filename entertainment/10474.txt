Actors Varun Dhawan and Alia Bhatt will recreate the song 'Tamma Tamma Loge', originally featuring Madhuri Dixit and Sanjay Dutt, for their upcoming romantic comedy film 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania'. Varun tweeted a picture from the sets, captioned, "#Tammatamma 2.0. Something's never change some songs never die." The film, directed by Shashank Khaitan, is scheduled to release on March 10, 2017.
