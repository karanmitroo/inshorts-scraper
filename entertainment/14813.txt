The Patralekha and Gaurav Arora starrer film 'Love Games', which released on Friday, is "just too dangerous for your mind", wrote Hindustan Times. The film "gives up quickly" on the storyline, The Indian Express said. The Vikram Bhatt directorial has been rated 0.5/5 by The Indian Express, 1.5/5 by Hindustan Times and NDTV, and 2/5 by Rediff.
