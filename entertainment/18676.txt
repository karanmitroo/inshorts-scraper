Rhea Chakraborty has been announced as the female lead of Y-Films' 'Bank-Chor'. She has a cracker of a role in the film where she will be seen with Riteish Deshmukh for the first time. 'Bank-Chor' is a comic-caper, that tells the story of 3 morons trying to rob a bank who pick the worst day possible when everything goes wrong.
