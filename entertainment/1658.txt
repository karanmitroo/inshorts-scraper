The release date of the upcoming Anil Kapoor and Madhuri Dixit starrer 'Total Dhamaal' has been announced as December 7, 2018. The third instalment in 'Dhamaal' franchise will also star Ajay Devgn. Directed by Indra Kumar, the original cast members from the first two films Riteish Deshmukh, Arshad Warsi and Jaaved Jaaferi have also been retained.
