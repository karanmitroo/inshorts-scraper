The song 'Phurrr', composed by Grammy Award-winning American record producer DJ Diplo and Indian music director Pritam for the Shah Rukh Khan and Anushka Sharma starrer 'Jab Harry Met Sejal', has been released. The track has been sung by Mohit Chauhan and Tushar Joshi with lyrics by Irshad Kamil. The film is scheduled to release on August 4.
