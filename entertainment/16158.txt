Hollywood's horror-comedy film 'Goosebumps' which released in India on Friday "is a pleasure ride" and "maintains its breakneck speed amidst the lunacy", wrote TOI. The film "is a fairly interesting translation of a book series (Goosebumps, written by RL Stine) into film", said ET. The Rob Letterman directorial has been rated 3.5/5 (TOI) and 3/5 (HT and ET).
