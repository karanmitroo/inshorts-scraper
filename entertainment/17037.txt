The first look of Amitabh Bachchan and Farhan Akhtar starrer 'Wazir' was released on YouTube on Wednesday. Big B will be seen in the role of Pandit Omkar Nath Dhar while Farhan plays the role of Daanish Ali in the film. The Bejoy Nambiar directorial, also starring Neil Nitin Mukesh, Aditya Rao Hydari, will release in December this year.
