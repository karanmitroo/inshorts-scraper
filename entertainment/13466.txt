Filmmaker Rohit Shetty has clarified that 'Golmaal 4' will not be a remake of the 2013 Tamil film 'Soodhu Kavvum'. "Though I have the rights of the Tamil film Soodhu Kavvum... I will make Soodhu Kavvum later," said Rohit. Ajay Devgn, Arshad Warsi, Tusshar Kapoor and Kunal Kemmu will reprise their roles in the fourth installment of the 'Golmaal' franchise.
