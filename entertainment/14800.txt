Filmmaker Kabir Khan on Thursday took to Twitter to announce that he will be working with actor Salman Khan in his next film. This will be the third time the duo will come together after the hit films 'Ek Tha Tiger' (2012) and 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' (2015). The film, which is yet untitled, will release on Eid 2017, the filmmaker added.
