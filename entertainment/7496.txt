The first poster of the Sidharth Malhotra and Manoj Bajpayee starrer 'Aiyaary' has been released. The film is reportedly a crime thriller, based on the 1987 Opera House heist which involved a group posing as CBI officers for a fake income tax investigation raid. Directed by Neeraj Pandey, 'Aiyaary' is scheduled to release on January 26, 2018.
