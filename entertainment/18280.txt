Modelled on London's Madame Tussauds, the wax museum in Kolkata was inaugurated by West Bengal CM Mamata Banerjee, and actors Amitabh Bachchan and Shah Rukh Khan on Monday. The museum, named 'Mother's Wax Museum' after Mother Teresa, was opened with 19 statues including waxen replicas of historical personalities to luminaries of the Indian cinema. It is located in New Town.
