Actor Amitabh Bachan won the Best Actor award for 'Piku' at the 63rd National Film Awards announced on Monday. Meanwhile, Kangana Ranaut won the award for the Best Actress for 'Tanu Weds Manu Returns'. Notably, this will be the third National Award for Kangana. Filmmaker Sanjay Leela Bhansali was named Best Director for 'Bajirao Mastani'.
