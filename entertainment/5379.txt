Parineeti Chopra and Arjun Kapoor will be seen together for the second time onscreen in Yash Raj Films' upcoming movie 'Sandeep Aur Pinky Faraar'. The 'dramatic thriller' will be directed by Dibakar Banerjee, who has earlier worked on films like 'LSD: Love Sex Aur Dhokha', 'Oye Lucky! Lucky Oye!' Previously, Parineeti and Arjun were seen together in 'Ishaqzaade' (2012).
