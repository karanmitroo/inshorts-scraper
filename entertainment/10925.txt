Russian producer Alexey Petrukhin has said that he has approached Shah Rukh Khan and Ranbir Kapoor for his film 'Viy: Journey To India', the third instalment in the 'Viy' franchise. "We have approached SRK to be the lead actor... We want to bring SRK and Ranbir together," said Petrukhin. Notably, Viy's second instalment will star Jackie Chan and Arnold Schwarzenegger.
