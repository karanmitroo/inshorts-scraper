Actor Hrithik Roshan has featured on the January cover of 'Filmfare' magazine. The 42-year-old actor can be seen wearing a T-shirt and printed pants, teamed with a leather jacket. The cover story on the actor has been titled, 'Braveheart Hrithik Roshan emerges triumphant'. Hrithik will next be seen in the film 'Kaabil', also featuring Yami Gautam.
