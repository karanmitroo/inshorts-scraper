Baahubali 2 has set the record as the highest grossing film in India by earning ₹534 crore across all languages in the first week of its release. It beat the earnings of it's own prequel Baahubali: The Beginning and other Hindi-language films including PK, Sultan and Dangal to set the record. Notably, Baahubali 2 has earned over ₹800 crore worldwide.
