Actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan walked the red carpet on the third day of the 70th Cannes Film Festival in a powder blue coloured ballroom gown by Dubai-based Filipino fashion designer Michael Cinco. The off-shoulder gown featured a plunging neckline. Aishwarya accessorised minimally with a few silver rings. Notably, this is Aishwarya's 16th year at the film festival.
