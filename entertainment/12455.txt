Actor Arjun Kapoor is set to star with his uncle Anil Kapoor for the first time in the film 'Mubarakan'. The film's official handle tweeted, "Madness is about to start with the Chacha-Bhatija jodi!" Also starring Ileana D'Cruz and Athiya Shetty, it reportedly features Arjun in a double role. Directed by Anees Bazmee, it will release on July 28, 2017.
