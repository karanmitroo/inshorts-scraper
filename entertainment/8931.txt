Twinkle Khanna, while commenting on the same day release of her husband Akshay Kumar's 'Jolly LLB 2' with Gurmeet Ram Rahim Singh's 'Hind Ka Napak Ko Jawab', jokingly tweeted, "Oh no! Both my favourites have movies coming out!" She further wrote, "Confused which one should I cheer for because both are Jolly good fellows." Both films released on Friday.
