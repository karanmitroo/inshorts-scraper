Shahrukh and Salman Khan will relaunch tennis player Sania Mirza’s autobiography ’Ace Against Odds’, which was released last week. Shahrukh will launch the book on July 13 in Hyderabad, while Salman will launch it in Mumbai on July 17. An event will be held in New Delhi on July 15, which will be attended by Parineeti Chopra and Farah Khan.
