As per reports, actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan will make a special appearance in director Sanjay Leela Bhansali's upcoming period drama film 'Padmavati'. She will reportedly have a few dialogues and a song sequence in the film, which sources suggest will be "completely different from anything seen before". 'Padmavati' will feature Deepika Padukone, Ranveer Singh and Shahid Kapoor in lead roles.
