Child actress Harshaali Malhotra, known for her role in Salman Khan's 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan', will be starring with Arjun Rampal in her second film titled 'Nastik'. Rampal will reportedly portray the role of an atheist in the film, which will be directed by Shailesh Varma. The film will be a social drama which will have a message for all age-groups.
