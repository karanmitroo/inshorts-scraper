The Ajay Devgn starrer 'Shivaay' which released today "suffers from the malice of overdose", wrote The Indian Express. Hindustan Times wrote, "[L]oose ends restrict 'Shivaay' from becoming a smooth ride". India Today said, "Ajay Devgn tries hard but fails to save this film". The film has been rated 1.5/5 (IT), 2/5 (TIE) and 2.5/5 (HT).
