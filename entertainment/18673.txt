The trailer of the much talked about remake of Hrishikesh Mukherjee's Khoobsurat starring Rekha and Rakesh Roshan is here. Directed by Shashank Ghosh, Khoobsurat features Sonam Kapoor and the Pakistani actor Fawad Khan in lead roles. It has been produced by Sonam's sister Rhea and father Anil Kapoor along with UTV, and releases on 19 September.
