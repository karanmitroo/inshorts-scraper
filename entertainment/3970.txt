Actress Priyanka Chopra along with PM Narendra Modi and Nobel Peace Laureate Kailash Satyarthi featured as top influencers in LinkedIn India's Power Profiles List of 2017. MP Shashi Tharoor and YourStory Media Founder-CEO Shradha Sharma also featured on the list. The Power Profiles List considers profiles of professionals that were most viewed in India on the employment-oriented social network.
