The making of 'Haanikaarak Bapu', the first song from the Aamir Khan starrer 'Dangal', has been released. The video features an unplugged version of the song being performed by music director Pritam along with behind-the-scenes footage of the song's shoot. The song features Aamir along with the child artistes Zaira Wasim and Suhani Bhatnagar, who play his daughters in 'Dangal'.
