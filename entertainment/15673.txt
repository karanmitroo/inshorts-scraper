Hollywood erotic-drama 'Fifty Shades of Grey' has been listed among the top nominees of worst films at the annual Razzies Awards. Other films nominated in the category are 'Pixels', 'Jupiter Ascending', 'Paul Blart: Mall Cop 2', and 'Fantastic 4'. Further, Johnny Depp, Mila Kunis, Oscar-winners Gwyneth Paltrow and Eddie Redmayne have been nominated as the worst performers of 2015.
