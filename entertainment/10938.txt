The 47th International Film Festival of India (IFFI), considered to be Asia's oldest film festival, began in Goa on Sunday. The Republic of Korea is the focus country of IFFI 2016. Around 300 films from 90 countries, including National Award-winning epic drama film 'Baahubali' and Salman Khan's 'Sultan' will be screened at the eight-day long festival on till November 28.
