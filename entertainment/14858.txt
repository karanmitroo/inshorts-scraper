Animated Hollywood film 'Kung Fu Panda 3' was released on Friday following which it was among the most trending Google searches in India. It was most looked up in Pondicherry, Goa, Uttarakhand and Tamil Nadu. 'Pratyusha Banerjee', 'LIC AAO Result', 'Mushfiqur Rahim', 'Darling 2' and 'Savitri Movie Review' were some of the other trending searches on the same day.
