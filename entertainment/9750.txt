Actor Akshay Kumar's upcoming film 'Padman' will co-star actresses Sonam Kapoor and Radhika Apte. "Thrilled to be a part of this prestigious project," wrote Sonam on Instagram. 'Padman' is based on the life of Arunachalam Muruganantham, the inventor of a low-cost sanitary pad-making machine. Akshay's wife Twinkle Khanna will produce the film, which will be directed by R Balki.
