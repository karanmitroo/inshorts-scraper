Aamir Khan and Zaira Wasim starrer 'Secret Superstar' has beaten the opening day collections of 'Dangal' in China by earning ₹43.35 crore. 'Dangal' was the first Indian film in China to cross collections of over ₹1,000 crore, making it the highest grossing non-Hollywood film in the country. 'Secret Superstar' released in India on October 19 last year.
