The official trailer of the Priyanka Chopra-starrer film 'Jai Gangaajal' was released on Tuesday. Directed by Prakash Jha, the film is a sequel to his 2003 film 'Gangaajal'. Priyanka plays an upright cop named Abha Mathur in the film. 'Jai Gangaajal' also marks the acting debut of Jha. The film is scheduled to release on March 4, 2016.
