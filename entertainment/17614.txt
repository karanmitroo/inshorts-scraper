Weeks after issuing the list of 'banned-cuss-words', Censor Board has come under a new controversy by asking 'Dum Laga Ke Haisha' makers to mute the word 'lesbian' from the film. Social media users have expressed their concern over the issue. Board member Ashoke Pandit, who previously made controversial remarks on AIB Roast, has also condemned the move calling it 'shameful'.
