Hollywood director Paul Feig, known for directing the film 'Bridesmaids' has said that he would "love" to work with actor Shah Rukh Khan. The 53-year old director also admitted that his wife, Laurie Feig is a "Bollywood fanatic" and a big fan of the Bollywood actor. He further said that Laurie's favourite SRK film is 'Om Shanti Om'.
