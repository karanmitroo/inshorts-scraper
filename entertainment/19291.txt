Ballad of Rustom, a film by Ajita Suchitra Veera has made it to the Oscars Reminder List in the Best Picture category. This is Ajita's second film to be nominated for Oscars, after “Notes on Her” (2003). Notably, Ajita is the only director to win Film Society’s ‘Indibor Bose Award for Excellence in Cinema’ in 2013 after over 45 years.
