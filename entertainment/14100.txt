Actor Suresh Chatwal, known for his role as the commissioner in the Hindi television series 'FIR', passed away on Saturday in Mumbai. His son Yaman Chatwal said that Suresh succumbed to his 'ongoing illness'. Chatwal, who made his debut with the 1969 film 'Rakhi Rakhi', worked in several Bollywood films including 'Karan Arjun', 'Munna Bhai MBBS' and 'Om Shanti Om'.
