'Baahubali' won five awards including the Best Film at the 63rd Britannia Filmfare (South) Awards in the Telugu films category. While Mahesh Babu won the Best Actor award for the Telugu film 'Srimanthudu', Mammootty won the award for the Malayalam film 'Pathemari'. Notably, these awards have been honouring professionals in the Telugu, Tamil, Malayalam and Kannada film industries since 1970.
