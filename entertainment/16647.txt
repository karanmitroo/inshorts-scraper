Amitabh Bachchan and Dharmendra starrer 'Sholay' will complete 40 years of its release on Independence Day this year. Recalling his memories about the film, Salman Khan said, "I remember we went to see the premiere of Sholay... dressed in school blazers." Abhishek Bachchan also said, "A lot of films age, I feel Sholay is ageless."
