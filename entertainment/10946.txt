Actor Amitabh Bachchan shared a photo of himself on social media greeting Chris Martin, the lead singer of British band 'Coldplay'. Actress Shraddha Kapoor shared a picture with the singer and captioned it, "One of the most memorable nights of my life". Parineeti Chopra, Vivek Oberoi, Sussanne Khan and Sridevi's daughter Jhanvi Kapoor also shared pictures with the 39-year-old singer.
