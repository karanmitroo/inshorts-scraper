The trailer of the Harshvardhan Kapoor and Saiyami Kher starrer 'Mirzya' was released on Thursday. The film is inspired by the Punjabi folk tale of the tragic romance of 'Mirza Sahiban'. The film also marks the Bollywood debut of Harshvardhan and Saiyami. Directed by Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra, the film is scheduled to release on October 7.
