Taking the AIB roast row further, the Pune Police on Thursday registered FIRs against AIB, YouTube, and bollywood stars Deepika Padukone, Sonakshi Sinha, among 14 others, under sections 292, Section 29 of the Indian Penal Code and section 67a of IT Act. The accused can face upto 5 to 10 years of imprisonment and fine upto Rs.2 Lakh.
