Actor Salman Khan's upcoming film 'Tiger Zinda Hai' will release on December 22, as announced by Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. "Tiger Zinda Hai to release on 22 Dec 2017 [Fri]... Christmas on 25 Dec [Mon]... SOLID 4-day weekend on the cards!," tweeted Taran. The film will be directed by Ali Abbas Zafar and will also star Katrina Kaif.
