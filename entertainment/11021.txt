A behind-the-scenes video of the Shah Rukh Khan and Alia Bhatt starrer upcoming film 'Dear Zindagi' has been released. The video, titled 'Be Flawsome', features Alia and director Gauri Shinde talking about how they "celebrate" their flaws. Also starring Kunal Kapoor, Ali Zafar and Aditya Roy Kapur, 'Dear Zindagi' is scheduled to release on November 25.
