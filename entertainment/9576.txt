'Jung Hai Humri Aatankwad Se', a new song from the Gurmeet Ram Rahim Singh starrer film 'MSG Lion Heart 2' has been released. The song has been composed, sung and written by Gurmeet Ram Rahim Singh. The film, part of the 'MSG' film franchise, revolves around India conducting surgical strikes when Pakistan's democracy is on the brink of military takeover.
