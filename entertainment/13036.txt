Actress Vidya Balan has featured on the cover of the fashion magazine 'Verve' for its August issue. In the picture, the 38-year-old actress is seen in a black ensemble accessorised with earrings from 'Om Jewellers' and a watch from the luxury Swiss watch manufacturer 'Audemars Piguet'. The cover story on Balan has been titled 'Vidya Balan, Roots Don't Lie'.
