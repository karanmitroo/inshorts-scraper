A new trailer for the upcoming animated superhero film, 'The LEGO Batman Movie' has been released. The film is a spin-off of the 2014 film 'The Lego Movie' and is about the journey of DC Comics character 'Batman'. Featuring the voices of actors Will Arnett, Zach Galifianakis and Michael Cera, the Chris McKay directorial will release on February 10, 2017.
