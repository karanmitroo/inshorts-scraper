Cricketer Harbhajan Singh will be judging the new season of MTV's reality show 'Roadies'. "I am really looking forward to experiencing the pulse of India's millennial generation. My all-time favourite contestant is and will always be Rannvijay," said Harbhajan. The 14th season is called 'Roadies Rising' and will mark the return of actress Neha Dhupia as a co-judge.
