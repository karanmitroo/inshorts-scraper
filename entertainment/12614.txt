Lyricist Prasoon Joshi has written and recited a poem 'Sharam Aa Rahi Hai Na', which he has dedicated to the girl child. The video begins with clips of India's female Olympians including PV Sindhu, Dipa Karmakar and Sakshi Malik. Prasoon questions the Indian society on issues like the objectification of women, while celebrating women who brought in a "new dawn".
