The makers of the Akshay Kumar and Bhumi Pednekar starrer 'Toilet - Ek Prem Katha' have announced that the film will release on June 2, 2017. The film is being produced jointly by 'Cape of Good Films', 'Friday Filmworks' and 'Abundantia Entertainment'. Meanwhile, Shree Narayan Singh will make his directorial debut with the film.
