Filmmaker Ram Gopal Varma has tweeted a picture of first-time BJP MLA Angoorlata Deka in Assam, thanking her and PM Narendra Modi, for making him "love politics" for the first time. "If MLA can look like this, Achche din aagaye hai," Varma tweeted. He later clarified his tweet saying that he was complimenting Deka "since most MLA's don't look good".
