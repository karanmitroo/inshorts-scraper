Kaushik Ghatak 'Samrat & Co.' featruing Rajeev Khandelwal, Madalsa Sharma and Gopal Datt, has been rated 0.5 (India Today), 1 (Indian Express; DNA) and 2 (Times of India). Khandelwal plays a Sherlock Holmes-inspired super detective called upon to investigate a mysterious series of events. "The script allows for lots of silliness, and clueless characters, and sad clues," writes Indian Express.
