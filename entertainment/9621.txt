Actress Vaani Kapoor has featured on the January cover of Elle India. The 28-year-old actress is seen wearing a ruffle top from designer Rahul Mishra's Spring Summer 2017 collection. Vaani, who features in the story 'Vaani Kapoor, Bollywood's next big thing', will also be the celebrity judge for 'ELLE Graduates 2017', where promising young designers in Indian fashion are chosen.
