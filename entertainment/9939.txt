Richa Chadha's look from her upcoming Indo-American film 'Love Soniya' has been revealed. Richa will portray the role of village girl caught up in human trafficking. Directed by Tabrez Noorani, the international project will also star Hollywood actress Demi Moore, Freida Pinto, Manoj Bajpayee, Anupam Kher and Raj Kummar Rao. The film will be shot in Los Angeles and Mumbai.
