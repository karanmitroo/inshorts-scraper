Stand-up comedian and TV actor Gaurav Gera, known for his 'Shopkeeper' and 'Chutki' series, on Friday took to Instagram to share a Dubsmash clip featuring actor Ranveer Singh. In the video, Ranveer can be seen donning a wig and a moustache to feature as the shopkeeper, usually played by Gera. Gera can be seen dressed as a woman.
