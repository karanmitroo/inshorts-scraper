'Tere Dil Mein', a new song from the Vidyut Jammwal starrer film 'Commando 2' has been released. The song has been composed by Mannan Shaah, with vocals by Armaan Malik and lyrics penned by Aatish Kapadia. Also featuring Esha Gupta and Adah Sharma, the action film is scheduled to release on March 3.
