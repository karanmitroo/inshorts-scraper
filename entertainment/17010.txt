Emraan Hashmi and Vidya Balan starrer 'Hamari Adhuri Kahani,' which released this Friday, "is a watchable film with satisfactory performances, however, Rajkumar Rao remained its real star," says Hindustan Times. The dialogues of the Mohit Suri directorial seemed to belong to some other bygone era, writes Mid-Day. The film has been rated 0.5/5 (Indian Express), 2/5 (HT) and 2.5/5 (Mid-Day).
