Actor Anupam Kher's look as former Prime Minister Dr Manmohan Singh has been revealed in the upcoming film The Accidental Prime Minister's first poster. "To reinvent yourself as an actor is to challenge yourself. Looking forward to portraying Dr Manmohan Singh," tweeted Kher while sharing the poster. Directed by Vijay Ratnakar Gutte, the film has been written by Hansal Mehta.
