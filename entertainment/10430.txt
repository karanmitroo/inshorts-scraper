The official trailer of 'The Fate of the Furious', the upcoming eighth instalment in the 'Fast & Furious' action film franchise has been released. Actor Dwayne Johnson will play Luke Hobbs while Vin Diesel will be seen portraying Dominic Toretto. Also starring Michelle Rodriguez and Nathalie Emmanuel, the film, directed by F Gary Gray, will release on April 14, 2017.
