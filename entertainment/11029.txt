Actress Vidya Balan has said that Ranbir Kapoor is the Kapoor of "all seasons" when asked to choose among Kareena Kapoor, Shahid Kapoor and Ranbir. "Ranbir yeah! He is too much of a Kapoor," she said. "I love him as an actor. I absolutely love him. 'Barfi' is my favourite film of his," added the 38-year-old actress.
