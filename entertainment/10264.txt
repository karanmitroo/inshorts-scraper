French disc jockey David Guetta will hold #Guetta4Good, a 'special charity performance' as part of electronic dance music festival 'Sunburn' in Bengaluru on January 12, 2017. All funds raised from the performance will be donated to help underprivileged children through an NGO. Guetta, who first toured India in 2012, will also perform in Mumbai, Hyderabad and Noida.
