A video shared by Dharma Productions shows actress Madhuri Dixit teaching a step from her song 'Tamma Tamma' from 1990 film 'Thanedaar' to actors Alia Bhatt and Varun Dhawan. Also featuring Sanjay Dutt, the song was originally sung by Bappi Lahiri and Anuradha Paudwal. A recreated version of the song will feature in Varun, Alia's upcoming film 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania'.
