Bollywood actress Deepika Padukone, who was born to former badminton player Prakash Padukone and wife Ujjala in Denmark, turned 30 years old on Tuesday. Padukone, a national level badminton player, undertook modelling as a full-time career at the age of 19. She first appeared in Himesh Reshammiya's song 'Naam Hai Tera', while her Bollywood debut was 'Om Shanti Om' (2007).
