According to reports, actor Ajay Devgn is set to play a cameo in Ranveer Singh starrer 'Simmba'. The film, which will be directed by Rohit Shetty, will feature Ranveer in the role of a policeman named 'Sangram Bhalerao'. Ajay, who has worked with Shetty in ten films so far, will reportedly appear as the character 'Singham' in the cameo.
