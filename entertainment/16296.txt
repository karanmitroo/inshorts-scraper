The first song of Ranbir Kapoor and Deepika Padukone starrer film 'Tamasha' was released on Thursday. The song titled 'Matargashti' has been composed by AR Rahman and sung by Mohit Chauhan. Ranbir can be seen mimicking Dev Anand in the song that is set in Corsica (France). Directed by Imtiaz Ali, 'Tamasha' is scheduled to be released on November 27.
