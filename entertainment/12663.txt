'Besabriyaan', the first song from the Sushant Singh Rajput starrer biopic 'MS Dhoni: The Untold Story', was released on Tuesday. The song has been sung by Armaan Malik and composed by Armaan's brother Amaal Mallik with lyrics by Manoj Muntashir. The song has been picturised on Sushant as Dhoni while portraying the cricketer's life as a school student.
