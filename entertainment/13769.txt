The Shahid Kapoor starrer 'Udta Punjab' has collected ₹33.80 crore in its opening weekend, making it the fifth highest opening weekend grosser of 2016. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film collected ₹10.05 crore on Friday, ₹11.25 crore on Saturday and ₹12.50 crore on Sunday. Notably, 'Housefull 3' remains the highest opening weekend grosser after collecting ₹53.31 crore.
