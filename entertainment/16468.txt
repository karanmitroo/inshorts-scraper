The first look of Shraddha Kapoor and Tiger Shroff starrer film 'Baaghi- Rebels In Love' was revealed on Twitter on Saturday. "Good morning ... Small treat for all you @ShraddhaKapoor & @iTIGERSHROFF fans- BAAGHI rebels in love", director Sabbir Khan tweeted. Co-produced by Sajid Nadiadwala and UTV Motion Pictures, the action-drama is scheduled to release on April 29, 2016.
