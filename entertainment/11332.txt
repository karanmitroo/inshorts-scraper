'Love You Zindagi', the first song from the Shah Rukh Khan and Alia Bhatt starrer 'Dear Zindagi' was released on Wednesday. It has been composed by Amit Trivedi, sung by Jasleen Royal with lyrics by Kausar Munir. SRK plays the character 'Jug' while Alia portrays the character 'Kaira'. Directed by Gauri Shinde, it is scheduled to release on November 25.
