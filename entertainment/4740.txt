Disha Patani turned showstopper for designer Manav Gangwani at the India Couture Week. She wore a velvet lehenga embellished with embroidery work with a fringed blouse and accessorised her look with a 'maangtika'. Talking about the collection, Manav said, "I've incorporated elements of design from different regions like Kashmir reflecting jamwars, bandhanis of Rajasthan and brocades of Varanasi".
