Yash Raj Films has released the first look of its upcoming flick, ‘Dum Laga Ke Haisha’ starring Ayushmann Khurrana (of Vicky Donor fame) in the lead role. Moreover, the actor’s female co-star is former YRF employee Bhumi Pednekar, who will be marking her acting debut with it. Ayushmann and Bhumi will play a middle-class married couple in the movie.
