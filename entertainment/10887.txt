Singer Selena Gomez, Lady Gaga and Ariana Grande attended the 2016 American Music Awards in designer wear, ranging from Prada to Roberto Cavalli. Selena wore a red gown from Prada while Lady Gaga was spotted in a white pantsuit by Brandon Maxwell paired with a wide-brim hat. The event's host model Gigi Hadid wore a Roberto Cavalli off-the-shoulder lace dress.
