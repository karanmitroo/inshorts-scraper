Actor Ajay Devgn tweeted, "Welcome to the crazy Golmaal family @ParineetiChopra," while confirming that actress Parineeti Chopra will feature in 'Golmaal Again', the fourth film in the 'Golmaal' franchise. "Thankyouuu!! I'm so excited to be a part of this family," Parineeti tweeted in response. Actors Ajay Devgn, Arshad Warsi and Tusshar Kapoor will reprise their roles in the film.
