Friday's release 'Interstellar', directed by legendary Hollywood film-maker Christopher Nolan, has been critically acclaimed. NDTV has described the space epic as "an allegory of its own aspirations, an argument for grandeur, scale and risk". The film stars Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain and Michael Caine, and has got 5 stars (The Telegraph), 4.5 stars (TOI) 4 stars (BBC).
