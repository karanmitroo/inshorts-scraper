Talking about Kareena Kapoor's relationship with her female colleagues on Karan Johar's show 'Koffee With Karan', actor Ranbir Kapoor said that his cousin Kareena  "hates all" her colleagues. Agreeing with him, the actress said she's not in the Hindi film industry to make friends. Kareena and Ranbir will feature on the second episode of the fourth season of the show.
