As per reports, Shahid Kapoor's sister Sanah Kapoor got engaged to actor Mayank Pahwa in a low-key ceremony earlier this month. Mayank is the son of actors Manoj Pahwa and Seema Pahwa, known for her role of 'Badki' in the Doordarshan series 'Hum Log'. Reportedly, the news of their engagement was accidentally revealed by late Om Puri's estranged wife Nandita.
