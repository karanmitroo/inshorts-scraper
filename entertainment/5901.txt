Amitabh Bachchan shared a picture on Instagram wherein he can be seen with a young Abhishek Bachchan. "Me recovering from my Coolie accident at home and a most caring and loving male nurse - Abhishek," read the caption alongside the picture. Amitabh was injured due to a mistimed jump during a fight scene with co-actor Puneet Issar on 'Coolie' sets.
