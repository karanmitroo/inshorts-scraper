Ranveer Singh has revealed he was slapped 24 times by co-star Raza Murad for a scene in his upcoming film 'Padmavati'. Raza Murad had to slap Ranveer for the particular sequence and director Sanjay Leela Bhansali took several retakes. Ranveer will be seen portraying the character 'Alauddin Khilji' in the film, which also stars Deepika Padukone and Shahid Kapoor.
