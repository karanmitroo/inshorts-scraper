Delhi University student Gurmehar Kaur, responding to actor Randeep Hooda's clarification that he should've been more careful while tweeting about her, has tweeted, "I didn't tweet, my hands did." Recently, Hooda explained that he retweeted Virender Sehwag's tweet because he had found it funny. "Whether it's a man or a woman, it's wrong to troll," he had added.
