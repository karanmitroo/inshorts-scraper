Actress Shraddha Kapoor has denied the reports that she will be doing Rohit Shetty's film 'Golmaal 4' with actor Ajay Devgn. "After the Haseena Parker film, I haven't signed anything else. I would love to work with Rohit Shetty sir, but there's no truth to the rumour," added Shraddha. Shetty is reportedly looking for an actress to replace Kareena Kapoor.
