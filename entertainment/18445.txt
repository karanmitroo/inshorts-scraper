The trailer of Yash Raj Films next multistarrer 'Kill Dil' released on Thursday. The trailer features Ranveer Singh and Ali Zafar as hitmen, Parineeti Chopra as bar dancer and Govinda as a gangster. Directed by Shaad Ali, who last made ‘Jhoom Barabar Jhoom’ in 2007, the movie will hit theaters on Nov 14.
