Microsoft co-founder Bill Gates mentioned Akshay Kumar's film 'Toilet: Ek Prem Katha', while sharing a list of tweets from 2017 that inspired him. Gates called the film a "Bollywood romance about a newlywed couple (that) educated audiences about India's sanitation challenge". Gates also tweeted news of the WHO certifying that Bhutan and Maldives had eliminated measles as "inspirational".
