Akshay Kumar and Nimrat Kaur-starrer 'Airlift', which hit the screens on Friday along with Tusshar Kapoor and Aftab-starrer 'Kyaa Kool Hain Hum 3', has earned more than the latter on its opening day. While 'Airlift' earned ₹12.35 crore, 'Kyaa Kool Hain Hum 3' grossed ₹8.15 crore at the box office in India, according to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh.
