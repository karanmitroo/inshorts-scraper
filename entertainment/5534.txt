The Salman Khan starrer Tubelight, with earnings of ₹64.77 crore in its opening weekend, has become the actor's lowest Eid opening in four years. In 2016, Sultan entered ₹100 crore club in its five-day extended weekend release. In 2014, Kick had earned ₹83.83 crore while the 2015 film Bajrangi Bhaijaan earned over ₹100 crore in its opening weekend.
