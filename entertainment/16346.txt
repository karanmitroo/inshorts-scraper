The Governor of Maharashtra, Chennamaneni Vidyasagar Rao, on Thursday rejected actor Sanjay Dutt's pardon plea in the 1993 Bombay blasts case. The plea was filed by former Press Council of India chief Justice Markandey Katju in 2013. Sanjay Dutt, who is guilty of acquiring illegal weapons from people involved in 1993 Mumbai bomb blasts, is currently out on parole.
