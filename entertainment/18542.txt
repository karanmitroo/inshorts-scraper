Rani Mukherjee starrer Mardaani, that hit the theatres on Friday, is described as a 'a revenge film featuring daredevil Mumbai Crime Branch inspector Shivani Sivaji Roy (Rani Mukerji) and Walt (Tahir Bhasin), a young-dynamic-organised criminal, as two fighting entities,' by Hindustan Times. The movie has been rated 2.5 (HT), 2 (Indian Express), 3 (TOI) and 3 (NDTV).
