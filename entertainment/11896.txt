'Teri Fariyad', the first video song from the Neha Sharma, Aditya Seal and Aashim Gulati starrer film 'Tum Bin 2' has been released. The track is based on the original song 'Koi Fariyad' from 'Tum Bin', which was sung by late Gazal maestro Jagjit Singh. The recreated version was composed by Ankit Tiwari and also features vocals by Rekha Bhardwaj.
