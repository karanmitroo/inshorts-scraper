'Bekhudi' song, from the Himesh Reshammiya-starrer film 'Teraa Surroor' was YouTube's tenth-most shared video on Facebook and Twitter in India on Friday. The song has been sung by Darshan Raval and Aditi Singh Sharma, while it has been composed by Himesh. Posted on the video sharing platform on Wednesday, the video has garnered over 6.23 lakh views so far.
