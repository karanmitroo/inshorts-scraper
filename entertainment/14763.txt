Actor Hrithik Roshan on Friday took to Twitter to announce that his upcoming film 'Mohenjo Daro' will release on August 12. Directed by Ashutosh Gowariker, the film is an epic adventure-romance set in the ancient city of Mohenjo-daro, Pakistan, in the era of the Indus Valley civilisation. It also features debutant Pooja Hegde and actor Kabir Bedi in pivotal roles.
