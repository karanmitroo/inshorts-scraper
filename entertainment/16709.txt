Salman Khan, Akshay Kumar and Amitabh Bachchan earn more than Hollywood actors Johnny Depp, Dwayne Johnson and Ben Affleck, according to Forbes' list of highest-paid actors. Of the 34 actors on the list, Indian stars comprised the second-largest number of listees. SRK was ranked 18th on the list, and Ranbir Kapoor 30th.
