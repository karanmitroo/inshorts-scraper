Emraan Hashmi-starrer 'Mr X' that hit the screens on Friday fails to engage the audience, writes News Nation. The film, where Hashmi gets visible and invisible simultaneously, has been called as a 'torture' by NDTV. Mr X, also starring Tanmay Bhat of AIB and Amyra Dastur, has been rated 2.5/5 (Zee News), 1/5 (NDTV), 1.5/5 (News Nation) and 3/5 (TOI).
