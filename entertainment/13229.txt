A cover of Sia's track 'Cheap Thrills' on the Indian stringed musical instrument 'veena' has been released by 'Veena Thambaps', a music duo consisting of Aranya Thambiappah and Athithya Thambiappah. The Thambiappah sisters reside in Toronto, Canada and have also shared the acoustic versions on the veena of other songs including 'Neruppu Da' from 'Kabali' and 'Laal Ishq' from 'Ram-Leela'.
