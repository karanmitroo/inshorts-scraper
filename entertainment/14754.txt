Punjabi actor Diljit Dosanjh on Wednesday took to Twitter to share his look in the upcoming Bollywood film 'Udta Punjab'. The actor plays cop Sartaj Singh in the film, which will mark his debut in Bollywood. Starring Shahid Kapoor, Alia Bhatt and Kareena Kapoor in pivotal roles, 'Udta Punjab' is scheduled to release on June 17.
