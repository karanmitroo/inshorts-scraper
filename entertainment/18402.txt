Megastar Amitabh Bachchan will be participating in a fund raising event for J&K flood victims. The 71-yr-old actor has also appealed to the Hindi film industry and his fans to participate in the relief work. “Met Film Guild and Event team to participate in Telethon for Kashmir Help... “HUM HAIN UMEED-e-KASHMIR”... on October 18", Bachchan tweeted.
