New posters of the upcoming Richa Chadha and Ali Fazal starrer 'Fukrey Returns' have been unveiled. The sequel to the 2013 film 'Fukrey', 'Fukrey Returns' also stars actors Pulkit Samrat, Varun Sharma and Manjot Singh. Directed by Mrighdeep Singh Lamba, 'Fukrey Returns' is scheduled to release on December 15.
