The finals of the inaugural Pro Kabaddi League (PKL) saw Jaipur Pink Panthers emerge as champions, at Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Stadium (Mumbai), on Sunday. The Abhishek Bachchan-owned team pulled off a 35-24 victory over U Mumba. The winners were granted a sum of Rs. 50 lakhs, while the runner-ups took away Rs.25 lakhs as prize money.
