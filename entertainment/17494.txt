As per reports, Sidharth Malhotra, Alia Bhatt and Fawad Khan will soon be seen together in Karan Johar's and Rohit Shetty's 'Ram Lakhan' remake. Sidharth and Fawad will reportedly play the roles of Ram and Lakhan, originally played by Jackie Shroff and Anil Kapoor, while Alia will play the role of Radha, earlier portrayed by Madhuri Dixit.
