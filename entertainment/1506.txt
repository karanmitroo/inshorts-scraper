Varun Dhawan won the Best Actor award for 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania' and Sridevi was named the Best Actress for 'Mom' at Zee Cine Awards 2018. The Viewer's Choice Best Actor award went to Akshay Kumar for 'Jolly LLB 2' while Alia Bhatt won Viewer's Choice Best Actress award for 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania'. Meanwhile, 'Golmaal Again' was named the Best Film.
