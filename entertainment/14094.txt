'Veerappan', which released on Friday, collected ₹5.95 crore in its opening weekend. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film earned ₹1.79 crore on Friday, ₹1.96 crore on Saturday and ₹2.20 crore on Sunday. Directed by Ram Gopal Varma, the film stars Sandeep Bharadwaj as the titular character and is based on the life of Indian bandit Veerappan.
