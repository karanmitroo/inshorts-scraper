Actors Shatrughan Sinha and Akshay Kumar on Sunday slammed Bollywood personalities for their 'intolerance' remarks. "[U]ps and downs happen in every nation and you can't get into so much straight away that you start giving such bold statements", Akshay said. "[P]eople from the film industry brought up the issues of intolerance in haste... it is childish", Sinha said.
