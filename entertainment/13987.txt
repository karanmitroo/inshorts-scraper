The trailer of the Johnny Depp starrer 'Yoga Hosers' was released on Monday. The upcoming  horror comedy also stars Depp's daughter Lily-Rose Depp and Harley Quinn Smith. The film is a spin-off of filmmaker Kevin Smith's 2014 horror film 'Tusk' and the second film in his 'True North trilogy' of horror comedy films. 'Yoga Hosers' will release on July 29.
