The Bombay High Court has ruled that the Hrithik Roshan and Pooja Hegde starrer 'Mohenjo Daro' has not been plagiarised. Justice GS Patel dismissed the petition​ against the film's release and imposed a fine of ₹1.5 lakh against Akashaditya Lama, who had accused director Ashutosh Gowariker of stealing his script for the film. 'Mohenjo Daro' will release on August 12.
