Having a rich cast comprising Dev Patel, Tena Desae, Maggie Smith, Judi Dench, Richard Gere, John Madden's 'The Second Best Exotic Marigold Hotel' released Friday, gaining much claps and awe. The "commentary on the clash of Indian and Western ideas" becomes a treat to watch, writes HT. It has got 3/5 (HT, IBN Live), 2.5/5 (Indian express) and 3.5/5 (TOI).
