Darsheel Safary, who played the character 'Ishaan' in the 2007 film 'Taare Zameen Par', has said people tell him their lives improved after the film released. "I always think that even if I do 100 films, and people still remember me for 'Taare Zameen Par', it'll still feel nice," he added. The film completed 10 years of release on Thursday.
