Suspense-thriller 'Detective Byomkesh Bakshy!', that hit the screens Friday, has extremely convincing performances by Sushant Singh Rajput and Neeraj Kabi, says mid-day. The Dibakar Banerjee directorial keeps you guessing till the very end, writes Deccan Chronicle. The film, set in early 1940s, has been rated 3.5/5 (DC, mid-day, NDTV), 3/5 (Indian Express) and 4/5 (HT).
