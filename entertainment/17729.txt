'Roy', which stars Ranbir Kapoor, Arjun Rampal and Jacqueline Fernandez, was released Friday. Vikramjit Singh's directorial debut was supposed to be a romantic thriller but there is nothing romantic or thrilling in it, writes India Today. According to critics, the movie is 'slow and confusing'. It has been rated 0.5/5 (Indian Express, India Today), 2/5 (NDTV) and 2.5/5 (Zee News).
