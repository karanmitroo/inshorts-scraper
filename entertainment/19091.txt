TV stars are gearing up for the small screen’s ‘Box Cricket League’ in which Kamya Punjabi (of Bigg Boss fame) has become the proud owner of a cricket team, Jaipur Joshiley. Members of her team include Pratyusha Banerjee (also the brand ambassador), Sidharth Shukla, Sangram Singh, Ajaz Khan, among several others. The cricket league will begin from March-23.
