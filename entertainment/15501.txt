Indian actress Priyanka Chopra has been chosen as one of the award presenters at the Oscars 2016, the official Twitter handle of The Academy Awards announced on Tuesday. She will be joining other presenters including Jared Leto, Kerry Washington, and Julianne Moore among others. Reacting to the news, Priyanka tweeted, "Looking forward @TheAcademy!! This will be an insaaaane night!".
