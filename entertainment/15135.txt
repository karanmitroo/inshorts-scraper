Bollywood actress Shraddha Kapoor on Wednesday unveiled the first official poster of her upcoming film 'Baaghi', also starring Tiger Shroff, on Twitter. "Abhi toh humne start kiya hai! Presenting the #BaaghiPoster. @iTigerShroff @BaaghiOffical @ngemovies @UTVfilms <3," Shraddha captioned the image. While the film is scheduled to release on April 29 this year, its trailer will be out on March 14.
