Varun Dhawan and Nawazuddin Siddiqui starrer ‘Badlapur’ has collected an estimated Rs 7 crore on Day 1 at the box office. With such box office collections, 'Badlapur' is now the fourth biggest opening grosser of 2015. This year’s biggest opener is ‘Roy’ with Rs 10.40 crore, followed by Rs 9.3 crore from ‘Baby’ and Rs 7.05 crore from ‘Tevar’.
