Actor Neil Nitin Mukesh got married to Rukmini Sahay on Thursday in Udaipur. Rukmini wore a red and orange lehenga embellished with stones, resham and sadhi work, while Neil wore a gold and maroon sherwani for the wedding. Both outfits were designed by Sarath Krishnan. Bollywood celebrities including Rishi Kapoor and Pamela Chopra, late Yash Chopra's wife, attended the wedding.
