After winning the Miss India title in 1984, Juhi Chawla went on to represent India at the Miss Universe contest, where she won the 'Best National Costume' award. The actress, who turned 49 on Sunday, made her acting debut with the 1986 film 'Sultanat'. She later won the Filmfare in the  'Best Actress' category for 'Hum Hain Rahi Pyar Ke'.
