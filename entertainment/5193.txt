Akshay Kumar, Shah Rukh Khan, Anupam Kher and Huma Qureshi were among Bollywood celebrities who slammed the terror attack on pilgrims returning from Amarnath Yatra. "Saddening to see innocent lives being taken. Prayers for the victims," tweeted SRK. "For years Kashmiri Muslims have helped Hindu brothers undertake #AmarnathYatra. Shame on terrorists. We will not let hate win," tweeted Huma.
