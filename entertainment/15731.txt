'Hua Hai Aaj Pehli Bar', the new song of upcoming film 'Sanam Re', featuring Pulkit Samrat and Urvashi Rautela was released on YouTube on Tuesday. The song, composed by Amaal Mallik, is sung by Amaal and Armaan Mallik and Palak Muchhal. The Divya Khosla Kumar directorial, also starring Yami Gautam, is scheduled to release on February 12, 2016.
