A new trailer for the upcoming movie, 'The Huntsman: Winter's War', which is a prequel to the 2012 film, 'Snow White and the Huntsman', has been released. Actor Chris Hemsworth returns as 'The Huntsman Eric', while Oscar winning actress Charlize Theron will reprise her role as the evil 'Queen Ravenna'. The movie is scheduled to release on April 22, 2016.
