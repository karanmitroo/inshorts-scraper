'Chandralekha', a new song from the upcoming film 'A Gentleman- Sundar, Susheel, Risky' shows actress Jacqueline Fernandez pole dancing. The song has been composed by Sachin- Jigar with vocals by Vishal Dadlani and Jonita Gandhi and lyrics by Vayu. Directed by Krishna DK and Raj Nidimoru, the film also stars Sidharth Malhotra and is scheduled to release on August 25.
