Actor-turned-author Twinkle Khanna launched her second book titled 'The Legend of Lakshmi Prasad' on Tuesday. Celebrities including her husband Akshay Kumar, mother Dimple Kapadia, Ranbir Kapoor, Alia Bhatt and Karan Johar were present at the book-launch event. Twinkle's first book was 'Mrs Funnybones: She's Just Like You and a Lot Like Me', which was published in 2015.
