Actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan was named 'Beautiful Global Indian Icon' at Vogue Beauty Awards 2017. Shahid Kapoor and his wife Mira Rajput were named the 'Most Beautiful Couple'. While Akshay Kumar won the award for 'Most Beautiful Man of the Decade', Varun Dhawan won the 'Most Beautiful Man Of The Year' award and Disha Patani was named 'Fresh Face'.
