Amitabh Bachchan recently sent a hand-written note to Randeep Hooda, praising his work  in 'Sarbjit'. "I had always admired your talent, but after watching you in 'Sarbjit' last night I could not resist writing to you and expressing my immense admiration for your work in the film," wrote Bachchan. Hooda took to Twitter to share a picture of the note.
