President Pranab Mukherjee Sunday presented the 62nd National Film Awards, including Dada Saheb Phalke Award to veteran actor Shashi Kapoor, at a ceremony held at Delhi. With 45 awards in Feature Film and 21 in Non-Feature film category, Best actress, Best actor and Best popular film honour were bagged by Kangana (Queen), Vijay (Nanu Avanalla Avalu) and Mary Kom respectively.
