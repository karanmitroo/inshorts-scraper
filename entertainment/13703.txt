The Indian band 'Euphoria', with vocalist Palash Sen, on Tuesday released a new single 'Halla Bol'. The song has been described by the band as "the war cry of the meek, the quiet and the non-pretentious common man". It features the band's commentary on India's various issues with spoofs on Arvind Kejriwal, Arnab Goswami, Vijay Mallya and Aamir Khan's 'PK'.
