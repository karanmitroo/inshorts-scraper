'Aave Re Hitchki', the new song from the Harshvardhan Kapoor and Saiyami Kher starrer 'Mirzya' has been released. The song has been sung by Mame Khan and Shankar Mahadevan and composed by the music director trio Shankar, Ehsaan and Loy. Gulzar has penned the lyrics for the song. Directed by Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra, the film will release on October 7.
