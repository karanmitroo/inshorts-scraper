The first look of actor Tiger Shroff from his upcoming film 'Munna Michael' has been revealed. The 26-year-old actor tweeted, "Enjoying every "step" of this journey no matter how difficult or complexed. Here's our tribute to the king!" The romantic-action film will be Tiger's third film with director Sabbir Khan, after his debut film 'Heropanti' and this year's release 'Baaghi'.
