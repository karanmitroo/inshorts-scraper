A new poster featuring actor Jimmy Sheirgill for the upcoming film 'Happy Bhag Jayegi' has been revealed. Sheirgill will be seen as the character 'Bagga' in the film while actress Diana Penty portrays the titular character 'Happy'. The film also stars Abhay Deol and Ali Fazal. Directed by Mudassar Aziz, the film is scheduled to release on August 19.
