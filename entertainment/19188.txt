Even after facing failure in acting career, Singer Sonu Nigam is gearing up for second innings for an upcoming project, directed by Aakashdeep. Aakashdeep sorted out his role for lead when he convinced Sonu to sing peppy number on screen for his film, Santa Banta Pvt. Ltd. Sonu has featured earlier in Betaab, Jaani Dushman:Ek Anokhi Kahani, Love In Nepal.
