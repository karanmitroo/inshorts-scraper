Pictures show Alia Bhatt, Varun Dhawan and Sidharth Malhotra celebrating Holi together on Monday. They were present at the annual Holi party hosted by Times Group MD Vineet Jain at the Sun & Sand Hotel in Mumbai. Filmmaker Ayan Mukerji, Urvashi Rautela, Nora Fatehi and Elli Avram were also among the celebrity guests at the party.
