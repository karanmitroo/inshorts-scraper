The first song 'Indiawaale' from Shah Rukh and Deepika starrer 'Happy New Year' has been released by the team of the film. The song features the entire star cast of the film, Shah Rukh Khan, Deepika Padukone, Abhishek Bachchan, Boman Irani and Vivaan Shah, dancing to the groovy number. The film is scheduled to release on October 24 this year.
