Yesteryears actress and Salman Khan's step mother Helen danced to the title track of Khan's upcoming film 'Prem Ratan Dhan Payo' (PRDP) along with Waheeda Rehman. The video which has been uploaded on YouTube was captured using the Dubsmash app. Both the actresses have worked together in various films including 'Baazi' and 'Kala Bazar'. PRDP releases on November 12, 2015.
