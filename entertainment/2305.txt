The trailer of the upcoming Richa Chadha and Ali Fazal starrer 'Fukrey Returns' has been released. 'Fukrey Returns' is a sequel to the 2013 film 'Fukrey' and also stars actors Pulkit Samrat, Varun Sharma and Manjot Singh. Directed by Mrighdeep Singh Lamba and co-produced by Farhan Akhtar, the film is scheduled to release on December 15.
