Abhishek Bachchan has backed out of 'Border' filmmaker JP Dutta's upcoming war drama 'Paltan', less than 24 hours before the crew left for shoot. Talking about Abhishek's exit, Dutta's daughter Nidhi said, "It has come as a shock to us." As per reports, actual army men will be a part of 'Paltan', which also stars Suniel Shetty and Arjun Rampal.
