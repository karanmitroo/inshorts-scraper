Emilia Clarke, who plays the character of Daenerys Targaryen in HBO's popular series 'Game of Thrones', has been named the Sexiest Woman Alive by Esquire magazine. The 28-year-old also featured in Arnold Schwarzenegger starrer 'Terminator: Genisys' which released this year. In 2015, Esquire had named Spanish actress and model Penelope Cruz as the Sexiest Woman Alive.
