George Barris, the creator of the 'Batmobile' as seen in the 1960s Batman television series, died at the age of 89 on Thursday in Los Angeles. Barris is credited with customising cars for Hollywood movies and TV series and even celebrities like Frank Sinatra, Elvis Presley and Sylvester Stallone among others. Notably, the Batmobile was auctioned for $4.2m in 2013.
