Actress Alia Bhatt's look from her upcoming film 'Raazi' has been unveiled. Directed by Meghna Gulzar and produced by Karan Johar, the film will also star actor Vicky Kaushal. 'Raazi' is an adaptation of Harinder S Sikka's novel 'Calling Sehmat', which is about a Kashmiri spy married to a Pakistani man during the Indo-Pakistan War of 1971.
