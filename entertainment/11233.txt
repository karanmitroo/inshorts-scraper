'Ki Kariye Nachna Aaonda Nahin', the new song from the Neha Sharma and Aditya Seal starrer 'Tum Bin 2' was released on Monday. The song, featuring television actress Mouni Roy, is a recreation of a Punjabi song by the same name. It is composed by Gourov Roshin, with vocals by Neha Kakkar, Raftaar, Hardy Sandhu and lyrics by Kumaar.
