Actors Tiger Shroff and Disha Patani will recreate the 1999 Punjabi song 'Mundian To Bach Ke' in the upcoming film 'Baaghi 2'. "Everybody is recreating Punjabi songs and Mundian To Bach Ke was the mother of all hits of that time," said the film's director Ahmed Khan. 'Baaghi 2' is scheduled to release on March 30.
