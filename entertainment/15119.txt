Actress Parineeti Chopra was featured on the cover of the March issue of Harper's Bazaar Bride. The 27-year-old actress, in what she described as a 'modern bride' avatar, wore an embellished wrap dress by Temperley London and accessorised her look with jewellery from Hazoorilal by Sandeep Narang. She also donned white henna on her hands in the cover shot.
