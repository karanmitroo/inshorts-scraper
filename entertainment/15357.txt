The Ryan Reynolds and Morena Baccarin starrer film 'Deadpool', that released today, is "fantastic" and a "gift from God", Hindustan Times wrote. It is "the kind of superhero movie which is definitely different", TOI said. "Deadpool is deliciously irreverent, subversive and uproariously funny", it added. It has been rated 3.5/5 (HT, NDTV) and 4/5 (TOI).
