Actress Radhika Apte has featured on the cover of the November issue of 'Femina Wedding Times' magazine. She can be seen in a saree by fashion designer Monisha Jaising with her hair and make-up done by award-winning artist Elton J Fernandez. She has been photographed by Rohan Shrestha. The cover story on Radhika is titled 'Radhika Apte: Anything but Predictable'.
