Bollywood actress Rekha, has emulated Nargis' idolatrous look from the Bollywood classic 'Mother India', as is revealed in the first look of her upcoming film 'Super Nani'. The movie, starring Sharman Joshi, Shweta Kumar, Randhir Kapoor and Anupam Kher alongside Rekha, will release on Diwali this year. The official trailer of the film will hit the screens on Sep-19.
