Indian television actor Parth Samthaan of 'Kaisi Yeh Yaariyan' fame has accused the producer of the show, Vikas Gupta, of 'touching him inappropriately'. Parth has also stated in a legal notice that Gupta has not paid him his fees. Notably, Parth was recently in news when a screenshot of a WhatsApp group, 'Whores and more Whores', created by him surfaced.
