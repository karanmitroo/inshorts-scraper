The Delhi HC has sent a notice to the director and producer of Aamir Khan starrer 'PK' over plagiarism. Novelist Kapil Isapuri filed a plea in the court alleging that certain portions of 'PK' had been plagiarised from his book 'Farishta' released in 2013. Isapuri has sought Rs 1 crore in damages from producer, director and scriptwriter of 'PK'.
