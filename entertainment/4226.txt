Akshay Kumar and Bhumi Pednekar starrer 'Toilet- Ek Prem Katha' was screened for over 13,000 economically less privileged children in various multiplexes in Kolkata. The screenings were organised as a charity initiative by the NGO Round Table India in collaboration with an online entertainment ticketing platform. The organisers said they hoped to inculcate good sanitation habits among children.
