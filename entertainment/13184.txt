The teaser for the upcoming song 'Kala Chashma', from the Sidharth Malhotra and Katrina Kaif starrer 'Baar Baar Dekho', has been released. The song is a rendition of the Punjabi song 'Tenu Kala Chashma Jachda Hai' by Amar Arshi. Sung by Amar Arshi, Badshah and Neha Kakkar, the new song will be released on July 27.
