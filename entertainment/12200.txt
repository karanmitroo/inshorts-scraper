Actor Amitabh Bachchan has recited the song 'Work' by Rihanna featuring rapper Drake. Bachchan said, "I don't know what it means but it is spelt W-E-R-K. So it's werk, werk, werk." In the video, the 73-year-old actor recites lyrics from the song including, "He say me have to... Work, work, work, work, work, work!..." and "Dry!... Me a desert him".
