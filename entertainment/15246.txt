'GF BF', the single featuring Sooraj Pancholi and Jacqueline Fernandez, was YouTube's most viewed video in India on Tuesday. The song, composed and sung by UK-based R&B and Hip Hop singer Gurinder Seagal, marks the singing debut of Jacqueline. Posted on the video sharing platform on February 17, the song has garnered over 44.04 lakh views so far.
