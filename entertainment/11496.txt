Actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan has said that the intimate scenes with actor Ranbir Kapoor in the upcoming film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' are "not frivolous sensuality". "It is about comfort level with my director... It wasn't [worrying]...It is about how you approach a character... It is done in a classy way," added Aishwarya.
