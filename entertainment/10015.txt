The Michael Fassbender starrer Assassin's Creed "finds a way to disappoint... everyone," wrote Hindustan Times. The Indian Express wrote, "(despite being) the most well-cast video-game movie...the script, performances...cannot keep pace."  (The film) becomes another exercise in squandering the... talents of its cast and crew," wrote Times of India. It was rated 1.5/5 (HT), 2/5 (TIE) and 3/5 (TOI).
