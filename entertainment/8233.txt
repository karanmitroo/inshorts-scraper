Television actor Karanvir Bohra, while on his way to Bhuj for a film shoot, met with a car accident on Tuesday wherein his car got completely smashed. Karanvir, who portrays the character 'Rocky Akhilesh Raheja' in the serial 'Naagin 2', said, "It's a miracle we survived." He also cautioned people to drive safely in India as traffic rules aren't followed.
