Bollywood superstar Shah Rukh Khan has shared the first look of 'Dilwale'- his upcoming film with actress Kajol. "Get ready for #DILWALE this December, Rohit Shetty Ishtyle! #Dilwale18Dec", he tweeted. The film, that brings SRK and Kajol together after 'My Name is Khan' (2010), also features Varun Dhawan and Kirti Sanon.
