The 17th Mumbai Film Festival was inaugurated by Maharashtra CM Devendra Fadnavis on Thursday. It was organised by the Mumbai Academy of the Moving Image (MAMI) and managed by Anupama Chopra, Kiran Rao and Nita Ambani. The festival was opened by Hansal Mehta’s “Aligarh”, a biopic on Shrinivas Ramachandra Siras, who was suspended from Aligarh Muslim University for being homosexual.
