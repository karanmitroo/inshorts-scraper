Hollywood actor Ryan Reynolds, who featured in 2016 superhero film 'Deadpool', was named 'Hasty Pudding Man of the Year 2017' by Harvard University's student theatre group, 'Hasty Pudding Theatricals'. The award is given to actors for their contributions to the entertainment industry. Reynolds will be roasted by the group on February 3, after which he'll receive a pudding pot-shaped trophy.
