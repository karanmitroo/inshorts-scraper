Actress Hema Malini got the nickname Dream Girl after she was described as 'Raj Kapoor's Dream Girl' in the posters of her debut Bollywood film 'Sapno Ka Saudagar'. Hema, who turned 69 on Monday, said, "Nowadays I feel embarrassed... I am hardly a girl anymore!" Interestingly, Hema Malini has been nominated for the Best Actress Filmfare award eleven times.
