Shah Rukh Khan sang a few lines of the song 'Tujhe Dekha Toh' from his film 'Dilwale Dulhaniya Le Jayenge' at an event in Muscat, Oman. Shah Rukh, who had stepped in for Amitabh Bachchan to inaugurate showrooms for a jewellery brand, also imitated Big B to entertain the audience. Malayalam actress Manju Warrier was also there at the event.
