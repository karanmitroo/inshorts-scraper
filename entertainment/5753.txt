Despicable Me 3 which released on Friday, "is 1.5 hours of silly fun with a filling of marshmallowy niceness", wrote Hindustan Times. TOI wrote, "Film achieves what it sets out to - entertain the kids". Catch News said, "Film manages to reel adults in...with visually funny scenes and carefully curated 80's nostalgia". It was rated 3.5/5 (HT), 3/5 (Catch News).
