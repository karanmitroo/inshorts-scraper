Though Akshay Kumar's Entertainment didn't stand up to critics' expectations, it has made its way to become the 6th highest Akshay-Kumar-grosser. Based on opening day collections, Entertainment has left behind films like Once Upon Ay Time In Mumbai Dobaara (Rs. 11.25 crores) and Khiladi 786 (Rs. 10.40 crores). However, Rowdy Rathore remains at the top of the list.
