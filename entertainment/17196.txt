Actor Jimmy Shergill of 'A Wednesday' fame will be seen playing a politician in an upcoming film, 'Zainab', based on the 2013 Muzaffarnagar (Uttar Pradesh) riots. The film, Pranav Singh's debut directorial venture, will also feature Sanjay Suri, Ashutosh Rana and Turkish actress Suha Gezento. Reportedly, the film's shooting will begin next week in Lucknow, the capital of UP.
