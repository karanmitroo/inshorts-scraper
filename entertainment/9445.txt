Sushant Singh Rajput performed on Salman Khan's song 'O O Jaane Jaana' at 'Umang', the annual Mumbai Police show. He also danced to Shah Rukh Khan's 'Ruk Ja O Dil Diwane' and Hrithik Roshan's 'Kaho Na Pyaar Hai'. Akshay Kumar, Sidharth Malhotra, Alia Bhatt and Katrina Kaif also performed while Anushka Sharma and Ranveer Singh were among the attendees.
