Actress Scarlett Johansson made her film debut in the 1994 fantasy comedy 'North', when she was 9 years old. The actress, who turned 32 on Tuesday, won the 'Best Actress' BAFTA Film Award in 2004 for the film 'Lost in Translation'. She is best known for films like 'Lucy' (2014), 'Her' (2013), 'Vicky Cristina Barcelona' (2008) and 'The Avengers' franchise.
