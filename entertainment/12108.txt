Dairy cooperative Amul has released a poster on the split of Hollywood couple Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie. The poster has been captioned 'Brunch Lena!', which has been derived from the term 'Brangelina'. The poster further says, "Amul: Have with Pitta Brad!". Angelina Jolie has filed for divorce from Brad Pitt after 12 years of relationship.
