'Suno Ganpati Bappa Morya', a new song from Varun Dhawan, Jacqueline Fernandez and Taapsee Pannu starrer 'Judwaa 2' has been released. The song was composed by Sajid-Wajid with vocals by Amit Mishra and lyrics by Danish Sabri. The film has been directed by David Dhawan, who's also credited for directing the 1997 film 'Judwaa', and will release on September 29.
