The release date of 'Dhadak', which marks Sridevi's daughter Janhvi Kapoor's debut and also stars Shahid Kapoor's half-brother Ishaan Khatter, has been postponed to July 20. The Karan Johar co-production was earlier scheduled to release on July 6. Directed by Shashank Khaitan, the film is a remake of the 2016 Marathi film 'Sairat', which is the highest grossing Marathi movie.
