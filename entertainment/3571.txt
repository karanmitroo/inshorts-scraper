Ranveer Singh took to Instagram to share a video in which he can be seen recreating dance steps of the song 'Sarkai Lo Khatiya' from the 1994 film 'Raja Babu' with Karisma Kapoor. Ranveer captioned the video, "Serendipity! Courtesy Raja Babu!...posted a random video from set today, and who I do meet at the airport two hours later!"
