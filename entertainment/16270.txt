Sixteen-year-old Faisal Khan has become the first child star to win the eighth season of reality dance show 'Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa Reloaded'. TV actors Sanaya Irani, Mohit Malik and former actress Shamita Shetty lost to Faisal, who had previously won Dance India Dance Li'l Masters 2. The finale featured performances by the judges and actress Alia Bhatt.
