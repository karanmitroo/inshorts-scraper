The lead singer of the Irish rock band 'The Cranberries' Dolores O'Riordan has passed away at the age of 46. Known for songs like 'Zombie' and 'Linger',  O'Riordan passed away in London where she had a short recording session, as per her publicist. In 2017, the band had to cancel a part of their tour owing to O'Riordan's health issues.
