'Baat Ban Jaye', a new song from the upcoming Sidharth Malhotra and Jacqueline Fernandez starrer 'A Gentleman- Sundar, Susheel, Risky' has been released. It has been composed by Sachin-Jigar, sung by Siddharth Basrur and Priya Saraiya, with lyrics by Priya Saraiya. Directed by Krishna DK and Raj Nidimoru, the film is scheduled to release on August 25.
