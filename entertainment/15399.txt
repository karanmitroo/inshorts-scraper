'Akkad Bakkad' song, from the Pulkit Samrat and Yami Gautam starrer film 'Sanam Re' was YouTube's fourth-most shared video on Facebook and Twitter in India on Wednesday. The song has been composed and sung by Badshah along with singer Neha Kakkar. Posted on the video sharing platform on February 7, the song has garnered over 12.67 lakh views so far.
