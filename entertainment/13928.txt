'Dama Dama Dam', the first song from the Irrfan Khan starrer 'Madaari' was released on Friday. The song has been composed by Vishal Bhardwaj and sung by Vishal Dadlani. Directed by  Nishikant Kamat, the film also stars Jimmy Sheirgill and Tushar Dalvi in leading roles. The film is scheduled to release on July 15.
