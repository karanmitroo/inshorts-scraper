Reacting to cricketer Mahendra Singh Dhoni's resignation as the captain of India's ODI and T20 International cricket teams, actor Sushant Singh Rajput tweeted, "There is no one like you". " You're the reason for millions of smiles. Take a bow my Captain," he further wrote. Sushant portrayed Dhoni in the 2016 biopic 'MS Dhoni: The Untold Story'.
