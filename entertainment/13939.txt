Actress Shraddha Kapoor and her brother Siddhant Kapoor will be playing Haseena Parkar and Dawood Ibrahim respectively in the upcoming biopic 'Haseena', their father Shakti Kapoor has confirmed. Reportedly, actress Sonakshi Sinha had earlier been finalised for the role of Haseena. However, she walked out of the film due to date issues. The film will be directed by Apoorva Lakhia.
