Muhammad Bin Qasim, the son of separatist leader Asiya Andrabi has come in support of actress Zaira Wasim and called her a "girl of substance" for tweeting against Sports Minister Vijay Goel. "I am applauding how she stood up against the hypocrites who target us," Qasim said. Zaira had asked Goel to not connect her with "discourteous depiction" of women.
