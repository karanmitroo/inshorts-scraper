Director duo Sajid-Farhad’s comedy flick ‘Entertainment’, starring Akshay Kumar and Tamannaah Bhatia, released this Friday. The movie has been rated 1 (NDTV, Hindustan Times), 1.5 (India Today) and 3 (Times of India). “The film fails on several levels, and there's nothing remotely entertaining in this film,” says Hindustan Times. “Entertainment is a bagful of boring tricks,” writes India Today.
