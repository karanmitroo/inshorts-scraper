Released this Friday, 'Kaun Kitney Paani Mein', starring Kunal Kapoor and Radhika Apte, has a well-told story and is worth the viewers' time, said HT. The comedy-drama which is a satire on water scarcity also featuring Gulshan Grover has a faulty execution, said Indian Express. It has been rated 3/5 (HT), 2.5/5 (Zee News) and 1.5/5 (Indian Express).
