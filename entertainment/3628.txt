The Arjun Rampal starrer film 'Daddy' "does not glorify its subject but it does worse," wrote Hindustan Times (HT). "The screenplay of this biopic offers no surprises," said Times of India (TOI). "'Daddy' demands patient viewing as it sets out to explore a new story delivery style," wrote Firstpost. It has been rated 2/5 (HT), 3/5 (Firstpost) and 3.5/5 (TOI).
