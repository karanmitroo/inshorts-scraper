Actor Salman Khan has gifted a white colored Rolls Royce Phantom car to his newly-wed sister Arpita, who got married on Nov 18. The car costs around Rs 4 crore in India. Before gifting Rolls Royce Phantom, Salman Khan had also gifted a Rs 16 crore terrace flat at Carter Road to the newly-wed couple, Arpita and Aayush.
