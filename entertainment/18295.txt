The first look of 'Tanu Weds Manu Returns', the sequel to 'Tanu Weds Manu', is out. It features Kangana sporting two different looks- one with curly, long hair, wearing a salwar-kameez, while the other donning a jeans and a jacket with a short crop hairstyle. The film will retain its original cast- R Madhavan, Jimmy Sheirgill along with Kangana Ranaut.
