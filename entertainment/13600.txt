Following the release of the song 'Befikra', starring Disha Patani and Tiger Shroff, 'Disha Patani' was the fifth most trending Google search in India on Tuesday. It was most looked up in Uttarakhand, Odisha and Goa. 'England vs Iceland', 'Arnab Goswami Interview With Narendra Modi' and 'Independence Day: Resurgence' were some of the other trending searches.
