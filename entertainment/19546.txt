Director Faraz Haider's comedy flick 'War Chhod Na Yaar' that released this Friday has received 2 (TOI critic, DNA), 2.5 (IBN Live) and 3 (Zee News). Promoted as India's first war comedy, the movie, starring Sharman Joshi, Jaaved Jaaferi and Soha Ali Khan, is a light-hearted satire with a message - `hate war, not the warrior`.
