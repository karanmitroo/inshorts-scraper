The Imaad Shah and Ira Dubey starrer 'M Cream', touted as 'India's First Stoner Film', which released on Friday, "does not get you high", wrote India Today. Times of India called 'M Cream' "incomprehensible in parts, poignant in others but... mostly... pointless" while Hindustan Times called it "indie in nature". It was rated 0.5/5 (India Today), 2/5 (TOI) and 3/5 (HT).
