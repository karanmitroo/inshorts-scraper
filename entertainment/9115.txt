A recreated version of the song 'Hare Krishna Hare Ram' from the Vidyut Jammwal, Adah Sharma and Esha Gupta starrer film 'Commando 2' has been released. The song has been recreated by music composer duo Gourov and Roshin with vocals by Armaan Malik and Ritika. Directed by Deven Bhojani, the film is scheduled to release on March 3.
