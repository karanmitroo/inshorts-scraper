'The Judge' and 'Iron Man' star Robert Downey Jr, won big at the People's Choice Awards held Wednesday, receiving two awards- 'favourite dramatic movie actor' and 'favourite movie actor'. Robert's 'Avengers' co-star Chris Evans won the 'favourite action movie actor' award. Sitcom 'The Big Bang Theory' also won multiple awards including 'favourite network TV comedy' and 'favourite TV show'.
