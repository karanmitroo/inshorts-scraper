Priyanka Chopra's new song as a featured artist 'Young and Free' has been released. It has been sung in collaboration with Australian producer and DJ Will Sparks. Priyanka has written the lyrics along with Rachel Rabin, Toby Gad and Tyrone William Griffin Jr. This is Priyanka's fourth single after 'In My City', 'Exotic' and 'I Can't Make You Love Me'.
