Karan K Cheema, a Maharashtra-based businessman has sent Karan Johar a cheque of ₹320 along with a letter, to compensate for not watching Johar's upcoming film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil'. "As a businessman I... feel sad for your losses so I am enclosing a cheque  amounting ₹320 which is generally charged by movie theatres in our city," read the letter.
