The horror film 'Rings', the third instalment in 'The Ring' franchise, is "strange, dull and very, very unnecessary", wrote Hindustan Times. "Not a single scene manages to leave you shaken," wrote Times of India while Zee News wrote, "It grips you from the word go and keeps you interested". It was rated 1/5 (HT), 2/5 (TOI) and 3/5 (Zee News).
