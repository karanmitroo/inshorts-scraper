Amitabh Bachchan's upcoming film with Farhan Akhtar, has been officially renamed from 'Do' to 'Wazir', announced Akhtar on Twitter, Monday. "The Vidhu Vinod Chopra/ Bejoy Nambiar film is now officially titled Wazir," he wrote. Reportedly, Bachchan plays a paralysed chess grandmaster and Farhan plays an ATS officer in the film. It also stars Aditi Rao Hyadri in a prominent role.
