Comedy-drama 'Birdman' won big at the 2015 Academy Awards held Sunday. Directed by Alejandro Gonzalez Inarritu, who won best director, the film bagged the best picture award. The Michael Keaton starrer film also won Oscars for cinematography and original screenplay. Other than this, Wes Anderson's comedy, 'The Grand Budapest Hotel', too, reigned the 87th Academy Awards with 4 Oscars.
