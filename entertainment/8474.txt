The first look poster of the Amitabh Bachchan starrer political thriller 'Sarkar 3' was unveiled on Twitter by the film's director Ram Gopal Varma. "Amitabh Bachchan Is Back Angrier Than Ever," read the caption alongside the poster. Also starring Jackie Shroff, Manoj Bajpayee, Yami Gautam and Amit Sadh, 'Sarkar 3' is scheduled to release on April 7.
