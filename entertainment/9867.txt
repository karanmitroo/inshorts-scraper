Aamir Khan and Salman Khan are the only Bollywood actors to have films in the ₹300 crore club. Only four films in Bollywood, namely, 'PK', 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan', 'Dangal' and 'Sultan' have grossed over ₹300 crore at the domestic box office. Aamir's 2014 film 'PK' was the first Bollywood film to cross ₹300 crore, followed by Salman's 2015 release 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan'.
