Filmmaker Karan Johar has shared a picture on Instagram of his twins Roohi and Yash with Kareena Kapoor's son Taimur while captioning it, "And a new friendship begins." Karan and Kareena are known to be close friends. The three children were spotted at the birthday party of Rani Mukerji and Aditya Chopra's daughter Adira Chopra, who turned two on Saturday.
