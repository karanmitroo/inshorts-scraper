The BJP youth wing, Bharatiya Janata Yuva Morcha, have lodged an FIR against singers Sona Mohapatra and Rituraj Mohanty at the Sambalpur Police Station, Odisha, on Wednesday. The singers are accused of tampering with the cult Odia song ‘Rangabati Rangabati’, which was recorded in 1979, in a remix that they sang in the popular show Coke Studio @MTV.
