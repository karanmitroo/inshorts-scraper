The motion poster for the Ajay Devgn starrer 'Shivaay' has been released. The action thriller has been produced and directed by Devgn under his banner 'Ajay Devgn FFilms'. Actress Sayyeshaa Saigal, the grandniece of veteran actress Saira Banu, is making her Bollywood debut in 'Shivaay'. The film, which also stars Polish actress Erika Kaar, will release on October 28.
