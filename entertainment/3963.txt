Arijit Singh has sung 'Rangdaari', a new song from the Farhan Akhtar starrer upcoming film 'Lucknow Central'. Arjunna Harjaie has composed the song, while Kumaar has penned the lyrics. Directed by Ranjit Tiwari and also starring Gippy Grewal, Diana Penty and Ronit Roy, the film is scheduled to release on September 15.
