The sequel of Rohit Shetty’s 2011 flick ‘Singham’ was launched on Saturday night at a function in Mumbai. Ajay Devgn and Kareena Kapoor will play the lead. “I'm looking forward to starting work on the film, which goes on floors in the first week of March. We will hit screens on August 15 (Independence Day) this year.” said Kareena Kapoor.
