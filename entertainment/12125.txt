Politician and former cricketer Navjot Singh Sidhu has quit the comedy show 'The Kapil Sharma Show'. Sidhu's wife Navjot Kaur said, "Sidhu will now completely focus on Punjab politics... that is why he has recorded all the shows till 30th September and has said goodbye to the cast." Sidhu was known for catchphrases like 'Thoko taali', used in the show.
