The track 'Beat Pe Booty' from Tiger Shroff's upcoming superhero film 'A Flying Jatt' has been released. The song has been picturised on Tiger and Jacqueline Fernandez, the film's lead actress. Composed by Sachin-Jigar, the song has been sung by Sachin, Jigar, Vayu and Kanika Kapoor.  'A Flying Jatt' is scheduled to release on August 25.
