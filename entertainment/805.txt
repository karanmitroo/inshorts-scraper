Actress Deepika Padukone has confirmed that she was paid more than her male co-stars Ranveer Singh and Shahid Kapoor for the film 'Padmaavat'. She confirmed this on the Neha Dhupia-hosted talk show 'BFFs with Vogue'. Earlier, it was reported that Deepika was paid ₹13 crore for the film, while Ranveer and Shahid got close to ₹10 crore each.
