Actress Kalki Koechlin has shared the first look poster of her film with Richa Chadha 'Jia Aur Jia'. In the film, the actresses play two different characters who share the same name 'Jia'. Arslan Goni plays the film's male lead, who was described by Kalki as the 'Aur'. The film has been directed by Howard Rosemeyer.
