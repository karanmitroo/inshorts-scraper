Alia Bhatt has bought a minority stake in Mumbai-based fashion-tech startup StyleCracker, as part of its pre-Series A funding round. "This has come from heart and not mind," Bhatt said, adding that StyleCracker's Co-founder Archana Walavalkar has been her stylist for many years. Founded in 2013, StyleCracker curates personalised boxes which include clothes and accessories for its customers.
