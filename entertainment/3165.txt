Anamika Majumdar, a social worker from Jamshedpur, has become the first person to win ₹1 crore on the ongoing ninth season of 'Kaun Banega Crorepati' (KBC). Anamika is said to have reached the jackpot question worth ₹7 crore but decided to quit as she did not know the answer. The episode, which was shot this week, is yet to air.
