'Main Faraar Sa', a new song from the Taapsee Pannu and Amit Sadh starrer film 'RunningShaadi.com' has been released. The song has been composed by Anupam Roy, with vocals by Anupam Roy and Hamsika Iyer, and lyrics penned by Tanveer Ghazi. Directed by Amit Roy, the romantic comedy film is scheduled to release on February 17.
