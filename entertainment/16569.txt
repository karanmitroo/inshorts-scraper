Director Umesh Shukla's 'All Is Well', starring  Abhishek Bachchan, Asin and Rishi Kapoor, hit the screens today. The movie, which revolves around a struggling singer who is ready to do whatever it takes to make it big in life, has been rated 0.5 star (Indian Express), 1 star (HT), 2 star (India Today) and 2.5 star (NDTV).
