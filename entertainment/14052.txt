The first look of actress Sonakshi Sinha in the upcoming film 'Noor' has been revealed. Sonakshi plays a Pakistani journalist named Noor Roy Chaudhry in the film, which is based on Saba Imtiaz’s 2014 novel, ‘Karachi, You’re Killing Me'. The film, to be directed by Sunhil Sippy, is a comedy crime thriller and will go on floors in July.
