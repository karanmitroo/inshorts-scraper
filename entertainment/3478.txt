Pavan Malhotra, who has acted in films like 'Black Friday' and 'Bhaag Milkha Bhaag', said people know him by his on-screen characters and not by his name. However, he added, "The fact that they recognise me...is a big compliment for me." "I'd be lying if I say I don't like the attention I get for [my] work," Pavan further said.
