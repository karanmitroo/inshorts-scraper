A behind-the-scenes video from the Taapsee Pannu starrer upcoming film 'Naam Shabana' has been released. Titled 'Naam Shabana Sizzle', the video shows Taapsee training for action scenes in the film. Prequel to 2015 film 'Baby', the film features Akshay Kumar in an extended cameo appearance. The film's trailer is scheduled to release on February 10.
