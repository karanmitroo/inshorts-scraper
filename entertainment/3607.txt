Former 'Bhabi Ji Ghar Par Hai' actress Shilpa Shinde has been trolled for being "too fat" in the newly released item song 'Maro Line' from the film 'Patel Ki Punjabi Shaadi'. Many others also trolled her for her choice of clothes. Shilpa had earlier quit 'Bhabi Ji Ghar Par Hai' alleging that the show's makers "mentally tortured" her.
