The Akshay Kumar starrer 'Housefull 3', which released on Friday, "features... people trying to make fun of the disabled and the coloured", wrote Indian Express. The film features "goofball gags and childish jokes", wrote Times of India, while India Today called it "entertaining only for its male leads". It was rated 0/5 (Indian Express), 3/5 (TOI) and 2/5 (India Today).
