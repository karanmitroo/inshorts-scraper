Registering its name in the list of the most controversial films of all time, Seth Rogen and James Franco starrer 'The Interview' was released worldwide on YouTube, and in 500 independent theatres, Friday. Lacking any satire, the film is still 'strangely enjoyable', wrote HT. It has received 3.5/5 rating (HT), and has been rated 48% (Rotten tomatoes) and 8.5/10 (IMDb).
