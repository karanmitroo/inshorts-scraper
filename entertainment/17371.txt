Despite being rated badly by critics, Sunny Leone-starrer 'Ek Paheli Leela' has surpassed films like 'NH10', 'Shamitabh' and 'Detective Byomkesh Bakshy' in terms of 1st day collection. The film, having Leone in a Rajasthani avatar, minted Rs 5.30 crores on Friday, leaving behind NH10 (Rs 3.35 crores), Shamitabh (Rs 3.5 crores) and Detective Byomkesh Bakshy (Rs 4.41 crores).
