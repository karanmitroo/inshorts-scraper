On the occasion of Ganesh Chaturthi, Sanjay Dutt released the 'Ganesh Aarti', which he has sung for his comeback film 'Bhoomi'. The music and programming of the track has been credited to Aditya Dev while the original lyrics have been retained. Directed by Omung Kumar, the film also stars Aditi Rao Hydari and is scheduled to release on September 22.
