A new trailer of the upcoming film 'Mubarakan', starring Arjun Kapoor for the first time with his uncle Anil Kapoor, was released on Thursday. Also starring Athiya Shetty and Ileana D'Cruz, the film features Arjun in a double role as twins Karan and Charan. Directed by Anees Bazmee, 'Mubarakan' is scheduled to release on July 28.
