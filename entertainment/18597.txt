Deepika Padukone has denied reports that said she recommended Ranveer Singh for the cameo in 'Finding Fanny'. "I don't have a say in casting", said that actress, to which co-star Arjun Kapoor confirmed. Ranveer, who plays Deepika's dead husband in the film, was signed on director Homi Adajania's suggestion, according to him. Apparently, he didn't charge anything for the appearance.
