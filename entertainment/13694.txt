The Yami Gautam and Pulkit Samrat starrer 'Junooniyat', which released on Friday, "lacks intensity", wrote Hindustan Times. "The film tells the same old pappy, pulpy story of a pair of young lovers up against sundry 'pyaar ke dushman'," wrote NDTV while Times of India wrote that it "lacks conviction". It has been rated 1.5/5 (Hindustan Times, NDTV) and 2.5/5 (TOI).
