Superstar Salman Khan's close friend Aamir Khan on Thursday  visited him at his residence, a day after the Mumbai Sessions Court convicted him in the 2002 hit-and-run case. Many other celebrities from the industry including Rani Mukerji, Preity Zinta and Sonakshi Sinha visited the actor on Wednesday. Superstar Shah Rukh Khan also met Salman before the verdict on Tuesday.
