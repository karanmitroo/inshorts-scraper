'Oye Oye', the new song from the Emraan Hashmi, Prachi Desai and Nargis Fakhri-starrer film 'Azhar' was released on Friday. The song, featuring Nargis, has been sung by Aditi Singh Sharma and Armaan Malik. It is a recreation of 'Oye Oye' song, featuring Sangeeta Bijlani, from the film 'Tridev' (1989). Notably, Nargis plays the role of Sangeeta Bijlani in 'Azhar'.
