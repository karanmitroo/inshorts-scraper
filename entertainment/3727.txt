Ajay Devgn's film 'Baadshaho', with total earnings of ₹12.03 crore on its opening day, has become the actor's 3rd biggest opener after 'Singham Returns' and 'Bol Bachchan'. While the 2014 film 'Singham Returns' earned ₹32 crore on its first day, the 2012 film 'Bol Bachchan' had opening day earnings of ₹12.10 crore. 'Baadshaho' also stars Emraan Hashmi and Ileana D'Cruz.
