Actor Hugh Jackman has revealed 'Logan' as the title of his upcoming film, where he reprises the role of 'Wolverine', in the film's first poster. The film, which is the tenth installment in the 'X-Men' film series, is the sequel to the 2009 film 'X-Men Origins: Wolverine' and the 2013 film 'The Wolverine'. 'Logan' will release on March 3, 2017.
