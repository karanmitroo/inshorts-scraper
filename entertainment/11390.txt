A promotional video of the first episode of celebrity talk show 'Koffee with Karan', featuring actors Shah Rukh Khan and Alia Bhatt has been released. The promo was shared by Karan Johar on Twitter, captioned, "#koffeewithkaran season 5!! Starts 6th November....season opener with @iamsrk and @aliaa08 on @StarWorldIndia #KWKonSW...." The first episode of the show will premiere on November 6.
