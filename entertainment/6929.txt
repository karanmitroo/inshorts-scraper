Actress Swara Bhaskar revealed that she was groped at Rajkot airport during the promotion of her 2015 film Prem Ratan Dhan Payo. She added, "There were around 2,000 people at the airport to see him [Salman Khan]. Despite the fact there was security, people got in and it was Anupam Kher who made sure I got into the car."
