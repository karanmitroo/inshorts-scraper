The Meryl Streep and Tom Hanks starrer 'The Post' "will be seen as one of the most important films of our times," wrote Hindustan Times. Koimoi wrote, "Meryl Streep...remodels her complex character with ease." Times of India called it a "stark reminder of the purpose of the press in any democracy." It was rated 5/5 (HT) and 4/5 (Koimoi, TOI).
