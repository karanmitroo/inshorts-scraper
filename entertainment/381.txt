Producer Prernaa Arora has filed a lawsuit against Abhishek Kapoor over the delay of his directorial 'Kedarnath', the debut film of Sara Ali Khan. Kapoor's unprofessional behaviour and mismanagement have been cited as the reason for the delay. Reports added Kapoor was supposed to complete 'Kedarnath' in October 2017 but the film has been in the making for 16 months.
