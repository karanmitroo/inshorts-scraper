The latest release in the Avengers franchise, 'Avengers: Age of Ultron' "is an overload of eye-popping action sequences", but "devoid of soul", writes NDTV. Directed by Joss Whedon, it has a plethora of stars including Robert Downey Jr., Chris Hemsworth, Mark Ruffalo, Chris Evans and Scarlett Johansson. It has been rated 2/5 (Indian Express), 2.5/5 (NDTV) and 3/5 (IBN Live).
