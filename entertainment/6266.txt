Music label T-Series has denied singer J Star's allegations of stealing the song 'Main Tera Boyfriend' for the upcoming film 'Raabta'. T-Series claims 'Main Tera Boyfriend' is an adaptation of their 2007 song 'Girl Friend Boy Friend'. "J Star infringed our song released... and he should be thankful we didn't take legal action against him," said T-Series in a statement.
