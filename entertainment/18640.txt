Dharmesh Tiwari, the actor who portrayed Kripacharya in the epic TV series Mahabharat, died Wednesday morning in Chandigarh, where he'd gone for a show. Mr Tiwari was also the general secretary of the Cine & TV Artistes Association (CINTAA) and president of the Federation of Western India Cine Employees (FWICE). He died at 63, reportedly due to high-diabetes.
