'Haanikarak Bapu', the first song from the Aamir Khan starrer upcoming biographical sports drama film 'Dangal' has been released. The song has been composed by Pritam, with vocals by Sarwar Khan and Sartaz Khan Barna and lyrics by Amitabh Bhattacharya. Directed by Nitesh Tiwari, the film is scheduled to release on December 23.
