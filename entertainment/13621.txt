Director Anurag Kashyap has shared a deleted scene from the recently released film 'Raman Raghav 2.0'. He tweeted, "We took this scene out from RR2.0, it came before Raghav goes to meet his father. Tell us if we shouldn't have." The scene features actor Vicky Kaushal as the  character 'Raghav', a cop, confessing his crimes in a video camera recording.
