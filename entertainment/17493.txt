Filmmaker Dibakar Banerjee is keen on making a sequel of his upcoming detective film, he said recently. “I am very interested in making ‘Detective Byomkesh Bakshy! 2′ because in this film, it’s the detective’s his first case...I want to...show the audience a new adventure of Byomkesh", he said. The character Byomkesh Bakshy was originally created by Bengali writer Saradindu Bandyopadhyay.
