An Indian-origin girl named Mayuri Bhandari has shared a video of herself dancing to Deepika Padukone's song 'Ghoomar' while figure skating at the Ice Centre in Las Vegas. "In honor of the film Padmaavat's release, here is my ice skating tribute to the beautiful song Ghoomar! As a Rajasthani, I skate to this song with pride," Mayuri captioned the video.
