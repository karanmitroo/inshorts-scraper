The Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor starrer 'Befikre', which released on December 9, has earned ₹34.36 crore in its opening weekend. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film earned ₹10.36 crore on Friday, ₹11.60 crore on Saturday and ₹12.40 crore on Sunday. The film released over 2,100 screens across India.
