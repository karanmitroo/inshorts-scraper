Actor Rupert Grint on Thursday met actor Paul Thornley, the actor who portrays the character 'Ron Weasley' in the play 'Harry Potter and the Cursed Child'. Grint, who played 'Ron' in the 'Harry Potter' films, met the other cast members after watching the play in London. The play stars Noma Dumezweni as 'Hermione Granger' and Jamie Parker as 'Harry Potter'.
