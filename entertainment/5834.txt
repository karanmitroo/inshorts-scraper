Actor Ajay Devgn took to social media to share his first look from the upcoming film 'Baadshaho'. The caption alongside the poster read, "Badass in the bandana." The film also stars Emraan Hashmi, Ileana D'Cruz, Vidyut Jammwal and Esha Gupta. Directed by Milan Luthria, the action thriller is scheduled to release on September 1.
