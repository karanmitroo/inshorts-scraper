Lyricist of the film 'Padmavati' AM Turaz has said the Sanjay Leela Bhansali directorial would have been super hit if it had released on its original release date December 1. "I have faith in the film. Whenever it's out, the world will love it," added Turaz. Film distributor Rajesh Thadani said, "Padmavati would have ended the lull phase of Bollywood."
