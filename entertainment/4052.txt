With earnings of ₹100.05 crore within eight days of its release, 'Toilet- Ek Prem Katha' has become Akshay Kumar's fifth film in nearly two years to enter the ₹100-crore club. It is reportedly based on Swachh Bharat Abhiyan. The other Akshay Kumar movies which earned over ₹100 crore, since January 2016, are 'Airlift', 'Housefull 3', 'Rustom', and 'Jolly LLB 2'.
