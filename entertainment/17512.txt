Shilpa Shetty Monday rubbished the allegations of Rs 9 crore fraud made against her by a Kolkata firm. Shilpa dared the company owner, Manoj Jain, to submit proofs supporting the accusations. The actress posted on Twitter, "Seeking legal advice...He's a defaulter & clearly a fraud..." Shilpa added, "His 2minute Fame can't b the cost of my hard earned reputation."
