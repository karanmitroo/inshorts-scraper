Actress Priyanka Chopra has shared a teaser featuring co-actor Dwayne Johnson
from upcoming Hollywood film 'Baywatch' on social media. The teaser was captioned, "Get the first peek of.... the world I'm gonna destroy! #wait4VictoriaLeeds #meetmyPrey." Priyanka will play the role of villain Victoria Leeds in the film. Directed by Seth Gordon, it is scheduled to release on May 26, 2017.
