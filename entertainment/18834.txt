Actress Deepika Padukone has topped the list of 'The Times 50 Most Desirable Women' of the year. She is followed by Katrina Kaif (same as last year), Jacqueline Fernandez and Sunny Leone. Moreover, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Kareena Kapoor Khan are the first two women to enter a separate list created for women, who remain 'Forever Desirable'.
