The Manoj Bajpayee starrer 'Traffic' which released on Friday, is "a must watch" for its "tight script" and "stellar performance", wrote Hindustan Times. NDTV called the film "racy and gripping" while India Today said that the film "lacks direction and pace". The film has been rated 4/5 (HT, NDTV) and 1.5/5 (India Today).
