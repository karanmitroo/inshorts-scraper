Ranvir Shorey-starrer 'Titli', which released on Friday, is the most impressive film of this year so far and keeps one hooked till the end, Hindustan Times said. The performances of all actors, including newcomer Shashank Arora who plays Titli, are excellent, wrote Indian Express. The Kanu Behl directorial has been rated 5/5 (HT), 4/5 (NDTV) and 3.5/5 (Indian Express).
