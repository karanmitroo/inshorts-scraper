Actor Ranveer Singh sang a birthday song on the sidelines of the IIFA Awards 2016 for actor Arjun Kapoor. Arjun, who turned 31 on Sunday, started his career in Bollywood as an assistant director to Nikhil Advani for the film 'Kal Ho Na Ho' in 2003. He made his acting debut with the 2012 film 'Ishaqzaade' opposite actress Parineeti Chopra.
