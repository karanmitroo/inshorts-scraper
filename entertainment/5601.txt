A video which has surfaced online shows actors Varun Dhawan, Taapsee Pannu and Jacqueline Fernandez practising a dance sequence for their film 'Judwaa 2'. They're seen dancing to the song 'Tan Tana Tan', which featured in the original 1997 film Judwaa. Their upcoming film is a remake of the 1997 film, which starred Salman Khan, Karisma Kapoor and Rambha.
