Rajkummar Rao has confirmed that he'll star opposite Aishwarya Rai in the upcoming film 'Fanney Khan'. "Aishwarya is the most beautiful woman in the world. It's a great story, there's a lot of love, and the content is not just entertaining but is detailed and different," said Rao. 'Fanney Khan' is the Hindi adaptation of the Dutch film 'Everybody's Famous!'.
