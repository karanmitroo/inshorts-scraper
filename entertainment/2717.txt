According to reports, television actress Mouni Roy will star in the Ranbir Kapoor, Alia Bhatt and Amitabh Bachchan starrer upcoming film 'Brahmāstra'. Details about her character haven't been revealed yet. The film, which will be the first part of a fantasy adventure trilogy, will be directed by Ayan Mukerji and is scheduled to release on August 15, 2019.
