Music composer AR Rahman Monday launched his new album "Raunaq", which has seven songs sung by artists including Lata Mangeshkar, Chitra, Shreya Ghosal, Mohit Chauhan, Shweta Pandit and Jonita Gandhi. Rahman has also played the role of a visual storyteller in the album. He dedicated the album to Vogue Empower, a social awareness initiative that draws attention to women's empowerment.
