Actress Sonam Kapoor on Thursday made her debut appearance at the fundraising event 'Cinema Against AIDS' (amfAR) gala at the Cannes International Film Festival. The 30-year-old actress was dressed in a pale blue Ralph & Russo gown with a cape like train and earrings by Kalyan Jewellers. Meanwhile, Mallika Sherawat also attended the gala in a purple Georges Hobeika dress.
