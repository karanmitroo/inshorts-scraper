Filmmaker Vipul Shah has said that the upcoming Arjun Kapoor and Parineeti Chopra starrer 'Namastey Canada' is not a sequel to his 2007 directorial 'Namastey London' and instead has a totally fresh script. Talking about the film, Parineeti said, "It is desi with a videsi heart...Arjun and I...will be playing to our Punjabi strengths but speak to the global audience."
