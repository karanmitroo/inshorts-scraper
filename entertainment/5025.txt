Shahid Kapoor, wife Mira Rajput, Salman Khan, Alia Bhatt and Varun Dhawan were among the celebrities who attended the IIFA Awards 2017. Mira wore a tangerine gown by Swapnil Shinde. Alia wore a black sparkly gown by Zuhair Murad and Shilpa Shetty chose a rose gold gown by Monisha Jaising. Meanwhile, Sonakshi Sinha wore a semi-sheer gown by La Bourjoisie.
