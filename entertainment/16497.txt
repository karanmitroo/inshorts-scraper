The teaser of bilingual romantic Indian film 'Size Zero', which has been shot in Tamil and Telugu, was released on Tuesday. Directed by Prakash Kovelamudi, the film features south Indian stars Anushka Shetty and Arya. The 34-second-teaser has no dialogue. The film, also starring Sonal Chauhan and Urvashi, is slated to release worldwide on October 2.
