Former India Under-23 footballer Prathamesh Maulingkar won the title of Mr India Supranational 2017 on Thursday and will represent India at the Mr Supranational 2018 competition. The 26-year-old, who hails from Goa, played as a defender for Pailan Arrows and Dempo in the I-League. Meanwhile, Jitesh Singh Deo from Lucknow bagged the title of Mr India World 2017.
