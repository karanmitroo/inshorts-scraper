Choreographer-director Remo D'Souza, who is celebrating the success of 'ABCD 2', Friday announced that 'ABCD 3' will also star actors Varun Dhawan and Shraddha Kapoor. "[O]nce we three are free from our other commitments, we will make ‘ABCD 3′,” Remo said. 'ABCD 2', which is a sequel to the 2013 film 'ABCD', has minted Rs. 104.44 crore since its release.
