Actors Bipasha Basu and Karan Singh Grover have featured on the Love issue of Harper's Bazaar Bride, the Bazaar Fashion. While Bipasha can be seen in a sequined gown by Manish Malhotra, Karan is in an all-black attire from Antar Agni by Ujjawal Dubey. The cover is titled 'Couple Goals' and this edition focuses on the couple's love story.
