Bhubaneswar’s 14-year-old Ananya Sritam Nanda was declared the winner of Indian Idol Junior 2 on Sunday as she beat her co-contestants Nityashree Venkataramanan and Nahid Afrin. She won Rs 10 lakh and vouchers from the show’s sponsors. The singing competition for children came to a finale on Sunday after 15 weeks of auditions and competition.
