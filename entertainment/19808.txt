Director Vikramaditya Motwane's Lootera, which released this Friday, has got a critics thumbs-up and is receiving flawless feedbacks from everywhere. The story's period has been set in 1953-54, and is about Varun(Ranveer Singh) who intrudes into a zamindar's house, wins his confidence and sweeps his girl(Sonakshi Singh). Music of Amit Trivedi and lyrics of Amitabh Bhattacharya have also been appreciated.
