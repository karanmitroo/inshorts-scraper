Actress Kajol, who has given several hit films with superstar Shah Rukh Khan, thinks Ranbir Kapoor scores higher than her long-time good friend and also Aamir Khan in terms of acting capabilities. In an upcoming episode of Koffee with Karan, Kajol ranked Ranbir No.1, SRK and Aamir second, Salman third and Hrithik fourth, in terms of their acting capabilities.
