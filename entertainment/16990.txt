In just 6 days of its release, Zoya Akhtar directorial 'Dil Dhadakne Do' has collected Rs 52.39 crore. The film was made on a budget of around Rs 85 crore. The film's first day collections were not strong. But, it picked up business on Saturday and Sunday by minting Rs 12.27 crore and Rs 14.25 crore respectively.
