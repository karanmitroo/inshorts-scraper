Actress Priyanka Chopra has been nominated at the Teen Choice Awards 2017 in the 'Villain' category for her Hollywood debut film 'Baywatch'. This is Priyanka's second nomination after she bagged one for her role in American television series 'Quantico' in 2016. Notably, actress Deepika Padukone has been nominated in four categories for her Hollywood debut 'xXx: Return of Xander Cage'.
