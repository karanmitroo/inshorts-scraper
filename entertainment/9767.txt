Actor Hrithik Roshan has featured on the January cover of 'Stardust' magazine. The 43-year-old actor can be seen wearing a denim blue shirt on the cover shot. The story on the actor has been titled, 'Hrithik's Reflections: I turn my insecurities to strengths'. Hrithik will be next seen in the film 'Kaabil', also featuring Yami Gautam.
