Tanu Weds Manu sequel has got embroiled in a legal tangle between director Aanand L Rai and the producers, after Rai announced the project. Producer Vinod Bachchan said, "Aanand is just the director. Tanu Weds Manu is my property. How can he make my film's sequel without my consent?" Bachchan is preparing to send a notice to Rai.
