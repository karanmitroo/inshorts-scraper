Actor Shah Rukh Khan's unreleased film 'Ahamaq' will be screened at the upcoming 18th edition of Jio MAMI Mumbai Film Festival. Directed by Mani Kaul, the film was released as a four-part TV mini-series on Doordarshan in 1991, but never got a commercial release. Jio MAMI Mumbai Film Festival will begin on October 20 and conclude on October 27.
