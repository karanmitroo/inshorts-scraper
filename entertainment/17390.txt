Ranveer Singh Tuesday revealed his first look from the film 'Dil Dhadakne Do', a Zoya Akhtar directorial, on Twitter. Ranveer, who is playing Kabir Mehta in the film, is seen posing on a cruise deck, donning a casual look, wearing T-shirt, denims and loafers. The multi-starrer film, about a dysfunctional Punjabi family on a cruise trip, will release on June-5.
