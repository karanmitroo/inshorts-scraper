The Vidya Balan and Arjun Rampal starrer 'Kahaani 2', which released on Friday, has earned  ₹16.97 crore in its opening weekend. The film collected ₹4.25 crore on Friday, ₹5.79 crore on Saturday and ₹6.93 crore on Sunday, as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. The film is the second instalment of the 'Kahaani' film franchise.
