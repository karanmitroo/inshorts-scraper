As per reports, Salman Khan's 2015 film 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' will release in China in 2018 with a new title. According to the Chinese website Douban, the film's Chinese title can be translated in English as 'Little Lolita Monkey King Bajrangi Bhaijaan'. Douban, which is reportedly like IMDb, also mistakenly lists Emraan Hashmi as one of the actors in the film.
