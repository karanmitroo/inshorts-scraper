Actress-filmmaker Nandita Das has confirmed that she and her husband Subodh Maskara have decided to separate. "Thankfully, it is very amicable. Our son is our foremost priority," added Nandita. Speaking further about her concern for her six-year-old son Vihaan, she said, "Separation is never easy, more so if you have a child... we are committed to ensuring his well-being."
