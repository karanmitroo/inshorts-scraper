According to reports, Pakistani actress Mahira Khan will feature along with Shah Rukh Khan in the upcoming film 'Raees'. Mahira is noted for her role in Pakistani drama television series, 'Humsafar', as Khirad Ehsan. She will be seen playing SRK’s spouse in ‘Raees’, which also features Nawazuddin Siddiqui and is jointly produced by Ritesh Sidhwani and Farhan Akhtar.
