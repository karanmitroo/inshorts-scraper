Actor Rajkummar Rao has been nominated in the 'Best Performance by an Actor' category at the 11th Asia Pacific Screen Awards (APSA) for his film 'Newton'. The Amit V Masurkar directorial has been nominated for the 'Best Screenplay Award'. Further, filmmaker Sanal Kumar Sasidharan also bagged a nomination in the 'Achievement in Directing' category for his Malayalam film 'Sexy Durga'.
