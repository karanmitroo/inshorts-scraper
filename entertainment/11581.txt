A video titled 'Surgical Strike Made Simple', featuring actress Pallavi Joshi, has surfaced online. Produced by 'Telling Tales Film', the actress uses an India-Pakistan cricket match as an example to explain how and why surgical strikes are conducted. Cricketers Sachin Tendulkar, Rahul Dravid and Shoaib Akhtar also feature in her explanation. The video has got over 60,000 views till date.
