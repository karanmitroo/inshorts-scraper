Actor Prabhas, known for his role in 'Baahubali: The Beginning', is set to get a wax statue at Madame Tussauds museum, Bangkok, becoming the first South Indian actor to achieve the feat. The statue will be unveiled in March 2017. Prabhas, 36, who made his debut with the 2002 film 'Eshwar', is known for his work mainly in Telugu cinema.
