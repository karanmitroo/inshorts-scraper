The Vidyut Jammwal and Adah Sharma starrer 'Commando 2', which released on Friday, "lacks heart and is all muscles", wrote Hindustan Times. "Vidyut has great screen presence and owns the action scenes," wrote India Today while Times of India called the film a "dreary supposed crime thriller". The film was rated 2/5 (HT), 3/5 (India Today) and 2.5/5 (TOI).
