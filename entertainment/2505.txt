Comedian Mallika Dua has responded to Twinkle Khanna's apology over her row with Twinkle's husband Akshay Kumar. In a Facebook post, Twinkle had written she reached a breaking point when her 5-year-old daughter Nitara was pulled into the row. Earlier, Mallika's father had slammed Akshay for the remark 'Mallikaji aap bell bajao, mai aapko bajata hu' on a laughter show.
