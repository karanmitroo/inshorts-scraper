The song 'Kawa Kawa' from the 2001 film 'Monsoon Wedding', which was sung by Sukhwinder Singh, has been recreated as 'Kaavaan Kaavaan' for Farhan Akhtar's upcoming film 'Lucknow Central'. The newly released track has been sung by Divya Kumar with lyrics penned by Kumaar. Directed by Ranjit Tiwari, the film is scheduled to release on September 15.
