Singer Selena Gomez won the 'Choice Female Artist' while Justin Bieber won the 'Choice Male Artist' at the Teen Choice Awards 2016, held in Los Angeles. 'Deadpool' won the Choice Movie award in the Action/Adventure category while Leonardo DiCaprio was declared the Choice Movie Actor (Drama) for 'The Revenant'. Meanwhile, 'Pretty Little Liars' won the Choice TV Show (Drama) award.
