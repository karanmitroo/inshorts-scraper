'Maahi Ve', a new song featuring actress Zarine Khan from the Sana Khan, Sharman Joshi and Gurmeet Choudhary starrer 'Wajah Tum Ho' has been released. It is a recreated version of the song 'Maahi Ve', featuring Malaika Arora Khan, from the 2002 film 'Kaante'. Composed by Gourov Roshin, the song has been sung by Neha Kakkar with lyrics by Kumaar.
