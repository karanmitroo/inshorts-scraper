Entertainment giant 'Disney' has set the record for highest grossing year at the international box office, by posting earnings of $5,851.4 million through November 1. The four highest-grossing films of 2016 include Marvel's 'Captain America: Civil War' with earnings of $1.15 billion, followed by 'Zootopia' with $1.02 billion. 'Finding Dory' is third on the list, followed by 'The Jungle Book'.
