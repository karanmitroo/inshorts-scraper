Alia Bhatt and Ranveer Singh walked the ramp as showstoppers for designer Manish Malhotra, whose show was the grand finale of India Couture Week 2017. Alia wore an ensemble with a trail lined with roses while Ranveer wore a black sherwani. Talking about his collection 'Sensual Affair', Malhotra said, "I wanted to focus on events like sangeet, mehendi and cocktail."
