The Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Vicky Kaushal starrer 'Raman Raghav 2.0', which released on Friday, has "some mesmeric bits... which belong to Siddiqui", wrote Indian Express. "The film has its quirks... but the writing lacks depth," wrote Times of India. Meanwhile, HT called it "a scary, sadistic and trippy ride". It was rated 2/5 (Indian Express), 2.5/5 (TOI) and 3.5/5 (HT).
