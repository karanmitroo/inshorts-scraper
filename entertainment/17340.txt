Actors Arjun Kapoor and Ranveer Singh will be seen hosting the International Indian Film Academy (IIFA) Awards 2015. The duo was last seen cracking jokes, mimicking, and sharing one-liners on the controversial AIB Roast show. IIFA 2015 will take place in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia from June 5 to 7. Last year's IIFA was hosted by Shahid Kapoor and Farhan Akhtar.
