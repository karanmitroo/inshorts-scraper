A new picture from the Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor starrer 'Befikre' was revealed on Tuesday on the occasion​ of Vaani's birthday. The official Twitter handle of Yash Raj Films wrote alongside the picture​, "She's as #Befikre as it gets & we love her for it! #HappyBirthdayVaani!!" Notably, Vaani made her acting debut in a supporting role in 'Shuddh Desi Romance'.
