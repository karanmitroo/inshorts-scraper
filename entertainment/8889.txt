Actress Alia Bhatt has said that she's not trying to step into Madhuri Dixit's shoes in the song 'Tamma Tamma Again', which features in her upcoming film 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania'. She said, "That's a totally impossible task," adding she had nightmares before the song's shoot. The song was originally picturised on Madhuri Dixit and Sanjay Dutt.
