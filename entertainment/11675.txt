According to reports, actress Deepika Padukone will be seen performing 'ghoomar', a Rajasthani traditional dance form in the upcoming period drama film 'Padmavati'. The dance sequence will reportedly be shot at a set constructed in Mumbai's Mehboob Studio. Deepika will play the role of 'Rani Padmini' in the film, which will be directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali.
