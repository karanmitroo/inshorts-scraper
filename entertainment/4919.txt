Actress Kangana Ranaut's sister Rangoli Chandel slammed writer Apurva Asrani on Twitter, after he accused Kangana of nepotism. "I hired my brother as addnl writer on my new film. #Kangana hired her sister as manager & now promotes her brother. All guilty of #nepotism?" read Apurva's tweet. Rangoli tweeted in response that watching films with your brother is not nepotism.
