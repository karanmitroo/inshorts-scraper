Actress Madhuri Dixit has been roped in as the brand ambassador of 'Beti Bachao Beti Padhao' campaign. The campaign will be launched by PM Narendra Modi on Thursday in Haryana's Panipat. Confirming the news, Madhuri posted on Twitter, "Honoured to be a part of #BetiBachaoBetiPadhao campaign. Looking forward to the launch tomorrow, in Panipat, by Hon'ble PM @narendramodi."
