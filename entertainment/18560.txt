Priyanka Chopra's much-awaited movie 'Mary Kom' has been declared tax free in Maharashtra. Thanking Maharashtra govt, Ajit Andhare, COO, Viacom18 Motion Pictures (producer) said, "This exemption will allow large segments of society to experience the spirit of magnificent Mary." Produced by Sanjay Leela Bhansali and Directed by Omung Kumar, the film will release on Sept 5.
