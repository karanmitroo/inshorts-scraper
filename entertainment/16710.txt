Superstar Salman Khan has lent his voice to the title track of Sooraj Pancholi's acting debut film 'Hero'. He reportedly recorded the song, titled ‘Main Hoon Hero Tera’ composed by Amaal Mallik, in 4 hours. Notably, Salman is launching Aditya Pancholi's son Sooraj in the film which stars Suniel Shetty’s daughter Athiya opposite him. 'Hero' will release on September 25.
