Actress Priyanka Chopra has shared a new teaser for the second season of the television series 'Quantico'. The 34-year-old actress tweeted, "Couldn't wait to share this sneak peek of #Quantico season 2! Back on #Sep25 with new secrets & new threats!" In the new season, the character 'Alex Parrish', played by Priyanka, will be working for the CIA.
