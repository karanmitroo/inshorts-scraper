Reacting to the demise of actress Carrie Fisher, known for playing 'Princess Leia' in the 'Star Wars' franchise, Ellen DeGeneres tweeted, "She was so much fun. I can't believe she's gone". "[Y]our role as Princess Leia will inspire many in the face of adversity," tweeted Prince Jackson. "She lived her life, bravely," her 'Star Wars' co-star Harrison Ford said.
