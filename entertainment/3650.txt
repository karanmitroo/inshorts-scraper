Bollywood celebrities including Shekhar Kapur, Javed Akhtar, Farhan Akhtar and Dia Mirza took to Twitter to condemn the murder of senior journalist Gauri Lankesh, who was shot dead at her Bengaluru house on Tuesday. Javed wrote, "If one kind of people are getting killed which kind of people are the killers," while Dia tweeted, "Culprits must be found and punished."
