The first look poster of Abhishek Bachchan and Rishi Kapoor starrer 'All is Well' was shared by the latter on twitter. “Tinguji goes next generation Lambuji-Tradition continues with Abhishek Bachchan. In All is Well,” he posted along with the poster that shows the actors posing back to back. The film, directed by Umesh Shukla, also has Asin and Supriya Pathak.
