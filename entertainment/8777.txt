Actor Amitabh Bachchan took to Twitter to share a photo to mark 48 years in the film industry. "Today on Feb 15th 1969 I officially joined the film industry... signed my 1st film 'Saat Hindustani'", he wrote. Amitabh Bachchan has won four National Awards for 'Best Actor' and 15 Filmfare Awards across categories in his career so far.
