Actors Ayushmann Khurrana, Kriti Sanon and Rajkummar Rao will star in 'Bareilly Ki Barfi' a film by Ashwini Iyer Tiwari, whose debut as a feature film director was with 'Nil Battey Sannata'. "It's a unique rom-com (romantic comedy) rooted in Uttar Pradesh. Kriti's family owns a mithai shop and that's where the story takes off," said Ashwini.
