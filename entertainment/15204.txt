BJP President Amit Shah and Union Minister Nitin Gadkari on Monday unveiled the poster of Randeep Hooda and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan-starrer 'Sarbjit', an Omung Kumar directorial. The poster launch event took place at Shah's residence in Delhi where Aishwarya was present along with Richa Chadha. "I'd like to congratulate the team for making a film on this topic," Shah said.
