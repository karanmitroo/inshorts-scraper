'La La Land', starring Emma Stone and Ryan Gosling, is a musical that "has all the joy and wonder of love", wrote Indian Express. "Though the film is hopelessly romantic, it's equally relatable," wrote Times of India while The Hindu called it a "good old fashioned Hollywood musical". It was rated 3.5/5 (Indian Express), 4.5/5 (TOI) and 4/5 (The Hindu).
