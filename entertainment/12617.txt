According to reports, Hrithik Roshan will star in filmmaker Siddharth Anand's upcoming film 'Fighter'. The film is reportedly inspired by Hollywood actor Tom Cruise's film 'Top Gun' and will be a big-budget project. This will be Hrithik's second collaboration with Anand after the 2014 film 'Bang Bang' which was reported to be inspired by Tom Cruise's film 'Knight and Day'.
