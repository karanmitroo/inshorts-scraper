Abhay Deol has denied reports that say he's starring in Dev Benegal's next, 'Bombay Samurai'. "So many reports about me doing 'Bombay Samurai'. Not true! Love Dev Benegal's work though. My best wishes to him and all involved", tweeted Abhay, who has earlier worked with Benegal for the critically-acclaimed film 'Road, Movie'. Bombay Samurai stars Kareena Kapoor in the lead.
