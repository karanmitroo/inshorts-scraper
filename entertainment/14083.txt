Actress Shraddha Kapoor has rejected reports which claimed that she is dating actor Farhan Akhtar. “There’s a limit to everything. You have to consider people’s sentiments & think about the people involved", she said. Speaking about actor Aditya Roy Kapur, Shraddha said, “We were never a couple. It was a rumour and people got carried away with Aashiqui 2”.
