Continuing the trend newly started by Ranbir Kapoor, who became producer with Besharam, Anushka Sharma has decided to turn producer with her film 'NH10' -a thriller about a road trip gone wrong. Anushka liked the script so much that she decided to co-produce it with Anurag Kashayap, Vikramaditya Motwane and Vikas Behl sources reported. The film will release in 2014.
