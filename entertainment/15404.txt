Emraan Hashmi today took to Twitter to reveal his look in the upcoming film 'Azhar', a biopic of former India cricket captain Mohammed Azharuddin. Wishing the cricketer on his birthday, Emraan wrote, "Here is my birthday present.Me as You in #Azhar". The film, directed by Tony D'Souza, also features Prachi Desai and Huma Qureshi. It will release in May 2016.
