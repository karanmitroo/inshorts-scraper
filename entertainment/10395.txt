'Haseeno Ka Deewana', a new song from 'Kaabil', featuring actress Urvashi Rautela has been released. A recreation of the song by the same name from the 1981 film 'Yaarana', it has been sung by Raftaar and Payal Dev. Notably, director Rakesh Roshan's brother Rajesh Roshan had composed the music for the original song.
