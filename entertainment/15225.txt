Bollywood actor Shahid Kapoor, the son of Pankaj Kapur and his first wife Neelima Azeem, turned 35 years old today. While Shahid made appearances in music videos, commercials and as background dancer initially, he made his Bollywood acting debut with 'Ishq Vishk' in 2003. Shahid received critical appreciation for his work in 'Haider' (2014) which received five National Film Awards.
