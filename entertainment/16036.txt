Actor Ranveer Singh on Wednesday took to Twitter to share a 'Dubsmash' video of him along with veteran actor Amitabh Bachchan. The duo recorded the video as Ranveer visited Bachchan on the show ‘Aaj Ki Raat Hai Zindagi’, hosted by the latter. In the video, Bachchan dubsmashed a dialogue from Ranveer's upcoming film 'Bajirao Mastani'.
