Neeraj Ghaywan's debut directorial 'Masaan', that won two awards at the prestigious Cannes film festival, released in India on Friday. While TOI called it a 'pure desi magic woven in', NDTV termed it as 'an unmissable gem'. TOI also appreciated the performances of the entire cast. 'Masaan' was rated 4/5 (Indian Express, TOI and Mid-Day) and 4.5/5 (NDTV).
