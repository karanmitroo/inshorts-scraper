A video by BuzzFeed India, supporting the '#SkillsNotScars' initiative by 'Make Love Not Scars', features comedy group AIB's Tanmay Bhat along with acid attack survivor Mamta. The video is titled '7 Times You And Your Work BFF Can Talk Without Speaking'. The '#SkillsNotScars' initiative describes itself as the 'world's first job portal for acid attack survivors'.
