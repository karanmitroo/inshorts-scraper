Bollywood actress Rani Mukerji (37) and her husband Aditya Chopra (44) were blessed with a baby girl on Wednesday. The couple, who had tied the knot on April 21, 2014, has named their daughter Adira. Making the announcement, Rani said, "I'd like to thank all my well wishers and fans. Today life has given us God's greatest gift with Adira."
