Director Neeraj Pandey announced on Twitter that actor Naseeruddin Shah will star in the Sidharth Malhotra and Manoj Bajpayee starrer Aiyaary. The film is Naseeruddin's second project with Neeraj after the 2008 thriller 'A Wednesday'. Aiyaary is reportedly a crime thriller and is scheduled to release on January 26, 2018.
