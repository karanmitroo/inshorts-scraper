Y-Films, youth division of Yash Raj Films, on Tuesday released the fourth film of its web series 'Love Shots'. Titled 'Scandal Point', the short film features veteran actors Farida Jalal and Kulbhushan Kharbanda. Directed by Ankur Tewari, the film revolves around an old couple, Mr and Mrs Chowdhury. The next film in the series will be released on April 5.
