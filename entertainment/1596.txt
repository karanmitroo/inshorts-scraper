Filmmaker Karan Johar took to social media to share a new picture from the sets of Janhvi Kapoor and Ishaan Khatter starrer 'Dhadak'. It is reportedly a remake of the 2016 Marathi film 'Sairat', which is the highest grossing Marathi movie. Directed by Shashank Khaitan, 'Dhadak' is scheduled to release on July 6, 2018.
