After a controversial yet blockbuster and critically acclaimed film 'PK', filmmaker Rajkumar Hirani said that he is busy with his next project, a biopic on actor Sanjay Dutt. The actor had worked with Hirani in 'Munnabhai MBBS' and 'Lage Raho Munnabhai'. Talking about Dutt, who is currently serving a five-year jail term, Hirani said, "His life is a big story."
