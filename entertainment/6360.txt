Telugu actor Chalapathi Rao, while speaking at an event, said, "Women are only fit for sleeping with men." The veteran actor made the comments at the audio launch of the film Rarandoi Veduka Chudham, which has a dialogue that translates to 'women are injurious to mental peace'. Actor Nagarjuna Akkineni, who is producing the film, slammed Chalapathi's "derogatory" comments.
