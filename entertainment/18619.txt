Noted actress-filmmaker Smita Talwalkar died at 59,  of ovarian cancer at the Jaslok hospital on Wednesday. She was best known for her work in the films 'Tu Saubhagyavati Ho' and 'Chaukat Raja'. She started as a news-reader, went on to become an actress and then a filmmaker, having 2 national awards as her achievement. She produced famous TV show 'Avantika'.
