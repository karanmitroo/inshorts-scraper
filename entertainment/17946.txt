The famous duo from Karan Johar's 'Dostana' John Abraham and Abhishek Bachchan will be seen together again in Firoz Nadiadwala's 'Hera Pheri 3'. The first two parts of the film featured Akshay Kumar, Suniel Shetty and Paresh Rawal in lead roles. A source said, "Suniel and Paresh from the original cast will also be part of the film.''
