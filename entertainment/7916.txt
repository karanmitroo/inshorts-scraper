Actress Rani Mukerji made her acting debut in the 1996 Bengali film 'Biyer Phool', which was directed by her father Ram Mukherjee. She made her Bollywood debut with the film 'Raja Ki Aayegi Baraat' later that year. Rani, who turned 39 on Tuesday, is best known for her movies 'Kuch Kuch Hota Hai', 'Mardaani', 'Black', and 'No One Killed Jessica'.
