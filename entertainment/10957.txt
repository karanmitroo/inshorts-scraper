'Oggatonama', the film which is Bangladesh's entry for the Oscars in the Best Foreign Language Film category, was screened at the ongoing 22nd Kolkata International Film Festival (KIFF). "Oggatonama (The Unnamed)... lays bare the reality of migration... It is a film about life, love and everlasting hope while delivering a strong global message," said the film's director Tauquir Ahmed.
