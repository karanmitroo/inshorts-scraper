A new poster of Anushka Sharma starrer 'Pari' has been released. This is Anushka's third film as a producer. Also starring Parambrata Chatterjee, Rajat Kapoor and Ritabhari Chakraborty, 'Pari' will mark the directorial debut of Prosit Roy in feature films and it is scheduled to release on the occasion of Holi on March 2.
