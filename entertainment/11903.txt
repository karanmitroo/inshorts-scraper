Actor Rana Daggubati has shared a picture of himself with his fitness trainer Kunal Gir for the upcoming film 'Baahubali 2' while adding that his look in the film will be "bigger, meaner and stronger". Rana tweeted, "Here goes the new and improved me!!" He played the role of 'Bhallaladeva' in the first film, 'Baahubali: The Beginning'.
