Film-maker Mahesh Bhatt announced through Twitter, Thursday that he will be producing the remake of Telugu film 'Maya' in Hindi as 'Murder 4'. "Remaking Telugu movie MAAYA produced by @madhurasreedhar in Hindi as MURDER 4 to be directed by @neelakanta18 @joshmevandana (sic)," tweeted Bhatt. The director of 'Maya', Neelkanta, will make his Bollywood directorial debut with 'Murder 4'.
