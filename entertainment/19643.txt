Megastar and Kaun Banega Crorepati-host Amitabh Bachchan formally kick-started the 'Hot Seat Aapke Shehar' van in Mumbai as part of the promotional activity for the 7th season of the show which is supposed to go live from September-9. The van will stay for 3-days each in 12 cities across the country and select participants to take part in the show.
