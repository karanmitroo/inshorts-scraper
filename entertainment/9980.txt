The first look poster of Rajinikanth's daughter and filmmaker Aishwaryaa Dhanush's upcoming Tamil directorial 'Mariyappan' has been released. The film is a biopic on Indian Paralympic high jumper Mariyappan Thangavelu who won gold for India during the 2016 Summer Paralympic games held in Rio de Janeiro. The poster was shared by actor Shah Rukh Khan on his Twitter handle.
