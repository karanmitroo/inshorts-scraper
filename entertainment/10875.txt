Actor Salman Khan, at the first look launch of the Rajinikanth-Akshay Kumar starrer film '2.0', said that Akshay Kumar is the only actor in Bollywood who has shown "growth". "We all remain stagnant, he's the only one who keeps growing," he added.  Salman further said that Akshay is "phenomenal" and only he could have done the role in '2.0'.
