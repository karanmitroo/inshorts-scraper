Actor Sanjay Dutt has revealed that the third installment of the 'Munna Bhai' franchise will release in 2018. Following questions on why the film has been delayed, he said, "That you should ask Raju Hirani and Vinod sir. Even I'm waiting for 'Munna Bhai'." Dutt also revealed that his other upcoming films included 'Marco' and a remake of 'De Dhakka'.
