Filmmaker Mahesh Bhatt will turn Co-Writer and Creative Director for his upcoming project, as he adapts Srijit Mukherjee’s Bengali drama ‘Rajkahini’ in Hindi. 'Rajkahini' features powerful female characters as it traces the tale of 11 inmates of a brothel in the partition-struck Bengal, with Rituparna Sengupta playing lead as Begum Jaan.
