SS Rajamouli-directed film 'Baahubali 2: The Conclusion' has crossed ₹500 crore mark in its opening weekend worldwide. The film, which released on 28 April in India, earned ₹385 crore in the country across all languages and grossed ₹120 crore in international markets. The film had made ₹121 crore on Friday, emerging as the highest opening day earner in India.
