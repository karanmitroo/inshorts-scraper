Singer-turned-Actor Himesh Reshammiya has reportedly lost 12 kilos, to play a suspended cop-turned-superstar, a character inspired by yesteryear eccentric actor Raaj Kumar. The film, titled 'The Xpose' is a murder suspense which is said to be based on real life events. The movie will also showcase Yo Yo Honey Singh in a negative role and Irrfan Khan in special appearance.
