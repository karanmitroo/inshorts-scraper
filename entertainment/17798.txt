Veteran Bollywood actor Amitabh Bachchan will make his commentary debut at the India-Pakistan match of the World Cup. Big B will join Kapil Dev, Shoaib Akhtar, Harsha Bhogle and other commentators for the much-awaited match on Feb-15. Bachchan said he was excited to make his commentary debut at the World Cup, as his movie 'Shamitabh' is also releasing in February.
