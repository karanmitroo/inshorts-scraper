Actress Sonakshi Sinha tweeted Independence Day wishes from the Buckingham Palace, United Kingdom. "No better way to wish my fellow Indians a Happy Independence Day than in front of the Queen's Palace! 70 years of freedom from this! #JaiHind," wrote Sonakshi. Earlier, Amitabh Bachchan, Priyanka Chopra, Aamir Khan and Shah Rukh Khan had tweeted Independence Day wishes.
