'Baahubali 2' will be the opening film at the 39th Moscow International Film Festival, as confirmed by the film's director SS Rajamouli on Twitter. Other Indian films to be screened at the festival include Konkona Sen Sharma's directorial debut 'A Death in the Gunj' and Gulshan Grover's 'Badman'. The festival will be held from June 22 to June 29.
