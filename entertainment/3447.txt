In a new promo of reality show 'Bigg Boss 11', Salman Khan can be seen singing Kishore Kumar's song 'Mere Saamne Wali Khidki Mein' from 1968 film 'Padosan'. The new season will have 'neighbours' as its theme. There will be two houses and celebrities and commoners will be neighbours. The show is scheduled to go on air on October 1.
