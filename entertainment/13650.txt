The title track of the Salman Khan and Anushka Sharma starrer 'Sultan' was released on Monday. The song has been composed by Vishal and Shekhar, and sung by Sukhwinder Singh and Shadab Faridi. It has been picturised on Salman, who plays the titular character, and his trainer, played by Randeep Hooda. The film will release on July 6.
