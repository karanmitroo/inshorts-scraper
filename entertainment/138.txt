Anushka Sharma starrer 'Pari', which released today, "is one of the best [films] to come out of the [horror] genre," wrote Koimoi. The Indian Express said, "The film fails to rise above its silliness". Bollywood Hungama wrote, "'Pari' has the right amount of chills and thrills." It has been rated 3.5/5 (Koimoi), 1/5 (The Indian Express) and 2.5/5 (Bollywood Hungama).
