In an exclusive interview to Filmfare, Bollywood actor Siddharth Malhotra, confessed that he would enjoy kissing actress Deepika Padukone on screen. Siddharth had expressed a similar desire to get 'intimate' with Deepika in the rapid fire round on 'Koffee With Karan'. After 'Student Of The Year' and 'Hasee Toh Phasee', Siddharth's upcoming film is 'Villain' opposite Shraddha Kapoor.
