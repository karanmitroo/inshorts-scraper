'Qatl-E-Aam', the first song from the Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Vicky Kaushal starrer 'Raman Raghav 2.0' was released on Friday. The song has been composed by Ram Sampath, sung by Sona Mohapatra and features Kaushal, Siddiqui and the film's female lead Sobhita Dhulipala. Directed by Anurag Kashyap, 'Raman Raghav 2.0' is scheduled to release on June 24.
