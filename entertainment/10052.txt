A new song titled 'Kuch Din' from the Hrithik Roshan and Yami Gautam starrer upcoming romantic action thriller film 'Kaabil' has been released. The song has been composed by Rajesh Roshan with vocals by Jubin Nautiyal and lyrics by Manoj Muntashir. Directed by Rakesh Roshan, the film is scheduled to release on January 25, 2017.
