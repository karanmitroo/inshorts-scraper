Eight-year-old child actor Sunny Pawar, who plays the younger version of Dev Patel in Oscar-nominated film 'Lion', said he likes the superhero character 'Krrish' and wants to act with Hrithik Roshan. "He is a superhero and I would one day want to meet him and get pictures clicked with him," he added. He further said he also likes Ajay Devgn.
