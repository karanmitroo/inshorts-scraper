Having collected Rs 40.13 crores, Sajid Khan's out-and-out entertainer Humshakals has recorded the 4th biggest opening weekend in 2014 after Jai Ho (60.68), Gunday (43.93) and Holiday (41.32). Moreover, the movie is expected to hold very well over the week days that follow. Humshakals is made at a cost of 75cr including the P&A.
