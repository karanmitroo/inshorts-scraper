The Akshay Kumar starrer 'Housefull 3' has entered the ₹100 crore club in India in just twelve days of its release. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film earned ₹99.65 crore till Tuesday while the morning and noon shows on Wednesday enabled it to cross the benchmark. Earlier this year, Akshay's film 'Airlift' also minted over ₹100 crore.
