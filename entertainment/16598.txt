The trailer of stand-up comedian Kapil Sharma's debut movie 'Kis Kisko Pyaar Karoon' was released on Thursday. The trailer portrays Kapil as SRK (Shiv, Ram and Kishan), who juggles between three wives. Other than the three wives, he also has a girlfriend, played by Eli Avram. Directed by Abbas-Mustan, the film also stars Arbaaz Khan, Varun Sharma and Supriya Pathak.
