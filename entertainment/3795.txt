Saif Ali Khan, while reacting to Salman Khan being signed for upcoming film 'Race 3', said, "Who better than Salman Khan?". "Ramesh Taurani (producer) said to me 'I want to make a Race 3, but...I want to take a new cast...I wish them both all the best," he added. Saif played the lead role in the previous instalments of 'Race'.
