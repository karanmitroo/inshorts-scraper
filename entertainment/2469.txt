Actress Kriti Sanon and actor Diljit Dosanjh will star together for the first time in Dinesh Vijan's directorial ‘Arjun Patiala... Larger Than Life!’ Kriti tweeted, "This one's gonna be a mad fun ride! Can't wait to start rolling." Kriti will reportedly play the role of a journalist while Diljit's character is said to be a quirky, small town guy.
