The official trailer of the Shraddha Kapoor and Aditya Roy Kapur's upcoming romantic drama film 'OK Jaanu' has been released. Produced by Karan Johar and Mani Ratnam, it is an official remake of Mani Ratnam's Tamil film 'OK Kanmani'. Also starring Naseeruddin Shah and Leela Samson, the film, directed by Shaad Ali, is scheduled to release on January 13, 2017.
