Actress Kajol has reportedly been approached to play a role in the Salman Khan starrer 'Dabangg 3', the third instalment in the 'Dabangg' film series. Earlier, director Arbaaz Khan had hinted at the "possibility of another heroine in the film". Also starring Sonakshi Sinha, the production of 'Dabangg 3' is expected to begin in mid-2017.
