The Sonakshi Sinha starrer 'Akira' has earned ₹16.65 crore in its opening weekend. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film collected ₹5.15 crore on Friday, ₹5.30 crore on Saturday and ₹6.20 crore on Sunday. Directed and produced by AR Murugadoss, the film also stars Anurag Kashyap and Konkona Sen Sharma.
