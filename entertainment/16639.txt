Akshay Kumar on Saturday introduced his character in the film 'Singh is Bliing', a sequel to the 2008 action comedy “Singh Is Kinng”. "Faster than a bullet...meet the Ama-SINGH Raftaar SINGH! #MeetRaftaarSINGH @SIBTheFilm", he tweeted. The news has been trending on Twitter with the hashtag #MeetRaftaarSINGH. 'Singh is Bliing' also stars Amy Jackson, Lara Dutta and Kay Kay Menon.
