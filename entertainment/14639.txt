Sabbir Khan, who is directing the Tiger Shroff and Shraddha Kapoor starrer film 'Baaghi', recently recreated a scene from 'Dil Se' in the 'Girl I need You' song from the upcoming film. “A lingering image from Srk & Preity Zinta’s ‘Dil Se’ ~My small tribute to Mani Ratnam & @TheFarahKhan #GirlINeedYou", Khan tweeted along with the picture.
