Actors Soha Ali Khan and Kunal Kemmu, who became parents to a baby girl on September 29, have named their daughter Inaaya Naumi Kemmu. Sharing the news on Twitter, Kunal wrote, "Little Inaaya is happy and healthy and she thanks all of you for your love and blessings." This is Soha's first child with Kunal whom she married in 2015.
