The poster of the Sonakshi Sinha starrer action film 'Akira' was released on Tuesday. Sonakshi, who plays the titular role, shared the poster with the caption, "She will fight back. No one will be forgiven!" The film also stars Konkona Sen Sharma, Anurag Kashyap and Sonakshi's father Shatrughan Sinha. AR Murugadoss directed 'Akira' is scheduled to release on September 2.
