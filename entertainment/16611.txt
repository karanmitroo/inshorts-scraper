Salman Khan, whose 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' has crossed the Rs 300-crore mark at the box-office, on Tuesday revealed that Aamir Khan was the makers' first choice for the movie. The initial script of the film was written by Telegu scriptwriter KV Vijayendra Prasad. "Yes, Aamir told me that Prasad had come to him and he sent him to me", Salman said.
