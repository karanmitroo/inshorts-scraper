A visually challenged fan of actor Hrithik Roshan, named Saija Dasari, has shared a video of herself dancing to the title track of the actor's upcoming film 'Kaabil'. "Saija! U made my heart dance !!... u r BEAUTIFUL !!... Love you for sending me this," tweeted Hrithik while sharing the video. Hrithik plays a visually challenged man in 'Kaabil'.
