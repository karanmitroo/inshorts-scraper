Anurag Kashyap has started work on the third instalment of 'Gangs of Wasseypur' and has roped in Zeishan Qadri, director of 'Meeruthiya Gangsters', to write the film's script and direct it. The film is scheduled to release in 2016, according to reports. "While the story in the second part ended in 2002, third would be forwarded till 2015", Qadri said.
