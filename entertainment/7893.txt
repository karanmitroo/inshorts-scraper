The first look of actors Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif from their upcoming film 'Tiger Zinda Hai' has been unveiled. "Back Together, in Tiger Zinda Hai," wrote Salman while sharing the photo. It is a sequel to the 2012 film 'Ek Tha Tiger'. Directed by Ali Abbas Zafar, 'Tiger Zinda Hai' is scheduled to release on December 22.
