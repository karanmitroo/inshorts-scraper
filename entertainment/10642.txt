A photo gallery compiles childhood pictures of 14 Bollywood celebrities including Salman Khan, Shah Rukh Khan and Aishwarya Rai. A photo of Ranbir Kapoor from his teens shows his face smeared with cake. Shahid Kapoor is seen wearing a glittering skin-suit, while a three-year-old Alia Bhatt can be seen posing with elder sister Shaheen in one of the pictures.
