Bollywood actor Tiger Shroff on Thursday shared the first look of co-actor Shraddha Kapoor from their upcoming film 'Baaghi' as the actress turned 27. "Girl power! @ShraddhaKapoor show them how it's done:) happy birthday my hero! #Baaghi #BeARebel," he captioned the picture. Notably, Shraddha had also shared Tiger's look from the film on Wednesday, which marked his 26th birthday.
