Prabhu Deva's action flick 'R... Rajkumar' starring Sonakshi Sinha and Shahid Kapoor released this Friday. The story is about a rowdy Romeo who falls in love with a village belle, but has to fight the enemies of love to take home his dulhaniya. The movie has been rated zero (Indian Express), one (NDTV, Zee News), two (DNA) and three (TOI).
