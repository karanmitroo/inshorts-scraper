Kareena Kapoor's item song 'Mera Naam Mary', from the film 'Brothers',  has crossed 1 million views on YouTube, within a week. "#MeraNaamMary hits 1M views,happy to see the love,keep it going @Brothers2015 @karanmalhotra21 #KareenaKapoor", actor Siddharth Malhotra tweeetd. Akshay Kumar and Siddharth Malhotra starrer Brothers is set to be released on Aug 14.
