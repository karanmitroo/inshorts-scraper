Olympic champions Tatiana Volosozhar and Maxim Trankov, the Russian skating duo,  danced to 'Nagada Sang Dhol', from the Indian film, 'Ram-Leela', at the European Figure Skating Championships in Slovakia. The duo chose Indian-inspired costumes and jewellery for the performance. After gaining the maximum points overall, Tatiana and Maxim won their fourth straight European pairs title at the event.
