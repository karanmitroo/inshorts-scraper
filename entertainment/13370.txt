The Vivek Oberoi, Aftab Shivdasani and Riteish Deshmukh starrer 'Great Grand Masti', which released on Friday, "rehashes the same old plot, one more time", wrote India Today. Hindustan Times called the film "puerile, utter nonsense and disgusting" while Times of India called it "a fun weekend watch". It has been rated 0.5/5 (India Today), 0/5 (HT) and 3/5 (TOI).
