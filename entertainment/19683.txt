Ritesh Batra's 'The Lunchbox' is chosen as the best film of 2013 by the audience at the Amsterdam Film Festival. Nawazuddin Siddiqui-Irfan Khan starrer movie, releasing on September-20 in India, received 4,396 votes, leaving behind international films like 'Gloria' and 'Songs of Redemption'. Earlier this year, it has also won Critics Week Viewers Choice Award at the Cannes Film Festival.
