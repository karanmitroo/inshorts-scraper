The first look of actors Parineeti Chopra and Ayushmann Khurrana from their upcoming film 'Meri Pyari Bindu' has been released. Parineeti and Ayushmann will portray the characters 'Bindu' and 'Abhimanyu' respectively. While Ayushmann will play a horror novelist, Parineeti will be seen as a singer. Directed by Akshay Roy, the film is scheduled to release on May 12, 2017.
