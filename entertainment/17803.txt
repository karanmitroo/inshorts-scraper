Actor Arjun Rampal has been summoned by the Mumbai police for meeting jailed gangster Arun Gawli. Rampal, who plays Gawli in under-production film 'Daddy', met him without official permission in a hospital's out-patient Department on Dec 29, 2014. Gawli  is serving a life term in connection with the murder of corporator Kamlakar Jamsandekar in 2008.
