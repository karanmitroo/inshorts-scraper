Actor Aditya Pancholi and his wife Zarina Wahab have sent a legal notice citing criminal and civil defamation to Kangana Ranaut. Zarina said, "She really crossed all limits. Kya zaroorat thi yeh sab baatein karne ki film promotion ke liye?" Zarina further said that they want a public apology for all the hurt and slander Kangana has caused.
