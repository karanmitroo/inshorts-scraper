The Emma Stone and Ryan Gosling starrer 'La La Land' has been named the 'Best Film' of 2016 by the New York Film Critics Circle (NYFCC). Casey Affleck was named 'Best Actor' for 'Manchester by the Sea' while Isabelle Huppert was awarded 'Best Actress' for 'Elle'. The NYFCC Awards are the oldest awards given by film critics in the US.
