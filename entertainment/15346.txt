The trailer of the Sidharth Malhotra, Alia Bhatt and Fawad Khan starrer upcoming film 'Kapoor & Sons' was YouTube's most viewed video in India on Monday. The romantic comedy has been been directed by  Shakun Batra and also features Rishi Kapoor. Posted on the video sharing platform on February 10, the trailer has garnered over 41.24 lakh views so far.
