Aishwarya Rai Bachchan's first look from her upcoming film 'Jazbaa', which is being called as her comeback film, was revealed by the film's director Sanjay Gupta on Tuesday. Aishwarya, who is returning to the silver screen after 5 years since her last release 'Guzaarish', is looking shattered and edgy in the poster. The film will release in October this year.
