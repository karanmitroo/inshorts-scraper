Smriti Irani shared actor Kit Harington's photo on Instagram and drew a parallel between characters Jon Snow from 'Game of Thrones' and Mihir Virani from 'Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi'. "Jon Snow comes back from the dead and people thought only Mihir could #comeback alive," she wrote. Smriti played the lead role in 'Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi'.
