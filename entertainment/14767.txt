'The Jungle Book', which released on Friday, collected ₹40.19 crore in its opening weekend in India, becoming the second biggest opening weekend grosser of 2016. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film collected ₹10.09 crore on Friday, ₹13.51 crore on Saturday and ₹16.59 crore on Sunday. Notably, the film released in India a week before its US release.
