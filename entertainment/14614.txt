Kareena Kapoor recently revealed that Saif Ali Khan and Shahid Kapoor, who are working together for their upcoming film 'Rangoon', "are getting along too well". Debunking rumours of cold vibes between the two, Kareena added "He (Saif) quite enjoys chatting with Shahid and finds him a really nice guy". Kareena, who is married to Saif, reportedly dated Shahid previously.
