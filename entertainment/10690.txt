A new song titled 'Je T'aime' from the Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor starrer upcoming romantic comedy film 'Befikre' has been released. It has been composed by Vishal and Shekhar with vocals by Vishal Dadlani and Sunidhi Chauhan. The song has a mix of French lyrics along with Hindi. Directed by Aditya Chopra, 'Befikre' will release on December 9.
