Actor Dilip Kumar was the first to win the Filmfare Award for Best Actor, for his role in the 1952 film 'Daag'. The actor, who turned 94 on Sunday, has won the Best Actor award a record eight times, a feat he shares with Shah Rukh Khan. He is best known for films like 'Devdas' (1955) and 'Kala Pani' (1958).
