Manjunath, the movie based on Indian Oil executive Manjunath Shanmugam who was murdered for questioning the oil mafia in UP, released this friday. The movie, directed by Sandeep Varma, has been rated 3 (IBN Live, TOI) and 3.5 (HT, India Today, NDTV). This is one inspirational story, which needs to be watched, says Times of India.
