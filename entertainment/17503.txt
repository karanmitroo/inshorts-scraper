Sony TV is reportedly making an Indian version of the popular American fantasy-drama series 'Game Of Thrones', to be named as 'Rani Mahal'. One of the major characters of the show Daenerys Targaryen's role is said to be played by Sakshi Tanwar of 'Bade Ache Lagte Hain' fame. Furthermore, Anita Hassanandani is rumoured to portray the role of Cersei Lannister.
