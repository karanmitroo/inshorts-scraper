Satiating some of the fans' appetite, the first motion poster of Ranbir Kapoor starrer 'Roy' was released on Tuesday. The 35-second clip shows Ranbir staring at a city skyline, and Arjun Rampal is spotted behind a typewriter. Ranbir plays a thief in the film, while Arjun is a filmmaker. The official trailer will be out on Dec-19
