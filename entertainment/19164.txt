Director and promoter of Karbonn Mobiles, Sudhir Hasija, has said that the company would be launching 1 million Rajinikanth-starrer 'Kochadaiiyaan' special edition Karbonn mobile handsets through 27,000 mobile outlet stores in South India. Intended to be launched this month, the mobile phones will have screen savers and must-have images from the film along with the film's trailer and signature tune.
