Television actress Shweta Tiwari's daughter Palak Tiwari will make her Bollywood acting debut opposite 'Taare Zameen Par' actor Darsheel Safary in the upcoming teen comedy 'Quickie'. Talking about her debut, Palak said, "I am really looking forward to it. I just hope it will be a great journey like I expected it to be, and people will appreciate this film."
