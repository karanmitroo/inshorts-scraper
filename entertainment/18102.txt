Filmmaker Karan Johar has launched a line of jewellery for Mumbai-based Gehna Jewellers. At a formal launch event, Karan unveiled the limited edition collection with Bollywood actresses Ileana D’cruz and Nimrat Kaur. “Fine jewellery requires lots of detailing and innovation. It was a challenge, considering this is one area that I have never dabbled in before," Karan said.
