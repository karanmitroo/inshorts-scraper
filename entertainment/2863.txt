According to reports, Karan Johar backed out of his commitment to launch Salman Khan's brother-in-law Aayush Sharma in Bollywood under the Dharma Productions banner. A source said, "We don't know what happened. But Johar backed out...leaving Salman livid." This reportedly led to Salman opting out of his joint production with Johar and Akshay Kumar based on the Battle of Saragarhi.
