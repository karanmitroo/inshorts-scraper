Filmmaker Vikram Bhatt's three film stories will be available in the form of novel, including his upcoming film "Khamoshiyan". The compilation of his "1920", "1920- evil returns" and "Khamoshiyan" is set to release in Jan. Khamoshiyan stars Ali Fazal of Bobby Jasoos fame and newcomers Gurmeet Choudhary and Sapna Pabbi. The film will hit the screens on Jan 30.
