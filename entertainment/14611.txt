A new trailer for the upcoming film 'Independence Day: Resurgence' was released on Friday. Directed by Roland Emmerich, it is a sequel to the 1996 film 'Independence Day'. Starring Liam Hemsworth, Jessie Usher and Maika Monroe, the film will also witness Jeff Goldblum, Bill Pullman and Brent Spiner reprising their roles. It is set to release on June 24.
