According to Trade analyst Taran Adarsh, Krrish-3 on Monday set new Box Office record of highest single day collection of Rs 35.91 crore. Elated Hrithik tweeted, "Nothing resonates more than BO records and the stupendous, record-shattering biz of #Krrish3 proves it." Krrish 3 also recorded the highest collection(Rs 25.20-crore) on opening day, for any non-holiday pre-Diwali Friday.
