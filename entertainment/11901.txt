As per reports, Sridevi and Boney Kapoor's elder daughter Jhanvi Kapoor will make her Bollywood debut opposite Varun Dhawan in the upcoming film 'Shiddat'. The film is reportedly being directed by '2 States' director Abhishek Varma and co-produced by Karan Johar and Sajid Nadiadwala. The film is said to begin its production by mid-2017.
