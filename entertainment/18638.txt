Salman Khan's ‘Kick’ has entered the 200 crore club, in 10 days of its release. Beating 'Jab Tak Hai Jaan', 'Dabangg 2' and 'Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani', it has become the sixth highest overseas grosser of Bollywood. The film has collected a total of Rs. 313 crores (including overseas), and is expected to surpass ‘Ek Tha Tiger’ (Rs 319 cr).
