Actress Nargis Fakhri shared a picture of herself with co-actor Raj Kummar Rao and director Namrata Singh Gujral from the sets of their upcoming Indo-American film '5 Weddings'. The film is about an American journalist who travels to India for a magazine feature on Bollywood weddings. '5 Weddings' features Nargis and Raj Kummar onscreen together for the first time.
