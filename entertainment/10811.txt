Actress Malaika Arora, while responding to rumours about a link-up with Arjun Kapoor has said that Kapoor is a "very good friend" of hers. "[P]eople give a very different meaning to it, which isn't true," said Malaika. Earlier, Malaika and her estranged husband Arbaaz Khan had said that their separation was not because of a third person.
