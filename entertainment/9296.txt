The seven-year-old Syrian girl, Bana Alabed, whose tweets documented the siege of the Aleppo, met actress Lindsay Lohan, Turkish President Recep Tayyip Erdogan and his wife Emine on Friday in Turkey. Sharing the picture of her meeting, Bana wrote, "Meeting with my friends @rt_erdogan, Emine and @lindsaylohan to support the people of Syria".
