Actor Varun Dhawan, who has been finalised to play the lead role in Karan Johar's 'Shuddhi', has rubbished rumours that the film is based on Amish Tripathi's book 'Immortals of Meluha'.  "The film is on a completely different subject," Varun said. 'Shuddhi' will be directed by Karan Malhotra of 'Agneepath' fame and will also feature actress Alia Bhatt.
