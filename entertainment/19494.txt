Debutant director Saurabh Varma's 'Mickey Virus' starring Manish Paul, Elli Avaram, Manish Choudhari, Puja Gupta, and Varun Badola released today. The story is about a hacker Mickey (Paul) who is hired by cops to crack a cyber-crime case. The movie has obtained a 2 star by Indian Express, 2.5 by NDTV, while a 3 star by TOI and India Today.
