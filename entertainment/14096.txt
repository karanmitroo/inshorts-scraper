'Baby Ko Bass Pasand Hai', the first song from the Salman Khan and Anushka Sharma starrer 'Sultan' was released today. Composed by Vishal and Shekhar, the song has been sung by Vishal Dadlani, Shalmali Kholgade, Ishita and Badshah. Directed by Ali Abbas Zafar and also featuring Randeep Hooda, 'Sultan' is scheduled for an Eid release.
