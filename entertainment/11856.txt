Actor Arjun Rampal has shared a picture to mark the end of filming of the upcoming biopic 'Daddy', where he portrays the character of gangster-turned-politician Arun Gawli. The film will reportedly cover Gawli's life from his 20s to his 60s and stars South Indian actress Aishwarya Rajesh as Gawli's wife. Directed by Gaurav Bavdankar, the film will release in 2017.
