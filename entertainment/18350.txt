Breaking all earlier records, Shah Rukh Khan and Deepika Padukone starrer ‘Happy New Year’ collected Rs 44.97 crore on the opening day (Friday). Despite receiving largely average reviews from critics all over, the Farah Khan directorial got a phenomenal response across the country. Earlier, Aamir Khan starrer ‘Dhoom 3’ was the big opener with Rs 36.32 crore.
