'Dheemi', the first song from the Rajkummar Rao starrer 'Trapped' has been released. The song has been composed by Alokananda Dasgupta, with vocals by Tejas Menon, and lyrics by Rajeshwari Dasgupta. 'Trapped' is about a man who accidentally gets shut in his apartment for 25 days without food and water. Directed by Vikramaditya Motwane, it will release on March 17.
