Arjun Kapoor and Alia Bhatt-starrer "2 States" - which released Apr 18 - has raked in about Rs 70 crore at the domestic box office so far. Among the new releases, 'Revolver Rani' is doing the best. It has minted approximately Rs.3.25 crore in two days. 'Kaanchi' has collected about Rs.1.25 crore and 'Samrat & Co.' around Rs.50 lakh.
