While talking about his upcoming film 'Manmarziyan', Abhishek Bachchan tweeted, "It's been just over 2 yrs since I faced a film camera...A new journey, a new film begins today." Directed by Anurag Kashyap, the film will also star Taapsee Pannu and Vicky Kaushal. This will be Bachchan's first collaboration with Kashyap.
