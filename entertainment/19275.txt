The first poster of SRK starrer ‘Happy New Year’ was launched on Jan-2. The film, directed by Farah Khan, also stars Deepika Padukone, Boman Irani, Abhishek Bachchan, Sonu Sood and Vivaan Shah. Notably, this is the first time that a film is being made with a unique genre like musical heist. ‘Happy New Year’ will release this Diwali.
