The official trailer of Katrina Kaif and Aditya Roy Kapur starrer 'Fitoor' was released on YouTube by UTV Motion Pictures on Monday. The Abhishek Kapoor directorial, also starring actress Tabu, is scheduled to release on February 12 this year. In the adaptation of Charles Dickens' novel 'Great Expectations', Aditya plays a Kashmiri shikara boy while Katrina portrays a wealthy lady.
