Bollywood celebrities walked the ramp on Saturday for 'Mijwan - The Legacy', a collection designed by Manish Malhotra, with their star parents. Sonakshi Sinha, Abhishek Bachchan, Sonam Kapoor and Shweta Bachchan walked the ramp with Shatrughan Sinha, Jaya Bachchan, Anil Kapoor and Amitabh Bachchan respectively. It was organised in support of NGO 'Mijwan Welfare Society', headed by Shabana Azmi.
