Trailer of Yash Raj Films' Shuddh Desi Romance, which releases on September-13, has been released Thursady. The movie, starring Parineeti Chopra, Rishi Kapoor, Sushant Singh Rajput and debutant Vaani Kapoor, will be a "fresh and very real love story about the hair-raising minefield between love, attraction and commitment. Finally, a romantic comedy that tells it like it is", says YRF.
