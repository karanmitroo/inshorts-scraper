The Mumbai police have summoned director Karan Johar to record his statement regarding the All India Bakchod roast of Ranveer Singh and Arjun Kapoor held last year. Johar allegedly used obscene words while he was the 'Roastmaster' or host. Meanwhile, he has reportedly promised his full cooperation to the police. Deepika Padukone, Singh and Kapoor will purportedly be summoned next.
