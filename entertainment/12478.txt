Actor Amitabh Bachchan has confirmed that he will be working with Aamir Khan in Vijay Krishna Acharya's upcoming film, tentatively titled 'Thug'. "It's a great honour and privilege to be working with Aamir and to be working with Yash Raj again," said Bachchan. Notably, this will be the first time that Amitabh and Aamir will be seen together onscreen.
