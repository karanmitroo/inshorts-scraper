Actress Jennifer Lawrence, who earned $46 million in 12 months, has been named the World's Highest-Paid Actress of 2016 by Forbes for a consecutive time in their annual list. Melissa McCarthy, who earned $33 million, has ranked second while Scarlett Johansson, with earnings of $25 million, ranked third. Further, Bollywood actress Deepika Padukone ranked tenth in the list.
