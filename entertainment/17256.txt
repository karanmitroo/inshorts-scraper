Akshay Kumar-starrer 'Gabbar Is Back' has minted Rs 24.40 crore in just two days of its release, announced industry trade analyst Taran Adarsh on Sunday. The movie, also featuring Shruti Hassan, earned Rs 13.05 crore at the box office on the opening day and Rs 11.35 crore on Saturday. The film is a remake of the 2002 Tamil film 'Ramanaa'.
