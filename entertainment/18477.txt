Actor Shah Rukh Khan participated in the Osian's auction of rare and vintage film memorabilia Friday and bought two original posters of Mughal-e-Azam, for Rs.6.84 lakh. The auction saw 163 of the 220 lots being sold for Rs.55.60 lakh. A painting by Arpana Caur, 'Pyaar hua, Iqraar hua' showcasing Raj Kapoor and Nargis, fetched the highest price of Rs.7.5 lakh.
