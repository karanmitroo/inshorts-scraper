Anil Kapoor and daughter Sonam Kapoor joined the 'Mirzya' lead pair, Harshvardhan Kapoor and Saiyami Kher, on the red carpet at the 60th BFI London Film Festival yesterday. While Sonam wore a blue Ralph & Russo Couture gown, Saiyami was dressed in a cream-coloured Sabyasachi ensemble. The Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra directorial film hits the theatres on Friday.
