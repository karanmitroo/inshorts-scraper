A district court in Bihar's Muzaffarpur on Tuesday directed the police to register an FIR against Amitabh Bachchan, Madhuri Dixit and Preity Zinta for endorsing Nestle's Maggi. They are booked under cheating, sale of noxious food, and malignant act likely to spread infection of disease dangerous to life. The court has also directed the police to arrest them 'if required'.
