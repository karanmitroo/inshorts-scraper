Actor Shah Rukh Khan has denied reports that 'Katrina Meri Jaan' is the title of his upcoming film which will star Anushka Sharma and Katrina Kaif. "Woh meri jaan hai, lekin that's not the title," joked Shah Rukh. He will reportedly play a dwarf while Anushka will reportedly play a differently-abled character in the film directed by Aanand Rai.
