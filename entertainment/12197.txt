Bollywood celebrities including Shah Rukh Khan (SRK), Riteish Deshmukh, Randeep Hooda, Shekhar Kapur and Madhur Bhandarkar offered condolences to the families of soldiers martyred during the Uri attack. "Saddened to hear of the cowardly attack in Uri. Prayers for the families of our martyred soldiers," tweeted SRK while Randeep wrote, "This is really sad and an attack on the state."
