While talking about Padmaavat's director Sanjay Leela Bhansali being slapped by Karni Sena members on the film's sets in Jaipur, Ranveer Singh said, "I was ready to go on a rampage." Talking further about the sets being vandalised, he added, "That is my place of worship... You don't assault somebody (Bhansali) I worship. I was full of rage."
