The fifth movie of the Mission Impossible series that released this week "is a must-watch for all those who love Tom Cruise" and "will not disappoint those who love the franchise", said India Today. "Its rehearsal-based intense action scenes are pure popcorn-crunching fun," wrote Hindustan Times. The film has been rated 3.5/5 (India Today, HT) and 4/5 (Mid-Day, TOI).
