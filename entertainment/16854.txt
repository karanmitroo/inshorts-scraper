As per reports, fashion designer Kunal Rawal, who is working on the wedding outfits of Shahid Kapoor, has created 10 outfits for the actor for different ceremonies. Shahid and the bride, Mira Rajput, will reportedly be wearing colour co-ordinated outfits for their wedding ceremonies. The duo is all set to tie the knot on July 7 at Oberoi hotel, Gurgaon.
