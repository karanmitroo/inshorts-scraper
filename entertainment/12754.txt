The Diana Penty, Abhay Deol and Ali Fazal-starrer film 'Happy Bhag Jayegi', which released on Friday, is "an inoffensive family entertainer", wrote India Today. The film is a "comedy that loses the plot", the Indian Express said. It "delivers harmless fun for the most part", NDTV wrote. The film has been rated 2/5 (Indian Express), 2.5/5 (NDTV) and 3/5 (India Today).
