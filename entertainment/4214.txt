The Akshay Kumar and Bhumi Pednekar starrer 'Toilet- Ek Prem Katha' has earned ₹51.45 crore in its opening weekend. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film collected ₹13.10 crore on Friday, ₹17.10 crore on Saturday and ₹21.25 crore on Sunday. Directed by Shree Narayan Singh, the film is said to be based on Swachh Bharat Abhiyaan.
