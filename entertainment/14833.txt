Actress Sonam Kapoor was named the worst actress for 'Prem Ratan Dhan Payo' at the 8th annual Golden Kela Awards held on Saturday. Sooraj Pancholi bagged the worst actor award for 'Hero'. Meanwhile, Imran Khan won the ‘Why Are You Still Trying’ award for his Kangana Ranaut-starrer “Katti Batti”. Sonakshi Sinha received ‘What The Hell’ award for her single 'Ishqholic'.
