'Da Da Dasse', the new song from the Shahid Kapoor, Ali Bhatt and Kareena Kapoor starrer upcoming film 'Udta Punjab, was released on Thursday. The song has been composed by Amit Trivedi, while it has been sung by Kanika Kapoor and Babu Haabi. Also featuring Diljit Dosanjh, it revolves around drug abuse in Punjab and will release on June 17.
