'Rock On 2' producer Ritesh Sidhwani has shared a video featuring the character posters for the film. "A teaser 2 the teaser on 5th September #Magik is here," tweeted Sidhwani. The film features Shraddha Kapoor as keyboard player and vocalist 'Jiah', Shashank Arora as bass guitarist 'Uday' while Farhan Akhtar, Arjun Rampal and Purab Kohli reprise their roles.
