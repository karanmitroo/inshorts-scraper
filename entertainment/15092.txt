As per reports, actress Alia Bhatt will be starring as the female lead in 'Aashiqui 3' opposite actor Hrithik Roshan. While Hrithik is currently busy with 'Mohenjo Daro', Alia is promoting 'Kapoor & Sons'. Previous reports said that female lead will be played by Sonam Kapoor who appeared with Hrithik in Yo Yo Honey Singh's music video 'Dheere Dheere Se'.
