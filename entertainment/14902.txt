The John Abraham-starrer film 'Rocky Handsome' has collected ₹16.12 crore in India in its opening weekend. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film, which premiered on Thursday, minted ₹1.77 crore on Thursday, ₹5.39 crore on Friday, ₹4.83 crore on Saturday and ₹4.13 crore on Sunday. The film, directed by Nishikant Kamat, also features Shruti Haasan and Sharad Kelkar.
