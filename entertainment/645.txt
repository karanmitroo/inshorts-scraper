Rajput group 'Karni Sena' founder Lokendra Singh Kalvi has clarified that his organisation is not withdrawing the protests against the film 'Padmaavat'. Earlier, Karni Sena's Mumbai leader Yogendra Singh Katar had declared that the organisation has withdrawn its protest. Kalvi added, "Other duplicate Karni Senas are creating a dubious situation by releasing contradictory and fake news."
