Following the demise of Shashi Kapoor on Monday, actor Govinda shared a picture of the veteran actor which he captioned, "My career's first film Ilzaam was with Shashi Ji." Talking further about interacting with Shashi during the film's shoot, Govinda wrote, "His first wise words to me have stayed with me ever since...'Kisi din Tum Unchaai chuoge... Be very professional'."
