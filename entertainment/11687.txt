Actor Nawazuddin Siddiqui's look from his upcoming film 'Munna Michael' has been revealed. The actor can be seen sporting earrings, sunglasses and a leather jacket. Directed by Sabbir Khan, the film will star Tiger Shroff and debutante actress Nidhhi Agerwal. The film will trace the journey of 'Munna', a street kid who aspires to be like Michael Jackson.
