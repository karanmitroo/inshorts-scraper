According to reports, Salman Khan will feature in a double role in Judwaa 2, starring Varun Dhawan. Salman will reappear as twins Raja and Prem, the characters he portrayed in Judwaa (1997), after a gap of 20 years, said a source. "His [Salman's] judwaa will come face-to-face with Varun's double role, possibly bantering in the climax scene," added the source.
