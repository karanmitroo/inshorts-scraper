Actor Shah Rukh Khan, who was in Delhi to promote his film 'Raees', danced to the song 'Chaiyya Chaiyya' from his 1998 film 'Dil Se' along with jawans. "Dancing with joy because all of you are loving Raees," he wrote on Twitter. Shah Rukh also performed Bhangra with the jawans. 'Raees', which released on Wednesday, also stars actress Mahira Khan.
