Rapper Honey Singh is all set for his Bollywood acting debut in ‘The Xpose’, also starring Himesh Reshammiya, Irrfan Khan, Zoya Afroz and Sonali Raut. The story follows the tale of two superstars after they are embroiled in the murder investigation of a sex symbol. Directed by Ananth Narayan Mahadevan, the movie will release on May 23.
