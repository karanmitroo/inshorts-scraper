Actor Shekhar Suman, in a veiled dig at actress Kangana Ranaut, tweeted, "Itna hungama...itna shor sharaba...nateeja? khoda pahad...nikli chuhiya!" Suman was reportedly referring to the box-office performance of Kangana's recent film 'Simran'. His son Adhyayen Suman has dated Kangana in the past and Suman was openly critical of Kangana and their relationship.
