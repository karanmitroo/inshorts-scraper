Singer Zayn Malik has been voted the 'GQ Best-Dressed Man in Britain 2017' by readers. The 23-year-old singer beat actor Tom Hiddleston to claim the title. This was the publication's first-ever 'Reader's Play-offs', where 59% of the readers' votes went to the 23-year-old singer. Notably, Zayn also won the magazine's 'Best Dressed' title in 2013.
