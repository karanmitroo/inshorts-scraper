Shahid Kapoor will be starring in an upcoming film titled 'Batti Gul Meter Chalu'. Directed by 'Toilet- Ek Prem Katha' director Shree Narayan Singh, the film deals with the issue of electricity bills. Tweeting about it, Shahid wrote, "Let's celebrate the festival of light with the wish that soon electricity will be a right and not a privilege for all."
