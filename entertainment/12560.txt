The first look of the Ranbir Kapoor, Anushka Sharma and Aishwarya Rai starrer 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' (ADHM) was revealed on Monday by filmmaker Karan Johar on Twitter. Karan returns as director for ADHM, four years after his last directorial venture, the 2012 film 'Student Of The Year'. Also starring Fawad Khan, the film is scheduled for a Diwali release.
