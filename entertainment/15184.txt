The first official poster of Akshay Kumar starrer 'Rustom' was revealed on Wednesday by the actor on Twitter. "3 shots that shocked the nation and changed his life! Find out what happened with #Rustom this August 12," Akshay captioned the image. The Tinu Suresh Desai directorial, wherein Akshay plays a Naval officer, also features Ileana D'Cruz and Esha Gupta.
