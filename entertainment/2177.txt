Reacting to India's representative Manushi Chhillar being crowned Miss World 2017 as India's 6th winner at the beauty pageant, actress Priyanka Chopra, tweeted, "We have a successor...Cherish and learn, and most importantly enjoy it." Manushi responded, "It means the #World to me! Have always looked up to you." Priyanka was the last Indian to win the title 17 years ago.
