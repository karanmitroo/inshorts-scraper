Actors Ranbir Kapoor and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan have featured in a photoshoot for a magazine. They will reportedly appear on the magazine cover. Ranbir and Aishwarya will be seen together onscreen for the first time in the Karan Johar directorial upcoming film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil', which is scheduled to release on October 28.
