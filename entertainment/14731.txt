The first look of Kareena Kapoor Khan in the film 'Udta Punjab' has been revealed. Kareena features as Dr Shivani Gupta in the film, starring Shahid Kapoor and Alia Bhatt. Earlier talking about her role in the film, Kareena said, "It's (the role) very simple and nice, good Samaritan kind of a thing". The film will release on June 17.
