Former cricketer Zaheer Khan and actress Sagarika Ghatge, who got married on November 23, have featured on the 'Just Married' special issue for the December-January edition of Harper's Bazaar Bride. While Sagarika can be seen in a yellow gown by Shantanu & Nikhil, Zaheer wore a black ensemble by the same designer duo paired with black moccasins by Christian Louboutin.
