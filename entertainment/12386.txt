Actor Shah Rukh Khan (SRK) has turned narrator for Sonu Sood's upcoming film 'Tutak Tutak Tutiya'. "It's kind of him to take the effort and go to a studio in Prague, record his voiceover and send it to us," said Sonu. Also starring Prabhudheva and Tamannaah Bhatia, the film is scheduled to release on October 7.
