Pakistani actress Humaima Malick's lip-lock with Emraan Hashmi in the former's Bollywood debut 'Raja Natwarlal', has caused a row in Pakistan. Several media reports have accused her of involving in a "publicity gimmick", that has sold out the nation's heritage and brought "infamy" to Pakistan. "Our people have to understand I am playing a role," said Humaima in an interview.
