Salman Khan's 'Sultan' has been listed as the second most popular film in India, according to Google Play's 'Best of 2016 List' for India. Science-fiction film 'The Martian' was named the most popular film in India in 2016. Fantasy film 'The Jungle Book' and Robert de Niro starrer 2015 film 'The Intern' stood third and fourth on the list respectively.
