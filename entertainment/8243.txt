The first look of the Chris Hemsworth starrer upcoming superhero film 'Thor: Ragnarok' has been released. Hemsworth, who plays 'Thor', along with Cate Blanchett and Tessa Thompson, who portray 'Hela' and 'Valkyrie' respectively, have featured on the cover of 'Entertainment Weekly' magazine. The cover story also features actors Tom Hiddleston and Jeff Goldblum.
