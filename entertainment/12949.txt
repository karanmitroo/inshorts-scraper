Actress Saiyami Kher has featured on the cover of the August issue of Harper's Bazaar Bride. The actress is seen in a blue ensemble with white embroidery by designer Rahul Mishra along with diamond jewellery from 'Nirav Modi'. Saiyami, who will be making her debut in Bollywood with 'Mirzya', "plays muse to the new Indian couturiers" in the issue.
