The Amitabh Bachchan starrer 'Pink' which released on Friday is "a powerful, brave Hindi mainstream film", wrote The Indian Express. "Pink keeps us gripped from all sides," said Hindustan Times. The Times of India wrote, "Pink is a powerful statement on the existing feudal mindset of... India." It has been rated 3.5/5 (The Indian Express), 4/5 (HT) and 4.5/5 (TOI).
