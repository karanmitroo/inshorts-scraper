The Shah Rukh Khan starrer film 'Fan' collected ₹19.20 crore in India on its first day, becoming the highest opening grosser of 2016. The film surpassed 'Airlift', which minted ₹12.35 crore on its first day, to achieve this feat. Directed by Maneesh Sharma, 'Fan' features Khan in a double role of that of a superstar as well as his fan.
