National Award-winning actress Neena Gupta has been cast as Rishi Kapoor's wife in Anubhav Sinha's 'Mulk' after she asked for work on Instagram. "After I saw her Instagram post, I didn't hesitate... to pick the phone and call her," said Anubhav. He added there are some people in the film industry who are timeless and Neena is one of them.
