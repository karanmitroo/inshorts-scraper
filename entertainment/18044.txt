The trailer of Gurmeet Choudhary starrer 'Khamoshiyan', which was released 10 days before, has got over two million hits on YouTube. “Content is always a winner! #Khamoshiyan trailer smashes past the 2 million view mark!”, film’s producer Mahesh Bhatt posted. Produced by Vishesh Films, the Karan Darra directorial is set to release Jan 30, 2015.
