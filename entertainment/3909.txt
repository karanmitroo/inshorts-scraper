Actor Hrithik Roshan and his estranged wife Sussanne Khan celebrated Ganesh Chaturthi on Friday with the Roshan family. A picture shows Sussanne and Hrithik with his father Rakesh Roshan, mother Pinky Roshan and sister Sunaina Roshan. Their children, 11-year-old Hrehaan and 9-year-old Hridhaan, can also be seen in the picture.
