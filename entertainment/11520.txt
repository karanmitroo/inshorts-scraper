Actress Sunny Leone and Indian designers including Shyma Shetty of 'Huemn', Ragini Ahuja of 'Ikai', Nitya Arora of 'Valliyan' are among those who are promoting positive body image in a new photoshoot. "I've always been on the bigger side... But I've embraced my body the way it is," said Sunny. "Being 'fit'... has nothing to do with size," said Ragini.
