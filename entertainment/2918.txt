Priyank Sharma, a former contestant on Bigg Boss 11, has denied rumours of dating co-contestant Vikas Gupta, who is gay. "Vikas is like a friend and brother to me," he added. Priyank was eliminated from the show by the host Salman Khan after he got into a scuffle with contestant Akash Dadlani, who was having an argument with Vikas Gupta.
