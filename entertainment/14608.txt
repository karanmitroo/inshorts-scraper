'Dard', the new song from the upcoming film 'Sarbjit' has been released. The song has been composed by Jeet Gannguli and sung by Sonu Nigam. It features Randeep Hooda, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Richa Chadda, the lead stars of the film. Directed by Omung Kumar, 'Sarbjit' is scheduled to release on May 20.
