A new song titled 'Saale Sapne' from Akshay Kumar starrer 'Pad Man' has been released. The song has been composed by Amit Trivedi with vocals by Mohit Chauhan and lyrics by Kausar Munir. Directed by R Balki and also starring Sonam Kapoor and Radhika Apte, 'Pad Man' is scheduled to release on January 25.
