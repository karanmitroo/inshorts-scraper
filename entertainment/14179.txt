Actor Daniel Craig, known for his role as 'James Bond', will reportedly visit India in June and may meet PM Narendra Modi during his visit. Craig will participate in a charity football match on June 11 where Bollywood personalities including Ranbir Kapoor, Arjun Kapoor and Sidharth Malhotra will play against a team of politicians including Manoj Tiwari and Babul Supriyo.
