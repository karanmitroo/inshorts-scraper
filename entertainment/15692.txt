'Tera Chehra' song from the film 'Sanam Teri Kasam' was YouTube's most shared video on Facebook and Twitter in India on Tuesday. The romantic film, featuring  Telugu actor Harshvardhan Rane and Pakistani actress Mawra Hocane, has been directed by Radhika Rao and Vinay Sapru. Posted on YouTube on January 8, the video has garnered over 12.30 lakh views so far.
