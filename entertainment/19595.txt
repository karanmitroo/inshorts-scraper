Ranbir Kapoor starrer 'Besharam' is set to release on next Wednesday, 2nd October, the national-holiday on account of Gandhi Jayanti.  The movie, produced by Himanshu Mehra, Sanjeev Gupta and Relaince Entertainment, also stars Ranbir's parents- Neetu Kapoor and Rishi Kapoor and promises to be a complete entertainer. The three will be sharing screen-space for the first time.
