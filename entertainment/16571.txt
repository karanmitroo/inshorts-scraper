Comedian-actor Kapil Sharma’s recent tweet reads that he is shooting for a new song “DJ Bajega to Pappu Nachega” for his Bollywood debut “Kis Kisko Pyaar Karoon”. The film is directed by the popular duo, Abbas-Mastan, and stars Elli Avram, Manjari Phadnis, Simran Kaur Mundi, Sai Lokur, Arbaaz Khan and Varun Sharma. It is slotted to release on September 25.
