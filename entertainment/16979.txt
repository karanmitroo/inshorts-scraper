Shahid Kapoor will soon be making his small screen debut as the actor will be seen judging the eighth season of the reality dance show 'Jhalak Dikhla Jaa'. Shahid will be replacing Madhuri Dixit, reports have claimed. Furthermore, judge Remo D'Souza will also be replaced by choreographer Ganesh Hegde. Karan Johar will continue as the third judge of the show.
