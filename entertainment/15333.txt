The Pulkit Samrat and Yami Gautam starrer film 'Sanam Re', which released on Friday, has collected ₹17.05 crore in its first weekend. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film earned ₹5.04 crore on Friday, ₹5.56 crore on Saturday and ₹6.45 crore on Sunday. Directed by Divya Khosla Kumar, the film also features Rishi Kapoor and Urvashi Rautela.
