The teaser of the Richa Chadda starrer upcoming film 'Cabaret' was released on Friday. Directed by Kaustav Narayan Niyogi, the romantic dance film also features Gulshan Devaiah. The film also marks the acting debut of cricketer S Sreesanth in Bollywood. Produced by Pooja Bhatt and Bhushan Kumar, the film is scheduled to release on June 5.
