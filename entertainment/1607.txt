Mark Hamill starrer 'Star Wars: The Last Jedi', which released on Friday, is "one of the finest blockbusters of the year," wrote Hindustan Times (HT). "'The Last Jedi' is everything a Star Wars film should be," said The Times of India (TOI) while Firstpost wrote that the film "surprises constantly". It has been rated 4/5 (TOI, Firstpost) and 5/5 (HT).
