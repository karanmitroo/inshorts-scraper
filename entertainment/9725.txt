According to reports, actor Peter Dinklage, known for portraying 'Tyrion Lannister' in the HBO series 'Game of Thrones' will star in 'Avengers: Infinity War' and its sequel. The actor will reportedly play a crucial role in the superhero films. The cast also includes Chris Evans and Robert Downey Jr. 'Avengers: Infinity War' is scheduled to release in 2018.
