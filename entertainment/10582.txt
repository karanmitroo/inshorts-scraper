A video shows actor Shah Rukh Khan's audition for the 2004 film 'Swades', which is a remake of the Kannada film 'Chigurida Kanasu'. In the video, he is seen auditioning for the role of 'Mohan Bhargava' while actress Gayatri Joshi auditions for the role of 'Geeta'. Notably, Shah Rukh received the 'Best Actor' Filmfare Award for this movie in 2005.
