Actor Rajkummar Rao, while referring to Ranbir Kapoor who was paired with Aishwarya Rai Bachchan in 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil', said he will be the next RK. Rao will be seen opposite Aishwarya in the upcoming film 'Fanney Khan'. "I am very excited. I can't wait to begin," he added while talking about working in the film.
