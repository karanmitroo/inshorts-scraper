The Amy Adams starrer 'Arrival', which released on Friday, is a "modern masterpiece", which "will never be forgotten". The film "makes you ponder the implications of time, love, life, and death", wrote Times of India, while The New Indian Express (TNIE) called it "a haunting discourse on inclusiveness". 'Arrival' has been rated 5/5 (HT), 4.5/5 (TOI) and 3.5/5 (TNIE).
