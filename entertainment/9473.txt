Actress Sanya Malhotra, who portrayed 'Babita Kumari' in the Aamir Khan starrer film 'Dangal' said that she was never a 'Khan fan' and admitted that she became Aamir Khan's fan after working with him. "But after working with him, I have so much respect for him," she added. She further revealed that she is a fan of actress Kangana Ranaut.
