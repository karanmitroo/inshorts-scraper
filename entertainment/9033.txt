Actor Varun Dhawan took to social media to share his first look from his upcoming film 'Judwaa 2'. Sequel to the 1997 Salman Khan-starrer 'Judwaa', the film will feature Varun playing characters Raja and Prem, a double role in the film. Also starring Jacqueline Fernandez and Taapsee Pannu, the comedy film will be directed by Varun's father David Dhawan.
