'Masaan', the Indian film that won two honours at the 68th Cannes International Film Festival, will be released across the country on July 24. The Neeraj Ghaywan directorial, featuring Richa Chadha and Sanjay Mishra, has won FIPRESCI and the Promising Future awards at the recently held Cannes festival. It also received a 5-minute long standing ovation at the event.
