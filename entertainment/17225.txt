Prominent filmmaker, Mani Ratnam (58), who was rumored to be hospitalized on Tuesday after complaining of chest pain, had actually visited Apollo hospital in Delhi for a regular health checkup. He got it done here to avoid public glare in Chennai. Mani Ratnam, a Padma Shri awardee, is known for films like Bombay (1995), 'Saathiya' and 'Guru'.
