Late actor Raj Kapoor's grandson Aadar Jain will be launched in an upcoming film, which will be produced by Aditya Chopra. Aadar will be launched in a romantic drama directed by Habib Faisal. Notably, Aadar's brother Armaan Jain made his Bollywood debut with the 2014 film 'Lekar Hum Deewana Dil', which was directed by Arif Ali.
