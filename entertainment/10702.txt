Team India cricketers attended the sangeet ceremony of cricketer Yuvraj Singh, who is getting married to actress Hazel Keech in Chandigarh on Wednesday. Among the attendees were captain Virat Kohli and coach Anil Kumble, besides Parthiv Patel, Ajinkya Rahane, Cheteshwar Pujara and other players from the team which won the Mohali Test. Kapil Dev was also present at the event.
