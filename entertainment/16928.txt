Actress Priyanka Chopra, who is currently busy with 'Quantico' American series, has reached the mark of 10 million followers on micro-blogging website Twitter. "10 million reasons why I feel so loved!10 million reasons why I’m the luckiest! #10MillionPCmaniacs thank u so much!”, the 32-year-old actress tweeted. Priyanka had joined the social network in January 2009.
