Bollywood superstar Salman Khan will perform with Swedish-Greek actress Elli Avram Saturday at the grand finale of the popular reality show "Bigg Boss - Saath 7". Elli, who made her Bollywood debut with 'Mickey Virus', is considered as one of Salman's favourite contestants on the show. He often referred to her as "paanch saal pehle ki Katrina Kaif".
