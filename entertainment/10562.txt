The Kangana Ranaut-starrer upcoming film 'Simran' will release on September 15, 2017, as per trade analyst Taran Adarsh. Directed by Hansal Mehta, Kangana will be seen playing the character of 'Praful Patel' in the film. 'Simran' is reportedly inspired by the life of an NRI nurse who was sent to prison for robbing banks to pay her debts.
