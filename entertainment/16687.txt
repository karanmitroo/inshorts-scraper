Mahira Khan, who is popularly known as Khirad of Pakistani popular telly show "Humsafar", has refused to do intimate scenes with Nawazuddin Siddiqui in the film "Raees". The duo will be playing an unhappy married couple in the film. Previously, Mahira's "Humsafar" co-star Fawad Khan had also refused to do love-making scenes in his upcoming film "Kapoor & Sons".
