The Akshay Kumar and Nimrat Kaur-starrer 'Airlift', which released on Friday, has collected ₹44.30 crore at the Indian box-office in its first weekend. According to film trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film minted ₹12.35 crore on Friday,  ₹14.60 crore on Saturday and ₹17.35 crore on Sunday. Directed by Raja Krishna Menon, 'Airlift' was made on a budget of ₹30 crore.
