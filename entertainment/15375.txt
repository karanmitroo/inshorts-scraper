'Teri Yaad', the new song from the Himesh Reshammiya starrer film 'Teraa Surroor', was YouTube's sixth-most shared video on Facebook and Twitter in India on Friday. The song has been sung by Himesh and Badshah, while it has been composed by Himesh himself. Posted on the video-sharing platform on Thursday, the video has garnered over 3.88 lakh views so far.
