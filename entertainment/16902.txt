Kendall Jenner has broken her sister Kim Kardashian's most-liked picture record on photo-sharing app Instagram. The photo, posted on May 25, that shows Jenner wearing a white lacy dress with her hair in the shape of hearts has 25,88,312 'likes' so far. Kim Kardashian and her husband Kanye West's wedding photo, posted on May 27 last year, has 24,73,754 'likes'.
