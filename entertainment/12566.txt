Actress Kareena Kapoor Khan, who is pregnant with her first child, on Sunday was the showstopper for designer Sabyasachi Mukherjee. The show, which was attended by celebrities including Deepika Padukone, was the grand finale of the Lakme Fashion Week (LFW) Winter/Festive 2016. "I must say it’s not one but two of us. It’s a very special moment," said Kareena.
