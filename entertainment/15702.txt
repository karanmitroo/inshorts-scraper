On the day the 2016 Golden Globe awards were announced, #GoldenGlobes was a top Twitter trend globally on Monday. Several users were discussing the dresses stars wore and DiCaprio's reaction to Lady Gaga walking by him tweeting, "Okay, I'm gonna sum up #GoldenGlobes for y'all... @ChanningTatum won best hair do. @JLo won best dress, & @LeoDiCaprio won for best humour".
