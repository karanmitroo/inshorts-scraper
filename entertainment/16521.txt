SS Rajamouli's block buster film 'Baahubali' has been chosen to be screened at the 20th Busan Film Festival, scheduled to be held in Busan (South Korea) from October 1 to October 10. "Baahubali will be screening at Busan International Film Festival on Oct 4th, 7th and 9th. I'll be attending on 4th", Rajamouli announced on Twitter.
