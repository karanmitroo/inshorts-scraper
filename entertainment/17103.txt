Anne Meara, the Emmy and Tony-nominated comedian, wife of Jerry Stiller and the mother of actor-director Ben Stiller, died Saturday, her family confirmed. Stiller and Meara were a top comedy act in the 1960s, appearing on “The Ed Sullivan Show” 36 times. The 85-year-old made a comeback with “Stiller & Meara” in 2010. Anne's cause of death has not been disclosed.
