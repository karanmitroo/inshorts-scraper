Pakistani singer Atif Aslam released his new song 'Younhi' on Sunday, on the occasion of his 34th birthday. "Let's go back to the roots where I started from," tweeted Atif while sharing the song's video, which he has featured in. Atif, known for songs like 'Aadat' and 'Tu Jaane Na', began his music career by co-founding the band 'Jal'.
