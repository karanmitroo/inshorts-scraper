The Brahman Ekta Seva Sanstha has lodged a 4 page complaint with the police against All India Bakchod and Bollywood stars Karan Johar, Arjun Kapoor and Ranveer Singh for their comedy show 'AIB Knockout'. "Officials of the culture department will examine the videos for the content. If found vulgar, further action will be initiated." Cultural affairs minister Vinod Tawde said.
