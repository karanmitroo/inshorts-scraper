Actor Arnold Schwarzenegger has announced that he will star as the titular character in a new Terminator film following the 2015 film, 'Terminator Genisys'. The 68-year-old actor said, "I am looking forward to it, absolutely." The sixth terminator film 'Genisys 2' was originally set to release in 2017. Notably, the first film, 'The Terminator' was released in 1984.
