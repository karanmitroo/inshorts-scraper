Actress Sonam Kapoor on Saturday started shooting for the biopic 'Neerja Bhanot', based on the life of an Indian flight attendant with the same name. Bhanot was murdered while saving passengers from terrorists on-board the hijacked Pan Am Flight 73 in 1986. Sonam took to Twitter to announce her excitement. "Day 1 of #NeerajBhanot... Let the journey begin!...," she tweeted.
