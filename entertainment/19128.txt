With earnings of $33 million in 2013, Hollywood heartthrob Angelina Jolie was the top-earning female actress followed by Jennifer Lawrence ($26 million) and Kristen Stewart ($21.5 million), according to eonline.com. Among the male actors, Robert Downey Jr. has topped the list with earnings of $75 million, followed by Channing Tatum ($60 million) and Hugh Jackman ($55 million).
