Disney on Wednesday released a new clip from its upcoming live-action adventure film 'The Jungle Book'. Titled 'Intro to Shere Khan', the clip features a confrontation between Shere Khan (voiced by Idris Elba), and wolf leader Akela (Giancarlo Esposito). The film, directed by Jon Favreau, features Neel Sethi as Mowgli and will release in India on April 8.
