The trailer of the Tiger Shroff and Shraddha Kapoor starrer upcoming film 'Baaghi' was released on Monday. Directed by Sabbir Khan, the film marks the debut of Telugu film actor Sudheer Babu in Hindi cinema. It is the second time that Sabbir and Tiger have come together after the film 'Heropanti'. 'Baaghi' is scheduled to release on April 29.
