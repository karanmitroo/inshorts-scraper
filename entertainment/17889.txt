Megastar Amitabh Bachchan's epic movie 'Deewar' Wednesday completed 40 years. Thanking the scriptwriters of the film, Bachchan tweeted, "40 years! 40 years ago, this day, Deewar was released! 21st Jan 1975.. Thank you Salim-Javed Saheb for the most perfect script". Released in 1975, the film was directed by Yash Chopra and also featured Shashi Kapoor, Parveen Babi and Neetu Kapoor
