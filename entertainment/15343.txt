The Aditya Roy Kapur and Katrina Kaif starrer film 'Fitoor', which released on Friday, has minted ₹14.11 crore in its first weekend in India. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film, whose collections grew on Saturday and Sunday, "benefitted from Valentine’s Day". It collected  ₹3.61 crore on Friday, ₹4.54 crore on Saturday, ₹5.96 crore on Sunday.
