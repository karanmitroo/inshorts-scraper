'Beautiful Myth', the theme song of the film 'Kung Fu Yoga', shows lead actor Jackie Chan wearing a kurta and dancing to the number choreographed by Farah Khan. 'Kung Fu Yoga' is an Indo-Chinese production and also stars Indian actors Sonu Sood, Disha Patani and Amyra Dastur. Directed by Stanley Tong, the film is scheduled to release on January 28.
