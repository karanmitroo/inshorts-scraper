Veteran Bollywood actor Dilip Kumar was born on December 11, 1922, in Peshawar, British India (now in Pakistan). Originally born as Muhammad Yusuf Khan, Dilip Kumar (93) was awarded Padma Bhushan in 1991 and Padma Vibhushan in 2015 for his contributions towards Indian cinema. He is married to actress Saira Banu since 1966, who is 22 years younger than him.
