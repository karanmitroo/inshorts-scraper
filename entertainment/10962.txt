Actress Zeenat Aman became the first Indian to win the Miss Asia Pacific title in 1970. The actress, who turned  65 on Saturday, made her acting debut with the 1971 film 'Hulchul'. She later went on to act in numerous films including 'Hare Rama Hare Krishna' (1971), 'Satyam Shivam Sundaram' (1978) and 'Insaf Ka Tarazu' (1980).
