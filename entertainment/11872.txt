A behind-the-scenes video of the upcoming film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' (ADHM) shows the cast and crew of the film talking about their experience of working with Karan Johar, the director of the film. While Ranbir says that it "feels surreal" to be a leading man in a Karan Johar film, Anushka says it was "perfect" working with Johar.
