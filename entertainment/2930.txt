Actress Vaani Kapoor will star with Hrithik Roshan in an upcoming movie by Yash Raj Films. The film, which will also star Tiger Shroff, will be directed by Siddharth Anand. "While the film is primarily Hrithik vs Tiger, there's only one girl in the film who is opposite Hrithik," said Siddharth. The untitled film will release on January 25, 2019.
