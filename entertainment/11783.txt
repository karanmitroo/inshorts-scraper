The trailer of the Bryan Cranston starrer science fiction superhero film 'Power Rangers' has been released. It is a reboot and the third feature film in the 'Power Rangers' franchise. The film stars Dacre Montgomery, Naomi Scott, RJ Cyler, Becky G and Ludi Lin as the teen superheroes. Directed by Dean Israelite, the film will release on March 24, 2017.
