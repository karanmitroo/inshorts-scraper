Actor Arjun Rampal tweeted about the music launch of his upcoming movie D-Day starring Irrfan Khan, Huma Qureshi, Shruti Haasan and Rishi Kapoor. The song called Alvida from D-Day is a romantic number by Nikhil D'Souza, Sukhvinder Singh, Loy and Shruti herself. While Arjun plays an Indian undercover agent in the movie, Shruti portrays a Pakistani prostitute.
