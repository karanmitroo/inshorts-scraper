Director Syed Ahmad Afzal’s drama-romance flick ‘Youngistaan’ - a film about a young man who is propelled into the nation’s highest political seat - featuring Jackky Bhagnani and Neha Sharma released this Friday. The movie has been rated 2 (Indian Express; koimoi.com), 3 (Times of India; Deccan Chronicle), 3.5 (India Today) and 4 (DNA; NDTV).
