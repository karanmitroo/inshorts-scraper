Actress Sangeeta Bijlani is set to return to the big screen with director Onir’s “Shab” after a span of 17 years. Bijlani, the ex-wife of former skipper Mohd Azharu-ddin, says, "I never thought of a comeback and am still not looking for a serious career reboot.” Sangeeta plays a high society fashionista in the film, which will role in June.
