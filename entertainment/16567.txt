The shooting of Housefull-3, starring Akshay Kumar, Riteish Deshmukh and Abhishek Bachchan, began on Sunday. While Akshay and Riteish were also a part of the previous two parts, Abhishek is a new addition. The film, also starring Jacqueline Fernandez, Nargis Fakhri and Lisa Haydon, is expected to hit theatres next year.
