The Alia Bhatt and Varun Dhawan starrer 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania' has earned ₹43.05 crore in its opening weekend. The film collected ₹12.25 crore on Friday, ₹14.75 crore on Saturday and ₹16.05 crore on Sunday, as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. The film is the second instalment in the 'Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania' franchise.
