Director and scriptwriter Luv Ranjan known for the 'Pyaar Ka Punchnama' films, is set to release 'Life Sahi Hai', a new web series. The series will feature Tarun Jain, Suhail Nayyar, Abhishek Saha and Siddhant Chaturvedi in a plot which revolves around the aspirations of youth in today's world. "Our stories and characters resonate with today's youth," said Ranjan.
