The recreated version of the song 'Dheere Dheere', which originally featured in 1990 film 'Aashiqui', has become the first Indian song to cross 200 million views on YouTube. Actors Hrithik Roshan and Sonam Kapoor featured in the song's video. The song was recreated by rapper Yo Yo Honey Singh and its video was directed by choreographer Ahmed Khan.
