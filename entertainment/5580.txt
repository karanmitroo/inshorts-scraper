Actress Sonam Kapoor took to Instagram to wish Karisma Kapoor on the occasion of her 43rd birthday today. Amrita Arora, Malaika Arora and filmmaker Karan Johar also shared pictures with Karisma to wish her on the occasion. Karisma is known for her performance in films like 'Raja Hindustani', 'Dil To Pagal Hai', 'Raja Babu', among others.
