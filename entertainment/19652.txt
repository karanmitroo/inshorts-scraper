Shoojit Sircar's 'Madras Cafe' starring John Abraham and Nargis Fakhri, which released this Friday, has been rated 3.5, 3 and 4 by India Today, Hindustan Times and NDTV respectively. The story is set against the backdrop of Sri Lankan civil war and assassination of Indian PM Rajiv Gandhi. "Madras Cafe dives boldly into terrain Bollywood hasn't touched before," says TOI.
