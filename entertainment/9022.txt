Actors Varun Dhawan and Alia Bhatt are set to appear on the talk show 'Koffee With Karan' season 5 for the second time. They will appear on the show to promote their upcoming film 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania'. A promotional video shows Alia naming 'orange' and 'santra' as two different citrus fruits, while responding to a question asked by Karan.
