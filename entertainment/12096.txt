The upcoming biopic 'M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story' has been declared tax-free in Mahendra Singh Dhoni's home state Jharkhand. The entertainment tax for the film has been waived off by the Jharkhand government, said an official. Directed by Neeraj Pandey and starring Sushant Singh Rajput, Kiara Advani and Disha Patani, the film will release on September 30.
