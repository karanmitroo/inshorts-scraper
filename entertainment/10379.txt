Disha Patani, who starred in the biopic 'M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story', was the most searched Bollywood actress in India, as per Google's 'Year in Search 2016'. Urvashi Rautela, who starred in 'Sanam Re' and 'Great Grand Masti' this year, ranked second on the list. Actress Pooja Hegde took the third spot in the list.
