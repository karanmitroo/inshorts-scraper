The soundtrack for the upcoming superhero film 'Suicide Squad', will feature songs from artistes including Eminem, Wiz Khalifa, Skrillex and Lil Wayne among others. Eminem's 2002 track 'Without Me' and a song titled 'Purple Lamborghini' by Skrillex and Rick Ross will be part of the soundtrack. The video for 'Purple Lamborghini' will reportedly feature 'The Joker' played by Jared Leto.
