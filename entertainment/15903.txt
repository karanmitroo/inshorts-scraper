With a total of 41 errors, 'Furious 7', the seventh instalment in the Fast and Furious franchise, is the most mistake-filled movie of 2015, according to popular website MovieMistakes. The annual list of mistakes in movies also includes Chris Pratt-starrer 'Jurassic World' (33), Ridley Scott's 'The Martian' (21) and James Bond movie 'Spectre' (16) in the top five spots.
