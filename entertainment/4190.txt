Priyanka Chopra, Amitabh Bachchan, Aamir Khan, Shah Rukh Khan and Ajay Devgn were among celebrities who tweeted wishes on the occasion of India's Independence Day on Tuesday. Priyanka tweeted, "Independence Day #Vibes #MyHeartBelongsToIndia." Devgn wrote, "Today I would like to thank our armed forces for retaining our independence and never letting us feel the price that they pay for it."
