Actor Shah Rukh Khan has shared a message against drunk driving in a new promotional video for his upcoming film 'Raees'. "This New Year's Eve be smart not stupid. Don't drink and drive. Love u all. #DontDrinkAndDrive #RaeesKiSuno," he captioned the video while sharing it on Twitter. Directed by Rahul Dholakia, 'Raees' is scheduled to release on January 25, 2017.
