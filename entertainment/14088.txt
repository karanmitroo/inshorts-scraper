'Waiting', which released on Friday, collected ₹1.30 crore in its opening weekend. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film collected ₹27 lakh on Friday, ₹48 lakh on Saturday and ₹55 lakh on Sunday. Directed by Anu Menon, the film stars Naseeruddin Shah and Kalki Koechlin as characters who forge a special bond when they face similar circumstances.
