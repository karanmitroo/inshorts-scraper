The Katrina Kaif and Sidharth Malhotra starrer 'Baar Baar Dekho' has earned ₹21.16 crore in its opening weekend. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film collected ₹6.81 crore on Friday, ₹7.65 crore on Saturday and ₹6.70 crore on Sunday. Directed by Nitya Mehra, the film was reportedly made on a budget of ₹50 crore.
