In a recent interview, director of the National Award winning film 'Baahubali', SS Rajamouli  said that in the film, Katappa killed Baahubali because he "told him to do so". The film has a cliffhanger ending, where Baahubali is killed by Katappa, the reason behind which may be revealed in the sequel, 'Baahubali: The Conclusion'. It will release in April 2017.
