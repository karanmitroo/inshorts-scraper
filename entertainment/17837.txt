Renowned actress Kamini Kaushal Saturday received 'The Lifetime Achievement Award' at the 60th Britannia Filmfare Awards 2014. She made her debut in Bollywood in 1946 as one of the leading ladies in Chetan Anand's Neecha Nagar. Kaushal, who has worked with leading actors like Ashok Kumar and Dilip Kumar, was last seen in Chennai Express, playing Shah Rukh Khan's grandmother.
