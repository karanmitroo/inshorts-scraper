The Best Foreign Language Film nominees at Oscars 2017 include the Swedish comedy-drama film 'A Man Called Ove' and Iranian-French drama film 'The Salesman'. Australian-Vanuatuan film 'Tanna', which depicts a true love story of a couple, has also been nominated. The other nominees are Danish-German historical drama film 'Land of Mine' and German-Austrian comedy-drama film 'Toni Erdmann'.
