With earnings of ₹30.14 crore on its first day, Ajay Devgn starrer film 'Golmaal Again' has become the second-highest Hindi opening grosser of 2017. The Hindi version of 'Baahubali 2', which earned ₹41 crore on its first day, is the highest opening grosser of 2017. Other films among the top five openers are 'Tubelight', 'Raees' and 'Judwaa 2'.
