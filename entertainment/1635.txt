Prabhas starrer 'Baahubali 2: The Conclusion' was the most searched query in India in 2017, Google India has revealed. The second-most searched query was the Indian Premier League, followed by Live Cricket Score. While Aamir Khan starrer 'Dangal' occupied the fourth spot, Arjun Kapoor and Shraddha Kapoor starrer 'Half Girlfriend' featured in the fifth spot on the list.
