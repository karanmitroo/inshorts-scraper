The song 'Premika' from Rohit Shetty's recently released film 'Dilwale' was YouTube's fifth most shared video on Facebook and Twitter in India on Tuesday. The romantic song featuring Varun Dhawan and Kriti Sanon, has been sung by Benny Dayal and Kanika Kapoor. Posted on the video-sharing platform on Sunday, the video has garnered over 2.68 lakh views so far.
