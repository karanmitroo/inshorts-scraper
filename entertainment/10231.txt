Malaika Arora Khan has shared an old picture of herself with Shah Rukh Khan from the sets of the song 'Chaiyya Chaiyya' from the 1998 film 'Dil Se'. "Omg!!!! I found this pic n got all nostalgic. Love it," wrote Malaika while sharing the picture on Instagram. The song's choreography was completed in under five days by Farah Khan.
