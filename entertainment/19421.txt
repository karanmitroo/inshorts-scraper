To commemorate 100-years of Indian cinema, Padma Bhushan-awardee actress Waheeda Rehman (77), star of classic films like Pyaasa and Guide, will be conferred  with the first Centenary Award for Indian Film Personality of the Year at the 44th International Film Festival of India starting from Nov-20 in Goa. The award is accorded the same importance as the Dadasaheb Phalke Award.
