Deepika Padukone, Ranveer Singh and Shahid Kapoor starrer 'Padmaavat' is "a spectacular looking period film that matches international standards", wrote Bollywood Hungama. Hindustan Times wrote, "Ranveer towers over Deepika, Shahid in this beautiful film". India Today wrote that the film is "mediocrity covered with jewels". It was rated 4.5/5 (Bollywood Hungama), 3/5 (HT) and 3.5/5 (India Today).
