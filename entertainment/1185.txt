Actress Yami Gautam will star along with Shahid Kapoor in the upcoming film 'Batti Gul Meter Chalu'. Announcing this on Twitter while tagging Shahid and Shraddha Kapoor, who is also said to be starring in the film, Yami wrote, "Super happy to commence this special journey." The film will be directed by 'Toilet: Ek Prem Katha' director Shree Narayan Singh.
