Actress Angelina Jolie attended the premiere of her upcoming directorial 'First They Killed My Father' along with her six children in Siem Reap, Cambodia. Her 15-year-old son Maddox, who was born in Cambodia, and 13-year-old son Pax were both involved in the film's production. The biographical historical thriller film is based on the non-fiction book written by Loung Ung.
