Actress Sana Khan, commenting on her upcoming film 'Wajah Tum Ho', has said that she finds it strange when asked about intimate scenes. "Today, every movie has a kissing scene. I find it weird when industry people talk like 'that kind of scenes'," she said. Also starring Sharman Joshi and Gurmeet Choudhary, 'Wajah Tum Ho' will release on December 2.
