The founder of women's vigilante group Gulabi Gang, Sampat Pal Devi, is one of the Congress candidates for the upcoming Uttar Pradesh Assembly elections. Pal's group, that fights against atrocities on women, had also inspired a Madhuri Dixit-starrer Hindi movie 'Gulaab Gang'. Notably, Pal was also one of the contestants in the sixth season of popular reality show 'Big Boss'.
