As per reports, TV actress Mouni Roy will play a villain in the Ranbir Kapoor, Alia Bhatt and Amitabh Bachchan starrer upcoming film 'Brahmāstra'. The film is the first to have cast Ranbir, Alia and Bachchan together. The Ayan Mukerji directorial will be the first part of a fantasy adventure trilogy and is scheduled to release on August 15, 2019.
