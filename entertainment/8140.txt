Actress Emilia Clarke, known for portraying 'Daenerys Targaryen' in the HBO series 'Game of Thrones', has been declared the new face of Italian luxury fashion house Dolce & Gabbana's new fragrance 'The One'. She will take over as the ambassador in September. "Emilia Clarke embodies perfectly the Dolce & Gabbana woman: she is radiant and lively," said the founders.
