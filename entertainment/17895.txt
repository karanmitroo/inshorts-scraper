Based on the life of mathematician Alan Turing, 'The Imitation Game' brings "a historical episode back to life", writes IBN Live. Having Benedict Cumberbatch and Keira Knightley in the lead, the Morten Tyldum directorial has been lauded for all aspects except the script. It has been rated 3.5/5 (IBN Live) and 4/5 (TOI, Indian Express, HT, Economic Times).
