The trailer of 'Julie 2', an erotic thriller starring Raai Laxmi, Ravi Kishen and Pankaj Tripathi, released on Monday. The film, which marks the Bollywood debut of Raai Laxmi, is a sequel to the 2004 Neha Dhupia starrer 'Julie'. The original film and the sequel have been directed by Deepak Shivdasani with Julie 2's release being scheduled for October 6.
