The makers of 'Baahubali 2: The Conclusion' have clarified that actor Shah Rukh Khan will not feature in the film. The official handle of the film tweeted, "We would have loved to have @iamsrk in our movie! Who wouldn't? But unfortunately it's a rumour!" Earlier, reports emerged that SRK plays a mediator between protagonist Prabhas and villain Rana Daggubati.
