Much awaited action thriller 'Badlapur', starring Varun Dhawan, Nawazuddin Siddiqui, Yami Gautam and Huma Qureshi, was released Friday. The movie has some amazing moments and a lot of credit goes to director Sriram Raghavan and his actors, says India Today. 'Badlapur' has received good response from critics and has been rated 3/5 (HT, NDTV) and 3.5/5 (Financial Express, India Today).
