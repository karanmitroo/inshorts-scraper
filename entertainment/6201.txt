The Akshay Kumar and Huma Qureshi starrer Jolly LLB 2 has generated the highest Television Rating Point (TRP) for a Hindi film television premiere, as per data shared by production house Fox Star Studios.  By generating 3.83 TV ratings, the film beat Sultan, which stands at 3.32 TRPs, and even international films like The Jungle Book, which has 2.39 TRPs.
