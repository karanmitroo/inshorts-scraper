As per reports, actors Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Abhishek Bachchan will feature together in filmmaker Anurag Kashyap's next film tentatively titled 'Gulab Jamun'. The couple was last seen together in Mani Ratnam's 2010 film 'Raavan'. Reportedly, the romantic comedy will also feature Amitabh Bachchan in a pivotal role.
