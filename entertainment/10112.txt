Shoe brand 'Bata' has sent legal notice to the makers of 'Jolly LLB 2', starring Akshay Kumar, for presenting their company in bad light. 'Bata' has pointed a dialogue in the film's trailer which conveys that one should feel humiliated if they wear 'Bata' footwear. Further, they've asked the makers to remove the dialogue and submit a written apology.
