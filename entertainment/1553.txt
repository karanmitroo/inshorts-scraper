The Shah Rukh Khan starrer 2004 film 'Swades' was the first Indian film to be shot inside the NASA research centre at the Kennedy Space Center in Florida, USA. Shah Rukh's character Mohan Bhargava in the Ashutosh Gowariker directorial worked on a rainfall monitoring satellite known as Global Precipitation Measurement (GPM). 'Swades' completed 13 years of its release on Sunday.
