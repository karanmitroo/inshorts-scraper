Actor Ranveer Singh took to social media to share a video of himself bobsleighing in the snow at Olympia bob run in Switzerland. "Faster than a speeding bullet !!! #inLOVEwithSWITZERLAND," read the caption alongside the video. Sharing his experience, Ranveer said, "Thundering down the ice track at the thrilling speed of up to 150 km/hr gets your heart pumping."
