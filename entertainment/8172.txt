Saif Ali Khan has revealed that his wife Kareena Kapoor was skeptical about sharing their son Taimur's pic and had said, 'Nazar lag jayegi'. He added, "I don't believe in all that. Agar nazar lagni hoti toh Kareena would be in the hospital by now." Saif further said that Taimur is already popular and he doesn't plan to hide him.
