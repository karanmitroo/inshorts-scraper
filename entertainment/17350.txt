The much awaited trailer of the upcoming comedy-drama film 'Dil Dhadakne Do' has been released. The movie, directed by Zoya Akhtar and produced by Ritesh Sidhwani and Farhan Akhtar, features Anil Kapoor, Shefali Shah, Priyanka Chopra, Ranveer Singh, Anushka Sharma and Farhan. The film is scheduled for release on June 5 this year.
