Universal Studios Hollywood on Tuesday announced that the 'Wizarding World of Harry Potter' will open in Los Angeles on April 7, 2016. The second “Harry Potter” themed park in the US will have the Hogsmeade village with the Honeydukes sweet-shop. The park’s rides will include “Flight of the Hippogriff” and a hyper-realistic 3D-HD “Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey”.
