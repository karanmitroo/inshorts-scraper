An FIR has been filed against comedian and TV host Kapil Sharma for destroying mangroves near his bungalow in Versova, Mumbai. The FIR was filed under the environment protection act and MRTP act. Earlier in April, the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) directed demolition of illegal portions of Kapil's residence in Goregaon and issued a notice in the Bombay High Court.
