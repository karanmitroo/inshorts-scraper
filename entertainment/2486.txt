According to reports, Alia Bhatt and Sidharth Malhotra will star in the upcoming film 'Sadak 2', which will see Pooja Bhatt and Sanjay Dutt reprising their roles from the 1991 film 'Sadak'. A source said, "Earlier, there were speculations that Sidharth and Alia are doing Mohit Suri's... 'Aashiqui 3', but the film has been put on hold."
