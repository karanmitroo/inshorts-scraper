The Merriam-Webster dictionary has added 850 new terms, including 'embiggen', a made-up word from a 22-year-old 'The Simpsons' episode that has been defined as 'to make bigger or more expansive'. Other words include 'wanderwort' (word borrowed from another language), 'aquafaba' (the liquid that results when beans are cooked in water), and 'chiweenie' (a cross between a Chihuahua and a dachshund).
