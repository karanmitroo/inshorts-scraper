Actor Sanjay Dutt, while talking about the upcoming biopic on him, said that he has not had an easy life and has made way too many mistakes. Sanjay added, "I want youngsters to really learn from it (my mistakes)...it was definitely worth telling my story." Directed by Rajkumar Hirani, the biopic will star actor Ranbir Kapoor as Sanjay Dutt.
