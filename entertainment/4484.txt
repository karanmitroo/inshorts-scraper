Actor Akshay Kumar, as part of promotions of his upcoming film 'Toilet- Ek Prem Katha', participated in a cleanliness drive in Lucknow organised by the UP government. He also took to Twitter to share a picture with UP CM Yogi Adityanath. The film, which will release on August 11, is said to be based on PM Modi's Swacch Bharat Abhiyan.
