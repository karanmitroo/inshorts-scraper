Self-styled critic Kamaal R Khan made derogatory comments against actor Kamal Haasan on Twitter.  "Mr. @ikamalhaasan you have only 367000 followers, means you are Chai Cum (kam) Paani compare[d] to Me Me Me the no.1 critic and superstar KRK," tweeted KRK. He made the comment while responding to Haasan's tweet on the demise of Carnatic musician M Balamuralikrishna.
