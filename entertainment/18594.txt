The Salman Khan starrer 'Kick' has taken its total box-office collections ahead of Shah Rukh starrer 'Chennai Express', in a mere 20 days of its release. The movie collected Rs. 1.2 crore on Wednesday, taking its total to Rs 227.65 crore, leaving behind the SRK blockbuster 'Chennai Express' which earned Rs 226.70 crore at the box office, last year.
