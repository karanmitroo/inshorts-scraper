Actor Salman Khan and actress Sunny Leone were the most searched ‘Indian Cinema’ stars in the past ten years, according to Google. Shah Rukh Khan, Akshay Kumar, Amitabh Bachchan and Rajinikanth featured in the top five most searched actors. Meanwhile​, Katrina Kaif, Kareena Kapoor, Kajal Aggarwal and Deepika Padukone were among the top five most searched actresses.
