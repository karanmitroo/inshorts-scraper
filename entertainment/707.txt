A new song titled 'Sayaani' from Akshay Kumar, Sonam Kapoor and Radhika Apte starrer 'Pad Man' has been released. The song has been composed by Amit Trivedi with vocals by Yashita Sharma, Jonita Gandhi, Yashika Sikka and Rani Kaur. Directed by R Balki, 'Pad Man' is scheduled to release on February 9.
