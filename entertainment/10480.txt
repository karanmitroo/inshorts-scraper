Katrina Kaif has featured on the December-January cover of 'Harper's Bazaar Bride' magazine. She is seen wearing a nude colour thigh-high slit lehenga with a choli, teamed with a cape jacket by Manish Malhotra. Her look has been accessorised with earrings and bracelet by Renu Oberoi. The cover story on her is titled, 'Killing it Katrina Style With Manish Malhotra'.
