Rohit Shetty's Chennai Express, which released on Friday, starring SRK and Deepika Padukone, has been rated 2.5 by The Indian Express, 3.5 by DNA and 3 by India today. Having all the ingredients - drama, action, comedy, songs, the car chases and the big finish - the story portrays romance of a 40-year-old North Indian guy and a gorgeous Tamilian girl.
