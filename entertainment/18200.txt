Starring Emraan Hashmi in the lead, 'Ungli', which released on Friday, reportedly made a slow start at the box office with a business of Rs 3.60 crore on the opening day. Another film which released, Friday, was erotic-cum-thriller 'Zid', which had Karanvir Sharma, Mannara and Shraddha Das in lead roles. 'Zid' reportedly minted Rs 2.82 crore on the opening day.
