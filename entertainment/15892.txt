The characters of Raj and Simran from the Bollywood film 'Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge', played by Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol, were voted as the 'Greatest Film Couple' in a UK poll. The online poll was conducted by the British Film Institute. Jesse and Celine of the 'Before' trilogy, played by Ethan Hawke and Julie Delpy, took the second spot.
