Varun Dhawan took to Twitter to share the first poster of his upcoming film 'Judwaa 2', a sequel to the 1997 Salman Khan and Karisma Kapoor starrer 'Judwaa'. "On David Dhawan's 65th birthday here is his 43rd film #Judwaa2. RAJA and PREM Double fun this Dussehra," tweeted Varun. David Dhawan, who directed the original, is also directing 'Judwaa 2'.
