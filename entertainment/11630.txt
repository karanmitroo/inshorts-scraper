Actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and actor Ranbir Kapoor have featured on the November cover of 'Filmfare' magazine. Both Ranbir and Aishwarya are seen wearing white shirts in the cover shot. The cover story on the duo, who will star in director Karan Johar's upcoming film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil', is titled 'Steamy! Aishwarya-Ranbir scorch it up'.
