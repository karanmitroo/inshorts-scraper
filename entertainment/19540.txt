Shuja Ali's 'Baat Bann Gayi' featuring Gulshan Grover, Ali Fazal (in a double role), Amrita Raichand and Anisa released on Friday. The movie has been rated 1 by Mumbai Mirror and 1.5 by TOI, India Today and OneIndia. Talking about the movie, TOI critic says, "Sadly, for a film that intends to be funny, this one ends up being tardy."
