Aamir Khan's film 'Dangal' has created numerous records within three days of its release. With earnings of ₹42.35 crore, it is the second-highest single day grosser in India ever. Further, 'Dangal' became the second highest opening day grosser of Bollywood in 2016 with collections of ₹29.78 crore. It also had the highest opening for any Hindi film ever in Australia.
