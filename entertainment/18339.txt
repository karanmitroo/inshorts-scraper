The Farah Khan directorial 'Happy New Year', which hit the screens on Friday, has been described as a 'loud, over-the-top, and blindingly bright' 'Bollywood spectacle' by IBNLive. The film, which flashes with the plethora of stars like Shahrukh Khan, Deepika Padukone, Abhishek Bachchan and Boman Irani, has been rated 2.5/5 (IBNLive, HT), 2/5 (NDTV, Indian Express) and 3/5 (TOI).
