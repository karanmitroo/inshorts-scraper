Jon Favreau's 'The Jungle Book' has collected ₹74.08 crore in India in its first week, making it the second biggest opening week grosser of 2016. The Akshay Kumar starrer film 'Airlift' so far remains the biggest opening week grosser of 2016, having minted ₹83.50 crore in its first week. 'The Jungle Book' previewed in the US on Thursday.
