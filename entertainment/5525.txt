According to reports, actor Sanjay Dutt will have a cameo appearance in the Ranbir Kapoor-Katrina Kaif starrer film 'Jagga Jasoos'. Sources suggest that Dutt will play the role of a 'sutradhar' (narrator) and Ranbir recommended Dutt's name for the part. Previously, reports of Shah Rukh Khan having a cameo in the film had also emerged.
