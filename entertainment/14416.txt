A teaser for the Tom Hanks and Irrfan Khan starrer 'Inferno' was released on Sunday. The film is a screen adaptation of Dan Brown's book 'Inferno' and a sequel to the 2006 film 'Da Vinci Code'. Director by Ron Howard, the film will have Hanks reprising his role as Robert Langdon. 'Inferno' will release on October 14 in India.
