According to reports, actor Shahid Kapoor and his wife Mira Rajput will appear together in the fifth season of celebrity talk show 'Koffee With Karan'. The episode will reportedly be aired in mid-December. Shahid has previously appeared with Esha Deol, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Priyanka Chopra and Sonakshi Sinha respectively in the previous seasons of the show.
