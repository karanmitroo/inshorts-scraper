T-Series on Wednesday released the official trailer of 'Sanam Re', starring Pulkit Samrat, on YouTube. The film, directed by 'Yaariyan' filmmaker Divya Khosla Kumar, also features Rishi Kapoor, Yami Gautam, and Urvashi Rautela. The film, a romantic drama with music composed by Mithoon, Jeet Ganguly and Amaal Mallik, is scheduled to release on February 12, 2016.
