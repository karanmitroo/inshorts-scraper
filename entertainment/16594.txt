'Gour Hari Dastaan' which released this Friday is a slow-paced biopic with certain irrelevant characters and scenes, but it deserves to be seen, wrote TOI. The film is about a silent warrior who struggles to prove his identity as a freedom fighter. NDTV has called it "less engaging." The film is rated 3/5 (TOI), 2.5/5 (NDTV), and 1/5 (Indian Express).
