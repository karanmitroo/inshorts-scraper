With over 6.4 million down votes, Canadian pop singer Justin Bieber’s 2010 single ‘Baby’ tops the list of most disliked videos on YouTube. Notably, it was the most-viewed video until November 2012, when it was surpassed by Psy’s ‘Gangnam Style’. Currently, with over 1.3 billion views, it is the 15th most viewed video on YouTube.
