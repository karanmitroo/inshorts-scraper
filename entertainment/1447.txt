As per reports, the Central Board of Film Certification (CBFC) has now asked the Mewar royal family to join a panel that would help in certifying Sanjay Leela Bhansali's film 'Padmavati'. Vishvaraj Singh from the royal family said that he has written to the CBFC seeking clarifications on certain aspects of the film that stars Deepika Padukone as queen Padmini.
