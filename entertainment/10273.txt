Actor Aamir Khan has referred to Deepika Padukone and Katrina Kaif as "female superstars". He said, "In Hindi cinema there have been female superstars right from Madhubala ji to Waheeda Rehman ji to Sridevi to Madhuri to Kajol to Juhi... We have had amazing female superstars." Aamir added that his film 'Secret Superstar' is about a superstar who's a girl.
