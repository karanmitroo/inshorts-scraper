As per reports, actress Anushka Sharma will appear in a minor role in filmmaker Rajkumar Hirani's biopic on actor Sanjay Dutt, where Ranbir Kapoor plays the lead role. Anushka is reportedly playing the role of a journalist who chronicles Sanjay's life. The movie could be the third film for Anushka and Ranbir after 'Bombay Velvet' and 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil'.
