The Zee Cine Awards in 2016 introduced the Jury awards for the first time where 'Bajirao Mastani' was awarded as the Best Film. The Best Actor Male (Jury) award was shared by Amitabh Bachchan for 'Piku' and Ranveer Singh for 'Bajirao Mastani' while Deepika Padukone received the Best Actor Female (Jury) for her role in Piku.
