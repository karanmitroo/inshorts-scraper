Actress Shraddha Kapoor took to social media to reveal her first look from the upcoming biopic 'Haseena: The Queen of Mumbai', based on the life of Haseena Parkar, late sister of Dawood Ibrahim. The film will trace Haseena's life from her teenage to her 40s. Shraddha's brother Siddhant Kapoor will be seen playing Dawood Ibrahim in the film.
