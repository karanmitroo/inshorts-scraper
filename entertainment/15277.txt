Bihar's Deputy CM and Lalu Prasad Yadav's son Tejashwi Prasad Yadav has blamed films and TV serials for portraying a negative image of Bihar. Speaking at the inauguration of Patna Film Festival on Friday, he held filmmakers like Prakash Jha responsible. He added, "Prakash Jha's Gangaajal and a TV serial currently on air, Bahubali, created a negative image of Bihar."
