With an £103m earning, the 23rd bond movie Skyfall became the highest grossing film in UK, knocking James Cameron's Avatar (2009) from top spot, according to the BFI's annual Statistical Yearbook. 2012's other top grossing movies in the list are - The Dark Knight Rises (£56.3m), The Hobbit: An Unexpected Journey (£52.2m) and The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn-2 (£35.8m).
