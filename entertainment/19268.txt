Actress Kangna Ranaut will reportedly play two roles in sequel of her 2011 hit film 'Tanu Weds Manu'. The sequel will feature Kangna and R Madhavan in leading roles as a married couple. Directed by Anand L Rai, 'Tanu Weds Manu 2' will start shooting in April 2014 and take off from where the first part ended.
