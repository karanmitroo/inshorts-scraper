Veteran actress Rekha's first look in Abhishek Kapoor's 'Fitoor' was revealed Monday night. Rekha can be seen sitting under a tree dressed in a heavy, rich attire designed by Manish Malhotra. Based on Charles Dickens' novel 'Great Expectations', the movie also features Aditya Roy Kapur and Katrina Kaif. Rekha portrays Ms Havisham, the spinster who gets left at the altar.
