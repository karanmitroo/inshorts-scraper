A new song titled 'Dhingana' from the Shah Rukh Khan, Mahira Khan and Nawazuddin Siddiqui starrer 'Raees' was released on Monday. The song, picturised on the film's titular character played by Shah Rukh, has been sung by Mika Singh with lyrics by Mayur Puri. Directed by Rahul Dholakia, 'Raees' is scheduled to release on January 25.
