Actress Juhi Chawla, who has earlier worked with stars like Aamir and Shahrukh, hopes to be paired with Salman Khan in a movie, someday. The actress, who is working with Madhuri for the first time in 'Gulaab Gang', says, “I don't see that(working with Salman) happening. But I never thought I will be working with Madhuri… so you never know.”
