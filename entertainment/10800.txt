A video showing the making of the song 'Khulke Dulke' from the Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor starrer upcoming film 'Befikre' has been released. The song is described as "high on energy and the happiest Punjabi song". It was shot on the last day of the film's shooting schedule. Directed by Aditya Chopra, 'Befikre' will release on December 9.
