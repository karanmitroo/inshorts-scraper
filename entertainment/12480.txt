The Sonakshi Sinha starrer 'Akira' has earned ₹5.15 crore on its opening day, as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. The film is said to be made for a budget of around ₹30 crore. Directed by AR Murugadoss, the film, also starring Konkona Sen Sharma and Anurag Kashyap, is a remake of the 2011 Tamil film 'Mouna Guru'.
