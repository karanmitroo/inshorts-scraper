Actor Shah Rukh Khan launched Sania Mirza’s autobiography, ‘Ace Against Odds’, on Wednesday in Hyderabad, where he called the tennis star ‘rani of racket’. “Sania has done more than anyone else to make our country proud. She is the ‘rani’ of racket,” said SRK. Talking about SRK, Sania said, “I couldn’t have thought of a better person to do it.”
