'Ice Age: Collision Course', the fifth installment of the 'Ice Age' film franchise, has earned ₹8.50 crore in its opening weekend in India. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film collected ₹1.90 crore on Friday, ₹3 crore on Saturday and ₹3.60 crore on Sunday. In India, the film was released in English, Hindi, Tamil and Telugu.
