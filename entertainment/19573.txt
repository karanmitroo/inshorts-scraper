Actress Poonam Pandey is ready to perform an item-number for the Kannada movie, Love is Poison, and will be paid Rs. 60-lakh for the three-day shoot, starting October-3. This could upset Kannada film lead actresses, who are paid not more than Rs.40 lakh. The movie is directed by Nandan Prabhu and the song will be choreographed by Chinni Prakash.
