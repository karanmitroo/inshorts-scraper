Actor Arjun Kapoor has shared a photo of him and actress Shraddha Kapoor recreating the iconic train sequence from the 1995 film 'Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge', during the shoot of their upcoming film 'Half Girlfriend'. Arjun and Shraddha will be seen together on-screen for the first time in a Bollywood movie. The film is directed by Mohit Suri.
