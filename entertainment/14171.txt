As per reports, Hollywood actress Amber Heard (30) has filed for divorce from her husband Johnny Depp (52) in Los Angeles Superior Court citing 'irreconcilable differences'. She has also sought for 'spousal support' (alimony) from Depp. The couple, who worked together in thriller-drama 'The Rum Diary' in 2011, had married 15 months ago in February 2015 and have no children.
