Brie Larson, Jacob Tremblay and Joan Allen starrer 'Room' which released across India on Friday is 'wise' and 'timeless', said HT. "If you are a sucker for films and other narratives where adversity is trumped, watch 'Room'," wrote Mumbai Mirror. The Lenny Abrahamson directorial, nominated for four Oscars, has been rated 5/5 (HT), 4.5/5 (Mumbai Mirror) and 4/5 (Indian Express).
