Television actress Shveta Salve and husband Hermit Sethi welcomed a baby girl on Wednesday. The news was confirmed by VJ Nikhil Chinapa on Twitter. Shveta, who has appeared in shows like 'Hip Hip Hurray' and 'Left Right Left' married Sethi in 2012. Earlier, the actress had walked the ramp while being pregnant and had also done a pregnancy photoshoot.
