Annoyed over reports suggesting that she recommended her family's(Usha Mangeshkar) and friend's(Suresh Wadkar) name for this year's Padma awards, Lata Mangeshkar(84) has defended it, saying that she recommended  them as  the committee asked  her suggestions .  "I haven't done anything wrong in suggesting the names of these multi-faceted artistes'. But from now onwards, I won't give any suggestions," she added.
