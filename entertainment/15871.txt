As per reports, Bollywood actress Genelia D'Souza Deshmukh is pregnant with her second child. While these rumours have been doing the rounds since quite some time, few pictures of the actress with the baby bump surfaced recently. Genelia, who had married actor Riteish Deshmukh in February 2012, already has a 1-year-old boy named Riaan who was born in November 2014.
