In her upcoming film 'Gulaab Gang', actress Madhuri Dixit will be singing a song for the first time. Interestingly, Madhuri’s mother Snehlata Dixit, who is a trained classical singer, will be starting the song from where Madhuri takes it on. The song is a folk song titled ‘Rangi Sari Gulaabi Chunariya’ that has been composed by movie director Soumik Sen.
