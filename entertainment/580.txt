Actor Abhishek Bachchan's Twitter account was hacked by a group calling themselves Turkish 'cyber army'. The hackers, who earlier hacked accounts of people including Anupam Kher, Pritish Nandy and Kaushik Basu, retweeted tweets that they had posted on these accounts. The name of his handle was also changed from 'juniorbachchan' to 'juniorbachchana' for a brief period of time.
