Actor Amitabh Bachchan, while speaking at the inauguration of the 22nd Kolkata International Film Festival praised his daughter-in-law Aishwarya Rai Bachchan's character in the film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil'. "Saba is a liberated and a divorced poetess who has turned the tables irrevocably on men," said Bachchan. He was speaking on the changing role of women in Indian cinema.
