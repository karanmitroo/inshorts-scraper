Actress Sonam Kapoor has featured on the cover of 'Elle India' for its September edition. The 31-year-old actress is seen wearing a colour block dress by Louis Vuitton paired with Adidas sneakers in the cover shot. Sonam has listed Ashi Studio, Stella McCartney, Delpozo, Louis Vuitton, Ulyana Sergeenko and Maticevski as some of her 'dream designers'.
