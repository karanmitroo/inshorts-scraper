The Arshad Warsi and Naseeruddin Shah starrer Irada "brings us face to face with harsh realities in Punjab", wrote Hindustan Times. "The...attempt to educate audience on perils of chemical contamination...is half-baked", wrote Times of India. NDTV said, "It is deserving of wide attention and hearty applause for concerns it articulates". It has been rated 3/5 (NDTV, HT) and 2.5/5 (TOI).
