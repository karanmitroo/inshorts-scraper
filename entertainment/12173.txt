The trailer for the Manoj Bajpayee, Kay Kay Menon and Anupam Kher starrer 'Saat Uchakkey' has been released. The film marks the debut of Ayushmann Khurrana's brother Aparshakti Khurrana and has been described by its makers as "the most quirky film of the year". Directed by Sanjeev Sharma, the film is scheduled to release on October 14.
