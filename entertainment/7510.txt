Television actress Saumya Tandon is reportedly planning to quit television serial 'Bhabi Ji Ghar Par Hai!' to focus on other projects. Shilpa Shinde, who earlier played 'Angoori bhabhi' in the show, had claimed that Saumya had not taken her sexual harassment allegations against producer Binaifer Kohli's husband Sanjay seriously. Shilpa added that Saumya said, "No one rapes in our industry."
