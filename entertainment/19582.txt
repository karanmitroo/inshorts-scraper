The second motion poster for the forthcoming movie "Dhoom-3" is out online, which showcases the 4-leading stars -Aamir Khan, Abhishek Bachchan, Katrina Kaif and Uday Chopra- with fire blazing all around them. Aamir will be seen in a negative role in the third installment of the franchise. Directed by Vijay Acharya, the movie is set to release on December-20.
