Jacqueline Fernandez, who was recently seen in 'Roy', has been approached to play the female lead opposite superstar Salman Khan in Karan Johar's 'Shuddhi'. A source said, "Karan seems to be keen on signing her...Jacqueline is now working out her dates for the movie." Reportedly, the role was earlier offered to Kareena Kapoor who later opted out of the film.
