Amitabh Bachchan, Aamir Khan, Salman Khan, Priyanka Chopra and Aishwarya Rai have been invited to join the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences as members and vote for the Oscar awards. Deepika Padukone, Irrfan Khan, documentary filmmaker Anand Patwardhan are among other Indians on the list. Notably, Shah Rukh Khan hasn't been issued an invite by the Academy.
