Actor Deepika Padukone, who delivered a slew of hits in 2013, including Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani, Chennai Express and Ram-Leela, was conferred as the CNN-IBN Indian of the Year, 2013 (Special Achievement) on Friday. Remarkably, she has become Bollywood's first leading lady to have 4 back to back Rs 100 crore hits in the same year.
