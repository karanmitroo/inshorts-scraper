Singer Avril Lavigne has slammed Facebook CEO Mark Zuckerberg for his jab at rock band Nickelback, saying it was in "poor taste" and accusing him of bullying. Lavigne, who was once married to Nickelback's Chad Kroeger, was referencing a video wherein Zuckerberg debuted AI system Jarvis. Zuckerberg asked Jarvis for "good Nickelback songs," and Jarvis replied saying there were none.
