Veteran film, theatre and television actress Sulabha Deshpande passed away on Saturday at the age of 79 at her residence in Mumbai. She was considered among those spearheading the experimental theatre movement in the 1970s. Some of her works include the films 'English Vinglish' and 'Gaman'. She won the Sangeet Natak Akademi Award in 1987 for her contribution to theatre.
