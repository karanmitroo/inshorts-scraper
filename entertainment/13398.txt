'Lipstick Lage Ke', a new song from the upcoming film 'Great Grand Masti' has been released. The song has been picturised on the lead actors Riteish Deshmukh, Vivek Oberoi and Aftab Shivdasani along with Sonali Raut, who has made a guest appearance. The track has been sung by Shaan and Payal Dev while Shaan, Gourov, and Roshin have composed it.
