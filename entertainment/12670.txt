'Parinda Hai Parinda', a new song from the Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Amy Jackson starrer 'Freaky Ali' has been released. The song has been composed by the duo Sajid-Wajid while Wajid has sung it. The lyrics for the track have been penned by Shabbir Ahmed. Directed by Sohail Khan, the film also stars Arbaaz Khan and will release on September 9.
