'Humne Pee Rakhi Hai', the item number from the Divya Khosla-directorial film 'Sanam Re', featuring Divya herself, was released on Tuesday. The song has been sung by Neha Kakkar and Jaz Dhami. 'Sanam Re' is a romantic drama, featuring Pulkit Samrat, Yami Gautam and Urvashi Rautela in lead roles. It is scheduled to release on February 12.
