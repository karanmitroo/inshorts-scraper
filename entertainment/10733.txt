Actor Suniel Shetty's 20-year-old son Ahan Shetty is set to make his Bollywood debut with a Sajid Nadiadwala film. Filmmaker Karan Johar called Ahan "a star in the waiting" while Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh tweeted, "Good wishes as you take your first big step, Ahan". Currently, Ahan is reportedly in London undergoing special training for the film.
