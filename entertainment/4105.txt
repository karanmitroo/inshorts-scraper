Ranbir Kapoor has said that Sanjay Leela Bhansali wanted to make his version of the 1976 film 'Balika Badhu' with him and Alia Bhatt. "Alia and I had done a photo shoot together for...Bhansali when I was 20...and she was about 12 or even younger," added Ranbir. He further said that he has been connected to her since then.
