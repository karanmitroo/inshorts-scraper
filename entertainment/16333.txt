The official trailer of Salman Khan and Sonam Kapoor starrer 'Prem Ratan Dhan Payo' was released on Thursday. The Sooraj Barjatya directorial, which releases on November 12, also features Neil Nitin Mukesh and Anupam Kher. Salman will be seen portraying a double role in the film, one of a prince and the other of a fighter.
