A Ukranian former journalist Vitalii Sediuk on Thursday almost pushed the reality TV star Kim Kardashian to the ground at Paris Fashion Week. Seduik pushed Kardashian when she arrived at the Balmain show for Paris Fashion Week along with mum Kris and husband Kanye. Earlier this year, Sediuk attacked actor Brad Pitt at the Los Angeles premiere of "Maleficent".
