Senior BJP leader LK Advani has praised Aamir Khan-starrer 'PK'. “Hearty greetings to Rajkumar Hirani and Vidhu Vinod Chopra for a wonderful and courageous film that they have produced in the shape of 'PK',” Advani said in a statement. He also said that the film has "excellent performances by the protagonists Aamir Khan, Anushka Sharma and Boman Irani".
