The Ryan Reynolds-starrer 'Deadpool' has collected $135 million in the US in the first three days, becoming the only R-rated film to achieve this feat. Previously, the record for biggest R-rated opening was held by 'The Matrix Reloaded' (2003) which opened to $91.8 million. 'Deadpool' has also surpassed '50 Shades of Grey' ($85.2 million), to become the biggest winter opener.
