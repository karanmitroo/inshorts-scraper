The teaser for 'Darr 2.0', a web series based on the 1993 film 'Darr', has been released. Produced by 'Y-Films', the youth division of Yash Raj Films, the series revolves around the characters Rahul, Kiran and Sunil in a plot exploring cyber stalking and digital crimes. The characters were originally portrayed by Shah Rukh Khan, Juhi Chawla and Sunny Deol.
