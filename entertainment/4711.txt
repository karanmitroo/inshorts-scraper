The trailer of the upcoming film 'Lucknow Central', starring Farhan Akhtar, Diana Penty and Ronit Roy, was released on Thursday. The film revolves around a man named Kishan Mohan Girhotra, played by Farhan, who dreams of forming a music band but ends up in Lucknow Central jail. Directed by Ranjit Tiwari, the film is scheduled to release on September 15.
