Actors Alia Bhatt and Varun Dhawan have featured on the cover of 'Filmfare' magazine. Alia is wearing a crop top, light-wash jeans and a red coat from British luxury fashion house Burberry. She is also sporting a bow headband. Varun is seen in an unbuttoned chambray shirt. The cover story on them is titled 'Alia-Varun: When Love Comes Calling'.
