Malaika Arora, while referring to the 'Chaiyya Chaiyya' song from 1998 film 'Dil Se', said the magic of it could only be recreated if Farah Khan, Shah Rukh Khan and she came together for it. She added, "Till date, I get nostalgic about the song. The kind of...opportunity I got post this...played a huge part in my career."
