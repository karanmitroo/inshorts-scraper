Late Bollywood actor Shammi Kapoor played a supporting role in the 1992 Tamil crime drama 'Amaran'. Kapoor, whose 85th birth anniversary was observed on October 21, 2016, made his Bollywood debut in 1953 with the movie 'Jeevan Jyoti'. The actor is best known for his work in movies like 'Junglee', 'Kashmir Ki Kali', and 'Dil Deke Dekho'.
