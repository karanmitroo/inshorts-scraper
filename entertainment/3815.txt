Actress Lara Dutta used husband and tennis player Mahesh Bhupathi's Grand Slam towels to stop rainwater from seeping into their home in Mumbai. "Putting our Wimbledon, US Open, Aus Open &French Open towels to good use," tweeted Lara, along with a picture. Mahesh, who has won 12 Grand Slam titles, replied, "Are u kidding me!!!! That's years of hard work."
