The Sidharth Malhotra, Alia Bhatt and Fawad Khan starrer film 'Kapoor & Sons', which hit the theatres on Friday, has minted ₹26.35 crore at the Indian box-office in its first weekend. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film collected ₹6.85 crore on Friday, ₹7.75 crore on Saturday and ₹11.75 crore on Sunday.
