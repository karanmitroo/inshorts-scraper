Filmmaker Anubhav Sinha has slammed those comparing the poster of Sanjay Dutt's 'Bhoomi' with that of the Hollywood film 'The Grey' starring Liam Neeson. "Bollywood is so full of some Bizarre, out of work, useless pests," wrote Anubhav on Facebook while criticising those who alleged that the poster of 'Bhoomi' was copied from the poster of 'The Grey'.
