After creating several controversies, 'MSG: The Messenger', which is produced, directed, written, acted and sung by Gurmeet Ram Rahim Singh, was finally released on Friday. India Today writes, the movie is nothing but a long format advertisement strictly for followers of the Dera Sacha Sauda chief. It has been rated 0/5 (koimoi.com), 1/5 (TOI) and 1.5/5 (India Today, Financial Express).
