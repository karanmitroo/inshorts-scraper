The producer of 'The Wolf of Wall Street' has settled a forfeiture lawsuit by the US alleging the movie was funded using money siphoned from Malaysian government fund 1MDB. The movie company, Red Granite Pictures, was co-founded by Malaysian Prime Minister Najib Razak's step-son. Notably, US has lodged multiple lawsuits related to the alleged misappropriation of $4.5 billion from 1MDB.
