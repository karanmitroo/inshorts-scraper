Actor Varun Dhawan Thursday revealed the first poster of his upcoming film 'Badlapur' on his Twitter account. The poster features two men, presumably- Varun Dhawan and Nawazuddin Siddiqui, standing across each other at a railway station with an empty railway track between them. Directed by Sriram Raghavan, the film also features Huma Qureshi, Yami Gautam and Vinay Pathak.
