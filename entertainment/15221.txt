Students of the Aligarh Muslim University (AMU) and the Millat Bedari Muhim Committee (MBMC) are protesting against the Manoj Bajpai-starrer film 'Aligarh'. While the MDMC has submitted a memorandum to the Information & Broadcasting Ministry seeking to change the movie title, AMU students action committee leaders are planning to lodge an FIR against director Hansal Mehta.
