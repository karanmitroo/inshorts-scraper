A new song from 'Secret Superstar' titled 'Sexy Baliye', which features actor Aamir Khan, has been released. The song has been composed by Amit Trivedi, with lyrics by Kausar Munir and vocals by Mika Singh. Zaira Wasim, who played Aamir's daughter in 'Dangal', stars as the female lead in the film which is scheduled to release on October 19.
