Actress Parineeti Chopra, in a pink gown by fashion designer Mark Bumgarner, was one of the celebrities who attended the 62nd Filmfare Awards held on Saturday. Alia Bhatt wore a dress by designer Prabal Gurung. Sonakshi Sinha chose an outfit from luxury fashion label Gauri & Nainika. Meanwhile, Sonam Kapoor was seen in a white outfit by Elie Saab.
