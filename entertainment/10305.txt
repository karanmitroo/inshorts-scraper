Actor Shah Rukh Khan thanked director Ashutosh Gowariker on Twitter for the 2004 drama film 'Swades', in which he played the lead role of Mohan Bhargava. "Whole cast/crew had such faith in goodness and Indianness," the 51-year-old actor further tweeted. Notably, 'Swades', which also featured Gayatri Joshi, released twelve years ago on December 17.
