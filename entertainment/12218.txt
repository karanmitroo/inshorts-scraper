Vogue India is launching its first television show 'Vogue BFFs', which will feature Deepika Padukone, Kareena Kapoor, Sonam Kapoor, Kajol, Anil Kapoor and Arjun Kapoor among others. The show pairs off the celebrities with their 'best friends'. For instance, Kareena appears with fashion designer Manish Malhotra for an episode. The talk show will air on September 24 on 'Colors Infinity'.
