The Viral Fever (TVF) CoCan Studio has released a new track called the "Monsoon Song" featuring 'Leakin' Park'. The song has been recreated by Vaibhav Bundhoo from the track 'In The End' by the American rock band 'Linkin Park'. TVF describes the track, which details the problems people face during monsoon, as "the only song that doesn't hype the season".
