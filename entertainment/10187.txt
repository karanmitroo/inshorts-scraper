Actor Salman Khan, with earnings of ₹270.33 crore, has topped the 2016 Forbes India Celebrity 100 List, beating actor Shah Rukh Khan. While SRK posted earnings of ₹221.75 crore, cricketer Virat Kohli, with earnings of ₹134.44 crore was ranked third on the list. The rankings of celebrities were based on two parameters- entertainment related earnings and estimates of their fame.
