Aamir Khan has become the most followed Indian on Twitter's Chinese clone, Weibo. His account, called 'Amier Han' in Mandarin, was created last month around the release of 'Dangal' in China and has over 6.5 lakh followers. Notably, he surpassed Prime Minister Narendra Modi, who created his account in 2015 ahead of his China visit and had 1.69 lakh followers.
