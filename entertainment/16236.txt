Megastar Amitabh Bachchan, his wife Jaya Bachchan and son Abhishek Bachchan will be the first beneficiaries of Uttar Pradesh's new pension scheme for people awarded the Yash Bharti Samman. Notably, the award is the highest state honour. Under the scheme, all the recipients of the award will be given a monthly pension of Rs 50,000 for life.
