'Mai Ri Mai', the first song from the Radhika Apte, Tannishtha Chatterjee and Surveen Chawla starrer 'Parched' has been released. The song has been composed by Hitesh Sonik and sung by Neeti Mohan and Harshdeep Kaur with lyrics penned by Swanand Kirkire. Directed by Leena Yadav and produced by Ajay Devgn, the film is scheduled to release on September 23.
