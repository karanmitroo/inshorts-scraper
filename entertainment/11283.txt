As per reports, actress Parineeti Chopra is set to star opposite Ajay Devgn in the upcoming film 'Golmaal Again', the fourth film in the 'Golmaal' franchise. The film's director Rohit Shetty is said to have offered the film to Shraddha Kapoor. However, he reportedly chose Parineeti as she readily agreed for the role. It will release during Diwali 2017.
