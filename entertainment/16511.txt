Actor Shahid Kapoor recently said at an event that Alia Bhatt, his co-star for his upcoming movie, "Shaandaar" was his inspiration for doing the film. "My inspiration for 'Shaandaar' is Alia. I wanted to work with Alia Bhatt and it was my only opportunity," he said. The 'Queen' fame Vikas Bahl directorial is set to release on October 22.
