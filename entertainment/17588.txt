Actor Anil Kapoor's first look from his upcoming film 'Dil Dhadakne Do', a Zoya Akhtar directorial, has been revealed. Producer Ritesh Sidhwani posted the look on Twitter with the caption, "Meet the Super cool Suave & Dashing Mr Mehra #DilDhadakneDo @AnilKapoor." The film also features Priyanka Chopra, Anushka Sharma, Ranveer Singh and Farhan Akhtar.
