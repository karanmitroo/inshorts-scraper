"Alia took me completely by surprise. She possesses both vulnerability and insouciance," said National award winning actress Shabana Aazmi, after watching her performance in Imtiaz Ali's 'Highway'. Moreover, Azmi rushed to the Bhatts' residence to congratulate the parents and their daughter Alia Bhatt for her extraordinary performance. Alia's father Mahesh Bhatt has compared her performance with Shabana's performance in 'Arth'.
