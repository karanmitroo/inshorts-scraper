American singer Beyoncé has been named the highest-paid woman in music in 2017 by Forbes. Her earnings, mainly from album 'Lemonade' and Formation World Tour, have been estimated to be $105 million (₹682 crore). Adele, with earnings of $69 million (₹448 crore) came second while Taylor Swift, with earnings of $44 million (₹286 crore), was ranked third on the list.
