Salman Khan-starrer films 'Prem Ratan Dhan Payo' (PRDP) and 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' have collectively minted ₹513 crore, making him the only Bollywood actor whose films collected over ₹500 crore in a year. While PRDP has earned ₹193.22 crores in 12 days in India, the total collections of 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' stood at ₹320.34 crores.
