Cricketer Virat Kohli and wife actress Anushka Sharma will not appear together on season 6 of Karan Johar's talk show 'Koffee With Karan', as confirmed by their spokesperson. Earlier, reports suggested that while Anushka and Virat were not keen on doing any talk show together, he was likely to appear on 'Koffee With Karan' to promote her film 'Pari'.
