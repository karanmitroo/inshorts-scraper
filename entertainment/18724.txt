First-time director Ajay Bhuyan's rom-com 'Amit Sahni Ki List' starring Vir Das, Vega Tamotia, Anindita Nayar and Kavi Shastri, has been rated 2 (Koimoi), 2.5 (Times of India, DNA, IBN Live) and 3.5 (NDTV). "Likeable in parts, Amit Sahni makes a decent watch for those who don't expect too much from their movies," quotes DNA.
