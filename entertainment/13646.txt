Actor Shah Rukh Khan on Monday tweeted a tribute to his 24 years in Bollywood with the names of 24 imaginary women, whose acronym he expanded into "life lessons". He tweeted lessons like "TINA. There is nothing abnormal", "ITI. Imagine the impossible" and "SKY. Smoking kills you". He also posted funny tweets like "SWATI. Superheroes wear acutely tight innerwear".
