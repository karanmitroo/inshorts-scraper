With an estimated earning of $33 million between June-2012 and June-2013, Angelina Jolie regained the top position after a 3-year break in Forbes' highest paid actress list, followed by Jennifer Lawrence ($26 million), Kristen Stewart ($22 million), Jennifer Aniston ($20 million) and  Emma Stone ($16 million). The list was compiled based on discussion with agents, managers and other in-the-know folks.
