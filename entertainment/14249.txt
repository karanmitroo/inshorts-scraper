Randeep Hooda and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan starrer 'Sarbjit', which released on Friday, has minted ₹3.69 crore on its opening day. Making the announcement on Twitter, Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh said, "#Sarbjit Fri ₹ 3.69 cr. India biz... Had better occupancy in evening/night shows." The Omung Kumar directorial, also starring Richa Chadha, is based on a real life story.
