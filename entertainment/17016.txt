The trailer of Nishikant Kamat directorial 'Drishyam', which is a remake of a 2013 Malayalam film starring Mohanlal, was released on Thursday. The thriller-drama, which features Ajay Devgn, Shriya Saran and Tabu in key roles, revolves around a common man whose family gets embroiled in a murder mystery. The film will hit theatres on July 31.
