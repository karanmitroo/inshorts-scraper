The official trailer of the Amitabh Bachchan starrer 'TE3N' was released on Thursday. Set in Kolkata, the emotional thriller stars Nawazuddin Siddiqui as a priest and Vidya Balan as a police officer. It revolves around the kidnapping of the granddaughter of John Biswas, the character played by Amitabh Bachchan. Directed by Ribhu Dasgupta, the film will release on June 10.
