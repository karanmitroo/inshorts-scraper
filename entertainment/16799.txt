Superstar Salman Khan starrer 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' that hit the screens on Friday has a perfectly paced story, said Hindustan Times. The Kabir Khan directorial works for both a Salman fan and a non-fan, wrote India Today. The film, also starring Kareena Kapoor and Nawazuddin Siddiqui, has been rated 3.5/5 (Mid-Day, India Today) and 2.5/5 (Indian Express, NDTV, HT).
