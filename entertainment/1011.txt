Farhan Akhtar posted a picture on Instagram to wish his father Javed Akhtar on his 73rd birthday on Wednesday while captioning the post, "Happy birthday pa...love you". "Wishing the master of wisdom & wit a very happy birthday...@Javedakhtarjadu sahab," tweeted Riteish Deshmukh. Meanwhile, Farah Khan tweeted, "Easily the wittiest, handsomest and most intelligent man...at least in our family."
