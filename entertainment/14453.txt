'Muchhi Re', the first song from filmmaker Ram Gopal Varma's upcoming film 'Veerappan' was released on Thursday. The song has been sung by Mohan Kanan with music by Jeet Gannguli. The film stars Sandeep Bhardwaj as Veerappan while Sachiin J Joshi, Usha Jadhav and Lisa Ray feature in pivotal roles. The film is scheduled to release on May 27.
