The trailer for the television mini-series 'Gilmore Girls: A Year In The Life' has been released. It is a follow-up to the series 'Gilmore Girls', which first premiered in 2000. Lauren Graham returns as 'Lorelai Gilmore' while Alexis Bledel will reprise her role as 'Rory Gilmore' in the four-part mini-series. It will premiere on Netflix on November 25.
