On the day Tusshar Kapoor and Sunny Leone starrer Bollywood movie 'Mastizaade' was released, it became the most trending Google search in India on Friday. It was most searched by Tripura, Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Chhattisgarh. R Madhavan starrer 'Saala Khadoos', which was released the same day, was the second most trending Google search.
