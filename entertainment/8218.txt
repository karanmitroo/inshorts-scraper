Actor Ryan Reynolds, who has produced and starred in the film 'Deadpool', has revealed that actress Zazie Beetz will portray the character 'Domino' in the film's sequel 'Deadpool 2'. Reynolds shared the image of an old Deadpool comic cover, where Zazie's face has been photoshopped. He earlier shared another image where Zazie's name had been spelt out using dominoes.
