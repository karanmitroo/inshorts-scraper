The teaser of Akshay Kumar and Nimrat Kaur starrer 'Airlift' was released on Wednesday. Directed by Raja Krishna Menon, the film is about the evacuation of Indians from Kuwait after Saddam Hussein's army invaded the country during the Gulf War of 1990. In the film, scheduled to release on January 22, Akshay plays Ranjit Katyal, an Indian-origin entrepreneur in Kuwait.
