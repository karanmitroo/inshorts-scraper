Amitabh Bachchan's daughter Shweta Bachchan Nanda and granddaughter Navya Naveli Nanda attended the pre-Diwali party hosted by fashion designers Abu Jani and Sandeep Khosla on Saturday. Both of them wore white outfits by the designer duo. Twinkle Khanna, Sonali Bendre and Shweta's brother Abhishek Bachchan were also a part of the party.
