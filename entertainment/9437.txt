Actor Jackie Chan sang the Hindi song 'Tu Meri' from Hrithik Roshan's 2014 film 'Bang Bang!', during the promotions of his upcoming Chinese-Indian co-production 'Kung Fu Yoga'. Disha Patani, who accompanied Chan, also sang along with him. She shared a video of Chan singing on Twitter, captioned, "Sharing and learning from each other, so much to learn from this man."
