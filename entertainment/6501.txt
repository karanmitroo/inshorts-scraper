Salman Khan recorded his debut Marathi song in 45 minutes, said music composer Vishal Mishra. He added, "I had the privilege to record it in his [Salman's] voice...It has now turned out to be the highlight of the film." The song 'Gachi', which is an adaptation of an old song, will feature in Mahesh Manjrekar's upcoming Marathi movie, 'FU-Friendship Unlimited'.
