The teaser of the Arvind Swamy-starrer film 'Dear Dad' has been released.  Directed by Tanuj Bhramar, the film also features Himanshu Sharma, Ekavali Khanna and Aman Uppal. The film revolves around a father-son duo, who go on a road trip from Delhi to Mussoorie, and how their trip takes unexpected turns. It is scheduled to release on May 6.
