Bollywood actor Nawazuddin Siddiqui and veteran actress Sridevi are set to work together in an upcoming film named ‘Mom’. Nawazuddin will have a “special appearance” in the film, his representative said. The film, which will be produced by Boney Kapoor, will reportedly be a women-centric movie. It will be directed by Ravi Udyawar and will go on floors next month.
