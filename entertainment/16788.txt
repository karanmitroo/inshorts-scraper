Salman Khan's 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' minted Rs 36.50 crore on Eid (Saturday), highest collection by a Salman Khan starrer in a single day. Earlier, his single day collection record was with 'Ek Tha Tiger' which collected Rs 32 crore. 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' has collected over Rs 63 crore at the box office in two days of its release.
