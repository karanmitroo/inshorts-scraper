As per a report by  Mid-Day, actors Farhan Akhtar and Kalki Koechlin are dating each other and are planning to move in together. The two reportedly became good friends while working on the 2011 film 'Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara'. While Farhan recently ended his 16-year-old marriage with Adhuna Bhabhani, Kalki had divorced her husband Anurag Kashyap in 2015.
