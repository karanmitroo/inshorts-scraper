According to reports, actors Sushant Singh Rajput and Bhumi Pednekar will star in filmmaker Abhishek Chaubey's upcoming film. Set in the 1970s, the film will reportedly revolve around Chambal dacoits. "Like Sushant, Bhumi too plays a dacoit... The film will be shot in the Chambal ravines and will roll early next year," a source was quoted as saying.
