As per reports, actor Ranbir Kapoor may replace Amitabh Bachchan as the host in the ninth season of television game show 'Kaun Banega Crorepati'. The show will mark Ranbir's television debut if he agrees to host the show. 'Kaun Banega Crorepati' has been hosted by Bachchan for seven seasons except in 2007, when it was hosted by Shah Rukh Khan.
