Actress Shilpa Shetty has announced the live game show 'Aunty Boli Lagao Boli: Sabse Kam Sabse Anokhi' as her first television production. Shilpa took to Twitter to share the poster of the game show which also featured actress Archana Puran Singh. Shilpa had earlier judged reality shows like 'Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa' and 'Nach Baliye'.
