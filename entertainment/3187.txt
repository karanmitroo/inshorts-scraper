A few pictures which have surfaced online show Kareena Kapoor and 'Permanent Roommates' actor Sumeet Vyas on the sets of their upcoming film 'Veere Di Wedding'. They were reportedly shooting in a bungalow at Central Delhi's Tolstoy Road. The film also stars actors Sonam Kapoor and Swara Bhasker and has been co-produced by Sonam's sister Rhea Kapoor.
