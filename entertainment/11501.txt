The second official trailer of the Ajay Devgn starrer upcoming action drama film 'Shivaay' was released on Monday. The film features Sayyeshaa Saigal and Erika Kaar in lead roles, along with British child actor Abigail Eames, who will play the role of Devgn's daughter. Directed and produced by Devgn, the film is scheduled to release on October 28.
