Actress Deepika Padukone was named the Most Stylish Female at the HT India's Most Stylish Awards 2018. She attended the award ceremony wearing a Benarasi saree, paired with gold jewellery. Shahid Kapoor got the Most Stylish Male award. Meanwhile, the Global Style Icon Award was given to actress Sonam Kapoor and Aishwarya Rai won the Timeless Style Diva Award.
