Kajol, who turned 43 on Saturday, has won the highest number of Filmfare awards in the Best Actress category. She has won the award for the films 'Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge' (1995), 'Kuch Kuch Hota Hai' (1998), 'Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham' (2001), 'Fanaa' (2006) and 'My Name is Khan' (2010). Interestingly, she shares the record with her maternal aunt Nutan.
