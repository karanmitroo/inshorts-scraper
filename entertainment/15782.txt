Content curation website 'Mode' has released a video, '100 Years of Fashion: Gals vs. Guys', exploring the evolution of fashion from 1915 to 2015. "Whether you like vintage dainty and dandy or prefer the wacky wonders of 1980s style, this gals-versus-guys recap is sure to stun," said the makers. The video is part of Mode's series, '100 Years of Fashion.'
