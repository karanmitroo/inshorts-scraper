Shatrughan Sinha was presented with the Lifetime Achievement Award at Filmfare Awards 2017. Filmmaker Subhash Ghai introduced the veteran actor and spoke about his career. The award, which is his first Filmfare, was presented to him by daughter Sonakshi Sinha. Further, Amitabh Bachchan, praising Sinha for his work, said, "Vishwa mein aise log bahut kam dekhne milte hain."
