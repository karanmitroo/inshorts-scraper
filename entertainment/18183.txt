Friday’s release ‘Bhopal: A Prayer For Rain’ is a historic-drama about the Bhopal gas tragedy that happened in 1984. "Filmmaker Ravi Kumar has captured everything that this drama needed," writes India Today. The film stars Martin Sheen, Kal Penn, Rajpal Yadav and Tannishtha Chatterjee, and has been rated 2.5/5 (Indian Express, HT), 3/5 (NDTV) and 3.5/5 (India Today).
