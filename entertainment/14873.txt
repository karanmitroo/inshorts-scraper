The official trailer of the Randeep Hooda starrer film 'Laal Rang' was released on Wednesday. The thriller film, directed by Syed Ahmed Afzal, also features  Akshay Oberoi and Piaa Bajpai in pivotal roles. It revolves around the thefts occurring in a blood bank in a town in Karnal. The film is scheduled to release on April 22.
