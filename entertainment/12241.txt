The Blake Lively starrer 'The Shallows' which released on Friday, "is visually appealing but predictable", wrote The New Indian Express (TNIE). Hindustan Times called the film "a magnificently crafted film, tightly paced, impeccably cut, with tense direction" while Times of India called it "the actress' most daring and challenging role to date". It was rated 3/5 (TNIE) and 3.5/5 (HT).
