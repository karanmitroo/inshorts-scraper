Bollywood superstar Aamir Khan recently said that there is no number game between him and actors Shah Rukh Khan and Salman Khan. "Trust me, among us, there isn’t any 1, 2, 3 number game. Salman and Shah Rukh are very big stars", Aamir said. He added, "[N]ot only I, but SRK, Salman, Akshay and Ajay (Devgn)… have been working successfully".
