'He Named Me Malala', the documentary film based on Nobel Peace Prize winner Malala Yousafzai, released on Friday. The Davis Guggenheim directorial, which offers a look into Malala's life before and after the Taliban attack, has been called "outstanding" by The Guardian. The film has been rated 5/5 (The Guardian), 3.5/5 (NDTV) and 3/5 (The Telegraph).
