A video shows the training of actresses Fatima Sana Shaikh, Sanya Malhotra, Zaira Wasim and Suhani Bhatnagar for their roles in 'Dangal'. They received training in wrestling from Indian Wrestling team's coach Kripa Shankar Bishnoi Beniwal. While Fatima plays Geeta Phogat, Zaira plays the same character's younger version and Sanya plays Babita Kumari while Suhani portrays Babita's younger version.
