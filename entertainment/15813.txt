On Bollywood actor Salman Khan's 50th birthday, #HBDMegastarSalmanKhan and #HappyBirthdaySalmanKhan were most trending on Twitter in India on Sunday. Aamir Khan tweeted, "Happy Birthday Salman and Happiness to family love".
