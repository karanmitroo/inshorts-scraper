Alia Bhatt and Varun Dhawan may return for a sequel to the 2014 film 'Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhaniya'. The actors along with Karan Johar tweeted about the possibility of the film's sequel, along with a picture. "I hear the 1st ever love franchise is in the air!" tweeted Varun, while Alia wrote, "The "dulhania" is on the move!!"
