E-commerce major Amazon has partnered with Warner Bros ahead of the release of the 'Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them' movie to add Harry Potter-related spells to the American website. By searching for 'reducto' on Amazon, the screen of the device will appear cracked. Other spells like 'incendio', 'orchideous', 'lumos', and 'aguamenti' also perform different actions on the platform.
