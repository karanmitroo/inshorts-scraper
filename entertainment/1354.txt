Indian spinner Harbhajan Singh took to Twitter to share a video of himself dancing with Bollywood actor Shah Rukh Khan at the wedding reception of Virat Kohli and Anushka Sharma in Mumbai. "Cheeku aur cheeki ki shaadi ne sabko khoob nachaya! @imVkohli  @AnushkaSharma.  @iamsrk  ur vibe and charm just makes everything so energetic! #BadshahKhan," Harbhajan captioned the video.
