Ajay Devgn and Parineeti Chopra-starrer 'Golmaal Again' has entered the ₹100-crore club by earning ₹103.64 crore within four days of its release, as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. The film, which is the fourth in the 'Golmaal' film franchise, is also the second highest Hindi opening day grosser of 2017 after the Hindi version of 'Baahubali 2'.
