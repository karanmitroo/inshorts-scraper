After actor Amitabh Bachchan denied any links to the offshore firms as revealed by "Panama Papers", #IAmWithAmitabhBachchan was a top Indian Twitter trend on Thursday. The actor tweeted, "Thank you all that support #IAmWithAmitabhBachchan ... it is so heartening and reassuring .. love to all". Users criticised the trend with tweets like, "people supporting corruption are promoting this hashtag".
