The first look of actress Richa Chadha in the film 'Sarabjit' has been released. Richa plays the wife of Sarabjit in the biopic of Sarabjit Singh, an Indian national convicted of terrorism and spying by a Pakistani court. Directed by Omung Kumar, the film features Randeep Hooda and Aishwarya Rai in lead roles. 'Sarabjit' will release on May 19.
