The first look of "Roy" starring Ranbir Kapoor, Arjun Rampal and Jacqueline Fernandez has been released. Ranbir, a thief in the movie, looks intense in one the posters while the second poster shows Rampal, playing a writer, in a pensive mood. The film, loosely based on Hollywood movie "Catch Me If You Can", is set to release on June-20, 2014
