Indian actor Irrfan Khan is set to star in Bangladeshi director Mostofa Sarwar Farooki's film 'No Bed Of Roses'. The film, which will be made in English and Bengali, will also be co-produced by Irrfan's production company 'IK'. It has been titled 'Doob' in Bengali. Irrfan will play male lead in the film opposite Bangladeshi actress Nusrat Imroz Tisha.
