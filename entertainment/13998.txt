Following District of Columbia's Deshauna Barber winning the Miss USA title, '#MissUSA' was trending on Twitter on Monday. Reacting to it, a user tweeted, "Attention: First Lieutenant Deshauna Barber is reporting for duty as #MissUSA 2016". "What a deserving woman. She's going from serving the country in combat boots to serving it with a crown! #MissUSA", read another tweet.
