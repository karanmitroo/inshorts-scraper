Maanayata Dutt on social media shared a picture of herself with husband Sanjay Dutt and twins Iqra and Shahraan sitting on a scooter. "Nothing compares to a simple ride on a scooter with family....exploring the lanes of Agra," read the caption alongside the picture. Dutt is currently shooting for 'Bhoomi', his first film after completing his jail term in Agra.
