An activist has sent a legal notice to Aamir Khan for naming his show “Satyamev Jayate” allegedly without central government’s permission. A part of the national emblem cannot be used without permission according to the State Emblem of India (Prohibition of Improper Use) Act 2009. Co-producer and Khan's wife Kiran Rao, and director Satyajit Bhatkal have also received the notice.
