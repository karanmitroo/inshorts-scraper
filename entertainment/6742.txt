The Aamir Khan, Fatima Sana Shaikh and Sanya Malhotra starrer Dangal has become the second highest grossing Indian film ever after it earned over ₹800 crore. The film surpassed Aamir's film PK, which was earlier the second highest grossing Indian film. Dangal collected ₹72.68 crore in its opening weekend in China, where it has released in around 7,000 screens.
