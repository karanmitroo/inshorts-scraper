Actor Irrfan Khan, when asked who he would like to kidnap, date and work with in Bollywood, said that he would kidnap Kangana Ranaut and date his 'Piku' co-star Deepika Padukone. He joked that he would kidnap Kangana to select roles and movies of his choices for her. "I would like to work with both of them," added Irrfan.
