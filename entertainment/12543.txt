The trailer for the Soha Ali Khan and Vir Das starrer '31st October' has been released. The film revolves around a Sikh family stuck in a riot-affected area in Delhi during the anti-Sikh riots after PM Indira Gandhi's assassination on 31st October, 1984. Directed by National Award winner Shivaji Lotan Patil, the film is scheduled to release on October 7.
