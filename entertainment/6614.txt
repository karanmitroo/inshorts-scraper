The Parineeti Chopra and Ayushmann Khurrana starrer Meri Pyaari Bindu which released on Friday, "is a light-hearted and charming love story with a twist-ending," wrote NDTV. Hindustan Times said, "The story lacks...passion...required for a love story to move the audience". Times of India wrote, "Chopra, Khurrana make for an electric couple." It was rated 2/5 (HT), 3/5 (NDTV, TOI).
