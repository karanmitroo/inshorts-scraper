While launching the trailer of his  upcoming film 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' along with actress Kareena Kapoor, Salman Khan disclosed that he was thrown out of Karan Johar's 'Shuddhi'. "I was thrown out of the film, why are you out of the film?" Salman asked Kareena. Earlier, Hrithik Roshan and Kareena Kapoor were roped in for the film but were later replaced.
