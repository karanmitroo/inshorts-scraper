The fourth season of BBC television series 'Sherlock' is set to premiere on January 1, 2017. The news was shared on the show's Facebook page, with a teaser captioned, "It's not a game anymore. #Sherlock Series 4 coming 1st January 2017." Actor Benedict Cumberbatch will reprise his role of detective 'Sherlock Holmes' while Martin Freeman will play 'Doctor John Watson'.
