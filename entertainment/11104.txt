US President-elect Donald Trump has featured in over ten Hollywood films, including 'Home Alone 2: Lost in New York' and 'Zoolander'. He has also appeared on various television shows including 'The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air', 'Sex and the City' and 'Saturday Night Live'. His first onscreen appearance was in 1985 in the sitcom 'The Jeffersons', where he played himself.
