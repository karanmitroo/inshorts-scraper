Aishwarya Rai Bachchan Sunday was felicitated at the Miss World 2014 event for her commitment to "beauty with a purpose". Aish won the beauty pageant in 1994 and is considered the most successful Miss World till date. The 41-yr-old was accompanied by husband Abhishek Bachchan, mother Vrinda and daughter Aaradhya on the stage to receive the felicitation.
