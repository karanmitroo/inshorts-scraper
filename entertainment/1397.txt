Indian cricket captain Virat Kohli and his wife actress Anushka Sharma held their wedding reception at The St. Regis in Mumbai on Tuesday. Sports personalities MS Dhoni, Virender Sehwag, Sunil Gavaskar, Saina Nehwal and Anil Kumble were spotted at the reception. Bollywood celebrities including Boman Irani, Aditya Roy Kapur, AR Rahman and Sara Ali Khan also attended the reception.
