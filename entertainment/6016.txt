Ranveer Singh, while sharing an incident about how Amitabh Bachchan disapproved of his outfit, said, "I went upto Mr Bachchan... asked him how I looked. He said, 'Son, you look like a plant.'" Ranveer revealed that he received the comment when he wore a patterned suit for a TV show shoot during the days of his 2014 film Kill Dil.
