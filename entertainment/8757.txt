Indian television actress Sweta Keswani will make her television debut in the USA with the show 'The Blacklist'. The series, which is currently in its fourth season, will feature her in the 15th episode titled 'The Apothecary'. Keswani is best known for her roles in Indian TV shows like 'Kahaani Ghar Ghar Kii', 'Des Mein Niklla Hoga Chand', among others.
