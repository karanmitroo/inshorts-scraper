Acclaimed film-maker Sanjay Leela Bhansali and celebrated writer-lyricist and ad-guru Prasoon Joshi were Sunday named for Padma Shri honour, for their contribution to art over the years. Bhansali is known to have connected with the audience with films like "Devdas", "Black" and "Ram-Leela". Prasoon Joshi is known for his heart-warming lyrics in songs like "Maa" from "Taare Zameen Par".
