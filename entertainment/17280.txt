Actress Katrina Kaif has denied the rumours that she would feature opposite John Abraham in 'Force 2', an Abhinay Deo directorial. There were reports that the 31-year-old will be playing the female lead in the film which is a sequel to the 2011 release film 'Force'. Genelia D'souza Deshmukh had featured as the female protagonist opposite John in 'Force'.
