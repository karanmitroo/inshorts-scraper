'Jabra Fan', the first song from the Shah Rukh Khan starrer film 'Fan' was released today at an event in Delhi's Hansraj College. The song, composed by Vishal and Shekhar and sung by Nakash Aziz, is the only song in the Maneesh Sharma-directorial. The film features SRK as a superstar named Aryan Khanna as well as his fan Gaurav Chanana.
