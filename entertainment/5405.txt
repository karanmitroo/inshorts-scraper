Actors Ranbir Kapoor and Katrina Kaif attended the South Indian International Movie Awards 2017 in Abu Dhabi, UAE to promote their upcoming film 'Jagga Jasoos'. Katrina wore a yellow sequined ball gown by Lebanese-Italian fashion designer Tony Ward, paired with stud earrings and a messy bun. Ranbir, who danced to 'Lungi Dance' on stage, was seen in a black suit.
