Google Doodle Sunday honoured Bollywood's "Showman" on his 90th birth anniversary. The Doodle depicts Raj Kapoor's famous character 'Raj' from the film 'Shree 420', which was released in 1955. The background of the Doodle shows the image from the movie's popular song 'Pyar huya ikraar hua'. Kapoor won 2 National Film Awards and 9 Filmfare Awards among others.
