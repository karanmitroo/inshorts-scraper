Bollywood actor Shah Rukh Khan has featured in a parody video of the song 'Gerua' from his recently released film 'Dilwale'. The spoof video is made by YouTube channel 'Shudh Desi Gaane'. In the video, Shah Rukh can be seen making fun of himself, his "age-old formula of romance" and his movies like 'Ra.one', 'Mohabbatein' and 'My Name Is Khan'.
