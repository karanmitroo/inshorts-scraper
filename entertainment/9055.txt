In her recent appearance on 'Koffee with Karan', when tennis player Sania Mirza was asked to select a co-star if she were to make her Bollywood debut, said she'd choose Salman Khan. Further, she named Salman as the "coolest" Khan while answering a question in the episode's 'Rapid Fire' segment. Notably, this was Sania's debut appearance on the show.
