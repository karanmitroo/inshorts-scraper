A teaser of Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra's upcoming film 'Mirzya' was released on Wednesday. With the film, actor Anil Kapoor's son, Harshvardhan Kapoor, will be making his acting debut. The film also marks the acting debut of Saiyami Kher, who is Tanvi Azmi's niece. 'Mirzya' is a love story based on the Rajasthani folk tale ‘The legend of Mirza– Sahiban’.
