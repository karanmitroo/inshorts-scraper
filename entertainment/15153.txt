A replica of the 'jhumka' (ear jewellery) which was worn by actress Sadhana in the song 'jhumka gira re Bareilly ke bazar mein' from the movie 'Mera Saya' will be preserved in Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh. According to the vice-chairman of the Bareilly Development Authority, the move will commemorate the song and dance. The actress had passed away in December 2015.
