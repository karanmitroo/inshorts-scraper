According to reports, filmmaker Rajkumar Hirani will play himself in the upcoming biopic on Sanjay Dutt. He will reportedly appear in a few scenes where Dutt, played by Ranbir Kapoor, is offered the 2003 film Munnabhai MBBS, which was directed by Hirani. "Raju sir making an appearance makes the movie so much more authentic," a source was quoted as saying.
