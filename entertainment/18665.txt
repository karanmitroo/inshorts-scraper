Within only two days of release, Salman Khan starrer 'Kick' has crossed the Rs 50 crore mark at Box Office. According to Koimoi, 'Kick' did a business of Rs 26 crores on Friday and Rs 27.10 crores on Saturday. The movie has surpassed Kabir Khan's 'Ek Tha Tiger', which had earned Rs 47.50 crores in 2 days.
