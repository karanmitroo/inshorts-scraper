Actress Mallika Sherawat on Wednesday walked the red carpet at the opening night of the 2016 Cannes Film Festival. Mallika, who was promoting her film 'Time Raiders', was wearing a Gorges Hobeika gown along with a Mellerio diamond neckpiece. Bollywood actresses including Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Sonam Kapoor will appear on the red carpet in the coming days.
