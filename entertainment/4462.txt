'Rocket Saiyaan', the first song from Ayushmann Khurrana and Bhumi Pednekar starrer 'Shubh Mangal Saavdhan' has been released. The song has been written and composed by Tanishk-Vayu. It has been sung by Ritu Pathak, Brijesh Shandilya and Tanishk Bagchi. The film, in which Ayushmann plays a character who has erectile dysfunction, is scheduled to release on September 1.
