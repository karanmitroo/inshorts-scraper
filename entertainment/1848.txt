Comedian Bharti Singh and television script writer Haarsh Limbachiyaa got married in a Hindu ceremony on Sunday in Goa, which was attended by their family members and close friends. Television celebrities like Jay Bhanushali, Anita Hassanandani, Rakhi Sawant, Sanaya Irani, Krushna Abhishek and Kashmira Shah were part of the various wedding ceremonies, which took place over three days.
