Actor Sushant Singh Rajput and actress Jacqueline Fernandez performed to the song 'Dil Mein Chhupa Loonga' from the film 'Wajah Tum Ho' at the Filmfare Awards 2017, which concluded on Saturday evening. "A sneak peek of the performance with the Fabulous @jacquelinef143. Aise na Mujhe Tum Dekho," wrote Sushant while sharing the video on Instagram.
