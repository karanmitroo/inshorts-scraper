'Jackie', biographical film on former US President John F Kennedy's wife Jacqueline Kennedy following his assassination, "gets a bit too forced in bits and pieces," wrote Hindustan Times. Indian Express wrote the film maintains "a reverential distance", while Times of India called it "a disturbingly intimate portrayal of Jacqueline." It was rated 3.5/5 (HT), 2.5/5 (Indian Express) and 4/5 (TOI).
