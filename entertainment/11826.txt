The Sushant Singh Rajput starrer biopic 'M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story' has been declared tax-free in Maharashtra. "We are humbled and thankful to Maharashtra Chief Minister Devendra Fadnavis and his team for making Mahi's biopic tax-free in the state," said producer Arun Pandey. The film was earlier declared tax-free in Dhoni's home state Jharkhand, as well as in Uttar Pradesh.
