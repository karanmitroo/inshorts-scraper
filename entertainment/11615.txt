The first episode of the tenth season of Salman Khan-hosted reality show 'Bigg Boss' was aired on Sunday. A total of 15 contestants, including commoners and celebrities, are participating in the show. The seven celebrities include television actors Gaurav Chopra, Rohan Mehra and Karan Mehra, model-actor Rahul Dev, Bhojpuri actress Antara Biswas, model Lopamudra Raut and TV presenter Gurbani Judge.
