Students of IIT-Roorkee have shared a video of their dance performance on Ed Sheeran's latest single 'Shape of You'. The video, which is themed on the idea of spending a "less lonely" Valentine's Day, has garnered over 11 lakh views on YouTube. Directed by Ankush Raut, it features Meet Saparia, Satyabrat Panda, Sambhav Jain, and Devarsh Tiwari from the college.
