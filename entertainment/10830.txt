Filmmaker Rajkumar Hirani has confirmed that Sonam Kapoor will feature in the upcoming biopic on Sanjay Dutt. It will be a special appearance and Sonam will portray the role of one of the actresses whom Dutt had dated in the 90's. Ranbir Kapoor will be portraying Sanjay Dutt in the film, which will also star Anushka Sharma as a journalist.
