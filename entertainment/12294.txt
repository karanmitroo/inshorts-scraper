Model-actor Milind Soman cycled from Guwahati to Nagaon in Assam, covering a total distance of 180 kilometres in a day. Soman had earlier completed the 'Ironman' triathlon that consists of a 3.8-kilometre swim, an 180.2-kilometre cycle ride, and 42.2-kilometre run in 15 hours in his first attempt. He is also the brand ambassador of 'Pinkathon', India’s biggest ‘women only’ marathon.
