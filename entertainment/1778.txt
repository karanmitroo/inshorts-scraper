Veteran actor Dharmendra, while sharing a picture of late actor Shashi Kapoor with his brother Raj Kapoor on social media, wrote, "You have been a childhood admiration." He further wrote, "A wonderful actor and a beautiful human being...this world is going to miss you Shashi!!" Dharmendra and Shashi have worked together in films like 'Krodhi' and 'Akhri Muqabla'.
