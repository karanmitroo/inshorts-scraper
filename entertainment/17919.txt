Filmmaker Pahlaj Nihalani was appointed the new chairperson of Censor Board on Monday for a period of three years and until further orders. Nihalani, who produced films like "Aankhen" and "Shola Aur Shabnam", replaced Leela Samson who resigned from the post of Censor Board chief on Saturday. Also, nine new members have been appointed in the Censor Board team.
