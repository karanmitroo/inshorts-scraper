Actor Paul Blackthorne, who portrayed 'Captain Andrew Russell' in the 2001 Aamir Khan-starrer epic sports-drama film 'Lagaan', will attend the Delhi Comic Con in December. Blackthorne will be present on all three days of the event, which will begin on December 9. Blackthorne currently stars in the American television series 'Arrow', in which he portrays detective 'Quentin Lance'.
