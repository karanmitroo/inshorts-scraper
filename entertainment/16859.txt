Actor Sunny Deol has been booked for abusing Hindu gods in his upcoming film 'Mohalla Assi', a Chandra Prakash Dwivedi directorial. A court in Muzaffarpur (Bihar) on Saturday directed the police to file an FIR against Deol and 8 others, including actors Sakshi Tanwar and Ravi Kishan, following a complaint filed by lawyer Sudheer Kumar Jha on June 30.
