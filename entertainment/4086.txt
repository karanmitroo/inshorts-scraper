Celebrities including Daler Mehndi, Vishal Dadlani, Riteish Deshmukh, Shraddha Kapoor, Shirish Kunder and Rajat Sharma took to Twitter to wish poet, lyricist and film director Gulzar on his 83rd birthday on Friday. "Gulzar-saab is to words what Michelangelo was to marble. Happy Birthday sir," wrote Vishal. Daler tweeted, "Birthday wishes to the man with interminable words and emotions!"
