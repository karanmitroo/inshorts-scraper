As per reports, 'Baahubali' director SS Rajamouli will be the advisor to Andhra Pradesh government for designing its new capital Amaravati. Reportedly, Chief Minister Chandrababu Naidu was impressed with 'Baahubali' sets and asked his team to contact Rajamouli. While Rajamouli has agreed to be a part of the project,  he will begin work after the completion of 'Baahubali 2'.
