Actor Nawazuddin Siddiqui starrer “Manjhi: The Mountain Man” hit the screens today. The movie is based on the life of Dashrath Manjhi, a poor labourer who carved a path through a 360 ft long, 30 ft wide and 25 ft high hillock using only a hammer and chisel. It has been rated 3 (NDTV), 3.5 (Zee News) and 4.5 (HT).
