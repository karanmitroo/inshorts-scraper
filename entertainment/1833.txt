Dairy cooperative Amul in its new poster has paid tribute to veteran actor Shashi Kapoor, who passed away on Monday. The poster was captioned, "Mere paas aapka cinema hai," and featured caricatures of his characters from films like 'Jab Jab Phool Khile' and 'Deewaar'. While sharing the poster, Amul's official Twitter handle wrote, "Tribute to an actor and a gentleman."
