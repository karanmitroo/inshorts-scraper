Actor Shah Rukh Khan has featured on the July edition of 'Femina' magazine alongside the winners of Miss India 2016, Priyadarshini Chatterjee, Sushruthi Krishna and Pankhuri Gidwani. He is seen in a white shirt, black suit and tie, surrounded by the Miss India 2016 winners. The cover story is titled 'India's Hottest Superstar King Khan with the Queens'.
