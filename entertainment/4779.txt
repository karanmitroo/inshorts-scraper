Actor Farhan Akhtar on Monday shared his first look from the upcoming film 'Lucknow Central' on Twitter. He captioned the picture, "Ye hai Kishan Mohan Girhotra...jail mein isse 1821 bulaate hain." Directed by Ranjit Tiwari, the film also stars Ronit Roy and Diana Penty and is scheduled to release on September 15.
