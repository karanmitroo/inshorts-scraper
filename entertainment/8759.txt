Actor-filmmaker Sohail Khan, while talking about his brother Salman Khan, said, "My father (Salim Khan) says that in India and also in the world there can only be one Salman Khan." Sohail added, "He (Salman) is a hardworking guy and we all wish that he continues his good run throughout." Sohail directed Salman in his directorial debut 'Auzaar' in 1997.
