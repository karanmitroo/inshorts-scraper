As per reports, filmmaker Sanjay Leela Bhansali's upcoming film 'Padmavati' is expected to cost ₹190 crore. While lead actress Deepika Padukone is said to be getting ₹12 crore, Ranveer Singh and Shahid Kapoor are reportedly charging around ₹8 crore each. Further, the sets and production cost for the film is expected to cost ₹130 crore to ₹140 crore.
