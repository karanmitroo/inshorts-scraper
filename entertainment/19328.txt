Actor Amitabh Bachchan (71) was bestowed with the Star of the Millennium award for his movie 'Satyagraha' at the fourth edition of Big Star Entertainment Awards function held on Wednesday. The event that was co-hosted by actor Sunil Grover and Shreyas Talpade also witnessed some scintillating performances by actors like Salman Khan, Kareena Kapoor and Sunny Leone.
