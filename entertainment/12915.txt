The trailer for the Riteish Deshmukh and Nargis Fakhri starrer 'Banjo' was released on Tuesday. In the musical drama, Riteish plays the character 'Taraat', a banjo player in a band in Mumbai while Nargis stars as Christina, a DJ from the US. Directed by Ravi Jadhav and produced by Krishika Lulla, the film is scheduled to release on September 23.
