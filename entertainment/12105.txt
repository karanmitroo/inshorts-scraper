Singer Abhijeet Bhattacharya has slammed filmmakers Karan Johar and Mahesh Bhatt and actor Shah Rukh Khan for working with Pakistani artistes, in the light of the recent terrorist attack in Uri. "I never said boycott... I said kick these Paki b******s... shame karanjohar MaheshNBhatt for... feeding them," tweeted Abhijeet. He further called them "anti-national" and "bigger enemies" than Pakistan.
