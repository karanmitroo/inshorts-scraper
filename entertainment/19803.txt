Activist Hemant Patil has filed a complaint in a local-court in Mumbai seeking action against actor Salman Khan for launching a website- www.Salmankhanfiles.Com, with information on the 2002 hit-and-run case involving him, alleging that it was contempt of court. "When the case is underway and matter sub-judice, how can he use Internet to spread information about the proceedings?" he said.
