Bowing down to fans' requests,  the producers Yash Raj films and Maratha Mandir theatre have mutually decided to continue the show of Shah Rukh and Kajol-starrer 'Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge'. The theatre had decided to stop the show, after a run of 1009 weeks, after which they extended it for another week. The news was confirmed by Yash Raj Films.
