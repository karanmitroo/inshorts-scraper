The teaser poster of Akshay Kumar's upcoming court drama film 'The State vs Jolly LLB 2' has been unveiled. Sequel to the National Award-winning 2013 film 'Jolly LLB', the film also features actors Huma Qureshi, Arshad Warsi and Saurabh Shukla. Directed by Subhash Kapoor, the film is scheduled to release on February 10, 2017.
