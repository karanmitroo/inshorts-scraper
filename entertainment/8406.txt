Actress Sonam Kapoor has featured on the Women's Day special issue cover of 'Femina' magazine. She can be seen in an outfit by Masaba Gupta. The magazine's cover has the message 'The Length Of My Skirt Does Not Define Me. It Shows Your Mindset'. The cover story on Sonam is titled '"I Was Molested at 13": Sonam Kapoor Supports #NotMyShame'.
