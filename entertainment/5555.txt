Actor Shah Rukh Khan tweeted, "Thanks for bearing me," on completing 25 years in Bollywood, as his debut film 'Deewana' released today in 1992. He played negative characters in 'Darr', 'Baazigar', and later gained popularity for romantic roles in 'Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge', 'Kuch Kuch Hota Hai'. He has won the Filmfare Award for Best Actor a record eight times.
