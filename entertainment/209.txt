As per reports, Kareena Kapoor Khan will star in the Hindi remake of the 2018 Marathi film 'Aapla Manus'. Kareena has reportedly been in talks with filmmaker Ashutosh Gowariker and will give her final approval after the co-stars and the script are finalised. 'Aapla Manus' was Ajay Devgn's first Marathi film as a producer.
