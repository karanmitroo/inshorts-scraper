Late actress Sridevi had once revealed that she was paid more than actor Rajinikanth for their roles in the 1976 Tamil film 'Moondru Mudichu'. She had said that her salary was ₹5,000, while Rajinikanth, who was a newcomer then, was paid ₹2000. Kamal Haasan, who featured reportedly in an extended guest appearance in the film, was paid ₹30,000.
