'Mitran De', a new song from the Arshad Warsi and Naseeruddin Shah starrer film 'Irada' has been released. It has been composed by Neeraj Shridhar, with vocals by Master Saleem, Kaur B and Earl Edgar, and lyrics penned by Sameer Anjaan. Also starring Sagarika Ghatge, Divya Dutta and Sharad Kelkar, the Aparnaa Singh directorial will release on February 17.
