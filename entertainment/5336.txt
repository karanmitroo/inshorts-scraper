President of Mumbai Regional Congress Committee Sanjay Nirupam has sent a letter to the CBFC chief, asking him to show them Madhur Bhandarkar's film 'Indu Sarkar' before it's censored. Nirupam wrote that since the film on Emergency revolves around senior Congress leaders like Indira Gandhi, Sanjay Gandhi, they want to ensure that their leaders are not shown in bad light.
