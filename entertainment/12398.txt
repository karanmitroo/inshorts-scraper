Actor Ajay Devgn has collaborated with UK-based teen band 'The Vamps' for his upcoming film 'Shivaay'. Devgn, who is directing and producing 'Shivaay', shared a behind-the-scenes video of the band with the film's music composer Mithoon working together on the soundtrack. The band has earlier worked with the music director duo Vishal-Shekher for the single 'Beliya'.
