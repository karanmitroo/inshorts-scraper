Actor Kamal Haasan in his upcoming trilingual film titled 'Sabash Kundu' in Hindi, will be seen reviving the witty character 'Balram Naidu' from his earlier work 'Dasavatharam'. The film also stars Saurabh Shukla in the Hindi version and is set to release additionally in Tamil and Telugu. The Tamil version will also star Shruti Haasan and veteran Telugu comedian Brahmanandam.
