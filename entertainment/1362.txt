'Lae Dooba', the first song from the Sidharth Malhotra, Manoj Bajpayee and Rakul Preet starrer 'Aiyaary', has been released. It has been sung by Sunidhi Chauhan, composed by Rochak Kohli with lyrics by Manoj Muntashir. The upcoming thriller has been directed by Neeraj Pandey and is scheduled to release on January 26, 2018.
