Following back-to-back hits- Rockstar, Barfi and Ye Jawaani Hai Deewani, Ranbir Kapoor has reportedly increased his fee to Rs 15 crore for Abhinav Kashyap's Besharam and Vicky Singh's Roy. At present, Ranbir also commands a percentage share of the profits his films make. Earlier, the actor had received Rs 8 crore for Barfi and Ye Jawaani Hai Deewani.
