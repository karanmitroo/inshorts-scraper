As per reports, Priyanka Chopra and Deepika Padukone will be getting paid ₹1.3 crore each for a four-minute performance at the IIFA Awards 2016. Both the actresses reportedly demanded ₹33 lakh per minute for their performance. Notably, Bollywood actresses are said to be usually paid around ₹70 lakh to ₹80 lakh for stage performances.
