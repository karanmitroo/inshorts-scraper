Actor Hrithik Roshan has said that he and Shah Rukh Khan will pop champagne and celebrate for each other when their upcoming films 'Kaabil' and 'Raees' will do well. "Hopefully both films will do well...[W]e completely understand 'Raees' was in the waiting for too long," added Hrithik. Both the films are scheduled to release on January 25, 2017.
