The poster of the Vidya Balan and Mangesh Desai starrer Marathi film 'Ekk Albela' was revealed on Friday. The film is a biopic on Bhagwan Dada, 'the first dancing superstar of Indian Cinema', played by Desai. Meanwhile, Balan in her debut Marathi film portrays the actress Geeta Bali. 'Ekk Albela' is scheduled to release on June 24.
