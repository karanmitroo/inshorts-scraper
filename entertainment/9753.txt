'Bawara Mann', a new song from the Akshay Kumar and Huma Qureshi starrer upcoming courtroom comedy drama film 'Jolly LLB 2' has been released. It has been composed by Chirrantan Bhatt with vocals by Jubin Nautiyal and Neeti Mohan. Junaid Wasi has penned the lyrics of the song. Directed by Subhash Kapoor, 'Jolly LLB 2' will release on February 10.
