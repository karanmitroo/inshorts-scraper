TV actor Sanjit Bedi, who was known for playing Dr. Omi on TV show 'Sanjeevani', died on Tuesday. Bedi was reportedly suffering from a brain virus and was in coma for sometime. He is survived by his wife Rekha Bhatia. Bedi had also appeared in various shows like ‘Jaane Kya Baat Huyi’, ‘Kya Hoga Nimmo Ka’ and ‘Kasautii Zindagii Kay'.
