Veteran Bollywood actress Waheeda Rehman turned 78 years old on Wednesday. Waheeda started her career with Telugu film 'Rojulu Marayi' in 1955, however, her Bollywood acting debut film was 'CID' (1956). She has won the National Award for best actress for her role as Reshma in 'Reshma Aur Shera' (1971). The government conferred her with the Padma Bhushan in 2011.
