Actress Shradhha Kapoor has sung a Kashmiri folk song titled 'Roshe Valle', along with Tabu, for her upcoming film 'Haider'. Shradhha had marked her debut as a singer with Teri Galliyan in her last film Ek Villain. Haider, which is Vishal Bhardwaj's directorial, is an adaptation of William Shakespeare's Hamlet, and stars Shahid Kapur in the lead.
