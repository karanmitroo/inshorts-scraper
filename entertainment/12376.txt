The Sidharth Malhotra and Katrina Kaif starrer 'Baar Baar Dekho', which released today falls "light years short of what the film could have been", wrote India Today. "[T]here is nothing over-the-top in this quietly accomplished film", said NDTV. Hindustan Times wrote that the film's "philosophy lacks strength". It has been rated 2/5(India Today), 4/5(NDTV) and 2.5/5(Hindustan Times).
