Actors Hrithik Roshan and Yami Gautam, who have been cast as lead actors in 'Kaabil', will be seen as a visionless couple in the film. "This is the first ever time where the lead actors are blind and fall in love", Yami said. To be directed by Sanjay Gupta, the film also features Ronit Roy, Rohit Roy and Sonu Sood.
