The first poster of Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra's upcoming directorial 'Merey Pyarey Prime Minister' has been released on the occasion of World Toilet Day on Sunday. The film revolves around the friendship of four kids living in a Mumbai slum. Mehra had earlier said that 'Merey Pyarey Prime Minister' is inspired from Mahatma Gandhi, who was a warrior on sanitation issues.
