The Tamil period drama 'Kochadaiiyaan', whose release date has been postponed repeatedly, will finally hit the screens May-9. The movie, which is touted to be India's first motion capture photo-realistic 3D animated film, has been directed by Soundarya Rajinikanth Ashwin and features an ensemble cast of Rajnikanth, Deepika Padukone, Jackie Shroff among several others.
