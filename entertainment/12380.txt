The Nawazuddin Siddiqui starrer 'Freaky Ali', which released today "drowns in its own silliness", wrote The Indian Express. It is Nawazuddin's "most experimental film yet", said India Today. Hindustan Times wrote, "Sohail Khan's... directorial venture brings out an unseen side of [Nawazuddin Siddiqui]". It has been rated 1.5/5(The Indian Express), 2/5(India Today) and 3/5(Hindustan Times).
