The release dates of actor Ranbir Kapoor's upcoming films 'Tamasha' and 'Jagga Jasoos' have been revealed. While 'Tamasha' will hit the theatres on Nov 27 this year, 'Jagga Jasoos' will be released on June 3 next year. "I am done with Tamasha. I have two days of shooting left. I still have lots of work on Jagga Jasoos...," Ranbir said.
