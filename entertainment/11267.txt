The Sonu Sood starrer historical adventure film 'Xuanzang' has been chosen by China as its official entry for the 89th Academy Awards (Oscars). The 43-year-old actor is making his debut in the Chinese film industry with the movie, where he portrays the character 'Harsha'. Directed by Huo Jianqi, the film also stars Indian actors Neha Sharma and Ali Fazal.
