Actor Sushant Singh Rajput will spend a month at US space agency NASA to prepare for his space film 'Chandamama Door Ke'. Speaking about his stay in NASA, Sushant said, "I'm playing an astronaut in the film. I'll spend almost a month...(in NASA) to understand an astronaut's body language and mindset." Actor Nawazuddin Siddiqui will also feature in the film.
