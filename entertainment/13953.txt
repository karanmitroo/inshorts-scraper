The Akshay Kumar starrer 'Housefull 3' has collected ₹80.10 crore in India in its first week, making it the second highest opening week grosser of 2016. The film surpassed the Shah Rukh Khan starrer 'Fan', which had earned ₹71.50 crore. Notably, Akshay's film 'Airlift' remains the highest opening week grosser of 2016 after earning ₹83.50 crore in its first week.
