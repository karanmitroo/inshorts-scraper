The first look of Farhan Akhtar's 'Rock On 2', the sequel to the 2008 film 'Rock On' has been revealed. Along with the original cast of the film, it also stars Shraddha Kapoor and Shashank Arora. The film's first official poster was also released on Friday. Directed by Shujaat Saudagar, 'Rock On 2' is scheduled to release on November 11.
