Radhika Apte-starrer short film 'Ahalya', a Sujoy Ghosh directorial, was released Monday on YouTube. The 14-minute film which has given a feminist spin to a story from the Ramayana will give you chills, said NDTV. As per Hindu mythology, Ahalya, the wife of the sage Gautama Maharishi, was seduced by Indra and cursed by her husband for infidelity.
