On the day Spain's national film awards were announced, #Goya2016 was a top global Twitter trend on Sunday. Actor Antonio Banderas shared a photo tweeting, "One year later, exciting memories of gratitude". Users appreciated the winners with one of the tweets reading, "Congratulations on your #Goya2016 win @pabloalboran! You're deserving of this and so much more! Next up @TheGRAMMYs".
