Shahid Kapoor has shared a picture from the sets of his next film 'Batti Gul Meter Chalu'. The picture was captioned, "Day 1. On set. Batti Gul Meter Chalu. Here we go. #goodpeople #goodvibes." The film is said to star Shraddha Kapoor opposite Shahid while Yami Gautam will reportedly play a lawyer. It is being directed by Shree Narayan Singh.
