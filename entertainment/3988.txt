Actor Ajay Devgn and his wife Kajol will be seen together in a film after 7 years. "Things didn't work out with (ad filmmaker) Raj Sarthi and now Pradeep Sarkar will be directing that film with me and Kajol in it," he added. Ajay and Kajol were last seen together in the 2010 film 'Toonpur Ka Super Hero'.
