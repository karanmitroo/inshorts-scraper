The Harshvardhan Kapoor and Saiyami Kher starrer 'Mirzya' "is all eye candy, minus the romance", wrote Indian Express. "In its own space... of a musical romantic tragedy, Mirzya is fabulous", wrote India Today. "It's a period drama trying hard to be a musical", said Hindustan Times. It has been rated 3/5 (India Today), 2.5/5 (Hindustan Times) and 1.5 (Indian Express).
