Television actress Shilpa Shinde, known for portraying 'Angoori Bhabhi', a popular character from the show 'Bhabi Ji Ghar Par Hai!', has appeared as the character in a new YouTube video. "For those wondering where Bhabhiji has gone missing, this show is an answer to that," said Shilpa. Earlier, she quit the television show alleging that the makers "mentally tortured" her.
