Bollywood actor Sushant Singh Rajput on Friday released a new image of himself on Twitter from upcoming film 'MS Dhoni: The Untold Story' wherein he will be portraying the role of the cricketer. "All you guys know who MS Dhoni is. Now let us tell you his untold story! #MSDhoniTheUntoldStory teaser out on March 15," the actor captioned the picture.
