Vinod Khanna, Suniel Shetty starrer ‘Koyelaanchal’ - a saga of the coal mafia of Dhanbad (Jharkhand) - released this friday. The movie, directed by Ashuu Trikha, has been rated, 1 (India Today), 1.5 (Times of India, Critic) and 3 (NDTV, Hindustan Times, Zee News). “Koyelaanchal is a film which is nothing less than torture,” says India Today.
