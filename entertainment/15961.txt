YouTube has released a list of top ten trending videos of 2015 based on “views, likes, shares, searches, parodies, remixes and responses". American rapper Silentó's debut single 'Watch Me (Whip/Nae Nae)' topped the list, followed by the official Super Bowl TV Commercial of the game 'Clash of Clans: Revenge'. The ten videos have collectively garnered over 500 million views.
