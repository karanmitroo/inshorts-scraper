Reacting to reports of a sequel of 'Andaz Apna Apna' being planned, actor Aamir Khan has said that he would "love" to star in it with Salman Khan. "We need a good script. It has to be a good film", he added. The 1994 comedy drama starred Aamir and Salman in the lead role, alongside Raveena Tandon and Karisma Kapoor.
