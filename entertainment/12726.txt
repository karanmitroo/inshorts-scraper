A new track titled 'Raaz Aankhein Teri' from the Emraan Hashmi starrer 'Raaz Reboot' has been released. The song has been composed by Jeet Gannguli and sung by Arijit Singh while the lyricist duo Rashmi Virag have penned the lyrics. Directed by Vikram Bhatt, the film also stars Kriti Kharbanda and Gaurav Arora and will release on September 16.
