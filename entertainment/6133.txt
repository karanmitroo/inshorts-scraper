Actors Rajinikanth and Kamal Haasan took to Twitter to grieve the demise of veteran Telugu director-actor Dasari Narayana Rao. Haasan tweeted, "My sympathy and condolences to the family of Daasari NaryaNa rao. His loss is truly a big loss for Telugu cinema." Dasari Narayana Rao passed away on Tuesday in Hyderabad at the age of 75 after prolonged illness.
