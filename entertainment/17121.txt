He posted, "KASHMIR bahut AMEER in natural beauty... Maashallah  Maashallah." Salman's another tweet read, "Maashallah Maashallah (a song from Ek-Tha-Tiger) se yaad aya katrina kaif bhi Kashmir se hai."
