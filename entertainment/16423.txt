The song 'Main Hoon Hero Tera', sung by Salman Khan, from the upcoming film 'Hero', has garnered 14 million views on YouTube within a month. The movie marks the debut of Aditya Pancholi's son Sooraj Pancholi and Sunil Shetty's daughter Athiya Shetty. Directed by Nikhil Advani and co-produced by Salman Khan, the film will release on September 11.
