The teaser of the first song 'Hug Me' from Sunny Leone's upcoming film 'Beiimaan Love' has been released. The song has been composed by Raghav Sachar, while it has been sung by Kanika Kapoor. Directed by Rajeev Chaudhari, the erotic thriller film features Sunny Leone and Rajneesh Duggall in lead roles. The film is scheduled to release on August 5.
