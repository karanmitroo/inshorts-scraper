The Viral Fever (TVF) has released a new video 'Barely Speaking with Arnub Dwitiya - Prologue', which features the fictional character 'Arnub with a U'. The video focuses on the return of Arnub, portrayed onscreen by TVF's Creative Director Biswapati Sarkar, as an anchor for the news channel 'Times Wow'. The web series is scheduled to air from July 24.
