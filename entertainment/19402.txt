"I wish to play Sachin on screen. If I get a good film, I will do it," said actor Aamir Khan(48), who had come to cheer for cricketer Sachin Tendulkar at Mumbai's Wankhede Stadium on Friday. Actor Hrithik Roshan(39) also joined Aamir saying, Sachin is the real superhero of human spirit. I'd love to play him on screen.
