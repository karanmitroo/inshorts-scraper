A new trailer of the Kangana Ranaut, Shahid Kapoor and Saif Ali Khan starrer film 'Rangoon' has been released. Set in the 1940s, Shahid will essay the role of soldier Nawab Malik while Kangana will be seen playing the role of actress Julia in the film. Saif will play Rusi Billimoria, Kangana's mentor in the film directed by Vishal Bhardwaj.
