Actor Arshad Warsi has said that 'Ishqiya 3' will "hopefully" be happening soon as director Abhishek Chaubey has reportedly found a script for the movie. The 48-year-old actor added that the film could happen sometime next year. The film will be the third film in the 'Ishqiya' franchise, after the 2010 film 'Ishqiya' and its 2014 sequel 'Dedh Ishqiya'.
