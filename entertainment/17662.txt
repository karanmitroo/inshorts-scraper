Actress Katrina Kaif, who will be soon seen with veteran actress Rekha in their upcoming film 'Fitoor', says she is completely "amazed" by Rekha's grace. "Every time I have met her, I have been amazed by her warmth, her grace and her immense enthusiasm towards life", Katrina said. Directed by Abhishek Kapoor, 'Fitoor' also features Aditya Roy Kapoor.
