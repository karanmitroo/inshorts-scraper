The body of Kannada actor Raghava Uday, who went missing after he jumped from a chopper into the Thippagondanahalli Reservoir during a movie shoot in Bengaluru, has been found. The stunt by Uday and another actor Anil was part of 'Masti Gudi', the film starring Duniya Vijay. The search operation is ongoing as Anil's body is yet to be found.
