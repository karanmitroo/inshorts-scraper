Bollywood star Ranveer Singh has won the FHM Bachelor of 2014 award, leaving behind big names like PM Narendra Modi, Indian cricketer Virat Kohli and Bollywood star Salman Khan. One of leading men's lifestyle magazine in India, FHM had also listed some other popular bachelors like Sidharth Malhotra, Kapil Sharma, Ranbir Kapoor, Hrithik Roshan and Karan Johar.
