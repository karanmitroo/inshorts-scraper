Amitabh Bachchan, while speaking at the launch of the teaser of late Shiv Sena founder Bal Thackeray's biopic, revealed that Thackeray had a photo of the actor next to his head on his deathbed. "I was stunned and that moment has been captured in my mind forever," he added. Amitabh further said Thackeray was like a father figure to him.
