Amitabh Bachchan on Friday crossed the mark of 23 million followers on social networking website Facebook. "On Facebook 23 MILLION followers!! BAAADUMMBAAA! 23!! THATS 2+3=5...5TH OF FEB...Abhishek's birthday!", he posted on Twitter. Notably, Amitabh is the second most followed Bollywood celebrity on Facebook after actor Salman Khan who has over 29 million followers.
