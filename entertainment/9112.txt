'Mannerless Majnu', a new song from the Taapsee Pannu and Amit Sadh starrer 'Runningshaadi.com' has been released. The song has been composed by Abhishek-Akshay, with vocals by Sukanya Purkayastha, and lyrics penned by Manoj Yadav. Directed by Amit Roy, the romantic comedy film is scheduled to release on February 17.
