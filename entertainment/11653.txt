Filmmaker Karan Johar took to Twitter to celebrate the completion of 18 years of 'Kuch Kuch Hota Hai'. "#18YearsOfKuchKuchHotaHai... the film that will always remain close to my heart," tweeted Johar. Replying to his tweet, Shah Rukh Khan wrote, "Of cool chains & friendship bands. Of love so uncomplicated all u had to say was kuch kuch hota hai."
