The release of actor Sushant Singh Rajput and Kriti Sanon starrer 'Raabta' has been shifted to  June 9, 2017. The film was earlier scheduled to release on February 10, 2017 which is also  the release date for the Akshay Kumar and Huma Qureshi starrer 'Jolly LLB 2'. 'Raabta' will mark the directorial debut of Dinesh Vijan.
