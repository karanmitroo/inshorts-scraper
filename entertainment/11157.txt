The Ranbir Kapoor, Anushka Sharma and Aishwarya Rai starrer 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' has entered the ₹100 crore club, making it Karan Johar's first directorial and Aishwarya's first Bollywood film to earn ₹100 crore. The film minted ₹102.05 crore in twelve days of its release. ADHM is Ranbir's third and Anushka's fourth film in the ₹100 crore club.
