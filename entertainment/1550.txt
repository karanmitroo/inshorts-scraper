National Award-winning child actor Naman Jain, known for acting in films like 'Chillar Party' and 'Raanjhanaa', will play the role of young Baba Ramdev in an upcoming biopic television series on the Yoga guru. Titled 'Swami Ramdev: Ek Sangharsh', the 65-episode series will air on Discovery's upcoming Hindi channel in India called 'Discovery Jeet'.
