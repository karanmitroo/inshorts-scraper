'Kahaani 2', a sequel to 2012 hit film 'Kahaani', will feature actor Arjun Rampal as the male lead opposite actress Vidya Balan. The film has gone on the floors and director Sujoy Ghosh confirmed the same on Twitter by sharing a picture of the clapperboard of 'Kahaani 2'. "[W]e will work even harder", he captioned the picture.
