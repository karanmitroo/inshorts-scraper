Hate Story 2 starring TV actor Jay Bhanushali and Punjabi film actress Surveen Chawla has received a heart warming welcome. The erotic-revenge drama has garnered Rs 5.46 crore on opening day. However, this weeks other releases Amit Sahni Ki List (Rs 68.6 lakh) and Pizza 3D (Rs 75.03 lakh) lack far behind.
