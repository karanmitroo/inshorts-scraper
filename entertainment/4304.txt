Filmmaker Imtiaz Ali, while talking about the feedback he received for his recent film 'Jab Harry Met Sejal' (JHMS), said, "Thanks for all the love...positive comments, thanks also for the criticism." Imtiaz added that he hopes the feedback helps him make better films for the audience in future. JHMS was Imtiaz's first film with Shah Rukh Khan and Anushka Sharma.
