Bollywood actress Parineeti Chopra is all set to star in Yash Raj Film's (YRF) next movie 'Meri Pyaari Bindu'. The film will feature actor Ayushmann Khurrana in the male lead. In a video uploaded by YRF recently on YouTube, Parineeti, who can be heard crooning a song, also announces that she will be singing a song in the film.
