A video shows Aishwarya Rai Bachchan's daughter Aaradhya Bachchan and Aamir Khan's son Azad Rao Khan dancing to the song 'Rail Gaadi' at the Annual Day event of their school. Aaradhya and Azad were part of a group performance at their school function. Aishwarya, Abhishek and Aamir were spotted sitting in the front row cheering for the children.
