Actor Shahid Kapoor has said that he is working with filmmaker Imtiaz Ali while adding that it's not 'Jab We Met 2'. He said this while responding to reports of Kareena and him working together with Imtiaz for 'Jab We Met 2'. Earlier, Shahid and Kareena have also worked together in films like 'Udta Punjab', 'Fida' and 'Chup Chup Ke'.
