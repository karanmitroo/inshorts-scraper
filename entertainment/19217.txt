Joining the likes of Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Sonam Kapoor and Freida Pinto, actress Katrina Kaif has become the new spokesperson of cosmetic brand L'Oreal Paris. "It is an incredible privilege to be joining this league of ladies. I'm looking forward to working with the L'Oreal Paris team in India," said Kaif, the fourth Indian to be the brand's face.
