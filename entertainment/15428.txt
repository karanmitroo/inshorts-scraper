Actor Matt Damon, who has been nominated in the Best Actor category at the 2016 Oscar Awards alongside Leonardo DiCaprio, has said that the latter may win the award. Leonardo, who was nominated four times at the Oscars previously, has not won an Academy Award till date. Leonardo's nominations include 'The Aviator' (2005), 'Blood Diamond' (2006) and 'The Revenant' (2016).
