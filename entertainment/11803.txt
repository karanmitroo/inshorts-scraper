A new trailer of the Kate Beckinsale starrer upcoming action horror film, 'Underworld: Blood Wars' has been released. The film is the fifth instalment in the 'Underworld' franchise. Beckinsale will reprise her role as 'Selene' in the film, which also stars Theo James and Lara Pulver. Directed by Anna Foerster, the film is scheduled to release on January 6, 2017.
