According to reports, actress Tabu will feature as herself in the upcoming biopic on actor Sanjay Dutt. Tabu, who had presented an award for the Best Actor in a Comic Role to Sanjay for 'Munna Bhai M.B.B.S.' in 2004, will reportedly feature as the award-presenter in the recreated scene for the film. Ranbir Kapoor stars as Sanjay in the biopic.
