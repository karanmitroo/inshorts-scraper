Actors Jacqueline Fernandez and Harshvardhan Kapoor have featured on the cover of the 3rd-anniversary issue of 'Harper's Bazaar Bride' magazine. Jacqueline is dressed in a white gown with ruffled flower details from Ralph & Russo. Harshvardhan can be seen wearing an unbuttoned shirt with a navy blue suit. The cover story on them is titled 'Cosying Up! Jacqueline & Harshvardhan'.
