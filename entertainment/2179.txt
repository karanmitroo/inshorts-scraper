Haryana BJP Chief Media Coordinator Suraj Pal Amu has announced a reward of ₹10 crore for beheading Sanjay Leela Bhansali and Deepika Padukone over 'Padmavati' row. He announced this while congratulating Meerut youth who earlier announced ₹5 crore bounty for beheading Bhansali and Deepika. Amu further threatened to cut Ranveer Singh's legs off over him supporting the film.
