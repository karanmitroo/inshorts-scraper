The trailer of the Shiv Pandit, Dhruv Ganesh and Siddharth Menon starrer romantic drama film 'Loev' has been released. Written and directed by Sudhanshu Saria, the film deals with male homosexual relationships. 'Loev' will premiere at the upcoming Mumbai Academy of the Moving Image (MAMI) Film Festival, which will be held from October 20-27.
