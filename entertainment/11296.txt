The trailer of the Ewan McGregor starrer upcoming black comedy crime drama film 'T2: Trainspotting' has been released. The film is based on Irvine Welsh's novel 'Porno' and also stars Ewen Bremner, Jonny Lee Miller, and Robert Carlyle. Directed by Danny Boyle, it is scheduled to release in the United Kingdom on January 27, 2017.
