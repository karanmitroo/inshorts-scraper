Actor Suniel Shetty has revealed that though he couldn't be a part of the upcoming film 'Sarkar 3', he and filmmaker Ram Gopal Varma plan to work together soon. "My character did not turn out the way we expected," the 55-year-old actor tweeted. Starring Amitabh Bachchan and Jackie Shroff, 'Sarkar 3' is the third installment in the 'Sarkar' film franchise.
