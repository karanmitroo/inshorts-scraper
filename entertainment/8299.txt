Bengali folk singer Kalikaprasad Bhattacharjee, known for his band 'Dohar', passed away aged 56 in a road accident in Hooghly, West Bengal on Tuesday. Bhattacharjee was travelling with members of his band, when their car was hit by a truck from behind and fell into the river. Bhattacharjee was known for popularising folk songs of rural Bengal through his albums.
