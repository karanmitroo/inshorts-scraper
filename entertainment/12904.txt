The trailer of the Amitabh Bachchan starrer 'Pink' was released on Tuesday. The film is a courtroom drama with Amitabh portraying the role of a lawyer, who suffers from bipolar disorder and experiences frequent mood swings. Also starring Taapsee Pannu, Kirti Kulhari and Andrea Taring, the film has been directed by Aniruddha Roy Chowdhury and will release on September 16.
