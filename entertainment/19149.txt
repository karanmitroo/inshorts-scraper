Director Ali Abbas Zafar’s action drama ‘Gunday’ starring Ranveer Singh, Arjun Kapoor, Priyanka Chopra and Irrfan Khan, released in theatres on Friday. The movie has been rated 1.5 (Indian Express), 2 (NDTV, IBNLive, DNA), 3 (Anupama Chopra for Hinduatan Times) and 3.5 (India Today, Times of India).
