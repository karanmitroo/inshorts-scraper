Actress Anushka Sharma has rapped in a new song titled 'Naughty Billo' from the upcoming film 'Phillauri'. The song has been composed by Shashwat Sachdev, with vocals by Diljit Dosanjh, Nakash Aziz and Shilpi Paul. Anvita Dutt has penned the lyrics of the song. Directed by Anshai Lal, 'Phillauri' is scheduled to release on March 24.
