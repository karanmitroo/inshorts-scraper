The teaser for the Hrithik Roshan and Yami Gautam starrer 'Kaabil', which has the tagline 'The Mind Sees All', has been released. The teaser features Hrithik's voice as he describes a state where despite understanding everything, one is unable to explain it to others. Directed by Sanjay Gupta, the film is scheduled to release on January 26, 2017.
