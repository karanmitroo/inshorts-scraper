Kareena Kapoor, when asked to choose the better actress between Deepika Padukone and Katrina Kaif, said that she will "go with Deepika". "There's no comparison between them as actresses," added Kareena. Further, when asked to choose between Alia Bhatt and Shraddha Kapoor, Kareena said, "Both are doing such great work... but since most people are saying Alia, I'll pick Shraddha".
