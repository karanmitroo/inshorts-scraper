A video shows actress Sonam Kapoor and actor Arjun Kapoor dancing to 'Tune Maari Entriyaan' at their cousin Akshay Marwah's wedding in Abu Dhabi, UAE. The song is from Arjun's 2014 film 'Gunday'. Their cousin Mohit Marwah, who has starred in 'Fugly', was also present with them on stage. Sridevi along with her daughters also attended the wedding.
