Aditi Rao Hydari, who was named the face of the Vogue Wedding Show, called the event "an ode to the most beautiful wedding". Designers like Sabyasachi, Manish Malhotra, Tarun Tahiliani, Anita Dongre, Gaurav Gupta and Rahul Mishra showcased their couture collections. The event included sessions with stylist Anaita Shroff Adajania, make-up specialist Namrata Soni and celebrity hairstylist Rod Anker.
