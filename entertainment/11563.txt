The teaser of the upcoming superhero film 'Logan' starring Hugh Jackman has been released.  It is a sequel to 2009's 'X-Men Origins: Wolverine' and 2013's 'The Wolverine'. Jackman will be seen reprising his role as the mutant Wolverine in the film. Directed by James Mangold, 'Logan' is scheduled to release on March 3, 2017.
