Bollywood actor Salman Khan on Sunday turned 50 years old. Born to screenwriter Salim Khan and his first wife Sushila Charak (a.k.a. Salma Khan), Salman's full name is Abdul Rashid Salim Salman Khan. He began his acting career with  'Maine Pyar Kiya' (1989). Honoured with the Rajiv Gandhi Award in 2008, Salman is the most googled Bollywood actor in 2015.
