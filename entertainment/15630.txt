Actor-turned-politician Shatrughan Sinha, in his biography 'Anything But Khamosh: The Shatrughan Sinha Biography' by Bharathi S Pradhan, has talked about his extra-marital affairs. On the sidelines of the Kolkata Literary Festival, Sinha said, "The book has the summary of my life... the girls who came into my life, ‘gharwali’ (wife) came and then ‘baharwali’."
