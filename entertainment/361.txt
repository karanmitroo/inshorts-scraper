Chadwick Boseman starrer 'Black Panther' has earned over ₹2,331 crore ($361 million) in its opening weekend worldwide. It also received the fifth-biggest weekend opening of all time in North America by minting over ₹1,239 crore while it earned ₹19.35 crore in India. The Ryan Coogler directorial also became the highest opening weekend grosser for an African-American director in North America.
