Director Kabir Khan has confirmed that Salman Khan will star in his upcoming film 'Tubelight'. “The film is on a territory that Salman and I have not ventured yet. So, we are excited about it," said Kabir. 'Tubelight' will be Salman’s third film with Kabir after the 2015 film 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' and the 2012 film 'Ek Tha Tiger'.
