The official trailer of the Tom Hanks and Irrfan Khan starrer 'Inferno' was released on Thursday. The film is based on Dan Brown's 2013 novel by the same name and has Hanks reprising his role as Robert Langdon, while Irrfan portrays the character 'Harry "The Provost" Sims'. Also starring Felicity Jones, the film will release on October 14 in India.
