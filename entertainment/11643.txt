Actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan has been named the Most Glamorous Star (Female) at the Absolut Elyx Filmfare Glamour & Style Awards. Actor Varun Dhawan received the award for the Most Glamorous Star (Male). Other awardees include actress Katrina Kaif who was named the Most Stylish Star (Female) and Priyanka Chopra who received the Global Icon of the Year (Female) award.
