A recreated version of the song 'Dil Kya Kare', which originally featured in 1975 film 'Julie', from the film 'Kaabil' has been released. Titled 'Kisi Se Pyaar Ho Jaye', the new version has been recreated by Gourov-Roshin, with vocals by Jubin Nautiyal. The original song was composed by Rajesh Roshan and sung by Kishore Kumar with lyrics by Anand Bakshi.
