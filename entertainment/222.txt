A photo gallery shows 50 years of late actress Sridevi's film journey, who passed away aged 54. She started her career as a child artiste in South Indian films like 'Thunaivan' and made her Bollywood debut as a lead actress in Solvaa Saawan (1979). After taking a 15-year break, Sridevi returned to Bollywood with the 2012 film 'English Vinglish'.
