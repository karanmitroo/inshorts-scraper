Rajkummar Rao has shared a new poster of his upcoming film 'Bareilly Ki Barfi' in which he can be seen in a saree. He captioned the picture, "Chirag hai pyar ki khoj mei, Bitti apni hi soch mei aur hmare sehme se Pritam Vidrohi in a saree bina petticoat ke". The film also stars Ayushmann Khurrana and Kriti Sanon.
