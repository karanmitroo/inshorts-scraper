Arbaaz Khan, director of the two instalments of the Salman Khan-starrer 'Dabangg' film series, has revealed that shooting for 'Dabangg 3' will begin in 2017. "I cannot say the exact time, but next year for sure", he said. Earlier, Salman had revealed that the third film in the series will be a prequel, showing "how Chulbul Pandey becomes Chulbul Pandey".
