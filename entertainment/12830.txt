Filmmaker Michael Bay has shared the first look of 'Bumblebee' from the upcoming film 'Transformers: The Last Knight' on Twitter. Earlier, Bay also revealed Megatron's look. The film is the fifth installment in the 'Transformers' franchise and the sequel to the 2014 film 'Transformers: Age of Extinction'. Starring Mark Wahlberg and Josh Duhamel, it will release on June 23, 2017.
