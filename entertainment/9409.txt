A video has emerged showing ex-Pakistan President Pervez Musharraf dancing to popular Hindi film song ‘Dilli wali girlfriend’ from the Ranbir Kapoor and Deepika Padukone-starrer 'Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani'. There was no information available of when and where the video was shot, but it was posted by a Pakistani journalist on Twitter. The video was shared over a thousand times.
