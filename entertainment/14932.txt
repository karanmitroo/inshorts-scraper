Filmmaker Bejoy Nambiar on Thursday compared actor Jesse Eisenberg’s role of villain Lex Luthor in 'Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice' to the negative characters portrayed by Shah Rukh Khan previously. “Did anyone else think Jesse Eisenberg’s Lex Luthor was Shah Rukh from ‘Darr’ and ‘Baazigar’", Nambiar, best known for films including 'Shaitan', 'David' and Wazir', tweeted.
