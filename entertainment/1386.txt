Actress Richa Chadha's look from her upcoming film 'Daasdev' has been unveiled. The film, which also stars Aditi Rao Hydari and Rahul Bhat, is a take on Sarat Chandra Chattopadhyay's novel 'Devdas'. Directed by Sudhir Mishra, who earlier made films like 'Hazaaron Khwaishein Aisi' and 'Yeh Saali Zindagi', 'Daasdev' is scheduled to release on February 16, 2018.
