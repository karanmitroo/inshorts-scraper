The music video for 'Hota Hai', the new song from the Harshvardhan Kapoor and Saiyami Kher starrer 'Mirzya', was released on Friday. The song has been composed by the music director trio Shankar, Ehsaan and Loy while it has been sung by Nooran Sisters, Sain Zahoor, Akhtar Chinnal and Daler Mehndi. Further, Gulzar has penned the lyrics for the song.
