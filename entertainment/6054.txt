Actor Vivek Oberoi has said that he wants actor Salman Khan's upcoming film 'Tubelight' to break the records set by SS Rajamouli directorial 'Baahubali 2' and become a superhit film. "I feel 'Tubelight' has all the capacity to make Hindi cinema proud all around the world," added Vivek. Notably, 'Tubelight' is scheduled to release on Eid this year.
