While congratulating cricketer Virat Kohli and actress Anushka Sharma on their wedding, batsman Rohit Sharma tweeted, "Anushka Sharma, keep the surname". Rohit's tweet also read, "Virat, I'll share the husband handbook with you." Sabyasachi Mukherjee, who designed the couple's wedding outfits, had referred to the actress as "Anushka Sharma Kohli" in an Instagram post.
