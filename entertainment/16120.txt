East India Comedy uploaded a video on YouTube where 'Ultimate Diwali carols' were sung. With tunes like "We wish you a merry bonus," "Jingle Sales" among others, the video offers a funny take on how Indians celebrate Diwali and had almost 75,000 views since its upload on Monday. The video also appealed to "stop causing pollution" during this festive season.
