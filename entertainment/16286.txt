Actress Amy Jackson, who debuted in Bollywood with 'Ekk Deewana Tha', has bagged the role of the female lead in 'Robot 2' opposite Rajinikanth. Aishwarya Rai Bachchan had played the part in the prequel which also had Rajinikanth in the male lead. 'Robot 2' is scheduled to release next year. Jackson was last seen in recently released 'Singh is Bliing'.
