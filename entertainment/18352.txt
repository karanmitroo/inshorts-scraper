Bollywood actress Katrina Kaif is the next personality to get waxed at Madame Tussauds, London, joining her contemporaries Kareena Kapoor, Madhuri Dixit and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, who already have their wax statues installed at the museum. Katrina was selected as she received the maximum votes in a poll conducted to decide who would be the next celebrity to be waxed.
