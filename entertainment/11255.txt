'Udja Re,' a new song from the Farhan Akhtar, Arjun Rampal and Shraddha Kapoor starrer upcoming musical drama film 'Rock On 2' has been released. The song has been composed by music composer trio Shankar, Ehsaan and Loy with vocals by Shraddha and lyrics by Javed Akhtar. Directed by Shujaat Saudagar, 'Rock On 2' will release on November 11.
