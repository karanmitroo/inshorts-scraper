Bollywood comic actor Razak Khan passed away on Wednesday after suffering from a heart attack. He was rushed to Holy Family hospital in Bandra, where doctors declared him dead on arrival. Razak, who started his acting career with the 1993 film 'Roop Ki Rani Choron Ka Raja', appeared in over 90 films including 'Raja Hindustani', 'Baadshah', 'Partner' and 'Action Jackson'.
