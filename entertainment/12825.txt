Actress Priyanka Chopra has been named Hollywood's 'It Girl' for the year 2015 by US-based women's fashion magazine 'Harper's Bazaar'. The 34-year old actress was included in the list for the success of her American television series 'Quantico'. The list includes other Hollywood names like Audrey Hepburn (1961), Meryl Streep (1979), Julia Roberts (1990) and Jennifer Lawrence (2011) among others.
