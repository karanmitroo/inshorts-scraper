Following the launch of Prime Minister Narendra Modi's '#Sandesh2Soldiers' campaign, actor Akshay Kumar has shared a video of himself wishing Indian soldiers on Diwali. "It's only because of our soldiers that we're able to celebrate Diwali with our families," he added. Actors Salman Khan and Aamir Khan also lauded PM's initiative, and urged people to send Diwali messages to soldiers.
