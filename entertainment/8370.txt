The Mark Wahlberg starrer 'Patriots Day', which released on Friday, pays "tribute to the victims of the Boston Bombing in the anti-Donald Trump movie America deserves right now", wrote Hindustan Times. Mid-Day wrote, "This film is really about reinstalling hope", while Times of India called the film "intense and thoughtful". It was rated 3.5/5 (HT, TOI) and 3/5 (Mid-Day).
