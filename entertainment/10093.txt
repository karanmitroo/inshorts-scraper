Salman Khan has played a character named 'Prem' in 15 films in his career. The films include 'Maine Pyar Kiya', his first Bollywood film in a leading role, 'Hum Aapke Hain Koun..!', 'Judwaa', 'Partner', 'Ready' and 'Prem Ratan Dhan Payo' among others. The actor, who turned 51 on Tuesday, made his Bollywood debut with 1988 film 'Biwi Ho To Aisi'.
