According to reports, actress Jacqueline Fernandez has started shooting for 'Race 3' with the song 'Allah Duhai Hai', a track repeated in instalments of the Race franchise. With Salman Khan in the lead role, 'Race 3' also stars actors Bobby Deol, Anil Kapoor and Daisy Shah. Directed by Remo D'Souza, the film is scheduled to release on Eid.
