The first song of Rohit Shetty directorial Singham Returns, Kuch Toh Hua Hai, in which we see Ajay Devgn romance Kareena Kapoor, has been revealed. The song is sung by Ankit Tiwari and Tulsi Kumar. Ankit Tiwari is also the music director and the lyrics have been done by Sandeep Nath and Abhendra Kumar Upadhay.
