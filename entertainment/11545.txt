A group of four girls called the 'Raga' have performed the Bharatnatyam dance to an Indian classical rendition of the theme song of television series 'Friends'. The group consists of Raksha Rai, Akkshitha Suresh, Gayathri Sivadas and Akshaya Balan. The classical version of the song has been created by Mahesh Raghavan, a music composer from the University of Edinburgh.
