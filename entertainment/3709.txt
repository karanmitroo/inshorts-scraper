Actor Rajkummar Rao took to Twitter to share a picture with the co-actors of his upcoming black comedy 'Newton', which he captioned, "With my most wonderful co actors from Newton." Rajkummar further used the hashtags #RealPeople and #RealIndia to describe them. Directed by Amit V Masurkar, the film revolves around a government clerk on election duty in a Naxal-controlled town.
