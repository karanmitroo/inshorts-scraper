Actor Rishi Kapoor took to Twitter to share a picture of his father Raj Kapoor on his death anniversary on Friday. In the caption, he wrote a line from the song 'Jeena Yahan Marna Yahaan' from Kapoor's 1970 film 'Mera Naam Joker'. Known for films like 'Shree 420' and 'Awara', Kapoor passed away due to asthma related complications aged 63.
