Actor Shah Rukh Khan on Twitter shared a photograph of actress Sunny Leone from his upcoming film Raees' song 'Laila Main Laila'. "...the ebullient @SunnyLeone... bringing back a blast from the past #Lailaaaraheehai", Shah Rukh captioned the picture. 'Raees', which will release on January 25, will also feature Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Pakistani actress Mahira Khan.
