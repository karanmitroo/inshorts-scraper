'Wazir', starring Amitabh Bachchan and Farhan Akhtar, will hit the screens on December 4 this year, film producer Vidhu Vinod Chopra said on Tuesday. The film was earlier speculated to release on October 2. The Bejoy Nambiar directorial, also featuring Aditi Rao Hydari, Neil Nitin Mukesh and John Abraham, is a thriller-drama where Amitabh is playing an ailing wheelchair-bound chess-master.
