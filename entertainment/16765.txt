Bollywood film 'Humshakals', a Sajid Khan directorial, has been featured among the list of "films considered the worst in the world" on Wikipedia. Notably, Humshakals' cast had also termed the film as their 'worst experience' post release. Other Indian films that feature on the page are Ram Gopal Verma's "Aag" (2007) and Debaloy Dey's "Machhli Jal Ki Rani Hai" (2014).
