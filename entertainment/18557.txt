Ashutosh Gowariker's 'Mohenjo Daro', starring Hrithik Roshan and introducing Pooja Hedge, will hit theatres on Jan 22, just before Republic Day in 2016. The film will be a romance set in the backdrop of the ancient Indus Valley civilisation. Jointly produced by Disney India and Ashutosh Gowariker Production, the film is set to go on floors in October 2014.
