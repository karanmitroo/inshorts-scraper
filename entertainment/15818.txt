As per data from piracy-tracking firm Excipio, Christopher Nolan's 'Interstellar' was the most pirated film of 2015, with over 46 million illegal downloads. It was followed by Vin Diesel and Paul Walker-starrer 'Furious 7' which had over 44 million illegal downloads. Robert Downey Jr-starrer 'Avengers: Age of Ultron' was third on the list with over 41 million illegal downloads.
