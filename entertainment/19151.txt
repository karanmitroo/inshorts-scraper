‘12 Years a Slave’ won the best movie award from British Academy of Film and Television Arts (BAFTA) presented Sunday. ‘Gravity’ won the maximum awards (total - 6), alongwith the best British film award. Alfonso Cuaron was named best director (‘Gravity’), while Chiwetel Ejiofor and Cate Blanchett were chosen the best actor (‘12 Years a Slave’) and actress (‘Blue Jasmine’).
