Actors Shraddha Kapoor and Aditya Roy Kapur have featured on the cover of 'Filmfare' magazine for its January issue. Shraddha is seen wearing a printed fit and flare dress while Aditya is wearing a white shirt and velvet jacket, accessorised with a bow tie. The cover story on them has been captioned, 'Lovers Reunite: Aditya Roy Kapur and Shraddha Kapoor'.
