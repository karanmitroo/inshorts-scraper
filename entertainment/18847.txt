The theatrical trailer of Shraddha Kapoor, Siddharth Malhotra and Riteish Deshmukh starrer 'Ek Villain' was released on Tuesday. Produced by Balaji Motion Pictures and directed by 'Aashiqui 2' director Mohit Suri, the film is a love story of a villain. 'Ek Villain' hits cinema screens on June 27, 2014.
