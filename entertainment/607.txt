Reacting to Shahid Kapoor's remark that he felt like an outsider on the sets of 'Padmaavat', Ranveer Singh said, "I did my best to...welcome (him)...and...be a supportive co-actor." "I think it is to do with the genre as well. If I'm not mistaken, it's his first costume drama. A costume drama has its own unique set of challenges," Ranveer added.
