Ramesh Sippy’s 1975 film ‘Sholay’, one of the biggest blockbusters, re-released in theatres Friday, converted from 2D into 3D. The movie, which ran continuously for five years in Mumbai's Minerva cinema, stars Dharmendra, Amitabh Bachchan, Hema Malini, Jaya Bhaduri, Sanjeev Kumar and Amjad Khan. The movie has been rated 2.5 (Bollywood Life), 4 (NDTV) and 5 (India Today, Bollywood Hungama).
