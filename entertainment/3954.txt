Arjun Kapoor, while sharing an old picture with his cousins, wrote, "Dude, like who are these people?" The picture featured his cousins Sonam Kapoor, Rhea Kapoor, Harshvardhan Kapoor, Shanaya Kapoor and his sister Anshula Kapoor. Earlier in an interview, Arjun had said, "Sonam is my favourite cousin... I am very protective about her and will always be in my life."
