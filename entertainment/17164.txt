Shraddha Kapoor, who has given her voice to the songs in 'Ek Villain' and 'Haider' in the past, is set to sing an unplugged version of 'Bezubaan' for her upcoming film 'ABCD-2'. Reportedly, her film's co-star Varun Dhawan will also be doing a rap score in the song. Dhawan has also sung a song in 'Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhaniya' before this.
