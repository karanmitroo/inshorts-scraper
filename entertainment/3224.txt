On the occasion of Ranbir Kapoor's 35th birthday on Thursday, filmmaker Karan Johar announced that Dharma Productions will collaborate with Ranbir again, making this the actor's fourth venture with the production house. Karan revealed that unlike the speculations, the film is not called 'Dragon'. Ranbir's other films with Dharma Productions include 'Wake Up Sid' and 'Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani'.
