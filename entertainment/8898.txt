The Akshay Kumar and Huma Qureshi starrer 'Jolly LLB 2' has earned ₹13.2 crore on its opening day. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film is expected to garner a "lucrative total" over the weekend. Also starring Annu Kapoor, the courtroom drama film is said to have been made on a budget of ₹30 crore.
