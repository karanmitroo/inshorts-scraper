The 8th anniversary mega issue of 'GQ' magazine features the 'Men of the Year' Awards 2016 winners. Actor Amitabh Bachchan is on the cover as 'Legend of The Year' while Ranveer Singh has featured as 'Actor of The Year' and Kangana Ranaut features as 'Woman of The Year'. Other cover stars include awardees like Saif Ali Khan and Tiger Shroff.
