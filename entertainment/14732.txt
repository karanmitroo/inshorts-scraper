The trailer of the upcoming horror thriller film 'The Neon Demon' has been released. Featuring Elle Fanning, Alessandro Nivola and Keanu Reeves, the film revolves around an aspiring model Jesse (Fanning), who moves to Los Angeles. Directed by Nicolas Winding Refn, the film will compete for  the 2016 Palme d'Or, the highest prize awarded at the Cannes Film Festival.
