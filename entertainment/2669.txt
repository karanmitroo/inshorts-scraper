Actress Tapsee Pannu took to Twitter to share a picture with Amitabh Bachchan, who worked with her in 'Pink' and wrote, "This man is pure addiction!" "Can never have enough of sharing screen space with him," she added. Taapsee will be appearing on the show 'Kaun Banega Crorepati 9', which is hosted by Amitabh Bachchan.
