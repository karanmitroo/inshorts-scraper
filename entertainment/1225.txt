As per reports, actresses Parineeti Chopra, Disha Patani and Kiara Advani will star in the fourth film in the 'Housefull' franchise. It has further been reported that Akshay Kumar, Riteish Deshmukh and Sanjay Dutt will star in the film. John Abraham is also said to be returning to the franchise after the 2012 film 'Housefull 2'.
