'Kya Tujhe Ab Ye Dil Bataye', the new song from the Pulkit Samrat and Yami Gautam starrer film 'Sanam Re' was released on Saturday. The song has been composed by Amaal Mallik, while it has been sung by Falak Shabir. 'Sanam Re', also featuring Rishi Kapoor and Urvashi Rautela, is scheduled to release on February 12.
