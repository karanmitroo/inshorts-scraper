The teaser for the Shashank Udapurkar starrer 'Anna', a biopic on social activist Anna Hazare, has been released. "[I]t is a mammoth task to present 75 years of his life in a two and half hour film," said Shashank, who has also written and directed the film. Also starring Tanishaa Mukerji and Govind Namdeo, it will release on October 14.
