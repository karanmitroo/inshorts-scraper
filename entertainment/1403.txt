According to reports, Aishwarya Rai will play a woman with multiple personality disorder in the remake of the Nargis starrer 1967 film 'Raat Aur Din'. Producer Prernaa Arora said, "I had spoken to Sanjay Dutt sir about it. I'm in talks with her...it's my wish that she does it." Nargis won the National Award for Best Actress for the film.
