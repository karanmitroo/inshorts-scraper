Sound designer Resul Pookutty, who won the Oscar award for Best Sound Mixing for 'Slumdog Millionaire', will make his acting debut in a Tamil film which will revolve around the Pooram festival held in Thrissur, Kerala. Pookutty will be seen playing himself in the film. Directed by Prasad Prabhakar, the film will also be made in Malayalam, Telugu and Hindi.
