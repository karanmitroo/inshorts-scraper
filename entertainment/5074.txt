Singer Beyoncé topped Billboard's Top 50 Money Makers of 2016 list with earnings of over ₹400 crore ($62.1 million). Rock band Guns N' Roses came in second on the list with earnings of over ₹272 crore while Drake, Adele and Coldplay were among those featured in the list's top ten. Late singer David Bowie ranked 34th on the list.
