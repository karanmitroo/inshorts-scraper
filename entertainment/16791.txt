Megastar Amitabh Bachchan has reportedly been approached by director Omung Kumar for his next film 'Sarabjit', a biopic of Sarabjit Singh. Bachchan has been offered the role of the Pakistani lawyer who fought Sarabjit Singh's case. The film features Aishwarya Rai as Dalbir Kaur, who struggled to get her brother Sarabjit out of Pakistani jail, and Randeep Hooda as Sarabjit.
