Model-actress Lisa Haydon on Friday married boyfriend Dino Lalvani after dating him for a year. Lisa wore a white gown while Lalvani teamed a blue shirt with a white blazer for the beach wedding. The 30-year-old actress shared a photograph from her wedding on Instagram, captioned, "Just Married". Lalvani is the son of a Pakistan-born British entrepreneur Gullu Lalvani.
