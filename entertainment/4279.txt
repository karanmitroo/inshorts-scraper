Sidharth Malhotra will be rapping for the song 'Bandook Meri Laila' from the upcoming film 'A Gentleman- Sundar, Susheel, Risky'. It will be sung by Ash King with Raftaar rapping along with Sidharth. Jigar Sarvaiya of music composer duo Sachin-Jigar said, "Sidharth has a great baritone which is rare in actors. When we asked him to rap...he immediately said yes."
