Singer Sunidhi Chauhan, who turned 34 on Monday, made her debut as a singer in Bollywood at the age of 13 in a song in the 1996 film 'Shastra'. Sunidhi has won three Filmfare Awards for songs including 'Sheila Ki Jawani' and the track 'Beedi' from Omkara. She made her acting debut in the short film 'Playing Priya' in 2016.
