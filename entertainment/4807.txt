A new trailer of the superhero film 'Justice League' was released at San Diego Comic-Con in USA. Based on the DC Comics superhero team, the trailer features Ben Affleck as 'Batman', Gal Gadot as 'Wonder Woman', Ezra Miller as 'The Flash', Ray Fisher as 'Cyborg' and Jason Momoa as 'Aquaman'. Directed by Zack Snyder, it will release on November 17.
