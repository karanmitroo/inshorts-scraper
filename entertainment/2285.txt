Veteran actress Shyama, known for her films like 'Aar Paar' and 'Barsaat Ki Raat' passed away on Tuesday at the age of 82. Shyama, whose original name is Khurshid Akhtar, also starred in films such as 'Sawan Bhadi', 'Milan' and 'Tarana'. For her role in 'Sharada', Shyama won the Filmfare Award in the Best Supporting Actress category.
