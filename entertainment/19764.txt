Rohit Shetty's Chennai Express starring SRK and Deepika Padukone, which was slated for release on Thursday, August-8 will now roll out a day later, on Friday, August-9. The movie was planned to release on August-8 keeping the 'Eid' in mind, but now since 'Eid' is on August-9, which also happens to be a holiday, the release date has been rescheduled.
