As per reports, actress Sonam Kapoor will portray the role of Madhuri Dixit in Rajkumar Hirani's upcoming biopic on Sanjay Dutt. Sanjay and Madhuri were rumoured to be dating in the 90's. Earlier, Hirani had confirmed that Sonam will have a special appearance in the film. Actor Ranbir Kapoor will be seen portraying Sanjay Dutt in the biopic.
