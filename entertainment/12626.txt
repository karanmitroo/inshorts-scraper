West Indies cricketer and singer Dwayne Bravo is set to lend his voice to a song in Bollywood director Anubhav Sinha's upcoming film 'Tum Bin 2'. The song, called 'Jager Bomb', will be composed by musician Ankit Tiwari, who will sing alongside Bravo. The West Indian will also feature in the music video, which will be shot in September.
