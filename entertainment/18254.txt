After grossing Rs 350 crore worldwide, Shah Rukh Khan-starrer 'Happy New Year' has now made it to the Oscars. Library of the academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences has officially selected the film's screenplay for its permanent Core Collection. Directed by Farah Khan, the movie also stars Deepika Padukone, Abhishek Bachchan, Sonu Sood, Boman Irani and Vivaan Shah.
