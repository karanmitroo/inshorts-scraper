'Suicide Squad' was ranked first in the list of 'Top 10 Movies of 2016' by IMDb, an online database for films, television programs and video games. The top five films on the list were all superhero films with 'Captain America: Civil War' ranking second while 'Deadpool' stood fourth. Other films in the list included 'The Jungle Book', 'Ghostbusters' and 'Warcraft'.
