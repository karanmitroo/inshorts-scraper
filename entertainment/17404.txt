Impressed by and excited about Kalki Koechlin's upcoming film, Aamir Khan along with wife Kiran Rao has decided to host a special screening of "Margarita, With a Straw", in Mumbai on April-8, said a spokesperson for the film. Rajkumar Hirani, Ayan Mukherji, Karan Johar, Alia Bhatt, Kangana Ranaut and Javed Akhtar are expected to attend the screening.
