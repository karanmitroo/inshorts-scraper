Filmmaker Zack Snyder has shared a behind-the-scenes video of the upcoming superhero film 'Justice League'. The video shows Ben Affleck, who plays 'Batman', filming the scene when he goes in search of 'Aquaman', portrayed by Jason Momoa. It also features 'Wonder Woman', played by Gal Gadot, Ezra Miller as 'The Flash' and 'Cyborg', portrayed by Ray Fisher.
