YRF Entertainment, the international arm of Indian production giant Yash Raj Films, on Friday announced that it will remake the critically acclaimed and commercially successful Sujoy Ghosh-directed and Vidya Balan-starrer 'Kahaani' for international audiences as 'Deity'. The remake will be directed by Niels Arden Oplev of 'The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo' fame and will be shot in Kolkata.
