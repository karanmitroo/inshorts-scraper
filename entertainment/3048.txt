Ryan Gosling and Harrison Ford starrer 'Blade Runner 2049' is "one of the greatest sequels ever made," wrote Hindustan Times. "It's an intelligent...piece of big budget cinema," wrote Firstpost. "If you liked [the original film], you simply cannot afford to miss 'Blade Runner 2049'," said The Times of India (TOI). It was rated 4/5 (Firstpost, TOI) and 5/5 (HT).
