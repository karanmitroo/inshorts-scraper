The teaser of Amitabh Bachchan and Rishi Kapoor starrer '102 Not Out' has been released. The film will feature the two actors together onscreen after 27 years with Bachchan portraying a 102-year-old father to a 75-year-old son, played by Rishi. Directed by 'Oh My God' director Umesh Shukla, the film is scheduled to release on May 4.
