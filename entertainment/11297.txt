Actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan has featured on the cover of the November issue of women's fashion magazine 'Harper's Bazaar' India. The actress is seen wearing a white gown by designer Manish Malhotra and jewellery by BG Jewellers on the cover, along with the caption, 'Isn't she lovely: Aishwarya Rai Bachchan'. Notably, the actress celebrated her 43rd birthday on November 1.
