National Award-winning actress Suchitra Sen, who had been in hospital since Dec-23, died at 82 on Friday in Kolkata. Sen symbolized the golden age of Bengali cinema with memorable films like 'Agnipariksha', 'Devdas' and 'Saat Paake Bandha'. Called Bengal's Greta Garbo, she was the first Indian actress to be internationally awarded the Best Actress at Moscow film festival in 1963.
