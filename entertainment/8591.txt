The Best Costume Design nominees at Oscars 2017 include costume designer Madeline Fontaine for the biographical film 'Jackie' and Mary Zophres for the musical 'La La Land'. Three-time Oscar-winning costume designer Colleen Atwood has been nominated for 'Fantastic Beasts and Where To Find Them'. The other nominees are Joanna Johnston for 'Allied' and Consolata Boyle for 'Florence Foster Jenkins'.
