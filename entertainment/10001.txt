As per reports, actor Abhishek Bachchan rejected the casting of his wife Aishwarya Rai in his upcoming film 'Lefty' as he wanted a fresh, young face to play the female lead. He reportedly considered Aishwarya too mature for the role. The movie is being directed by Prabhudheva and is being co-produced by Abhishek and his friend Bunty Walia.
