Newbie Sakshi Chaudhary, who will be first seen opposite John Abraham in the next instalment of ‘Hera Pheri,’ has been finalized to play Priyanka Chopra in the upcoming film ‘67 Days’. The movie, directed by Aseem Merchant (Priyanka’s alleged boyfriend during her modelling days), is based on the actress' infamous fallout with her former secretary Prakash Jaju over finances.
