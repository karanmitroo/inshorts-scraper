A duet version of the song Maana Ke Hum Yaar Nahin from the film Meri Pyaari Bindu, featuring the vocals of Sonu Nigam and Parineeti Chopra, has been released. The song has been composed by the music director duo Sachin-Jigar with lyrics by Kausar Munir. Meri Pyaari Bindu is scheduled to release on May 12.
