"Bhaag Milkha Bhaag", directed and produced by Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra and based on the life of Indian athlete Milkha Singh, starring Farhan Akhtar and Sonam Kapoor, hits the big screen today. It will be the first film to donate all proceeds to India's needy athletes. Movies "Shorts", "Sixteen" and Hollywood sci-fi "Pacific Rim" are  also releasing today.
