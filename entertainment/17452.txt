The sequel of the hit 2010 movie 'Raajneeti', a Prakash Jha directorial, will feature actress Katrina Kaif. However, actor Ranbir Kapoor who starred in the prequel opposite Katrina Kaif will not be seen in 'Raajneeti 2'. According to reports, the sequel will revolve around the journey of Indu, played by Katrina, and the recent power shift in the country.
