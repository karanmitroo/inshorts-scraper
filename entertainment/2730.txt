Salman Khan's upcoming film 'Bharat', his third film with filmmaker Ali Abbas Zafar, will release on Eid 2019. The film is an official adaptation of 2014 South Korean film 'Ode to My Father'. Talking about the project, producer Atul Agnihotri said, "It's the journey of a country and also a person, both of whom go by the name of Bharat."
