A new promotional video of Anushka Sharma starrer 'Pari' has been released. "She will grow on you...#1MonthToPari," wrote Anushka while sharing the teaser on Instagram. Also starring Parambrata Chatterjee, Rajat Kapoor and Ritabhari Chakraborty, 'Pari' will mark the directorial debut of Prosit Roy in feature films. The film is scheduled to release on March 2.
