The recreated version of the song 'Gazab Ka Hai Din' from Taapsee Pannu and Saqib Saleem starrer 'Dil Juunglee' has been released. The new version has been composed by Tanishk Bagchi, with vocals by Jubin Nautiyal and Prakriti Kakar. Directed by Aleya Sen, 'Dil Juunglee' is scheduled to release on February 16.
