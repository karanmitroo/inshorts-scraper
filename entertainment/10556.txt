Singer Beyoncé leads with nine nominations for her album 'Lemonade' in the 59th Annual Grammy Awards for 2017. Meanwhile, Drake, Rihanna and Kanye West received eight nominations each. Beyoncé's 'Lemonade', Adele's '25', Drake's 'Views', Justin Bieber's 'Purpose' and country singer Sturgill Simpson's 'A Sailor's Guide to Earth' will be competing for the album of the year, a top Grammy award.
