Actress Vidya Balan will make a special appearance as a cop in Ribhu Dasgupta's upcoming film 'Te3n', the actress revealed on Saturday. Produced by Sujoy Ghosh, the film stars Amitabh Bachchan and Nawazuddin Siddiqui in lead roles. "I was excited because it’s a fabulous script… it’s a chance to shoot again in Kolkata", Vidya said.
