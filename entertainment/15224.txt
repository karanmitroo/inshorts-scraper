Leonardo DiCaprio-starrer 'The Revenant' which released in India today "has a mature and committed performance" by the actor, wrote HT. "This Man vs Wild survival drama works as a pure, visceral experience," said The Hindu. "The visual canvas of this film is the work of perfectionists," said TOI. The film has been rated 4.5/5 (TOI) and 4/5 (HT, Koimoi).
