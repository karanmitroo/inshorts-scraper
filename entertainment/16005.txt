'Manma Emotion Jaage', the second song from Rohit Shetty's 'Dilwale', was released on Thursday. The fast paced song, featuring Varun Dhawan and Kriti Sanon, has been composed by Pritam. It has been sung by Amit Mishra, Anushka Manchanda and Antara Mitra. Featuring Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol in lead roles, 'Dilwale' is scheduled to release on December 18.
