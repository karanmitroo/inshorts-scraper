Aamir Khan launched the shoot of Shah Rukh Khan's 2004 film 'Swades' by giving the mahurat clap. Sharing pictures from the sets of 'Swades', which completed 13 years of release on Sunday, director Ashutosh Gowariker wrote, "Thank you for your immense LOVE." In the pictures, Aamir can be seen sporting his look from the 2005 film 'Mangal Pandey: The Rising'.
