In connection to Shri Rajput Karni Sena's demand to change the name of TV-show 'Jodha Akbar,' Ekta Kapoor said, we are ready to change the name from 'Jodha Akbar' to 'Akbar'. We are also ready to change the characters' name. It was only after this, the movie OUATIMD got released in Rajasthan on Friday, a day after its India release.
