Megastar Amitabh Bachchan (70) will be honoured with the Global Diversity Award in Delhi. The actor has appeared in over 180-films and earlier was honoured with Padma Shri (1984), the Padma Bhushan (2001) and Frances highest civilian honour, the Knight of the Legion in 2007, by the French government for his exceptional career in the world of cinema and beyond.
