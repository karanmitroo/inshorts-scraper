Aamir Khan will be rapping the song 'Dhaakad' at an event, which will be hosted by Shah Rukh Khan. "[W]e have shot it (Dhaakad) specially for that event and not as part of the Dangal OST. I really enjoyed Dhaakad and will be singing it in its entirety," said Aamir. The event is said to be scheduled for December 17.
