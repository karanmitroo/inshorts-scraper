An accident on the sets of the Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh starrer 'Padmavati' has resulted in a labourer's death. Reportedly, the deceased, Mukesh Dakia, died after falling on his head from a height of five feet while painting. The police is investigating the incident. Directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali, 'Padmavati' also features actor Shahid Kapoor.
