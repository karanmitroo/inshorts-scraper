Veteran actor Amitabh Bachchan has been named 'Timeless Fashion Icon' at Filmfare Glamour Style and Fashion Awards. The 72-year-old actor took to Twitter to inform about the same. Actress Alia Bhatt won Emerging Face of Fashion (Female) and Sidharth Malhotra was named the Emerging Face of Fashion (Male) at the award ceremony, which was held in Mumbai on Thursday.
