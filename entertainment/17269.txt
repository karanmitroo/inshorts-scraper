After starring together in 'Khoobsurat', Sonam Kapoor and Pakistani actor Fawad Khan will be seen in 'Battle for Bittora'. Shooting for the film, to be produced by Sonam's sister Rhea Kapoor, will reportedly begin in June this year. The film, an adaptation of Anuja Chauhan's novel with the same name, is about two ambitious political families pitted against each other.
