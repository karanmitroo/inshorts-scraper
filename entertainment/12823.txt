Actress Katrina Kaif has shared a video on Facebook featuring Sidharth Malhotra, Alia Bhatt, Aditya Roy Kapur and Parineeti Chopra. The stars, along with Varun Dhawan, Karan Johar and Badshah, will perform live as part of 'Dream Tour', a multi-city event in the US. "This is how the dream team prepares at 30,000 feet. See u all soon," wrote Katrina.
