The Tiger Shroff and Jacqueline Fernandez starrer 'A Flying Jatt' has earned ‎₹29.45 crore in its extended four-day long opening weekend. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film earned ₹7.10 crore on Thursday, ₹6 crore on Friday, ₹7.35 crore on Saturday and ₹9 crore on Sunday. The film has become the tenth highest opening weekend grosser of 2016.
