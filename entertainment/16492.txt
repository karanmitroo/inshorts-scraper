Actors Hrithik Roshan and Sonam Kapoor have featured in the reprised version of 1990's popular romantic song 'Dheere Dheere Se' from the movie Aashiqui. The song has been recreated by rapper Yo Yo Honey Singh and directed by choreographer Ahmed Khan. The original song, sung by Kumar Sanu and Anuradha Paudwal, was composed by Nadeem Saifi and Shravan Rathod.
