The trailer for 'Anthropoid', which stars '50 Shades of Grey' actor Jamie Dornan, was released on Friday. The film revolves around 'Operation Anthropoid' carried out by the Czechoslovakian army-in-exile during World War II, to assassinate the high-ranking Nazi officer Reinhard Heydrich, who was third in command after Adolf Hitler. Also starring Cillian Murphy, the film will release on August 12.
