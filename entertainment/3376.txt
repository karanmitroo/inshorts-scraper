Rakhi Sawant will be playing rape convict Gurmeet Ram Rahim Singh's adopted daughter Honeypreet Singh Insan in an upcoming biopic on him. The biopic, which will be directed by Ashutosh Mishra, will reportedly also star former Bigg Boss contestant Ajaz Khan. Gurmeet Ram Rahim has appeared in films like 'MSG: The Messenger' and 'Jattu Engineer'.
