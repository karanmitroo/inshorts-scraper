The song 'Tu Cheez Badi Hai Mast Mast' from the 1994 Akshay Kumar starrer thriller film 'Mohra' will be recreated for the Abbas-Mustan directorial upcoming film 'Machine'. Akshay Kumar will reportedly launch the recreated version of the song. The film stars actress Kiara Advani and it also marks the debut of Mustafa, the son of filmmaker Abbas.
