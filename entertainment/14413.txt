The trailer for the Mahesh Babu starrer Telugu film 'Brahmotsavam' was released on Saturday. A family drama, the film stars Kajal Aggarwal, Samantha Ruth Prabhu and Pranitha Subhash as the lead actresses while veteran actors including Revathi, Sathyaraj and Jayasudha are also part of the cast. Directed by Srikanth Addala, the film is scheduled to release on May 20.
