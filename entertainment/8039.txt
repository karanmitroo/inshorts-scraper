Actress Priyanka Chopra, speaking about her Hollywood debut film 'Baywatch', said that not many women get a chance to intimidate Dwayne Johnson (The Rock) in films. She added, "I play the villain...that was the funnest thing to do...I'm so mean to Zac Efron and Johnson." Priyanka said this on her recent appearance on 'The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon'.
