The trailer of Amitabh Bachchan, Deepika Padukone and Irrfan Khan starrer 'Piku', a Shoojit Sircar directorial, was released on Wednesday. The comedy-drama is the story of a young working woman who shoulders the responsibility of her ageing father. At the trailer launch, Amitabh Bachchan said that working on the film made him miss his family a lot.
