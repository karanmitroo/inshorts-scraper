The first motion poster of Arjun-Sonakshi starrer 'Tevar' has been launched. The film, an action-romance based in Agra, has been produced by Arjun's uncle Sanjay Kapoor and directed by Amit Sharma. Shruti Hassan has also performed an item song for it. Tevar is a remake of 2003 Telugu film Okkadu and is scheduled to release in Jan 2015.
