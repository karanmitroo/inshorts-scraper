Director Christopher Nolan's film Dunkirk which released on Friday, "is one of the greatest war movies ever made," wrote Hindustan Times. The Guardian wrote, "It's a visceral piece of film-making," while Times of India called it "the first real contender for Best Picture" at Oscars. The film has been rated 5/5 (Hindustan Times, The Guardian) and 4.5/5 (Times Of India).
