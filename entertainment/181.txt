Vidya Balan starrer 'Tumhari Sulu' is set to be remade in Tamil. Actress Jyotika will play the character 'Sulu', portrayed by Vidya in the original film. Radha Mohan, who will direct the Tamil version, said, "I'm excited to be directing such an appreciated film...I was impressed with the realistic presentation of Tumhari Sulu and hope to recreate the magic."
