A new trailer of the Hugh Jackman starrer superhero film 'Logan' has been released. Jackman will be seen reprising his role as mutant 'Wolverine' in 'Logan', which will be the third Wolverine solo film after 'X-Men Origins: Wolverine' (2009) and 'The Wolverine' (2013). Directed by James Mangold, the film is scheduled to release on March 3, 2017 in the US.
