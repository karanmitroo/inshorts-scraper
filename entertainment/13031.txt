Actor Ranveer Singh has tweeted a video to promote Akshay Kumar's upcoming film 'Rustom'. Using the hashtag #9DaysToRustom, the actor shared the video which shows him dressed as a Navy officer, calling himself 'Chupa Rustom' and dancing to the song 'Zeher hai ki pyaar hai' from Akshay's 1995 film 'Sabse Bada Khiladi'. 'Rustom' is set to release on August 12.
