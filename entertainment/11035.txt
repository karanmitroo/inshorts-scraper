Actors Shah Rukh Khan and Anushka Sharma did a pole dance on the Riteish Deshmukh and Sajid Khan hosted talk show 'Yaaron Ki Baraat'. The two actors danced to the song 'Chammak Challo' from Khan's 2011 film 'Ra.One'. Shah Rukh and Anushka will be seen together in the upcoming film 'The Ring', directed by Imtiaz Ali.
