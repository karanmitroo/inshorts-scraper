Abhishek Bachchan on Instagram shared a picture of himself with father Amitabh Bachchan when he was hospitalised due to a neuromuscular ailment in mid-80s. He revealed that when Amitabh was hospitalised after the accident on 'Coolie' sets, he described hospital equipment as 'kites' to Abhishek. "All I thought was...why isn't he allowing me to play with these kites," added Abhishek.
