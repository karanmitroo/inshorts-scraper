Aamir Khan-starrer 'PK' has collected Rs 95.21 crore at the box-office in its opening weekend, said the producers. The Rajkumar Hirani directorial, that also stars Anushka Sharma, hit about 6k screens, including 5.2k in India, on Dec-19. The movie did business of Rs 26.63 crore on Friday, Rs 30.34 crore on Saturday and Rs 38.24 crore on Sunday.
