Dancing diva Madhuri Dixit is all excited to see Priyanka Chopra and Deepika Padukone perform the lavani dance in Sany Leela Bhansali's 'Bajirao Mastani'. "Anything about dance always excites me, so I am very, very excited to see Priyanka Chopra and Deepika Padukone's 'lavani'...", she said. Madhuri had performed 'lavani' in the song 'Humko aajkal hai' in the film 'Sailab'.
