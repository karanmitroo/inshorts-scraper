Superstar Salman Khan starrer 'Prem Ratan Dhan Payo', a Sooraj Barjatya directorial, will hit the screens on Diwali this year, incidentally a long 5-day weekend. With this film, Salman is returning as Prem onscreen after a gap of about 16 years. The film, being shot in Rajasthan, also stars Sonam Kapoor, Neil Nitin Mukesh and Anupam Kher.
