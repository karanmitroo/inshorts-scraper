As per reports, Indian actress Priyanka Chopra will be seen in Dwayne Johnson and Zac Efron starrer 'Baywatch' which will mark her film debut in Hollywood. Reportedly, filmmakers are currently working out the dates for film’s shoot and Priyanka’s 'Quantico' commitment. The film is based on 1990's popular TV series on California lifeguards with the same name, starring Pamela Anderson.
