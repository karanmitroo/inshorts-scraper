Late actress Sridevi was down with a fever of 103 degrees when she was shooting for the song 'Na Jaane Kahan Se Aayi Hai' from the 1989 film 'Chaalbaaz'. In the song, she featured with Sunny Deol, who was one of the lead actors in the film along with Rajinikanth. Sridevi played a double role in 'Chaalbaaz'.
