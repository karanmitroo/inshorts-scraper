The title song from the Vidyut Jammwal and Esha Gupta starrer 'Commando 2' has been released. The song has been composed by Mannan Shaah, with vocals by Aditi Singh Sharma, and lyrics by Kumaar. The action film is about a black money agent who has been arrested in Malaysia. Also featuring Adah Sharma, 'Commando 2' will release on March 3.
