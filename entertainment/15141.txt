Sonam Kapoor starrer 'Neerja' which released in India on February 19 has been declared tax-free in Rajasthan by the state government. The film is based on a 1986 flight hijack incident which was made unsuccessful by a flight attendant named Neerja Bhanot, who was posthumously awarded the Ashok Chakra. 'Neerja' is also tax-free in Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra and Gujarat.
