Actor-turned filmmaker Pooja Bhatt recently  took to Twitter to praise the song 'Dheere Dheere Se' from Mahesh Bhatt-directorial 'Aashiqui' (1990). "In a world full of imitations there is no beating the original- Dheere Dheere Se", Bhatt tweeted. Music composer and singer Yo Yo Honey Singh had recently released a remix version of the song, featuring Hrithik Roshan and Sonam Kapoor.
