Ragini MMS 2, the sequel to 2010 horror film Ragini MMS, directed by Bhushan Patel and produced by Ekta Kapoor and starring Sunny Leone, Parvin Dabas, Sandhya Mridul and Divya Dutta, released on Friday. The movie has been rated 1.5 (Suhani Singh, India Today), 2.5 (Indian Express) and 3 (Times of India; DNA; koimoi.com).
