A song titled 'Swagpur Ka Chaudhary' from Saif Ali Khan starrer 'Kaalakaandi' has been released. It has been composed by Sameer Uddin with both vocals and lyrics by Akshay Verma. Directed by Akshat Verma and also starring Deepak Dobriyal, Kunaal Roy Kapur and Shenaz Treasury, 'Kaalakaandi' is scheduled to release on January 12, 2018.
