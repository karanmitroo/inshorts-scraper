Actress Richa Chadha took to Instagram to share the first poster of her upcoming film 'Fukrey Returns', a sequel to the 2013 film 'Fukrey'. The original cast members Pulkit Samrat, Varun Sharma, Manjot Singh and Ali Fazal have been retained in the sequel. Directed by Mrighdeep Singh Lamba, the film is scheduled to release on December 8.
