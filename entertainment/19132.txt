Clash of the titans is in the offing as Amitabh Bachchan's 'Bhootnath Returns' and Rajinikanth's 'Kochadaiiyaan' are slated to release on the same day (April 11) at the box office. ‘Both have fans across the globe and their films' collections will be followed closely,’ an industry watcher said. 'Bhootnath Returns' is a horror comedy while 'Kochadaiiyaan' remains a period drama.
