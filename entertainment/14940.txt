The teaser of 'The Lego Batman Movie', a 3D computer-animated action comedy film, has been released. Directed by Chris McKay, it is a spin-off of the film 'The Lego Movie' (2014). The film, featuring Will Arnett as Batman, along with Ralph Fiennes and Rosario Dawson, is scheduled to release in the United States on February 10, 2017.
