Bollywood blockbusters 'Kick' and 'Bang Bang' have been nominated in US' Taurus World Stunt Awards, alongside Hollywood hits “The Amazing Spider-Man 2” and “Transformers”. While Salman Khan-starrer 'Kick' is nominated in the Best Stunt Rigging category, Hrithik Roshan's 'Bang Bang' is competing for the Best Fight award. 'Bang Bang' has also been nominated for 'Best Work With a Vehicle' award.
