Actor Arunoday Singh got married to Canadian girlfriend Lee Elton in Bhopal on Wednesday. The couple met during the shooting of Arunoday's 2010 film 'Aisha'. The actor shared a photograph from the wedding, captioned, "Married the girl of my dreams, drove her home in an old Cadillac. Micdrop." Arunoday was last seen in Hrithik Roshan-starrer 2016 film 'Mohenjo Daro'.
