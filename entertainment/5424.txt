Kareena Kapoor and Abhishek Bachchan's debut film Refugee, which released on June 30, 2000, completed 17 years on Friday. Directed by JP Dutta, the film is said to have been inspired by the short story 'Love Across the Salt Desert' by Keki N Daruwalla. Kareena won the 'Filmfare Award for Best Female Debut' for the film.
