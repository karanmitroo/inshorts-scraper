'Ikk Kudi', the new song from the Shahid Kapoor starrer 'Udta Punjab' was released on Wednesday. The song has been composed by Amit Trivedi and sung by Diljit Dosanjh, who is making his acting debut in Bollywood with this film. The song has been picturised on Alia Bhatt and Diljit. The film is scheduled to release on June 17.
