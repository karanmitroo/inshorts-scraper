Actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan on Tuesday met French President Francois Hollande at a lunch hosted by the French ambassador to India, Francois Richier, in Delhi. Hollande, who attended the Republic Day parade in the morning, had also invited Shah Rukh Khan and Aditya Chopra. Aish who wore traditional red Banarasi saree to the luncheon was accompanied by her daughter, Aaradhya.
