A day after reports claimed that 'Udta Punjab' has leaked online, actor Shahid Kapoor tweeted, "Blood and sweat of many in this film... Watch UdtaPunjab in theatres". "[S]ee you at the theatres #piracydimaadi", tweeted Alia Bhatt. Filmmaker Karan Johar wrote, "Don't succumb to online leaks", while actor Sidharth Malhotra tweeted "say no to piracy #UdtaPunjab at a theatre near u".
