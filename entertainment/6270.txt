Kamal Haasan has said that he has been more socially responsible than the person who hosted television show Satyamev Jayate, while referring to actor Aamir Khan. Kamal said this when asked why he chose to host Bigg Boss' Tamil version over a show like Satyamev Jayate, which discusses social issues. Notably, Satyamev Jayate marked the television debut of Aamir Khan.
