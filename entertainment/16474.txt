Music composer Aadesh Shrivastava, who is battling cancer, is in a terminal stage, Shrivastava's brother-in-law and music composer Lalit Pandit has said. "This is his third relapse... he is beyond that stage where we can start a new therapy", Pandit said. Shrivastava, who had fought cancer in 2010, is being treated at the Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital in Mumbai.
