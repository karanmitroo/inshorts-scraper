American comedian Jimmy Kimmel has tweeted that he loves Malayalam song 'Jimikki Kammal' from the new Mohanlal-starrer 'Velipadinte Pusthakam'. A Twitter user had asked Kimmel if he had heard the song and tweeted a link to a video showing college students dancing to it. Meanwhile, people tweeted, "he's popular coz of this song in South India" and "Lots of love."
