The Shahid Kapoor starrer 'Udta Punjab' has cracked Bollywood's most expensive music deal by selling the rights to its soundtrack and album to T- Series for ₹18 crore. The film broke the earlier record of ₹17 crore set by 'Prem Ratan Dhan Payo'. Notably, Udta Punjab's music director Amit Trivedi has incorporated psychedelic trance and hip-hop elements in the soundtrack.
