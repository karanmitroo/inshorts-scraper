Actress Deepika Padukone has said that she will not invite Katrina Kaif to her wedding. Deepika said this on the talk show 'BFFs with Vogue' while responding to a question by the host Neha Dhupia. Deepika's ex-boyfriend actor Ranbir Kapoor went on to date Katrina after their break up. Meanwhile, Deepika is said to be currently dating Ranveer Singh.
