Salman Khan tweeted a picture of himself with Shah Rukh Khan and Hrithik Roshan, thanking Rakesh Roshan for 1995 film 'Karan Arjun'. "Congrats duggu on #17YearsOfKNPH," he captioned the photo, congratulating Hrithik for 17 years of his debut film in a leading role 'Kaho Naa... Pyaar Hai'. Salman also wished luck to the actors for their films 'Raees' and 'Kaabil'.
