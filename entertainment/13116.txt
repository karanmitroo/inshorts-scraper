The second track 'Toota jo kabhi tara' from actor Tiger Shroff's upcoming superhero film 'A Flying Jatt' has been released. The song has been picturised on Tiger and Jacqueline Fernandez, the film's lead actress. It has been composed by music composer duo Sachin-Jigar and sung by Atif Aslam and Sumedha Karmahe. 'A Flying Jatt' will release on August 25.
