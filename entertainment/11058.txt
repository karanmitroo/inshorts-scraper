Actors Kareena Kapoor Khan, Deepika Padukone and Shahid Kapoor have supported PM Modi's move of demonetising ₹500 and ₹1000. "Everybody is finding it difficult, but if it leads to less corruption, black money... it's a bold step...," said Shahid while Kareena commented that it was a "very good decision". Deepika said it was an "exceptional" move and "congratulated" the government.
