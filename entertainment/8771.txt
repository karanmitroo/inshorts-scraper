The Rana Daggubati and Taapsee Pannu starrer 'The Ghazi Attack' which released on Friday is "an engaging piece of cinema", wrote Hindustan Times. NDTV wrote, "like the torpedoes fired...it...misses its mark more often than not", while Times of India said, "Despite the hitches, this underwater thriller is worth a watch". It was rated 4/5 (HT), 3/5 (TOI) and 2.5/5 (NDTV).
