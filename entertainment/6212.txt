Actors Salman and Sohail Khan recently performed the 'Radio Song' at Bandra Fort to promote their upcoming film 'Tubelight'. The brothers danced to the song for an episode of talent show 'India Banega Manch'. Notably, 'Radio Song' is the first track of the film, for which the video has been released with subtitles in 14 languages, including Gujarati, and Chinese.
