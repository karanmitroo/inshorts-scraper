Karan Johar, Diljit Dosanjh, Riteish Deshmukh and Sonakshi Sinha starrer 'Welcome To New York', which released today, "is a never-ending pain," wrote Hindustan Times (HT). NDTV said, "It is a mindless yet harmless comedy." The film "doesn't always manage to hit the bull's eye," wrote Times of India (TOI). It has been rated 0.5/5 (HT), 2/5 (NDTV) and 2.5/5 (TOI).
