The Rann of Kutch is emerging as the favourite destination for filmmakers now a days with movies like Ramleela, R...Rajkumar, Gori Tere Pyar Mein and The Good Road, being shot there.  And with The Good Road being selected as the official entry into the Oscars, Kutch now seems to be going international. Previous movies shot there include Lagaan, Magadheera, etc.
