Following the release of Vaani Kapoor and Ranveer Singh starrer Befikre's new poster, 'Vaani Kapoor' was the most trending Google search in India on Thursday. It was most looked up in Jammu and Kashmir, Goa and Odisha. 'Te3n', 'NIOS result' and 'Brazil vs Haiti' were some of the other trending searches in India on the same day.
