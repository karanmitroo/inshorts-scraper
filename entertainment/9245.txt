France's dental surgery student Iris Mittenaere was crowned the 65th Miss Universe on Monday in the Philippines. The 24-year-old is only the second Miss Universe winner from France and first since 1953. Haiti's Raquel Pelissier was the first runner-up while Colombia's Andrea Tovar came third. India was represented by Bengaluru's Roshmitha Harimurthy, who was eliminated in the preliminary round.
