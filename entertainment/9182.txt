The official trailer of the Alia Bhatt and Varun Dhawan starrer 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania' has been released. Varun will portray 'Badrinath Bansal' while Alia will play 'Vaidehi Trivedi' in the romantic comedy. The film marks the second instalment of a franchise that began with 2014 film 'Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania'. Directed by Shashank Khaitan, it will release on March 10.
