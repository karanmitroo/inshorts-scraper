On the auspicious occassion of Akshya Tritiya on Tuesday, Bollywood actor Vivek Oberoi and his wife Priyanka Alva were blessed with a baby girl. Priyanka delivered the baby in Bangalore which is her home town. The couple, who got married in October 2010, also has a 2-year-old son Viaan. The actor was last seen as a villain in 'Krrish 3'.
