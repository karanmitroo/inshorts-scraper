Actress Raveena Tandon took to Instagram to share an old cover of 'Stardust' magazine which features her along with Karisma Kapoor, Urmila Matondkar and Pooja Bhatt. The cover is from the year 1991. "#throwback90s #magicalera #girlgang90s," wrote Raveena while sharing the photo. The magazine cover reads, "Rebellious! The Age of Aquarius".
