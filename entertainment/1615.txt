The trailer of Akshay Kumar, Sonam Kapoor and Radhika Apte starrer 'PadMan' has been released. The film, which has been directed by filmmaker R Balki, is based on the life of Arunachalam Muruganantham, a man who made sanitary pads accessible to the women of his village. Produced by Akshay's wife Twinkle Khanna, 'PadMan' will release on January 26, 2018.
