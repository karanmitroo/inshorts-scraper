According to reports, 'Dangal' actress Sanya Malhotra is set to play the female lead in filmmaker Anurag Kashyap's next film titled 'Manmarziyan'. As per reports, the male lead in the film will be a newcomer. 'Dum Laga Ke Haisha' actors Bhumi Pednekar and Ayushmann Khurrana were also approached for the film, sources claimed.
