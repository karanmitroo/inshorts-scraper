Bollywood actress Rani Mukherji and Aditya Chopra were spotted together celebrating Diwali at Rani's Juhu bungalow on Sunday night. A picture also showed Rani feeding Diwali delicacy to Aditya Chopra. Rani has never acknowledged her alleged relationship with Aditya Chopra. Others at the celebration were Karan Johar, choreographer Vaibhavi Merchant and Aditya's mother and late Yash Chopra's widow Pamela Chopra.
