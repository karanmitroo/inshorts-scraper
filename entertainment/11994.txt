An 18-year-old picture of Ranbir Kapoor and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan has been shared online by writer-director Rumy Jafry. The picture was taken on the set of the 1999 film 'Aa Ab Laut Chalen', for which Ranbir was an assistant director. Ranbir and Aishwarya will be seen together onscreen for the first time in the upcoming film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil'.
