As per reports, actor Shah Rukh Khan will portray poet Sahir Ludhianvi in Sanjay Leela Bhansali's biopic tentatively titled 'Gustakhiyan'. Reportedly, Shah Rukh is currently reading the script, which has a lot of poetry. 'Gustakhiyan' will revolve around Sahir's relationships with women, including poetess-novelist Amrita Pritam, and his contribution to the Hindi film industry.
