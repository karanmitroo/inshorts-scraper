Television actress Kamya Punjabi, who was a close friend of Pratyusha Banerjee, has claimed that there were "injury marks" on Pratyusha's nose and cheeks. Kamya, who addressed the media after completing Pratyusha's last rites, added that Pratyusha had a "troubled" relationship with her boyfriend Rahul Raj. "She had distanced us away from her owing to her troubled relationship", she added.
