Actor Aamir Khan's look from his upcoming film 'Thugs of Hindostan' has surfaced online. He can be seen sporting a bearded look paired with a red turban. The film will also star Amitabh Bachchan. This will be the first time that the two will be working together. Directed by Vijay Krishna Acharya, the film will release on Diwali in 2018.
