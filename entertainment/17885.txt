Kangana Ranaut and R Madhavan starrer 'Tanu Weds Manu Returns', a sequel to the 2011 hit romantic comedy 'Tanu Weds Manu', will release on June 12 this year. Directed by Aanand L Rai, the film has Kangana in a double role. "We are confident that the sequel will be just as memorable and entertaining," producer Krishna Lulla said.
