With 'Jolly LLB 2' becoming actor Akshay Kumar's seventh film to earn over ₹100 crore, he equalled the number of ₹100 crore films that Shah Rukh Khan has. 'Jolly LLB 2' was also Akshay's 4th consecutive ₹100 crore film after 'Airlift', 'Housefull 3' and 'Rustom'. The film entered the ₹100 crore club on the twelfth day of its release.
