The teaser of the Suriya-starrer Tamil action film 'S3', which was released on Monday has received over 2 million views in less than 24 hours. 'S3' is the third part in the 'Singam' franchise directed by Hari. Suriya will reprise his role as police officer 'Durai Singam'.  Also starring Anushka Shetty and Shruti Haasan, 'S3' will release on December 16.
