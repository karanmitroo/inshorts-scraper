Hollywood film 'Star Wars: The Force Awakens' which released in India on Friday, "is thoroughly entertaining, mysterious and nostalgic", wrote India Today. "It is much better than the middle three films," said Indian Express. The film "is action-packed, exhilarating fun," wrote HT. The JJ Abrams directorial has been rated 4.5/5 (TOI), 4/5 (India Today, HT) and 3/5 (Indian Express).
