'Ishtehaar', a new song from Diljit Dosanjh and Sonakshi Sinha starrer 'Welcome To New York' has been released. It has been sung by Rahat Fateh Ali Khan and Dhvani Bhanushali, composed by Shamir Tandon with lyrics penned by Charanjeet Charan. Also starring Karan Johar, Lara Dutta, Riteish Deshmukh and Boman Irani, the film is scheduled to release on February 23.
