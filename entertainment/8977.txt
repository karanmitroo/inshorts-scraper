Late Ghazal maestro Jagjit Singh is the only person to have composed songs written by former Prime Minister Atal Bihari Vajpayee. He composed the songs for two albums 'Nayi Disha' (1999) and 'Samvedna' (2002). Singh, whose 76th birth anniversary was observed on Wednesday, is best known for songs 'Hothon Se Chhu Lo Tum' and 'Tum Itna Jo Muskura Rahe Ho'.
