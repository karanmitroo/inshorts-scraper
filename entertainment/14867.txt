The official trailer of the Emraan Hashmi starrer film 'Azhar' was released on Friday. The film, a biopic of former India cricket team captain Mohammed Azharuddin also features Prachi Desai as Naureen, the cricketer's first wife. Nargis Fakhri plays Sangeeta Bijlani, his second wife. Produced by Ekta Kapoor and directed by Tony D'Souza, the film will release on May 13.
