Actor Sanjay Dutt, while speaking about veteran actress Reema Lagoo's demise, said that he has lost a mother again. "Her demise has left a void which is difficult to fill. One of the best talents that we had," added Sanjay. Sanjay and Reema worked together in films including 'Vaastav: The Reality' (1999), 'Saajan (1991), among others.
