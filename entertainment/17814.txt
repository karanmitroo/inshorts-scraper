According to sources, Hollywood star Johnny Depp and his 28-year-old actress fiancee Amber Heard, will marry in an intimate ceremony on his private island Little Hall's Pond Cay, in Bahamas. The guest list has 50 people, including Heard's family and Depp's children with former partner Vanessa Paradis. Heard and Depp chose the Feb 7-8 weekend for the nuptials.
