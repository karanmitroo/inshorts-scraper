Akshay Kumar and Siddharth Malhotra’s “Brothers” has minted Rs 15.02 crore at the Box Office on day one. This makes it the biggest ever opener for an Akshay Kumar-starrer. The film is jointly produced by Dharma Productions and Fox Star Studios and released on Friday to become the second biggest opener of 2015, after Salman Khan’s “Bajrangi Bhaijaan”
