Actor Rana Daggubati's look from the upcoming epic historical fiction film 'Baahubali: The Conclusion' has been revealed. The film is a continuation of the 2015 film 'Baahubali: The Beginning'. Daggubati will reprise his role as the antagonist 'Bhallaladeva' from the first film. Directed by SS Rajamouli, the film is scheduled to release on April 28, 2017.
