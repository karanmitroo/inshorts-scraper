MAMI’s Mumbai Film Festival closed on Thursday amid celebrity presence including Saif Ali Khan and Vidya Balan with Salman Khan as the guest of honour. The gala ended with an award show where Guatemalan filmmaker Jayro Busatamante's “Ixcanul Volcano” won the Golden Gateway of India for best film, international category, and Nawazuddin Siddiqui-starrer “Haraamkhor” won the Silver Gateway of India.
