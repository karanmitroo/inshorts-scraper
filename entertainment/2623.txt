Twelve-year-old Shreyan Bhattacharya from West Bengal and 11-year-old Anjali Gaikwad from Maharashtra have won the singing reality show 'Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Li'l Champs 2017'. The show is judged by Neha Kakkar, Himesh Reshammiya and Javed Ali and hosted by Aditya Narayan. The grand finale also featured performances by TV actors Ravi Dubey and Parag Tyagi.
