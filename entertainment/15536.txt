The NAAM foundation, formed by actors Nana Patekar and Makarand Anaspure, has announced to adopt two more drought-hit villages, namely Kutubkheda and Kaudar, in Maharashtra's Marathwada region. The NAAM foundation works for the welfare of farmers and their families living in the drought-hit regions of Maharashtra. In October, the foundation had adopted the Dhondalgaon and Gogalgaon villages.
