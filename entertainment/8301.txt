A video showing the live recording of the song 'Tamma Tamma', which first featured in 1990 film 'Thanedaar', has surfaced online. The song's lead singers Bappi Lahiri and Anuradha Paudwal, along with the chorus, can be seen recording the song in the video. The song was picturised on actors Madhuri Dixit and Sanjay Dutt in the film.
