Imtiaz Ali's next directorial venture starring Ranbir and Deepika in the lead, 'Tamasha', has been postponed till early 2016. The film, that was seeing a Christmas release this year, was postponed as Ranbir already has 3 films releasing this year. Also, Sanjay Leela Bhansali's 'Bajirao Mastani' (starring Ranveer-Deepika), and Rohit Shetty's film starring Shah Rukh are releasing the same day.
