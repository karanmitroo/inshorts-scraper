The Vin Diesel and Deepika Padukone starrer action film 'xXx: The Return of Xander Cage' has become the world's highest grossing film of 2017 till now. The film has earned over ₹2,000 crore (over $311 million) worldwide. In India, the film minted ₹54 crore as of early February. The action film marked the Hollywood debut of actress Deepika Padukone.
