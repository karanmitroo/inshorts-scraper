Police officials have taken TV star Pratyusha Banerjee's boyfriend Rahul Raj Singh into custody for interrogation amid reports that Pratyusha committed suicide because of her relationship. Pratyusha's friends claimed that she "couldn't have committed suicide". Rahul had discovered the Balika Vadhu star's body and rushed to Mumbai's Koikilaben Hospital where the 24-year-old was declared dead.
