Varun Dhawan shared a picture on Twitter wherein he has recreated a scene from 'Baahubali'. The picture, which features actor Prabhas, shows them enacting the scene wherein Kattappa killed Baahubali. "Did only what #katappa did before this. #Prabhas is really cool and down to earth more power to him but iv [I've] got his sword now," read the picture's caption.
