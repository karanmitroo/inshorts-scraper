Late singer Prince's new albums 'Prince 4Ever' and a deluxe reissue of 'Purple Rain', which are being released posthumously, will feature unreleased music. The compilation will include an official release of the track 'Moonbeam Levels', which was recorded by Prince in 1982. Further, the 'Purple Rain' reissue was initially announced in 2014 to mark the 1984 film's 30th anniversary.
