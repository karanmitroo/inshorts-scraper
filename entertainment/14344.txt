Shraddha Kapoor is reportedly set to star in Apoorva Lakhia’s upcoming film 'Hasina', a biopic on the life of Dawood Ibrahim’s sister Haseena Parkar. The film would be the first time that Shraddha portrays a grey character. Earlier, reports stated that Sonakshi Sinha was set to be part of the project but walked out of the film citing date issues.
