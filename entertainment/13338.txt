Actress Anushka Sharma on Saturday tweeted, "Choosing motherhood over career is empowering. Choosing your career over motherhood is empowering. The Freedom to CHOOSE is empowering." 'Aarfa', Anushka's character in 'Sultan' chose motherhood over her career. Anushka also shared a statement by tennis player Victoria Azarenka, who chose to skip a season as she was pregnant, to explain her decision.
