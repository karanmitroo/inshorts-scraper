Music composer AR Rahman has featured in the Oscars list for the third time. Rahman's scores for Rajinikanth's movie 'Kochadaiiyaan' and Hollywood films 'The 100-Foot Journey' and 'Million Dollar Arm' have been listed in 114 music scores released by the Academy. Singer Sonu Nigam and percussionist Bickram Ghosh have also made it to the list with their score for Jal.
