Actress Taapsee Pannu has said that shooting for 'Judwaa 2' is like going back to school as her first Hindi film was also directed by David Dhawan. 'Judwaa 2' will feature Varun Dhawan in a double role along with actress Jacqueline Fernandez. The film is a sequel to Salman Khan's 1997 film 'Judwaa', which was also directed by David Dhawan.
