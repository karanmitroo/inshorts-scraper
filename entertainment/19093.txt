Defeating heavyweights like Meryl Streep and Sandra Bullock, Cate Blanchett (44) has claimed the best actress Oscar for her performance in writer-director Woody Allen's ‘Blue Jasmine’. Notably, Blanchett has swept up practically every acting award this season - winning lead actress at the Screen Actors Guild Awards, the Golden Globes, the BAFTA awards and the Film Independent Spirit Awards.
