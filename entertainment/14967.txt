Director-choreographer Farah Khan will choreograph actor Jackie Chan in his upcoming Indo-Chinese film 'Kung Fu Yoga'. It will be a special Bollywood song and will also feature Sonu Sood and Amyra Dastur. The song will be shot in Rajasthan and Beijing. 'Kung Fu Yoga' is an action-adventure film being directed by Stanley Tong, scheduled to release this year.
