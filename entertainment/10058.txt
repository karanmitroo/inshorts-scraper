Actress Deepika Padukone, while reacting to the 'Koffee with Karan' episode featuring Anushka Sharma and Katrina Kaif, tweeted, "[T]hese girls were on fire! What a fun episode." When the show's host Karan Johar asked Anushka and Katrina if they consider Deepika a good friend, the two actresses refrained from calling her a friend.
