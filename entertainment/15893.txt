The trailer of the Sonam Kapoor and Shabana Azmi starrer film 'Neerja' was released on YouTube on Thursday. Directed by Ram Madhvani, the film is the biopic of a Mumbai-based flight attendant Neerja Bhanot, who was killed by terrorists who hijacked a Mumbai-New York flight in 1986. The film is scheduled to release on February 19, 2016.
