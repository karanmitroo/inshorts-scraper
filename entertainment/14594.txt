'Pyar Ki Maa Ki', the first song from the film 'Housefull 3' was released on Sunday. The film features Akshay Kumar, Riteish Deshmukh and Abhishek Bachchan as the male leads, while Jacqueline Fernandes, Lisa Haydon and Nargis Fakhri appear as the female leads. Directed by Sajid-Farhad, the film also features Boman Irani, and is scheduled to release on June 3.
