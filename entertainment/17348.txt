National award winning actress Kangana Ranaut-starrer 'Tanu Weds Manu Returns' trailer was released Wednesday on YouTube. The story of the film revolves around Manu, played by R Madhavan, who falls in love with Haryanavi speaking Kusum, who resembles his wife Tanu. The two distinct roles will be played by Kangana. The Aanand L Rai directorial will release on May 22.
