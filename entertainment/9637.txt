Actor Paresh Rawal will be seen playing the role of Sunil Dutt, Sanjay Dutt's father, in his upcoming biopic directed by filmmaker Rajkumar Hirani. "The film will showcase a beautiful father-son track," said the film's spokesperson. Ranbir Kapoor will be seen playing the role of Sanjay Dutt while Anushka Sharma and Sonam Kapoor will also feature in the biopic.
