Actor Rishi Kapoor has revealed that Sanjay Dutt thought he was having an affair with actress Tina Munim, who was seeing Dutt at the time. Sanjay Dutt once visited Neetu Kapoor in her bungalow to pick a fight with Rishi. However, Neetu, who was Rishi's girlfriend then, handled the situation and clarified that Rishi and Tina were just colleagues.
