Actress Kangana Ranaut turned showstopper for fashion designer Manish Arora in Mumbai as part of the 12th edition of Blender's Pride Fashion Tour. Kangana wore a colourful ensemble which had neon elements in it that flashed bright under black light. Arora also used elaborate headgear to accessorise his creations, which were mostly embellished and embroidered skirts, blouses and jackets.
