Sonu Sood revealed that while shooting the climax of 'Dabangg', Salman Khan accidentally punched him hard and broke his nose. He added, "There was so much bleeding...I was rushed to the hospital. I shot for 5-6 more days with that broken nose." The Abhinav Kashyap directorial in which Sonu played antagonist 'Chedi Singh', completes seven years of release on Sunday.
