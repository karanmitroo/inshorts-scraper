PM Narendra Modi Sunday attended actor-politician Shatrughan Sinha's son Kush's wedding held in Mumbai. Bollywood actress Sonakshi Sinha, who had earlier visited Modi with parents Shatrughan and Poonam to invite him, tweeted a photograph from the wedding. She captioned it, "Thank you @narendramodi sir for keeping your promise to me! You made our day truly even more special."
