Filmmaker Sanjay Gupta, who directed the Hrithik Roshan starrer film 'Kaabil', took to social media to share pictures of his physical transformation. "This is where six months of discipline and dedication get you. Still a long way to get to where I wanna be," he wrote alongside the photo. Other films directed by Sanjay Gupta include 'Kaante' and 'Zinda'.
