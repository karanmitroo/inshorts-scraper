The much-awaited PK, which hit the screens on Friday, has a 'brilliant storyline' and Aamir Khan's 'exceptional act', says Zee News. According to India Today, the Rajkumar Hirani directorial, that also stars Anushka Sharma, Sushant Singh Rajput and Sanjay Dutt, is a 'must-watch' film. The film has been rated 4.5/5 (India Today, Zee News, Mid-Day) and 5/5 (NDTV).
