Within just eight days of release, Aamir Khan starrer PK has grossed Rs 368 crore worldwide. According to Koimoi (.) com, PK minted Rs 283 crore in India and Rs 85 crore abroad. It is doing exceptionally well in both multiplexes and single screen theaters. Aamir's '3 Idiots' had grossed Rs 395 crore - third highest all time grosser.
