As per reports, Pahlaj Nihalani may be replaced as the Censor Board chief. Reports suggested that filmmaker Prakash Jha, TV producer-actor Chandraprakash Dwivedi and even filmmaker Madhur Bhandarkar have been considered as a replacement for the current Censor Board chief. Nihalani was appointed as the chairperson of the Censor Board in January, 2015 for a period of three years.
