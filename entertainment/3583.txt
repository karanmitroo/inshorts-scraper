Kangana Ranaut's sister Rangoli Chandel has slammed Aditya Pancholi's wife Zarina Wahab for denying that Kangana sought her help during her abusive relationship with Pancholi. "Shame on you it's because of women like you, evil creeps like Pancholi exist in this society," Rangoli tweeted. She further wrote that Zarina had also pleaded Kangana not to go to the police.
