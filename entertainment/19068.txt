A part of the track - Locha-E-Ulfat - from Abhishek Varman's 2 States featuring Arjun Kapoor and Alia Bhatt, has been shot at IIM-A, a first for a Bollywood film. The reason being that the book on which the movie is based on, is also set in the institute. The movie, which is produced by Dharma Productions, releases April 18.
