Riteish Deshmukh, Hrithik Roshan, Abhishek Bachchan, Farah Khan and Shirish Kunder were among the Bollywood celebrities who took to social media to wish Rishi Kapoor on his 65th birthday. Sharing an old picture of Rishi, Shirish wrote, "The man who invented the "airport look". Happy Birthday," while Hrithik tweeted, "Happy birthday Chintu uncle...thank you for the inspiration, the laughs."
