Actress Sonam Kapoor has featured on the June-July cover of the bi-monthly magazine 'Harper's Bazaar Bride'. She has worn a light pink sheer Marc Zwillinger gown accesorised with a headgear by Tami Bar-Lev and a statement ring. The actress shot in various locations in Israel and wore ensembles from other designers including Rohit Bal and Rahul Mishra for the shoot.
