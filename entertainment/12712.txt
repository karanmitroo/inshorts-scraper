Actress Karisma Kapoor has shared a picture of herself with her sister Kareena Kapoor from the time when Kareena was a teenager. The picture was from when Karisma won her first Filmfare award in the Best Actress category for 'Raja Hindustani' in 1997. Karisma, who posed with a 17-year-old Kareena, captioned the picture, "Major throwback".
