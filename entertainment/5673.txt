Ritesh Sidhwani, the producer of the new 'Don' film franchise, has said that the script of 'Don 3' is currently being written. "We have found the right idea and...will make an official announcement soon," added Ritesh. While actors Shah Rukh Khan and Priyanka Chopra featured in both 'Don' and 'Don 2', the cast of 'Don 3' hasn't been revealed yet.
