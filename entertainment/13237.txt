Actress Priyanka Chopra has featured on the cover of the American magazine 'Flaunt'. The 34-year-old actress is seen in an ensemble by the Italian fashion house 'Etro'. She was styled by Zoe Costello while fashion photographer Frederic Auerbach shot the cover image. Priyanka while sharing the cover tweeted, "What you lookin' at???
