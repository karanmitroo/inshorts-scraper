Kamaal R Khan (KRK) has tweeted a text message he received from Amitabh Bachchan. He sent KRK a text after he predicted that 'Sarkar 3' would be a hit on watching the trailer. Bachchan's message reads, "Thank you Kamaal... gracious of you to say so. You have become the barometer of the box office. Hoping that this prediction comes true."
