A song titled 'Laila Main Laila' featuring Sunny Leone, from Shah Rukh Khan starrer upcoming action crime thriller film 'Raees' has been released. It is a recreated version of an original song composed by Kalyanji and Anandji. Pawni Pandey has sung the song, with additional lyrics by Javed Akhtar. Directed by Rahul Dholakia, 'Raees' will release on January 25, 2017.
