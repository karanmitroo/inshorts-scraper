The trailer of Jamie Foxx starrer action thriller 'Sleepless', where he portrays the character of a police detective 'Vincent Downs' has been released. The film, a remake of the French thriller 'Nuit Blanche' also stars actors Michelle Monaghan and Drew Sidora. Directed by Baran bo Odar, 'Sleepless' is scheduled to release on February 24, 2017.
