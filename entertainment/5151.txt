Girish Malik, the director of the Sanjay Dutt starrer Torbaaz, has confirmed that television actress Aankita Lokhaande is not part of his film. "We are currently doing our pre-production. I don't know Aankita, and apart from Sanjay Dutt, no one else has been signed or even approached," said Girish while denying earlier reports of Aankita signing the film.
