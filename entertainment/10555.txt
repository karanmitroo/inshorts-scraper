Actor Shah Rukh Khan was named 'Kids' Ikon of the Year' at Nickelodeon's Kids Choice Awards. Actor Salman Khan won the Best Actor award for his film 'Sultan', while Deepika Padukone was named Best Actress for historical romance film 'Bajirao Mastani'. Meanwhile, actors Varun Dhawan and Alia Bhatt were named Nickelodeon 'Kamaal Ki Jodi' of the year.
