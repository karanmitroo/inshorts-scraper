Madhur Bhandarkar's recently released film 'Indu Sarkar' will open the 15th Annual Bollywood Film Festival, Norway to be held in September. The film is based on the period between 1975 to 1977, when the then Prime Minister Indira Gandhi declared a state of Emergency in the country. Actors Kirti Kulhari, Neil Nitin Mukesh and Anupam Kher starred in the film.
