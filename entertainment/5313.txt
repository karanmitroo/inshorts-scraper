Legendary actor-filmmaker Raj Kapoor's grandson Aadar Jain was officially launched by Yash Raj Films as their 'new boy' on Wednesday. Pictures featuring Aadar were shared online with captions like, "Even before I was an actor. I had a godfather" and "Kapoor is not my surname but films run in my blood". YRF also launched Anya Singh as their 'new girl'.
