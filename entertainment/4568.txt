Actor Rishi Kapoor tweeted an old picture with legendary singer Mohammad Rafi on his 37th death anniversary today. Rafi made his debut in Lahore as a playback singer in the song 'Soniye Nee, Heeriye Nee' for the Punjabi film 'Gul Baloch', which released in 1944. Rafi is known for songs like 'Kya Hua Tera Wada' and 'Baharon Phool Barsao'.
