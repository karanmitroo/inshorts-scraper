Before 'Padmaavat', Sanjay Leela Bhansali's film 'Bajirao Mastani' met with opposition from workers of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) over the portrayal of Peshwa Bajirao I. Protesters also demanded a ban on his 2013 film 'Goliyon Ki Raasleela Ram-Leela' over the depiction of Kshatriyas in the film. Aamir Khan starrer 'PK' was called 'anti-Hindu' by Bajrang Dal and Shiv Sena.
