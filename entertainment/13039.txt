The motion poster of the Katrina Kaif and Sidharth Malhotra starrer 'Baar Baar Dekho' has been revealed. Directed by debutante Nitya Mehra, it is based on an original story by New York-based writer Sri Rao. The film's promotional song 'Kala Chashma' was released ahead of its trailer, which will release on August 3. The film will release on September 9.
