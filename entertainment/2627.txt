The release date of Rajinikanth and Akshay Kumar starrer '2.0' has been postponed to April 13, 2018, as tweeted by Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. The film, which also stars Amy Jackson, was earlier scheduled to release on January 25, 2018. Notably, the new release date of Akshay Kumar starrer 'Padman' was announced today as January 26, 2018.
