Filmmaker Sanjay Leela Bhansali's next film is reportedly based on Alauddin Khilji, the Khilji dynasty's second ruler and his obsessive love for Padmavati, the queen of Mewar. Tentatively titled 'Padmavati', the film will be written by Prakash Kapadia who has earlier worked with Bhansali on films including Devdas and Bajirao Mastani. The film will reportedly release on December 15, 2017.
