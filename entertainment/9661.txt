Actress Deepika Padukone has said that she hasn't asked rumoured boyfriend Ranveer Singh if he liked her Hollywood debut film 'xXx: Return of Xander Cage'. "I haven't had the time to ask anyone who has seen the film," she added. The film's premiere in India was attended by Bollywood celebrities, including Ranveer, Shahid Kapoor and Irrfan Khan, among others.
