Superstar Rajesh Khanna, whose death anniversary was on Monday, holds the record for delivering 17 consecutive superhit films in a span of three years from 1969 to 1971. Out of the 17 films, including 'Aradhana', 'Do Raste', 'Sachaa Jhutha' and 'Haathi Mere Saathi', 15 were solo superhits. The record has not been broken by any Bollywood actor till date.
