Former Miss World and Bollywood actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, who is all set make her comeback in the industry, has started shooting for 'Jazbaa'. The film, an action-thriller, directed by Sanjay Gupta also casts Irrfan, Shabana Azmi and Anupam Kher. 'Jazbaa' is said to be a remake of the South Korean film 'Seven Days'.
