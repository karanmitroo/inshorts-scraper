Punjabi film Qissa on Wednesday swept the Indian International Film Festival at Queensland (Australia). The film won the best actor award (Irrfan Khan), best actress award (Tillotama Shome), best director award (Anup Singh) and best cinematography (Sebastian Edschmid). Qissa tells the tale of a Sikh refugee Umber Singh who vows to oppose his destiny when he loses all in 1947.
