According to reports, actress Deepika Padukone will be the first guest on the upcoming tenth season of the Salman Khan-hosted reality show 'Bigg Boss'. She will reportedly be seen promoting her debut Hollywood film 'xXx: Return of Xander Cage' on the show. Deepika had also appeared as a guest in the previous season of 'Bigg Boss' to promote 'Bajirao Mastani'.
