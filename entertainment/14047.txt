'Ud-daa Punjab', the title track from the Shahid Kapoor starrer 'Udta Punjab' was released on Friday. The song has been composed by Amit Trivedi while he also lends his voice to the song along with Vishal Dadlani. It has been picturised on Shahid's character 'Tommy Singh', a Punjabi rockstar. The film also stars Alia Bhatt, Kareena Kapoor and Diljit Dosanjh.
