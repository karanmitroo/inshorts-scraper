Reacting to an 18-year old photo with Aishwarya from 'Aa Ab Laut Chalen' sets, and a picture from their recent photo shoot for 'Filmfare', actor Ranbir Kapoor said, "Dream big, dreams come true!" Ranbir and Aishwarya will be seen together onscreen for the first time in the upcoming film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil', scheduled to be released on October 28.
