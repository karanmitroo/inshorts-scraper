Priyanka Chopra starrer ‘Jai Gangaajal’ was the most booked movie on the online movie and events ticketing platform BookMyShow last week. With a 3.9 user rating on BookMyShow, the film is a sequel of the 2003 movie 'Gangaajal'. Sonam Kapoor starrer ‘Neerja’ was the second most booked movie followed by Disney’s animated film Zootopia.
