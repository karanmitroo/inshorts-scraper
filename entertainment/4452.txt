Amitabh and Abhishek Bachchan were among the celebrities who attended boxer Vijender Singh's double title bout against China's Zulpikar Maimaitiali in Mumbai on Saturday. Among other attendees were actors Randeep Hooda, Gulshan Grover, Sonu Sood and Karan Kundrra, besides yoga guru Baba Ramdev. This was Vijender Singh's ninth straight victory after he turned professional in 2015.
