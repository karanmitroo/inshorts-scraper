The Aamir Khan starrer film 'Dangal' was named the Best Film at the 62nd edition of the Filmfare Awards. 'Dangal' also won three other awards including Best Actor and Best Action awards while the film's director Nitesh Tiwari was named the Best Director. Meanwhile, the Sonam Kapoor starrer 'Neerja' won the Critics' Award For Best Film at the awards show.
