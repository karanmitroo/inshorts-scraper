'Sarbjit', which released on Friday, collected ₹13.96 crore in its opening weekend. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film collected ₹3.69 crore on Friday, ₹4.56 crore on Saturday and ₹5.71 crore on Sunday. Directed by Omung Kumar, the Randeep Hooda and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan starrer is a biopic about Sarabjit Singh, the Indian prisoner who died in Pakistan.
