Actress Asin Thottumkal has given birth to a baby girl, her first child with husband Rahul Sharma, on Tuesday. Sharing the news on Twitter, Rahul wrote, "Ecstatic to announce that Asin & I have been blessed with an Angelic Baby Girl...Thank you for all your love & wishes." Asin married Rahul, co-founder of Micromax Informatics, in January 2016.
