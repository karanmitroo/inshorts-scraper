The song 'Badri Ki Dulhania' from Varun Dhawan and Alia Bhatt's 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania' has been named the Top Trending Music Video of 2017 in India, with over 320 million views, as per a playlist released by YouTube. The song 'Tamma Tamma Again' from the same film ranked second. Meanwhile, Guru Randhawa's 'High Rated Gabru' took the third spot.
