Lauren Gottlieb, who had participated in the Season 6 and Season 7 of the celebrity dance reality show 'Jalak Dikhla Jaa', is set to join the Season 8 of the show as a judge. Lauren will be one of the four judges on 'Jhalak Dikla Jaa', whose judging panel includes actor Shahid Kapoor, director Karan Johar and choreographer Ganesh Hegde.
