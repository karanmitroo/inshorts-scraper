Ajay Devgn has said that he is happy to see Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol back on-screen. "I am very happy that they are working together. I want to see them together on-screen. They are good friends. People are waiting to see them," Devgn, who is Kajol's husband, said. SRK and Kajol will be seen together in Rohit Shetty's 'Dilwale'.
