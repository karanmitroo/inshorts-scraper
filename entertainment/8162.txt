Singer Lata Mangeshkar congratulated PM Narendra Modi and BJP President Amit Shah for winning the state Assembly elections in UP and Uttarakhand on Saturday. "Namaskar. Aadarniya Narendra Modi ji, Amit Shah ji aur BJP ke sabhi karyakartao'n ko is shaandaar jeet ki bahut badhaai," she tweeted. Replying to this, Modi wrote, "Lata Didi, thank you very much for the wishes."
