The second teaser of the Shah Rukh Khan and Alia Bhatt starrer 'Dear Zindagi', titled 'Dear Zindagi Take 2: Always Recycle' was released on Monday. The teaser introduces the film's characters, Jehangir Khan, played by Shah Rukh Khan and Kaira, portrayed by Alia Bhatt. Written and directed by Gauri Shinde, the film is scheduled to release on November 25.
