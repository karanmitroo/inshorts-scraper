Action-thriller 'Phantom', starring Saif Ali Khan and Katrina Kaif, which released on Friday minted Rs 8.46 crores in India on its first day. The film, however, had a good opening in UAE. "#Phantom has an impressive start in UAE. Day 1 is approx $ 180,000 or AED 662,000 [Rs 1.19 cr] across 62 screens", trade analyst Taran Adarsh tweeted.
