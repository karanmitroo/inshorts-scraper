Lauding Sanjay Leela Bansali's 'Goliyon Ki Raasleela Ram-leela' as the "most memorable experience" for him, superstar Amitabh Bachchan wrote on his blog that he watched the movie 3-times in 24-hour. He also added that he was in awe of the accomplishments and professionalism of the movie's cast and crew and believed that they were the "brightest future of Indian cinema".
