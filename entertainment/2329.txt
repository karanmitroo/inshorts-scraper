The release date of Shahid Kapoor's upcoming film 'Batti Gul Meter Chalu' has been announced as August 31, 2018. Directed by 'Toilet- Ek Prem Katha' director Shree Narayan Singh, the film will deal with the issue of electricity bills. Tweeting about it, Shahid had earlier written, "Let's...hope that soon electricity will be a right and not a privilege for all."
