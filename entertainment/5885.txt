Actress Shraddha Kapoor took to Instagram to share a behind-the-scenes photo from the sets of her upcoming film 'Haseena: The Queen of Mumbai'. Captioned, "When we fell in love", the picture shows Shraddha with actor Ankur Bhatia, who plays her husband Ibrahim Parkar in the film. Shraddha will play Haseena Parkar, late sister of underworld don Dawood Ibrahim.
