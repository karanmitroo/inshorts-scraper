Sacked Censor Board Chairman Pahlaj Nihalani, while talking about meeting Kangana Ranaut for the first time when he signed her for her first film 'I Love You Boss', said, "There was no Mumbaiyya influence (on) her." Pahlaj further said, "I felt she was star material...she was very open, straight-forward and transparent...she was very candid, there was no hanky-panky about her."
