Sajid Nadiadwala, the producer of 'Housefull 3' has confirmed that the 'Housefull' franchise​ will continue with 'Housefull 4'.  "It is a good franchise. Definitely, we will be doing (Housefull 4) very soon," said Nadiadwala. Meanwhile, the film's lead actor Akshay Kumar said, "It will take one, one-and-half years to begin." Notably, 'Housefull 3' has earned ₹80.10 crore in a week.
