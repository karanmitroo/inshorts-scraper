Celebrities including Priyanka Chopra, Sophia Vergara and Brie Larson wore designer wear, ranging from Monique Lhuillier to Atelier Versace, for the 22nd Screen Actors Guild (SAG) Awards. While Priyanka Chopra opted for a Monique Lhuillier lace detail gown, Sophia Vergara wore a pink Vera Wang dress. Meanwhile, Brie Larson chose to wear an Atelier Versace dress with a thigh-high slit.
