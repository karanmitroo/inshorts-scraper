As per reports, actor Shahid Kapoor's younger brother Ishaan Khattar will make his debut in the Deepika Padukone starrer film 'Floating Gardens', directed by Iranian filmmaker Majid Majidi. He will reportedly be seen playing the role of Deepika's brother in the film. Earlier, reports of Ishaan making his debut opposite Jhanvi Kapoor in the Hindi remake of 'Sairat' had emerged.
