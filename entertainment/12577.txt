'Ya Ali Murtaza', the new song from the Nawazuddin Siddiqui, Amy Jackson and Arbaaz Khan starrer 'Freaky Ali', has been released. The song has been composed by the music director duo Sajid-Wajid while Wajid along with Danish Sabri and Payal Dev sang the track. Directed by Sohail Khan, the film is scheduled to release on September 9.
