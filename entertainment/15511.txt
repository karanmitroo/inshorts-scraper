Actress Alia Bhatt has been featured on Grazia's cover for the February issue. Alia is seen wearing an Anamika Khanna bra top over a Rasa Jaipur camisole paired with blue trousers from Shift by Nimish Shah. The look is accessorised with a rust-orange pair of Jimmy Choo shoes and jewellery from international and indigenous labels including Bhane, Pippa+Bella and Fendi.
