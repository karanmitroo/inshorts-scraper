Actress Shraddha Kapoor has been roped in by the makers of 'Baaghi' to play the female lead opposite Tiger Shroff. Earlier, there were rumours that the role will be played by Diksha Patani who would make her acting debut with the film. Baaghi will be directed by Sabiir Khan, who also directed Tiger Shroff-starrer in 'Heropanti' in 2014.
