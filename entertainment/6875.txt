'Baahubali 2: The Conclusion' has ranked third at the US box office in its opening weekend, with earnings of $10.13 million (approximately ₹65 crore). It beat Hollywood film 'The Circle', starring Tom Hanks and Emma Watson, which earned $9.32 million (about ₹59.8 crore). Meanwhile, 'The Fate of the Furious' topped the box office collections for the weekend.
