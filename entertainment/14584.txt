Veteran actor Jeetendra, filmmaker Sanjay Leela Bhansali and actor Ranveer Singh were honoured with the 74th Master Dinanath Mangeshkar Awards on Sunday. While Jeetendra received the Lifetime Achievement Award, Bhansali and Ranveer were given special awards for direction and acting for 'Bajirao Mastani'. Pandit Ajay Chakravorty and Marathi actor Prashant Damle were among the other awardees.
