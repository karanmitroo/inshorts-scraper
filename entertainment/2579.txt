Shahid Kapoor and his wife Mira Rajput have featured on the cover of 'Hello!' magazine, marking their first appearance together on a magazine cover. While Mira is dressed in a mint green choli and pink lehenga by Raw Mango, Shahid is dressed in a silk suit by Anita Dongre. Mira accessorised her look with a bajubandh, choker and a kadaa.
