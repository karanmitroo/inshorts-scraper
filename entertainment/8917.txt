Amitabh Bachchan visited his alma mater Kirori Mal College of Delhi University (DU) in New Delhi to help raise funds for rebuilding the college theatre in the name of drama teacher Frank Thakurdas. The alumni association organised a felicitation ceremony at the event. Other Bollywood celebrities including Satish Kaushik, Sushant Singh, Divyendu Sharma were also present at the event.
