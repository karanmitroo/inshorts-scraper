A video shows late actress Sridevi dancing with her brother-in-law Anil Kapoor at the wedding of their nephew Mohit Marwah in Dubai. They can be seen dancing to the song 'Chittiyaan Kalaiyaan' from the 2015 film 'Roy'. Anil and Sridevi have worked together in films including 'Mr India', 'Laadla', 'Judaai' and 'Roop Ki Rani Choron Ka Raja'.
