The upcoming comedy film 'Housefull 4', which will be directed by Sajid Khan who directed the first two films in the 'Housefull' franchise, will release on Diwali 2019. Producer Sajid Nadiadwala, while sharing the news, wrote, "We have a funny thematic twist...as the plot is that of reincarnation." The cast of 'Housefull 4' is yet to be announced.
