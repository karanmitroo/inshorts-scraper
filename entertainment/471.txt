Actress Shraddha Kapoor's look from the upcoming film 'Batti Gul Meter Chalu' has been revealed. She will be seen portraying the character 'Lalita Nautiyal' in the film. Directed by 'Toilet- Ek Prem Katha' director Shree Narayan Singh, the film will also star Shahid Kapoor and actress Yami Gautam, who will be playing the role of a lawyer.
