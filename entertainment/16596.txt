Deepika Padukone and Amitabh Bachchan-starrer 'Piku', Shahid Kapoor's 'Haider' and Kangana Ranaut's 'Tanu Weds Manu Returns' will be screened at Russia's Indian Film Festival to be held in Moscow from September 3-6.  "Madras Cafe" and "Dil Dhadakne Do" will also be screened. The four-day fest aims to bridge the gap between the entertainment industries of Russia and India.
