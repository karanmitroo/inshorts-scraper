Based on the lost-and-found story of a small boy, Gyan Correa's National award-winning Gujarati film 'The Good Road' is India's official entry in the Best Foreign Film category at next year's Oscars. The film, which was chosen over 22 entries including 'The Lunchbox' and 'Bhaag Milkha Bhaag', portrays the unknown India, said Gautam Ghosh, chairman of the Oscar selection committee.
