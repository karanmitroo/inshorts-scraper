As per reports, the all India distribution rights (except Central India) of Salman Khan's upcoming film 'Tubelight' have been sold for a record ₹132 crore, making it the highest distribution deal till date. The previous record was held by Shah Rukh Khan's 'Dilwale', the rights of which were sold for ₹125 crore. 'Tubelight' is scheduled to release around Eid.
