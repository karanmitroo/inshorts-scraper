The character of Rosesh Sarabhai, played by Rajesh Kumar, in the series Sarabhai vs Sarabhai has released 'The Ultimate Momma Rap' on the occasion of Mother's Day. The rap is dedicated to the character's 'momma' Maya Sarabhai. The video 'Rosesh Ka DJwala Tribute' is a compilation of Rosesh's poems written for Maya in the first season of the show.
