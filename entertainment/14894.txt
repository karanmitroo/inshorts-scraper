Actress Madhuri Dixit is set to be the third judge for the upcoming Indian version of the international television dance show, 'So You Think You Can Dance'. The 48-year-old actress will be joining choreographers Terence Lewis and Bosco Martis as judges for the  show, which will be aired in April. Earlier, she also judged the dance show, 'Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa'.
