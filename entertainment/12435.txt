The poster of the upcoming Tamil film 'Bairavaa' starring actor Vijay has been revealed. The action thriller also stars Keerthy Suresh, Sathish and Jagapathi Babu. The film is said to have been given the tentative title 'Vijay 60' earlier as it is Vijay's 60th film. Directed by Bharathan, the film is scheduled to release on January 14, 2017.
