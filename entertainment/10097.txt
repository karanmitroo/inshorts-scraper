Actress Priyanka Chopra shared a Christmas themed picture featuring the cast of the upcoming film 'Baywatch'. "May your days be beachy and bright. Merry #Christmas from our Baywatch family to yours! #BeBaywatch," tweeted Priyanka, who plays the character Victoria Leeds in the film. Dwayne Johnson and Zac Efron were seen wearing Santa hats and swimwear in the picture.
