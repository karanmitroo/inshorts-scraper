The teaser for the Rajinikanth starrer upcoming Tamil film 'Kabali' was released on Sunday. The 65-year-old actor will be seen playing the role of an ageing don in the film. The crime drama also features Radhika Apte as Rajinikanth's wife, while Dinesh Ravi, John Vijay and Dhansika also appear in pivotal roles. The film is scheduled to release this May.
