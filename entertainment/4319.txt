Bollywood actor Sitaram Panchal, known for his role in films like 'Jolly LLB 2' and 'Paan Singh Tomar', passed away on Thursday aged 54 after suffering from lung cancer for three years. The actor had earlier appealed for financial help on Facebook for his treatment. Panchal also featured in films including 'Bandit Queen', 'Slumdog Millionaire' and 'Peepli Live'.
