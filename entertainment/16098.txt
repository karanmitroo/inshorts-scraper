In just three days of its release, Salman Khan-starrer 'Prem Ratan Dhan Payo' has collected ₹100 crore at the Indian box-office, becoming the fourth-fastest film to do so. Trade analyst Taran Adarsh tweeted, "#PremRatanDhanPayo Thu 40.35 cr, Fri 31.05 cr, Sat 30.07 cr. Total: ₹101.47 cr". The film is the fifth film of 2015 to enter the ₹100 crore club.
