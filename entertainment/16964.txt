Actor Ranbir Kapoor, who has reportedly been approached by Rajkumar Hirani to play the lead in a biopic on Sanjay Dutt, has said that it would be a challenging role to portray. "I would really look forward to getting Sanjay Dutt alive on screen. It's a very challenging character, very colourful... he's had a very challenging life", Ranbir said.
