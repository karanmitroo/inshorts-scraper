Director Rakesh Omprakash Mehra announced that his upcoming film, 'Mirzya', will release on October 7. The film will mark the debut for Anil Kapoor’s son, Harshvardhan and Tanvi Azmi’s niece, Saiyami Kher in Bollywood. The film is a love story inspired from the folk tale of the 'legend of Mirza– Sahiban’.
