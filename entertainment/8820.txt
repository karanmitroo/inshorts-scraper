Actor Anupam Kher has made his debut in short films with 'Kheer', a short film on Valentine's Day by 'Terribly Tiny Talkies'. The film has been described as "a delightful serving of love and companionship where secrets get accidentally spilt and shared". Directed by Surya Balakrishnan, the film also stars Natasha Rastogi, Abhimanyu Chawla and Stuti Dixit.
