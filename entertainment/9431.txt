A new trailer of the Akshay Kumar starrer film 'Jolly LLB 2' was released on Monday. The court drama film will feature Akshay playing the role of lawyer Jagdishwar Mishra or Jolly. Also featuring Huma Qureshi, Annu Kapoor, the film has been directed by Subhash Kapoor. Sequel to 2013 film 'Jolly LLB', it will release on February 10.
