Actor Vin Diesel, during an interview in India, referred to Ranveer Singh as Deepika Padukone's boyfriend. "Ranveer, her boyfriend, paid me this really sweet compliment," referring to an interaction with Ranveer at the premiere of 'xXx: Return of Xander Cage'. Rumours of Ranveer and Deepika dating have been circulating for the past few years, but they've never confirmed their relationship.
