The trailer for the Sonakshi Sinha and Anurag Kashyap starrer action drama film 'Akira' was released on Monday. A remake of the Tamil film 'Mouna Guru', it stars Sonakshi as the titular character and Anurag in a negative role. The AR Murugadoss directed film also features Mithun Chakraborty, Konkona Sen Sharma and Amit Sadh. 'Akira' will release on September 2.
