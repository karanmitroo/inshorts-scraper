Punjabi folk singer Manpreet Akhtar, known for the song 'Tujhe Yaad Na Mei Aayi' from the film 'Kuch Kuch Hota Hai', passed away on Sunday aged 55. Breathing her last at a Patiala hospital, Akhtar died of brain haemorrhage. Punjab Deputy CM Sukhbir Singh Badal expressed his grief over her death and many Punjabi singers visited her family.
