Supernatural erotica 'Khamoshiyan' has surpassed biopic 'Hawaizaada' and thriller 'Rahasya' at the domestic box office. 'Khamoshiyan', starring Gurmeet Choudhary, Sapna Pabbi and Ali Fazal, has reportedly minted Rs 4.13 crore in two days. Meanwhile, Ayushmann Khurrana-starrer 'Hawaizaada' and 'Rahasya', which is based on Aarushi murder case, have earned just Rs 60 lakh and Rs 25 lakh respectively.
