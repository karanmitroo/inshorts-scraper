Akshay Kumar starrer 'Singh is Bliing', which released on Friday, has become the actor's biggest opener till date. The film has minted Rs 20.67 crore on the first day of its release, confirmed Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. 'Singh is Bliing' has also become the second biggest opener of the year, after Salman Khan's 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' (Rs 27.25 crore).
