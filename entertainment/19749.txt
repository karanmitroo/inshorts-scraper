Director Anand L Rai's Raanjhanaa has reportedly become the latest entry to the coveted 100 crore club in Bollywood, grossing Rs 90 crores in domestic and Rs 10 crores in the international market. The film, which marks Dhanush's Bollywood debut, narrates the story of a Muslim girl (Sonam Kapoor) falling in love with a Hindu boy (Dhanush).
