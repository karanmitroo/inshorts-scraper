Priyanka Chopra-starrer 'Jai Gangaajal', which hit the screens on March 4, has been declared tax-free in Madhya Pradesh by the state government. Notably, 'Neerja' has also been declared tax-free in the state. The Madhya Pradesh Chief Minister's Office on Thursday made the announcement on Twitter stating that films promoting women empowerment are being made tax-free by CM Shivraj Singh Chouhan.
