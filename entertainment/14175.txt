The Naseeruddin Shah and Kalki Koechlin starrer 'Waiting', which released on Friday, is "at once heart-wrenching and uplifting", NDTV wrote. It is a film where "both Naseer and Kalki are good fits for their parts", wrote Indian Express. Hindustan Times called it "a movie about love, life and their complexities". It has been rated 2.5/5 (Indian Express) and 3.5/5 (NDTV).
