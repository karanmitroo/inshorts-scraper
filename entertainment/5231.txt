Salman Khan has shot a cameo for Varun Dhawan, Jacqueline Fernandez and Taapsee Pannu starrer Judwaa 2, a sequel to the original film Judwaa that released twenty years ago. Judwaa 2 cast shared pictures and videos with Salman on social media. Varun captioned the picture, "Shot with my childhood hero Salman Khan. I've grown old but he's definitely the same."
