Action thriller Bang Bang, which was tagged as "mindless", "loud", "dumb", "pointless" and "exhausting", has emerged as a clear winner by outshining Haider at the box office. Bang Bang collected Rs 27.54 crore on the first day while Haider did business of just Rs 6.14 crore. Hrithik and Katrina’s chemistry has overpowered the intense love story of Vishal Bhardwaj’s Haider.
