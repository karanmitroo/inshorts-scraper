The Dhanush and Kajol starrer Tamil film 'VIP 2' will release on the actor's birthday on July 28. The film is a sequel to the 2014 film 'Velai Illa Pattadhari', also known as VIP. Dhanush has penned the story and is also co-producing the film while his sister-in-law Soundarya Rajinikanth is directing it.
