A new poster of the upcoming biopic 'M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story' has been revealed. The poster shows the film's lead actor Sushant Singh Rajput posing as Dhoni, the current ODI captain of the Indian cricket team. Directed by Neeraj Pandey, the film also stars Kiara Advani and Anupam Kher and is scheduled to release on September 30.
