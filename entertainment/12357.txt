The trailer for the Prabhudeva, Sonu Sood and Tamannaah Bhatia starrer 'Tutak Tutak Tutiya' has been released. Directed by AL Vijay, the upcoming horror comedy film is also being released in Tamil as 'Devi(L)' and in Telugu as 'Abhinetri'. The film, which marks Sonu's debut as a producer, is scheduled to release on October 7.
