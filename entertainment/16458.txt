Cricketer Suresh Raina is all set to make his singing debut in Bollywood with the film “Meeruthiyan Gangsters”. The film marks the directorial debut of 'Gangs of Wasseypur' co-director Zeishan Quadri. "I love old Hindi songs and immediately came on board for this melodious track of ‘Meeruthiyan Gangsters’", Raina, who is a close friend of Quadri, said.
