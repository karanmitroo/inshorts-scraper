'Soch Na Sake', the first song of Akshay Kumar and Nimrat Kaur-starrer 'Airlift', was released on YouTube on Thursday. The song has been composed by Amaal Mallik and has been sung by Amaal Mallik, Arijit Singh & Tulsi Kumar. 'Airlift' is based on the evacuation of Indians from Kuwait in 1990. The film is scheduled to release on January 22.
