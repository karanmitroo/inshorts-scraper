Filmmaker Sajid Nadiadwala has confirmed that actors Salman Khan and Karisma Kapoor will make a special appearance in 'Judwaa 2'. The two actors starred in the 1997 film 'Judwaa'. Varun Dhawan will be playing the lead role in the film, which will also star Jacqueline Fernandez and Taapsee Pannu. Directed by David Dhawan, 'Judwaa 2' will release on September 29.
