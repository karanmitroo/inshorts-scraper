Actors Salman Khan, Anil Kapoor and Fardeen Khan will be playing triple roles in the upcoming flick ‘No Entry Mein Entry’ - sequel of the hit film ‘No Entry’. The film, to be directed by Anees Bazmee and produced by Boney Kapoor, goes on floors in Jan 2015, and also stars Bipasha Basu, Lara Dutta and Celina Jaitley.
