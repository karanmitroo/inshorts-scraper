The release date of Aamir Khan's 'PK' has been delayed and it will now hit cinema houses in Christmas 2014, producers Vinod Chopra Films and Rajkumar Hirani Films announced on Tuesday. The film, a political satire directed by Rajkumar Hirani, was earlier schedule to release in June-6. Besides Aamir, the film also stars Anushka Sharma and Sushant Rajput, among others.
