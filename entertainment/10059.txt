Information and Broadcasting minister Venkaiah Naidu and Andhra Pradesh CM Chandrababu Naidu launched the audio of the Telugu film 'Gautamiputra Satakarni', which is actor Nandamuri Balakrishna's 100th film, in Tirupati on Tuesday. "[T]his film is a great attempt to throw light on the history of Telugu people," said Venkaiah. Shriya Saran and Hema Malini were also present at the launch.
