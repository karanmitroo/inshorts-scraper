A behind-the-scenes video showing actor Shahid Kapoor's transformation into Punjab based rockstar 'Tommy Singh', his character in the upcoming film 'Udta Punjab', has been released. The video shows Shahid, who has grown his hair for the film, trying the getup for 'Tommy' which includes piercings, flashy costumes and accessories. Directed by Abhishek Chaubey, the film will release on June 17.
