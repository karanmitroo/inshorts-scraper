The teaser trailer of director Ali Abbas Zafar's upcoming film 'Gunday' has been released. Starring Ranveer Singh and Arjun Kapoor, ' Gunday' is a story about two orphans. The film that also stars Priyanka Chopra does not make a mention of her in its teaser. Produced by Yash Raj Films, 'Gunday' will release on February 14, 2014.
