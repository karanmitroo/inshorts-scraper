The first official look of Aishwarya Rai in the upcoming film 'Sarbjit' was released today. The film, a biopic of Indian prisoner Sarabjit Singh who died in a Pakistan jail in 2013, features Aishwarya as Sarabjit's sister, Dalbir Kaur. The titular role will be played by Randeep Hooda in the Omung Kumar-directorial. It is scheduled to release on May 20.
