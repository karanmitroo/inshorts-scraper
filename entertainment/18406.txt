Actor Kamal Haasan has accepted PM Narendra Modi's invitation to work towards a cleaner India, and invited nine million people to join ‘Swachh Bharat Abhiyan’. “I thank our honourable PM for naming me among this list," Kamal said. Apart from Kamal, the PM has also nominated actors Priyanka Chopra, Salman Khan and cricketer Sachin Tendulkar among others.
