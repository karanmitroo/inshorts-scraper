At a Diwali bash hosted by the Bachchan family on Sunday, November 3, Bollywood's rumored couple Ranbir Kapoor and Katrina Kaif made their first public appearance together, in the same car with a friend. Katrina was wearing a bright orange lehenga-choli while Ranbir went ethnic. Many other were also present in the bash including SRK, Gauri Khan and Anushka Sharma.
