Actress Sonakshi Sinha performed the song 'Rajj rajj ke' from her upcoming film 'Akira' live during the launch of the track at a college. The track, which has been sung by Sonakshi and Vishal Dadlani, marks Sonakshi's singing debut in films. Earlier, she had sung the song 'Ishqholic' along with Meet Bros. 'Akira' will release on September 2.
