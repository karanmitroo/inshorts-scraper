Bollywood actor Rajesh Khanna was born on December 29, 1942, in Punjab. The late actor's original name was Jatin Khanna and he made his debut with 'Aakhri Khat' (1966), which made entry into the Oscars. Khanna, the 50th Filmfare Lifetime Achievement awardee who is dubbed as Bollywood's first superstar, gave 15 consecutive superhits as a solo lead actor between 1969-1971.
