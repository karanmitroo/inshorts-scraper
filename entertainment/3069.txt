The trailer of Sidharth Malhotra and Sonakshi Sinha starrer thriller 'Ittefaq' has been released. It will also star Akshaye Khanna, who will be seen playing a police officer in the film. 'Ittefaq', which is an adaptation of the 1969 film of the same name, has been directed by late filmmaker BR Chopra's grandson Abhay Chopra.
