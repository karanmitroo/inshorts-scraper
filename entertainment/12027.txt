Actress Sushmita Sen has been served a notice by the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) over mosquito breeding spots found in the area surrounding her house in Khar, Mumbai. As per the notice, the metropolitan magistrate can levy a fine of up to ₹10,000 to ₹20,000 on the actress. Earlier, actor Shahid Kapoor received a similar notice by the BMC.
