An old CCTV footage showing actor Aditya Pancholi punching and assaulting his neighbour Bhargav Patel (55) has been released. Police said, "On Jan-15, Pancholi showed up at Patel's doorstep with a few men, dragged Patel out and punched him". Recently, the actor's young son, Sooraj, was booked by police on charges of allegedly abetting the suicide of actress Jiah Khan.
