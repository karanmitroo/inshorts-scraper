Actor Salman Khan, who topped the Forbes India Celebrity 100 list with earnings of ₹232.83 crore, earned ₹164.83 crore more than actress Priyanka Chopra, who had an annual income of ₹68 crore in 2017. The difference in their income was 109.58%. Further, Priyanka was also the only female who featured in the list's top ten.
