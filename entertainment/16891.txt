Bollywood superstar Salman Khan has reportedly booked Eid 2017 for the release of his home production 'Dabangg 3'. Salman has been releasing his films on the auspicious festival since the year 2009, with 2013 being the only exception. This Eid, Salman's 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' will hit the screens while his next film 'Sultan' will release on Eid 2016.
