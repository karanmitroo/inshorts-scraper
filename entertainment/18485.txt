Akshay Kumar and Shruti Hassan starrer ‘Gabbar’ is slated to release on April 3, next year. The film is a remake of ‘Ramanaa’ (Telugu Film) and is directed by Krish. Produced by Sanjay Leela Bhansali and Viacom18 Motion Pictures, it will be the second film where Bhansali and Akshay will be working together after the success of ‘Rowdy Rathore’.
