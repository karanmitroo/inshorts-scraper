The official trailer for the Alicia Vikander starrer period drama 'Tulip Fever' was released on Thursday. Directed by Justin Chadwick, the film is based on Deborah Moggach's novel of the same name, which explores Netherlands in the early 17th-century. Also starring Christoph Waltz, Dane DeHaan, Zach Galifianakis and Cara Delevingne, the film is scheduled to release on July 15.
