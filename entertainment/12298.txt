Actress Parineeti Chopra and tennis player Sania Mirza will be the first guests on television show 'Yaaron Ki Baarat', which will be hosted by actor Riteish Deshmukh and filmmaker Sajid Khan. It is said to be a 'light-hearted' show celebrating 'filmi' friendships with fun and games. The show will reportedly go on air in October on television channel Zee TV.
