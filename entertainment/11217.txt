The second episode of Koffee With Karan, Season 5 will feature host Karan Johar's childhood friend Twinkle Khanna and her husband actor Akshay Kumar. Star World shared the episode's promo while tweeting, "Now you know why she is @mrsfunnybones!" Twinkle is seen telling Johar that if she writes a book on Bollywood, she will call it 'Tentacles to Testicles'.
