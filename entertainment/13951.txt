The Randeep Hooda and Kajal Aggarwal starrer 'Do Lafzon Ki Kahani', which released on Friday, is "a mushy romantic film that echoes the fairytale idea of love" wrote Hindustan Times. NDTV called it a "cliche-ridden melodrama" while Times of India wrote, "Guilt-ridden... bruised, he (Randeep) essays his role with conviction". It was rated 2.5/5 (HT) and 2/5 (TOI).
