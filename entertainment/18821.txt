Akshay Kumar and Sonakshi Sinha starrer ‘Holiday’ - remake of the Tamil film Thupakki - hit cinemas this friday. The action thriller, directed by AR Murugadoss, revolves around the life of a soldier. It has been rated 1.5 (Indian Express), 2 (India Today, NDTV) 2.5 (Zee News) and 3 (Times of India).
