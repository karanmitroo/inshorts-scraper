Filmmaker R Balki, who is directing the Akshay Kumar starrer 'Padman', has said that the life of real 'Padman' Arunachalam Muruganantham is equal to 'Sholay' and is as entertaining as the "biggest masala entertainers". Talking about the film which focuses on menstruation and women's health, Balki added, "I...feel sanitary napkins as a subject has not been touched in this country."
