The real life father-daughter duo, Shatrughan Sinha and Sonakshi Sinha, will be seen portraying the same relationship onscreen in 'Akira'. As per reports, Shatrughan's character dies while Sonakshi's character is still a child and they never share screen space in the film. However, on Shatrughan's demand, efforts to pen down one such sequence featuring them together are on.
