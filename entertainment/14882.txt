'Paani Paani', the first song from the upcoming romantic dance film 'Cabaret' was released by T-Series on Tuesday. The song features the lead actress Richa Chadda. Directed by Kaustav Narayan Niyogi, the film also stars Gulshan Devaiah and will mark the acting debut of cricketer S Sreesanth in Bollywood. The film is scheduled to release on June 5.
