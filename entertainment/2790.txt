Actors Farhan Akhtar and Sanjay Dutt will star in Ajay Devgn's upcoming production, which will be a remake of National Award-winning 2014 Tamil film 'Jigarthanda'. While Farhan will play the role of an assistant director, Sanjay will portray a gangster in the film. It will be directed by Nishikant Kamat, director of the Ajay Devgn starrer 2015 film 'Drishyam'.
