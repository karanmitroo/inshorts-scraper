The Ryan Gosling and Emma Stone starrer 'La La Land' won the People’s Choice Award at the Toronto International Film Festival (TIFF) 2016. The Dev Patel and Nicole Kidman starrer 'Lion' came in second while Mira Nair’s 'Queen of Katwe', starring Lupita Nyong'o and Madina Nalwanga, ranked third. Meanwhile, the Natalie Portman starrer 'Jackie' won the Platform Prize at TIFF.
