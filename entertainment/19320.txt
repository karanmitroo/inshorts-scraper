The trailer of Kangana Ranaut starrer 'Queen,' which was released on Friday, has crossed 1 lakh views on YouTube. The trailer shows Rani (played by Kangana) preparing for her pehli shaadi, and most importantly, her pehla honeymoon in Amsterdam and Paris. Rajkumar Yadav, who was last seen in 'Kai Po Che!' and 'Shahid', is playing Kangana's beloved.
