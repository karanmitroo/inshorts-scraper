Evergreen actors Amitabh and Jaya Bachchan will play a cameo in R Balki’s next, “Ki and Ka”. The Bachchans will play themselves in the film, where their scenes will be shot in their own house. The film stars Kareena Kapoor and Arjun Kapoor in the lead roles. Amitabh Bachchan also played a cameo in Balki’s production “English Vinglish”.
