Fashion magazine 'Harper's Bazaar' misspelled actress Deepika Padukone's name as 'Peedika Padukone' in its article which featured the list of 'World's Highest-Paid Actresses 2016' by Forbes. Deepika, with earnings of $10 million, was the only Indian actress to feature on the list at the tenth spot. Notably, Padukone has featured in the Indian edition of Harper's Bazaar several times.
