Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Thursday attended the wedding reception of Indian captain Virat Kohli and his wife, actress Anushka Sharma, which was held at the Taj Diplomatic Enclave in New Delhi. Kapil Dev, Yuvraj Singh, Gautam Gambhir, Suresh Raina and Virender Sehwag were also expected to attend the reception. Singer Gurdas Maan also performed at the event.
