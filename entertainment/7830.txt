The Alia Bhatt and Varun Dhawan starrer 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania' has entered the ₹100 crore club in 15 days since its release on March 10. This is Varun's third film after 'ABCD 2' and 'Dilwale' to earn over ₹100 crore while Alia's only other film in the ₹100 crore club is '2 States'. The film was directed by Shashank Khaitan.
