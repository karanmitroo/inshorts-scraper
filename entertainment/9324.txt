Filmmaker Karan Johar, while praising Hrithik Roshan's performance in 'Kaabil', said that the actor has delivered his best in the film. "As a filmmaker it's exhilarating to watch an actor deliver his best....Hrithik is a powerhouse talent and is exemplary in #kaabil," he tweeted.  'Kaabil' tells the story of a visually-challenged man driven to avenge the murder of his wife.
