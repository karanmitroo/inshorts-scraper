Ranbir Kapoor, Anushka Sharma and Karan Johar starrer 'Bombay Velvet', an Anurag Kashyap directorial, managed to make a business of Rs 16 crore in the opening weekend. Disclosing the film's box-office business on Twitter, Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh posted, "#BombayVelvet is a DEBACLE! Fri 5.20 cr, Sat 5.10 cr, Sun 5.80 cr. Total: Rs.16.10 cr. India biz."
