Actors Ranbir Kapoor, Ranveer Singh and Karan Johar attended the 32nd birthday celebrations of actor Arjun Kapoor. Aditya Roy Kapur and Harshvardhan Kapoor were also spotted at Arjun's residence. Son of Boney Kapoor and Mona Kapoor, Arjun worked as an assistant director in films including Kal Ho Naa Ho, before pursuing acting.
