A desi version of Marilyn Monroe's famous 'Happy Birthday Mr President' rendition, that she sang  for JF Kennedy in 1962, has been attempted by Mallika Sherawat, on Narendra Modi's 64th birthday. The actress, who is to be seen in her upcoming show 'The Bachelorette India' gave her version, cooing "Happy birthday Narender-ji, Happy Birthday Narender Modi-ji".
