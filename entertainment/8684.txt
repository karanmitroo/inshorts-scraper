The teaser of the Rajkummar Rao starrer survival drama film 'Trapped' has been released. It is about a man, played by Rao, who gets trapped inside a house for days without food, water and electricity. Directed by Vikramaditya Motwane, who is known for directing films like 'Udaan' and 'Lootera', the film's trailer will be released on February 22.
