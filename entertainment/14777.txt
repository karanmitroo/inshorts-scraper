Priyadarshini Chatterjee from Guwahati was crowned the Miss India World 2016, at the 53rd edition of the beauty pageant held at the Yash Raj Studios on Saturday. Sushruthi Krishna from Bengaluru and Pankhuri Gidwani from Uttar Pradesh became the first and second runners-up respectively. Chatterjee will now represent India at the International platform of Miss World 2016.
