'Har Mod Par Umeed Hai', a new song from the upcoming Kalki Koechlin and Sumeet Vyas starrer 'Ribbon', has been released. The song has been composed by Sagar Desai, with lyrics by Puneet Sharma and vocals by Jasleen Kaur Royal. Directed and co-written by Rakhee Sandilya, 'Ribbon' revolves around a young couple who have to deal with an unplanned pregnancy.
