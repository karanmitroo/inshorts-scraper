According to reports, the teaser of Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif starrer Tiger Zinda Hai, sequel to 2012 film Ek Tha Tiger, will be played during Judwaa 2's screening in theatres. Judwaa 2 is a remake of Judwaa and both have been directed by David Dhawan. A source added, "It all ties in... as Salman and David are old buddies."
