Forty years after its release, Amitabh and Dharmendra-starrer 'Sholay' is all set to be released in Pakistan. People in the country have only seen the pirated version of the film, owner of Mandviwalla Entertainment Nadeem Mandviwalla said. Mandivwalla plans to release it on March 23. "We feel the audience will want to come watch it, given the opportunity," he said.
