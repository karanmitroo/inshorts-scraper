Actress Shraddha Kapoor has been roped in by producer Ritesh Sidhwani to play the female lead in Farhan Akhtar and Arjun Kapoor starrer 'Rock On 2'. Earlier, there were speculations that the role will be played by Alia Bhatt. However, confirming the news, Shraddha said, "I am extremely excited to be a part of 'Rock On' sequel."
