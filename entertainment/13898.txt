Actor Shahid Kapoor has called filmmaker Vishal Bhardwaj his 'Martin Scorsese', while comparing Bhardwaj to the iconic Hollywood filmmaker. "It’s an amazing opportunity to work with Vishal... He has cast me for the third time so, it’s very cool," said Shahid, who will be seen in Bhardwaj's next film 'Rangoon'. Further, Shahid called 'Rangoon' a "different yet mainstream film".
