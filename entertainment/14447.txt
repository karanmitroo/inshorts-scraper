The Vidya Balan starrer 'Kahaani 2' will release on November 25, as per a tweet by Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. The film, which also stars Arjun Rampal, is a sequel to the 2014 thriller 'Kahaani'. Directed by Sujoy Ghosh, the film's crew is currently shooting in Kolkata, which was also the setting for the first film.
