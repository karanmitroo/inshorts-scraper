Several Bollywood actors took to Twitter to praise the Indian Army and the government for its action against terrorists across the Line of Control. Shah Rukh Khan wrote, "Thnx Indian army for the action against terrorism" while Amitabh Bachchan tweeted, "Don't mess with the Indian Army!!". "Bravo PM @narendramodi. Congratulations on your firm action," wrote Shatrughan Sinha.
