Yash Raj Films (YRF) on Wednesday launched India's first transgender band, named the '6 Pack Band', consisting of six musicians from the transgender community. YRF's youth wing 'Y-Films' has also released the band's debut single song ‘Hum Hain Happy’ on its official YouTube channel. Sonu Nigam will release a single ‘Rab De Bande’ in collaboration with the band, Y-Films said.
