'Zinda Hai', a new song from the upcoming Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif starrer 'Tiger Zinda Hai', has been released. The song has been composed by music composer duo Vishal and Shekhar with vocals by Sukhwinder Singh and rap by Raftaar, and lyrics by Irshad Kamil. Directed by Ali Abbas Zafar, the film is scheduled to release on December 22.
