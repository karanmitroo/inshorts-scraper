Actor Salman Khan has become the only Bollywood actor to have two films in the ₹300 crore club. 'Sultan' is Salman's second film to mint over ₹300 crore at the box-office after the 2015 film 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan'. Salman, who made his debut with the 1989 film 'Biwi Ho To Aisi', has appeared in over a hundred films till now.
