Acting Chairman of the International Film Festival of India (IFFI) Rahul Rawail has claimed that the director of 'S Durga' Sanal Kumar Sasidharan planned the "whole drama" of adding "sexy" to the film's title to gain publicity. Rawail added, "Sanal had started publicising his film as 'Sxxx Durga' and sold it in a day, thanks to the xxx connotation."
