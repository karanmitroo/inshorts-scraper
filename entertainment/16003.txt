French President Francois Hollande and PM Narendra Modi have launched Grammy winner Ricky Kej’s new music album “Shanti Samsara” at the Paris climate change conference. The album has music for "environmental consciousness" and features 300 musicians from across the world, including Amitabh Bachchan and Shankar Mahadevan. Its features include folk music and verses from Zulu, Turkey and by Rumi.
