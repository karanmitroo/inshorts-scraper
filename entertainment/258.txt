Mourning the sudden demise of Sridevi, actor and politician Kamal Haasan tweeted, "Sadma's lullaby haunts me now...We'll miss her." She was his co-star in the 1983 film 'Sadma'. He further said, "Have witnessed Sridevi's life from an adolescent teenager to the magnificent lady she became...Many happy moments with her flash through my mind including the last time I met her."
