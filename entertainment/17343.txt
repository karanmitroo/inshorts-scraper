Comedian Kapil Sharma's show 'Comedy Nights with Kapil' is set to take a leap of one year, confirmed his rumoured girlfriend Preeti Simoes. Reportedly, dadi (Ali Asgar) will lose her memory, bua (Upasana Singh) will become a millionaire and Gutthi (Sunil Grover) will run her own beauty parlour. The changes are being brought to add freshness to the show.
