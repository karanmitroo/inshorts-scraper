As per reports, actress Shraddha Kapoor will star opposite Shahid Kapoor in the upcoming film 'Batti Gul Meter Chalu'. The film will reportedly feature Shahid and Shraddha as lawyers. Directed by 'Toilet: Ek Prem Katha' director Shree Narayan, the female lead was earlier reportedly offered to Katrina Kaif, who opted out of the film owing to prior commitments.
