Filmmaker Karan Johar, while speaking about the success of 'Baahubali 2', said that the film's director SS Rajamouli has become India's biggest superstar. He added, "I feel short of words when I speak about 'Baahubali'. It has become a milestone film." Notably, Johar is the distributor of the Hindi versions of 'Baahubali' film franchise.
