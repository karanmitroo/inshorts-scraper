The trailer of the Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor starrer upcoming romantic drama film 'Befikre' has been released. Ranveer and Vaani will portray the characters 'Dharam' and 'Shyra' respectively. The film marks Aditya Chopra's return as a director after eight years, following the 2008 film 'Rab Ne Bana Di Jodi'. 'Befikre' is scheduled to release on December 9.
