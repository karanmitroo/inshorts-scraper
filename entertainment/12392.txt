Actor Amitabh Bachchan has featured on the cover of the September edition of 'Hi! Blitz' India magazine. The actor can be seen in a checked suit with a printed shirt and navy blue tie from 'The Arvind Store'. He has been photographed by Avinash Gowariker. The cover story on Bachchan is titled 'Amitabh Bachchan: The Colossus'.
