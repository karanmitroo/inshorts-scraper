A new trailer for the upcoming 3D science fiction action-comedy film 'Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Out of the Shadows' was released on Tuesday. Featuring Megan Fox, Stephen Amell and Will Arnett, the film is a sequel to the 2014 film 'Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles'. Directed by Dave Green, the film is scheduled to release on June 3.
