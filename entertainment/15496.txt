A song entitled 'Main Woh Chaand' from the Himesh Reshammiya-starrer Bollywood movie 'Teraa Suroor 2' was among the most shared YouTube videos in India on Sunday. The video was posted on 29th January and has garnered more than 6,00,000 views. This was the first video song from the movie, that is set to be released on 11th March 2016.
