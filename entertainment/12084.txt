Actor Akshay Kumar, in order to wish his 'Housefull' film series co-star Riteish Deshmukh luck for the film 'Banjo', has shared a picture of them wearing wigs. "Just when you thought your look had become soooo cool @Riteishd, I dig up this beauty", tweeted Akshay. Riteish had sported long hair for his look as a band member in 'Banjo'.
