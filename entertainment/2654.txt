Kalki Koechlin and Richa Chadha starrer 'Jia Aur Jia' is an "utter bore that borders on torture", wrote Hindustan Times (HT). NDTV said, "'Jia Aur Jia' is a disaster from the very outset." "[The film] lacks humour, heart and a story," wrote The Times of India (TOI). It has been rated 0.5/5 (HT), 1/5 (NDTV) and 1.5/5 (TOI).
