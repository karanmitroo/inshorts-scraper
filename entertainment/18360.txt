Several Bollywood actors including Aamir Khan, Sushant Singh Rajput and Parineeti Chopra Saturday came together for a concert titled HUM HAIN... Umeed-e-Kashmir - in Delhi to raise funds for the flood victims of Kashmir, Assam and Visakhapatnam. The initiative was led by the Event and Entertainment Management Association (EEMA), the Film and Television Producers Guild and ZEE.
