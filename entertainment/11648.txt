Bollywood actress Hema Malini along with husband Dharmendra converted to Islam to get married after they fell in love while filming Sholay in 1975. They decided to convert to Islam as Dharmendra's first wife, Prakash Kaur, refused to divorce him. The couple has two daughters, Esha Deol and Ahana Deol. Hema Malini turned 68 years old on Sunday.
