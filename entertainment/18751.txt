Actress and former dancer, Zohra Sehgal, who was also known as the grand old lady of Bollywood, died at a hospital in New Delhi on Thursday, aged 102. She had acted in many films including Veer-Zara, Saaya, Cheeni-Kum and Hum Dil De Chuke Sanam. Sehgal had received the Padma Shri, Padma Bhushan and Padma Vibhushan, among other awards.
