'Angry Mix', the first song from the Amitabh Bachchan starrer upcoming film 'Sarkar 3' has been released. The song has been composed by Ravi Shankar with vocals by Sukhvinder Singh and Mika Singh. Directed by Ram Gopal Varma and also starring Jackie Shroff, Manoj Bajpayee and Yami Gautam, 'Sarkar 3' is scheduled to release on April 7.
