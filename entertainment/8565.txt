Actor Shahid Kapoor featured as a background dancer in 'Kahin Aag Lage' song from the 1999 film 'Taal'. The actor, who turned 36 on Saturday, made his big screen debut with the movie 'Ishq Vishk' in 2003 and was awarded Filmfare for Best Male Debut for his role. He is best known for his movies 'Jab We Met' and 'Haider'.
