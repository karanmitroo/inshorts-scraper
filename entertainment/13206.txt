The trailer of the upcoming superhero film 'Justice League' has been released. It is based on the DC Comics superhero team and its trailer features Ben Affleck as 'Batman', Gal Gadot as 'Wonder Woman', Ezra Miller as 'The Flash', Ray Fisher as 'Cyborg' and Jason Momoa as 'Aquaman'. Directed by Zack Snyder, the film will release on November 17, 2017.
