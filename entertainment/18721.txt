The first poster of the Priyanka Chopra starer biopic 'Mary Kom' has been launched. The film, where Priyanka plays the role of the Olympic medalist boxer Mary Kom, is set to hit the theaters on Sep-5. "Heart. Blood. Sweat. Soul. I've given it my all. Over to you guys now! #MaryKomFirstLook #FightLikeAGirl ..here goes", tweeted the actress, with the poster.
