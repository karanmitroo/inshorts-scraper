Late singer Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan's song 'Mere Rashke Qamar' has been recreated for the upcoming film 'Baadshaho'. Actors Ajay Devgn and Ileana D'Cruz have featured in the song. The song has been sung by Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan, with additional vocals by Rahat Fateh Ali Khan. It has been composed by Tanishk Bagchi, with lyrics by Manoj Muntashir.
