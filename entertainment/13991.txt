The Viral Fever CoCan Studio has released the Hindi version of the Game Of Thrones theme song. The song is in collaboration with Shivam Shankar, Mahesh Raghavan and Karan Chitra Deshmukh, who earlier made their own renditions of the theme song. For this version, Shivam is the lyricist, Mahesh plays the theme on an iPad while Deshmukh plays the tabla.
