While sharing his experience of working with late actress Sridevi, Rishi Kapoor said, "I don't think there has ever been a female actor in Bollywood who attained such glory and marvellous heights." He further said, "She was a complete actor...a fabulous dancer and a person who could just switch faces." Rishi worked with Sridevi in films like 'Chandni' and 'Nagina'.
