The teaser poster of Diana Penty's upcoming romantic comedy 'Happy Bhaag Jayegi' was released on Thursday. Directed by Mudassar Aziz, 'Happy Bhaag Jayegi' will mark Diana's return to Bollywood after her 2012 debut film 'Cocktail'. Also starring Abhay Deol and Ali Fazal in lead roles, the film is slated to release on August 19.
