To bring in the chemistry between Sushant Singh Rajput and Parineeti Chopra for the song 'Tere Mere Beech Mein kya Hai' in the movie 'Shuddh Desi Romance', director Maneesh Sharma came up with his unique tactics to lock Sushant and Parineeti together in a room for 3 days. The movie, produced by Aditya Chopra, will release on September 6.
