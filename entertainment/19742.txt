Sonam Kapoor, enjoying blockbusters Raanjhanaa and Bhaag Milkha Bhaag,  reportedly auditioned for the fifth-part of the popular Pirates of the Caribbean franchise which will also star Johnny Depp. Buzz is that Sonam was recommended for the auditions by her actor-father Anil Kapoor, when an Indian celebrity management company contacted him on behalf of the makers of the Hollywood film.
