The character played by actor Akshaye Khanna in the upcoming film 'Dishoom' has been introduced in a new promotional video. The film, which stars John Abraham and Varun Dhawan in lead roles, features Akshaye as the villain 'Wagha' who kidnaps an Indian cricket player, played by Saqib Saleem. Akshaye's last film was the 2012 comedy 'Gali Gali Chor Hai'.
