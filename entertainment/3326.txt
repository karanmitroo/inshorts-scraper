The trailer of the upcoming Ajay Devgn, Parineeti Chopra and Tabu starrer 'Golmaal Again', which comes seven years after the last film in the franchise, has been released. Actors from 'Golmaal 3' Arshad Warsi, Tusshar Kapoor, Shreyas Talpade and Kunal Kemmu have been retained in the fourth instalment. Directed by Rohit Shetty, 'Golmaal Again' will release on October 20.
