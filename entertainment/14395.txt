Filmmaker Anurag Kashyap has said that he does not want a single cut from the Censor Board in his latest film 'Raman Raghav 2.0. "Censor Board has been... kindest to this film. They actually liked it... To avoid any kind of cuts whatever it will take, we will do," said Kashyap. Starring Nawazuddin Siddiqui, it will release on July 24.
