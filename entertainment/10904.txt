Actresses Jacqueline Fernandez and Taapsee Pannu will feature in 'Judwaa 2', actor Varun Dhawan confirmed on Twitter. Sequel to 1997 Salman Khan-starrer 'Judwaa', it will feature Varun in a double role for the first time. Directed by Varun's father David Dhawan, 'Judwaa 2' marks the second time the father-son duo will be working together after 2014 film 'Main Tera Hero'.
