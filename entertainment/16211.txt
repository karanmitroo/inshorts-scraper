Television actor and Bigg Boss 8 winner Gautam Gulati is set to play the role of cricketer Ravi Shastri in the upcoming film 'Azhar'. Confirming the news, Gulati said, "Yes, I'm doing it, but can't talk about it in detail." The film is based on the life of former Indian skipper Mohammad Azharuddin who was implicated in a match-fixing scandal.
