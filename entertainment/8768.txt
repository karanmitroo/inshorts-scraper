'Humsafar', a new song from the Alia Bhatt and Varun Dhawan starrer film 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania' has been released. The song has been composed by Akhil Sachdeva, with vocals by Akhil Sachdeva and Mansheel Gujral, and lyrics penned by Akhil Sachdeva. Directed by Shashank Khaitan, the romantic comedy film is scheduled to release on March 10.
