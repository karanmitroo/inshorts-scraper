A new song titled 'Bhangda Pa' from Tiger Shroff's upcoming superhero film 'A Flying Jatt' has been released. The song has been composed by music composer duo Sachin-Jigar and sung by singers Vishal Dadlani, Divya Kumar and Asees Kaur. The lyrics have been penned by Mayur Puri. Also starring Jacqueline Fernandez, 'A Flying Jatt' will release on August 25.
