Katrina Kaif, Kareena Kapoor and Miss World Manushi Chhillar were among the personalities who attended the Filmfare Glamour & Style Awards. While Katrina wore a gown by Jean Paul Gaultier, Kareena was seen in a white sheer gown by Galia Lahav. Meanwhile, Manushi chose a gown by Falguni Shane Peacock. Deepika Padukone and Sonam Kapoor also attended the event.
