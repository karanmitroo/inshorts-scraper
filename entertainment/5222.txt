Arjun Kapoor has tweeted that actor Akshay Kumar is a true khiladi. He said this on being asked to describe Akshay in one word during an Ask Me Anything session on Twitter. Akshay has done numerous films like Khiladi (1992), Main Khiladi Tu Anari (1994), International Khiladi (1999), among others, which have the word 'khiladi' in the title.
