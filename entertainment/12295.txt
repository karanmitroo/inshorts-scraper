'Jaago', the first song from the Farhan Akhtar and Arjun Rampal starrer 'Rock On 2' has been released. The song has been composed by Shankar, Ehsaan and Loy. It has been sung by Farhan Akhtar and Siddharth Mahadevan with lyrics penned by Javed Akhtar. Directed by Shujaat Saudagar and also starring Shraddha Kapoor, the film will release on November 11.
