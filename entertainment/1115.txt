Sanjay Leela Bhansali, while speaking about how he coped with the row over his directorial 'Padmaavat', said, "It's Lataji's (Mangeshkar) songs and cigarettes which have kept me going all these months." The film has been facing protests from groups like Rajput organisation Karni Sena. 'Padmaavat', which will reportedly release on January 25, was earlier scheduled to release on December 1.
