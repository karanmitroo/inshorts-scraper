Actress Rani Mukerji turned 38 on Monday. Rani started her acting career with a supporting role in the Bengali film 'Biyer Phool' (1996), directed by her father Ram Mukherjee. She made her Bollywood debut in the 1997 film 'Raja Ki Aayegi Baraat'. Last seen in the film 'Mardaani' (2014), Rani was honoured with the Rajiv Gandhi Award in 2004.
