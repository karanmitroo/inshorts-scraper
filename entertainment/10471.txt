A new poster from the upcoming romantic drama film 'OK Jaanu', starring actors Shraddha Kapoor and Aditya Roy Kapur has been released. It is an official remake of Mani Ratnam's Tamil film 'OK Kanmani' and has been directed by Shaad Ali. Also starring Naseeruddin Shah and Leela Samson, the film is scheduled to release on January 13, 2017.
