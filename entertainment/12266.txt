Tickets worth ₹5,000 for the upcoming 'Global Citizen Festival' featuring British band 'Coldplay' were sold out within a few hours of ticketing website 'Book My Show' initiating the booking process. The website started the next 'phases' of tickets which were worth ₹7,500-₹15,000, which have been sold out as well. Currently, tickets worth ₹35,000 and above are available on the website.
