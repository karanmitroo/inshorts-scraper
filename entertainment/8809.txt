As per reports, actor Amitabh Bachchan has been cast in the superhero film 'Dragon', also starring Ranbir Kapoor and Alia Bhatt. According to reports, Bachchan will play a pivotal role in the film and start shooting for it this year. Directed by Ayan Mukerji, Ranbir will play a superhero with powers to emit fire from his hand in the film.
