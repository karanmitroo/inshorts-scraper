Actor Ranveer Singh will be seen as Deepika Padukone's dead husband, in Homi Adajania's next flick 'Finding Fanny'. A glimpse of the actor can be seen in the trailer. Singh also posted a pic of him in the Finding-Fanny-look on Twitter. 
The movie, expected to release on Sep-12, stars Deepika Padukone, Arjun Kapoor, Dimple Kapadia, Naseeruddin Shah and Pankaj Kapur.
