Filmmaker Karan Johar will join actors Mark Wahlberg and Isabelle Huppert for the 41st Toronto International Film Festival in September. He will be a part of the 'In Conversation With' segment, which features on-stage conversations with members of the film industry. Notably, Karan's film 'Kabhi Alvida Naa Kehna' was screened at the 31st edition of the festival in 2006.
