Ranbir Kapoor and Anushka Sharma Monday unveiled the second trailer of their upcoming film 'Bombay Velvet'. The Anurag Kashyap directorial, releasing May 15, also features film-maker Karan Johar. Karan, who plays a negative role in the film, was also present at the event. "I think it's been the best experience of my life being directed by Anurag Kashyap," Johar said.
