Actors Sidharth Malhotra and Sonakshi Sinha will reportedly star in a remake of the 1969 murder mystery 'Ittefaq'. As per reports, filmmaker BR Chopra's grandson, Abhay Chopra is set to produce a remake of the film with Shah Rukh Khan and Karan Johar. Abhay's brother Kapil will co-produce the film. 'Ittefaq' (1969) featured Rajesh Khanna and Nanda in lead roles.
