Actress Alia Bhatt, while interacting with a journalist during the trailer launch of the film 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania', said, "You seem quite shocked that I look intelligent in the film." Alia plays a character based in Kota, Rajasthan in the film. Alia added, "You're saying it like... Finally ban gayi tum intelligent film mein. I am quite the intelligent one."
