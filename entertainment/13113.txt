As per reports, actor Rajinikanth is set to earn ₹80 crore as total remuneration for his latest film 'Kabali', making him the highest paid actor in India. While he was paid ₹35 crore as remuneration from the film's budget of ₹110 crore, he will also reportedly get ₹45 crore as share from the film's collection at the box-office.
