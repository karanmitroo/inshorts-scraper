The list of contestants of 'Bigg Boss 9' has been revealed, as per media reports. Actor Ranbir Kapoor's cousin Armaan Jain, who appeared in film 'Lekar Hum Deewana Dil', is one of the listed contestants. Some other names on the list are- Mohit Malhotra, Sunil Grover, India's first gay brand ambassador Nakshatra Bagwe and Salman Khan's look-alike Najeem Khan.
