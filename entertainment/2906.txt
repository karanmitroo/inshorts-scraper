Vidya Balan, while tweeting about her film 'Bhool Bhulaiyaa' completing 10 years of release on Thursday, wrote, "Thank you all for all your love for Manjulika." While sharing a clip from the film which shows her character Manjulika dancing, Vidya further wrote, "Thank you Bhool Bhulaiyaa team & Priyadarshan sir for this film that has brought me so much love."
