A video featuring the behind-the-scenes footage from the Akshay Kumar starrer 'Rustom' has been released.  The video features Akshay along with other cast members including Ileana D'Cruz, Esha Gupta and Arjan Bajwa. Commenting on Ileana's character, who cheats on Akshay's character, the actor said, "I go to work and she goes to somebody else's house. My god!"
