Actor Anil Kapoor's son Harshvardhan Kapoor will be starring in the upcoming film 'Bhavesh Joshi', to be directed by Vikramaditya Motwane. "The film will be going on floors from next month,” said Motwane. Notably, Harshvardhan is set to make his acting debut in Bollywood with filmmaker Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra’s upcoming film 'Mirzya'.
