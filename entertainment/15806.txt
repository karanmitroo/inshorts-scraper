On Bollywood actor-producer Kamaal R Khan's birthday, #HappyBirthdayKRK was trending on Twitter in India on Friday. Actor Riteish Deshmukh tweeted "From One Villain to Another - Happy Birthday dear @kamaalrkhan - - have a great one and a super year ahead". Some users trolled the trend with a tweet saying "Breaking News: Twitter reaches its new low! #HappyBirthdayKRK is trending!"
