'Teen Gawah', the new song from the Harshvardhan Kapoor and Saiyami Kher starrer 'Mirzya' has been released. The song has been composed by music composer trio Shankar, Ehsaan and Loy. It has been sung by Siddharth Mahadevan and Sain Zahoor with lyrics penned by Gulzar. Directed by Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra, 'Mirzya' is scheduled to release on October 7.
