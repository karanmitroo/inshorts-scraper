Actress Sunny Leone beat Priyanka Chopra to become the most-searched female celebrity in 2017, as revealed by Yahoo India. Other names on the list include Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Katrina Kaif, Deepika Padukone, Disha Patani and Esha Gupta. Notably, the actress was the most-searched personality in 2016, beating other personalities like PM Narendra Modi and Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal.
