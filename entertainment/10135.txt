'Dangal', starring Aamir Khan, Fatima Sana Sheikh and Sanya Malhotra, has become the second highest opening day grosser in Bollywood this year. It earned ₹29.78 crore, as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. While 'Dangal' surpassed ₹21.30 crore minted by 'M.S.Dhoni: The Untold Story', it failed to replace 'Sultan' from the top spot as the film had collected ₹36.54 crore.
