Bollywood's rumoured couple Ranbir Kapoor and Katrina Kaif reportedly threw a house-warming party, on Sunday night, after moving in together in a flat in Bandra. According to sources close to the actors, famous names of the industry including Ayan Mukherji, Hrithik Roshan, Kangna Ranaut, Anushka Sharma, Kabir Khan, Mini Mathur and Karisma Kapoor turned up for the party.
