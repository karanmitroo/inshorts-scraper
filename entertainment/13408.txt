A musical tribute to the recently killed Pakistani qawwali singer Amjad Sabri will be held on July 17 at Kamani Auditorium in New Delhi. Sufi singer Hamsar Hayat Nizami and Sufi Hayat Brothers and Haji Iqbalshad Sabri and Dilshad Sabri of the Sabri Sufi Brothers will perform famous Qawwalis of Amjad Sabri. The tickets are priced between ₹565 to ₹2261.
