The makers of the Deepika Padukone, Ranveer Singh and Shahid Kapoor starrer upcoming film 'Padmavati' have cleared all issues with Rajput organisations in Jaipur, as revealed by an official statement released by the makers. "We have clearly stated that there is no romantic scene or song or dream sequence between Rani Padmavati and Alauddin Khilji," read the statement.
