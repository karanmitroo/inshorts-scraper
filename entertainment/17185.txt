Aishwarya Rai Bachchan will unveil the first look of her upcoming film 'Jazbaa' at the Cannes Film Festival 2015 where she will be representing fashion-brand L'Oreal as its ambassador. Director Sanjay Gupta took to Twitter to make the announcement and posted, "Just 3 days to go before we reveal the first glimpse of our labour of love JAZBAA at Cannes.''
