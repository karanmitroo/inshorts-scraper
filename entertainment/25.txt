As per reports, actor Abhay Deol will play Katrina Kaif's love interest in the upcoming film 'Zero'. Katrina and Abhay had earlier starred together in Zoya Akhtar's 2011 film 'Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara'. 'Zero', which also stars Shah Rukh Khan and Anushka Sharma, is scheduled to release on December 21, 2018.
