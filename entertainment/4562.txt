Actress Kajol, who completed 25 years in Bollywood today, shared an old picture on Instagram from 1992, the year her first film 'Bekhudi' released. Kajol captioned the picture, "Throwback to 25 years back. So much love for so long. Truly humbled!" Kajol, who is a Padma Shri awardee, is best known for roles in films like 'Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge'.
