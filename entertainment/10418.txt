A behind-the-scenes video shows actress Sakshi Tanwar as 'Daya Kaur' in the upcoming film 'Dangal'. Aamir Khan, who plays her husband Mahavir Singh Phogat in the film, revealed that he suggested Sakshi's name for the role based on his mother's recommendation, who loved Sakshi in television serials. Sakshi said that her experience of working in the film was "superb".
