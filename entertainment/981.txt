Actress Kareena Kapoor Khan has turned showstopper for fashion designer Vikram Phadnis' show in Doha, Qatar. The actress was seen in a pink lehenga which was paired with pearl earrings, a bejewelled choker, a maang tika and a statement ring. With Vikram's show, Kareena is said to have become the first Bollywood actor to walk the ramp in Doha.
