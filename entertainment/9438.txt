Hammad Shoaib, who hails from Pakistan and is said to resemble Bollywood actor Ranveer Singh, has said that the resemblance is God-gifted. He said, "I don't imitate Ranveer Singh but I like his personality. I don't compare myself with him but people do." Hammad added, "My phupo (aunt) says I am a better version of Ranveer Singh."
