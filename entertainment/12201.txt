Actress Priyanka Chopra attended the 68th Emmy Awards as a presenter wearing a Jason Wu red gown. Ahead of her red carpet appearance, when asked if she was nervous, Priyanka said, “No nerves, only for the heat outside, can’t wait to get inside air-conditioning... I am conserving my energy." Priyanka and Tom Hiddleston presented the award to director Susanne Bier.
