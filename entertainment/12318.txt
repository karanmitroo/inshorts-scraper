Actor Amitabh Bachchan has said that he has not watched the teaser of 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil', which also stars his daughter-in-law Aishwarya Rai. In the past, Bachchan has tweeted about Aishwarya's films including 'Jazbaa' and 'Sarbjit'. Earlier, reports had emerged that Bachchan asked the film's director Karan Johar to remove intimate scenes of Aishwarya and Ranbir Kapoor.
