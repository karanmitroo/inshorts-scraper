Actors Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor have featured on the cover of the October issue of 'Harper's Bazaar Bride' magazine. While Ranveer can be seen in a suit, Vaani is seen wearing a plunge-neck gown. They have been styled by Divyak D'Souza and photographed by Prasad Naik. The cover story on them is titled 'When Ranveer Met Vaani'.
