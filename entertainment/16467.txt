The trailer of  Pan Nalin-directorial Hindi drama 'Angry Indian Goddesses' has been released. The film features  Sandhya Mridul, Tannishtha Chatterjee, Sarah-Jane Dias, Anushka Manchanda, Amrit Maghera, Rajshri Deshpande and Pavleen Gujral in lead roles. Called India's first 'female buddy film', the film traces the journey of seven girl-friends who are celebrating a bachelorette party in Goa.
