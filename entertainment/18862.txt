Filmmaker Sanjay Gupta  confirmed that actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan will essay the female lead in his new movie "Jazbaa", putting to rest rumours about her presence in the film. Aishwarya's official spokesperson also confirmed the news. Known for directing entertainers like "Kaante" and "Shootout at Lokhandwala", Gupta is collaborating with Aishwarya for the first time.
