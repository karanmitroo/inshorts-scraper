Baltasar Kormákur’s adventure-thriller “Everest” is out with its trailer. The film is based on the 1996 Mount Everest disaster and is adapted from Jon Krakauer's book, ‘Into Thin Air: A Personal of The Mt. Everest Disaster’. Starring Jason Clarke, Josh Brolin, John Hawkes, Emily Watson and Keira Knightley, the film is set to release on September 18 in the USA.
