A curtain raiser of fashion photographer Daboo Ratnani's 2016 Calendar shoot was YouTube's third most shared video on Facebook and Twitter in India on Thursday. The calendar's 17th edition, features various Bollywood actors including Amitabh Bachchan, Shah Rukh Khan, Hrithik Roshan, Priyanka Chopra and Alia Bhatt. Posted on YouTube on Wednesday, the video has garnered 55,386 views so far.
