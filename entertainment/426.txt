According to reports, Kriti Sanon will be starring opposite Sidharth Malhotra in the upcoming film 'Ek Villain 2'. The film, which will be a sequel to the 2014 movie 'Ek Villain', will be directed by Mohit Suri. The original film starred Shraddha Kapoor as the lead actress and Riteish Deshmukh as the villain.
