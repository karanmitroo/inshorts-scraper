Actress Rekha was presented with the Lifetime Achievement Award at the 22nd edition of Star Screen Awards on Sunday. Deepika Padukone presented the award to the veteran actress while Sonam Kapoor paid tribute to her and performed on her songs. Later, Rekha joined Sonam onstage and danced to her song 'Pardesia' from the 1979 film 'Mr. Natwarlal'.
