Actor Nawazuddin Siddiqui, in his biography 'An Ordinary Life: A Memoir', has revealed that his mother Mehroonisa Siddiqui used to deposit her jewellery as a collateral and take a loan to pay his school fees. "My education was her biggest priority," added Nawazuddin. He further said, "Being the oldest, I experienced her qurbaniyan (sacrifices) first-hand."
