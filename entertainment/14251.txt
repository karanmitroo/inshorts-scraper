Actress Amy Jackson on Friday attended the screening of 'Neon Demon' at the Cannes International Film Festival. She wore a golden shimmery short strapless dress by the designer duo Abu Jani and Sandeep Khosla. The dress was paired with stilettos from Gianvito Rossi. Notably, the 24-year-old actress is making her debut appearance at Cannes this year.
