Aditya Narayan's latest single 'Zindagi' was one of YouTube's most shared videos on Facebook and Twitter in India on Thursday. Published on the video-sharing website on Tuesday, the video has garnered over 2 lakh views so far. The song, starring Aditya Narayan and new comer Evgeniia Belousova, has been composed by Harshit Chauhan. It has been written by Prashant Ingole.
