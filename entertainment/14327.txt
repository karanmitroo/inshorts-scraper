'Azhar', which released on Friday, collected ₹20.80 crore in its opening weekend. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film collected ₹6.30 crore on Friday, ₹7 crore on Saturday and ₹7.50 crore on Sunday. Directed by Tony D'Souza, the Emraan Hashmi starrer is a biopic about former Indian cricketer Mohammad Azharuddin.
