Actor Aamir Khan launched the shoot of the Ajay Devgn starrer film 'Total Dhamaal' by giving the mahurat clap. Directed by Indra Kumar, the film also stars Anil Kapoor, Madhuri Dixit, Riteish Deshmukh and Arshad Warsi. The film is the third instalment in the 'Dhamaal' film series and is scheduled to release on December 7, 2018.
