Reportedly, the upcoming biopic on cricketer MS Dhoni will have Alia Bhatt in the role of Dhoni's wife Sakshi. Sources also claim that Pakistani actor Fawad Khan will play the role of Virat Kohli, while John Abrahan will be seen as Ishant Sharma in the film. The Sushant Singh Rajput starrer will also have Gautam Gulati and Aftab Shivdasani.
