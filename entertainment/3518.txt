Choreographer-filmmaker Farah Khan took to social media to share a picture of Raveena Tandon from the sets of her upcoming TV show 'Lip Sing Battle'. Raveena can be seen imitating Anil Kapoor's look from the 90s in the picture. "Guess who??! Taking #LipSingBattle to another level!! Epic episode!!" wrote Farah in the photo's caption.
