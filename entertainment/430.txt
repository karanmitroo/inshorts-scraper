The official teaser of Irrfan Khan starrer comedy film 'Blackmail' has been released. In the teaser, Irrfan can be heard narrating how his life of a perfect married middle-class man changed in just five days. Directed by Abhinay Deo and also starring Kirti Kulhari, Arunoday Singh, Divya Dutta and Omi Vaidya,  the film is scheduled to release on April 6.
