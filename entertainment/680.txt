Veteran actor Dharmendra shared a picture of himself on Twitter from the sets of his upcoming film 'Yamla Pagla Deewana: Phir Se'. He wrote, "Aapse jude haseen khwaabon ka mere, silsila ye...yun-hi chalta rahe! Yun-hi chalta rahe!" The film is the third instalment of the 'Yamla Pagla Deewana' franchise and stars Sunny Deol, Bobby Deol and Kriti Kharbanda.
