A video showing actors Akshay Kumar and Ranveer Singh singing and dancing to the song 'Hookah Bar' from Akshay's 2012 film 'Khiladi 786' has surfaced online. The actors were performing at a wedding in Hyderabad. Jacqueline Fernandez, Shilpa Shetty and Sridevi were among the other Bollywood celebrities who attended the wedding.
