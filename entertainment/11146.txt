Actress Priyanka Chopra's first Punjabi film as a producer, titled 'Sarvann' is set to release in December, as announced by the actress on Twitter. The film will feature actor Amrinder Gill and will be produced by Priyanka's production house 'Purple Pebble Pictures'. Priyanka earlier produced the Marathi film 'Ventilator', which stars Ashutosh Gowariker and several other Marathi actors.
