The teaser for the song 'Phurrr' composed by American record producer DJ Diplo for the Shah Rukh Khan and Anushka Sharma starrer 'Jab Harry Met Sejal' has been released. While Pritam co-composed the song, it has been sung by Mohit Chauhan and Tushar Joshi with lyrics by Irshad Kamil. The film will release on August 4.
