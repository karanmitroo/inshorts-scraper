Bollywood actor Mithun Chakraborty has been signed for the upcoming Telegu film 'Malupu', which will release on February 19. Mithun will be playing a larger than life character in the thriller, according to director Sathya Prabhas Pinisetty. The film is based on a true incident, with Aadhi Pinisetty and Nikki Galrani in the lead roles.
