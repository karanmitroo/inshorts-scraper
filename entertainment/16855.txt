Varun Dhawan and Shraddha Kapoor-starrer "ABCD 2" has entered Rs 100 crore club by minting Rs 101.91 crore in its third-week. "With #ABCD2, Varun as well as director Remo D'Souza will march into Rs.100 crore club," trade analyst Taran Adarsh tweeted.  It is the second Bollywood movie in 2015 to enter the club after "Tanu Weds Manu Returns".
