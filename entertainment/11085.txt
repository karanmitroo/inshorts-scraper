Actor Ranveer Singh, took to Twitter, to share his look from his upcoming period drama film 'Padmavati'. The look features only the eyes of his character 'Alauddin Khilji'. Ranveer's portrayal of Khilji will mark the first time that the actor plays a negative character on-screen. Directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali, the film will also star Deepika Padukone and Shahid Kapoor.
