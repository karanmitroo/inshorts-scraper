As per reports, actor Shahid Kapoor may quit celebrity dance reality show 'Jhalak Dikhla Jaa' to give time to his wife. Shahid had recently tied the knot with Mira Rajput on July 7. Reportedly, he has not got any time to spend with his wife because of the show. Shahid is one of the four judges on 'Jhalak Dikhla Jaa'.
