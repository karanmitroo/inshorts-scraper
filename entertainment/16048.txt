Filmmakers like Shyam Benegal, Prakash Jha and Shekhar Kapur have opposed  returning of national awards as a mark of protest against alleged increase in intolerance in the country. National awards should not be confused with political honours, Benegal said. Earlier, filmmakers such as Saeed Mirza, Kundan Shah and Anand Patwardhan had returned their awards to protest against alleged intolerance.
