Rishi Kapoor won the Best Supporting Actor (Male) award for 'Kapoor & Sons' while Shabana Azmi was named Best Supporting Actor (Female) for the film 'Neerja' at the Filmfare Awards 2017. In the debut awards category, Diljit Dosanjh won Best Debut (Male) for 'Udta Punjab', while Ritika Singh bagged the Best Debut (Female) award for 'Saala Khadoos'.
