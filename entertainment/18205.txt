Actress Anita Hassanandani has confirmed that she has been roped in for the remake of Subhash Ghai's 1983 film, 'Hero', which will also feature Salman Khan. "It's produced by Salman and directed by Nikhil Advani. It should release sometime next year," said Anita, who is currently seen in the TV show 'Yeh Hai Mohabbatein'.
