Director Abhishek Sharma’s ‘The Shaukeens’, starring Anupam Kher, Lisa Haydon and Akshay Kumar (cameo role), released this Friday. The movie, a loose reworking of Basu Chatterjee’s 1982 film “Shaukeen”, has been rated 1 (India Today), 1.5 (Indian Express, NDTV), 2 (Hindustan Times), 3 (TOI). “This film has no soul and no character. It's time waste, paisa waste,” writes India Today.
