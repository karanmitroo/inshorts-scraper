Maharashtra-based cold-pressed juice startup RAW Pressery has raised ₹3.5 crore in fresh funding from Bollywood actress Jacqueline Fernandez. The funding reportedly values the startup at ₹120 crore. Last year, RAW Pressery raised about ₹30 crore in a Series B round from Saama Capital Management and DSG Consumer Partners, along with existing investor Sequoia Capital.
