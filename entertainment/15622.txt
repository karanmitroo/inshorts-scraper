The official trailer of upcoming Bollywood film 'Tere Bin Laden: Dead or Alive' was released on YouTube on Tuesday. The Abhishek Sharma directorial, releasing on February 19, 2016, stars Manish Paul and Pradhuman Singh among others. The makers have dubbed it as a 'spin-off' to Ali Zafar starrer 'Tere Bin Laden' (2010), a comical satire on the post 9/11 world.
