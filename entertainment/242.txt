Mourning the sudden demise of actress Sridevi, Priyanka Chopra tweeted, "I have no words. Condolences to everyone who loved Sridevi. A dark day." Sushmita Sen tweeted, "I am in shock... can't stop crying." Katrina Kaif shared Sridevi's picture on Instagram and wrote, "My favorite actress...a legend... unbelievable tragedy this is." AR Rahman and Farhan Akhtar also shared condolence messages.
