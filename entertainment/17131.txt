Deepika Padukone and Amitabh Bachchan starrer 'Piku' has earned a total of Rs. 56.02 crore so far, making it a smash hit. In its second weekend the film earned Rs. 3.10 crore on Friday, Rs. 5.10 crore on Saturday and Rs. 6.40 crore on Sunday. Padukone and Bachchan's father-daughter relationship has reportedly entertained people of all age groups.
