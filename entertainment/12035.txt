The second official trailer of the Harshvardhan Kapoor and Saiyami Kher starrer 'Mirzya' has been released. The film's story is inspired by the Punjabi folklore of Mirza Sahiban. Directed by Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra, the film also stars Om Puri, Art Malik and KK Raina and is scheduled to release on October 7.
