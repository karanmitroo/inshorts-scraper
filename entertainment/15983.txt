Pan Nalin-directorial 'Angry Indian Goddesses' has been passed by the Censor Board with 17 cuts, including the muting of words like 'Lakshmi', 'Kali' and 'lunch'. The board also asked the makers to blur out the images of Indian goddesses used in the film, producer Gaurav Dhingra said. It also wanted the name of the movie changed, he added.
