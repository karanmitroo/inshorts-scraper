'Jab Harry Met Sejal', with ₹15.25 crore first-day earnings, has become Shah Rukh Khan's lowest opening grosser in five years. This year, 'Raees' had earned ₹20.42 crore on its opening day. Meanwhile, 'Fan' (2016) and 'Dilwale' (2015) grossed ₹19.20 crore and ₹21 crore respectively. His other major releases in the last five years include 'Happy New Year' and 'Chennai Express'.
