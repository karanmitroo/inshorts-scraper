John Abraham will be seen in the sequel of film 'Force' to be directed by Abhinay Deo. While 'Force' was a remake of Tamil movie 'Kaakha Kaakha', 'Force 2' will have an original script. The film will be released in early 2016 and will be produced by Vipul Amrutlal Shah, who also produced action-packed films 'Force', 'Commando' and 'Holiday'.
