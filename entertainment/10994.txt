The music video of song 'Woh Jahaan', sung by Shraddha Kapoor and Farhan Akhtar for the musical drama film 'Rock On 2' has been released. Composed by Shankar-Ehsaan-Loy, the lyrics of the song have been penned by Javed Akhtar. Also starring Arjun Rampal and Prachi Desai, the film, directed by Shujaat Saudagar, released on November 11.
