Actress Parineeti Chopra has shared an image of her performing in Texas to the song 'Desi Girl', which was picturised on her cousin Priyanka Chopra. Parineeti tweeted, "My tribute to the ultimate desi girl! Wearing that sari was empowering. Mimi didi do you approve??" To this, Priyanka responded, "Love it baby girl! It is so empowering being a desi girl!"
