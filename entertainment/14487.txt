'Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge', starring Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol, was voted as the most evergreen love story in Bollywood, according to a survey by SoGoSurvey. The survey had 4,100 participants, of which 47.33% people voted for the film. The Aditya Roy Kapur and Shraddha Kapoor-starrer 'Aashiqui 2' came second while Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh's 'Bajirao Mastani' was third.
