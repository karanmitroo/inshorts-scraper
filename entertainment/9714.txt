Dairy cooperative Amul has released a poster on Hollywood actress Meryl Streep's acceptance speech at the Golden Globe awards, in which she criticised US President-elect Donald Trump for imitating a disabled reporter in 2015. The poster has been captioned, "Meryl Lynched Trump!". It further reads, "Amul Golden Glob". Following Streep's speech, Trump called her "overrated" and a "Hillary flunky".
