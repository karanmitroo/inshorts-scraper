The Keanu Reeves starrer 'John Wick: Chapter 2', which released on Friday, "hits the sweet spot," wrote Hindustan Times. "The entire appeal of the movie lies in its beautifully choreographed action sequences," wrote Times of India while Mid-Day wrote, "The surfeit of violence is meant to be cool but it's not." It was rated 4/5 (HT, TOI) and 2/5 (Mid-Day).
