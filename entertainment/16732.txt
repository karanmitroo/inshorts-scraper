The film 'Manjhi - The Mountain Man', releasing August 21, will be tax-free in Bihar, said the state government. Directed by Ketan Mehta, starring Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Radhika Apte, Manjhi is based on the life of Dasrath Manjhi of Gaya district in Bihar. He took 22 years to carve a path through a mountain, using only a hammer and chisel.
