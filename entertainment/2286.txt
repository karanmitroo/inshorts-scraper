Soha Ali Khan and Kunal Kemmu, who became parents to baby girl Inaaya Naumi Kemmu on September 29, took to social media to share the first picture of their daughter. "Wishing all the children everywhere and my little munchkin a...happy children's day," wrote Kunal while sharing the picture. Inaaya is Soha's first child with Kunal, who she married in 2015.
