The teaser poster of 'Note Down', a short film on demonetisation of ₹500 and ₹1,000 currency notes in India, has been unveiled. The film's producer Ashoke Pandit shared the poster on Twitter, captioned, "History was created on 8th Nov 2016.... with my short film #NoteDown it would be celebrated." The film has been directed by Divyansh Pandit.
