The movie D-Day, starring Arjun Rampal, Rishi Kapoor, Irrfan Khan, Shruti Haasan, and Huma Qureshi, released on July-19 has been rated 4-star by IANS and Bollywood Hungama while NDTV and Mumbai Mirror gave it 3-star.  The movie revolves around a group of four Indian undercover agents  in an unofficial mission to bring back a terrorist from Karachi.
