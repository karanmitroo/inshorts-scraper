The third teaser of the Shah Rukh Khan and Alia Bhatt starrer film 'Dear Zindagi', titled 'Dear Zindagi Take 3: Love. BreakUp. Repeat' was released on Monday. The new teaser also features actors Kunal Kapoor, Angad Bedi and Ira Dubey along with the main leads. Written and directed by Gauri Shinde, 'Dear Zindagi' is scheduled to release on November 25.
