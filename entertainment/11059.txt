Actor Akshay Kumar, when asked who would "last longer" in the "race of stardom" among Shah Rukh Khan, Aamir Khan, Salman Khan and him, said that he would, if the three Khans did not stop smoking. Host Karan Johar asked Akshay the question in his recent appearance with wife Twinkle on the celebrity chat show 'Koffee with Karan'.
