The Aamir Khan starrer film 'Dangal', which released on December 23, has earned 
₹106.95 crore within three days of its release. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the biographical sports drama earned ₹42.35 crore on Sunday. With the earnings posted on Sunday, 'Dangal' has become the second-highest single day grosser in India ever.
