Actor Ayushmann Khurrana has posted a video where he and actress Parineeti Chopra can be seen dancing to the song 'Gazab Ka Hai Din' from the 1988 film 'Qayamat Se Qayamat Tak'. The actor shared the video from the sets of the upcoming film 'Meri Pyaari Bindu'. Directed by Akshay Roy, the film will release in 2017.
