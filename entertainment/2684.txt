The Sonam Kapoor and Kareena Kapoor starrer upcoming film 'Veere Di Wedding' will release on May 18, 2018. It also stars Swara Bhasker, Shikha Talsania and Sumeet Vyas. The film, which revolves around four friends attending a wedding, has been produced by Sonam's sister Rhea Kapoor and directed by Shashanka Ghosh.
