Indian television actress Pratyusha Banerjee committed suicide on Friday, Mumbai police said. The actress, best known for her portrayal of Anandi in 'Balika Vadhu', reportedly hanged herself earlier in the day and was admitted at Kokilaben Ambani Hospital in Mumbai, where she was declared dead. Pratyusha, who also appeared on Bigg Boss 7, was last seen in 'Sasural Simar Ka'.
