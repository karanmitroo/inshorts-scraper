Priyanka Chopra has featured in the '150 Most Fashionable Women Now' list by fashion magazine Harper's Bazaar. She has been listed under the category 'The New Guard'. "Thank you for including me as one of your 150 Most Fashionable Women, among such an extraordinary group!," tweeted Priyanka. Angelina Jolie, Emilia Clarke and Margot Robbie have also featured in the list.
