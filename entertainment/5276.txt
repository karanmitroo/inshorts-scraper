As per reports, Dhinchak Pooja, known for her songs like 'Selfie Maine Leli Aaj' and 'Swag Wali Topi' has been approached to be a part of the reality show Bigg Boss 11. Television actress Nia Sharma, filmmaker-actor Kabir Bedi and wrestler Geeta Phogat have already denied rumours that they were approached for the show.
