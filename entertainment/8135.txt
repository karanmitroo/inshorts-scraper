A promotional video featuring Prabhas as the character 'Baahubali' in the upcoming film 'Baahubali 2' has been released. Also starring Anushka, Tamannaah, Rana Daggubati and Sathyaraj, it is a sequel to the 2015 film 'Baahubali: The Beginning'. Directed by SS Rajamouli, 'Baahubali 2' is scheduled to release on April 28.
