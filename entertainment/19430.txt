After 32 years of parting ways, veteran scriptwriters of 1970s and 1980s, Salim Khan and Javed Akhtar  have decided to come together for the trailer launch of "Sholay 3D" on Thursday. Amitabh Bachchan and Dharmendra starrer Sholay, which was released 38-years-ago, has been converted into 3D and is slated to release on Jan-3.
