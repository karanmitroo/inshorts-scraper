Actress Saumya Tandon will be seen donning a Catwoman costume for an upcoming storyline of the Meow Gang in the serial 'Bhabi Ji Ghar Par Hai'. Saumya added, "I have always been fascinated with Catwoman; three super talented actresses (Michelle Pfeiffer, Halle Berry and Anne Hathaway) have played this character. I created my own costume like a tribute to them."
