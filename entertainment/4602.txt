Alia Bhatt, Shahid Kapoor and Salman Khan were among the celebrities who attended the Zee Big Entertainment Awards on Saturday. Alia wore a pink gown by designers Sachin and Babi. Sunny Leone chose a strapless gown by Pria Kataaria Puri. Disha Patani was seen in a red gown by Swapnil Shinde while Kajol wore a shimmery gown by Prabal Gurung.
