Rani Mukerji, who'll be making a special appearance with Kajol in Shah Rukh Khan's upcoming Aanand L Rai directorial, said it felt like 'Kuch Kuch Hota Hai 2'. Shah Rukh, Kajol and Rani had featured in the 1998 film 'Kuch Kuch Hota Hai'. Other actresses including Alia Bhatt and Sridevi will also reportedly have a cameo appearance in the film.
