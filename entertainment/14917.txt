Director Anubhav Sinha has revealed the cast for 'Tum Bin 2' which will feature actors Neha Sharma, Aditya Seal and Aashim Gulati in lead roles. "We decided on these three after... 1,500 screen tests," said Sinha. He however clarified that the film as an intense love story is similar but not a sequel to the 2001 film 'Tum Bin'.
