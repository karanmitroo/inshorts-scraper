Richard Linklater has again proved his mettle with recently released directorial 'Boyhood', which is "a unique, terrific, intuitive movie about growing up", writes IBN Live. The movie was shot from 2002 to 2013 with the same principal cast including Ellar Coltrane, Patricia Arquette and Lorelei Linklater. It's been rated 4/5 (IBN Live, HT) and 4.5/5 (Indian Express, TOI).
