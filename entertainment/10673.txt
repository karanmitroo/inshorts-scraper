According to reports, actor Sanjay Dutt said to Ranbir Kapoor that he could not believe how he was cast as Dutt for an upcoming biopic on him. "What made you do a film like [Barfi]?... [Y]ou are playing me... You should be a macho man," Sanjay reportedly told Ranbir. He further said that Ranbir has "no connect with the masses".
