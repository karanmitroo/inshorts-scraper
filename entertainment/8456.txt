Filmmaker Karan Johar shared a picture from the sets of the Sushant Singh Rajput and Jacqueline Fernandez starrer upcoming film 'Drive'. The film is reportedly a remake of the 2011 Hollywood film of the same name starring Ryan Gosling and  Carey Mulligan. It will be directed by Tarun Mansukhani and produced by Karan Johar under Dharma Productions.
