Singer Meiyang Chang is set to sing a recreated version of the song 'Laila O Laila' from the 1980 film 'Qurbani'. The song will be released by record label Times Music. It is expected to release in the first quarter of the year. He had earlier sung the recreated versions of 'Ek Ladki Bheegi Bhaagi Si' and 'Saara Zamana'.
