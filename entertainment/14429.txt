'Captain America: Civil War', which released on Friday, collected ₹11.85 crore on its opening day in India. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film collected ₹8.53 crore at the ticket window itself. Notably, the Hollywood film has beaten the opening day collections for 'Traffic', 'One Night Stand' and '1920 London', the three Bollywood releases this week.
