Superstar Shah Rukh Khan on Tuesday unveiled the first look of the much-awaited Salman Khan starrer 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan'. "I believe Being a brother is bigger than being a Hero. ‘Bhaijaan’ coming Eid 2015. How do u like the first look?", SRK posted on Twitter along with the film's first poster. The film also stars Kareena Kapoor and Nawazuddin Siddiqui.
