Actor Salman Khan on Thursday tweeted a new poster and the release date of the film 'Hero', which will mark the debut of Sooraj Pancholi. "Back off, HERO coming on 4th September @HeroTheFilm," Khan tweeted. The film is a remake of the 1983 film of the same name and is a co-production of Salman Khan and filmmaker Subhash Ghai.
