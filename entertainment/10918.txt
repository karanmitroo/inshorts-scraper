British rock band 'Coldplay', the headlining act at the Global Citizen Fest in Mumbai, performed songs like 'Fix You', 'The Scientist', 'Heroes' and 'Hymn For The Weekend' among others. The band was joined by music composer AR Rahman, who sang 'Vande Mataram'. Coldplay's vocalist Chris Martin sang the Hindi song 'Channa Mereya' before singing 'A Sky Full of Stars'.
