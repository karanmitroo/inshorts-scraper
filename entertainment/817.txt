Denying reports that she got engaged to rumoured boyfriend Ranveer Singh in January during their vacation in the Maldives, Deepika Padukone said, "I wasn't engaged." She said this on the Neha Dhupia-hosted talk show 'BFFs with Vogue'. Deepika's sister, Anisha Padukone, who appeared on the show with her, jokingly added, "She has been engaged successively for the last four years."
