Bollywood actor Inder Kumar has passed away at the age of 43 at his residence in Andheri, Mumbai. The cause of his death is reportedly a cardiac arrest. Inder, who made his debut in the 1996 film 'Masoom', was best known for playing supporting roles in films like 'Wanted', 'Kahin Pyaar Na Ho Jaaye' and 'Tumko Na Bhool Paayenge'.
