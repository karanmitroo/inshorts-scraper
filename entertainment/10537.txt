The official trailer of Shah Rukh Khan starrer film 'Raees' was released on Wednesday. Set in Gujarat in the 1980s, the film revolves around bootlegger Raees Alam, played by SRK. It also features Nawazuddin Siddiqui as a police officer and Pakistani actress Mahira Khan as Mohsina. Directed by Rahul Dholakia, 'Raees' is scheduled to release on January 25.
