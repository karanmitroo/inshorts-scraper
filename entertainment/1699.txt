The Richa Chadha, Pulkit Samrat and Ali Fazal starrer 'Fukrey Returns' has earned ₹32.20 crore in its opening weekend. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film minted ₹8.10 crore on Friday, ₹11.30 crore on Saturday and ₹12.80 crore on Sunday. Directed by Mrighdeep Singh Lamba, the film is a sequel to the 2013 film 'Fukrey'.
