According to reports, Sidharth Malhotra and Aditya Roy Kapur will star in the upcoming Salman Khan starrer 'Race 3'. A source said, "Sidharth and Aditya can bring nothing extra to the project. They are needed only because the script needs two other leading men." The film also stars Jacqueline Fernandez and will reportedly be directed by Remo D'Souza.
