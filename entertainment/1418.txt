'Tiger Zinda Hai' has entered the ₹100 crore club by earning ₹114.93 crore within three days of its release, as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. It is Salman Khan's 12th film to cross the ₹100 crore mark, the maximum for any Bollywood actor. The film is also this year's second highest Hindi opening grosser after Baahubali 2's Hindi version.
