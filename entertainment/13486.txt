Costume designer Michele Clapton, and jewellery designers Yunus Ascott and Eliza Higginbottom, for the HBO series 'Game of Thrones' have collaborated with 'MEY Designs' to launch a jewellery line based on the series. The line includes jewellery inspired by the character 'Daenerys Targaryen' such as the 'Daenerys Drogon Neck Sculpture'. The collection starts at $95 and goes up to $2,730.
