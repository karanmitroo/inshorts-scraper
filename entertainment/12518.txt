The Arnold Schwarzenegger starrer science-fiction thriller film 'Terminator 2: Judgement Day', the 1991 sequel to 'The Terminator' will be re-released in 3D in 2017. The original 'Terminator' film released in 1984 and was followed by a franchise consisting of four sequels. James Cameron, co-creator of the 'Terminator' franchise had announced the 3D conversion earlier in 2015.
