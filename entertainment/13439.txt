Filmmaker Ritesh Batra, known for directing the Irrfan Khan starrer 'The Lunchbox', has been reportedly roped in by Netflix to direct the film 'Our Souls At Night', which stars Robert Redford and Jane Fonda. Notably, Redford and Fonda will be reuniting onscreen after the 1967 film 'Barefoot in the Park' and the 1979 film 'The Electric Horseman'.
