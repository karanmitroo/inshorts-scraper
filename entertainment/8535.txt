Nine films have been nominated in the Best Picture category at Oscars 2017, which include the Emma Stone and Ryan Gosling starrer 'La La Land' and Barry Jenkins' directorial 'Moonlight'. While the Dev Patel starrer 'Lion' has bagged a nomination, other nominees include 'Arrival', 'Fences', 'Manchester By The Sea', 'Hacksaw Ridge', 'Hidden Figures' and 'Hell or High Water'.
