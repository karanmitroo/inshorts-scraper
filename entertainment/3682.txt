Rishi Kapoor made his first onscreen appearance in the song 'Pyar Hua Ikrar Hua' from his father Raj Kapoor's 1955 film 'Shree 420' at the age of two. Rishi revealed that a chocolate bribe by Nargis Dutt convinced him to do the scene. Rishi, who turned 65 on Monday, is known for films like 'Bobby', 'Amar Akbar Anthony' and 'Karz'.
