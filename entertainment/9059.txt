The title song from the Alia Bhatt and Varun Dhawan starrer film 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania' has been released. The song has been composed by Tanishk Bagchi, with vocals by Dev Negi, Neha Kakkar, Monali Thakur and Ikka, and lyrics penned by Shabbir Ahmed. Directed by Shashank Khaitan, the romantic comedy film is scheduled to release on March 10.
