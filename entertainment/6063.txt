Actor Tusshar Kapoor on Instagram shared a picture of his son Laksshya with Kareena Kapoor and Saif Ali Khan's son Taimur Ali Khan. The picture was captioned, "#futurebffs (Best Friends Forever) #angelsndivas #godbless #laksshya #taimur #firstbirthday." The photograph was taken at Laksshya's first birthday celebrations held earlier this week, which was also attended by Karan Johar and Tabu.
