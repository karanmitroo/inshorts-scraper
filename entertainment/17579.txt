Bollywood actress Sonam Kapoor was Saturday discharged from the hospital after being treated for swine flu. "Discharged! Home sweet home!," the 29-yr-old actress announced on Twitter. Sonam was diagnosed with the swine flu while shooting in Rajkot for the Salman Khan-starrer "Prem Ratan Dhan Paayo". She was admitted to a Mumbai hospital on Feb 28.
