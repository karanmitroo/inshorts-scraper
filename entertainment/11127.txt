Hollywood actor Vin Diesel has quoted the Bhagavad Gita in his new film 'Billy Lynn's Long Halftime Walk'. The film has been directed by Academy Award-winning filmmaker Ang Lee, known for directing the 2012 film 'Life of Pi'. Diesel will be next seen in the action-adventure film 'xXx: The Return of Xander Cage', which will release on January 20, 2017.
