'Tere Bina', the first song from the Shraddha Kapoor starrer upcoming film 'Haseena Parkar' has been released. It has been composed by music composer duo Sachin- Jigar with vocals by Arijit Singh and Priya Saraiya. The lyrics have also been penned by Priya. Directed by Apoorva Lakhia, 'Haseena Parkar' is scheduled to release on September 22.
