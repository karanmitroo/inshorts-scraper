Irfaan Khan and Nimrat Kaur-starrer 'The Luchbox' was Friday nominated to the British Academy Film Awards 2015, in the 'Film Not in the English Language' category. Directed by Ritesh Batra, the film is competing with movies like "Ida", "Leviathan", "Trash", and "Two Days, One Night". The movie has done its round of international film festivals, including those Cannes and Toronto.
