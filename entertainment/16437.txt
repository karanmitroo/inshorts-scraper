People Magazine on Wednesday named Hollywood actress Reese Witherspoon as the best dressed person of 2015. Witherspoon (39) topped the list to beat Hollywood stars Julianne Moore, Kate Hudson, and 15-year-old actress Kiernan Shipka. The magazine also named singer John Legend and his wife Chrissy Teigen, and  actor Channing Tatum and wife Jenna Dewan Tatum as best dressed couples.
