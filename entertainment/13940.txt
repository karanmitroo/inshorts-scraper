Actor Anil Kapoor on Wednesday unveiled the official trailer of the second season of the television drama show '24'. While Anil and Neil Bhoopalam reprise their roles, actors Sakshi Tanwar, Ashish Vidyarthi, Surveen Chawla, Sikander Kher and Sudhanshu Pandey have joined the new season. The latest season will be aired on Colors from July 17 onwards.
