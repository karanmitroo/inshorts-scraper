Actor Pulkit Samrat's estranged wife Shweta Rohira, who earlier blamed actress Yami Gautam for ruining their marriage, has now said that she does not know any Yami Gautam. "Who is this Yami? Main koi Yami ko nahin jaanti. I am going through a memory loss," she said. However, she maintained that Pulkit was not loyal in their marriage.
