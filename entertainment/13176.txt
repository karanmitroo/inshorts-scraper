'Sarsariya', a new song from the Hrithik Roshan and Pooja Hegde starrer 'Mohenjo Daro' was released on Monday. The song has been composed by AR Rahman with lyrics penned by Javed Akhtar and sung by Shashwat Singh and Shashaa Tirupati. It has been picturised on the lead pair of Hrithik and Pooja, who will make her Bollywood debut with 'Mohenjo Daro'.
