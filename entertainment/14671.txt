The official trailer of Ram Gopal Varma's upcoming film 'Veerappan' has been released. The thriller docu-drama is about 'Operation Cocoon', which was carried on to capture or kill Indian dacoit Veerappan. The film features Sandeep Bhardwaj as Veerappan while Sachiin J Joshi, Usha Jadhav and Lisa Ray appear in pivotal roles. The film is scheduled to release on May 27.
