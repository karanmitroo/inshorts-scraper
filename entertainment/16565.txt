The police said on Monday that actor Morgan Freeman’s step-granddaughter, E'Dena Hines, was killed by her live-in boyfriend, Lamar Davenport. Hines was found with multiple fatal stab wounds on a street near her house early on Sunday. Witnesses claimed to seeing Davenport attack her while yelling "Get out, devils. I cast you out, devils”. He was hospitalised for mental evaluation.
