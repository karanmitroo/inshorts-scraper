Amitabh Bachchan emerged as the greatest Bollywood star in the survey '100 Greatest Bollywood Stars' conducted by British Asian weekly newspaper 'Eastern Eye'. Bachchan, who has a career spanning 40-years, was also the first Bollywood star to be honoured with a statue at Madame Tussauds. The other position holders are Dilip Kumar(2), SRK(3), Madhuri Dixit(4), Salman Khan(11), Aamir Khan(14).
