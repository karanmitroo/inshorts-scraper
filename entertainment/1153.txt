Actress Anushka Sharma, while sharing the first video of her look from her upcoming film 'Pari', tweeted, "Sweet dreams guys." The video announces the new release date of the film on the occasion of Holi on March 2. Also starring Parambrata Chatterjee, Rajat Kapoor and Ritabhari Chakraborty, 'Pari' will mark the directorial debut of Prosit Roy in feature films.
