The release date of actress Anushka Sharma's second production 'Phillauri' has been shifted to March 24, 2017. "Likh ke Rakho abhi se... Bohot maza aney wala hai...," tweeted Anushka on the film's release date. The film, starring Anushka along with Diljit Dosanjh, Suraj Sharma and Mehreen Pirzada, was earlier scheduled to release on March 31, 2017.
