A new still from Alia Bhatt and Vicky Kaushal starrer 'Raazi' has been released. "Just about a 100 days to go," wrote Alia while sharing the photo. Based on the book 'Calling Sehmat: A Novel' by Harinder Sikka, the Meghna Gulzar directorial is about a Kashmiri girl who is married to an army officer.
