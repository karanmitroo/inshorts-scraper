The first look from the Taapsee Pannu and Akshay Kumar starrer 'Naam Shabana' has been revealed. Akshay, who features in an extended cameo appearance in the film, tweeted, "This film belongs to you Baby!!!" Directed by 	Shivam Nair and also starring Manoj Bajpayee, Anupam Kher and Elli Avram, the film is scheduled to release on March 31, 2017.
