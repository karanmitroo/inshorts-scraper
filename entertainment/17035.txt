Vikas Bahl’s directorial ‘Queen’ won the best picture award and Kangana Ranaut won the best actress award at the 16th International Indian Film Academy Awards (IIFA), held in Kuala Lumpur (Malaysia) on Sunday. Shahid Kapoor and Tabu won the best actor and the best supporting actress awards respectively for 'Haider'. Rajkumar Hirani bagged the best director award for 'PK'.
