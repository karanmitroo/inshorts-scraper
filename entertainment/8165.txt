A video shows actresses Fatima Shaikh and Sanya Malhotra, who played Phogat sisters in the Aamir Khan starrer 'Dangal', dancing to Justin Bieber's song 'Sorry'. It is a behind-the-scenes video from a fashion magazine photoshoot in which the two featured. "Taking on the long weekend with a little help from our favourite #Dangal duo," reads the caption alongside the video.
