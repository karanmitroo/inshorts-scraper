Renuka Shahane has said that if there is a remake of her 1994 film 'Hum Aapke Hain Koun..!', she would love to see Alia Bhatt and Varun Dhawan play the new-age Nisha (Madhuri Dixit) and Prem (Salman Khan). She further said that she is always open to working with the film's producers while adding, "It's like my mykaa (parents' home)."
