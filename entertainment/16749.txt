Superstar Salman Khan has been booked for posting tweets in support of 1993 Mumbai serial blasts death row convict Yakub Memon saying that the latter shouldn't be executed. A case against him has been filed under the section 124A (sedition) of IPC by advocate Sushil Kumar Mishra who termed Salman's tweets as anti-national. Notably, Salman had later "retracted" his tweets.
