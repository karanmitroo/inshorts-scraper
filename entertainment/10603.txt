A video which shows the making of the song 'Every Night And Day', sung by Himesh Reshammiya and actor Salman Khan's rumoured girlfriend Iulia Vantur, has been released. Iulia is seen singing in Hindi for the song, which was composed by Himesh with lyrics by Manoj Muntashir. The song is part of Himesh's upcoming album 'Aap Se Mausiiquii'.
