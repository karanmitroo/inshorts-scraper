According to reports, actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan will make her singing debut in the upcoming film 'Fanney Khan'. Directed by debutant Atul Manjrekar, the musical comedy will reportedly feature Aishwarya as a singer. The film will mark Aishwarya starring opposite Anil Kapoor after 17 years. It is said to be a remake of the Oscar-nominated film 'Everybody's Famous!' (2000).
