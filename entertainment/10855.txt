The engagement ring worn by actor Kristen Stewart in the Twilight film franchise has been auctioned for ₹11.5 lakh. The ring was sold during a two-day auction at the Hollywood Chinese Theatre. Over 900 pieces of memorabilia were sold at the event, including Edward's journal for ₹5.3 lakh and a meadow costume Kristen wore onscreen for ₹5.7 lakh.
