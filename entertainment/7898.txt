The Emma Watson and Dan Stevens starrer 'Beauty and the Beast' has earned over ₹2,300 crore ($357 million) in its opening weekend. The film's opening weekend collections are nearly three times higher than that of 'Cinderella', 'Moanna' and 'Zootopia', as revealed by Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. In India, the fantasy musical earned over ₹6 crore in its opening weekend.
