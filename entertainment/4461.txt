Actor Amitabh Bachchan's granddaughter Navya Naveli Nanda has said there is no way she will make a career in acting. "I am enjoying my current internship at an advertising agency," she added. Nineteen-year-old Navya, who is the daughter of Shweta Bachchan Nanda and businessman Nikhil Nanda, is currently studying at a university in New York.
