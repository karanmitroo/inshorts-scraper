According to reports, an action sequence for Prabhas starrer 'Saaho' will be shot in Dubai's Burj Khalifa, which is the world's tallest building. The film's makers have hired Hollywood stuntman Kenny Bates to choreograph the action sequences. Also starring Shraddha Kapoor, Jackie Shroff and Chunky Pandey, the action-thriller is scheduled to be released in 2018.
