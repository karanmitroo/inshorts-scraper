A promotional video featuring Shah Rukh Khan on reality television show 'Bigg Boss 10', hosted by Salman Khan, has been released. In the promo, Salman is seen saying, "Raees ka dialogue sun na chaiye, toh Shah Rukh ki zubani, kyunki Sultan ko aati hai bas dosti nibhani." Shah Rukh will appear in the semi-final episode to promote his film 'Raees'.
