Shah Rukh Khan-starrer 'Dilwale' and Ranveer Singh-starrer 'Bajirao Mastani' which released on Friday, have earned  ₹21 crore and ₹12.80 crore respectively in India on their opening day. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, 'Dilwale' has become 2015's third highest opening day grosser after 'Prem Ratan Dhan Payo' (₹40.35 crore) and 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' (₹27.25 crore).
