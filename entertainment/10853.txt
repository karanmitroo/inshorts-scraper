As per reports, actor Amitabh Bachchan and Akshay Kumar will star together in R Balki's film. While the film will be Akshay's first with Balki, the actor has previously worked with Bachchan in several films. Akshay's first film with Bachchan was Suneel Darshan's 2001 family drama 'Ek Rishtaa: The Bond of Love'.
