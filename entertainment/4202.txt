As per reports, TV and film producer Ekta Kapoor was responsible for getting Pahlaj Nihalani fired as the Censor Board chief. "For a very long time, Ekta had been grumbling against Nihalani's high-handedness," said a source. It was reported that she complained to Information & Broadcasting Minister Smriti Irani when the Censor Board slammed her film 'Lipstick Under My Burkha'.
