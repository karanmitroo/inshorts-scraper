'Labon Ka Karobaar', the first song from the Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor starrer 'Befikre' was released today. The song has been sung by Papon and composed by the music director duo Vishal-Shekhar, while lyrics for the song have been penned by Jaideep Sahni. Written, produced and directed by Aditya Chopra, the film is scheduled to release on December 9.
