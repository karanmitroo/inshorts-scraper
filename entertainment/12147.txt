'Banjo Party', the new song from the Riteish Deshmukh-Nargis Fakhri starrer 'Banjo' has been released. The song has been composed by the music director duo of Vishal-Shekhar, while Vishal Dadlani and Neeti Mohan have sung it. Vishal has also penned the lyrics for the song, along with the film's director Ravi Jadhav. 'Banjo' is scheduled to release on September 23.
