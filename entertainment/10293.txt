Bollywood actor Govinda inaugurated the Jaipur Wax Museum on Saturday at the 18th-century Nahargarh Fort, which overlooks the city. The museum houses wax sculptures of around 25 personalities, including actor Amitabh Bachchan and warriors Maharana Pratap and Sawai Jai Singh. Govinda said, "I hope there will more activities like this in Jaipur."
