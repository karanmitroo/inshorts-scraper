Actor Anupam Kher and actress Frieda Pinto have been roped in for the film 'Love Sonia', a real-life inspired drama about sex trafficking. The film will mark the directorial debut of Tabrez Noorani, who produced the film 'Slumdog Millionaire'. The film will also reportedly feature Mrunal Thakur, Paul Dano, Manoj Bajpai, Richa Chadda and Rajkummar Rao.
