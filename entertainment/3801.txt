Actress Sanya Malhotra, known for starring in 'Dangal', will be seen opposite Nawazuddin Siddiqui in her second film. The film will be directed by Ritesh Batra, who directed the 2013 film 'The Lunchbox'. Speaking about the film, Ritesh said, "Set in Mumbai, it's a love story of sorts between a poor photographer and his muse."
