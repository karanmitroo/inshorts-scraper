The Sushant Singh Rajput starrer 'MS Dhoni: The Untold Story' has recorded the second highest opening of 2016 after Salman Khan's 'Sultan', as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. It earned ₹21.3 crore on its first day. The film also surpassed the opening day earnings of Shah Rukh Khan's 'Fan' (₹19.20 crore) and Akshay Kumar's 'Housefull 3' (₹15.21 crore).
