The official poster of the Sunny Leone starrer film 'One Night Stand' was released on Thursday. The romantic drama has been directed by Jasmine Moses D'souza and also features actress Rati Agnihotri's son, Tanuj Virwani, and Nyra Banerjee. The film has been produced by Furquan Khan and Pradeep Sharma. It is scheduled to release worldwide on April 22.
