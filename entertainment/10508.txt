Actor Ranveer Singh has featured on the cover of 'Man's World' as he is part of the 'Men Of The Year' list by the magazine. Ranveer was called one of 'The Outliers' by the magazine. The list also includes cricketer KL Rahul, fashion designer Suket Dhir, lyricist Amitabh Bhattacharya, engineer Sonam Wangchuk, musician Nucleya and cricket commentator Charu Sharma.
