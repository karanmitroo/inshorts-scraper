Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie are selling their 5,920 square foot New Orleans property for $6.5 million. Built in the 1830s, the three-storied estate has five bedrooms, three full bathrooms and two half-baths. The house boasts of arched French doors, a grand staircase and an elevator in the property that was reportedly bought in 2006 for $3.5 million.
