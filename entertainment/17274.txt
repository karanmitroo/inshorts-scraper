Directed by Noah Baumbach, 'While We're Young' is "an amusing comedy of ageing manners" that revolves around a mid-aged couple Josh (Ben Stiller) and Cornelia's (Naomi Watts) efforts to revive their lives, "reminding them of the idealistic, carefree souls they once were", writes The Guardian. The film has been rated 4/5 (The Guardian, mid-day) and 3.5/5 (TOI, IBN Live).
