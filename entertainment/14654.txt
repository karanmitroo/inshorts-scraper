Disney’s recently released film 'The Jungle Book' has become the highest-grossing Hollywood film in India, having minted ₹113.7 crore within 12 days of its release. Released in English, Hindi, Tamil and Telugu, the film crossed the earnings of 'Fast & Furious 7' (2015), which collected ₹111.5 crore in India. Currently, India ranks third in worldwide earnings for the film.
