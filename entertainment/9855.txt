The official trailer of the Shahid Kapoor, Kangana Ranaut and Saif Ali Khan starrer film 'Rangoon' has been released. Kangana will be seen playing the role of Julia, an actress from the 1940s, while Shahid will essay the role of a soldier. Saif plays a filmmaker and Kangana's mentor. Directed by Vishal Bhardwaj, 'Rangoon' will release on February 24, 2017.
