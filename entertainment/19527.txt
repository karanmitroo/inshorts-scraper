Director Anthony D'Souza's 'Boss' starring Akshay Kumar, Mithun Chakraborty, Shiv Pandit and Aditi Rao Hydari, released this Wednesday. The action-comedy masala flick, which is a remake of 2010 Malayalam film- Pokkiri Raja, has received 1.5 (India Today, IBN Live, Indian Express), 3 (TOI critic) and 3.5 (DNA). Akshay Kumar is playing a Haryanvi contract killer in the movie.
