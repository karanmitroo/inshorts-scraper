'Gilehriyaan', a new song from the Aamir Khan starrer upcoming biopic 'Dangal' has been released. The song has been composed by Pritam with vocals by Jonita Gandhi and lyrics by Amitabh Bhattacharya. Aamir will be seen portraying wrestler Mahavir Singh Phogat in the film. Directed by Nitesh Tiwari, 'Dangal' is scheduled to release on December 23, 2016.
