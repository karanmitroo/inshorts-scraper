Filmmaker Sanjay Leela Bhansali's directorial 'Padmaavat' will release on January 25, as confirmed by the film's production house Viacom18 Motion Pictures. According to the official press release, it is the first Indian film that will have a global IMAX 3D release. The film's release became controversial after Rajput groups protested against it claiming that it distorted facts about Queen Padmini.
