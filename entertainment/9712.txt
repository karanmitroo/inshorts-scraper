Fashion photographer Dabboo Ratnani has released a teaser for his 2017 calendar. In the teaser, the celebrities can be seen describing their experience of shooting for the calendar. The calendar features 24 celebrities, including Shah Rukh Khan, Amitabh Bachchan, Aishwarya Rai, among others. Sunny Leone, Tiger Shroff and Disha Patani have featured on the calendar for the first time.
