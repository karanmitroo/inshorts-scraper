Akshay Kumar has donated ₹9 lakh to the family of a martyred jawan who lost his life in a shootout with terrorists in Tinsukia, Assam. Akshay reportedly spoke to the jawan's wife on learning about the family's hardship. He had earlier donated ₹9 lakh to another martyred jawan who had succumbed to his injuries after being targeted by Pakistani snipers.
