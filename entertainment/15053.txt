The trailer of the Boman Irani and Vir Das starrer suspense comedy film 'Santa Banta Pvt. Ltd.' has been released. Directed by Akashdeep Sabir, it features Boman as Santeshwar Singh Solhar and Vir Das as Banteshwar Singh Bolhar. The film, which is scheduled to release on April 22, also features Neha Dhupia, Lisa Haydon and Vijay Raaz.
