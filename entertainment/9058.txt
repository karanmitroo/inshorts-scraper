The official trailer of the Anushka Sharma and Diljit Dosanjh starrer upcoming film 'Phillauri' has been released. Anushka plays the role of Shashi, a ghost who is in search of her lover played by Diljit Dosanjh. The romantic comedy film will also feature Suraj Sharma and Mehreen Pirzada. Directed by Anshai Lal, 'Phillauri' is scheduled to release on March 24.
