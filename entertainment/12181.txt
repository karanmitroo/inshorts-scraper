American show 'Game of Thrones' was named Outstanding Drama Series at the 68th Emmy Awards on Sunday. 'Veep' won the Outstanding Comedy Series award, while 'Sherlock: The Abominable Bride' was named Best Television Movie. While Rami Malek was named Best Actor in a Drama Series for 'Mr Robot', Tatiana Maslany won the best actress award for drama series 'Orphan Black'.
