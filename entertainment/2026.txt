A new poster of the upcoming Akshay Kumar starrer 'PadMan' has been unveiled. Sharing the poster on his social media, Akshay wrote, "Super hero hai yeh pagla, aa raha hai 26th January, 2018 ko." The film also stars Radhika Apte and Sonam Kapoor. 'PadMan' is based on the life of Arunachalam Muruganantham, the inventor of a low-cost sanitary pad-making machine.
