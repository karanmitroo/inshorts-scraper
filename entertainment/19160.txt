After three months of scintillating performances, popular television couple Rithivik and Asha Negi won the sixth season of celebrity dance reality show 'Nach Baliye' with majority of votes. The couple emerged victorious after defeating competitors Debina-Gurmeet, Ripu-Shivangi and Vinod-Raksha. They were awarded Rs.35 lakh cash prize, a Renault Duster and a Gold Coast (Australia) trip.
