'Bholi Surat', the new song from the Marathi film 'Ekk Albela' has been released. The song, which has been sung by Anwesshaa and Vinay Mandke, is a rendition of the song 'Bholi Surat Dil Ke Khote' from the 1951 film 'Albela'. The song is picturised on Mangesh Desai who portrays Bhagwan Dada and Vidya Balan who portrays Geeta Bali.
