On singer Shreya Ghoshal's 32nd birthday, #HappyBirthdayShreyaGhoshal was a top Indian Twitter trend in Saturday. Singer Vishal Dadlani tweeted, "Arre, haan, can't not use the official @shreyaghoshal fan hashtag! #HappyBirthdayShreyaGhoshal , and have a great year!". Fans wished the singer with one tweet reading, "Happy Birthday My Melody Queen #HappyBirthdayShreyaGhoshal".
