The official trailer of animated film 'Kubo And The Two Strings' has been released on YouTube. The film, a Travis Knight directorial, is being produced by animation studio Laika. Several actors including  Art Parkinson, Matthew McConaughey, Charlize Theron, Rooney Mara, Ralph Fiennes and Brenda Vaccaro have lent their voices for the film. It will release on August 19, 2016.
