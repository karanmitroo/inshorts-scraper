In spite of the IPL fever, Arjun Kapoor and Alia Bhatt starrer '2 States', based on the bestselling novel by Chetan Bhagat, has been declared the biggest hit of 2014 so far, making Rs 40 crore in its opening weekend. Chetan tweeted, "2 States Emerges Biggest Hit Of 2014. 2 States opening wknd ~Rs40cr, similar to 3 Idiots opening wknd!"
