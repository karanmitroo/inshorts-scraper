Actor Anil Kapoor's look from the upcoming romantic comedy film 'Mubarakan' has been revealed. He will be playing the character 'Kartar Singh' in the film, which will also star Anil's nephew Arjun Kapoor, Ileana D'Cruz and Athiya Shetty. Written and directed by Anees Bazmee, 'Mubarakan' is scheduled to release on July 28.
