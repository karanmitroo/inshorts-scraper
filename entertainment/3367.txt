According to reports, comedian Kapil Sharma who was undergoing "detoxification" at an Ayurveda centre in Bengaluru for 40 days, has left the facility in 12 days. A source said, "Kapil is feeling much better....(He) has stopped drinking completely... for now." The source added, "He needed to return to Mumbai to complete his film 'Firangi' and start promoting and marketing it."
