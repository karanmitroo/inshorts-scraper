'The Breakup Song', a new song from the Ranbir Kapoor, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Anushka Sharma starrer 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' has been released. Composed by Pritam, it has been sung by Arijit Singh, Badshah, Jonita Gandhi and Nakash Aziz, with lyrics by Amitabh Bhattacharya. Directed by Karan Johar, the film is scheduled to release on October 28.
