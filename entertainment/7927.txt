Actor Nawazuddin Siddiqui has said that contract killers can also be romantic but they romance with the help of guns "Romance can be done in many ways. Every person has their own way to express romanticism," added Nawazuddin. The actor, who has already played a gangster in 'Gangs of Wasseypur', will be seen playing a contract killer in 'Babumoshai Bandukbaaz'.
