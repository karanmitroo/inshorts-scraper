Actress Karisma Kapoor has shared a picture on Instagram of herself and her sister Kareena Kapoor spending time with their mother Babita Kapoor. The picture, which has been captioned "#familylove #lovelyafternoon", also shows Rima Jain, who is Karisma and Kareena's aunt, and Karisma's daughter Samaira. Kareena is said to have visited her mother's house with her son Taimur Ali Khan.
