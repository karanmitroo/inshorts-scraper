Actors Akshay Kumar, Ranveer Singh, Sonam Kapoor and Alia Bhatt, and cricketer Virat Kohli have featured on different covers of the January issue of 'Forbes' India magazine. They featured in Celebrity 100 list of Forbes 2016. Virat was ranked 3rd in the list, while Akshay came 4th. Meanwhile, Ranveer, Sonam and Alia took the 12th, 18th and 42nd spots respectively.
