The Aamir Khan and Anushka Sharma starrer 'PK', which released on December 19, 2014, was the first Indian film to enter the ₹300 crore club. Directed by Rajkumar Hirani, the film collected ₹792 crore worldwide, making it the highest grossing Indian film. Notably, Salman Khan's 'Sultan' on Wednesday
became the third Bollywood film to earn ₹300 crore in India.
