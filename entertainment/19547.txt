For the first time Bhojpuri actor Ravi Kishan will be seen donning a woman avatar for a scene in 'Bullet Raja' starring Saif Ali Khan and Sonakshi Sinha. He is seen in a green and pink choli with a lot of jewellery. Directed by Tigmanshu Dhulia, a Fox Star Studios Presentation and Moving Pictures Production, 'Bullett Raja' releases on November-29.
