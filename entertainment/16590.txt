Akshay Kumar and Sidharth Malhotra starrer "Brothers", released this Friday, is a one-time watch, especially for those who are fight junkies, wrote Mid-Day. Only Akshay Kumar's performance is good in the action-drama film which has some fantastic fight sequences, said Hindustan Times. The Karan Malhotra directorial has been rated 3/5 (Mid-Day, TOI), 2.5/5 (HT) and 2/5 (Indian Express).
