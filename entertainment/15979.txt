The Delhi Comic Con 2015 opened at Okhla's NSIC Grounds on Friday for a three-day event that will end on December 6. Described by Comic Con India's founder, Jatin Varma, as the “biggest show till date”, the event's highlight will be Kristian Nairn, who plays Hodor in 'Game of Thrones'. Fans will also get to meet Batman artist Ty Templeton.
