The trailer of the Akshay Kumar starrer film 'Airlift' was released on YouTube on Saturday. A teaser of the film was released in November. The film, also starring Nimrat Kaur and Purab Kohli, revolves around the evacuation of Indians from Kuwait during the Iraq-Kuwait war in 1990. Directed by Raja Krishna Menon, 'Airlift' is scheduled to release on January 22.
