The poster for the Ajay Devgn starrer action film 'Shivaay' was revealed on Sunday. Directed and produced by Devgn, 'Shivaay' comes with the tagline, 'This Diwali there will be destruction'. The film has been shot in locations including Bulgaria, Hyderabad and Uttarakhand. The film also stars Sayesha Saigal in a lead role and is scheduled to release on October 28.
