Tobias Strebel, the 35-year-old man who had spent $100,000 (Rs 65,97,045 approx) on plastic surgery to look exactly like Justin Bieber, has been found dead in Los Angeles. Strebel, who appeared on 'My Strange Addiction' in 2014, went missing three days before his dead body was recovered from a hotel room. The cause of death has not yet been determined.
