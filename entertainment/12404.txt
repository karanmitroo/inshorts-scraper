Poster of the Manoj Bajpayee, Kay Kay Menon and Anupam Kher starrer 'Saat Uchakkey' has been revealed. The film, which marks the debut of Ayushmann Khurana's brother Aparshakti Khurrana, had reportedly been on hold for three years as the Censor Board made 90 verbal cuts in the film. Directed by Sanjeev Sharma, it is scheduled to release on October 14.
