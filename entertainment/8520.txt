Actress Jacqueline Fernandez, who will be seen in upcoming action-comedy film 'Judwaa 2', said that she is watching a lot of Karisma Kapoor's films to prepare for her role. "Karisma... is an extremely amazing actress," she added. Karisma had starred in the original film 'Judwaa', which released in 1997. Also starring Varun Dhawan, 'Judwaa 2' will release on September 29.
