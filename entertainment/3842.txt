Karisma Kapoor has said that Varun Dhawan was seven or eight years old when she and Salman Khan were shooting for 'Judwaa' and he would visit the sets with his father director David Dhawan. "He always had a spark...he's the perfect choice after Salman," added Karisma. She further said that she's looking forward to watch 'Judwaa 2' with her children.
