Three Bollywood films have been selected to be screened at the BFI London Film Festival, to be held from March 31 to May 31, to pay tribute to William Shakespeare on his 400th death anniversary. The films are Vishal Bhardwaj's Shakespeare-inspired trilogy ‘Maqbool’ (2004), ‘Omkara’ (2006), and ‘Haider’ (2014) based on his classics ‘Macbeth’, ‘Othello’, and ‘Hamlet’ respectively.
