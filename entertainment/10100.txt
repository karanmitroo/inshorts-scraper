As per reports, actress Kriti Sanon will feature opposite Aamir Khan in the upcoming film 'Thugs of Hindostan'. The film is about a group of thugs in the pre-Independence era. Notably, 'Thugs of Hindostan' will mark the collaboration of Amitabh Bachchan and Aamir Khan in a film for the first time. The film is expected to release on Diwali 2018.
