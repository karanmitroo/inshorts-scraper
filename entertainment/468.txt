Malayalam actress Priya Prakash Varrier, whose clip started trending after the song 'Manikya Malaraya Poovi' went viral on the internet, has said Sanjay Leela Bhansali is her dream director. "I saw his latest film 'Padmaavat' and fell in love. I adore his craft," she added. The 18-year-old actress will make her acting debut in the Malayalam film 'Oru Adaar Love'.
