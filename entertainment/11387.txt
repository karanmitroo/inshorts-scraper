Actress Sonam Kapoor, in a Dice Kayek couture outfit, was among the celebrities who attended the closing ceremony of 18th Jio MAMI Mumbai International Film Festival on Thursday. Katrina wore a red dress by Prabal Gurung, while Neha Sharma wore a black ensemble from Aruni Designs. Sanjay Dutt, Richa Chadha and Kunal Kapoor also attended the closing ceremony.
