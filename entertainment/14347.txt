Following the clarification by Bollywood actor Nargis Fakhri that she went to New York to address her health concerns, 'Nargis Fakhri' was the fifth most trending Google search in India on Saturday. It was most looked up in Goa, Jammu and Kashmir and Jharkhand. 'Sangeeta Bijlani', 'Real Madrid' and 'RCB vs GL' were some of the other trending searches.
