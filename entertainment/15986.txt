According to the 'Year on Twitter 2015' report, released by the micro blogging-site on Monday, actor Amitabh Bachchan is most follwed in India. Bachchan topped the 'Most Followed Accounts' list with 18.1 million followers, followed by Shah Rukh Khan with 16.5 million followers. PM Modi ranked third in the list with 16.4 million followers.
