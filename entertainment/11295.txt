Model-actor Milind Soman won the national swimming championship for four consecutive years since 1984. Soman, who turned 51 on Friday, also won the 'Ironman' title by winning the Ironman Triathlon held in Zurich, Switzerland in July 2015. He started his modelling career in 1989 and debuted as an actor in 1996 with 'A Mouthful Of Sky', India's first English serial.
