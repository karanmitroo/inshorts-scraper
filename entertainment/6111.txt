Comedian Bharti Singh has slammed media for wrongly reporting about her engagement with Haarsh Limbaachiya. She clarified that her 'roka' with Haarsh happened six months back and added it's funny that media made statements based on a social media post. Reports of her engagement surfaced after Bharti shared a picture with Haarsh captioned, "#memoriablemoment #rokatime #baby #love #familytime."
