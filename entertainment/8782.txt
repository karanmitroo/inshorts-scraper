Deepika Padukone was among the front row attendees at the Michael Kors show at New York Fashion Week. The 31-year-old actress wore a navy blue dress from Kors' Spring 2017 collection paired with a white top and blue and white wedges. Deepika was seen with actress-model Emily Ratajkowski, model Jourdan Dunn and Japanese actress Hikari Mori at the show.
