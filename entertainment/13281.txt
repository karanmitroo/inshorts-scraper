As per reports, actor Shah Rukh Khan has convinced producer Rakesh Roshan to change the release date of the Hrithik Roshan starrer 'Kaabil', which is slated to release on the same date as SRK's 'Raees'. SRK tweeted, "Met friend/mentor/family after many a days. Mr. R. Roshan reminded me…it’s not important to do 2 much, it’s important to get 1 right."
