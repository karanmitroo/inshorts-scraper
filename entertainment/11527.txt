Actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan has revealed that her 4-year-old daughter Aaradhya hugged actor Ranbir Kapoor mistaking him for her father Abhishek Bachchan. "Because he was wearing this jacket and cap like AB and he had the stubble. Thinking he's AB, she hugged him and he was like 'Aww'," added Aishwarya. She further said that Aaradhya is shy around Ranbir.
