As per reports, actor Hrithik Roshan has been roped in to play the lead role in 'Aashiqui 3', a sequel to the musical superhit franchise 'Aashiqui'. Hrithik has recently shot a video song 'Dheere Dheere Se' from the original 'Aashiqui' with Sonam Kapoor. The actor is presently busy shooting for Ashutosh Gowarikar's 'Mohenjo Daro'.
