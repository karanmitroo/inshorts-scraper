'Tu Mera Bhai Nahi Hai', a new song from the Pulkit Samrat and Richa Chadha starrer 'Fukrey Returns' has been released. It has been composed by Sumeet Bellary with vocals by Gandhharv Sachdeav and rap by Raftaar. Directed by Mrighdeep Singh Lamba, 'Fukrey Returns' is scheduled to release on December 8.
