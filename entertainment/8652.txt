Pakistani actress Mahira Khan, who debuted in Bollywood opposite Shah Rukh Khan in 'Raees', has shared a video of herself dancing in a mustard field. Alongside the video, she wrote, "#cheapthrills what we couldn't do in Raees!" Earlier, Mahira said, "I might be the only one whom Shah Rukh didn't spread his arms for!" while referring to SRK's iconic pose.
