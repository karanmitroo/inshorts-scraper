Three of director Vishal Bhardwaj's films, Maqbool, Omkara and Haider will get adapted in the form of books, that will be published by Harper Collins. The 3 are adaptations of famous William Shakespeare plays Macbeth, Othello and Hamlet, respectively. Haider will hit the screens this year, while Maqbool and Omkara were released in 2003 and 2006.
