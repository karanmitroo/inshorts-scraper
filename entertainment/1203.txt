Salman Khan has become the first Bollywood actor with three films in the ₹300 crore club after his film 'Tiger Zinda Hai' crossed the milestone on the 16th day of its release. Salman's other films in the ₹300 crore club are 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' and 'Sultan'. 'Tiger Zinda Hai' had earned over ₹100 crore within three days of release.
