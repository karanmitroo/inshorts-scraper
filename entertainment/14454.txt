The Chris Evans and Robert Downey Jr starrer 'Captain America: Civil War' which released on Friday, is "among Marvel's best offerings so far", wrote India Today. The Times of India wrote that the film "is... a battle royale between superheroes" while Hindustan Times called it "a fast-paced... firework." It was rated 4.5/5 (India Today) and 4/5 (HT, TOI).
