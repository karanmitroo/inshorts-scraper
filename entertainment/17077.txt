Veteran filmmaker Subash Ghai will be conferred with the Lifetime Achievement honour, for his outstanding contribution to Indian cinema, at the IIFA awards ceremony in Kuala Lumpur in June. The award will be presented to him by actors of his blockbuster "Ram Lakhan"- Anil Kapoor and Jackie Shroff. Ghai, 70, has made notable films like "Karz", "Hero" and "Taal".
