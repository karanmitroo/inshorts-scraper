A new song titled 'Har Gully Mein Dhoni Hai' from the biopic 'MS Dhoni: The Untold Story' starring Sushant Singh Rajput, has been released. The song has been composed and sung by Rochak Kohli with lyrics by Manoj Muntashir. Directed by Neeraj Pandey and also starring Kiara Advani and Disha Patani, the film will release on September 30.
