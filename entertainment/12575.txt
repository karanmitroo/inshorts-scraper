The teaser for 'Dandruff Nahi Chalega', a promotional film for 'Head & Shoulders', starring actor Ranveer Singh has been released. It has been directed by Farah Khan and stars Ranveer as four different types of boys in a college. The video starts with Ranveer reportedly imitating actor Ranbir Kapoor's look from the song 'Dilliwaali Girlfriend' from 'Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani'.
