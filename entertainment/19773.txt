As per trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the movies D-Day and Ramaiya Vastavaiya, which released this week, have earned Rs.13.69-crore and Rs.15.30-crore at the domestic box office respectively during the weekend. The biopic Bhaag Milkha Bhaag, released last week is still the first choice of movie goers as the film earned Rs.17.50-crore in its second weekend, bringing the total to Rs.70-crore.
