Actor Akshay Kumar on Friday revealed the poster of his upcoming film, 'Housefull 3'. Directed by Sajid Samji and Farhad Samji, the film also stars Riteish Deshmukh and Abhishek Bachchan, while Lisa Haydon, Jacqueline Fernandez and Nargis Fakhri have been cast as lead actresses. The third instalment of the 'Housefull' franchise, the film is scheduled to release on June 3.
