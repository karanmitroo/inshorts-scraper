Hollywood actors Michael Fassbender and Kate Winslet will feature in the biopic on Apple co-founder Steve Jobs, to be directed by Danny Boyle. While Fassbender will play Jobs, Winslet will feature former Macintosh marketing boss Joanna Hoffman. Seth Rogen will portray Steve Wozniak. Furthermore, Katherine Waterston will feature as Jobs' former girlfriend Chrisann Brennan.
