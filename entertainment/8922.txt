The Akshay Kumar and Huma Qureshi starrer film 'Jolly LLB 2', which released on Friday, "feels stretched at times," wrote India Today. Hindustan Times said the "theme, not Akshay Kumar, is the star of this satire". NDTV wrote that "Akshay Kumar's film is both funny and thought-provoking". The film has been rated 3/5 (Hindustan Times, NDTV) and 3.5/5 (India Today).
