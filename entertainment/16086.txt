The trailer of Amitabh Bachchan and Farhan Akhtar starrer film 'Wazeer', which was released on Wednesday, has got over 4.46 lakh views on YouTube in 24 hours. The film, directed by Bejoy Nambiar, also features Aditi Rao Haidri and John Abraham in key roles. Neil Nitin Mukesh plays the main antagonist. 'Wazir' is scheduled to release on January 8.
