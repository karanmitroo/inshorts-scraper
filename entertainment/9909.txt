Malaika Arora Khan, Saiyami Kher and fashion designer Masaba Gupta have featured in a photoshoot in the January edition of Vogue India. They are seen posing inside a gym. While Malaika is seen in a bralet and a cut-out midi skirt from Murmur, Saiyami wears a Jeremy Scott swimsuit. Further, Masaba is seen wearing a Balmain dress for the shoot.
