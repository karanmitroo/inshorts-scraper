TIME magazine in its list of 'The 30 Most Influential Teens of 2016' included reality television star Kylie Jenner, actors Chloe Grace Moretz, Jaden Smith and Maisie Williams. Maddie Ziegler, the 14-year-old dancer known for featuring in Singer Sia's music videos, also featured in the list. The list also included Malala Yousafzai and President Barack Obama's daughters Sasha and Malia.
