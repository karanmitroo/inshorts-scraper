Amitabh Bachchan and Dhanush starrer ‘Shamitabh’ has collected only Rs 3.5 crore on its opening day at the box office. According to reports, the film has opened to a dismal start with just 15 per cent occupancy rate. 'Shamitabh’, which got mixed reviews, witnessed somewhat better response in metros but overall opening day occupancy had been poor in other sectors.
