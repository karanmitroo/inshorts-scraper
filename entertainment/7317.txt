TV producer Ekta Kapoor took to Instagram to share a picture with actress Sakshi Tanwar and Union Minister of Textiles Smriti Irani. While Sakshi played the female lead in 'Kahaani Ghar Ghar Kii', Smriti was known for her portrayal of the character Tulsi Virani in 'Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi'. Both the shows were produced by Ekta Kapoor.
