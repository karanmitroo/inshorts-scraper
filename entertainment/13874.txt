The trailer for the Rajeev Khandelwal and Gauahar Khan starrer 'Fever' was released on Tuesday. The suspense thriller revolves around Khandelwal as an assassin hiding under a different identity. The film also stars Italian actress and former 'Bond' girl Caterina Murino, who starred in the film 'Casino Royale'. Directed by Rajeev Jhaveri, 'Fever' will release on July 22.
