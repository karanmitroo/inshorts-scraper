The first look of Vidya Balan and Emraan Hashmi-starrer 'Humari Adhuri Kahani' was revealed Thursday by the film's producer Mahesh Bhatt on Twitter. The film, scheduled to release on June 12 next year, is reportedly based on Bhatt's life. After 'The Dirty Picture' and 'Ghanchakkar', this is Emraan and Vidya's third film together.
