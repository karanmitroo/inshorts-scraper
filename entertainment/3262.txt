A picture revealing Amitabh Bachchan's look from the upcoming film 'Thugs of Hindostan' has surfaced online. In the picture, Amitabh can be seen in a suit of iron armour, with a sword and black turban. Also starring Aamir Khan, Katrina Kaif and Fatima Sana Shaikh, 'Thugs of Hindostan' is scheduled to release on November 7, 2018.
