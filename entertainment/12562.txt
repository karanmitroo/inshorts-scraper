The teaser of the Ranbir Kapoor, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Anushka Sharma starrer film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' has been released. The upcoming romantic drama film marks Karan Johar's return as a director after the 2012 film 'Student of The Year'. Also starring Fawad Khan, 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' is scheduled to release on October 28.
