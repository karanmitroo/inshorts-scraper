'Sau Tarah Ke', the first song from the John Abraham, Varun Dhawan and Jacqueline Fernandez starrer upcoming film 'Dishoom' was released on Thursday. The song, featuring all three actors of the film, has been sung by Jonita Gandhi and Amit Mishra, while it has been composed by Pritam. 'Dishoom' also features actor Akshaye Khanna and will release on July 29.
