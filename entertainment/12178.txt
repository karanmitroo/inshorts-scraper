Beverage brand 'Paper Boat' has released an animated advertisement film titled 'Hope, the Boat', which aims to 'instil a spirit of optimism' among its consumers. It is reportedly India's first 3D animated short film and took nearly 75 days to make. The film has been directed by Dhruv Sachdeva and Clifford Afonso, with the sound score composed by Aradhana Mathews.
