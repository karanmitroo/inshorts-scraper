Shoojit Sircar directorial ‘Piku’, featuring Amitabh Bachchan, Deepika Padukone and Irrfan Khan, released this Friday. “..it is one of those endearing narratives where the actors' performances and nuanced ways in which the director tells the tale overpower the predictable storyline,” said Hindustan Times. The movie is rated 2.5 (Indian Express), 3.5 (HT, NDTV) and 4 (TOI).
