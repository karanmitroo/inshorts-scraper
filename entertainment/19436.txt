Producer Sajid Nadiawala has announced that his directorial debut 'Kick' featuring Salman Khan, Jacqueline Fernandez and Randeep Hooda would be released on Eid in 2014. The first schedule of the movie wrapped up in London and Salman will start shooting for the movie today in Mumbai. Notably, Salman's first hit on Eid was Sajid Nadiadwala's 'Judwaa' 16-years ago in 1997.
