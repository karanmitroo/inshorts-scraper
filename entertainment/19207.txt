Noted actor-director Kamal Haasan and classical singer Begum Parveen Sultana have been selected for the prestigious Padma Bhushan award this year. The Padma Shri awardees include, Vidya Balan, Paresh Rawal, Sabitri Chatterjee (Bengali actresses), Vijay Ghate (tabla player), Musafir Ram Bhardwaj (musician), Rani Karnaa (Kathak dancer), among others.
