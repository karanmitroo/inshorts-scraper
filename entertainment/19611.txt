Opening with a collection of Rs.12.51 crore, adult comedy "Grand Masti" has generated Rs.66.48 crore within a week of release. The film collected Rs.13.21 crore on Saturday, Rs.14.46 crore on Sunday and Rs.26.3 crore through the week, via both single screens and multiplexes. The film, co-produced by Ashok Thakeria and Indra Kumar, stars Ritesh Deshmukh,Vivek Oberoi and Aftab with 6-actresses.
