The trailer of Akshay Kumar-starrer 'Brothers', which was released on Wednesday, has got over one million hits on YouTube in just one day. “Super fast 1million views! Thank you for all the love.....@karanmalhotra21's labour of love,” film’s producer Karan Johar tweeted. Produced by Dharma Productions, the Karan Malhotra directorial, also starring Sidharth Malhotra, is set to release on Aug 14.
