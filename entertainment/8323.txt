'Cheez Badi', a recreation of the song 'Tu Cheez Badi Hai Mast' from the Akshay Kumar and Raveena Tandon starrer 1994 film 'Mohra', has been released. The new version of the song features in the film ''Machine" and has been picturised on the lead stars Mustafa and Kiara Advani. Udit Narayan and Neha Kakkar have sung the new version.
