Actor Shah Rukh Khan's former chartered accountant Moreshwar Ajgaonkar has told Income Tax officials that he used forged documents to purchase Alibag plots on the instructions of SRK. The forged documents include a record of rights that show the existence of a bungalow from pre-1991. Ajgaonkar, who was director of SRK's Deja Vu Farms, was mentioned as the property's owner.
