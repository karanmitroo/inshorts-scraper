A new trailer of Rohit Shetty's 'Dilwale', starring Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol, was released on Friday. The trailer gives a glimpse of the love story of Raj (SRK) and Meera (Kajol). The film also features Varun Dhawan and Kriti Sanon with a huge supporting cast including Varun Sharma and Boman Irani. 'Dilwale' is scheduled to release on December 18.
