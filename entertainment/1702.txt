Actress Ankita Lokhande, while talking about the upcoming biopic on Rani Laxmibai titled 'Manikarnika: The Queen of Jhansi', said, "Kangana is the hero of the film, and she's launching me as the heroine of this film." Ankita further said that her character in the biopic 'Jhalkari Bai' was one of the greatest heroes who fought with Rani Laxmibai.
