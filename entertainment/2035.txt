Actress Raai Laxmi's Bollywood debut 'Julie 2', which released on Friday, is "cringeworthy," wrote India Today. "Give 'Julie 2' a miss," said The Times of India (TOI) while Bollywood Hungama wrote, "'Julie 2' is a senseless saga and has very few sizzling scenes." It has been rated 1/5 (India Today, Bollywood Hungama) and 2/5 (TOI).
