Bollywood actor, director and producer Raj Kapoor was born on December 14, 1924, in Peshawar, British India (now in Pakistan). Kapoor's first film as a lead actor was 'Neel Kamal', however, he first acted as a child actor in 'Inquilaab'. A recipient of Dada Saheb Phalke award, two National Awards and Padma Bhushan (1971), Kapoor died of asthma in 1988.
