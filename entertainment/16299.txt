Comedy group AIB's advertising wing 'AIB Vigyapanti' has made a qawwali video for dating app Truly Madly. In the video, featuring Shweta Basu Prasad, two girls describe the kind of messages they get from 'creeps' in their Facebook profile's 'other' folder. The video, which was uploaded on YouTube by Truly Madly on Thursday, has over 1 lakh 18 thousand views.
