Bhaag Milkha Bhaag has garnered the third-highest opening weekend collections in 2013 so far. The Rs.30-crore film opened to favourable response earning Rs.32.25 crore at the domestic box-office over its first three days since release. This is only behind the first-weekend collections of Race 2(Rs.51.3 crore) and Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani (Rs.62.11 crore ) this year.
