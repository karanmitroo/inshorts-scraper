Rajkummar Rao said that after watching his performance in 'Bareilly Ki Barfi', many girls reached out to him on social media and asked him to marry them. Responding to how rumoured girlfriend Patralekhaa reacted to this, Rajkummar quoted a dialogue from Salman Khan's 'Wanted'. He joked, "Ek baar jo maine commitment kardi, uske baad main apneaap ki bhi nahi sunta."
