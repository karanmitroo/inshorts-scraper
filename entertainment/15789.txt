Arjun Kapoor on Friday took to Instagram to share the first look of his upcoming film 'Ki and Ka', also featuring Kareena Kapoor. "Kia & Kabir's romance begins...#kiandka coming soon", he captioned the picture. Directed by R Balki, 'Ki and Ka' is a romantic-comedy which revolves around a married couple played by the duo. It will release on April 1.
