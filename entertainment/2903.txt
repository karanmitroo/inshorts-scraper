Filmmaker Vishal Bhardwaj, who co-produced the film 'Talvar' based on the Aarushi Talwar murder case, reacted to the verdict on the case and tweeted, "Overwhelmed and deeply relieved upon hearing the news of the acquittal." Meghna Gulzar, who directed the film said, "Factually, there is no conclusive evidence against the Talwars... It is a huge victory for justice."
