Cartoon Network on Tuesday launched the first look of the new Powerpuff Girls series, set to launch globally in 2016. The series will feature the voices of Amanda Leighton as 'Blossom', Kristen Li as 'Bubbles' and Natalie Palamides as 'Buttercup'. The original series that premiered in 1996 had earned two Emmy Awards and countless animation honors throughout its 78-episode run.
