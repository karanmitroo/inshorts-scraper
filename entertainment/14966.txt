Bollywood actress Kangana Ranaut turns 29 on Wednesday. Kangana made her Bollywood debut in 2006 with the film 'Gangster' and went on to win the Filmfare Award for Best Female Debut. She has been a recipient of two National Awards - Best Supporting Actress for 'Fashion' (2008) and Best Actress for 'Queen' (2014).  She will next be seen in 'Rangoon'.
