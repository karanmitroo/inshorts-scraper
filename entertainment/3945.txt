'Tan Tana Tan' song from 'Judwaa' has been recreated as 'Chalti Hai Kya 9 Se 12' after 20 years for 'Judwaa 2', starring Varun Dhawan, Jacqueline Fernandez and Taapsee Pannu. The new version has been co-composed by Sandeep Shirodkar and Anu Malik, who also composed the original song, with vocals by Dev Negi and Neha Kakkar.
