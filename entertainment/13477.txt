The teaser of the song 'Jaaneman Aah’, featuring Parineeti Chopra, from the Varun Dhawan and John Abraham starrer ‘Dishoom' was released on Friday. "The hardest I have worked and the most fun I have had," tweeted Parineeti while sharing the teaser of the song where she has made a special appearance. The film also stars Jacqueline Fernandez and Akshaye Khanna.
