Actress Vidya Balan will be portraying a Radio Jockey (RJ) in the upcoming film 'Tumhari Sulu'. "This gem of a script will make you cheer and laugh. The character of Sulu is fun, happy-go-lucky but grounded, everything that Vidya is even in real life," said Atul Kasbekar, who is co-producing the film. The film will be directed by Suresh Triveni.
