The Randeep Hooda and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan starrer 'Sarbjit' premiered at the Cannes Film Festival on Sunday. Aishwarya along with her co-star Richa Chadha, director Omung Kumar and producer Jackky Bhagnani attended the film's premiere while Randeep Hooda was not present for the screening. The film is based on Sarabjit Singh, an Indian prisoner who died in a Pakistan prison.
