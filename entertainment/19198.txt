Farhan Akhtar was conferred with the ‘Best Actor in Leading Role (Male)’ award on Friday, for Bhaag Milkha Bhaag, at the 59th Idea Filmfare Awards 2013. And, the ‘Best Actor in Leading Role (Female)’ went to Deepika Padukone for Ram-Leela. Actors Dhanush (Raanjhana) and Vaani Kapoor (Shuddh Desi Romance) were awarded with Best Debut, Male and Female, respectively.
