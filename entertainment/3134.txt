TV celebrities who will participate in the reality show 'Bigg Boss 11', which premiered on Sunday, include 'Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi' actor Hiten Tejwani, former actress of 'Bhabi Ji Ghar Par Hai' Shilpa Shinde, and Hina Khan. Dawood Ibrahim's relative Zubair Khan, godwoman SsHivani Durga, and a peon's daughter Jyoti Kumari are also among the 18 contestants.
