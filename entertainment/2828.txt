Riteish Deshmukh, while sharing a picture on Twitter of people walking in ways to avoid potholes on the road, captioned it, "Moon walk". Earlier this year, RJ Malishka had released a song titled 'Sonu Song Pothole Mix'. It criticised the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) over the multitude of potholes in Mumbai and the issues that it causes during monsoon.
