The trailer for the Arshad Warsi, Aditi Rao Hydari and Boman Irani starrer 'The Legend of Michael Mishra' was released on Friday. Warsi stars as the titular character, who is described as a "kidnapping specialist". The film also stars Boman's son Kayoze Irani. Directed and written by Manish Jha, the film is scheduled to release on August 5.
