Malayalam actress Priya Prakash Varrier beat Sunny Leone to become the most searched celebrity in India on Google today after her clip from the song 'Manikya Malaraya Poovi' went viral. Deepika Padukone, Alia Bhatt and Katrina Kaif were the most-searched celebrities after Leone. Interestingly, the Malayalam actress gained over 2.3 million followers on Instagram after the video was released.
