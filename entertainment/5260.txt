The superhero film Spider-Man: Homecoming, which released on Friday, "is fresh, funny and fairly exciting", wrote Hindustan Times. Firstpost called the film the "most enjoyable Marvel movie since The Avengers" while Times of India wrote that lead actor Tom Holland is "the most relatable Spider-Man, till date". The film was rated 3.5/5 (Hindustan Times) and 4/5 (Firstpost, Times of India).
