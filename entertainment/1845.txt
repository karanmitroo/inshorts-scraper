Rajkummar Rao was named Best Actor (Critics Choice) for 'Newton' while Irrfan Khan won the Best Actor award for 'Hindi Medium' and Vidya Balan was named Best Actress (Critics Choice) for 'Tumhari Sulu' at Star Screen Awards 2017. Rajkummar also won the Best Supporting Actor award for 'Bareilly Ki Barfi'. Further, 'Newton' won the award for Best Film (Critics Choice).
