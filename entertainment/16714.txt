Actress Sonakshi Sinha has been roped in to play the role of underworld don Dawood Ibrahim's sister Haseena in an upcoming biopic, to be directed by Apoorva Lakhia. "It is a brilliant script... I can't wait to play haseena," Sonakshi tweeted. The 28-yr-old actress had played Dawood's love interest in the film "Once Upon a Time in Mumbaai Dobaara!".
