A song titled 'Nachle Na' from Taapsee Pannu and Saqib Saleem starrer 'Dil Juunglee' has been released. With vocals by Guru Randhawa and Neeti Mohan, the song has been composed by Randhawa and Rajat Nagpal. The film, which is scheduled to release on February 16, also stars former RJ Abhilash Thapliyal and actresses Nidhi Singh and Srishti Shrivastava.
