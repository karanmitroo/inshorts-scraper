Actor-singer Himesh Reshammiya's 'The Xpose' has collected over Rs 10 crores in India in the opening weekend, as per trade analyst Taran Adarsh. The movie, directed by Ananth Mahadevan, had reportedly hit 800 screens in India on Friday. The Xpose also stars Irrfan, Sonali Raut, Zoya Afroz and rapper-composer Honey Singh, who makes his acting debut with the thriller.
