Director Vishal Bhardwaj has roped in Shahid Kapoor, Shraddha Kapoor and Tabu for his next movie, 'Haider', an Indian adaptation of Shakespeare's Hamlet. Shahid will be essaying the lead role of Hamlet while Shraddha and Tabu's roles have not been disclosed. The movie will be shot in the beautiful locales around Kashmir, starting by the end of 2013.
