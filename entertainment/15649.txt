Fashion designer Manish Malhotra, in collaboration with the NGO Sahachari Foundation, on Thursday showcased his collection, 'Regal Threads,' with Bollywood actors Aditya Roy Kapur and Katrina Kaif as showstoppers. While Katrina wore a glittery lehenga, Aditya wore a black and gold sherwani. The collection focused on reviving traditional weaves as a part of 'Make In India.'
