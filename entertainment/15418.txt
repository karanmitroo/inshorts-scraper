Bollywood film 'Sanam Teri Kasam', which released on Friday, has earned ₹4.66 crore at the Indian box office in its opening weekend. According to trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film grossed ₹1.25 crore on Friday, ₹1.51 crore on Saturday and ₹1.90 crore on Sunday. The Radhika Rao and Vinay Sapru directorial stars Pakistani actress Mawra Hocane and Harshvardhan Rane.
