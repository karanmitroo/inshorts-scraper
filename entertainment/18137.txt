Actor Nawazuddin Siddiqui has replaced Farhan Akhtar in Rahul Dholakia directorial - 'Raees'. Earlier, Farhan was selected for the role but he had to opt out of the film as the dates clashed with his 'Rock On 2' schedule. The film also has Shah Rukh Khan playing the role of a Gujarati don. Nawazuddin will be seen playing a policeman.
