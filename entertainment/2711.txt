Actress Niharika Singh, who starred with Nawazuddin Siddiqui in 'Miss Lovely', has slammed the actor for revealing details of their affair in his biography 'An Ordinary Life: A Memoir'. Niharika said, "He...wants to sell his book and...is willing to exploit and disrespect a woman just to do so." She added whatever he has written about them, is without her consent.
