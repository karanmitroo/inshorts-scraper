Gilu Joseph, who was slut-shamed for breastfeeding a baby on the cover of Malayalam magazine 'Grihalakshmi', said, "If I wanted publicity I'd have posed for some other magazine like Playboy." "Why would I go for such a noble cause [of destigmatizing breastfeeding in public]?" she added. Gilu further questioned, "Do [people] think an unmarried girl opened her buttons for publicity?"
