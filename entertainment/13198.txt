The trailer for the upcoming animated superhero film 'The LEGO Batman Movie' has been released. The film is a spin-off of the 2014 film 'The Lego Movie' and revolves around the DC Comics character 'Batman', voiced by actor Will Arnett. Directed by Chris McKay and also featuring Zach Galifianakis and Michael Cera, the film will release on February 10, 2017.
