TV and film producer Ekta Kapoor has shared a picture of her friend Union Minister of Textiles Smriti Irani holding her brother Tusshar Kapoor's son Laksshya. Ekta captioned the picture, "Friends like family!" while Smriti also reposted the picture with the caption, 'Viva la familia'. She has worked with Ekta in the television serial Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi.
