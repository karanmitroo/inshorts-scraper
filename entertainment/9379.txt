According to reports, actors Alia Bhatt and Varun Dhawan will appear on the fifth season of talk show 'Koffee With Karan' for the second time. They will reportedly be on the show to promote their upcoming film 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania'. Alia had appeared on the show's first episode with Shah Rukh Khan, while Varun had appeared with Arjun Kapoor.
