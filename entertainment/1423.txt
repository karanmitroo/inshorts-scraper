The title song from Akshay Kumar, Sonam Kapoor and Radhika Apte starrer 'PadMan' has been released. It has been composed by Amit Trivedi with vocals by Mika Singh and lyrics by Kausar Munir. 'PadMan' is based on the life of Arunachalam Muruganantham, a man who made sanitary pads accessible to the women of his village.
