'Celebrity Classico 2016', a charity football match between Bollywood personalities led by Ranbir Kapoor and Indian cricketers led by Virat Kohli ended in a 2-2 draw on Saturday in Mumbai. The match was an initiative of Abhishek Bachchan's charity 'Playing for Humanity' and the Virat Kohli Foundation. "We had done this... to raise... funds for drought victims," said Abhishek.
