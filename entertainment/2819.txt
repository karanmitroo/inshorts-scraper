Rishi Kapoor took to social media to share his look from the upcoming film 'Mulq'. Directed by Anubhav Sinha, the film also stars Taapsee Pannu, Neena Gupta, Ashutosh Rana and Prateik Babbar. Talking about the film, Rishi had earlier said, "I've not done drama in a long time and when Anubhav narrated the film to me, I loved the concept."
