'Dum Dum', the first song from the Anushka Sharma and Diljit Dosanjh starrer upcoming romantic comedy film 'Phillauri' has been released. The song has been described as the first Sufi love song of the year. It has been composed by Shashwat Sachdev with vocals by Romy and Vivek Hariharan. Directed by Anshai Lal, 'Phillauri' will release on March 24.
