The title track from the upcoming Ajay Devgn and Parineeti Chopra starrer 'Golmaal Again' has been released. The song has been composed by Thaman S with lyrics by Kumaar, and vocals by Brijesh Shandilya and Aditi Singh Sharma. Produced and directed by Rohit Shetty, 'Golmaal Again' is scheduled to release on October 20.
