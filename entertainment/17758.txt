Shahid Kapoor, who began shooting his upcoming film 'Udta Punjab', says his role in the movie is toughest and scariest so far. The Abhishek Chaubey directorial, also stars Alia Bhatt and Shahid's ex-girlfriend Kareena Kapoor Khan. "Scariest part I have played yet have no clue how I'm going to get it right. Wish me luck guys," Shahid posted on Twitter.
