Actor Rajinikanth, popularly called the 'superstar of South', turned 65 on Saturday. Having started his career in 1975 with Tamil film 'Apoorva Raagangal', Rajinikanth has also acted in Hollywood film 'Bloodstone'. He was honoured with the Padma Bhushan in 2000. Rajinikanth, who has spent over 30 years in the industry, made his Bollywood debut with 'Andha Kanoon' alongside Amitabh Bachchan.
