Filmmaker Mahesh Bhatt has sold the remake rights of his 1982 film 'Arth'. "The film will be a given a 21st-century interpretation, contemporized by a new mind as per the new age. I have suggested... a writer who can work on the script," said Mahesh. The original film starred Shabana Azmi, Kulbhushan Kharbanda, Smita Patil and Raj Kiran.
