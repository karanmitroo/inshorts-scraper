India has topped entries from the Indian subcontinent with two entries in the Cannes Film Festival's official selection. A short Nepali-language film 'Gudh' by Saurav Rai from Kalimpong and the documentary 'The Cinema Travellers' by Shirley Abraham and Amit Madheshiya are the two entries. Meanwhile, Anurag Kashyap’s 'Raman Raghav 2.0' will premiere as part of the festival's 'Directors’ Fortnight'.
