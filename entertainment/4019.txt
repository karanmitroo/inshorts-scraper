Mika Singh has sung the song 'Laddoo' for the upcoming Ayushmann Khurrana and Bhumi Pednekar starrer film 'Shubh Mangal Saavdhan'. The song has been penned and composed by Tanishk Bagchi and Vayu. The film, in which Ayushmann plays a character who has erectile dysfunction, is scheduled to release on September 1.
