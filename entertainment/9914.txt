Singer Sia's lyric video for 'Never Give Up', the song which features in the soundtrack for the Dev Patel and Nicole Kidman starrer 'Lion', has been released. The video features two faceless kids, with Sia's signature hairstyle, trying to run away from a deserted railway station. The film also stars Indian actors Nawazuddin Siddiqui, Tannishtha Chatterjee and Deepti Naval.
