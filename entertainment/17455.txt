Actor Abhishek Bachchan will be seen doing a cameo in his wife Aishwarya Rai starrer 'Jazbaa'. The action film is being directed by Sanjay Gupta, who took to Twitter to make the announcement. Gupta tweeted, "Big excitement at the shoot today as Abhishek Bachchan shoots his special appearance in JAZBAA." The film is scheduled to release on Oct 9.
