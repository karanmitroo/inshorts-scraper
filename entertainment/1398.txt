Rapper-singer Yo Yo Honey Singh's comeback song in Bollywood after two years 'Dil Chori' from the upcoming film 'Sonu Ke Titu Ki Sweety' has been released. The song is a recreated version of Hans Raj Hans' song 'Dil Chori Sada Ho Gaya'. Further, the new version has been composed by Honey Singh with him also contributing to the song's lyrics.
