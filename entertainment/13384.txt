Actor Colin Firth will return as 'Harry Hart' in 'Kingsman: The Golden Circle', the sequel to the 2015 film 'Kingsman: The Secret Service'. The actor's image was shared by co-star Pedro Pascal with the caption "Hide behind Harry. Safe Place. Kingsman 2", confirming that 'Harry' was not killed in the first film. 'Kingsman 2' will release on June 16, 2017.
