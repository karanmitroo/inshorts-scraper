The Alia Bhatt, Sidharth Malhotra and Fawad Khan starrer film 'Kapoor & Sons' was the most booked movie on the online ticketing portal BookMyShow in the third week of March. The romantic comedy-drama film directed by Shakun Batra and produced by Dharma Productions is rated 78%. 'Batman vs Superman' and 'Oopiri' were the second and third most booked respectively.
