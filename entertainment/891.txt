As per reports, Bigg Boss 11 runner-up Hina Khan got ₹46 lakh more than the money received by the winner Shilpa Shinde. While Shilpa reportedly took home ₹1.29 crore, Hina is said to have received ₹1.75 crore. The difference in the total amount is said to be due to the different contracts signed by Hina and Shilpa for the show.
