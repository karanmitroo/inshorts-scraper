Filmmaker Ram Gopal Varma has tweeted that he likes the 'Sarkar' of Amitabh Bachchan, in a reference to the film series, better than PM Narendra Modi's sarkar (government). RGV further wrote, "But love the SARKARgiri of Narendra Modi more because of upcoming Ram mandir." Meanwhile, in another tweet, RGV wrote, "I feel Narendra Modi is far bigger God than ShriRamJi."
