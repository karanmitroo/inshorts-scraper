The Shraddha Kapoor and Aditya Roy Kapur starrer 'OK Jaanu' has earned ₹13.8 crore in its opening weekend. The romantic drama film minted ₹4.08 crore on Friday, ₹4.90 crore on Saturday and ₹4.82 crore on Sunday, as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. Produced by Mani Ratnam and Karan Johar, the film is a remake of Tamil film 'OK Kanmani'.
