Chris Pratt-starrer 'Jurassic World' has become the first film to gross over $500 million globally in a single weekend. As of Sunday, the film had collected $511.8 million, surpassing the collections of  'Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows Part 2' ($483.2). Directed by Colin Trevorrow, the film also features Bollywood actor Irrfan Khan in a significant role.
