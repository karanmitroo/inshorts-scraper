The Aamir Khan, Fatima Sana Shaikh and Sanya Malhotra starrer 'Dangal' has been made tax free in Haryana. The state's CM Manohar Lal Khattar said that such a decision was taken as 'Dangal' was giving a boost to the 'Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao' mission in the state. The film has already been made tax free in Uttar Pradesh and Uttarakhand.
