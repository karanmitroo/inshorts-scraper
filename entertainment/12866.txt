'Din Mein Karengey Jagrata', the first song from the Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Amy Jackson starrer 'Freaky Ali', has been released. The song has been composed by the music director duo Sajid-Wajid while Wajid along with Divya Kumar and Swati Sharma have lent their vocals for the track. The song has been picturised on Siddiqui and his co-star Arbaaz Khan.
