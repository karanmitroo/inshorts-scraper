The teaser poster of Vidya Balan starrer upcoming comedy drama film 'Tumhari Sulu' has been released. Vidya, who plays a fan of Sridevi in the film, will be seen recreating the song 'Hawa Hawai' from the film 'Mr India'. Directed by Suresh Triveni and also starring Neha Dhupia and Manav Kaul, 'Tumhari Sulu' is scheduled to release on December 1.
