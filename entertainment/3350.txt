Activist and Nobel Peace Laureate Malala Yousafzai took to social media to share a picture with actress Priyanka Chopra from a United Nations event. "Can't believe I met Priyanka Chopra," wrote Malala. Responding to this, Priyanka tweeted, "No words will be enough...I can't believe I..met..U!! You're just a young girl with so much heart...and such achievements. So proud."
