Actor Bobby Deol will star in Salman Khan's upcoming film 'Race 3', as confirmed by the film's producer Ramesh Taurani. "He will be styled differently in the film and you will see him in a never seen before avatar," said Taurani. This will be the producer's third film with Bobby Deol after 'Soldier' (1998) and 'Naqaab' (2007).
