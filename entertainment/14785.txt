Priyanka Chopra will receive the 'Best Actress of the Year' award by the Dadasaheb Phalke Film Foundation for her performance in Sanjay Leela Bhansali's 'Bajirao Mastani'. The award will be given to her on the occasion of 147th Dadasaheb Phalke Jayanti. The award ceremony will be held on April 24. Notably, this is Priyanka's second award from the Foundation.
