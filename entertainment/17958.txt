Bollywood actor and Politician Shatrughan Sinha along with his wife Poonam Sinha and actress-daughter Sonakshi Sinha met PM Narendra Modi on Tuesday in New Delhi. "With Party colleague & popular actor, Shatrughan Sinha ji and his family. A delightful meeting," the PM tweeted along a picture. Earlier in the day, Spiritual leader Sri Sri Ravi Shankar also met PM Modi.
