Actor Salman Khan's sister Arpita Khan Sharma has revealed that Salman's brand 'Being Human' will launch a jewellery line next month. "The Being Human jewellery basically is affordable to all the consumers. It's 70 per cent ladies' collection and 30 per cent the men's," said Arpita. The brand has a clothing line with Salman himself often wearing 'Being Human' T-shirts.
