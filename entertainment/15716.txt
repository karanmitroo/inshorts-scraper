Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, who will feature in fashion photographer Dabboo Ratnani's 2016 calendar, started the shoot on Tuesday. She wore a floral, light-hued Georges Hobeika Couture gown, which she earlier wore to the Stardust Awards. The calendar's 17th edition also features Aishwarya's father-in-law Amitabh Bachchan among other Bollywood A-listers like Shah Rukh Khan and Priyanka Chopra.
