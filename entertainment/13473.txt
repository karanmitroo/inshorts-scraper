'Brahman Naman', Netflix's first Indian release premiered globally on the online streaming site on Wednesday. Directed by Qaushiq Mukherjee, popularly known as 'Q', it is a comedy film set in the 80's. The film, which premiered first at the 2016 Sundance Film Festival, stars Shashank Arora, Vijay Mallya's son Siddharth Mallya and stand-up comedian Biswa Kalyan Rath among others.
