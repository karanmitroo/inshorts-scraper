Irrfan Khan and Konkona Sen Sharma starrer 'Talvar', which released across India on Friday, "is an intriguing and gripping thriller and boasts of brilliant performances, witty dialogues", said HT. In the film, based on Aarushi Talwar murder case, "Irrfan steals the show", wrote India Today. The film has been rated 4.5/5 (TOI) and 3.5/5 (HT, NDTV and India Today).
