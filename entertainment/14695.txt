Three students from IIT Madras have made a music video titled 'Be Our Pondati (Wife)' which is a parody of Carly Rae Jepsen's song 'Call Me Maybe'. Krupa Varghese, who features in the video, talks about what Indians look for in brides. The video, produced for a parody contest, has clocked over 2.6 lakh views in less than two weeks.
