According to reports, Shahid Kapoor has replaced Ranveer Singh in the Hindi remake of the 2017 Telugu film 'Arjun Reddy'. "After 'Padmavati', Ranveer is voluntarily off-limits for dark characters... So (director) Sandeep Vanga went to Ranveer's Padmavati co-star Shahid Kapoor," said a source. The film revolves around a character who turns into a drug-addict after having a break-up.
