Musician Elton John has confirmed that he will be star in 'Kingsman: The Golden Circle', the sequel to the 2014 film 'Kingsman: The Secret Service'. The sequel will star Taron Egerton, while Channing Tatum is a new addition to the cast. Julianne Moore and Halle Berry will also reportedly join the sequel, which will release on June 16, 2017.
