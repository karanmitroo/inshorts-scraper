With an estimated net worth of $30 million, actress Preity Zinta, who is a significant shareholder in the KXIP, is the least wealthy among all the IPL team owners, according to Wealth-X. The top spot is occupied by MI owner Mukesh Ambani, having an estimated net worth of $21.2 billion, followed by SH owner Kalanithi Maran ($2.2 bn).
