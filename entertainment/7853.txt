Actor Nawazuddin Siddiqui has said that he has no equation or relationship with actor Irrfan Khan. He added, "Mere acting ka alag tareeka hai...Uska apna alag tareeka hai." Nawazuddin and Irrfan, who featured together in the 2013 film 'The Lunchbox', reportedly shared an apartment when they were trying to get into Bollywood.
