The first official teaser of the Arjun Rampal starrer upcoming biopic 'Daddy' has been released. Arjun will play the role of gangster-turned-politician Arun Gawli in the film. The biopic will reportedly cover Gawli's life from his 20s to his 60s. Directed by Ashim Ahluwalia, 'Daddy' is scheduled to release in 2017.
