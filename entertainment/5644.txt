Sujoy Ghosh, who made his directorial debut with 'Jhankaar Beats', has revealed he forgot to say 'cut' on the first day of the film's shoot. Sujoy added that someone from the crew explained that he had to say 'cut' after every scene. The film, starring Sanjay Suri, Juhi Chawla and Rahul Bose, completes 14 years of its release on Tuesday.
