Actor Amitabh Bachchan took to Twitter to share a poem in the memory of late actress Sridevi. He added that the poem, written by poet Kaifi Azmi at the time of Guru Dutt's demise, was narrated to him by Javed Akhtar at Sridevi's funeral held on Wednesday. Amitabh and Sridevi had worked together in films like 'Inquilaab' and 'Aakhree Raasta'.
