The first poster of the Shraddha Kapoor and Aditya Roy Kapur starrer upcoming romantic drama film 'OK Jaanu' was shared by co-producer Karan Johar on Twitter. Directed by Shaad Ali, it is an official remake of Mani Ratnam's Tamil film 'OK Kanmani'. Also starring Naseeruddin Shah and Leela Samson, the film is scheduled to release on January 13, 2017.
