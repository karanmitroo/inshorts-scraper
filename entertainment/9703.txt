Filmmaker Karan Johar, in his biography, has said that actress Kajol is out of his life. "I don't think she deserves me," he added. Karan further said it was "completely over" for him when Kajol tweeted, "Shocked", following her husband Ajay Devgn's allegations that Karan had bribed Kamaal R Khan to slam Devgn's film 'Shivaay'.
