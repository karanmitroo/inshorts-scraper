Akshay Kumar-starrer "Baby" has minted Rs 36.07 crore at the box office in its opening weekend. The movie collected Rs 9.3 crore on opening day, while on Saturday and Sunday, it did business of Rs 11.17 crore and Rs 15.60 crore respectively. Meanwhile, Sonam Kapoor-starrer "Dolly Ki Doli" managed to collect just Rs 10 crore in three days.
