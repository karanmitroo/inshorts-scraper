Dwayne Johnson starrer 'Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle', which released today, has "well-timed humour combined with slapstick comedy," wrote Bollywood Hungama. Koimoi wrote that the film is a combination of "fun and freshness in equal dosage" while The Times of India (TOI) called it a "fun, holiday film."  It has been rated 3/5 (Bollywood Hungama, Koimoi) and 4/5 (TOI).
