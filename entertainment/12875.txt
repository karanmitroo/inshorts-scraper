Yesteryear actress Zeenat Aman will make her digital debut with upcoming web series 'Love, Life & Screw Ups'. The youth-based web series will be a romantic comedy and will feature the 64-year old actress as 'Joanna', a spinster. It will be directed by Kapil Sharma, who played the lead role in the 2010 film 'Dunno Y Na Jaane Kyon'.
