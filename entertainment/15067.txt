Actor Amitabh Bachchan, on his official blog, has confirmed that 'Pink' is the title for Shoojit Sircar's upcoming film for which the 73-year-old actor recently shot in Delhi alongside actress Taapsee Pannu. Bachchan had earlier dismissed rumours that the film was titled 'Eve'. The film marks Bachchan's third venture with Sircar after 'Piku' and the yet-to-be released film, 'Shoebite'.
