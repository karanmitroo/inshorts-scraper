Hrithik Roshan-starrer 'Bang Bang' inscribed its name in the 100-crore-club, by making Rs 175.61 crore gross worldwide in four days. The film is also one of the top 5 films to have received an overwhelming response on its release day. Raking in Rs 41.14 crore over the weekend, it has also become the highest overseas opener of 2014, surpassing 'Kick'.
