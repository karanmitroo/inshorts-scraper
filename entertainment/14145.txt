'Fake Ishq', the new song from the upcoming film 'Housefull 3' has been released. The song has been sung by Kailash Kher, Nakassh Aziz and Altamash Faridi while the music for the track has been composed by Tanishk Bagchi. Starring Akshay Kumar, Riteish Deshmukh and Abhishek Bachchan as the lead actors, the film is scheduled to release on June 3.
