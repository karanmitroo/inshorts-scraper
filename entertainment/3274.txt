According to reports, TV actor Karan Wahi will star opposite Urvashi Rautela in the upcoming film 'Hate Story 4'. Talking about the film, which will be the fourth instalment in the erotic thriller franchise, director Vishal Pandya had said, "Erotica will definitely not be the mainstay of the film." Notably, Karan made his Bollywood debut with the 2014 film 'Daawat-e-Ishq'.
