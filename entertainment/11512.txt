The Sushant Singh Rajput starrer biopic 'MS Dhoni: The Untold Story' has become the second highest grosser of 2016 by earning ₹129.50 crore in India. The Neeraj Pandey directorial film surpassed the earnings of Akshay Kumar's 'Rustom', which earned ₹127.42 crore, and 'Airlift', which collected ₹129 crore. Salman Khan's 'Sultan', after minting over ₹300 crore, remains 2016's highest grosser.
