Bollywood superstar Salman Khan and his 'Kick' co-star Jacqueline Fernandez were in Colombo (SriLanka) on Monday to campaign for President Mahinda Rajapaksa. Local website Asianmirror said Khan was invited for the campaign by Rajapaksa's son Namal. Jacqueline, a former Miss Sri Lanka, is a friend of Namal. Salman is the first Bollywood actor to participate in the country's poll campaign.
