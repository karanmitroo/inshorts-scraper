Renowned novelist Paulo Coelho, popularly known for penning down 'The Alchemist', on Saturday praised Shah Rukh Khan starrer Bollywood film 'My Name Is Khan'. He took to Twitter and posted, "My name is Khan" (2009) is the best movie I watched this year." Replying to the tweet, film's director Karan Johar said, "What an honour sir!!! Thank you so much!!"
