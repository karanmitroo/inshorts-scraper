According to reports, TV actor Karan Tacker has been approached to play the antagonist in the upcoming Tiger Shroff starrer 'Student Of The Year 2' (SOTY 2). A source said, "Karan's turn will see him come up with obstacles for the hero. He has already started his preparation." Tacker is known for serials like 'Ek Hazaaron Mein Meri Behna Hai'.
