'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' director Kabir Khan has clarified that actor Aamir Khan was not offered the lead role in the film. There were reports that Aamir had sent the filmmaker to Salman after reading the script. Later, Salman also gave a similar statement. Meanwhile, Bigg Boss contestant Ali Quli Mirza has claimed he was offered Nawazuddin Siddiqui's role in the film.
