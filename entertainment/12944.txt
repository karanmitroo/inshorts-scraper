As per reports, actor Aamir Khan will replace actor Hrithik Roshan in the upcoming film 'Thug' by Yash Raj Films (YRF). The film will reportedly be directed by Vijay Krishna Acharya, who has worked with Aamir in 'Dhoom: 3'. 'Thug' is said to have been rejected by Hrithik as he was not satisfied with the script and wanted some changes.
