Actress Deepika Padukone on Sunday walked the red carpet at the MTV Europe Music Awards 2016. Deepika wore an embellished black top, teamed with a high-waist olive green skirt and a black jacket by designer Monisha Jaising. She accessorised her look with a black belt, tassel earrings by Valliyan by Nitya Arora and black stilettos from Balmain.
