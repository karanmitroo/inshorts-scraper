On the day singer-writer Shawn Mendes announced his world tour, #ShawnWorldTour was a top Twitter trend globally on Thursday. The singer made the announcement tweeting, "#ShawnWorldTour announce starting now on me & @iHeartRadio's snapchats & Twitter ! Follow along !". Some users trolled the singer tweeting, "I love that Shawn is standing in his bathroom and announcing tour dates #ShawnWorldTour".
