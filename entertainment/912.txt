The Zaira Wasim and Aamir Khan starrer 'Secret Superstar' has earned over ₹100 crore in two days of its release in China. The film broke the opening weekend record of ₹72.68 crore set by Aamir's film 'Dangal' at the Chinese box office. Zaira was named Best Actress (Critics) for 'Secret Superstar' at Filmfare Awards this year.
