Actor Ranbir Kapoor has clarified that he will be shooting for both Ayan Mukerji's upcoming superhero film tentatively titled 'Dragon' and Rajkumar Hirani's biopic on Sanjay Dutt. "They are both supposed to start in early 2017, so we're in talks to sort it out and go ahead with both projects," said Ranbir while insisting that the shooting would not clash.
