Filmmaker Karan Johar took to Instagram to share an old picture with Kajol and Shah Rukh Khan which he captioned, "Lifetime bonds!" Johar and Kajol had a fallout in 2016 after she supported Ajay Devgn over a controversy regarding same day release of 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' and 'Shivaay'. However, there were recent reports that Karan and Kajol have reconciled.
