The menu of Google's Indian restaurant Baadal has dishes named after actress Shilpa Shetty's songs. Some of the dishes on the menu include 'Barasja ae Baadal Rice' and 'Kadai Chole Ke Dil Ka Karaar Lootne'. "Google's cafeteria did a special menu on my songs... [H]ow cute is that," she wrote while sharing a photo of the menu on Instagram.
