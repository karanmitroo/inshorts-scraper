The trailer of the upcoming web series 'Bose Dead/Alive', starring Rajkummar Rao as the freedom fighter Subhash Chandra Bose, has been released. The web series has been inspired by Anuj Dhar's book 'India's Biggest Cover-up' and revolves around the mystery surrounding Bose's "alleged death". Directed by Pulkit, the web series also stars Patralekhaa and Edward Sonnenblick.
