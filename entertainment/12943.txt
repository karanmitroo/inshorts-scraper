The trailer for the Nawazuddin Siddiqui starrer 'Freaky Ali' was released on Sunday. Nawazuddin stars as Ali, a common man who becomes a golfer by chance. The film, which also stars Amy Jackson and Arbaaz Khan, has been directed by Arbaaz and Salman Khan's younger brother Sohail Khan. 'Freaky Ali' is scheduled to release on September 9.
