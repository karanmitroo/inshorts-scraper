Actor Abhishek Bachchan will reportedly star in 'Shootout 3', the third instalment in filmmaker Sanjay Gupta's 'Shootout At Wadala' and 'Shootout At Lokhandwala' film franchise. "I have liked the idea of Sanjay’s film. It’s an intense gangster drama," said Abhishek without sharing specific details. The film is said to be based on Mumbai Police encounter specialist Vijay Salaskar.
