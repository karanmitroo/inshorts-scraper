The motion poster for the upcoming musical drama 'Banjo' was unveiled on Saturday. The film stars Riteish Deshmukh and Nargis Fakhri in lead roles and is about banjo players from Maharashtra. While Deshmukh portrays a Banjo player, Fakhri plays the role of a DJ in the film. Directed by Ravi Jadhav, the film is scheduled to release on September 23.
