The Akshay Kumar and Ileana D'Cruz starrer 'Rustom', which released on August 12, has entered the ₹100 crore club within nine days of its release. According to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, it earned ₹101.08 crore till Saturday in its second week. Notably, 'Rustom' is Akshay's third film in 2016 to earn over ₹100 crore after 'Airlift' and 'Housefull 3'.
