'Manzar Naya', a new song from the Farhan Akhtar and Arjun Rampal starrer 'Rock On 2' has been released. The song has been composed by music composer trio Shankar, Ehsaan and Loy with vocals by Farhan Akhtar and lyrics by Javed Akhtar. Directed by Shujaat Saudagar, 'Rock On 2' is scheduled to release on November 11.
