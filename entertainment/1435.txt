Actor Ajay Devgn took to Twitter to share a video, in which he has announced his debut Marathi film 'Aapla Manus', which translates to 'Our Person'. The film will also star Nana Patekar, Sumeet Raghavan and Irawati Harshe. Directed by Satish Rajwade, 'Aapla Manus' is scheduled to release on February 9, 2018.
