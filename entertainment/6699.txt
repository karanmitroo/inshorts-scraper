As per reports, actor Salman Khan's rumoured girlfriend Iulia Vantur will make her Bollywood debut with the upcoming film 'Dabangg 3'. According to sources, she won't replace lead actress Sonakshi Sinha but director Arbaaz Khan has an 'interesting' role for her. Earlier, Salman had revealed that the film will be a prequel, showing "how Chulbul Pandey becomes Chulbul Pandey".
