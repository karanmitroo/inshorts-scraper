Actress Alia Bhatt is set to record her first single, which will be a romantic track under Bhushan Kumar's production. The song will be composed by Mithoon with lyrics by Manoj Muntashir. Earlier, Alia has lent her voice for the song 'Sooha Saha' from 'Highway' and has sung the unplugged version of 'Main Tenu Samjhawan' from 'Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania'.
