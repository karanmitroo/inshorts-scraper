'Jeete Hain Chal', the first song from the Sonam Kapoor and Shabana Azmi-starrer 'Neerja' was released on Tuesday. Composed by Vishal Khurana, the song has been sung by Kavita Seth. 'Neerja' is a biopic of flight attendant Neerja Bhanot, who was murdered by terrorists who hijacked a flight in 1986. Directed by Ram Madhvani, it will release on February 19.
