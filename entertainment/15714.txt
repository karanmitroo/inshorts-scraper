Fashion photographer Dabboo Ratnani, known for shooting Bollywood A-listers, on Thursday released a teaser for his 2016 calendar shoot featuring celebrities like Amitabh Bachchan, Shah Rukh Khan, Priyanka Chopra and Ranveer Singh. The calendar's 17th edition also features new entrants like Athiya Shetty, Kriti Sanon and Lisa Haydon. SRK said that his look resembles "Mandakini under the waterfall."
