Actor Emraan Hashmi has featured as the 'Joker', a villain in the Batman film and comic series, on the cover of FHM magazine for its October issue, which pays tribute to "iconic Super Villains". Emraan has imitated the character based on Heath Ledger's portrayal in 'The Dark Knight'. He has also featured as 'Hannibal Lecter' and Breaking Bad's 'Walter White'.
