Actor Aamir Khan, when asked about who he considers a superstar, said, "For me, firstly comes Amitabh Bachchan. Then comes Salman Khan, Shah Rukh Khan, Akshay Kumar, Ajay Devgn, Hrithik Roshan." Aamir added that even Deepika Padukone and Katrina Kaif are superstars. He further said that stardom does not get affected by one or two films.
