Arshad Warsi has said that actor Akshay Kumar's work in the film 'Jolly LLB 2' is ten times better than his performance in 'Jolly LLB'. He added, "It's a very intriguing film, the whole thing about...a bad lawyer becoming a good lawyer is very...interesting." Notably, Arshad played the titular character in the 2013 film 'Jolly LLB'.
