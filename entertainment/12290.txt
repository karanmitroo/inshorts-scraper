An FIR has been filed against comedian Kapil Sharma and actor Irrfan Khan over illegal construction in their flats in Goregaon, Mumbai. If convicted, they can face jail term from one month to three years along with a fine of ₹2,000 to ₹5,000. The Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation claimed that it found major violations in 15 flats in the Goregaon building.
