Pictures on social media show actor Sanjay Dutt performing aarti with his wife Maanayata at a Ganpati pandal in Lokhandwala. Recently, Sanjay had recorded a Ganesh aarti for his comeback film 'Bhoomi'. He had said, "I am happy to sing a great song for Bappa...We celebrate Ganesh Chaturthi at my house. We worship him a lot."
