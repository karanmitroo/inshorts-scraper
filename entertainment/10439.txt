As per reports, actor Aamir Khan and his co-stars from 'Dangal' Sanya Malhotra and Fatima Sana Shaikh will feature in an upcoming episode of 'Koffee With Karan', the talk show hosted by filmmaker Karan Johar. However, Aamir had earlier said that he wants to appear on the show this season but Karan has not invited him yet.
