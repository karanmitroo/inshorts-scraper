On her 29th birthday, actress Sonakshi Sinha on Thursday took to Twitter to share a flash video revealing her character in the upcoming film 'Noor'. The video shows Sonakshi's different looks as Pakistani journalist Noor Roy Chaudhry, the protagonist of the film. To be directed by Sunhil Sippy, 'Noor' is based on Saba Imtiaz’s 2014 novel ‘Karachi, You’re Killing Me'.
