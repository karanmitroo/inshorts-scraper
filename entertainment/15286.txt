Actor Arjun Kapoor was spotted on the red carpet of the Zee Cine Awards 2016 on Saturday wearing a suit paired with red heels. The actor was sporting the look from his film, Ki & Ka's song, 'High Heels'. Celebrities including Sonam Kapoor in a Vionnet Paris gown and Kajol in a Manish Malhotra anarkali also attended the event.
