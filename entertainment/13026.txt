As per reports, actress Deepika Padukone is Bollywood's highest paid actress as she is getting paid ₹12.65 crore for her upcoming film 'Padmavati'. This reportedly makes her the first female actor to cross the ₹10 crore barrier. While, 'Padmavati' is her third film with filmmaker Sanjay​ Leela Bhansali, it is said that Deepika charged ₹7 crore for 'Bajirao Mastani'.
