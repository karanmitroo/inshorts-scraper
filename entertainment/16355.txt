The Income Tax department on Wednesday raided the houses and offices of actors of Tamil film 'Puli', including Vijay, Nayantara and Samantha. As per the IT officials, raids were carried out following the observation that several investments in the film were not explained. Over 35 places, including residences of Puli's producers, were raided in Chennai, Madurai, Kochi, Trivandrum and Hyderabad.
