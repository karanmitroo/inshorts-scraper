Upcoming comedy flick, '102 Not Out', has actor Amitabh Bachchan playing a 102-year-old man and Paresh Rawal as his 75-year-old son. Directed by Umesh Shukla, the movie is based on one of his Gujarati plays that has completed 300 shows and is still running. With the film, Shukla will also launch his banner of Benchmark Pictures.
