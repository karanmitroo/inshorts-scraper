Actress Aditi Rao Hydari has been featured as the cover girl for Femina India's February issue. The 29-year-old actress wore a hand-embroidered peplum top from KHEM over high waisted eyelet detailed black shorts by Salita Nanda. The look was accessorised with a blue faux fur scarf from Gas Jeans and silver jewellery by Ritika Sachdeva.
