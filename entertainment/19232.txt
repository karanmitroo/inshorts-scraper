Romantic comedy 'Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani' is leading in the nominations at the 59th Filmfare Awards with 9 nods including best film, best director for Ayaan Mukerji and best actor for Ranbir Kapoor. It is closely followed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali's 'Goliyon Ki Raasleela Ram-Leela', which has landed seven nominations including best film and best actress for Deepika Padukone.
