Abhinav Kashyap's Besharam, starring Ranbir Kapoor, Pallavi Sharda, Rishi Kapoor and Neetu Kapoor, released this Wednesday. The movie has been rated 1 (India Today, IBN Live), 1.5 (Indian Express), 2.5 (DNA) and 3.5 (TOI). While IBN Live in its review says the movie is "a major misfire in every way", India Today critics advices to "skip this Besharam film!"
