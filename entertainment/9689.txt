A Jodhpur court on Friday summoned Bollywood actors Salman Khan, Saif Ali Khan, Sonali Bendre and Tabu to appear before it on January 25 in the blackbuck poaching case. In September 1998, while shooting for the movie 'Hum Saath Saath Hain', five Bollywood actors Salman Khan, Saif Ali Khan, Sonali Bendre, Tabu and Neelam were seen hunting two blackbucks.
