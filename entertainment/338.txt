Reacting to Union Minister-singer Babul Supriyo's demand to remove Pakistani singer Rahat Fateh Ali Khan's voice from 'Ishtehaar' song from 'Welcome To New York', director Chakri Toleti said, "I don't understand why we are being targeted." He added, "Our film...releases this Friday and this is just too much stress...(We) made an entertaining film and we don't want to hurt anyone."
