Veteran actor Rishi Kapoor, in his autobiography 'Khullam Khulla', revealed that his father Raj Kapoor had affairs with his co-stars Nargis and Vyjayanthimala.  "My father loved his cinema, his booze, his leading ladies and his work," said Rishi in the book. Rishi further revealed that he and his mother moved into a hotel when his father was involved with Vyjayanthimala.
