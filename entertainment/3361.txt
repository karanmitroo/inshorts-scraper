Television shows 'Bhabi Ji Ghar Par Hai!' and 'Hum Paanch' will be adapted into an Anglo-Spanish and British sitcom respectively. While 'Bhabi Ji...' will be produced as 'Love Thy Neighbour', 'Hum Paanch' will be adapted as 'Lala's Ladiez'. They will be produced by ZEE Studios International, which has been set up to produce global content for audiences across the world.
