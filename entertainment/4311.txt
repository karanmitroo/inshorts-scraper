Actor Akshay Kumar's wife Twinkle Khanna took to Twitter to praise his new film 'Toilet- Ek Prem Katha' and wrote, "Very proud of Mr K- It's a highly entertaining and equally important movie! #MustWatch". The film is said to be based on PM Modi's Swachh Bharat Abhiyan. Directed by Shree Narayan Singh, 'Toilet- Ek Prem Katha' also stars Bhumi Pednekar.
