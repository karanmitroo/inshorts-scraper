Actor Amitabh Bachchan has topped a poll conducted by an entertainment website on who should portray politician Balasaheb Thackeray onscreen for the biopic 'Saheb'. The 73-year-old actor received the highest number of votes beating Shah Rukh Khan, Akshay Kumar and Manoj Bajpayee, the other actors included in the poll. The biopic is being produced by Balasabeb's daughter-in-law Smita Thackeray.
