The first teaser poster of filmmaker Madhur Bhandarkar's upcoming film 'Indu Sarkar', based on the Emergency, a 21-month long period from 1975 to 1977, declared by the then Prime Minister Indira Gandhi, has been unveiled. It will feature actors Neil Nitin Mukesh and Kirti Kulhari in lead roles. The film is scheduled to release in the year 2017.
