A video released by Paramount Pictures shows actress Deepika Padukone describing her character Serena Unger in her upcoming Hollywood debut film 'xXx: Return of Xander Cage'. "She's independent, can stand up for herself. Serena is a representation of women in today's world," Deepika says about her character. She further explains why Xander, portrayed by Vin Diesel, is attracted to Serena.
