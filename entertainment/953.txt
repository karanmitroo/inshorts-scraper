'Padmaavat' actors Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh thanked the team of 'Pad Man' for postponing the release date to February 9, to avoid a clash with 'Padmaavat' on January 25. Deepika tweeted, "'Only from heart can you touch the sky'...a big thank you... for your support, generosity!" Ranveer's tweet read, "Big Man with a Big Heart! @akshaykumar...grateful to you Sir."
