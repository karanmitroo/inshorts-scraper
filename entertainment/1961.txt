'Drishyam' actress Ishita Dutta got married to actor Vatsal Seth, known for starring in the film 'Taarzan: The Wonder Car' and television show 'Ek Hasina Thi', on Tuesday at the ISKCON temple in Juhu, Mumbai. The two reportedly started dating each other while shooting for the television series 'Rishton Ka Saudagar-Baazigar' despite signing a "no dating" contract.
