Singer-composer Anu Malik has sung the recreated version of 'Oonchi Hai Building' for the Varun Dhawan, Taapsee Pannu and Jacqueline Fernandez-starrer upcoming film 'Judwaa 2'. The new song, titled 'Oonchi Hai Building 2.0', has been co-composed by Sandeep Shirodkar and also features vocals by Neha Kakkar. Directed by David Dhawan, 'Judwaa 2' will release on September 29.
