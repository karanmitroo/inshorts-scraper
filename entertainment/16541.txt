As the news of actor Aamir Khan crying while watching nephew Imran Khan-starrer 'Katti Batti' spread, many people started mocking him on micro-blogging website Twitter. One such user with the Twitter handle @BolshoyBooze posted a photo of Aamir, wherein he can be seen inside a water body (sea), and captioned it "Aamir Khan about to drown in his own tears".
