Actress Katrina Kaif has joked that she is not running after her 'Baar Baar Dekho' co-star Sidharth Malhotra for marriage but said that she and Sidharth "love each other". Sidharth and Katrina Kaif, when asked about their personal love stories in real life, admitted that they have fallen in love in real life. 'Baar Baar Dekho' releases on September 9.
