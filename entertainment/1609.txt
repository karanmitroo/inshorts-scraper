Actor-director Neeraj Vora, who starred in films like 'Baadshah' and 'Hello Brother', passed away on Thursday after battling coma for a year. He had suffered a heart attack, followed by a brain stroke in October last year. Neeraj, who was reportedly being taken care of at friend and producer Firoz Nadiadwala's residence, also directed the 2006 film 'Phir Hera Pheri'.
