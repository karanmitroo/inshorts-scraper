The Ben Affleck and Henry Cavill starrer film 'Batman Vs Superman: Dawn of Justice', which released in India on Thursday, "isn't a bad film. But it isn't a good film, either", wrote DNA. The film is "dreary, overproduced and underbaked", wrote NDTV. It has been rated 1.5/5 (Indian Express, NDTV), 2.5/5 (DNA) and 3.5/5 (TOI).
