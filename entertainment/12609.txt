Actor Shahid Kapoor's wife Mira Rajput on Friday gave birth to a baby girl, their first child, at the Hinduja Healthcare Surgical hospital in Mumbai. The 35-year-old actor tweeted, "She has arrived and words fall short to express our happiness. Thank you for all your wishes." Shahid and Mira got married in a low-key gurudwara wedding on July 7, 2015.
