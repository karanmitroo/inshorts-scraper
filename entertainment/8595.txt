The trailer of the Swara Bhaskar 'Anaarkali of Aarah' was released on Thursday. The film revolves around Anaarkali, played by Swara, who makes a living out of singing and dancing and is from Aarah in Bihar. Directed by Avinash Das and also starring Sanjay Mishra, Pankaj Tripathi and Sandiip Kapur, the film is scheduled to release on March 24.
