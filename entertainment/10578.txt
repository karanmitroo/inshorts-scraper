Alia Bhatt and Sidharth Malhotra have been confirmed as the lead stars in 'Aashiqui 3'. "Since it is part of the Aashiqui franchise, Alia is playing a singer," said the film's director Mohit Suri. The film's script is reportedly being penned by Alia's sister Shaheen Bhatt. Notably, Shraddha Kapoor and Aditya Roy Kapur starred in the 2013 film 'Aashiqui 2'.
