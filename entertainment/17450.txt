Actor Hrithik Roshan, who will next be seen in Ashutosh Gowariker's 'Mohenjo Daro', has said that success doesn't mean fame and money. Hrithik also said, "Success to me is becoming the best version of yourself." He added, "...if you're certain enough to say 'I can do this'..., if you're not stuck in a monotonous everyday journey, that... is success."
