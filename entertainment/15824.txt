American TV series 'Game of Thrones' (GoT) has become the most pirated television show of the year 2015, according to piracy-tracking site TorrentFreak. The second most illegally downloaded TV series was 'The Walking Dead', followed by 'The Big Bang Theory'. Notably, GoT has topped the piracy list for the fourth consecutive year with an estimated 14.4 million downloads via BitTorrent.
