'Kaala Doreya', the recreated version of Punjabi folk song 'Kala Doria', from Saif Ali Khan starrer 'Kaalakaandi' has been released. The new version has been composed by Sameer Uddin with vocals by Neha Bhasin. Directed by Akshat Verma and also starring Deepak Dobriyal and Kunaal Roy Kapur, 'Kaalakaandi' is scheduled to release on January 12, 2018.
