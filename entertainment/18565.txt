'Singham Returns' has become the highest opening day grosser for Ajay Devgn as well as Kareena Kapoor after collecting Rs. 32.09 crores on Friday. Ajay Devgn's  previous highest grosser 'Himmatwala' had minted Rs. 12.1 crore, while Kareena's 'Bodyguard' had collected Rs. 21.60 crores. Interestingly, 'Singham Returns' prequel, 'Singham', had an opening day earnings of just Rs. 8.94 crores.
