Actor Hrithik Roshan launched the trailer of Marathi film 'Hrudayantar', which marks the directorial debut of fashion designer Vikram Phadnis. The film will feature actors Mukta Barve and Subodh Bhave while Hrithik will be seen in a cameo appearance. In December 2016, Shah Rukh Khan had performed the mahurat clap to commence the film's shooting.
