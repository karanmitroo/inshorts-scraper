Actor Arjun Kapoor's look from the upcoming film 'Mubarakan' has been revealed. Arjun will be seen in a double role in the film. He will be portraying the characters 'Karanveer Singh' and 'Charanveer Singh'.  'Mubarakan' will also star Arjun's uncle Anil Kapoor. Directed by Anees Bazmee, the film is scheduled to release on July 28, 2017.
