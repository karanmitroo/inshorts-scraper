Actor Tusshar Kapoor has become a father to a baby boy through surrogacy using in vitro fertilisation (IVF). The 39-year-old actor will be a single parent to his son, who has been named Laksshya, as he is unmarried. Tusshar’s parents, Jeetendra and Shobha Kapoor said, "We... are completely supportive of Tusshar’s decision. This is... an exciting time in our lives."
