Bollywood actor Amitabh Bachchan Tuesday disagreed with West Bengal CM Mamata Banerjee's suggestion to honour him with Bharat Ratna. Big B posted on Twitter, "@MamataOfficial Mamata di… I am not deserving of such recognition… Most humbled and honoured by what the Nation has given me..!!" Amitabh Bachchan has been chosen for this year’s Padma Vibhushan, India's second highest civilian award.
