A new still of Raj Kummar Rao from his upcoming film 'Omerta' was revealed by director Hansal Mehta on Twitter. "Produced by Swiss Entertainment & Karma Features, #Omerta has @RajkummarRao playing yet another real life character," Mehta captioned the photo. Notably, this will be Rao and Mehta's fourth film together, after 'Shahid' (2013), 'CityLights' (2014) and 'Aligarh' (2016).
