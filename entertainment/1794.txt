The Aamir Khan starrer 'Dangal' was named the Best Asian Film at the Australian Academy of Cinema and Television Arts Awards (AACTA) 2017. The Amitabh Bachchan starrer 'Pink' was also nominated in the category. Actress Shabana Azmi, who was a jury member for Best Asian Film category which was chaired by Russell Crowe, tweeted it was a unanimous decision.
