A new poster of the Kunal Kapoor starrer 'Veeram' has been revealed. Directed by National Award-winning filmmaker Jayaraj Nair, it is an adaptation of William Shakespeare's play 'Macbeth' and revolves around Chandu Chekavar, a warrior in the 13th century in North Malabar. The film, which recently premiered at the BRICS Film Festival, has been made in Malayalam, Hindi and English.
