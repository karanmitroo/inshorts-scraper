Parineeti Chopra and Aditya Kapoor's "Daawat-e-Ishq" managed to collect Rs 13.6 crore over the opening weekend. Despite mixed reviews, the film showed upward trend in its business on Saturday and Sunday, when it fetched Rs 4.35 crore and Rs 5.53 crore respectively. On the opening day (Friday), the film had fetched in Rs 3.72 crore.
