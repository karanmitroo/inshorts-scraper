Actress Konkona Sen Sharma's directorial debut 'A Death In The Gunj' which released today, "is a brave experiment despite loopholes", wrote HT. "It's a sensitive, profoundly moving portrait of an angst-ridden young man derailed by a world bereft of empathy", wrote NDTV. Times of India wrote, "Konkona uses her actors beautifully". It was rated 4/5 (NDTV), 3.5/5 (TOI), 3/5 (HT).
