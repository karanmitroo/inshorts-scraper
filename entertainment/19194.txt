Rajkumar Hirani's 3 Idiots, starring Aamir Khan and Kareena Kapoor, has been nominated for the 37th Japan Academy Awards in the best Outstanding Foreign Language Film category. According to the awards' official website, "3 Idiots", mentioned as "I'm sure, go well", has been nominated with international movies like "Captain Phillips", "Django Unchained", "Gravity" and "Les Miserables".
