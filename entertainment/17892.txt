Famous 1993 film 'Mrs Doubtfire', starring Robin Williams, is being prepped as a musical for the stage, Disney film composer and producer Alan Menkin, who created 'Aladdin' and 'The Little Mermaid', has confirmed. 'I am writing now, a musical of Mrs. Doubtfire,' Menken said. The Golden Globe-winning film was a huge hit and collected $441 million worldwide.
