New posters of Kangana Ranaut and Rajkummar Rao starrer 'Mental Hai Kya' have been released. This will be Kangana and Rao's second film after 'Queen'. Describing the film, Rao had earlier said, "It's an amazing script as it is very quirky and different comedy." 'Mental Hai Kya' is being produced by Ekta Kapoor.
