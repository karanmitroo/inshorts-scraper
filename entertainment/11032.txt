Amitabh Bachchan, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Alia Bhatt and Sushant Singh Rajput have featured on the latest cover of 'Filmfare' magazine. They are seen showcasing their look from the Absolut Elyx Filmfare Style & Glamour Awards. The magazine also featured a picture of Kajol, Karan Johar and Sidharth Malhotra in the same issue which describes the celebrities as 'Dazzlers & Sparklers'.
