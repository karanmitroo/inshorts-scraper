The hundredth episode promotional video of Koffee with Karan, featuring Salman Khan and brothers Sohail and Arbaaz has been released. "I'm not the nicest guy in relationships," Salman tells host Karan, to which Arbaaz adds, "He was good to other people at that time." Salman is also seen dancing to 'Sheila Ki Jawani', picturised on rumoured former girlfriend Katrina Kaif.
