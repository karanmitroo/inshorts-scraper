RTI queries have revealed the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation had issued notices to Amitabh Bachchan and seven others for alleged illegal constructions in 2016. They changed main maps to make illegal constructions in their Goregaon bungalows, RTI activist Anil Galgali said. Reportedly, Galgali has written letters to state CM Devendra Fadnavis seeking demolition of the illegal constructions.
