Speaking about the ongoing row on Sanjay Leela Bhansali's film 'Padmavati', Aditi Rao Hydari tweeted, "Can I have my country back please." "I love my country, but I don't understand it anymore," she added. Aditi was earlier called a "love jihadi victim" by a Twitter user for supporting 'Padmavati', in which she has played the role of Ranveer Singh's wife.
