Justin Bieber has bought a three-storey penthouse in Amsterdam for £22 million (around ₹180 crore). It is located in a neighbourhood close to where the Dutch royal family lives. It has four bedrooms, and comes with a butler and elevator. Bieber recently moved into a 15-bedroom London mansion that costs around £1.3 million (around ₹10.5 crore) to rent per year.
