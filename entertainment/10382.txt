Sushant Singh Rajput, who played the titular role in 'M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story', was the most searched Bollywood actor in India, as per Google's 'Year in Search 2016'. The 70-year-old actor Kabir Bedi, who married his long term partner 41-year-old Parveen Dusanj this year, is the second-most searched. Harshvardhan Kapoor, who debuted in 'Mirzya', ranked third on the list.
