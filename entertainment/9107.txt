According to reports, actress Jacqueline Fernandez took pole dancing classes for a song in her upcoming action film 'Reload' to look convincing on screen. The song, titled 'Chandralekha', has been choreographed by Adil Shaikh. Directed by Raj Nidimoru and Krishna DK, the film also stars Sidharth Malhotra. 'Reload' is scheduled to release on August 25.
