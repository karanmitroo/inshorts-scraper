The song 'Tum Bin' from the Pulkit Samrat and Yami Gautam starrer upcoming film 'Sanam Re' was YouTube's fifth-most shared video on Facebook and Twitter in India on Thursday. The song has been composed by Jeet Ganguly, while it has been sung by Shreya Ghoshal. Posted on YouTube on Tuesday, the video has garnered over 1.95 lakh views so far.
