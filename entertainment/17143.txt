Indian film 'Masaan' that was showcased at the Cannes International Film Festival on Tuesday, received a standing ovation at the event. "Really proud moment for India #Masaan #Cannes2015 Applause went on for five mins!," tweeted writer Sudhish Kamath. Furthermore, film's debutant director Neeraj Ghaywan thanked everyone on Twitter and revealed that he broke down at the unstoppable applause.
