Priyanka Chopra’s look in her upcoming film ‘Gangaajal 2’ was revealed Monday. In the Prakash Jha directorial, Priyanka plays the role of IPS officer Abha Mathur. She can be seen wearing a khakhi uniform and minimal make up in the picture. Gangaajal 2 is a sequel to 'Gangaajal' which featured Ajay Devgn. However, he won't be seen in Gangaajal 2.
