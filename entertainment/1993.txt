Actor Akshay Kumar has revealed that while signing him for his first film, filmmaker Pramod Chakravorty gave him a cheque of ₹5000. "(Chakravorty) signed me up for three films...He gave me...₹50,000 for second film, and the third cheque had ₹1.5 lakh on it," added Akshay. He further said that this happened on the day he missed an important modelling assignment.
