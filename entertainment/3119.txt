According to reports, Shah Rukh Khan, Ranveer Singh or Saif Ali Khan will replace Varun Dhawan in 'Judwaa 3'. A source said, "Every Judwaa film will feature a new A-lister in a double role." Filmmaker David Dhawan, who directed 'Judwaa' and 'Judwaa 2', will reportedly also direct all the upcoming films in the franchise.
