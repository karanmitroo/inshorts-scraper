Rallying past ‘Krrish 3’, Salman Khan’s ‘Kick’ has become the 4th highest worldwide grosser of Bollywood.  Kick has earned nearly Rs.375 crore and is behind 3 Idiots, Chennai Express and Dhoom 3 in terms of worldwide box-office collection. Kick also made Salman Khan the first Bollywood actor to add seven Rs.100 crore movies to his credit.
