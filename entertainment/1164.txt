Actor Abhishek Bachchan, while wishing filmmaker Farah Khan on her 53rd birthday, wrote, "Happy Birthday my Farahbia." He shared a picture with Farah from the sets of the 2006 film 'Kabhi Alvida Naa Kehna'. Abhishek wrote, "Inside the hard, loud, careless exterior demeanor she tries to project, she's actually a very emotional, loving and caring person."
