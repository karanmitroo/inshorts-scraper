Indian captain Virat Kohli and his wife, actress Anushka Sharma, made their first public appearance post wedding at their reception at the Taj Diplomatic Enclave in New Delhi on Thursday. While Anushka is wearing a Benarasi sari, Virat can be seen sporting a bandhgala kurta, both by designer Sabyasachi Mukherjee. PM Narendra Modi has also been invited to the reception.
