On the 87th birth anniversary of singer Kishore Kumar, #KishoreKumar was a top Indian Twitter trend on Thursday. Actor Amitabh Bachchan tweeted, "#KishoreKumar his multitalented genius shall remain ever... a phenomenal star himself, he made stars too!" Another user tweeted, "Happy birthday to my favourite entertainer of all time. Sang, acted, directed, wrote, produced and turned lunacy into art!"
