Director Ritesh Batra's first English-language feature film The Sense of an Ending, which released in India today, "manages to engage the interest of the viewer," wrote Hindustan Times. "This is, in every sense, a memorable one," wrote Mid-Day while Times of India called the film "simple and magical". The film was rated 3.5/5 (HT) and 4/5 (TOI, Mid-Day).
