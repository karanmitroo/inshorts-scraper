'Taang Uthake', the new song from upcoming movie 'Housefull 3' was released on Friday. The song has been composed by Sohail Sen and he has sung it along with Mika Singh, Neeti Mohan, Mamta Sharma and Hrishikesh Chury. The song's video features the lead stars including Akshay Kumar, Riteish Deshmukh and Jacqueline Fernandez. The film will release on June 3.
