Former cricketer Sachin Tendulkar's biopic 'Sachin: A Billion Dreams' has won two awards at the recently concluded 11th Tehran International FICTS Festival 2018. The biopic's director James Erskine got the award for the 'Best Director of a Long Documentary', and an honorary diploma. Producer Ravi Bhagchandka received the 'Special Award for the Best Film' in the Long Documentary section.
