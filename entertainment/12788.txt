The teaser for the Emraan Hashmi starrer 'Raaz Reboot' has been released. The film is the fourth installment in the 'Raaz' film franchise following the 2012 film 'Raaz 3D', which starred Emraan and Bipasha Basu. Directed by Vikram Bhatt, 'Raaz Reboot' also stars Kriti Kharbanda and Gaurav Arora and is scheduled to release on September 16.
