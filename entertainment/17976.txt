Sonali Raut and Puneet Issar Saturday got evicted from the popular reality show 'Bigg Boss 8'. The five finalists will now face 5 challengers Sambhavna Seth, Mehek Chachal, Sana Khan, Ajaz Khan and Rahul Mahajan in the show's spin off series Bigg Boss Halla Bol. The extended new format of the show will now be hosted by filmmaker Farah Khan.
