A day after Marathi actress Sulabha Deshpande passed away, 'Sulabha Deshpande' was a top Twitter trend in India on Sunday. A user reacted to it by tweeting, "Theatre, Big Screen, TV. Glow, grace, vintage dignity. Sulabha Deshpande, RIP". While another user's tweet read, "RIP Sulabha Deshpande. Wonderfully natural actress. Our image of the ideal mother and grandmother".
