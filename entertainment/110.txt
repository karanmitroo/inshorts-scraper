Google CEO Sundar Pichai, while replying to a statement posted by Boney Kapoor after the demise of his wife Sridevi, wrote, "I've special memories of watching Sridevi with my family." "She was a pioneer and an inspiration to so many of us," he added. Pichai also wrote that Sridevi's performance in the 1983 film 'Sadma' was one of his favourites.
