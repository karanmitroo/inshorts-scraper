Twinkle Khanna, Hrithik Roshan and Farhan Akhtar were among the Bollywood personalities who took to social media to wish Akshay Kumar on his 50th birthday. "#AkshayturnsGold in more ways than one...Happy 50th," tweeted Farhan. "Wishing the planet's fittest half centurion a very happy bday," wrote director Sajid Khan. Meanwhile, Hrithik tweeted, "Happy birthday Akshay Kumar keep shining."
