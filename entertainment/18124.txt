Rajinikanth starrer 'Lingaa', which released Friday, has received good response from the critics. "With Rajini in almost every scene, it is paisa vasool for his fans," writes India Today. Co-starring Anushka Shetty and Sonakshi Sinha, the film has been rated 2.5/5 (HT), 3/5 (NDTV, TOI, Indian Express), 3.5/5 (India Today).
