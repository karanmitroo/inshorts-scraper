Filmmaker Karan Johar Wednesday announced that Salman Khan will play the lead in his upcoming venture, Shhuddhi. "#Shhuddhi will star Salman Khan and will release Diwali 2016," tweeted Karan. Notably, this will be the first time Salman has appeared in a Karan Johar film as the lead hero. They last worked together 15 years ago in 'Kuch Kuch Hota Hai'.
