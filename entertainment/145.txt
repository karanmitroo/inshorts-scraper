'Mundiyan', the first song from Tiger Shroff and Disha Patani starrer 'Baaghi 2' has been released. The song is a recreated version of the 1999 song 'Mundian To Bach Ke'. Its music has been recreated by Sandeep Shirodkar, with vocals by Navraj Hans and Palak Muchhal. Directed by Ahmed Khan, 'Baaghi 2' will release on March 30.
