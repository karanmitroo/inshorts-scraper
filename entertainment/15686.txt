Kiku Sharda was arrested on Wednesday for mimicking Dera Sacha Sauda chief Gurmeet Ram Rahim Singh Insan on television following which, he became the most trending search of the day, according to Google trends. 'Kiku Sharda' was most googled in the state of Haryana followed by Punjab, Uttarakhand and Goa, while Mohali and Noida were the top cities googling him.
