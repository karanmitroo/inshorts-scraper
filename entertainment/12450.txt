Actor Rishi Kapoor, who turned 64 on Sunday, celebrated his birthday with his granddaughter Samara. She is the daughter of Rishi's elder child Riddhima Kapoor Sahni. The actor's picture with Samara was shared by his wife Neetu Kapoor alongside the caption, "Celebrating NANA's birthday". Celebrities including Amitabh Bachchan, Karan Johar, Huma Qureshi and Riteish Deshmukh wished Rishi on Twitter.
