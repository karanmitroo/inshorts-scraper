On Sunday, Sandeep Acharya(29), winner of Indian Idol(2006) , passed away at a Gurgaon hospital. The singer had been suffering from jaundice for a fortnight and was admitted to a local-hospital in his hometown, Bikaner, in a critical condition. He was later transferred to Gurgaon where he breathed his last at 9am, leaving behind his wife and a one-month-old daughter.
