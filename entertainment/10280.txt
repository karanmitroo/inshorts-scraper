Actor Salman Khan has revealed that he will release his app on his 51st birthday, which is on December 27. The actor tweeted an image to share the news. Earlier in August, Salman announced the 'Being Salman Game', which featured three of his onscreen characters Chulbul Pandey (Dabangg), Tiger (Ek Tha Tiger) and Prem (Prem Ratan Dhan Payo).
