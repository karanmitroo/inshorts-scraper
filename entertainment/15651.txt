Celebrities like Deepika Padukone, Alia Bhatt, Sonam Kapoor and Aditi Rao Hydari were spotted on the red carpet of the 61st Britannia Filmfare Awards in designs ranging from Ralph & Russo to Gauri & Nainika. Deepika wore a glittery backless red Ralph & Russo gown. While Alia wore a Sachin & Babi floral-print dress, Sonam chose an Anamika Khanna ensemble.
