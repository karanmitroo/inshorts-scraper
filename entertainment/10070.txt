The trailer for the Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Shweta Tripathi starrer 'Haraamkhor' has been released. Nawazuddin plays a teacher in rural India while Shweta plays his student, who is in love with him. The Censor Board had earlier banned the film calling it "very provocative". Written and directed by Shlok Sharma, the film will release on January 13, 2017.
