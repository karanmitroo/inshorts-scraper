Ben Affleck starrer 'Justice League', which released on Friday, "is a film that has hope in its eyes and optimism in its heart," wrote Hindustan Times. The Times of India called it a "highly enjoyable comic book film" while NDTV wrote, "Even Wonder Woman can't save this mess." It was rated 3/5 (HT), 3.5/5 (TOI) and 2/5 (NDTV).
