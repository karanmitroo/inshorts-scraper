Aamir Khan and Anushka Sharma starrer 'PK' has marked its entry in the 100-crore club of Bollywood, in 4 days of its release. The Rajkumar Hirani directorial minted Rs. 116.63 crores till Tuesday. Aamir's Dhoom-3 along with Shah Rukh's Happy New Year were the fastest to make Rs 100 crores, in just 3 days of their release.
