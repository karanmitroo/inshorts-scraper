Joseph Gordon Levitt starrer Hollywood film 'The Walk', which hit the screens today, has great visual effects and great performances, wrote HT. Based on the real life story of rope-walker Phillipe Petit, the Robert Zemeckis directorial is worth a watch, said Zee News. The film has been rated 2.5/5 (Indian Express), 3.5/5 (HT) and 4/5 (Zee News).
