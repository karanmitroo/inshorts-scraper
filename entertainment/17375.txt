Shah Rukh Khan who is pairing up with actress Kajol for 'Dilwale', a Rohit Shetty directorial, said Sunday that he was hoping to recreate their onscreen magic again. The duo is credited with many hits like 'DDLJ', 'Baazigar' and 'My Name Is Khan' among others. "It's very exciting to be back with Kajol... She's my warmest, closest friend," said SRK.
