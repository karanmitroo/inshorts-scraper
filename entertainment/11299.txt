'Ude Dil Befikre', a new song from the Ranveer Singh and Vaani Kapoor starrer film 'Befikre' was released on Thursday. The song has been sung by Benny Dayal and composed by the music director duo Vishal-Shekhar, with lyrics penned by Jaideep Sahni. Written, produced and directed by Aditya Chopra, the film is scheduled to release on December 9.
