Actress Alia Bhatt has tweeted the first picture from the sets of her upcoming film 'Dear Zindagi', which also stars Shah Rukh Khan. "Dear Zindagi, Is it OK not to be OK? Alia," wrote Alia alongside the picture to which SRK responded by sharing another tweet. Directed by Gauri Shinde, the film also stars Aditya Roy Kapur and Ali Zafar.
