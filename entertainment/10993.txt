A new poster of the Shah Rukh Khan and Alia Bhatt starrer upcoming film 'Dear Zindagi' has been released. Actress Alia Bhatt shared the poster on Twitter, captioned, "Wake up. Open your arms and just smile! Love love love life! #DearZindagi". Directed by Gauri Shinde, the film, also starring Aditya Roy Kapur and Kunal Kapoor will release on November 25.
