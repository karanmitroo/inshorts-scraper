The first poster of Shahid Kapoor and Alia Bhatt starrer film 'Shaandaar' was released on Wednesday, a day after its teaser poster was revealed. In the poster, Alia can be seen planting a kiss on Shahid's cheek as he grins ear to ear. A Vikas Bahl directorial, the film is about destination wedding. 'Shaandaar' will release on Oct 22.
