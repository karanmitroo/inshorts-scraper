Shobita Dhulipala, representing India at Miss Earth 2013 pageant, won the 'Miss Photogenic' title and followed it up with another win by bagging the 'Miss Eco-beauty' title. With over 5000 votes, Dhulipala has taken lead over second placer Miss Macau Ashley Qian and third placer Miss Turkey Ezgi Avci. The final contest will be held on Dec 7 in Philippines.
