The lineup of performers for India's first 'Global Citizen Festival' has been released. It includes British band 'Coldplay' and rapper Jay Z along with support from Indian celebrities including Ranveer Singh, Katrina Kaif, Aamir Khan, Farhan Akhtar, Shraddha Kapoor, Arjun Kapoor and Arijit Singh among others. The concert will be held on November 19, which is 'World Toilet Day'.
