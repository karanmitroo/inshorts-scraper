A 13-year-old North Eastern girl from Mizoram is Tinkle comics' latest superhero, appearing first in the November edition. Mapui Kawlim or “WingStar,” a reluctant teenager becomes her neighbourhood's saviour with superpowers like ‘super flight’ and ‘super strength.' Mapui as the first North East superhero character by Tinkle will add to other female superheroes like PsyMage by the comic series.
