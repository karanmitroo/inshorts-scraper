The first look of Bipasha Basu's upcoming horror film 'Alone' has hit the social media. Directed by Bhushan Patel, whose last venture was Ragini MMS 2, the film features famous TV actor Karan Singh Grover opposite Bipasha. The poster showcases two Bipashas, both entwined to Karan's body. As per reports, Bipasha would be playing conjoined twins in the film.
