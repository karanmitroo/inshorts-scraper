The makers of the Saif Ali Khan starrer 'Baazaar' have announced its release date as April 27, which is also the release date for Kangana Ranaut's film 'Manikarnika - The Queen of Jhansi'. While 'Manikarnika' is a historical film on Rani Laxmibai, Saif's film is a crime thriller based on the stock market.
