Alia Bhatt and Arjun Kapoor-starrer '2 States' and Shahid Kapur-starrer 'Haider' are leading the IIFA nominations with 9 and 8 nominations respectively. They are followed by Aamir Khan's 'PK' (6 nominations) and Kangana Ranaut's 'Queen (5 nominations). The 16th edition of the awards is due to take place in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, from June 5-7.
