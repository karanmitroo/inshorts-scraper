Bollywood celebrities on Sunday participated in the International Yoga Day celebrations. While Shilpa Shetty, Amrita Rao, Boman Irani and Juhi Chawla performed yoga postures, others like Amitabh Bachchan, Anupam Kher and Sidharth Malhotra created awareness about the benefits of Yoga online. Furthermore, some Bollywood celebrities even praised PM Narendra Modi for his Yoga initiative.
