Rakhi Sawant has said that she will play a Radhe Maa-like character called Twinkle Mata in the TV serial 'Bhaag Bakool Bhaag'. Further talking about her cameo, Rakhi added, "My character is very seductive...just like Radhe Maa who is beautiful and wears a lot of jewellery." Rakhi further said the self-styled godwoman Radhe Maa is her good friend.
