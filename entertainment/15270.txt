Bollywood actor Amitabh Bachchan is set to attend the upcoming second edition of the Times of India Film Awards (TOIFA) in Dubai. The event is scheduled to be held on March 18 this year. Celebrities including Shah Rukh Khan, Varun Dhawan, Jacqueline Fernandez, Kareena Kapoor Khan and Yo Yo Honey Singh are scheduled to perform at the awards ceremony.
