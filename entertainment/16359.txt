Comedian Kapil Sharma starrer 'Kis Kisko Pyaar Karoon' has managed to collect over Rs 30 crore within four days of its release. Notably, Kapil's Bollywood acting debut earned Rs 28.81 crore in its opening weekend at the box office. Making a business of Rs 4.5 crore on Monday, the Abbas-Mustan directorial has made a total collection of Rs 33.31 crore.
