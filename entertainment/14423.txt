'Captain America: Civil War', which released on Friday, collected ₹27.13 crore in its opening weekend. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the film collected ₹8.53 crore on Friday, ₹8.79 crore on Saturday and ₹9.81 crore on Sunday. Directed by Joe Russo and Anthony Russo, the Marvel Studios film stars Chris Evans and Robert Downey Jr.
