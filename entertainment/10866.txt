The John Abraham, Sonakshi Sinha and Tahir Raj Bhasin starrer 'Force 2' has earned ₹20.05 crore in its opening weekend. It collected ₹6.05 crore on Friday, ₹6.50 crore on Saturday and ₹7.50 crore on Sunday, as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. Notably, the film's collection on its release day was John’s highest opening day collection in India till date.
