Actress-filmmaker Pooja Bhatt, who will be directing 'Jism 3', has said that the film will be the "boldest" in the erotic-thriller franchise, 'Jism'. "I will start working on 'Jism 3' this year and the film will release sometime in 2017," said Pooja. The series's first film 'Jism' released in 2003 followed by a sequel in 2013 starring Sunny Leone.
