On Saturday, television actress Shweta Tiwari tied knot with Abhinav Kohli, with whom she has been in a relationship for three-and-a-half-years. Shweta had divorced her ex-husband, Raja Chaudhary due to his abusive behaviour towards her. She has a daughter named Palak from Raja, and the girl is supposed to stay in her mother's custody as per the court's orders.
