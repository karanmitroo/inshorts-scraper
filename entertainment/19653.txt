After no release this year, Salman Khan has blocked all major dates in 2014 to release his films.  As per plans, Sohail Khan's 'Mental' will be released on Republic Day and Sajid Nadiadwala's 'Kick' on Eid. Also, Prabhudeva's second flick with Salman might grab a Christmas or Diwali opening.  Salman will also be seen hosting Bigg Boss-7 on TV soon.
