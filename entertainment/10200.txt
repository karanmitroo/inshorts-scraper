The Nawazuddin Siddiqui starrer 'Haraamkhor', which was banned by the Censor Board has now been cleared by the Film Certification Appellate Tribunal (FCAT). The film, which also stars Shweta Tripathi, revolves around the relationship between a school teacher and his teenage student. Directed by Shlok Sharma, 'Haraamkhor' is scheduled to release on January 13, 2017.
