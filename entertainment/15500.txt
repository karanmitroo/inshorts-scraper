In ten days of its release, the Akshay Kumar and Nimrat Kaur starrer film 'Airlift' has minted ₹102.76 crore at the Indian box office, Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh revealed on Monday. Worldwide, the film has minted over ₹135.50 crore. 'Airlift' is Akshay's fourth film to enter the ₹100-crore club after 'Housefull 2' (2012), 'Rowdy Rathore' (2013) and 'Holiday' (2014).
