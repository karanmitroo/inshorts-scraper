The trailer for the Tom Hanks and Irrfan Khan starrer 'Inferno' was released on Monday. Based on Dan Brown's book 'Inferno' and a sequel to the 2006 film 'Da Vinci Code', the film also stars Felicity Jones. Director by Ron Howard, 'Inferno' will release on October 14 in India, two weeks ahead of its US release on October 28.
