The teaser of Nawazuddin Siddiqui starrer upcoming crime thriller 'Monsoon Shootout' has been released. Co-produced by Anurag Kashyap and also starring Vijay Varma and Tannishtha Chatterjee, the film focuses on the story of a policeman facing a life-altering decision where he must decide whether to shoot or not. Directed by Amit Kumar, 'Monsoon Shootout' will release on December 15.
