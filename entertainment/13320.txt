'Luv Letter', the first song from the Arshad Warsi, Aditi Rao Hydari and Boman Irani starrer 'The Legend of Michael Mishra' was released on Monday. Picturised on the lead actress Aditi, the song has been composed by Meet Bros Anjjan and sung by Meet Bros along with Kanika Kapoor. Directed by Manish Jha, the film will release on August 5.
