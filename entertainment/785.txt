Aamir Khan and Zaira Wasim starrer 'Secret Superstar' has earned over ₹300 crore within 8 days of its release in China. It entered the ₹100 crore club within two days and earned over ₹200 crore within four days in China. It also broke the opening weekend record of ₹72.68 crore set by Aamir's 'Dangal' at the Chinese box office.
