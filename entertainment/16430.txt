Stephen Colbert succeeded David Letterman and debuted on US TV network CBS’s “The Late Show” on Tuesday to 6.6 million viewers. This was 3.6 million more viewers than Jimmy Fallon’s “The Tonight Show” and 4.8 million more than ABC’s “Jimmy Kimmel Live” on the same night. Colbert had actor George Clooney and presidential candidate Jeb Bush among the first guests.
