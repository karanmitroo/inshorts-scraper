Singer Rihanna on Friday presented her first ever collection, Fenty Puma Fall 2016, at the New York Fashion Week (NYFW). As sportswear brand Puma's creative director, Rihanna's fashion show featured a mix of high-end fashion and sportswear. Models including Gigi Hadid wore oversized silhouettes, textured hoodies, high rise platform sneakers, wide-cut pants and velvet dresses from the collection.
