The poster of the upcoming film, 'Kapoor & Sons', was revealed by filmmaker Karan Johar on Wednesday. Alia Bhatt, Sidharth Malhotra, and Fawad Khan will be seen in lead roles in the Dharma Productions film. Rishi Kapoor will be playing the role of Fawad and Sidharth's grandfather. The film is scheduled to release on March 18.
