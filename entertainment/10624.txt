Following their Sikh wedding on November 30, Yuvraj Singh got married to Hazel Keech as per Hindu rituals in Goa on Friday. The wedding was attended by Virat Kohli, Anushka Sharma, Mukesh and Nita Ambani, besides cricketers Ashish Nehra, Rohit Sharma and Zaheer Khan among others. Hazel wore a sari by designer Ashley Rebello, while Yuvraj sported a beige sherwani.
