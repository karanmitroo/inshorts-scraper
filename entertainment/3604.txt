Badminton player Saina Nehwal shared pictures with actress Shraddha Kapoor and India's national badminton coach Pullela Gopichand from a badminton practice session with the actress for an upcoming biopic. In the biopic, Shraddha will be seen portraying Saina, who is India's first badminton player to win a medal at the Olympics. The film will be directed by Amole Gupte.
