As per reports, actor Shah Rukh Khan will play the role of a magician in the Salman Khan starrer upcoming film 'Tubelight'. The co-producer of 'Tubelight', Amar Butala, took to Twitter to confirm that SRK will be a part of the film. Directed by Kabir Khan, 'Tubelight' is set in the backdrop of the 1962 war between China and India.
