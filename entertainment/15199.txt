Filmmaker Karan Johar today shared the first look of Aditya Roy Kapur and Shraddha Kapoor starrer 'OK Jaanu' on Twitter. The Shaad Ali directorial is a remake of the Tamil film 'OK Kanmani' and will be produced under Johar's 'Dharma Productions' banner. The lead pair has earlier worked together in 'Aashiqui 2'.
