Censor Board of Film Certification (CBFC) chief Pahlaj Nihalani has been attacked again, this time by filmmaker Ashok Pandit. Launching a scathing attack on Nihalani, Pandit tweeted "Finally Mukesh & @MaheshNBhatt get their Censor Certificate which was cleared bt illegally withheld by the Chairman. #MrX #Anarchist". He also wrote that Rs 60 lacs were wasted due to unnecessary delays.
