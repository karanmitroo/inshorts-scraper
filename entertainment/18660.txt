Comedian Kapil Sharma will be the first guest on the 8th season of the game show ‘Kaun Banega Crorepati’ hosted by megastar Amitabh Bachchan. "So episodes over, its rehearsals for the Surat show...with some incomparable people like Kapil Sharma," Bachchan shared on his blog. The show will air from Aug-2 on Sony.
