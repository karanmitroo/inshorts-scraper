Sweeping 12 Emmys, American television series 'Game of Thrones' (GOT) has won the most Emmys ever in a year in history. GOT won the outstanding drama series award at the 67th Primetime Emmy Awards. Peter Dinklage, who plays Tyrion Lannister in GOT, won the outstanding supporting actor in a drama series award. Notably, the show had the most nominations (24).
