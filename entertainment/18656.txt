To promote their upcoming film 'Singham Returns', actors Ajay Devgn and Kareena Kapoor, and director Rohit Shetty have launched special movie merchandise in Mumbai. The collection includes items like shirts, tiffins, lunch boxes, popcorns, etc. The film, which is a sequel to the Ajay Devgn starrer 'Singham', is expected to hit the theatres on August 5 this year.
