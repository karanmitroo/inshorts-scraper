Late Pratyusha Banerjee's boyfriend Rahul Raj Singh was on Tuesday booked for assault and abetment to suicide by Mumbai police, after Banerjee's mother filed an FIR against him. He has been booked under various sections of the IPC including Section 323 (punishment for voluntarily causing hurt) and Section 506 (criminal intimidation). TV actress Pratyusha had committed suicide on April 1.
