After the first looks of Ranbir Kapoor and Anushka Sharma, 'Bombay Velvet' makers have released the much-awaited look of Karan Johar from the film. Johar, who is playing the role of villain Kaizad Khambatta, is seen sporting a moustache in the picture. 'Bombay Velvet' is filmmaker Karan Johar's first acting venture with a full fledged villainous role.
