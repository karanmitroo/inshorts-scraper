'Padmaavat' has entered the ₹100 crore club, making it actor Shahid Kapoor's first film to earn ₹100 crore in his 15-year-long Bollywood career. 'Padmaavat' earned ₹114 crore within four days of its official release and a limited paid preview of the film on January 24. Previously, Shahid's highest grossing film was the 2013 film 'R... Rajkumar'.
