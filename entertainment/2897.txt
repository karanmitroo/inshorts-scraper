The motion poster of comedian Kapil Sharma's upcoming film 'Firangi' has been released. Sharing the poster on Twitter, Kapil wrote, "KICK-starting this journey with #FirangiKaMotionPoster!" Directed by Kapil's friend Rajiev Dhingra, the film also stars 'Drishyam' actress Ishita Dutta and Indian-American model Monica Gill, and is scheduled to release on November 24.
