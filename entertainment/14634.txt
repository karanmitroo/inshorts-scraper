The official trailer for the film 'Waiting', starring Naseeruddin Shah and Kalki Koechlin was released on Friday. Directed by Anu Menon, the film is about the story of two strangers who cross paths and become friends as they experience similar circumstances. The film which also stars Rajat Kapoor, Sushasini Maniratnam and Arjun Mathur, is scheduled to release on May 27.
