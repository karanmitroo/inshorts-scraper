Anushka Sharma starrer 'NH10' which hit the screens Friday is definitely not for the fainthearted people, says India Today. The performances, screenplay, and the brutally honest story, makes this film a hell of a scary ride but absolutely worth watching, writes mid-day. The Navdeep Singh directorial has been rated 4.5/5 (India Today), 4/5 (mid-day), 3/5 (NDTV, ABP) and 2.5/5 (HT).
