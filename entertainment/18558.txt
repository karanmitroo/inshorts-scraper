Patna police watched Rani Mukherjee's 'Mardaani' in the theatre, Wednesday night, after a letter by Arvind Pandey, IG, CID (weaker sections), instructed all the district SPs, Railway SPs and Commandants of Bihar Military Police (BMP) to arrange shows. The move was taken to make policemen take lessons and inspiration from the movie, which contemplates upon the problem of human trafficking.
