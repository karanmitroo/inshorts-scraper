Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath on Friday announced the appointment of actor Akshay Kumar as the Brand Ambassador of Swachh Bharat Mission in the state. CM Yogi also declared the actor's film 'Toilet: Ek Prem Katha' as tax-free. Notably, Akshay participated in a cleanliness drive organised by the UP government in Lucknow as part of his film's promotion.
