Actress Alia Bhatt has shared a video to promote Akshay Kumar's film 'Rustom', where she can be seen dancing to the song 'Tip Tip Barsa Paani' from Akshay's 1994 film 'Mohra'. The 23-year old actress can be seen in a yellow saree, resembling actress Raveena Tandon's look from the song while also wearing a navy officer's cap for the video.
