The trailer of Akshay Kumar and Sonakshi Sinha’s action thriller ‘Holiday: A Soldier Never Off Duty’ is out. The movie is the remake of Tamil blockbuster ‘Thuppakki’. Written and directed by AR Murugadoss (the man behind superhit ‘Ghajini’), it is slated to release on Jun-6 and is likely to clash with Ajay Devgn’s ‘Action Jackson’.
