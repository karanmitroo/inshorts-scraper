Actor Kunal Nayyar, who is famous as the nerdy astrophysicist Dr. Rajesh Kothrappali in American sitcom 'The Big Bang Theory', said in an interview that he is looking forward to acting in a Bollywood movie, but is waiting for the right script. The 33-year-old Delhiite has also produced cricket documentary ‘Cricket World Cup 2011: Beyond All Boundaries’, releasing in October.
