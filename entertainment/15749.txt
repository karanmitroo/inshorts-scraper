On actor Hrithik Roshan's 42nd birthday, #HappyBirthdayHrithik was trending on Twitter across India on Sunday. Some users expressed their love for Hrithik tweeting, "Kaho Na Pyaar Hai" and "The reason i watch Bollywood movies is only for youu".
