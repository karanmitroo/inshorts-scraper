Actress Sunny Leone has shared a dubsmash video of herself on social media mimicking a dialogue from Shah Rukh Khan's upcoming film 'Raees'. "Koi dhandha chota nahi hota, aur dhande se bada koi dharam nahi hota," is the dialogue which Sunny mimics in the video. Notably, Sunny has featured in the recreated version of song 'Laila O Laila' in 'Raees'.
