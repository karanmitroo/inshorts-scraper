The Indian Film Festival of Melbourne (IFFM) 2015 on Saturday conferred Shoojit Sircar’s “Piku” with the best film award for 2015. “Dum Laga Ke Haisha” actress Bhumi Pednekar received the best actress award, Irrfan Khan won the best actor award for “Piku” and Shoojit Sircar got the best director award for the same film at the gala.
