The motion poster of the Emraan Hashmi starrer 'Raaz Reboot' was revealed on Wednesday. The supernatural thriller is the fourth installment of the 'Raaz' film series. Directed by Vikram Bhatt, the film also stars Kriti Kharbanda and Gaurav Arora. It is scheduled to release this July. Notably, the last film in the series 'Raaz 3D' was released in 2012.
