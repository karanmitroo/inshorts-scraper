Actor Ranveer Singh on Thursday took to Twitter to share a new poster of his upcoming film 'Befikre'. The film, also starring Vaani Kapoor, is being directed by Aditya Chopra. "Oops, we did it again", Ranveer captioned the poster, which features the lead couple kissing. Media reports had earlier claimed that 'Befikre' features 23 kissing scenes between Ranveer and Vaani.
