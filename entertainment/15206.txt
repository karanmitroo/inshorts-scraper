The team of upcoming film, 'Sarbjit', which stars Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Randeep Hooda, has been granted permission to shoot close to the Indo-Pakistan border near Attari. Aishwarya reportedly requested top government officials for permission to shoot scenes for the biopic on Sarabjit Singh, an Indian farmer who was convicted of terrorism and spying in Pakistan and sentenced to death.
