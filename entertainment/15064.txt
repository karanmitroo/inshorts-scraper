The first look of actress Nargis Fakhri from the upcoming musical drama, 'Banjo', was revealed on Monday. The 36-year-old actress plays the role of a DJ from New York who meets a band member of a Banjo party, played by Riteish Deshmukh, when she visits Mumbai. Directed by Ravi Jadhav, the film is scheduled to release on August 19.
