Parineeti Chopra and Aditya Roy Kapoor starrer, 'Daawat-e-Ishq' released on Friday. The Habib Faisal directorial has been rated 1.5 (Indian Express), 3 (TOI, NDTV), 3.5 (India Today). 'Daawat-e-Ishq' may not be a perfect film but it's an adorable love story and the two young actors make it paisa vasool, said India Today.
