According to Inshorts Youth of the Nation Poll (YOTN) 2, 62% of the 46,938 respondents said they did not approve of the Censor Board functioning under Chairman Pahlaj Nihalani. Meanwhile, 10% respondents said they approved of Nihalani while 28% were undecided. The Censor Board was in June taken to court after it demanded 89 cuts in Anurag Kashyap's 'Udta Punjab'.
