'Mere Miyan Gaye England', a new song from the Kangana Ranaut, Shahid Kapoor and Saif Ali Khan starrer upcoming drama film 'Rangoon' has been released. The song has been composed by Vishal Bhardwaj with vocals by Rekha Bhardwaj. It is a tribute to the 1949 song 'Mere Piya Gaye Rangoon'. Directed by Vishal Bhardwaj, 'Rangoon' will release on February 24.
