Actress Nargis Fakhri of 'Rockstar' fame has been signed to do a cameo in John Abraham's upcoming film 'Dishoom'. An action-adventure, the film will be directed by Rohit Dhawan and will also feature Varun Dhawan and Jacqueline Fernandes. As per reports, Akshay Kumar will also make a guest appearance in the film. However, no confirmation has been received from him.
