Singer Taylor Swift (26), with earnings of $170 million, has been named the world's highest paid musician of 2016 by Forbes. With earnings of $110 million, English-Irish pop boy band 'One Direction' ranked second, while Adele, with earnings of $80.5 million stood third. Singer Katy Perry, who topped the 2015 list of world's highest paid musicians, ranked 25th this year.
