The official trailer of 'Begum Jaan', starring Vidya Balan as a brothel owner, has been released. Set in 1947, the film is an adaptation of Bengali film 'Rajkahini', and also features actors Naseeruddin Shah, Chunky Pandey, and Gauahar Khan among others. The film, scheduled to release on April 14, marks the directorial debut of Bengali filmmaker Srijit Mukherji in Bollywood.
