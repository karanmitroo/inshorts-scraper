The logo of Anil Kapoor and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan starrer 'Fanne Khan' has been unveiled. "It's almost time for curtain call!! The show begins soon!!" tweeted Anil while sharing the logo. The film, which is scheduled to release on Eid in 2018, also stars actor Rajkummar Rao who has been paired opposite Aishwarya.
