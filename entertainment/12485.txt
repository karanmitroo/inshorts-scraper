An advertisement for the 'Lux Golden Rose Awards', featuring Shah Rukh Khan (SRK), Deepika Padukone, Sharmila Tagore, Madhuri Dixit and Sridevi, has been released. “Bollywood is coming together to celebrate beauty, glamour and style of iconic performances by women in Bollywood.
