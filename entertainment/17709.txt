Critically acclaimed for his performance in 'Aamir', actor Rajeev Khandelwal will be seen portraying a lover boy image in 'Abhi Nahi Toh Kabhi Nahi'. Film's producers Virendra K Arora and Arjun N Kapoor said, "This romantic avatar of Rajeev will surely make him an iconic heartthrob for girls everywhere." The film is set to release on Valentine's Day in 2016.
