A new poster for the Radhika Apte starrer 'Phobia' was shared by Bollywood trade analyst  Taran Adarsh on his Twitter account. The film revolves around Radhika, who plays an agoraphobic, a person who has an irrational fear of open or public places. Directed by Pawan Kripalani of 'Ragini MMS' fame, the film is scheduled to release on May 27.
