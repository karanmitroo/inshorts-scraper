Actor Irrfan Khan has signed director Nishikant Kamat's next film, a hard-hitting social thriller, called 'Madaari'. After 'Mumbai Meri Jaan', this is the second time the actor-director duo is working together. The film, which will also star Jimmy Shergil, is expected to release around July-August this year, said Madaari's producer Shailesh Singh.
