The Central Board of Film Certification (CBFC) has refused to give a certificate​ to the Nawazuddin Siddiqui starrer film 'Haraamkhor'. The award-winning​ film revolves around a teacher's illicit relationship with his 14-year-old student. The film's "objectionable" theme has been cited as the reason for the refusal, as per the film producer Guneet Monga.
