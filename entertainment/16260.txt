As reported by 'Emirates24/7', Shiv Sena's film wing Chitrapat Sena has said it will not allow Fawad Khan, Mahira Khan and other Pakistani actors to promote their films in Maharashtra. Criticising the signing of Pakistani actors in Indian films, Sena said, "Karan Johar, Farhan Akhtar, Shah Rukh Khan... are responsible citizens and they should not cast Pakistanis in their film".
