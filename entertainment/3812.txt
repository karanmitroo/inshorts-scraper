The first look motion poster of actor Nagarjuna's upcoming film 'Raju Gari Gadhi 2' has been released on the occasion of his 58th birthday on Tuesday. The film, which is said to be a horror comedy, also stars Samantha Ruth Prabhu and Seerat Kapoor in the lead roles. 'Raju Gari Gadhi 2' has been directed by filmmaker Ohmkar.
