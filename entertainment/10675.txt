Actor Amitabh Bachchan and Amruta Fadnavis, Maharashtra CM Devendra Fadnavis' wife, will soon be seen together in a music video for a song called 'Phir Se'. The music video's director Ahmed Khan said that the video shows Amruta seeking admission to a performing arts institute, which is headed by Amitabh. "There is a conversational scene between the two," added Ahmed.
