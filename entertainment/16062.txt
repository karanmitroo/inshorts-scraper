The first trailer of Ranveer Singh, Priyanka Chopra and Deepika Padukone starrer 'Bajirao Mastani' was launched on Friday. It gives a glimpse of the love triangle between Maratha Peshwa Bajirao I (Ranveer Singh), Mastani (Deepika) and Kashibai (Priyanka), in the backdrop of battle cries and war. Directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali, the film will hit the theatres on December 18.
