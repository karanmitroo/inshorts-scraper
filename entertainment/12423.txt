The music video for the title song of the Ranbir Kapoor, Anushka Sharma and Aishwarya Rai starrer 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' was released on Tuesday. The song has been sung by Arijit Singh and composed by Pritam, with lyrics by Amitabh Bhattacharya. Directed by Karan Johar, the film also stars Fawad Khan and is scheduled to release on October 28.
