The first poster of the Michael Fassbender starrer upcoming science-fiction horror film 'Alien: Covenant' has been released. It is the sequel to the 2012 film 'Prometheus' and also stars Noomi Rapace and Guy Pearce. Fassbender, Rapace and Pearce will be reprising their roles from 'Prometheus'. Directed by Ridley Scott, the film is scheduled to release on May 19, 2017.
