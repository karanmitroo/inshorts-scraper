Singer Beyoncé, who is pregnant with twins, performed at this year's Grammy Awards. She sang 'Love Drought' and 'Sandcastles' from her Grammy-nominated album 'Lemonade'. Towards the end of her performance, Beyoncé recited the line, "If we're going to heal, let it be glorious. One thousand girls raise their arms," from Warsan Shire's poem for 'Lemonade'.
