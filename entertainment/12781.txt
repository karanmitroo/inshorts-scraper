Actor Shah Rukh Khan today took to Twitter to appreciate Ranveer Singh for his recreation of the song 'Tu Mere Saamne' from his 1993 film 'Darr'. "@RanveerOfficial woke up & saw Darr song, thot  ‘o wow I can dance’ till I realised it was u my man. Dont know Tujhe dekhoon ke pyaar karoon!", Khan tweeted.
