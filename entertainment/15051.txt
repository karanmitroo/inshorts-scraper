TV actress Shilpa Shinde, who plays Angoori bhabhi in 'Bhabhiji Ghar Par Hain', has been served a legal notice by the serial's production house for allegedly breaching her contract. According to the show's producers, Shinde, who is set to join 'The Kapil Show', had earlier signed an 'exclusivity clause' with them. However, she has denied signing such a bond.
