A motion poster, introducing Akshay Kumar's character from the upcoming court drama film 'Jolly LLB 2' has been released. Akshay shared the poster on Twitter captioned, "Ek aur jhalak Jolly ki..." Also featuring actors Huma Qureshi, Arshad Warsi and Saurabh Shukla, the film, directed by Subhash Kapoor is scheduled to release on February 10, 2017.
