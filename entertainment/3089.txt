TV actress Divyanka Tripathi on Sunday slammed Jet Airways over a delay, tweeting, "We all have a life far more important post deboarding." Jet Airways took note of the delay, following which she tweeted, "I wish this was done irrespective of passenger's social status." Notably, Tripathi had to resume work for 'Yeh Hai Mohabbatein' after visiting several cities during Navratri.
