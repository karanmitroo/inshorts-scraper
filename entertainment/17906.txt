Bipasha Basu-starrer 'Alone', which is a remake of a 2007 Thai film by the same name, released on Friday. Bhushan Patel directorial, that also stars Karan Singh Grover, is neither a love story, nor it scares anyone, writes Hindustan Times. The movie has been rated 1/5 (HT), 1.5/5 (Indian Express), 2/5 (NDTV) and 2.5/5 (TOI).
