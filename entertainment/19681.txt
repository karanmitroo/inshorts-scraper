The trailer of Ritesh Batra's debut film 'Lunchbox' starring Irrfan, Nimrat Kaur and Nawazuddin Siddiqui is out. The movie, which bagged the Critics Week Viewers Choice Award at Cannes Film Festival, revolves around a mistaken delivery in Mumbai's efficient Dabba service. This wrong delivery of a lunchbox connects a young housewife to an old man. The film releases on September-20.
