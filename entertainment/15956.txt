YouTube released a 'Rewind' video titled 'Now Watch Me 2015' featuring viral videos and stars of 2015 like John Oliver, Superwoman, Kingsley set to the soundtrack of 2015's popular music. It shows YouTubers dancing to Silento's 'Watch Me' and Major Lazer, DJ Snake's 'Lean On'. It also shows the site's defining videos over the last decade including Rebecca Black's 'Friday'.
