Animated Hollywood film 'The Good Dinosaur' is a delight to watch though it loses plot once it adopts a Jungle Book approach, wrote TOI. The script of the film is simple, lively and enthusiastically layered to enthral its viewers, said Free Press Journal. The Peter Sohn directorial has been rated 3.5/5 (TOI) and 3/5 (IANS and Free Press Journal).
