The trailer of the Hugh Jackman starrer upcoming superhero film 'Logan' has been released. Jackman will be seen reprising his role as the mutant 'Wolverine' in 'Logan'. The film is a sequel to 'X-Men Origins: Wolverine' (2009) and 'The Wolverine' (2013). Directed by James Mangold, the film is scheduled to be released on March 3, 2017.
