The first look of actor Riteish Deshmukh in director Karan Anshuman's "Bangistan" was revealed on Twitter on Monday. Deshmukh plays a bumbling terrorist Hafeez Bin Ali in the film. Farhan Akhtar, who is the producer of the film, tweeted a picture of Deshmukh captioned, "Morning all.. Look who's shown up in the papers today.. @Riteishd from #Bangistan #firstlook @krnx."
