The trailer of Hrithik Roshan starrer 'Krrish 3', the biggest venture by the Roshan family, gathered 12-million views in just 10-days, leaving behind  two of the biggest superhero releases of Hollywood, 'Avengers'(almost 6-million) and 'Thor'(almost 4-million) and thus emerged as the most-awaited movie of the year. The superhero-science fiction film, along with its 3D format, will be released during Diwali(4-November-2013).
