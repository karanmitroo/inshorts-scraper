Three Indian films, including the Konkona Sen Sharma starrer 'Lipstick Under My Burkha', will be showcased at the upcoming Cairo International Film Festival. Malayalam film 'The Narrow Path' (Ottayaal Paatha) by Satish and Santosh Babusenan, has been selected for the main International Competition and will be competing along with 15 other films. Marathi film 'Half Ticket' has also been selected.
