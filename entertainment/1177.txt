Amid reports of Sanjay Leela Bhansali's 'Padmavati' releasing as 'Padmavat' on January 25, Rajasthan CM Vasundhara Raje has said that the film will not be released in the state. She said that the decision was taken considering the sentiments of the people. The CM further said that Queen Padmini was "more than just history" for the people of Rajasthan.
