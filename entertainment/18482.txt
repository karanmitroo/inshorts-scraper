Bollywood’s 'Hero no.1', Govinda, will finally return to the silver screen with the romantic action flick ‘Kill Dil’. His co-star Ranveer Singh shared Govinda's poster on Twitter. In typical Govinda style, the actor who reportedly plays a negative role in the film is sporting bright pink pants, a dark shirt and aviator sunglasses, with the caption, ‘#WILLKILLFORPAISA'.
