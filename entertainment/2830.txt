According to reports, YouTube personality Dhinchak Pooja will be one of the wild card contestants on reality show 'Bigg Boss 11'. Dhinchak Pooja is known for songs like 'Dilon Ka Shooter' and 'Baapu Dede Thoda Cash'. Meanwhile, it has been confirmed that the other wild card contestant will be Priyank Sharma, who was earlier evicted from the show.
