Filmmaker Karan Johar took to Twitter to wish Rajinikanth on the occasion of his 67th birthday on Tuesday and wrote, "Happy birthday to the mega superstar." Actor Riteish Deshmukh's tweet read, "Happy Birthday Thalaiva... the one and only @superstarrajini Sir. Wishing him long life & best of health." Meanwhile, Akshay Kumar wrote, "They don't call you superstar just like that."
