Jitesh Singh Deo from Lucknow was declared the winner of the 2017 Mr India pageant which was held on Thursday. Indian footballer Prathamesh Maulingkar won the title of Mr India Supranational and will compete at Mr Supranational 2018. Abhi Khajuria was declared the first-runner up while Pavan Rao was the second runner-up. Actress Kangana Ranaut felicitated the winners.
