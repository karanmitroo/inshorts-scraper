Singer Ed Sheeran has been sued for allegedly copying segments of Marvin Gaye's 1973 song 'Let's Get It On' for his hit single 'Thinking Out Loud'. An infringement lawsuit has been filed by Ed Townsend, who composed and co-wrote the song's lyrics. Notably, Sheeran is facing another lawsuit for supposedly copying the 2009 song 'Amazing' for his single 'Photograph'.
