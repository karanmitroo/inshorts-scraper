The Benedict Cumberbatch starrer superhero film 'Doctor Strange', which released on Friday, "is a simplistic origin tale", wrote India Today. Indian Express wrote that it "raises the bar on what's possible in Marvel's comic book universe." Meanwhile, Hindustan Times wrote that the film features "yet another lacklustre villain". It was rated 2.5/5 (India Today), 3.5/5 (Indian Express) and 3/5 (HT).
