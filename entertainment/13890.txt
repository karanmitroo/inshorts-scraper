Following American female singer Christina Grimmie's death after being shot dead while signing autographs after her concert in Florida, 'Christina Grimmie' was the fourth most trending Google search in India on Saturday. It was most looked up in Mizoram, Nagaland and Meghalaya. 'Cricinfo', 'Cricbuzz' and 'Ku Results' were some of the other trending searches on the same day.
