'Tu Hi Na Jaane', the new song from the Emraan Hashmi, Prachi Desai and Nargis Fakhri-starrer film 'Azhar' has been released. The song has been sung by Sonu Nigam and Prakriti Kakar and features the three lead characters. Based on the life of former cricketer Mohammad Azharuddin, the film is scheduled to release on May 13.
