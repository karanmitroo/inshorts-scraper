As per reports, the Central Board of Film Certification (CBFC) has replaced the word 'chinal', the Hindi word for slut, with 'jhoothi' (liar) in the film 'Rangoon'. Two other cuss words bastard and bak**** have also been reportedly replaced, with 'rascal' being used instead of bastard while 'bakchor' was used for 'bak****. The film has been awarded a U/A certificate.
