'Sweety Tera Drama', the first song from the Kriti Sanon, Ayushmann Khurrana and Rajkummar Rao starrer 'Bareilly Ki Barfi', has been released. It has been composed by Tanishk Bagchi, sung by Dev Negi, Pawni Pandey and Shraddha Pandit with lyrics by Shabbir Ahmed. Directed by Ashwiny Iyer Tiwari, the film is scheduled to release on August 18.
