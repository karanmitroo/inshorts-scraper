Following reports that the Marathi-movie 'Sairat' is being remade in all four South Indian languages, 'Sairat' was the second most trending Google search in India on Monday. It was most looked up in Maharashtra, Goa, and Dadra and Nagar Haveli. 'JIPMER', 'AirAsia' and 'Lenovo Vibe K5' were some of the other trending searches.
