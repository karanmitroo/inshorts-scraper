Radhika Apte starrer 'Parched' will be premiered at the Toronto International Film Festival this year. The 10-day film festival will take place September 10 onwards. Parched, directed by Leena Yadav, also features Adil Hussain, Surveen Chawla, Tannishtha Chatterjee and Sayani Gupta. Leena Yadav is accredited for films like Shabd and Teen Patti.
