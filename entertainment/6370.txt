Sonam Kapoor wore custom made Kalyan Jewellers earrings and 'hath-phool', designed by her sister Rhea Kapoor, for her first red carpet appearance at the Cannes Film Festival 2017. Her choice of outfit for the event was an embellished custom-made haute couture dress by Elie Saab. Sonam along with Aishwarya Rai and Deepika Padukone are representing a beauty brand at Cannes.
