Akshay Kumar starrer action-comedy 'Singh is Bliing' has collected Rs 54.44 crore at the box-office in just three days of its release. It minted Rs 20.67 crore on its opening day, followed by Rs 14.50 crore and Rs 19.27 crore on Saturday and Sunday respectively. The film, which is Akshay's biggest opener, has also become his highest weekend collector.
