Uttarakhand CM Harish Rawat has granted tax-free status to Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Radhika Apte starrer ‘Manjhi: The Mountain Man’ in the state. The Ketan Mehta directorial was already declared tax-free in Bihar, Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh. The film is based on the life of Dashrath Manjhi, a poor labourer hailing from Gehlaur village in Bihar’s Gaya district.
