The Amitabh Bachchan starrer upcoming film 'Pink' will be a thriller set in Delhi, producer Shoojit Sircar has revealed. Bachchan, who will be the lead actor in the film, will essay the role of a lawyer. To be directed by Bengali filmmaker Aniruddha Roy Chowdhury, the film will also feature actress Taapsee Pannu of 'Baby' fame.
