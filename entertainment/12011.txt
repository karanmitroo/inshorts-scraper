The season premiere of the Priyanka Chopra starrer American series 'Quantico' titled 'Kudove', which aired on Monday, "runs at the speed of a bullet", wrote Hindustan Times. The Times of India wrote, "From kicking butt in high heels to jumping off the plane, Priyanka... steals the show." Meanwhile, The Indian Express wrote, "A lot of questions are bound to linger."
