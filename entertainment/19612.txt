Ritesh Batra's The Lunchbox, winner of Critics' Week Viewers Choice Award (Cannes) and World Cinema Amsterdam Audience Award(Amsterdam), released this Friday. The movie that features Irrfan Khan, Nimrat Kaur and Nawazuddin Siddiqui has been rated 3.5 (Indian Express), 4 (DNA, NDTV) and 4.5 (Zee News). "The Lunchbox holds riches that aren't likely to be forgotten in a hurry", says NDTV.
