Filmmaker Karan Johar, while responding to a fan video on Twitter indicated that his film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' will release as scheduled. "[S]ee you at the cinemas on the 28th of this month! #AeDilKiDiwali," wrote Johar. The film's release has been suspended in Maharashtra, Gujarat, Goa and Karnataka by the Cinema Owners and Exhibitors Association.
