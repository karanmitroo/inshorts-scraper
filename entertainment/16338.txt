Hollywood film 'The Martian', which released in India on Friday, has all- science fiction, emotions, humour, a fight for survival- in perfect balance, wrote India Today. The Ridley Scott directorial, starring Matt Damon, is an exciting, hopeful story about humanity at its best, said The Indian Express. It has been rated 4/5 (India Today, TOI) and 3.5/5 (Indian Express).
