Actor Farhan Akhtar's look from the upcoming biopic 'Daddy' has been revealed. The actor will reportedly portray indicted racketeer and terrorist Dawood Ibrahim in the film. 'Daddy' will star actor Arjun Rampal in the lead role. He will be seen playing gangster turned politician Arun Gawli. Directed by Gaurav Bavdankar, the film will release in 2017.
