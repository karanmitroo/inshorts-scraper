Actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan's look from her upcoming film 'Fanney Khan' has emerged online. The actress has been shooting in Mumbai for the film. Also starring actors Rajkummar Rao and Anil Kapoor, 'Fanney Khan' is the Hindi adaptation of the 2000 Dutch movie 'Everybody's Famous!' and stars Aishwarya as a popular singer.
