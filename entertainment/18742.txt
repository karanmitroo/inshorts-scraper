Director Samar Shaikh’s ‘Bobby Jasoos’ starring Vidya Balan and Ali Fazal released this Friday. The romantic comedy, which marks the debut of its director, has been rated 2 (HT, India Today), 2.5 (IBN Live) and 3 (Times of India, Zee News, NDTV). The film is just about watchable and the only reason is Vidya Balan, writes India Today.
