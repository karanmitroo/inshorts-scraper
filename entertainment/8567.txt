The Kangana Ranaut, Shahid Kapoor and Saif Ali Khan starrer 'Rangoon' earned ₹6.07 crore on its opening day, as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. "Rangoon has a dismal start... Will need to show massive growth on Sat + Sun to sustain and survive," tweeted Adarsh. The film is said to have been made on a budget of ₹80 crore.
