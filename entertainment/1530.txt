The trailer of the upcoming Sidharth Malhotra and Manoj Bajpayee starrer 'Aiyaary' has been released. The film revolves around two Indian Army officers and their relationship as a mentor and pupil. The Neeraj Pandey directorial, which also stars Anupam Kher, Naseeruddin Shah and Pooja Chopra, is scheduled to release on January 26, 2018.
