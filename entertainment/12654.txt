Actor Kamaal R Khan (KRK) has shared a video of himself dancing to the song 'Beat Pe Booty' from the Tiger Shroff and Jacqueline Fernandez starrer superhero film 'A Flying Jatt'. KRK further nominated Mika Singh, Rahul Vaidya and Yuvika Choudhary to take up the '#BeatPeBooty' challenge. The challenge is being used as a promotional activity by the film's makers.
