Ranveer Singh on Wednesday revealed the first poster of 'Bajirao Mastani'. The film, directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali, also features Deepika Padukone and Priyanka Chopra. The three actors shared their respective looks in the film on Instagram. While Ranveer plays Maratha Peshwa Bajirao I, Deepika plays his second wife Mastani. Priyanka plays the role of his first wife Kashibai.
