A picture of actor Sanjay Dutt, posing with the cast of the upcoming film 'Golmaal 4' was shared by actor Ajay Devgn on Twitter. "The original rockstar," read the caption alongside the picture. Actors Arshad Warsi, Parineeti Chopra, Tabu, Kunal Kemmu, Shreyas Talpade, Tusshar Kapoor can be seen in the picture, along with the film's director Rohit Shetty.
