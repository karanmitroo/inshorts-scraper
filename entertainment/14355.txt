'Khallas', the new song from Ram Gopal Varma's upcoming film 'Veerappan' was released on Saturday. The song has been composed and sung by the duo Shaarib and Toshi, while Jasmine Sandlas has also lent her voice to the song. Picturised on Zareen Khan, the song is a rendition of the original song featuring Isha Koppikar from the 2002 film 'Company'.
