Randeep Hooda-Alia Bhatt starrer ‘Highway’, directed by Imtiaz Ali - the maker of Socha Na Tha, Jab We Met, Love Aaj Kal and Rockstar - released today. The movie has been rated 1.5 (Rohit Khilnani of India Today), 2 (Indian Express), 2.5 (Vinayak Chakravorty of India Today; Koimoi.com), 3 (NDTV), 3.5 (Times of India; Zee News) and 4 (DNA).
