Actor Rishi Kapoor while discussing Baahubali 2's success, tweeted, "BAHU"t"BALI"yaan chadengi filmon ki (many films will be sacrificed) to match this film's triumph and business." The wordplay Bahutbali was on the film's title Baahubali. Rishi further called the film "an Indian celebration" and said that he was glad to be part of this business of films.
