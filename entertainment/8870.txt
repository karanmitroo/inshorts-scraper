Singer David Bowie, who passed away on January 10, 2016, won five awards posthumously for his album 'Blackstar' at the Grammy Awards 2017. He won in the 'Best Rock Performance', 'Best Rock Song', 'Best Alternative Music Album', 'Best Engineered Album-Non-Classical' and 'Best Recording Package' categories. Notably, Bowie was honoured with the Lifetime Achievement Award at the Grammys in 2006.
