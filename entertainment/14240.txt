The teaser for the upcoming Marathi film 'Ekk Albela' has been released. The biopic on Bhagwan Dada portrayed by Mangesh Desai marks the debut of Vidya Balan in the Marathi film industry. Balan appears as the actress Geeta Bali in the film, which commemorates Bhagwan Dada as 'the first dancing superstar of Indian Cinema'. It will release on June 24.
