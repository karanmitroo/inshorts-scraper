The readers of 'Fitness' magazine have named Oscar winning actress Lupita Nyong'o as the best celebrity body of 2014. Australian recording artist and model Iggy Azalea has been ranked second for her single 'Fancy'. Singer Taylor Swift also was ranked high on the first-ever Fitness Magazine's 'Fit List', with her song 'Shake It Off' claiming 'Favourite Workout Song' title.
