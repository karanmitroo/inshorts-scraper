According to reports, actress Aishwarya Rai will star opposite actor Anil Kapoor after 17 years in an upcoming film. The film, titled 'Fanney Khan', will be produced by Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra. Directed by debutant Atul Manjrekar, the musical comedy is said to be inspired by Dominique Deruddere's Oscar-nominated 'Everybody's Famous!' (2000).
