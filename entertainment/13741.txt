Twitter users have criticised the magazine 'Maxim India' for its cover which features actress Priyanka Chopra, alleging that her armpits have been photoshopped in the picture. A user tweeted, "Priyanka Chopra's armpit in the Maxim cover is so unrealistic" while another tweet read, "Wish I was as stunning as #PriyankaChopra 's armpit without Photoshop." Meanwhile, another user tweeted, "Who cares?"
