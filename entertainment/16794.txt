As per a latest Bollywood industry buzz, superstar Shah Rukh Khan will be seen playing the role of a dwarf in director Aanand Rai's next film. Reportedly, the role was previously offered to Salman Khan. Shah Rukh will also be co-producing the film with Rai, who is known for making the 'Tanu Weds Manu' franchise and 'Raanjhanaa'.
