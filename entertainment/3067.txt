Actress Vidya Balan has revealed that Emraan Hashmi jokingly asked her what her husband Siddharth Roy Kapur would say after watching her kissing scene with Emraan in 'Ghanchakkar'. She added, "Just before the scene he'll say something really ridiculous...He kept telling me- You think I'll get my last cheque?" Siddharth Roy Kapur was one of the producers of 'Ghanchakkar'.
