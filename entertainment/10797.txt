Actor Salman Khan's rumoured girlfriend Iulia Vantur has sung a song for music composer-singer Himesh Reshammiya's upcoming album 'Aap Se Mausiiquii'. The song is titled 'Every Night and Day' and is a Hindi track. "Iulia is talented and has sung beautifully. She is a professional singer, so she's well-versed with the technicalities of recording," said Himesh.
