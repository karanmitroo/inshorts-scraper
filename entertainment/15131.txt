Bollywood actor Shah Rukh Khan recently offered a job to his fan, Shivam Jemini, who uploaded a re-created video of 'Fan' trailer on YouTube using the VFX technique. "Come and work with us at the vfx studio man. Well done," SRK posted on Twitter along with the link to the video. "#MadeMyDay I'd love to work under you!," Jemini replied.
