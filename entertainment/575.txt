Sonakshi Sinha has revealed that a celebrity model once body-shamed her, while adding that she made the remark, "What is this cow doing on the catwalk?" She revealed this on Neha Dhupia's talk show 'BFFs with Vogue', where she appeared with Manish Malhotra. On being asked the model's name, Manish revealed that it was former Miss India Earth Shamita Singha.
