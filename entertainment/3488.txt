Farhan Akhtar starrer 'Lucknow Central', which released on Friday, "fails to utilise its resources...and loses out on chance to become...engaging," wrote Hindustan Times. The Quint wrote, "(It's) utterly forgettable" while Times of India called it a "fascinating tale of dreamers and fighters, who refuse to give up on life." It was rated 2.5/5 (HT), 1.5/5 (The Quint) and 3.5/5 (TOI).
