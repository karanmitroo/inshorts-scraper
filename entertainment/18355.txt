Amitabh Bachchan revealed the first look of his upcoming film 'Do', on Twitter, Thursday. The poster shows a wheel-chair ridden Bachchan and Farhan Akhtar sitting across a table, playing chess and sipping coffee, with the Hauz Khas Fort covering the background. The movie is directed by Bejoy Nambiar, with the script-writer and the producer being Vidhu Vinod Chopra.
