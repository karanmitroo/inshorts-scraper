Actress Kriti Sanon celebrated Raksha Bandhan on Monday by tying rakhi on her sister Nupur Sanon. Kriti shared a picture with her sister on Instagram which she captioned, "Happy Rakhi to the cutest,funniest and most beautiful sister in the whole world." She further wrote, "From troubling me with millions of questions to telling stories...in the funniest...way...I love you for everything."
