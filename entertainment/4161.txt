Composer duo Salim-Sulaiman dedicated a song titled 'Mera Desh Hi Dharam' to the Armed Forces on the occasion of India's 71st Independence Day on Tuesday. Talking about the idea behind the song, Salim said, "A few months back, we met PM Narendra Modi who suggested we...make a song for the country." The song has been penned by Sandeep Shrivastava.
