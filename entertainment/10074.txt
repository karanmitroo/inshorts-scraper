A new video shows the making of the film 'OK Jaanu', while focusing on how Shraddha Kapoor and Aditya Roy Kapur portray the characters Tara and Adi. Sharing the video, Dharma Productions tweeted, "As romantic as they are on screen, their goofy side doesn't rest while they're together and off-screen!" The film is scheduled to release on January 13, 2017.
