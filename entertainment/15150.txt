Malayalam actor and singer Kalabhavan Mani passed away at a hospital in Kochi on Sunday at the age of 45 years owing to a liver ailment. Mani won the National Film Award for his role in the 1999 Malayalam film, 'Vasanthiyum Lakshmiyum Pinne Njaanum.' Notably, Mani had also worked as an autorickshaw driver prior to his film career.
