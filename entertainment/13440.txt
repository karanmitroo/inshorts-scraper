Actress Alia Bhatt has featured on the cover of the film magazine 'Cine Blitz' for its July issue in the cover story titled 'I'm Setting Milestones!' Alia has worn a blue 'Pull&Bear' t-shirt which bears the caption 'Girls On Board' paired with a black pair of jeans. Smokey eyes and her hair pulled back in a ponytail complete the look.
