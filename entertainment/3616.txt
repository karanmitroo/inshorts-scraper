Actress Alia Bhatt's pictures as a Kashmiri girl from the sets of her upcoming film 'Raazi' have surfaced online. The film is based on Harinder Sikka's novel 'Calling Sehmat' and also stars Vicky Kaushal in the lead role. Earlier, Alia revealed that she is learning Urdu for the film, which is being directed by Meghna Gulzar.
