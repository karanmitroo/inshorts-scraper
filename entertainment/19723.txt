Rakesh Omprakash Mehra, director of the blockbuster BHAAG MILKHA BHAAG said that he struggled for 3-years to raise money for the movie. The movie, which earned Rs.40 crore in the first week, is all set to join the 100-crore-Club.  Biopics now seems to be the trend with one more biopic on Mary Kom in line and others under consideration.
