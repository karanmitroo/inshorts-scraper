YouTube channel 'Movie Muchies' has released a new video of Disney princesses singing their trademark songs in their native languages. The UHD compilation shows Elsa from 'Frozen' singing in Norwegian, Ariel from 'The Little Mermaid' singing in Danish, Jasmine from 'Aladdin' singing in Arabic, Mulan from 'Mulan' singing in Mandarin, and Rapunzel from 'Tangled' singing in German.
