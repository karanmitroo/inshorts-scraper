Actor Nawazuddin Siddiqui will star in the drama series 'McMafia', co-produced by AMC, BBC and Cuba Pictures . "It ws an overwhelming experience working with James Watkins (Dir) & Hossein Amini (writer)," tweeted Siddiqui. Watkins will be directing all eight episodes of the series, which is inspired from Misha Glenny's 2008 book by the same name.
