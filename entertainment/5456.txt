The trailer of PARTITION: 1947, the Hindi version of Gurinder Chadha's film starring Huma Qureshi, Manish Dayal and late actor Om Puri, has been released. The British-Indian film's English version is titled Viceroy's House and marks Huma's debut in Hollywood. Directed by Gurinder Chadha, both versions of the film will release on August 18.
