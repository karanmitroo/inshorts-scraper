In nine days of its release, Sanjay Leela Bhansali's historical romance film 'Bajirao Mastani' has collected ₹120.45 crore at the Indian box office. According to trade analyst Taran Adarsh, the Ranveer Singh, Deepika Padukone and Priyanka Chopra starrer film picked up in its second weekend, collecting ₹34.30 crore. Globally, the film has minted over ₹155 crore.
