Deepika Padukone has featured in a lead role in four consecutive ₹100 crore movies, which is considered to be a record for any Bollywood actress. This includes all her 2013 releases 'Ram-Leela', 'Chennai Express', 'Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani' and 'Race 2'. Deepika, who turned 31 on Thursday, has given a total of six ₹100 crore movies throughout her Bollywood career.
