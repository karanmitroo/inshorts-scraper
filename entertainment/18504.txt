According to reports, Hrithik Roshan and Katrina Kaif will be seen together on the silver screen for a third time after Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara (ZNMD) and Bang Bang, in Zoya Akhtar's next. The movie, allegedly titled 'Road', will feature her brother Farhan Akhtar, and also actor Abhay Deol. The 4 were seen together in Zoya's ZNMD.
