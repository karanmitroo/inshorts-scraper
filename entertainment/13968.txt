A day after the motion poster for Ashutosh Gowariker's upcoming film Mohenjo Daro was released, '#MohenjoDaro' was trending in India on Twitter on Tuesday. A user reacted to the poster release by tweeting, "Before Westeros there was #MohenjoDaro". While another user's tweet read, "Amazing visual effects and brilliant music by AR Rahman, can't wait till the 12th of august #MohenjoDaro".
