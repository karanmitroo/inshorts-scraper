Veteran actor Dharmendra, originally named Dharam Singh Deol, was born on December 8, 1935, in Punjab. Dharmendra first married Parkash Kaur at the age of 19 and had four kids, including Sunny Deol and Bobby Deol. He later converted to Islam to marry actress Hema Malini without separating from his first wife. Further, Dharmendra was awarded Padma Bhushan in 2012.
