Director James Gunn has shared a video which gives a 'sneak peek' into the upcoming superhero film 'Guardians of the Galaxy Vol 2'. It is a sequel to the 2014 film 'Guardians of the Galaxy'. The film stars Chris Pratt, Zoe Saldana, Dave Bautista, Vin Diesel and Bradley Cooper among others and is scheduled to release on May 5, 2017.
