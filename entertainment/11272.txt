A deleted song from Karan Johar's film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil,' featuring actors Ranbir Kapoor and Anushka Sharma has been released. They can be seen dancing to the song 'An Evening in Paris' from the 1967 film of the same name which starred Shammi Kapoor and Sharmila Tagore. Notably, Ranbir is the grand-nephew of late actor Shammi Kapoor.
