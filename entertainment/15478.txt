Badminton player Saina Nehwal has said that for the upcoming biopic on her, she wants actress Deepika Padukone to play the lead role. "She can do justice to the role," said Saina, while calling Deepika a "good" shuttler. Filmmaker Amol Gupte will be making the biopic on Nehwal, the first Indian to win a medal in Badminton at the Olympics.
