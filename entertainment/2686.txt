'Ghoomar', the first song from the upcoming Deepika Padukone, Shahid Kapoor and Ranveer Singh starrer 'Padmavati', has been released. The song has been composed by the film's director Sanjay Leela Bhansali, with lyrics by AM Turaz and vocals by Shreya Ghoshal and Swaroop Khan. The historical drama revolves around Ranveer's character Alauddin Khilji's infatuation with the Rajput queen Padmavati.
