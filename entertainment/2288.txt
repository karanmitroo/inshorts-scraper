Actress Sridevi, while talking about the comparison between her daughter Jhanvi and Saif Ali Khan's daughter Sara, said, "There's nothing wrong with it. Competition keeps you on your toes." She added, "Competition can never be eliminated from this industry." While Sara will be making her debut with 'Kedarnath', Jhanvi's debut film will be the Hindi remake of Marathi film 'Sairat'.
