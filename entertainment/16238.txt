The trailer of 'Hate Story 3', featuring Sharman Joshi, Zarine Khan, Daisy Shah and Karan Singh Grover, was released on Friday. It is the third film in the 'Hate Story' series, the first film of which was released in 2012. Written by Vikram Bhatt and directed by Vishal Pandya, the film is scheduled to release on December 4.
