Actor Akshay Kumar has shared a still of himself with co-star Bhumi Pednekar, to mark the end of film Toilet-Ek Prem Katha's shoot. The caption alongside the picture reads, "Keshav and Jaya's unique love story coming to you on June 2." Both actors are dressed in wedding attire, with Bhumi wearing a red lehenga and Akshay in a mustard suit.
