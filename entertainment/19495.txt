Saif Ali Khan will be doing a multi-starrer comedy film with director Sajid Khan, featuring Saif in lead role. The film will be produced by Disney-UTV and is expected to be released at the end of 2014. Saif is currently working with Sajid in 'Hamshakals', which is scheduled for release on June, 2014. Saif plays a triple role in Hamshakals.
