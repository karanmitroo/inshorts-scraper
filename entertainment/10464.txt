As per reports, actor Rajinikanth has refused to dub for Aamir Khan's character in the Tamil version of the upcoming biographical sports drama film 'Dangal.' A source said, "Aamir screened the film for Rajinikanth... Although moved by the film, he politely turned down the offer." 'Dangal', featuring Aamir as wrestler Mahavir Singh Phogat, will be released in Tamil and Telugu.
