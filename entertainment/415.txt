Shiv Sena MP Sanjay Raut has said actor Nawazuddin Siddiqui was the only choice for 'Thackeray', the upcoming biopic on Shiv Sena founder Bal Thackeray. "I think people all across the world will appreciate the movie," he added. Directed by Abhijit Panse, the film will be shot in Hindi and Marathi and it will also be dubbed in English.
