Actress Priyanka Chopra, who turned 35 on Tuesday, made her acting debut opposite Vijay in the 2002 Tamil film 'Thamizhan'. She also made her singing debut in the same film with the track 'Ullathai Killathe'. Priyanka, who is a Padma Shri awardee, made her Bollywood debut in the Sunny Deol starrer 'The Hero: Love Story of a Spy' in 2003.
