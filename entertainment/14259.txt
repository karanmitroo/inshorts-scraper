Actor Abhishek Bachchan on Thursday walked away from a photo-op with his wife and 'Sarbjit' lead actress Aishwarya Rai Bachchan at the premiere of the biopic 'Sarbjit'. In the video, Abhishek is initially seen ignoring requests to pose for pictures together with Aishwarya. Later, when he poses with her, he leaves midway asking the media to click just Aishwarya's pictures.
