The 14th edition of Sansui Stardust Awards, hosted by Abhishek Bachchan, Riteish Deshmukh, Farah Khan and Maniesh Paul, was held in Mumbai on Monday. The event was attended by celebrities including Shah Rukh Khan, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Priyanka Chopra among others. While Parineeti Chopra paid a tribute to cousin Priyanka, Iulia Vantur performed to rumoured boyfriend Salman Khan's songs.
