'Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri' "is an anomaly...there's not a...moment...that feels like something you've already...experienced," wrote Hindustan Times. "It is filled with curiosity about what it...(holds): banality or brilliance," wrote NDTV while Times Of India called it "a fine line between a... dark comedy and a hard punch to the guts." It was rated 5/5 (HT) and 4/5 (NDTV, TOI).
