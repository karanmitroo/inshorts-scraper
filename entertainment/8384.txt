The trailer of Disney's 'DuckTales', a reboot based on an animated series of the same name which premiered in the 90s, has been released. The first season, which will air on the channel 'Disney XD', will consist of 21 half-hour episodes and two one-hour specials. The cartoon will feature characters Scrooge McDuck and his three grandnephews Huey, Dewey and Louie.
