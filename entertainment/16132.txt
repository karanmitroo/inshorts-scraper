Megastar Amitabh Bachchan's debut film 'Saat Hindustani', a Khwaja Ahmad Abbas directorial, released on November 7, 1969. While Bachchan had lent his voice in National Award winning film 'Bhuvan Shome' before this, 'Saat Hindustani' was the first Bollywood film he acted in. The film revolves around seven Indians and their attempt to liberate Goa from the Portuguese colonial rule.
