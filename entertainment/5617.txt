The title song of the Anil Kapoor and Arjun Kapoor starrer film 'Mubarakan' has been released. The song has been sung by Juggy D, Yash Narvekar, Badshah and Sukriti Kakar. It has been composed by Rishi Rich, with lyrics by Kumaar. Also starring Athiya Shetty and Ileana D'Cruz, the film is scheduled to release on July 28.
