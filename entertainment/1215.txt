Pictures of Abhishek Bachchan and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan's ₹21 crore apartment, located in Bandra-Kurla Complex, have been featured in an article by a magazine. The couple had bought the apartment in 2015. It has been designed by an in-house team, said the magazine. Sonam Kapoor reportedly has a ₹35 crore apartment in the same complex.
