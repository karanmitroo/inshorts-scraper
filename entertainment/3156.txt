As per reports, actress Parineeti Chopra will star opposite Akshay Kumar in 'Kesar', a film based on the Battle of Saragarhi. The film will reportedly be co-produced by Salman Khan and Karan Johar's production houses. "Sets of the Saragarhi and Gulistan forts, as well as a village, will be erected in Wai, a town in Satara (Maharashtra)," said a source.
