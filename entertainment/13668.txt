On the 77th birth anniversary of late Bollywood music director, RD Burman, #RDBurman was a top Indian Twitter trend on Monday. Actor Ayushmann Khurrana tweeted, "I listen to Pancham Da's songs everyday. Don't know how differently I can celebrate his bday. #RDBurman." Another user posted, "There can be only one musical god for me and that is #RDBurman."
