National award winning actress Kangana Ranaut-starrer 'Tanu Weds Manu Returns', an Anand L Rai directorial, has minted over Rs 38 crore in its opening weekend. The film, also starring R Madhavan, made a business of Rs 8.85 crore on Friday, Rs 13.20 crore on Saturday and Rs 16.10 crore on Sunday, tweeted trade analyst Taran Adarsh on Monday.
