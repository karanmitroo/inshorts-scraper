Actor Ajay Devgn took to social media to share the first picture of 'Golmaal 4' cast on director Rohit Shetty's birthday on Tuesday. While Ajay Devgn, Arshad Warsi, Tusshar Kapoor, Shreyas Talpade and Kunal Kemmu will reprise their roles, actresses Parineeti Chopra and Tabu will also feature in the film. This will be the fourth film in the 'Golmaal' franchise.
