The YouTube trailer of Satish Kaushik's forthcoming movie ‘Gang of Ghosts’ crossed 2 lakh hits on Sunday, since its launch on Feb-11. The film features Anupam Kher, Jackie Shroff, Sharman Joshi, Rajpal Yadav, Saurabh Shukla, Mahie Gill and Meera Chopra. “Good natured, cultured bhoots (ghosts) are going to be seen in theatres on Mar-21,” the 57-year-old filmmaker tweeted on Sunday.
