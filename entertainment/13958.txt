The Amitabh Bachchan, Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Vidya Balan starrer 'TE3N', which released on Friday, is "edgy, gripping and dark" wrote Hindustan Times. India Today wrote, "The strength of TE3N lies in its performances" while Times of India wrote that 'TE3N' has the "ability to maintain the suspense right till the end". It was rated 3/5 (India Today) and 3.5/5 (TOI).
