Actress Deepika Padukone has featured on the cover of Vogue India for its November issue. Deepika "plays muse" to fashion designer Sabyasachi Mukherjee, whose designs have featured in the cover story title 'Vogue presents the Benares story'. The actress has worn a green sari by the designer accessorised with an embellished belt, chunky bangles, and cuffs, for the cover shot.
