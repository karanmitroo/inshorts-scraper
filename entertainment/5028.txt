Actress Taapsee Pannu was named the Woman of the Year at the 18th edition of the IIFA Awards held in New York. Taapsee was presented the award by actress Preity Zinta. Meanwhile, actress Alia Bhatt was given the Style Icon of the Year Award for 2017. Notably, Priyanka Chopra had received the Woman of the Year award at IIFA 2016.
