Actor Rajinikanth and his daughter Aishwaryaa R Dhanush visited Yogaville or Satchidananda Ashram in Virginia, USA. "Appa n I at his guru Satchidananda's "Lotus all faiths temple" 30th anniversary Yogaville Virginia #BlessedSunday," tweeted Aishwaryaa. The 65-year-old-actor was in the US owing to medical issues but is expected to return to India before his film Kabali releases on July 22.
