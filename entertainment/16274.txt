Veteran Tamil actor Manorama, referred to as 'Aachi', passed away on Saturday aged 78 in Chennai owing to multiple organ failure. Manorama started her career with Tamil film "Malaiyitta Mangai" in 1958 and went on to act in over 1000 films in all four South Indian languages with stalwarts like MGR and Sivaji Ganesan, along with a few in Hindi.
