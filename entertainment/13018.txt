Actor Rajinikanth's daughter Aishwaryaa R Dhanush said that the 65-year-old actor's Twitter account was hacked on Tuesday night but has now been retrieved. Following the hack, the official handle reportedly followed celebrities including Kamal Haasan, Aamir Khan, Shah Rukh Khan and Kabali director Pa Ranjith but later unfollowed them all. Currently, he has over three million Twitter followers.
