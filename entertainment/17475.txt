Veteran actor Anupam Kher has slammed director Vishal Bharadwaj for dedicating national awards bagged by his film 'Haider' to Kashmiri Pandits. Terming Bharadwaj's move as 'fraudish', Kher, a Kashmiri Pandit himself, tweeted, "When was last time Vishal Bhardwaj spoke about d plight of #KashmiriPandits?" He further added, "He in fact humiliated us by doing dance of devil at our temple."
