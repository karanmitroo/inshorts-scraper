'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' director Kabir Khan was accosted by a group of angry protesters at the Karachi International Airport in Pakistan on Wednesday. The angry protesters, many of them wielding shoes, shouted 'shame shame' and directed anti-India and pro-Pakistan slogans at Khan. Notably, his 2015 film 'Phantom', starring Saif Ali Khan, was banned in Pakistan for its anti-Pakistan content.
