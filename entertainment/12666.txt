'Udan Choo', the new song from the Riteish Deshmukh and Nargis Fakhri starrer 'Banjo' has been released. The song has been composed by music director duo Vishal-Shekhar and sung by Hriday Gattani. The lyrics for the track have been penned by Amitabh Bhattacharya. Directed by Ravi Jadhav, the film is scheduled to release on September 23.
