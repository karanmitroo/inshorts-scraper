Actor Ranveer Singh took to social media to share a photo of a letter sent to him by Amitabh Bachchan, praising him for his performance in 'Padmaavat'. "Mujhe mera award mil gaya," wrote Ranveer while sharing the photo. The actor had also received a hand-written note from Bachchan in 2015 for his performance in 'Bajirao Mastani'.
