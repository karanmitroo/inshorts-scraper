Actress Alia Bhatt has featured on the cover of 'Cosmopolitan' for its September issue. She is seen wearing a pair of shorts and a shirt from Tommy Hilfiger paired with a Zara choker, Dior rings and a cuff from The Red Box. Alia has featured in the story titled, 'Alia Bhatt On Fashion, FOMO and Why She's Not a Feminist'.
