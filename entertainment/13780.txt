'Hug Me', the first song from the Sunny Leone starrer 'Beiimaan Love' has been released. Picturised on Leone, the song has been sung by Kanika Kapoor and Raghav Sachar, who has also composed the song. Directed by Rajeev Chaudhari, the erotic thriller also stars Rajneesh Duggall. The film is scheduled to release on August 5.
