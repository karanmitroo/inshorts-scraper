The music video of Sukhbir's 'Gal Ban Gayi' has been released. The video is a new rendition of the song, which originally came out in 1997. Composed by Meet Bros, the song features Urvashi Rautela and Vidyut Jammwal. It has been sung by Meet Bros, Sukhbir and Neha Kakkar, while Yo Yo Honey Singh has performed the rap.
