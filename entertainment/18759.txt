Following the success of blockbuster Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani, Deepika Padukone and Ranbir Kapoor are all set to begin shooting for director Imtiaz Ali's Tamasha in France. "I am very blessed that I can work with an actor like Deepika, because she adds so much to a film with her beauty, mind, talent and sheer presence," Ranbir said.
