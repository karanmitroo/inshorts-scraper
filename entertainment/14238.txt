Web streaming site 'Netflix' on Friday unveiled the poster and official title for the 'Gilmore Girls' reboot 'Gilmore Girls: A Year in the Life'. The new season will consist of four 90-minute episodes, each covering a different season, namely winter, spring, summer and fall. It will see Lauren Graham and Alexis Bledel reprise their roles as 'Lorelai' and 'Rory' respectively.
