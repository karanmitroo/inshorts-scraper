Actor Amitabh Bachchan on Twitter said that daughters are the best gift in the world. "Betian khaas hoti hain ... Mere liye, Samaaj ke liye, Desh ke liye," the 74-year-old actor tweeted. Further, he dedicated his Star Screen Award to daughter Shweta Bachchan Nanda. Notably, Bachchan was named Best Actor at Star Screen Awards 2016 for courtroom drama film 'Pink'.
