Filmmaker-actor Farhan Akhtar, whose directorial debut film 'Dil Chahta Hai' completed 16 years on Thursday, tweeted, "Dil Chahta Hai turns sweet 16 today...Ya toh aapka lagav gehra hai, ya film 3D thi." The film starred Aamir Khan, Saif Ali Khan and Akshaye Khanna in lead roles. It won the National Award For Best Feature Film in Hindi in 2002.
