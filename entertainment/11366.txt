Actor Aamir Khan, praising Ranbir Kapoor's performance in the recently released romantic drama film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil, has said that he's the "best actor". "Ranbir, Aishwarya and Anushka have just killed it," the 51-year-old actor tweeted, further adding that the Karan Johar directorial is a "must watch". Notably, both 'Shivaay' and 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' released on October 28.
