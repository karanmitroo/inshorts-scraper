Telugu actor Akkineni Nagarjuna, while commenting on his son Naga Chaitanya's engagement to actress Samantha Ruth Prabhu, said, "My mother is my daughter now." This was a reference to the 2014 film 'Manam', where Samantha had played the mother's role to Nagarjuna's character. Sharing pictures from the engagement ceremony, he further tweeted, "It's official now... Couldn't be happier!!"
