A new song titled 'Kaun Tujhe' from Sushant Singh Rajput's upcoming film 'MS Dhoni: The Untold Story' has been released. The song, featuring Sushant and Disha Patani, has been composed by Amaal Mallik and sung by Palak Muchhal. Manoj Muntashir has penned the lyrics. Directed by Neeraj Pandey and also starring Kiara Advani, the film will release on September 30.
