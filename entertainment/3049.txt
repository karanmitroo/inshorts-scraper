Actor Arjun Kapoor has said that he is going to be linked-up with actress Parineeti Chopra next year, as they will be working in two films together. Arjun added, "First in 'Sandeep aur Pinky Faraar' and then...we will come together in 'Namestey Canada' as well." Notably, Arjun made his acting debut opposite Parineeti in the 2012 film 'Ishaqzaade'.
