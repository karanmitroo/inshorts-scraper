Actor Sidharth Malhotra has featured on the cover of the men's magazine 'Maxim India' for its October issue along with models Carla Dennis and Ninja Singh. The 31-year-old actor is seen wearing a blue suit paired with a shirt and black shoes. The 'Special Style Issue' features Sidharth's interview in the article, 'Sidharth Malhotra Shows Us He's Got Game'.
