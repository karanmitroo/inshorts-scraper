Actor Shah Rukh Khan on Wednesday hosted dinner for Apple CEO Tim Cook, who is on his first official visit to India. The dinner, held at SRK's Mumbai residence Mannat, was attended by Amitabh Bachchan, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Jaya Bachchan, Aamir Khan, Madhuri Dixit and AR Rahman, among others. Reportedly, Cook had earlier expressed his wish to meet SRK.
