Hit cinematographer, Rohit Shetty is going to direct remake of Subash Ghai’s 1989 film ‘Ram Lakhan’, which will be produced by Karan Johar. “Here’s the big announcement! Rohit Shetty & Karan Johar, in association with Mukta Arts, present RAM LAKHAN in 2016,” Karan’s Dharma Productions tweeted Friday. The cast for the remake are yet to be decided.
