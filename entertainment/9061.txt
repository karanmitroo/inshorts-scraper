A new trailer of 'The Fate of the Furious', the upcoming eighth instalment in the 'Fast & Furious' action film franchise was released at this year's Superbowl. Vin Diesel will be seen portraying Dominic Toretto while Dwayne Johnson will play Luke Hobbs in the film. Directed by F Gary Gray, the film is scheduled to release on April 14, 2017.
