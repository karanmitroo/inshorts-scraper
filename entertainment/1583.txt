Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Vijay Varma's film 'Monsoon Shootout', which released on Friday, "fails to engage despite being backed by powerhouse talents," wrote Hindustan Times. "Monsoon Shootout rests more on style than substance," wrote Times Of India. Firstpost called it "a nicely engaging film with...morally compelling questions at its core." It was rated 2/5 (HT), 3/5 (TOI) and 2.5/5 (Firstpost).
