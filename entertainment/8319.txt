Filmmaker Farah Khan said 'main hoon na' (I'm there), if Karan Johar, who recently became father to twins Roohi and Yash via surrogacy, needs a nanny to take care of the kids. Interestingly, Farah's directorial debut film in Bollywood was titled 'Main Hoon Na'. Farah further said she is thrilled that Karan has become a parent.
