Actress Deepika Padukone has ranked tenth in the list of 'World's Highest-Paid Actresses 2016' by Forbes. With a net worth of $10 million, the 30-year-old actress is the only newcomer and the second Asian actress, after Chinese actress Fan Bingbing, in this year's top ten list.  Notably, Deepika will make her Hollywood debut in 'xXx: Return of Xander Cage'.
