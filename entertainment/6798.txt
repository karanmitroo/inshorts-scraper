Actress Fatima Sana Shaikh, known for portraying 'Geeta Phogat' in 'Dangal', will star with actor Aamir Khan in the upcoming film 'Thugs of Hindostan'. The film will also star Amitabh Bachchan. "Through a rigorous round of auditions and action workshops, we found in Fatima a truly dedicated actor and a gifted performer of action," said director Vijay Krishna Acharya.
