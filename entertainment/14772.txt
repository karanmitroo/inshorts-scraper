Leonardo DiCaprio was named the best male actor for 'The Revenant' at the 2016 MTV Movie Awards held in California on Saturday. Meanwhile, Charlize Theron received the 'Best Female Performance' award for 'Mad Max: Fury Road'. Chris Pratt won the 'Best Action Performance' award for 'Jurassic World' while Ryan Reynold's act in 'Deadpool' won him the Best 'Comedic Performance' title.
