Music composer-singer Vishal Dadlani has apologised to Jain monk Tarun Sagar at his ashram in Chandigarh. As per reports, the monk asked the composer to hold his ears and ask for forgiveness and Dadlani followed instructions, while repeating after him, "Main kaan pakadke maafi maangta hoon!" Earlier, Dadlani had called Sagar's speech in the Haryana Assembly a 'Monkery of Democracy'.
