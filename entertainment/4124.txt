According to reports, Amitabh Bachchan will star in actor Chiranjeevi's upcoming multi-lingual historic drama 'Uyyalawada Narasimha Reddy' based on the life of the popular freedom fighter from Kurnool in Andhra Pradesh. "Amitji is officially on board. He plays a pivotal role," a source was quoted as saying. The film is being directed by Surender Reddy.
