Oscar-winning actor Ian McKellen starrer "Mr. Holmes" will hit Indian theatres on July 24. Directed by Bill Condon, the Sherlock Holmes film is based on a novel "A Slight Trick of the Mind" written by Mitch Cullin in 2005. McKellen will be seen playing Sherlock Holmes in the film. The film also stars Laura Linney, Hiroyuki Sanada and Milo Parker.
