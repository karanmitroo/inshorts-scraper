Maharashtra Navnirman Sena chief Raj Thackeray has said that he did not broker a deal for the release of Karan Johar's film 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil'. "[S]ome people... started spreading lies  that I had brokered a deal... let me tell [them] that Raj Thackeray is not for sale and our stand on the issue is clean and clear," added Thackeray.
