Ranveer Singh has said his rumoured girlfriend Deepika Padukone is the best kisser. "Have you seen that song, 'Ang laga de re, mujhe rang laga de re'," he added while referring to the song, which featured a kissing scene between them, from their 2013 film 'Goliyon Ki Rasleela Ram-Leela'. Ranveer and Deepika reportedly started dating while shooting for the film.
