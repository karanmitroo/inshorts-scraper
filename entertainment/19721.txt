Oscar winner musician A.R. Rahman announced his India road tour "Rahmanishq" in October, the first such event in two-decades. The tour will encompass cities like Kolkata, Visakhapatnam, Jaipur and Ahmedabad. A concert will also be held in Sydney in August. Singers like Javed Ali, Neeti Mohan, Shweta Pandit, and Harshdeep Kaur among others, will also join Rahman during the tour.
