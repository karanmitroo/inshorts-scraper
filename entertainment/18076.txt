The first official teaser of 'Wazir', produced by Rajkumar Hirani and Vidhu Vinod Chopra, was released on Friday, along with 'PK", which is also produced by the two. The teaser incorporates drama and an undertone of violence, giving a glimpse of the film. Initially named 'Do', the film has Farhan Akhtar and Amitabh Bachchan in pivotal roles.
