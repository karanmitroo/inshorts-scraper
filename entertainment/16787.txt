Aamir Khan on Sunday watched Salman Khan-starrer 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' and said that it was the latter's best performance till date. In a series of tweets, Aamir also praised the film's story, screenplay, dialogues and writing. Calling it 'outstanding', he further praised film's director Kabir Khan and child actor Harshaali Malhotra who plays the speech-impaired Pakistani girl in the film.
