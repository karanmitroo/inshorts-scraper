The teaser of Disney's 'DuckTales', has been released, as the cartoon series is set to get a reboot in the summer of 2017. The first season, which will air on the channel 'Disney XD', will consist of 21 half-hour episodes and two one-hour specials. The cartoon will primarily feature the characters Scrooge McDuck and his grandnephews Huey, Dewey and Louie.
