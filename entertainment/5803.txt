Singer Asha Bhosle will get her wax statue at the upcoming Madame Tussauds museum in Delhi. Actor Amitabh Bachchan, singer Shreya Ghoshal, cricketer Kapil Dev, footballers David Beckham and Lionel Messi will also have wax statues at the museum. This will be India's first Madame Tussauds museum, and will be inaugurated in July.
