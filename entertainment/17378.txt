Bollywood superstar Salman Khan Tuesday took to Twitter to praise his Dabangg co-star Sonakshi Sinha for her weight loss. Salman posted a picture of a 'slimmer' Sonakshi and captioned it, "Dekh lo dubli patli sonakshi devi ko. Abb yakeen aah ya, wah! Kamaal karti ho yaar rajjo". In the picture, Sonakshi, wearing an all-black outfit, is smiling widely.
