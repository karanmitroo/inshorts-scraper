The first poster of Aishwarya Rai and Irrfan Khan starrer 'Jazbaa' was released on Saturday. "The edgy surprise you have been waiting for is here! #AishwaryaRai & @irrfan_k Khan racing against time in #Jazbaa", the official Twitter handle of the film read. Directed by Sanjay Gupta, the film marks the comeback of Aishwarya after her daughter Aaradhya's birth.
