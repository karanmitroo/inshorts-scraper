Steven Spielberg's 'The BFG', which premiered at Cannes Film Festival on Saturday, "proves hugely charming", wrote The Guardian. The Telegraph wrote that the film is "about as good as cinema can do these days". The Irish Times called it "an undeniably warm-hearted film". The film has been rated 4/5 (The Guardian), 5/5 (The Telegraph) and 3/5 (The Irish Times)
