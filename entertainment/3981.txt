Actor Rajkummar Rao, while responding to Ayushmann Khurrana's statement that he was the one who suggested Rajkummar's name for 'Bareilly Ki Barfi', said, "I wasn't aware of it, but that's very kind of him." Rajkummar added, "I hope he suggests my name for all his exciting films and I'll also do the same." He further thanked Ayushmann for recommending him.
