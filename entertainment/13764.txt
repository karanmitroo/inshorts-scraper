'Watch Your Words with Raman', a series of comic strips have been released on social media to promote the Nawazuddin Siddiqui starrer 'Raman Raghav 2.0'. The comic strips show how the film's character 'Raman', a serial killer, played by Siddiqui, would misinterpret common English phrases in various situations. Directed by Anurag Kashyap, the film releases on June 24.
