On the 42nd birthday of Bear Grylls, the British Adventurer and host of Discovery Channel's 'Man vs Wild' series, 'Bear Grylls' was the fifth most trending Google search in India on Tuesday. It was most looked up in Andhra Pradesh, West Bengal and Tamil Nadu. 'Udta Punjab', 'Chennai Weather' and 'Datsun Redi go' were some of the other trending searches.
