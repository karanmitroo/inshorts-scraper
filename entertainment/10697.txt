'Let's Break Up', a new song from the Alia Bhatt and Shah Rukh Khan starrer 'Dear Zindagi', has been released. Sung by Vishal Dadlani, the song has been composed by Amit Trivedi with lyrics by Kausar Munir. The film also stars Kunal Kapoor, Angad Bedi and Ali Zafar and has been directed by Gauri Shinde.
