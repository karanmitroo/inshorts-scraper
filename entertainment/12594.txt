Actor Ranveer Singh has shared a video of himself on Twitter playing the accordion while singing the song 'Pardesi Pardesi' from the 1996 film 'Raja Hindustani'. "Belting out a cult hit for confused tourists in Switzerland. Just because. #JustBefikre," tweeted Ranveer. The actor has been shooting in Switzerland for the upcoming film 'Befikre', which is being directed by Aditya Chopra.
