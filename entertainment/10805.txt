Filmmaker Ram Gopal Verma has announced that the Amitabh Bachchan starrer upcoming political crime thriller film 'Sarkar 3' will release on March 17, 2017. It is a sequel to the 2008 film 'Sarkar Raj' and the third film in the 'Sarkar' film series. The film also stars Manoj Bajpayee, Jackie Shroff, Yami Gautam and Ronit Roy.
