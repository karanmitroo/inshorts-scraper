Filmmaker Karan Johar has confirmed that actor Shah Rukh Khan will feature in a cameo appearance in the Ranbir Kapoor starrer 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' (ADHM). "[A]fter seven years when I directed him, I realised nothing had changed. He knew exactly what I was expecting of him and I knew exactly what he was going to do," said Johar.
