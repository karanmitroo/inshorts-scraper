Actors Jackie Chan and Sonu Sood were seen dancing to Daler Mehndi's track 'Tunak Tunak Tun' at the 19th Shanghai International Film Festival. Sood tweeted the video calling it "impromptu dance moves" while referring to Chan as his brother. The 62-year-old actor will be appearing alongside Sood and Indian actress Amyra Dastur in the Indo-Chinese production, 'Kung Fu Yoga'.
