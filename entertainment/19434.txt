Title song of 'Dhoom-3' -- 'Dhoom Machale Dhoom'  released on Thursday and the song was dedicated to Sachin Tendulkar who played his 200th Test match at the Wankhede stadium in Mumbai. The song, which features a grooving Katrina Kaif, is sung by Aditi Singh Sharma and the music is by Pritam. The film releases on Dec-20.
