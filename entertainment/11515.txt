Indian web series 'The 'Other' Love Story' has secured six nominations at the 2016 NYC Web Fest awards including 'Best Director' nomination for Roopa Rao and 'Best Actress' nominations for Spoorthi Gumaste and Shweta Gupta. Said to be India's first same-sex web series, the series is set in Bangalore in the 1990s and revolves around two female friends in love.
