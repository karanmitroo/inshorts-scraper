Actress Deepika Padukone danced to the song 'Lungi Dance' from her 2013 film 'Chennai Express', while Vin Diesel accompanied her onstage at the premiere of their film 'xXx: The Return of Xander Cage'. Both Deepika and Vin wrapped lungis over their outfits and put on sunglasses, imitating the song's look. The film will release in India on January 14.
