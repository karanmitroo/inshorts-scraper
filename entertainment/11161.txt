The look of actors Varun Dhawan and Alia Bhatt from their upcoming romantic comedy film 'Badrinath Ki Dulhania' has been unveiled. The film marks the second installment of a franchise that began with the 2014 film 'Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania'. Directed by Shashank Khaitan, the film is scheduled to release on March 10, 2017.
