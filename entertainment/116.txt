Meryl Streep is among the nominees for the Best Actress in a Leading Role category at Oscars 2018 for the film 'The Post'. The other nominees include Saoirse Ronan for 'Lady Bird' and Sally Hawkins for 'The Shape of Water'. While Frances McDormand was nominated for 'Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri', Margot Robbie has received a nomination for 'I, Tonya'.
