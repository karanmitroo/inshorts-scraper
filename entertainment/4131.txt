La La Land actress Emma Stone has topped Forbes' list of World's Highest-Paid Actresses 2017, earning $26 million during the 12-month scoring period. The 28-year-old actress was followed by Jennifer Aniston, who earned $25.5 million, and Hunger Games actress Jennifer Lawrence, who earned $24 million. Notably, the world's 10 highest-paid actresses combined for $172.5 million over the year.
