According to reports, actor Shah Rukh Khan will release the trailer of his upcoming film 'Raees' on the occasion of his 51st birthday on November 2. The action thriller also stars Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Pakistani actress Mahira Khan. Earlier, it was rumoured that the trailer would be attached to Karan Johar's 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil', which releases on October 28.
