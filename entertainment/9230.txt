The first look of the Sandra Bullock and Rihanna starrer 'Ocean's 8', an all-female spinoff of the Ocean's Trilogy, was revealed on Monday. Bullock stars as 'Debbie Ocean', who attempts to pull off the heist of the century at New York City's star-studded annual Met Gala. The cast also includes Cate Blanchett, Mindy Kaling and Anne Hathaway among others.
