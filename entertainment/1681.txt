Dairy cooperative Amul has released a new poster on the wedding of cricketer Virat Kohli and actress Anushka Sharma. The poster has been captioned, "Kohli sajaake rakhna, mehndi lagaake rakhna..." and it further reads, "Amul Vi rush for maska". While sharing the poster, Amul's official Twitter handle wrote, "#Amul Topical: Much awaited Indian cricket captain- Bollywood actress marriage!"
