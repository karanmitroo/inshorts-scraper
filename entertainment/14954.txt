The song 'Jungle Jungle Baat Chali Hai' was released on Tuesday as part of the upcoming film, 'The Jungle Book.' The song has been composed by Vishal Bhardwaj with lyrics by Gulzar, the same team responsible for the original song for Doordarshan's hit TV series in the '90s. Young singers including Jia Wadkar and Abhirup Dass have voiced the track.
