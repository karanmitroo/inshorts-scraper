'Madhubala' fame actress Drashti Dhami and her partner Salman Yusuf Khan have won the celebrity dance-reality-show 'Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa 6' and the prize-money of Rs.50-lakh; while, Lauren Gottlieb and Sonali-Sumanth were declared as the first and second runner-ups respectively. Singer Shaan was the fourth-finalist. The show's grand-finale also saw performances by Hrithik Roshan, Priyanka Chopra, Remo D'souza and Karan Johar.
