Actor Amitabh Bachchan, who paid Rs.37 crore as tax for financial year 2012-2013, was the highest tax payer in Central, the Income Tax (IT) department in Mumbai revealed. Moreover, the Bachchan family have been declared one of the highest tax payers (Rs. 53.81 cr) for the year ending March 2013. The IT department also met Mr Bachchan to congratulate him.
