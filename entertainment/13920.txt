The teaser for the upcoming suspense thriller 'Fever' was released on Saturday. Directed by Rajeev Jhaveri, the film stars Rajeev Khandelwal, Gauahar Khan and Victor Banerjee among others. 'Fever' also marks the Bollywood debut of British actress and lingerie model Gemma Atkinson. The film, which has been shot entirely in Switzerland, also stars Ankita Makwana and Italian actress Caterina Murino.
