Actor Salman Khan has a total of 9 films in the ₹100 crore club, the maximum for any Bollywood actor. Interestingly, Salman achieved this feat in the last 5 years, with 'Dabangg' (2010) being his first film to mint ₹100 crore at the Indian box-office. Salman's other films which crossed the ₹100 crore mark include 'Ready', 'Kick' and 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan'.
