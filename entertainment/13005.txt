Late Bollywood singer Kishore Kumar's song 'Pal Bhar Ke Liye', from the movie 'Johny Mera Naam', was featured in an episode of the 17th season of the American sitcom 'The Simpsons'. Titled 'Kiss Kiss, Bang Bangalore', the episode shows the sitcom's characters dancing to the song at the end. The legendary singer was born on August 4, 1929.
