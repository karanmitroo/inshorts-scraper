Filmmaker Ram Gopal Verma has reportedly begun the shooting for his bilingual movie “Killing Veerappan”. The film is the true account of the “deadliest man hunt ever” in the Asian crime history. Actor Shivarajkumar will reportedly play the role of the officer who killed Veerappan, who will be played by an aspiring actor, Sandeep. It is Verma's debut Kannada film.
