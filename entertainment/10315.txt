The trailer of the Nicolas Cage and John Cusack starrer 'Arsenal' has been released. The film revolves around Cage's character 'Eddie' kidnapping 'Mikey Lindel', played by Johnathon Schaech, and how Mikey's brother 'JP', portrayed by Adrian Grenier, takes the help of John Cusack's character 'Sal' to rescue 'Mikey'. Directed by Steven C Miller, it will release on January 6, 2017.
