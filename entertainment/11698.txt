The Irrfan Khan and Tom Hanks starrer film 'Inferno' "burns up in a raging fireball," wrote Hindustan Times. "Irrfan Khan is the saving grace of this ride through hell," said The Indian Express. Times Of India wrote that "the film is a competent adaptation and offers mild cerebral entertainment". It has been rated 2/5 (Indian Express, HT) and 3.5/5 (TOI).
