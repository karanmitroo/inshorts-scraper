The official trailer of Hema Malini's Telugu period drama film 'Gautamiputra Satakarni' has been released. Hema will be seen portraying Gautami Balashri while actor Nandamuri Balakrishna will play ruler Gautamiputra Satakarni, on whom the film is based. Also starring Shriya Saran and Kabir Bedi, the film, directed by National Award-winning Krish Jagarlamudi, will release on January 12, 2017.
