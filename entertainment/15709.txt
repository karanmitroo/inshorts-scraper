Singer and musician David Bowie passed away on Monday at the age of 69, following 18 months of suffering from cancer. The two-time Grammy Award winner was also known as his flamboyant, androgynous alter-ego 'Ziggy Stardust' and for hit singles like 'Space Oddity,' 'Let's Dance' among others. His latest album 'Black Star' was released on January 8, his 69th birthday.
