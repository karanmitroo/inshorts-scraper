Filmmaker Karan Johar, who hosts the talk show 'Koffee With Karan', has revealed that initially he was told not to do a talk show. People advised him against it while saying, "[I]t will take away the mystique as a filmmaker. It will make you sound frivolous." Johar added, "I am all about 'Koffee With Karan' the fun, the frivolousness..."
