Kangana Ranaut and Imran Khan starrer film 'Katti Batti' managed to mint only Rs 10.76 crore at the box-office till Saturday. Trade analyst Taran Adarsh tweeted, "#KattiBatti biz hardly witnesses much growth on Sat. Fri 5.28 cr, Sat 5.48 cr. Total: Rs 10.76 cr". Directed by Nikhil Advani, the film was released on September 18.
