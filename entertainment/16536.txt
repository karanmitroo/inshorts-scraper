The trailer of Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Irrfan Khan starrer “Jazbaa” has been released. The Sanjay Gupta directorial stars the duo in powerful and dramatic roles where Irrfan plays a cop and Aishwarya, a lawyer who is blackmailed into fighting the case of a rape and murder accused. The film marks Aishwarya’s comeback on the silver screen after 5 years.
