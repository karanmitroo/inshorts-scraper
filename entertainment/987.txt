A promo video for the multi-starrer film 'Welcome To New York' has been released in the form of a conversation among the film's cast. The film stars Karan Johar, Sonakshi Sinha, Diljit Dosanjh, Lara Dutta, Riteish Deshmukh and Boman Irani. Directed by Chakri Toleti, the comedy film is scheduled to release on February 23.
