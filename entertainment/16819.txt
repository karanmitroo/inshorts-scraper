Superstar Aamir Khan on Thursday took to Twitter to express his eagerness to watch Salman Khan starrer 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan', releasing on July 17. "Can’t wait to watch ‘Bajrangi Bhaijaan’!!!," Aamir tweeted. Aamir, who is one of the closest friends of Salman in Bollywood, was also among the first ones to unveil the first look of the film.
