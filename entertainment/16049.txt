Actress-model Sofia Vergara, who stars in the American TV series 'Modern Family', on Sunday got married to actor-director Joe Manganiello at the Palm Beach in Florida, USA. The wedding was attended by Channing Tatum and Vergara's 'Modern Family' co-stars including Reese Witherspoon. Vergara (43) and Manganiello (38) started dating in July 2014 after the latter praised Vergara in an interview.
