The teaser trailer of Kunal Kapoor's upcoming period drama film 'Veeram' has been released. Kunal will be seen portraying the character 'Chandu Chekaver,' a warrior from 13th century Kerala. Directed by National Award-winning filmmaker Jayaraj Nair, the film is an adaptation of William Shakespeare's play 'Macbeth'. 'Veeram' is reportedly being made in Malayalam, Hindi and English.
