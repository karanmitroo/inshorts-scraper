The poster of the Shahid Kapoor, Kangana Ranaut and Saif Ali Khan starrer 'Rangoon' was revealed on Sunday. "Presenting the most-awaited love story of 2017, #Rangoon," wrote the film's production company Nadiadwala Grandson while sharing the poster on Twitter. Directed by Vishal Bhardwaj, the film's trailer will be released on January 6.
