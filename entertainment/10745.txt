The Shah Rukh Khan and Alia Bhatt starrer film 'Dear Zindagi' has earned ₹32.5 crore in its opening weekend in India. It collected ₹8.75 crore on Friday, ₹11.25 crore on Saturday and ₹12.50 crore on Sunday, as per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. Written and directed by Gauri Shinde, the film also stars actors Kunal Kapoor and Angad Bedi.
