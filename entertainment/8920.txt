As per reports, actress Shraddha Kapoor has been cast in the Amitabh Bachchan and Aamir Khan starrer upcoming film 'Thugs of Hindostan'. According to sources, Shraddha did a look test for a role opposite Aamir recently. Based on the novel 'Confessions of a Thug' by Philip Taylor, the film is about a group of thugs in the pre-Independence era.
