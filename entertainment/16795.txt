Salman Khan and Rockline Venkatesh, producers of the film "Bajrangi Bhaijaan'', have decided to donate money from the profits of the film to farmers, BJP leader Shaina NC revealed. The BJP leader, along with the film director Kabir Khan and Salman's sister Alvira Agnihotri, met Maharashtra Revenue Minister Eknath Khadse on Wednesday and invited the politicos for watching 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan'.
