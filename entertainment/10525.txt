The trailer of Shah Rukh Khan-starrer film 'Raees', which was released on Wednesday, has garnered over 10 million views (over 1 crore) within 24 hours. Further, the trailer has also got over 2.5 lakh likes on YouTube. Set in 1980s Gujarat, the film also features Nawazuddin Siddiqui as a police officer and Pakistani actress Mahira Khan as Mohsina.
