'Finding Dory', the sequel to the 2003 animated film 'Finding Nemo', which released on Friday, is a "sweet and sentimental story... bound to leave you teary-eyed", wrote Times of India. The New Indian Express (TNIE) called the film "visually delightful, fairly entertaining" while NDTV called it a "pleasant, familiar... tale". It was rated 4/5 (TOI), 2/5 (TNIE) and 3/5 (NDTV).
