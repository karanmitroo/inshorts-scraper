Surpassing Chennai Express, Aamir Khan starrer 'Dhoom 3' has become the fastest (within only 3 days) Bollywood flick to enter the Rs 100 crore club. SRK's Chennai Express had achieved the feat in four days of its release. The Dhoom franchise had collected Rs 107.61 crore till Sunday. Notably, the movie is also the highest opening day grosser.
