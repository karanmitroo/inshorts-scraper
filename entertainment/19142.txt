The shooting of forthcoming Bollywood romantic action drama 'Roy', starring Ranbir Kapoor, Arjun Rampal and Jacqueline Fernandez, is scheduled to start on Mar-28. "We'll be shooting the 15 day spell in Mumbai. Then, there is also a 45 day international schedule," producer Bhushan Kumar said. Jacqueline will be seen in a double role in the movie; a first for her.
