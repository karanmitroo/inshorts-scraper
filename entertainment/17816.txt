Indian film-maker Prakash Nair's 'Umrika', featuring 'Life of Pi' fame actor Suraj Sharma, has won the audience award in the World Cinema Dramatic Competition section of the 2015 Sundance Film Festival. ‘Umrika’, which is Nair's second film after the critically acclaimed "Delhi in a Day”, is jointly produced by Manish Mundra and Swati Shetty.
