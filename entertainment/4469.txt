Actor Amitabh Bachchan, while commemorating late singer Kishore Kumar's 88th birth anniversary, tweeted that the legendary singer sang over 130 songs for him in 51 films. Other than being a playback singer, Kishore was also an actor, lyricist, composer, producer, director and screenwriter. He is best known for songs like 'Ek Ladki Bheegi Bhagi Si' and 'Roop Tera Mastana'.
