Bollywood actress Alia Bhatt turned 23 on Tuesday. Born to filmmaker Mahesh Bhatt and actress Soni Razdan, Alia's first on-screen appearance was as a child artist in the 1999 film, 'Sangharsh'. She marked her Bollywood debut as a lead actress with 'Student of the Year' in 2012, and is known for her roles in films including 'Highway' and '2 States'.
