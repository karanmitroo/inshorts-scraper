'Masta', a new song from the upcoming film 'Tum Bin 2' has been released. It has been sung by Vishal Dadlani and Neeti Mohan, composed by Ankit Tiwari with lyrics by Manoj Muntashir. The song has been picturised on Neha Sharma and Aditya Seal. Written and directed by Anubhav Sinha, the film is scheduled to release on November 18.
