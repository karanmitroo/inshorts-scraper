Actor Salman Khan, who has ventured into full-fledged production with films "Bajrangi Bhaijaan" and "Hero", is interested in making a Marathi film. "Of course, I'll make (Marathi film), why not? If I get a good plot and good subject", Salman said. Previously, Salman had played a cameo in his friend Riteish Deshmukh's Marathi film "Lai Bhaari".
