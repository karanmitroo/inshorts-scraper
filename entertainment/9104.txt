Actor Amitabh Bachchan shared a photograph of son Abhishek Bachchan on social media on the occasion of his 41st birthday on Sunday. In the black and white picture, a young Abhishek can be seen with sister Shweta. The picture has been captioned, "A Chelsea win..a Pizza from Daughter's favorite..a wishes at 12..and another year for Abhishek gone by ..#HappyBirthdayAB."
