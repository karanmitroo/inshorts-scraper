Actor Amitabh Bachchan took to Twitter to welcome Aadar Jain, the grandson of late actor-filmmaker Raj Kapoor, to Bollywood. "His first film... wishing you the very best Aadar... love and blessings... We are Family!!" he wrote while sharing the poster of Aadar's debut film 'Qaidi Band'. Directed by Habib Faisal, the film also stars debutante Anya Singh.
