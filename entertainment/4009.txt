Salman Khan, while sharing the trailer of Varun Dhawan's upcoming film 'Judwaa 2' on Twitter, wrote, "Judwaa 1 tweeting for Judwaa 2." 'Judwaa 2', which also stars Taapsee Pannu and Jacqueline Fernandez, is a sequel to the 1997 Salman and Karisma Kapoor starrer 'Judwaa'. Replying to Salman, Varun tweeted, "You will always be the original judwaa bhai."
