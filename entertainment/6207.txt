Kannada film director Madan and four others were arrested for allegedly kidnapping Paramesh, the owner of an advertising agency. Paramesh was paid ₹16.3 lakh for promoting Madan's film Eradu Kanasu. However, Madan asked Paramesh to return ₹8 lakh as he was unhappy with the agency's work. After kidnapping Paramesh, Madan demanded ransom from his family.
