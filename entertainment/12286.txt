The producers of 'Anna: Kisan Baburao Hazare', a biopic on social activist Anna Hazare, have announced that the film will release on October 14. The film's story starts from Hazare's childhood days and traces his journey through his 'India Against Corruption' movement which led to laws like the RTI and Lokpal legislations. The film stars Shashank Udapurkar and Tanishaa Mukerji.
