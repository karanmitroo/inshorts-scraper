Actress Scarlett Johansson has been named the highest grossing actor of 2016 by Forbes. Her films earned a total of over ₹8,000 crore ($1.2 billion) worldwide. Chris Evans and Robert Downey Jr shared the second position, with over ₹7,000 crore ($1.15 billion) each. All three of them starred in 'Captain America: Civil War', which became the top-grossing film of 2016.
