'Itni Si Baat Hai', the second song from the Emraan Hashmi starrer film 'Azhar', has been released. Featuring Emraan with Prachi Desai, the song has been composed by Pritam and sung by Arijit Singh and Antara Mitra. 'Azhar' is a biopic of former Indian cricketer Mohammed Azharuddin and also features Nargis Fakhri. It is scheduled to release on May 13.
