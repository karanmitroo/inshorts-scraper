After portraying a plethora of characters on-screen, actor Raajpal Yadav is all set to play a transgender villain in Faisal Saif's upcoming film 'Amma'.  "Excited about my role in the upcoming Faisal Saif's 'Amma' where I play the main antagonist, a transgender...," Raajpal Tweeted. Also starring Ragini Dwivedi, 'Amma' is filmed in Hindi, Kannada, Telugu, Tamil and Malayalam.
