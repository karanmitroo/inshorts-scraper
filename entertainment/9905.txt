Actress Helen Mirren, who is 71, threatened to beat up Vin Diesel if he did not give her a role in the 'Fast and Furious' film franchise. "[T]he wonderful and amazing Helen Mirren accosted me at a Golden Globes party," revealed Vin. Earlier, Mirren said she was "very nervous" as she would be the "newcomer" in 'Fast & Furious 8'.
