Actress Kangana Ranaut has featured on the February cover of the magazine L'Officiel India. Kangana is seen in a pink gown by the Italian fashion house Ermanno Scervino paired with Christian Louboutin heels and diamond earrings by the jewellery brand Arkish Jewels by Vibha Gupta. The actress features in the story 'The Unbreakable Kangana Ranaut'.
