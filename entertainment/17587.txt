Deepika Padukone is gearing up for wearing an over 20 kg armour in her next film 'Bajirao Mastani', where she stars opposite Ranveer Singh. Reportedly, she will be performing action scenes for some war sequences, sporting the armour, in the Sanjay Leela Bhansali directorial. In Ram Leela, her previous venture with the director, she had worn a 30 kg lehenga.
