Veteran actress Jaya Bachchan hosted a dinner for the female nominees and winners of Filmfare Awards 2018. The dinner was attended by actresses including Vidya Balan, Swara Bhasker, Konkona Sen Sharma, Seema Pahwa and Meher Vij. Director Ashwiny Iyer Tiwari and lyricist Kausar Munir were also spotted at the dinner party.
