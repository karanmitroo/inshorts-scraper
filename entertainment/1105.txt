Filmmaker Anurag Kashyap's 'Mukkabaaz', which released on Friday, "scores thanks to the performances, dialogues [and] action," wrote Bollywood Hungama. The film is a "total knockout," said The Times of India (TOI). Koimoi wrote that the film "ends up testing patience at some junctures." It has been rated 2.5/5 (Bollywood Hungama), 3.5/5 (Koimoi) and 4/5 (TOI).
