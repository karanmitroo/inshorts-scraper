Priyanka Chopra co-presented the Emmy Award for 'Outstanding Variety Talk Series' with 'Black-ish' actor Anthony Anderson this year. Discussing the event, Priyanka later wrote on Instagram, "What a night it's been at the Emmys...Congratulations to all the winners, thank you for revolutionising the world of Television." Notably, her US TV series 'Quantico' received no Emmy nominations this year.
