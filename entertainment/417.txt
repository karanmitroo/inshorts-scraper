Filmmaker Mohit Suri has denied reports that he is making 'Ek Villain 2', the sequel to the 2014 film 'Ek Villain', with Sidharth Malhotra and Kriti Sanon as the lead actors. "Ek Villain is a Balaji Telefilms property and they haven't approached me yet," he added. Mohit further clarified he's working on a thriller which will feature Aditya Roy Kapur.
