Shahid Kapoor and Alia Bhatt starrer 'Shaandaar' which released on Dussehra (Thursday) has managed to earn ₹20.65 crore within first two days of its release, according to Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh. The Vikas Bahl directorial, which had relatively poor critic reviews, earned ₹13.10 crore on Thursday which dropped to ₹7.55 crore on Friday.
