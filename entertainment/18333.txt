Shah Rukh-Deepika starrer "Happy New Year" is set for the biggest Bollywood release worldwide on Oct 24. According to sources, it will be released in 5,000 screens in India and 1,000 abroad. This will surpass releases of films such as Dhoom 3 (2013), Kick (2014) and Bang Bang (2014), all of which which were released in 4,500 screens worldwide.
