The 2012 brawl case against actor Saif Ali Khan has reportedly been referred to a mediation centre. The case was filed by businessman Iqbal Mir Sharma, accusing Khan and his friends of assaulting him at Taj hotel. Khan was with Kareena Kapoor, Karisma Kapoor, Malaika Arora Khan and other friends then, and had claimed Sharma to have abused the group.
