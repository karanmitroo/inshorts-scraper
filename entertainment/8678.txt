The first look poster of Kalki Koechlin and Rajat Kapoor starrer 'Mantra' has been revealed. 'Mantra' is set in 2004, in the midst of a nationwide 'India Shining' campaign. Directed by Nicholas Kharkongor and also starring Adil Hussain, Shiv Pandit, Lushin Dubey and Rohan Joshi, the film is scheduled to release on March 17.
