Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal on Monday met 2018 Under-19 World Cup final centurion Manjot Kalra and congratulated him on the World Cup victory. The Chief Minister also assured the Delhi-born 19-year-old of any assistance that he would ever need in the future from the government. Kalra was India's third-highest run-getter in the Under-19 World Cup with 252 runs.
