Cricketer S Sreesanth will star in the upcoming thriller film 'Aksar 2', which is a sequel to the 2006 film 'Aksar'. "I am so glad to be part of Aksar 2 and I'm very confident it will be appreciated by the audience," said Sreesanth. Directed by Ananth Narayan Mahadevan, the film stars Gautam Rode and Zareen Khan in lead roles.
