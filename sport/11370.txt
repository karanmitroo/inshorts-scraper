India's top division I-League side Bengaluru FC, which was founded in 2013, became only the third Indian football club to reach the semifinal stage of the Asian Football Confederation Cup. The Bengaluru-based club defeated Singapore's Tampines Rovers 1-0 on aggregate to reach the semi-finals. They will now face defending champions Johor Darul Ta'zim of Malaysia in the AFC Cup semi-finals.
