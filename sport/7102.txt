Saurashtra batsman Sheldon Jackson was dismissed for obstructing the field during the Vijay Hazare Trophy match against Chhattisgarh on Friday. Jackson is now the second Indian after Mohinder Amarnath to be dismissed in this fashion in List A cricket, which consists of 40 to 60 over games. Yusuf Pathan is the only other Indian given out for obstructing the field.
