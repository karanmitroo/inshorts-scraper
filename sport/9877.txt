Indian Test captain Virat Kohli averages 84.46 across all three formats in 36 innings this year, which is the highest for any player. He has scored 6 centuries and 11 fifties in 2016, including a career-best Test score of 211. He averages 106.83 in 15 T20I innings, 68 in 9 Test innings and 92.37 in 10 ODI innings.
