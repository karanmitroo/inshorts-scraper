Celebrity hairstylist Sapna Bhavnani shared a throwback video on Instagram in which ex-Indian captain MS Dhoni can be seen dancing to 'Jhak Maar Ke' song from actor John Abraham's film Desi Boyz. In the video shot in Abraham's vanity van, Dhoni's wife Sakshi can be seen laughing uncontrollably. "This is the cutest thing you will see all decade!" Bhavnani wrote.
