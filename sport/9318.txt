The commentators of the India-England series re-enacted their playing days when Sunil Gavaskar and Michael Atherton came out to bat and were bowled to by L Sivaramakrishnan. Gavaskar, however, was run out on Sanjay Manjrekar's throw which was collected by Deep Dasgupta and further directed towards stumps in a fashion similar to what Indian wicketkeeper MS Dhoni does.
