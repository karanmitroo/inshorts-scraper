Shuttler PV Sindhu, who became the first Indian to win Korea Open on Sunday, thanked Virender Sehwag for his wishes. "Sir Bowled by your yorker!! but very inspiring indeed. Will remember always to excell in my career!", tweeted Sindhu. Sehwag had congratulated Sindhu, tweeting, "At 22...Sindhu is a legend. What a player! Congratulations on this stunning finals win.Most breathtaking badminton."
