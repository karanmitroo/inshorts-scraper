Indian cricketer Yuvraj Singh and actress Hazel Keech, who are set to tie the knot on November 30th, will have two wedding ceremonies. The November 30 ceremony will be held as per Sikh tradition at a Gurudwara in Chandigarh, while the second ceremony will be hosted by Hazel's family on December 2 as per Bihari-Hindu tradition in North Goa.
