Former Indian wicketkeeper-batsman Farokh Engineer's India national cap and the Rest of the World blazer were sold for  £680 (₹57,000) and £700 (₹59,000) respectively at an auction in London. The cap and the blazer were reportedly owned by an England cricketer, who was presented the items by Engineer. Engineer played 46 Tests and 5 ODIs for India from 1961 to1975.
