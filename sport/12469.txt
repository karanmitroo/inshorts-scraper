Icelandic actor Hafþór Björnsson, who portrays 'The Mountain' on the American TV show Game of Thrones (GoT), lost his European strongman title to England's Laurence ‘Big Loz’ Shahlaei. The two-time European strongman title winner failed to win his third straight title at the competition, which includes deadlifting 420 kilograms and a running a 20-metre course while carrying a 450-kilogram​ weight.
