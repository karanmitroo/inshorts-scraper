India's women's cricket team reached the final of the Asia Cup T-20 2016 after defeating Sri Lanka by 52 runs in the semifinal on Thursday. Batting first, Mithali Raj's 62(59) helped India reach 121/4. In reply, Sri Lanka could only reach 69/9, after Preeti Bose and Ekta Bisht picked three wickets each. The final will take place on December 4.
