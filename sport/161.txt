Indian captain Virat Kohli and wicket-keeper Mahendra Singh Dhoni have been rested for the upcoming Nidahas Trophy T20I tri-series featuring Sri Lanka and Bangladesh. In Kohli's absence, Rohit Sharma will lead the side while Shikhar Dhawan has been named the vice-captain. Wicket-keepers Dinesh Karthik and Rishabh Pant have also been included in the squad.
