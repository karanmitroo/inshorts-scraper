World number two Andy Murray won his second Wimbledon men’s singles title on Sunday, defeating Canadian Milos Raonic 6-4, 7-6, 7-6. This was Murray’s third Wimbledon final, with his first Championship victory coming in 2013 against Novak Djokovic. Since the 2013 Wimbledon final, Murray has played three and lost all Grand Slam finals, coincidentally to Djokovic, until this win.
