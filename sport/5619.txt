The "Sachin" anthem from Sachin Tendulkar's upcoming biopic "Sachin: A Billion Dreams" was launched on Tuesday. While AR Rahman has composed the music for the film, the anthem is sung by Sukhwinder Singh with lyrics by Irshad Kamil, and features the "Sachin Sachin" chant. The first song from the movie, "Hind Mere Jind" was launched earlier on the cricketer's birthday.
