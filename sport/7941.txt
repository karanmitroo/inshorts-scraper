Expressing shock at the sudden demise of Under-19 team trainer Rajesh Sawant, BCCI CEO Rahul Johri said, "It's a very unfortunate development...Today when the boys left for practice, after reaching the stadium, it was found...Rajesh was not with team." The 40-year-old Sawant was found unconscious in his hotel room and was rushed to Bombay Hospital, where he was declared dead.
