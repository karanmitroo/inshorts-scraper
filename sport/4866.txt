Indian shuttler Kidambi Srikanth today defeated Japan's Kazumasa Sakai 21-11, 21-19, to become the first Indian man to win the Indonesia Superseries Premier title. The 24-year-old shuttler also became the first Indian man to win Superseries Premier, Superseries and Grand Prix Gold titles. With the win, Srikanth has become the second-most successful Indian after Saina Nehwal in Superseries events.
