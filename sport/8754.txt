Former Indian cricketer Sachin Tendulkar's biopic 'Sachin: A Billion Dreams' will reportedly release on April 14, 2017, a year after its official teaser was unveiled. The movie will be Sachin's debut on the big screen and it also includes his son Arjun Tendulkar and Mayuresh Pem in the lead cast. The biopic is directed by Emmy-nominated director James Erskine.
