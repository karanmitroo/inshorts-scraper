Royal Challengers Bangalore defeated Delhi Daredevils by 10 runs on Sunday. Batting first, Bangalore scored 161/6, helped by Virat Kohli's 58(45). Delhi fell short of the target despite Rishabh Pant's 45(34) as Pawan Negi and Harshal Patel picked up three wickets each for Bangalore. This is the first time Bangalore have finished at the bottom of IPL points table.
