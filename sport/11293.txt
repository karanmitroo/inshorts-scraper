Indian table tennis player Sathiyan Gnanasekaran won India's first international men's singles table tennis title in half a decade, after securing the Belgian Open on Saturday. The 24-year-old paddler defeated Cedric Nuytinck 15-13, 11-6, 11-2, 17-15, to win his maiden men's singles title. With the win, Sathiyan could enter the world top 125 for the first time in his career.
