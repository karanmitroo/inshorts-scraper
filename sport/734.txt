Rajasthan Royals began their spending on the second day of the IPL auction with a ₹6.2-crore deal for Karnataka spinner Gowtham Krishnappa, sold for 31 times his base price of ₹20 lakh. Murugan Ashwin, with a base price of ₹20 lakh, was bought by Royal Challengers Bangalore for ₹2.2 crore. Rahul Chahar was bought for ₹1.9 crore by Mumbai Indians.
