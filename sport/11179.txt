All-rounder Ravichandran Ashwin has become the fastest Indian to complete the double of 200 wickets and 1,500 runs in Tests. Ashwin, who took 38 Tests to achieve the feat, surpassed England's Ian Botham, who completed the double in 41 Tests. Ashwin reached the 1,500-run mark during the second Test against New Zealand. while he had completed 200 wickets earlier.
