Zafar Ansari, a Cambridge University first-class degree holder in both Politics and Sociology and a concert pianist, is in England's playing eleven for the first Test against India. The 24-year-old all-rounder, who made his Test debut against Bangladesh in October, is widely considered to be England's 'smartest-ever cricketer'. Zafar's the first Cambridge alumnus to play for England in 13 years.
