Indian all-rounder and ICC Cricketer of 2016, Ravichandran Ashwin on Saturday pledged to donate his eyes after signing up with Chennai's Rotary Rajan Eye Bank. Sharing the news, Ashwin tweeted, "This eye donation has been on @prithinarayanan dream list for a long time now. #letscontribute." Ashwin further said that every responsible citizen must contribute to this noble cause.
