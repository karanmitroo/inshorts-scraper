Pakistan defeated Ireland by 255 runs in Dublin on Thursday, registering their largest ever ODI victory. They posted a target of 338 riding on Sharjeel Khan's 152(86), who now has the fastest century by a Pakistani player not named Afridi, in 61 balls. Meanwhile, Imad Wasim's 5/14 became the best figures by a Pakistani left-arm bowler, surpassing Wasim Akram's 5/15.
