Former cricketer Virender Sehwag posted a video on Twitter on Tuesday, extending his support to PM Modi's #Sandesh2Soldiers campaign. In the video, Sehwag says, "Our fauj is the reason for our mauj. Kisi Bhi Fauji Ko Dekho,Toh Ek Salute Thoko." PM Narendra Modi started the campaign to encourage people to send videos with Diwali wishes for India's defence personnel.
