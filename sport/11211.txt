Spanish Champions Barcelona, who were playing without Argentinian forward Lionel Messi, came back from a goal down to win 2-1 against German side Borussia Mönchengladbach in the Champions League group stage on Wednesday. Borussia's Thorgan Hazard opened the scoring in the first-half, with Arda Turan and Gerard Piqué scoring in the second-half to give the Catalan giants all three points.
