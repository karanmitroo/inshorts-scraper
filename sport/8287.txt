Indian tennis players Sania Mirza and Rohan Bopanna won their respective women's and men's doubles first round matches in straight sets at the Australian Open on Wednesday. While Sania, partnering with Barbora Strycova, defeated the British duo of Jocelyn Rae and Anna Smith 6-3, 6-1, Bopanna and his partner Pablo Cuevas defeated Thomaz Bellucci and Maximo Gonzalez 6-4, 7-6.
