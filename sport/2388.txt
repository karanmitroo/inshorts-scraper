Former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag quoted a dialogue from Mithun Chakraborty-starrer Gunda, asking users to share their favourite dialogues from the film. "Roti hoti hai khaane ke liye, Boti hoti hai chabaane ke liye. #Gunda, kya dialogue baaji. Aapka favourite Gunda dialogue batao," tweeted Sehwag. The post fetched over 1,000 replies on Twitter.
