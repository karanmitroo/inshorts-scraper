Rising Pune Supergiant player MS Dhoni became the second wicketkeeper to complete 100 dismissals in the IPL, reaching the landmark against Delhi Daredevils on Friday. Dhoni's 100th victim was Shreyas Iyer, with the former taking his catch off Jaydev Unadkat's bowling. Dinesh Karthik was the first wicketkeeper to effect over 100 dismissals in IPL, reaching the landmark last month.
