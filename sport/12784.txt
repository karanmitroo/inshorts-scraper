Manchester City have signed Spanish winger Nolito from Celta Vigo on a four-year contract for a reported fee of £14 million. The Spaniard played for Barcelona, Benfica and Granada before moving to Celta Vigo, where he scored 39 goals in 103 matches. The 29-year-old became City manager Pep Guardiola's third signing of the summer, after İlkay Gündoğan and Aaron Mooy.
