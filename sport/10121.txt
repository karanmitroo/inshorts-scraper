Centuries by Cheteshwar Pujara and Murali Vijay took India to 319/4 on the third day of the first Test against England at Rajkot, reducing England's lead to 218 runs. The two put up a 209-run second wicket stand, with Vijay scoring 126 (301) and Pujara making 124 (206). The match has now seen five individual centuries in three days.
