The Committee of Administrators in its fifth status report to the Supreme Court revealed that the BCCI spent ₹4.62 crore on its office-bearers over the last two years. Out of the ₹4.62 crore, ₹3.27 crore was spent on treasurer Anirudh Chaudhry and acting secretary Amitabh Choudhary. Ousted BCCI President Anurag Thakur’s total expenditure was ₹24 lakh during the same period.
