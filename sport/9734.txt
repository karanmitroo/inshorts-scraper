Former Chelsea and Barcelona footballer Samuel Eto'o could get 10 years six months in jail, besides a fine of £12.11 million (over ₹103 crore) for alleged tax evasion. Eto'o defaulted on tax payments amounting to £3.21 million (around ₹27 crore) on four occasions between 2006 and 2009. Earlier, the player said he was not directly involved in his tax matters.
