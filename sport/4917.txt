Cricketers Shikhar Dhawan, Ajinkya Rahane and Rohit Sharma had a combined family lunch while on their way to London for the ICC Champions Trophy final against Pakistan on Sunday. Shikhar posted a picture on Instagram, captioned, "Enjoyed having lunch together at stop over before we reach london .@rohitsharma45 @ajinkyarahane." The picture showed the players and their wives, and Dhawan's son.
