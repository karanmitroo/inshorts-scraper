America's Bethanie Mattek-Sands and Czech Republic's Lucie Safarova claimed their fourth Grand Slam title after winning the Australian Open women's doubles final on Friday. The duo celebrated the victory by presenting a dance performance on the court at the Rod Laver Arena. The second-seeded pair beat the 12th-seeded pairing of Andrea Hlavackova and Peng Shuai 6-7 (4/7), 6-3, 6-3.
