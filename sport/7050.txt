Pakistani cricketer, Kamran Akmal was named the best batsman, the best wicketkeeper and the best player of the Pakistan Super League 2017. Akmal was PSL's top scorer with 353 runs in 11 innings, with a highest score of 104, best for any batsman in the tournament. Akmal, with 12 dismissals, was also the wicketkeeper with most dismissals in PSL 2017.
