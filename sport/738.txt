All the eight players bought by CSK on the first day of the IPL auction on Saturday are aged above 30. Imran Tahir, aged 38, is the oldest, followed by Harbhajan Singh (37) and Shane Watson (36). The three players are followed by Dwayne Bravo (34), Faf du Plessis (33), Ambati Rayudu (32), Kedar Jadhav (32) and Karn Sharma (30).
