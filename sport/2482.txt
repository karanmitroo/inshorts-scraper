Virat Kohli slammed his 31st hundred in his 200th ODI on Sunday, 13 more than what Sachin Tendulkar had hit in his first 200 ODIs. Sachin had scored 7,305 runs in his first 200 ODIs, averaging 41.74, while Virat has scored 8,888 runs at 55.55. Kohli has the most runs, most hundreds and best average in ODIs after 200 matches.
