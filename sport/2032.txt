World number two Roger Federer took his prize money to $110,235,682 (nearly ₹720 crore) after beating Alexander Zverev in the ATP Finals on Tuesday, becoming the top prize money-earning athlete. Federer overtook American golfer Tiger Woods, who has earned $110,061,012 (over ₹717 crore) in prize money. Federer has won 95 career titles, including a men's record 19 Grand Slam titles.
