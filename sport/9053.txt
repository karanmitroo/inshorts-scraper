Actor Aamir Khan and his co-stars from 'Dangal' Fatima Sana Shaikh and Sanya Malhotra felicitated the winner of the Lokmat Chi Dangal Kushti in the women's category on Monday in Kolhapur. "I have wonderful memories associated with this city since 14 years of age when I came to Kolhapur for a Tennis Competition and won it too," said Aamir.
