Former Indian cricketer Rahul Dravid will reportedly be paid ₹5 crore annually by BCCI after getting an extension as coach of the India A and U-19 teams. BCCI on Friday announced a two-year contract for Dravid, who stepped down as mentor of IPL franchise Delhi Daredevils. Dravid was earlier paid ₹2.62 crore for a 10-month stint with both the teams.
