Former South Africa captain Graeme Smith is set to make his coaching debut in the South Africa T20 Global League tournament, taking charge as head coach of Benoni Zalmi. Benoni Zalmi's owner, Pakistan's Javed Afridi, who also owns Pakistan Super League (PSL) team Peshawar Zalmi, made the official announcement on Monday. SA's Quinton de Kock is Benoni Zalmi's marquee player.
