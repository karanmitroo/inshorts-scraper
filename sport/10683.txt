Virender Sehwag, who turned 38 on Thursday, scored a century in each of India's three highest ODI scores. His highest individual ODI score of 219 came against WI in 2011, when India posted 418, their highest-ever ODI total. India's second and third best ODI total of 414 in 2009 and 413 in 2007 saw Sehwag score 146 and 114 respectively.
