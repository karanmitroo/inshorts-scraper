India's Shubman Gill, currently playing in the Under-19 Cricket World Cup, has an average of 101.60, the highest in any international format for any batsman with over 1,000 runs. Gill, who currently averages better than Australian legend Don Bradman, also joins him as the joint second-fastest to reach 1,000 international runs. Gill completed 1,000 international runs in his 13th innings.
