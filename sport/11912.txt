Former Pakistani cricketer Rameez Raja has picked his all-time XI team, featuring three Indians – namely Sunil Gavaskar, Virender Sehwag and Sachin Tendulkar. All-rounder Imran Khan, who is also the squad's captain, is the only representative from Pakistan, while West Indies has the most representation with four players. The wicketkeeper-batsman​ in the side is Australia's Adam Gilchrist.
