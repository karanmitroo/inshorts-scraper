Following the death of national level wrestler Vishal Kumar Verma due to electrocution in Ranchi's Jaipal Singh Stadium, former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag expressed his anger over the 'pathetic state' of the stadium. "Another Negligence.We have lost a National Wrestler #VishalKumarVerma ,due to electrocution at this water logged stadium in Ranchi.Pathetic!", Sehwag tweeted.
