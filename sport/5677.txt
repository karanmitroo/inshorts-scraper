Sunrisers Hyderabad captain David Warner became the first player to score 500-plus runs in four consecutive IPL seasons, going past the landmark against Rising Pune Supergiant on Saturday. Warner, IPL 2015's highest run-getter, is presently the leading run-scorer this season, scoring 529 runs in 11 matches. Virat Kohli is the only other player to score 500-plus runs in four seasons.
