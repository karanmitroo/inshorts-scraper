Britain's Andy Murray is just one win away from the world number one ranking after Novak Djokovic was defeated 6-4, 7-6(7-2) by Marin Cilic in the quarter-finals on Friday at Paris Masters. Murray cruised to the semi-finals after defeating Tomas Berdych 7-6(11-9), 7-5. A win in the semi-finals will see Murray claim the top spot for the very first time.
