Former wrestler and coach Sukhchain Singh Cheema, who won bronze in the 1974 Asian Games, passed away in a road accident near Patiala on Wednesday. The 67-year-old died on the spot when his car collided with another while he was returning home from his farm. Sukhchain, a Dronacharya awardee, was the son of Rustum-e-Hind Olympian wrestler Kesar Singh Cheema.
