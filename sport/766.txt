Indian fast bowler Ishant Sharma, who had a base price of ₹75 lakh as compared to ₹2 crore last year, went unsold for the second consecutive year in the IPL auction on Saturday. Meanwhile, pacer Umesh Yadav was sold to Royal Challengers Bangalore for ₹4.2 crore while Mohammad Shami was retained by Delhi Daredevils for ₹3 crore through RTM.
