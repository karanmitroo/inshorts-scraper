Cricketer Harbhajan Singh tweeted a picture with wrestlers Geeta and Babita Phogat, calling them the 'Asli Dangal' girls. "With the #AsliDangal girls- the #PhogatSisters, at #PWL2! We're proud of you. You are rockstars," wrote Harbhajan. The cricketer is the co-owner of the Pro Wrestling League team Mumbai Maharathi, which defeated UP Dangal in Delhi on Saturday.
