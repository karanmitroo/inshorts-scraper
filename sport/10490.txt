World number eight Dominika Cibulkova defeated ninth-ranked Svetlana Kuznetsova 1-6, 7-6, 6-4 to reach the final at her debut WTA Finals in Singapore. The Slovak will face the winner of the clash between Angelique Kerber and Agnieszka Radwanska in Sunday's final. "This is my first time here and it's one of my dreams come true, just incredible," said Cibulkova.
