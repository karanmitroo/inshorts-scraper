Former New Zealand captain, Brendon McCullum, who is T20 international cricket's highest run scorer, has been sold to Royal Challengers Bangalore for ₹3.6 crore. David Miller and Aaron Finch were sold to Kings XI Punjab for  ₹3 crore and ₹6.2 crore respectively. English batsman Jason Roy was sold to Delhi Daredevils for ₹1.5 crore.
