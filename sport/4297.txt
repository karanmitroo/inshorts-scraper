Veteran tennis players including Mansour Bahrami, Michael Llodra, Lleyton Hewitt and Mark Philippoussis played a 61-shot rally in a Wimbledon exhibition match on Sunday. Bahrami, who is known for his trick shots, played three tweeners from behind the baseline. Earlier, a member of the Wimbledon audience wore a skirt and faced a service from former Wimbledon champion Kim Clijsters.
