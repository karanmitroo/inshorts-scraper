Bengaluru-based water tanker driver G Balakrishna was crowned Mr Asia 2016 at the fifth Phil-Asia bodybuilding championships in the Philippines. The 25-year-old, known as the 'Arnold Schwarzenegger of Whitefield,' had previously won the 2014 Mr Universe title at the world championship in Greece. Balakrishna started the water tanker business in 2010 to supplement his income as a gym instructor.
