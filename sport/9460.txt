India's women's doubles pair of Ashwini Ponnappa and N Sikki Reddy finished as runners-up at the $17,500 Welsh International Challenge badminton tournament. The duo lost 21-16, 21-11 to the top-seeded Russian pair of Olga Morozova and Anastasia Chervyakov in the final that lasted 37 minutes. This was the pair's second tournament together following Ponnappa and Jwala Gutta split.
