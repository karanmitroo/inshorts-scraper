The teaser poster of the upcoming biopic on former Indian hockey captain Sandeep Singh, starring actor-singer Diljit Dosanjh, has been unveiled. Sharing the look on his social media, Diljit wrote, "A winner wins the game, here's one who will win all your hearts!" Directed by Shaad Ali, the film also stars Taapsee Pannu and Angad Bedi in lead roles.
