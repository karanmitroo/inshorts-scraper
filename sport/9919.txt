Indian top order batsman Cheteshwar Pujara reached his third hundred in consecutive Tests with a six during the second Test's opening day in Visakhapatnam on Thursday. Facing England's Adil Rashid while on 99, Pujara pulled a short-pitched delivery towards the deep mid-wicket boundary for a six. Notably, Pujara and Virat Kohli both scored tons and posted a 226-run partnership.
