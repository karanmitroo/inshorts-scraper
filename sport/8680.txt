Mumbai teenager Pranav Dhanawade became the first cricketer to score 1,000 runs in an innings on January 5, 2016. Playing for KC Gandhi School in an inter-school cricket tournament, the 15-year-old scored 1,009*(327) runs and helped his side register a total of 1465/3. Dhanawade's school won the match by an innings and 1,382 runs.
