Forty-year-old golfer Tiger Woods is the youngest to be featured in the Forbes 20 wealthiest American celebrities list, being ranked seventh with an estimated net worth of $740 million (₹5,041 crore). Former NBA player Michael Jordan is fourth on the list, with a worth of $1.2 billion (₹8,174 crore). The list is led by filmmaker George Lucas, worth $4.6 billion.
