Indian cricketer Rohit Sharma trolled teammates Virat Kohli, Shikhar Dhawan, Hardik Pandya and KL Rahul over their picture with wrestler The Great Khali. The players met Khali in Sri Lanka, posting pictures with the wrestler. Rohit uploaded a picture of the customised WWE belt given to Mumbai Indians by Triple H, posting, "Well tried fellas, but the belt is here."
