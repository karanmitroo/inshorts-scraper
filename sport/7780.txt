Twenty-two-year-old Indian tennis player Ramkumar Ramanathan defeated New Zealand's Finn Tearney 7-5, 6-1, 6-0, in their Davis Cup Asia/Oceania Group 1 match on Sunday. The win sealed India's place in the Asia/Oceania Group 1 second round match against Uzbekistan. India's next Davis Cup round will be played from April 7 to April 9.
