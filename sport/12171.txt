Four South African cricketers, including former international wicketkeeper Thami Tsolekile, have been banned by Cricket South Africa, for attempting to fix matches in the 2015 domestic T20 league. While Tsolekile received a 12-year ban, domestic players Pumelela Matshikwe, Ethy Mbhalati and Jean Symes have been given bans ranging from seven to 12 years, by the Board.
