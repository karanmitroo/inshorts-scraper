In an apparent reference to the appointment of VK Sasikala as his home state Tamil Nadu's CM, Ravichandran Ashwin tweeted, "To all the youngsters in TN, 234 job opportunities to open up shortly," pointing to 234 assembly seats in the state. "Guys please cool it down, it is a job creation drive.Nothing to do with Politics. #howmuchtwisting," Ashwin later posted.
