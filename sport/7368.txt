Kings XI Punjab beat Pune and Kolkata to grab left-arm medium pacer T Natarajan for ₹3 crore, starting from a base price of ₹10 lakh, in the IPL auction on Monday. The 25-year-old bowler had an economy rate of 7.33, in addition to a four-wicket haul, in the Tamil Nadu Premier League last year. Natarajan is considered a death-overs specialist.
