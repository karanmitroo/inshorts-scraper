Swiss tennis player Stanislas Wawrinka has become the oldest player to reach the French Open men's singles' final in 44 years. The 32-year-old defeated world number one Andy Murray 6-7, 6-3, 5-7, 7-6, 6-1 in the semi-final on Friday. Wawrinka will play the winner of the semi-final between nine-time French Open winner Rafael Nadal and Dominic Thiem in Sunday's final.
