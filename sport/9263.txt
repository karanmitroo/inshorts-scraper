Yuvraj Singh, before venturing into cricket, had acted in Punjabi movies as a child artist. He performed cameos in two Punjabi movies, Putt Sardaran De and Mehndi Shagna Di, in 1992. Yuvraj Singh, who is celebrating his 35th birthday on December 12, 2016, won the Player of the Tournament award in India's 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup win.
