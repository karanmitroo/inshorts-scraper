Yuvraj Singh slammed his joint second-slowest fifty in T20s after reaching the half-century mark in 40 balls for Punjab in a domestic T20 match against Delhi on Tuesday. Yuvraj had earlier hit 50 off 40 balls for Delhi Daredevils against Mumbai Indians in 2015. Yuvraj's slowest T20 fifty came off 41 balls while playing for Punjab against Kerala in 2016.
