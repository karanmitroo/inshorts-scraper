Sri Lanka ended the day three of the Colombo Test at 209/2 after being asked to follow on, trailing India by 230 runs. India dismissed Sri Lanka for 183 in the first innings, helped by Ravichandran Ashwin's 26th five-wicket haul. Ravindra Jadeja, who took two wickets in the first innings, became the fastest left-arm spinner to take 150 Test wickets.
