Cricket legend Sachin Tendulkar's son Arjun picked up a five-wicket haul, his second in three matches, to help Mumbai beat Railways in the Cooch Behar Under-19 Trophy. Left-arm pacer Arjun ended with figures of 11-1-44-5 in the second innings and helped Mumbai bowl out Railways for 136. Arjun has taken 16 wickets in four Cooch Behar matches this year.
