Rio Olympic silver medallist PV Sindhu returned to competitive badminton with a 21-14, 21-19 first round win at the Denmark against China's He Bingjiao on Wednesday. With the win, Sindhu improved her head-to-head record against Bingjiao to 2-3. The 21-year-old, who was last year's finalist at the event, will next take on world rank 12 Sayaka Sato of Japan.
