Uncapped 27-year-old Indian batsman Mayank Agarwal has broken Sachin Tendulkar's 15-year-old record of most runs by an Indian in a List A tournament. Agarwal slammed 723 runs in eight matches in the Vijay Hazare Trophy, surpassing Tendulkar who slammed 673 runs in the 2003 World Cup. Agarwal is the only player to slam 2,000+ runs in an Indian domestic season.
