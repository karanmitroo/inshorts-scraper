Former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag slammed Shobha De after Sakshi Malik's bronze medal at the Rio Olympics tweeting, "Bilkul , isliye Shobha Na De aise kaam nahi karne chahiye". Bollywood actor Amitabh Bachchan replied to the tweet saying, "hahaha .. Viru ji .. aapka sense of humour ne ek aur sixer maar diya Stadium ke bahar !!".
