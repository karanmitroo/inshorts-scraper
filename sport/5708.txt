South African Rio Olympics gold medal winning runner Caster Semenya has called former Indian captain MS Dhoni's story inspiring. "@msdhoni i have just watched your film. Inspiring story it is.. #msdhoniuntoldstory... Salute..," Semenya wrote in her Twitter post. Semenya had won gold in 800-metre at Rio and also a silver medal in the 800-metre sprint at the 2012 London Olympics.
