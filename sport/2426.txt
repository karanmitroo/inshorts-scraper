India registered a six-wicket win over New Zealand in the 100th ODI between the two sides in Pune on Wednesday, levelling the three-match series 1-1. Bhuvneshwar Kumar registered his ODI career's sixth three-wicket haul to help India restrict New Zealand to 230/9, while Dhawan slammed his 22nd ODI fifty. The series decider will take place in Kanpur on October 29.
