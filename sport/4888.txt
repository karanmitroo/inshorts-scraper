The All India Gaming Federation revealed that approximately ₹2,000 crore will be bet on the India-Pakistan Champions Trophy final on Sunday. India is touted to be a favourite, with a bet of ₹100 yielding ₹147, while betting ₹100 on Pakistan will yield ₹300. Additionally, AIGF CEO  Roland Landers has said approximately ₹2 lakh crore is bet on India matches yearly.
