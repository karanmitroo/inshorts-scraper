Ravichandran Ashwin became the fastest bowler to claim 250 wickets in Tests, after dismissing Mushfiqur Rahim on day four of the Hyderabad Test against Bangladesh on Sunday. Ashwin achieved the feat in his 45th Test, going past former Australian bowler Dennis Lillee, who took 48 Tests. Ashwin is the sixth Indian bowler and 40th overall to reach the milestone.
