Indian pacer Bhuvneshwar Kumar bowled a sharp inswinger that uprooted Mehedi Hasan's leg stump on the fourth day of the one-off Test against Bangladesh on Sunday. Bhuvneshwar picked up the wicket of Hasan in the first over of the day. Hasan had scored 51 and had built an 87-run partnership with Mushfiqur Rahim for the seventh wicket.
