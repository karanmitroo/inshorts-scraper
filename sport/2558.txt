Pakistan chief selector Inzamam-ul-Haq's nephew Imam-ul-Haq became only the second Pakistani cricketer to slam a ton on ODI debut following his 125-ball 100 against Sri Lanka on Wednesday. Imam also became the third youngest to notch a ton on ODI debut at the age of 21 years and 310 days. He smashed five fours and two sixes during his innings.
