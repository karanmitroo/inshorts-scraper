Former Indian cricketer Sachin Tendulkar and son Arjun Tendulkar cleaned the streets of Bandra in Mumbai on Tuesday to extend support to government's 'Swachhata Hi Seva' campaign. "Happy to see wide scale participation by youngsters, including Arjun in #SwachhataHiSeva. Our Yuva Shakti will make a Swachh Bharat," PM Narendra Modi tweeted, hailing their efforts.
