Suryansh Shedge, a 13-year-old cricketer from Mumbai, scored 326 runs off 137 balls in the Giles Shield tournament on Friday. His knock, consisting of 53 fours and three sixes, helped his team put up 454 in 45 overs, with 37 being the next best individual score for the team. The opposition was later bundled out for 60 runs.
