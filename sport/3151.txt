Former Navy commando Praveen Teotia, who took four bullets in the 26/11 Mumbai attacks, successfully finished the 72-km-long Khardung La Challenge in Ladakh earlier this week. Teotia, who damaged one of his lungs and lost partial hearing in the attacks, completed the run in 12.5 hours. Khardung La Challenge involves running at altitudes higher than 18,380 ft.
