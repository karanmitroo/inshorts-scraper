Virender Sehwag, Sourav Ganguly and Sunil Gavaskar, who are in England for the Champions Trophy, dined together before the India-Pakistan clash on Sunday. Ganguly and Sehwag were accompanied by their wives and children. Sehwag shared a picture, writing, "Blood makes you related. Love and loyalty makes you a family. Great time with the legends #SunnySir and Dada with family."
