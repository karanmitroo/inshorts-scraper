After naming his 2016's Test Team, Harsha Bhogle revealed his ODI Team of 2016 on Tuesday. David Warner, who scored most ODI runs in 2016, was chosen as the opener alongside England's Alex Hales. Bhogle named India's Test captain Kohli as the captain of his side. Mitchell Starc, Jasprit Bumrah, Sunil Narine and Imran Tahir constituted the bowling attack.
