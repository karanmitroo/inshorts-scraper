India's mixed doubles players Sania Mirza and Rohan Bopanna will face each other alongside their respective partners on Monday in the quarterfinals of the French Open. The second-seeded pair of Sania and Ivan Dodig beat Elina Svitolina and Artem Sitak 6-2 6-4, while seventh-seeded Bopanna and Gabriela Dabrowski defeated France's Chloe Paquet and Benoit Paire in round two.
