BCCI on Monday announced a 15-member-strong Indian squad for the ICC Champions Trophy with Rohit Sharma, Manish Pandey and Mohammed Shami making comebacks to the team. India begin their Champions Trophy title defence against Pakistan on June 4. Notably, Rishabh Pant, Kuldeep Yadav, Shardul Thakur, Suresh Raina and Dinesh Karthik have been put on standby.
