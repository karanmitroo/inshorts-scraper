Liverpool made a profit of almost ₹1,880 crore (£218.9 million) by selling just three players. Philippe Coutinho, who became the world's second-most expensive footballer at £142 million, was bought by Liverpool for £8.5 million. Earlier, Liverpool had bought Luis Suarez for £23 million and sold him for £65 million, while Raheem Sterling cost £600,000 before being sold for £44 million.
