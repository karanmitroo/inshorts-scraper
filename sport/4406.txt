World number 26 Luxembourg's Gilles Müller defeated world number two Rafael Nadal in the longest match this Wimbledon lasting four hours 48 minutes. Müller, who had five match points in the game, advanced to the quarter-finals for the first time after the scoreline read 6-3, 6-4, 3-6, 4-6, 15-13. This is the second time Müller ousted Nadal at The Championships.
