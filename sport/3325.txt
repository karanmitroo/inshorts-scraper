Following his 30th ODI ton against Sri Lanka on Sunday, Virat Kohli reached 887 rating points, equalling Sachin Tendulkar's record for the highest ODI rating points by an Indian batsman. Kohli maintained his position at the top of the ODI batsmen rankings following his 330 runs in India-Sri Lanka series. Notably, Tendulkar had achieved 887 rating points in November 1998.
