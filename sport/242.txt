Former India captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni slammed his second fifty of his over 11-year T20I career, against South Africa on Wednesday. Playing his 77th T20I innings, Dhoni smashed 52* off 28 balls including 3 sixes and 4 fours. Dhoni, who had not scored any fifty in his first 65 T20I innings, has now scored two in last 12 innings.
