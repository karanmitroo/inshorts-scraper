India declared their first innings at a record total of 759/7, driven by Karun Nair's 303*(381), on Day 4 of the Chennai Test against England. Ravichandran Ashwin and Ravindra Jadeja scored 67(149) and 51(55) respectively to consolidate India's first innings lead. In reply, England reached 12/0 in five overs in their second innings, trailing India by 270 runs.
