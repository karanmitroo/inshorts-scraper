According to Football Leaks, Argentine forward Ezequiel Lavezzi earns around ₹4.2 crore approximately per week, making him the world's highest-paid footballer, ahead of Real Madrid's Cristiano Ronaldo and Barcelona's Lionel Messi. Lavezzi plays for a Chinese football club called Hebei China Fortune and his weekly wage is ₹1.9 crore more than Messi's and around ₹1.1 crore more than Ronaldo's.
