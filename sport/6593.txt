Following India's debutant spinner Kuldeep Yadav's four-wicket haul in the Dharamsala Test against Australia, Shane Warne tweeted, "Long live the wrist spinners." Kuldeep had called Warne his idol crediting him for his first international wicket, that of David Warner on a 'flipper' taught to him by Warne. Warne added he was "aways happy to help young spinners".
