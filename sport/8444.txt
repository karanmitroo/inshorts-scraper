Sania Mirza and her Czech partner Barbora Strycova lost the Sydney International final to Hungary's Timea Babos and Russia's Anastasia Pavlyuchenkova on Friday. Mirza-Strycova, who were top-seeded in the tournament, lost the match 4-6, 4-6 to their unseeded opponents. Mirza and Strycova, who partnered for the first time in August last year, will play the Australian Open as a team.
