Virat Kohli on Thursday won the Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy, becoming the fourth Indian to win the ICC Cricketer of the Year award. The first-ever Indian to win the award was Rahul Dravid who achieved the feat at the inaugural ICC awards in 2004. Sachin Tendulkar was named Cricketer of the Year in 2010, followed by Ravichandran Ashwin in 2016.
