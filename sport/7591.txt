In a first, BCCI has allowed a male contracted player to play in a foreign league, as 34-year-old Indian all-rounder Yusuf Pathan signed for Hong Kong T20 Blitz side Kowloon Cantons. "I am very excited about being a part of the Kowloon Cantons family," said Pathan. Interestingly, BCCI had earlier maintained that Indian players were only available for the IPL.
