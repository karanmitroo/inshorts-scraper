Ravichandran Ashwin revealed on Twitter that the Bengaluru Test, which India won, is the best cricket match of his life. Ashwin led India to victory after registering figures of 6/41 in Australia's second innings. Earlier, Ashwin praised Ishant Sharma for making six crucial runs, tweeting, "This man's 6 runs set us up today!Bowled his heart out too..Well done Ishy!"
