Newly appointed India coach Ravi Shastri was the team director from 2014 to 2016. Under him, Virat Kohli became the Test captain and India became the top-ranked Test team in January 2016. Shastri also led India to the semi-finals of the T20 and ODI World Cups, besides winning four ODI, two Test, and two T20I bilateral series during his tenure.
