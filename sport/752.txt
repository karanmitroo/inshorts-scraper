Indian shuttler Saina Nehwal beat Thailand's Ratchanok Intanon 21-19, 21-19 to reach the final of the Indonesia Masters Super 500 tournament on Saturday. The 27-year-old, who had earlier beaten compatriot PV Sindhu to reach the semifinal, will face either of Tai Tzu Ying of Chinese Taipei or China's He Bingjiao in her sixth final in Jakarta tomorrow.
