Congratulating Indian Kabaddi team for their third straight World Cup triumph, yoga guru Baba Ramdev tweeted, "Patanjali Chyawanprash ki taakat rang laayi. Kabaddi ke yoddhaon ko Vishwa Cup jeet ki badhaai." PM Narendra Modi also posted on Twitter, "Congratulations to Indian team for winning the Kabaddi World Cup. The team showed exceptional skills, grit & determination. Well done!"
