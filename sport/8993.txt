Congratulating Ravichandran Ashwin on winning the prestigious 'ICC Cricketer of the Year' award, former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag tweeted, "Badhai Ho @ashwinravi99, ICC Cricketer of the Year. Adjusting in all situations like Aaloo ki Sabji. #AshwinRocks." Ashwin became the third Indian cricketer after Rahul Dravid (2004) and Sachin Tendulkar (2010) to win the award.
