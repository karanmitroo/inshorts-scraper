In the first match after winning his fifth Ballon d'Or, Cristiano Ronaldo struck a brace as Real Madrid scored five goals in the first-half in their thrashing of Sevilla in La Liga on Saturday. Nacho, Toni Kroos, and 19-year-old Achraf Hakimi also scored. Madrid, who defend their Club World Cup title next, are third on the table with 31 points.
