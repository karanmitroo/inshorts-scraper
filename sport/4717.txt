On music composer's RD Burman's birth anniversary on Tuesday, former cricketer Virender Sehwag revealed he would sing his 1979 composition 'Aane Wala Pal' from the film 'Gol Maal' frequently while batting. Earlier, a video of Sehwag from the Cricket All Stars match had emerged, which showed him singing Bollywood song 'Tu Jaane Na' before hitting a six.
