Sakshi Dhoni, wife of former India captain MS Dhoni, posted a picture on Instagram of her two-year-old daughter Ziva and Shikhar Dhawan's three-year-old son Zoravar. The picture, taken at Jolly Harbour, Antigua, West Indies, shows the two kids playing on the beach. Earlier, Sakshi had shared a photo of herself with Zoravar and West Indies cricketer Dwayne Bravo's son.
