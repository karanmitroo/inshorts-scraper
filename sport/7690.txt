Delhi batsman Mohit Ahlawat, who became the first player to score a T20 triple ton, has received a trial call from IPL team Delhi Daredevils. Ahlawat, who has represented Delhi in Ranji Trophy, scored 300*(72) in a local T20 tournament in Delhi on Tuesday. Ahlawat had hit 50 runs in the final two overs, while his team finished at 416/2.
