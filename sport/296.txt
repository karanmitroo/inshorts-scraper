Former India captain MS Dhoni has set the record for most catches by a wicketkeeper in T20 cricket, taking his 134th catch against South Africa on Sunday. Playing in his 275th T20, Dhoni surpassed former Sri Lankan wicketkeeper Kumar Sangakkara's record of 133 catches in 254 T20s. Earlier, Dhoni became the third wicketkeeper to take 600 catches in international cricket.
