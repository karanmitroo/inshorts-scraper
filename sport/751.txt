Kings XI Punjab bought India's second Test triple centurion Karun Nair, who also smashed two tons in India's domestic T20 tournament this month, for ₹5.6 crore. Meanwhile, Lokesh Rahul and Manish Pandey were sold to Kings XI Punjab and Sunrisers Hyderabad for ₹11 crore each, the most for any Indian player in the 2018 IPL auction so far.
