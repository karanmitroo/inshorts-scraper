India defeated New Zealand by seven wickets in the third ODI at Mohali on Sunday, to take a 2-1 lead in the five-match series. Batting first, NZ put up a target of 286, which India achieved with 10 balls to spare. Virat Kohli and MS Dhoni put up a 151-run partnership,  with Kohli making 154* (134) and Dhoni 80 (91).
