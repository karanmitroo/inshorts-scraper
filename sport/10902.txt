Wrestler Goldberg has announced he will return to the WWE following a 12-year absence after he was challenged to a rematch with Brock Lesnar. "Booking my flight to answer that big mouth @HeymanHustle's challenge next week in person on #MondayNightRaw #WhosNext? #seeyathere @WWE", tweeted Goldberg. Goldberg and Brock last faced each other in 2004, with Goldberg coming out on top.
