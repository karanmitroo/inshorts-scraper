Arsenal defeated Stoke City 3-1 on Saturday to top the EPL points table. Charlie Adam's 29th-minute penalty opened the scoring for Stoke after Arsenal's Granit Xhaka elbowed Joe Allen. Theo Walcott scored his club football career's 100th goal in the 42nd minute before Mesut Ozil and Alex Iwobi secured the result with goals in the 49th and 75th minute respectively.
