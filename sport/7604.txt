Pakistani pacer Mohammad Irfan has been questioned by Pakistan Cricket Board's anti-corruption unit as part of the ongoing investigation for spot-fixing in the Pakistan Super League (PSL). PCB also questioned Karachi Kings' Shahzaib Hasan and Quetta Gladiators' Zulfiqar Babar. Notably, Sharjeel Khan and Khalid Latif were suspended by PCB and sent to Pakistan for alleged spot-fixing.
