The Indo-Swiss duo of Sania Mirza and Martina Hingis crashed out of the year-end WTA Finals tournament after a 6-3, 2-6, 6-10 defeat to Russia's Ekaterina Makarova and Elena Vesnina in the semifinal on Saturday. The duo had reunited to defend their year-end WTA Finals' title after having split as a pair earlier in 2016.
