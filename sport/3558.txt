Virender Sehwag, Chris Gayle, Shahid Afridi and Kumar Sangakkara will be part of a T10 league, named Ten Cricket League, in the UAE. The league will have 10-over-a-side matches with 90 minutes of action, spread over four days. The event, which is set to begin from December 21, will feature teams like Team Punjabis, Team Pakhtoons, Team Maratha among others.
