VVS Laxman got out for a duck in his career's first and last ODI. He began his ODI career against Zimbabwe at Cuttack on April 9, 1998, and was caught leg before by Pommie Mbangwa. Laxman played his last ODI against South Africa at Centurion on December 3, 2006, and was caught by Graeme Smith on a Shaun Pollock delivery.
