Sri Lanka ended the second day of the Colombo Test at 50/2 on Friday, trailing India by 572 runs. India's total of 622/9 was their sixth 600-plus score since December 2016. Ravichandran Ashwin, playing his 51st Test, crossed 2000-plus runs during his 54-run knock, becoming the quickest player to achieve the double of 2000-plus runs and 250-plus wickets in Tests.
