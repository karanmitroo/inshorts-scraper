Virat Kohli has overtaken Salman Khan to become the second most-liked Indian celebrity on Facebook. Kohli, who is also the most followed cricketer on Facebook, has over 35 million followers, leading Salman by over 600,000 fans. PM Narendra Modi is the only Indian ahead of Kohli in the list, which features Priyanka Chopra, Deepika Padukone and Amitabh Bachchan.
