Indian women's cricket team captain Mithali Raj will be presented a BMW car by Hyderabad-based businessman V Chamundeswaranath, following the team's performance in the women's World Cup. Chamundeswaranath, who is also a former skipper of the Andhra Ranji team, had earlier presented a Chevrolet car to Mithali in 2007. Chamundeswaranath also gave a BMW to PV Sindhu last year.
