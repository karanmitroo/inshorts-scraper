Virat Kohli went past former captain Sourav Ganguly to become the fourth highest run-getter as India's Test captain, during the first Test against Sri Lanka on Monday. Virat now has 2,664 runs in 30 Tests as skipper, overtaking Ganguly, who scored 2,561 runs in 49 Tests as captain. Kohli is still behind Mohammad Azharuddin, Sunil Gavaskar and MS Dhoni.
