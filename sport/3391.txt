Indian captain Virat Kohli became the leading ODI run-scorer this year, during his 131-run knock against Sri Lanka in the fourth ODI on Thursday. Kohli, who has now scored 907 runs this year, overtook South Africa's Faf du Plessis, who has 814 runs in 2017. Kohli's 907 runs have come in 17 ODIs, at an average of 82.45.
