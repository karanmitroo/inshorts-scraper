Irfan Pathan was the last Indian cricketer to take an international cricket hat-trick, accomplishing the feat on January 29, 2006, against Pakistan in Karachi. Claiming the wickets of Salman Butt, Younis Khan and Mohammad Yousuf, Irfan also became the second Indian to take a Test hat-trick after Harbhajan Singh, who claimed a hat-trick in the 2001 Kolkata Test against Australia.
