Hungarian swimmer Katinka Hosszu received her third gold medal in four days, after winning the 200-meter individual medley on Tuesday, successfully​ completing a sweep of the Individual Medleys. Hosszu also set an Olympic record with her winning time of 2 minutes, 6.58 seconds. The 27-year-old had earlier won golds in the ​400-metre individual medley and the 100-metre backstroke.
