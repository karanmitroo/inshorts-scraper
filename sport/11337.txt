India finished at 291/9 on day one of the first Test against New Zealand at Kanpur, with Umesh Yadav and Ravindra Jadeja still at the crease. NZ's Trent Boult and spinner Mitchell Santner took three wickets each, while opener Murali Vijay and Cheteshwar Pujara both made half-centuries for the hosts. India is playing its 500th Test match.
