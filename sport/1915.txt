Former Indian cricketer Sachin Tendulkar's son Arjun Tendulkar picked up a five-wicket haul for Mumbai against Madhya Pradesh in the Cooch Behar Under-19 Trophy. Arjun's five-wicket haul came in Madhya Pradesh's second innings and included wickets of the top four batsmen. Tendulkar registered figures of 26-4-95-5 in the second innings and also scored 31 runs in the match.
