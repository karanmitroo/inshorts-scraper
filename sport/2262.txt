Former Indian batsman Virender Sehwag smashed his first Test ton on his Test debut batting at number six against South Africa at Bloemfontein on November 3, 2001. Sehwag, who slammed 105(173), added 220 runs for the fifth wicket with Sachin Tendulkar in the first innings. Sehwag ended his career with a total of 23 Test tons, including two triple hundreds.
