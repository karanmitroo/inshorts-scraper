Indian all-rounder Hardik Pandya took to Twitter to share a picture of himself with Dinesh Karthik and called the latter a "funny guy". "Almost 10 years age gap. Close to 0 years mental gap. @DineshKarthik #funnyguy #Respect," Pandya wrote alongside the picture. Pandya and Karthik are currently at the National Cricket Academy in Bengaluru, working on their fitness.
