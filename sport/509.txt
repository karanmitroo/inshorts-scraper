Former India captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni has become the first Indian wicketkeeper and fourth overall to effect 400 ODI dismissals, achieving the feat in the third ODI against South Africa on Wednesday. Only Kumar Sangakkara (482), Adam Gilchrist (472) and Mark Boucher (424) have made more dismissals in ODIs than Dhoni. The 36-year-old has effected 770 dismissals across all formats.
