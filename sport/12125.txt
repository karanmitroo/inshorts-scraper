Indian archer Bombayla Devi advanced to the pre-quarterfinals in Rio’s women’s individual archery recurve event, beating Chinese Taipei archer Lin Shih-chia 6-2 in the round of 32 on Wednesday. Bombayla will now face Mexico's Alejandra Valencia in the round of 16 on Thursday. Meanwhile, Indian archer Deepika Kumari will face Georgia’s Kristine Esebua in the round of 64 later tonight.
