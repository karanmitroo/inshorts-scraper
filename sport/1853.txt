Indian boxers won five gold medals at the AIBA Women's Youth World Boxing Championships in Guwahati on Sunday. This was India's best performance in the tournament's history so far. Nitu, Jyoti, Sakshi and Shashi Chopra from Haryana and Assam's Ankushita Boro won golds in their respective weight categories. Anupama and Neha Yadav won bronze medals in their respective categories.
