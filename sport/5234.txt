South African bowlers on Monday reduced England to 20/6 in the first five overs, effecting the quickest fall of first six wickets in ODI history. England managed to reach 153 as Kagiso Rabada picked up four wickets for the Proteas. Hashim Amla's 54-ball 55 and Quinton de Kock's 39-ball 34 helped South Africa reach the target with 21.1 overs remaining.
