World number three Roger Federer lost for the first time in a Wimbledon semi-final on Friday. The seven-time Wimbledon champion lost 3-6, 7-6, 6-4, 5-7, 3-6 to the sixth-seeded Milos Raonic, who became the first Canadian to enter a Grand Slam final. This was Federer's first loss in eleven Wimbledon semifinal matches.
