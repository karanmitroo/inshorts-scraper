Rafael Nadal became the first player in the Open Era (since 1968) to win a singles tournament on 10 different occasions, winning his 10th Monte-Carlo Masters final on Sunday. Nadal defeated fellow Spaniard Albert Ramos-Vinolas 6-1, 6-3 for his record 50th clay-court title. It is also Nadal's 29th ATP World Tour Masters 1000 victory, one shy of record-holder Novak Djokovic.
