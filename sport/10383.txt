Real Madrid drew 3-3 against Polish club Legia Warsaw in the Champions League group stage's fourth game week on Wednesday. Gareth Bale and Karim Benzema gave the Los Blancos a two-goal cushion before Legia went on top through goals from Vadis Odjidja-Ofoe, Miroslav Radović and Thibault Moulin. Later, Mateo Kovačić's 85th-minute equaliser helped Real earn a point from the tie.
