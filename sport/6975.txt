PV Sindhu and Saina Nehwal entered the women's singles' quarter-finals of All England Open following their respective victories in the second round on Thursday. Sindhu defeated Indonesian Dinar Dyah Ayustine 21-12, 21-4 in 30 minutes, to enter quarters of the tournament for the first time while 2015 runner-up Nehwal ousted German Fabienne Deprez 21-18, 21-10.
