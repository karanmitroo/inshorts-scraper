Cristiano Ronaldo scored two second-half goals to help his side Real Madrid thrash Cyprus-based APOEL 6-0 in the Champions League group stage on Tuesday. Ronaldo took his UCL goals tally to 18 for 2017, the most in a calendar year. Meanwhile, Manchester City claimed a 1-0 win over Dutch club Feyenoord in another group match, courtesy Raheem Sterling's late winner.
