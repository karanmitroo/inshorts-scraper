Rohit Sharma hit a world record 16 sixes while bringing up his first ODI double century, against Australia in Bengaluru on November 2, 2013. Rohit, who scored 209(158), then became the third ODI batsman to hit a double ton, after Sachin Tendulkar and Virender Sehwag. Meanwhile, during his world record 264 against Sri Lanka in 2014, Rohit hit nine sixes.
