Indian women's team pacer Jhulan Goswami's World Cup semi-final winning jersey will be displayed on the walls of Fanattic Sports Museum in Kolkata. The 7,000-square-feet museum features jerseys and memorabilia from sportspersons including Pele, Roger Federer, Sachin Tendulkar and Usain Bolt among others. "[H]ope this sporting memorabilia will inspire youngsters to take up sport," Goswami said.
