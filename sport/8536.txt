Real Madrid's Portuguese forward Cristiano Ronaldo was named as The Best FIFA Football Player of 2016, ahead of Lionel Messi and Antoine Griezmann, on Monday. The 31-year-old forward led Portugal to their maiden EURO triumph alongside helping Real Madrid clinch the Champions League in 2016. Notably, Ronaldo is also the winner of four Ballon d'Ors, the second-most for any footballer.
