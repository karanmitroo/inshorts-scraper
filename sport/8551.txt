FIFA on Monday announced the FIFPro World XI of 2016, which includes nine players from Spain's La Liga. The team includes five-time Ballon d'Or winner Lionel Messi, four-time Ballon d'Or winner Cristiano Ronaldo and Spanish captain Sergio Ramos, among other players. Notably, Real Madrid has the most representation with five players, while no Premier League player features in the team.
