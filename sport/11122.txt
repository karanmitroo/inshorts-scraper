India finished at 227/8 at stumps on the third day of the second Test against New Zealand at Kolkata, to register a 339-run lead over the Kiwis. Following a jittery start in the second innings, Rohit Sharma's 82(132) helped stabilise India's scoring. Earlier, Mohammed Shami's three-wicket haul and Bhuvneshwar Kumar's 5/48 wrapped up the NZ innings at 204.
