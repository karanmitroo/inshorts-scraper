Cricketer-turned-commentator Virender Sehwag congratulated Indian Army and Jammu and Kashmir police for eliminating over 200 terrorists in 2017, calling it an "unbeaten double century". This comes after Jammu and Kashmir DGP SP Vaid revealed that over 200 militants were killed in J&K this year which is the highest since 2010 and called it "a huge landmark for establishing peace".
