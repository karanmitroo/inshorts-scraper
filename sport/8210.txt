Fourth seeds Sania Mirza and her Czech partner Barbora Strycova reached the third round of the Australian Open after defeating Australian-Chinese pair Samantha Stosur and Shuai Zhang 6-1, 6-4 on Friday. Meanwhile, 15th seed Rohan Bopanna, playing men's doubles with new partner Pablo Cuevas of Uruguay, was knocked out by unseeded Australians Alex Bolt and Bradley Mousley in round two.
