On the occasion of Sunil Gavaskar’s 67th birthday, former cricketer Virender Sehwag tweeted, “What Gavaskar did without helmet,its difficult for ppl these days to do with all equipments.If cricket were a film,Sunil Gavaskar is Sholay.” Former Indian batsman Sachin Tendulkar wished Gavaskar by tweeting, “Happy Birthday to the legend Sunil Gavaskar. Thank you for inspiring me over the years.”
