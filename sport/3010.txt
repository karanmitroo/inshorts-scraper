Pacer Ishant Sharma has replaced 36-year-old Gautam Gambhir as Delhi Ranji captain ahead of the upcoming season. Gambhir, who had captained Delhi in the Ranji Trophy for the past four seasons, wrote to DDCA stating that he wanted to continue just as a player. Last season, Gambhir was removed as Delhi one-day captain with Rishabh Pant replacing him.
