England's Hedley Verity, who was born on May 18, 1905, has dismissed Australian legend Don Bradman eight times in his Test career, the most for any bowler against Bradman. Verity bowled 932 balls to Bradman in Tests in the 1930s with the latter scoring 401 runs. Notably, Verity once picked 10 wickets for 10 runs in an innings against Nottinghamshire.
