Afghanistan spinner Rashid Khan has become the youngest to occupy the top spot in the ICC ODI bowlers' rankings, breaking former Pakistani spinner Saqlain Mushtaq's 21-year-old record. Rashid, aged 19 years 152 days, picked 16 wickets in the Zimbabwe series, to share the top spot with India's Jasprit Bumrah. Rashid also broke into the top five in the all-rounders' rankings.
