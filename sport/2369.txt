The Indian women's hockey team defeated China 4-1 to register its second straight win in the group stage at the Women's Hockey Asia Cup 2017. For the Indian team, Gurjit Kaur (19th minute), Navjot Kaur (32nd), Neha Goyal (49th) and skipper Rani Rampal (58th) found the back of the net. India will next play Malaysia in the final group match.
