As per reports, former Argentina captain Diego Maradona has not been invited for footballer Lionel Messi's wedding in Rosario, Argentina, on Friday. The guest list features over 250 people, including Messi's Barcelona teammates Luis Suarez and Gerard Piqué, and Chelsea midfielder Cesc Fàbregas. Maradona coached the Argentina team from 2008-2010, of which Messi was a part.
