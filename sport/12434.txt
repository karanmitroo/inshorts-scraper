Sehwag tweeted, “Three Li’s are in fashion - KohLi, MooLi (Paratha), KabaLi. Enjoy all today,eat MooLi Paratha and watch Kabali and then KohLi in d evening.” Cricketers Suresh Raina and R Ashwin also tweeted about Rajinikanth's movie.
