Seven-time Wimbledon champion, Switzerland's Roger Federer, became the third tennis player to hit over 10,000 aces on the ATP Tour, after reaching 10,004 aces in the Wimbledon first round win over Alexandr Dolgopolov. Federer lies behind Croatia's 38-year-old world number 23 Ivo Karlovic (12,062 aces) and former Wimbledon-winning Croatian Goran Ivanisevic (10,131 aces).
