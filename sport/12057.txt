Former cricketer Sachin Tendulkar scored his first international century while playing against England on August 14, 1990. With a score of 119*, Sachin, then aged 17 years and 112 days, is the third youngest cricketer to score a century, behind Pakistan's Mushtaq Mohammad and Bangladesh's Mohammad Ashraful. Sachin went on to score a total of 100 centuries in international cricket.
