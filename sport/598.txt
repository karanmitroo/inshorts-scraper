All-rounder Yuvraj Singh is the only Indian cricketer who has won the Under-19, T20 and 50-over World Cups. Yuvraj was player of the tournament in the 2000 U-19 World Cup and the 2011 ICC World Cup, and was also a part of India's 2007 World T20 winning team. India's U-19 team is vying for its fourth World Cup title today.
