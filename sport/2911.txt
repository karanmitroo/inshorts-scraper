Former athlete Milkha Singh's wax statue, which will be installed at Delhi's Madame Tussauds museum, has been unveiled. Milkha will be among 50 personalities whose wax figures will be displayed at the museum's inauguration on December 1. Sharing the picture of the statue, Milkha's son tweeted, "Such a proud moment for the Milkha Singh family! Dad immortalised in wax."
