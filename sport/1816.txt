The first official poster of Diljit Dosanjh starrer 'Soorma', based on ex-hockey captain Sandeep Singh has been released. Diljit will be seen portraying Sandeep in the film, which will also star actors Angad Bedi and Taapsee Pannu in pivotal roles. Directed by Shaad Ali, 'Soorma' will be released on June 29, 2018.
