A memoir on gymnast Dipa Karmakar, who finished fourth in Rio Olympics, is reportedly being written by Vimal Mohan, Digvijay Singh Deo, and her coach Bishweshwar Nandi. The book, tentatively titled 'Dipa Karmakar: The Small Wonder' is slated to release in August next year and will have untold stories of 23-year-old Dipa's struggle and triumph.
