Indian shuttler PV Sindhu reached the Hong Kong Open Superseries final for the second straight year after defeating former world number one Ratchanok Intanon on Saturday. World number three Sindhu won the match with the scoreline of 21-17, 21-17 in 43 minutes. In 2016, Sindhu had finished second in the tournament, losing to Tai Tzu-ying in the final.
