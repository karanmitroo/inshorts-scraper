Former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag congratulated Virat Kohli on his match-winning innings for India, with his tweet, which read, "Haazme ki goli, Rangon ki holi, Aur batting me kohli Poore India ko pasand hain". Virat's unbeaten 85-run innings from 81 balls	included nine fours and a lone six, helping India reach the total in 33 overs.
