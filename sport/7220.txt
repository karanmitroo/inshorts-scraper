Manchester United won their fifth League Cup title after defeating Southampton 3-2 in the final at the Wembley Stadium on Sunday. Zlatan Ibrahimovic and Jesse Lingard gave United a 2-0 lead before Manolo Gabbiadini's brace made it 2-2. Ibrahimovic scored the winner in the 87th minute of the match with a header, netting his 26th goal of the season.
