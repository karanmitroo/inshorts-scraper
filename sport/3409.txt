Premier League clubs spent a record £210 million (over ₹1,700 crore) on transfer deadline day  to break last year's deadline-day record of £155 million. The total summer spendings by the clubs reached £1.4 billion (over ₹11,600 crore), a 23% year-on-year increase. The biggest deals on the deadline day were of Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain (Liverpool, £35m) and Danny Drinkwater (Chelsea, £35m).
