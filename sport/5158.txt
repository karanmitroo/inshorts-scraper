Indian shuttler Sai Praneeth reached the men's singles final of the Thailand Open on Saturday, while Saina Nehwal exited the tournament after losing the semi-final. Third seed Praneeth registered a  21-11, 21-15 win over local player Pannawit Thongnuam in the semi-final, while Saina lost 19-21, 18-21 against Busanan Ongbamrungphan. Praneeth will face Indonesia's Jonatan Christie on Sunday.
