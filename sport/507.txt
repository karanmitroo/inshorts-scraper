India bowling coach Bharat Arun has said Ravichandran Ashwin and Ravindra Jadeja are "not out of the race" for the 2019 World Cup despite Kuldeep Yadav and Yuzvendra Chahal cementing their spots. Arun revealed Yadav and Chahal were included for South Africa ODIs based on the conditions. Ashwin and Jadeja last played an ODI in June and July 2017 respectively.
