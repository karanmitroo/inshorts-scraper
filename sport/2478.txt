Jammu and Kashmir's 22-year-old athlete Hamid Aziz ran non-stop from Srinagar to Baltal, covering a distance of 110 km in 10.45 hours. Hamid's previous non-stop running record was of a 60-km distance, which he had run in March this year. Hamid also plans to run 450 km in 2018 from Srinagar to Leh in seven days.
