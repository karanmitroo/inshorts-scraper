Indian spinner Ravichandran Ashwin displaced Bangladesh's Shakib Al Hasan to regain the top spot in the ICC Test all-rounder rankings. Ashwin, who has picked up 15 wickets in the on-going Test series against Australia, has a rating of 434, 31 points ahead of the Bangladeshi all-rounder. Notably, Ravindra Jadeja occupies the third spot with a rating of 360.
