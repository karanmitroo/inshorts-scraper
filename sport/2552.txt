Former Indian cricketer Sachin Tendulkar's son Arjun Tendulkar bowled to Indian captain Virat Kohli and his other Team India teammates in nets at the Wankhede Stadium on Friday. The 18-year-old was also seen interacting with India's bowling coach Bharat Arun. The left-arm seamer had earlier bowled to the Indian women's team before their World Cup final against England in July.
