Wicketkeeper-batsman Sanju Samson, who represented Rajasthan Royals from 2013-2015, was bought by the team for ₹8 crore after a bidding war with Mumbai Indians in the IPL auction. RR spent ₹4.4 crore on England wicketkeeper-batsman Jos Buttler, who played for MI in last two seasons. Earlier, Royals bought Ben Stokes for ₹12.5 crore, the highest amount for any player today.
