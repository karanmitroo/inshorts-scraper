India won four medals, two golds and two silvers, on Day 1 of the World Powerlifting Championship in England. Mukesh Singh Gehlot and Gaurav Sharma won golds in the 125 kg and 140 kg categories respectively, while Vaibhav Rana and Kanwardeep Singh got silvers in 125 kg and 110 kg respectively. Kanwardeep had won silver in the last edition too.
