Sania Mirza and Czech partner Barbora Strycova defeated the Canadian-Spanish pair of Gabriela Dabrowski and Maria Jose Martina Sanchez 4-6, 6-3, 10-5 to enter the Toray Pan Pacific Open finals, in Tokyo on Friday. They will play the winners of the China-US encounter in Sunday's final. This is Mirza-Strycova's third tournament together, having won the Cincinnati Open earlier in August.
