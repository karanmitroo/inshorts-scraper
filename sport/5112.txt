West Indian left-handed batsman Brian Lara scored a record 308 runs in boundaries, including 62 fours and 10 sixes, in his world record innings of 501* for Warwickshire against Durham on June 6, 1994. Lara, who faced over 71 overs in his almost 8-hour-long innings, was bowled off a no-ball on 12 and was dropped by the wicketkeeper on 18.
