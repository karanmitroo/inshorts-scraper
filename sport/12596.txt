Pacer Mohammed Shami has been paid ₹2.2 crore by the BCCI, as “loss of pay for IPL season 2015 due to injury”. Shami missed the IPL 2015 due to a knee injury, which aggravated during the 2015 World Cup and Australia tour. The compensation has been revealed in BCCI’s official listing of payments over ₹25 lakh, made in June.
