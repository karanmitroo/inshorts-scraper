Canada's Ted-Jan Bloemen won a silver medal in men's 5,000m speed skating with a margin of 0.002 seconds after a photo finish at the Winter Olympics on Saturday. The 31-year-old registered a timing of 6:11.616, edging out Norway's Sverre Lunde Pedersen by two-thousandths of a second. Netherland's Sven Kramer won the event after clocking Olympic record timing of 6:09.76.
