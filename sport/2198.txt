Hyderabad's women's Under-19 cricket team's vice-captain is a 12-year-old all-rounder G Trisha. Trisha played her first Under-16 match at the age of eight and is now playing in her fourth season on the domestic circuit. Indian women's team captain Mithali Raj, who made her international debut at the age of 16, has given Trisha a kit bag and other equipment.
