UFC champion Conor McGregor will reportedly overtake Cristiano Ronaldo in Forbes' annual list of highest-paid athletes, following his fight with Floyd Mayweather. McGregor will get over £77 million (₹640 crore) from the fight, while Ronaldo currently tops the list with £71-million (₹590-crore) earnings. McGregor had predicted he would overtake Ronaldo, when he met the Real Madrid player last year.
