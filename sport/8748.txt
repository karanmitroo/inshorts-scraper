New Zealand thrashed Bangladesh by six wickets in the first T20I between the sides on Tuesday. Batting first, Bangladesh reached 141/8 with Mahmudullah's	47-ball 52 being the innings' highest score, while debutant Lockie Ferguson picked up two wickets on his first two deliveries in T20Is. In reply, Kane Williamson's 73*(55) helped New Zealand chase down the target in 18 overs.
