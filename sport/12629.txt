India's Jinson Johnson qualified for the Rio Olympics in the men’s 800-metre sprint, at the Indian Grand Prix in Bengaluru on Monday. Johnson clocked 1:45.98 seconds, with the qualification mark for Rio being 1:46:00 seconds. July 11 is the last day for qualifications, with over 100 sportspersons already qualified from India, making it the country’s largest contingent at the event.
