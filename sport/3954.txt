Cricketers Cheteshwar Pujara and Harmanpreet Kaur, Paralympic medalists Mariyappan Thangavelu and Varun Bhati, and golfer SSP Chawrasia are among 17 sportspersons to be conferred the Arjuna Award this year. Tennis player Saketh Myneni and wrestler Satyawart Kadian, who is Khel Ratna awardee Sakshi Malik's husband, are also among the recommendations for the award, which will be given on August 28.
