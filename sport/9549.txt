Migom Kumbang, an IIT Guwahati student, ran onto the pitch to hug NorthEast United midfielder Romaric during the team's Indian Super League match against Delhi Dynamos in Guwahati on Wednesday. Kumbang jumped the barricades to hug Romaric in the 89th minute after he scored the team's second goal. The player hugged him back, with NorthEast United tweeting about the incident.
