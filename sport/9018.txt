Many Indian sports personalities, mostly cricketers, tied the knot in 2016. Cricketer Yuvraj Singh married his girlfriend actress Hazel Keech in November. India's first woman wrestler to compete at the Olympics, Geeta Phogat also got married in November to fellow wrestler Pawan Kumar. Further, Indian batsman Robin Uthappa tied the knot with his girlfriend Sheethal Goutham in Bengaluru this March.
