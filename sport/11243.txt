Defending Champions Real Madrid played out a 2-2 draw in the Champions League group stage against Borussia Dortmund at the Signal Iduna Park on Tuesday. For the Los Blancos, Cristiano Ronaldo and Raphaël Varane were the goal scorers, while Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang and André Schürrle netted one each to end the match level and earn a point for the German side.
