All-rounder Krunal Pandya was bought by Mumbai Indians through RTM for ₹8.8 crore, 22 times his base price of ₹40 lakh, in the IPL auction on Saturday. While Pandya's selling price to base price ratio was the highest, Australian opener D'Arcy Short came in second, fetching ₹4 crore (₹20 lakh). Jofra Archer was bought for ₹7.2 crore (₹40 lakh).
