IPL's most expensive overseas player Ben Stokes was bought by RR for ₹12.5 crore, making him the biggest buy on day one of the auction. Lokesh Rahul was snapped up by KXIP for ₹11 crore, the joint-second costliest player of the day along with SRH's Manish Pandey. KKR bought Chris Lynn for the third-highest amount of the day (₹9.6 crore).
