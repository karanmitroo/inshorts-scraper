Former New Zealand batsman Bert Sutcliffe played 42 Tests in his career, between 1946 and 1965, without ever being on the winning side. Sutcliffe was recognised as a fine left-hander and was even named one of 'Wisden's Cricketers of the Year' in 1950. The batsman, born on November 17, 1923, made three of his five Test hundreds in India.
