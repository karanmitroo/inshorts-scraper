Twenty-time English champions Manchester United have signed Alexis Sanchez from Arsenal in a straight swap deal, with Henrikh Mkhitaryan joining the Gunners. Sanchez, who joined Arsenal from Barcelona in 2014 for £31.7 million, will receive a reported salary of £500,000-a-week at United. Sanchez will don the number 7 jersey, previously worn by players like Cristiano Ronaldo and David Beckham.
