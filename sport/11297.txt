Leander Paes and his German partner Andre Begemann lost the men's doubles final of the St Petersburg Open in Russia on Sunday. Paes-Begemann conceded a one-set advantage to lose 6-4, 3-6, 10-12 to the British-Finnish pair of Dominic Inglot and Henri Kontinen. Paes last won an ATP men's doubles title at the 2015 New Zealand Open in Auckland.
