Spanish tennis player Rafael Nadal became the first player in the Open Era to win a single Grand Slam title ten times, winning his 10th French Open on Sunday. Nadal, playing his 22nd Grand Slam final, defeated Stan Wawrinka 6-2, 6-3, 6-1 for his 15th Grand Slam title. Nadal's career record at Roland Garros reads 79 wins and two losses.
