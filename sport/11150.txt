Quinton de Kock smashed 178(113) to help South Africa register a six-wicket win over Australia in the first ODI of the five-match series on Friday. Batting first Australia posted 294, driven by George Bailey's 74(90). In reply, de Kock's 178-run innings, the second highest score for SA in ODIs, helped his team chase down the target in 36.2 overs.
