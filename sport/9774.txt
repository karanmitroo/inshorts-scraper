A video of actors Salman Khan, Parineeti Chopra and tennis player Sania Mirza dancing to the song 'Baby Ko Bass Pasand Hai' from Salman's 2016 film 'Sultan' has surfaced online. They were seen dancing to the song on the occasion of Sania's sister Anam's sangeet ceremony. Anam got married to Hyderabad-based businessman, Akbar Rasheed on November 18.
