Australian club West Augusta's Josh Dunstan hit 40 sixes to score 307 runs in a 35-over match hosted by Port Augusta Cricket Association. He scored 86.72% of his side's 354-run total, which is more than Viv Richards' 33-year-old ODI record for the highest percentage of a team's total. Five Augusta batsmen fell for ducks, while the second-highest score was 18.
