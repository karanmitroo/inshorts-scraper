Differently-abled Sri Lankan cricket fan Gayan Senanayake attended the wedding reception of Indian cricket team captain Virat Kohli and actress Anushka Sharma in Mumbai on Tuesday. Senanayake, who has been to around 200 matches supporting the Sri Lankan team at home and abroad, clicked selfies with Virat and Anushka, Sachin Tendulkar, Ravindra Jadeja and Jasprit Bumrah among others.
