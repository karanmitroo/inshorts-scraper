Indian female basketball player Poonam Chaturvedi, who is suffering from brain tumour, scored 44 points to help Chhattisgarh beat Karnataka 79-78 in the semi-final of Senior National Basketball Championship. The 22-year-old was diagnosed with brain tumour in 2014 and has been undergoing treatment ever since. With a height of 6 feet 11 inches, she is India's tallest female basketball player.
