Indian cricketer Cheteshwar Pujara, his father Arvind and uncle Bipin have started 'The Cheteshwar Pujara Cricket Academy', around 16 kilometres from Rajkot. The academy, where youngsters will train free of cost, will also provide free transportation to all the trainees. The academy is spread over six-acres and has five turf pitches, bowling machines, and required gym equipment.
