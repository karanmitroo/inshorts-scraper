India's Sania Mirza and Rohan Bopanna crashed out of the Cincinnati Masters after losing their semi-final and quarter-final clashes with their respective partners in the women's and men's doubles. Bopanna and Croatia's Ivan Dodig lost to second seeds Lukasz Kubot of Poland and Marcelo Melo, while Mirza and Shuai Peng lost to Hsieh Su-Wei of Taiwan and Romania's Monica Niculescu.
