Virender Sehwag took to Twitter to congratulate boxer Vijender Singh, after the latter defeated China's Zulpikar Maimaitiali to win the WBO Oriental Super Middleweight belt in a double title bout on Saturday. Sehwag, who posted a photo of Vijender, said he enjoyed having "hakka noodles" while watching the fight, and the Indian made the Chinese boxer "hakka bakka".
