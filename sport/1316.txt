Team India captain Virat Kohli was seen dancing to Shah Rukh Khan's song 'Chaiyya Chaiyya' with the Bollywood actor himself at his reception in Mumbai on Tuesday. Shah Rukh later took to Twitter to share a picture of himself with Kohli and Anushka Sharma and wrote, "My love and most most happiness to @AnushkaSharma @imVkohli...so happy to see them married."
