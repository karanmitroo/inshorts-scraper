Former India captain MS Dhoni has become the first wicketkeeper to effect 100 stumpings in ODI cricket history, achieving the feat in the fifth ODI against Sri Lanka on Sunday. Dhoni's 100th victim was Sri Lanka's Akila Dananjaya, who was dismissed off Yuzvendra Chahal's delivery. Nayan Mongia has the next-highest stumpings for India, effecting 44 stumpings in 140 ODIs.
