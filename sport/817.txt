India's second test triple centurion Karun Nair slammed a 51-ball ton in a T20 match against Jharkhand on Wednesday, his second in the last five matches. Nair smashed seven sixes and eight fours in his knock of 100(52) and helped Karnataka put up a total of 201/4. Jharkhand were bowled out for 78 as Karnataka sealed a 123-run win.
