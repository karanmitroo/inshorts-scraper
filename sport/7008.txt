For the first time in Test cricket history, four different bowlers took six-wicket hauls in a match, during the India-Australia Test at Bengaluru on Tuesday. While Australian spinner Nathan Lyon took 8/50 in the first innings, his teammate Josh Hazlewood claimed 6/67 in the second. Indian spinners Ravindra Jadeja and Ravichandran Ashwin had figures of 6/63 and 6/41 respectively.
