BCCI has received 10 applications for the India head coach post this year, compared to 57 CVs coming in for the same post last year. The 10 applicants for the post this year include Ravi Shastri, Virender Sehwag, Tom Moody, Phil Simmons, among others. BCCI called for applications in May, extending the deadline till July 9 after Anil Kumble's resignation.
