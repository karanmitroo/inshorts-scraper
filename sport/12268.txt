Reacting to NADA's clean chit to Narsingh Yadav to participate in Olympics, cricket commentator Harsha Bhogle tweeted, "If life were a movie, Narsingh would win gold in Rio!". "#सत्य परेशान हो सकता है पराजित नही। #NADA से पहलवान नरसिंह यादव को मिली क्लीन चिट, जय हिंद #NarsinghYadav", read wrestler Yogeshwar Dutt's tweet.
