Bangladesh batsman Sabbir Rahman scored 122 runs off 61 balls for Rajshahi Kings against Barisal Bulls in the Bangladesh Premier League on Sunday, to break Chris Gayle's record for the highest individual score in BPL. The then Barisal Burners' player Chris Gayle had scored 116 runs off 61 balls on 14 February 2012 against Dhaka Gladiators.
