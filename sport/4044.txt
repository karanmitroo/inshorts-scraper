Indian women's cricket team captain Mithali Raj will receive a cash prize of ₹1 crore and a 600-square-yard house plot by the Telangana government. CM K Chandrasekhar Rao met Raj and her family on Friday and also announced ₹25 lakh reward for her coach RSR Murthy. Raj led India to a second-place finish in the recently concluded Women's World Cup.
