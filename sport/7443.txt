Wishing South African cricketer AB de Villiers on his 33rd birthday, cricketer-turned-commentator Virender Sehwag tweeted, "Happy Birthday Mr. 360 degrees @ABdeVilliers17, the man who can bat in any pose he wishes to #IPLlogo." Sehwag shared a picture along with his tweet in which AB de Villiers can be seen batting in the pose similar to that of IPL's logo.
