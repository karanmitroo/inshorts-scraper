Former cricketer VVS Laxman has been awarded honorary life membership of the Marylebone Cricket Club. The 42-year-old was inducted as a member on Thursday, joining Sachin Tendulkar, Sourav Ganguly, Virender Sehwag and Rahul Dravid in the list of Indians with the honour. The MCC has over 300 honorary life members out of a total membership of 18,000.
