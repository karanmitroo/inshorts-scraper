New Zealand finished at 93/4 at stumps on Day four of the first Test against India at Kanpur, requiring 341 runs more to win. Ravichandran Ashwin took three wickets in NZ's second innings, to become the fastest Indian to complete 200 Test wickets. Earlier in the day, India declared at 377/5 with Cheteshwar Pujara top-scoring with 78(152).
