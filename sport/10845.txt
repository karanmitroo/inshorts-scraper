Gautam Gambhir is the only Indian to score centuries in five consecutive Tests. Starting with India's Test against New Zealand at Napier in March 2009 in which Gambhir scored 137, Gambhir's century streak continued till January 2010 in Chittagong, with consecutive scores of 167, 114, 167 and 116. The longest Test century streak is of six hundreds, by Don Bradman.
