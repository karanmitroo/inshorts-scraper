Rohan Bopanna and his Canadian partner Gabriela Dabrowski lost in the mixed doubles quarter-finals at Wimbledon on Thursday, ending the Indian challenge at the All England Club. French Open winners Bopanna-Dabrowski, seeded 10th, lost 7-6(4), 4-6, 5-7 to Finland's Henri Kontinen and Great Britain's Heather Watson, who had earlier defeated Sania Mirza and Ivan Dodig in the pre-quarters.
