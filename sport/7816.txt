Kings XI Punjab will participate in the 2017 IPL auction with an amount of ₹23.3 crore in its purse, the maximum among all teams. The Mohali-based team retained 19 players from the previous season. Having retained 20 players, the purse size of Mumbai Indians will be the smallest with ₹11.5 crore, while Royal Challengers Bangalore will have ₹12.8 crore.
