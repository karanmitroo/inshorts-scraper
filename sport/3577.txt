Former Manchester United striker Dimitar Berbatov has joined Sachin Tendulkar-owned Indian Super League side, Kerala Blasters. The 36-year-old will rejoin his former United teammate Wes Brown and head coach Rene Meulensteen, who was Sir Alex Ferguson's assistant at Manchester United from 2007 to 2013. Berbatov, Bulgaria's all-time leading goal-scorer, last played for Greek club PAOK Salonika.
