Giving into demands made by Twitter users, Anand Mahindra is set to gift a golden Mahindra Thar to Paralympic gold medallist Mariyappan Thangavelu. The industrialist later added a ₹10 lakh award for the athlete by tweeting, "[A]m adding a gift of ₹10 lakh from my personal a/c. His Paralympic Gold was achieved against higher odds. His was a #Rise moment."
