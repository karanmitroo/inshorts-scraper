Real Madrid drew 2-2 with Borussia Dortmund in their final Champions League group stage match on Wednesday. A goal on either side of half-time by Karim Benzema gave Madrid a 2-0 lead before Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang narrowed it in the 60th minute. A late equaliser by Marco Reus denied Madrid coach Zinedine Zidane a win in his 50th match in charge.
