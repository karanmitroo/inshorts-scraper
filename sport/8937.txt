Manchester United won their fourth consecutive match in the Premier League after defeating Sunderland 3-1 at Old Trafford on Monday. Daley Blind opened the scoring for United in the 39th minute before Ibrahimovic netted his 12th league goal of the season in the 82nd minute. It was followed by Henrikh Mkhitaryan's 86th-minute strike and Fabio Borini's lone goal for Sunderland.
