England ended day one of the Rajkot Test against India at 311/4, led by a 180-ball 124 by Joe Root. Winning the toss and batting, England were driven by a 179-run stand between Root and Moeen Ali, with the latter remaining unbeaten at 99(192). Ravichandran Ashwin took two wickets, while Umesh Yadav and Ravindra Jadeja got one each for India.
