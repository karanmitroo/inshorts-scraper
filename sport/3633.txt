Defending champions Chelsea defeated Tottenham Hotspur 2-1 in the Premier League on Sunday, earning their first win of the season. Marcos Alonso converted a free-kick to give Chelsea a 1-0 lead in the 24th minute before Tottenham equalised through an 82nd-minute own goal by Michy Batshuayi. Alonso scored his second in the 88th minute to give Chelsea the three points.
