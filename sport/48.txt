The actor who plays 'The Mountain' in Game of Thrones (GoT), Hafþór Júlíus Björnsson, deadlifted 472 kg during the Arnold Strongman Classic competition to set the Elephant bar deadlift world record. Björnsson, a seven-time Iceland’s Strongest Man winner, went on to bag his first Arnold Strongman Classic crown, winning a cash prize of over ₹46 lakh.
