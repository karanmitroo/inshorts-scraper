A promotional video for the biopic 'M.S.Dhoni: The Untold Story' shows cricketer Mahendra Singh Dhoni and actor Sushant Singh Rajput visiting the house in New Delhi, which was Dhoni's till 2012. Located in Air India Colony, it was the house where Dhoni celebrated India's 2011 World Cup win. The house was also his point of departure to marry Sakshi Singh.
