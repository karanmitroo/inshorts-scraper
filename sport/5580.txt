Delhi Daredevils defeated Rising Pune Supergiant by seven runs in the IPL on Friday, ending Pune's four-match winning streak. Batting first, Karun Nair's 45-ball 64 had helped Delhi score 168/8 in their 20 overs. Pune fell short of the target despite Manoj Tiwary's 45-ball 60 as Zaheer Khan and Mohammed Shami picked up two wickets each for Delhi.
