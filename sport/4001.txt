Indian captain Virat Kohli has donated an autographed cricket bat to former Pakistani cricketer Shahid Afridi's charitable foundation. Afridi posted a picture of the bat on his Twitter account, thanking Kohli for his gesture. Virat replied to the tweet, posting, "You're most welcome Shahid Bhai.. Wishing you and the @SAFoundationN all the luck for the upcoming events. #HopeNotOut."
