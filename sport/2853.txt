South African batsman Hashim Amla, who had become Test cricket's 60,000th dismissal in 2010, became the 70,000th wicket to fall in Test cricket against Bangladesh on Friday. Bangladesh's Shafiul Islam became the bowler to take the 70,000th wicket in Tests after dismissing Amla for 137 runs. In December last year, Amla had become Test cricket's 10,000th LBW dismissal.
