Mumbai batsman Shreyas Iyer and Hyderabad pacer Mohammed Siraj have been included in the Indian squad for the three-match T20I series against New Zealand. Ashish Nehra has been picked for the first T20I, which is expected to be his final match. Ravichandran Ashwin, Ravindra Jadeja and Murali Vijay have been named in the Test squad for the Sri Lanka series.
