Ranji team Baroda collapsed from 71 for the loss of one wicket to 93 all out against Tamil Nadu in their Ranji Trophy match on Saturday. The side lost eights wickets in 13.4 overs with Tamil Nadu bowlers Krishnamoorthy Vignesh and Aswin Crist picking up four wickets apiece. The Baroda innings lasted for a total of 34.3 overs.
