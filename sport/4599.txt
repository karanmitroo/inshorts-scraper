India defeated West Indies by 93 runs in the third ODI at North Sound on Friday, to take a 2-0 lead in the five-match series. Spinner Ravichandran Ashwin completed 150 wickets in ODIs, while MS Dhoni overtook Adam Gilchrist to become the second-highest run scorer among wicketkeeper-batsmen in the 50-over format. Notably, this was India's 600th overseas ODI.
