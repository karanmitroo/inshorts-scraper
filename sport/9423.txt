The hashtag used for the Rio Summer Olympics, #Rio2016 was the most popular hashtag in India and globally this year, as per Twitter's #YearOnTwitter 2016 report. The second-most popular hashtag in India was #INDvPAK, following the teams' ICC World T20 encounter in March, with more than 1.1 million tweets recorded. #WT20 and #PVSindhu were also among the most-used hashtags.
