Reacting to Bengaluru FC becoming India's first football club to reach AFC Cup's final, Mahesh Bhupathi tweeted, "Astounding stuff @bengalurufc..keep dreaming cause you achieve one by one @ParthJindal11 @MustafaGhouse #AFCCup2016". Commentator Harsha Bhogle tweeted, "Oh wow @bengalurufc, congratulations. Another step on India's footballing journey. Was a bit worried when the away goal was scored."
