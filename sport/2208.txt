Indian spinner Axar Patel lost in a race against MS Dhoni and skipper Virat Kohli. Axar, who is 13 years younger than Dhoni and 6 years younger than Kohli, later tweeted, "Hard task to beat these two legends in a race. #triedmybest @BCCI". Axar was part of the playing XI in the first two New Zealand T20Is, picking two wickets.
