Pakistan Super League side Peshawar Zalmi flew in 13 Pakistani cancer-stricken children to Dubai to witness the opening ceremony of the T20 tournament and other Zalmi matches. Team's owner Javed Afridi stated the step was taken to strengthen the motto of Peshawar Zalmi Foundation, which is to 'Bring Back Smiles'. The children posed with cricketers and Indian actor Kapil Sharma.
