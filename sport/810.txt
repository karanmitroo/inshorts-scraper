India's Rohan Bopanna has entered the Australian Open mixed doubles semi-finals for the first time in his career. Bopanna and his partner Timea Babos of Hungary defeated defending champions Juan Sebastian Cabal and Abigail Spears with a scoreline of 6-4, 7-6(7-5) in the quarter-finals on Wednesday. Bopanna has won one Grand Slam mixed doubles title so far.
