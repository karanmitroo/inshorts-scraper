As many as 136 wide balls were bowled during a BCCI U-19 women's one-day match between Nagaland and Manipur in Dhanbad on Wednesday. Manipur bowled 94 wide balls (15.4 overs) while Nagaland bowled 42 wides (7 overs). Manipur, who lost the match by 117 runs, gave away a total of 112 extras, including 15 no-balls.
