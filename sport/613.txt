India captain Virat Kohli slammed his 33rd ODI hundred and his first in South Africa during the first ODI against South Africa at Durban on Thursday. Kohli has now scored 54 international hundreds and is only behind Jacques Kallis (62), Kumar Sangakkara (63), Ricky Ponting (71) and Sachin Tendulkar (100). Kohli's previous highest ODI score in South Africa was 87*.
