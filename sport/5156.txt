South Africa on Saturday defeated Sri Lanka by 96 runs in the first Group B encounter of the Champions Trophy 2017, helped by opener Hashim Amla's 25th ODI ton and spinner Imran Tahir's 4-wicket haul. Amla became the fastest batsman to score 25 ODI centuries, reaching the milestone in his 151st innings, surpassing Virat Kohli's record of 162 innings.
