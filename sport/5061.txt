Tennis player Serena Williams is the only woman in Forbes' 2017 list of world's 100 highest paid athletes. Serena's features at the 51st spot, with earnings of $27 million (₹173.7 crore), which includes a salary of $8 million (₹51.5 crore) and endorsements of $19 million (₹122.3 crore). Footballer Cristiano Ronaldo leads the list, with Virat Kohli being the lone Indian.
