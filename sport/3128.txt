Afghanistan spinner Rashid Khan has become the youngest cricketer to take 100 wickets in T20s, at the age of 18 years and 360 days. Khan overtook English left-arm spinner Danny Briggs, who reached 100 T20 wickets aged 23 years and 56 days. Khan, who achieved the feat in his 66th T20, also became the joint-second fastest to 100 T20 wickets.
