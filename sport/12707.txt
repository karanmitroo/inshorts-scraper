Reacting to Roger Federer's comeback win over Marin Čilić at Wimbledon, former Australian cricketer Tom Moody tweeted, "In awe...the earth stops when this man is on court!". Commentator Harsha Bhogle tweeted, "Betterer and betterer. @rogerfederer lives". Australia cricketer Steven Smith tweeted, "That is 1 of the greatest comebacks I've ever seen and that is why he is an absolute champion!".
