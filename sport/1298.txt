Indian all-rounder Yuvraj Singh trolled former Pakistani pacer Shoaib Akhtar, after the latter shared a picture of a motivational quote, wherein he can be seen wearing glasses and holding a helmet. Reacting to Akhtar's picture, Yuvraj wrote, "Oh ta theek hai payan tusi welding karan kithe chale ho (That is fine, but where are you going to do welding)."
