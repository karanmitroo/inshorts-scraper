Hungary's 'Iron Lady' Katinka Hosszu broke the world record in the women's 400 metres individual medley (IM) on Saturday to claim her first Olympic medal at her fourth Olympic Games. Hosszu clocked 4:26.36 minutes in the final to break the previous best of 4:28.43 minutes. United States' Madeline Dirado took the silver medal and Spain's Mireia Belmonte Garcia the bronze.
