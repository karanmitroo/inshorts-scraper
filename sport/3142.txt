Indian shuttler PV Sindhu reached the Korea Open Superseries final, wherein she will face her World Championships final opponent, Japan's Nozomi Okuhara. World number nine Okuhara had defeated fourth-ranked Sindhu in a closely contested final last month, and holds a 4-3 head-to-head record against the Indian. Sindhu reached the Korea Open final after defeating China's He Bingjiao  in the semis.
