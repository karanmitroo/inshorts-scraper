Striker Sunil Chhetri has broken former Indian football team captain Bhaichung Bhutia's record of most goals scored in Indian league football. The 32-year-old surpassed Bhutia's record of 89 goals during Bengaluru FC's I-League match against Aizawl FC on Wednesday. In the past, Chhetri has also broken Bhutia's record of most goals for India and most caps for the national team.
