Punjab captain Yuvraj Singh scored an unbeaten 164*(241) on the first day of the team's Ranji Trophy match against Madhya Pradesh on Thursday. Yuvraj's ton, which was his 25th first-class hundred, helped Punjab post a record 347/3, the highest first-day score at Lahli, surpassing Haryana's 339/7 against Rajasthan during the last edition of the tournament.
