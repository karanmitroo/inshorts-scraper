Indian tennis player Yuki Bhambri, ranked 200 worldwide, defeated world number 22 and defending champion Gael Monfils at the Citi Open in Washington on Wednesday. Bhambri, who entered the main draw of the ATP 500 tournament via the qualifying rounds, beat the Frenchman 6-3, 4-6, 7-5 in the second round. India's Ramkumar Ramanathan exited the tournament in the first round.
