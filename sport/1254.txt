Former captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni, who led India to 27 victories in 60 Test matches, is the most successful Indian Test captain. Dhoni is followed by former captain Sourav Ganguly, who led India to 21 Test wins in 49 matches. The 36-year-old, who retired from Tests on December 30, 2014, played 90 Tests, scoring 4,876 runs and effecting 294 dismissals.
