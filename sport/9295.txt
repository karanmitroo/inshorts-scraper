India's all three centurions, Murali Vijay, Virat Kohli and Jayant Yadav, were dropped by England fielders before their respective centuries. Adil Rashid dropped a return catch of Kohli when he was on 68*, while Jayant Yadav was dropped by Joe Root when he was on 8*. England keeper Jonny Bairstow missed stumping Murali Vijay when he was on 45*.
