Suresh Raina scored his T20I career's fourth fifty after scoring 63(45) against England in the Bengaluru T20I, his first T20I fifty after over six years. Raina's last T20I fifty had come on June 13, 2010 when he scored 72*(44) against Zimbabwe in Harare. Raina's knock included two fours and five sixes and helped India reach 202/6 in 20 overs.
