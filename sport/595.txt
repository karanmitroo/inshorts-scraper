India Under-19 team vice-captain Shubman Gill, who was named Player of the 2018 U-19 Cricket World Cup, is the only cricketer with 1,000-plus runs in an international format at an average of 100-plus. Gill has played 15 innings in Youth ODIs and has hit 1,149 runs at an average of 104.46, including four hundreds and six fifties.
