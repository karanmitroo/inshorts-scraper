Indian cricketer Shikhar Dhawan posted a picture on Instagram from his family vacation in Hong Kong post the West Indies tour. The picture, taken at Disneyland, shows Dhawan posing with his wife Aesha, their two daughters Aliyah and Rhea, and three-year-old son Zoravar. The photo, which has over 80,000 likes, has been captioned, "Family holiday started... Disneyland."
