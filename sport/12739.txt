West Indies batsman, Marlon Samuels has filed a defamation case against former Australian pacer, Geoff Lawson for ‘erroneous, malicious and unjustifiable comments’ believed to have been​ directed at him. The comments made by Lawson were on a radio podcast called the Big Sports Breakfast, in April, where he reportedly accused Samuels of being ‘tied up with gangs in Jamaica’.
