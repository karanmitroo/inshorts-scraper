Former Indian batsman Virender Sehwag announced his retirement from international cricket and the Indian Premier League on his 37th birthday in 2015. The batsman, who celebrates his 38th birthday on October 20, 2016, featured in 104 Test matches and 251 ODI matches, scoring over 8,000 runs in both the formats. Sehwag also has 96 ODI scalps to his name.
