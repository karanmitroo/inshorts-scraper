World number three Indian shuttler PV Sindhu defeated world number two Japan's Akane Yamaguchi on Friday to enter the semi-finals of the $400,000 Hong Kong Open Superseries tournament. The 22-year-old won the match with the scoreline of 21-12, 21-19 in 37 minutes. Sindhu will face world number six Thailand's Ratchanok Intanon in the semi-finals on Saturday.
