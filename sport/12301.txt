Reacting to Sri Lanka’s second victory over Australia in Test cricket, sports columnist Ayaz Memon tweeted, “Topsy-turvy, astonishing Test. Sri Lanka have suddenly found new heroes and rediscovered self-belief.” Former SL captain Kumar Sangakkara tweeted, “Congrats to @Angelo69Mathews and the team for achieving something no other Sri Lankan team has since 1999. You have done Sri Lanka proud.”
