India’s female shooters, Ayonika Paul and Apurvi Chandela, failed to qualify for the finals of women’s 10-metre air rifle event. While Chandela finished 34th overall, Paul ranked 43rd at the end of the qualification round. India’s medal hopes in the men’s category are still alive, with Jitu Rai and Gurpreet Singh competing in the 10-metre air pistol event later tonight.
