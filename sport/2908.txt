Shah Rukh Khan, women's cricket team captain Mithali Raj and Nita Ambani featured on Vogue India's 10th anniversary special edition cover. Seven other celebrities have featured on two other covers to mark Vogue India's 10th anniversary. While one cover features Twinkle Khanna, Sonam Kapoor, Anushka Sharma and Karan Johar, the other features Priyanka Chopra, Padma Lakshmi and Natalia Vodianova.
