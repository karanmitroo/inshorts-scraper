Atletico de Kolkata and FC Goa played out a 1-1 draw, as Goa earned its first points of the season on Sunday. Kolkata's Sameehg Doutie's 6th-minute volley opened the scoring, while Joffre equalized from the penalty spot in the 77th minute. The match's second half witnessed two red cards in the form of Kolkata's Stephen Pearson and Goa's Sanjay Balmuchu.
