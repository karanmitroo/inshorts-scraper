West Indian Shivnarine Chanderpaul and his son Tagenarine Chanderpaul scored a fifty each while playing for Guyana against Jamaica in a first class match on Saturday. The 42-year-old father, batting at number five, scored 57 while 20-year-old opener Tagenarine scored 58. Tagenarine made his first-class debut in 2013 two years before Shivnarine played his last Test for West Indies.
