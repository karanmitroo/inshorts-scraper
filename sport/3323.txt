Indian cricketer Gautam Gambhir has offered to bear life-long education expenses of Zohra, the daughter of assistant sub-inspector (ASI) Abdul Rasheed, from Jammu and Kashmir, who was martyred in Anantnag. Zohra thanked the cricketer for his gesture, saying, "Thank you Gautam Sir, me and my family are very happy with your gesture, I want to become a doctor."
