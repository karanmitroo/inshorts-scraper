Bangladesh ended day two of the Hyderabad Test at 41/1, trailing India by 646 runs. Earlier in the day, the hosts had declared their innings at 687/6, their fifth-highest total in Tests, with captain Virat Kohli scoring his career's fourth double ton. Wriddhiman Saha scored his career's second Test century, while Ajinkya Rahane, returning from an injury, made 82 (133).
