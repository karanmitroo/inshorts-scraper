Australia defeated Pakistan by 92 runs in the first ODI at Brisbane Cricket Ground, driven by wicketkeeper-batsman Matthew Wade's unbeaten run-a-ball 100. Batting first after winning the toss, Australia posted 268/9 helped by Wade's ton and Glenn Maxwell's 60(56). Pakistan were bowled out for 176 with pacers James Faulkner and Pat Cummins picking up four and three wickets respectively.
