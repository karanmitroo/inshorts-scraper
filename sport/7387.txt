India's top-ranked table tennis player Achanta Sharath Kamal lost to 13-year-old Japanese player Tomokazu Harimoto in the semi-finals of the Indian Open on Saturday. Harimoto, who is the current junior world champion, defeated 34-year-old Sharath to be the youngest player to compete for the title. Meanwhile, Sharath, ranked 62, has not reached an ITTF tour final in seven years.
