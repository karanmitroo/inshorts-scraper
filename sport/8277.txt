India's Olympic medal winning-wrestler Yogeshwar Dutt tied the knot with Sheetal Sharma in a private ceremony held at Singhola on the outskirts of New Delhi on Monday. Sheetal is the daughter of Haryana Congress leader Jaibhagwan Sharma. The wedding was attended by Indian cricketer Ishant Sharma, boxers Akhil Kumar and Jitender Kumar, among others.
