Sachin Tendulkar posted pictures on Instagram on Monday, which showed him celebrating Ganesh Chaturthi with former South African cricketer Jonty Rhodes. Sachin posted a series of three pictures, with one of them captioned, “Lovely to have the father of baby girl 'India' seeking blessings of the Lord! Always fun meeting #jontyrhodes.” Cricketer Yuvraj Singh also featured in the photographs.
