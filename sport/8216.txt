Cricketer Yuvraj Singh hit his career's 14th ODI hundred, and the first since 2011, in the ongoing Cuttack ODI against England. He also completed 150 ODI sixes in the innings, becoming the fourth Indian to do so. Yuvraj, making his fifth comeback in the team since 2011, went on to score 150 (127), his career's best ODI score to date.
