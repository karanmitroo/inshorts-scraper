Indian archer Deepika Kumari beat Italy’s Guendalina Sartori 6-2 in the women’s individual archery recurve round of 32 on Wednesday. Deepika now moves into the pre-quarterfinals of the event, in which she will compete with Chinese Taipei archer Tan Ya-ting on Thursday. Meanwhile, Indian archer Bombayla Devi also entered the pre-quarterfinals of the same event.
