Barcelona's Lionel Messi scored with a looping header in their 6-1 La Liga win over Sporting Gijon on Wednesday. On the ninth-minute mark, defender Javier Mascherano sent a forward cross from near the half-line for Messi to loop the goalkeeper with a header. The match also saw Luis Suárez, Neymar, Paco Alcácer and Ivan Rakitić score for the Catalan giants.
