Indian all-rounder Yuvraj Singh, who is the most expensive buy in IPL, was bought by Kings XI Punjab at his base price of ₹2 crore in the IPL auction on Saturday. Yuvraj became the most expensive buy in IPL history in 2015 when Delhi Daredevils bought him for ₹16 crore. KXIP also bought spinner Ravichandran Ashwin for ₹7.6 crore.
