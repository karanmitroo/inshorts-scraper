Indian cricketer Stuart Binny was interviewed by wife Mayanti Langer, on the occasion of their anniversary, after a Karnataka Premier League match on Friday. The 33-year-old scored 46-ball 87 and took two wickets to help Belagavi Panthers beat Bengaluru Blasters by 23 runs. Binny was adjudged Man of the Match and was interviewed by Mayanti, who was covering the match.
