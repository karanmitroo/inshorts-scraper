FC Pune City drew 1-1 with defending champions Chennaiyin FC in the Indian Super League in Pune on Sunday. Jeje Lalpekhlua gave Chennaiyin FC the lead in the 28th minute, which was cancelled out by Pune substitute Anibal Rodriguez's 82nd minute free-kick. The result leaves Chennaiyin in third place with eight points, while Pune remain sixth with five points.
