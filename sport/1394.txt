Captain Komal Zanzad picked up nine wickets to help Vidarbha dismiss Haryana for 31 runs in 18.4 overs in the quarter-final of the plate division of the Senior Women's One-Day League on Thursday. Zanzad ended with bowling figures of 9.4-5-8-9, with none of the Haryana players managing to cross double digits. Vidarbha chased down the target in 4.4 overs.
