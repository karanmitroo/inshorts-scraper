Former Indian cricketer Sachin Tendulkar met Indian WWE wrestler Jinder Mahal, alongside his son Arjun Tendulkar. Mahal invited Sachin to his WWE match, which is scheduled to be held in December. Sachin later tweeted, "You make us proud @JinderMahal. Thanks for inviting us to #WWELive in Delhi! The WWE fans will see you on Dec. 8th!"
