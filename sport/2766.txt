Indian pacer Bhuvneshwar Kumar got engaged to family friend Nupur Nagar in Greater Noida on Wednesday. On Tuesday, the cricketer had revealed Nupur's identity in an Instagram post which was captioned, "Here's the better half of the picture @nupurnagar." Bhuvneshwar's father revealed Bhuvneshwar has known Nupur for long and that the marriage will take place in Meerut later this year.
