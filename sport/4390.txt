Odisha CM Naveen Patnaik felicitated all Indian athletes who won medals at the recently concluded Asian Athletics Championships in the state. The CM awarded ₹10 lakh each to gold medalists, ₹7.5 lakh each to silver medalists and ₹5 lakh each to bronze winners. India finished with 29 medals at the event, including 12 gold, five silver and 12 bronze.
