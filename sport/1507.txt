Indian shuttler PV Sindhu reached the semi-final of the year-ending Dubai World Superseries Finals after defeating Japan's Sayaka Sato 21-13, 21-12 in her second group match. Earlier in the day, Kidambi Srikanth crashed out of the tournament with his second loss in a row. He lost to Chinese Taipei's Chou Tien Chen 21-18, 21-18.
