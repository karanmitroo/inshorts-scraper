With 220 scalps so far, Indian spinner Ravichandran Ashwin has the most number of wickets after having played in 39 Test matches. The spinner, who registered figures of 13/140 in the third Test, is followed by former pacers Waqar Younis (208) and Dennis Lillee (206). Notably, Ashwin was also the second fastest bowler to reach 200 wickets in Test cricket.
