Barcelona thrashed Alaves 6-0 in the La Liga on Saturday, extending their unbeaten run in the league to 15 matches. Barcelona led 2-0 before scoring four goals in eight minutes in the second-half to make it 6-0. Luis Suarez scored twice while Messi, Neymar and Rakitic scored one each. Suarez, with 18 goals, is La Liga's leading scorer this season.
