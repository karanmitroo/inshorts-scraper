Reacting to the car invasion that stalled the Delhi-Uttar Pradesh Ranji Trophy match, Delhi captain Ishant Sharma tweeted, "Drive in theater just progressed to #DriveIn match. Shocking scene in between #RanjiTrophy match today witnessed with @GautamGambhir." Further, UP captain Suresh Raina said, "[D]isturbing to see our...game being interrupted in this fashion...Thankfully all players are safe."
