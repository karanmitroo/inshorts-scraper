With his century against England in the first ODI, Indian captain Virat Kohli has now scored centuries in all three of India's 350+ run chases. Kohli scored 100*(52), fastest century by an Indian, against Australia on October 16, 2013 when India chased down 362. In the same series, Kohli hit 115(66) on October 30, 2013 helping India chase down 351.
