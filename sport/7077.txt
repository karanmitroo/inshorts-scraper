Playing in her 57th ODI, Australia's Meg Lanning smashed her 10th One Day International hundred and broke England's Charlotte Edwards' record of 9 centuries. Lanning scored 104*(116) against New Zealand on Sunday, helping Australia clinch the three-match ODI series 2-1. Notably, Mithali Raj, with 5 centuries, holds the record for most ODI tons by an Indian female cricketer.
