After Indian cricket captain Virat Kohli signed with German sporting brand Puma, Jamaican sprinter Usain Bolt, who also endorses the brand, tweeted, "Great choice @imVkohli Time to go to the next level #ForeverFaster." Kohli replied to Bolt's tweet with, "I wish I could get as fast as you someday @usainbolt. Thanks legend. @PUMA  #ForeverFaster."
