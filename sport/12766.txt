Defending Wimbledon champion and world number one Novak Djokovic was knocked out of the Championship after a 6-7, 1-6, 6-3, 6-7 loss in the 3rd round. Djokovic lost to world number 41 USA's Sam Querrey who started the match as an underdog with 20-1 odds. The loss also meant an end to Djokovic's 30-match winning run in Grand Slam matches.
