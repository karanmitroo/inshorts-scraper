Batsman Cheteshwar Pujara got run-out for 19(47) in the second innings of the Centurion Test on Wednesday, becoming the first Indian cricketer to be run-out twice in a single Test in India's 520-Test history. In the first innings, Pujara was run-out for his first Test golden duck. He is the 23rd player overall to be run-out twice in a Test.
