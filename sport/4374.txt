Commenting on Ravi Shastri's appointment as India coach, Cricket Advisory Committee member Sourav Ganguly said they picked the "best person" after consulting captain Virat Kohli. Ganguly also added that the decision on Shastri by the three-member CAC, which also includes Sachin Tendulkar and VVS Laxman, was "unanimous". Meanwhile, Shastri said the current team can be the "best" across all formats.
