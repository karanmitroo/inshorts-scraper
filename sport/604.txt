World number four PV Sindhu entered the India Open semi-finals for the third time after defeating Spain's Beatriz Corrales in the quarter-final on Friday. Defending champion Sindhu won the match with a scoreline of  21-12, 19-21, 21-11 in 54 minutes. Meanwhile, Saina Nehwal, who finished second in Indonesia Masters last week, lost her quarter-final match against America's Zhang Beiwen.
