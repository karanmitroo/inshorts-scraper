Virat Kohli-led India defeated South Africa in a low-scoring final in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia on 2 March, 2008, clinching their second Under-19 World Cup. Batting first, India were bowled out for 159. SA needed 116 to win from 25 overs as rain curtailed the match but managed 103/8 with Ravindra Jadeja, Ajitesh Argal and Siddarth Kaul taking two wickets each.
