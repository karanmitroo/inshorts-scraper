Shikhar Dhawan has received a 1300% pay hike from the BCCI, earning a retainership fee of ₹7 crore up from ₹50 lakh last year. Dhawan has been upgraded to the newly introduced Grade A+ from Grade C. Further, Rohit Sharma, Bhuvneshwar Kumar and Jasprit Bumrah, who were in Grade B (₹1 crore) last year, will earn ₹7 crore each.
