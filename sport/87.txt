Former India captain Sourav Ganguly has revealed Sachin Tendulkar taped the "cracked" handle of his bat during his debut at Lord's in 1996. "I was batting at 100 at tea...I quickly came in after batting for six hours," he added. "(Sachin) said 'you just rest and finish your...tea because you've to go and bat, I'll do the job'," Ganguly added.
