Indian captain Virat Kohli posted a photograph of him with his elder sister on the occasion of Rakshabandhan. Kohli's post was captioned, "A very Happy Rakshabandhan to everyone across the world celebrating, missing Didi and everyone at home today. #Rakhi #Rakshabandhan #Family". Former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag also posted a photograph with his sisters.
