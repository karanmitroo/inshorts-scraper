While wishing singer Sonu Nigam on his birthday, former cricketer Virender Sehwag tweeted, “Happy Bday @sonunigam, Enjoyed singing many of ur songs while batting, "Ab mujhe Raat Din"when I scored 165 vs South Africa at EdenGardens, 2010”. Earlier, a video of Sehwag singing a Bollywood song while hitting a six in the Cricket All-Stars Series had gone viral.
