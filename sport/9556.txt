Moa Ruschel expressed her relief after her footballer husband Alan Ruschel survived the Colombian plane crash which claimed 71 lives, including members of her husband's football club, Chapecoense. "Thank God Alan is in the hospital. We are praying for everyone who wasn't saved", read her Instagram post. Notably, Ruschel's teammates Jakson Follman and Zampier Neto also survived the crash.
