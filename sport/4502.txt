Real Madrid footballer Cristiano Ronaldo earns over ₹2.6 crore (£310,000) with each Instagram post, a study by Instagram schedulers Hopper has claimed. Ronaldo is third on the list of highest earners on Instagram, with singer Selena Gomez and socialite Kim Kardashian above him. Ronaldo has the third-highest number of followers on the platform, with over 106 million people following him.
