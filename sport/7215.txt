Lionel Messi's 86th-minute strike helped Barcelona defeat Atletico Madrid 2-1 on Sunday,  taking the Catalan club to top of the La Liga table. After a goalless first-half, Rafinha gave Barcelona the lead in the 64th minute which was cancelled out by Diego Godin's 70th-minute header. Barcelona lead Real Madrid by 2 points with the latter having two games in hand.
