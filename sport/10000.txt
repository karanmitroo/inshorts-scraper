India's Cheteshwar Pujara moved up two places to 11th spot, while Virat Kohli rose one place to be 14th in the latest ICC Test rankings for batsmen. Ajinkya Rahane remains India's top-ranked batsman at 9th spot, despite dropping four places. Ravichandran Ashwin has retained the top spot in the bowlers' rankings, while Ravindra Jadeja is ranked fifth.
