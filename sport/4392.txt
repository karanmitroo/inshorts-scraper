The organisers of the boxing fight between Floyd Mayweather and Conor McGregor have set a record pay-per-view pricing of $99.95 (₹6,500). The record price has been set for a high-definition stream of the match, while the pay-per-view price for the standard-definition stream has been set at $89.95 (₹5,805). The bout is scheduled to be held on August 26.
