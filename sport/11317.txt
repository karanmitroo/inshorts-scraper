American runner Karl Meltzer became the fastest to complete America's Appalachian Trail, taking 45 days, 22 hours and 38 minutes to cover the 3,524 km-long stretch. Meltzer started on August 3, running 14.8 hours a day and crossing 14 states. Almost 11,000 people have completed the run, with the previous record being 46 days, eight hours and seven minutes.
