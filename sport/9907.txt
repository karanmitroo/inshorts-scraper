Tons from Cheteshwar Pujara and Virat Kohli helped India reach 317/4 on the opening day of the second Test against England in Visakhapatnam on Thursday. India were driven by a 226-run stand between Pujara and Kohli, with the latter remaining unbeaten on 151 after registering his 14th Test hundred. England's James Anderson picked three of India's four wickets.
