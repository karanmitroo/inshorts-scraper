Pakistan's Bilal Irshad, who works in a mobile recharge shop, smashed an unbeaten 175-ball 320 for Shaheed Alam Bux Cricket Club, becoming the first triple centurion of the Fazal Mahmood National Club Cricket Championship. The 26-year-old opener hit 42 fours and nine sixes in the innings, leading his side to a total of 556/2 in 50 overs.
