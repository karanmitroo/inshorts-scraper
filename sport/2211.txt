Indian cricket team's head coach Ravi Shastri visited the Padmanabhaswamy Temple in Thiruvananthapuram on Monday ahead of the third and final T20I against New Zealand. Shastri, who offered a coconut to the 'Agrashala Ganapati', had reportedly visited the temple with his mother when he was four years old. The T20I series against New Zealand is currently tied 1-1.
