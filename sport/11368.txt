Former Indian wicketkeeper-batsman Mannava Sri Kanth Prasad has been made BCCI's new chief selector. Prasad, who replaces Sandeep Patil in the role, will head the selection panel consisting of five members. Devang Gandhi, Gagan Khoda, Sarandeep Singh and Jatin Paranjape are the other members of the committee. Meanwhile, Ajay Shirke was reappointed unopposed as the BCCI Secretary.
