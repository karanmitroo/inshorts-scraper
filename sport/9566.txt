Former Real Madrid player and current manager Zinedine Zidane's son Enzo Zidane scored in his debut match, which saw Madrid rout the third division side Cultural Leonesa 6-1 in Wednesday's Copa Del Rey clash. Madrid's Mariano Díaz struck a hat-trick, while James Rodríguez scored one alongside a Leonesa own-goal. With the win, Real Madrid reached the tournament's round of 16.
