Former pacer Rashid Patel hit opposition batsman Raman Lamba with a stump during the Duleep Trophy final on January 29, 1991. North Zone's Lamba was batting along with Ajay Jadeja when Patel, who was the bowler, grabbed a stump on his follow through and attacked Lamba. Jadeja was also hit while defending Lamba when the crowd also started stone-pelting.
