Indian shuttler Sourabh Varma won the Chinese Taipei Masters Grand Prix title after his opponent Malaysia's Daren Liew retired hurt in the final in Taipei City on Sunday. Liew, ranked 106th worldwide, was trailing 10-12, 10-12, 3-3 to world number 82 Sourabh when he quit the game. This is Sourabh's fifth international title overall and his first Grand Prix win.
