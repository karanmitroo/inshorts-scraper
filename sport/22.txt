As per BCCI's new contract structure, Indian men cricketers have been divided into A+, A, B and C grades. Grade A+ has been introduced for the first time and players under this category will get the highest amount (₹7 crore) each. Meanwhile, women cricketers are divided into Grade A (₹50 lakh), Grade B (₹30 lakh) and Grade C (₹10 lakh).
