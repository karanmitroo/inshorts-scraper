Congratulating shuttler PV Sindhu on her China Open win, Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi tweeted, "Congratulations to @Pvsindhu1 for her first super series title. Well played! #ChinaOpen". "Congratulations @Pvsindhu1 on winning the #ChinaOpen series. You keep making us proud. Keep it up", read former Indian cricketer VVS Laxman's tweet.
