India defeated England in the second T20I by 5 runs to level the three-match series 1-1. India had scored 144/8 from 20 overs, helped by KL Rahul's 71(47) while Chris Jordan picked three wickets for England. England failed to score eight runs in the final over as Jasprit Bumrah took 2 wickets and gave only 2 runs in the over.
