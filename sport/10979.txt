Seven schoolboys from the Indus International School, Bengaluru scaled Africa's highest peak, Mount Kilimanjaro. The students, belonging to grade 9 and 10, scaled the 5,895-metre high peak on September 29 after a 36 kilometres long trek as a part of the ten-day long 'Peak to Lead' expedition. Notably, Mount Kilimanjaro is the tallest free-standing mountain in the world.
