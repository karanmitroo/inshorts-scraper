Pakistan captain Misbah-Ul-Haq on Thursday became the first Pakistani player to win the ICC Spirit of Cricket Award. The previous winners of the award include MS Dhoni (2011), Daniel Vettori (2012), Mahela Jayawardena (2013), Katherine Brunt (2014) and Brendon McCullum (2015). "I feel honoured and humbled to have won the ICC Spirit of Cricket Award", said Misbah.
