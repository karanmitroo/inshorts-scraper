Windies' Chris Gayle smashed his 23rd ODI hundred off 79 balls against UAE in the World Cup Qualifier tournament on Tuesday, overtaking Sourav Ganguly and Tillakaratne Dilshan who had hit 22 ODI hundreds each. Gayle slammed 123 off 91 balls, including 11 sixes and seven fours. This was the second time that Gayle hit 10+ sixes in an ODI innings.
