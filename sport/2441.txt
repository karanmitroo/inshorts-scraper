Out of the 91 hat-tricks witnessed in international cricket till now, only one has been an all-LBW hat-trick which was achieved by Pakistani bowler Aaqib Javed against India on October 25, 1991. Javed took the wickets of Ravi Shastri, Mohammad Azharuddin and Sachin Tendulkar in consecutive deliveries to help Pakistan win the ODI tri-series final at Sharjah.
