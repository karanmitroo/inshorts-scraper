Rohit Sharma hugged Sri Lankan pacer Lasith Malinga after he took the wicket of Virat Kohli in the fourth ODI on Thursday. Malinga dismissed Kohli to pick up his 300th ODI wicket, following which the non-striker and his Mumbai Indians teammate Rohit congratulated him. Another MI teammate Hardik Pandya, who replaced Kohli, also congratulated Malinga with a fist bump.
