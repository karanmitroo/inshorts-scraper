India defeated England by 15 runs in the second ODI at Cuttack, thereby taking an unassailable 2-0 lead in the three-match series and winning their fourth consecutive ODI series against England. India posted 381/6 in 50 overs, helped by Yuvraj Singh's 150(127) and MS Dhoni's 134(122). Ravichandran Ashwin took three wickets as England were restricted to 366/8.
