India's World Cup-winning Under-19 head coach Rahul Dravid was paid ₹2.4 crore by the BCCI for a 6-month period that ended on December 31, 2017. Recently, Dravid was also awarded a ₹50-lakh cash prize after India won a record fourth Under-19 Cricket World Cup. Further, retired left-arm pacer Ashish Nehra was granted a one-time benefit of ₹60 lakh.
