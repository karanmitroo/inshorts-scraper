Rwandan cricketer Cathia Uwamahoro set the Guinness World Record for the longest net session by a woman, after batting for 26 hours straight. Uwamahoro revealed that she chased the record in a bid to promote women cricket in Rwanda. Interestingly, the record for the longest net session by a man (51 hours) is also held by a Rwandan, Eric Dusingizimana.
