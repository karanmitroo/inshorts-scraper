Indian top-order batsman Rohit Sharma scored ODI cricket's third double century after hitting 209 off 158 balls against Australia in Bangalore on November 2, 2013. The double ton was also the third by an Indian, following Sachin Tendulkar and Virender Sehwag. The match witnessed a record number of 38 sixes, of which 16 were hit by Rohit Sharma.
