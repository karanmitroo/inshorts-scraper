Real Madrid came from a goal down to beat Napoli 3-1 in their Champions League last-16 second leg tie in Napoli on Tuesday, registering a 6-2 aggregate win. Napoli's Dries Mertens opened the scoring in the 24th minute, after which Sergio Ramos gave Madrid the lead with two headed goals in the second half followed by Alvaro Morata's 91st-minute strike.
