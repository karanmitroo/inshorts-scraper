Paris Saint-Germain's 21-year-old central defender Presnel Kimpembe has got a 2,350% increase in salary, taking his wages from £7,100 to £174,000 a month in a three-year contract. Kimpembe had made seven appearances for the club this season, and was called up for the French national squad. The defender had reportedly asked for £223,000 a month, before the deal was finalised.
