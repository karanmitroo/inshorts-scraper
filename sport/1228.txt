Cricket Australia has named Virat Kohli as the captain of both its ODI XI and Test XI of 2017. Apart from Kohli, the other Indians included in the ODI XI are Rohit Sharma and Hardik Pandya, while Cheteshwar Pujara and Ravindra Jadeja made it to the Test XI. Interestingly, no Australian was included in the ODI XI of 2017.
