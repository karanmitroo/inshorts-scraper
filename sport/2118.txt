Afghanistan's Baheer Shah, who slammed an unbeaten 256 in his first-class career's first-ever innings, scored a triple century in his career's fourth match. Eighteen-year-old Shah, who slammed 303* for Speen-Ghar against Boost, became the second-youngest cricketer to score a triple ton in first-class cricket. His scores in career's first six innings read 256*, 34, 11, 111, 116 and 303*.
