Virat Kohli has been rested for the ODI series against Sri Lanka, while Rohit Sharma has been named captain for the series. Shreyas Iyer, who made his T20I debut earlier this month, and Siddarth Kaul were included in the ODI squad for the first time. All-rounders Ravichandran Ashwin and Ravindra Jadeja have been rested for the fourth straight ODI series.
