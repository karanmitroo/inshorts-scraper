Indian cricket captain Virat Kohli will lead Indian cricketers in a celebrity football match called 'Celebrity Clasico 2017', against Bollywood stars scheduled to be held in Mumbai on October 15. Kohli-led All Heart team also includes MS Dhoni while the Bollywood team, All Stars, will be led by Abhishek Bachchan. Last year's match had ended in a 2-2 draw.
