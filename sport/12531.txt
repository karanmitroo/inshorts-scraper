Tennis player Leander Paes posted a video on Twitter of the Davis Cup team performing a dance to celebrate their 4-1 win over South Korea in Chandigarh on Sunday. The players, including Leander Paes, Saketh Myneni, Rohan Bopanna and others, are seen performing a synchronised dance on the song ‘Afghan Jalebi’ from the Katrina Kaif-film ‘Phantom’.
