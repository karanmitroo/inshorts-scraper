Sachin Tendulkar and Virender Sehwag paid tribute to legendary batsman Don Bradman on his 109th birth anniversary. Tendulkar posted a photograph taken during his meeting with Bradman and wrote, "To the one and only...Happy Birthday to Sir Donald Bradman." Sehwag called the day "Bradman Jayanti" and tweeted, "A day for every cricket lover to remember...the greatest batsman of all times."
