The Pallekele International Cricket Stadium in Kandy, Sri Lanka, has had three T20I centuries being scored at the ground, the most for any venue. Before Glenn Maxwell's 145* on Tuesday, New Zealand’s Brendon McCullum and Sri Lanka’s Tillakaratne Dilshan both made hundreds at the venue. Johannesburg, Dharamsala and Lauderhill each have had two centuries being scored at their stadiums.
