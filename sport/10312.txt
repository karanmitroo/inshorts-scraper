Sachin Tendulkar scored his 45th ODI century against Australia in Hyderabad seven years ago on November 5, making 175 off 141 balls and completing 17,000 ODI runs. Tendulkar and Virender Sehwag opened the Indian innings, chasing 351. Tendulkar was dismissed in the 48th over with India requiring only 19 runs off 17 balls, but finally fell short by four runs.
