Indian opener Rohit Sharma slammed 108 runs in boundaries, including 12 fours and 10 sixes, during his record 118-run knock against Sri Lanka in the Indore T20I. The percentage of runs scored by Rohit in boundaries is 91.52%, highest by any batsman in an innings of 75-plus runs. Rohit's 10 sixes are the highest by an Indian in a T20I.
