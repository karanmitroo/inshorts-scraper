Former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag bagged a duck in his maiden appearance in a T10 match after getting out on the first ball off Pakhtoons' Shahid Afridi. Sehwag was also dismissed for a three-ball duck on his T20 debut for county side Leicestershire against Yorkshire on June 16, 2003. Sehwag is captaining Maratha Arabians in the ongoing T10 League.
