Left-arm spinner Sarfaraz Ashraf took six wickets without conceding a run for Young Pioneer Cricket Club against Mercara Youth Cricket Club in the Karnataka State Cricket Association's T20 tournament in Bengaluru. The first over of Ashraf's spell included five consecutive wickets. Ashraf, who plays for Jharkhand in BCCI's domestic T20 tournament, ended the match with bowling figures of 3-3-0-6.
