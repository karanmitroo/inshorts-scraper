Reacting to India levelling the Test series against Australia by winning the second Test on Tuesday, Indian cricketing legend Sachin Tendulkar tweeted, "Chak de India!! #INDvAUS." Former Indian cricketer VVS Laxman tweeted, "Congrats Team India on a fabulous, scintillating win, Loved the determination, fight & aggression of each and every player, Keep it up."
