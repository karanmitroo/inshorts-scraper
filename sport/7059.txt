As per reports, members and officials of the Hyderabad Cricket Association raked up a food bill of ₹75 lakh during the India-Bangladesh Test last month, with the association losing ₹44 lakh by hosting the Test. The revelations were made in a report by BCCI-appointed observer Ratnakar Shetty. Reportedly, 11,500 out of 39,000 seats at the stadium were given away complimentarily.
