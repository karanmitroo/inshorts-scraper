Rio Olympics silver medalist PV Sindhu exited the Denmark Open following her second round loss to Japan's Sayaka Sato on Thursday. Sindhu, who was playing her first tournament post the Rio Olympics, lost 13-21, 23-21, 18-21 to the world number 12. With her exit, the Indian campaign at the tournament ended, with Ajay Jayaram and HS Prannoy also suffering losses.
