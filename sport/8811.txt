Rio Olympic finalists PV Sindhu and Carolina Marin will play the inaugural match of the Premier Badminton League (PBL) Season 2 between Hyderabad Hunters and Chennai Smashers  on January 1 in Hyderabad. Sindhu will be playing for Chennai Smashers while the world number two will represent Hyderabad Hunters. Notably, Sindhu defeated Marin in their last meeting at Dubai Superseries Finals.
