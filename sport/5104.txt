Rising Pune Supergiant's owner Sanjiv Goenka is reportedly buying a stake in Rajasthan Royals, which will return to the IPL next year. Rising Pune Supergiant's two-year IPL contract ended this season, with the team losing the trophy by one run. Goenka is reportedly in London to meet RR owner Manoj Badale, who has a 40% stake in the franchise.
