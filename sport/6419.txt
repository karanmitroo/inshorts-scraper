La Liga leaders Real Madrid have maintained their lead on second-placed Barcelona after registering a 3-0 home win over Deportivo Alavés on Sunday. Karim Benzema, Isco and Nacho Fernández were the scorers for the Los Blancos. Elsewhere, Barcelona, with goals from Luis Suárez, Neymar, Ivan Rakitić and Paco Alcácer, defeated Granada 4-1.
