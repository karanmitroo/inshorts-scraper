Footballer Lionel Messi, who is getting married to childhood sweetheart Antonella Roccuzzo on Friday, has invited his teammates to the wedding. The guest list comprises around 250 people, including Messi's Barça teammate Gerard Pique and his pop star wife Shakira. Luis Suarez and Neymar are also expected to be present, while Sergio Agüero's partner will perform at the wedding.
