Second seeds Leander Paes and Ramkumar Ramanathan crashed out of the ATP Challenger tournament in Pune on Thursday, losing 6-2, 3-6, 4-10 to unseeded Hugo Nys and Luca Margaroli in the second round. The tournament was Paes' first ATP Challenger at home after almost two decades. Meanwhile, top seeds Purav Raja and Divij Sharan advanced to the next round.
