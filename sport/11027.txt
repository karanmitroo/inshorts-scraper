FC Pune beat FC Goa 2-1 in the Indian Super League at Goa on Saturday, to register their first win this season. Pune's Arata Izumi opened the scoring with a 25th-minute goal, which was equalised shortly after by Brazilian Goa striker Rafael Coelho Luiz. Pune substitute Momar Ndoye scored in the 90th minute to seal the scoreline at 2-1.
