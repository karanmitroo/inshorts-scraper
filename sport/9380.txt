Young Indian racer Jehan Daruvala has been signed by Formula 3's top team Carlin for the 2017 FIA F3 European Championship. The 18-year-old started his racing career in karting in 2009 and won his first national title in 2010 aged 11. Notably F1 champions like Nico Rosberg, Lewis Hamilton, and Michael Schumacher, also participated in F3 before getting into F1.
