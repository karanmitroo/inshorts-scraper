Nineteen-year-old Afghan spinner Rashid Khan, bought for ₹4 crore last year, was retained by Sunrisers Hyderabad for ₹9 crore in the IPL auction on Saturday. Kings XI Punjab placed the final bid of ₹9 crore before Sunrisers exercised the Right to Match option to retain him. Meanwhile, Yuzvendra Chahal was also retained by RCB for ₹6 crore through RTM.
