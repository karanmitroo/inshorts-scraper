Bulgaria’s Grigor Dimitrov defeated Belgium’s David Goffin to win his maiden ATP World Tour Finals on Sunday. Dimitrov, who won all five matches and claimed $2.5 million (₹16.5 crore) cash prize, became the first player to win the season-ending tournament on debut since 1998. Further, Goffin became the sixth player to beat Roger Federer and Rafael Nadal in one tournament.
