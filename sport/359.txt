Indian shuttler Kidambi Srikanth posted a video on Instagram wherein he is seen trying to copy Swiss tennis star Roger Federer's service. "Trying to copy the legend @rogerfederer . #rogerfederer #copyingtheserve #tennis #badmintonplayer #srikanthkidambi #slowmotion", the caption of the video read. Srikanth was ousted in the second round of the India Open.
