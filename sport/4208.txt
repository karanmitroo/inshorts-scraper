Indian table tennis player Sanil Shetty, ranked 201 worldwide, defeated world number 29 Aruna Qadri of Nigeria in Ultimate Table Tennis, a franchise-based tournament being staged in India, on Wednesday. Representing Falcons TTC in the tournament, Shetty registered the win with a scoreline of 11-6, 11-10, 11-9. Shetty also won in mixed doubles, combining with a Hong Kong player.
