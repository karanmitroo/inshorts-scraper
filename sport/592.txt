Mohammad Kaif and Yuvraj Singh were among five cricketers from India's 2000 Under-19 World Cup-winning squad who went on to represent the senior team. From India's 2008 Under-19 World Cup-winning squad, Virat Kohli, Ravindra Jadeja, Abhinav Mukund, Manish Pandey and Saurabh Tiwary played for Team India. Meanwhile, the only player to represent India from the 2012 squad was Sandeep Sharma.
