Sachin Tendulkar tweeted wishes for his former coach Ramakant Achrekar on the latter's 84th birthday. Posting pictures with Achrekar, Tendulkar wrote, "One of the major reasons for what I am today! Happy birthday Ramakant Achrekar sir :) Thank you for everything." Achrekar, a former cricketer, was given the Dronacharya award, the highest Indian honour for coaches, in 1990.
