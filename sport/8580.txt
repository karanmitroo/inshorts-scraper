Indian cricketer Irfan Pathan on Sunday shared a video on Facebook, expressing his resentment over the Bengaluru mass molestation case. Irfan said, "What happened in Bengaluru is unbelievable... We all should come together and stand against it." Irfan added, "If we cannot respect women, who go through tremendous pain during childbirth, then what is the difference between us and animals."
