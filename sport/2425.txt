World number four Indian shuttler Kidambi Srikanth beat defending champion Shi Yuqi of China to reach the French Open semis on Friday. Srikanth, who beat the Chinese 8-21, 21-19, 21-9, will face fellow Indian HS Prannoy in his fifth Superseries semi-final of the year. Meanwhile, PV Sindhu reached her maiden French Open semis by beating world number 10 Chen Yufei.
