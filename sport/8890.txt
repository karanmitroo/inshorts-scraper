Commonwealth gold-winning wrestlers Geeta and Babita Phogat, on whose lives the Aamir Khan-starrer 'Dangal' is based, recently did a photoshoot in a wrestling 'akhara' in New Delhi. The two sisters were dressed in bridal attire wearing gold jewellery and sunglasses. Geeta, who got married to wrestler Pawan Kumar in November, posed with him before taking a picture with other wrestlers.
