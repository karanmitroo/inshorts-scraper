Umpire Paul Reiffel, officiating in the ongoing India-England Test at Mumbai, went off the field after getting hit on the head from an outfield throw by Bhuvneshwar Kumar. Reiffel was stationed at square leg when Kumar threw the ball from the deep towards Cheteshwar Pujara, but ended up hitting the umpire. Third umpire Marais Erasmus replaced Reiffel on the field.
