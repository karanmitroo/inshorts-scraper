Manchester United registered their fourth straight draw at home after playing out a 1-1 stalemate against West Ham on Sunday. United conceded 90 seconds after the kickoff with Diafra Sakho's header, the second-earliest goal ever conceded by United at Old Trafford in the Premier League. United were able to equalise with Zlatan Ibrahimovic's 21-minute header off Paul Pogba's lofted ball.
