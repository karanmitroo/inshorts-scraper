Playing in a Caribbean Premier League match against Barbados Tridents on Friday, Trinbago Knight Riders' Hashim Amla (81) and Dwayne Bravo (66*) registered a 150-run stand, the highest ever fifth-wicket partnership in T20 cricket. The partnership, which was made in 15.2 overs, surpassed YV Takawale and SV Bahutule's 149-run fifth-wicket stand for Maharashtra against Gujarat in 2007.
