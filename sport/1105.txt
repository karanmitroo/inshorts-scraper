Barcelona thrashed Levante 3-0 on Sunday to go 16 points ahead of rivals and La Liga defending champions Real Madrid, who were held to a 2-2 draw by Celta Vigo. Barcelona's win saw goals from Luis Suarez, Lionel Messi, and Paulinho. Later in the day, Gareth Bale's first-half brace was cancelled out by an 82nd-minute equaliser from Celta Vigo.
