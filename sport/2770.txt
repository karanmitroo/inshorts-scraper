Indian Under-17 team defender Jitendra Singh paid tribute to his mother by sporting a haircut that reads 'Ma' (mother) ahead of the FIFA Under-17 World Cup. The Indian football team's official Twitter handle shared the picture of the 16-year-old and captioned it, "Self Explanatory. #BackTheBlue #FIFAU17WC." Jitendra Singh's mother Hema Devi sustains the family with her tailoring.
