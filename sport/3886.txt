Former Sri Lankan captain Mahela Jayawardena trolled a fan on Twitter who claimed that Indian cricketer MS Dhoni was faster than Usain Bolt. Following the Jamaican sprinter's final 100m race, Jayawardene had tweeted, "Respect @usainbolt", to which the fan replied, "Also respect to @msdhoni who is more faster than bolt." Jayawardena asked the fan if Dhoni was on his bike.
