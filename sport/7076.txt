Australia ended day two of the Bengaluru Test at 237/6, leading India by 48 runs. Starting the day at 40/0, Australia managed 197 runs during the day, scoring at 2.18 runs per over, with 47 runs and two wickets coming out of the first session. Ravindra Jadeja took three wickets for India, while Shaun Marsh top-scored for Australia with 66(197).
