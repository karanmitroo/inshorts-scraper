South Africa's Quinton de Kock, playing his 74th ODI innings, became the fastest wicketkeeper-batsman to complete 3,000 runs in ODIs during his 109-run knock in the fifth match against Sri Lanka on Friday. The 24-year-old surpassed MS Dhoni, who had taken 90 innings to achieve the feat. De Kock also became the fastest batsman to hit 12 ODI hundreds.
