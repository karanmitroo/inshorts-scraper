German goalkeeper, Marco Kwiotek was escorted from his club, SV Vonderort's training ground, by the police five days after conceding a record-breaking 43 goals in a match against PSV Oberhausen. Kwiotek conceded a goal almost every two minutes, as SV Vonderort were 35-0 down at half-time. The 25-year-old keeper said, "They [the police] just want to clear some things up."
