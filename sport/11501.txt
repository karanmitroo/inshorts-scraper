Indian cricketer Mahendra Singh Dhoni is seen quizzing actor Sushant Singh Rajput on details about his life in the new promotional video for the upcoming biopic 'M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story'. Titled 'Game on Mahi', the video shows Dhoni and Sushant in a gym. Directed by Neeraj Pandey, the film is scheduled to release on September 30.
