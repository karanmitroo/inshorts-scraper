In her first match after the Rio Olympics' bronze win, wrestler Sakshi Malik registered a 16-0 win over Jaipur Ninjas' Pooja Dhanda in the Pro Wrestling League on Friday. Sakshi had taken an 8-0 lead in the first round and sealed the match with technical superiority in the next. Sakshi's team Delhi Sultans, however, lost overall 3-4 to Jaipur.
