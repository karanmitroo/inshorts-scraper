Virat Kohli has a 53.85% 50-to-100 conversion rate, which is the third-best among cricketers with 10+ Test hundreds. Virat, with 14 hundreds and 12 fifties in his 50 Tests, is only behind former cricketers Donald Bradman and George Headley, who had conversion rates of 69.05% and 66.67% respectively. After Kohli, Mohammad Azharuddin has the best conversion rate (51.16%) among Indians.
