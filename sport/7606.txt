Bangladeshi captain Mushfiqur Rahim's unbeaten 81(206) helped Bangladesh reach 322/6 at the end of day three of the one-off Test against India in Hyderabad on Saturday. Chasing India's first innings total of 687/6d, Bangladesh lost two wickets to Umesh Yadav, while Ravichandran Ashwin, Ravindra Jadeja and Ishant Sharma picked up a wicket each.
