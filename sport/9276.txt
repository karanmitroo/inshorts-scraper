Ravindra Jadeja became the third quickest Indian left-arm spinner to take 100 wickets in Test cricket after Vinoo Mankad and Pragyan Ojha. Jadeja, playing in his 24th Test dismissed Alastair Cook on Sunday to achieve the feat. Mankad picked up his 100th wicket in his 23rd match while Ojha, the quickest to achieve the milestone, did it in his 22nd.
