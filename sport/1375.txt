India on Friday achieved their highest-ever T20I total by scoring 260/5 against Sri Lanka at the Holkar Stadium in Indore. On December 8, 2011, India had achieved their highest-ever ODI total by putting up 418/5 against Windies at the same stadium. India's highest-ever Test total of 759/7d had come in December last year at the MA Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai.
