India defeated Nepal by 152 runs on Wednesday to clinch their sixth successive victory in the T20 World Cup for Blind. Batting first, India posted 289/1, helped by Ajay Kumar Reddy's 109(50) and Venkateswara Rao D's  82*(33). In reply, Nepal were restricted to 137/5. India secured 24 points in the first stage, winning eight out of their nine matches.
