Indian shooter Ankur Mittal bagged the gold medal in men's double trap at the 7th Asian Shotgun Championship in Astana, Kazakhstan on Saturday. Mittal beat UAE's Khaled Alkaabi 71-70 in the final to clinch his second individual gold in Asian championships. The 25-year-old also won the team gold medal in men's double trap event alongside Sangram Dahiya and Mohammad Asab.
