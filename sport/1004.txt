Kamlesh Nagarkoti, an 18-year-old pacer from Rajasthan, clocked speed of 149 kmph during India's opening round win against Australia in the ongoing ICC Under-19 World Cup in New Zealand. Nagarkoti, who regularly bowled at speeds around 145 kmph, ended with figures of 3/29 in his seven overs. Nagarkoti was accompanied by Uttar Pradesh's Shivam Mavi who also crossed 145 kmph.
