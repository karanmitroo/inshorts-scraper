Virat Kohli is the only Indian and lone cricketer in Forbes' world's highest-paid athletes list of 2017. Kohli is at 89th spot, with earnings of $22 million (₹141.6 crore), of which $19 million (₹122.3 crore) comes from endorsements. His salary is listed to be $3 million (₹19.3 crore), which is the second-lowest in the list consisting of 100 athletes.
