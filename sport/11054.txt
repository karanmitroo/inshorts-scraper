Delhi Dynamos got off to a winning start in the Indian Super League (ISL), defeating defending champions Chennaiyin FC 3-1 in Chennai on Thursday. Delhi's Marcelinho, playing his first ISL match, scored from the penalty spot in the 26th minute. Chennai's Dudu equalised soon after, but Delhi's lead was restored with Marcelinho's second and Badara Badji's 84th-minute header.
