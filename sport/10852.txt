Kenya's Eliud Kipchoge, who won gold in the 2016 Rio Olympics' men's marathon, will participate in the Airtel Delhi Half Marathon on November 20. Kipchoge has a winning streak of seven successive marathons, and is unbeaten in the full 42 km version in three years. He has a personal best of 59:25 in half marathons, with Delhi's record being 59:06.
