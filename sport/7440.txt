Imran Tahir registered career-best figures of 5/24 to help South Africa beat New Zealand in the one-off T20I in Auckland on Friday. Batting first, Hashim Amla's 43-ball 62 guided South Africa to 185/6 in their 20 overs. In reply, New Zealand were dismissed for 107 in 14.5 overs, with seven Kiwi batsmen having single digit figures at the innings' end.
