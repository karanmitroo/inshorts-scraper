India scored 234/5 at stumps on Day 1, after West Indies put them to bat on winning the toss in the third Test at Gros Islet on Tuesday. Spinner Ravichandran Ashwin top-scored for India, remaining unbeaten at 75, while opener KL Rahul scored his first half-century. WI debutant Alzarri Joseph and spinner Roston Chase took two wickets each.
