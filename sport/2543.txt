Indian woman cricketer Veda Krishnamurthy has become only the third player from India to sign up for Women's Big Bash League after Harmanpreet Kaur and Smriti Mandhana. The 25-year-old, who represented India in the 2017 World Cup, has signed a one-year deal with Hobart Hurricanes and will be available for 10 matches of the tournament, starting December 9.
