Indian wrestler Bajrang Punia won the gold medal for India at the Mayor’s Trophy International Invitational Wrestling competition in Pune on Saturday. Punia defeated Mongolia’s Batchuluun Batmagnai 6-4 in the Men’s 65-kg category's final. Wrestlers Nitin and Jitender settled for silver medals in the 57-kg and 74-kg categories respectively at the event, which saw participation from eight nations.
