Wishing former Indian pacer Zaheer Khan on his 39th birthday, his former teammate Virender Sehwag tweeted, "Happy Birthday to one of India's greatest ever bowler and one of the sharpest cricketing brain, Gyaan Baba @ImZaheer #LastBachelorsBirthday". "Happy birthday to a wily bowler, wonderful man and a great friend @ImZaheer. Have a great year Zak!", tweeted VVS Laxman.
