Liverpool began their Premier League campaign with a 4-3 away win over Arsenal on Sunday. Theo Walcott gave the Gunners the lead in the first half, following which two goals from Philippe Coutinho and one each of Adam Lallana and new signing Sadio Mane helped Liverpool take a 4-1 lead. Later, Arsenal's Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain and Calum Chambers completed the scoreline.
