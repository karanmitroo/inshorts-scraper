Indian umpires Anil Chaudhary, CK Nandan and Chettithody Shamshuddin, who officiated in the IPL 2016, were reportedly paid in the region of ₹40 lakhs. While Chaudhary officiated in 39 matches, Nandan and Shamsuddin umpired in 22 matches. Former Indian bowler Javagal Srinath, who served as a match referee during the tournament, was paid over ₹26 lakhs for his services.
