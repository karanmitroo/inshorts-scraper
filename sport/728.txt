Eighteen-year-old Kamlesh Nagarkoti, who has bowled at speeds of over 145kmph in the ongoing Under-19 World Cup, was bought by KKR for ₹3.2 crore in the IPL auction. The amount is the highest for any current India U-19 World Cup player sold on Saturday. Nagarkoti had a base price of ₹20 lakh and was bought after a three-way bidding war.
