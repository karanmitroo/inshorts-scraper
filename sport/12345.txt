BCCI posted a video of Team India’s ‘FIFA Room’ in West Indies, with opener Shikhar Dhawan introducing the FIFA players of the team. The video featured Virat Kohli and Shardul Thakur playing a match against Cheteshwar Pujara and Shikhar Dhawan, with Bhuvneshwar Kumar commentating. The room, belonging to wicketkeeper Wriddhiman Saha, is converted into a gaming room on off days.
