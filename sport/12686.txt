The top-seeded Indo-Swiss pair of Sania Mirza and Martina Hingis was ousted from Wimbledon after losing to fifth seeds Yaroslava Shvedova and Timea Babos 2-6, 4-6 in the quarter-finals. The duo were the defending champions at Wimbledon. This is the second consecutive Grand Slam where the pair have been eliminated before the semi-finals.
