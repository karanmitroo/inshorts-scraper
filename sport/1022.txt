Imran Tahir, the third-highest wicket-taker for South Africa in World Cup history, represented his birth-nation Pakistan in the 1998 U-19 World Cup. Eoin Morgan, England's 2015 World Cup captain, played for Ireland in the 2004 U-19 WC. Grant Elliott, who guided New Zealand to the final of 2015 World Cup, had played for South Africa in the 1998 U-19 WC.
