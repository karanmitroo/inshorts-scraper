Harshvardhan Kapoor took to social media to share a picture of his father Anil Kapoor with retired Olympic gold medal winner Abhinav Bindra's father. "The reel and real life dads... Dad beat me to the meeting with Bindra senior," wrote Harshvardhan. Anil will portray Harshvardhan's father in the upcoming biopic on Bindra, which will feature Harshvardhan in the lead role.
