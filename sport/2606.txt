Former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag thanked the Indian hockey team for giving an "advance Diwali gift" in the form of their 3-1 win against Pakistan in the Asia Cup Hockey. "Wonderful effort from @TheHockeyIndia to beat Pakistan 3-1 in Asia Cup Hockey. The whole nation is proud of you", tweeted former cricketer VVS Laxman on the win.
