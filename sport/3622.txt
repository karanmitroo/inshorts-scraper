Russian world champion powerlifter, Andrey Drachyov, died after sustaining head injuries during a street brawl in Russia's Khabarovsk. The brawl, caught on CCTV, had reportedly started as Drachyov and the other men argued which sporting discipline was better and more effective. The killer, identified as Anar Allakhveranov, fled the scene and has been placed on a wanted list.
