Former India captain Rahul Dravid's 12-year-old son Samit slammed 150 runs in an under-14 one-day match to help Mallya Aditi International School amass 500/5 in 50 overs. The son of former Indian cricketer Sunil Joshi and Samit's teammate, Aaryan, top-scored with 154 runs. The opposing team was dismissed for 88 runs as Samit's team clinched the match by 412 runs.
