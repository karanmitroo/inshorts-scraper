Former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag again trolled English TV presenter Piers Morgan on Twitter after India's win in the Kabaddi World Cup. Sehwag wrote, "India invented Kabaddi & r World Champs for 8th time.Elsewhere some country invented Cricket & r yet only good in correcting typos. #INDvIRN." Morgan had corrected a typo in Sehwag's tweet on Tuesday.
