Indian shooters Akhil Sheoran and Vinita Bhardwaj won gold medals at the 6th World University Championships at Bydgoszcz, Poland. The 21-year-old Sheoran beat Poland’s Tomasz Bartnik en route to the men’s 10m air rifle gold. While Bhardwaj edged out Rio Olympic bronze medallist Yi Siling of China to win the women’s 10-metre air rifle gold.
