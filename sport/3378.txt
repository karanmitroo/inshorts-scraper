Indian cricketer Lokesh Rahul shared a picture of himself with all-rounder Hardik Pandya on Instagram and mocked the latter's hairstyle. Reacting to Pandya sporting a hairstyle similar to that of Windies' all-rounder Andre Russell, Rahul said that someone should ask Pandya to get a haircut again. Pandya was slammed for his latest hairstyle on social media.
