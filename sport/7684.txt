South Africa defeated Sri Lanka by 40 runs on Tuesday in Cape Town to take an unassailable 4-0 lead in the five-match ODI series. Faf du Plessis smashed 185(141), the second-highest individual score by a South African, helping SA amass 367/5 in 50 overs. In reply, SL were dismissed for 327 in 48.1 overs with Wayne Parnell taking four wickets.
