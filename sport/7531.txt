Sachin Tendulkar announced on Twitter that his biopic, 'Sachin: A Billion Dreams' will release on May 26. Incidentally, this is the same date, Sachin announced his IPL retirement in 2013. "The answer to the question that everyone's asking me is here. Mark your calendars and save the date," Sachin tweeted. The James Erskine-directed movie features music by AR Rahman.
