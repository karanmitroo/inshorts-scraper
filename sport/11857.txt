England registered a four-wicket win over Pakistan in the second ODI at Lord's on Saturday, taking a 2-0 lead in the five-match series. Batting first, Pakistan posted a total of 251, driven by Sarfaraz Ahmed's 105(130). In reply, England chased down the target in 47.3 overs, courtesy Eoin Morgan's 68*(80) and Joe Root's 89(108), his fourth straight ODI half-century.
