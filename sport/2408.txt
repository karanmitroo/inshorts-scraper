Irfan Pathan is the only cricketer to take a hat-trick in a Test's first over, in 2,279 matches in the format's 140-year history. The left-arm pacer dismissed Pakistan's Salman Butt, Younis Khan and Mohammad Yousuf on the last three deliveries of the first over at Karachi in 2006. Pathan is the only Indian pacer to take a Test hat-trick.
