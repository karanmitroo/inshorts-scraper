Indian cricketing legend Sachin Tendulkar shared a congratulatory message on Twitter after Indian captain Virat Kohli was named the ICC Cricketer of the Year. Tendulkar's message read, "No surprises there at all. You deserved it. Many congratulations, @imVkohli! #ICCAwards @ICC". Notably, Kohli is only the fourth Indian to win the Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy.
