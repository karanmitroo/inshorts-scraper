Virat Kohli registered his 37th win as India's ODI captain against South Africa on Tuesday, equalling the all-time win record after first 48 ODIs as captain. Kohli matched the win record of former captains Clive Lloyd (Windies), Hansie Cronje (South Africa) and Ricky Ponting (Australia). Notably, Kohli is the first Indian captain to win an ODI series in South Africa.
