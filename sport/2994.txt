Real Madrid's Cristiano Ronaldo, Barcelona forward Lionel Messi, and Messi's former Barcelona teammate Neymar Jr are the final three players in contention for this year's FIFA Best Men's Player of the Year award. The final three nominees for the Best FIFA Men's Coach of the Year award are Real Madrid's Zinedine Zidane, Juventus' Massimiliano Allegri, and Chelsea's Antonio Conte.
