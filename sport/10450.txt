Congratulating India for defeating Pakistan to win the Asian Champions trophy, Virender Sehwag tweeted, "Yesterday Moral of the story: Maa ki Mamta Jeet aasan bana deti hai. Today Moral of the story: #BaapBaapHotaHai #IndVsPak #NoMaukaMauka". Sehwag's tweet also included reference to the Indian cricket team having their mothers' names on the jerseys in the final ODI against New Zealand.
