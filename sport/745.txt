IPL history's most expensive bowler Tymal Mills, who was bought by RCB for ₹12 crore from a base price of ₹50 lakh in 2017, went unsold in the 2018 IPL auction on Sunday. Meanwhile, Mills' compatriot Chris Jordan was sold for ₹1 crore to SRH. Further, Australian spinner Nathan Lyon, who was the highest Test wicket-taker in 2017, went unsold.
