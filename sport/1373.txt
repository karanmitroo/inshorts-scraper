Spinner Brad Hogg has become the oldest cricketer, aged 46 years and 318 days, to take a wicket in a T20. Melbourne Renegades' Hogg achieved the feat by dismissing Hobart Hurricanes' opener D'Arcy Short in the Big Bash League on Thursday. Hogg overtook former Sri Lankan player Ajith Ekanayake, who had achieved the feat aged 46 years and 179 days.
