Former eight-division world champion, Manny Pacquiao beat Jessie Vargas to reclaim the WBO welterweight title in Las Vegas on Saturday. Pacquiao won on all the three ringside scorecards 114-113, 118-109 and 118-109, to regain the title Vargas had won in his last fight. Notably, it was Pacquiao's first professional bout after coming back out of retirement.
