Dev Patel, a 15-year-old Mumbai boy, took 10 wickets in an innings during the U-16 Harris Shield tournament, organised by the Mumbai Cricket Association, on Friday. Patel ended with figures of 10/9, bowling out the opponents for 29 in 11.5 overs. In the tournament, two schoolboys, Shreyan Pai and Aryan More, put up an unbeaten 425-run stand in 45 overs.
