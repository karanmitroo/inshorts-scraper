ICC has decided to hike the prize money for the upcoming ICC Champions Trophy 2017 by ₹3.2 crore to ₹29 crore, with the winning team set to pocket around ₹14 crore. The team ending as the runner-up of the tournament will get a cheque of ₹7 crore, while the other two semifinalists will earn ₹2.8 crore each.
