Varanasi-based wrestler Rajkumar Yadav has pledged to give up the 'banarsi paan' till Akhilesh Yadav wins the upcoming UP elections. "I am addicted to 'paan' but have now decided to quit it till my leader is back to where he belongs," said the wrestler. Yadav has won several national wrestling competitions for the disabled and many medals in local competitions.
