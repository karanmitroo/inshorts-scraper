Legendary singer Lata Mangeshkar on Monday congratulated the Indian cricket team following their win over England and dedicated 'Aakash Ke Us Paar Bhi' song to captain Virat Kohli, who made his career best score of 235 in the match. Lata tweeted, "Khas taur se Virat Kohli ko badhaai deti hun jinhone 235 run banaaye."
