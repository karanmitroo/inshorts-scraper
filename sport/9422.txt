Following umpire Kumar Dharamsena's decision against Aussie captain Steve Smith being overturned by a DRS review, Virender Sehwag tweeted, "Bas itte Se reh gaya,Smith ko sahi decision dene Se. After grand success in India&Bangladesh, #DharmasenaReviewSystem now in Australia too." Sehwag had earlier called DRS Dharmasena Review System, following some controversial decisions in India-England series.
