Defending champions Sunrisers Hyderabad on Wednesday defeated Royal Challengers Bangalore by 35 runs in the first match of IPL 2017. Yuvraj Singh's 27-ball 62 and Moises Henriques' 37-ball 52 helped Hyderabad reach 207/4 in their 20 overs. In reply, Bangalore were dismissed for 172 in 19.4 overs as Afghanistan leg-spinner Rashid Khan picked two wickets in his debut IPL match.
