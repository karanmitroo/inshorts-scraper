Jasprit Bumrah on Thursday took his maiden five-wicket haul in Tests, becoming the fourth Indian pacer to take a five-for in a Test in South Africa. Javagal Srinath, Venkatesh Prasad and Sreesanth are the other three Indian pacers who have achieved the feat in South Africa. Bumrah, playing just his third Test, registered figures of 18.5-2-54-5 in the first innings.
