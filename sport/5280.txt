Indian all-rounder Hardik Pandya has credited his success to former captain Rahul Dravid. Pandya said that Dravid's immense trust in his talents during his stint with India A team worked wonders for him. "[Dravid] used to tell me, 'Hardik you are the one guy who has the talent to represent India, so you need to...take charge of situations,'" Pandya added.
