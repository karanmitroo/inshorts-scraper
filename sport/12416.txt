Indian off-spinner Ravichandran Ashwin notched up his third Test century, in the first Test against the West Indies on Friday. Interestingly, all three of Ashwin's centuries in Test matches have come against the Windies. Ashwin is also the fourth Indian after Polly Umrigar, Brijesh Patel, and Sanjay Manjrekar to make a century in their maiden Test innings in West Indies.
