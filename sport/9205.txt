England's youngest-ever Test opener 19-year-old Haseeb Hameed met Sachin Tendulkar during the team's ongoing series in India. Hameed, who is recuperating from an injury, posted a picture on Instagram captioned, "Incredible getting to spend time with my childhood inspiration, the genius @sachintendulkar #TheMaster." Hameed, who scored 82 in his debut Test, has been ruled out of the series.
