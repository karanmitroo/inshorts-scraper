Following actress Zaira Wasim's apology on social media for acting in 'Dangal' and meeting Jammu and Kashmir CM Mehbooba Mufti, former cricketer Mohammad Kaif tweeted, "Such a shame that Dangal superkid #ZahiraWasim had to apologise. What wrong did she do?" Kaif further said that Zaira is a role model and she should not have been put in such a situation.
