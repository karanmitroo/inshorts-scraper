Indian shuttler Saina Nehwal will face Mirzya actress Saiyami Kher in a friendly match after the latter recalled facing Saina in a Junior Open Nationals' match when she was a junior-level player. "Jr. Open Nationals in Thane where everyone was dreading having @NSaina in the same draw! 3&2 of the toughest points I’ve earned in my life”, Saiyami had tweeted.
