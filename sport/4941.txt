Opener Rohit Sharma and pacer Jasprit Bumrah have not been picked for the West Indies tour, while wicketkeeper-batsman Rishabh Pant and spinner Kuldeep Yadav have been included in the squad. Virat Kohli will lead the team while Anil Kumble has got an extension as coach. India will play five ODIs and one T20I on the tour, which begins June 23.
