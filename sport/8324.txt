Geeta Phogat and Babita Phogat have come out in support of 16-year-old actress Zaira Wasim, who had become a subject of trolls after her meeting with Jammu and Kashmir CM Mehbooba Mufti. Geeta said that even she had undergone such difficulties and was able to move on. Babita added every citizen is with Zaira and she need not fear anyone.
