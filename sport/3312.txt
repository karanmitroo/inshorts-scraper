Indian shuttler PV Sindhu has produced a digital film based on her coach Pullela Gopichand's journey. The film, made in association with sports drink Gatorade, will be broadcast on the Teacher's Day, which falls on Tuesday. Under Gopichand's tutelage, India has produced several title-winning shuttlers including Sindhu, Saina Nehwal, Parupalli Kashyap and Kidambi Srikanth among others.
