Former cricketers Virender Sehwag and Sourav Ganguly attended cricketer Yuvraj Singh and Hazel Keech's wedding reception in Delhi on Wednesday. India's limited-overs captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni was also present, besides politicians Arun Jaitley and Rahul Gandhi. Earlier, Yuvraj hosted a sangeet on Monday, which was attended by Sachin Tendulkar and Shikhar Dhawan, among other cricketers.
