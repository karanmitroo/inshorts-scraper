Gujarat Lions' Dinesh Karthik became the first wicketkeeper to claim 100 dismissals in the IPL, reaching the landmark after taking the catch of Kings XI Punjab's Manan Vohra on Sunday. Karthik has claimed 74 catches and 26 stumpings as of now, playing for five teams since making his IPL debut in 2008. MS Dhoni is next with 94 keeping dismissals.
