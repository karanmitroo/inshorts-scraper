Seven-time Wimbledon champion Roger Federer came back from two sets down to reach a record 11th Wimbledon semi-final. The seventeen-time​ Grand Slam champion beat Croatia’s Marin Čilić 6-7, 4-6, 6-3, 7-6, 6-3. The 34-year-old is now the second oldest player ever to reach the Wimbledon semi-finals, after the 39-year-old Ken Rosewall in 1974.
