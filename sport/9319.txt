World number 95 Indian shuttler Parupalli Kashyap defeated world number 44 South Korea's Jeon Hyeok Jin on Friday to reach the semis of the Korea Masters Grand Prix Gold. Kashyap came from a set behind to beat Jin 18-21, 21-8, 21-16 in the match that lasted 58 minutes. Kashyap will face Korean Son Wan Ho in the semifinal on Saturday.
