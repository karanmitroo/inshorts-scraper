IPL 2017 auction's most expensive player Ben Stokes, who was bought for ₹14.5 crore by Rising Pune Supergiant, scored 316 runs in 12 innings, with his each run costing ₹4.58 lakh. Each run scored by Virat Kohli was most expensive, costing ₹4.87 lakh. Gautam Gambhir was least expensive among the highest-paid batsmen, with his each run costing ₹2 lakh.
