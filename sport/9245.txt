Wishing Yuvraj Singh on his 35th birthday, Sachin Tendulkar tweeted, "From dressing rooms to packed stadiums, victories to losses, we've seen it all together. Happy birthday @YUVSTRONG12! Wish you the best always." Yuvraj Singh, who was adjudged Man of the Tournament in 2011 Cricket World Cup, had dedicated the World Cup victory to Sachin Tendulkar.
