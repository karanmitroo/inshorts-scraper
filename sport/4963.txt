A British-Indian entrepreneur has bought a painting depicting Virat Kohli's IPL journey over 10 years for ₹23.7 crore (£2.9 million). The painting by artist Sasha Jaffrey, was bought at the recent charity dinner organised by the Indian captain in London. Sasha has, in the past, collaborated with athletes like David Beckham, MS Dhoni, Yuvraj Singh for their charities.
