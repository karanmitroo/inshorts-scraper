Ex-Australian captain Michael Clarke named former teammate Shane Warne captain of his Dream Test 12. The team also included Sachin Tendulkar, Brian Lara, and his former teammate and skipper Ricky Ponting. Clarke also picked Michael Slater, Matthew Hayden, Jacques Kallis, Adam Gilchrist, Mitchell Johnson, Dale Steyn, Glenn McGrath and Muttiah Muralitharan in his team.
