Dismissing the possibility of S Sreesanth's return to the Indian team over his involvement in the spot-fixing scandal, cricketer-turned-commentator Aakash Chopra tweeted that those who put the nation at stake do not deserve a second chance. Responding to it, Sreesanth tweeted, "how can u be so 2 faced?? Bro?? Ashamed to even call u that... really sad to know."
