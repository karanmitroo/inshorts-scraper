Indian shuttler Sourabh Verma, ranked 82nd worldwide, defeated rank 22 and top seed Hsu Jen Hao of China to enter the Chinese Taipei Masters' final on Saturday. The Indian shuttler defeated Hao 11-4, 11-7, 11-9 in the 33-minute long semifinal. Verma will now meet the winner between Malaysia's Daren Liew and fifth seed Lin Yu Hsien, in Sunday's final.
