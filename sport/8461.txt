Former cricketer Rahul Dravid holds the record for most time spent at the crease by a Test batsman, recording 735 hours and 52 minutes on the pitch in his 164 Tests-long career. He also tops the list for most balls faced by a batsman, facing 31,258 deliveries. Dravid made 13,288 runs in Tests, the fourth-highest overall and second-highest among Indians.
