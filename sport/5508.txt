New Premier League champions Chelsea registered a record-equalling 29th league win this season, defeating Watford 4-3 at Stamford Bridge on Monday. Making nine changes from their title-sealing victory on Friday, Chelsea were given the lead by captain John Terry, who was making his first start since September. Michy Batshuayi, César Azpilicueta and Cesc Fàbregas were the other Chelsea scorers.
