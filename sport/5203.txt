Former women's doubles world number one Sania Mirza crashed out of the French Open after she and her partner Yaroslava Shvedova slumped to a first round loss against Daria Gavrilova and Anastasia Pavlyuchenkova on Wednesday. The fourth seeded Indo-Kazakh pair lost to the unseeded pair 6-7, 6-1, 2-6 in the match that spanned 2 hours and 23 minutes.
