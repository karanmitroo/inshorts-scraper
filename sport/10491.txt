Arsenal's Alexis Sanchez and substitute Olivier Giroud scored a brace each to secure a 4-1 away win against Sunderland, to temporarily propel Arsenal to the PL top-spot. Sanchez scored his first in the 19th-minute before Sunderland's Jermain Defoe equalised with a penalty in the 65th-minute. Arsenal scored three goals in eight minutes in the second-half to clinch the win.
