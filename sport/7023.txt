Gautam Gambhir was reportedly involved in a verbal spat with Delhi coach KP Bhaskar following their Vijay Hazare Trophy match on Monday. Gambhir reportedly mocked Bhaskar, who is in his first season as Delhi coach, with personal insults. Notably, Gambhir was sacked as captain before the tournament and he had expressed his displeasure when Delhi replaced Vijay Dahiya as coach.
