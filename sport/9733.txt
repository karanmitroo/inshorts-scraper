PV Sindhu reached the semi-finals of the Hong Kong Open Super Series, defeating Singapore's Liang Xiaoyu in the quarters on Friday. Sindhu overturned a six-point deficit over Xiaoyu in the final game to seal the scoreline at 21-17, 21-23, 21-18. Meanwhile, Saina Nehwal lost her quarter-final match against Hong Kong's Cheung Ngan Yi, who will face Sindhu in the semi-final.
