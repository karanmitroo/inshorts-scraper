Shuttler PV Sindhu was referred to as a "national volleyball player" by All India Majlis-e-Ittehad-ul Muslimeen MLA Mumtaz Ahmed Khan at an event in Hyderabad on Friday. A video has emerged which shows the MLA asking Deputy CM Mohammad Mahmood Ali about Sindhu, after which the former announces, "We thank our national volleyball player." Sindhu is seen ignoring the gaffe.
