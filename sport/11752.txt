Indian tennis stars Leander Paes and Rohan Bopanna crashed out of the US Open men's doubles event after losing their respective opening round matches on Friday. Paes and Germany's Andre Begemann lost 6-2, 5-7, 4-6 to France's Stephane Robert and Israel's Dudi Sela. Bopanna and Denmark's Frederik Nielsen lost 2-6, 6-7 to American Brian Baker and New Zealand's Marcus Daniell.
