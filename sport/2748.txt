PM Narendra Modi attended India's first match in the FIFA U-17 World Cup 2017 against USA on Friday at Delhi's Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium. PM Modi was accompanied by Sports Minister Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore, AIFF President Praful Patel, and other FIFA officials. Football players Bhaichung Bhutia and Sunil Chhetri were also a part of the event.
