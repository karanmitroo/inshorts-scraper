The Indian football team, ranked 152nd worldwide, defeated 114th-ranked Puerto Rico 4-1 in an international friendly in Mumbai on Saturday. Puerto Rico went a goal ahead, before Narayan Das, Sunil Chhetri and Jeje Lalpekhlua helped India end the first half at 3-1. Jackichand Singh scored the final goal of the match in the 56th minute, to end it 4-1.
