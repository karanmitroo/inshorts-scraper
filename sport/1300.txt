Haryana shooter Anisa Sayyed won the women's 25m pistol gold with a new finals national record at the 61st National Shooting Championship Competitions on Wednesday. The 37-year-old shot a score of 33 in the final to erase previous national record of 28 set by Annu Singh. Shital Shivaji Thorat came second with 30, while Rani finished third with 28 points.
