Kieron Pollard, Sunil Narine, Darren Bravo and Andre Russell are among Windies' players who will miss 2019 World Cup Qualifiers in March to play in the Pakistan Super League. Selection chief Courtney Browne further revealed Dwayne Bravo wasn't contacted after he indicated he won't play for Windies. Senior players Chris Gayle and Marlon Samuels have been included in the squad.
