New Zealand completed a 3-0 clean sweep over Bangladesh in the T20I series with a 27-run win at the Bay Oval, Mount Maunganui on Sunday. Corey Anderson's unbeaten 41-ball 94, which included 10 sixes, helped New Zealand post a total of 194/4. In reply, Bangladesh managed to reach 167/6, with Trent Boult and Ish Sodhi picking up two wickets each.
