Cricketer Virender Sehwag made his Test debut 15 years ago against South Africa at Bloemfontein. Walking in at number six with India at 68/4, Sehwag scored 105 (173) and shared a 220-run stand with Sachin Tendulkar before being dismissed by Shaun Pollock. He went on to play 104 Tests for India, scoring 23 centuries with a career-best score of 319.
