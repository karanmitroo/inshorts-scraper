World number eight Indian shuttler Kidambi Srikanth defeated world number one Denmark's Viktor Axelsen to advance to the semi-finals of the Denmark Open on Friday. Srikanth, who already has clinched two Superseries titles this year, won with the scoreline of 14-21, 22-20, 21-7. Meanwhile, HS Prannoy and Saina Nehwal crashed out of the tournament after losing their respective quarters.
