Real Madrid winger Cristiano Ronaldo has been named the world's highest paid athlete by Forbes for the second year in a row, with earnings of $93 million (₹598.6 crore). Ronaldo's pay includes a salary of $58 million (₹373 crore) and endorsements worth $35 million (₹225 crore). Ronaldo's cumulative earnings in 2016 were $88 million with a salary of $56 million.
