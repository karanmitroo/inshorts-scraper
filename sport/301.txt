Delhi-based Soumya B broke the national race walking record by 12 seconds, winning the women's 20 km event at the National Championships on Sunday. Soumya, who clocked 1:31.28.72, also qualified for the upcoming Commonwealth Games in Australia. Trailing after 19 laps out of the 20, Soumya overtook Olympian and then national record holder Khushbir Kaur in the last lap.
