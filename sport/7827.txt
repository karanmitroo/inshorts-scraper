India defeated England by 10 wickets in the T20 World Cup for Blind 2017 on Thursday, clinching their third win in four matches. Batting first, England were bowled out for 158 in 19.4 overs. India chased down the target in 11 overs without losing any wicket, with openers Sukhram Majhi and Ganesh Babubhai Mundakar scoring 67*(32) and 78*(34) respectively.
