Kuldeep Yadav and Yuzvendra Chahal have taken 21 wickets of the 28 picked by Indian bowlers in three ODIs against South Africa. India and SA have used 13 other bowlers so far, who took combined 16 wickets. Yadav and Chahal picked four wickets each on Wednesday, marking the first-ever instance of two Indian spinners taking 4+ wickets in an ODI.
