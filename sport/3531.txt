Shuttler Saina Nehwal defeated Scotland's Kirsty Gilmour 21-19, 18-21, 21-15 on Friday to reach the Badminton World Championships semifinals for the second straight time, assuring India of two medals. Earlier, PV Sindhu had defeated China's Sun Yu to enter the semis. This will be the first time that India will have two singles' medalists at the Badminton World Championships.
