India ended day two of the Dharamsala Test against Australia at 248/6, trailing by 52 runs with KL Rahul and Cheteshwar Pujara both scoring half-centuries. Spinner Nathan Lyon picked up four wickets, while pacers Pat Cummins and Josh Hazlewood got a scalp each for Australia. Ravindra Jadeja and Wriddhiman Saha remained unbeaten at 16 and 10 respectively at stumps.
