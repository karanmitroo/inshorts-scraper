Recalling former captain Sourav Ganguly's 'shirtless' celebration after winning the NatWest series, Virender Sehwag tweeted, "14 years ago on this day we won the NatWestSeries & Sourav Ganguly did a Salman Khan & won everyone's hearts". Sehwag had contributed with an innings of 45 runs in 49 balls in the final.
