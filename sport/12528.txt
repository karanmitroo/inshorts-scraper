India sealed a spot in the Davis Cup World Group Playoff on Saturday after taking an unassailable 3-0 lead in their Group 1 tie against South Korea in Chandigarh. After Ramkumar Ramanathan and Saketh Myneni won their respective matches, Leander Paes and Rohan Bopanna made it 3-0 with a straight sets 6-3, 6-4, 6-4 win.
