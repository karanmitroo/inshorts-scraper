Former Sri Lankan cricketer, Muttiah Muralitharan has claimed that the BCCI owes him and his teammate Mahela Jayawardene $400,000 and $500,000 respectively​. Both the players were part of the IPL franchise Kochi Tuskers, which did not compensate the players and was later terminated by BCCI. Notably, a court order required BCCI to pay ₹550 crores for terminating the franchise.
