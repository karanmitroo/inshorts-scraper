South Africa defeated hosts New Zealand by four wickets (D/L method) in the first ODI of the five-match series. NZ posted 207/7 in 34 overs (reduced due to rain), driven by Kane Williamson's 59(53) while SA pacer Chris Morris took four wickets. SA achieved the target with 1 ball remaining, helped by De Kock's 69(64) and De Villiers' unbeaten 37(34).
