South African opener Hashim Amla has become the fastest batsman to score 7000 runs in ODI cricket, reaching the milestone against England in his 150th innings on Monday. Interestingly, the Proteas batsman was also the fastest to reach 2000, 3000, 4000, 5000, and 6000 ODI runs. The record for the fastest 7000 ODI runs was earlier held by Virat Kohli.
