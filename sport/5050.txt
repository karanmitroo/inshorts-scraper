Former cricketers Sunil Gavaskar and Ravi Shastri were paid ₹44.5 lakh each for commentating in the four-Test India-Australia series earlier this year. The highest paid commentator by the BCCI was former Australian captain Michael Clarke, who received ₹61.3 lakh for the four Tests. The least-paid commentator was Sanjay Manjrekar, who received ₹33.6 lakh from the BCCI.
