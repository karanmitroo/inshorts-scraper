The Cricket Academy of Pathans, a coaching institute run by Indian cricketers Irfan and Yusuf Pathan, has sponsored the training of two youngsters from Jammu and Kashmir at their Noida-based academy. Under the program, which is being run in association with the Indian Army, 18-year-old Danish Qadeer and 20-year-old Shahrukh Hussein have been selected after trials in the Kupwara district.
