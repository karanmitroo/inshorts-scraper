Santi Cazorla, Mesut Özil and Alexis Sánchez scored one goal apiece to hand Arsenal a 3-1 win over Watford on Saturday. Cazorla opened the scoring with a 9th-minute penalty followed by Sánchez and Özil scoring within a gap of five minutes near the half-time mark. Watford managed to pull one back courtesy Roberto Pereyra's goal in the 57th minute.
