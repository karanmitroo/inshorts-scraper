Ajit Agarkar, who is the quickest fast bowler to reach 50 ODI wickets, slammed the fastest ODI fifty for India off 21 deliveries against Zimbabwe on December 14, 2000. Agarkar surpassed Kapil Dev, who had scored fifty off 22 balls on March 29, 1983, against Windies. Agarkar scored 67*(25) and took three wickets to help India win the match.
