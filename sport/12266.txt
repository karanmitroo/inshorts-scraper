Opener KL Rahul's 158 took India to 358/5 at stumps on Day 2 in the second Test against West Indies in Jamaica on Sunday. Spinner Roston Chase took two wickets for WI, while Shannon Gabriel and Devendra Bishoo got one wicket each. India lead the hosts by 162 runs, with Ajinkya Rahane and Wriddhiman Saha still at the crease.
