Indian opener Rohit Sharma scored his first ODI century as captain and overall 16th ODI hundred against Sri Lanka in Mohali on Wednesday. Sharma surpassed Sehwag's 15 ODI tons and is only behind Sachin Tendulkar (49), Virat Kohli (32) and Sourav Ganguly (22). It was Rohit's 50th 50+ score in ODI cricket.
