MS Dhoni scored a first-half brace to help Virat Kohli-led All Heart FC defeat Abhishek Bachchan-led All Stars FC 7-3 in the charity football match called 'Celebrity Clasico' on Sunday. Dhoni opened the scoring for All Heart FC in the 7th minute before converting a free-kick later in the first-half. Virat Kohli and Shikhar Dhawan also scored a goal each.
