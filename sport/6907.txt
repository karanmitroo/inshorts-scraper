Afghanistan's Mohammad Shahzad displaced Indian captain Virat Kohli as the fourth highest run scorer in T20Is after scoring a 43-ball 72 against Ireland in the series' last T20I on Sunday. Shahzad, who has scored 1,779 runs in 58 T20I matches so far, is only behind Brendon McCullum, Tillakaratne Dilshan and Martin Guptill in top scorers list in T20Is.
