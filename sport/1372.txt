Windies' all-rounder Dwayne Bravo has become the first cricketer in history of T20 cricket to take 400 wickets. Bravo achieved the feat by taking his T20 career's second five-wicket haul while playing for Melbourne Renegades against Hobart Hurricanes in the Big Bash League on Thursday. Bravo is followed by Lasith Malinga, who has taken 331 wickets in T20s.
