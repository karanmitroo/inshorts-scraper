Nineteen-year-old Rishabh Pant has been named the captain of the Delhi one-day team, replacing Gautam Gambhir. Pant, whose first assignment as captain will be the upcoming Vijay Hazare Trophy, made his debut for India in the final T20I against England, becoming India's youngest player in T20I cricket. Notably, Pant was the fourth-highest run-scorer in Ranji Trophy 2016-17.
