Indian shuttler PV Sindhu, who is the first Indian woman to win a silver medal in Olympics shared a picture of herself on the first anniversary of her medal win and called the win 'a dream'. "This was the day 'A DREAM' RIO OLYMPICS 2016 19/08/2016 #throwback #oneyearalready #cantbelieve #silvermedal#thosemomentsinlife," wrote Sindhu, who is also the youngest Indian Olympic medalist.
