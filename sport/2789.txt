Indian all-rounder Hardik Pandya on Tuesday clarified about the viral picture a media house circulated of  himself alongside a girl, whom they called 'mystery lady'. Pandya responded to their tweet, writing, "Mystery solved! That's my sister." Reacting to the 23-year-old all-rounder's response, his brother Krunal Pandya tweeted, "[M]an of the match goes to @hardikpandya7  killed it bro."
