Indian spinner Ravichandran Ashwin registered his 19th five-wicket haul in Test cricket, after posting figures of 6-132 in New Zealand's second innings on Monday. Ashwin's overall figures of 10-225 was his fifth 10-wicket haul in Test cricket, equalling compatriot Harbhajan Singh's tally to have the joint second most number of ten-wicket hauls by an Indian bowler.
