Switzerland's world number three Stan Wawrinka will face Serbia's Novak Djokovic in his first US Open final on Sunday. Wawrinka overcame Japan's Kei Nishikori 4-6, 7-5, 6-4, 6-2, following Djokovic's 6-3, 6-2, 3-6, 6-2 win over Frenchman Gael Monfils. Chasing his third Grand Slam title, Wawrinka has two wins in five Grand Slam matches against 12-time Grand Slam champion, Djokovic.
