Former Indian cricketer Sachin Tendulkar posted a video on Twitter, wishing Happy Diwali to Indian soldiers. The tweet's caption read, "Snow-capped mountains, blazing deserts, dense forests & a lot more couldn't stop them. Let us send our love & #Sandesh2Soldiers this Diwali". Earlier cricketers including Virender Sehwag, Virat Kohli and Yuvraj Singh had also posted similar videos.
