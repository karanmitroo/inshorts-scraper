The official song of the FIFA Under-17 World Cup, Kar Ke Dikhla De Goal, has been formally launched. The music video of the song features former Indian football captain Bhaichung Bhutia, woman footballer Bala Devi and cricket legend Sachin Tendulkar. The song has been sung by artists including Shaan and Sunidhi Chauhan, and also includes a rap by Abhishek Bachchan.
