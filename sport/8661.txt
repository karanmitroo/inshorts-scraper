Argentina's Horacio Zeballos took a selfie with world number two Novak Djokovic after losing to him 6-3, 6-4 in the Qatar Open on Wednesday. Djokovic was packing his kit bag when Zeballos requested for a picture, which he later posted on Twitter. "Making a selfie, that's the first time I have had this experience...Horacio, well done, very original." said Djokovic.
