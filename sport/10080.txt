England ended Day 4 of the Rajkot Test at 114/0 and now lead India by 163 in the second innings. India, helped by Ashwin's 70 late in the innings, earlier finished their innings at 488 and conceded a first innings lead of 49. Adil Rashid claimed four wickets, while the other spinners, Moeen Ali and Zafar Ansari bagged two each.
