American tennis star Venus Williams, aged 37 years, became the oldest Wimbledon semi-finalist in 23 years after defeating French Open 2017 champion Jelena Ostapenko 6-3, 7-5 on Tuesday. French Open 2016 champion Garbine Muguruza powered past seventh seed Svetlana Kuznetsova 6-3, 6-4 to enter the Wimbledon semi-finals. This will be Venus' 10th semi-final appearance at Wimbledon.
