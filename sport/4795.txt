BCCI has decided to invite more applications for the position of Indian cricket team's head coach after Anil Kumble stepped down on Tuesday. Kumble refused to extend his contract till the West Indies tour, citing captain Virat Kohli's reservation over his 'style' of functioning. Virender Sehwag, Tom Moody, Lalchand Rajput, Richard Pybus, Dodda Ganesh are already in the application process.
