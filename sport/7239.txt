Karachi Kings' West Indian all-rounder Kieron Pollard hit two sixes off the final two balls of the match to help his side clinch a 5-wicket win against Lahore Qalandars in the Pakistan Super League. Pollard, who scored 45*(20), helped Karachi bring down the equation to 14 off 6 balls from 37 off 18, before finishing the match off.
