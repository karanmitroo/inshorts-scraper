South Africa took a 4-0 lead in the five-match ODI series against Australia after registering a six-wicket win in the fourth ODI on Sunday. Batting first, Australia were dismissed for 167 in 36.4 overs, courtesy Kyle Abbott's figures of 4/40. In reply, South African captain Faf du Plessis' 69(87) helped South Africa chase down the total in 35.3 overs.
