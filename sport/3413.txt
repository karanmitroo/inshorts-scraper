Sri Lankan pacer Lasith Malinga on Thursday picked up his 300th wicket in ODIs after dismissing Indian captain Virat Kohli, who scored 131 runs off 96 balls. Malinga took 203 matches to reach 300 wickets, becoming the fifth fastest bowler in terms of matches to achieve the feat. Notably, Malinga's 200th ODI dismissal was the then Indian captain MS Dhoni.
