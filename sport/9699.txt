Ravichandran Ashwin first dropped England captain Alastair Cook at 23* during the Mohali Test on Saturday but took his wicket five overs later. In the 11th over, Cook played a Mohammed Shami delivery towards midwicket, where Ashwin dropped a sitter. In the 16th over, Ashwin bowled a short delivery which was caught behind by Parthiv Patel, dismissing Cook at 27.
