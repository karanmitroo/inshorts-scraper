Reportedly, an FIR has been filed against Sakshi Dhoni, wife of MS Dhoni, following unpaid dues over shares bought in a private firm. Gurgaon-based Dennis Arora filed a multi-crore fraud case against Sakshi and co-directors of Rhiti MSD Almode, alleging that they bought shares in his company, Sportsfit World Private Limited, but paid only ₹2.25 crores instead of ₹11 crores.
