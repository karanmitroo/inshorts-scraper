Wishing former Indian pacer Venkatesh Prasad on his 47th birthday, ex-Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag tweeted, “Hpy Bday Venkatesh.The Prasad u gave all Indians by getting Aamir Sohail bowled in 96WC is still fresh in our hearts.” Sehwag referred to Prasad picking up Pakistani opener Aamir Sohail’s wicket in the quarterfinal of 1996 World Cup.
