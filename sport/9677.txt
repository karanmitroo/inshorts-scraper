India reached 271/6 at the end of the second day of the third Test against England at Mohali on Sunday. The Indian innings was driven by Cheteshwar Pujara's 51(104) and Virat Kohli's 62(127), while England's Adil Rashid picked up three wickets. All-rounders Ravichandran Ashwin and Ravindra Jadeja remained not out on 57(82) and 31(59) at stumps.
