English Premier League club, Chelsea have completed the signing of French midfielder, N'golo Kante from Leicester City on a five-year-long contract. Reportedly, the fee paid by Chelsea for the 25-year-old is around £32 million. The Foxes had signed Kante for £5.6 million from Caen before the start of the 2015/16 season, during which they won their maiden Premier League title.
