South Africa's Andile Phehlukwayo slammed 23 runs in five balls in the Pink ODI against India to record the highest strike rate (460) in an international innings (minimum five balls). Phehlukwayo surpassed Windies' Dwayne Smith, who scored 29(7) at a strike rate of 414.28 against Bangladesh in 2007. Phehlukwayo's innings helped SA beat India to stay alive in the series.
