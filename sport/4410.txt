Windies defeated India by 9 wickets in the one-off T20I at Kingston on Sunday, chasing down 190 to register their highest successful T20I chase at home. Windies' Evin Lewis became the third batsman to score two centuries in T20I cricket. This was Chris Gayle's first international match since April 2016, while Dinesh Karthik featured in a T20I after seven years.
