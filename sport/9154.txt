Virender Sehwag has suggested that in-form Virat Kohli should change his name to 'Badal'. Praising Kohli for his phenomenal form, Sehwag tweeted, "Kohli ko paper me name change Ad daalna chahiye. He should call himself Badal, aka Cloud. Har waqt Badal ki tarah Chaaye rehte hain,Kohli!" Kohli is the highest scorer in the ongoing Test series with 640 runs.
