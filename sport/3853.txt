Cricketer Yuvraj Singh likened pacer Umesh Yadav to 'Sattu Supari' after the latter shared a picture of himself with The Great Khali. Sattu Supari is a character in Bollywood movie 'Golmaal: Fun Unlimited', played by Siddharth Jadhav. Yadav had captioned the picture, "When Strength meets Size...Met the big man with an even bigger heart...Glad to have met you Great Khali."
