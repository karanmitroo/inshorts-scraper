Indian triple jumper Renjith Maheshwary qualified for the Rio Olympics, at the 4th Indian Grand Prix in Bengaluru on Sunday with a jump of 17.3 metres. Maheshwary beat the Olympic qualification mark of 16.85 metres and also broke the 17.17 metres national record. Maheshwary has competed in the Beijing 2008 and London 2012 Olympics, and Rio will be his third.
