Indian opener Rohit Sharma and captain Virat Kohli slammed a hundred each against New Zealand in the third ODI on Sunday to become this year's top two ODI run-scorers. Sharma notched his 15th ODI ton, overtaking Joe Root to become 2017's second-highest run-scorer in ODIs behind Kohli, who slammed his 32nd century and crossed 1,400 ODI runs this year.
