India ended Day 3 of the Chennai Test at 391/4, driven by opener Lokesh Rahul's career-best score of 199. Resuming the day at 60/0, Rahul along with Parthiv Patel (71) put up an opening stand of 152 runs, highest by Indian openers in Tests this year. Adil Rashid, Moeen Ali, Ben Stokes and Stuart Broad took one wicket each.
