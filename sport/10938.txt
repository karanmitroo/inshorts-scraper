Neha Tomar, an 18-year-old female wrestler from Dehradun, defeated a male champion, Nawaab, in a wrestling bout in Bareilly on Monday. This was the second instance of Neha defeating a male wrestler, having won a bout in the same competition against an Uttarakhand wrestler in 2014. The 58 kg wrestler, nicknamed 'Sultan', has been wrestling since she was 11.
