Sevilla ended Real Madrid's record-setting 40-match unbeaten streak with a 2-1 win over the table-toppers on Sunday. Cristiano Ronaldo scored his 12th league goal of the season after converting a penalty in the 67th minute, before Sergio Ramos' 85th-minute own goal levelled the match. Sevilla's Stevan Jovetic scored the match-winner in the 91st minute to end Madrid's unbeaten streak.
