Luis Suárez's 90th-minute goal helped Barcelona salvage a 1-1 draw against Real Betis in the La Liga on Sunday, their sixth league draw this season. Betis took the lead with Álex Alegría's 75th-minute strike. With his 16th goal, Suárez became the leading scorer of La Liga 2016-17. Barcelona are currently second in La Liga with 42 points.
