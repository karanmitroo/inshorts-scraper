The Williams sisters have earned combined ₹28.8 crore (Australian $5.6 million) as prize money after the Australian Open women's singles final. Serena played against sister Venus in a Grand Slam final after eight years, with their last meeting being in Wimbledon 2009. Serena, the winner of the final, was awarded A$3,700,000 (₹19 crore), while Venus got A$1,900,000 (₹9.8 crore).
