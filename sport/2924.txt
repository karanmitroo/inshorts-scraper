Cristiano Ronaldo found the net twice as Real Madrid registered a 3-1 win over Borussia Dortmund in the Champions League group stage on Tuesday. The brace helped Ronaldo, UCL's all-time leading goal scorer, to take his tally to 109 goals. Elsewhere, Manchester City claimed a second straight victory in the competition with a 2-0 win over Ukrainian club Shakhtar Donetsk.
