FIFA on Wednesday announced a ten-member shortlist for the Best Men's Coach of 2016, its manager of the year award. The list includes Wales' coach Chris Coleman, former Bayern Munich coach Pep Guardiola, Real Madrid's Zinedine Zidane, Atletico Madrid's Diego Simeone and Leicester City's Claudio Ranieri among others. The three finalists for the award will be unveiled on December 2.
