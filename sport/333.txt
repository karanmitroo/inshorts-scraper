With his 28th Man of the Match award against South Africa in the sixth ODI, India captain Virat Kohli has now won the third-most Man of the Match awards by an Indian cricketer. He surpassed veteran Indian all-rounder Yuvraj Singh, who has won 27 awards. Kohli is now only behind Sourav Ganguly (31) and Sachin Tendulkar (62) among Indians.
