Second seeds Sania Mirza and her Croatian partner Ivan Dodig reached the Australian Open mixed doubles semi-finals, after defeating Rohan Bopanna and Canada's Gabriela Dabrowski 6-4, 3-6, 12-10 in the quarter-final on Wednesday. Sania and Dodig had crashed out in the semi-finals in the tournament's 2016 edition. Meanwhile, Leander Paes will play the quarters alongside Martina Hingis on Thursday.
