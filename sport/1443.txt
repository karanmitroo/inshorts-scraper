World number three and Olympic silver medallist PV Sindhu settled for a silver at the year-ending World Superseries Finals in Dubai on Sunday. She lost to Japan's 20-year-old world number two Akane Yamaguchi 21-15, 12-21, 19-21 in the final. Sindhu was aiming for her fourth title of the year, after having won Syed Modi International, India Open and Korea Open.
