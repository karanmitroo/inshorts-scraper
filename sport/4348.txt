World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) COO Triple H has gifted a customised WWE Championship belt to IPL 2017 winners Mumbai Indians. Triple H tweeted a photo of the customised championship belt with the caption, "@MIPaltan @ImRo45 as promised, the @WWE Title is on its way...Congratulations! @WWEIndia." Notably, MI won IPL the same day Indian-origin wrestler Jinder Mahal was crowned WWE champion.
