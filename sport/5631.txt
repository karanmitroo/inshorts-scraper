PV Sindhu and Saina Nehwal teamed up for a doubles match, representing Bharat Petroleum at a tournament in Bengaluru on Monday, but ended up losing the match. The team of India's top-ranked shuttlers was defeated 19-21, 21-18, 20-22 by N Sikki Reddy and Aparna Balan. The two had earlier teamed up for the Uber Cup in Delhi in 2014.
