Out of the 90 hat-tricks witnessed in international cricket till now, only one has been an all-LBW hat-trick which was achieved by Pakistani bowler Aaqib Javed against India in 1991. Javed took the wickets of Ravi Shastri, Mohammed Azharuddin and Sachin Tendulkar in  consecutive deliveries to help Pakistan win the ODI tri-series final at Sharjah.
