Chennaiyin FC came out on top in the repeat of last year's final against FC Goa, defeating them 2-0 at the Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium on Thursday. Hans Mulder gave Chennaiyin the lead in the 15th minute, which was doubled by Mehrajuddin Wadoo with a deflected strike in the 26th minute. FC Goa remain bottom of the table with zero points.
