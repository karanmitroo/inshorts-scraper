On the occasion of Indian cricketer Suresh Raina's 30th birthday, former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag tweeted, "When people ask, Have you seen Aaina? (mirror) Answer is, I have seen Raina. Happy Birthday Suresh Raina. @ImRaina". Suresh Raina has played 18 Tests, 223 ODIs and 62 T20Is for India and is the first Indian to score a T20I hundred.
