PM Narendra Modi and President Ram Nath Kovind congratulated team India on winning the Under-19 World Cup for a record fourth time. PM Modi said, "Absolutely thrilled by the stupendous achievement of our young cricketers. Congratulations to them on winning." "Proud of captain @Shaw_Prithvi and his mates, as well as of coach Rahul Dravid," part of President Kovind's tweet read.
