Virat Kohli recorded his sixth Test 150, his fifth as captain, against Bangladesh at Hyderabad on Friday, to equal the record for most 150-plus scores by an Indian captain in Tests. Kohli equalled Sunil Gavaskar and Mohammad Azharuddin's record, who both have five 150-plus Test scores each as captain. Notably, Kohli's last five Test centuries have been of 150+ runs.
