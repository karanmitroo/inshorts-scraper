Indian batsman Gautam Gambhir, who turned 36 on Saturday, was the top scorer for India in the 2007 and 2011 World Cup finals that India won. Gambhir smashed a 54-ball 75 against Pakistan in the final of the inaugural World T20 in 2007, which India won by five runs. In the 2011 World Cup, Gambhir scored 97(122) against Sri Lanka.
