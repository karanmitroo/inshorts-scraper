India registered a six-wicket victory over New Zealand in the first ODI of the five-match series, in Dharamshala on Sunday. Winning the toss and chasing, India achieved the 191-run target with 16.5 overs to spare. Virat Kohli top-scored for the hosts, scoring 85* off 81 balls, while bowlers Amit Mishra and debutant Hardik Pandya both grabbed three wickets each.
