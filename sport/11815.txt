Sports minister Vijay Goel called shuttler PV Sindhu and wrestler Sakshi Malik Rio gold medalists, during a media interaction on Sunday. Sakshi had won a bronze medal at the Rio Olympics, while Sindhu became India's first female individual silver medalist. Earlier, Goel had tweeted wishes for athlete Srabani Nanda posting Dutee Chand's photo, besides misspelling gymnast Dipa Karmakar's name.
