Bengal pacers Mohammed Shami and Ashok Dinda bowled with nine slips during their Ranji Trophy match against Chhattisgarh on Tuesday. The fielding placement was used for Chhattisgarh's second innings, in which they were following on after Bengal's 529/7d, eventually losing by an innings and 160 runs. Shami and Dinda combined for a total of 18 wickets during the match.
