India collapsed for 189 on the first day of the Bengaluru Test against Australia on Saturday, with KL Rahul top-scoring with 90(205). Aussie spinner Nathan Lyon, registering the best bowling figures by an overseas bowler in India, grabbed 8/50, while Steve O'Keefe and pacer Mitchell Starc got one wicket each. Australia ended the day at 40/0 in their first innings.
