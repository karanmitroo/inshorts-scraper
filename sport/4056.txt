Cricketer Rohit Sharma posted a picture on Instagram from Team India's FIFA session following their win over Sri Lanka in the first Test on Saturday. The picture, captioned, "Victory tastes as good when we play FIFA #PostMatchFifaSessions #Mates," includes Shikhar Dhawan, Cheteshwar Pujara, KL Rahul and Wriddhiman Saha. Rohit did not play the match while Dhawan and Pujara scored centuries.
