Pakistan-born South African cricketer Imran Tahir on Saturday became the first spinner to take a hat-trick in the Pakistan Super League. Multan Sultans' Tahir achieved the feat by picking up the wickets of Quetta Gladiators' Hasan Khan, John Hastings and Rahat Ali. Overall, Tahir is the third bowler with a hat-trick in the PSL after Junaid Khan and Mohammad Amir.
