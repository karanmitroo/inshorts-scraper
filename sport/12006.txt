Former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag brought forth the issue of female infanticide after Sakshi Malik's bronze medal at the Rio Olympics. Malik thanked Sehwag for the wishes tweeting, "@virendersehwag thank you sir...your wishes means a lot". Sachin Tendulkar also congratulated Malik tweeting, "Congratulations #SakshiMalik & All Indians! आखिरकार भारत को सम्मान दिलाने का काम एक बेटी ने ही किया".
