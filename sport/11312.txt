India finished at 159/1 at stumps on day three of the first Test against New Zealand at Kanpur, with Cheteshwar Pujara and Murali Vijay at the crease. Pujara scored his second half-century of the match to give India a 215-run lead over NZ. Earlier, all-rounder Ravindra Jadeja took a five-wicket haul to restrict NZ to 262 in their first innings.
