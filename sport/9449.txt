On the occasion of former Indian pacer Ajit Agarkar's 39th birthday, former Indian batsman Virender Sehwag tweeted, "Happy Birthday @imAagarkar. 288 wickets,191 ODI matches - big achievement. 100 at Lord's - bigger achievement. 5 Test Ducks in a row - Biggest achievement. #ModernDayAryabhatt." Notably, Ajit Agarkar holds the record for the fastest fifty in ODIs by an Indian.
