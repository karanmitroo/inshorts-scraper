Pragyan Ojha, who won the Purple Cap for taking most wickets in the 2010 IPL, went unsold for the third straight IPL auction on Sunday. Ojha had a base price of ₹50 lakh this year as compared to ₹30 lakh last year. The left-arm spinner, who represented Deccan Chargers and Mumbai Indians, has taken 89 wickets in 92 IPL matches.
