Indian batsman Cheteshwar Pujara scored his 13th Test century and completed 4,000 Test runs during the second Test against Sri Lanka, which is his career's 50th Test. Pujara took 84 innings to reach the 4,000-run landmark, the same as Rahul Dravid. This is Pujara's third century in five innings on Sri Lankan soil, while nine have come in India.
