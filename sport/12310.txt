India finished Day 1 of the second Test against West Indies at 126/1 after dismissing the Windies' lineup at 196 on Saturday. West Indies' Jermaine Blackwood scored 62 before getting dismissed by Ravichandran Ashwin, who finished with 5/52. Lokesh Rahul (75*) and Cheteshwar Pujara (18*) held the crease at the end of the play, as India trail by 70 runs.
