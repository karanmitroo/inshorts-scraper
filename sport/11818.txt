Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna awardee, Indian gymnast Dipa Karmakar's coach Bishweshwar Nandi was awarded the Dronacharya Award on Monday. "[My] joy is doubled as my coach also got the Dronacharya Award, I am very happy for him," Dipa said after receiving her award on Monday. Moreover, Virat Kohli's coach Raj Kumar Sharma was also conferred​ with the Dronacharya Award.​​
