Indian tennis player Sania Mirza, who is on vacation in London, shared a picture on Instagram with Bollywood actress Shilpa Shetty after the two met in the city. The picture, captioned, "London nights @theshilpashetty," shows Sania and Shilpa, both dressed in black, posing along with a friend. Sania has been vacationing in London after Wimbledon with husband Shoaib Malik.
