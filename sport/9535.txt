Dairy cooperative Amul has released a new poster on cricketer Yuvraj Singh's wedding with Hazel Keech. The poster has been captioned, "Yuvraj as Dulharaj". It further reads, "Amul Isse Hazzel Karo'. Yuvraj and Hazel got married on Wednesday at the Fatehgarh Sahib gurudwara in Punjab. The wedding was preceded by a mehendi and a sangeet ceremony on Tuesday.
