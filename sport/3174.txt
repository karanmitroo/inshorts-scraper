The Indian contingent of swimmers won 47 medals, including eight gold medals, at the 9th Asian Age-group Aquatic Championship. Alongside the eight golds, India won 15 silver and 24 bronze medals. Sajan Prakash, Anshul Kothari, and Shivani Kataria won two medals each at the championship, which started on September 8 in Tashkent, Uzbekistan.
