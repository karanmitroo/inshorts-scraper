World number two Andy Murray reached the Wimbledon semi-final after defeating Frenchman Jo-Wilfried Tsonga 7-6, 6-1, 3-6, 4-6, 6-1 in their quarter-final match on Thursday. Despite Tsonga's comeback from two sets down, Murray won the final set to reach his seventh Wimbledon semi-final. The Scotsman will face Czech Republic's Tomas Berdych in the semi-final.
