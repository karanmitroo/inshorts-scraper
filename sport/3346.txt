Virat Kohli crossed 1000 ODI runs in the calendar year 2017 during the fifth ODI against Sri Lanka on Sunday. This was the fifth time Kohli crossed 1000 ODI runs in a year, having done it before in  2011, 2012, 2013 and 2014. Sachin Tendulkar holds the record for scoring 1000+ runs in a calendar year most times, at 7.
