Indian captain Virat Kohli equalled MS Dhoni and Rahul Dravid's record of most consecutive ODI victories for an Indian captain. Dhoni led India to nine straight victories from November 14, 2008 to February 5, 2009 while Dravid won nine straight ODIs as captain in 2006. Kohli-led India has won nine successive ODIs from July 6, 2017 to September 24, 2017.
