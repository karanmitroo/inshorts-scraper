Indian Test captain Virat Kohli remained the top-ranked T20I batsman, according to ICC's latest T20I rankings. Following his 145-run innings against Sri Lanka, Australian batsman Glenn Maxwell jumped​ 16 positions to reach a career best ranking of third. Maxwell also jumped two places above Pakistan’s Shahid Afridi and Bangladesh’s Shakib Al Hasan to become the top-ranked all-rounder​ in T20Is.
