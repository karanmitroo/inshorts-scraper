As per reports, BCCI's legal bill since October 2013 exceeded ₹100 crore, with the board spending more than ₹9 lakh per day in court. The expenditure comprised expenses to fight the 2013 IPL spot-fixing scandal and included the appearance fee of legal experts including Lodha case counsel, Kapil Sibal. Reportedly, the expenses of the Lodha Committee were ₹3.5 crore.
