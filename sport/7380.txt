Manchester United defeated Blackburn 2-1 at Ewood Park on Sunday, thereby reaching the quarter-finals of the FA Cup. Danny Graham gave Blackburn the lead in the 17th minute before Marcus Rashford cancelled it out with a 27th-minute strike. Zlatan Ibrahimovic scored the winner, his 24th goal of the season, with his first-touch from Paul Pogba's assist in the 75th minute.
