Kamlesh Nagarkoti, the most expensive India U-19 World Cup team player at IPL auction, revealed that he sat inside the washroom while his bidding was going on as he was nervous. The 18-year-old fast bowler was bought by Kolkata Knight Riders for ₹3.2 crore. Pacer Shivam Mavi, sold for ₹3 crore, was the second-most expensive among India U-19 players.
