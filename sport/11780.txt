World number one Andy Murray progressed to the second round of US Open 2016, defeating Czech Republic's Lukas Rosol 6-3, 6-2, 6-2 on Wednesday. Murray, coming off a 22 match win streak which ended in Cincinnati Open final, won the Wimbledon and Olympic gold during the run. Meanwhile, Australia's world number 16 Nick Kyrgios also progressed to the second round.
