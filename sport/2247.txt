Virat Kohli, during his 65-run knock against New Zealand on Saturday, became the first Indian cricketer to score 7,000 runs in T20 cricket. Kohli reached the 7,000-run mark in his 212th T20 innings, becoming the second fastest to achieve the feat behind Windies' Chris Gayle (192). Gayle holds the record for most T20 runs, with 10,571 runs in 309 matches.
