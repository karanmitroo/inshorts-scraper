India won their fourth match in the T20 World Cup for Blind after defeating South Africa by 9 wickets on Friday. Batting first, South Africa managed to score 157/8 in their 20 overs with India's Ganesh Babubhai Mundakar picking two wickets for 14 runs. Fifties by Iqbal Jafar and Muhammed Farhan helped India reach the target in 13.5 overs.
