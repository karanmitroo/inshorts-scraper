Jacques Kallis slammed 90*(37) to help Royals defeat Virender Sehwag-led Palace Diamonds by 8 wickets in the last T20 match of the St Moritz Ice Cricket tournament on Friday. Batting first, Diamonds scored 205/5 driven by Andrew Symonds' 67*(42) and Mohammad Kaif's 57(30). Shahid Afridi-led Royals chased down the target in 16.4 overs to record a 2-0 whitewash.
