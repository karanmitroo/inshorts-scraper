World no 124 Jana Cepelova beat French Open women’s champion Garbine Muguruza in the second round of Wimbledon on Thursday. Unseeded Cepelova won the match in straight sets 6-3, 6-2 marking her third win over a top-five opponent in her career. Muguruza reached the women’s singles final in The Championships last year, but lost to Serena Williams.
