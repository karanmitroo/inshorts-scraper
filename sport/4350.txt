Cricketer Gautam Gambhir, who was blessed with his second child on June 21, has shared a picture of his daughters on Twitter, naming his newborn daughter 'Anaiza'. Gambhir, who got married in October 2011, already has a three-year-old daughter named Aazeen. The cricketer captioned the picture, "Two little princess who rule our hearts, Aazeen and Anaiza!"
