South Africa's Shane Dadswell hit the highest known 50-over individual score of 490 runs off 151 balls on his 20th birthday on Saturday. The knock included 27 fours and 57 sixes and helped his side NWU Pukke post 677/3 against Potch Dorp. The previous highest known individual score in 50-over cricket was 486*, scored by Ooty's Sankruth Sriram in 2014.
