Cricketer Harbhajan Singh shared an old picture of himself with Yuvraj Singh on Instagram on Thursday. The photo, captioned, "Oye ki dekh reha mere phone ch @yuvisofficial," shows Yuvraj looking into Harbhajan's phone while the latter is typing something. Harbhajan recently shared a video of himself, congratulating Yuvraj when the latter played his 300th ODI for India.
