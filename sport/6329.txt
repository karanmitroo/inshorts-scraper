Former cricketer and India U-19 coach Rahul Dravid missed his felicitation ceremony held during the inauguration of IPL 10 in Hyderabad on Wednesday. Dravid reportedly failed to reach the venue on time since his flight from Delhi to Hyderabad got delayed. He was supposed to be felicitated by BCCI along with Sachin Tendulkar, Sourav Ganguly, VVS Laxman and Virender Sehwag.
