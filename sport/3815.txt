A 24-year-old Swiss football fan has been handed a prison term of 36 months for setting off fireworks at a football match which damaged another supporter's hearing. He has also been fined 9,700 Swiss francs (₹6.4 lakh) and ordered to pay 15,000 francs (₹10 lakh) in court costs and 12,000 francs (₹8 lakh) to the fan whose hearing was damaged.
