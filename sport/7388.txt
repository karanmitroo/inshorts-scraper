Sri Lankan all-rounder Asela Gunaratne hit three boundaries in the final over of the 2nd T20I against Australia to hand SL the three-match series 2-0. Nuwan Kulasekara's four-wicket haul helped Sri Lanka dismiss Australia for 173. In reply, Chamara Kapugedera's 32-ball 32 and Gunaratne's unbeaten 46-ball 84 helped Sri Lanka beat Australia in the final over of the match.
