World number four Indian shuttler Kidambi Srikanth defeated compatriot HS Prannoy in the French Open semi-final on Saturday to reach his fifth Superseries final of the year. Srikanth won the match with a scoreline of 14-21, 21-19, 21-18 in 62 minutes. Out of the four Superseries finals this year, Srikanth has won three and lost one.
