Indian batsmen Murali Vijay and Cheteshwar Pujara slammed their 10th and 14th Test tons respectively as India ended the second day of the second Test at 312/2, leading Sri Lanka by 107 runs. Pujara crossed 1,000 runs in Test cricket in 2017, becoming the first Indian to do so. Pujara and Vijay's 209-run stand was their fourth consecutive 100-run stand.
