The Wrestling Federation of India (WFI) has recommended two-time Olympic medal-winning wrestler Sushil Kumar's name to the Sports Ministry for the Padma Bhushan. Kumar, who had applied for the Padma Bhushan last year as well, is the lone wrestler to be recommended by the WFI. He was awarded the Padma Shri in 2011 and the Arjuna Award in 2005.
