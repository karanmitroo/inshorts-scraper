Indian all-rounder Irfan Pathan and his wife Safa Baig have become parents for the first time after the birth of their son on Tuesday. "Is ehsas ko Bayaan karna Mushkil hai...is me Ek behtareen si Kashish hay, blessed with a baby boy," the 32-year-old tweeted. Irfan married Safa on 4 February, 2016.
