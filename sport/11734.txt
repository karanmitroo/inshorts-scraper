Lauding Yogeshwar Dutt's gesture to let late wrestler Besik Kudukhov's family keep his Olympic silver medal, ex-cricketer Sachin Tendulkar tweeted, "A golden heart @DuttYogi. The gesture speaks volumes of your sporting spirit & sensitive personality. Proud of your act and achievements!" Earlier, Kudukhov failed a London 2012 doping retest, after which Dutt's bronze medal was upgraded to silver.
