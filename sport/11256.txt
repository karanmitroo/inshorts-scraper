Former Indian batsman Virender Sehwag named Indian spinner Ravichandran Ashwin as RecordChandran Ashwin in a tweet congratulating the bowler on becoming the fastest Indian to reach 200 wickets in Test cricket. Sehwag's tweet read, "RecordChandran Ashwin. Well done on the first set of 200 wickets. Surely many more sets to go @ashwinravi99."
