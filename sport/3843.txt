Arsenal player Theo Walcott was trolled on Twitter for the 'Om Namah Shivaya' tattoo in Devanagari script on his back, with users claiming that it was spelled as 'Om Namah Shavaya'. Users reacted with tweets like, "You've got incorrect letters. Last 3 letters mean dead body. Need to fix it," and "Theo Walcott is getting a BJP ticket in 2019."
