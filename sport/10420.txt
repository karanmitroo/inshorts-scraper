Cricketer Ajinkya Rahane presented his India jersey to Bayern Munich footballer Arjen Robben on Diwali, after the German club sent their personalised jersey for the cricketer. Bayern posted Robben's picture with Rahane's jersey on Facebook and captioned it, "Arjen Robben certainly enjoyed receiving his Diwali gift from Ajinkya Rahane! We hope all our Indian fans had a very happy Diwali!"
