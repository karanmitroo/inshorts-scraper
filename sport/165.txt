BCCI has accepted India Under-19 head coach Rahul Dravid's demand for parity in the cash prize awarded to the Under-19 team's support staff, following the team's World Cup victory. Dravid and other support staff members will now get ₹25 lakh each. Earlier, the BCCI had announced a ₹50-lakh reward for Rahul Dravid and ₹20 lakh each for other members.
