Following Indian shuttler PV Sindhu's silver medal finish at the Rio Olympics on Friday, users have been searching 'PV Sindhu caste' on Google. On typing 'PV Sindhu' in Google's search box, 'PV Sindhu caste' appears as the fifth search suggestion in the list. Google's search statistics show that search queries for Sindhu's caste-identity began after her semifinal win in Rio.
