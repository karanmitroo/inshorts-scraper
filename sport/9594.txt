Delhi cricketer Harsh Tyagi picked up five wickets without giving away a single run in an inter-school T20 match in Noida. Captaining his team, Tyagi bowled just 2.3 overs to claim the five-wicket haul. The opposition team was bowled out for 46, handing Tyagi's team victory by 215 runs. The bowler is currently a part of Delhi's Under-23 team.
