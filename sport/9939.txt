Indian cricket team coach Anil Kumble participated in a puja performed adjacent to the pitch by Andhra Cricket Association members before the India-England Test at Visakhapatnam on Thursday. The 'mahurat' was 9.30 am, with rituals like breaking a coconut and lighting incense sticks being performed. This is the first time Dr YSR Reddy ACA-VDCA stadium is hosting a Test match.
