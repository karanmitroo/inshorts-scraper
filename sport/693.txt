Royal Challengers Bangalore have bought all four fast bowlers who dismissed them for IPL's lowest-ever total of 49 runs, in last year's edition. Chris Woakes was bought for ₹7.4 crore, Umesh Yadav for ₹4.2 crore, and Nathan Coulter-Nile and Colin de Grandhomme for ₹2.2 crore each. Kolkata Knight Riders had defended 131 runs, with no RCB batsman reaching double digits.
