Great Britain's Andy Murray progressed through to the Wimbledon 2016 final after defeating Czech Republic's Tomas Berdych 6-3, 6-3, 6-3 in the semi-final on Friday. Murray, who won the Wimbledon 2013 title, will be playing in his third Wimbledon final against Canada's Milos Raonic on Sunday. The 29-year-old Scot holds a 6-3 head to head advantage over the Canadian.
