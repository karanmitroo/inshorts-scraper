Undefeated professional boxer Floyd Mayweather Jr. earned at least ₹37.7 lakh ($59,000) per second during his victory over Conor McGregor in the richest fight in boxing history on Saturday. Mayweather, who knocked out McGregor within 30 minutes, had a guaranteed purse of $100 million (₹640 crore). His final takeaway will include pay-per-view receipts, foreign sales and other revenue streams.
