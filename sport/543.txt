Former Pakistani captain Wasim Akram, aged 51, dismissed 36-year-old all-rounder Shoaib Malik for 2(4) during a 10-over exhibition match on Sunday. Sultan XI's Akram first beat Toofan XI's Malik off the second ball of his second over and then dismissed him caught behind off the next delivery. Akram ended with figures of 2-0-9-1 as his team won by 7 wickets.
