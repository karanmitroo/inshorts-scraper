India's middle-distance runners PU Chitra and G Lakshmanan bagged gold medals at the 5th Asian Indoor and Martial Arts Games in Turkmenistan on Tuesday. Chitra bagged the gold in women's 1,500m event while Lakhsmanan won the men's 3,000m to take India's gold tally in the event to three. Meanwhile, 18-year-old wrestler Dharmender won bronze in the men's freestyle 70kg category.
