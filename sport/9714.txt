Real Madrid's Cristiano Ronaldo scored two first-half goals to help his side register a 2-1 win over Sporting Gijon at the Santiago Bernabeu on Saturday. Ronaldo opened the scoring by converting a 5th-minute penalty, before doubling the lead with an 18th-minute header. Gijon's Carlos Carmona scored in the 35th minute before Duje Cop missed a penalty in the 78th minute.
