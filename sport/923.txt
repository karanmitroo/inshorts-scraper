World number nine CoCo Vandeweghe was fined $10,000 (over ₹6 lakh) for hurling abuse at her opponent Timea Babos in her first round Australian Open match. Vandeweghe was heard saying, "F*** off, you f***ing b***h" to Babos. Vandeweghe, who lost the match, said that she was irritated by her opponent's "excessive celebration" during the match.
