Reacting to a feud between siblings and Mumbai Indians players Hardik and Krunal Pandya, Virender Sehwag tweeted, "Baap Bada Na Bhaiyya, Sabse Bada Rupaiyya." A tweet by Hardik had hinted at a problem between the brothers to which Krunal had replied, "I am bade bhaiyya for a reason." Sehwag intervened and tweeted, "Lado mat yaar", with the hashtag #PandyaBrosFight.
