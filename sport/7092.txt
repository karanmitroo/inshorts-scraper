Indian skipper Virat Kohli has never fielded the same playing 11 in two consecutive Test matches during his 25-match captaincy tenure in the format. For the Bengaluru Test against Australia, which started on Saturday, Kohli made two changes, bringing in opener Abhinav Mukund in place of Murali Vijay, while Karun Nair was re-inducted to bring in six specialist batsmen.
