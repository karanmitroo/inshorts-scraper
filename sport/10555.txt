India went to the top of the table, defeating China 9-0 in the Asian Champions Trophy hockey in Malaysia on Tuesday. Akashdeep Singh, Affan Yousuf and Jasjit Kular scored a brace each, while Rupinder Pal Singh, Nikkin Thimmaiah and Lalit Upadhyay struck one each for India. The team are unbeaten in the tournament, winning three and drawing one match.
