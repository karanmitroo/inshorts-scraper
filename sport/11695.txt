Top-seeded pair of India's Sania Mirza and Croatia's Ivan Dodig crashed out of the 2016 US Open mixed doubles, after losing 3-6, 4-6 against unseeded Barbora Krejčíková and Marin Draganja in the second round on Sunday. However, Mirza along with her Czech partner Barbora Strycova have reached the third round in women’s doubles.
