After MS Dhoni and Bhuvneshwar Kumar's record 100-run eighth-wicket partnership helped India win against Sri Lanka in the second ODI, coach Ravi Shastri tweeted, "Witnessed a jail break in Kandy exactly half an hour to midnight #TeamIndia #SLvIND."
