Indian women's spinner Ekta Bisht ended with bowling figures of 5 wickets for 8 runs as India defeated Pakistan by seven wickets in the ICC Women's World Cup Qualifier on Sunday. Pakistan were all out for 67 in 43.4 overs, their 10th lowest total in ODIs. Ekta's figures of 10-7-8-5 are the fourth-best by an Indian in women's ODIs.
