Indian cricketer Jhulan Goswami has become the top-ranked female bowler in the ICC ODI rankings, seven years after she last held the spot. Goswami has 730 points, leading second-placed West Indian Stafanie Taylor by 79 points. The 33-year-old has picked 175 wickets for India in 148 games, and was part of the 2005 World Cup squad, which finished as runners-up.
