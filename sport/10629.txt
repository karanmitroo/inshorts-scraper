Wrestlers Geeta Phogat and Babita Phogat, the daughters of wrestler Mahavir Singh Phogat have praised actor Aamir Khan, who will be seen portraying Mahavir in his upcoming film 'Dangal'. "Despite being a star, he's very humble. He used to stop the shooting to meet our father," said Geeta. Babita said that Aamir is very "down to earth".
