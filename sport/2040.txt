Twenty-three-time Grand Slam champion Serena Williams will get married to her fiancé, Reddit Co-founder Alexis Ohanian, on Thursday. The wedding, which will be held in New Orleans, Louisiana, will cost over $1 million and will be attended by Prince Harry, Beyoncé and Eva Longoria, among others. Serena gave birth to a baby girl, named Alexis Olympia Ohanian Jr., in September.
