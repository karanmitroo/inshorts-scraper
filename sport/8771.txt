Manchester United defeated West Ham 2-0 in the Premier League on Monday, extending their unbeaten run to 11 matches. West Ham were down to 10 men after Sofiane Feghouli was sent off in the 13th minute. Juan Mata, assisted by Marcus Rashford, scored United's first in the 63rd minute before Zlatan Ibrahmovic's 78th-minute strike, his 13th goal of the season.
