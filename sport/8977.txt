Congratulating the Indian Under-19 team for its Asia Cup win, former cricketer Virender Sehwag tweeted, "Asia ka Ek hi Champion: 2016 Asia Cup Champions:India, 2016 U19 Asia Cup Champions: India, 2016 Women's Asia Cup Champions: India #U19AsiaCup." Indian women's team won the Asia Cup earlier this month, while the men's team had won the tournament in March.
