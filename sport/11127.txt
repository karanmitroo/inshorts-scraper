Defending La Liga Champions Barcelona suffered their campaign's second loss as they went down 3-4 away at Celta Vigo on Sunday. Pione Sisto, Iago Aspas and Pablo Hernández were the goal scorers for Celta, with Jérémy Mathieu netting an own goal. For the Catalan giants, Gerard Piqué scored on either side of Neymar's successful spot kick in the second half.
