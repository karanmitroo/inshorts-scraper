American UFC fighter Ronda Rousey earned over ₹42 lakh per second in her 48-second-long bout against Amanda Nunes in the UFC 207 title fight. In comparison, Nunes who won the title fight earned only ₹3.5 lakh per second. Rousey pocketed a total of $3 million (₹20 crore) for the bout, while Nunes earned $250,000 (approximately ₹1.7 crore).
