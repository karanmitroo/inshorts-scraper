Commentator Harsha Bhogle picked his all-time Indian XI, led by current coach and former captain Anil Kumble. The batsmen included Sunil Gavaskar, Virender Sehwag, Rahul Dravid and Sachin Tendulkar, while Zaheer Khan featured as the seamer. Former captain Mohammad Azharuddin was a notable inclusion in the team, which also featured Kapil Dev, VVS Laxman, MS Dhoni and Ravichandran Ashwin.
