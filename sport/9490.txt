Bangladesh all-rounder Afif Hossain became the youngest bowler to claim a five-wicket haul in T20s, getting match figures of 5/21 in the Bangladesh Premier League on Saturday. Hossain, aged 17 years 73 days, was playing the debut match of his career and became the first Bangladeshi and 16th player in the world to take a 5-wicket haul on T20 debut.
