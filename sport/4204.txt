The season's last grand slam, US Open, will become the first tennis tournament to offer over $50 million in prize money, following a nine percent increase in the total purse. The men's and women's singles titles' winners will each earn $3.7 million (₹23.8 crore), while runners-up will get $1.825 million (₹11.7 crore) from the $50.4 million (₹324 crore) total pot.
