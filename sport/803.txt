MS Dhoni was the most expensive buy in the first-ever IPL auction in 2008, bought by CSK for $1.5 million. In 2015, Delhi Daredevils bought Yuvraj Singh for ₹16 crore, making him the most expensive buy of IPL ever. Last year, England's Ben Stokes became the most expensive overseas buy, after Rising Pune Supergiant bought him for ₹14.5 crore.
