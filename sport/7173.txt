Indian batsman KL Rahul shut down a Twitter troll who tried to mock the batsman following a tweet Rahul posted about teammate Ravichandran Ashwin following him on Twitter. The troll tweeted, "ye sab chhodo.. runs kaise bane uspe dhyan de.." to which Rahul replied, "Plz come and teach us bhai. Im sure aapko pata hain kaise banate hain runs."
