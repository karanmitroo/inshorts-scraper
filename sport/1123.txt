England's Alastair Cook beat Sachin Tendulkar's record of being the youngest cricketer to reach 12,000 Test runs, achieving the milestone during the last Ashes match on Sunday. Cook beat Tendulkar by a margin of over two years. Tendulkar is still the youngest cricketer to reach the first six 1,000-run landmarks, while the 33-year-old Englishman is youngest to the next six.
