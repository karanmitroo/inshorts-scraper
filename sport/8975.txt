Indian Under-19 coach Rahul Dravid said the success of Karun Nair and Jayant Yadav is because of the process that has been put in place by Virat Kohli and Anil Kumble. "Credit to Virat and Anil for the set-up," said Dravid. The former cricketer added that his job as Under-19 coach is to provide quality players for the national team.
