Following his 153 in the Centurion Test, India captain Virat Kohli leapfrogged to second spot in the ICC Test rankings and reached 900 Test rating points. With this, Kohli became only the second Indian to achieve the 900-point mark after Sunil Gavaskar, who reached the landmark in 1979. Kohli is the 31st Test batsman overall to reach 900 points.
