Indian openers KL Rahul and Shikhar Dhawan have attained career-high positions in the latest ICC Test rankings. Rahul has broken into the top-10 after moving up two positions, with career-best 761 rating points, to equal his career-best ninth position attained in July. Dhawan, highest scorer in the recently concluded India-Sri Lanka Test series, moved up 10 places to 28th position.
