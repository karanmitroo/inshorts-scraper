Madhya Pradesh's left-arm spinner Manish Majithia bowled 20 overs and took a wicket without conceding a run in the second innings against Railways in a Ranji Trophy match on November 14, 1999. In the first innings, Majithia had registered figures of 12.3-9-3-1. On the last day, Railways scored only 83 runs from 104 overs to manage a draw.
