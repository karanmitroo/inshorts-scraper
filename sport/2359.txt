Indian tennis player Sania Mirza took to Twitter to share a photo of herself with husband Shoaib Malik, singer Atif Aslam and ex-Pakistani cricketer Azhar Mahmood after the Pakistan-Sri Lanka T20I in Lahore. "The last ones standing after kheema,parathas,lassi,chai and god knows what else at 5 am," Mirza wrote. Malik was awarded Man of the Match in the Lahore T20I.
