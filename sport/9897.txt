England trailed India by 352 runs at stumps on day two of the Vizag Test, finishing the day at 103/5. Earlier in the day, India wrapped up their innings at 455, led by centuries from Virat Kohli and Cheteshwar Pujara. Ravichandran Ashwin claimed two wickets in England's first innings, while Mohammed Shami and debutant Jayant Yadav got a scalp each.
