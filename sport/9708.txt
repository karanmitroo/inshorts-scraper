Swimmer Michael Phelps and Jamaican sprinter Usain Bolt are among those nominated for American publication Sports Illustrated's 2016 Sportsperson of the Year award. The list also includes American baseball team Chicago Cubs, who won the 2016 World Series after 108 years, and first-time Premier League champions Leicester City. LeBron James Katie Ledecky and Simone Biles are also on the list.
