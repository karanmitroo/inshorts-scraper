In response to Sachin Tendulkar's tweet about India's Olympic 'champions', Rio bronze medalist Sakshi Malik tweeted, "Champion toh aap ho sir. Hammne toh chakke chudana bas ab seekh liya hai! Thank you for this golden memory." Sachin had posted a picture with Sakshi, PV Sindhu, Dipa Karmakar and Pullela Gopichand while tweeting, "In company of champs who proved their mettle."
