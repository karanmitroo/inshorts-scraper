Rio Olympics' silver medalist PV Sindhu defeated world number two Japanese Akane Yamaguchi to top women's singles' Group A at the Dubai World Superseries Finals on Friday. Sindhu, who is already assured of a semi-final spot, won with the scoreline of 21-9, 21-13 in 36 minutes. Sindhu will face world number eight China's Chen Yufei in the semi-finals on Saturday.
