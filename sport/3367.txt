Virat Kohli earns over ₹2 crore every week during the IPL, which is more than the weekly salary of 2017's most expensive Premier League player, Romelu Lukaku. The Belgian striker, who plays for Manchester United, earns over ₹1.6 crore every week. Kohli's weekly salary is also more than that of recently added players Arsenal's Alexandre Lacazette and Chelsea's Alvaro Morata.
