Pep Guardiola's winning run as Manchester City's new boss ended with a 3-3 draw against Scottish football club Celtic in the Champions League group stage on Wednesday. For Celtic, Moussa Dembélé scored two goals while Raheem Sterling scored an own goal. Fernandinho, Sterling and Nolito netted one each for the English side, wrapping up the 3-3 scoreline inside 60 minutes.
