British number one Andy Murray progressed to the fourth round of the US Open 2016 after defeating Italy's Paolo Lorenzi 7-6, 5-7, 6-2, 6-3 on Saturday. Murray will next face Bulgaria's Grigor Dimitrov on Monday. US Open third seed Stanislas Wawrinka saved a match point against Britain's Dan Evans to complete a 4-6, 6-3, 6-7, 7-6, 6-2 win on Saturday.
