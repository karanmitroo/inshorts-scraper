The Delhi Daredevils-Gujarat Lions match on Thursday featured 31 sixes, the most in an IPL game and joint fourth-most in a T20. Chasing a target of 209, DD hit 20 sixes, which are the most by a team batting second in a T20. The previous record of 30 sixes was set in 2010 in a Chennai Super Kings-Rajasthan Royals game.
