World number one tennis player Novak Djokovic, who admitted to suffering from “serious personal issues”, is seeing Pepe Imaz, a 42-year-old former Spanish tennis player who is guiding the Serbian on a ‘spiritual basis’. Imaz, who was introduced to Novak through Marko Djokovic, preaches his self-made philosophy of ‘Peace and Love’ and believes in the “power of long hugs.”
