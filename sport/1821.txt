Former India captain MS Dhoni was welcomed with slogans hailing former Pakistani all-rounder Shahid Afridi during his visit to Kunzer, Jammu and Kashmir. Locals chanted 'Boom Boom Afridi' and also reportedly took name of militant 'Zakir Musa'. Dhoni, who holds an honorary rank of Lieutenant Colonel in Indian Army, was interacting with aspiring cricketers during an Army-organised event.
