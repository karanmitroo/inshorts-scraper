England all-rounder Ben Stokes (₹14.5 crore) and pacer Tymal Mills (₹12 crore) were the top earners among the players who went under the hammer in the IPL auction on Monday. The franchises invested in fast bowlers as South Africa's Kagiso Rabada and New Zealand's Trent Boult were bought for ₹5 crore each. Australian pacer Pat Cummins also fetched ₹4.5 crore.
