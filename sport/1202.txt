Apart from the winners' prize money of ₹2 crore, recently crowned Ranji Trophy champions Vidarbha will also be awarded ₹3 crore by the Vidarbha Cricket Association. Vidarbha Cricket Association President Anand Jaiswal said that the players will also be felicitated by the association. Vidarbha became the Ranji champions for the first time after beating Delhi by nine wickets on Monday.
