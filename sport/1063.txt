Cricketer-turned-commentator Virender Sehwag compared Rahul Dravid, who is nicknamed 'The Wall', to the Great Wall of China in a birthday message for the former India captain. Sehwag shared pictures of Great Wall of China and Rahul Dravid, and wrote that the former "may or may not shake or break" but Dravid is "unshakable and unbreakable". Dravid turns 45 today.
