England's James Anderson picked up his 290th wicket while playing in England to become the fast bowler with most wickets in home Tests. He surpassed former Australian pacer Glenn McGrath's tally of 289 wickets. Anderson is placed fourth in the list of overall highest wicket-takers in Tests at home, behind Muttiah Muralitharan (493), Anil Kumble (350), and Shane Warne (319).
