Ravichandran Ashwin became the first Indian to complete 200 wickets in T20 cricket, after dismissing Kieron Pollard in the second T20I against West Indies in Florida on Sunday. Ashwin took 195 matches to reach the milestone, with spinner Amit Mishra following at 199 wickets in 168 matches. Ashwin is also the first Indian to take 50 wickets in T20 internationals.
