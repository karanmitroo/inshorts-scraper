Reportedly, 687 Indian athletes have been banned by the National Anti-Doping Agency since 2009, an average of 100 athletes per year. Among those banned, 70% belonged to athletics, weightlifting and wrestling, with 176 bans coming around the 2012 London Olympics. A World Anti-Doping Agency report in 2014 ranked India third in the world's doping offenders' list, behind Russia and Italy.
