Sunrisers Hyderabad defeated Mumbai Indians by 7 wickets on Monday to remain on the fourth spot in the IPL 2017 points table. Batting first, Rohit Sharma's 45-ball 67 helped Mumbai score 138/7 as Siddarth Kaul picked three wickets for Hyderabad. Shikhar Dhawan's 46-ball 62* and Moises Henriques' 35-ball 44 helped Hyderabad chase down the traget with 10 balls to spare.
