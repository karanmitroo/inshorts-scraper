Cricket commentator Harsha Bhogle on Monday named his Test XI of 2016 with Virat Kohli as the captain of the side. Bhogle chose five batsmen with Pakistan's Azhar Ali and West Indies' Kraigg Brathwaite as openers, two allrounders in Ravichandran Ashwin and England's Ben Stokes. Meanwhile, spinner Rangana Herath with pacers Kagiso Rabada and Mitchell Starc constituted the bowling attack.
