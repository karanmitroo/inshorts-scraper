Indian leg-spinner Yuzvendra Chahal became the first Indian and the second overall to take a six-wicket haul in T20Is, taking 6/25 in the Bengaluru T20I against England on Wednesday. Chahal now has the third-best bowling figures in T20Is, behind former Sri Lankan bowler Ajantha Mendis' two six-wicket hauls. Chahal is the only Indian to take a T20I five-wicket haul.
