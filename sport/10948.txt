India's 321-run win over New Zealand at Indore on Tuesday is their second-biggest in terms of runs, surpassing their 320-run win over Australia at Mohali in 2008. Captain Virat Kohli scored a career-best 211, while Ravichandran Ashwin took his career's 21st five-for at Indore. India's biggest win by runs was a 337-run victory over South Africa in Delhi in 2015.
