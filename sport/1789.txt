The Competition Commission of India (CCI) has imposed a fine of ₹52.24 crore on BCCI for "anti-competitive practices" and "abuse of dominant position" with respect to IPL media rights. The penalty is 4.48% of BCCI's average turnover during last three financial years, which is said to be ₹1,164.7 crore. BCCI was fined ₹52.24 crore in February 2013 by the CCI.
