Rio Olympics gold medallist Carolina Marin became the most expensive buy in the Premier Badminton League-2 auction after being bought by Hyderabad Hunters for ₹61.5 lakh on Wednesday. India's PV Sindhu will represent Chennai Smashers, being bought for ₹39 lakh. In men's category, India's Kidambi Srikanth was bought for the highest amount of ₹51 lakh by Awadhe Warriors.
