India Under-19 World Cup team captain Prithvi Shaw was bought by Delhi Daredevils for ₹1.2 crore after a three-way bidding war with Rajasthan Royals and Mumbai Indians in the IPL auction. Vice-captain Shubman Gill, who averages over 100 in Youth ODIs, was sold to Kolkata Knight Riders for ₹1.8 crore. Both the players had a base price of ₹20 lakh.
