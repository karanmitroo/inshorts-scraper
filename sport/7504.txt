Sachin Tendulkar on Tuesday shared pictures of him playing with and carrying Harbhajan Singh's six-month old daughter Hinaya Heer on Twitter. Sachin captioned the picture, "With little Hinaya Heer! She's a bundle of joy. @harbhajan_singh." Sachin was seen wearing Mumbai Indians' jersey. Harbhajan's wife Geeta Basra also shared a photo of Sachin and Hinaya captioned, "Little Masters little fan".
