French midfielder Paul Pogba scored with a 30th minute right-footed long-range shot from 35 yards out against the Netherlands in France's World Cup 2018 qualifier match on Tuesday. Pogba received the ball from Dimitri Payet, before unleashing a right-footed swerving shot past a diving Maarten Stekelenburg into the right corner of the goal, giving France the match-winning 1-0 lead.
