Former Pakistani captain Younis Khan, who is set to retire from international cricket alongside Misbah-ul-Haq, once said about his form, "Whenever I am out of form, I get to play against India." While talking about his nickname 'Tuk-Tuk', Misbah had once said, "Call me 'Tuk-Tuk' and keep saying it, but remember I am the captain of Pakistan."
