India chased down 350 to register a three-wicket win over England in Virat Kohli's first match as the full-time skipper of the ODI team. India were helped by Kohli's 27th ODI century, while Kedar Jadhav also contributed with a 120-run innings off 76 balls. Earlier, Joe Root's 78(95) and Jason Roy's 73(61) had helped England reach 350.
