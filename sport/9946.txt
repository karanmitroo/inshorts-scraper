Sachin Tendulkar retired from cricket on November 16, 2013, and delivered a speech in the Wankhede stadium describing his "life between 22 yards for 24 years". The cricketer thanked his family, fans and fellow cricketers, including Sourav Ganguly and Rahul Dravid. "'Sachin, Sachin' will reverberate in my ears till I stop breathing," said Tendulkar while ending his speech.
