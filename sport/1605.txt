Former Indian captain MS Dhoni became the second wicketkeeper after former Sri Lankan captain Kumar Sangakkara to score 16,000 runs in international cricket after crossing the milestone figure against Sri Lanka on Sunday. Dhoni is also the sixth Indian cricketer overall to reach the figure after Sachin Tendulkar, Rahul Dravid, Sourav Ganguly, Virender Sehwag and Virat Kohli.
