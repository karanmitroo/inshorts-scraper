England ended Day 4 of the Wankhede Test at 182/6 in the second innings, trailing India by 49 runs. Ravichandran Ashwin and Ravindra Jadeja picked up two wickets each. Earlier in the day, India posted 631, driven by Virat Kohli's career-high Test score of 235. Jayant Yadav hit 104 becoming the first Indian to score a century at number 9.
