Barcelona rose to the top with a 6-1 rout over Sporting Gijon, while Real Madrid dropped points with a 3-3 draw against Las Palmas in the La Liga on Wednesday. Real managed to draw after being 1-3 down with two late Cristiano Ronaldo strikes. Meanwhile, Luis Suárez, hit a brace for Barça, with Messi and Neymar being among the scorers.
