Windies' Chris Gayle smashed a 45-ball hundred, his 19th T20 ton, for Rangpur Riders against Khulna Titans in the Bangladesh Premier League on Friday. Gayle hit 14 sixes during his knock of 126*(51), becoming the first cricketer to slam 800-plus sixes in T20 cricket. Gayle is the only player to hit 10-plus sixes in a T20 innings 14 times.
