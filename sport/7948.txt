Mission Smile, a Mumbai-based social organisation, has released a calendar on amputees under their project #RisingAbove. It features Paralympic gold medalist Mariyappan Thangavelu, para-badminton player and Tokyo 2020 aspirant Manasi Joshi, para-swimmer Suyash and India's first woman blade runner Kiran Kanojia, among others. Specially-abled people in professional fields have also been featured.
