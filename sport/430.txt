India's Under-19 vice-captain Shubman Gill slammed six sixes during his 123*-run knock on Sunday, equalling Yuvraj Singh and Harbhajan Singh's record of most sixes in a List A innings for Punjab. It was Shubman's highest score in domestic one-day cricket. The 18-year-old was named 2018 Under-19 World Cup's player of the tournament after slamming 372 runs in the tournament.
