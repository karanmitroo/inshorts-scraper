Indian golfer Gaganjeet Bhullar won his sixth Asian Tour title, winning the Korea Open in Incheon on Sunday. The 28-year-old, whose last Asian title came in 2013, was awarded a cash prize of $196,000 for the win. Bhullar, an Arjuna awardee, has been a part of India's silver-winning golf team at the 2006 Asian Games in Doha.
