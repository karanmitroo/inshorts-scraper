Five-year-old Indian archer Cherukuri Dolly Shivani fired 103 arrows from 10 metres in 11 minutes 19 seconds to enter the India Book of Records and Asia Book of Records on Sunday. The Vijaywada-based girl set another record, firing 36 arrows from 20 metres in five minutes eight seconds. Out of a possible 360 points, Shivani scored 290 points.
