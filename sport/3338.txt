Indian captain Virat Kohli scored his 30th ODI century against Sri Lanka on Sunday to stand joint-second with Ricky Ponting on the list of most ODI centuries. Kohli is now only behind Sachin Tendulkar who scored 49 tons in his 463-match ODI career. Kohli, who reached the landmark in his 186th innings, also became the fastest to 30 ODI centuries.
