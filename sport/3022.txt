Belagavi Panthers' Anand Doddamani and D Avinash took a hat-trick each in space of 16 balls against Hubli Tigers in Karnataka Premier League's first semi-final on Wednesday. Hubli Tigers had scored 79/3 when left-arm spinner Doddamani took the first hat-trick in the 13th over before pacer Avinash picked up a hat-trick in the 15th over to dismiss Tigers for 98.
