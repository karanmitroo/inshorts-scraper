Karnataka's Vinay Kumar bagged a hat-trick against Mumbai by 2.2 overs of the quarterfinal match on Thursday, the earliest instance of a hat-trick in a Ranji match.  Kumar took the wickets of Prithvi Shaw, Jay Gokul Bista and Akash Parkar with his three successive deliveries. Kumar became the sixth bowler to take a hat-trick in a Ranji knockout game.
