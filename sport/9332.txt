Murali Vijay's 136 and Virat Kohli's 147* helped India end Day 3 of the Mumbai Test at 451/7, leading England by 51 runs in the first innings. England spinners Moeen Ali, Adil Rashid and Joe Root picked up two wickets each. The day also saw Virat Kohli reach 4,000 career Test runs and 1,000 Test runs in this calendar year.
