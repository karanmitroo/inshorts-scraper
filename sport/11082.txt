The India-New Zealand Test at Kolkata, which India won by 178 runs on Monday, saw 15 LBW dismissals, most in a Test in India. The previous record was of 13 LBWs, against South Africa at Ahmedabad in 1996. There have been 25 LBW dismissals in the ongoing India-NZ series, most for a three-match series in India, with one Test remaining.
