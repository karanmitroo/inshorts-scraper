Indian all-rounder Ravindra Jadeja took to Twitter to slam a fan, who mistakenly referred to him as former Indian cricketer Ajay Jadeja while congratulating him. "Someone came to me and said 'well ball ajay. you bowled brilliantly in last match'.played 9 years of international cricket for country and still ppl dont remember my name.#stupidity #gavaar," the 29-year-old wrote.
