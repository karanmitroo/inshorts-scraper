Following South African opener Quinton de Kock's century against Australia, Virender Sehwag tweeted, "De Kock what a Knock. @QuinnyDeKock69 ka dhamaakedaar Knock, Australia Shock." De Kock scored a career-best 178 of 113 balls in SA's six-wicket victory over Australia at Centurion on Friday. It is the fourth-highest individual score by a batsman while chasing.
