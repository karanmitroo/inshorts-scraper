Indian captain Virat Kohli earns ₹3.2 crore ($500,000) per promotional post through Instagram, as per a recent report by Forbes. Kohli is tied with Real Madrid forward Cristiano Ronaldo at the second place while comedian Kevin Hart tops the chart with ₹6.4 crore per post. Kohli's brand endorsement contract was reportedly worth ₹143 crore in 2017.
