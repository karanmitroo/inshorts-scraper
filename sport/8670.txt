Chairman of selectors MSK Prasad has revealed MS Dhoni only said "OK, that's it" while stepping down as India's limited-overs captain on Wednesday. Prasad and Dhoni met during the Gujarat-Jharkhand Ranji Trophy match in Nagpur, where Dhoni was present as mentor for Jharkhand. Prasad praised Dhoni's "amazing clarity of thought" but did not give more details citing "professional confidentiality".
