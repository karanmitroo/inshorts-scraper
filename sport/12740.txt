Germany's Angelique Kerber advanced to her second Wimbledon semifinal after defeating Romania's Simona Halep 7-5, 7-6(7-2) on Tuesday. The match, which lasted for an hour and 33 minutes, witnessed a total of 13 breaks of service. Kerber, the Australian Open 2016 champion, is set to face five-time Wimbledon champion Venus Williams in her semifinal clash on Thursday.
