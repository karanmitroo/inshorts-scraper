Cricketers Shahid Afridi and Kumar Sangakkara will play in the 2017 Hong Kong T20 Blitz, the second edition of the country's domestic T20 league. While Sangakkara has signed up for the Galaxy Gladiators Lantau franchise, Afridi will play for the Kowloon Cantons. West Indies all-rounder Darren Sammy will also feature in the five-team league, which begins on March 8.
