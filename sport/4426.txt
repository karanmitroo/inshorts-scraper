Afghanistan's national team wicketkeeper Shafiqullah Shafaq recently hit a double ton in a  local tournament. He scored 214 in 71 balls, with his knock including 21 sixes and 16 fours, taking his team, Khateez Cricket Academy's total to 351. Shafiqullah has played 20 ODIs and 35 T20Is for Afghanistan, never having scored a century in either format.
