Hafþór Júlíus Björnsson, known for playing 'The Mountain' in 'Game of Thrones', posted a video of himself deadlifting 330 kilograms while listening to Adele's song 'Hello'. "Being a savage all day everyday is hard. Sometimes I just go to the gym and...work in a calm and relaxing environment. 330kg 3 sets of 2 with Adele in the background," wrote Björnsson.
