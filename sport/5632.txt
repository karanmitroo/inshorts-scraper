Kings XI Punjab defeated Kolkata Knight Riders by 14 runs in the IPL on Tuesday to stay in the contention for a playoff berth. Batting first, KXIP put up 167/6 driven by Glenn Maxwell's 44(25) and Wriddhiman Saha's 38(33). KXIP managed to restrict KKR to 153/6 with Rahul Tewatia and Mohit Sharma picking up two wickets each.
