Former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag scored ODI cricket's second double-ton and the then highest ODI individual score on December 8, 2011. Sehwag smashed 219, while allegedly humming Hindi film songs, against West Indies in Indore. The Delhi batsman scored 142 runs in boundaries, comprising 25 fours and seven sixes. Sehwag's innings helped India reach 418, India's highest ODI total.
