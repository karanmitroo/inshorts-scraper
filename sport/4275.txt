Spain's Garbine Muguruza, who won her maiden Wimbledon title on Saturday, tweeted to the men's singles champion Roger Federer, "[A]re you ready to dance? #ChampionsDinner." Federer, who won his eighth Wimbledon title on Sunday, replied to Muguruza with, "Bring it on, champ @GarbiMuguruza", alongside emojis of a dancing man and woman.
