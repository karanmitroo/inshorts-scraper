Virender Sehwag tweeted to Ravichandran Ashwin on the latter winning a record seventh Man of The Series title in the recently-concluded New Zealand Test series. Sehwag posted, "Congrats @ashwinravi99 for an incredible 7th Man of the series. Only a married man can understand d urgency of going home early. #FamilyTime." Ashwin finished with 27 wickets in the three-match series.
