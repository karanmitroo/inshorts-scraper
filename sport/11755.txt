According to a report, the Sports Authority of India said some Rio Olympians went a "bit overboard" with their demands while preparing for the Games. Records revealed that marathoner Kavita Raut had submitted a ₹26.41 lakh proposal for her training, which included ₹18.9 lakh for accommodation. Sprinter Dutee Chand made demands for shoes after being allotted ₹30 lakh aid.
