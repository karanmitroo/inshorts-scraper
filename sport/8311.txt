Cricketer Gautam Gambhir has tweeted in support of 'Dangal' actor Zaira Wasim, after she posted an apology on Twitter for acting in 'Dangal' and meeting Jammu and Kashmir CM Mehbooba Mufti. Gambhir posted a series of tweets, saying it was "naked suppression" to demand an apology from the 16-year-old. He added the whole episode "smells of sexual bias".
