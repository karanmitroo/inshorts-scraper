The €222 million (₹1,680 crore) record transfer fee of Brazilian striker Neymar is equal to the combined GDP of three nations. As per data, Tuvalu, Montserrat and Kiribati have a GDP of €28.8 million, €53 million and €140 million respectively, which adds up to €221.8 million. Neymar's fee can also wipe out Fiji's national debt of €61 million.
