Virat Kohli became the sixth Indian to take 100 ODI catches as a fielder, achieving the feat in the sixth ODI against South Africa on Friday. Kohli joined Sourav Ganguly and Suresh Raina, who also have taken 100 ODI catches each. Only Rahul Dravid (124), Sachin Tendulkar (140) and Mohammed Azharuddin (156) have taken more catches than Kohli among Indians.
