As per data released by footballer Cristiano Ronaldo's management, the player earned ₹1,627 crore (€227.3 million) in the last financial year. The documents were released following allegations of tax evasion of up to ₹1,073 crore by Ronaldo. "This document confirms that Spain's Tax Agency is knowledgeable of all of Cristiano Ronaldo's assets and income," the statement said.
