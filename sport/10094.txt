Canadian wrestler of Indo-Fijian descent, Darryl Sharma has reportedly been signed up for a development contract by the WWE. The 32-year-old, who is currently a coach at Santino Marella's academy, has earlier worked as a pro-wrestling coach at The Great Khali's CWE academy in Punjab. Popularly known as Hornet, Sharma owns a Toronto-based construction company named 'Hornet Construction' as well.
