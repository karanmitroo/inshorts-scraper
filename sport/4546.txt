Celebrities including Amitabh Bachchan, Anil Kapoor, Anupam Kher and Diana Penty congratulated the Indian women's cricket team on winning against Pakistan in a match in the ICC Women's World Cup. Bachchan tweeted, "Congratulations... for your most convincing win! May you continue to prosper and bring greater glory!" Anupam tweeted, "Superb win in ICC Women's World Cup. You are the bestest."
