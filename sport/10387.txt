Gautam Gambhir has been named in the Indian squad for the first two Tests against England, alongside Hardik Pandya and Karun Nair, who have been handed their maiden Test call-ups. Led by Virat Kohli, the 15-man squad includes Cheteshwar Pujara, Ajinkya Rahane, while Shikhar Dhawan has been excluded. Ravichandran Ashwin, Wriddhiman Saha and Umesh Yadav are also in the squad.
