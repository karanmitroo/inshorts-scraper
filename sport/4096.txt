Indian cricketer Yuvraj Singh has invested an undisclosed amount in Delhi-based startup Creator's Gurukul. Co-founded by Abhinav Tandon and Mohammed Sirajuddin, Gurukul offers shared office spaces. Through his investment fund YouWeCan Ventures, Yuvraj Singh has previously invested in startups like JetSetGo, cargo logistics firm Moovo, and online education startup EduKart among others.
