England's Jonny Bairstow broke the record for most dismissals registered by a wicketkeeper in a calendar year, after claiming his 68th dismissal of 2016 on Monday. Bairstow went past Ian Healy (1993) and Mark Boucher (1998), who jointly held the record with 67 dismissals. Bairstow also holds the record for most number of runs by a wicket-keeper in a year.
