Ex-Manchester United forward Carlos Tevez has reportedly become the highest paid footballer in the world after signing a $40 million (around ₹270 crore) per season deal with Chinese club Shanghai Shenhua. The club has reportedly paid around $87 million (over ₹590 crore) for Tevez, making him the world's sixth-most expensive player behind Paul Pogba, Bale, Cristiano Ronaldo, Higuain and Neymar.
