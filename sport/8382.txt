Congratulating Parthiv Patel on leading Gujarat to its first ever Ranji Trophy title, former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag tweeted, "Nikke se @parthiv9 ne kar diya itna bada kaam. Chota Packet, Bada Dhamaka. #ChotaChetan helps Gujarat win #RanjiTrophy." Gujarat's captain Patel was adjudged Man of the Match for his 143 which helped Gujarat chase down 312 against Mumbai in the final.
