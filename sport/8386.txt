PV Sindhu-led Chennai Smashers won the second edition of the Premier Badminton League, defeating the Mumbai Rockets 4-3 in the final on Saturday. Sindhu, who had defeated Awadhe Warriors' Saina Nehwal in the semifinals, beat Mumbai Rockets' Sung Ji Hyun 11-8, 11-8 in the final. This is Chennai's first title, while Mumbai finished as runners-up for the second consecutive year.
