Former India captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni on Friday became the third wicketkeeper to take 600 catches in international cricket. Dhoni achieved the feat by taking the catch of Hashim Amla off pacer Shardul Thakur's bowling during the sixth India-South Africa ODI. Dhoni has taken 256 catches in Tests, 297 in ODIs and 47 in T20Is as a wicketkeeper so far.
