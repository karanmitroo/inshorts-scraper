The year 2017 saw many youngsters emerge with breakout performances in several sports. Indian cricket saw the rise of spinners Yuzvendra Chahal and Kuldeep Yadav, while Pakistan's 23-year-old pacer Hasan Ali became top-ranked ODI bowler. Other sports also witnessed breakout youngsters like 12-year-old R Praggnanandhaa in chess and Manav Thakkar in table tennis.
