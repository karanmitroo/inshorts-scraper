India captain Virat Kohli slammed his 34th ODI hundred on Wednesday against South Africa, reaching the landmark in 101 fewer innings than Sachin Tendulkar. Kohli scored his 34th ODI hundred in his 197th innings while Sachin had reached the landmark in his 298th innings against Namibia in 2003. Kohli has now hit 55 international hundreds, the fifth most in history.
