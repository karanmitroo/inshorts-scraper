India captain Virat Kohli has become the leading Test run-getter as Team India captain, surpassing MS Dhoni's record of 3,454 runs. Kohli achieved the feat during his 41-run knock in the third Test against South Africa on Friday. Dhoni had scored 3,454 runs as captain in 60 Tests (96 innings), while Kohli crossed him in only 35 Tests (57 innings).
