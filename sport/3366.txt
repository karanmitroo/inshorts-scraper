Actor Amitabh Bachchan invited the Indian women's cricket team on his reality show 'Kaun Banega Crorepati'. The cricketers were a part of a new segment on the show, titled 'Nayi Chaah Nahi Raah'. Bachchan also surprised Smriti Mandhana when he called up her favourite singer Arijit Singh, who even sung a song for her.
