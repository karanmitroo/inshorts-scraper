Former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag quoted a dialogue from 2001's Aamir Khan-starrer Lagaan after India's three-wicket win over England on Sunday. The former cricketer's tweet read, "Hahahahahaha ! Dus Guna Lagaan Vasool. #INDvENG". Former England captain Michael Vaughan tweeted, "Best Test player ... KOHLI Best ODI player ... KOHLI. Best T20 player ... KOHLI".
