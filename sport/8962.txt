Bengal's Pankaj Shaw scored an unbeaten 413 in the three-day Cricket Association of Bengal First Division tournament in Kolkata on Sunday. The 28-year-old, who came in at number four, hit 44 fours and 23 sixes to register the first-ever 400-plus score in the three-day CAB tournament. Shaw has played 12 first-class matches for Bengal, with his previous best being 118.
