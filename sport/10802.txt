Indian shuttler and defending champion Ajay Jayaram progressed to the final of the Dutch Open after a 21-18, 13-21, 21-13 win over Denmark's Anders Antonsen in the semifinal on Saturday. Tournament top seed Jayaram, who has clinched the Dutch Open title three times in his career, will face the winner of the Tzu Wei Wang-Emil Holstfifth clash in Sunday's final.
