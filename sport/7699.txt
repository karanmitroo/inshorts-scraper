South Africa equalled India's record of 23 350-plus team totals in ODIs, scoring 367/5 in 50 overs against Sri Lanka at Cape Town on Tuesday. India had registered their 23rd 350-plus total last month during the Cuttack ODI against England. Notably, Sri Lanka is now the side to concede most 350-plus totals in ODIs, on Tuesday being their 12th instance.
