New Zealand opener Martin Guptill set the record for most 180-plus individual ODI scores, bringing up his career's third against South Africa on Wednesday. Guptill, who scored 180* in Hamilton, has previously hit 237* against West Indies in 2015 and 189* against England in 2013. Vivian Richards, Sachin Tendulkar and Rohit Sharma have two 180-plus ODI scores each.
