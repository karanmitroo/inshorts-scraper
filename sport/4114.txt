Cricketer Yuvraj Singh trolled teammate Shikhar Dhawan for wearing slippers in the official kit photoshoot of the team. Addressing Shikhar as 'Jatt ji', Yuvraj asked Shikhar in Punjabi to wear shoes for the photoshoot. Shikhar also replied in Punjabi, saying he did not know the entire picture would be uploaded, but the picture still had swagger despite the slippers.
