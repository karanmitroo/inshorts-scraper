Following his 13-wicket haul in the recently concluded Test against Bangladesh, Australian spinner Nathan Lyon has become the leading wicket-taker in Tests in 2017. Lyon, who has 46 wickets from seven Tests at an average of 21.95 in 2017, overtook India's spin duo of Ravichandran Ashwin and Ravindra Jadeja, who have each taken 44 wickets till now.
