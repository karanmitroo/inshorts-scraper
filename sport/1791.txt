Karnataka opener Mayank Agarwal has hit over 1,000 Ranji runs in 27 days, going past the 1,000-run mark with two centuries in the match against Railways on Monday. Agarwal has scored 1,033 runs in four matches in November, with his scores being 304*, 176, 23, 90, 133*, 173 and 134. The 26-year-old has represented India A, coached by Rahul Dravid.
