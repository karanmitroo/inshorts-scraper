Uruguayan tennis player Pablo Cuevas served underarm to Spaniard Albert Ramos-Vinolas while on his fourth match point to win the Brasil Open in Sao Paulo recently. Cuevas said he made the move since he was on his second serve and already had 12 double faults in the game. Cuevas won 6-7(3), 6-4, 6-4 to seal his sixth ATP title.
