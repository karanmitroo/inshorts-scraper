Indian spinner, Ravichandran Ashwin, who registered his 26th five-wicket haul in the Colombo Test against Sri Lanka, has moved past Harbhajan Singh on the list of most five-fors for India in Tests. Ashwin is now second only to Anil Kumble's tally of 35 five-wicket hauls in 132 Tests in the list of most five-wicket hauls among Indian bowlers.
