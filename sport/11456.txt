Table-toppers Manchester City cruised to their fifth straight win with a 4-0 victory against Bournemouth AFC at the Etihad Stadium on Saturday. Playing without suspended forward, Sergio Aguero, City were helped with goals by Kevin De Bruyne, Kelechi Iheanacho, Raheem Sterling and İlkay Gündoğan. City striker Nolito was sent off late in the game for clashing with Bournemouth's Adam Smith.
