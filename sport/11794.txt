Indian pacer Jasprit Bumrah has set the record for the most T20I wickets in a calendar year, taking 28 wickets in 21 matches in 2016. Bumrah, who took four wickets in the recently-concluded T20I series against West Indies, overtook Australian Dirk Nannes, who took 27 wickets in 14 matches in 2010. Bumrah made his T20 debut in January this year.
