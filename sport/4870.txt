The tickets of the Champions Trophy final between India and Pakistan are being resold for amounts up to ₹1.6 lakh (£2,000), 15 times the regular price. ICC allows reselling tickets on the official channel at the same value, but ticketing websites are allowing bids. A fan who had bought the ticket for ₹11,500 (£140) resold it for ₹90,000 (£1,100).
