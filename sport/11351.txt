Manchester United entered the EFL Cup fourth round after a 3-1 win over League One side Northampton on Thursday. United opened the scoring with Michael Carrick's 17th-minute goal, which was equalised by Northampton's Alex Revell with a 42nd-minute penalty. United's Ander Herrera and Marcus Rashford put the match beyond Northampton with their 68th-minute and 75th-minute goals respectively.
