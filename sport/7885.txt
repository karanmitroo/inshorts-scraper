Ajinkya Rahane and wicketkeeper Wriddhiman Saha have been named in the Indian squad for the one-off Test against Bangladesh, which begins February 9. The squad, led by Virat Kohli, also features opener Abhinav Mukund, who last played a Test for India in 2011. Ravichandran Ashwin, Ravindra Jadeja, Bhuvneshwar Kumar and Hardik Pandya are also in the team.
