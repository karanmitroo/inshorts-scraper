Former India captain Rahul Dravid spent 44,152 minutes (735 hours and 52 minutes) at the crease in Tests, the most for any batsman. Dravid, who turned 45 today on Thursday, faced 31,258 balls in Tests, setting the record for most balls faced by a batsman. With 210 catches, Dravid also holds the record for most Test catches by a non-wicketkeeper.
