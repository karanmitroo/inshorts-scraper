Indian tennis player Rohan Bopanna finished as runner-up in his career's third Grand Slam final after losing the Australian Open mixed doubles final on Sunday. Bopanna and his Hungarian partner Timea Babos lost 6-2, 4-6, [9-11] to Croatian-Canadian team of Mate Pavic and Gabriela Dabrowski. Interestingly, Bopanna had won his first Grand Slam with Dabrowski at French Open last year.
