Wishing Indian fast bowler Mohammed Shami on his 26th birthday on Saturday, former cricketer Virender Sehwag tweeted, "Thank you to Shami ki Mummy. You gave birth to 1 of India's most spirited bowler. Hpy Bdy @MdShami11 #ShamiKababRocks". Shami made his international debut for India in 2013 and has taken 58 Test wickets and 87 ODI wickets since then.
