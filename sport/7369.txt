Following Indian women's cricket team's win over Pakistan in the ICC Women's World Cup Qualifier on Sunday, former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag tweeted, "Congratulations @BCCIWomen for defeating Pakistan by 7 wickets in Women's World Cup qualifier 2017 #IndvPak. Ab to Aadat Si Hai!" Sehwag took a dig at Pakistan failing to dominate India in the ICC tournaments.
