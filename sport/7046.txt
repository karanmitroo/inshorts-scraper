Jammu and Kashmir cricket team captain Parvez Rasool took a hat-trick against Saurashtra in the Vijay Hazare Trophy on Sunday. The 28-year-old had earlier hit a 48-ball 53 to help his team reach 170 batting first. Rasool, who is the first player from Jammu and Kashmir to bag an IPL contract, finished with four wickets to seal his team's win.
