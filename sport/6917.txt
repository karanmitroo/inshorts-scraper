Sania Mirza and her Czech partner Barbora Strýcová reached the quarter-finals of the women's doubles event at the Indian Wells tournament in the USA on Saturday, defeating Italy's Sara Errani and Poland's Alicja Rosolska 6-2, 6-3. Meanwhile, Leander Paes, pairing up with Argentina's Juan Martín del Potro, lost in the first round to Luxembourgish Gilles Müller and American Sam Querrey.
