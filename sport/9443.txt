Australia defeated New Zealand by 116 runs in the second ODI at Canberra on Tuesday, to take an unassailable 2-0 lead in the three-match series. Batting first, Australia posted 378/5 driven by opener David Warner's 119 runs, his sixth ODI hundred of 2016. In reply, NZ were bowled out for 262 in 47.2 overs, with Pat Cummins taking four wickets.
