Former cricketer Virender Sehwag has been named in a 12-member committee which will pick this year's Khel Ratna and Arjuna awardees. The committee, which also includes former athlete PT Usha, will be headed by CK Thakkar and will meet on August 3 to select the awardees. Notably, Sehwag had won the Arjuna award in 2002 while PT Usha in 1983.
