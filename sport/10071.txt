Professional kabaddi players walked the ramp at a fashion show organised by the Lakme Fashion Week in collaboration with multi-designer store Ensemble in Mumbai on Thursday. The five players included two from the recent World Cup-winning squad, namely Mohit Chhillar and Surender Nada, and others from the Pro Kabaddi League. The designers included Ujjwal Dubey and Tarun Tahiliani, among others.
