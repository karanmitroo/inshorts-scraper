As per reports, BCCI has warned captain Virat Kohli to ensure the team performs well following the exit of coach Anil Kumble. Kohli had reportedly told BCCI's Cricket Advisory Committee, comprising Sourav Ganguly, Sachin Tendulkar and VVS Laxman, about his lack of trust for Kumble. Kumble, on his part, had told the panel that he had no problems with Kohli.
