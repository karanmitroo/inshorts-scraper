Manchester United were held to a 1-1 draw by Russian club FC Rostov in the first of their Europa League last-16 match in Russia on Thursday. United managed an away goal, with midfielder Henrikh Mkhitaryan shooting from close range in the 35th minute, assisted by Zlatan Ibrahimovic. Rostov levelled in the second half with Rostov forward Aleksandr Bukharov's low strike.
