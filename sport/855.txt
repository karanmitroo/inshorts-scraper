Indian batsman Suresh Raina became the second Indian after national team captain Virat Kohli to score 7,000 runs in T20 cricket. He reached the landmark during his unbeaten 59-ball 126-run innings for Uttar Pradesh against Bengal on Monday. Raina, the ninth cricketer overall to reach the landmark, also recorded the highest individual score in the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy history.
