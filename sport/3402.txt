Indian spinner Ravichandran Ashwin picked up a five-wicket haul in the second innings of his county debut for Worcestershire, taking a total of eight wickets in the match. Ashwin ended with figures of 5/68 to help Worcestershire dismiss Gloucestershire for 211 in the second innings, helping his team win by 189 runs. The five-for was Ashwin's 38th in first-class cricket.
