Reacting to New Zealand captain Kane Williamson's felicitation in Kanpur by the BCCI and Uttar Pradesh Cricket Association, Virender Sehwag tweeted, "Looks like foreigner rewarded at Baraat in Kanpur, With Mausaji, Babuji, Fufaji & Bhaukaal wale Mamaji." Williamson was felicitated on the occasion of India's 500th Test, along with Virat Kohli and former Indian Test captains.
