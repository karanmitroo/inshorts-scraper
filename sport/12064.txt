The Indian pair of Sania Mirza and Rohan Bopanna lost 6-2, 2-6, 3-10 to USA's Venus Williams and Rajeev Ram in the semi-finals of the mixed doubles event at Rio Olympics. Mirza and Bopanna will now compete for the bronze medal on Sunday. Notably, Leander Paes' bronze medal in Atlanta 1996 remains India's only Olympic medal in tennis so far.
