Playing his 19th ODI, Indian pacer Jasprit Bumrah took his ODI career's first five-wicket haul against Sri Lanka in the third ODI on Sunday. Bumrah, who had taken four wickets in the second ODI, ended with figures of 10-2-27-5. Bumrah's five-for was India's first since Amit Mishra picked up 5/18 against New Zealand on October 29, 2016.
