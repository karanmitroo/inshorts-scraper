Rohan Bopanna has become the fourth Indian to win a Grand Slam, winning the French Open mixed doubles along with Canadian partner Gabriela Dabrowski on Thursday. Seventh seeds Bopanna-Dabrowski defeated Germany's Anna-Lena Groenefeld and Colombia's Robert Farah 2-6, 6-2, 12-10. Mahesh Bhupathi was the first Indian to win a Grand Slam, with Leander Paes and Sania Mirza being the others.
