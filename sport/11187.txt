Real Madrid forward Cristiano Ronaldo, who scored against Borussia Dortmund in Madrid's Champions League group match, gifted his matchday shirt to Dortmund forward Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang's son. Aubameyang posted a picture of his son posing with Ronaldo's jersey, captioned, "@cristiano you've made my son happy". The clubs will face each other in the reverse fixture on December 8.
