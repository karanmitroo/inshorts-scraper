French midfielder Paul Pogba will reportedly draw weekly wages of ₹2.5 crore ($377,000) at Manchester United, to become Premier League’s highest-paid player. Fellow United player Wayne Rooney draws ₹2.26 crore weekly, while Zlatan Ibrahimovic at third spot earns ₹1.9 crore a week. Notably, Pogba had left United in 2012 due to pay issues, after demanding weekly wages of ₹56 lakh.
