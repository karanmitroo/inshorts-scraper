Delhi-based Kshitij Kumar Singh, a 13-year-old striker has signed for Netherlands-based football club NEC Nijmegen. A product of the Reliance Foundation Young Champs, Kshitij, who started playing football at the age of six, will represent Nijmegen's Under-15 youth squad, which features at the highest level in Dutch Youth Leagues and competes against top European clubs in international tournaments.
