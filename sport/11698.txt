Indian tennis player Leander Paes' campaign at the US Open ended after he and his mixed doubles' partner Martina Hingis lost 7-6, 3-6, 13-11 to USA's Coco Vandeweghe and Rajeev Ram on Sunday. Meanwhile, the Indo-Canadian pair of Rohan Bopanna and Gabriela Dabrowski progressed to the quarterfinals with a 5-7, 6-3, 10-7 win over Lukasz Kubot and Andrea Hlavackova.
