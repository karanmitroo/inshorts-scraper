A single-wicket cricket match was held in Yorkshire, England, which lasted three days and ended on October 17, 1857. Two local cricketers John Grange and James Sadler faced off in the two innings a side game. Grange defeated Sadler by 24 runs, scoring 27(159) and 24(96) in his two innings, compared to Sadler's 24(93) and 3(18).
