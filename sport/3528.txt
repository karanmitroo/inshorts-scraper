Barcelona on Friday announced Ousmane Dembélé's signing from Borussia Dortmund for a fee of €105 million (₹800 crore), along with potential €42m in add-ons, making him the second most expensive player ever behind Neymar. Dembélé agreed a five-year contract with a buyout clause of €400 million (₹3,000 crore). The 20-year-old forward will wear the number 11 shirt, vacated by Neymar.
