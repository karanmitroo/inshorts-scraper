India's Olympic bronze medal-winning shuttler Saina Nehwal progressed to the quarterfinal of the Macau Open after defeating Indonesia's Dinar Dyah Ayustine 17-21, 21-18, 21-12 on Thursday. The top-seeded Indian shuttler will next face China's 19-year-old shuttler Yiman Zhang in the quarterfinal on December 2. Notably, India's PV Sindhu had earlier pulled out of the tournament.
