Indian skipper Virat Kohli holds the record for the most T20 runs at a single venue, scoring 2,096 runs in 64 innings at Bengaluru's Chinnaswamy Stadium. Virat has scored three hundreds at the stadium, with a personal best of 113. The record for the second-most T20 runs at a single venue belongs to Chris Gayle, also at the Chinnaswamy Stadium.
