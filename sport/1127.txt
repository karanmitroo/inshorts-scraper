World number three shuttler PV Sindhu defeated world number one Tai Tzu Ying of Chinese Taipei in the Premier Badminton League on Saturday. Chennai Smashers' Sindhu won the match with a scoreline of 15-11, 10-15, 15-12 to hand Ahmedabad Smash Masters' Tai Tzu Ying her first loss of the season. Chennai later won the tie against Ahmedabad 2-1.
