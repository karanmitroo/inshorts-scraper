Australia captain Steve Smith has reached a career-high 939 rating points in the latest ICC Test rankings for batsmen. Smith's rating points are the sixth best ever after Don Bradman (961), Len Hutton (945), Jack Hobbs and Ricky Ponting (both 942) and Peter May (941). Smith, who scored 109 in Pune, now leads Indian captain Virat Kohli by 66 points.
