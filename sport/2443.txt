Russian kickboxer Andrey Potapov hit his opponent Sergey Krutelev after losing his fight at a tournament in Russia, before chasing an angry fan who had mocked the kickboxer for his actions. Following the judges' decision to hand Krutelev the victory, Potapov said, "I need a rematch" while receiving his medal. "You will get it," Krutelev replied before Potapov punched him.
