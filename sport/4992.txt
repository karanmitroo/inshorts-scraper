Sri Lankan pacer Lasith Malinga on Monday became the second highest wicket-taker in ICC ODI tournaments after taking his 68th wicket against Pakistan in the Champions Trophy 2017 group match. Malinga surpassed his former teammate Chaminda Vaas's tally of 67 wickets. Notably, Zaheer Khan is the highest wicket taker for India in these tournaments with 59 wickets in 32 matches.
