Yuvraj Singh and Suresh Raina reportedly failed the 'Yo-Yo' endurance test at the NCA, before the India squad for limited over series against Sri Lanka was announced. The 'Yo-Yo' test is considered the most important fitness test and both the cricketers scored below the permissible level of 19.5 in it, reports added. Virat Kohli scores around 21 in the test.
