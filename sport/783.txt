Indian tennis player Rohan Bopanna has entered his career's third Grand Slam final after reaching the Australian Open mixed doubles final on Friday. Bopanna and his partner Timea Babos of Hungary defeated Marcelo Demoliner and Martinez Sanchez with a scoreline of 7-5, 5-7 [10-6] in the semi-final. Bopanna had become the fourth Indian to win a Grand Slam last year.
