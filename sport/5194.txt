Arsenal footballer Mesut Özil retweeted Bollywood actor Ranveer Singh's tweet from the FA Cup final, after the Gunners defeated Chelsea at Wembley on Saturday. Ranveer had attended the match and posted a series of tweets, including a video of him saying, "Ya Gunners Ya". Özil retweeted the video, writing, "#YaGunnersYa! This guy knows what's going on! #FACup #Champions #Wembley."
