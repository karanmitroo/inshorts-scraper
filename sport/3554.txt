MS Dhoni effected his 99th ODI stumping against Sri Lanka on Thursday to equal the world record for most stumpings in 50-over cricket. Dhoni, playing his 298th ODI, joined former Sri Lankan captain Kumar Sangakkara, who had 99 stumpings to his credit in 404 ODIs. With the dismissal, Dhoni takes his stumpings tally in international cricket to 160.
