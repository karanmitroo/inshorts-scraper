Former Indian captain MS Dhoni hit his first List A century for Jharkhand at the Eden Gardens in Kolkata, while playing against Chhattisgarh on Sunday. Dhoni's 107-ball 129 included 10 fours and six sixes. Previously, Dhoni had featured in 8 List A matches at the stadium, including five ODIs, but his best score was only 75* against England in 2011.
