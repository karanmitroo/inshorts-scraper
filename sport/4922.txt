Indian captain Sunil Chhetri, who is the fourth best international goal scorer among active international players, has the best goals-per-game ratio among the top-5 highest active international goalscorers. Chhetri's goalscoring rate of .57 goals per match is better than Portugal captain Cristiano Ronaldo's rate of .53 goals per match and Lionel Messi's conversion rate of .49 goals per game.
