Cousins Vinesh and Ritu Phogat bagged gold medals at the National Wrestling Championship on Thursday. Commonwealth Games gold medalist Vinesh, representing Railways, finished first after beating Haryana's Manisha in the women's 55-kg category, while Ritu won the women's 50-kg event after beating Nirmala. Sakshi Malik, Geeta Phogat and Sushil Kumar are also taking part in the nationals.
