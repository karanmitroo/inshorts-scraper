Rising Pune Supergiant's captain Steve Smith scored an unbeaten 54-ball 84 to help the team chase 185 in their IPL encounter against Mumbai Indians on Thursday. Mumbai put on a total of 184/8, with Nitish Rana, Hardik Pandya and Jos Buttler scoring 30s. Mumbai Indians' Kieron Pollard failed to defend 13 runs in the match's final over.
