Commenting on the government's decision to demonetise old ₹500 and ₹1000 notes, former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag tweeted, "After 31st March too, 5 banks assigned to accept 500/1000. Banks of Ganga,Yamuna, Saraswati, Narmada, Godavari. #ModifiedForward #DeMonetisation." The government had announced that banks will not accept any older versions of ₹500 and ₹1000 notes from March 31, 2017.
