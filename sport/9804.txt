Indian spinner Ravichandran Ashwin has become the leading wicket-taker in Tests in 2016, taking 55 wickets in nine matches. He surpassed Sri Lanka's Rangana Herath, who has 54 wickets from eight matches. With six five-wicket hauls and two ten-fors in a match this year, Ashwin is the only bowler to take 50+ Test wickets in two consecutive years.
