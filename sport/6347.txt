Sunrisers Hyderabad pacer Ashish Nehra completed 100 wickets in IPL, taking two wickets in two balls against Royal Challengers Bangalore in the opening match of IPL 10 in Hyderabad on Wednesday. He is the eighth bowler overall to accomplish the feat and the first left-arm bowler to do so. Nehra finished with figures of 2/42 in his stipulated four overs.
