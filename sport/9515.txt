A video has emerged of Virat Kohli and Anushka Sharma dancing with newlyweds Yuvraj Singh and Hazel Keech at the latter's wedding reception in Goa on Friday. While Anushka is seen dancing with guests on the song 'Senorita', Virat is shaking a leg with the couple on 'Gangnam Style'. Earlier in the day, Virat and Anushka attended the wedding ceremony.
