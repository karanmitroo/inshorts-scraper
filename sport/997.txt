Premier League table-toppers Manchester City's unbeaten streak was ended at 30 games, starting from last season, with a 3-4 away defeat against Liverpool on Sunday. During the unbeaten streak, Man City, who last won at the Merseyside ground in May 2003, had an 18-game win streak, longest in PL history. Elsewhere, Arsenal suffered a 1-2 defeat against Bournemouth.
