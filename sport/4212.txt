BCCI paid former India head coach Anil Kumble ₹48.75 lakh as professional fee for the month of April, the board's monthly list of payments has revealed. Cricketer Rohit Sharma was paid ₹1.12 crore as share of gross revenue from the 2015-16 season, while spinner Ravichandran Ashwin received ₹1.01 crore. IPL franchise Mumbai Indians received over ₹56 crore from the board.
