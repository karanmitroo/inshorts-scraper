Spinner Akshay Karnewar, playing for the Board President's XI in the warm-up match against Australia, bowled using both his arms on Tuesday. Akshay bowled off-spin to left-handers and left-arm spin to right-handers, taking the wicket of Travis Head. Akshay started as a right-arm off-spinner but batted and threw left-handed, after which his coach convinced him to bowl left-arm as well.
