India's compound men's archery team beat Colombia 226-221 to win the gold medal at the season's first World Cup in Shanghai on Saturday. The fourth-seeded team, comprising Abhishek Verma, Chinna Raju Srither and Amanjeet Singh had defeated USA on Friday to reach the final. Verma and Jyothi Surekha Vennam will compete in the bronze play-off in the mixed pair event.
