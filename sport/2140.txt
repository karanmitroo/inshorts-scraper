After Twitter increased the character limit from 140 to 280, ICC trolled Sri Lankan cricketers through a tweet that included full names of four Sri Lankan cricketers, which were over four words each. Reacting to it, a user wrote, "How cud you forget our very own Vangipurappu Venkata Sai Laxman aka @VVSLaxman281 and darji-in-form Luteru Ross Poutoa Lote Taylor."
