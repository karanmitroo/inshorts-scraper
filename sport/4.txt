MS Dhoni has been downgraded to the second tier in BCCI's new contract system, following which pacers Bhuvneshwar Kumar and Jasprit Bumrah will be paid a higher retainership fee than the former captain. Dhoni, who is in Grade A, will get ₹5 crore, while the five Grade A+ cricketers, including Bhuvneshwar and Bumrah, will get ₹7 crore.
