Indian Olympic Association has sent a 208-member contingent, its largest ever, to the 5th Asian Beach Games starting on September 24 in Danang, Vietnam. Athletes will participate in 13 disciplines including swimming, kabaddi, handball and Ju-Jitsu, among others. India won 10 medals at the last Asian Beach Games in Phuket in 2014, including two golds, one silver and seven bronze.
