Switzerland's Roger Federer advanced to the last-16 of the Australian Open 2017 after defeating tenth-seeded Czech Tomas Berdych 6-2, 6-4, 6-4 on Friday. The four-time Australian Open champion dispatched Berdych in an hour and 32 minutes. Top-seed Britain's Andy Murray also progressed to the last-16 after a straight-set win over USA's Sam Querrey.
