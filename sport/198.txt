Virat Kohli will not be playing the T20I series decider against South Africa at Cape Town due to a stiff back. The Indian team will be lead by opener Rohit Sharma and includes Axar Patel, Dinesh Karthik and Jasprit Bumrah in place of Yuzvendra Chahal, Virat Kohli and Jaydev Unadkat respectively. This will be Rohit's fourth T20I match as captain.
