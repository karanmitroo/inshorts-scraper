Defender Jitendra Singh, aged 16 years, six months and 13 days, became the youngest goalscorer in I-League history after scoring for Indian Arrows against Shillong Lajong on Tuesday. Singh, who represented India in the 2017 U-17 World Cup, went past winger Baoringdao Bodo, who was earlier the youngest to score, at 17 years, 3 months and 18 days.
