Actor Aamir Khan has revealed that Sachin Tendulkar appealed for an English wicket while watching 'Lagaan'. In a video released by Aamir to wish Sachin for his upcoming biopic 'Sachin: A Billion Dreams', Aamir said, "My favourite Sachin memory is seeing him appeal for the first British wicket in Lagaan...it was a run-out and Sachin was out of his seat."
