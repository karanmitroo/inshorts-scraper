Mumbai Indians defeated Kolkata Knight Riders by nine runs in Kolkata on Saturday to finish the IPL 2017 group stage as table toppers. Batting first, MI set KKR a target of 174, with Ambati Rayudu and Saurabh Tiwary scoring half-centuries. KKR wrapped up their innings at 164/8, but are still assured of a playoff berth and will play the eliminator.
