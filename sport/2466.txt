NBA player Stephen Curry has been fined ₹32 lakh for throwing his mouthguard "in the direction of an official" during his team Golden State Warriors defeat against Memphis Grizzlies. The two-time NBA MVP was ejected with 43.6 seconds remaining in the game. In 2016 NBA Finals as well, Curry was fined ₹16 lakh for throwing the mouthguard towards a fan.
