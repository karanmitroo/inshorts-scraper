Team India's sponsorship from 2017 to 2022 is expected to fetch BCCI over ₹538 crore. BCCI has set a base price of ₹2.2 crore per match for the 238 bilateral games in that period, while each of the 21 ICC matches are priced at ₹70 lakh. The tender process has started for rights' distribution, and will end on March 7.
