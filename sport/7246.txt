A goal apiece from Cesc Fabregas, Pedro, and Diego Costa helped Chelsea widen the gap at top of the Premier League table to 11 points, after a 3-1 win over Swansea. Fabregas opened the scoring in the 19th minute before Fernando Llorente's equaliser in the first half's injury time. Pedro and Costa scored in the 72nd and 84th minute, respectively.
