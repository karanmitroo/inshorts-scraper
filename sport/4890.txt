Cricketer Yuvraj Singh called Indian tennis player Sania Mirza 'Mirchi' during a Twitter conversation. BCCI had tweeted a photograph of Yuvraj posing alongside his doppelganger, to which Yuvraj had replied, "No chance." Sania, retweeting the picture, posted, "Same!!!!", at which Yuvraj wrote, "@MirzaSania Eh no' Mirchi." Cricketer Robin Uthappa also commented on the doppelganger's picture.
