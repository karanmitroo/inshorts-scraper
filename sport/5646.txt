As per reports, a Bollywood film on football club Aizawl FC coach Khalid Jamil titled 'Zero To Hero' is being planned. As per Aizawl FC owner Robert Romawia Royte, Ajay Devgn will be playing the lead role of Jamil in the film. Aizawl FC became the first Northeast team to win the Indian football tournament I-League on April 30, 2017.
