A late fightback by India denied Argentina their third win of the Six Nations Invitational Hockey Tournament as the Men in Blue drew 3-3 at Valencia on Saturday. Matias Paredes, Gonzalo Peillat and Lucas Vila scored for Argentina while Rupinder Pal Singh, Ramandeep Singh and Devinder Sunil Walmiki scored for India. India will now take on Spain on July 3.
