Indian all-rounder Ravichandran Ashwin has said that he will retire the day he reaches 618 Test wickets. "I'm a big fan of Anil Kumble and he has got 619 wickets...if I get to 618 wickets, I'll be very thankful too and...that will be my last Test match," the 31-year-old said. Ashwin has taken 292 wickets in 52 Test matches.
