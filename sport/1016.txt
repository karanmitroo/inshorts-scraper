India won its first Under-19 Cricket World Cup in 2000 under the leadership of Mohammad Kaif, who later went on to play 13 Tests and 125 ODIs for India. India's current senior team captain Virat Kohli led the Under-19 team to victory in the 2008 edition. India last clinched the title in 2012 under the captaincy of Unmukt Chand.
