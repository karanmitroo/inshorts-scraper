The Indo-Czech pair of Sania Mirza and Barbora Strycova finished as the runner-up in the Wuhan Open, after a 1-6, 4-6 loss to fifth-seeds Bethanie Mattek-Sands and Lucie Safarova on Saturday. Since Mirza's split with former partner Martina Hingis, she has won three out of the five doubles tournaments she has participated in.
