Bollywood actor Amitabh Bachchan hailed Indian women's cricket team captain Mithali Raj for her response to a journalist who had asked about her favourite men's cricketer. Mithali had snapped at the reporter saying, "Do you ask the same question to a male cricketer? Do you ask them who their favourite female cricketer is?" Amitabh reacted to her response, tweeting, "Shabash...!"
