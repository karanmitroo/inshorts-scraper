PV Sindhu's jersey, which she wore during her silver win at the Rio Olympics, and Sachin's Tendulkar's gloves, worn during his 100th international ton, have been donated to a Kolkata museum. The museum, which opens on January 29, will also showcase memorabilia donated by Abhinav Bindra and Paralympians Devendra Jhajharia and Deepa Malik, among others.
