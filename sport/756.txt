South African batsman Hashim Amla, who scored two tons for Kings XI Punjab in last year's IPL, went unsold during the IPL 2018 auction on Saturday. Former Rajasthan Royals batsman Shane Watson was sold to CSK for ₹4 crore. Chris Woakes was bought by RCB for ₹7.4 crore, while Windies' Carlos Brathwaite was sold to Sunrisers Hyderabad for ₹2 crore.
