India ended the day three of the Visakhapatnam Test at 98/3, leading England by 298 runs in the second innings. England, helped by Ben Stokes' 70 late in the innings, posted a total of 255, with Ravichandran Ashwin claiming his 22nd five-for in Tests. Indian captain Virat Kohli scored a half century and remained not-out on 56(70).
