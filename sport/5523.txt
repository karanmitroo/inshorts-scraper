Royal Challengers Bangalore batsmen scored a total of 1,608 runs in 13 matches in IPL 2017, 52 runs less than what Virat Kohli and AB de Villiers scored in IPL 2016. Kohli and de Villiers piled up 1,660 runs last year, while they scored a combined 466 runs this season. RCB play their last IPL 2017 match on Sunday.
