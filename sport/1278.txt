Sydney Thunder wicketkeeper-batsman Jos Buttler slammed four sixes in the 12th over off Hobart Hurricanes medium-pacer Thomas Rogers in the Big Bash League on Saturday. His fourth six of the over travelled a distance of 110 metres, surpassing the distance covered by his second maximum, which measured 108 metres. Buttler top-scored for Sydney Thunder, with 67 runs off 41 balls.
