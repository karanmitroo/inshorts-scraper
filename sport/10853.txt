On the occasion of cricketer Gautam Gambhir's 35th birthday, Sachin Tendulkar tweeted, "Wishing a wonderful birthday and a wonderful life ahead. Hope the coming days bring in a lot of happiness @GautamGambhir!" Virender Sehwag posted, "Many Many happy returns of the day Gautam Gambir," to which Gambhir replied, "I was expecting a wish in your commentary style bhai."
