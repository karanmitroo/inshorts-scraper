Following his comeback into the Indian team, Yuvraj Singh shared a joke about himself and fast bowler Ashish Nehra on his Instagram page. The joke compared Nehra and Yuvraj to ₹500 and ₹1,000 notes. Yuvraj reacted to the joke saying, "Good one ! Whoever made this." Notably, Yuvraj Singh is making his return to the ODI side after three years.
