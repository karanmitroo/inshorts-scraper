Commentators at the India-England Test did the Mannequin Challenge in the commentary box at Rajkot on Friday. The video, posted by BCCI, shows Ravi Shastri, Kapil Dev, Ian Botham, Sanjay Manjrekar, among others, remaining still for the duration of the video. The Mannequin Challenge is a viral trend which requires people to remain still while a video is being recorded.
