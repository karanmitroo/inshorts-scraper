Driven by Jonny Bairstow's fighting 89 run-innings off 177 balls, England ended the opening day of the third Test against India at 268/8 at Mohali on Saturday. India's Umesh Yadav, Jayant Yadav and Ravindra Jadeja picked up two wickets each. Notably, Bairstow is the highest run-scorer in Tests in 2016 so far.
