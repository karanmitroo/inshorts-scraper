Atletico Madrid forward Kevin Gameiro scored the fastest hat trick in the La Liga in 22 years after scoring three goals in 4 minutes and 43 seconds against Sporting Gijon on Saturday. Gameiro's hat-trick was second only to Brazilian forward Bebeto's four-minute hat-trick against Albacete in 1995. Prior to Gameiro's hat-trick, the match was being played at 1-1.
