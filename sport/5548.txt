Sunrisers Hyderabad defeated Gujarat Lions by 8 wickets on Saturday to qualify for the IPL 2017 playoffs. After Dwayne Smith and Ishan Kishan's fifties, GL registered IPL's worst collapse to be dismissed for 154 after Mohammed Siraj picked up four wickets. David Warner and Vijay Shankar scored unbeaten sixties to help SRH reach the second spot with 17 points.
