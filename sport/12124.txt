Real Madrid players gatecrashed manager Zinedine Zidane’s post-match press conference, to celebrate their 3-2 win over Sevilla in the UEFA Super Cup on Tuesday. Captain Sergio Ramos entered with defender Marcelo and striker Álvaro Morata, among other players, singing and spraying water over Zidane. This was Zidane’s second title win as Madrid's manager, after the Champions League win in May.
