On the occasion of Anil Kumble's 46th birthday, his former teammate Sachin Tendulkar tweeted, "A champion in every sense of the word, happy birthday @anilkumble1074! May you lead our country to jumbo victories always!". "Happy Birthday @anilkumble1074 Immortal 1st innings in Indian cricket, hope to see you go one further in the 2nd innings.Have a great day!", tweeted Gautam Gambhir.
