India's junior women's hockey team defeated hosts Spain 3-2 in a five-nation invitational hockey tournament on Wednesday. Spain's Clara Ycart opened the scoring in the 15th minute before Jyoti's 28th-minute equaliser. India took the lead in the 42nd minute with Ritu's goal, which was cancelled out two minutes later. A 62nd-minute strike by Sangita Kumari won India the match.
