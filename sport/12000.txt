Shuttler PV Sindhu will be gifted a BMW by the Hyderabad District Badminton Association, V Chamundeshwarnath, president of the association, announced on Thursday. Sindhu became the first Indian to reach an Olympic badminton final after she defeated Japan’s Nozomi Okuhara in straight sets on Thursday. The shuttler will now play for the gold medal against Spain’s Carolina Marin on Friday.
