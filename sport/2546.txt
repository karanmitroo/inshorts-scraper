World number 14 HS Prannoy defeated three-time Olympic silver medalist and former world number one Malaysia's Lee Chong Wei 21-17, 11-21, 21-19 to enter the quarterfinals of Denmark Open on Thursday. Meanwhile, in women's singles category, world number 12 Saina Nehwal also booked a place in the quarters after defeating Thailand's Nitchaon Jindapol 22-20, 21-13.
