Thirty-year-old wicketkeeper-batsman Abid Ali slammed the highest-ever score by a Pakistani in List A cricket by smashing 209*(156) in a domestic one-day match on Tuesday. Ali slammed 24 fours and 5 sixes during his knock against Peshawar, helping Islamabad put up a total of 374/1 in 50 overs. The previous highest List A score by a Pakistani was 207.
