Reacting to PV Sindhu's one hour, 50 minute-long World Championships' final against Japan's Nozomi Okuhara, Saina Nehwal said, "Mera petrol khatam ho gaya dekhte, dekhte (I ran out of fuel, just watching this game) Wonderful match." Carolina Marin, who defeated Sindhu in the gold medal match at Rio Olympics, tweeted, "Amazing final girls!! Congrats to both of you @nozomi_o11 @Pvsindhu1".
