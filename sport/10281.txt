Sachin Tendulkar called Virat Kohli a 'shararati guy' while wishing him on Twitter on his 28th birthday on Saturday. Sachin tweeted, "Happy Birthday @imVkohli! To the most (shararati guy) in our team, stay the way you are always!" Coach Anil Kumble posted, "Happy birthday @imVkohli. Hope you have a fantastic year ahead. Wish you the best of everything."
