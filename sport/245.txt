Norway's Havard Lorentzen won the men's 500-metre speed skating by a margin of just 0.01 seconds to set a Winter Olympics record. Lorentzen finished the race in a time of 34.41, surpassing the previous record of 34.42 set by USA's Casey Fitzrandolph in 2002. South Korea's Cha Min-kyu (34.42) won the silver medal, while China's Gao Tingyu (34.65) finished third.
