Wishing Indian batsman Cheteshwar Pujara on his 29th birthday, former cricketer Virender Sehwag on Wednesday tweeted, "Happy Birthday @cheteshwar1 Pujara. May your batting continue like Dadi Amma's story, never ending. Zindagi Na Milegi Pujara!" Indian cricket coach Anil Kumble also wished the batsman, tweeting, "Many happy returns of the day @cheteshwar1 wishing you success! @BCCI."
