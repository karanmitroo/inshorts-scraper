Lyricist Javed Akhtar on Tuesday slammed wrestler Yogeshwar Dutt and former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag for trolling Delhi University student Gurmehar Kaur while terming them as "hardly literate". "If a hardly literate player or a wrestler troll a pacifist daughter of a martyr its understandable but whats wrong with some educated folks," tweeted Akhtar.
