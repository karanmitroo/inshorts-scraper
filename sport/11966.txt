Australian pacer Mitchell Starc became the fastest bowler to take 100 ODI wickets, while playing against Sri Lanka in his 52nd ODI on Sunday. He surpassed Pakistani spinner Saqlain Mushtaq's 53 ODI mark, set in 1997. Starc took 2,452 deliveries to reach the landmark, which is also the quickest in terms of deliveries, ahead of Shoaib Akhtar's 2,707-ball mark.
