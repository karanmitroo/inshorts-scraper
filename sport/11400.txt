Wrestler Yogeshwar Dutt on Tuesday shared a poem he wrote for the Indian soldiers killed in the Uri terror attack. "गद्दारों के महल बन गए .. मुंह ढँककर सियासत सोती रही। उन्होंने शहीद कहकर फुसला लिया .. बेवा मुआवजे को रोती रही।" read the tweet. The wrestler had also written a poem on the JNU controversy earlier this year.
