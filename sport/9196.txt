Cricketer Yuvraj Singh and wife Hazel Keech have featured on the December-January cover of 'Harper's Bazaar Bride' magazine. Yuvraj is wearing an embroidered JJ Valaya sherwani while Hazel is wearing a maroon JJ Valaya lehenga, teamed with a velvet blouse, and a dupatta over her head. The cover story on them is titled,' Yuvraj and Hazel tie the knot'.
