World number 133 Ukrainian tennis player Kateryna Bondarenko on Saturday became the first  mother to win a WTA singles title since former player Kim Clijsters won the 2011 Australian Open. Bondarenko, who had become a mother in 2012, defeated Hungary's Timea Babos 6-4, 6-4 in the final of the Tashkent Open. The victory was Bondarenko's first title in nine years.
