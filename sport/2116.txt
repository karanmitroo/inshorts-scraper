Indian cueist Pankaj Advani clinched his 17th world title after winning the IBSF World Billiards Championship in Doha on Sunday. Advani defeated arch-rival Mike Russell of England to defend the 150-up format title he had won in Bengaluru last year. Advani, who won his first major title in 2003, has won 13 world titles in Billiards and four in Snooker.
