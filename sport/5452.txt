Commenting on the IPL eliminator between Kolkata Knight Riders and Sunrisers Hyderabad finishing at 1.27 am on Wednesday, KKR pacer Nathan Coulter-Nile said one cannot be playing cricket at 2 am. "Rules probably need to be looked at. You can't be playing cricket at 2 am...There was so much time, we had to get on," said Coulter-Nile.
