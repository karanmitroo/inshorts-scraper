Virat Kohli has become the highest run-getter while chasing in T20Is after his 82-run knock against Sri Lanka on Wednesday. Kohli, who now has 1,016 runs while chasing, went past former New Zealand captain Brendon McCullum's tally of 1,006 runs. Kohli also became the third highest run-getter in T20Is with 1,830 runs, and is only behind Tillakaratne Dilshan and McCullum.
