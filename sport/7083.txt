Lionel Messi scored a brace as Barcelona routed Celta Vigo 5-0 to return to the top of La Liga on Saturday. Messi opened the scoring in the 24th minute, dribbling 45 yards to net the ball, before Neymar made it 2-0. Barça increased their lead with goals from Ivan Rakitić and Samuel Umtiti before another solo effort from Messi.
