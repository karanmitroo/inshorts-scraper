Yorkshire scored 260/4 against Northamptonshire in the NatWest T20 Blast to post English domestic cricket's highest and the overall joint-second highest total in T20 history. Yorkshire were helped by Adam Lyth's 73-ball 161, the highest score a batsman has been dismissed for in T20s. Lyth's century was his first in 85 T20s and included 20 fours and 7 sixes.
