Cash rewards amounting to ₹90 lakh have been announced for the Rio Paralympics medalists by the Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment, under its Awareness Generation and Publicity scheme. Gold winners Mariyappan Thangavelu and Devendra Jhajharia will be awarded ₹30 lakh each, while silver medalist Deepa Malik will receive ₹20 lakh. Bronze winner Varun Bhati will get ₹10 lakh.
