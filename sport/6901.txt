World number one Andy Murray was defeated by world number 129, Canada's Vasek Pospisil,  at the BNP Paribas Open at Indian Wells on Sunday. Pospisil, a qualifier at the tournament, defeated Murray 6-4, 7-6 (7-5) in the second round, to register his first victory against the Briton in five meetings. Pospisil has won the Wimbledon men's doubles title in 2014.
