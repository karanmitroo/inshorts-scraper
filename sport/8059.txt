Rio Olympics bronze medal-winning wrestler Sakshi Malik and Olympic gymnast Dipa Karmakar are set to receive the Padma Shri, India's fourth-highest civilian honour. Paralympic Games medalists Deepa Malik and Mariyappan Thangavelu are also among the Padma Shri awardees. The list further includes hockey captain PR Sreejesh, four-time Olympian Vikas Gowda and blind cricket team captain Shekar Naik.
