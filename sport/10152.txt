India ended day two of the first Test at Rajkot at 63/0, trailing England by 474 runs. Earlier in the day, England wrapped up their first innings at 537, driven by hundreds from Joe Root, Moeen Ali and Ben Stokes. While Ravindra Jadeja ended with figures of 3/86, Mohammad Shami, Umesh Yadav and Ravichandran Ashwin got two wickets each.
