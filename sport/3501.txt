Indian shuttler PV Sindhu has entered the final of the Badminton World Championships for the first time, assuring India of at least a silver medal. The 22-year-old defeated world number 10 China's Chen Yufei 21-13, 21-10 in the semi-final on Saturday. Sindhu, who has won two bronze medals at the world meet previously, will face Japan's Nozomi Okuhara on Sunday.
