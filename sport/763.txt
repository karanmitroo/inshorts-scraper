India's Dinesh Karthik was sold to Kolkata Knight Riders for ₹7.4 crore, while Parthiv Patel, who is playing for India in the third Test against South Africa, went unsold. India's Wriddhiman Saha was sold to Sunrisers Hyderabad for ₹5 crore, while KKR used their Right to Match option to buy Robin Uthappa for ₹6.4 crore.
