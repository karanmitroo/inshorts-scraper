Defending FA Cup champions Manchester United defeated Wigan Athletic 4-0 at Old Trafford to seal their spot in the FA Cup Round of 16. Marouane Fellaini opened the scoring in the 44th minute followed by three second-half goals by Chris Smalling, Henrikh Mkhitaryan and Bastian Schweinsteiger. Notably, Schweinsteiger made his first start for United in over a year.
