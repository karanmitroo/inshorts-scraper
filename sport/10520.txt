New Zealand defeated India by 19 runs in the fourth ODI at Ranchi on Wednesday, to level the five-match series 2-2. Batting first, the Kiwis made 260/7, with opener Martin Guptill top-scoring with 72 (84). India were then bowled out for 241, driven by Ajinkya Rahane's 57(70), while Tim Southee claimed three wickets for NZ.
