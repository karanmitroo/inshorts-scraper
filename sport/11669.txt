World number five Rafael Nadal will play Spain's Davis Cup World Group play-off tie against India in New Delhi from September 16-18. India will be represented by Ramkumar Ramanathan, Saketh Myneni, Leander Paes and Rohan Bopanna. The 14-time Grand Slam winner has earlier played in the country, featuring for Indian Aces in the International Premier Tennis League in December 2015.
