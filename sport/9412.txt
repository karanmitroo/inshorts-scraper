BCCI tweeted a video of team India celebrating the birthday of Ravindra Jadeja and Karun Nair. The video is captioned, "Happy birthday @imjadeja & @karun126 Say cheese to the cake face" and shows the birthday boys cutting cakes. Ravichandran Ashwin, Umesh Yadav and Cheteshwar Pujara are seen smearing the cake on Jadeja and Nair's faces.
