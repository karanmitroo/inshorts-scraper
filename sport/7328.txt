Mumbai Cricket Association (MCA) has demanded an explanation from middle-order batsman Suryakumar Yadav regarding a tweet he had retweeted on his omission from the team for Inter-state T20 tournament. Yadav's name was not included in the 14-member Mumbai squad for Vijay Hazare Trophy while the 15th member's slot has been left vacant till a decision by MCA's Managing committee.
