India B's Shikhar Dhawan scored a ton while pacer Dhawal Kulkarni bagged a hat-trick in India B's 23-run win over India A in the Deodhar Trophy. Dhawan's 128(122) helped India B reach 327/8 in 50 overs. Kulkarni dismissed Shardul Thakur off the 47th over's last ball before dismissing Deepak Hooda and Siddarth Kaul off the 49th over's first two balls.
