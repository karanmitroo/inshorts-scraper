WBO's Asia Pacific super middleweight champion, Vijender Singh has claimed that his bout with British-Pakistani boxer Amir Khan will be the 'Fight of the Century'. "[W]hether Delhi or Lahore or Karachi, let's do it. I'm ready for the bout anywhere in the world", Vijender added. Earlier, Amir had congratulated Vijender, asking him to be careful in wishing for the bout.
