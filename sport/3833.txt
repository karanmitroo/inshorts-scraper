Windies' cricketer Chris Gayle, who is the only player to score 10,000 runs in T20 cricket, said in a recent interview, "T20 cricket has been made for me...or I should say I created T20 cricket." Gayle further added that he loves entertaining the crowd, whose support gives him goosebumps. Chris Gayle has slammed 750 sixes in 298 T20 matches.
