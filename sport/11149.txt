Bangladeshi batsman Tamim Iqbal overtook Shakib Al Hasan to record the most number of ODI hundreds (7) for Bangladesh. He scored his record-breaking ton against Afghanistan on Saturday. The 27-year-old's run-a-ball 118, which included 11 fours and 2 sixes, helped Bangladesh reach 279/8. Tamim Iqbal reached this historic feat in his 156th match for the nation.
