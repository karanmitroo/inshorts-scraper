Former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag has been elected as an Honorary Life Member of the Marylebone Cricket Club (MCC). The Indian opener joined compatriots Sachin Tendulkar, Rahul Dravid and Sourav Ganguly on the list of Honorary Life Members. "It is always a pleasure to welcome new Honorary Life Members to the Club, and Virender is no exception", said MCC's chief.
