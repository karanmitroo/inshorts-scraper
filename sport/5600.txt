Delhi Daredevils defeated Gujarat Lions by 2 wickets in the Indian Premier League on Wednesday. Aaron Finch's 69(39) helped GL put up 195/5 in their 20 overs before DD chased down the target with 2 balls to spare, helped by Shreyas Iyer's 96(57). Notably, both the teams are out of the contention for a playoff berth in the tournament.
