On the occasion of former Indian pacer Zaheer Khan's 38th birthday, Virender Sehwag tweeted, "Happy Birthday Gyan Baba. Most popular love story- Ranjha & Heer, The cleverest bowler ever @ImZaheer. Heer nahi asli Heera hai Zaheer." Zaheer announced his retirement from international and first-class cricket in October 2015, after playing 92 Tests, 200 ODIs and 17 T20Is for India.
