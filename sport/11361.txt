Ahead of India's 500th Test starting Thursday, cricket magazine Wisden has named an all-time Indian Test XI, led by the current limited-overs captain MS Dhoni. Sunil Gavaskar and Virender Sehwag are the openers, followed by Rahul Dravid at number three. The team includes Sachin Tendulkar, VVS Laxman, Javagal Srinath and Zaheer Khan, with former captain Mohammad Azharuddin named 12th man.
