Former Indian captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni on Wednesday became the cricketer with most fielding dismissals in T20Is. With his 4 dismissals in the T20I against Sri Lanka, Dhoni took his overall tally to 74 dismissals, two more than South African cricketer AB de Villiers. Out of his 74 dismissals, 47 are catches while the remaining 27 are stumpings.
