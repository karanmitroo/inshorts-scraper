New Jersey's Scott Blumstein won ₹52 crore ($8,150,000) after claiming the World Series of Poker championship on Sunday. This was Blumstein's first time ever playing the championship's Main Event. Daniel Ott, also playing in his first Main Event, finished runner-up with a prize money of ₹30 crore ($4,700,000). This was World Series of Poker's third-largest Main Event.
