Indian woman cricketer Veda Krishnamurthy took to Twitter to share a picture of herself imitating ex-Pakistan captain Shahid Afridi's celebration after taking a wicket in Women's Big Bash League. "Did I just do a @SAfridiOfficial... how gud is that? #feelswhenutakeawicket," she wrote. Responding to it, Afridi wrote, "Good try, work harder and more importantly keep taking wickets you'll get there."
