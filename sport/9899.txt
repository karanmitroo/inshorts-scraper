European football body, UEFA on Friday revealed their 'Ultimate Team of the Year'. The team was decided based on the previous team of the year nominations. Real Madrid and Barcelona dominate with combined nine players in the all-time XI. The team, with a 4-3-3 formation, includes Casillas, Ramos, Pique, Puyol, Lahm, Gerrard, Xavi, Iniesta, Henry, Ronaldo, and Messi.
