World number 222 India's Ramkumar Ramanathan defeated French Open semi-finalist and world number 8 Dominic Thiem to enter the quarterfinals of the Antalya Open in Turkey on Tuesday. The 22-year-old took 59 minutes to upset Thiem with a scoreline of 6-3, 6-2. Ramanathan became the first Indian tennis player to beat a top 10 player in singles since 2014.
