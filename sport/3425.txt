Former Indian pacer Javagal Srinath, who turned 48 on Thursday, picked up a hat-trick in his first innings of his Ranji Trophy debut. Srinath, who is popularly known as the Mysore Express, finished with figures of five for 85 in that innings. Notably, Srinath is India's second highest wicket-taker in ODIs, having scalped 315 wickets in the format.
