Seventh-seeded pair of Sania Mirza from India and Barbora Strycova from Czech Republic defeated the American-Japanese pair of Nicole Gibbs and Nao Hibino 6-4, 7-5 on Monday to enter the quarterfinals of 2016 US Open. In the last 8 round, the Indo-Czech pair will face the tournament's top seeds Kristina Mladenovic and Caroline Garcia from France.
