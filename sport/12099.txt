Former Indian batsman Sachin Tendulkar was the only Indian in the greatest XI picked by former Australian captain Ricky Ponting. The team, captained by Kumar Sangakkara, comprised of Justin Langer, Matthew Hayden, Jacques Kallis, Brian Lara and Adam Gilchrist in the batting lineup. Shane Warne, Wasim Akram, Curtly Ambrose and Glenn McGrath formed the team's bowling attack.
