Premier League's 2016 summer transfer window saw the total amount reach record £1.165 billion (over ₹10,000 crore), topping the previous best of £870 million in 2015. While 13 clubs broke their transfer records, Manchester City led the window, spending £174.05 million. In the same period, Paul Pogba, signed by Manchester United, became the world's most expensive footballer at £93.25 million.
