Brazilian forward Neymar, who made a €222 million record move from Barcelona to PSG, was unveiled by the French club in Paris on Friday. Neymar, who has been signed on a five-year contract, will reportedly take home a salary of €600,000 (₹4.5 crore)-a-week after tax. He will sport the number 10 jersey, after Javier Pastore offered it to him.
