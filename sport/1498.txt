Former Pakistani captain Shahid Afridi took a hat-trick with his first three balls in the 10 overs-a-side  cricket while playing for the Pakhtoons in the T10 League. Afridi dismissed Maratha Arabians' Rilee Rossouw, Dwayne Bravo, and captain Virender Sehwag. The Pakhtoons made 121/4 in their 10 overs, before restricting the Arabians to 96/7 in the 10 overs.
