Pacer Jasprit Bumrah's maiden international scalps have been former or current world number one batsmen in different formats. Before dismissing AB de Villiers as his maiden Test scalp on Friday, Bumrah's first ODI wicket on his debut in 2016 was Australian captain and top-ranked Test batsman, Steve Smith. On his T20I debut, Bumrah dismissed former top-ranked ODI batsman, David Warner.
