India's Chinaman bowler Kuldeep Yadav, who took a hat-trick in the second ODI against Australia on Thursday, credited former captain MS Dhoni and Virat Kohli for his feat. After the match, Kuldeep said, "Asked Mahi bhai, (he said) tujhe jaisa bhi lagta hai daal de. Virat and Dhoni gave me the confidence (before the hat-trick ball)."
