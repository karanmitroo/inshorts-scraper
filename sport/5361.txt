The Indian women's cricket team defeated the South African women's team by 8 wickets to clinch the Women's Quadrangular ODI Series on Sunday. Batting first, SA were bundled out for 160, with Jhulan Goswami and Poonam Yadav picking up three wickets each. In reply, Punam Raut's 92-ball 70* and Mithali Raj's 79-ball 62* helped India win in 33 overs.
