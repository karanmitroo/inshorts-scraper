Argentina football captain Lionel Messi has signed a new five-year contract with Barcelona which reportedly contains a buy-out clause of over ₹2,200 crore (€300 million). His previous contract was due to run out in 2018. The 30-year-old, who has scored a club-record 507 goals in 583 matches, is currently on vacation with his newly-wed wife, Antonella Roccuzzo.
