India defeated Windies by 105 runs in the second ODI on Sunday, taking a 1-0 lead in the five-match ODI series. Shikhar Dhawan and Ajinkya Rahane recorded their sixth consecutive 50+ stand in ODIs, helping India post their 96th 300+ ODI score, most for any team. This was India's biggest win in terms of runs against Windies in the Caribbean.
