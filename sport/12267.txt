Two-time Olympic medal-winning wrestler Sushil Kumar took to Twitter to congratulate Narsingh Yadav after the latter was given a clean chit to participate in the Rio Olympics. The 33-year-old wrestler tweeted, "बहुत ख़ुशी की बात है l मेरा सपोर्ट पहले भी था आज भी है और कल भी रहेगा l Go win for me and the country #NarsinghYadav".
