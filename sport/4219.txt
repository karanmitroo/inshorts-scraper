Former cricketer Sachin Tendulkar has said he and Wimbledon champion Roger Federer connect over PlayStation. "Roger follows cricket... When he is playing video games, he prefers Sachin and when I am playing any racquet game, I prefer to be Roger," said Sachin. The cricketer was present at the semi-final, where Federer defeated Tomáš Berdych to reach his 11th Wimbledon final.
