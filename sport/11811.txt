Wrestling Federation of India (WFI) President Brij Bhushan Sharan Singh has said the organisation​ has no money to reward Rio Olympics bronze-winning wrestler Sakshi Malik. Singh blamed the government and Sports Authority of India for not financially aiding the federation and the sport. Sakshi has reportedly been awarded ₹5 crore cumulatively, including ₹2.5 crore by the Haryana state government.
