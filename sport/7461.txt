Zlatan Ibrahimovic scored his first hat-trick for Manchester United and 17th overall, as United beat French club St Etienne 3-0 in the Europa League round of 32 first leg at Old Trafford. The 35-year-old opened the scoring with a 15th-minute free-kick before scoring two in 13 minutes in the second-half. Ibrahimovic has now scored 23 goals this season across competitions.
