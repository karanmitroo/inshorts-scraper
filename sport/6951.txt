Pakistani Test captain Misbah-ul-Haq smashed 6 sixes off 6 consecutive deliveries in the 2017 Hong Kong T20 Blitz tournament. Misbah hit the final two balls of Imran Arif's 19th over for sixes, before smashing four more in Ashley Caddy's 20th over. Off the seven consecutive balls Misbah faced in the 19th and 20th overs, he managed to score 40 runs.
