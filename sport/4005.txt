Indian cricketer Yuvraj Singh on Monday shared a throwback picture of himself and teammates Zaheer Khan, Harbhajan Singh, Sachin Tendulkar and VVS Laxman sky walking during Indian team's tour of New Zealand in 2009. Yuvraj captioned the picture, "Turn your face to the sun and the shadows fall behind you. #throwbackmemories #MondayMotivation #livedareinspire."
