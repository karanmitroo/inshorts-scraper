After being replaced by KL Rahul in the ongoing India-England Test series, Indian opener Gautam Gambhir will feature in Delhi's Ranji Trophy match against Rajasthan. Gambhir will open the innings along with Shikhar Dhawan, who is returning to cricket after recovering from a thumb injury. Leg-spinner Amit Mishra will also be featuring for Haryana in the Ranji match against Goa.
