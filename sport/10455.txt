A nine-year-old Liverpool fan from Dublin, Sam Breen, dressed up as Liverpool manager Jurgen Klopp for his school's fancy dress to celebrate Halloween. Sam's father posted a picture on Twitter, in which the boy's wearing a blonde wig, thick-rimmed glasses and sports a beard similar to Klopp's. The picture has been retweeted over 1,000 times and received over 3,000 likes.
