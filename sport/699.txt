In the IPL 2018 auction, uncapped Karnataka spinner Krishnappa Gowtham was bought by Rajasthan Royals for ₹6.2 crore, 31 times his base price of ₹20 lakh. All-rounder Krunal Pandya's selling price to base price ratio was the second-highest as he fetched ₹8.8 crore, 22 times his base price. D'Arcy Short came in third, fetching ₹4 crore in the auction.
