The IPL Governing Council has increased minimum auction reserve price for players ahead of the 2018 season. The reserve price for uncapped players was escalated from ₹10 lakh, ₹20 lakh, and ₹30 lakh to ₹20 lakh, ₹30 lakh, and ₹40 lakh respectively. For the capped, new reserve prices are ₹50 lakh (previously ₹30 lakh) and ₹75 lakh (previously ₹50 lakh).
