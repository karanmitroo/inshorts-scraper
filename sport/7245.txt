Polish striker Robert Lewandowski scored a hat-trick as Bayern Munich thrashed Hamburg 8-0 in Carlo Ancelotti's 1,000th competitive match as a head coach. Bayern youngster Kingsley Coman scored a brace while Arjen Robben, David Alaba, and Arturo Vidal scored a goal each. Bayern hold a 44-3 aggregate score over Hamburg in the past seven home matches.
