India's Test squad for the upcoming series against New Zealand has been announced. The batting unit consists of Shikhar Dhawan, Murali Vijay, Cheteshwar Pujara, Virat Kohli, Ajinkya Rahane, Rohit Sharma and KL Rahul. Wriddhiman Saha will keep wickets, while Ravichandran Ashwin, Ravindra Jadeja, Amit Mishra, Ishant Sharma, Mohammad Shami, Bhuvneshwar Kumar and Umesh Yaadav form the bowling attack.
