Bangladesh came out on top against England in the second ODI at Dhaka by 34 runs on Sunday to level the three-match series 1-1. Batting first, Bangladesh posted a total of 238 riding on Mahmudullah's 75-run knock. Later, England's lineup, despite skipper Jos Buttler's 57(57), got dismissed at 204 in 44.4 overs, as Mashrafe Mortaza finished with the figures 4/29.
