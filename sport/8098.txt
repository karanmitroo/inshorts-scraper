Sania Mirza and Ivan Dodig will face Rohan Bopanna and Gabriela Dabrowski in the Australian Open mixed doubles quarter-finals on Wednesday, after both pairs defeated their respective opponents in round two on Tuesday. Meanwhile, Leander Paes and Martina Hingis also reached the quarters with a 6-2, 6-3 win over Australians Casey Dellacqua and Matt Reid.
