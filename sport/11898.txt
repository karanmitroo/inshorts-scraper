Rio Olympics' silver medalist PV Sindhu remained world number 10, while Saina Nehwal dropped four places to the ninth spot in the latest Badminton World Federation rankings. Japan's Nozomi Okuhara, who lost to Sindhu in the Rio semifinals, jumped from sixth to the third spot. Meanwhile, India's Kidambi Srikanth, who lost in the men's quarterfinals, moved to the 10th spot.
