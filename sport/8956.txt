New Zealand defeated Bangladesh by 77 runs in the first ODI in Christchurch on Monday. Tom Latham's 121-ball 137 helped New Zealand amass 341/7 in their 50 overs. In reply, Bangladesh could only reach 264/9 in 44.5 overs after Lockie Ferguson and Jimmy Neesham picked three wickets each. Bangladesh next play New Zealand in the 2nd ODI in Nelson.
