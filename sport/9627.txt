Madhya Pradesh's Devendra Bundela has set the record for the most Ranji Trophy appearances, playing his 137th match in the tournament on Tuesday. The 39-year-old surpassed former Mumbai captain Amol Muzumdar to become the most-capped player in the 83-year history of Ranji Trophy. Thirty-five players have played more than 100 games in the tournament, of which four are still active.
