Nine sportspersons were appointed Deputy Superintendent of Police under the sports quota by the Punjab government on Monday. Among those given the post were six hockey players from India's 2014 Asian Games gold-winning team, including current members Manpreet Singh and Akashdeep Singh. Others given the post included Asian Games medal-winning athletes Mandeep Kaur, Khusbir Kaur and Amandeep Kaur.
