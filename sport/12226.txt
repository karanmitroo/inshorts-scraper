Former Indian captain Sourav Ganguly has picked his all-time XI, which includes former teammates Rahul Dravid and Sachin Tendulkar. Australia's Ricky Ponting was chosen the team's captain alongside batsmen Matthew Hayden, Alastair Cook, Jacques Kallis and Kumar Sangakkara. The bowling attack comprised of Glenn McGrath, Shane Warne, Muttiah Muralitharan and Dale Steyn.
