Pakistan captain Misbah-ul-Haq broke ex-New Zealand skipper Brendon McCullum's record of most sixes in Tests by a captain, in the second Test against West Indies at Abu Dhabi on Friday. McCullum, who captained NZ in 31 Tests, hit 59 sixes, while Misbah, playing his 48th Test as skipper, has hit 61. Ex-India captain MS Dhoni is third with 51 sixes.
