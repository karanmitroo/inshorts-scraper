West Indies all-rounder Carlos Brathwaite's championship winning knock in the 2016 World T20 final was awarded the ICC T20I Performance of the Year for his. Needing 19 off the last over, Brathwaite struck four sixes off the first four balls to grab the win. Meanwhile, Afghanistani opener Mohammad Shahzad was named the ICC Associate and Affiliate Cricketer of the Year.
