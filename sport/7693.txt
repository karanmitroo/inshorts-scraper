India's U-19 team scored 382/9, their highest ODI total at home, as they defeated England U-19 by 230 runs in Mumbai on Monday to gain an unassailable 3-1 lead in the series. Overall, this was India's second-highest total in U-19 ODIs after the 425/3 against Scotland in 2004. India's previous highest total at home was 321/5 against Australia in 2013.
