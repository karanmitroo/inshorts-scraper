Following Delhi Daredevils' seven-wicket win over Gujarat Lions on Thursday, mentor Rahul Dravid told players, Rishabh Pant and Sanju Samson, that he was "glad they haven't been watching too many videos of me bat" when the team needed 208 in 20 overs. Pant and Samson put up 143 runs for the second wicket in 10.3 overs, hitting 16 sixes.
