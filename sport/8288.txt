Jamaica's bobsled team has started a crowdfunding campaign for a coach to help them prepare and qualify for the 2018 Winter Olympics. The campaign, started on the site GoFundMe, looks to raise $60,000 (₹40.7 lakh) and has managed over $1,000 (₹68,000) in nine days. The 2018 Olympics will mark 30 years since the team debuted at the Winter Olympics.
