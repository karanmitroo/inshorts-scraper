Indian shuttler Saina Nehwal made a winning start at the Malaysian Masters 2017 after a 21-9, 21-8, win over Thailand's 16-year-old shuttler Chasinee Korepap on Wednesday. The 26-year-old will next face Indonesian badminton player Hanna Ramadini in the second round of the tournament. Notably, defending champion India's PV Sindhu is not playing in the tournament.
