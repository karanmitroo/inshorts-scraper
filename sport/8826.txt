New Zealand completed a 3-0 clean sweep of the ODI series against Bangladesh after defeating the visitors by 8 wickets in the final ODI on Saturday. Batting first, Tamim Iqbal's 59(88) helped Bangladesh reach 236/9 in their 50 overs. In reply, New Zealand chased down the target in 41.2 overs with Neil Broom and Kane Williamson sharing a 179-run partnership.
