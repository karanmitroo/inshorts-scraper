Pakistani cricketer Umar Akmal broke the record for most duck dismissals in T20 cricket after registering his 24th dismissal on zero while playing for Pakistan Super League side Lahore Qalandars against Peshawar Zalmi. Gautam Gambhir leads the Indians' chart with 18 duck dismissals in 214 innings while Harbhajan Singh comes next with 17 duck dismissals in 130 innings.
