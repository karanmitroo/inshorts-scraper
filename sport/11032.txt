Indian all-rounder Suresh Raina has made a comeback to the Indian team after a year, being named in the squad for the first three ODIs against New Zealand starting October 16. MS Dhoni will lead the team, which includes ODI debutantes Mandeep Singh and Jayant Yadav. Ravichandran Ashwin, Ravindra Jadeja and Mohammed Shami have been rested for the three matches.
