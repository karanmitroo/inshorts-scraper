Responding to a Pakistani follower who tried to troll him over the International Court of Justice verdict on Kulbhushan Jadhav, Virender Sehwag tweeted, "Kutta Paalo, galat fahmi mat paalo." Sehwag had tweeted "Satyamev Jayate" after the verdict, at which the follower had slammed him. Sehwag replied to the tweet with a comparison to Pakistan's losses to India in World Cups.
