On the occasion of Australian cricketer David Warner's 30th birthday, Virender Sehwag tweeted, "Happy Bday @davidwarner31. After having the legend Warne, now Aus have Warner. When is Warnest coming? Also,no warning given by both greats." Warner replied to tweet, posting, "Thanks Mr Sehwag @virendersehwag." Sehwag and Warner played together for the IPL franchise Delhi Daredevils from 2009 to 2013.
