Veteran pacer Ashish Nehra and wicketkeeper-batsman Dinesh Karthik were named in the Indian squad for the T20I series against Australia starting October 7. Nehra, aged 38, last played a T20I in February this year against England while Karthik played in July against Windies. Shikhar Dhawan, who missed the ODI series due to his wife's illness, has also been included.
