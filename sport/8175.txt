Former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag posted a picture of him meeting Yoga Guru Baba Ramdev. Sehwag's caption for the picture read, "Pleasure meeting @yogrishiramdev, who has made Swadesi come back in fashion again,popularised Yoga and a man who is really really cool." "Cricketer @virendersehwag is a hard core nationalist. Nation is proud of such patriots," tweeted Ramdev for Sehwag.
