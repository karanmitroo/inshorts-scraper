Reacting to American swimmer Michael Phelps winning his 21st Olympic Gold medal, former Indian cricketer Virender Sehwag tweeted, "Michael Phelps only man likely to beat Bappi Lahiri. #GoldKiBarsaat". With a total of 25 medals, Phelps is the most decorated Olympian of all time, with his 21 Olympic Golds being more than those of 174 countries, including India.
