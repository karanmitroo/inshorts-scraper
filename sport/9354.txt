India ended day two of the Mumbai Test at 146/1, trailing England by 254 runs. England were dismissed for 400 in their first innings, with Indian spinners Ravichandran Ashwin and Ravindra Jadeja claiming six and four wickets respectively. Murali Vijay and Cheteshwar Pujara put on an unbeaten 107-run stand after opener Lokesh Rahul got out on a score of 24.
