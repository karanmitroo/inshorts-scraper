Saina Nehwal defeated defending champion, Japan's Nozomi Okuhara, 21-15, 21-14, while sixth-seeded PV Sindhu defeated Denmark's Mette Poulsen 21-10, 21-11 to advance to the second round of the All England Open on Wednesday. India's HS Prannoy advanced to the men's singles round two, while Manu Attri and B Sumeeth Reddy made a first-round exit in men's doubles.
