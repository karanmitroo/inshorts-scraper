Members of the Indian cricket team attended the premier of Sachin Tendulkar's biopic 'Sachin: A Billion Dreams' in Mumbai on Wednesday. "Had goosebumps watchin your movie.U always inspire me n ur movie will nw inspire billions," tweeted KL Rahul, while Bhuvneshwar Kumar wrote, "Revived some inexplicable memories...Attended the premier of Sachin A Billion Dreams, great story by the great man."
