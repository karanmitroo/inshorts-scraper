Bangladesh's Tamim Iqbal, during his 76-run knock against Zimbabwe on Tuesday, overtook ex-Sri Lankan captain Sanath Jayasuriya to record most ODI runs on a single venue. Iqbal has hit 2,549 runs in 74 ODIs at Dhaka's Sher-e-Bangla National Stadium, while Jayasuriya recorded 2,514 runs at Colombo's Premadasa Stadium. Iqbal also became the first Bangladeshi to hit 6,000 ODI runs.
