Indian spinner Ravindra Jadeja thanked former Indian captain MS Dhoni and current skipper Virat Kohli after becoming the number one ranked bowler and all-rounder in Test cricket. "My humble journey 2 be #1 Test AllRounder & bowler was possible due 2 @msdhoni @imVkohli, my fans & family #bcci #icc #teamindia", read Jadeja's tweet.
