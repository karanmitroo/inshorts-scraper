Former cricketer Virender Sehwag called Harmanpreet Kaur's 171-run innings against Australia in the women's World Cup semi-final an "innings of a lifetime". Other cricketers also tweeted about the innings, with Rohit Sharma posting, "That's brutal power by @ImHarmanpreet! Some serious hitting there." Anil Kumble tweeted, "Serious batting by @imharmanpreet! What a knock! #IndvsAus #WWC17."
