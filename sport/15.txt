Rohit Sharma's four-ball duck against Sri Lanka in the T20I tri-series opener on Tuesday was his 12th international duck since March 6, 2013, the joint-most ducks by an Indian in the last five years. Rohit joined fast bowler Ishant Sharma, while pacer Bhuvneshwar Kumar follows them with 11 ducks. Overall, it was Rohit's fifth duck in T20I cricket.
