UAE news portal Emirates 24/7 mistook actor Sushant Singh Rajput to be MS Dhoni, when it tweeted a picture of the actor in an article about the former Indian skipper. The article was about Virat Kohli replacing Dhoni as skipper, in which Rajput's picture was used alongside Kohli's. Rajput had played the role of MS Dhoni in the cricketer's biopic.
