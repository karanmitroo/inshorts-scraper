Indian shuttlers Kidambi Srikanth and HS Prannoy crashed out of Japan Open after losing their respective men's singles quarterfinals on Friday. World number eight Srikanth lost 17-21, 17-21 to defending world champion Viktor Axelsen while world number 19 Prannoy lost 15-21, 14-21 to Shi Yuqi of China. On Thursday, PV Sindhu and Saina Nehwal had crashed out of the tournament.
