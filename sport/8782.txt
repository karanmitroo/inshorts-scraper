Pakistan's Aleem Dar on Monday started officiating in the Sri Lanka-South Africa test match which is his 332nd international cricket match as an umpire, thereby surpassing South African Rudi Koertzen's record of 331 international matches as an umpire. Aleem Dar started his umpiring career in 2000 and has since supervised 182 ODIs, 109 Tests and 41 T20Is.
