Sachin Tendulkar, Sourav Ganguly and VVS Laxman have written to Committee of Administrators head Vinod Rai, asking him to clarify that Rahul Dravid and Zaheer Khan were made consultants after discussion with Ravi Shastri. "There have been suggestions the CAC exceeded its ambit...It has pained us to see the light in which the CAC has been portrayed," wrote Sachin.
