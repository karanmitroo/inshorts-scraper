India defeated South Africa by 189 runs in a warm-up match ahead of the ICC U-19 World Cup in New Zealand on Tuesday. Sixteen-year-old Aryan Juyal slammed 86(92) to help India score 332/8 before Ishan Porel took 4/23 to dismiss South Africa for 143 runs. India will start their Under-19 World Cup campaign against Australia on January 14.
