South Africa scored 384/6 against Sri Lanka in the final ODI in Centurion on Friday, their 24th 350-plus total in ODI cricket, breaking India's record of 23 350-plus team totals in ODIs. SA had equalled India's record in their previous ODI against Sri Lanka, scoring 367/5 in Cape Town. Notably, Australia follows South Africa and India with 18 350-plus totals.
