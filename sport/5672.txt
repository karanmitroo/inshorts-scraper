The Pakistan Cricket Board has demanded a compensation of ₹447 crore from the BCCI for dishonouring the MoU signed between the two countries. The MoU stated that India and Pakistan would play six series between 2014-2023. PCB recently sent a legal notice to BCCI, claiming it suffered losses of $69,576,405 (₹447 crore) following India's refusal to play against Pakistan.
