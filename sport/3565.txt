Indian shuttler Kidambi Srikanth won his 12th consecutive match to reach the Round of 16 at the Badminton World Championships in Glasgow on Wednesday. World number eight Srikanth defeated France's Lucas Corvee 21-9, 21-17 to book the last 16 spot. Saina Nehwal also entered the pre-quarterfinals in the women's singles' category after beating Sabrina Jaquet 21-11, 21-12 in 33 minutes.
