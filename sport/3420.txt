MS Dhoni became the sixth Indian cricketer to feature in 300 ODIs, after taking the field in the fourth India-Sri Lanka ODI on Thursday. Dhoni, who made his ODI debut in 2004 against Bangladesh, has scored over 9,600 runs and made 377 dismissals. Dhoni, who is the fourth-highest ODI run-scorer for India, was presented a memento by Virat Kohli.
