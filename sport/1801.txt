Shuttlers Kidambi Srikanth and PV Sindhu won the Sportsman and Sportswoman of the Year (individual) awards respectively at the inaugural Indian Sports Honours. Srikanth has won four Superseries titles till now this year while Sindhu has won two. Indian cricketers Ravichandran Ashwin and Mithali Raj were honoured with the Sportsman and Sportswoman of the Year (team) awards respectively.
