Real Madrid forward Cristiano Ronaldo could face six years in prison if found guilty of tax evasion. Earlier, Spanish newspaper El Mundo had reported that Ronaldo had directed around €150 million (approximately ₹1,090 crore) through tax havens since 2009 which include the British Virgin Islands. Earlier, Barcelona's Lionel Messi and Javier Mascherano were handed prison sentences for tax evasion.
