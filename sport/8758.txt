Indian shuttler Saina Nehwal lost her maiden match of the 2017 Premier Badminton League to Rio Olympics' gold medallist Carolina Marin. The Spaniard, playing for Hyderabad Hunters, defeated Awadhe Warriors' Saina in straight sets, 15-14, 11-5 to clinch her second victory of the tournament. Marin had earlier defeated India's PV Sindhu in a repeat of the Rio Olympics' final.
