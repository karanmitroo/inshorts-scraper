West Indian Dwayne Smith smashed a century off 31 balls to register one of the fastest tons in T20 cricket while playing for Kowloon Cantons against City Kaitak in the Hong Kong T20 Blitz. The 33-year-old went on to score an unbeaten 121 off 40 balls including 7 fours and 6 sixes, helping his side chase 200 in 14.3 overs.
