Goa’s Protocol Minister Dilip Parulekar has told the Legislative Assembly that only ₹500 were spent by the government on Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal’s two visits to the state. The question about the expenses on Kejriwal's visits was tabled by Goa Vikas Party MLA Fransisco Miccky Pacheco. Kejriwal was presented with a bouquet of ₹500 on his first visit, said Parulekar.
