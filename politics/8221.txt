The CPI(M) on Sunday slammed the Central government for levying Swachh Bharat cess, saying that the move will burden the Indian consumers. CPI(M) General Secretary Sitaram Yechury has demanded the government to declare the amount spent on the Swachh Bharat initiative. The government on Sunday implemented Swachh Bharat cess of 0.5%  on all services currently eligible for service tax.
