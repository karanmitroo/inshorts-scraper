Just 2 months before the end of her tenure, Mizoram governor Kamla Beniwal(who had a running battle with Narendra Modi) was sacked Wednesday night, a month after she was shifted from Ahmedabad to Aizawl. "The President has directed that Dr. (Smt.) Kamla shall cease to hold the office of the Governor of Mizoram," said a a Rashtrapati Bhavan communique.
