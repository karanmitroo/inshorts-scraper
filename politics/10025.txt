As per Election Commission, BJP spent more than Rs.714 crore in 2014 General Elections and Assembly polls in Andhra Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh, Odisha and Sikkim, while Congress spent Rs.516 crore. NCP and BSP spent over Rs.51 crore and Rs.30 crore respectively in the elections. The parties submitted their expenditure statements to the EC, much after the stipulated deadline of Aug.
