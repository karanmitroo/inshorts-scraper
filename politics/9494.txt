AAP has suspended two of its party leaders from the National Executive for openly supporting Yogendra Yadav and Prashant Bhushan. Suspended UP leader Rakesh Sinha had raised questions about Bhushan's removal as the chief of its disciplinary committee. Vishal Sharma Lathe, the second suspended leader, had voted against the resolution to sack Bhushan and Yadav from the Political Affairs Committee.
