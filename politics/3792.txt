The Bharatiya Janata Party has released a video on Facebook targeting Samajwadi Party chief Mulayam Singh Yadav's younger son, Prateek Yadav, over the latter's Lamborghini car worth ₹5 crore. Prateek was seen revving the car in the video and a caption describing the scene read, “₹5 crore ki Lamborghini par sawaar samajwad (the socialist ideology riding on a ₹5-crore Lamborghini).”
