The Delhi government on Wednesday presented its budget for the financial year 2017-18 and announced the national capital’s Gross State Domestic Product (GSDP) rose to ₹6,22,385 crore from ₹5,51,963 crore last year. The government also proposed an expenditure of ₹5,736 crore for the health sector and ₹11,300 crore, which is about 24% of the total budget, for the education sector.
