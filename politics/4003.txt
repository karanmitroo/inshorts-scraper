Punjab Congress Chief Captain Amarinder Singh on Monday said Navjot Singh Sidhu will campaign against Finance Minister Arun Jaitley if he decides to contest the Lok Sabha by-election in Amritsar. "Sidhu will join the Congress in a day or two," Amarinder Singh added. Sidhu was BJP MP from Amritsar and had won the seat in 2004, 2007 by-election and 2009.
