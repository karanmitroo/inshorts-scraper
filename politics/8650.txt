Delhi BJP spokesman Ashwini Upadhyay on Sunday requested PM Narendra Modi to officially rechristen Delhi's Shahjahan Road to Dashrath Manjhi Road, claiming the ruler was a "symbol of lust". Upadhyay said Manjhi would serve a better role model to youngsters. Manjhi, popularly known as 'Mountain Man', is famous for carving a road through a hill utilizing a hammer and chisel.
