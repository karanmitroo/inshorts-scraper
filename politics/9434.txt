Shiv Sena leader and MP Sanjay Raut has said that the voting rights of Muslims should be withdrawn in order to end vote bank politics. Raut, in an article in the party's mouthpiece Saamana, also called All India Majlis-e-Ittehadul Muslimeen leaders - Asaduddin Owaisi and Akbaruddin Owaisi - a threat to the nation.
