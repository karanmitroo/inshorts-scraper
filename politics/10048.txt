UP CM Akhilesh Yadav's 'Radio Saifai', which could be on the lines of PM Narendra Modi's 'Man ki baat' programme, has received a tremendous response from people. The digital radio, which was launched by the CM in Saifai Mahotsava on Jan-8, has got over two lakh hits so far. Akhilesh is expected to connect with the people through Radio Saifai.
