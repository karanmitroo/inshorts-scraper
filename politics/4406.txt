The BJP accounted for 88% (₹67.99 crore) of the total donations over ₹20,000 received by the six national parties from the corporate sector, which stood at ₹77.28 crore in 2015-16, an ADR report claimed. In comparison, the Congress party received ₹8.83 crore from Indian corporates. Further, individual donors donated ₹8.86 crore and ₹11.24 crore each to BJP and Congress, respectively.
