Former India footballer and Trinamool Congress (TMC) MP Prasun Banerjee said gymnast Dipa Karmakar should be named India's sports ambassador. He added the party would raise the issue in Parliament's winter session, which begins November 9. TMC organised a felicitation function for Dipa and coach Bisheshwar Nandi in Agartala on Thursday, which was attended by TMC Vice President Mukul Roy.
