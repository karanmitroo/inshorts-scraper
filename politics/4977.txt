TMC leader Mukul Roy has claimed that West Bengal CM Mamata Banerjee would become the Prime Minister in 2019 as her party will emerge as the largest party in General Elections. The RJD, JD(U) may win 20/25 seats, BJD may win 18/20 seats, DMK or AIADMK will secure 39/40 seats while Netaji or Mayawatiji may win 25/30 seats, he predicted.
