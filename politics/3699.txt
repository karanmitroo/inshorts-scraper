Senior Samajwadi Party leader and Mulayam Singh Yadav’s brother Shivpal Singh Yadav’s name is missing from the 40 star campaigners' list the party released for the UP Assembly election on Tuesday. The list which has been sent to the Election Commission contains the names of Akhilesh Yadav, Mulayam, Ramgopal Yadav, Dimple Yadav, Jaya Bachchan, Azam Khan, Abu Azmi among others.
