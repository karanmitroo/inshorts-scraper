As a part of AAP's 2017 Punjab polls agenda, party leaders will visit Europe from September, said AAP’s UK unit. AAP Convenor Arvind Kejriwal will visit Italy along with party leaders Kumar Vishwas, Jarnail Singh and Adarsh Shastri, it added. This will be followed by Vishwas' visit to the UK to hold the gatherings across the country’s gurdwaras.
