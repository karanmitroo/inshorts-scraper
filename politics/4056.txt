Former PM Atal Bihari Vajpayee feared a coup by a BJP section in 2002 which wanted to replace him with the then Deputy PM Lal Krishna Advani, a book has claimed. "The Untold Vajpayee: Politician and Paradox" by journalist Ullekh NP, claims that Vajpayee had been asked by an RSS functionary to become the President and leave the PM's post.
