Congress' Chief Ministerial candidate in Uttar Pradesh, Sheila Dikshit on Wednesday said Samajwadi Party's Akhilesh Yadav is a better CM candidate than her, reports said. The 78-year-old Congress leader also added that she would "be happy" to make way for Akhilesh. "I am hopeful that Samajwadi Party and like-minded parties will help to form a secular exercise," Dikshit added.
