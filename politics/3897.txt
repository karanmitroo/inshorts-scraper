AAP Convenor Arvind Kejriwal on Saturday said that cricketer-turned-politician Navjot Singh Sidhu should contest Punjab polls from only Jalalabad seat and Congress Punjab chief Amarinder Singh should contest it from only Lambi. "No safe seats. Direct fight," he tweeted. Reacting to his tweet, Amarinder tweeted, "Why doesn't @ArvindKejriwal distribute tickets for all parties?(sic)"
