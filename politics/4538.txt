While backing VK Sasikala for the post of AIADMK General Secretary, Lok Sabha Deputy Speaker and AIADMK propaganda secretary M Thambidurai on Sunday said that we started calling Sasikala as 'Chinnamma' several years ago and Jayalalithaa also recognised it. He added, "Chinnamma is the only person who is capable, who has (the) acumen and experience, to lead the AIADMK party."
