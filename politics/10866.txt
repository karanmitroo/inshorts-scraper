Congress chief Sonia Gandhi's Iftar party, hosted after a gap of three years, saw her sharing table with old ally RJD chief Lalu Prasad and JD(U) leader Sharad Yadav. Her son Rahul Gandhi played host to the diplomats, including Pakistan High Commissioner Abdul Basist. Former PM Manmohan Singh and NCP leader Tariq Anwar were among the other political leaders present.
