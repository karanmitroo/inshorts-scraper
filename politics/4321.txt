The Enforcement Directorate on Monday detected cash deposits totalling over ₹104 crore in an account belonging to the Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP) and ₹1.43 crore in BSP supremo Mayawati's brother's account, reports said. Adding that deposits of ₹15-17 crore were made every day, officials said the deposits were done in old ₹500 and ₹1,000 notes post note ban.
