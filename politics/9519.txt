After being expelled from the party's National Executive, AAP leaders Yogendra Yadav and Prashant Bhushan, Saturday accused Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal's supporters of using violence against them. "The assaulters were not party workers but professional bouncers," Yadav said. Referring to the sting done on Kejriwal, Bhushan said, "...whatever was revealed about Kejriwal in the sting... was executed".
