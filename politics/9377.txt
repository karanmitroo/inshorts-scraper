Congress vice president Rahul Gandhi is all set to take a trek to Kedarnath on Thursday, 4 days after returning from his 56-day sabbatical. He will trek around 21 kilometres, from Gaurikund to Lord Shiva's holy shrine. He will be accompanied by Uttarakhand CM Harish Rawat, state Congress president Kishor Upadhyay and Ambika Soni, the Congress leader-in-charge of party affairs.
