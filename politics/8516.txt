As per reports, JD(U) chief Sharad Yadav was on Thursday booked for allegedly breaching the Model Code of Conduct while campaigning toward the Bihar polls. Yadav on Wednesday claimed people not voting for the JD(U) candidate would find themselves in "Jehanum" or hell. Recently Amit Shah and Lalu Prasad Yadav were also booked for allegedly violating the code of conduct.
