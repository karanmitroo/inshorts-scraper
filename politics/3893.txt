Hours after BJP Haryana Minister Anil Vij said that PM Narendra Modi is a better brand than Mahatma Gandhi, Hindu Mahasabha Meerut Mahanagar President Bharat Rajput urged the Centre to remove Mahatma Gandhi’s picture from currency notes. Removing Mahatma Gandhi’s picture from Khadi calender was the first good work done by government in last 70 years, he added.
