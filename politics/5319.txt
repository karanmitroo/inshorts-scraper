AAP MLA Naresh Balyan was on Friday arrested on charges of assault, becoming the 13th MLA to be arrested since the party came to power in February 2015. Balyan, however, was granted bail later that day. A case was registered against Balyan under Sections 323 (voluntarily causing hurt), 341 (wrongful restraint), 451 (assault) and 506 (criminal intimidation) of the IPC.
