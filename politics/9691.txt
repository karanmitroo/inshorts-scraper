PM Narendra Modi on Saturday attended 'tilak' ceremony of SP supremo Mulayam Singh Singh Yadav's grandnephew Tej Pratap Singh Yadav, who will marry RJD president Lalu Prasad Yadav's daughter Raj Laxmi. The PM was welcomed by Mulayam and Lalu upon his arrival at the venue in Saifai village of UP. Actor Amitabh Bachchan is also scheduled to attend the ceremony.
