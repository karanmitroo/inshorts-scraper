BJP leader SN Channabasappa was arrested on Tuesday for his allegedly "provocative" and "intimidating" remarks against Karnataka CM Siddaramaiah. Channabasappa had on Monday threatened to behead the latter for his remarks on eating beef. Channabasappa has been apprehended under IPC Sections 506 (Punishment for criminal intimidation) and 153 (Wantonly giving provocation with intent to cause riot).
