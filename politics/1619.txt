On Dr APJ Abdul Kalam's second death anniversary, NDA's Vice-Presidential candidate Venkaiah Naidu on Thursday turned the former President's name into acronym 'APJAK'. Naidu added that the acronym stands for 'Anything is Possible with Just Attitude and Karma'. In the past, Naidu had coined an acronym of Prime Minister Narendra Modi's surname and expanded it as 'Maker Of Developed India'.
