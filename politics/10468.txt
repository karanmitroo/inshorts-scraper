Breaking his silence over the speculations that he might be induced as the Union Defence Minister, Manohar Parrikar has said that he will resign from the post of Goa's CM on Saturday. Parrikar, however, refused to disclose any further details in this matter. Francis D'Souza, Laxmikant Parsekar and Assembly Speaker Rajendra Arlekar are seen as possible successors of Parrikar.
