Samajwadi Party President Mulayam Singh Yadav on Monday told the Election Commission the convention called by Uttar Pradesh CM Akhilesh Yadav's camp was "unconstitutional" and the party's electoral symbol should remain with him. Reportedly, he also told EC that Ram Gopal Yadav, who had called the convention in which Akhilesh was made the party president, was expelled from the outfit.
