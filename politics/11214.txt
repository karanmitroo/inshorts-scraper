A total of 8,163 candidates are in fray in the 2004 Lok Sabha polls, more than 50% jump compared to 2009 elections (5,435 candidates). Out of 8,163 candidates, 2,208 (27%) are crorepatis - a sharp increase over 2009, when 1,249 (16%) of the candidates were crorepatis, and 2004, when only 13% were crorepatis.
