Jan Adhikar Party leader Rajesh Ranjan alias Pappu Yadav on Wednesday invited RJD MP Mohammad Taslimuddin to join the party and criticised RJD for serving him a show cause notice for comments against Bihar CM Nitish Kumar. "Why action is being taken only against Taslimuddin and not against Raghuvansh Prasad Singh?" Yadav, who had also been expelled from RJD, questioned.
