A CBI court on Monday convicted RJD leader Lalu Prasad, Former Bihar CM Jagannath Mishra, JD(U) MP Jagdish Sharma and 42 others in the fodder scam case. The quantum of sentence, which will be between 3 to 7 years, will be announced on October-3. The fodder scam is a series of fraudulent transactions totalling Rs.950-crore during 1990s.
