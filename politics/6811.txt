Shiv Sena MP Sanjay Raut on Sunday said, "People of Goa are fed up with the BJP and the Congress. Therefore, Shiv Sena is the only alternative left." The Shiv Sena will contest around 20-22 of the 60 seats in the 2017 Goa Assembly polls and party's star campaigner would be Uddhav Thackeray, announced Raut, party incharge of Goa.
