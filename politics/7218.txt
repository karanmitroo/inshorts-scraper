The younger daughter-in-law of Samajwadi Party chief Mulayam Singh Yadav, Aparna Yadav, has entered politics and will be contesting from Lucknow's Cantonment Assembly seat in 2017. The announcement was made by Mulayam's brother Shivpal Singh Yadav today. At present, the Cantonment seat is being held by Congress' Rita Bahuguna Joshi. Aparna is the wife of Mulayam's younger son Prateek Yadav.
