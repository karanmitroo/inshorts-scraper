Reacting to the Income Tax (I-T) raids at late Tamil Nadu Chief Minister J Jayalalithaa's Poes Garden residence, sidelined AIADMK leader TTV Dhinakaran on Saturday said the action has been taken in order to destroy the family. Calling the raids "an assault on Amma's legacy", he blamed Tamil Nadu CM E Palaniswami and O Panneerselvam behind the searches.
