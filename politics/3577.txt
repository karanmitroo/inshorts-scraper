Samajwadi Party leader Mulayam Singh Yadav's younger son Prateek Yadav had taken a loan of ₹4.5 crore to buy a luxury Lamborghini car worth ₹5.23 crore. This information was revealed in Prateek's wife Aparna Yadav's election affidavit. Aparna is the SP candidate from Lucknow Cantt Assembly constituency and she has declared assets worth ₹23 crore in the affidavit.
