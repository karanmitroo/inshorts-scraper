MNS chief Raj Thackeray on Wednesday said his party would set up a museum in the memory of late Shiv Sena supremo Bal Thackeray. "We are in the planning stage of making the museum," he added. "Babasaheb Purandare (noted historian) has agreed to provide the requisite material pertaining to the Shivaji era for the making of the museum," Thackeray announced.
