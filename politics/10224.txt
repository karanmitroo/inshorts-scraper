In a major setback for Shiromani Akali Dal, Punjab Revenue Minister Bikram Singh Majithia has been summoned by the Enforcement Directorate (ED) in the Rs 6k crore drug racket case. However, CM Prakash Singh Badal has come out in Bikram's defence. Notably, Bikram Singh is also the younger brother of Union Minister Harsimrat Kaur Badal.
