Samajwadi Party Rajya Sabha MP Amar Singh on Friday said that Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Akhilesh Yadav who grew up at his uncle Shivpal Yadav's home is opposing Shivpal today. Claiming that baseless allegations are being put on him, Amar Singh said, "My contribution to CM Akhilesh Yadav's growing up life is well known and is in public domain."
