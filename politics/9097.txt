Ex-RAW Chief A.S. Dulat revealed on Thursday, that former Prime Minister Atal Behari Vajpayee was unhappy over the Gujarat riots, and admitted it to be a "mistake". Dulat, whose book "Kashmir: The Vajpayee Years" is releasing shortly, said that one "could see the sadness in his eyes". He also revealed Vajpayee's suspicion of Mehbooba Mufti having links with Hizbul Mujahideen.
