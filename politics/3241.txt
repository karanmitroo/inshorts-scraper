Activist Irom Sharmila has ₹2.6 lakh in her possession and is without any land or house, according to an affidavit she filed with the election commission. In the affidavit, Sharmila stated that she has ₹10,000 as cash in hand and ₹2.5 lakh in a bank account. The affidavit further claims she does not possess any gold or a vehicle.
