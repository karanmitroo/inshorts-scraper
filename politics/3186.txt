In a letter, AIADMK general secretary Sasikala has asked her party workers to celebrate late Tamil Nadu CM Jayalalithaa's birthday on February 24 on a grand scale conducting public welfare events. Sasikala, who is currently lodged at Bengaluru's Parappana Agrahara Central Jail, further slammed rebel party leader O Panneerselvam in the letter for betraying the legacy of Jayalalithaa and MGR.
