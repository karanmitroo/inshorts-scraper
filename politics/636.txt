Bharatiya Janata Party's chief ministerial candidate in the Himachal Pradesh Assembly elections, Prem Kumar Dhumal, is trailing by 1,709 votes from Sujanpur. Dhumal has served as Himachal's Chief Minister twice earlier, from 1998 to 2003 and then from 2007 to 2012. Meanwhile, Congress' CM candidate Virbhadra Singh is leading by 1,162 votes from Arki.
