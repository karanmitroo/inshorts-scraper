Lucknow based Swarajya Party has declared Rajeev Mishra and Vinayak Rao Tope - descendants of freedom fighter Chandra Shekhar Azad and 1857 revolt hero Tatya Tope respectively - as its candidates for the LS polls. Vinayak is going to contest from Kanpur against BJP's Murli Manohar Joshi while Rajeev will contest from Unnao against sitting MP Anu Tandon.
