The Hyderabad Police has pulled down posters that showed AIMIM President Asaduddin Owaisi as Aamir Khan in 'Dangal' movie. Titled 'UP ka Dangal', the posters also featured BJP President Amit Shah, UP CM Akhilesh Yadav, Congress VP Rahul Gandhi and BSP supremo Mayawati. The posters were reportedly put up near Afzalgunj and Madina, near Naya Pul.
