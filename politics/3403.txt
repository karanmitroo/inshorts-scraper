Hailing Congress PMs, including Indira Gandhi, Rajiv Gandhi, PV Narasimha Rao and Manmohan Singh for the country's development, Shiv Sena has said, without Congress, India would have been like Somalia or Burundi. Wrong to say that Congress only looted India and didn't do development, Sena stated. The country has been built on the efforts of its past rulers, it added.
