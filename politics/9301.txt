Former Rashtriya Janata Dal (RJD) leader Rajesh Ranjan alias Pappu Yadav on Sunday announced the formation of a new party 'Jan Kranti Adhikar Morcha' aimed at providing a strong third option to Bihar voters. The Madhepura MP, who was expelled from RJD for six years on May 7 for anti-party activities charges, said his party will contest the assembly polls.
