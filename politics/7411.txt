The Congress declared the highest total income (₹1,687.12 crore) among all political parties during 2010-14, according to a report by the Association for Democratic Reforms. Congress was followed by the BJP with ₹1,475.44 crore, CPI(M) with ₹552.30 crore, and Bahujan Samajwadi Party with ₹391.21 crore. Notably, voluntary contributions accounted for 46.49% of the combined income of all parties.
