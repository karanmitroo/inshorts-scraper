Asaduddin Owaisi, President of All India Majlis-e Ittihad al-Muslimin (AIMIM) on Monday said that ISIS is a “blot on Islam”. The Lok Sabha member from Hyderabad added that the terror outfit has so far massacred 1.5 lakh Muslims and that its activities should not be linked with religion. ”All scholars have condemned them and I also condemn," Owaisi further said.
