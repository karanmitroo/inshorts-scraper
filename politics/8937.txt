Janata Dal (United) on Sunday formally invited actor and BJP MP Shatrughan Sinha to join the party. JD(U) state chief Bashishth Narayan Singh said, "It's for Shatrughan-ji to take a call... we'll welcome him with open arms." Shatrughan has, however, said, "I have been with BJP in good and bad times and there is no question of joining JD(U)."
