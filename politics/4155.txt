Protesting against the arrest of TMC MP Sudip Bandyopadhyay, West Bengal BJP leader Krishna Bhattacharya's house was allegedly attacked by TMC workers on Tuesday. The police have registered a case in this regard. Stones were also pelted on BJP headquarters in Kolkata, injuring several party workers. Notably, Bandyopadhyay was arrested in connection with the Rose Valley chit fund scam.
