The Shiv Sena on Monday accused Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal of playing "politics of convenience.” This comes after Kejriwal blamed the Centre for the rape of two minors in Delhi. Referring to the health ministry being under his ambit, an editorial in Sena mouthpiece 'Saamana' stated, "How will a government that cannot stop mosquitoes, stop goondaism, rapists and terrorists?”
