Andhra Pradesh CM Chandrababu Naidu landed in a major controversy on Monday after an audio tape surfaced, which alleges that he was involved in cash-for-vote scam. The tape, released by TNews, belonging to Telangana CM K Chandrasekhar Rao's family, shows Naidu trying to bribe nominated MLA Elvis Stephenson to support his Telugu Desam Party in the Telangana Legislative Council election.
