Congress on Thursday called the pay hike recommended by the Seventh Pay Commission a "jumla (gimmick)" for defence personnel, who feel "cheated and ignored". Congress spokesperson Randeep Surjewala also slammed the Centre over the non-implementation of the One Rank-One Pension scheme. "98 lakh Central government workers feel "disillusioned" and "let down" by the "inadequate" hike, Surjewala added.
