BJP's prime ministerial candidate Narendra Modi is the most searched Indian politician in India, as per the Google's Zeitgeist report, 2013. The other most searched person in the 'politicians' category is Congress vice president Rahul Gandhi, followed by Sonia Gandhi, Manmohan Singh, J Jayalalithaa, Arvind Kejriwal, Akhilesh Yadav, Nitish Kumar, Digvijay Singh and Sushma Swaraj.
