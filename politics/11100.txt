The Majlis-e-Ittehadul Muslimeen on Monday struck an alliance with the Telangana Rashtra Samithi to counter the TDP-BJP alliance in the state. MIM-TRS would contest the Greater Hyderabad Municipal Corporation (GHMC) elections in November together besides allying in Nizamabad and Bodhan municipalities. TDP-BJP won 14 of 24 assembly and 2 of 4 LS seats in the GHMC area in the polls.
