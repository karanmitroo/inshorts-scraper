Samajwadi Party chief Mulayam Singh Yadav on Monday said that Bihar CM Nitish Kumar will be the chief ministerial candidate of JD(U)-RJD alliance in the upcoming Legislative Assembly polls. He further added that RJD chief Lalu Prasad Yadav proposed Nitish Kumar's name for being Janata Parivar's CM candidate. "There are no differences with Nitish," Lalu said while confirming the news.
