Senior Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) leader Yogendra Yadav has offered to resign from the Political Affairs Committee of the party on the condition that Gopal Rai and Manish Sisodia too tender their resignations. "(I) will resign but if Gopal Rai and Manish Sisodia resign too as they are ministers," he said. AAP is not likely to stop him from resigning.
