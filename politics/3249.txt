The All India Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (AIADMK) on Thursday announced that the Tamil Nadu Assembly will hold the Vote of Confidence on February 18. Earlier in the day, Edappadi K Palanisamy was sworn in as the Chief Minister by Governor C Vidyasagar Rao. Notably, Palanisamy has been given 15 days to prove his majority in the Assembly.
