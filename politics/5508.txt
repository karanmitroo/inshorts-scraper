Reacting to the Maharashtra Navnirman Sena's (MNS) warning to Pakistani artists and actors to leave India within 48 hours, the Samajwadi Party has dared MNS chief Raj Thackeray to send his "suicide bombers" to Pakistan. Stop intimidating people who have come to India legally, said SP leader Abu Azmi. "Forget about Pakistan...your reach is limited to Maharashtra only", he added.
