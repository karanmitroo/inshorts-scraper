Police registered a case against Shiv Sena MLA Harshavardhan Jadhav for slapping a police officer who is a member of Uddhav Thackeray's security unit. Reportedly, Uddhav was holding a meeting in a hotel room and the cop, Parag Jadhav, was on duty outside the room. Harshavardhan tried to enter the room and when the cop denied, he allegedly slapped him.
