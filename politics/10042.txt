Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) leader Arvind Kejriwal Sunday visited Bengaluru to raise funds for his party. People can get their selfies clicked and have lunch with Kejriwal after paying a certain amount. "AAP, Bengaluru has launched a fund-raising lunch called 'Lunch with Arvind'. Donors with a minimum contribution of Rs 20,000 may book their passes," the party said.
