Rajasthan recorded 72.87% voting on Sunday, the highest ever surpassing the 2003 average of 68.18%, with the highest polling percentage in the four districts of Hanumangarh, Jaisalmer, Sriganganagar and Chittorgarh, each of which recorded voting above 80%. While Marwar region's Nagaur had the lowest voter turnout at 65.88%, capital city Jaipur witnessed 72.0% voting.
