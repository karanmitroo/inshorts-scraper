Reacting to Congress leader P Chidambaram's remarks on Afzal Guru's role in the Parliament attack, BJP leader Subramanian Swamy said "it will have a negative effect on Kashmir". "It is primarily to encourage all the terrorists to get active," Swamy added. Questioning his silence during Afzal's execution in the Congress regime, Swamy demanded his persecution terming his remarks as 'anti-national'.
