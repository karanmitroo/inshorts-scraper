Slamming the Sangh Parivar for demanding the settlement of the Babri Masjid dispute on the basis of 'aastha' (faith), AIMIM chief Asaduddin Owaisi said the matter can be settled only through evidence. Stating that the case was still pending in the court, he criticised Vishva Hindu Parishad leaders' statements promising the construction of Ram Temple by next year.
