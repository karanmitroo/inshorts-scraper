A day before the first phase of the Gujarat Assembly elections, Dinesh Bambhaniya, one of Patidar Anamat Andolan Samiti (PAAS) leader Hardik Patel's key aides, resigned from PAAS on Friday. He was the co-coordinator of PAAS and was one of the closest leaders to Patel. Bambhaniya is the fourth leader to leave PAAS within a month.
