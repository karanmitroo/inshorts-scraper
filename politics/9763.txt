PM Narendra Modi will not attend the swearing-in ceremony of Delhi CM-designate Arvind Kejriwal on Feb 14 due to prior commitment, confirmed AAP leader Manish Sisodia. He was addressing the media after meeting the PM along with Kejriwal on Thursday. Reportedly, instead of the oath-taking ceremony, Modi would be attending an event in NCP chief Sharad Pawar's Baramati constituency on Saturday.
