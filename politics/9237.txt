The Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) on Wednesday opened its account in electoral politics in Kerala by winning by-election in the 21st ward (electoral subdivision) at Cherthalai-Arthungal in Alappuzha district. AAP candidate, Tomi Ellessery, contested the by-elections which were held for 10 wards in Kerala. The party is likely to contest for Aruvikara assembly by-election scheduled for June 27.
