Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal on Thursday claimed PM Narendra Modi's one dress costs ₹2 lakh and he changes clothes five times a day, making the expense ₹10 lakh per day. Maintaining that AAP released advertisements worth ₹76 crore as against the alleged ₹526 crore, Kejriwal added that money spent on Modi's clothes since 2014 was more than AAP's ad budget.
