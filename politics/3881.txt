Samajwadi Party (SP) Chief Mulayam Singh Yadav on Monday said, "I am trying my best to save the party and cycle, and if he (Akhilesh Yadav) doesn't listen, then I will fight against him." Saying that he would accept whatever the Election Commission decides on the party symbol, Mulayam added, "My son is acting on the behest of Ramgopal Yadav."
