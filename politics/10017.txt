Former Delhi minister Kiran Walia was named as the Congress' candidate from the New Delhi seat, against AAP leader Arvind Kejriwal, on Friday, after it revealed the 4th list of candidates for the Delhi polls. The party also revealed names of Raj Kumar Maggo, Dhuli Chand Lohia, Vishan Swaroop Agarwal, Zakir Khan and Satanpal Dayma, apart from Walia.
