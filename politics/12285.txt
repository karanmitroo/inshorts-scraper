Raman Singh was sworn-in as the CM of Chhattisgarh for the third consecutive time on Thursday. The oath-taking ceremony was attended by Rajnath Singh, LK Advani, Sushma Swaraj, Arun Jaitley, Narendra Modi and Shivraj Singh Chouhan, among others. BJP registered victory in Chhattisgarh by winning 49 of the 90  assembly seats.
