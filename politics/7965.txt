Majlis-e-Ittehadul Muslimeen (MIM) chief Asaduddin Owaisi on Thursday said that Hyderabad was only for Hyderabadis and not for outsiders. He urged the people of Hyderabad to choose a Hyderabadi and reject outsiders in the upcoming elections of Greater Hyderabad Municipal Corporation (GHMC). He added that his party will contest 70-75 out of 150 seats in the polls.
