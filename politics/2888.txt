In the recently held Assembly elections in five states, Goa witnessed the maximum number of voters who opted for NOTA (None of the Above). In Goa, 1.2% of the voters chose NOTA, followed by 1% in Uttarakhand, 0.9% in Uttar Pradesh, 0.7% in Punjab and 0.5% in Manipur. Goa saw hung Assembly as no party could secure a majority.
