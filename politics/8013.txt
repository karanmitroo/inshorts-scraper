Taking a dig at BJP leader Subramaniam Swamy, Congress spokesman Raj Babbar accused the BJP of hiring a 'supaaribaaz' (contractual) litigant. Babbar further accused that the BJP is working towards destroying the legacy of Nehru. Several Congress leaders alleged that Z+ security and government accommodation were "BJP's rewards for Subramanian Swamy dragging Sonia and Rahul Gandhi to court."
