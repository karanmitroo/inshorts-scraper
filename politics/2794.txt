President Pranab Mukherjee's daughter Sharmistha Mukherjee on Tuesday slammed Congress for sharing a picture of Rajiv Gandhi and Pranab Mukherjee, reportedly with the caption, "Shri Rajiv Gandhi and Pranab Mukherjee, 1985". Responding to it, Sharmistha tweeted, "At least have the courtesy to add 'Shri' before Pranab Mukherjee as he happens to be the current President of India."
