UP CM Akhilesh Yadav Friday defended the annual 'Saifai Mahotsav' amid criticism that SP government spent crores of rupees and invited top Bollywood stars at a time when Muzaffarnagar riot victims were suffering in relief camps. Seeking an apology from the media, the CM lashed out media for ‘false’ reporting and said only Rs.6-7 crore were spent for the event.
