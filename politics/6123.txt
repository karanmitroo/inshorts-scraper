Reacting to incidents of cow protection vigilantism, AIMIM President Asaduddin Owaisi on Wednesday said that India urgently requires ‘Insaan Raksha Samiti. “Will Badshah of Delhi Sultanate look into it or allow Insaans to be humiliated in name of COW,” he tweeted. Owaisi’s remarks came a day after a mob thrashed two women in Madhya Pradesh over suspicion of carrying beef.
