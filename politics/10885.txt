The ruling Congress in Uttarakhand recorded a big win in the bypoll Friday to three assembly seats, wresting two of them from the BJP. The Dharchula seat which was retained went to CM Harish Rawat. Doiwala and Someshwar went to Hira Singh Bisht and Rekha Arya respectively. Rawat won by over 20,000 votes, Bisht by 6000 and Arya by 9000.
