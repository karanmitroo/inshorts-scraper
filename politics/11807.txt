Trinamool Congress chief Mamata Banerjee Wednesday announced that film personalities Moon Moon Sen Deb and Shatabdi Roy, and former Indian soccer skipper Bhaichung Bhutia figure in the party's list of candidates for the coming Lok Saba polls. Bhutia will contest from Darjeeling, Deb from Ghatal in West Midnapore district and Sen will be in the fray from Bankura.
