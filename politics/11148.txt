With 5.16 lakh votes, Narendra Modi on Friday won the Varanasi seat and left behind Arvind Kejriwal by a massive margin of 3.37 lakh votes. All the other major players - Ajay Rai of Congress, Vijay Kumar Jaiswal of BSP and Kailash Chaurasia of SP - were made to bite the dust. About 10.28 lakh votes were polled there.
