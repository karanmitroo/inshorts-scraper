A BJP MLA from Rajasthan's Barmer constituency, Kailash Chaudhary, has been booked for calling Congress Vice President Rahul Gandhi a 'traitor'. An FIR against the MLA was registered in Amethi by the police on Tuesday. Notably, Chaudhary had also said that Gandhi should be hanged or shot for standing with JNU students, whom he referred to as 'anti-nationals'.
