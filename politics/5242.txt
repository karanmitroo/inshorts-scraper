Censor Board chief Pahlaj Nihalani has said that the central and state government had extended support to 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' (ADHM) but it was two industry associations and the "local political party" that "created ruckus". The Maharashtra Navnirman Sena (MNS) has threatened to vandalise multiplexes which will screen the film as the party is opposing its screening across Maharashtra.
