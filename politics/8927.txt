Shivji Rai, a 45-year-old RJD leader, was shot dead by an unidentified assailant on Tuesday in the Vaishali district of Bihar. According to SP Rakesh Kumar, he was sleeping in his house in Daudnagar village when the incident happened. Rai was also the panchayat mukhiya of the village, he added. The motive behind the killing is yet to be ascertained.
