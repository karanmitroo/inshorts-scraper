Congress UP CM face Sheila Dikshit has equated ex-Union Minister Ghulam Nabi Azad with BJP chief Amit Shah. “If BJP has Amit Shah then the Congress has Ghulam Nabi Azad,” she reportedly said. Dikshit also expressed confidence that the Congress will emerge victorious in the 2017 UP polls. Notably, Dikshit's father-in-law was a prominent congress leader in UP.
