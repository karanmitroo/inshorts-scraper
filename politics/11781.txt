Sonia Gandhi, Rahul Gandhi, Lok Sabha Speaker Meira Kumar, IT expert Nandan Nilekani, cricketer Mohd Kaif and Bhojpuri film star Ravi Kishen figure in Congress's first list of 194 candidates for Lok Sabha elections released Saturday. Party sources said 35% of the candidates are aged under-50. Nikhil Kumar, who resigned as Kerala Governor two days ago, has also been fielded.
