Delhi Water Minister Kapil Mishra on Monday said the Anti-Corruption Bureau, which is probing the ₹400 crore water tanker scam, is trying to give ex-CM Sheila Dikshit a clean chit in the case. "Only focus [is] to frame me or [CM] Arvind Kejriwal," Mishra tweeted. The scam pertains to the alleged​ irregularities found while hiring water tankers during Dikshit's tenure.
