Shiv Sena candidate Vishwanath Mahadeshwar was on Wednesday elected as the Mayor of Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation after BJP corporators extended support in his favour. Mahadeshwar is a three-time corporator from Bandra and was the Principal of Raje Sambhaji Junior College. Sena had won 84 of the 227 seats in the BMC election last month while BJP had bagged 82 seats.
