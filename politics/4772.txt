BJP chief Amit Shah on Wednesday appointed actor-turned-politician Manoj Tiwari as the BJP's Delhi unit President. Tiwari, who is an MP from North East Delhi constituency, replaced Satish Upadhyay as the party's Delhi chief. Meanwhile, BJP leader Nityanand Rai was appointed as the BJP's Bihar unit chief. The MP from Bihar's Ujiarpur constituency replaced Mangal Pandey as the Bihar chief.
