An RTI reply by the Delhi government has revealed that Congress MLA, Vipin Sharma, claimed Rs 1.32 crore in medical reimbursement during the fourth Delhi assembly. With a claim of only Rs.8,182, BJP MLA OP Babbar was the least medical spender in 5-years. Other major claimants included independent MLA Bharat Singh (Rs.27.5 lakh) and BJP MLA HS Balli (Rs.17.4 lakh).
