Samajwadi Party National General Secretary and Mulayam Singh Yadav's cousin Ram Gopal Yadav on Thursday said the party leadership committed a "mistake" by removing Chief Minister Akhilesh Yadav as the party state chief. "Akhilesh’s removal led to misunderstanding, the matter will be resolved,” he added. Yadav further denied reports of infighting saying that there "is no crisis in SP".
