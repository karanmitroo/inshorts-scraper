BJP MP Subramanian Swamy, during a debate with AIMIM chief Asaduddin Owaisi at a Lit Fest, stated that he would support the construction of Babri Masjid across the Saryu river in Ayodhya. "I will tell the government to build the grandest Babri Masjid across the Saryu river but not on the Ram janambhoomi site," he added.
