Showering praises on PM Narendra Modi, Chairman of Indian Council for Cultural Relations Loksh Chandra has called him a "phenomenon".  "Woh bhagwan ke avatar hain (He is reincarnation of God)," the 87-yr-old said. PM Modi is "a man of ideas and not ideologies," according to Chandra and "from a practical point of view supercedes the Mahatma."
