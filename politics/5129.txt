Reacting to the recent Madhya Pradesh's Bhopal jailbreak and encounter controversy, the Shiv Sena in its mouthpiece 'Saamana' said that the national security is 'Ram bharose' (relying on Lord Ram). "It was a disgrace for the government that 8 terrorists were able to flee from the jail. Prabhu Ramchandra's exile has not finished despite BJP's rule," the editorial further read.
