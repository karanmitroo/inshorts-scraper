Former PM Rajiv Gandhi wanted commission paid by defence suppliers to be used for running the Congress, claimed ex-CBI director Dr AP Mukherjee in his recently launched autobiography "Unknown Facets of Rajiv Gandhi, Jyoti Basu, Indrajit Gupta". Gandhi said this would prevent collusive nexus (secret linking to achieve illegal goal) between middlemen, ministers and bureaucrats in defence deals, added Mukherjee.
