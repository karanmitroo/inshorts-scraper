Defeating her nearest Shiromani Akali Dal rival, Congress leader and former union minister Preneet Kaur on Monday won the Patiala assembly seat. Kaur, wife of former CM Amarinder Singh, polled 52,967 votes and won by a margin of 23,282, said an election office spokesperson. Patiala seat was vacated by Amarinder, after he got elected to Lok Sabha from Amritsar.
