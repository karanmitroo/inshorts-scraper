Filmmaker Ram Gopal Varma's biopic on Telugu actor and former Andhra Pradesh CM Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao (NTR) will be titled 'Lakshmi's NTR'. Sharing a picture of late CM NTR and his wife Lakshmi on Facebook, RGV wrote, "Lakshmi's NTR will be the true story of the real...truth behind what actually really happened in NTR's life especially towards the end."
