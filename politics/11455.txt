AAP nominee from Amethi, Kumar Vishwas, on Tuesday declared his assets worth about Rs.4 crore, including that of his wife, in his Lok Sabha nomination papers. The market value of his immovable properties, including two residential flats in Ghaziabad is Rs.1.29 crore, while that of his wife is Rs.65 lakh. The PhD scholar has liabilities of Rs.6.73 lakh on him.
