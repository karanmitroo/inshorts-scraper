Rebel JDU leader, Gyanendra Singh, has said Jitan Kumar Manjhi should be party's chief ministerial candidate and not Nitish Kumar, reports said Wednesday. Recently, Singh and many other JD(U) leaders had slammed Nitish for joining hands with RJD chief Lalu Prasad Yadav. Lalu and Nitish have been warming upto each other after their poor performance in the recent elections.
