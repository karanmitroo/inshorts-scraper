Delhi CM and AAP chief Arvind Kejriwal on Friday took to Twitter to slam Prime Minister Narendra Modi for 'endorsing' Reliance Jio as he featured in a newspaper advertisement of the product. Calling Modi​ 'Mr Reliance', Kejriwal tweeted, "PM of India openly endorses Reliance product," and added, "Any more proof required to prove that - Modiji is in Ambani's pocket."
