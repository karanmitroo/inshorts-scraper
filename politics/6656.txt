As many as 428 elected MLAs, over 50% of the elected representatives, in the recently concluded 2016 Assembly Elections have declared assets worth over ₹1 crore, a survey by the Association for Democratic Reforms stated. The survey analysed poll affidavits of 812 elected representatives. Puducherry has the maximum 83% of crorepati MLAs, with average assets per MLA amounting to ₹13.45 crore.
