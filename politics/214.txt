The Maharashtrawadi Gomantak Party (MGP) and the Goa Forward Party (GFP), who are allies of the BJP government in Goa, have said they will not withdraw support till Manohar Parrikar is Chief Minister. The BJP has 14 legislators in the 40-member Goa Assembly, and the support of three MLAs each of MGP and GFP, as well as three Independent legislators.
