Controversial independent MLA Raghuraj Pratap Singh alias Raja Bhaiya, who had to resign from the UP Cabinet in March after due to DSP Zia-ul-Haque murder case, has been re-inducted as a minister. Raja Bhaiya was sworn-in by Governor B.L. Joshi in the presence Mulayam Singh Yadav, Akhilesh Yadav and senior Cabinet Minister Azam Khan.
