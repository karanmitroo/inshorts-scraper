The Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) has sought cancellation of National Conference leader Farooq Abdullah's candidature from Srinagar Lok Sabha constituency, saying he had "lied" about his age in his affidavit. Abdullah has mentioned his age as 77 in his fresh nomination papers, AAP leader Raja Muzaffar said, adding that in 2008 elections, he had mentioned his age as 66.
