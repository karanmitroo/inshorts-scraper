According to Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS), Shakhas have grown by 13% in India between July 2012 and July 2014. The growth of Shakhas in West Bengal is three times of the national average (nearly 38%) in the corresponding period, an official said, adding that, RSS had 34,761 active Shakhas in 2012 and now (last July) it stands at 39,396.
