The AIADMK party has now split into three factions led by party General Secretary Sasikala Natarajan along with her nephew TTV Dinakaran, O Panneerselvam, and Chief Minister E Palaniswami, party leader KC Palanisamy said. Further, Palanisamy claimed that "blasphemous" utterings by some members who have joined AIADMK in the recent past were affecting the party.
