RJD chief Lalu Prasad Yadav was booked on Friday for his alleged offensive statements against PM Narendra Modi and BJP chief Amit Shah, officials said. Two FIRs were filed against Lalu, one for allegedly referring to Shah as ‘narbhakshi’ and 'pagal', and another for his alleged comments ‘narbhakshi’ and ‘brahmpishach’ against Modi and Shah.
