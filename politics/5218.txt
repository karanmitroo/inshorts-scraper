Bahujan Samaj party (BSP) on Saturday said the people of UP wants to get rid of CM Akhilesh Yadav and his party, and "they will be thrown into the dustbin of history by the people". Akhilesh represents misgovernance, crime, murder, rapes, and communal riots, BSP added. This comes amid reports of an ongoing feud in the Samajwadi Party.
