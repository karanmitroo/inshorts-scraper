Tweeting about the BJP forming government in Gujarat, Bihar Deputy Chief Minister Sushil Kumar Modi on Monday said that "RAM" had won over "HAJ". Earlier, election campaign posters termed CM Vijay Rupani, BJP chief Amit Shah, and PM Narendra Modi as "RAM". The posters called Patidar leader Hardik Patel, OBC leader Alpesh Thakor, and Dalit leader Jignesh Mevani as "HAJ".
