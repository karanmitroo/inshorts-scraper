Following the death of AIADMK supremo Jayalalithaa, DMK chief M Karunanidhi expressed his "deepest condolences" to the lakhs of supporters grieving her loss. Despite their parties having a "difference in opinion", nobody could deny that she "acted courageously" for her party's welfare, he wrote. Even though she passed away at a young age, her fame will last forever, Karunanidhi added.
