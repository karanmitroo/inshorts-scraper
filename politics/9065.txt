The Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK) on Monday moved to the Supreme Court challenging the acquittal of Tamil Nadu CM Jayalalithaa by the Karnataka HC in the disproportionate assets case. The petition was filed by DMK general secretary K Anbazhagan. All India Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (AIADMK) chief Jayalalithaa was acquitted by the HC on May 11 this year.
