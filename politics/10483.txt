Senior JD(U) and former Bihar CM, Nitish Kumar, will hold 'Sampark Yatra' across Bihar from Nov 13, where he will address 32 public meetings in as many districts. The 16-day Yatra will be held from Nov 13 to Nov 29. JD(U) state President said that the Yatra aims to activate the party at the grassroot level, energise and strengthen it.
