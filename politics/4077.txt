Amid the ongoing tussle in the Samajwadi Party, SP leader Ambika Chaudhary on Saturday said, "Everything will be fine, the party will remain united." This comes amid reports of a split in the party into two factions, led by party Founder Mulayam Singh Yadav and UP CM Akhilesh Yadav. Meanwhile, Akhilesh's faction has staked a claim to the party symbol.
