Following a 40 Km long roadshow, Congress vice president Rahul Gandhi on Saturday filed nomination papers from Amethi (UP), in the presence of Congress president Sonia Gandhi. A two-time MP from Amethi, Rahul is locked in a triangular contest this time with BJP candidate Smriti Irani and AAP's Kumar Vishwas pitted against him. Amethi goes to polls on May-7.
