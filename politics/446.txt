All India Majlis-E-Ittehadul Muslimeen Chief Asaduddin Owaisi has said BJP wants India to be "Muslim-mukt (Muslim-free)", while RSS wants a "Dalit-mukt Bharat". Owaisi also called for Dalit-Muslim unity to fight against Hindutva ideology. Commenting on the Centre's Triple Talaq bill, Owaisi said, "The law is nothing but to send Muslim men to jail and leave Muslim women on roads."
