As much as 94% or ₹766 crore of Samajwadi Party's income between 2004-05 and 2014-15 was from unknown sources, according to an Association for Democratic Reforms report. Among regional parties, Shiromani Akali Dal received 86% (over ₹88 crore) of its income from unknown sources, while Congress and the BJP received maximum income from unknown sources among national parties.
