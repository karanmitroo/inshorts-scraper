RSS-affiliated Akhil Bharatiya Vidyarthi Parishad (ABVP) today won three out of four posts – President, Vice- President and Secretary – in the Delhi University Students' Union (DUSU) polls. The remaining post of Joint Secretary was won by Congress-affiliated National Students' Union of India (NSUI). This year's DUSU polls witnessed the lowest voters' turnout in six years, with around 36% voting.
