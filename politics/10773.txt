Bypolls to the Vadodra Lok Sabha seat vacated by PM Narendra Modi and Mainpuri Lok Sabha seat vacated by SP chief Mulayam Singh Yadav will be held on Sept 13, announced the Election Commission Saturday. Bypoll will also be held to Medak Lok Sabha seat vacated by TRS chief K Chandrasekhar Rao after he became the first CM of Telangana.
