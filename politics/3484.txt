An online petition against Sasikala Natarajan's appointment as the next Tamil Nadu Chief Minister has garnered over 1.7 lakh signatures. The petition was titled 'Dissolve Tamil Nadu government and stop Sasikala from becoming Tamil Nadu CM'. Earlier, AIADMK announced Sasikala will take over as the state's CM. Notably, CM O Panneerselvam had claimed that he was forced to resign.
