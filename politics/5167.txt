Fireworks bearing the names of politicians like senior Samajwadi Party leader Amar Singh have surfaced in the market ahead of Diwali celebrations. This comes amid the ongoing feud in SP involving Shivpal Yadav, Akhilesh Yadav, Mulayam Singh Yadav and others. Notably, SP leader Abu Azmi had earlier slammed Amar Singh for causing turmoil in the Yadav family.
