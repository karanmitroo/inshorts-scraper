Ahead of a possible floor test to prove the majority in Tamil Nadu Assembly, Sasikala Natarajan has taken the 131 AIADMK MLAs supporting her to an unknown location. They would reportedly be kept till the time they meet President Pranab Mukherjee to prove Sasikala's majority. However, interim CM Panneerselvam had said he had the support of all the AIADMK MLAs.
