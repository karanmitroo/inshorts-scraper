After the administration in Lucknow announced on Thursday that Ghulam Ali will be performing at the 'Lucknow Mahotsav' on December 3, the Shiv Sena has threatened to disrupt the programme. "[I]f the state government goes ahead with Ghulam Ali's concert, he would meet with the same fate as Sudheendra Kulkarni, or perhaps even worse", the party's UP unit said.
