JD(U) Bihar President Bashistha Narain Singh on Monday suspended 21 party members from their primary memberships over 'anti-party activities'. The list of suspended members includes eight-time MLA and former minister Ramai Ram, former MP Arjun Rai and former MLA Rajkishore Sinha. Notably, JD(U) on Saturday removed Sharad Yadav as its leader in Rajya Sabha due to his 'anti-party activities'.
