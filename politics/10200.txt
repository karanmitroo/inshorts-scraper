Former Jharkhand CM and senior BJP leader Arjun Munda on Tuesday lost from Kharsawan (ST) constituency to JMM's (Jharkhand Mukti Morcha) Dashrath Gagrai. Munda, who had won the Kharsawan seat four times earlier, said, "win and loss are normal process in politics. I am grateful to the voters of Kharswan, who gave me four times opportunity to serve them."
