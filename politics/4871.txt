Samajwadi Party (SP) chief Mulayam Singh Yadav on Monday cancelled his birthday celebrations for November 22 due to the derailment of the Indore-Patna Express in Kanpur, which claimed over 140 lives. The announcement was made by Uttar Pradesh CM Akhilesh Yadav. Notably, the Samajwadi Party celebrates annual Saifai Mahotsav to mark the occasion of Mulayam Singh Yadav's birthday every year.
