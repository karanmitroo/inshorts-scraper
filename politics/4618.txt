Actor Kamal Haasan's brief tweet in Tamil condoling Tamil Nadu CM Jayalalithaa's demise has been slammed on Twitter. Haasan had tweeted, "Deep sympathy for those who depend on Jayalalithaa." Reacting to this, a user tweeted, "You're exponentially becoming a bigger embarrassment almost every time you air an opinion." Meanwhile, another tweet read, "[H]e never respect(s) people, sadist fellow."
