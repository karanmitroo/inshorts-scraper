AAP Sunday released the video of party chief Arvind Kejriwal's speech during Saturday's national council meet where Yogendra Yadav and Prashant Bhushan were expelled from the party's National Executive. In the video, Kejriwal can be heard saying, "There're attempts to weaken me, weaken the party... Now, you've to decide whether you're with me or them (Yadav and Bhushan)."
