AAP convener Arvind Kejriwal and his supporters were heckled on Sunday by a group of women protesters during a roadshow in Ayodhya Nagar (Amethi). The women protesters, shouting slogan "Rahul Gandhi zindabad, Delhi ka bhagoda wapas jao (escapist of Delhi go back), showed black flag to Kejriwal. Amethi goes to poll on May-7.
