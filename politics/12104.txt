BJP leader Subramanian Swamy has revealed that the businessman being protected by Union home minister Sushilkumar Shinde  in relation to 2G scam was Shahid Usman Balwa. Even the 2G probe on Balwa, who is linked to many more UPA ministers, took a long time because he was protected by the government, added Swamy, demanding a full-fledged inquiry into the matter.
