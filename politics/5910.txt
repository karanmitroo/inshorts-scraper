The Nationalist Congress Party’s (NCP) Maharashtra MLA, Suresh Lad, was caught slapping and threatening Deputy Collector Abhay Kalgudkar in his office in Raigad. As per reports, during a meeting called by the bureaucrat to solve land acquisition related issue, Lad lost his cool and assaulted the officer. However, Lad has reportedly claimed the officer wasn’t heeding to the public’s demands.
