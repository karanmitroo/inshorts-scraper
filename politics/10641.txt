Trinamool Congress MP Subhendu Adhikari was questioned Wednesday, by the CBI in the multi-crore Saradha chit fund scam. "I have answered all questions by the CBI," Adhikari said after leaving the agency's office. He said that he had been called under Sec-160 of the CrPC. Adhikari is the fourth TMC MP to be questioned in connection to the case.
