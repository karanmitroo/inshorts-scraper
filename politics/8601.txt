An FIR under IPC section 506 has been lodged against Samajwadi Party chief Mulayam Singh Yadav, on charges of threatening IPS officer Amitabh Thakur. The Hazratganj police, who lodged the FIR, filed a status report before the Chief Judicial Magistrate on Thursday. Thakur lodged a complaint on July 10 against Singh, for threatening him over the phone.
