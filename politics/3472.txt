The Election Commission has ordered re-polling in as many as 48 polling stations in Punjab over snag in Voter-Verified Audit Paper Trail electronic voting machines. This includes 16 booths in Amritsar (for Lok Sabha bye-election), 12 in Majitha, one in Moga, nine in Muktsar, four in Sardulgarh, and six in Sangrur. The re-polling will be held on February 9.
