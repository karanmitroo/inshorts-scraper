DMDK founder Vijayakanth’s wife Premalatha has been booked for allegedly telling Tamil Nadu voters to accept money from other political parties to vote. As per the recordings of her speech, Premalatha said, "Some political parties will offer to give ₹2,000 to ₹3,000 per vote. You ask them ₹1 lakh per vote." The complaint was filed by local AIADMK workers.
