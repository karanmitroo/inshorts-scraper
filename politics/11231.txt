BSP Hoshiarpur Lok Sabha candidate, Bhagwan Singh Chohan, has placed a bounty of Rs 1 crore on yoga guru Baba Ramdev's head for his alleged "anti-Dalit" remarks. "I am ready to pay Rs. 1 crore to the person who will bring his (Ramdev) head to me," the BSP leader said Sunday while burning Ramdev's effigy near Hoshiarpur bus stand.
