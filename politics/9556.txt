Following the return of Aam Aadmi Party chief Arvind Kejriwal to Delhi, party leaders Sanjay Singh, Kumar Vishwas, Ashish Khetan and Ashutosh met Yogendra Yadav Tuesday, who was recently removed from the party's decision-making body. As per reports, the talks were positive. "The talks will continue and we will let you know if and when we reach a decision," Yadav said.
