The union government will release a special postage stamp on January 17 to mark the birth centenary of actor and former Tamil Nadu CM MG Ramachandran (MGR). The state's current CM O Panneerselvam had also urged PM Narendra Modi to issue a commemorative coin. MGR founded his own political party the All India Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (AIADMK) in 1972.
