Firebrand Hindutva leader and Gorakhpur MP, Yogi Adityanath, was on Thursday, named by BJP as one of its star campaigners, for the bypoll in UP. He is one of the three star campaigners for the bypolls, scheduled for Sep-13. Adityanath has been a vocal leader, who courted controversy recently, after video footage showed him daring people behind 'Love jihad'.
