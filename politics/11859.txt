Demanding land rights for 85,000 people living in and around Guwahati, a Krishak Mukti Sangram Samiti protester immolated himself in front of the Dispur secretariat complex on Monday. Pranab Boro a daily wage earner, died of his injuries at Gauhati Medical College Hospital. Assam CM described this incident, which took place a day before Rahul Gandhi's visit, as unfortunate.
