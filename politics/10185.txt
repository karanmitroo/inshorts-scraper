Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) leader Arvind Kejriwal launched the 'Mission One Lakh Votes' in Delhi's Karawal Nagar constituency, in order to strengthen the party's base. The mission requires both the booth in-charges of the constituency to reach out to 1 lakh voters before the elections. The candidate from Karawal Nagar said that specific targets have been set for 250 booths.
