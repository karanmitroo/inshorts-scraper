Days after taking over as the Tamil Nadu CM following the demise of J Jayalalithaa, O Panneerselvam is facing the first revolt against him within the AIADMK, reports said. AIADMK leader E Madhusudanan and Revenue Minister RB Udayakumar have openly appealed Jayalalithaa's close aide VK Sasikala to take over the CM and party's General Secretary post.
