Chhattisgarh CM Raman Singh has completed his 13 years rule in the state which is affected by Naxalism. The 64-year-old Ayurvedic doctor-turned politician had first assumed office of Chief Minister on December 7, 2003. "In 2003, the budget of the state was ₹7,000 crore which has now expanded to a whopping ₹78,000 crore," Singh has claimed.
