The Goa Police have booked state BJP Vice-President Anil Hoble for allegedly assaulting his daughter-in-law for dowry. Hoble, his wife and son were booked after an FIR was filed at a women's police station by the victim's mother Suchitra Shirodkar, who is based in Mumbai. "Hoble also threatened to shoot us and chop us up with a knife," Shirodkar alleged.
