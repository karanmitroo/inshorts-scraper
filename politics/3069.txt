AAP leader Kumar Vishwas has uploaded a video reciting Om Prakash Aditya's poem titled 'Idhar bhi gadhe hain' (Donkeys are here as well) to mock Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Akhilesh Yadav's recent statements on 'Gujarat donkeys'. He recited the lines, saying those who scream on microphones are the real donkeys.
