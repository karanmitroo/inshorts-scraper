Samajwadi Party President Akhilesh Yadav’s wife and Lok Sabha MP Dimple Yadav on Wednesday campaigned for her sister-in-law Aparna Yadav who is contesting election from  Lucknow cantonment. "Please vote for our contestant Aparna Yadav. Her victory is also party’s victory. Young leaders like her will change the fate of Uttar Pradesh," Dimple stated.
