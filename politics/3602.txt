Addressing a poll rally, Samajwadi Party Chief and Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Akhilesh Yadav on Monday said, "There's nothing new in the BJP manifesto. It was copied from ours." Further talking about the Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP), Akhilesh said, "If the 'hathi wali party' (BSP symbol: elephant) comes to power, they will start erecting 'hathi' everywhere."
