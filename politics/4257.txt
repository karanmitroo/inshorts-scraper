Reacting to the expulsion of UP CM Akhilesh Yadav from the Samajwadi Party, BJP on Friday said that the people of UP had already waved Akhilesh off. "In family-centered parties, if the family breaks up, the party also falls apart," BJP leader Shrikant Sharma stated. SP supremo Mulayam Singh Yadav has expelled his son Akhilesh for six years.
