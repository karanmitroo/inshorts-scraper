Late BJP leader Gopinath Munde's daughter, Pritam Munde, scripted history on Sunday with a record victory margin of around 7 lakh votes in the Lok Sabha by-elections from Beed (Maharashtra). Earlier, the record was held by Anil Basu of CPI(M), who won by 5,92,502 votes from Arambagh in West Bengal during the 2004 Lok Sabha elections.
