Addressing public in Hyderabad on Monday, All India Majlis-e-Ittehadul Muslimeen (AIMIM) chief Asaduddin Owaisi asked them to vote for his party if they want to continue eating beef. In the address given ahead of the Greater Hyderabad Municipal Corporation elections, Owaisi added, "If AIMIM is defeated... the members of minority community will have to forget about eating beef."
