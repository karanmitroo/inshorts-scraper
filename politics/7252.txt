Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal on Thursday tweeted to thank PM Modi for following him on the micro-blogging website. "@narendramodi Sir, thank u for following me. Happy Holi. (Aaj gile shikwe bhoolne ka din hai). Hope better Centre-Delhi cooperation in future," Kejriwal posted on Twitter. Further, Deputy CM Manish Sisodia thanked Modi and stated that the latter has also followed him.
