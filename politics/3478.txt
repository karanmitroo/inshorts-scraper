AIADMK leader Manoj Pandian on Tuesday said Jayalalithaa had told him that she never wanted Sasikala Natarajan to become Tamil Nadu Chief Minister. He was joined by AIADMK leader PH Pandian who strongly opposed Sasikala's elevation as the party chief. PH Pandian also demanded a probe on the reports that Jayalalithaa was manhandled and pushed down before her hospitalisation.
