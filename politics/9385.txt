Union MoS for Parliamentary Affairs Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi on Wednesday offered a 'chadar' at the Ajmer dargah on Sufi saint Hazrat Khwaja Moinduddin Chisty's 803rd Urs on behalf of PM Narendra Modi. Reading out the message from Modi after offering the chadar, Naqvi said, "...the PM has wished for the welfare of the nation."
