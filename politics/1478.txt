The Congress on Wednesday expelled eight Gujarat MLAs for six years for cross-voting in the Rajya Sabha elections held for three seats on Tuesday. The suspended MLAs include Shankersinh Vaghela, who revolted against the party recently. Other MLAs who have been expelled by Congress are Mahendrasinh Vaghela, Raghavji Patel, Amit Chaudhary, Bholabhai Gohil, CK Raulji, Kamsi Makwana, and DM Jadeja.
