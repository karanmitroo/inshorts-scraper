The Shiv Sena on Tuesday called Samajwadi Party leader Azam Khan more dangerous than Dawood Ibrahim following Khan's alleged support for the Paris attack and 1993 Mumbai blasts. In an editorial in the Sena's magazine, Saamana, Khan was additionally told to "learn a lesson" from AIMIM chief Asaduddin Owaisi as "Owaisi takes a balanced approach to situations."
