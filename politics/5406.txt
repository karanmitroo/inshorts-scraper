Slamming Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal, Rashtriya Janata Dal (RJD) Chief Lalu Prasad Yadav on Friday said, "Kejriwal ab dharti mei gad raha hai (Kejriwal is now sinking deeper)." Yadav added, "Iska koi na koi mantri phaste jaa raha hai, imaandari ka pol khulte jaa raha hai (His ministers are getting caught and the facade of honesty is slowly getting exposed)."
