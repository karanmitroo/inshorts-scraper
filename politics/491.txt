Rajasthan Education Minister Vasudev Devnani on Monday said that he does not mention  Jawaharlal Nehru University too much and no Kanhaiya should take birth in Rajasthan. Devnani said this while speaking about "violence, ruckus and arson, and such anti-national activities", and how state universities stop them. Student leader Kanhaiya Kumar was at the centre of protests at JNU in 2016.
