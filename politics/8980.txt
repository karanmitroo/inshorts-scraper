Seeking action against senior BJP leaders for their alleged role in the Lalit Modi row and the Vyapam Scam, Left parties staged nation-wide protests on Monday. CPI(M) General Secretary Sitaram Yechury said that Union Minister Sushma Swaraj, Rajasthan CM Vasundhara Raje and Madhya Pradesh CM Shivraj Singh Chouhan should step down until their roles are being probed.
