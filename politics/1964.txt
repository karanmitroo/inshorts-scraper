BJP's MP from Fatehpur (Uttar Pradesh) Sadhvi Niranjan Jyoti has an attendance record of 100%, making her the most active MP in terms of attendance. She was followed by BJP MP Jayshreeben Patel with 99% attendance. Notably, NCP's MP from Maharashtra Supriya Sule has asked the most questions in the Parliament among all the female MPs. Sule had 95% attendance.
