Activist Anna Hazare on Friday announced his support to AAP nominee Medha Patkar and two "Mahayuti" candidates for their "moral and social character" and "credibility" of their parties. Besides Patkar, Anna also extended support to Raju Shetty and Sadabhau Khot, leaders of Swabhimani Shetkari Sanghatna, one of the constituents of BJP-led "Mahayuti" (grand alliance).
