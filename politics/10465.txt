In a move to allow Arun Jaitley to focus on improving the economy of the country, PM Narendra Modi will relieve Jaitley of his defence portfolio, a senior BJP official said Thursday.  Jaitley is heading both ministries (Finance and Defence) since May. Goa CM Manohar Parrikar will replace Jaitley as defence minister, said Goa BJP general secretary Sadanand Tanavada.
