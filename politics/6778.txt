Of the five poll-bound states, the black money surveillance teams appointed by the Election Commission have seized maximum cash in Tamil Nadu, standing at ₹88.22 crore. Tamil Nadu is followed by Kerala (₹22.54 crore), West Bengal (₹20.75 crore), Assam (₹12.33 crore) and Puducherry (₹3.50 crore). A total of ₹147 crore has been seized from the five election-bound states.
