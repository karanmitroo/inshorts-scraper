Early trends in the counting of votes in Meghalaya, Tripura and Nagaland Assembly elections show that Congress is leading in Meghalaya, BJP in Tripura and Naga Peoples Front in Nagaland. Congress is leading in 14 seats in Meghalaya, followed by National People's Party leading in 9. While BJP is leading with 17 in Tripura, with the ruling-CPI(M) leading in 13.
