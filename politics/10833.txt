The Aam Aadmi Party has set up its women's wing in Delhi, by the name of Aap Ki Mahila Shakti. Party convener Arvind Kejriwal made the announcement on the first death anniversary of AAP activist Santosh Koli, who died in a road accident last year. The wing, made to attract female voters, will be headed by Shalimar Bagh MLA-Bandana Kumari.
