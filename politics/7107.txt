A Trinamool Congress (TMC) member was beaten to death, allegedly by Congress and CPI(M) supporters in West Bengal's West Midnapore district, which goes to polls on Monday, the police said on Saturday. Joydeb Jana (30) was attacked with sharp weapons, lathis and iron rods. Jana's wife has filed an FIR against 22 people, including former Congress Minister Manas Bhunia.
