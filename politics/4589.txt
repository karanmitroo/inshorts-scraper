BJP MLA Radharani Panda has alleged that a woman was asked to remove her black petticoat before entering the venue of Odisha CM Naveen Patnaik's public meeting on November 25. Reportedly, policemen had asked women attending the meeting to remove black stoles, scarves, and shawls. Panda also alleged that a 5-year-old child died due to suffocation at the CM's meeting.
