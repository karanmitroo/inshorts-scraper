North East Democratic Alliance (NEDA) candidate Dasanglu Pul of BJP on Tuesday won the Arunachal Pradesh's Hayuliang Assembly by-poll by 942 votes. She defeated her lone rival Yompi Kri, an Independent candidate. Dasanglu is the youngest of three wives of former state CM Kalikho Pul. The strength of BJP, the ally of PPA-led NEDA government, has now risen to 12.
