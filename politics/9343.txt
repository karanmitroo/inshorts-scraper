Paying tribute to Bollywood, German ambassador to India Michael Steiner, along with former foreign minister Salman Khurshid, has made a remake of the 'Kal Ho Na Ho' song titled "Lebe Zetzt- Kal Ho Na Ho". While Steiner plays Shah Rukh's role, Khurshid plays the role of Saif Ali Khan. Preity Zinta's role is played by Steiner's wife Eliese.
