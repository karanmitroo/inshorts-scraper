The Samajwadi Party (SP) on Saturday said that the appointment of OBC leader Keshav Prasad Maurya as BJP state chief will help the SP win 300 out of the 403 seats in the 2017 assembly polls. Uttar Pradesh SP Chief Shivpal Yadav asserted that "OBCs, Dalits and upper caste are with SP due to [the] development done by its government".
