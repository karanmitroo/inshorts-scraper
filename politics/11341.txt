The 6th phase of Lok Sabha elections  across 117 constituencies concluded Thursday, where West Bengal led the way with 82% turnout of voters. With this, polling ended in Assam (77%), Chhattisgarh (65%), Jharkhand (62.62%), MP(61%), Maharashtra(60%), Rajasthan (62.9%) and Tamil Nadu(73%). Most of the regions recorded a surge in turnout.
