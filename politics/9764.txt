Arvind Kejriwal took the oath to become the 8th chief minister of Delhi at Ramlila Maidan on Saturday. Apart from Mr Kejriwal and Deputy chief minister Manish Sisodia, AAP leaders Satyendra Jain, Jitendra Tomar, Gopal Rai, Sandeep Kumar and Asim Ahmed Khan also took their oaths. Kejriwal reiterated his pledge to make Delhi India's first corruption free city.
