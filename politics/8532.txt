The NDA has roped in Bollywood actor Ajay Devgn to campaign for the Bihar Assembly elections, which kick off on October 12. Devgn, while addressing rallies in Jamui, Lakhisarai and Nawada districts, said, “Incidents of murder, kidnapping and loot in daylight used to be the order of the day in Bihar which were portrayed well in Gangaajal and Apharan.”
