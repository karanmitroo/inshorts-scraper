MNS President Raj Thackeray has slammed Railways Minister Piyush Goyal a day after a stampede at Mumbai's Elphinstone Railway Station, saying, "This Goyal is useless, (Suresh) Prabhu was good." He further targeted PM Narendra Modi and said, "We have never seen a PM who is such a big liar. He made big promises and then dismissed them as ‘election jumlas’."
