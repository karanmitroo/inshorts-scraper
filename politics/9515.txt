Rebel AAP leaders Yogendra Yadav and Prashant Bhushan Friday struck down any possibility of making peace with party chief and Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal. Bhushan accused Kejriwal of having a dictatorial style of functioning. Kejriwal is keen on running the Delhi government with Congress support, Bhushan added. Yogendra Yadav accused the pro-Kejriwal AAP members of spreading malicious charges.
