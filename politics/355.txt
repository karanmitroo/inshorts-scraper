West Bengal CM Mamata Banerjee-led Trinamool Congress (TMC) has won the bypolls for the Lok Sabha constituency of Uluberia and the Assembly seat of Noapara. While the TMC candidate in Uluberia secured a margin of 4.74 lakh votes, the candidate in Noapara won with a margin of 63,000 votes. This comes months ahead of local elections in rural West Bengal.
