Reacting to the Congress’ outrage over PM Narendra Modi’s "raincoat" jibe at ex-PM Manmohan Singh, BJP leader Ravi Shankar Prasad asked, has Congress Vice-President Rahul Gandhi apologised for insulting Manmohan. "Who had torn an ordinance and called it nonsense? Has he apologised for that?" he asked. The so-called credibility of Manmohan Singh was abused by the dynasty, he added.
