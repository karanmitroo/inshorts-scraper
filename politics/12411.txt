Congress has complained to the Election Commission against Narendra Modi, accusing him of making "intemperate and defamatory" remarks by referring Congress's symbol-- Hand as "Khooni Panja" and "Jalim Haath". Congress also sent DVD & text of Modi's speech at Dongargarh in which he allegedly asked public to vote for BJP and save Chhattisgarh from falling into 'brutal' and 'bloodied' hand.
