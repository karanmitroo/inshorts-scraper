Jan Adhikar Party chief Pappu Yadav has said that he would request Asaduddin Owaisi, President of the All India Majlis-e-Ittehadul Muslimeen (AIMIM), to join the Third Front and contest the upcoming Bihar Assembly elections together. Yadav also added that he would ask Muslims "to take the revenge by not voting for RJD-JD(U) combine".
