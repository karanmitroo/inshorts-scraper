The Samajwadi Party would have an edge over other political parties in the 2017 UP Assembly election, claimed a survey conducted by ABP News-Lokniti, Centre for the Study of Developing Societies (CSDS). The survey has given 141-151 seats to SP, followed by 124-134 seats to BJP and 103-113 seats to BSP. The Congress is lagging far behind with 8-14 seats.
