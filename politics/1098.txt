Tamil Nadu Minister KT Rajenthra Bhalaji on Saturday said, "Prime Minister Narendra Modi is with us, nobody can shake our party... nobody can destroy AIADMK." Claiming that 92% of general council members supported the Chief Minister EK Palaniswami's (EPS) camp, Balaji added, "The ‘two leaves’ symbol will come to EPS camp... there is no doubt about it."
