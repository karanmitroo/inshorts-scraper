A coffee-table book on Gujarat CM Anandiben Patel's 1-year tenure, 'Gujarat- Marching Ahead', was released in Ahmedabad on Sunday. Published by Pune-based 'Ameya Inspiring books', the book focuses on initiatives taken by Anandiben for women empowerment. Anandiben, who was present on the occasion, said that the state government will now reach to people who were left behind in the development.
