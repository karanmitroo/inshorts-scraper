Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi has condoled the death of Tamil Nadu CM J Jayalalithaa. "Deeply saddened at the passing away of Selvi Jayalalithaa. Her demise has left a huge void in Indian politics," tweeted Modi. President Pranab Mukherjee also tweeted a condolence message for Jayalalithaa while calling her one of India's "most charismatic & popular leaders".
