Days after his controversial comments against HRD minister Smriti Irani, JDU leader Sharad Yadav Wednesday expressed his regret in the upper house of the parliament. Finance Minister Arun Jaitley had asked Yadav to clarify his comment "I know what kind of person you are" made against Irani. Regretting his statement, Yadav said, "I respect her (Irani)."
