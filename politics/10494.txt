 Shiv Sena chief Uddhav Thackeray is "very keen"; on forming  coalition with the BJP in Maharashtra,  Republican Party of India chief Ramdas Athawale Friday claimed. Adding that the portfolios will be discussed once the talks take place, Athawale said, "Uddhav is completely ready to support BJP". RPI is a part of BJP-led alliance in Maharashtra which fought the assembly elections.
