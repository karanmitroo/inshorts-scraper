In the ongoing counting of votes for the Assembly elections in Kerala, the LDF is leading over Congress' UDF in 87 of 140 seats. The UDF was ahead in 51 seats, so far. The LDF's Chief Ministerial candidate VS Achuthanandan is leading from Malampuzha by 4056 votes and CM Oommen Chandy is leading from Puthuppally by 2019 votes.
