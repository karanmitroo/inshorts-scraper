Criticizing CM Arvind Kejriwal, Shiv Sena party president Uddhav Thackeray has compared him to item girl Rakhi Sawant saying that Rakhi would be a better administrator. He added that those who criticised Rakhi as an Item girl should now felicitate her. He further said that whatever 'tamasha' Kejriwal did in front of the Parliament, has shamed every political party.
