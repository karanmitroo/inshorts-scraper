The BJP-AJSU alliance won with absolute majority in Jharkhand, Tuesday, bagging 43 seats, two more than the required seats, to form a government in the 81-member Assembly. BJP won 38 seats, while AJSU bagged 5 seats. However, BJP's mood was darkened by the defeat of its heavyweight leader Arjun Munda, a three-time-CM who was a frontrunner to become CM again.
