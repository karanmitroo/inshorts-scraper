The Aam Aadmi Party released its second list of 13 candidates, Saturday, for the upcoming Delhi assembly polls. Ex-Ministers Manish Sisodia and Rakhi Birla, who figured in the second list, have been fielded from Patparganj and Mangolpuri respectively. AAP chief Arvind Kejriwal's name will be announced at the end after all candidates propose his name as party's CM candidate.
