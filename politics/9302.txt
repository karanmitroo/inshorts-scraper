Congress MP Abhijit Mukherjee, the son of President Pranab Mukherjee, fainted during a party rally in Jangipur, West Bengal on Sunday. The 55-year-old Lok Sabha member reportedly suffered from low blood-pressure and hyper-acidity, and was rushed to hospital where he regained consciousness. Abhijit Mukherjee started his political career in 2011 after resigning as the General Manager from SAIL.
