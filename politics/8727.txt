A state-wide bandh over the issue of special category status began in AP on Saturday. Called by the Yuvajana, Shramika, Rythu Congress Party, the day-long bandh has so far seen party members protesting in front of AP State Road Transport Organisation bus depots. Over 250 people, including 60 party workers, have been taken into preventive custody in Visakhapatnam and Vijayawada.
