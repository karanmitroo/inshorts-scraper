Terming the National Convention called by Ram Gopal Yadav on Sunday 'unconstitutional', Samajwadi Party supremo Mulayam Singh Yadav, in a letter addressed to party workers, nullified the elevation of his son Akhilesh Yadav as party President. He further called a National Convention on January 5 in Lucknow. "Some people want to benefit BJP by insulting him (Mulayam)," read the letter.
