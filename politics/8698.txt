Lok Janshakti Party (LJP) on Friday released its first list of 12 candidates for the upcoming Bihar Assembly elections. As per the list, party chief Ram Vilas Paswan's brother Pashupati Kumar Paras has been fielded from the reserved Alouli assembly seat. Notably, LJP, an ally of NDA, will contest 40 seats in the 243-member state assembly.
