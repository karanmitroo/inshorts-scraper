A wax statue of Shiv Sena founder late Bal Thackeray was unveiled at Sunil's Celebrity Wax Museum in Maharashtra's Lonavala, six years after it was created. The statue sports a pair of dark glasses, saffron-coloured silk kurta, a lungi and a double rudraksha-mala. The museum has around 100 wax statues of public figures including Mahatma Gandhi and PM Narendra Modi.
