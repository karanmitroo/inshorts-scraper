The great grandson of Bhim Rao Ambedkar, Rajratna Ambedkar, on Sunday came out in support of the Samajwadi Party-Congress alliance in the ongoing Uttar Pradesh Assembly polls. JD(U) and former Uttar Pradesh Governor Aziz Qureshi also backed the alliance. Qureshi said he was removed by the BJP as the state's Governor, and he supported SP to take revenge.
