The latest Hindustan Times-C fore survey, conducted between Jan-27 and Feb-1, predicts 36-41 seats for the AAP, 27-32 seats for the BJP and 2-7 seats for the Congress. Arvind Kejriwal, who was supported to be Delhi CM by 43% people in the previous poll, is now backed by 46% people, followed by Kiran Bedi (37%) and Ajay Maken (9%).
