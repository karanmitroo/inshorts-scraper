The BJP on Saturday confirmed that its PM candidate Narendra Modi would contest from Varanasi in the Lok Sabha elections. Senior leader Murli Manohar Joshi, whose home constituency is Varanasi, will vacate it for Modi and will instead contest from Kanpur. Party president Rajnath Singh will contest from Lucknow, while leader of opposition in Rajya Sabha Arun Jaitley from Armitsar.
