Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU) students, affiliated to the Congress-backed National Students' Union of India (NSUI), on Tuesday, burnt an effigy of PM Narendra Modi, BJP chief Amit Shah, and others on the occasion of Dussehra. "For us, Modi and RSS are symbols of untruth", an NSUI member said. Later, the University said that it is "investigating the matter".
