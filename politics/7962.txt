The AAP government on Saturday suspended three senior officials of Delhi’s Transport Department for alleged corruption. The officials, S Roy Biswas, Manish Puri and Anil Yadav, were accused of irregularities in issuance of Letter of Intents for new autorickshaws. Deputy CM Manish Sisodia tweeted, “The matter would be handed over to CBI...can not tolerate corruption at any level”.
