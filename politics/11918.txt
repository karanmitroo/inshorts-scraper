AAP on Sunday announced its first list of 20 candidates for LS elections. Ashutosh will contest from Chandni Chowk (Delhi) against Kapil Sibal, while Kumar Vishwas would take on Rahul Gandhi. Activist Medha Patkar will contest from northeast Mumbai and Yogendra Yadav from Gurgaon. Candidates against Nitin Gadkari, Salman Khurshid, Manish Tewari and Congress heavyweight Milind Deora were also announced.
