BJP MLA Suresh Bhardwaj was suspended from the Himachal Pradesh Assembly for the rest of the winter session for occupying the Chair of the Himachal Pradesh Speaker on Wednesday. Along with Bhardwaj, senior BJP MLAs Rajeev Bindal and Randhir Sharma were also suspended for approaching the podium. CM Virbhadra Singh has termed the act by the BJP MLAs as 'reprehensible'.
