The Bharatiya Janata Party has announced its third list of 11 candidates for the Bihar assembly elections starting October 12. The list includes a Muslim nominee, Abdur Rahman, from the Kochadhaman constituency. So far, the party has declared 153 of its 160 seats. BJP's allies, LJP, RLSP and HAM, will contest for 40, 23 and 20 seats respectively.
