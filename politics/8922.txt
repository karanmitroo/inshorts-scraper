India's top-selling carmaker Maruti Suzuki said on Tuesday its first-quarter net profit rose 56%. The carmaker's profit for the April-June quarter was Rs 11.9 billion, significantly higher than the Rs 7.9 billion profit it earned during the same quarter last year. The company's net sales rose about 18% to Rs 130.8 billion. Maruti's shares have risen over 25% since January.
