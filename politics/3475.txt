Samajwadi Party patriarch Mulayam Singh Yadav's younger daughter-in-law Aparna Yadav has said, "We are a well-to-do family; so why should we take any caste-based reservation?" As per reports, in an interview to a news website, Aparna had said she will not seek reservation for her daughter though she comes from a Yadav (Other Backward Caste) family.
