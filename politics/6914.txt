After Nationalist Congress Party Chief Sharad Pawar called Nitish Kumar 'a credible face of an anti-BJP alliance', Janata Dal (United) hailed his remarks. "Sharad Pawar is among senior leaders in our country... His statement is important for the country and the countrymen," JD(U)'s Ali Anwar said. He further said that Pawar's statement is what the thoughts of the people are.
