Syed Ahmed Bukhari, Shahi Imam of Jama Masjid in Delhi, on Friday extended support to the Congress party, while asking the nation to vote for 'humanity'. He also expressed solidarity with Lalu Prasad's RJD, a Congress ally. Bukhari also praised Mamata Banerjee's TMC. Bukhari, who had supported the Samajwadi Party in 2009, said Mulayam Singh Yadav's party had betrayed Muslims.
