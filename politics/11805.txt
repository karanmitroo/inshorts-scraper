At a rally in Kanpur, the Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) on Sunday indicated a direct contest between its leader Arvind Kejriwal and BJP's prime ministerial candidate Narendra Modi in Varanasi. "If Modi does not respond (to gas price issue), we will go to Benaras and demand a response. Arvind Kejriwal will ask these questions,"  said AAP leader Manish Sisodia.
