The BJP will win 17-21 seats in the 40-member Goa Assembly if the election is held now, as per the opinion poll conducted by the India Today-Axis. While the poll gives 13-17 seats to the Congress, it predicts only 1-3 seats for the AAP. Furthermore, over 70% of the respondents found Laxmikant Parsekar government 'good', the survey stated.
