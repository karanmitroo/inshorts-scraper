Responding to the government's letter seeking participation of the Aam Aadmi Party in the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, party chief Arvind Kejriwal said the cleanliness drive cannot possibly "make India clean". "It is being heard that on October 2, the Prime Minister will pick up the broom at Valmiki Sadan. That's good, though it is merely symbolic," Kejriwal added.
