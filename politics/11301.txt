As seven states and two Union Territories go to polls today, big names- Congress chief Sonia Gandhi, BJP leaders Narendra Modi, LK Advani, Rajnath Singh and Arun Jaitley are at stake. Some riveting contests are on the menu like- Jaitley versus Amarinder (Amritsar), Modi versus Congres leader Madhusudhan Mistry (Vadodara), BJP’s Rajnath Singh versus AAP’s Javed Jafri (Lucknow), amongst others.
