The Arvind Kejriwal-led Aam Aadmi Party on Wednesday released its 50-page manifesto for the Dec-4 Delhi polls, promising to pass the Jan Lokpal Bill within 15 days after coming to power. Moreover, the party promises a corruption-free government, introduction of 'Swaraj' (self-rule) to decentralise power, cheaper electricity, and special focus on security of women, among its various other promises.
