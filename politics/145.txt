The vehicle which killed nine children in an accident on Saturday belonged to BJP leader Manoj Baitha, RJD chief Lalu Prasad Yadav's son Tejashwi Yadav has said. There was a BJP nameplate on the vehicle which read 'Manoj Baitha, Pradesh Mantri, Mahadalit Manch', as per reports. However, leaders in state BJP have denied the party having any connections with Baitha.
