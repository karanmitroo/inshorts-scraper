Madhya Pradesh CM Shivraj Singh Chouhan said, "Modern education should also be imparted along with religious training in madrasas as we want children to be skillful." While addressing the 20th foundation day of MP Madrasa Board in Bhopal on Friday, Chouhan also announced to raise the annual infrastructure development fund of each madrasa in the state from ₹25,000 to ₹50,000.
