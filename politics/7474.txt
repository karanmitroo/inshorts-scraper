Andhra Pradesh's Social Welfare Minister Ravela Kishore Babu's son, Ravela Susheel, has been booked by the Hyderabad Police for allegedly harassing a woman. The woman accused Susheel of misbehaving with her while he was reportedly in an inebriated condition. Susheel has attempted to clarify the incident in a Facebook post and termed it "a cruel act" by their "political opponents".
