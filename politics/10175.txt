According to the number of votes counted till Tuesday afternoon, BJP has gained the maximum vote share in J&K. BJP's vote share stood at 23% (10, 99,961 votes), while PDP's vote share stood at around 22.6% (10,83,388 votes). The National Conference's vote share was 20.7% (9,90,532 votes) and Congress' vote share was only 6.8% (3,27,807 votes).
