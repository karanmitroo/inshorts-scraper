Cracking the whip on party rebels, JD(U) national President Sharad Yadav Monday suspended Mahua MLA Ravindra Rai. "Sharad Yadav telephoned and informed me about my suspension with immediate effect. He did not say anything about the period (of suspension)," Rai said. The move came on the eve of the deadline for the filing of nominations for the Rajya Sabha polls.
