The first phase of Lok Sabha elections begins Monday in six constituencies in two states. They are, five constituencies in Assam (Tezpur, Kaliabor, Jorhat, Dibrugarh and Lakhimpur) and one in Tripura (Tripura West). In the 3nd and 3rd phase on April 9 and 10, a total of 93 constituencies spread across 18 states will go to polls.
