Bihar's Kishanganj district administration on Monday filed an FIR against All India Majlis-e-Ittehad-ul Muslimeen MLA Akbaruddin Owaisi for making a provocative speech on Sunday. Owaisi had allegedly made provocative remarks against BJP leaders, including PM Narendra Modi. The police is also probing if the damage done to two idols of a temple in Kishanganj was a result of his speech.
