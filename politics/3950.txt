Addressing a gathering on Wednesday, Congress Vice President Rahul Gandhi said that he found Congress' symbol in Lord Shiv, Guru Nanak, Buddha and Mahavir's pictures. "Asked Karan Singh ji (Congress leader) what does it mean... he said it means 'daro mat, sachhayi ka saamna karo' (don't be afraid, face the truth)," he said.  "Congress philosophy says daro mat," Rahul added.
