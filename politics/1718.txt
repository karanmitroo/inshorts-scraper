All India Majlis-e-Ittehadul Muslimeen (AIMIM) President Asaduddin Owaisi on Monday said, "We have decided to support Meira Kumar. But a non-Congress candidate would have been better for this post." Notably, the voting to choose the 14th President of India between NDA's Ram Nath Kovind and Opposition's Meira Kumar began at the Parliament and State Legislative Assemblies on Monday.
