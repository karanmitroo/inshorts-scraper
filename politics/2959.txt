Samajwadi Party patron Mulayam Singh Yadav's wife Sadhna has said she was insulted a lot and accused of creating the Samajwadi Party feud. However, Sadhna stated she was unaware who had "misled" her stepson Akhilesh Yadav. "I want...Akhilesh to become the chief minister again," she added. She also said she wanted her biological son Prateek Yadav to enter politics.
