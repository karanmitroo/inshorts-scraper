Punjab Deputy Chief Minister Sukhbir Singh Badal on Wednesday demanded that the Bharat Ratna be conferred upon freedom fighters Bhagat Singh, Sukhdev and Rajguru. Speaking on their 85th martyrdom day, he also inaugurated the 'Amar Shaheed Jyoti' at the memorial of the martyrs. Badal further announced that the international airport at Mohali will be named after Bhagat Singh.
