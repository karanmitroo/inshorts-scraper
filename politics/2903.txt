Samajwadi Party Founder Mulayam Singh Yadav's younger daughter-in-law Aparna Yadav on Saturday lost to BJP leader Rita Bahuguna Joshi in Lucknow Cantt constituency in Uttar Pradesh. She has lost by over 33,000 votes. Notably, Mulayam and Dimple Yadav had personally campaigned for Aparna. Rita Bahuguna, on the other hand, had quit Congress and joined the BJP before polls.
