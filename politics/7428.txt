Telugu actor and Telugu Desam Party MLA Nandamuri Balakrishna on Tuesday in the Andhra Pradesh Assembly apologised for his allegedly 'objectionable' and 'vulgar' remarks on women at an event. Balakrishna's comments led to a section of lawyers lodging a police complaint against him. Meanwhile, the Opposition, YSR Congress, has demanded an apology from the CM and Balakrishna on the issue.
