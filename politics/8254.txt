The Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) has suspended its member Munish Raizada, who had called RJD chief Lalu Prasad 'a convicted corrupt criminal'. Lalu, a convict in the fodder scam case, is a partner in Bihar CM Nitish Kumar-led grand alliance, which was supported by AAP. According to AAP, Raizada was suspended for creating a website without the party's permission.
