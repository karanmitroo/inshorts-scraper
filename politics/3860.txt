RJD chief Lalu Prasad Yadav on Tuesday said he and his son Bihar Deputy CM Tejaswi Yadav will campaign for Uttar Pradesh CM Akhilesh Yadav in the upcoming Assembly elections. RJD will not contest in Uttar Pradesh to strengthen the Samajwadi Party, he added. "I have suggested to Sonia Gandhi to join hands with Akhilesh Yadav," Prasad said.
