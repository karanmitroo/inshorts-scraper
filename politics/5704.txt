In the run-up to the 2017 polls in Uttar Pradesh, Congress Vice President Rahul Gandhi today started his 2,500 km-long 'Kisan Yatra', having interactions with farmers over 'Khaat Sabhas'. Starting his 'Deoria to Dilli yatra', he collected Kisan Maang Patras outlining farmers' demands, while promising loan waivers and reduction in power tariffs if Congress is voted to power.
