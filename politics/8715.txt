Akhil Bharatiya Vidyarthi Parishad (ABVP), the student wing of RSS, won all the four seats in the Delhi University Students Union (DUSU) elections on Saturday. ABVP's Satinder Awana, Sunny Dedha, Anjali Rana and Chhattarpal Yadav won posts of President, Vice President, Secretary and Joint Secretary, respectively. Notably, the elections which were held on Friday saw a turnout of 43.3 percent.
