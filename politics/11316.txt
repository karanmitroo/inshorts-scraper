In the run-up to the polls, election commission on April 21 announced that Indian-Made Foreign Liquor worth Rs 12.57 crore have been seized so far in Gujarat where liquor is banned. Not only this, in the last three years, police have seized 4.07-lakh litres of country liquor and 13.21-lakh bottles of IMFL, worth Rs 36 crore, in this dry state.
