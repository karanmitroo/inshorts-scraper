A total of four Vishwa Hindu Parishad (VHP) workers were arrested on Sunday for burning a statue of Pakistan-based terror outfit Jamat-Ud-Dawa (JuD) chief Hafiz Saeed in Vadodra (Gujarat). Police said, the four people also disrupted law and order situation in Vadodra. They protested against Saeed for allegedly saying that Pakistanis and Kashmiris are "blood brothers" who cannot be separated.
