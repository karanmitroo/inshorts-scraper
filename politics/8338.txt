Results of the Kalyan Dombivli Municipal Corporation (KDMC) elections in Maharashtra were declared on Monday, with Uddhav Thackeray-led Shiv Sena winning 52 seats. The BJP won 42 seats while Raj Thackeray's MNS won nine seats followed by Cong-NCP combine winning six seats. Asaduddin Owaisi's AIMIM won two seats while Independents and BSP won nine seats in total.
