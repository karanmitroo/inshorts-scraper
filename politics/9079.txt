The BJP has defeated the Congress in the by-election for Madhya Pradesh's Garoth seat, the voting for which was held on Saturday. BJP's Chander Singh Sisodia defeated Congress candidate Subhash Sajotiya by nearly 14,000 votes. In March, BJP's Rajesh Yadav, the legislator from Garoth, had passed away. BJP's victory came despite the on-going controversy related to the Vyapam scam.
