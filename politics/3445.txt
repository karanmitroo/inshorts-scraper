A Samajwadi Party candidate in the forthcoming Uttar Pradesh Assembly elections, Gayatri Prajapati, was a BPL (Below Poverty Line) card holder in 2002 but has now disclosed assets worth ₹1.83 crore, as per reports. Earlier in 2011, Prajapati had reportedly donated ₹25 lakh to the party. Prajapati was reportedly close to party patriarch Mulayam Singh Yadav.
