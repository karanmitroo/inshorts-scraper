Samajwadi Party leader Amar Singh is the "root of the fight" between party chief Mulayam Singh Yadav and his son Akhilesh Yadav, Samajwadi Party MLA Brijlal Sonkar said on Sunday. Earlier, Amar Singh had denied the allegations and had urged Mulayam to reveal it to everyone if at all the party supremo felt he was being manipulated.
