Uttarakhand's BJP-led government has spent ₹5.85 crore in over 11 months on helicopter rides for CM Trivendra Singh Rawat and VIPs, an RTI reply revealed. The reply stated that Rawat made 93 air trips, including 45 by state-owned choppers. Last month, an RTI reply revealed that the CM's office had spent over ₹68 lakh on refreshments in 10 months.
