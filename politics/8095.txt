The Shiv Sena on Saturday urged RSS chief Mohan Bhagwat to announce a date for the commencement of the construction of a Ram Temple in Ayodhya. The Sena welcomed Bhagwat's statement on Wednesday in which he stated that the Ram temple will be built within his lifetime. In its mouthpiece ‘Saamana,’ the Sena stated, "This issue cannot be prolonged anymore."
