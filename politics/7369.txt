According to reports, the Arvind Kejriwal-led Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) will contest the Goa Assembly elections, scheduled to be held in February 2017. AAP national organisation building in-charge Durgesh Pathak recently visited the state for a five-day tour. Notably, senior party leader Ashutosh had also visited the state on March 10. Goa has 40 assembly constituencies.
