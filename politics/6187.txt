Reacting to reports that ex-cricketer Navjot Singh Sidhu will join AAP after his resignation as BJP's Rajya Sabha MP, a Twitter user wrote, "I guess Siddhu will be happy to join AAP as he always worked with Comedians and Jokers." Users discussed Sidhu's resignation with tweets like, "Sidhu was constantly searching for a new comedy show", and "Thoko taali, Thoko...#AAPKaSidhu."
