Samajwadi Party leader Shivpal Singh Yadav replaced Uttar Pradesh CM Akhilesh Yadav as the party's state President on Tuesday, reports said. This comes after Akhilesh rejected a party merger initiated by his uncle Shivpal, who had alleged corruption within the ruling government. Later, party chief Mulayam Singh Yadav alleged conspiracy and added that the party would split if Shivpal leaves.
