After the Election Commission allotted the Samajwadi Party's 'cycle' symbol to the faction led by Uttar Pradesh CM Akhilesh Yadav on Monday, Akhilesh tweeted that the cycle will keep moving forward. Reportedly, Akhilesh later met his father Mulayam Singh Yadav at his residence. Akhilesh loyalist Ram Gopal Yadav said SP may form an alliance with Congress depending upon Akhilesh's decision.
