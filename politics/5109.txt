Samajwadi Party leader Akhilesh Yadav remains the top choice for CM with 31% voters in his favour ahead of the 2017 UP Assembly election, a survey conducted by ABP News-CICERO stated. Akhilesh is followed by Mayawati with 27% preferring her and BJP’s Yogi Adityanath with 24% voters. However, 55% respondents weren’t in favour of Akhilesh forming a new party.
