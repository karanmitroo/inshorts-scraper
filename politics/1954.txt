BJP MP Shatrughan Sinha took to Twitter to praise AAP convener Arvind Kejriwal for his credibility, struggle, and commitment towards the society. Slamming the BJP, Sinha said, "Our BJP surely believes in honesty and transparency, which seldom go together." He also slammed the BJP leaders for indulging in negative politics and mudslinging on political opponents, including Kejriwal and Lalu Yadav.
