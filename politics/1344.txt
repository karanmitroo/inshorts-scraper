The Aam Aadmi Party candidate Ram Chander has won the by-election for Delhi's Bawana seat with a margin of over 24,000 votes. While AAP has retained its seat in Bawana with 59,886 votes, the BJP and Congress secured 35,834 and 31,919 votes respectively. The by-election was held after AAP's sitting MLA Ved Prakash resigned from the party and joined BJP.
