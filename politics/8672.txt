Praveen Manjhi, son of former Bihar CM Jitan Ram Manjhi, was released on bail on Sunday, hours after he was detained in Jehanabad (Bihar) for carrying cash worth Rs 4.65 lakh. However, "the money seized from him, is being analysed by the Income Tax officials", SP, Jehanabad, Aditya Kumar, said. Praveen's documents regarding the money are also being analysed.
