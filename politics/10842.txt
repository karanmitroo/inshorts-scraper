JMM's Simon Marandi, JDU's Gopal Krishna Patar and Dulal Bhuyan of the Jharkhand Vikas Morcha (Prajatantrik) joined BJP on Thursday. "I am happy to join the BJP ... as Jharkhand cannot be developed by regional parties," Marandi said. Earlier also, Marandi had quit JMM to join the Congress before coming back to the Shibu Soren party.
