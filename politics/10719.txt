After DMK chief Karunanidhi expressed disappointment over the renaming of Teacher's Day to 'Guru Utsav', Senior DMK leader TKS Elangovan, Monday, made a demand saying "Teachers' Day is the birthday of Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan. He was born in Andhra Pradesh and lived in Chennai. So if at all his birthday it is to be and should be in Telugu or Tamil."
