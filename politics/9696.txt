The Shiv Sena Saturday defended PM Narendra Modi for auctioning his pinstripe monogrammed suit saying that the suit was valued highly because of the person who wore it. "Instead of criticising, Congress can auction the wardrobe and footwear of Rahul Gandhi and see what amount they fetch. Similarly, Arvind Kejriwal can auction his muffler...", Sena's mouthpiece 'Saamana' read.
