On the 131st foundation day of the Indian National Congress, #131YrsYoungCongress was trending on Twitter in India on Monday. One tweet read, "Glorious past. Strong foundations. Correct understanding of India. Bright future". Some people slammed the Congress, with one tweet reading, "Shut this INC Company down. That's not #131YrsYoungCongress. That's 131 Years of Patience by Indians".
