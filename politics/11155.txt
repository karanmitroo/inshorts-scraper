The Aam Aadmi Party, which had fielded a whopping 426 candidates, could manage to win only four seats, all in Punjab. Interestingly, AAP had fielded more candidates than Congress (414) and BJP (415). The winners are, comedian Bhagwant Mann (Sangrur), retired teacher Sadhu Singh (Faridkot), former diplomat Harinder Singh Khalsa (Fatehgarh Sahib) and Dr Dharamvir Gandhi (Patiala).
