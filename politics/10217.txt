Launching a scathing attack on the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS), senior Samajwadi Party leader Azam Khan has said that the organization should be banned for glorifying Nathuram Godse, the man who assassinated Mahatma Gandhi. Azam further said, Godse was the 'first terrorist' of the country. He also asked PM Narendra Modi to break his silence on the conversion controversy.
