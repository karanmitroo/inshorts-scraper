Lok Sabha Speaker Meira Kumar will have access to the Delhi bungalow of her father and former deputy prime minister, Babu Jagjivan Ram, for at least another 25 years. The bungalow was allotted to 'Babu Jagjivan Ram National Foundation' in 2013 till 2038 by the Directorate of Estates. Kumar was the last official occupant of her father's bungalow.
