Ahead of the upcoming Assembly polls in Goa, Shiv Sena on Tuesday announced an alliance with the Maharashtrawadi Gomantak Party (MGP) and the Goa Suraksha Manch (GSM). This comes after MGP withdrew its support to the state BJP government and declared Sudin Dhavalikar as its Chief Ministerial candidate. Notably, polling will be held in Goa on February 4.
