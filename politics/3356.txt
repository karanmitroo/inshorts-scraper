Akhil Bhartiya Kalyan Party, contesting in 22 constituencies of Uttar Pradesh, has a 'frock' as its symbol, while the symbol of Desh Shakti Party, contesting in 403 constituencies of the state, is a 'syringe'. Uttarakhand-based party Apni Zindagi Apna Dal will contest the elections with 'cauliflower' as its symbol. Punjab's Democratic Swaraj Party went to polls with the symbol 'saw'.
