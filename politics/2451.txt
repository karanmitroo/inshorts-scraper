Samajwadi Party's Founder Mulayam Singh Yadav's younger daughter-in-law has appealed the party President Akhilesh Yadav to hand over the control of the party back to his father. "Bhaiya (Akhilesh) promised that after the Assembly elections he will hand back the post of SP National President to Netaji. I expect him to live up to his words," she added.
