In a new scheme, Tamil Nadu CM J Jayalalithaa announced Tuesday, ‘Amma Baby Care Kits’ for all babies born in state government hospitals. This scheme which would benefit 6.7 lakh children, has been allotted a sum of Rs.67 crore. The Kit will have- baby towel, dress, bed, mosquito net, napkin, rattle, toys, sanitizer, soap for mother, amongst several others items.
