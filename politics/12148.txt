Corporate houses and businessmen donated Rs.379 crore to political parties in 8-years (2004-12), a report released Wednesday by Association for Democratic Reforms said. BJP received the maximum donation of Rs.192.47 crore from 1,334 corporate houses, followed by Congress at Rs.172.25 crore from 418 donors. Aditya Birla Group was the top corporate donor to INC (Rs.36.4 crore) and BJP (Rs.26.57 crore).
