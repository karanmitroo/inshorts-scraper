Samajwadi Party Founder Mulayam Singh Yadav’s second wife Sadhna Yadav has described both Akhilesh and Prateek Yadav as her two eyes. "The word stepmother which has been used by people is entirely wrong," she added. Notably, Akhilesh is Mulayam’s first wife Malti Devi’s son and Prateek was born to Sadhna.
