Madhya Pradesh Chief Minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan attended the wedding ceremony of the daughter of Ramashankar Yadav, the head constable who was killed in Bhopal jailbreak. Chouhan also gave a letter for a Group 3 job to the deceased cop's daughter as a wedding gift. Later, Chouhan tweeted, "By welcoming the Baraatis, I fulfilled the duty of a father."
