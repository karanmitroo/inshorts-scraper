India's tenth Prime Minister, Atal Bihari Vajpayee, turned 91 years old on Friday. Vajpayee, a BJP leader, was the first non-Congress Prime Minister to serve a full five-year term (1998 to 2004). Notably, Vajpayee also served as the PM first in 1996 for 13 days. He was awarded the Padma Vibhushan in 1992 and the Bharat Ratna in 2015.
