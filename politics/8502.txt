Shiv Sena president Uddhav Thackeray on Tuesday met and appreciated the Shiv Sainiks, released on bail after being arrested, for smearing black paint on politician Sudheendra Kulkarni. Thackeray asked them to continue their 'good work'. Referring to Kulkarni, Sena mouthpiece Saamana said, "When there are people like him present here, Pakistan does not need to send people like Kasab."
