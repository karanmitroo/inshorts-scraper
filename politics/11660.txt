Only 70% of Rs 93.75 crore that was made available to Lok Sabha MPs in Delhi under the MPLAD fund, was utilised, leaving around Rs 28.80 crore unspent, according to data released for 2009-2013. Congress MP from North East Delhi Jai Prakash Agarwal has best spent the funds while his South Delhi counterpart Ramesh Kumar is the worst-performing MP.
