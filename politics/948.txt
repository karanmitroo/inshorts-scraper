YSR Congress President YS Jagan Mohan Reddy on Monday kicked-off his 3,000-km Praja Sankalpa Yatra, covering 125 Assembly segments across Andhra Pradesh. During the yatra that is expected to last six months, Reddy will reportedly address 180 public meetings. He started the yatra slamming Andhra CM N Chandrababu Naidu over "rampant corruption, nepotism, and utter inefficiency" in his government.
