Followed by opening of tea stalls by BJP, RJD too has opened 'Lalu chai dukaan' in the name of party chief Lalu Prasad, claiming that he was the "real" tea seller. BJP leader Sushil Kumar Modi has termed it as RJD's "monkey trick" saying, "By aping him (Narendra Modi) Lalu Prasad will not be able to impress people".
