Kerala Chief Minister Pinarayi Vijayan on Monday said that the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) do not have any support from the people of the state and accused “BJP-RSS people” of propagating lies. Notably, a BJP leader had earlier cited incidents of political violence in Kerala and said that Vijayan was the "chief murderer" of Kerala.
