Twenty-one Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) members from Sangrur, Punjab joined the Congress on Saturday in the presence of Captain Amarinder Singh, the recently appointed Punjab Congress chief. Further, 34 members from the Shiromani Gurdwara Parbandhak Committee and the Shiromani Akali Dal have also joined the Congress. Earlier, Congress spokesperson Sukhpal Singh Khaira joined AAP's Punjab unit on Friday.
