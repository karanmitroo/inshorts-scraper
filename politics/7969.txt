Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal on Monday refused to render a public apology to Union Finance Minister Arun Jaitley saying that "no clean chit" has been given in the DDCA probe. He tweeted, "Let Jaitley ji be cross-examined in defamation cases." Kejriwal added that the enquiry commission has been recommended "to fix responsibility" and the report "confirmed several instances of wrongdoings".
