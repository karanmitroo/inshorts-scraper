The day when AAP chief Arvind Kejriwal was “detained”, his party saw Rs 24,05,078 being donated online from 1,544 people, a hike of 260% compared to Rs 6,67,852 received from 377 people as donations a day before (Tuesday). Moreover, on Thursday also, 1,133 people donated Rs 20,02,229. However, the daily donation earlier ranged only between Rs 8 and 10 lakh.
