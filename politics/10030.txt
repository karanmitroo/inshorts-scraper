The RSS Saturday rubbished the reports about its chief Mohan Bhagwat being unhappy over former IPS officer Kiran Bedi's inclusion in the BJP. "News about Dr Bhagwatji disapproving Kiran Bedi's entry to BJP is baseless. Its an evil design to show a rift between RSS & Govt," RSS Prachar Pramukh Manmohan Vaidya said on twitter.
