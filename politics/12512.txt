Times Now-CVoter survey shows that BJP will emerge as the largest party in next general elections with 162 seats while congress will get only 102. It projects the NDA to get 186 (35% votes) and the UPA 117 (27% votes), leaving 240-seats with others including Left(32), BSP(31), AIADMK(28), SP(25), Trinamool(23) and JD-United(9); and thus boosting chances of a hung parliament.
