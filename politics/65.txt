The Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP) has decided to extend its support to the Samajwadi Party (SP) for the upcoming Phulpur and Gorakhpur bypolls in Uttar Pradesh. "Our workers want to eliminate Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)," BSP Zonal Coordinator Ashok Gautam said. BSP supremo Mayawati later clarified that the alliance will not extend to the 2019 general elections.
