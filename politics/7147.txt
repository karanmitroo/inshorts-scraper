On Bharatiya Janata Party's (BJP) 36th Foundation Day, #BJPFoundationDay was a top Indian Twitter trend on Wednesday. PM Narendra Modi tweeted, "On #BJPFoundationDay, I salute crores of BJP Karyakartas who have always served the BJP with admirable vigour & great dedication. @BJP4India". Users expressed their support for BJP with tweets like, "The party that uprooted the Family Raj from India".
