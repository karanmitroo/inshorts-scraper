Reacting to Shiv Sena's criticism of the government, the RSS' alleged mouthpiece, Tarun Bharat, has dared Sena chief Uddhav Thackeray to walk out of the government. An editorial in Tarun Bharat has emphasised how the Sena was desperate to join the Fadnavis government post polls. "In the last two years, we have swallowed insults from Sena leaders," BJP stated.
