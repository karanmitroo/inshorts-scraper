Maharaja of Amethi and Congress leader Sanjay Singh's estranged wife Garima Singh will contest UP Assembly elections from Amethi constituency against the leader's present wife Ameeta Sinh. Garima Singh would be contesting the elections on a BJP ticket while Ameeta would fight as an independent candidate. Ameeta contested polls from Amethi in 2012 but had lost to SP's Gayatri Prajapati.
