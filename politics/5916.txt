BJP MP from Jharkhand, Nishikant Dubey, has demanded allocation of the Lok Sabha seats in Pakistan-occupied Kashmir and Gilgit-Baltistan. I want the government to introduce a bill in the Parliament for LS seats in these regions, he said. His remarks came after Union Minister Jitendra Singh launched a Tiranga Yatra in J&K, stating tricolour will be hoisted in PoK.
