Twitter users slammed Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal over his comments about the authenticity of PM Narendra Modi's educational qualifications. Users used #KejriwalSaySorry to discuss the issue with tweets like, "This is only an attempt by Kejriwal to take the attention off #AgustaScam, Rahul & Sonia!!" and "Kejriwal is like that kid who always blame others for his own mistakes".
