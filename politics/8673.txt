The Samajwadi Party (SP) on Thursday joined hands with the Samajwadi Janata Dal-Democratic (SJD-D), National People’s Party (NPP) and Nationalist Congress Party (NCP) to offer a ‘Third Front' to voters. The alliance aims to offer an alternative to the BJP-led National Democratic Alliance and Nitish Kumar-led Grand Alliance. Bihar will vote in five phases, beginning October 12.
