Newly appointed Samajwadi Party's Uttar Pradesh chief Shivpal Yadav on Thursday said he is ready to make any sacrifice for SP. On being asked if he was fine with Akhilesh Yadav being SP's UP CM face, Shivpal said, "I accept whatever decision Netaji (Mulayam Singh) takes." Shivpal added, "No one in our party has the authority to defy Netaji's decisions."
