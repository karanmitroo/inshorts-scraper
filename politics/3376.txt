All India Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam General Secretary VK Sasikala Natarajan said that no sinister intentions can hurt her till the time she has Jayalalithaa's 1.5 crore party brothers and sisters with her. "Amma is showing us betrayers of our party. People who think they can separate our party that has 1.5 crore people will only face defeat," Sasikala added.
