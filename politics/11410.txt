On Thursday, an FIR was lodged against RJD chief Lalu Yadav's daughter Misha Bharti, who is contesting from Pataliputra seat in Bihar, for forcibly entering a polling booth and allegedly damaging an EVM machine. However, Bharti has denied the allegations. Meanwhile, RJD spokesperson Manoj Jha said a counter-FIR will be lodged as he alleged that the party candidate was manhandled.
