All India Majlis-e-Ittehadul Muslimeen (AIMIM) leader Asaduddin Owaisi was not given permission by the police in Kalyan, Maharashtra, to hold an election rally in the town. Elections for the Kalyan Dombivli Municipal Corporation will be held in November and the AIMIM is contesting them. Police said two AIMIM candidates using terrorist Yakub Memon's name in their campaign prompted the move.
