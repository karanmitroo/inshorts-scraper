Comparing the power tariffs of Delhi and the BJP-ruled Gujarat, CM Arvind Kejriwal on Monday alleged that PM Narendra Modi wants Delhi’s power tariffs to be the same as that of Gujarat. The cost of 200 and 400 units of electricity in Delhi is ₹550 and ₹1,200 while in Gujarat it is ₹1,100 and ₹2,300 respectively, he stated.
