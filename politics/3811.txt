Former CM of Uttar Pradesh and Uttarakhand Narayan Dutt Tiwari (91) and his son Rohit Shekhar on Wednesday joined BJP ahead of the Assembly polls in the two states. Though Tiwari may not contest the election, his son may contest from Uttarakhand, a report stated. The ex-Congress leader had thrice assumed the CM office in UP and once in Uttarakhand.
