Leaders of six Janata Parivar parties, including Samajwadi Party (SP), Janata Dal (United) and Rashtriya Janata Dal, announced their merger Wednesday. SP chief Mulayam Singh Yadav will head the new party, which is likely to be named Samajwadi Janata Party or Samajwadi Janata Dal. It will have 15 MPs in the Lok Sabha and 30 MPs in the Rajya Sabha.
