44% of people would vote for an AAP candidate if there was one in their constituency, while 26% felt AAP could win 51-100 seats in 2014 LS elections, says a TOI opinion-poll conducted across country's eight most-populous cities. 58% want Modi as PM, while 25% favour Kejriwal and 14% support Rahul. Interestingly, 31% preferred Kejriwal in Modi's home turf Ahmedabad.
