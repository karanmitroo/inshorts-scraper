An RSS leader Elamthottathil Manoj (42) was killed, Monday by an unidentified gang at Diamond junction in the Kannur district. The BJP and RSS have accused the CPM of carrying out the attacks, in suspected retaliation of the attempted murder attacks of a CPM leader in 1999. The RSS has called for a state-wide hartal, 6 a.m.-6 p.m. on Tuesday.
