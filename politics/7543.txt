Ahead of Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal's visit to Punjab's Jalandhar on Sunday, posters  questioning the Delhi government's one-year performance have sprung up across the city. The posters written in Punjabi read 'Ek Saal, Delhi Behaal' with an image of Kejriwal. Earlier, Kejriwal on a 5-day visit to Punjab ahead of assembly polls had blamed the ruling SAD-BJP for policy failures.
