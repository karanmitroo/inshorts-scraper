The Shiv Sena will contest the 2019 Lok Sabha elections for two seats in Goa in alliance with the Goa Suraksha Manch (GSM), Shiv Sena spokesperson Sanjay Raut said on Monday. Claiming that the Sena has emerged as one of the key political opponents in the state, Raut further said that party chief Uddhav Thackeray will visit Goa next month.
