Yoga guru Baba Ramdev, on Saturday announced that he would organize a door-to-door campaign for Narendra Modi in the run up to the 2014 polls. "Corruption exists in all parties. But politicians like Modi, Mamata Banerjee and Naveen Patnaik are not corrupt", said Ramdev. Moreover, Ramdev referred Modi as 'Himalayas' whereas Congress vice-president Rahul Gandhi as a 'Bachcha'.
