Former Congress leader Jagdambika Pal and popular stand-up comedian Raju Srivastava Wednesday joined BJP ahead of the Lok Sabha polls. "I welcome Jagdambika Pal and Raju Srivastav into the party. Their entry will add strength to the party," BJP chief Rajnath Singh said. Earlier, Raju Srivastava had returned the Samajwadi Party ticket to contest from Kanpur in Uttar Pradesh.
