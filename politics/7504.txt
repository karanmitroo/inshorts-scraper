Congress today questioned the appointment of former CAG Vinod Rai as the Chairman of the Banks Board Bureau (BBB). Congress spokesperson Randeep Surjewala recalled Finance Minister Arun Jaitley's statement when he was the Leader of Opposition in Rajya Sabha that no post-retirement jobs should be given to CAG, CEC, among others. Surjewala also questioned conferring of Padma award to Rai.
