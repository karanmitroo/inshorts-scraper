VP Singh Badnore on Monday took oath as the Punjab Governor and the Chief Justice of Punjab and Haryana High Court, SJ Vazifdar, administered the pledge of office and secrecy in Chandigarh. Badnore was also sworn in as the Administrator of Chandigarh Administration. The 68-year-old, four-time BJP legislator in the Rajasthan Assembly, will replace Kaptan Singh Solanki at the posts.
