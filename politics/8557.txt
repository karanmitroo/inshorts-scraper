Samajwadi Party head Mulayam Singh Yadav on Thursday said that the conspiracy behind the Dadri lynching was hatched by "three persons of a specific party" and those behind Muzaffarnagar riots were also involved. Yadav alleged that "someone" is hatching a conspiracy to destroy communal harmony. "The Akhilesh Yadav government should crush communal forces even if the government falls", Yadav said.
