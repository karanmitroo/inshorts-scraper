Breaking previous records, Rajasthan registered a historic voter turnout of 63% (15% increase from last parliamentary elections) in both phases of the recently concluded Lok Sabha elections. Ganganagar recorded the highest turnout of 72.85%, followed by Barmer, where 72.43% voters exercised their franchise, according to the chief election office. Karauli-Dholpur constituency  witnessed the lowest voter turnout at 54.70%.
