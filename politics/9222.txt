Ramchandra Chandravanshi, the health minister of Jharkhand was on Sunday caught on camera slapping Rashtriya Janta Dal (RJD) leader Shambhunath Chandrawanshi in public. As per the RJD leader, Ramachandra asked him to resign from his post so that he could appoint his son. When Shanbhunath refused to do so, Ramchandra hit him and abused him verbally.
