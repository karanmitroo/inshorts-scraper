Delhi Deputy CM Manish Sisodia on Thursday took a swipe at journalist Arnab Goswami with a picture and its caption, ‘I am having Aloo Tikki with my Bua ji (aunt). Run it as breaking news’. Earlier, Goswami had alleged the AAP leader was ‘holidaying’ in Finland. Later, Sisodia faced flak over a picture showing him eating ice-cream in Finland.
