Uttar Pradesh Minister Vijay Mishra who had joined BSP after being denied ticket for the state Assembly elections has been sacked from the Akhilesh Yadav-led government. Mishra, who had been serving as Minister of State (Independent Affairs) for Religious Works, had joined BSP alleging that the ruling Samajwadi Party was anti-Brahmin. "Akhilesh Yadav is surrounded by sycophants," Mishra had said.
