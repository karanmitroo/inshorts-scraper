After the Congress-led Opposition named former Lok Sabha Speaker Meira Kumar as its presidential candidate, 17 parties have extended their support. The major parties include Samajwadi Party (SP), Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP), Nationalist Congress Party (NCP), Communist Party of India (Marxist) (CPI(M)), Rashtriya Janata Dal (RJD) and National Conference. The NDA has around 60% votes in its favour.
