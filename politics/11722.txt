"Due to demand of party leaders and workers, Samajwadi Party supremo Mulayam Singh Yadav will contest from both Azamgarh and Mainpuri seats (which he presently represents in Parliament)," party's general secretary Ramgopal Yadav said Tuesday. Earlier, the party had  declared UP Cabinet Minister Balram Yadav as the party's candidate from Azamgarh, which has a fairly large Muslim and Yadav population.
