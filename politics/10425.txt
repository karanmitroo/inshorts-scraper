At least twenty new ministers will be inducted in the Union Cabinet today. Ex-Goa CM Parrikar, Sena's Suresh Prabhu; and Bangaru Dattareya and Rajiv Pratap Rudy as MoS (Independent) were among the first to be sworn in. There is still no clarity on Sena's Anil Desai. Olympic medalist Rajyavardhan Rathore attended PM's breakfast, sparking rumors about a likely Cabinet post.
