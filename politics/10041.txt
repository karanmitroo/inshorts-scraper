A Rampur court Wednesday sentenced Union minister Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi to one-year in  jail for breaching prohibitory orders during the 2009 Lok Sabha polls. Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi, and his 19 co-accused, have been taken in the court's custody. The case relates to a demonstration by BJP workers, led by Naqvi, during which they allegedly barged into a Rampur police station.
