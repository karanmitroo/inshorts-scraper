Initial analysis of the applicants seeking AAP tickets has revealed that 87.9% of the aspirants are predominantly between 40-50 years of age. Also, 21.3% candidates are graduates, 18.4% are post-graduates, 15.5% have a law degree, 7.5% hold a management degree while 7.5% have passed Class 12. AAP's first list of candidates for LS is likely to be out by Feb-12.
