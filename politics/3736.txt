After Samajwadi Party’s guardian Mulayam Singh Yadav skipped the party’s manifesto launch, party President Akhilesh Yadav on Sunday shared a picture of him with his father and wife Dimple Yadav. Noticing Mulayam and his brother Shivpal Singh Yadav’s absence in the function, SP leader Azam Khan had rushed to Mulayam’s bungalow to bring him to the event, as per reports.
