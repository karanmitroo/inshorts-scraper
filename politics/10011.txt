Days after BJP leaders Sakshi Maharaj and Sadhvi Prachi's controversial 4-kid remarks, Shankracharya Vasudevanand Saraswati of Badrikashram has urged Hindus to maintain their majority status by producing 10 kids. The godman further said, "It is because of Hindus unity that Modi has become the PM." He also said that there should be no ban on Ghar Wapasi.
