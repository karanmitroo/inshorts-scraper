Reacting to Samajwadi leader Shivpal Yadav floating a new front, BJP on Saturday asserted that the people of Uttar Pradesh are now irritated with the SP family feud. It is a never ending dispute and shows that their political ideology has stooped to a new low, it added. Shivpal Yadav had announced the formation of 'Samajwadi Secular Morcha' on Friday.
