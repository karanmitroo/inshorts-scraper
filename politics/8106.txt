Three people, including Congress MP and former Madhya Pradesh CM Digvijay Singh, have been booked for fraud by the state Economic Offences Wing. Singh, who was in power in Madhya Pradesh from 1993-2003, and his Technical Education Minister Raja Pateria, have been accused of reducing the penalty imposed on RKDF college over wrongful admissions from ₹24 lakh to ₹2.5 lakh.
