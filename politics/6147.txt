Union Minister Harsimrat Kaur Badal on Friday threatened to move a privilege motion against Congress MPs Renuka Chowdhury and Jairam Ramesh for allegedly ‘misbehaving’  with her in Rajya Sabha. Kaur alleged that the two did not let her speak on national security issue in the House. "They...started fighting with me" and Renuka Chowdhury even used "bad words", she said.
