Samajwadi Party’s UP President Shivpal Singh Yadav on Monday reportedly sacked seven ‘loyalists’ of UP CM Akhilesh Yadav from the party. The members have been sacked for anti-party activities and issuing statements against SP supremo Mulayam Singh Yadav, claimed an India Today report. On Sunday, Shivpal had sacked a relative of senior party leader Ram Gopal Yadav.
