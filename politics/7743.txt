Former CM of Arunachal Pradesh Nabam Tuki on Friday alleged that he was 'threatened, frisked, abused and video-graphed' before and during an earlier meeting with the state's governor JP Rajkhowa at Raj Bhawan in Itanagar. Rajkhowa had also raised similar allegations of threat and abuse, against Tuki and his ministers, in a report submitted to President Pranab Mukherjee.
