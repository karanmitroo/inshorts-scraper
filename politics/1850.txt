Slamming Madhya Pradesh Chief Minister Shivraj Singh Chauhan, Congress leader Jyotiraditya Scindia said, "Your government is gunning down people, it is sending people to hospitals and you sit on a fast...Is it a joke?" Scindia also began a 72-hour satyagrah in Bhopal on Wednesday in support of farmers demanding a loan waiver and a higher price for their produce.
