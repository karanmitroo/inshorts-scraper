Campaigning for the crucial sixth phase of Lok Sabha elections in 117 constituencies (across 12 states) will end on Tuesday as voting in all these constituencies will start on Apr-24. As many as 116 candidates including 52 independents are in fray, including some big names like - Milind Deora, Meera Sanyal, Shahnawaz Hussain, Tariq Anwar and Mohammad Taslimuddin.
