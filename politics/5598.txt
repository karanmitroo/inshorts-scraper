Samajwadi Party chief Mulayam Singh Yadav on Friday clarified that there's no fight between his brother Shivpal Singh Yadav, and son Akhilesh Yadav. “Samajwadi​ Party is a family...as long as I’m here, there can’t be any rift,” said the former UP CM. This came hours after he met both Akhilesh and Shivpal at his residence.
