Raman Singh is an Ayurvedic doctor-turned-politician who has been the Chief Minister of Chhattisgarh for 13 years. Singh, the second CM of the state, had banned Naxalite organisations in Chhattisgarh in 2005. He also had been MoS for Commerce and Industry from 1999 to 2003 and was President of Bharatiya Jan Sangh Yuva Morcha from 1976 to 1977.
