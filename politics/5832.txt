Union HRD Minister Prakash Javadekar has clarified his remark wherein he recently said that freedom fighters like Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose, Sardar Patel and Jawaharlal Nehru were 'hanged'. Calling it an issue of confusion over punctuation, Javadekar said, "I mentioned leaders like Gandhi, Nehru, Subhash. It was a full stop. In the next sentence, I recounted those who were hanged...".
