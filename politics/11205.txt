With about 2.5k minutes (or 33.21%) of the prime-time news telecast during this Lok Sabha election, BJP's PM candidate, Narendra Modi got higher prime-time coverage than any other politician, said a study by CMS Media Lab. His closest competitor was AAP leader Arvind Kejriwal (10.31%). Rahul Gandhi, who is leading the campaign of the Congress, came a distant third (4.33%).
