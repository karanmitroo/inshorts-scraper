A survey  by CICERO associates suggests that AAP is likely to sweep Delhi with 38-50 seats in the December-4  Assembly polls. Whereas the survey gives 8-14 seats to Congress and 11-17 seats to BJP. CICERO,  which interviewed 1,643 people in 17 Assembly areas, said that AAP is expected to get 36% of the vote share, BJP 27% and Congress 26%.
