The Bahujan Samaj Party's (BSP) Punjab Chief Avtar Singh Karimpuri has announced plans to get 25,000 NRIs onboard within three months to campaign for the 2017 state elections. "We told them that we will work on problems like drugs, unemployment,...and rising farmer suicides," he said. He clarified that the BSP is seeking “moral and not financial” support from the NRIs.
