Slamming West Bengal CM Mamata Banerjee, BJP leader SN Singh on Monday said, "Queen of Saradha, Narada is trying to unite the opposition against government." Adding that PM Narendra Modi's demonetisation plan has shaken up the corrupt, BJP called Banerjee the "cheerleader of all anti-social elements". Notably, Saradha scam and Narada sting operation happened when Banerjee had been Chief Minister.
