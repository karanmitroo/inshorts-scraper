As per the figures compiled by Association of Democratic Reforms (ADR) and National Election Watch (NEW), 128 out of the 607 legislators (21%) in the five states (Delhi, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh and Mizoram) heading for assembly polls in November-December have criminal backgrounds. Delhi has the maximum (43%) and  Mizoram has the minimum (7%) legislators with declared criminal cases.
