President Ram Nath Kovind took to Twitter to invite people to visit the Rashtrapati Bhavan the next time they are in Delhi. "It (the Presidential House) belongs to Indians all over the world #PresidentKovind," he tweeted. Reacting to the tweet, which has been liked over 8,000 times, a user tweeted, "Breakfast tomorrow?" while another posted, "List it on @trivago."
