Congress Vice President Rahul Gandhi has alleged that the Prime Minister Narendra Modi-led government has waived loans worth ₹1.40 lakh crore owed by 50 rich people. Further, he said the Centre has refused to write off ₹50,000 crore owed by farmers. In comparison, the UPA government had waived off farmers' loans worth over ₹70,000 crore within 10 days, he added.
