Over ₹83 crore cash, 7 lakh litres of liquor worth ₹12.65 crore and 1,485 kilogram narcotics have been seized by the Election Commission-appointed surveillance teams in the five poll-bound states. As per official data compiled since the announcement of polls early this year till Saturday, UP has witnessed the maximum seizure of ₹79.13 crore, followed by ₹4.05 crore from Punjab.
