Accusing Andhra Pradesh CM Chandrababu Naidu of not fulfilling his poll promises, YSR Congress leader Roja organised a protest against him in Chittoor where her supporters sported flowers on their ears. The protesters, including student leaders, raised slogans such as "Babu, where is the job" during the rally. Naidu has failed to fulfil 600 promises made during elections, Roja claimed.
