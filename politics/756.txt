Slamming Congress Vice President Rahul Gandhi, Maharashtra Congress Secretary Shehzad Poonawalla on Sunday said, "Main Shehzad hoon, shehzada nahin. (I am Shehzad Poonawalla, not a prince)." Terming himself a whistleblower, Poonawalla said the Congress internal polls were rigged in the favour of Rahul Gandhi. He also thanked PM Narendra Modi for supporting his allegations and praising him.
