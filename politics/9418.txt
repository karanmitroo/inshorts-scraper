The Commmunist party of India (Marxist) Sunday elected Rajya Sabha member Sitaram Yechury (62) as the fifth general secretary of the 50-year-old party. Yechury, who is an Economics graduate from St. Stephen's College, Delhi, replaces Prakash Karat who has stepped down after three terms. The decision on the new general secretary was taken by the party's Central Committee at Vishakhapatnam.
