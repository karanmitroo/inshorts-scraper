Illegal cash of over ₹53 crore has been confiscated from the five states facing Assembly elections. The maximum, at ₹21.45 crore, was seized from Tamil Nadu, while ₹11.66 crore was seized from Assam. The states were followed by Kerala, where ₹9.65 crore was seized, and West Bengal, where ₹9.61 crore was seized. Money was also seized from Puducherry.
