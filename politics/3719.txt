The Congress on Tuesday released its list of 40 star campaigners for the upcoming Assembly polls, naming Priyanka Gandhi as one of the star campaigners for Uttar Pradesh Assembly elections. The daughter of Congress President Sonia Gandhi will be campaigning in Amethi, Rae Bareli, Chandauli, Ghaziabad and Saharanpur. Sheila Dikshit, Manmohan Singh are amongst the other star campaigners.
