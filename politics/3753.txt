
BJP on Sunday released its second list of candidates for 155 seats of the UP elections. BJP has now declared candidates for 304 constituencies out of the total 403 seats. Among BJP's notable candidates are Home Minister Rajnath Singh's son Pankaj Singh, contesting from Noida, and former PM Lal Bahadur Shastri's grandson Siddharth Singh, who will contest from Allahabad.
