After Jamat-ud-Dawah (JuD) chief Hafiz Saeed claimed that India can't prove his role in 26/11 Mumbai attacks, MoS Prime Minister's Office Jitendra Singh said that India is "prepared for any threats". Singh added that "officials are placing proof" against Saeed. Referring to Saeed as a "terrorist", minister Mukhtar Naqvi added that Saeed's statement isn't important"...it’s important what we say.”
