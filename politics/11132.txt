Thanking Baba Ramdev for "awakening" the voters, senior BJP leader Arun Jaitley Sunday said, "The role played by Ramdev in awakening the voters is similar to the struggles undertaken by Mahatma Gandhi and Jayprakash Narayan." Notably, the yoga teacher had vowed not to return to his Patanjali ashram in Haridwar, till the Congress-led UPA government was defeated.
