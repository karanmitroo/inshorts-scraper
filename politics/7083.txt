Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK) Chief M Karunanidhi on Sunday released his party's manifesto, promising to give laptops or tablets with 3G/4G facility with 10 GB of downloads per month to 16 lakh students. The party also promised to implement a liquor ban in Tamil Nadu as well as the establishment of a Lokayukta. Smartphones for poor families were also promised.
