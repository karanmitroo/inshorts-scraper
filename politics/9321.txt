Arvind Kejriwal-led Delhi government's anti-corruption helpline has received over 1,25,065 calls in one month, revealed a senior official on Wednesday. The highest number of complaints were registered against officials of civic bodies, police, transport and Jal Board, he added. The 1031 helpline, first introduced during AAP's 49-day stint after the 2013 Assembly polls, was relaunched on Apr 5.
