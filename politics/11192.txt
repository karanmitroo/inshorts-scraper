With a total poll expense of Rs 30 lakhs in Varanasi, BJP PM candidate Narendra modi has spent less than Congress candidate Ajay Rai (Rs. 52.88 lakh) and AAP chief Arvind Kejriwal (Rs. 41.50 lakh), according to Election Commission. Kailash Chaurasia of SP stands fourth spending Rs. 21 lakh, while BSP candidate Vijay Jaiswal finished fifth with Rs. 19.10 lakh.
