In a fresh setback to Aam Aadmi Party, four candidates including those pitted against high-profile opponents, like Salman Khurshid, Sachin Pilot  withdrew nominations Monday citing lack of party support or alleging corruption in the party. They are, Mukul Tripathi (Farukkhabad, UP), Dilip Yadav (Etah, UP), Ravinder Singh (Agra) and Ajay Somani (Ajmer).
