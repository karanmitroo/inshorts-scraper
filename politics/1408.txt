Claiming that the AIADMK merger talks are going on smoothly, O Panneerselvam said that a concrete decision over it will be taken in a day or two. There is no difference of opinion between the AIADMK factions and we would carry forward on the path laid by MGR (Maruthur Gopalan Ramachandran) and Amma (J Jayalalithaa), Panneerselvam added.
