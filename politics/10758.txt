President Pranab Mukherjee on Tuesday appointed four new governors for Rajasthan, Karnataka, Maharashtra and Goa. Former Uttar Pradesh CM Kalyan Singh was appointed governor of Rajasthan, while Gujarat speaker Vajubhai Vala has been appointed governor of Karnataka. Former union minister C Vidyasagar Rao was appointed as governor of Maharashtra and 71-year Mridula Sinha was appointed as governor of Goa.
