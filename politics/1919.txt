As the PM Narendra Modi-led NDA government completed its three years of regime on Friday, the BJP leader's most retweeted tweet is the 2014 general elections victory post. The tweet, "India has won! भारत की विजय। अच्छे दिन आने वाले हैं।" has been retweeted over 95,484 times. Notably, Modi has posted 15,200 tweets from his personal micro-blogging account so far.
