BJP's national general secretary Kailash Vijayvargiya has said that the Vyapam scam is a "small incident" and the party's morale is not down. "For us, it (Vyapam) is a chutput (small) incident. It may be big for you," Vijayvargiya reportedly said. Recently, he had allegedly said after TV journalist Akshay Singh's death, "Is a journalist bigger than me?"
