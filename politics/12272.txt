BJP leader Nitin Gadkari on Friday said, if his party comes to power at the Centre, it would create the separate state of Vidarbha out of Maharashtra. He reminded that it was BJP led NDA which had formed the states of Uttarakhand, Jharkhand and Chhattisgarh during its rule. Notably, Gadkari himself hails from Nagpur, the main city of Vidarbha region.
