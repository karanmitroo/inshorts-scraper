The West Bengal government under CM Mamata Banerjee has filed an FIR against President Bimal Gurung of Gorkha Janmukti Morcha (GJM), the ruling party in Darjeeling, over protests in the region. The protests outside the venue of Mamata's cabinet meeting in Darjeeling turned violent. The people were protesting against Mamata's decision to teach Bengali language in school in the hills.
