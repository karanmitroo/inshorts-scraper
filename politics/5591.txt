Criticising the Delhi government for the absence of its ministers when the city is witnessing a rise in Dengue and Chikungunya cases, BJP said that AAP has become a "Khaas Aadmi Party". The party also criticised Deputy Chief Minister Manish Sisodia for 'holidaying' in Helsinki, Finland. He should instead be taking charge in Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal's absence, BJP added.
