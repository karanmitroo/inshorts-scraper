In a major reshuffle in the Shiv Sena, key veteran leaders have been dropped from the party. They include Murli Manohar Joshi who questioned Uddhav Thackeray's leadership style, Saamna editor Sanjay Raut for "speaking out of turn", and Thackeray's close aid Subhash Desai for his misplaced advice on Sena-BJP relations.
