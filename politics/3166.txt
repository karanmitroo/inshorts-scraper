Polling for Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) election is going on in Maharashtra on Tuesday as 2,275 candidates are contesting the polls for its 227 seats. Considered as the biggest municipal corporation in the country, BMC is witnessing a multi-cornered fight between BJP, Shiv Sena, NCP, Congress, MNS, Samajwadi Party, BSP and others. The results will be declared on February 23.
