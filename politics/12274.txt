In a public meeting at New Delhi's Jantar Mantar, the Aam Aadmi Party on Wednesday hinted that one of its leaders, Kumar Vishwas, would be contesting against Rahul Gandhi from Amethi in the upcoming Lok Sabha elections. Moreover, AAP convener Arvind Kejriwal  appealed to "honest" people from all spheres and parties, including Congress and BJP, to join the party.
