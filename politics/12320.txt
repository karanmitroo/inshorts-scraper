Mafia turned politician, Atiq Ahmed, who has 73 criminal cases registered against him, has been declared as Samajwadi Party's candidate from Sultanpur (UP) parliamentary constituency. Ahmed is an accused in the murder of BSP MLA Raju Pal and in the infamous state guesthouse incident. Notably, he was elected to the Lok Sabha in 2004 from Allahabad's Phulpur as SP candidate.
