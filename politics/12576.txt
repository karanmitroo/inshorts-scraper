After 16 months in Jail, over corruption charges, YSR Congess leader Jagan Mohan Ready was granted bail on condition that he wouldn't leave Hyderabad, would respond to court summons and pay a surety of Rs. 2-lakh. He is facing 10 charges including 'accumulating assets beyond his known sources of income', for which he was arrested on May-27, 2012 by CBI.
