Hitting back at Narendra Modi for his 'AK-49' jibe at him, Arvind Kejriwal Wednesday said, "He should talk on issues and not get into bad mouthing. Such words don’t suit a man who is a PM candidate… He refuses to respond on issue of giving tickets to corrupts like Yeddyurappa.” Interestingly, AAP received Rs.1 crore as donations the same day.
