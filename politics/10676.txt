Based on a woman's complaint, the Borivali police (Mumbai) has booked a case against NCP leader and former minister of state Laxmanrao Dhobale. "The woman alleged that Dhobale had some obscene pictures and raped her between 2011 and January 2013 by threatening her," said DCP Balsingh Rajput. Dhobale has been booked under section 376 (punishment for rape) of the IPC.
