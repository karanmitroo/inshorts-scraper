Demanding revocation of ban on Kambala buffalo race, Karnataka MLA Abhaya Chandra Jain said, "It's my duty to support kambala. For the sake of public, I'm ready to go to jail." He further said, "Kambala is our tulunadu culture, if Centre can give opportunity to Jallikattu, this is a question of our tulunadu and we are very particular about Kambala."
