Reiterating that the BJP is the world's largest political party, party president Amit Shah announced, Thursday, that it has crossed the 10-crore-members mark. He also said that the "aggressive" membership drive, that brought in 10 crore and 43 lakh people till April-30 will be stopped now, to make way for the "Maha-Sampark Abhiyan" to establish person-to-person contact with new members.
