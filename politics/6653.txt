In the ongoing counting of votes for the Assembly Elections in Puducherry, the Congress-DMK alliance is leading in 13 of the 30 seats. Chief Minister N Rangaswamy-led ruling All India NR Congress (AINRC) was ahead in 12 seats and AIDMK in 2 seats, so far. Former CM and Congress leader V Vaithilingam has won by 5,106 votes in Kamaraj Nagar.
