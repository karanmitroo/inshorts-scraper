Shiv Sena has disclosed to the Election Commission of India that the home appliances maker, Videocon Industries contributed ₹85 crore (nearly 98%) of the total ₹86.84 crore it received in 2015-16, according to reports. While the BJP has not declared its list of donors yet, Videocon reportedly donated ₹25 lakh to Sharad Pawar's NCP besides Shiv Sena.
