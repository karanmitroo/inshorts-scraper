Speaking in the Tamil Nadu Assembly, AIADMK MLA Mariappan Kennady listed the ten avatars of Vishnu and said that the eleventh avatar of the Lord was Late Chief Minister J Jayalalithaa. Terming Sasikala as the perfect replacement for Jayalalithaa, Kennady added, "Every day, I hear Jayalalithaa's spirit telling me, 'I've given you the responsibility of 1.5 crore children'."
