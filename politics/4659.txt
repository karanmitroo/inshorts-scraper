During a debate in the Upper House of the Parliament, Samajwadi Party MP Naresh Agarwal used the word “Bhaandgiri” while replying to the BJP. First, BJP Minister Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi took a jibe at Agarwal, stating whenever the SP took a decision it was equivalent to a hit wicket. Responding to it, Agarwal said BJP is resorting to “Bhaandgiri”.
