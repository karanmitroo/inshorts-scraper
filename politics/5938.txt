On the occasion of India’s 70th Independence Day, Baloch Republican Party (BRP) leader Ashraf Sherjan on Monday raised ‘Jai Hind’ slogan and thanked PM Narendra Modi for highlighting Balochistan issue. “Inshallah soon we will celebrate India's and Balochistan's independence day together”, he said. PM Modi had earlier said Pakistan will have to answer for "atrocities" committed on Baloch people.
