Bahujan Samaj Party's Aligarh unit chief Shankar Lal Pippal has been arrested for allegedly posting an objectionable picture of Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath on Facebook. Another person named Javed Alam was also arrested along with Pippal on Tuesday night after senior Bharatiya Janata Party leaders filed a police complaint over the matter.
