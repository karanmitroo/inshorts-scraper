People's Democratic Party President Mehbooba Mufti on Sunday said that her late father, Mufti Mohammad Sayeed, “never believed in U-Turn politics”. Addressing around 2,000 party workers, she added, "[P]rosperity of J&K and country will be our priority". "Mufti sahab believed that a leader like Modiji... take decisions which can bring India and Pakistan close", she added.
