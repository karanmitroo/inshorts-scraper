Cricketer-turned-politician Navjot Singh Sidhu's wife, Navjot Kaur's resignation from BJP was accepted by the  party's Punjab unit on Saturday, a press release sent by the party said. This comes after Navjot Singh Sidhu announced a new political front 'Awaaz-e-Punjab' and resigned from BJP. Kaur, who is the MLA from Amritsar East, had earlier submitted a one-line resignation to the party.
