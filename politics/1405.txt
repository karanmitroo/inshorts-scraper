The convoy of Congress MP from Supaul, Ranjeet Ranjan, the wife of Pappu Yadav, crushed three people to death at the Nirmali-Sikarhata Main Road in Bihar on Monday. As per reports, Nirmali block chief Ramprasesh Yadav, who was also present at the site, took the injured to a hospital. At least two injured are said to be in critical condition.
