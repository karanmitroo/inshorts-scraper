The Election Commission has till Sunday seized illegal cash worth over ₹62 crore from the five poll-bound states. Tamil Nadu leads the list with the maximum seizures of ₹24.90 crores, followed by ₹12.84 crore and ₹12.33 crore in West Bengal and Assam respectively. While polling in Assam has concluded, two of six phases of voting have culminated in West Bengal.
