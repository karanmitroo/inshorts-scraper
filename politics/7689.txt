Congress leader Anand Sharma has demanded immediate resignation of Gujarat CM Anandiben Patel alleging that the government had allotted 250 acres of government land to a company associated with Patel's daughter. Sharma alleged that the land was allotted in 2010 by the revenue department for ₹60000/acre whereas the notified price was ₹50 lakh/acre. Anandiben was the state's then Revenue Minister.
