To mark the completion of one year of Modi government, the BJP will hold 200 rallies and 5,000 “jan sabhas” across India. PM Narendra Modi will address the first rally at ideologue Deen Dayal Upadhyay’s native-village in Mathura on Monday. A week-long celebration “Jan Kalyan Parv” will be aimed to apprise people about the one year's achievements of the government.
