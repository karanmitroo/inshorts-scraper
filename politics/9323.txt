RJD chief Lalu Prasad Yadav Thursday expelled MP Pappu Yadav from the party for six years for 'anti-party activities'. Yadav, whose real name is Rajesh Ranjan, had spoken up against Lalu's announcement to join a new alliance of six parties, all members of the former Janata Parivar. Yadav had refused to work under Bihar CM and JDU head Nitish Kumar.
