Social activist Anna Hazare met Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal and Deputy CM Manish Sisodia in Delhi on Sunday. Raising the issue of Yogendra Yadav and Prashant Bhushan's expulsion with Kejriwal, Anna suggested that there should be reconciliation as the two are "old colleagues". In the meeting, Kejriwal also briefed Anna about the work done by his government in Delhi.
