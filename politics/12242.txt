AAP chief and Delhi's CM designate Arvind Kejriwal on Tuesday sent the list of party MLAs, to be sworn in as ministers, to the Lt. Governor Najeeb Jung. This list includes: well-known activist Manish Sisodia, IIT graduate and lawyer Somnath Bharti, former architect Satyendra Kumar Jain, former journalist Rakhi Birla, engineer turned lawyer Saurabh Bhardwaj and activist Girish Soni.
