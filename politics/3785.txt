Navjot Singh Sidhu, who recently joined Congress and is contesting Punjab Assembly polls, declared his total assets worth ₹45.91 crore. His wealth includes watches worth ₹44 lakh, residential plots worth ₹30 crore, two land cruisers, and a mini cooper. The former Lok Sabha MP also declared his annual income to the tune of ₹9.66 crore for the financial year 2015-16.
