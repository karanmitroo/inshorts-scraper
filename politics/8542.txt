Rashtriya Janata Dal chief Lalu Prasad Yadav's sons, Tejaswi Yadav and Tej Pratap Yadav, have filed their nomination papers from Bihar's Raghopur and Mahua constituencies, respectively. As per the affidavits, younger son (Tejaswi) is 26 years old and elder son (Tej) is 25. Notably, 9th pass Tejaswi holds movable assets worth ₹1.40 crore while 12th pass Tej owns ₹1.12 crore.
