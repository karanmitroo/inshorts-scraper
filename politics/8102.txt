The Bihar Assembly on Wednesday unanimously elected senior JD(U) legislator Vijay Chaudhary as the 16th Bihar Assembly Speaker. Members of all the parties, including the BJP, Hindustani Awam Morcha (HAM) and Lok Janshakti Party (LJP) seconded the proposal to name Chaudhary the Speaker. Notably, Chaudhary was a member of the Congress before he left to join JD(U).
