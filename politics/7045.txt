Congress MP S Jaipal Reddy on Tuesday described HRD Minister Smriti Irani as 'Manu Smriti Irani' in an attack on her stand over the suicide of Dalit research scholar Rohith Vemula. "She has Manu in her heart," he said. Manu is believed to have proposed the four-rung caste system in the ancient legal text 'Manu Smriti.'
