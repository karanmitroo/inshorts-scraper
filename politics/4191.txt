Samajwadi Party's National Executive has passed a resolution demanding the immediate removal of Shivpal Yadav as the party's state president, SP leader Ram Gopal Yadav said on Sunday. "Shivpal Yadav has been taking wrong decisions in the name of Netaji (Mulayam Singh Yadav)," he added. Notably, Shivpal Yadav had been made the party's state chief by replacing Akhilesh Yadav.
