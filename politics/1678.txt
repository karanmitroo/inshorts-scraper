A Mizoram Congress MLA showed an increase of 2,406% in his assets after being the MLA for five years. In 2008, Lalrobiaka had shown his movable and immovable assets at ₹5.81 lakh, while in 2013, he showed his assets at ₹1.4 crore. The legislator called it a 'misunderstanding', saying he didn't include details of his ₹1 crore residence in 2008.
