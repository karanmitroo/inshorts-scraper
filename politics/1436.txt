A case has been registered against former Deputy Chief Minister of Bihar, Tejashwi Yadav in Darbhanga on Thursday for allegedly disrespecting ‘Vande Mataram’. The complaint has been filed against his recent comment on Gorakhpur tragedy, over which, he tweeted, "Sahi kaha inka ‘Vande Mataram’ = Bande maarte hai hum (You are right. Their ‘Vande Mataram’ means killing of people)."
