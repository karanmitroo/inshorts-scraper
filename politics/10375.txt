Pointing out the instability of the Fadnavis govt in Maharashtra, Shiv Sena supremo Uddhav Thackeray said that they have decided to sit in Opposition for now. He was echoing doubts raised earlier by NCP leader Sharad Pawar, over the longevity of the current government. Thackeray said that the people were "upset" about the "dalliances" between former foes NCP and BJP.
