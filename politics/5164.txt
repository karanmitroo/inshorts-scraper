Samajwadi party UP chief Shivpal Yadav on Wednesday started vacating his official residence in Lucknow, three days after he was sacked from the Cabinet by UP CM Akhilesh Yadav, reports said. Earlier in the morning, Shivpal's ministerial nameplate was removed from his official residence by his staff. Shivpal had also surrendered his official vehicle to 'Rajya Sampatti Vibhag', reports said.
