Samajwadi Party's Alapur candidate, Chandrashekhar Kanaujia, died following a heart attack during an election campaign event on Sunday in Ambedkar Nagar. He was rushed to a nearby hospital where he was declared brought dead. Notably, the Samajwadi Party had given Kanaujia the ticket against its current legislator Bhim Prasad Sonkar. The constituency will go for polling on February 27.
