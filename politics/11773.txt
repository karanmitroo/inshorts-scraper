The Delhi government had spent Rs 13.41 lakh on the oath-taking ceremony of Arvind Kejriwal as Delhi CM at Ramlila Maidan, an RTI query has revealed. The government spent Rs 6.33 lakh on the Maidan ceremony and Rs 6.7 lakh on its publicity. The figure, however, does not include the expenses incurred on water, fire, power, transportation and decoration services.
