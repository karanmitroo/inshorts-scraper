Newly elected Assam MLAs used a total of seven languages for taking oaths in the 14th state assembly session on Wednesday. While Chief Minister Sarbananda Sonowal and former chief minister Tarun Gogoi took their oaths in Assamese, three BJP MLAs, including Assamese actor Angoorlata Deka, took their oaths in Sanskrit. Other languages used included Bengali, Bodo, English, Hindi, and Nepali.
