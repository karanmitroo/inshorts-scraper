Rashtriya Janata Dal (RJD) chief Lalu Prasad Yadav said on Sunday that the process of merger of six Janata Parivar parties, including RJD, Samajwadi Party (SP) and Janata Dal (United), had been completed. However, the formal announcement will be made by SP chief Mulayam Singh Yadav. Lalu also coined the slogan “Ek jhanda, ek nishan maang raha hai Hindustan.”
