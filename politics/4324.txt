Bollywood music composer duo Sajid Ali and Wajid Ali on Sunday joined BJP in the presence of Maharashtra CM Devendra Fadnavis in Mumbai. "There is only one promise we can make ourselves on Atalji's birthday, to make India corruption-free," Fadnavis said. Notably, Sajid-Wajid have composed music for films like 'Pyar Kiya Toh Darna Kya', 'Dabangg' and 'Partner' among others.
