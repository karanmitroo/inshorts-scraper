Suspended AAP leader Kapil Mishra has tweeted photographs of posters terming Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal as the 'messiah of Muslims'. Mishra said the posters were seen in Delhi's Khajuri area, where a party rally is scheduled to take place on Sunday. Earlier, Mishra had tweeted a video of a man slamming Kejriwal for politicising Muslim issues to get votes.
