The BJP, on Monday issued a show cause notice to its MLA Sher Singh Dagar, for allegedly bribing an AAP MLA in Delhi. In a video released by AAP, it alleges that, Dagar had offered around Rs. 4 crore to its MLA Dinesh Mohania. BJP's Delhi chief Satish Upadhyay said that, Dagar has to give clarification on the matter.
