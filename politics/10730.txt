Following the resignation of BV Wanchoo, renowned hindi writer and senior BJP leader Mridula Sinha has been sworn in as the governor of Goa. The oath of office was administered by Bombay high court chief justice Mohit Shah, at a ceremony graced by Goa CM Manohar Parrikar, in the Raj Bhawan. Sinha is the first woman full-time governor of Goa.
