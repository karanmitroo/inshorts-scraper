Claiming that the demonetisation plan was a failure, BJP leader Shatrughan Sinha tweeted, "Whom to blame? Nehru, Congress, Khilji or our one man show two man army?" "Terrorism has peaked...SBI posted a loss for the first time in 19 years, most PSU banks in the red, unemployment all around," he added countering reasons cited by PM Narendra Modi for demonetisation.
