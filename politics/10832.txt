In a jolt to DMK, its Organizing Secretary PV Kalyanasundaram has resigned from the party demanding MK Stalin be declared as the party’s Chief Ministerial candidate for 2016 assembly elections. Kalayanasundaram, a stalin loyalist, also demanded that Rajya Sabha MP Kanimozhi and former union misiters A Raja and Dayanidhi Maran be removed from the party to restore party’s image.
