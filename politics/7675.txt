Commenting on David Headley's revelation that the Lashkar-e-Taiba was targeting Late Bal Thackeray and the Sena's headquarters, Shiv Sena leader Sanjay Raut today said that it is like the party received the Mahavir Chakra and Paramvir Chakra. Raut also added that Thackeray has fought many "battles" with Pakistan and it is not surprising that terrorists would target him.
