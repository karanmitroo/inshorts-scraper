At SP's 25th-year celebrations on Saturday, Uttar Pradesh CM Akhilesh Yadav said that the BJP government is dividing people and SP's aim is to defeat it in the upcoming Assembly elections. "Next year's elections will decide the fate of the country," he added. "I congratulate Netaji (Mulayam Singh Yadav) for raising this party (SP) and making it sturdy," Akhilesh said.
