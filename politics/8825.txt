While defending herself in the Lalit Modi row, External Affairs Minister Sushma Swaraj on Wednesday alleged that Congress helped Bofors accused Ottavio Quattrocchi and Bhopal gas leak accused Warren Anderson flee from India. Mocking Congress VP Rahul Gandhi, Swaraj told him to read his family history on next 'holiday' and ask 'momma' how much money had been taken from Quattrocchi.
