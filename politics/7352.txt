A high-level probe panel of JNU has found two students – Umar Khalid and Anirban Bhattacharya – "guilty" of arousing communal, caste or regional feelings or creating disharmony among students. Anirban and Umar, currently in judicial custody in a sedition case, are among 21 students issued show-cause notices by the five-member committee for violating university norms and disciplinary rules.
