Shortly after assuming charge of office, CM Arvind Kejriwal Saturday announced portfolios of his ministers. While Kejriwal got power and finance departments, Manish Sisodia will handle education and PWD portfolios. Rakhi Birla got women and child welfare department. Law and tourism went to Somnath Bharti, development and employment to Girish Soni, health to Satyendra Jain and Saurabh Bhardwaj got transport.
