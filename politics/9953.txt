In surprise comments, three-time BJP MP Kirti Azad Saturday praised TMC chief Mamata Banerjee and said he did not believe "in the wildest of dreams" that she is dishonest. "I've known Mamata Didi for more than two decades. She is my Didi and I am her chhota bhai", Azad said. The BJP called called Azad's comments "his personal view".
