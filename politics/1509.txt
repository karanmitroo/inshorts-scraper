Rashtriya Janata Dal (RJD) chief Lalu Yadav on Tuesday accused the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) of trying to take the life of Congress Vice President Rahul Gandhi. Lalu's comment comes after the Lok Sabha witnessed an uproar on Tuesday over the recent attack on Rahul Gandhi's convoy in Gujarat.
