According to an India Today Group-Cicero Post Poll survey, the Narendra Modi-led BJP and its allies are expected to win 41-49 seats of the 81-member state Assembly in Jharkhand. Hemant Soren-led Jharkhand Mukti Morcha (JMM) may bag 15-19 seats. The Congress and its allies could get just 7-11 seats, while other parties, including independents, are projected to win 8-12 seats.
