A JD(U) MLA named Manjit Kumar Singh from Gopalganj (Bihar) on Tuesday lodged an FIR in Patna demanding the arrest of Haryana BJP leader OP Dhankar for his controversial 'Bihari bride for Haryana boys' remark. The FIR was registered under sec-153A, 153B and 294. Dhankhar had reportedly made the controversial remark while addressing a meeting of party workers in Haryana.
