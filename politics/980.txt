Former Trinamool Congress (TMC) leader Mukul Roy was inducted into the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) at its Delhi headquarters on Friday. Roy had resigned from his Rajya Sabha seat and quit the West Bengal CM Mamata Banerjee-led party last month. Roy, who was a founding member of TMC cited "lack of principle" as the reason for quitting the party.
