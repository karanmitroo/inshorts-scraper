RJD chief Lalu Prasad Yadav's party, a major partner in JD(U)-led grand alliance that won a clear majority in the Bihar polls, became the single largest party. RJD won 80 seats, surpassing JDU which won 71 and Congress which won 27 seats. Lalu's sons Tej Pratap and Tejaswi won their seats and will make their maiden entry into Bihar Assembly.
