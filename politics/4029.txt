AIADMK General Secretary Sasikala Natarajan on Saturday named a baby girl of a party functionary as 'Jayalalithaa'. She kissed the girl gently on the forehead before blessing her in ex-Tamil Nadu CM J Jayalalithaa's style, said the party. While taking over the party post, Natarajan had said, "for Amma party was the life and for me, Amma is my life."
