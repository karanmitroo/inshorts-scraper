A Congress leader from Uttar Pradesh's Gorakhpur has initiated various campaigns, based on party President Rahul Gandhi, to promote 'Brand Rahul' since 2014. Anwar Hussain initiated 'Rahul Milk' campaign when BJP launched 'chai pe charcha' during the 2014 Lok Sabha elections for its then PM candidate Narendra Modi. He also served 'Rahul herbal tea' outside banks after the 2016 demonetisation.
