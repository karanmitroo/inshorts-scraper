Congress leader Jyotiraditya Scindia slammed Prime Minister Narendra Modi in the Lok Sabha for the demonetisation move saying that the decision was based on an advertisement whose slogan was 'aaj kuch toofani karte hain'. Meanwhile, Congress leader Mallikarjun Kharge also said that the government should bring back the smiles of millions of people who have been crying due to demonetisation.
