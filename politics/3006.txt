AIADMK leader PH Pandian has claimed that ex-Tamil Nadu CM J Jayalalithaa was pushed down by "someone" at her residence before she was admitted to hospital on September 22, 2016. "Nobody knows what happened...after that. A police official called an ambulance, and she was taken to hospital," Pandian said. Jayalalithaa remained in hospital until her death on December 5, 2016.
