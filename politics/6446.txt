After President Pranab Mukherjee withheld assent to the Delhi government's 'office of profit' bill, CM Arvind Kejriwal on Tuesday accused PM Narendra Modi of "not allowing" the state government to function. The bill seeks retrospective exemption for 21 MLAs who were serving as parliamentary secretaries in the Delhi government. Defending the bill, Kejriwal reasoned the MLAs were working for free.
