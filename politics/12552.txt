AAP chief Arvind Kejriwal has accused Delhi police of targeting his party, saying they have registered cases against two of the party's candidates for putting up banners. Gulab Singh Yadav(Matiala candidate) and Mukesh Kumar Dagar(Najafgarh candidate) are facing charges under Defacement of Property Act, 2007. Police denies reports saying 14 out of 18 FIRs are against other parties as well.
