AAP leader Somnath Bharti has indicated that the Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) might contest in the upcoming Assembly election in Tamil Nadu. AAP will be contesting as part of the People's Welfare Front that includes Marumalarchi Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (MDMK), CPI and CPI(M). Bharti is likely to meet Vaiko, General Secretary of MDMK today for discussion.
