A Telugu political drama on Telangana CM Kalvakuntla Chandrashekar Rao (KCR) is being made by filmmaker Madhura Sreedhar Reddy. The film's shooting will reportedly begin on June 2, 2017, which marks three years of Telangana's formation, while its release is scheduled for KCR's birthday on February 17. "[C]hallenges KCR faced were on par with what Mahatma Gandhi... faced," said Reddy.
