The Congress on Wednesday called the renaming of Gurgaon as 'Gurugram' and Mewat as 'Nuh', "mere superficialities". It advised Haryana Chief Minister Manohar Lal Khattar to create an atmosphere of investment, development, peace and harmony instead of following a vitriolic policy of divide and rule. The Haryana government had on Tuesday decided to change the name of Gurgaon to 'Gurugram'.
