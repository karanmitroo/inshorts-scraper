Newly appointed Samajwadi Party's Uttar Pradesh chief Shivpal Yadav resigned from all party and state Cabinet posts on Thursday. However, Shivpal's resignation from the Cabinet has reportedly been rejected by his nephew, Uttar Pradesh CM Akhilesh Yadav. Further, Shivpal's son Aditya Yadav has also resigned from Uttar Pradesh Cooperative Federation Chairman's post.
