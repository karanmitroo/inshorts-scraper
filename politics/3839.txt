Congress leader Ghulam Nabi Azad on Tuesday announced there will be a Congress and Samajwadi Party alliance in the upcoming Uttar Pradesh Assembly elections. "Details about it will be decided in coming days, for now, can say there will be Congress-SP alliance under the leadership of Akhilesh Yadav," Azad said. "We will think about 'maha-gathbandhan' in coming days," he added.
