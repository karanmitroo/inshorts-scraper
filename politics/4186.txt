Samajwadi Party's National Executive has unanimously elected Uttar Pradesh CM Akhilesh Yadav as the party's National President, SP's Ram Gopal Yadav said on Sunday. It also passed a resolution to appoint current SP chief Mulayam Singh Yadav as the party's 'Margdarshak', he added. The National Executive also expelled SP General Secretary Amar Singh from the party, Yadav added.
