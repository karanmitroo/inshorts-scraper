Tamil Nadu Chief Minister Edappadi K Palaniswami on Saturday slammed former state CM O Panneerselvam, saying that he worked against late J Jayalalithaa's government. After winning the vote of confidence with 122 MLAs supporting him, Palaniswami said, "This day brought forward real Amma supporters." Ahead of the floor test, Panneerselvam had appealed to AIADMK MLAs to vote against Palaniswami.
