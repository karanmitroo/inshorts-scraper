Stating that AAP wants to keep the corrupts out from the parliament, Arvind Kejriwal on Friday released the list, "India's most corrupt leaders." The notable names in the list are Rahul Gandhi, Sushilkumar Shinde, P Chidambaram, Nitin Gadkari, BS Yeddyurappa, Ananth Kumar, Mayawati, Mulayam Singh Yadav and Sharad Pawar.
