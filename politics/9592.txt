BJP president Amit Shah's national executive committee, constituted recently, has seen a drop of some top ministers, including Union Human Resource Department (HRD) minister Smriti Irani. The committee has many ministers like Rajnath Singh, Arun Jaitley, Sushma Swaraj, and other ministers like Piyush Goyal, Dharmendra Pradhan  and Nirmala Sitharaman.
