After Prime Minister Narendra Modi replaced Mahatma Gandhi from Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) calendar, the Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) on Friday said it won't be surprised if the same happened with currency notes. AAP leader Dilip Pandey said, "The whole thing actually...brings down PM's stature. It does not behoove his post. He has reduced himself to a megalomaniac."
