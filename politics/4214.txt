Arunachal Pradesh CM Pema Khandu along with 32 People's Party of Arunachal (PPA) legislators on Saturday joined the BJP. "Arunachal has a BJP government now. BJP government has 45 BJP and two independent MLAs' support in a 60-member Arunachal Pradesh Assembly," BJP General Secretary Ram Madhav stated. "PPA is left with only 10 MLAs in the 60-member House," he tweeted.
