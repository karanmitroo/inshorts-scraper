Andhra Pradesh CM Nara Chandrababu Naidu's 18-month-old grandson is richer than him, according to income declarations made by the CM's son Lokesh. Naidu's grandson Devansh holds properties worth ₹14.5 crore, apart from fixed deposits of ₹2.4 crore and a cash balance of ₹2.31 crore, while the CM's net holdings are less than ₹8 crore.
