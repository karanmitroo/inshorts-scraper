Slamming the union government's new regime of Goods and Services Tax, sidelined BJP leader Shatrughan Sinha on Friday said, "People in Bihar ask each other in Bhojpuri the meaning of GST. The reply is 'Gail Sarkar Tohar (your government is gone)'." He further raised concerns over the policy of demonetisation, claiming it caused panic among the poor and businessmen.
