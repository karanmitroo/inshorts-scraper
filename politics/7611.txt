Kailash Chaudhary, a BJP MLA from Rajasthan has said that Congress Vice President Rahul Gandhi should be hanged or shot. He called Gandhi a 'traitor' for standing with “anti-national” students at Delhi’s Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU). Chaudhary further demanded an apology from Gandhi for the same along with seeking his resignation. The state Congress later asked for Chaudhary's suspension.
