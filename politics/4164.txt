Addressing a rally ahead of Mumbai civic body (BMC) polls, AIMIM chief Asaduddin Owaisi on Sunday said that many Muslims were part of Maratha warrior king Shivaji's army, adding, "Shivaji Maharaj was pro-poor". On the ₹3,600 crore Shivaji memorial, he said, "We are not against the memorial…but what about stopping troublesome floods in the city during the monsoon."
