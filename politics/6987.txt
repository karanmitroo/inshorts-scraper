A student was on Thursday expelled from college, a day after being arrested for hacking RJD Chief Lalu Prasad Yadav's Facebook account, officials said. "We have expelled Divyanshu, a third-year student, from the college," said Santlal, director of the Patna Sahib Engineering College. Divyanshu Kumar alias Golu, was arrested from his village Mehmadabad in Vaishali district by the Bihar Police.
