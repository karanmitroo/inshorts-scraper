While there were rumours that Narendra Modi's wife, Jashodaben, had left for Char Dham Yatra, a news magazine has claimed that she has been whisked away and kept at yoga guru Ramdev's ashram in Rishikesh, Uttarakhand. Jashodaben, who will stay there until the elections are over, is protected by top-level security, having ties with Gujarat security services, the report added.
