All India Majlis-E-Ittehadul Muslimeen (AIMIM) chief Asaduddin Owaisi on Wednesday said that India cannot be governed on the basis of "Hindi, Hindu, and Hindustan". Citing a news report about a scuffle breaking out between BJP and BSP members because a corporator took oath in Urdu, Owaisi said he had taken oath in Urdu himself three times as an MP.
