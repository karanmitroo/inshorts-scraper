Senior Samajwadi Party leader Beni Prasad Verma on Saturday said party chief Mulayam Singh Yadav is very unhappy and upset with Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Akhilesh Yadav. He added that Mulayam was hurt that his son was silent after suspended MLC Udayveer Singh said in a letter to the party chief that Akhilesh's stepmother was using black magic on him.
