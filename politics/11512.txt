The Congress has targeted BJP’s PM candidate Narendra Modi by bringing forward Atal Bihari Vajpayee’s remark on ‘Rajdharma’ after the communal riots in Gujarat in 2002. Underlining that Modi failed to perform his 'Rajdharma' as the CM, a Congress website article said that Vajpayee believed that Modi had been inept and downright partisan in handling of the riots.
