A Hindustan Times-C fore survey, done between Jan 10 and 19, predicts a hung Delhi assembly after a year of president's rule. The BJP and AAP can get 31-36 seats each, while the Congress can bag 2-7 seats. AAP's Kejriwal is predicted the most preferred CM candidate (43% votes), followed by BJP's Kiran Bedi (39%) and Congress' Ajay Maken (12%).
