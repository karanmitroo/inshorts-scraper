In less than three months, over 2,000 shakhas of RSS, the Hindu nationalist group which started shrinking during the UPA tenure, have sprouted across India. By 2013-end, there were 44,982 shakhas, of which 8,417 were in UP alone. The numbers had peaked in 2004, when there were 51,000 functioning shakhas. They shrunk during UPA tenure, hitting a low of 39,283.
