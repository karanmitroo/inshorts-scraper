BJP MP Meenakshi Lekhi said that for the Congress OROP has been “One Rahul, One Priyanka”. Reacting to the suicide committed by the ex-Army jawan, she said, “When the ex-soldier was getting ₹4000 pension, he didn’t commit suicide. But he killed himself when he was getting ₹24,000 pension.” Several mysterious things need to be looked into, she added.
