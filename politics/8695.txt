
After 14 years, the Akhil Bhartiya Vidyarthi Parishad (ABVP) won a seat in the student union elections at Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), Delhi. With 1154 votes, ABVP's Saurabh Sharma bagged the post of the Joint Secretary, defeating the All India Students' Association's (AISA) candidate Hamid Raza, who got 1126 votes. The AISA won two seats out of four this year.
