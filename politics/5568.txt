Reacting to the reports that Jaish-e-Mohammed was behind the Army base attack in Jammu and Kashmir, Congress leader Digvijaya Singh on Monday said that India compromised by releasing JeM leader Masood Azhar following the 1999 Indian Airlines hijack. “Lesson ? Never compromise with National Security,” he tweeted. Notably, Vajpayee-led NDA government had released Azhar in exchange for passengers.
