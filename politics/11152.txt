Riding on the Telangana sentiment, the Telangana Rashtra Samiti on Friday won a majority in the new state by bagging 63 of the 119 Assembly and 11 of the 17 Lok Sabha seats. The tally of Congress in the Assembly was 21 and two in Lok Sabha. The TDP-BJP combine bagged 20 (15+5) Assembly seats and two Lok Sabha seats.
