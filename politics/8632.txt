Anil Shastri, son of former PM Lal Bahadur Shastri, has demanded declassification of files related to his father. Anil, who is also a Congress leader, added that the truth about Shastriji's death can be revealed after the declassification of files. Notably, it has been said that Shastriji died of heart attack in Tashkent in 1966, however, many suspect foul play.
