Hours after VK Sasikala was convicted by the Supreme Court in the Disproportionate Assets case, Tamil Nadu Interim CM O Panneerselvam urged the AIADMK MLAs to "sink the differences" and unite to continue "Amma’s rule". "Without any other party's support our own government will continue," he added. Meanwhile, Sasikala’s alleged loyalist Edappadi K Palanisamy has been elected as AIADMK chief.
