An exit polling, conducted by Axis-My-India after Tuesday's BMC election, has predicted that both Shiv Sena and BJP will have a vote share of 32% each. The exit poll further gave 86 to 92 seats to Shiv Sena and 80 to 88 seats to BJP. The Congress, MNS and NCP were given 30-34 seats, 5-7 seats and 3-6 seats respectively.
