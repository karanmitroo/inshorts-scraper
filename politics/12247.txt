The anti-corruption crusader, Arvind Kejriwal was chosen as the CNN-IBN Indian of the Year, 2013 (Politics) on Friday. The Aam Aadmi Party's spectacular performance in its debut election in Delhi has got him the honour. Notably, the former IT officer had defeated Delhi's ex-CM Sheila Dikshit from her constituency New Delhi by a huge margin of over 26,000 votes.
