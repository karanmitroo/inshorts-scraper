Incumbent Nagaland CM TR Zeliang from Naga People's Front (NPF) has won from his constituency Peren in the Nagaland Assembly elections. He defeated Nationalist Democratic Progressive Party's Iherie Ndang with a margin of 5,432 votes. As per current trends, NPF is leading in 23 constituencies in the 60-member assembly, while BJP-NDPP alliance is trailing with a total of 26 leads.
