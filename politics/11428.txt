The TDP-BJP alliance in Andhra Pradesh is on the verge of collapse, with TDP boss Chandrababu Naidu seeking the return of four assembly seats allotted to the right-wing party in Seemandhra. The TDP chief is reportedly miffed by BJP's move to nominate his estranged sister-in-law Purandeswari from Rajampet, as Naidu did not want her to be given any ticket.
