An FIR has been lodged by Congress leader Omkar Singh against unknown persons in Badaun (UP), for distributing books allegedly containing objectionable material against the Gandhi family. The book titled 'Rahul ki Ravan Leela', allegedly contains offensive material against Congress chief Sonia Gandhi, her son Rahul Gandhi, daughter Priyanka Gandhi Vadra and son-in-law Robert Vadra, police said.
