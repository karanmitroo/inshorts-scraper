Uttar Pradesh Minister and Samajwadi Party leader Azam Khan has criticised Prime Minister Narendra Modi for raising 'Jai Shree Ram' slogan at the Dussehra function. Khan said that the PM belonged to the nation and should have raised slogans of all religions. Further, he also opposed the Bharatiya Janata Party's move to build a Ramayana museum in Ayodhya.
