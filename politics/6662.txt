Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK) Spokesperson TKS Elangovan and Organising Secretary Alandur RS Barathi were on Saturday nominated by party Chief M Karunanidhi for the Rajya Sabha elections to fill six vacancies from Tamil Nadu. AIADMK is expected to name four nominees for the June 11 elections. The tenure of six RS members from Tamil Nadu will end on June 29.
