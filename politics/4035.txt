After Congress deployed poll strategist Prashant Kishor in Uttarakhand ahead of Assembly polls, CM Harish Rawat compared him to 'Chyawanprash', stating that people resort to such 'tonics' as they age. Furthermore, state Congress chief Kishore Upadhyay said that the strategy of the party will now have an edge and added that Kishor is providing his services to the party.
