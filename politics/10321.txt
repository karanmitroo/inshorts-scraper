Samajwadi Party chief Mulayam Singh Yadav and RJD chief Lalu Prasad Yadav are set to turn their political friendship into relationship. Lalu and Mulayam are all set to forge a new alliance through matrimony. Mulayam Singh Yadav’s grand-nephew Tej Pratap Yadav, the newly elected SP MP from Mainpuri, will be tying the knot with Lalu Yadav’s youngest daughter Raj Lakshmi.
