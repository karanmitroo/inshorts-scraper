A day after terming the Jan Lokpal draft as 'mahajokepal', Former Aam Aadmi Party member Prashant Bhushan on Sunday challenged Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal to a public debate on the Bill. Bhushan also warned of an agitation backed by the Swaraj Abhiyan outside the Delhi Assembly on Monday, where the proposed legislation is set to be tabled.
