Lok Sabha polls 2014 has split the Hindi film industry into for and against Narendra Modi. In an open letter signed by sixty film personalities including, Imtiaz Ali, Vishal Bhardwaj and Mahesh Bhatt, have appealed the nation to protect the secular foundation of the country. Whereas, Bollywood personalities like Lata Mangeshkar and Anupam Kher have voiced their support to Modi.
