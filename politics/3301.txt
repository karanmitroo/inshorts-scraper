Following AIADMK General Secretary VK Sasikala's conviction in the disproportionate assets case, Tamil Nadu Chief Minister O Panneerselvam was removed from the primary membership of the party. In response, Panneerselvam asserted, "We will take Amma's ideals forward without any other party's help," Notably, Sasikala had earlier removed Panneerselvam from the post of the party's Treasurer.
