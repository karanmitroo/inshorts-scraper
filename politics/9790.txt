Bollywood celebrities Tuesday congratulated AAP for its historic victory in Delhi polls. "Young Indian voters has sent a clear message to the Nation. Mess with us and you will not survive...", tweeted Shekhar Kapur. Actor-director Farhan Akhtar tweeted, "Congratulations to Arvind Kejriwal and his team". Celebrities like Vishal Dadlani, Dia Mirza and Pritish Nandy also tweeted to congratulate AAP.
