AAP MLA from Okhla Amanatullah Khan, who is facing sexual harassment allegations by his sister-in-law, was arrested by the Delhi Police on Wednesday. “Came here at @DCP OFFICE sarita Vihar for general discussion. but they arrested me (sic),” he tweeted. Khan had, earlier, gone to Jamia Nagar police station to surrender but the police had refused to apprehend him.
