Congress Vice President Rahul Gandhi on Wednesday said that the tyre of Samajwadi Party's cycle was already punctured but Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Akhilesh Yadav has now thrown its wheel out. Gandhi's statement came after Akhilesh removed SP’s new UP chief Shivpal Yadav from key ministerial posts on Tuesday. Notably, 'cycle' is the election symbol of Samajwadi Party.
