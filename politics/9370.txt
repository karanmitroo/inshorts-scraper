All India Majlis-e-Ittehadul Muslimeen chief Asaduddin Owaisi said on Monday that 26/11 mastermind Hafiz Mohammad Saeed was unaware about the teachings of Islam. "People like Hafiz Saeed are unaware about teachings of Islam, jihad in Islam. They are killing innocent lives in Pakistan, children are being killed... The Government of India should take strict action against it," Owaisi said.
