Arvind Kejriwal led Aam Aadmi Party(AAP), which aims to bring honest and transparent politics in India, is receiving Rs.6-7 lakh every day from common people including soldiers from Siachen, NRIs, students, pensioners, and even rickshaw pullers. AAP has received Rs.11.5-crore from 45,000 donors, whose name has been disclosed online. Recently, Rajya Sabha member Rajeev Chandrasekhar donated Rs.5-lakh to the party.
