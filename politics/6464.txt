Union Minister Rajiv Pratap Rudy has alleged that some files related to the fodder scam were reported misplaced to get RJD chief Lalu Prasad a clean-chit. "Mr. Lalu Prasad Yadav and (Bihar CM) Nitish Kumar are friends and they are partners in crime," he said. Fodder scam refers to irregularities worth ₹1,000 crore,​ reported when Lalu was Bihar's Chief Minister.
