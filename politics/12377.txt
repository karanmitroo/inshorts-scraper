Contesting against CM Sheila Dikshit, Aam Aadmi Party chief Arvind Kejriwal on Saturday filed his nomination papers from New Delhi constituency, declaring assets worth Rs.2 crore including a plot of land in his name in Ghaziabad and a flat in his wife's name in Gurgaon. Kejriwal also declared two defamation cases were filed against him by Dikshit and her secretary.
