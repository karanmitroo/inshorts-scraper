In an RTI reply, Central Public Works Department (CPWD) has revealed that it has spent over Rs.51 lakh during 2013-14 for maintaining the electrical fittings at 10 Janpath, the official residence of UPA chairperson, Sonia Gandhi. For the same house, CPWD had spent Rs 7.8 lakh and Rs 2.6 lakh during 2012-13 and 2011-12 respectively.
