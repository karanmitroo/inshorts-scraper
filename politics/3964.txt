Ahead of 2017 Punjab Assembly polls, Punjab CM Parkash Singh Badal has declared assets to the tune of ₹14.48 crore which is up from ₹6.75 crore declared during 2012 Assembly polls, a report claimed. Furthermore, the assets of Punjab’s Deputy CM Sukhbir Badal and his wife Harsimrat Badal have grown by 13% during this period.
