The Janata Dal-United (JD-U), Rashtriya Janata Dal (RJD), Congress and the Nationalist Congress Party (NCP) will campaign jointly from August for the upcoming Bihar elections. The decision was taken after JD-U president Sharad Yadav met Bihar CM Nitish Kumar and RJD chief Lalu Prasad on Sunday. The leaders also discussed their grand alliance's strategy against BJP-led NDA for the polls.
