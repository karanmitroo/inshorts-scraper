Madhya Pradesh CM Shivraj Chouhan today said PM Narendra Modi is so popular that Congressmen think of him even in their dreams. "Wherever you go, it is 'Modi Modi', you go to America, England, Dubai ... And Congress also does 'Modi Modi'," Chouhan said. He was addressing a function felicitating Modi for launching the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojna.
