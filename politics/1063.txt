The Sharad Yadav faction of the JD(U) on Saturday announced election to party posts on March 11 and released a list of interim office-bearers. The list of interim office-bearers included Gujarat MLA Chhotu Bhai Vasava as working president and Ali Anwar as one of the vice-presidents. Further, Former Bihar Minister Ramai Ram has been made ad-hoc president of Bihar unit.
