Responding to Minority Affairs Minister Najma Heptullah's allegations, Majlis-e-Ittehadul Muslimeen Chief Asaduddin Owaisi, on Tuesday said he does not want to break India. "I want to show Muslims are respected and first class citizens of the country," Owaisi said. "I want to eradicate poverty among Muslims and... I want to see the girls and boys are educated," he added.
