Uttarakhand Congress Chief Kishore Upadhyay on Wednesday accused National Security Advisor Ajit Doval of being involved in toppling Congress governments in Arunachal Pradesh and Uttarakhand. Claiming that his "friends" in Arunachal have confirmed Doval's role in toppling the Assembly, he added, "Three-four MLAs out of the nine who sided with BJP in Uttarakhand Assembly were in touch with Doval."
