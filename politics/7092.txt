Fresh violence was reported in Burdwan division and Paschim Medinipur district in West Bengal, even after voting closed on Monday. CPI-M supporters accused TMC activists of attacking them in Laudoha and Pandaveswar (Burdwan), and Dantan and Keshiari (Paschim Medinipur). Several people were hurt after clashes broke out between Trinamool Congress supporters and BJP activists in New Egra (Ranigunj).
