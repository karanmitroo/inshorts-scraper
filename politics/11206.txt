A Twitter account supporting the Aam Aadmi Party in Varanasi has suddenly turned against the party, with tweets mocking party leader Arvind Kejriwal. Till Apr-17, the tweets from the account @AAP_Varanasi, with nearly 1,300 followers, promoted the AAP activities. But the account, which was dormant for about 15 days, suddenly, from May-5, began sending tweets against the party.
