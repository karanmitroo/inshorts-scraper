Thirty three-year-old C Devi is the first transgender person to contest in Tamil Nadu's assembly elections. Devi will contest against Tamil Nadu CM Jayalalithaa in Chennai's RK Nagar constituency on the Naam Tamilar Katchi (NTK) party's ticket this May. Devi has been involved with NTK for the last three years and currently runs a shelter named Thaimadi for the homeless.
