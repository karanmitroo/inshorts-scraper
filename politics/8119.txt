Deputy CM of Bihar Tejaswi Yadav was on Monday appointed the leader of RJD legislature party in the new Bihar assembly while RJD chief Lalu Prasad Yadav's wife Rabri Devi was appointed as a member of state legislative council. Lalu Prasad Yadav who was authorised to take this decision by party MLAs made the announcement, MLA Bhola Yadav told PTI.
