Delhi CM and AAP convener Arvind Kejriwal on Tuesday took to Twitter and said the Uttarakhand floor test outcome was a huge setback to PM Narendra Modi-led NDA government. "Hope they (the Centre) will stop toppling Govts (state governments) now," Kejriwal added. Notably, the outcome of Uttarakhand Assembly floor test will be declared tomorrow, however, MLAs have hinted BJP's defeat.
