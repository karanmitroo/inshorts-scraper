After five years, Amitabh Bachchan and MNS chief Raj Thackeray shared the dais for the welfare of cine artistes on the occasion of Maharashtra Navnirman Chitrapat Sena's seventh anniversary in Mumbai on Monday. The relationship between Thackeray and Bachchan had soured after the actor faced flak from Raj, for acting in Bhojpuri movies and becoming the ambassador of UP.
