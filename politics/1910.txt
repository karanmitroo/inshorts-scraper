A chopper with Maharashtra Chief Minister Devendra Fadnavis onboard crash-landed on Thursday in Latur. Soon after the incident, Fadnavis tweeted, "Our helicopter did meet with an accident...but I and my team are absolutely safe and ok. Nothing to worry." Notably, Fadnavis' chopper had developed a snag due to excessive heat in a Naxal-affected area on May 12 before take off.
