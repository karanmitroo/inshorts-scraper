Union HRD Minister Smriti Irani and Bihar's Education Minister from Congress, Ashok Choudhary, on Tuesday indulged in a Twitter spat after the latter addressed BJP leader with the word 'dear'. Choudhary had tweeted, "Dear @smritiirani ji, when will we get New Education policy?". Reacting to this, Irani posted, "@AshokChoudhaary mahilaon ko 'dear' keh ke kab se sambodhit karne lage Ashokji?".
