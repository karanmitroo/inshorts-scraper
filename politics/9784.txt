As Arvind Kejriwal-led AAP swept Delhi polls by heavy majority, five of its candidates won by a huge margin of over 50,000 votes. The highest margin of victory was achieved by Vikas Puri candidate Mahinder Yadav, who defeated BJP's Sanjay Singh by 77,665 votes. Yadav was followed by Prakash (63,937), Amanatullah Khan (63,657), Sanjeev Jha (63,122) and Ved Parkash (50,023).
