Actor Jaaved Jaaferi will be contesting on an AAP ticket from Lucknow against BJP president Rajnath Singh, as the party Monday announced its 13th list of candidates for 22 Lok Sabha constituencies. The party named candidates for Bihar, Jharkhand, Gujarat, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Chattisgarh, HP and Rajasthan. Riturajbhai Maheta will be the party's candidate against LK Advani from Gandhinagar.
