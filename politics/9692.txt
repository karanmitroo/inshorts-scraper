Uttar Pradesh CM Akhilesh Yadav, West Bengal CM Mamata Banerjee, RJD president Lalu Yadav and Samajwadi Party (SP) president Mulayam Singh Yadav will attend JD(U) leader Nitish Kumar's oath-taking ceremony as the chief minister of Bihar on Sunday. Notably, JD(U), RJD and SP were in talks to form a grand alliance but it did not materialise.
