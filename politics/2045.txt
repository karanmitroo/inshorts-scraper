Sacked AAP minister Kapil Mishra said on Monday that Delhi Health Minister and AAP MLA Satyendar Jain had settled a ₹50-crore land deal for party chief Arvind Kejriwal's brother-in-law. He added that Jain was also given the task of settling fake PWD bills amounting to ₹10 crore for the 7-acre farmhouse land in Delhi's Chhatarpur.
