The BJP Monday won Goa's Panaji constituency, for which by-polls were held on Feb 13, for the sixth time in a row. BJP candidate Siddarth Kuncoliekar won the by-election by a margin of 5,368 votes defeating Congress candidate Surendra Furtado. The Panaji seat is dominated by BJP since 1994, when Defence Minister Manohar Parrikar got elected for the first time.
