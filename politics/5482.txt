A man on Monday hurled a shoe at Congress Vice-President Rahul Gandhi during the latter’s ‘Kisan Yatra’ in Uttar Pradesh’s Sitapur district. While the shoe narrowly missed Gandhi, the man has been arrested. As per reports, the man felt that Gandhi’s ‘Kisan Yatra’ is a farce. Rahul has been carrying out 'Kisan Yatra' in UP from September 6.
