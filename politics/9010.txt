PM Narendra Modi on Friday acknowledged that Parliament's Monsoon Session will see a 'muqabla' (contest). The PM, who was sharing the dais with Leader of Opposition in Rajya Sabha, Ghulam Nabi Azad and senior J&K Congress leader Karan Singh, said, "We all are sitting here, but just wait to see the 'muqabla' (contest) we will have in some days."
