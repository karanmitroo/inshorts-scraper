Following son Vikas Barala's arrest, the picture of Haryana BJP Chief Subhash Barala has not been put up on posters on Union Minister Nitin Gadkari's upcoming visit to Gurugram. The posters have pictures of party leaders like Narendra Modi, Amit Shah, CM Manohar Lal Khattar among others. A legislator said that no official instruction was given in the regard.
