AIADMK Madurai (South) MLA SS Saravanan has filed a kidnapping case against ex-party chief VK Sasikala and newly-appointed legislature party leader Edappadi K Palanisamy. Saravanan had earlier alleged that he was humiliated physically and mentally during his five-day stay at Koovathur resort. He had also claimed that he scaled the wall of the resort to escape.
