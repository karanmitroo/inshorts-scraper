The third phase of Bihar polls for 50 constituencies kicked off on Wednesday at 7am with an approximate 9% turnout in the first two hours. As per reports, voting machines malfunctioned at a few places in Digha, Masaurhi, Mahnar and Buxar. Two sons of RJD chief Lalu Prasad Yadav are contesting from Mahua and Raghopur constituencies in this phase.
