Election affidavits analysed by the Association for Democratic Reforms and the National Election Watch, reported that 90% of ministers from Telangana are facing criminal charges. Andhra Pradesh follows second with 56% of ministers with serious criminal charges of rape, attempt to murder, kidnapping or electoral violations. State ministers with no criminal charges include Chhattisgarh, Mizoram, Meghalaya, Nagaland, Tripura and Sikkim.
