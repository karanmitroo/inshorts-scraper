Amit Shah, who recently took over as BJP chief, announced his new team on Saturday, comprising of 11 vice-presidents and 8 general secretaries. While vice-presidents lists include names like BS Yedurappa, Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi, the general secretaries list has names of Ram Madhav and Rajeev Pratap Ruddy. The announcement of the new team comes in significance of upcoming state-level elections.
