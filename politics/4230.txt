Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal on Saturday welcomed the new Lieutenant Governor of Delhi Anil Baijal, stating, "We welcome you, sir. We look forward to work together for Delhi's development". Kejriwal also attended Baijal's swearing-in ceremony at Raj Niwas in Delhi's Civil Lines area. "I hope we will work together with the elected government," Baijal said after taking the oath.
