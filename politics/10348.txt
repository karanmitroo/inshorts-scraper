Union Transport and Highways Minister Nitin Gadkari on Sunday adopted 'Pachgaon' village in Umrer taluka, about 15 kms from Nagpur city in Maharashtra, under the 'Sansad Adarsh Gram Yojna'. Gadkari was accorded a warm welcome in the village. "There will not just be physical, but social and cultural change in the village in future," Gadkari said, speaking on the occasion.
