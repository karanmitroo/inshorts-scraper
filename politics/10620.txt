The police on Saturday night filed 3 FIRs against DMK president M Karunanidhi and his son MK Stalin based on the complaint. AIADMK workers alleged in the complaint that DMK workers attacked them near Karunanidhi's residence in Chennai, police said. Minor clashes were reported between AIADMK and DMK workers after the Jayalalithaa's conviction in disproportionate assets case on Saturday.
