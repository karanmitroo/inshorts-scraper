SP expelled Sarika Baghael, its LS candidate from Agra City seat, and her husband Devendra from the party on Friday. Devendra Baghael had made an objectionable reference to SP Chief Mulayam Singh Yadav, calling him old, during an argument with a traffic police official on Friday afternoon. SP's GS Ram Gopal Yadav announced Maharaj Singh Dhangar as the new candidate.
