AAP chief, Arvind Kejriwal's meeting with a maulana has been lashed out by the exiled Bengali Author, Taslima Nasreen as the maulana faced allegations of incitement of communal violence. The maulana had caused uproar in 2007 when he announced a bounty on Taslima Nasrin and former US president George Bush of Rs.5 lakh and Rs.1 crore respectively.
