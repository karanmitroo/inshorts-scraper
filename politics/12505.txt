The Supreme Court on Monday said it would hear the appeal of CBI against dropping of conspiracy charge against senior BJP-leaders: LK Advani, Kalyan Singh, Uma Bharti, Murli Manohar Joshi and 16 others, in Babri Masjid demolition case on Dec-12. The case was filed by CBI challenging the verdicts of a special CBI court and Allahabad HC on 10-May-2010.
