The Trinamool Congress (TMC) on Saturday registered a landslide win in elections to Municipal Corporations in Bidhannagar and Asansol and Bally municipalty. TMC won 37 off the 41 wards in Bidhannagar and 74 off 106 wards in Asansol. It also wrested the Bally municipality from the CPI(M)-led Left Front, which held it for 40 years, by winning all 16 wards.
