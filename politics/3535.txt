Reacting to the violent protests in Nagaland against 33% quota for women in urban local bodies, CPI(M) leader Sitaram Yechury on Friday asked PM Narendra Modi, "Nagaland is burning. what happened to the Naga Accord?" "Besides Nagaland, Manipur troubled. Situation in Assam volatile. Security concerns in Arunachal. And BJP focussed solely on grabbing power," he added.
