All India Majlis-e-Ittehadul Muslimeen (AIMIM) chief Asaduddin Owaisi on Monday said that if PM Narendra Modi believes in what he is saying about Pakistan meddling in Gujarat elections, he should order the arrest of those involved. "Why doesn't he charge them under Unlawful Activities (Prevention) Act and National Security Act and have them arrested?" Owaisi questioned.
