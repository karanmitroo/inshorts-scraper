After three-member Shunglu panel submitted its report to L-G Najeeb Jung over alleged irregularities in the Delhi government’s administration, CM Arvind Kejriwal said PMO, L-G are planning to arrest Deputy CM Manish Sisodia. “PMO/LG target Manish through Shunglu (Ex-Comptroller and Auditor General VK Shunglu)…Shunglu facing charges of malpractices in DPS. Will PMO save him in return?” he tweeted.
