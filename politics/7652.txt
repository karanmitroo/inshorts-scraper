Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal was slammed on Twitter over a cartoon he posted on the micro-blogging platform today. The cartoon, by cartoonist Surendra, depicts a Lord Hanuman-like figure with his tail on fire, addressing PM Modi with “Done Sir. All attention is on JNU”. Users slammed Kejriwal with one such tweet reading “This is Blasphemous. Mocking Shree Hanuman…”.
