AAP chief Arvind Kejriwal, who's fighting against BJP's PM nominee Narendra Modi in Varanasi, secured the highest percentage of 'yes' votes, beating Modi by a huge margin, in the Time magazine's readers poll of world's 100 Most Influential People. Remarkably, Modi had a greater percentage of 'no' votes than any other person. Congress vice-president Rahul Gandhi ranked a distant 40.
