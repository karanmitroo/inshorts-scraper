The office of Congress vice president Rahul Gandhi on Tuesday joined the micro-blogging site Twitter. Going by the handle 'OfficeOfRG', the account will tweet Rahul's activity. "On 12th May Rahul Gandhi will begin a 15km padyatra in Telangana's Adilabad Dist- covering 5 villages, starting from Wadial Village," it tweeted on Thursday. The account has already got 17,000 followers so far.
