Pakistan on Thursday kept the suspense on whether its PM Nawaz Sharif will attend Narendra Modi's swearing-in ceremony on May-26, with a decision on the same likely to be taken on Friday. Other key SAARC leaders including, Sri Lankan President Mahinda Rajapaksa, Afghanistan President Hamid Karzai, Nepal PM Sushil Koirala and Maldivian President Abdulla Gayoom have confirmed the presence.
