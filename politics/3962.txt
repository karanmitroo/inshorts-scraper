Samajwadi Party Chief Mulayam Singh Yadav on Wednesday claimed that Ram Gopal Yadav wanted to form a new party, 'Akhil Bhartiya Samajwadi Party', with a motorcycle as the symbol. "I don't want the party to split, the party should always be united. We will save SP, I want to save the party. I want to save our cycle," Mulayam said.
