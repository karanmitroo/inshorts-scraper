The DMK-Congress alliance is set to form the government in Puducherry, according to India Today-Axis My India exit polls. It predicted that the DMK-Congress alliance will have the majority after winning 15-20 seats in the 30-member assembly while the incumbent AINRC will win 8-10 seats. India TV-CVoter polls also projected that the DMK-Congress alliance will form the government in Puducherry.
