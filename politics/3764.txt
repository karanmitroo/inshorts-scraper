Reacting to RSS leader Manmohan Vaidya's remarks on the reservation, RJD chief Lalu Prasad Yadav has said RSS should first review the 100% reservation in it, asking why no woman, Dalit have ever become RSS chief. "Modiji your RSS spokesman is again talking rubbish...We drubbed you in Bihar, but probably more remains to be done, which UP will," he added.
