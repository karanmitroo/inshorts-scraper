Results for district panchayat polls in Uttar Pradesh for 3,112 wards in 74 districts were declared on Monday with 615 candidates backed by Mayawati's BSP reportedly victorious. The SP and BJP couldn't gain much with SP winning 25 seats while losing in Mulayam's Etawah and Azamagarh, and BJP winning eight out of 48 seats while losing in PM's constituency Varanasi.
