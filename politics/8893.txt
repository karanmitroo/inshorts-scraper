Aaditya Thackeray, son of Shiv Sena President Uddhav Thackeray, met PM Narendra Modi on Saturday, and suggested to expand the Digital India campaign to education sector. Aaditya also demonstrated the educational tab which Shiv Sena is planning to provide in Mumbai's municipal schools from next year. “This could be a part of Modiji’s ambitious Digital India project,” he said.
