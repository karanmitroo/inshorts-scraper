Samajwadi Party leader Ram Govind Chaudhary on Sunday accused the CM Yogi Adityanath led-government in Uttar Pradesh of working according to the agenda of Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS). Claiming that PM Narendra Modi and BJP President Amit Shah failed in Gujarat, Chaudhary said, "Apart from playing the Hindu card, the BJP has no other option."
