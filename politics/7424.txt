Samajwadi Party MLA Jagram Paswan's brother Annu Yadav has allegedly killed a man with a gunshot following a dispute over their vehicles brushing against each other's while overtaking. The police arrested Yadav on Monday from Lucknow's Gomti Nagar area while the hunt for others accompanying him continued. Jagram Paswan is an MLA from Balrampur Sadar constituency of Uttar Pradesh.
