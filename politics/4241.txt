Samajwadi Party supremo Mulayam Singh Yadav on Friday said he will decide who will become the new Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh after expelling his son and current CM Akhilesh Yadav from the party. "Maine akele hi party banayi thi, Inka kya yogdaan hain? Ram Gopal aur Akhilesh Yadav party khatam kar rahe hain," he said.
