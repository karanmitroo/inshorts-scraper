In indications of emergence of a Third Front ahead of Lok Sabha polls, 11 non-Congress and non-BJP parties on Wednesday formally joined hands as a block in Parliament to pitch for pro-people, anti-communal and federal agenda. The announcement was made jointly by these parties including the four Left parties, SP, JD(U), AIADMK, AGP, Jharkhand Vikas Morcha, JD(S) and BJD.
