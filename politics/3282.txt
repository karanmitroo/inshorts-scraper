As voting ended for the second phase of Assembly elections in Uttar Pradesh on Wednesday, the Election Commission announced that the state witnessed voter turnout of 65.5% till 5 pm. While no incident of violence was reported, cash worth ₹16.52 crore, 3.29 lakh litre liquor, 1.9 kg gold and drugs worth ₹1.16 crore were seized, the EC added.
