BJP spokesperson Sambit Patra, who re-tweeted Times of Islamabad's fake news article 'NDTV data reveals that Make In India is a flop show' with caption "Hmmm...Agenda!!", has now issued a clarification over the issue. "Do I not have FOE to call out a Pak publication (real not FAKE) which runs an anti India AGENDA? Why are some riled?" Patra tweeted.
