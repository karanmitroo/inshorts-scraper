Income Tax department on Wednesday raided AAP MLA Kartar Singh Tanwar's residence in south Delhi and locked down the premises, claimed reports. “ModiJi had unleashed CBI, ACB, DP earlier, now IT too AAP wont bow down, won't bend (sic),” tweeted AAP leader Dilip K Pandey. Recently, the party’s Okhla MLA Amantullah Khan was arrested by Delhi Police on molestation charges.
