UP CM Akhilesh Yadav has reportedly submitted his own list of 403 probable candidates for the UP polls to Samajwadi Party supremo Mulayam Singh Yadav. However, Mulayam didn't approve his way of going ahead with the list bypassing the senior party leaders, reports added. Akhilesh is also believed to have raised doubts over the list announced by Shivpal Singh Yadav.
