CBI on Friday questioned BSP chief and former Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Mayawati, regarding the National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) scam. Probing the NRHM scam since 2011, CBI has registered over 74 cases and filed 48 chargesheets to date. Hundreds of local politicians, doctors, private contractors have been probed by the CBI for their alleged irregularities in the state.
