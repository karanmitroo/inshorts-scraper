Delhi Deputy CM Manish Sisodia was on Wednesday detained by the police during a visit to RML Hospital to meet the family of the ex-serviceman who committed suicide over One Rank One Pension scheme. Reacting to the reports, Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal tweeted, "He (Manish Sisodia) is elected Dy CM. What's wrong with you Modiji (PM Narendra Modi)? So insecure?"
