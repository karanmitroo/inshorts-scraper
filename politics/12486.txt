Tagged as the "non-resident PM", Manmohan Singh has surpassed Atal Bihari Vajpayee and many other former PMs, with over 70 foreign trips and nearly Rs.650-crore spent on his travel since 2004. Moreover, Singh made 15-trips while Parliament was in session. The highest expenditure (Rs.26.94-crore) was during his Mexico and Brazil visit in 2012 to attend G20 and Rio+ 20 Summit.
