BJP-led Haryana government minister, Anil Vij, on Wednesday said that those supporting Delhi University student Gurmehar Kaur are "pro-Pakistan" and should be "thrown out of the country". In a video, Kaur had held a placard, stating "Pakistan did not kill my father, War did". Earlier, Union Minister Kiren Rijiju had tweeted, "Who's polluting this young girl's mind?".
