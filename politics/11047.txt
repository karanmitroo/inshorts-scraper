In the second political killing this week, in Maharashtra's Gadchiroli District, NCP functionary and former Ettapalli Panchayat Samiti member, Ghissu Matami, was shot on Thursday night by 4 suspected Maoists.

When taken to a nearby hospital, he was declared brought dead.

Mr.Mattami's wife is the President of the Samiti and his killing in his guarded official residence has shocked authorities.
