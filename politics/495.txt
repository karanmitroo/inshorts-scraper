'Welcoming' Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath to Karnataka, CM Siddaramaiah said Adityanath could learn a lot from the state. He asked Adityanath to visit Karnataka's Indira Canteens to address the starvation deaths reported in UP. Slamming Siddaramaiah over farmer suicides in Karnataka, Adityanath tweeted, "As UP CM I am working to undo the misery and lawlessness unleashed by your allies."
