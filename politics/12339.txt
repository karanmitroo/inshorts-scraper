According to various exit polls, BJP is set to make an impressive comeback in Rajasthan and comfortably retain Madhya Pradesh and Chhattisgarh. The polls gave BJP 130-140 seats in the 200-member Rajasthan Assembly, while 121-128 seats in the 230-member MP assembly. In Chhattisgarh, BJP might have a slight edge of 1-2 seats over Congress, whereas in Mizoram, Congress would lead.
