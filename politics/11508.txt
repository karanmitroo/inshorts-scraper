With a fan base of 3.7 million, Narendra Modi remains the 'most-followed' Indian politician on Twitter, succeeded by Shashi Tharoor (2.13M). However, third-ranked Arvind Kejriwal (1.62M) has gained the most, with 6.3 lakh new followers in just three months. Manmohan Singh comes fourth, followed by Sushma Swaraj and beauty queen-turned-politician Gul Panag.
