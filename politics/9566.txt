Despite speculations about his being relieved of the post due to old age, Suresh Bhaiyyaji Joshi was unanimously elected the RSS General Secretary for a third straight term, on Saturday. His post, that of the 'Sarkaryavah', is second only to the 'Sarsanghchalak', held by Mohan Bhagwat. Joshi (67) is known for his organisational and planning skills.
