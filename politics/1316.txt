Ahead of the inauguration of the Lucknow Metro by Uttar Pradesh CM Yogi Adityanath, Samajwadi Party chief Akhilesh Yadav posted a series of tweets claiming credit for the service. He tweeted photos with metro staff using the caption, "Engine toh pehle hi chal dia tha, dibbey to peechey aanee hi thee (Engine had already started running, bogies had to follow)."
