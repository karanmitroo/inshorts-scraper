The ruling AIADMK on Friday expelled 132 workers for bringing 'disrepute' to the party after it lost the RK Nagar bypolls to sidelined AIADMK leader TTV Dhinakaran. Tamil Nadu CM Edappadi K Palaniswami and Deputy CM O Panneerselvam requested party members to disassociate themselves from the expelled workers. The AIADMK also expelled 44 party leaders for supporting Dhinakaran.
