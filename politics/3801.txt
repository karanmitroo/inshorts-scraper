Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Akhilesh Yadav's uncle Shivpal Yadav's name has figured in the political party's list of 191 candidates for the first three phases of the seven-phase Assembly election starting February 11. The senior Samajwadi Party leader has also got a ticket from Jaswantnagar. Notably, Shivpal Yadav supported Mulayam Singh in the fight for claim over party's symbol.
