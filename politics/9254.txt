The Election Commission on Tuesday announced bypolls in six Assembly constituencies, including Dr. Radhakrishnan Nagar from where Tamil Nadu Chief Minister J. Jayalalithaa is likely to contest. The bypolls in Chokpot (Meghalaya), Aruvikkara (Kerala), Pratapgarh (Tripura), Surma (Tripura), Garoth (Madhya Pradesh) and Dr. Radhakrishnan Nagar (Tamil Nadu) will be held on 27 June.
