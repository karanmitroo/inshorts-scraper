The CBI has summoned Lalu Prasad Yadav and his son Tejashwi Yadav on Thursday in connection with the Railways irregularities case. Earlier, CBI had raided Yadav family's properties over irregularities in awarding tenders for IRCTC hotels during Lalu's tenure as Railway Minister. Lalu has to appear in court on September 11 and Tejashwi on September 12, as per the summon.
