While senior Congressmen are emphatic that incumbent MP Rahul Gandhi will win a third term, they concede in private of an "opposition onslaught" that is "worrying", saying that the victory margin might be narrowed down. Remarkably, Sonia Gandhi held her first rally in 10-years in Amethi last week. Rahul faces stiff opposition from AAP's Kumar Vishwas and BJP's Smriti Irani.
