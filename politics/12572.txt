Replying to HRD's showcause notice regarding his membership of Aam Adami Party, Prof Yogendra Yadav said that UGC Act-1956 doesn't bar UGC member from participation in politics. He added, UGC doesn't regulate political parties and he hasn't violated any UGC rule 1992(on condition of service termination). He also claimed about his association with SJP (registered party) when he joined UGC.
