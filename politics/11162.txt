A tweet by BJP's PM candidate, Narendra Modi, on counting day, has been retweeted 39,742 times and favourited 21,691 times, thereby becoming the most retweeted tweet ever in the country. "India has won! We're approaching the good days," Modi tweeted. A star-studded selfie by American TV host, Ellen DeGeneres, holds the world record with 3,42 million retweets.
