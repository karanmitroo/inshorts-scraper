An RTI query has revealed that the total expenditure of Travel Allowance and Dearness Allowance of all members of the Lok Sabha from April 2016 to March 2017 is over ₹95.7 crore. While the expenditure of all Rajya Sabha members during the same time period is ₹35.9 crore, West Bengal's CPM leader Ritabrata Banerjee claimed nearly ₹6 lakh/month, the highest.
