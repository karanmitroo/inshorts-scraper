Bihar BJP MLA Vinay Bihari stripped to his half-pants and an undershirt, sending his kurta to Union Minister Nitin Gadkari and pyjama to Bihar CM Nitish Kumar. Bihari was protesting the alleged slow progress of the construction of a road between Manuapul and Navalpur Ratwal Chowk Path. Kumar had promised that the road would become a state highway, Bihari claimed.
