Lauding the measure of demonetisation, Union Minister Ramdas Athawale said that even Babasaheb Ambedkar had suggested a "change in currency every ten years". He added that his party, Republican Party of India (Athawale) will observe the first anniversary of demonetisation on November 8, as the 'white money day', in order to thank PM Modi for targeting black money through 'notebandi'.
