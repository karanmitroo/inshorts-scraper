The CBI Friday claimed that, former telecom minister Dayanidhi Maran received a Rs.600-crore bribe in the Aircel-Maxis deal from Malaysian business tycoon Ananda Krishnan. The agency charged Dayanidhi Maran, his brother Kalanidhi Maran and Ananda Krishnan with corruption. The charge sheet filed, also named four companies — Sun Direct TV, South-East Entertainment Holdings and Malaysia’s Maxis Communications, as accused.
