In an attempt to reach out to large number of voters, AAP Convenor Arvind Kejriwal will address 21 rallies in poll-bound Punjab from November 20 to November 30. The areas to be covered by him during his rallies will be Bathinda, Moga, Muktsar, Sangrur, Rupnagar, Ludhiana, Jalandhar, Kapurthala, Hoshiarpur, Pathankot, Amritsar and Tarn Taran.
