AIMIM chief Asaduddin Owaisi on Monday said the ruling Samajwadi Party was 'insecure' of him, a day after the authorities stopped him from entering Azamgarh in Uttar Pradesh to hold public meetings. “Samajwadi party is stopping from holding public meetings, enter Azamgarh remember @yadavakhilesh power is not ETERNAL I will be coming more (sic),” Owaisi tweeted.
