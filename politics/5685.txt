Tea and snacks served to guests by Delhi's Cabinet have cost the national capital over ₹1 crore in 18 months, an RTI query has revealed. Spending nearly ₹47.29 lakh on refreshments, Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal topped the list followed by Deputy CM Manish Sisodia (₹11.28 lakh). Further, Food and Supply Minister Imran Hussain  spent the least on snacks (₹5.89 lakh).
