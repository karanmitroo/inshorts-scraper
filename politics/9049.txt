Congress vice president Rahul Gandhi on Thursday accused PM Narendra Modi of shielding BJP chief ministers Vasundhara Raje and Shivraj Chouhan in two different cases. "Why is PM allowing corruption in Rajasthan, in MP; When is he bringing back Lalit Modi?" Gandhi said. Raje and Chouhan are facing heat for their alleged involvement in Lalitgate and Vyapam scam respectively.
