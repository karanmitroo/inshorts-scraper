The Maharashtra BJP will, on Monday, kick start a massive election campaign 'Mulukh Maidan', under which the party will hold 300 rallies in two days. As per a BJP spokesperson, these rallies "will reach out to maximum voters". Union Ministers Praksh Javdekar, Smriti Irani, Uma Bharati, Kailash Mishra and Santosh Ganagawar will address rallies during the campaign.
