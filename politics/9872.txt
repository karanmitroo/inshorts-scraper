AAP Volunteer Action Manch (AVAM) on Monday alleged corruption in AAP, claiming that the party is gathering donations from fake companies. According to AVAM, AAP received four donations of Rs 50 lakh each on April 5 last year from bogus companies. Questioning claims of 'transparency' by the Arvind Kejriwal-led party, AVAM accused AAP of making dummy entries to evade tax.
