In a personal attack on PM Narendra Modi, Akhil Bhartiya Congress Mahasamiti member Deshbandhu Kaushik called him a womanizer, a thief and a criminal. Slamming Modi, Kaushik said, “He is a thief, a criminal. All that he knows is to fill his coffers…” Deshbandhu also said that Modi first got married and then left his wife to join the RSS.
