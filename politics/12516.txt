UP's Samajwadi Party government has banned Vishwa Hindu Parishad's Sankalp Yatra, which was planned for Oct-18 in Ayodhya. VHP had decided to observe Valmiki Jayanti on Oct-18 to take a fresh vow for the construction of Ram temple. In past also, UP government took measures to stop the VHP planned "84 kosi parikrama yatra" of Hindu saints.
