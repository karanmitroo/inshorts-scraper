Funding pattern of Aam Aadmi Party reveals that Delhi has contributed only 20% (Rs.4.5 crore) to AAP's total collections of about Rs.22.3 crore. NRIs contributed 30%, about Rs.6.7 crore, while the rest came from across the country, with Maharashtra alone chipping in Rs.2.8 crore. The big cities were the major sources, with the top 11 contributing 73% of the total.
