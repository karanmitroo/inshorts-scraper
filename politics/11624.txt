TV star-turned-politician Smriti Irani will take on Rahul Gandhi in Amethi turning the contest into a high profile clash with AAP's Kumar Vishwas already in the fray. Irani, a Rajya Sabha MP, is seen to be close to  Narendra Modi. BJP also decided to pitch former SP leader Ajay Aggarwal, a Supreme Court lawyer, against Sonia Gandhi in Rae Bareli.
