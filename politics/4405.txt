Ahead of the upcoming Assembly elections in the state, Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Akhilesh Yadav on Tuesday inaugurated over 5,500 projects in about six hours. He began by inaugurating a new building at a hospital in Faizabad, and later in Lucknow he inaugurated 3,180 projects worth ₹1,932 crore and laid the foundation stone of 2,022 projects worth ₹1,103 crore.
