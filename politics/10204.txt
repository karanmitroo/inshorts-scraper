As part of the  'Sansad Adarsh Gram Yojna', Finance Minister Arun Jaitley has decided to adopt four villages namely, Karnali, Pipaliya, Vadiya and Baglipura, all covered under Karnali group panchayat in Vadodara. "The district administration will develop various facilities in the villages and make Karnali a tourist spot...", Vadodara District Collector Vinod Rao said.
