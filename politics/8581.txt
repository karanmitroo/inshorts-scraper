Bihar Animal Husbandry and Fisheries Minister Baidyanath Sahni on Friday resigned from the Janata Dal (United) government in the state to join the BJP. President of BJP's Bihar unit, Mangal Pandey, welcomed Sahni to the party. Sahni, who was the outgoing JD(U) MLA from Morwa Assembly, had been denied a ticket from the Morwa seat for the upcoming assembly elections.
