The Samajwadi Party (SP) on Saturday announced its 45-member National Executive with newly-elected Rajya Sabha members Amar Singh and Beni Prasad Verma nominated as new members. Party chief Mulayam Singh Yadav was named the National President. While Kiranmoy Nanda has been nominated as Vice President, Ram Gopal Yadav would remain the National General Secretary and Spokesman.
