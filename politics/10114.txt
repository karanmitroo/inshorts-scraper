BSP chief Mayawati has criticized the NDA government for discriminating against Dalit icon Kanshi Ram while awarding Bharat Ratna to Atal Bihari Vajpayee and Madan Mohan Malviya. Kanshi was a politician who worked for the upliftment and political mobilisation of the Dalits. Mayawati also demanded the award for activist Jyotiba Phule for his efforts to establish equality in society.
