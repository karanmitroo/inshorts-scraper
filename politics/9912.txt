UP CM Akhilesh Yadav regretted on Monday that he missed the opportunity to welcome US President Barack Obama at the Taj Mahal. Akhilesh took to twitter to express his feelings and tweeted, "Attended the President's dinner. Missed the opportunity to welcome President @BarackObama at the Taj Mahal... ". Obama's Taj visit, which was scheduled on Jan-27, was cancelled on Jan-24.
