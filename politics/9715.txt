Xiaomi, founded in 2010, was the top smartphone company in China last year, narrowly outpacing Samsung, market intelligence firm IDC said Thursday. Xiaomi more than doubled its market share in 2014 to 12.5 percent from 5.3 percent in 2013, while Samsung saw its market share erode to 12.1 percent in 2014 from 18.1 percent in 2013.
