Marumalarchi Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (MDMK) snapped ties with the National Democratic Alliance (NDA) on Monday, becoming the first ally to quit the alliance. Vaiko-led MDMK passed a resolution to quit the NDA at a meeting of the district secretaries in Chennai. Accusing Modi-led govt of acting against Tamils, Vaiko said the govt had gone against its promise.
