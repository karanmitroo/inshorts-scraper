Slamming Delhi Chief Secretary Anshu Prakash for making "false claims" about being assaulted by AAP MLAs at CM Arvind Kejriwal's residence, AAP MLA Naresh Balyan on Friday said, "Such officials must be thrashed." He added that officials who block development work over "commission" should be thrashed. Two AAP MLAs accused in the case have been sent to a 14-day custody.
