Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP) Chandigarh unit chief​ Jannat Jahan on Thursday announced a ₹50 lakh reward to the person who gets her the tongue of expelled BJP Uttar Pradesh Vice President Dayashankar Singh. This comes after Singh compared BSP chief Mayawati to a​ prostitute while talking about the ticket distribution policy, following which he was sacked from all party posts.
