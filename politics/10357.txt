A day after his lavish birthday celebrations, SP supremo Mulayam Singh Yadav rebuked his son, UP CM Akhilesh Yadav, for the slow pace with which his government was working, despite enjoying complete majority. He was addressing a gathering while laying the foundation stone for the 302 km long-access controlled six-lane Lucknow-Agra Expressway, one of the state's most ambitious projects.
