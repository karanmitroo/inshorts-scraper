As the general elections inch closer, AAP leader Prashant Bhushan on Sunday held meeting with anti-Kudankulam protests leader SP Udayakumar in Idinthakarai (Tamil Nadu). According to sources, Bhushan has urged Udayakumar to attach himself to AAP and contest in 2014 Lok Sabha elections. By this move, AAP is expected to get at least four constituencies in Kanyakumari, Tuticorin and Nagercoil.
