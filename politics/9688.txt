As per reports, Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal will join Anna Hazare's protest at Jantar Mantar against Land Acquisition Bill in Delhi on Tuesday. The decision came after Kejriwal, along with Manish Sisodia, held an hour long meeting with Hazare on Monday. "Arvind and I will sit on dharna with Anna after the (Delhi assembly) session at 3 pm", Sisodia said.
