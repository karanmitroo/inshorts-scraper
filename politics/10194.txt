Jharkhand CM Hemant Soren Tuesday won from the Barhait assembly constituency in the state. Soren defeated BJP candidate Hemalal Murmu by more than 23,000 votes. Meanwhile, former CM of the state Madhu Koda lost the election from the Majhgao constituency. He was defeated by Jharkhand Mukti Morcha (JMM) candidate Nial Purty by over 20,000 votes.
