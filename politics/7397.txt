Thirty days after being accused of raping a minor girl, suspended RJD MLA Raj Ballabh Prasad surrendered before a court in Bihar Sharif, Nalanda, on Thursday and was sent to judicial custody for two weeks. Prasad, who had been a minister in the Rabri Devi cabinet, allegedly raped a 15-year-old on February 6. An FIR was filed on February 9.
