Karnataka is all set to witness a contest of five former CMs - M Veerappa Moily, N Dharam Singh, BS Yeddyurappa, DV Sadananda Gowda and HD Deve Gowda - in the upcoming Lok Sabha polls. Gowda's son HD Kumarswamy, who had headed the Karnataka government for about 20 months has indicated that he may also join the race.
