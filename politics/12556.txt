Soon after the special CBI-court in Ranchi convicted RJD Chief Lalu Prasad Yadav in 'Bihar Fodder Scam' case, setting October-3rd as the date to pronounce the quantum of sentence, his son Tejeswi Yadav and wife Rabri Devi have told reporters that they'll appeal against this conspiracy in the high-court. Meanwhile, senior RJD leaders have expressed their complete support with Lalu.
