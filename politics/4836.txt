Punjab Congress leader Captain Amarinder Singh on Wednesday announced that Dr Navjot Kaur Sidhu, wife of Awaz-e-Punjab leader Navjot Singh Sidhu will join the Congress on November 28. Former Akali Dal MLA Pargat Singh will also join the party, he added. Notably, the Awaz-e-Punjab leader had vowed to join the Congress only if Amarinder Singh was not in the party.
