AIMIM chief Asaduddin Owaisi on Thursday attacked the Samajwadi Party (SP) at a Faizabad rally for failing to fulfil their promises regarding Muslim reservations, ahead of the February 13 Bikapur bypoll in Uttar Pradesh. Owaisi also questioned the Centre over its election claims that ceasefire violations and terror attacks would stop and criticised PM Narendra Modi for his Pakistan visit.
