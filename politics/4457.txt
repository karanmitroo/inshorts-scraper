Replacing BCCI chief Anurag Thakur, BJP National President Amit Shah has appointed party MP Poonam Mahajan as the President of Bharatiya Janata Yuva Morcha, BJP's youth wing. Thakur had held the position for six years. Poonam Mahajan, daughter of former party leader Pramod Mahajan, had joined BJP following her father's murder in 2006. She is currently the party's national secretary.
