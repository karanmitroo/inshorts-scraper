Reacting to the demonetisation of ₹500 and ₹1,000 currency notes, RJD Chief Lalu Prasad Yadav has said that the atmosphere in the country has become explosive after the government's move. "People won't get relief with dramatic speeches like what the PM made in Goa. The situation is alarming. People are hugely troubled and the PM is giving speeches," Lalu added.
