In his first public address since announcing his entry into politics, actor Rajinikanth on Monday said he will fill the vacuum left in Tamil Nadu due to AIADMK leader Jayalalithaa's demise and ill-health of DMK leader Karunanidhi. "God is on my side," Rajinikanth added. He was speaking at the unveiling of former CM MG Ramachandran's statue at a  Chennai university.
