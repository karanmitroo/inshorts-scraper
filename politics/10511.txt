Haryana CM Bhupinder Singh Hooda has retained his constituency Garha Sampla Kiloi in Jind district in the state assembly polls. Hooda defeated Indian National Lok Dal's (INLD) Satish Kumar Nandal by over 25k votes. BJP is running ahead in the assembly polls counting in Haryana, while INLD is on the 2nd spot followed by the ruling congress in the state.
