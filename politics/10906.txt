An RTI query has revealed that the Akhilesh Yadav government of UP has cut the budget of the state women's commission by 85% to a mere Rs 75-lakh in three years, but has got the money for two seven-seater Mercedes cars and two Land Cruisers. The Samajwadi Party always had a "pathetic" record on women's empowerment, said activist Neelam Ranjan.
