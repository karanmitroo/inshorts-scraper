In 24 hours after Arvind Kejriwal tweeted appealing to people to donate "clean money" for election campaign in Varanasi and Amethi, the party received more than Rs 80 lakh donation online from across the world. Of this, Rs 16 lakh was donated by people in UP including Rs 2.10 lakh and Rs 2.50 lakh specifically for Amethi and Varanasi, respectively.
