According to ABP News poll, Aam Aadmi Party will win 26% votes and 18 seats in the 70-member Delhi assembly. It also indicates Kejriwal as the most-preferred CM candidate with 32% votes, beating Sheila Dikshit and Vijay Goel(27% each); and projects BJP(34% votes) as the largest party ahead of Congress(27% votes), with no party in a position to form government.
