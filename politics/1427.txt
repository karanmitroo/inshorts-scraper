The highest amount of corporate funding to national parties was received by the Bharatiya Janata Party between 2012-13 and 2015-16, according to a report by the Association for Democratic Reforms. Of the total ₹956.77 crore received by five national parties, BJP received ₹705.81 crore from 2987 donors, followed by Indian National Congress (INC), which received ₹198.16 crore from 167 donors.
