Andhra Pradesh and Telangana politicians have reportedly been altering their accounts to avoid the Election Commision. TRS chief Chandrasekhar Rao, TDP chief Chandrababu Naidu and YSR CP president Jaganmohan Reddy are some of the netas manipulating their accounts. Most of them prefer to keep chopper expenditures, along with those of huge publicity at various platforms, under the counter.
