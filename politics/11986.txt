With the confrontation on power issue intensifying, Arvind Kejriwal on Tuesday attacked Anil Ambani whose group operates two power distribution companies in the city. "Anil Ambani playing politics wid delhi's electricity? Whose politics is he doing?," Kejriwal tweeted. Reliance Infrastructure spokesperson reacted strongly to Kejriwal's comment, saying "Reliance Group is extremely disappointed with CM's tweet attacking our Group Chairman."
