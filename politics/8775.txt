Lok Janshakti Party (LJP) chief Ram Vilas Paswan on Sunday said that former Prime Minister Atal Bihari Vajpayee wanted him to become the Chief Minister of Bihar. "Atalji said that make Ram Vilasji the CM, the government will run smoothly as he (Paswan) has a nature of taking everyone along. Then I... said don't push me into this," Paswan said.
