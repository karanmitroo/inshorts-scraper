Bollywood actor Rahul Roy, known for the 1990 film 'Aashiqui', joined the BJP in Delhi on Saturday, in the presence of party leader Vijay Goel. Rahul praised PM Narendra Modi and Amit Shah for "changing the way how the world looks at India now". Rahul also sang the song "Saanson Ki Jarurat Hai Jaise" from Aashiqui, during the briefing.
