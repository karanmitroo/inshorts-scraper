Congress candidates have won Ajmer and Alwar parliamentary seats and the Assembly seat for Mandalgarh in the Rajasthan bypolls. While Congress leader Raghu Sharma won the Ajmer seat by a margin of 84,414 votes, Karan Singh Yadav from Alwar won by over 1.96 lakh votes. Meanwhile, Congress' Vivek Dhakar won the Mandalgarh Assembly seat in the BJP-ruled state.
