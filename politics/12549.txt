Anti-Telangana-bifurcation protesters were undettered by the curfew and shoot-at-sight orders issued to police in Andhra Pradesh on Saturday. Protesters hit the streets in Vizianagaram town and clashed with the police on Sunday. To disperse them, police fired rubber bullets at Kothapeta area and used batons against them in Palliveedhi area. Some were taken into custody. Two-dozen police personnel sustained injuries.
