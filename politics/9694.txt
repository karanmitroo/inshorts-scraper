The CBI Wednesday filed a supplementary chargesheet naming West Bengal transport minister Madan Mitra among other accused in the Saradha scam. The agency charged Mitra, suspended TMC MP Kunal Ghosh, ex-Trinamool MP Srinjoy Bose and Naresh Balodia. The accused were charged with criminal conspiracy, cheating, criminal breach of trust under the Prize Chits and Money Circulation (Banning) Act.
