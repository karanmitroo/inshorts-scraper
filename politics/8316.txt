Attacking BJP over ensuing controversy regarding actor Shah Rukh Khan's "extreme intolerance" comment, Delhi Tourism Minister Kapil Mishra on Thursday tweeted, "SRK is Hafeez Saeed, Badal is Mandela, Modi is biggest thing happened to India and 70 percent Indians are Pakis (Pakistani) for not voting BJP." Earlier, PM Modi had compared Punjab CM Parkash Singh Badal to Nelson Mandela.
