The Election Commission on Thursday finalised VK Sasikala camp's party name 'AIADMK Amma', whereas former Tamil Nadu CM O Panneerselvam camp's party was named 'AIADMK Puratchi Thalaivi Amma'. While Sasikala's camp has been allotted hat as the party symbol, Panneerselvam's camp has been allotted electric pole as the party symbol.
