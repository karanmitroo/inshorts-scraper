Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal on Saturday announced that he is donating ₹50,000 to assist Manipur activist Irom Sharmila's election campaign. "I am donating ₹50,000 as my small contribution to her and appeal to everyone to support her," he tweeted. Sharmila's party Peoples' Resurgence and Justice Alliance (PRJA) tweeted in response, "Honest politics always gets support of genuine people."
