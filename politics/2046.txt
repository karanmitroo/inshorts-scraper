Ousted AAP MLA and ex-Delhi Minister Asim Ahmed Khan has alleged CM Arvind Kejriwal had asked him to pay ₹5 crore. "They needed ₹25 crore to buy cable network out of which ₹5 crore were asked from me and ₹20 crore were asked from four other MLAs," he said. Asim was sacked for allegedly demanding a bribe from a builder.
