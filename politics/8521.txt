The Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) has reportedly witnessed a 35% increase in online applications in July-September. The organisation received an average of 5,300 online applications per month during January-June, while the figure rose to 6,083 (July), 6,555 (August) and 8,808 (September). Overall, RSS recorded an estimated 5 lakh new entrants in 2013 and 6 lakh in 2014, the election year.
