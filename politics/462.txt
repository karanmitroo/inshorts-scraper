Addressing people in Rajasthan's Barmer on Tuesday, PM Narendra Modi took a dig at Congress saying, "Congress aur aakaal judwa bhai hain (Congress and drought go hand in hand)." "Jahan Congress jayegi wahan aakaal jayega (Wherever Congress will go, drought will follow)," PM Modi added. The PM is in the state to inaugurate commencement of work of an oil refinery.
