According to the data given by the Union Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, 48 districts in 13 states across India have no blood banks. Surprisingly, the VVIP constituency of Congress vice-president Rahul Gandhi is among the states like Uttar Pradesh, Jharkhand, Uttrakhand, Madhya Pradesh and Chattisgarh, and the whole of the North-East region, that don't have any blood banks.
