Union Minister Smriti Irani and Congress Vice President Rahul Gandhi on Friday indulged in a Twitter war over the ban of an anti-Modi IIT Madras students' group. "IIT student group banned for criticizing Modi Government. What next?..", tweeted Rahul. To this, Irani replied, "@OfficeofRG give me a time n place n I'm ready to debate everything re governance including education".
