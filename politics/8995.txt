Congress vice-president Rahul Gandhi reached Rajasthan on Thursday for a two-day visit, during which he will undertake a ‘padyatra’ in the Hanumangarh district and address party leaders in a conference in Jaipur. In his nine-kilometre padyatra, Gandhi will cover Khotanwali, Surawali, and Amarsinghwala villages and interact with farmers and different groups of people.
