After fiercely contested Delhi elections ended Saturday, the exit polls predicted majority for Arvind Kejriwal-led AAP. According to Times Now-C-Voter survey, AAP may win 31-39 seats, BJP will likely get 27-35 seats, while Congress will manage 2-4 seats. ABP News-Nielsen survey also predicted majority for AAP by projecting 39 seats for it, 28 for BJP and 3 seats for Congress.
