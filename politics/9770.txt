AAP chief Arvind Kejriwal, who bagged 57,213 votes, Tuesday won from the New Delhi constituency, defeating BJP's Nupur Sharma by 31,583 votes. Kejriwal bettered his victory margin this time as he had defeated Sheila Dikshit by 25,864 votes in the previous Delhi elections. Meanwhile, BJP's CM candidate Kiran Bedi lost Krishna Nagar seat to AAP's SK Bagga by 2,476 votes.
