The Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) has won 14 mayoral seats in the Uttar Pradesh municipal elections, while the Bahujan Samaj Party has won two seats. Notably, Lucknow has elected a woman Mayor, Sanyukta Bhatia, for the first time in 100 years. Interestingly, the BJP won the Amethi Nagar panchayat elections, which is the constituency of Congress Vice President Rahul Gandhi.
