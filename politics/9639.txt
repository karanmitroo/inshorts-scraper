Rahul Gandhi's disappearance has sparked a 'poster war' in Allahabad. While Priyanka Gandhi's supporters have put up posters asking her to take charge of the Congress, ex-Congressman Amar Vaishya has come up with a banner announcing a cash prize for finding Rahul. "Congress commander Rahul is absconding and whosoever locates him will get a hefty prize", the banner reads.
