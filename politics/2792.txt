Samajwadi Party leader and minister in Akhilesh Yadav's Cabinet, Gayatri Prajapati, was on Wednesday arrested from Lucknow on charges of rape. While the police had arrested six of his accomplices, Prajapati was absconding since February 27. Prajapati was booked on a Supreme Court directive for allegedly gang raping a woman and attempting to rape her minor daughter.
