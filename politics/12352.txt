BJP's PM candidate Narendra Modi is among Time magazine's shortlisted 42 global leaders, entrepreneurs, and celebrities for its 'Person of the Year 2013' title. He will compete with Japan's PM Shinzo Abe, Barack Obama, Malala Yousafzai, Miley Cyrus, Amazon CEO Jeff Bezos, NSA whistle-blower Edward Snowden and Prince George, among others. Time will announce the winner next month.
