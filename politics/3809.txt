Samajwadi Party, on Friday, released a list of 191 candidates for the upcoming Uttar Pradesh Assembly polls. Party supremo Mulayam Singh Yadav's brother Shivpal Singh Yadav got the ticket from Jaswantnagar. Expelled party leader Naresh Agarwal's son was among the ones who featured on the list. Party leader Azam Khan's son also got the ticket for one of the constituencies.
