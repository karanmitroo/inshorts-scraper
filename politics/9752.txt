Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal will take charge of key portfolios such as finance, power and home. By virtue of being the chairperson of Delhi Jal Board, Kejriwal will also have control over water. Deputy-CM Manish Sidodia will retain education, urban development and PWD portfolios. Jitendra Tomar will be the law minister, while Gopal Rai will be handling transport and labour.
