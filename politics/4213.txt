The expulsion of Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Akhilesh Yadav from the Samajwadi Party has been cancelled with immediate effect after directions from party chief Mulayam Singh Yadav, party's Shivpal Yadav said on Saturday. Akhilesh, on Friday evening, was expelled from the party for six years for alleged indiscipline. Party's General Secretary Ram Gopal Yadav's suspension was also annulled.
