Nokia, which had launched a 41MP smartphone last year, has again launched a 41MP Windows smartphone, Lumia-1020. It takes two shots at the same time, one at 38MP resolution and the other at 5MP. Other specs include 4.5-inch PureMotionHD+ Amoled display, 1.5GHz dual-core processor, 2GB RAM, and 32GB internal storage. The smartphone will hit US on July-26, priced at $299.
