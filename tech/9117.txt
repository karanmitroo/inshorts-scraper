The website of the Freedom 251 smartphone, developed by Noida-based Ringing Bells, crashed on Thursday after it received "approximately six lakh hits per second". The company said, "[W]e are…taking a pause and upgrading the service and will revert within or before 24 hours". Priced at ₹251, Freedom 251 is the world’s cheapest smartphone and available for pre-order till February 21.
