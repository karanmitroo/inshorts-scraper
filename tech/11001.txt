Defence PSU Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) has delivered the 'Orbiter Craft Module Structure' of Chandrayaan-2 to ISRO. Consisting an orbiter, lander and rover configuration, Chandrayaan-2 aims to place an Orbiter around the moon and sending a Lander and Rover to the moon's surface. The three tonne Orbiter Module is made up of a central composite cylinder, shear webs and deck panels.
