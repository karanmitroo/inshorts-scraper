Samsung has set up free solar-powered charging facilities at five of its exclusive outlets in Bengaluru. The company has installed the solar chargers on a 10-feet tall pole at CMH Road, Indiranagar, Total Mall, Sarjapura Road, Sahakaranagar, Malleshwaram and Vijaynagar. The company said it was a pilot project and did not comment on expanding the facility to other cities.
