Wikipedia, the crowd-sourced encyclopedia founded by Jimmy Wales and Larry Sanger, was launched on January 15, 2001. According to Alexa, Wikipedia, whose first words were, "Hello, world!", is the seventh-most visited website in the world. Notably, ‘George W Bush’ is the most edited article on the non-profit website with 45,862 edits and ‘India’ features at 15th position with 22,271 edits.
