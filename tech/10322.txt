Microsoft on Tuesday unveiled its first ever laptop- Surface Book, starting at $1500. The 13.5-inch laptop, with a resolution of 3000x2000 pixels, comes with a choice of 8GB or 16 GB RAM, sixth-generation Intel Core i5 or Core i7 processors, and a maximum storage capacity of 1TB. The trackpad supports five-finger multi-touch while the screen has ten-finger multi-touch support.
