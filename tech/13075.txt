On Wednesday, Motorola announced its re-entry into the Indian market with Moto G smartphone, to be launched in January-2014. The android smartphone, priced at $179 for 8GB storage model and $199 for 16GB variant in US, has a 4.5-inch 720x1280p display, 5MP camera, 1.2GHz processor with 1GB Ram. Notably, there is no option for expandable storage available.
