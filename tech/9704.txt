Telecom Minister Ravi Shankar Prasad on Thursday said that investment in the electronics manufacturing sector reached ₹1.14 lakh crore by December 2015. This is a six-fold increase from last year as investment in the said sector had reached ₹17,390 crore by December 2014. Further, while the investment in 2015 involved 159 units, the investment in 2014 had involved 53 units.
