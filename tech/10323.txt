Microsoft on Tuesday unveiled its new tablet- Surface Pro 4, starting at $899. The tablet has a 12.3-inch screen and is backed by 16GB of RAM (4GB starting) and 1TB of SSD storage (128GB starting). Further, it has a fingerprint scanner, an ethernet port, four USB 3.0 ports, 8-megapixel rear camera and offers up to 9 hours of battery life.
