Motorola has launched its Moto G in India, available on Flipkart Wednesday onwards. The 8GB variant is priced at Rs. 12,499 and 16GB variant at Rs. 13,999. Powered by a quad-core 1.2GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon 400 processor, the dual-SIM smartphone runs Android 4.3 and features 4.5-inch HD display (720 x 1280-pixel resolution), 1GB RAM, 5MP rear camera and 1.3MP front camera.
