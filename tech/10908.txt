Samsung on Thursday launched its new tablet 'Galaxy Tab 3V', an addition to its existing Galaxy Tab series, in the Indian market at a price of Rs 10,600. The 7-inch WSVGA (1024x600p) LCD display device is powered by a 1.3GHz quad-core processor and 1GB RAM. It supports 3G data and has an 8GB internal storage, 3,600mAh battery and 5MP camera.
