Samsung has launched the 2014 Edition of Galaxy Note 10.1 tablet in India priced at Rs.49,990 for the 32GB variant. It has a 10.1-inch Super clear LCD display with 2560x1600 pixels resolution. Powered by a 1.9GHz Exynos-5 Octa processor and 3GB of RAM, it runs on Android 4.3 Jelly Bean and features a 8-megapixel rear camera and an 8,220mAh battery.
