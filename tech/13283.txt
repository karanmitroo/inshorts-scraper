As per a report of Strategy Analytics, Google's OS Android has topped the world's smartphone market with 80% of handsets sold in Q2-2013 accounting to 182.7-million handsets. Apple's iPhone sales dropped to 14% and Microsoft lags behind it with only 4%. 34.6-million Android slates had been sold during that time period, which is more than double the 14.6-million iPads sold.
