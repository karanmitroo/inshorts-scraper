 To correct a congenital problem of precocious puberty, rare medical condition where children attain puberty before 8, a 3-year old underwent a successful skull-based 3-hour surgery to remove hamartoma (abnormal tissue) at JIPMER. The boy was brought to JIPMER after his parents noticed abnormal physical and behavioural changes in him. On evaluation, doctors found that he had excessive sex hormones.
