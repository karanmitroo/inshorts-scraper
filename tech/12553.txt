With the launch of Canvas Win W121 and W092, Micromax has become the first Indian smartphone-maker to introduce Windows Phone devices. The five-inch W121 is priced at Rs. 9,500, and sports a 1.2 GHz quad-core processor, 1GB RAM and 8MP camera. The W092, which is priced at Rs. 6,500, has the same spec, but a downgraded camera and small screen.
