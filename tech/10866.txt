Taiwanese smartphones manufacturer HTC on Thursday launched the Desire 820G+ dual-SIM smartphone in India, priced at Rs 19,990. The smartphone was launched in HTC's domestic market in early June. It sports a 5.5-inch display, 16GB internal memory, 13MP rear camera with LED flash and 8MP front-facing camera. The device is powered by 1.7GHz Octa-Core MediaTek MT6592 processor and 1GB RAM.
