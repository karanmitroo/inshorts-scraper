Bajaj has launched Pulsar AS150 and Pulsar AS200 under its Adventure Sports series at an introductory price of Rs 70,000 and Rs 91,550 respectively. Bikes AS150 (17ps) has rear drum brakes and 170 mm ground clearance and AS200 (23.5ps) has rear disc brakes and 167 mm ground clearance. However, both the bikes don't have ABS as an option.
