As per reports, tech company Sony will launch its recently unveiled smartphone Xperia Z4 as Xperia Z3+ in markets outside Japan. Sony is planning to make Xperia Z3+ available in May this year. The device will have 5.2-inch display, octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 810 processor, 3GB RAM, 20.7MP rear camera, 5MP selfie camera, Android 5.0 Lollipop and 2,930 mAh battery.
