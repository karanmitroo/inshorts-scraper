Hyderabad based Celkon Mobiles has launched its Ultra Q500 smartphone in India at a price of Rs. 9,999, exclusively on Snapdeal. The specifications include - 5-inch display, 1.2 GHz quad-core processor, Android 4.4 KitKat OS and 2 GB RAM. The dual SIM phone is a 3G model and is powered by a 2500 mAh.
