Spice Mobility on Thursday launched Spice Stellar 431 smartphone at Rs 3,499. The device is powered by a 1.0 GHz Dual Core processor and Android KitKat 4.4, and is equipped with a large 4-inch capacitive touch screen. The device is equipped with a 1500 mAh battery, 512 MB RAM and 4GB ROM expandable up to 32 GB.
