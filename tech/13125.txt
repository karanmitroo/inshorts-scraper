The fourth annual Global Brand Simplicity Index released by Siegel+Gale, places Amazon(2nd), Google(3rd), and Samsung(8th) in top ten simplest tech brands of 2013, with ALDI on top. The list was made after surveying more than 10,000 consumers in North America, Europe, Asia and the Middle East. Brands were evaluated based on questions related to the simplicity/complexity of products, services etc.
