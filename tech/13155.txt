Blackberry will launch its new flagship smartphone Blackberry Z30 (also known as A10 or A-Series under the codename 'aristo') on October-24 in India, to be priced below Rs.45000. It runs on company's latest 10 OS version 10.2, with a 5-inch display and 8MP rear camera.  Powered by 1.7Ghz Snapdragon S4 Pro processor, it has 2GB RAM and 16GB internal storage.
