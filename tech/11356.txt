India based mobile manufacturer Micromax on Monday launched the low budget smartphone Unite 3 Q372 with Android 5.0 Lollipop priced at Rs 6569. Powered by 1.3GHz quad-core MediaTek processor, the smartphone has a 1GB RAM along with 8GB internal memory, expandable upto 32GB. The dual-SIM device sports an 8MP rear camera with LED flash and a 2MP front-facing camera.
