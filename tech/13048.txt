After the success of its RX100 premium compact camera in India, Sony has launched a 'bridge-style' compact Cyber-shot RX10 and two high-end full frame mirror-less cameras, the Alpha 7 and Alpha 7R. While the RX10 is priced at Rs 84,990, the Alpha 7 would cost Rs 94,990 and the 7R is worth Rs 1,24,990.
