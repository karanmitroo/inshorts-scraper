Acer on Monday launched the Android-powered Liquid Jade 2 smartphone. The phone features a 5.5" AMOLED display, 3 GB of RAM, 21 MP/8 MP cameras, a USB Type-C port and is powered by a hexa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 808 processor. It offers up to 1 TB of hybrid cloud storage and will start selling in global markets in April.
