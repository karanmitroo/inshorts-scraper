Making another addition to its Aqua series, India-based electronics manufacturer Intex on Friday launched low-budget smartphone 'Aqua Q5', priced at Rs 5,090. The dual-SIM device is powered by a 1.3GHz quad-core processor with 1GB RAM support and 8GB inbuilt storage. The 3G-enabled smartphone sports a 4.5-inch display, an 8MP rear camera and a 2MP front-facing camera.
