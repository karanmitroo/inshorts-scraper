For this Christmas season, Amazon India has offered special discounts on its e-reader range -- Kindle and Kindle Paperwhite -- from Dec-20 to Dec-25. Kindle, priced at Rs.5,999, is available at a discounted price of Rs. 4,999, whereas the Paperwhite Wi-Fi model at Rs. 9,999 (down from Rs.10,999) and Paperwhite 3G model at Rs. 11,999 (down from Rs.13,999).
