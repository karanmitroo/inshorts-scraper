Huawei has launched its Honor 5X and Honor Holly 2 Plus smartphones in India at ₹12,999 and ₹8,499. Honor 5X features a 5-inch HD display, 2 GB of RAM and is powered by a 64-bit octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon processor. Honor Holly 2 Plus features a 5-inch HD display, 2 GB of RAM and is powered by a quad-core MediaTek processor.
