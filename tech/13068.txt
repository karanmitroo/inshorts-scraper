Google's Android phone, Nexus 5, and the new Nexus 7 tablet with FullHD screen are finally available on Google's Play store for Indian consumers. The 16GB model of Nexus 5 and Nexus 7 (Wi-Fi version) sells for Rs 28,999 and Rs 20,999 respectively. Nexus 5, which is made by LG, is a 4.9-inch device running on Android 4.4 aka Kitkat.
