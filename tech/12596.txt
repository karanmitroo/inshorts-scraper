As expected, Nokia has finally launched the Lumia 630 in India with two models (single  and dual SIM variants) available from May-14 onwards, starting at Rs 10,500. The Lumia 630 is the first Windows Phone 8.1 device, and is powered by a quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 400 clocked at 1.2GHz and coupled with 512MB RAM.
