US-based electronics manufacturer InFocus on Wednesday launched its new smartphone M350 with 4G-LTE support in India at Rs 7,999. The dual-SIM device that comes with a 5-inch HD display is powered by a 1.5 GHz Quad-Core MediaTek MT6732 processor with Mali-T760 GPU. It also features 2GB RAM, 16GB internal memory, 8MP rear camera with LED flash and 8MP front camera.
