US-based technology company Dell on Thursday launched its new high-end tablet, the Venue 8 7000 in India priced at Rs 34,999. At 6.1mm, it is the thinnest tablet in the world. The device sports an 8.4-inch display, 2.3GHz quad-core Intel Atom Z3500 processor with 2GB RAM. It also features 16GB internal storage and an Intel RealSense Snapshot Depth Camera.
