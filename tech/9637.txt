At the Consumer Electronics Show 2016, LG has unveiled the first smartphones of its K-series, K10 and K7, both of which are mid-range and are powered by a quad-core 1.3GHz processor. K10 (3G variant) features a 5.3-inch HD display and an 8MP camera, whereas K7 (3G variant) features a 5-inch display and either an 8MP or a 5MP camera.
