Apple has said it will charge over ₹4,600 ($69) for every lost or broken AirPod, while those with battery issues can be repaired for about ₹3,300 ($49) under warranty. Priced at $159 in the US, the wireless headphones are expected to cost over ₹15,000 in India. The launch of the Bluetooth-connected AirPods was delayed by over two months.
