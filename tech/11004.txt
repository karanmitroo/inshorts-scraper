Indian electronics manufacturer Micromax on Friday launched "Canvas Tabby P469", a dual-mode family tablet that features a specialised kids-mode. Running on Android KitKat 4.4, the Tabby is powered by a 1.3Ghz processor, 1GB RAM and 8GB ROM. Priced at Rs. 6,499, it will be available exclusively on e-commerce website Flipkart for three weeks starting June 22.
