Chinese smartphone maker Umi Mobiles, known in China as the competitor for Xiaomi, has entered the Indian market with its new smartphone, Umi Hammer. Exclusively available on Flipkart at Rs 10,999, the pone features a 5-inch display with 720p resolution. It runs on Android 4.4 KitKat out-of-the-box (upgradable to Lollipop) and has a 13MP rear and a 3MP front camera.
