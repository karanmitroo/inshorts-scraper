OnePlus, a Chinese phone company founded last year, on Wednesday announced its first Android smartphone, OnePlus One, a flagship device, loaded with good design and powerful hardware, worth just $299 (lower than even the price of Nexus-5). It is powered by 2.5GHz Snapdragon 801 quad-core processor, 3GB RAM, 16GB to 64GB storage, 5.5-inch screen (1080p) 13MP rear-camera and 5MP front-camera.
