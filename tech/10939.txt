Chinese electronics company Xiaomi on Tuesday slashed the price of 'Redmi Note 4G' in India by Rs 2,000. The device, earlier priced at Rs 9,999, is now available for Rs 7,999. The 5.5-inch display phablet is powered by 1.6GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon 400 processor coupled with 2GB RAM. The 4G LTE capable handset sports 13MP rear camera and 5MP front camera.
