Former Executive Vice President of software company LinguaNext, Narendra Nayak on Tuesday took over as Managing Director of BlackBerry India. An IIM-Bangalore alumnus with prior experience in IBM, CSC and Motorola, Nayak replaces Sunil Lalvani, who quit BlackBerry in June to join Qualcomm. Through all the channels in India, Nayak will look after the company's software and services business.
