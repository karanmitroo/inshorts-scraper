Homegrown handset maker Intex Thursday launched India's first octa-core processor powered device, Aqua Octa, priced at Rs 19,999. The processor offers a speed of 1.7GHz, enabling better video and gaming experience. The dual SIM phablet features a six-inch HD display, 2GB RAM, 13MP rear camera, 5MP front camera, 16GB internal memory (expandable upto 32GB) and runs Android 4.2 Jelly Bean.
