Google on Friday launched Xolo Chromebook and Nexian Air Chromebook in India. Both the Chromebooks have 11.6-inch display and are priced at Rs 12,999. The devices are powered by 1.8GHz Rockchip RK3288 Quad-Core Cortex-A17 processor with ARM Mali-T764 GPU, 2GB DDR3 RAM and 16GB internal storage. Both are packed with 4200mAh battery with upto 10 hours of working battery life.
