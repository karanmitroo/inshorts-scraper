As per a forecast of IDC, Microsoft's share of the tablet market would grow from 5.1% in 2014 to 14.1% in 2019. Android is expected to retain around 62.9% of the market in 2019. IDC said it expects tablet sales growth of just 2.1% this year, after an increase of 4.4% in 2014 and more than 50% in 2013.
