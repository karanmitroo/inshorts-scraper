After Snapchat went for IPO on Thursday, its Senior Vice President of Engineering, Timothy Sehn gained ₹334 crore from his stake in Snapchat. While Chairman Michael Lynton gained ₹147 crore, Snapchat's Chief Strategy Officer Imran Khan pocketed ₹140 crore. Snapchat's IPO is the biggest IPO by a technology driven company since Alibaba went public in 2014.
