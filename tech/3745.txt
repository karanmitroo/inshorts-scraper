Canada-based company Dbrand, which makes personalised skins for gadgets like smartphones and laptops, has unveiled a skin for iPhone 7 and 7 Plus priced at ₹650 to make them look similar to the newly-unveiled Red edition by Apple. The limited-edition Red iPhone 7 and 7 Plus are available in 128 GB and 256 GB options, starting from ₹70,000 in India.
