The iPhone 8 and 8 Plus, which come with glass back and wireless charging technology, will be available in India from September 29 with a starting price of ₹64,000 and ₹73,000 respectively. The iPhone X, which has an edge-to-edge display, face recognition, and wireless charging technology, will be available from November 3 at ₹89,000 (64 GB) and ₹1,02,000 (256 GB).
