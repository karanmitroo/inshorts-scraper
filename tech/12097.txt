Dell has launched its latest Android tablets- the Dell Venue 7 and Dell Venue 8- in India. Equipped with voice calling and 3G, both the tablets feature a 4,550 mAh battery, the latest dual-core Intel Processor, and Android 4.4 KitKat.  While Venue 7 is priced at Rs 14,999, Venue 8 will be available for Rs 18,999.
