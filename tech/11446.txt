India based electronics company iBall on Monday launched a new smartphone Andi 5F Infinito with a powerful battery of 4000mAh, priced at Rs 7,999. The 5-inch qHD capacitive display smartphone has a 1.3 GHz quad-core cortex A7 processor along with a 1GB RAM. It also comes with 8GB internal storage, 8MP rear camera with LED flash and 3.2MP selfie camera.
