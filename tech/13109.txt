After Galaxy Golden and W789, Samsung has unveiled its third Android flip phone this year -- W2014, without revealing its pricing and availability details. W2014 has two 3.7-inch Super AMOLED touschscreen displays, 13MP rear camera, dual-SIM support, 32GB inbuilt storage and 1900mAh battery. It runs Android 4.3 Jelly Bean and is powered by a quad-core 2.3GHz processor with 2GB RAM.
