Samsung on Friday launched two new Galaxy smartphones under its J-series, the Galaxy J5 and the Galaxy J7, in China. While J5 comes with a 5-inch display and 1.2GHz quad-core processor, J7 features a 5.5-inch display and an octa-core (1.4GHz + 1GHz) processor. The former is priced at CNY 1,398 (Rs 14,360) and the latter costs CNY 1,798 (Rs 18,468).
