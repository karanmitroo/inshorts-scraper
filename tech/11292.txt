Smartphone maker YU, a sub-brand of electronics manufacturer Micromax, launched its new smartphone Yuphoria on Tuesday at Rs 6,999. The dual-SIM device runs on Android Lollipop (Cyanogen OS 12) and is powered by 1.2GHz Snapdragon 410 quad-core 64-bit processor coupled with 2GB RAM. It sports a 4.7-inch HD display, 8MP rear camera, 5MP selfie camera, 16GB storage and 2,230mAh battery.
