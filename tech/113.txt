Apple Co-founder Steve Wozniak has said while Steve Jobs could "still deliver sometimes on his promises", Tesla Co-founder Elon Musk "never does". Calling Musk a "very good salesman", Wozniak said, "I like Elon Musk very much... I like him for imagining things no one has ever." Wozniak also credited Musk with "reimagining the entire electric vehicle space".
