Motorola Tuesday launched the 3G variant of the 2nd-generation Moto E smartphone in India priced at Rs 6,999. The device is powered by 1.2GHz quad-core processor, sports a 4.5-inch display and runs on Android 5.0 Lollipop operating system. It also comes with a VGA selfie camera, 5MP rear camera, 1GB RAM and 8GB inbuilt storage, expandable up to 32GB.
