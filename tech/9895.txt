Motorola has launched its Moto G Turbo Edition smartphone in India for ₹14,499. The phone, currently exclusively on Flipkart, features a five-inch HD display, 2GB of RAM and a 13-megapixel camera and runs Qualcomm’s Snapdragon 615 octa-core processor. Further, with its quick charge support, the device claims to charge for up to six hours of usage in 15 minutes.
