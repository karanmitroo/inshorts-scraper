BSNL has launched its WiFi facility at the on-going Ardhkumbh mela at Har-ki-Pauri ghats in Haridwar, with first 15 minutes of free usage. The government-owned company has installed nine axis points at Har-ki-Pauri, each covering an area of 200 meters. BSNL said, “We will see the response of people and gradually extend the facility across all the ghats of Haridwar”.
