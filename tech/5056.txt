Amazon, which started as an online book retailer, has announced Harry Potter and the Cursed Child as the best-selling book of 2016. The playscript by JK Rowling, Jack Thorne and John Tiffany, was also named this year's 'Most Wished For' and 'Most Gifted' book. Three Harry Potter-related books have been included in 2016's 'Kids and Teens' bestsellers list.
