Lenovo on Wednesday unveiled three laptops- Lenovo Z41, Lenovo Z51 and Ideapad 100. The 14-inch Z41 and 15-inch Z51 are powered by Intel-Core i7 CPU while the IdeaPad 100 comes with Intel N3540 processor. The Z51 also features an Intel RealSense 3D-camera. These laptops will be available from June with starting price $499 (Z41, Z51) and $249 (IdeaPad 100).
