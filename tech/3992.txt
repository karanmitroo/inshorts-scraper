Yahoo's CEO Marissa Mayer has taken a pay cut of ₹93 crore over the world's largest data breach affecting a billion Yahoo accounts in 2013 and 500 million accounts in 2014. Mayer refused cash bonus of ₹13 crore from 2016 and ₹80 crore stock award for 2017. The hack of another 32 million accounts was disclosed by Yahoo on Wednesday.
