Having completed three years in India, Oneplus has announced 10,000 complimentary tickets for 'Star Wars: The Last Jedi'. The tickets will be available on December 13 and 15 on oneplusstore.in and Paytm. It is the first of several engagements planned for the OnePlus community to experience the OnePlus 5T Star Wars Limited Edition which goes on sale on December 15 on Amazon.in and oneplusstore.in.
