Panasonic on Tuesday launched a new dual-SIM smartphone in India, the Eluga Z, priced at Rs 13,490. The 5-inch display device sports a 13-megapixel rear camera with LED flash, and a 5-megapixel front facing camera. It is powered by a 1.4GHz octa-core processor coupled with 2GB of RAM and 16GB of inbuilt storage. It also has a 'metal blade' design.
