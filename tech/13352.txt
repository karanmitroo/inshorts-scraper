After over a year-long wait for the full-time director of IIT-KGP, protests have started to emerge among the students, alumni and faculties, against the delay which is affecting several important programs in the institute. Partha Pratim Chakrabarti was chosen by MHRD for the position of Director, IIT-Kharagpur, over a-year back, but he still awaits clearance by Central Vigilance Commission(CVC).
