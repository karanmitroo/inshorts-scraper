Japanese electronics company Panasonic on Monday launched its 4G-enabled Eluga L smartphone in India priced at Rs 12,990. The 5-inch (720X1080p) IPS HD display device is powered by a 64-bit 1.2GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon 410 quad-core processor paired with 1GB RAM. Packed with 8GB internal memory, it also sports an 8MP primary camera and 5MP selfie camera with 'blink play' feature.
