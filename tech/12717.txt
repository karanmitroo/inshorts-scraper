Lenovo has introduced a new range of Android-powered tablets - its new A-series. While the A7-30 and A7-50 are 7-inch tablets, A8 measures 8-inches and A10 features a 10.1-inch wide-view HD display and is said to be made "for movie lovers." They are priced at Rs 10,000- 17,000 (UK pricing) and will be launched in Q2 2014.
