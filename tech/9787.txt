Establishing an international brand in India, Spice Mobiles has launched the Nexian NV-45 smartphone for ₹3,799 in the country. The dual-SIM smartphone features a 4.5-inch display, 1GB of RAM, a 5MP rear camera and runs a 1.2GHz Spreadtrum Quad-core processor. The device is exclusively available on Flipkart in two color variants of Black and Grey.
