Low-cost tablet PC Aakash 4 will be available in the market within two months for Rs 3,999, said Telecom Minister Kapil Sibal on Monday. The tablet comes with a 7-inch scratch resistant touch screen, 2G, 3G and 4G connectivity, 4GB internal storage, front camera, etc. Brainchild of Sibal, the project was to provide low-priced device at subsidised rate to students.
