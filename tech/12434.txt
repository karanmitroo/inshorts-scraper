Reliance Digital, a venture of Reliance Group, launched the Reconnect Quad Core 4.7" dual SIM 3G smartphone on Thursday. Priced at Rs. 12,999, the phone has quad core 1.3 GHz Cortex A7 processor, with 1GB RAM and 8GB internal memory. The handset, weighing 132.8 gm, runs on Android 4.2.2 Jelly Bean operating system, and is available in white and black.
