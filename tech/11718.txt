Aiming to step into affordable smartphone market, Sony Tuesday launched Xperia E4 with an exceptionally long battery life. The company claims the handset's 2,300mAh battery can deliver two days of "active use" on a single charge. The phone has a 5-inch display, 1.3GHz quad-core MediaTek processor paired with 1GB RAM, 8GB internal storage, 5MP rear camera and 2MP front camera.
