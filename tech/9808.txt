The prices of iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus smartphones have reportedly been slashed in India by up to 16%. Retailers including Amazon, Flipkart and Snapdeal are offering price cuts on both devices across all variants. The price of iPhone 6s (16GB variant), initially launched at ₹62,000, is listed at ₹48,499 on Flipkart and ₹51,999 on Apple’s premium reseller Infibeam.
