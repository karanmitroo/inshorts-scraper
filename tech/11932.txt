Actor Amitabh Bachchan has topped Twitter's popularity list, leaving behind all other actors of the industry. Big B also came out as the most active Bollywood celebrity of 2014 with an average of 20 tweets, re-tweets and replies per day. The 72-yr-old has 12 million followers on Twitter, while superstar Shah Rukh Khan, 2nd on list, has 10.4 million followers.
