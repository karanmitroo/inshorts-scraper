German car-maker Audi on Thursday launched the new Audi TT Coupe in India at Rs 60.34 lakh. The car's 230 horsepower engine accelerates it from zero to 100 km an hour in 5.3 seconds. "Re-engineered for more power, the all-new Audi TT Coupe blends dynamic aesthetics with innovative technology and inspired performance," Audi India head Joe King said.
