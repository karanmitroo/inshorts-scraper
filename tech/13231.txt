Sales of smartphones with screen-sizes from 5-inch to 6.9-inch rose 17-times from 2012  for second-quarter ended June-13, according to research-firm IDC. Also, a global handset-maker official said contribution of 5-inch smartphones in 25-million smartphone market has grown from 3% in 2012 to 20% in 2013. Reportedly, Apple also plans to offer 4.8-6-inch iPhones.
