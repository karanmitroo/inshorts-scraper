Joining the likes of Lenovo, Micromax has launched its large-battery ( 4000mAh) smartphone - Canvas Power - for Indian market at Rs. 9,900. The dual-SIM phone reportedly delivers up-to 5.5 hours of talktime and 450 hours of standby time. The other features include Android 4.1 Jelly Bean OS, 1.3GHz quad-core processor, 512MB RAM, 4GB inbuilt storage and 5MP camera.
