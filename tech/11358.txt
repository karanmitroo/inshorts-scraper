China's biggest phone manufacturer Xiaomi Wednesday, unveiled the new Mi Note Pro smartphone at an event in Beijing. The phone has a 3D curved back glass, comes with a 13MP rear camera with optical image stabilization (OIS) and two-tone flash. The specifications of the 4G phablet include 5.7-inch display (2560x1440p), Qualcomm Snapdragon 810 64-bit 8-core-processor, 4GB RAM and 64GB storage.
