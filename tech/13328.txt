India on Friday successfully launched its advanced meteorological spacecraft INSAT-3D by a European rocket, ARIANE-5, from Kourou in French Guiana. The INSAT-3D, which has a lift-off mass of 2060 kg, is aimed to enhance the country's capability in weather forecasting and disaster warning services for the next seven years. ARIANE-5 rocket also launched Alphasat, Europe's largest telecommunication satellite ever manufactured.
