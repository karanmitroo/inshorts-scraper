In a bid to intensify competition in big-size smartphone segment, Google is reportedly all set to release its next phablet ‘Nexus 6’ with a 5.9-inch screen this month. The new Nexus, in all likelihood, will come running Android L, the next version of Google's mobile OS. The phone has Quad-core 2.7 GHz snapdragon processor, 3GB of RAM, and 12MP camera.
