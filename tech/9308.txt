Japanese electronics company Panasonic launched the HD5 and BTD5 headphones in India on Monday. The HD5 is wired, and sports a 40mm HD audio driver unit that delivers high-resolution audio. The BTD5 is Bluetooth-enabled and comes with NFC technology. The Panasonic HD5 and BTD5 headphones have been priced at ₹9,490 and ₹6,490 respectively, and will be available across channels.
