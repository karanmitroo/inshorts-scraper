HTC on Tuesday launched Desire 326G smartphone in its desire series in India. The smartphone will be available at major retail stores across the country from the second half of May. However, the company didn't reveal the price of the device. The dual-SIM 4.5-inch display smartphone has 1.2GHz quad-core Spreadtrum SC7731G processor, 1GB RAM, 8MP camera and 8GB internal storage.
