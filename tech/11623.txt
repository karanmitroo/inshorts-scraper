Mobile manufacturer Xolo Tuesday launched its smartphone "Q1001" in India priced at Rs 6,199. The dual SIM smartphone runs on Android KitKat, has a 5-inch display and is powered by a 1.3GHz quad-core processor coupled with 512MB RAM. The device sports a 5MP camera with LED flash, a 0.3MP selfie camera and comes with 4GB inbuilt storage, expandable upto 32GB.
