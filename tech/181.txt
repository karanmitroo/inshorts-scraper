A website named 'whereisroadster.com' is tracking Elon Musk's Tesla Roadster sent into space. According to the website, the Roadster, moving at a speed of around 10,800 kmph, is currently 3.3 million km from Earth and over 222 million km from Mars. The website also states the car has exceeded its 57,936 km warranty 573.6 times while circling the Sun.
