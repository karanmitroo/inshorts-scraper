Nokia accidentally revealed the specifications of its biggest upcoming smartphone, the 6-inch Lumia 1520, on Tmall store - Nokia's official Internet marketplace for the Chinese market. Lumia-1520 will be the company's first phablet with quad-core processor (Snapdragon 800) and sport a full-HD display. The listing also says the device will feature a 20MP PureView camera and cost 4,999 yuan ($819).
