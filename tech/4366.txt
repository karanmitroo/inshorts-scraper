Global smartphone manufacturer Vivo is back with its ‘Vivo Super Day’ where users will be able to grab the newly-launched Vivo V5Plus at retail stores, which started on Wednesday. The smartphone launched for ₹27,980 features 20 MP and 8 MP dual front cameras, along with a “moonlight selfie” flash, which allows users to add DSLR-like bokeh effects to their selfies.
