The luxury sports car maker, Automobili Lamborghini Holding SpA, Monday launched the Lamborghini Huracan LP 610-4 for the Indian market at a price of Rs 3.43 cr. The two-seater supercar reaches a top speed of over 325km/h and accelerates from 0-100 km/h in 3.2 seconds. Huracan had made its global debut at the Geneva (Switzerland) Motorshow in March.
