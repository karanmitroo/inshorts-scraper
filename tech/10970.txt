Mumbai-based electronics company iBall on Monday launched its Andi Avonte 5 smartphone with an 8MP rotating camera with dual-LED flash for Rs 5,999. The 5-inch IPS display smartphone is powered by 1.3 GHz quad-core processor coupled with 1GB RAM and 8GB internal memory. The dual-SIM device runs on Android 4.4 (KitKat) and sports a 2150mAh battery.
