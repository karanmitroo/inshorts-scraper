Maruti Suzuki on Wednesday launched a new car, the S-Cross, in its premium vehicles category. The car will be offered at a starting price of Rs 8.34 lakh which goes up to Rs 13.74 lakh. S-Cross will compete with Ford EcoSport, Renault Duster and Nissan Terrano in the automobile market. It is available in 1.3 and 1.6-litre diesel variants.
