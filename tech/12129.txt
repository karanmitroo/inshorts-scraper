Spice has officially listed two new smartphones on its website in India, the Stellar 517 and Stellar 470, both priced at Rs 7,999. They feature dual-SIM (GSM+GSM) support and run on Android 4.4 KitKat out-of-the-box. Stellar 517's specifications match with the recently launched Stellar 518, except for a minor deviation in the front-facing camera and the handset's battery.
