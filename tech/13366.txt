Indian smartphone maker Micromax launched its flagship device, Micromax Canvas 4, on Monday priced at Rs. 17,999. The 5.0-inch HD (720x1280) display smartphone is powered by a 1.2GHz quad-core processor alongside 1GB RAM and 16GB internal storage. It comes with a 13-megapixel rear camera, 5-megapixel front camera and runs Android 4.2.1 Jelly Bean.
