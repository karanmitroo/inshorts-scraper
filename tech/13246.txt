Within two weeks of its global unveiling, Sony has unveiled Xperia -Z1 in India, priced at Rs 44,990. The key properties include a 5-inch LCD display, 2.2GHz quad-core Snapdragon 800 processor, 2GB RAM and 20.7MP camera with 27mm wide-angle G Lens optics, first for any Smartphone. This marks the Japanese manufacturer's seventh product launch in India since April this year.
