Electronics manufacturer Lenovo launched its midrange phablet "K3 Note" in China market on Tuesday, priced at CNY 899 (approximately Rs 9,000). It is a 4G LTE enabled dual-SIM device with 5.5-inch display. It runs on 1.5GHz octa-core MediaTek MT6752 processor coupled with 2GB RAM. It has 16GB internal storage, 13MP rear camera with LED flash and 5MP front camera.
