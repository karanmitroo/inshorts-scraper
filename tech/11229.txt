Apple on Tuesday announced the launch of new 15-inch MacBook Pro and 27-inch iMac in India. The new MacBook Pro features Retina display and Force Touch trackpad with built-in force sensors and is available starting at Rs 159,900. The iMac starts at Rs 179,900 and has features like Retina 5K display and 3.5 GHz quad-core Intel Core i5 processor.
