Alcatel OneTouch Thursday introduced its octa-core selfie smartphone - Flash - at a price of Rs 9,999. The specifications are - 5.5-inch HD IPS display, Android 4.4.2 KitKat OS, 1.4 GHz octa-core processor, 1 GB RAM, 13 MP Full HD main camera and 5 MP 1080p front camera. The phone will be available exclusively on Flipkart starting November 21.
