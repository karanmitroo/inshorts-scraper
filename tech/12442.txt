Panasonic has launched a 5-inch smartphone, Eluga A, for Rs. 9,490 in India. The phone, which is an addition to the Eluga smartphone series, is based on a quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 200 processor, and runs on Android 4.3 Jelly Bean. It has a 1GB RAM and a 4GB ROM, expandable upto 32GB, and is available in 2 colours.
