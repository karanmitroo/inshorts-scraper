Chinese electronics giant Xiaomi launched Monday its tablet "MiPad," priced at Rs 12,999, in India. The 7.9-inch device, powered by a 2.2GHz processor and paired with 2GB RAM, will go on sale on March 24, for which registration is not required. It also has 8MP rear camera and 5MP front-facing camera along with 16GB internal storage, expandable up to 128GB.
