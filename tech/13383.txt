Online statistics portal Statista, which chronicled the iPhone's 6-years long journey, reveals that more than 356-million iPhones have been sold since it became available on 29-June-2007. The iPhone was single-handedly responsible for generating half of Apple's revenue in Q2, 2013. In fact, with $22.96-billion revenue in Q1, 2013, Apple's iPhone business alone is larger than giants like Microsoft($20.49-billion) and Coca-Cola($11.04-billion).
