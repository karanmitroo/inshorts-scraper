India-based smartphones manufacturer Karbonn on Wednesday launched its new budget smartphone 'Titanium Mach One Plus', under its Titanium series in India, at Rs 6,990. The smartphone that supports 22 Indian languages sports a 4.7-inch display. It is powered by a 1.3GHz quad-core processor and 2GB RAM. It also features 16GB internal storage, 8MP rear camera and 5MP front-facing camera.
