The new Sony Xperia Z3+ smartphone was Friday launched in India at Rs 55,990. Identical to the Japan-only Xperia Z4 model, the smartphone is a minor upgrade from the Xperia Z3. Apart from being slimmer and lighter, it also sports the new Qualcomm Snapdragon 810 SoC. The phone comes with Android 5.0 (Lollipop) and features a 20.7-megapixel auto-focus rear camera.
