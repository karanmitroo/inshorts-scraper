India-based electronics manufacturer Micromax on Tuesday launched the Canvas Nitro 2 E311 smartphone priced at Rs 10,399. It sports a 5-inch IPS display, a 13MP auto-focus rear camera with LED Flash and a 5MP front-facing camera. The device is powered by 1.4GHz Octa-Core MediaTek MT6592M processor with Mali 450 GPU, 2GB RAM and 16GB of built-in memory.
