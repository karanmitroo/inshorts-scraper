Harley Davidson has launched three new bikes in India. The first, 2015 Breakout, has a 21-inch front wheel and is available for Rs 16.28 lakh. The Street Glide Special is the second one. Priced at Rs 29.7 lakh, it has a 6.5-inch touch screen entertainment system. The final one, the CVO Limited, comes with one of the largest Harley engines.
