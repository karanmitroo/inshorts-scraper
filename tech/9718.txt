Karbonn has launched two budget smartphones, Titanium S205 2GB and Titanium Moghul, in India for ₹6,790 and ₹5,790, respectively. Both the smartphones are dual-SIM, feature an 8MP camera and run a 1.2GHz quad-core processor. While the Titanium Moghul features a 5-inch qHD display and 1GB of RAM, the Titanium S205 2GB features a 5-inch HD display and 2GB of RAM.
