Spice Retail has launched its first smartwatch - Smart Pulse M 9010 - which features dual-SIM support along with voice calling. Priced at Rs. 4,999 (MRP), the smartwatch will be available with an MOP of Rs. 3,999 via HomeShop18 from July 11. It also features a 1.5-inch touchscreen and runs the company's proprietary Java-based operating system.
