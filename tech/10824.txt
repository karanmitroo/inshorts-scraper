OnePlus on Tuesday launched the 'OnePlus 2' smartphone in India, which will go on sale August 11 onwards. Its 16GB variant is priced at Rs 22,999 and 64GB variant costs Rs 24,999. It is powered by a 1.8GHz 64-bit Qualcomm Snapdragon 810 SoC and runs on OxygenOS. It also comes with a Type-C USB charging port and fingerprint sensor.
