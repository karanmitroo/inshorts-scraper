Microsoft has announced the launch of its cheapest internet-ready phone, Nokia 215. Priced at Rs 1850, the phone aims to introduce first-time mobile phone buyers to the internet. Available in both single-SIM and dual-SIM variants, Nokia 215 features a 2.4-inch display, VGA camera, Nokia Series 30+ operating system, 1100mAh battery. It comes with pre-installed Twitter, Facebook and Messenger.
