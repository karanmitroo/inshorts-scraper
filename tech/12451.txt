Samsung has unveiled the Galaxy start 2 Plus smartphone in India, at a price of Rs. 7,335. The budget phone, launched to compete against Moto E, Micromax Unite 2 and their likes, is an Android 4.4 (KitKat) handset. With a 4.3-inch screen, 1.2GHz processor, 512MB RAM and Samsung's TouchWiz skin on top, it is to be available for sale, soon.
