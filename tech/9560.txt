France-based video game developer Ubisoft released 'Assassin's Creed Chronicles: India' for PC, Xbox One, and PS4 platforms on Tuesday. This was the second release of the company's three-game series 'Assassin's Creed Chronicles'. The plot line of the game takes place in 19th century India during the dominance of the British Empire and revolves around an assassin named Arbaaz Mir.
