According to password managing firm SplashData, numeric combination '123456' has been ranked first on the 'Worst Passwords of 2017' list. While 'password' was the second most popular password, other numeric passwords like '12345678' and '12345' ranked at third and fifth place respectively. Words including 'qwerty', 'starwars', 'admin', 'welcome' and 'login' also made it to the list.
