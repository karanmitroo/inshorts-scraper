Gionee has launched another 'super sleek' smartphone Elife S7 with 5.5 mm thickness. The phone features 5.2-inch Super AMOLED display, the sensor array and Gorilla Glass Class 3 coating to guard against scratches. It runs Amigo 3.0 based on Android 5.0 Lollipop and has 13MP rear camera and an 8MP front-facing camera.
