After its official listing on the company's website, Micromax Bolt A064 is now available in India on some e-commerce websites at Rs 3301. The smartphone comes with dual-SIM support (GSM+GSM) and runs the recent Android 4.4.2 KitKat out-of-the-box. It features a 3.5-inch (320x480 pixels) HVGA display. Bolt A064 has no 3G connectivity onboard, but support 2G EDGE networks.
