Domestic phone maker, Celkon, has launched the cheapest Windows Phone 8.1 smartphone - Win 400 - in India at Rs 4,979. The specifications are - 4-inch display (800x480p), 1.2GHz quad-core Snapdragon 200 processor and 512MB RAM. The Dual-SIM phone comes with 4GB storage and a microSD card slot that supports cards of up to 32GB.
