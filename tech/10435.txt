Google is holding an event on September 29 that will be live-streamed through its channel on YouTube. The company is expected to launch its Nexus 5 (2015) and Nexus 6 (2015) smartphones at the event, built by LG and Huawei respectively. Google is also rumoured to unveil its Android 6.0 Marshmallow operating system and the second version of its media streaming device Chromecast.
