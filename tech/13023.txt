Micromax has launched dual-SIM smartphone Canvas Blaze, which includes one SIM slot supporting MTS' 3G equivalent CDMA network and another for GSM SIM, for about Rs 11,000. Powered by 1GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon processor, the 5-inch Blaze comes with Android Jelly Bean, 8 megapixel rear camera with flash, expandable memory of up to 32GB and 2GB of internet usage for 6-months.
