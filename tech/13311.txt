Doctors at the American Oncology Institute, Hyderabad, removed a 10-kg cancerous tumour after an eight-hour marathon surgery, from the hip of 72-year-old Pardha Saradhi on July-15. For the past 5-years, Pardha had been suffering from synovial sarcoma - a rare cancer . Earlier, several doctors said Pardha that amputation from the hip was the only way to save his life.
