Apple on Wednesday launched the ninth generation of its flagship phone- iPhone 6s and 6s Plus- at the 'Hey Siri' event. The phones, powered by third generation 64-bit A9 processor with M9 co-processor, have a 5MP FaceTime HD camera with retina flash, 12MP iSight camera and 4K video recording. They also have a Force Touch-enabled display with ‘3D Touch’.
