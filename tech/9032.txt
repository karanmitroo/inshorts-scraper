At the Mobile World Congress 2016, HP launched the business-centric Elite x3 smartphone. The Windows 10 Mobile-powered device features a 5.9" WQHD display, 4 GB of RAM, 16 MP/8 MP cameras and is powered by a Snapdragon 820 SoC processor. The phone can also be converted into an ultrabook to sport a bigger display, speakers, a trackpad and a keyboard.
