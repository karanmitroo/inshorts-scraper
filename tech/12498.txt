After Bharti Airtel launched 4G services in 5 states of India, Aircel Ltd followed suit by offering them in Andhra Pradesh, Assam, Bihar and Odisha. The telecom service provider has a 20 MHz of spectrum in the 4G LTE 2300 MHz band across eight circles, including the north-east and Jammu and Kashmir, and a pan-India spectrum across all 22 circles.
