A number of high-profile websites will be taking part in an online protest on July-4 against the NSA's surveillance of online activity and phone calls. The protest, which is organized by non-profit organization Fight for the Future, will see participation from thousands of sites, including WordPress.org, Namecheap, Reddit, 4chan, Mozilla, Fark, TOR, Cheezburger, Demand Progress, MoveOn, and EFF, among others.
