Micromax has launched a compact version of the company's flagship phone, Canvas Turbo, named Canvas Turbo Mini at Rs 14,490. The 4.7-inch android-based smartphone is powered by a 1.3GHz quad-core processor, 1GB RAM, 8MP rear camera, 5MP front camera and 4GB internal storage (expandable up to 32GB). The dual-sim handset is available in black, white and blue colours.
