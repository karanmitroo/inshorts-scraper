Sony on Monday unveiled the ‘Xperia Tablet Z2’ - measuring 6.4 mm (thickness) and 426 gm (weight) - at the Mobile World Congress in Barcelona. Sony claims that it is the world's thinnest and lightest tablet. The 10.1-inch Android tablet has 2.3GHz quad-core processor, 3GB RAM and 8MP camera. The waterproof tablet will be available globally from March.
