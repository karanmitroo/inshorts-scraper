US President Donald Trump owns 3,643 website domain names, according to CNN Money, which investigated 20 years of internet records. Only 450 domains including TrumpEmpire.com, TrumpOrganization.com, and TrumpBuilding.org are for official and business purposes. Trump also owns domains like VoteAgainstTrump.com, TrumpFraud.com, and NoMoreTrump.com to keep them out of the possession of his critics.
