Google's Nexus 5X smartphone took a price cut of ₹7,000 and costs ₹24,990 now, as opposed to ₹31,990 at its launch for its 16GB version. The phone's 32GB version's price was also reduced, with its new price at ₹31,500, ₹4,000 less compared to its launch price. The cost of the Nexus 6P, however, remained unchanged.
