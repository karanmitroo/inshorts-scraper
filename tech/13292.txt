In a major step initiated to promote 20 Indian Institutes of Information Technology(IIITs), five new IIITs at Chittoor in Andhra Pradesh, Kota in Rajasthan, Tiruchirapalli in Tamil Nadu, Guwahati and Vadodara have started functioning from the current academic session, said the HRD ministry. Five more IIITs in Kerala, Himachal, MP, Tripura and Karnataka are expected to start from next year.
