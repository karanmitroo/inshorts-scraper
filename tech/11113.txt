Hewlett-Packard launched a new Deskjet 3636 All-in-one printer priced at $64 (approx Rs 3800) at the HP Innovation Day in Singapore. The new Laser-jet printer comes with jet-intelligence technology that gives 33% more prints per cartridge and consumes 53% less energy. Compatible with a mobile environment, the unique bowl-shaped printer is also 40% smaller than a regular printer.
