Honda will recall 1.39 million Accord and Civic sedans due to  faulty front passenger-side airbags manufactured by Japan's Takata, the company said on Monday. These include 2001-2005 Civic sedans and 2003-2007 Accord sedans sold in the US. With this move, the total number of Accord and Civic sedans recalled due to faulty airbags will reach 2.3 million.
