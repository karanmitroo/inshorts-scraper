Volvo on Wednesday launched the new version of its luxury hatchback V40, priced up to Rs 27.7 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi). The car will be available in two variants: V40 Kinetic priced at Rs 24.75 lakh and V40 R-Design tagged at Rs 27.7 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi). The new version boasts of several new high-tech features along with safety and support systems.
