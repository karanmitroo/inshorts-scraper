Filmmaker Kabir Khan's movie Tubelight, starring Salman Khan, has become the first Bollywood movie to have its own character emoji on Twitter. Kabir shared the news, to which Salman replied, "Twitter ko full light kar dega ab yeh #TubelightKiEid emoji!" The film, set in the backdrop of the Sino-Indian war of 1962, is scheduled to release on June 23.
