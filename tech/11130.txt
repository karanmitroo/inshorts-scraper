Phicomm, one of China's leading mobile brands, on Wednesday launched its first smartphone, Passion 660 in India. The dual-sim smartphone runs Android KitKat and sports 5-inch (1080x1920p) full HD display. It is powered by a 64-bit octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon processor coupled with 2GB RAM. Priced at Rs. 10,999, the smartphone will be exclusively available via e-commerce site Amazon from Tuesday.
