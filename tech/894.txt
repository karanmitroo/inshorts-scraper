Technology giant Google has revealed that only 0.5% of Android devices are running the latest version of its Android operating system, Oreo. While Android 7.0 and 7.1 have a combined 23.3% share, Android Marshmallow and Android Lollipop have shares of 29.7% and 26.3% respectively. Google introduced features like picture-in-picture mode as part of Android Oreo, which was launched in August.
