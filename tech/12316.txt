HTC on Tuesday launched new smartphones - Desire 816G, Desire 820 and Desire 820q in India. The 816G Dual SIM priced at Rs.18,990 will be available in October. The Desire 820, available in two variants, is the first 64-bit Smartphone in India which will ship around November. Both the smartphones, Desire 820 and Desire 820q, have a 5.5-inch 720p display.
