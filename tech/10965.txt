Intex on Friday launched a new smartphone 'Aqua Extreme 2' in its Aqua series in India, priced at Rs 9,590. The 5-inch display device has a dual-SIM support system and is powered by a 1.4GHz octa-core MediaTek MT6592M processor coupled with 2GB of RAM. It also features a 13-megapixel rear camera with LED flash and a 5-megapixel front-facing camera.
