Tech giant Samsung has launched its NX mini interchangeable lens camera - possibly the lightest and thinnest camera featuring interchangeable lenses - in India. The camera, which was introduced in March, is priced at at Rs 22,990 when coupled with the NX-M9mm F3.5 ED lens, and at Rs 27,490 with the NX-M9-27mm F3.5-5.6 ED OIS lens. It is available exclusively at Amazon.
