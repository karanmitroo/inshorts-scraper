Google on Saturday announced the new Samsung Chromebook for India at Rs. 26,990. The Chromebook, weighing 1.1kg, comes with a 11.6 inch screen, 1.7GHz Exynos 5 dual processor, 2GB of DDR3L RAM, and 16GB of SSD. The company has already released Chromebooks from HP (for Rs. 26,990) and Acer ( for Rs. 22,999) in India recently.
