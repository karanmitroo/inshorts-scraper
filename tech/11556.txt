Smartphone manufacturer HTC Monday unveiled its latest phablet device 'HTC One E9+' in China. The 5.5-inch quad HD display smartphone has a half-metal and half-plastic body along with 1080p resolution and 2GB RAM. It also comes with a 20MP primary camera 2GHz octa-core processor, 16GB internal storage (expandable up to 128GB). However, HTC didn't reveal its price and release date.
