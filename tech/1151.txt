OnePlus is connecting with its fans in a unique way by live streaming the OnePlus 5T launch in PVR Theaters in five cities namely Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Pune. Tickets for the screening were sold out, soon after going live. Those who missed it can still watch the live-stream of the event on November 16 at 9:30 PM on www.oneplusstore.in/event.
