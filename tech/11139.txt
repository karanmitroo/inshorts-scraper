Taiwan-based electronics manufacturer HTC on Thursday launched Desire 326G dual-SIM smartphone in the Indian market priced at Rs 9,590. The 4.5-inch FWVGA display device is powered by a 1.2GHz quad-core Spreadtrum SC7731G processor with Mali-400 GPU, along with 1GB RAM. It runs on Android KitKat and features 8GB internal storage, 8MP rear camera with LED flash and 2MP front camera.
