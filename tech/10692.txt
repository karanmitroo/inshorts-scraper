Chinese smartphone-maker Xiaomi on Thursday announced the successors to the Redmi Note Phablet, in the Redmi Note 2 and Redmi Note Prime. The phones will be the first with Xiaomi's latest MiUi-7 user-interface based on Android Lollipop. The Redmi Note 2 will have 16 and 32GB storage variants, while the Redmi Note Prime will only have 32 GB storage.
