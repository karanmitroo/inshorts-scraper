Chinese electronics giant Lenovo launched its smartphone 'Lenovo P70' in the Indian market, priced at Rs 15,999. The pre-orders for the handset have begun and shipping will start April 14 onwards. The 5-inch HD display phone is powered by 1.7GHz octa-core MediaTek processor coupled with 2GB RAM and runs on Android 4.4 KitKat. It has a powerful battery of 4,000mAh.
