Android Jelly Bean (both v4.1 and v4.2) with a market share of 37.9% has become the most popular Android version surpassing Gingerbread (v2.3) which accounts for 34.1% market share. With 23% share, Ice Cream Sandwich (v4.0) is at third position. Gingerbread was first released in December-2010 while Android-4.1 and Android-4.2 were released in June and November 2012 respectively.
