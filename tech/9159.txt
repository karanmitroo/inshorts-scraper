The Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation (KSRTC) will offer free Wi-Fi facility at 144 major bus stations of Karnataka including in Bengaluru, Mandya, Mysuru, Mangaluru and Davangere. Currently, a pilot is being conducted at four bus stations in Bengaluru. The service will be available at a speed of 512 kbps for one hour or 100 MB, whichever is consumed first.
