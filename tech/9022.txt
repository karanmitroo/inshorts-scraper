At the pre-MWC 2016 event on Sunday, Alcatel introduced its Plus 10 convertible tablet. The Windows 10-powered hybrid device comes with a detachable keyboard that sports a built-in SIM slot for 4G connectivity. The tablet features a 10.1" HD display, an Intel Cherry Trail T3 processor with 2 GB of RAM, 32 GB of storage and 5 MP/2 MP cameras.
