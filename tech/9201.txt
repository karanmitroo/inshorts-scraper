UK-based David Anderson and Mathew Hine have entered the Guinness World Records by developing the world's fastest mobility scooter that can hit a speed of 107.6 mph. To achieve this, go-cart wheels and an 80-horsepower, four-cylinder Suzuki engine was added to a scooter designed for 8 mph. The previous record for the fastest mobility scooter was marked at 71.59 mph.
