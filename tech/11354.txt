Samsung's recent launch in India is the new Solid State Disk (SSD), an external storage device smaller than a credit card, weighing less than 30gms. With Samsung's 3D NAND technology, the device works upto the speed of 450mbps, reading 3-7 times and writing 100 times faster than other devices. The three variants cost Rs12,000 (250GB), Rs18,000 (500GB) and Rs35,000 (1TB).
