Indian smartphones manufacturer Intex on Monday launched another smartphone 'Xtreme V' under its Aqua series, priced at Rs 11,290. The device has a 5-inch HD display, 16GB inbuilt storage, 1.7GHz octa-core processor coupled with 2GB RAM. Its 2000 mAh battery can deliver upto 7hrs of talk-time. The smartphone sports a 13MP camera with LED flash and a 5MP selfie camera.
