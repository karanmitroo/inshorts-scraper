India-based mobile phone manufacturer Micromax on Monday launched the Q335 smartphone in its Bolt series. The device sports a 4.5-inch display and is powered by 1.2GHz quad-core Spreadtrum (SC7731) processor coupled with 512MB RAM. It runs on Android 4.4.2 KitKat and comes with 4GB internal storage, 5MP primary camera and 2MP secondary camera. However, its price has not been revealed.
