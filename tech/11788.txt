The first sale of Lenovo's much-awaited 4G smartphone, the Lenovo A6000, became a huge success after 10,000 units got sold out in 2 seconds from e-retailer Flipkart, on Wednesday. Flipkart and Lenovo had received 2,55,000 registrations for the phone which competes with the likes of Xiaomi Redmi Note. Registrations for the next sale on Wednesday 2 pm have been started.
