Chinese smartphone maker Oppo mobile launched its first smartphone, Oppo N1, in India, - claimed to be the world's first smartphone with a rotating camera. The 13MP rotating camera features a 206-degree rotation, and will lock at any angle. N1 features a 5.9-inch Full HD screen, 1.7GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon 600 quad-core processor along with 2GB RAM and 16GB internal storage.
