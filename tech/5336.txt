Technology giant Apple has launched a photo book called 'Designed by Apple in California' showcasing 450 of its most iconic products from the last 20 years. The white linen-bound hardcover book is dedicated to the memory of Apple's Co-founder Steve Jobs. The book is priced at $299 (approx ₹20,000) for the bigger and $199 (approx ₹13,500) for the smaller version.
