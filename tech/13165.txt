Sony has announced its smartphone Xperia Z1 f, a mini-variant of the Xperia Z1, under Japanese operator NTT DoCoMo's winter smartphone line-up. The android smartphone, expected to be released in Dec-13, features a 4.3-inch HD(720x1280) display, 2.2GHz quad-core Qualcomm processor, 2GB-RAM, 20.7 MP camera and 16GB internal-memory.  No pricing details for the newly introduced Xperia Z1 f have been revealed.
