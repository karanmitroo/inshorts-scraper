Samsung has launched its next-generation smartwatches- Gear 2 and Gear 2 Neo- both of which are powered by Tizen (Linux-based operating system). Just like Galaxy Gear, Gear-2 also sports a 1.63-inch Super AMOLED display (320X320p), and is powered by a 1GHz dual-core processor and 512MB RAM. It has 4GB internal storage and 2MP camera and is water and dust resistant.
