Samsung on Thursday launched its two new mid-range tablets - Galaxy Tab A and Galaxy Tab E - in India. Galaxy Tab A, which features an 8-inch screen and runs on a 64-bit 1.2GHz quad-core processor, is priced at Rs 20,500. Galaxy Tab E, that has a 9.6-inch screen and is powered by a 1.3GHz quad-core processor, costs Rs 16,900.
