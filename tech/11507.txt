Chinese smartphone maker Oppo on Wednesday launched its new device Oppo N3 priced at Rs 42,990 in India. The 5.5-inch display smartphone sports a rotating camera of 16 megapixel which rotates upto 206 degree with just a touch of a fingerprint sensor. The device comes with a 2.3GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon 801 processor, 2GB RAM, and 32GB internal storage.
