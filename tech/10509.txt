Samsung has launched its flagship phablet Galaxy Note 5 in India at Rs 53,900 for the 32 GB and Rs 59,900 for the 64 GB variant. The device sports a 5.7 inch QHD (1440x2560p) Super AMOLED screen with a 4 GB LPDDR4 RAM and 3,000 mAh battery that supports fast-charging. The phablet will be available in India from September 20.
