The base model of Samsung's Galaxy S8 smartphone is more expensive to manufacture than Apple's iPhone 7 and Google's Pixel XL. The Galaxy S8, which has been priced at ₹57,900 in India, costs the company about ₹19,870 to make. In comparison, the smartphones by Apple and Google are priced higher but cost the companies ₹14,530 and ₹18,470 to make respectively.
