Electronics maker Sony has announced its first 'SIM-free' smartphone Xperia J1 Compact (D5788) in Japan priced at 54,800 yen (Rs 30,000 approx). Unlike other Sony devices, this smartphone is not a network-specific phone and will allow users to connect to any service of their choice. The 4.3-inch display phone has 2.2GHz quad-core processor, 20.7MP camera and is dust/water resistant.
