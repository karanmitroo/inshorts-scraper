Ahead of its India launch, the iPhone 6 is being sold for Rs.1 lakh on eBay India, while a 16 GB iPhone 6 Plus was being offered at Rs.1.1 lakh. Officially, the phone will be launched in the US and elsewhere on Sept-19 and in India on Oct-17. The 16GB iPhone 6 is expected to be priced at Rs 48k-Rs 50k.
