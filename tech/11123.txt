Smartphone manufacturer Meizu on Tuesday launched its M2 Note phablet in the Chinese market. The device comes in 16GB and 32GB variants, priced at 799 Yuan (Rs 8,300) and 999 Yuan (Rs 10,300) respectively. It sports a 5.5-inch Full-HD display, 13MP rear camera, 5MP front-facing camera and is powered by a 1.7GHz octa-core processor coupled with 2GB RAM.
