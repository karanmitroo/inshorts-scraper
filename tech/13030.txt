BlackBerry has announced that its popular messaging platform BlackBerry Messenger (BBM) will soon be coming pre-installed on LG smartphones. The company said the G Pro Lite, an Android-based smartphone, would be the first LG device to offer BBM out-of-the-box. BlackBerry has also inked deals with Indian companies like Micromax, Spice, Zen, and Celkon to pre-load BBM on their smartphones.
