Smartphone maker HTC on Saturday launched two handsets – HTC One A9 and HTC Desire 828 – for an undisclosed price. The Desire 828 features a 1.5GHz octa-core processor,  2,800 mAh battery, 2GB RAM, a 5.5-inch display and 13 MP camera. The HTC One A9 smartphone features a 5-inch display, 3GB RAM, 13 MP camera, 32GB memory, 2,150 mAh battery.
