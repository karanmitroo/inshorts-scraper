Microsoft on Monday launched the Lumia 532 at Rs 6,499. The Lumia 532, a dual-sim smartphone, has a 4-inch screen and runs on Windows Phone 8.1 OS. The smartphone packs a 1.2GHz quad-core Snapdragon 200 processor with 1GB RAM and has 8GB internal storage. The device also features a 5MP rear camera and 0.3MP front camera.
