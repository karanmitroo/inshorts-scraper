After Facebook’s Mark Zuckerberg, Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella will visit India on November 5. During his one-day visit to Mumbai, he will deliver a keynote address at ‘Microsoft's Future Unleashed’. Mahindra Group Chairman Anand Mahindra, Axis Bank head Shikha Sharma and Tata Starbucks CEO Avani Davda are among the key industry people meeting the India-born CEO.
