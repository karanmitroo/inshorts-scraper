iBall has launched mSLR Cobalt4, priced at Rs 8,499, which comes with four detachable mSLR lenses. The detachable lenses include a zoom lens with 8x zoom, a fisheye lens with 175-180-degree viewing angle, a macro lens with 10x magnification and a wide-angle lens with 130-degree viewing angle. The device is powered by 1.4GHz octa-core processor and runs on Android 4.4.
