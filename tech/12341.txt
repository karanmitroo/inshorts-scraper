The luxury Porsche-designed BlackBerry P’9983 was launched at around $2,300 last-week. The phone is the first of the Porsche-BlackBerry collaboration to include a physical Qwerty keyboard with “glass-like” keys, created by the silver font characters. The touch screen is 3.1-inches and has a 720 x 720 pixel resolution. It has 64GB of on board memory, can be expanded upto 128GB.
