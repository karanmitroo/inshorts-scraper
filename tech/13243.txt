Samsung on Tuesday announced the launch of Galaxy Note 3 smartphone and Galaxy Gear smartwatch (priced at Rs.49,900 and Rs.22,990 respectively), which will be available in India by September-25. While the 5.7-inch FullHD Note is the first smartphone having 3GB RAM, Gear is a smartwatch, with 1.63-inch SuperAMOLED screen and 1.9MP camera, which allows users to receive or make calls.
