Search giant Google on Tuesday launched real-time traffic information for 12 more Indian cities, including Kolkata, Thiruvananthapuram and Bhopal, on Google Maps. The other cities are Coimbatore, Lucknow, Surat, Indore, Ludhiana, Visakhapatnam, Nagpur, Kochi, and Madurai. The traffic feature, available on both mobile and desktop versions of Google Maps is now available in 34 Indian cities.
