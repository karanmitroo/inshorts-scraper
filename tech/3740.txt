Swiss watchmaker Louis Moinet has created the 'Whiskey Watch', with a single drop of what it claims is the world's oldest whiskey, Old Vatted Glenlivet 1862. The gold version of the watch will sell for about ₹30 lakh, while a steel version will cost around ₹11 lakh. A bottle of Old Vatted Glenlivet 1862 can be bought for ₹5 lakh.
