Bangalore-based artificial intelligence startup Boxx.ai has raised $500,000 (Rs. 3.2 crore) from venture capital firm Unicorn India Ventures and other investors. The startup was launched in 2016 by IIT alumni Ajay Kashyap, Prakhar Raj and Shitiz Bansal. Its proprietary algorithms predict what each customer is likely to buy in the future, and then deliver personalized customer experiences.
