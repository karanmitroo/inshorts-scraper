Apple's average profit per handset was $151 (₹9,700) in the July-September quarter, five times more than its closest rival Samsung, according to research firm Counterpoint. Samsung had a profit per unit of $31 (₹2,000) in the same period. Further, Apple accounted for nearly 60% of total profits in mobile handset segment, followed by Samsung (26%) and Huawei (4.9%), Counterpoint said.
