Alibaba on Friday announced its acquisition of video website Youku Tudou for about $3.7 billion. Alibaba currently holds 18.3% stake in the company. The deal valued Youku Tudou at $4.8 billion (enterprise value), however, accounting for $1.1 billion cash held by Youku Tudou, Alibaba would pay $3.7 billion. With this, the e-commerce firm will gain access to over half a billion online video users.
