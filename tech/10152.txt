Samsung has announced the ATIV Book 9 Pro, its first-ever laptop with 4K display and sixth-generation Intel Core i7 Skylake processor. The laptop features a 15.6-inch touchscreen display, a 4K HDMI port, and 8 GB of RAM. It, however, has a battery life of 6.5 hours. The device will be available on Samsung.com and Best Buy in November for $1,600.
