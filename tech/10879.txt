Honda on Friday launched 110cc bike, Livo, in India, at a starting price of Rs 52,989. Mated to a 4-speed transmission, Livo's engine churns out a maximum power output of 8.25bhp and 8.63Nm. The bike also features a mileage-enhancing technology that improves engine combustion, reduces friction and optimizes transmission. Livo comes as a replacement for Honda CB Twister.
