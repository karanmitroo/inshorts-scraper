Luxury smartphone maker Vertu has released its latest update to the premium Constellation handset, finished with leather sourced "from a specialist, family-run tannery in Italy". An inset ruby doubles as a button that offers access to Vertu's 24-hour Concierge service. The gadget has a 5.5-inch WQHD AMOLED display, Qualcomm Snapdragon 820 processor, 4GB of RAM, and 128GB of storage.
