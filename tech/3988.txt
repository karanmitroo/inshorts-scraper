Actress Sunny Leone, in partnership with the Emojifi app, has launched a series of 'Sunny Leone sticker emoticons' for usage on messaging apps like WhatsApp, Facebook, FB Messenger, Hangout and Hike. The stickers pop up as suggestions as a user types during a chat. The stickers include a dance emoji based on 'Laila Main Laila', Sunny's song in 'Raees'.
