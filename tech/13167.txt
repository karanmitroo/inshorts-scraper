Prices of Apple iPhone5S and iPhone5C, to be launched in India on November-1, have been confirmed by Apple. iPhone5S will be available for Rs. 53,500 for the 16GB model, Rs. 62,500 for the 32GB model and Rs. 71,500 for the 64GB model. iPhone5C will be available for Rs. 41,900 for the 16GB model and Rs. 53,500 for the 32GB model.
