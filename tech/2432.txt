Hewlett-Packard Enterprise CEO Meg Whitman has stepped down as the Chairman and board member of HP. Chip Bergh, who is CEO of Levi Strauss, will take over as Chairman of HP. In 2015, Hewlett Packard had split into two companies, 'Hewlett-Packard Enterprise' and 'HP'. Whitman's exit comes amid reports that she has been shortlisted by Uber for CEO post.
