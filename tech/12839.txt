Spain-based startup Geeksphone has released ‘Revolution’ - a smartphone that can run both Android and Firefox OS - priced at around Rs 19,000. The device offers a Geeksphone's proprietary MultiOS technology, a "1-click system" for alternating between operating systems. Powered by a dual-core 1.6GHz processor, the smartphone features a 4.7-inch Multi-touch display, 1GB RAM, 4GB internal storage and 8MP rear-camera.
