LG Electronics on Thursday introduced Optimus G Pro phablet, the newest edition of its Optimus family, priced at Rs 42,500 in India. The device runs on Android Jellybean v4.1.2 and is powered by a 1.7 GHz quad-core processor, 2GB RAM and a 3140mAh battery. The smartphone has a 5.5-inch 400ppi pixel density full-HD display and a 13-megapixel camera.
