French luxury fashion house Louis Vuitton has launched a smartwatch named Tambour Horizon, which has been priced at approximately ₹2 lakh. It measures 45 millimetres in diameter and comes with a choice of 60 interchangeable straps, priced between ₹19,000 and ₹32,277 each. Owners will also be able to choose between three case styles in the colours grey, black and brown.
