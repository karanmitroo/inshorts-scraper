Paramount Technologies launched its state-of-the-art 6D theatre- iRido 6D- at the IGI Airport, Delhi, making it India's first airport to get its own 6D cinema. iRido is the only multi-dimensional theatre with six degrees of seat movements and 21 special effects, simulating experiences like water-spray, air-blast, bubbles, smoke, leg-ticklers and pokers. Tickets are priced at Rs.250 for every 15-minute show.
