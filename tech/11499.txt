The smartphones manufacturer HTC on Thursday launched another handset, Desire 626G+, in its Desire series priced at Rs 16,900 in India. The dual-SIM smartphone has a 5-inch HD display, 13MP rear camera, and 5MP selfie camera. The device is powered by a 1.7GHz octa-core processor paired with 1GB RAM and comes with 8GB of internal storage, expandable up to 32GB.
