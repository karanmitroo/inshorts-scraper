LG launched its first ever Chromebase all-in-one computer for Rs. 32,000 in India on Thursday. Complete with 21.5-inch Full HD IPS display with 1920 x 1080 pixels resolution and powered by fourth generation Intel Processor, it is raft of advanced features. “It is a successful combination of simplicity, power, user friendliness and great design”, said MD of LG Electronics India.
