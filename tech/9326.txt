Domestic vendor Intex, on Monday, launched two 32-inch LED TVs dubbed LED TV 3215 and 3213. The LED TV 3215 offers full HD display, while the LED TV 3213 sports HD screen. Both are equipped with Eye Safe T-Matrix technology that removes time lag for smoother viewing. While the 3215 is priced at ₹27,999, the 3213 costs ₹23,999.
