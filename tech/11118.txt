HTC on Thursday unveiled its new smartphone One ME in China. It is the world's first handset to be powered by MediaTek's Helio X10 processor- 64-bit, 2.2 GHz octa-core chipset, which was announced earlier this year. It features 5.2-inch QHD display, 3GB RAM, 32GB internal storage (expandable upto 2TB), 20MP primary camera with LED flash and 4-ultra pixel secondary camera.
