Google CEO Sundar Pichai, Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella, and Gates Foundation Co-founder Melinda Gates on Thursday tweeted about National Intern Day in the US. Nadella shared a photo with 2,000 Microsoft interns with the caption, "Their energy & passion are contagious!" and Pichai retweeted a collage of Google's interns captioned, "Thanks for all you do to keep Google Googley".
