More than three months after it got globally unveiled, Sony has finally launched its dual-sim phablet, Xperia C, in India at Rs 21490. The 5inch android phablet has 1GB RAM, 1.2GHz quad-core Mediatek processor, 4GB internal memory, 8MP camera and 2390 mAh battery. With 6-months of accidental warranty offer, the device is expected to compete with Samsung Galaxy Grand.
