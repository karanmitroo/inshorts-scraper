Samsung has slashed prices for two of its phablets, Galaxy Note 3 and Note 3 Neo in India. The launch price of Note 3, a year back, was Rs. 49,990, and is now available for Rs. 40,698 at Samsung estore and for Rs. 38,112 at Flipkart. 
Note 3 Neo was launched at Rs. 40,990, and is available for Rs.27,249, lowest.
