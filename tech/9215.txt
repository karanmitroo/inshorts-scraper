Lenovo has launched the Yoga 900 convertible and the Yoga Tab 3 Pro tablet in India for ₹1,22,090 and ₹39,990, respectively. The 13.3-inch convertible offers modes like stand, tent, tablet and laptop, and can project images sized up to 70 inches. The 10.1-inch tablet sports a built-in rotatable projector and is powered by a 2.24 GHz quad-core Intel processor.
