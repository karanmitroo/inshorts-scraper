Motorola has launched its new smartphone, Moto Turbo, in India. The phone is up for pre-order on Flipkart starting March 9. Priced at Rs 41,999, the phone runs on Android v5.0(Lollipop) OS. The phone's USP is its 3900mAh battery- one of the largest batteries in any smartphone. Moto Turbo sports a 21MP camera with dual-LED flash and  8x digital zoom.
