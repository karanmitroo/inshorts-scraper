Nokia on Tuesday unveiled Lumia-625 budget smartphone, having 4.7-inch screen, the biggest ever in a Nokia handset, at an event in London. The smartphone, priced at 220-euros (before taxes), has 1.2GHz dual-core processor, 512MB RAM, Super Sensitive Touch technology screen and Windows Phone 8 OS. It is expected to be launched in India in the third-quarter of the year.
