The Modi fanphone, named SmartNaMo Saffron One, is now available on Snapdeal.com for pre-order. Available in two variants: 16GB with 1GB RAM (priced at Rs.18000) and 32GB with 2GB RAM (priced at Rs.23000), the dual-sim device can be booked by paying Rs.999 in advance. The key features include 1.5GHz quad-core processor, 5-inch IPS touch-screen, Android 4.2.1 and 13MP rear camera.
