Domestic phone manufacturing company, Celkon, has launched the Octa 510 smartphone at a price of Rs 8,990. The dual-SIM (GSM+GSM) device runs Android 4.4 KitKat, features a 5-inch HD IPS OGS display and is powered by a 1.4GHz octa-core MediaTek processor coupled with 1GB of RAM. The phone can be purchased online, through the website of eBay India.
