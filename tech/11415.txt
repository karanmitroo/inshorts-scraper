Indian electronics company Micromax on Friday launched calling tablet Canvas Tab P480 priced at Rs 6,999. The 7-inch display tablet supports dual-SIM cards and is powered by a 1.3 GHz quad-core processor. It runs on Android KitKat 4.4 operating system currently and has a 5MP auto-focus rear camera. The device comes with 1GB RAM, 2MP selfie camera and 3000mAh battery.
