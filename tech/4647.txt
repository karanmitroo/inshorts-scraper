Gaming equipment maker Razer is offering a reward of $25,000 (over ₹17 lakh) for information regarding the theft of two laptop prototypes which were stolen from its booth at CES 2017. The prototypes stolen were of Project Valerie, the world's first laptop with three screens. Razer's CEO Min-Liang Tan said, "It's cheating, and cheating doesn’t sit well with us."
