With 40.34% of total cyber attacks, Russia has topped the list of cyber-attacks on mobile devices, followed by India (7.9%), Vietnam (3.96%) and Ukraine (3.84%), Russian cybersecurity solutions firm Kaspersky Lab said. Moreover, it said that about 98.1% of all mobile malware detected in 2013 targeted Android devices. During 2013, nearly 100,000 new malicious programs for mobile devices were detected.
