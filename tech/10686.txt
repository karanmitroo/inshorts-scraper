Samsung on Thursday unveiled its two new smartphones- Galaxy S6 edge+ and Galaxy Note 5. While the Note 5 will be have a starting price of around Rs 48,400, the price of S6 edge+ will start from Rs 59,800. Both the phones run on a 5.1 Android Lollipop operating system and feature  5.7-inch QHD (1440x2560 pixels) Super-AMOLED displays.
