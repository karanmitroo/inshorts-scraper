Dimension NXG, which has developed India's first augmented reality (AR) headset 'AjnaLens', has raised an undisclosed amount from investors including Paytm Founder Vijay Shekhar Sharma. AjnaLens creates high-resolution 3D computer holographic visuals which users can experience and interact with. Founded in 2014, the startup specialises in Artificial Intelligence (AI), Computer Vision and AR.
