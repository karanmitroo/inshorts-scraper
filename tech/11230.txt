Mumbai-based electronic company Videocon on Wednesday launched its new smartphone Infinium Z45 Nova+ in Indian market at Rs 4,900. The 4.5- Inch FWVGA IPS display device is powered by a 1.2 GHz Quad-Core processor coupled with a 1GB RAM. It also comes with an 8GB internal memory, a 5MP primary camera, a 2MP selfie camera and a 1,800mAh battery.
