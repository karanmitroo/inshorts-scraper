Stjepan Sucec, a man from Croatia, on Friday found a 100 million-year-old fossil tooth of a giant 18-metre long megalodon shark in Kupa river near Pokupsko. The shark is believed to have lived approximately 16 to 2.6 million years ago during Neogene period in Cenozoic era. It is also regarded as one of the largest and most powerful predators in history.
