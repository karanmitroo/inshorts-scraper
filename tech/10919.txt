South Korea-based electronics company LG on Wednesday launched its new phablet 'G4 Stylus' in India, priced at Rs 24,990. The 5.7-inch IPS display device is powered by a 1.2GHz quad-core Snapdragon 410 processor with 1GB RAM and 16GB inbuilt storage. It also sports a 13MP rear camera with LED flash along with laser autofocus and a 5MP front-facing camera.
