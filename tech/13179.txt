Google revealed its new and light (2.3-pounds) laptop-'HP Chromebook 11' at $279 on Tuesday in New York. It has an 11.6-inch in-plane switching(IPS) display with 300-nits brightness, 176-degrees off-angle viewing, 2GB RAM, 16GB storage drive and 6-hours battery life. Running Google's Chrome OS with a dual-core Samsung Exynos processor, it's a cloud-based system and is available in black and white.
