With a 31.5% market share(in revenue), Samsung became the smartphone market leader in India dethroning Nokia this year. The other two top players are Nokia(27.2%) and Micromax(8.7%). While the overall market has grown by 14.7% in 2012-13 to touch Rs 35,946-crore, Samsung recorded a growth of 43.6%, Nokia recorded a decline of 18% and Micromax recorded a growth of 58.6%.
