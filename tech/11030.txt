Taipei-based technology firm BenQ on Wednesday unveiled two gaming monitors - XL2430T and XL2730Z in India. The 27-inch Quad-HD, AMD Freesync supported XL2730Z and the 24-inch XL2430T full-HD, will be available at retail prices of Rs.50,000 and Rs.36,000 respectively. The monitors will come with a Steelseries Fnatic rival mouse, a Steelseries Fnatic QCK and an Asphalt edition mouse pad.
