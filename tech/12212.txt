Google on Wednesday unveiled its latest Nexus 6 smartphone. The Motorola Nexus 6 has a contoured aluminum frame, a six-inch quad HD display, 13 megapixel camera and 3220 mAh battery. Being the first to run the new Android 5.0 Lollipop, Nexus 6 will be powered by the new Snapdragon 805 quad-core 2.7GHz processor with the Adreno 420 GPU.
