E-commerce giant Flipkart on Thursday forayed into the self-branded mobile and tablet space with the launch of 7 inch ‘Digiflip Pro XT712’ tablet, priced at Rs 9999. The specs of the device include 1.3GHz quad-core cortex A7 processor, 1GB RAM and 5MP camera. It runs on Android Jelly Bean, supports 3G dual sim and weighs just 285 grams.
