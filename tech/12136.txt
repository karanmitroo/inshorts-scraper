Google's next flagship smartphone, Nexus 6, that was announced by Google last month, has been listed online on the website of Flipkart with a 'coming soon' tag. The phone sports a 6-inch QHD(2560x1440p) display, and is powered by a 2.7GHz quad-core Snapdragon 805 processor and 3GB RAM. It comes in 32GB and 64GB storage variants.
