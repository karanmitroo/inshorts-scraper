Sony on Wednesday launched Xperia Z5 and Z5 Premium smartphones in India priced at ₹52,990 and ₹62,990 respectively. Notably, Xperia Z5 Premium is the first smartphone with a 4K-screen display. Z5 will hit the stores from October 23 while Z5 Premium will be available from November 7. Both the phones will be available in dual-sim variants.
