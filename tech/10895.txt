Intex on Wednesday unveiled its first Android smartwatch 'iRist' in India, priced at Rs 11,999. The smartwatch that comes with in-built 3G SIM card slot will be available in the market August onwards. The device has a 1.56-inch sapphire glass screen, a 1.2Ghz dual-core processor combined with 512 MB RAM and 4GB internal storage, expandable up to 32GB.
