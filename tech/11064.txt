Union Telecom Minister Ravi Shankar Prasad on Tuesday launched Wi-Fi facility at the Taj Mahal complex. For the first half-an-hour, users will be able to browse the internet for free, and after that, they will have to pay Rs 30 per hour. The Wi-Fi facility at Taj Mahal is being operated by BSNL in collaboration with Bengaluru-based company Quadgen.
