In a move to foster its growth in the PC+ era, Lenovo India announced the launch of a new range of feature-rich gaming PCs. Powered by Windows 8 and Intel Core i7 processor, the new range includes Lenovo IdeaPad Y510p priced at Rs 69,990, Lenovo IdeaCentre Horizon 27 priced at Rs 1,36,500 and Lenovo IdeaPad Z510 priced at Rs 50,990.
