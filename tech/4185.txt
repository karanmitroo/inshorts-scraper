ISRO's Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle on Wednesday put 714-kg Cartosat-2 series earth observation satellite and 103 other satellites in their orbits. ISRO's two nano satellites (INS-1A and INS-1B) were also launched, which are technology demonstration satellites. Out of the remaining satellites, 96 were from the US, with one each from the Netherlands, Switzerland, Israel, Kazakhstan and the UAE.
