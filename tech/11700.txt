BMW Wednesday launched its costliest car in India, the 'i8' sports car, at Rs 2.29 crore ex-showroom Mumbai. Also the first BMW model hybrid car, i8 sports a 1.5-litre petrol engine and a 131-hp electric engine. The machine also offers a combined power of 266 hp, a combined mileage of 47 kmpl and can gain a speed of 230 kmph.
