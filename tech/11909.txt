Mozilla, in association with KDDI, is all set to launch the first Firefox phone in Japan. Named FX0, the USP of the phone is its transparent shell. Priced around $420, the phone will be available in Japan from Dec 25. The Fx0 features a 1.2GHz Snapdragon 400 processor, 1.5GB RAM, 16GB of storage space, and a 2,370mAh battery.
