Xiaomi sold 40,000 units of its smartphone Redmi 1S, in a mere 4.5 seconds in its second flash sale on Flipkart, on Tuesday. Priced at Rs 5,999, the Redmi 1S competes with the likes of Moto E and Asus Zen Phone4 in the country. Registrations are open for the next sale that will take place on Sep-16.
