Electronics manufacturer Intex on Thursday launched the 'Aqua Y4', a new addition to its Aqua series, priced at Rs 4,190. The 4.5-inch display smartphone is powered by a 1GHz dual-core MediaTek (MT6572X) processor coupled with 512MB RAM and 4GB internal storage. It also sports a 5MP primary camera, a 2MP secondary camera and supports 3G, GPRS/EDGE, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and GPS.
