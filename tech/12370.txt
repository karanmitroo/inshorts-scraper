Motorola unveiled the second generation of its smartphones Moto G and Moto X, on Friday. The Moto G, which has a 5-inch screen, 1.2 Ghz quad core processor, Android KitKat OS and 1GB RAM, will be available for sale at Rs. 12,999 only on Flipkart, starting on Friday midnight. The Moto X will be available later this month.
