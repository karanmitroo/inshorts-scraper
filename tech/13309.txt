Sony has announced high-end Sony Xperia Z Ultra in India for Rs 44990 online. The 6.44-inch weather-resistant phablet is powered by 2.2GHz quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon-800 processor, runs on Android-4.2.2 and an Adreno 330 GPU to deliver high-quality mobile graphics. It also features 1080p resolution TFT display, 2GB RAM + 16GB internal storage, 8MP rear camera and a non-removable 3050-mAh battery.
