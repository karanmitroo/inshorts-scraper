Apple on Tuesday launched two new phones 'iPhone 8' and 'iPhone 8 Plus' with wireless charging technology with prices starting from $699 and $799 respectively. The new iPhones have new Apple-designed Image Signal Processors, faster low light autofocus, better pixel processors and hardware multi-band noise reduction. While iPhone 8 has a 4.7-inch display, iPhone 8 Plus has a 5.5-inch display.
