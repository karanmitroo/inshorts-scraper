According to the GlobalWebIndex, Google Maps is the most used smartphone app with over 54% people using it in the last month, beating Facebook app by a 10% lead. Google also claimed the 3rd and 4th positions with YouTube (35%) and Google+ (30%). Others in the Top-10 are Weixin/WeChat (27%), Twitter (22%), Skype (22%), Facebook Messenger (22%), WhatsApp(17%) and  Instagram(11%).
