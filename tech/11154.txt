Electronics manufacturer LG is all set to launch the dual SIM variant of its LG G4 smartphone in India. The 5.5-inch Quad HD display device 'LG G4 Dual (D818N)' will be made available at a price of Rs 51,500. It is powered by a 1.8 GHz hexa-core Snapdragon 808 processor with Adreno 418 GPU, 3GB RAM and 32GB internal storage.
