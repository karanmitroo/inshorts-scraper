The second successful developmental trial of indigenously developed nuclear-capable 'Agni-V' long-range ballistic missile was conducted from Wheeler Island off Odisha coast (first was on 19-April-2012). Unlike other missiles of Agni series, 'Agni-V', with a strike range of over 5000-km, is the most advanced version having some new technologies incorporated with it in terms of navigation and guidance, warhead and engine.
