Amazon on Thursday announced a new low-cost tablet costing $49.99 that will start shipping on September 30. The Fire HD tablet, as it is known, measures 7 inches diagonally and has a front-and-back camera. The device runs on the new Fire OS 5 "Bellini", which is built on Android 5 Lollipop. Amazon also introduced 8-inch and 10-inch Fire HD tablets.
