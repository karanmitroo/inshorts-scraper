Samsung on Tuesday unveiled the Galaxy S5 at the Mobile World Congress in Barcelona, Spain. The dust and water-resistant device will be available worldwide in April. The notable features of the phone include, biometric sensor button, 2.5GHz quad-core processor, 2GB RAM, 5.1 inch Super AMOLED (1080 X 1920p) display, Android (KitKat) OS and 16MP camera.
