With an expectation of increasing its present market share (13.33%), Micromax has launched Canvas Tab-P650, its first tablet under Canvas brand. Priced at Rs. 16500, the tablet comes with an 8-inch display, voice calling facility, 4800mAh battery and an upgradable OS -Android 4.2. The features also includes a 1.2GHz quad-core processor paired with 1GB RAM and 32GB expandable memory.
