LG Electronics on Sunday announced  its 8.3-inch tablet, G Pad, which will come with a 1.7GHz Quad-Core Processor, 1900 x 1200 display, 16GB of memory, 2GB RAM and weighs 338 grams. LG touts that the G Pad includes "real-life benefits" include multitasking with its "slide aside" feature, which allows you to control up to three different apps without any interruption.
