According to a study by research firm eMarketer, more teenagers are using Instagram and Snapchat instead of Facebook. The firm estimates Facebook's user base among 12 to 17-year-olds in the US will shrink by 3.4% in 2017 while Instagram is expected to grow by 8.8%. Also, Snapchat will grow by 19.2% in 18 to 24-year-old age group.
