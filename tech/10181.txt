Xiaomi will launch a one-year insurance cover programme 'Mi Protect' starting from ₹275, for its smartphones and tablets sold in India. Redmi 2 and Redmi 2 Prime will get covered for ₹275 while Mi 4i 16GB, Mi 4 16GB and Mi Pad will be covered for ₹499. Accidental and liquid damage will be covered under the scheme, Xiaomi said.
