China-based electronics manufacturer Huawei on Friday launched its two new smartphones Honor 4C and Honor Bee in the Indian market. With 4.7-inch display, 2GB RAM, and a Hi-Silicon octa-core processor, Honor 4C is priced at Rs 8,999. While Honor Bee is priced at Rs 4,999 and sports a 4.5-inch display, 1.2GHz SpreadTrum quad-core processor, 1GB RAM and 8GB internal storage.
