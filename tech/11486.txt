Indian Navy will launch its new indigenous stealth destroyer 'INS Visakhapatnam' in Mumbai on Sunday. The ship- Visakhapatnam, a 7,300-tonne destroyer is first of the four ships to be made under 'Project 15B' and will cost approx Rs 29,600 Cr. With the enhanced capability to operate in nuclear, biological and chemical atmosphere, it will be inducted into Navy by 2018.
