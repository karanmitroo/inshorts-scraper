As per Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI), mobile number portability (MNP) requests crossed the 9.8 crore mark at July end this year, since MNP's introduction in January-2011. The largest number of MNP applications were received in Karnataka (1.14 crore), followed by Rajasthan (95.8 lakh), Andhra Pradesh (89 lakh), Gujarat (83.3 lakh) and Maharashtra (79.5 lakh).
