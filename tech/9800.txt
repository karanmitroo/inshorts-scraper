Micromax's Yu Yureka was the most googled mobile phone in India in 2015. The cities that googled the device most were Chinchwad, Gurgaon and Indore while Chhattisgarh, Haryana and Orissa were the top states googling it. Apple's iPhone 6S and Lenovo K3 Note completed the top three. Notably, three Micromax and three Lenovo phones made it to the top ten.
