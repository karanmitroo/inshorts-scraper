Fiat Chrysler has launched the Abarth 595 Competizione in India at Rs 29.85 lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi). The automatic version of the car can sprint from 0-100 km/h in 7.4 seconds and has a top speed of 205 km/h. It will be sold as a completely built-up unit in India and rub its shoulders with the Mini Cooper in the segment.
