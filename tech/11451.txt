Electronics manufacturer Sony on Monday unveiled its new high-end smartphone Xperia Z4 in Japan. The 5.2-inch display device is packed with a 2930 mAh battery and is thinner than its predecessor Xperia Z3. The water and dust resistant smartphone sports a 20.4MP rear camera and a 5MP selfie camera. It is powered by Qualcomm's 64-bit octa-core Snapdragon 810 processor.
