Electronics company Lenovo on Thursday launched 4G enabled A6000 Plus, an upgraded version of A6000, in the Indian smartphone market. The 5-inch HD display device comes with a 1.2GHz 64-bit quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 410 processor. The smartphone also sports two micro SIM-card slots, 8MP primary camera and 2MP selfie camera. However, Lenovo has not revealed the cost of the device.
