Samsung launched its newest flagship smartphones Galaxy S7 and Galaxy S7 Edge on Sunday. The S7 Edge model offers a Quad HD 5.5-inch display, while the S7 sports a Quad HD 5.1-inch display. Both the models feature a 12-megapixel rear-facing camera, a microSD card slot, and are water resistant at a depth of 1.5 meters for up to 30 minutes.
