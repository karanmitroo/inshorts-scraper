Google on Wednesday announced its 8.9-inch tablet, Nexus 9. Built by HTC, the tablet comes with brushed metal sides, Nvidia Tegra K1 64-bit dual-core processor, 8MP rear and 1.6 MP front cameras. It will come in 16GB, 32GB and 32GB LTE models, priced between $399 to $599. Android 5.0 Lollipop will come pre-installed on the tablet.
