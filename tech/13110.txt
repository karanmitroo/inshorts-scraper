According to the data released by Internet and Mobile Association of India (IAMAI), Mumbai has 12 million internet users, which is highest in India, followed by Delhi(8.1 million) and Hyderabad(4.7 million). In terms of year-on-year increment in internet users since 2012, Kolkata(with 47% growth rate) is on top followed by Mumbai (45%). Ahmedabad is on bottom with 26% growth rate.
