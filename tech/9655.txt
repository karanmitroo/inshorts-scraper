According to Flipkart's year-end report #FlipTrends, Lenovo was the top smartphone brand of 2015, with its A6000 Plus and K3 Note being the "top two most sought-after smartphones" of the year. Motorola's Moto G (3rd Gen) smartphone grabbed the third spot, followed by Xiaomi's Mi 4i and Redmi Note 4G at the fourth and the fifth spots, respectively.
