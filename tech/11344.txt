Motorola Monday announced a price cut of Rs 5,000 on Moto 360 smartwatch across all its variants. The two variants, leather and metal, will now be available on Flipkart at Rs 12,999 and Rs 14,999 respectively. Launched in September 2014, the watch is compatible with devices running on Android 4.3 or above, supports wireless-charging and have full day's battery backup.
