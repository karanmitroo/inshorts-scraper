A user on eBay has put up his iPhone X pre-order on sale for $59,999.99 (nearly ₹40 lakh) on the e-commerce platform. The iPhone X for sale is a 256 GB 'Space Gray' model that sells for $1,150 (about ₹75,000) in the US. Pre-orders for iPhone X started from October 27, a week before its official launch on November 3.
