Lenovo has announced the launch of a new convertible device A10, the company's first laptop having an Android-4.2 OS. The dual-mode laptop, weighing less than 1kg, has a 10.1-inch HD display with a screen flippable up to 300-degrees. The device sports a 1.6GHz quad-core processor, stereo speakers and a 9-hour battery backup. Pricing and availability have not been revealed yet.
