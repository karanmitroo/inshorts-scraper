According to Gartner, Information Technology spending globally is projected to exceed $3.6 trillion in 2016, a 1.5% increase from 2015. Spending on Internet of Things (IoT) hardware will also surpass $2.5 million every minute in 2016. “The algorithmic economy will power the next great leap in machine-to-machine evolution in the IoT,” Peter Sondergaard from Gartner said.
