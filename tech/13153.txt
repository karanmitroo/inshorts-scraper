With an aim to enhance the 3G internet speed in the in National Capital Region (NCR), MTNL has floated a tender of Rs. 400 crore. The project, for which Nokia Solutions and Networks, Alcatel Lucent, Huawei and ZTE are in race, aims to increase maximum speed on MTNL's 3G networks from 3.6 mbps to 21.1 mbps by using HSPA+ technology.
