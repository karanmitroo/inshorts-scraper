Electronics company LG on Thursday launched 4.7-inch curved display smartphone Volt in Korea. The device is powered by 1.2GHz Snapdragon 410 processor coupled with 1GB RAM. It also has 8GB internal storage (expandable up to 32GB), 8MP primary camera with an LED flash and 1MP secondary camera. Furthermore, the device is 4G enabled and packs a 2100mAh removable battery.
