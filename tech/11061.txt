Wickedleak on Tuesday launched its new smartphone Wammy Note 4 at Rs 14,990. The 5.5-inch full-HD display device is available for pre-booking on the company's official website. The dual-SIM smartphone is powered by 1.7GHz octa-core MediaTek MT6752 processor coupled with 3GB RAM and 16GB internal memory. It sports a 13MP rear camera with LED flash and a 5MP front-facing camera.
