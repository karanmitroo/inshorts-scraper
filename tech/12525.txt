Samsung has launched its two latest tablets — Galaxy Tab S 10.5 and 8.4 — in the Indian market at Rs 37,800 and Rs 44,800, respectively. The tablets feature Super AMOLED displays (2560x1600p), powered by an Exynos 5 Octa processor and it runs on Android 4.4 (KitKat). Samsung claims these are the thinnest and lightest devices available in the market.
