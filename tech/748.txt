India has produced many business leaders in the world, including Madurai-born Sundar Pichai, who is the CEO of technology giant Google. Indian-American Ajay Banga is the CEO of MasterCard and Hyderabad-born Satya Nadella is the CEO of Microsoft. India-born Sanjay Jha is the CEO of GlobalFoundries, a semiconductor firm that produces chips for giants like Broadcom and Qualcomm.
