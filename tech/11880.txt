Domestic smartphone maker, Xolo, Friday launched Q700 Club smartphone at a price of Rs 6,999. The device sports a 4.5-inch IPS display and is powered with 1.3 GHz quad-core processor, coupled with 1 GB RAM and 8 GB internal memory, expandable upto 32 GB. It runs Android 4.4.2 KitKat and comes with a 2000 mAh battery.
