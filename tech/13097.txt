Google on Thursday unveiled its next-generation smartphone, the LG-manufactured Nexus 5, the first device to ship with Android 4.4 KitKat. The device, available in 16GB ($349) and 32GB ($399) flavors, is a 5-inch smartphone powered by 2.3GHz quad-core Snapdragon 800 processor, 2GB RAM and advanced 8MP camera. Google reported that the new mobile OS has just a 512MB memory profile.
