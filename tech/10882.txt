Japanese electronics company Panasonic on Monday launched its low budget smartphone 'T33' in the Indian market, priced at Rs 4,990. The device that sports a 4-inch display is powered by a 1.2GHz quad-core processor and runs on Android 4.4.2 KitKat. It also features a 3-megapixel camera with LED flash and comes with a 1500mAh battery.
