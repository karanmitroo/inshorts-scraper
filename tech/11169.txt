Google on Monday celebrated 86th birth anniversary of late actress Nargis Dutt with a doodle depicting a digital sketch of her. Nargis, who was the wife of late actor Sunil Dutt and mother of Sanjay Dutt, made her screen debut as a child in Talash-E-Haq (1935). Her best performances include her role as Radha in 'Mother India' (1957).
