US-based startup Saygus has developed a $650 smartphone 'V Squared' with 400 GB of expandable storage space and 64 GB in-built memory. The dual-sim, waterproof handset runs on Android 5.1.1 Lollipop and features a 5-inch full HD display, 3 GB RAM, 21 MP rear and 13 MP front camera. It comes with Qualcomm's 2.5 Ghz Quad Core Snapdragon processor.
