HTC Friday launched a new Desire 816G dual SIM variant in India priced at Rs 19,990. The smartphone has a 5.5-inch display, 1GB RAM, 13MP camera and Android 4.4 (KitKat) just like its previous model, which was launched last year. However, the new device is powered by a faster 1.7GHz Octa-Core SoC processor and also has 16GB of internal storage.
