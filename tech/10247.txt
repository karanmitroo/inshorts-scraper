Rohan Murthy, Infosys founder NR Narayana Murthy’s son, is reportedly providing $1 million to a robotic astronomy project called Robo-AO. Knowns as the "world's only robotic laser adaptive optics system,” the autonomous project collaborates with telescopes to enable users to better view the solar system. Robo-AO is currently being moved to the Kitt Peak National Observatory, Arizona.
