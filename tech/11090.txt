Japan-based electronics manufacturer Sony on Friday launched its new smartphone, the Xperia C4 selfie, in the Indian market, priced at Rs 29,490. The 5.5-inch display smartphone is powered by a 1.7 GHz octa-core Mediatek MT6752 SoC processor coupled with 2GB RAM. The device also features 13MP camera with LED flash, 5MP front camera, 16GB internal storage and 2,600 mAh battery.
