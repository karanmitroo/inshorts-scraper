Brazil-based telecommunications company Vivo on Wednesday launched its X5Pro smartphone in the Indian market, priced at Rs 27,980. The device sports a 5.2-inch display with 1080x1920 pixels resolution and is powered by a 64-bit octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 615 processor. It has 2GB RAM and Adreno 405 GPU. On the camera front, it has a 13MP rear and 8MP front-facing camera.
