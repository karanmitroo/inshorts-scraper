In the latest figures provided by Strategy Analytics for Q3 2013, Google's Android, with a record 81.3% global share, leads the global market of smartphone shipments, followed by Apple's iOS at 13.4% and Microsoft's Windows Phone at 4.1%. Global smartphone shipments reached 251.4 million units, up 45% from a year earlier. Notably, Windows Phone managed to double its share year-over-year.
