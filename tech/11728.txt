German luxury car maker Mercedes-Benz on Wednesday launched the diesel variant of its C-Class sedan at a price up to Rs 42.9 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi), the PTI reported.
The C-Class diesel is launched in two trims, the ‘C 220 CDI Style’ variant priced at Rs 39.9 lakh and the ‘C 220 CDI Avantgarde’ priced at Rs 42.9 lakh.
