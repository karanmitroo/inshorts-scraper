Amsterdam-based company TomTom on Thursday launched its series of sports-geared smartwatches, called Spark GPS Fitness, in India priced between ₹13,999 and ₹25,999. The watches come in four options and offer several features including 3 GB of music storage space, heart rate monitors, and 24×7 activity trackers. Water resistant up to 40 meters, they are available on Flipkart, Amazon and Snapdeal.
