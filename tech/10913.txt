German luxury car maker BMW on Tuesday unveiled its new variant model of X3 "xDrive30d M Sport" in India. Priced at Rs 59.9 lakh, the new model is powered by a 3-litre six-cylinder diesel engine and is locally produced at the company's Chennai plant. It accelerates from 0-100 km/h in 5.9 seconds with a top speed of 232 km/h.
