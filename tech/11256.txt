Chinese electronics giant Xiaomi on Thursday announced the launch of a slimmer and lighter 10,000mAh Mi Power Bank which is set to replace its existing 10,400mAh variant. The device is 30% more energy-dense and 21.4% slimmer than the previous version, the company said. It is 60.4mm-wide and weighs 207g as compared to the 77mm-wide 10,400mAh Power Bank that weighs 250g.
