HTC has launched Desire 516 dual sim in India at Rs 14,200. The phone sports a 5-inch qHD display. Powered by a 1.2GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon quad-core CPU and 1GB RAM, the phone comes with 4GB internal storage and runs Android 4.2 Jelly Bean. It sports a 5MP rear camera and expandable memory up to 32GB.
