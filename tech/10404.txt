China-based Lenovo on Tuesday launched its new smartphone, 'Vibe Shot', in India on its online store, 'TheDoStore'. Priced at Rs 25,499, it is a camera-centric phone featuring a 16MP rear and an 8MP front camera. The phone is powered by a 1.7GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon 615 octa-core processor. It has a 5-inch-HD IPS display with Gorilla Glass protection.
