South Korean company LG has introduced a hybrid smartphone called Wine Smart which brings back the old flip phone design. The phone is powered by Android Lollipop 5.1.1 and has a 3.2-inch touch-screen. The phone offers wi-fi, Bluetooth, 4G/LTE connectivity and memory expansion via MicroSD card. The front-facing camera is webcam quality while the rear-facing unit is 3-megapixels.
