Mahindra & Mahindra Monday launched a limited edition of its sports utility vehicle XUV500 at Rs 14.48 lakh (ex-showroom Mumbai). The edition comes with various features, including electric sunroof, reverse camera and voice messaging system. M&M would produce only 700 vehicles of the XUV500 Xclusive Edition, and the limited edition will be available on the W8 model of the XUV500.
