Indian technology company iBerry has launched Auxus Nuclea N2 smartphone at a six-day introductory offer price of Rs 19,990. The octa-core processor-based N2 comes with 16GB internal memory (expandable up to 64GB), a 3,500mAh Li-ion battery and a 13MP rear camera. The dual-sim smartphone is the first handset by an Indian brand equipped with wireless charging feature.
