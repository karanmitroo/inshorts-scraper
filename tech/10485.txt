Wearable firm Misfit has launched its products in India in partnership with Snapdeal. Misfit activity trackers namely Misfit Shine, Misfit Flash and Misfit Link priced at Rs 5,499, Rs 2,499 and Rs 1,999 respectively will now be available in India. Co-founded by former Apple CEO John Sculley, the company was formed in 2011 on the day Steve Jobs passed away.
