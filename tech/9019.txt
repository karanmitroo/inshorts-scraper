Lenovo has launched three new Android tablets, Tab 3 7, Tab 3 8 and Tab 3 10, for $129, $99 and $199, respectively. The tablets sport optional 4G support, quad-core processors, Android Marshmallow operating systems, and will start selling in June. The Tab 3 7 and 3 8 are family-friendly devices, whereas the Tab 3 10 is designed for businesses.
