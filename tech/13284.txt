Samsung announced the rumoured Hennessy (W789) dual-screen Android flip phone on Wednesday. The Hennessy has two 3.3-inches 320 x 480 pixels touch displays, powered by a 1.2 GHz quad-core processor running Android 4.1. Other key specs include a 5 megapixel camera, microSD card slot, and 1,500 mAh battery. It comes with support for both CDMA and GSM.
