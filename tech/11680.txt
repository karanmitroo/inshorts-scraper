Microsoft has unveiled Lumia 640 and Lumia 640 XL affordable smartphones at the ongoing Mobile World Congress 2015. While Lumia 640 comes with a 5-inch HD display, 8MP camera with flash and 1GB RAM; Lumia 640 XL has a 5.7-inches display, 13MP camera and is loaded with Windows Phone 8.1. The devices will hit the market in April 2015.
