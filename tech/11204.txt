Mahindra & Mahindra on Monday unveiled an updated version of its sports utility vehicle XUV500 which will have features including an electric sunroof, push-button start, and 6-way adjustable seats. The company, which commands 40% market share in the segment,   priced the lowest model W4 at Rs 11.21 lakh and the highest version W10 at Rs 15.99. lakh.
