Partnering with LG and Huawei, Google on Tuesday launched two new phones Nexus 5X and Nexus 6P in India. The lowest variant of Nexus 5X is priced at Rs 31,990 while price of Nexus 6P starts from Rs 39,999. Both the phones are available for pre-booking starting Tuesday. Reportedly, Nexus 5X will be available from October 21.
