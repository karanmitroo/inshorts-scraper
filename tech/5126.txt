Google India has named selfie editing and camera app 'BeautyPlus Me' as the most popular Android app of 2016 on Google Play. It was followed by media company Viacom 18's 'Voot TV Shows Movies Cartoons' content streaming app and Google's own video calling app 'Duo'. Fashion app 'Mr Voonik' and 'Tap Emoji Keyboard' rounded off 2016's top trending Android apps.
