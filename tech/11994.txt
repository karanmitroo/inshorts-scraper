Chinese smartphone maker, Vivo, will make its entry into the Indian market on Dec 15, with the launch of X5Max, considered to be the world's slimmest smartphone. The thickness of the phone measures 4.75 mm. The specifications include - 5.5-inch full-HD (1080x1920p) display, 1.7GHz MediaTek octa-core processor, 2GB RAM and Android 4.4 KitKat.
