At the ongoing Samsung forum in Malaysia, the company has launched its Galaxy A5 and Galaxy A7 2016 editions for India. The smartphones offer 1.6 GHz octa-core processors, 16 GB of expandable memories, 13 MP cameras and run the Android 5.1 Lollipop operating system. The devices will go on sale on February 15 at ₹29,400 and ₹33,400, respectively.
