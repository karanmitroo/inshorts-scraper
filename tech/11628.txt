German automaker BMW Monday launched Mini Cooper S luxury hatchback in India, at a price of Rs 34.65 lakh (ex-showroom). The 3-door car has a maximum speed of 233 km/hr and takes only 6.7 seconds to attain a speed of 100 km/hr. It features a 2.0-litre 4-cylinder twin-turbo unit that produces 192PS and 280Nm of torque.
