Electronics manufacturer Panasonic on Thursday launched its new smartphone ‘P55 Novo’ in the Indian market, priced at Rs 9,290. The 5.3-inch HD IPS display device is powered by 1.4GHz Octa-Core processor coupled with 1GB RAM and 8GB internal memory. The dual-SIM smartphone also sports a 13MP rear camera with triple LED flash and a 5MP front-facing camera with LED flash.
