Amazon India has announced that OnePlus 5, which is priced at ₹32,999, has become the highest revenue-grossing smartphone on Amazon.in within its launch week. Sales of OnePlus 5 have grown by over three times compared to the brand's previous OnePlus 3T smartphone. Amazon has also extended the cashback offer of ₹1,500 on the OnePlus 5 from SBI till July 2.
