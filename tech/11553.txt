LG Monday launched a new curved midrange smartphone 'LG Spirit' in Indian market priced at Rs 14,250. The dual-SIM smartphone, announced in February, runs on Android Lollipop and has a 4.7-inch slightly curved display. It also has 1.3GHz quad-core processor, 1GB RAM, 8MP rear camera, 1MP selfie camera and 8GB inbuilt storage. However, its availability details are not announced yet.
