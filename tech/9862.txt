Xiaomi has launched its Redmi Note Prime smartphone in India for ₹8,499. This is Xiaomi’s second 'Made in India' smartphone after Redmi 2 Prime. The phone features a 5.5-inch 267ppi HD display, 2GB of RAM, a 13MP rear camera and runs Qualcomm’s 64-bit Snapdragon 410 quad-core processor. The smartphone will be available on Amazon India and Mi.com from December 15.
