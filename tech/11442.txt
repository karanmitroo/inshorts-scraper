US based electronics manufacturer Motorola's recently launched 4G LTE enabled smartphone Moto E 2nd Generation will go on sale on e-commerce giant Flipkart from midnight. Priced at Rs 7,999, the device sports a 4.5-inch qHD display, 5MP rear camera and VGA front camera. It runs on 64-bit quad-core 1.2GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon 410 SoC and Adreno 306 along with 1GB RAM.
