Dell launched Inspiron 13 7000 Series and Inspiron 11 3000 tablet-cum-PC in India Friday, priced at Rs 52,990 and Rs 33,990 respectively. The devices allow users to switch laptop to tablet mode with a light push on the screen, with the help of a rotating hinge. Inspiron 13 7000 sports 13.3-inch HD display while Inspiron 11 3000 has 11.6-inch screen.
