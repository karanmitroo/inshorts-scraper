India's smartphone shipments rose over 219% Y-o-Y to 14.5 million units in Jan-Mar quarter, with more than half of the handsets shipped being 3G enabled, according to CyberMedia Research. However, on a Q-o-Q basis, shipments rose a mere 1.9% from 14.2 million units in Oct-Dec 2013. Samsung lead the race with 43.2%, followed by Micromax (17.5%).
