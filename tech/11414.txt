Motorola has launched its smartwatch Moto 360 in India at a price of Rs 19,999. Compatible with most devices running Android 4.3 or above, the watch has a 1.5-inch display, built-in heart rate monitor, and pedometer. Motorola claims the smartwatch to be waterproof up to 3.3 feet for 30 minutes. It will be exclusively available on Flipkart.
