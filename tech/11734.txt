Samsung has launched Galaxy A7 smartphone in India at Rs 30,499. The device sports a 5.5-inch full-HD Super AMOLED display and is powered by a 1.5GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon 615 oct-core processor and 2GB RAM. Galaxy A7 runs Android 4.4 KitKat with Samsung's TouchWiz UI skin on top. The smartphone also features a 13MP rear camera besides a 5MP front-facing camera.
