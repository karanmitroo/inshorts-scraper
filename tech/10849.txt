Smartphone maker YU, a sub-brand of electronics manufacturer Micromax, on Monday launched Yureka Plus smartphone for Rs 9,999. The smartphone has a 5.5-inch full HD Gorilla Glass 3 display. It is powered by Snapdragon 615 octa-core processor coupled with a 2GB RAM. The device also features 13MP rear camera, 5MP front camera and 16GB internal storage.
