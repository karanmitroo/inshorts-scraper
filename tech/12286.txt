The French automaker Renault has unveiled a hybrid concept car named ‘EOLAB’, which claims to consume just 1 litre of petrol per 100Km. It has a three-cylinder 1.01 petrol engine, top speed of 118.4 kmph and emits just 22 gm of CO2/Km. The concept car was unveiled by Renault at the 2014 Paris Motor Show on Thursday.
