Xolo has announced its latest Windows 8.1 smartphone Xolo Win Q1000 priced at Rs 8,499. The 5-inch HD display smartphone is powered by a 1.2 GHz quad-core Qualcomm processor and comes with 8-megapixel rear camera with LED flash. It is a dual sim phone with 1GB RAM, 8GB internal memory and 2MP front-facing camera.
