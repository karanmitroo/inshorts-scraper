Apple CEO Tim Cook took home an estimated pay of ₹940 crore in 2016. His pay primarily came from 12.6 lakh shares vested during the fiscal year, along with ₹19.5 crore salary, ₹34.7 crore cash bonus and perks worth ₹2.5 crore. Cook was the highest-earning CEO among all other S&P 500 bosses in the US for the year.
