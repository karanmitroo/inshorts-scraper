According to Boston Consulting Group(BCG)'s "Most Innovative Companies" 2013-list, tech-firms are topping the list with Apple Inc. holding the 1st-spot for the 9th-consecutive year, followed by Samsung Electronics Co. and Google Inc. But many automative-companies have also geared up as 9 of them made it into the top-20 like Toyota Motor Corp.(5th), Ford Motor Co.(8th) and BMW AG(9th).
