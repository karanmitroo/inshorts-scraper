Japanese brand Panasonic has expanded its smartphone portfolio with the launch of Eluga Turbo on Monday. The new Eluga phone sports a 5-inch display, offers 4G LTE connectivity, a sleek 6.95mm profile, and is powered by 1.5GHz octa-core processor coupled with 3GB of RAM. Running Android 5.1, the Turbo will be exclusively available on Snapdeal starting January 27.
