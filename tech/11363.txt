Electronics manufacturer Micromax on Thursday launched its new laptop-tablet hybrid device, the Canvas LapTab, at a price of Rs 14,999. The device will be available exclusively on Amazon.in starting May 6, 2015. The LapTab sports a 10.1-inch WXGA IPS touch-display runs on Windows 8.1 and is powered by Intel Atom Z3735F processor, paired with 2GB RAM and 32GB internal storage.
