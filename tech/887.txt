Apple has registered slowest revenue growth in India at 17% in FY2017, according to filings, its lowest in the past 6 years. The technology giant earned ₹11,618.7 crore in revenue in 2016-17 period ending March compared to ₹9,937 crore earned the year before. The data also suggested that Apple's growth in the country was impacted due to demonetisation last year.
