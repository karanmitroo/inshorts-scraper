Micromax on Tuesday launched its new smartphone 'Canvas Xpress 2', priced at Rs 5,999. It sports a 5-inch HD display and is powered by 1.4GHz octa-core MediaTek MT6592M SoC processor coupled with 1GB RAM. It also features 13MP rear camera, 2MP front-facing camera and 8GB internal memory. The smartphone will go on a flash sale on Flipkart on August 4.
