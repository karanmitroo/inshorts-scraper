Videocon has launched a new smartphone, Infinium Z51 Nova, at a price of Rs 5,400 in India. The Android 4.4.2 KitKat-based smartphone comes with dual-SIM support and features a 5-inch FWVGA (480x854 pixels) IPS display. Powered by a 1.2GHz quad-core processor alongside 1GB RAM, it includes a 5MP rear camera with auto focus and a 2MP front-facing camera.
