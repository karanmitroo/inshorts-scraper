Microsoft on Tuesday unveiled its two flagship phones, Lumia 950 and its phablet variant Lumia 950 XL. Lumia 950, starting at $549 has a hexacore processor and a 5.2-inch display (518 pixels per inch). Meanwhile, Lumia 950 XL, starting at $649 has an octacore processor with a 5.7-inch screen (564 ppi). Both models have a 20-megapixel camera with natural flash.
