Nokia has launched its biggest-ever smartphone Lumia 625 (priced at Rs 19,999) as well as the new flagship handset Lumia 925 (Rs 33,499) in India. Lumia 625 features a 4.7-inch screen, 1.2GHz dual-core processor and 512MB RAM while Lumia 925 has a 4.5-inch screen, 1GHz dual-core processor and 1GB RAM. Nokia is offering movies worth Rs 4,275 with Lumia 625.
