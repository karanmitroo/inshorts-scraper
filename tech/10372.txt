In a first, Indian IT firms - TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Tech Mahindra and NIIT have joined hands to work on large-scale projects planned by China's Guizhou, including a cloud-based data centre. Guizhou government had approached the NIIT and Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) for these projects. NIIT has also signed an MoU for $16 million to train IT professionals.
