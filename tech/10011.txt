Miniature computer Raspberry Pi Zero, measuring 65mm x 30mm x 5mm, was on Thursday launched at a price of $5 in the US. Developed by the non-profit Raspberry Pi foundation, it is powered by 1Ghz processor and has 512MB RAM, a micro-SD card slot, a mini-HDMI socket and two micro-USB sockets. Notably, Raspberry Pi is the best-selling British computer ever.
