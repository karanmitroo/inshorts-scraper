Smartphone manufacturer InFocus, which entered the Indian market last month, is set to launch InFocus M330 priced at Rs 9,999. The company has silently listed the smartphone on e-commerce giant Snapdeal. The device comes with a 5.5-inch HD display, 1.7GHz octa-core processor, 2GB RAM, 16GB storage (expandable upto 64GB) and 13MP camera. However, its availability have not been revealed yet.
