Hrithik Roshan on Tuesday took to Twitter to unveil his gaming avatar, that would appear on a mobile game. Hrithik has partnered with mobile game publisher 'Nazara Games' which will make games on the various action characters that the actor has portrayed onscreen. "Ready4some action? Check out 1of my digital avatars!Excited 2 b working wth @NazaraGames 4mobile games", he tweeted.
