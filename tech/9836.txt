Apple has officially launched the Apple Pencil and the Smart Keyboard in India for ₹8,600 and ₹14,900 respectively. In addition to these iPad Pro accessories, the device’s Silicon Case has been listed at ₹6,100 on Apple India’s website. Notably, Apple’s 12.9-inch iPad Pro is now officially selling in the country, with prices starting from ₹67,900 and going up to ₹91,900.
