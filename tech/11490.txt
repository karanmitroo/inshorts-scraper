The video-sharing website YouTube has added 15 new languages to its navigation, taking the total number of languages count to 76. The newly included languages also include north India's regional language Punjabi. Other languages added by YouTube are Azerbaijani, Armenian, Georgian, Kazakh, Khmer, Kirghiz, Lao, Macedonian, Mongolian, Myanmar (Burmese), Nepali, Sinhala, Albanian, and Uzbek.
