Google will reportedly be launching its new Nexus 5 (2015) and a bigger Nexus 6 (2015) on September 29 at an event in San Francisco. However, Google has refused to comment on the matter. Earlier, reports had claimed that the smaller Nexus (5.2-inch screen) will be manufactured by LG, while the larger one (5.7-inch screen) would be made by Huawei.
