Without any official explanation, Apple has removed an app named 'Finder for AirPods' from its App Store, that let users find a missing piece of AirPods through Bluetooth signal. AirPods is a pair of wireless earphones introduced by Apple at around ₹15,000 after it removed the headphone jack from iPhone 7. Notably, Apple charges over ₹4,600 for every lost AirPod.
