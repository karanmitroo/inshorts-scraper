Electronics company iBall on Monday launched a budget smartphone Andi 5M Xotic in India priced at Rs 8,199. The 5-inch HD display smartphone is powered by 1.3GHz Cortex-A7 quad-core processor coupled with 2GB RAM. The device comes with 16GB internal storage, 8MP camera with LED flash, 3.2MP selfie camera and a 2000mAh battery.
