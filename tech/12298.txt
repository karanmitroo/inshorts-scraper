Indian smartphone maker XOLO Thursday launched Q2100, which offers fingerprint scanner for just Rs 13,499. The 5.5-inch dual sim phone also features the Smart Touch Gesture to open apps with pre-assigned gestures, even when the phone is in sleep mode. It runs on Android 4.4 KitKat and has a quad-core 1.3 GHz CPU with 1GB LPDDR3 RAM.
