Apple on Monday unveiled its iMac Pro desktop computer at a starting price of ₹3.2 lakh with 4 TB storage and 5K display. The company also launched 21.5-inch and 27-inch iMac computers, which will be priced from ₹70,000 and have up to 32 GB and 64 GB of RAM respectively. The computers will run on Apple's newly-announced macOS High Sierra.
