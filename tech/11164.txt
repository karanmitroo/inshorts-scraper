Italian luxury sports car maker Ferrari has released the ex-showroom prices for its cars in India. This is Ferrari's second-innings in India with a new dealer Navnit Motors. Ferrari will be selling California T (Rs 3.30 cr), 488 GTB (Rs 3.84 cr), 458 Speciale (Rs 4.25 Cr), 458 Spider (Rs 4.07 cr) and the F12-Berlinetta (Rs 4.72 cr) in India.
