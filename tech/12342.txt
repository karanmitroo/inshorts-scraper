Intex Technologies Tuesday launched its lowest priced KitKat smartphone, Aqua T2 at Rs 2,699. It can be purchased exclusively from Flipkart. The Android KitKat 4.4 powered smartphone has a 3.5 inch display and comes equipped with 2MP rear camera with flash and VGA front camera. The device also features Wi-fi and FM Radio and comes with preloaded games and apps.
