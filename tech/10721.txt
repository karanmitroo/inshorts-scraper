As per reports, the specifications and features of Google Nexus 5 (2015) smartphone, which will be launched later this year, have been leaked. The phone will reportedly have a 5.2-inch P-OLED screen with Full HD (1080x1920p) resolution. The LG-made phone will run on the upcoming Snapdragon 620 processor. It will have a 13MP rear and a 4MP front camera.
