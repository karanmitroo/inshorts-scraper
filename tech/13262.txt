Extending its roots in education sector, iBall has launched an Edu-Slide tablet featuring educational content that includes a master problem package and eBooks for Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics with personalization tools for IIT-JEE entrance test aspirants. Sporting a 10.1-inch HD display, the device runs Android 4.1 and is available in Wi-Fi and 3G variant priced at Rs.30,999 and Rs.35,999 respectively.
