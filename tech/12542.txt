In a move to boost sales, Apple has launched a cheaper iMac, priced at Rs 79,900 in India. The new model, which is Rs 13,000 cheaper than the current iMacs, has a slower processor, lesser storage space and lower-end graphics. The new iMac has a 21.5-inch Full HD screen, dual-core 1.4GHz Intel i5 processor, 8GB RAM and 500GB hard disk.
