Asus on Tuesday launched its smartwatch ZenWatch 2 in two sizes in India. While the smartwatch with smaller 1.45-inch screen will cost ₹11,999, the one with bigger 1.63-inch screen is priced at ₹14,999. The Android-based waterproof ZenWatch 2, with stainless steel casing, syncs notifications with a smartphone and offers 4 GB internal memory along with fitness tracking features.
