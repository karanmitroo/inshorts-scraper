iBall on Wednesday unveiled its new Windows powered tablet 'Slide i701' in India for Rs 4,999. The 7-inch HD IPS display tablet runs on Windows 8.1 operating system and is powered by Intel Atom quad-core processor along with 1GB DDR3 RAM. The device comes with 16GB internal storage, 2MP rear camera with flash, VGA front camera, and 3200mAh battery.
