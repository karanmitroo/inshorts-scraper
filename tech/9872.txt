According to media reports, Reliance Industries’ telecom subsidiary Reliance Jio is set to launch four 4G-VoLTE capable smartphones, namely Earth, Water, Wind and Fire, under the independent brand LYF. Out of these, super-premium smartphone Earth 1, and three premium smartphones, Water 1, Water 2, and Water 3, will be launched this month. The devices will be priced between ₹4,000 and ₹25,000.
