Adding another phone to its Aqua series, Intex Tuesday launched budget smartphone Intex Aqua Star HD 5.0 priced at Rs 6,999. The 5-inch HD display device has a 13MP rear camera with LED flash, 5MP front-facing camera and runs on Android KitKat. The dual-SIM phone also comes with 8GB internal memory, 1.3 GHz quad-core MediaTek processor along with 1GB RAM.
