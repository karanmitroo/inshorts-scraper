'Grippity' has reportedly unveiled the first-ever transparent dual-side operable tablet, with both front and rear panels supporting capacitive multi-touch input. The tablet, which runs on a Cortex A8 processor and Android 4.2.2 Jelly Bean, features a semi-transparent 7-inch (800 x 480) display. Users can pay 235 dollars to reserve a slate for delivery by Oct 2014.
