Chinese company Oppo has announced the launch of its thinnest smartphone- Oppo R5- in India at Rs 29,990. Equipped with an octa-core Qualcomm MSM8939 processor, it is 4.85mm in thickness. It lets you shoot with a maximum focus time of 0.18 seconds and delivers 50 megapixel, Oppo said. The phone will be available for pre-booking in India from January 1.
