Samsung has launched a dual-SIM variant of its Galaxy Note5 smartphone in India at ₹51,400 (32 GB storage). The device features a 5.7-inch QHD display, 4 GB of RAM, a 16 MP camera and is powered by the Exynos 7420 processor. Further, the price of the single-SIM Galaxy Note5 smartphone has been slashed to ₹47,900 from ₹53,900 (32 GB storage).
