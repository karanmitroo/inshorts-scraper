The version 3.0 of the 6-meter-long steel and carbon-fiber flying car 'Aeromobil' was unveiled at the 2014 Pioneers Festival in Vienna, on Wednesday. The car can drive at a speed of upto 200 kmph on land and also take off inside 300 metres (ideally on grass) and land within 50 metres. It has a flight range of 875 kilometres.
