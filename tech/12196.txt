Dell unveiled the refreshed versions of its Venue 7 and 8 tablets, Friday. The 7-inch Venue 7 comes in different versions, starting from Rs 11,999. The Venue 8, which has an 8-inch screen, makes its starting from Rs 12,999. Both of them run on Android 4.4 KitKat OS. Free accessories worth Rs 5000 will be offered if bought before Oct-26.
