On the first day when the government allowed passengers to book e-rail tickets 120 days in advance rather than 60 days, a record 13.45 lakh rail tickets were booked on April 1. Out of the total tickets booked, 1.25 lakh were Tatkal. Total collection from online booking was Rs 2,50,6552,814 out of which Rs 43,033,947 would be IRCTC service charge.
