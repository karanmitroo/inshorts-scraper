Sony has unveiled a string of new products. The Xperia Tablet Z(thinnest tablet in the 10-inch class, at slightly over 0.25 inch, water and dust-proof body) is a bright new contender in the 10-inch tablet market while the 6.4-inch Xperia Z Ultra(5.5-inch screen, Snapdragon 800 processor, waterproof) is poised to compete in the high-end smartphone market.
