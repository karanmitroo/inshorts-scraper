Electronics manufacturer Lenovo on Monday launched a new S-series smartphone, the S60, in India at Rs.12,999. The 5-inch HD display smartphone comes with a dual-SIM support system along with a 13MP rear camera with LED flash and a 5MP selfie camera. It is powered by a 1.2GHz quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 410-processor with Adreno 306 GPU, 2GB RAM and 8GB storage.
