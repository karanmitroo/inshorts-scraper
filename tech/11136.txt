Samsung on Tuesday launched its first SUHD TV in India, months after it was showcased in Las Vegas. Powered by the Tizen OS, the TV features Nano-Crystal Technology, SUHD re-mastering engine and a curved display design. It will be available in two variants - 65 inches and 55 inches and will be priced at Rs 4,40,900 and Rs 3,14,900, respectively.
