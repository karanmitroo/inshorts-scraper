Microsoft on Thursday launched a trio of dual-USB power banks with capacities of 5,200mAh, 9,000mAh and 12,000mAh. These chargers called DC-32, DC-33, and DC-34 will go on sale in the coming weeks with prices $35, $45 and $55 respectively. The power banks can hold their charge for extended periods, and still have up to 80% capacity after several months.
