Taiwanese hardware company Asus is set to launch its ROG Swift-PG348Q gaming monitor in India in April, for a price ranging from ₹1.2 to ₹1.5 lakh. It has a 34-inch curved Ultra-Wide Quad HD 3440 x 1440 display and has additional features like Asus GamePlus which displays in-game information like frames-per-second and elapsed game-time. It also has built-in stereo speakers.
