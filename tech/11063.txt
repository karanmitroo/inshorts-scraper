Oppo, the China-based electronics manufacturer, on Monday launched its Neo 5 smartphone in the Indian market for Rs 9,990. The 4.5-inch display smartphone comes with dual-SIM support system and is powered by a 1.3GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon 400 quad-core processor with 1GB RAM. It also sports a 5MP rear camera with LED flash, a 2MP front-facing camera and 4GB internal storage.
