Lenovo on Thursday launched its new 5.5-inch full-HD display phablet 'K3 Note' in India at Rs 9,999. The device will be sold exclusively on Flipkart through flash sale, beginning July 8. The smartphone is powered by a 1.7GHz 64-bit MediaTek octa-core processor, MALI T760 GPU and 2GB RAM. It also sports 16GB internal storage and runs on Android 5.0 Lollipop.
