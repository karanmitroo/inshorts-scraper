Switzerland-based bike company Feline Motorcycles has recently unveiled high-tech deluxe motorcycle, Feline One, which will become the world's most expensive bike when its first model will be launched in 2016. Priced at $280k (approx Rs.1.75 crore), the bike will be equipped with a 801cc, 3-cylinder, 170 horsepower bespoke streetfighter. The company will produce only 50 units of the Feline One.
