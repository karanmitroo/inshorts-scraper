Price comparison website BuyHatke raised $1 million from Infosys co-founder S Gopalakrishnan and Japanese e-commerce company Beenos in its first round of funding. "We will use the funds for marketing the site and working on new products, besides expanding the team," said CEO Gaurav Dahake who heads a team of 15 with his IIT Kharagpur counterpart and co-founder Prashant Singh.
