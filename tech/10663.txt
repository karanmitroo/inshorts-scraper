Chinese technology company Lenovo has launched its latest smartphone Lenovo A2010 in India, priced at Rs 4,990. Lenovo claims it to be India's most economical 4G LTE dual-SIM smartphone. The phone, which runs the latest Android 5.1 Lollipop OS, features a 4.5-inch display, 64-bit quad-core 1.0GHz processor and a 5 megapixel rear camera.
