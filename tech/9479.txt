In the list for the 25 worst internet passwords of 2015 by software security firm SplashData, '123456' retained its top position for the third consecutive year. While 'password' ranked second on the list, 'Starwars' related passwords like 'starwars,' 'solo' and 'princess' appeared on the list. The firm based its list on an analysis of 2 million leaked passwords in 2015.
