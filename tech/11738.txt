After launching Aqua Power in December last year, Intex Tuesday launched Aqua Power HD, its new addition in the Aqua series, priced at Rs 9,444 in India. The smartphone has 5-inch HD screen, 1.4 GHz octa-core processor, Android 4.4 (KitKat) and a powerful 4000mAh battery. It comes with 2GB RAM, 16GB internal memory and 13MP rear camera with LED flash.
