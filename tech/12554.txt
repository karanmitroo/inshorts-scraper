Vertu, the English manufacturer of luxury mobile phones, is back with the 'Signature Touch' at Rs 7.8 lakh. The phone, having 4.7-inch full HD screen and 2.3 GHz Quad-core processor, comes with superior handcrafted materials like grade 5 titanium and hand-finished calf leather. Vertu is available from around 500 stores, including 70 of its Boutiques, in 66 countries.
