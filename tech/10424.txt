Smartwatch maker Pebble has launched its first round smartwatch, Pebble Time Round, for $249. Claimed to be the thinnest and lightest smartwatch in the world, the device is 7.5 millimeters thin and weighs 28 grams. It features a microphone for voice actions, a 64-colour display with backlight, splash resistance and can run for nearly two days with a 15-minute charge.
