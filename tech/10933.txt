Google (Motorola) Nexus 6, launched in India in Dec 2014, has received a price cut. The 32GB variant, which was launched for Rs 44,999, is available on Flipkart for Rs 34,999. Similarly, the 64GB variant, originally priced at Rs 49,999, is available for Rs 39,999. The discount is limited to Flipkart, which is the exclusive retail partner of Motorola.
