Sony has launched its 4G enabled version of Xperia E4 smartphone priced at Rs 9,100 and it will hit the market in April. The 4.7 inch display phone is powered by 1.5GHz quad-core processor, 1GB RAM, 8GB internal storage, android KitKat, 5MP rear camera with LED flash and 2MP selfie camera. Xperia E4g will also be available in dual-SIM version.
