Dell has launched its two recently unveiled Android powered tablets, Venue 7(Rs. 10,999, 7 inch screen) and Venue 8(Rs.17,499, 8 inch screen), in India. Some of the shared features of both the tablets are 2GHz dual core Intel processor, IPS Display with HD (WXGA 1280 x 800) resolution, Intel HD Graphics and 2GB of DDR2 RAM.
