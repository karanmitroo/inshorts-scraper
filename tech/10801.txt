Motorola on Tuesday launched Moto G (Gen 3) smartphone with 4G LTE support in India at a price of Rs 11,999. It sports a 5-inch display and is powered by a 1.4GHz quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 410 processor coupled with an Adreno 306 GPU and 1GB RAM. The device also features a 5MP front facing camera and a 13MP rear camera.
