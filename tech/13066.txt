HTC has announced its latest phablet, the One Max (16GB variant) with a 'fingerprint scanner' at an MRP of Rs. 61,490 in India and a best buy price of Rs. 56,490. Powered by a 1.7GHz quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 600 processor, it comes with a 5.9-inch full-HD LCD display, 2GB of RAM and runs Android 4.3 Jelly Bean.
