Samsung has launched the Galaxy Core 2 smartphone, which was announced globally earlier this month, at a price of  Rs 11,900 in India. It is a dual-sim smartphone with a 4.5-inch display, runs on Android 4.4 (KitKat) and is powered by 1.2GHz quad-core processor with 768MB of RAM. It is only available for purchase via Samsung's India e-store at present.
