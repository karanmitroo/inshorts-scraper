Chinese computer technology company Lenovo on Wednesday launched the Yoga Tablet 2 with AnyPen technology in India. The 8-inch HD display tablet, priced at Rs 22,499, can convert any pen or pencil into a stylus with its AnyPen technology. The device powered by a 1.86GHz quad-core Intel Atom Z3745 processor and 2GB RAM has a 32GB internal storage.
