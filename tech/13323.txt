As per Akamai Technologies, Hong Kong with an average peak speed of 63.6Mbps - more than three times the global average (18.4Mbps), topped the list of countries with fastest internet. The US, considered a king of the Internet world, ranked 11th (36.6 Mbps). India with a speed of 10.6Mbps ranked 109, second lowest among Asian countries, followed only by China (8.3Mbps).
