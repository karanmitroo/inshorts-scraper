Piaggio Vehicles Pvt. Ltd. Wednesday launched a limited edition Vespa Elegante, at Rs 78,999 in India. It will be offered in two new metallic colours- Marrone Crete Senesi and Bianco Perla. Launched specially for the festive season, it is powered by a 125cc 3 valve high efficiency engine, sports disc brakes, tubeless tyres and alloy combination wheels as standard provisions.
