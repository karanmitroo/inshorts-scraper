America-based electronics manufacturer InFocus on Monday launched its new smartphone M810 in the Indian market, priced at Rs 14,999. The 5.5-inch full HD display device is powered by a 2.5GHz quad-core Qualcomm processor coupled with 2GB RAM. The smartphone comes with a 13MP rear camera with LED flash, a 5MP front-facing camera, a 16GB internal memory and a 2600mAh battery.
