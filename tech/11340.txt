Electronics company Mitashi on Wednesday launched its 50-inch full HD smart TV, MiDE050v01 FS TV, in India priced at Rs 51,990. The HDTV runs on Android 4.4 KitKat and is powered by dual-core processor coupled with 512 MB RAM. It also sports a DLED screen and offers three HDMI ports, a PC input along with USB, WiFi and Ethernet connectivity.
