US-based mobile company InFocus has entered the Indian market with the launch of InFocus M2 smartphone priced at Rs 4,999. The dual-SIM smartphone runs on Android 4.4 KitKat and is powered by a 1.3GHz quad-core processor coupled with 1GB RAM. It has 8GB inbuilt storage, expandable up to 64GB and sports 8MP front and rear cameras, both with LED flash.
