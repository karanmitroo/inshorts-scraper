The OnePlus 5 smartphone has emerged as the 'bestseller' in India during the Amazon Prime Day sale. Amazon Prime members were offered the ₹32,999 OnePlus 5 smartphone at an additional cashback of ₹2,000. The smartphone is powered by the most powerful processor, Qualcomm Snapdragon 835 and comes with upto 8GB RAM along with the proprietary 'Dash Charge' technology of OnePlus.
