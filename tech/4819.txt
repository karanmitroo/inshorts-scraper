Many technology majors launched their flagship smartphones in India this year, including Apple's iPhone 7 without the headphone jack and the first-ever 'Made by Google' phone Pixel starting at ₹60,000 and ₹57,000 respectively. However, the 256 GB version of iPhone 7 Plus with a 5.5-inch display and dual-lens rear camera was the most expensive phone launched at ₹92,000.
