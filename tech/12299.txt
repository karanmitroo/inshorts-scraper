Sony launched 2 new smartphones, Xperia Z3 and Xperia Z3 Compact on Thursday. The 2 water and dust resistant phones, come with 20.7MP main cameras and 2.2MP front cameras, along with 2.5GHz quad core Qualcomm Snapdragon 801 processor. The 4.6-inch Z3 Compact is priced at Rs 44990, while the 5.2-inch Z3 would be available for Rs 51990.
