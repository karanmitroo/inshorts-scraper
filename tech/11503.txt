Lenovo on Tuesday launched its new 4G enabled smartphone A7000, the successor of A6000, in the Indian market priced at Rs 8,999. The device, exclusively available on Flipkart, will be put for the first flash sale on April 15. The 5.5-inch display smartphone sports an 8MP camera with LED flash, 1.5GHz octa-core processor, 2GB RAM and a 5MP selfie camera.
