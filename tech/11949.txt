As per Google India's '2014 Year in Search' list, Sunny Leone remains the most searched celebrity of the year, for the second time in a row. She is followed by Narendra Modi, Salman Khan, Katrina Kaif, and Deepika Padukone. Bollywood thriller Ragini MMS 2 topped the chart as the most searched-for movie, followed by Salman Khan starrer 'Kick'.
