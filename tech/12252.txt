Lenovo has launched a new entry level smartphone A328, at a price of Rs 7,299. The dual-sim smartphone features a 4.5-inch FWVGA (480x854p) display and is powered by a 1.3GHz quad core processor and 1GB RAM. It runs Android 4.4 KitKat and comes with 4GB of built-in storage, which can be further expanded up to 32GB.
