The Xolo Q3000 was launched in India on Tuesday at a price of Rs 20,999. The dual-SIM (GSM + GSM) phablet runs on Android 4.2 Jelly Bean and comes with 5.7-inch full-HD display and 1.5GHz quad-core processor that is coupled with 2GB of RAM. The device features 16GB of inbuilt storage, which is further expandable up to 32GB via microSD.
