Samsung has partnered with luxury brands to design covers for its S6 range. Swarovski, Romero Britto, Rebecca Minkoff, Montblanc and Burton were named Samsung's design partners. Swarovski for crystal embellishment, Montblanc for leather-made cases and flip covers, Britto for vibrant colours, Minkoff for flower prints and Burton to incorporate snowboarding motifs, the Samsung Tomorrow blog said.
