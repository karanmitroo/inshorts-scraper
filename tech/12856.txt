Lenovo on Tuesday launched the Vibe Z smartphone for the Indian market at Rs. 35,999, which it had first revealed back in November. The dual-SIM android smartphone features a 5.5-inch full-HD touchscreen, 2.2GHz processor, 2GB of RAM, 13 megapixel rear camera and 16GB of built-in storage. Earlier in December, Lenovo had launched the Vibe X in India at Rs. 25,999.
