A seller on e-commerce website Etsy has made a sticker priced at $4.99 (₹340) to disguise Apple's AirPods as dental floss to prevent them from being stolen. The stickers are designed to fit the charging case of the wireless headphones. The Bluetooth-enabled headphones are priced at ₹15,400 in India and each AirPod costs $69 (over ₹4,500) to replace.
