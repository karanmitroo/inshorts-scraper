China-based electronics company Meizu on Monday entered the Indian market with its 5.5-inch full-HD display smartphone M1 Note. The smartphone is powered by a 1.7GHz octa-core MediaTek MT6752 64-bit processor coupled with 2GB RAM. It sports a 13MP rear camera with dual-LED flash and a 5MP front camera. M1 Note will be available for sale on Amazon.in beginning May 20.
