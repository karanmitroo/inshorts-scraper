Acer has launched its iPad Mini lookalike, Iconia A1-830 Wi-Fi tablet for Rs. 11299 in India. The tablet runs on Android Jelly Bean 4.2, but can be upgraded to KitKat 4.4. It has a dual core 1.6GHz Intel Atom Z2560 chip, 7.9-inch 1024 x 768p LCD touchscreen, 1GB of RAM and 16GB ROM, and is available on online retailer- Flipkart.
