Intex has unveiled its Aqua Q7 Pro smartphone for ₹4,190. The dual-SIM smartphone supports 1GB of RAM, a 4.5-inch display and 2MP camera. It runs a 1.2GHz quad-core processor with the Android 5.1 Lollipop operating system. The device, available in two colours, white and grey, is an upgraded version of Intex’s Aqua Q7 smartphone that was launched last month.
