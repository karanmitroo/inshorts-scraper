Micromax has launched its new smartphone, the Micromax Canvas Knight Cameo A290 in India at a price starting from Rs. 11500. The 4.7-inch display phone runs on Android 4.4.2 KitKat, and has an 8MP main camera and a 5MP front lens. The handset has 1 GB of RAM, 8GB ROM and 32GB expandability, and is available in 3 color combinations.
