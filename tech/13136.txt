LG has released its curved smartphone G Flex in Korea only, with no pricing announced for now. The smartphone, to be available in November, has a 6-inch 720p Plastic-OLED display- curved from top-to-bottom; runs on Android 4.2.2 Jelly Bean and features 2.26GHZ processor with 2GB RAM, 13-megapixel rear camera and 3,500mAh curved battery which uses patented 'Stack & Folding' technology.
