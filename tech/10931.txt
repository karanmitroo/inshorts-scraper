Japan-based photography and imaging company Fujifilm on Monday launched its instant polaroid camera 'Instax Wide 300', priced at Rs 9,550. The camera weighs 612 grams without the batteries. Its retractable lens can focus from 0.9m to 3m in normal mode and from 3m to infinity in landscape mode. It has a shutter speed range of 1/64 sec to 1/200 sec.
