Dubbed as 'the world's most-efficient car', the Volkswagen XL1 hybrid, which can travel 100kms in only 0.9-litres, was debuted during an event in Beijing. The car, which has an aerodynamic design, weighs only 1,753-pounds and can be run as all-electric, all gasoline or as a hybrid. Supposedly only 250 units of the car will be produced for sale worldwide.
