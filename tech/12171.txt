Piyush Agarwalla, founder of a Mumbai-based Indian start-up Vimbas Navrachna, has come up with 'Venus'- a bucket-mounted mini washing machine. Users need to fill water in a bucket, add detergent, and mount the machine. It lets users wash upto 2.5 kgs load per wash cycle. Priced at Rs 1500, the device will be available in the market by April 2015.
