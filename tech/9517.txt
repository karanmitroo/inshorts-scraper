Telecom Minister Ravi Shankar Prasad on Monday said that state-run telecom operator BSNL is ramping up its network for the month-long Simhastha-Kumbh mela starting April 22 in Ujjain. Prasad said, “Optical fibre line and copper cable will be laid in 50 km and 45 km respectively”. BSNL will also set up 150 WiFi points and 29 new mobile towers.
