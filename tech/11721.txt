Chinese handset maker Oppo on Thursday launched the Mirror 3 smartphone in India at Rs 16,990. The Oppo Mirror 3 is powered by Android 4.4-based ColorOS 2.0.1 and uses a 4.7-inch screen. It is powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon MSM8916 1.2GHz 64-bit quad-core processor and an Adreno306 graphics processor. It also features 1GB RAM and 8GB ROM.
