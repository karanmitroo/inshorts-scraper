Micromax has launched the Canvas Doodle 3 at Rs 8,500. While Doodle 2 measured 5.7-inch, Doodle 3 sports a slightly larger 6-inch display and runs on older Android 4.2.2 Jelly Bean. It comes powered by a 1.3GHz dual-core processor coupled with 512MB RAM, 4GB onboard storage (expandable upto 32GB) and a 2500mAh battery that promises 9 hours of talk time.
