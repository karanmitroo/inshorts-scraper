Samsung on Tuesday announced its new smartphone, the Galaxy S6 Active, in the United States. The device will be exclusively available at AT&T website in the US beginning June 12. The company, however, didn't reveal the price of the smartphone. It has a 5.1-inch display, 1.5GHz processor, 16MP primary camera, 5MP front camera, 3GB RAM, 32GB storage and 3500mAh battery.
