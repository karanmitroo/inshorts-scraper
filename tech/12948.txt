BSNL, in partnership with Champion Computers, has launched two budget smartphones -- My Phone SM3512 and SM3513, priced at Rs 3,225 and Rs 4,499 respectively. Both the models have the same hardware and software features, including 3.5-inch touchscreen, Android 4.2 (Jelly Bean) operating system and 1,300mAh battery. However, the SM3513 has the additional feature of 3G internet access.
