Chinese smartphone manufacturer ZTE on Tuesday launched its flagship smartphone 'Nubia Z9 Mini' in India at Rs.16,999. The 5-inch full-HD display device comes with a 64-bit 1.5GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon 615 octa-core processor, Adreno 405 GPU and 2GB RAM. It also features a 16MP primary camera and an 8MP front-facing camera along with a 16GB internal storage, expandable upto 128GB.
