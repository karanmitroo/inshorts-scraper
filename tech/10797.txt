Electronics manufacturer Samsung on Monday launched its slimmest smartphone 'Galaxy A8' in the Indian market, priced at Rs 32,500. The smartphone is 5.9mm thick and has a metal-clad body. It sports a 5.7-inch Full HD display and is powered by a 1.8GHz octa-core processor coupled with 2GB RAM and 32GB internal storage. It features a 16MP rear and 5MP front-facing camera.
