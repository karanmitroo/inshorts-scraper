Google on Tuesday unveiled its Pixel C tablet, with USB Type-C, at a starting price of $499 (approximately Rs 32,800). Having a 10.2-inch display, it is powered by an NVIDIA X1 quad-core processor and runs on Android 6.0 Marshmallow. Google has also offered a Bluetooth keyboard accessory with the tablet, which attaches via magnets, priced at $149 (approximately Rs 9,800).
