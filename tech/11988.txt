ISRO announced on Thursday that the launch of India's latest communication satellite GSAT-16 by Arianespace rocket from Kourou in French Guiana has been postponed because of inclement weather at the launch base. GSAT-16 was scheduled to carry 48 transponders into space at 02.08 am on Friday (IST). DIRECTV-14 was also scheduled for launch on Friday, in the dual satellite mission.
