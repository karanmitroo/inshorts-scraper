According to CyberMedia Research, Indian smartphone segment recorded a growth of 167.3% on an annual basis during January-April. A total of 73.5 million mobile handsets were shipped during January-April, recording 11% year-on-year growth. The Indian companies, Micromax(19.3%) and Karbonn(8.6%), grabbed the second and third positions respectively in market share for the first time while Samsung(40.7%) maintained its top position.
