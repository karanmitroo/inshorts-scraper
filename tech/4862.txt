Delhi High Court has barred Indian electronics maker Intex from selling its Aqua-branded mobile phones and accessories, following a trademark infringement petition filed by Aqua Mobiles. The suit claims Aqua Mobiles was selling 'Aqua' branded mobiles and accessories from 2009, while Intex adopted 'Aqua' brand in 2012. The court has given Intex time to dispose of their current stocks.
