US-based electronics company Motorola is offering its entire range of products on e-commerce giant Flipkart at discount prices to celebrate the occasion of Mother's Day. While Moto E (Gen 1) is available for Rs.4,999, Moto E (Gen 2) is being offered at Rs.6,499. Furthermore, Moto G is retailing at Rs.10,990 and exchange offer upto Rs.15,000 is available on Nexus 6.
