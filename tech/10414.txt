Renault has launched its entry-level car Kwid in India for a starting price of Rs 2.57 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi). The hatchback is available in four variants—Standard, RxE, RxL and RxT—and provides a mileage of 25.17 km/l. The localisation level of Kwid is reportedly 98%, “the highest ever by an auto manufacturer in India at the onset of launch.”
