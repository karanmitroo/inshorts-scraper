Reliance Jio will launch its 4G telecom services by December 2015, chief of Reliance Industries, Mukesh Ambani announced on Friday. Furthermore, Ambani said that Reliance will partner with various handset makers to provide 4G-supported smartphones at a price below Rs 4,000. Reliance Jio plans to provide 4G services using LTE technology in 800MHz, 1800MHz and 2300MHz bands.
