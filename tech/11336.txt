Sony on Wednesday announced single-SIM and dual-SIM variants of Xperia C4. With a 5.5-inch full-HD IPS display, the smartphone is powered by a 1.7GHz octa-core MediaTek processor and 2GB RAM. The devices, also having 16GB internal storage, 13MP rear and 5MP front camera, will be made available June onwards. However, the company did not reveal the prices of the smartphones.
