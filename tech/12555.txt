Reliance Jio Infocomm will commercially launch the 4G services in 2015, entailing investments of Rs 70,000 crore, Mukesh Ambani said Wednesday. The 4G service will cover all states, around 5,000 towns and cities, accounting for over 90% of urban India and over 215,000 villages. "Eventually, the network will encompass each of our over 6 lakh villages," Ambani added.
