Samsung has launched its new Galaxy Tab 3 range in India in two variants, one 7-inch and the other 8-inch. The 7-inch variant, available at Rs.17745 (3G version), has 1.2GHz dual-core processor, 1GB RAM and Android-4.1 Jelly-Bean OS. While the 8-inch variant, available at Rs 25,725 (3G version), has 1.5GHz dual-core processor, 1.5GB RAM and Android 4.2 Jelly Bean OS.
