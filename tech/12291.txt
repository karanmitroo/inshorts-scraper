Microsoft Devices Wednesday launched three new handsets - Lumia 730, 830 and 930 - priced at Rs 15,299, Rs 28,799 and Rs.38,649, respectively. The Lumia 830 comes with Windows Phone 8.1 OS, OneDrive with 15 GB of free storage, and will be available from October 8. Lumia 730 and 930 will be available from October 6 and 15 respectively.
