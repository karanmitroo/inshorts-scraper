Micromax on Wednesday launched its new Android tablet, the Funbook P280, priced at Rs 4,650. The tablet sports a 7-inch 5-point capacitive multi-touch screen and runs on Android 4.2.2 Jelly Bean. Powered by a 1GHz Cortex A8 processor, the tablet comes with 512MB RAM and 4GB internal storage (expandable up to 32GB) and a 0.3MP rear camera.
