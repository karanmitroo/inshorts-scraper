LG on Tuesday made its Android Wear-based smartwatch Watch Urbane up for purchase on Google Store in the US. Priced at $349 (approximately Rs 22,000), it is the most expensive Android Wear device to date. It features a 1.3-inch circular P-OLED display and is powered by 1.2GHz quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 400 processor coupled with 512MB RAM and 4GB internal storage.
