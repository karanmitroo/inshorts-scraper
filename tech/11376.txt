The Range Rover Autobiography LWB Black, priced at Rs 3.83 crore (ex-showroom Mumbai, petrol version), is now the most expensive SUV in the country. The petrol version comes with 510 bhp and 625 Nm torque, while the diesel version has 339 bhp and 740 Nm of torque. The model was initially showcased at the Los Angeles Auto Show in 2013.
