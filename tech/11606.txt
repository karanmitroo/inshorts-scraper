Hyundai Tuesday launched its new car, i20 Active, in India priced up to Rs 8.89 lakh. A sports-styled version of its premium i20, the i20 Active features a higher ground clearance, a more rugged bodywork and retuned 1.2-litre petrol and the 1.4-litre diesel engines. While the petrol version gives a fuel efficiency of 17 kmpl, the diesel returns 21.19 kmpl.
