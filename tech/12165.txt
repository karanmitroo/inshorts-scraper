At a global launch event at Singapore, Chinese phone-maker Oppo launched its flagship N3 and an R5 smartphone, on Wednesday. The R5, having a width of 4.85mm, has replaced the 5.1mm Gionee Elife S5.1 smartphone as the thinnest phone, from the Guinness Book of World Records. The N3 features a 16 megapixel rotating camera, that can rotate 206 degrees.
