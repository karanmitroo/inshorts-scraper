Triggered by strong adoption of cloud or subscription-based services, India's software market grew 10% to $4.76 billion in 2013, research firm Gartner said. In 2012, the software market stood at $4.334 billion. Microsoft was the market leader with a 20% share ($957.3 million), followed by Oracle ($505 million), IBM ($446.6 million) and SAP ($324.3 million).
