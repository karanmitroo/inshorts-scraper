Samsung's mobile business in India has seen an increase of 27% in revenue to more than ₹34,000 crore for the year ended March 2017. Samsung India's total revenue for 2016-17 has increased 20% to ₹57,000 crore from ₹47,000 crore in 2015-16. Meanwhile, Apple also saw a growth of 17% to ₹11,618 crore in revenue for the year ended March 2017.
