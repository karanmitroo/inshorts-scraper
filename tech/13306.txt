LG on Wednesday announced G2, its new flagship Android phone, in which the power and volume buttons are placed under the main camera module on the back cover. Powered by 2.26GHz quad-core Qualcomm processor, it features 5.2-inch FullHD (1080 x 1920 pixels/423 ppi) display, 32GB/16GB memory, 2GB DDR3 RAM, 13MP rear camera, 2.1MP front camera, Android 4.2.2 and 3,000mAh battery.
