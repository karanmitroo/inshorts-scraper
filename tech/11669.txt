Mobile manufacturer Blackberry announced Tuesday its full-touchscreen smartphone BlackBerry Leap priced at Rs 17,000 at the Mobile World Congress 2015. The 5-inch HD display smartphone is powered by a 1.5GHz dual-core processor and has a 2GB RAM. The 4G LTE smartphone comes with 8MP autofocus rear camera and has 16GB of inbuilt storage along with a 2800mAh non-removable battery.
