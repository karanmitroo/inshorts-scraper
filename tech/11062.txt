India-based smartphone manufacturer Intex on Monday launched its new device in the Aqua series, named Aqua Q4, in the domestic market at the price of Rs 5,290. The dual-SIM smartphone sports a 5-inch display and is powered by  a 1.2GHz quad-core processor coupled with 1GB RAM. It also features 5MP rear camera, 2MP front camera and 4GB internal memory.
