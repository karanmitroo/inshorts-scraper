With questions being raised over the ₹251-price of 'Freedom 251', the world's cheapest smartphone, the Telecom Ministry has said that the phone's manufacturing cost would be around ₹2,300. The Indian Cellular Association has worked out the retail price of such a smartphone as ₹4,100. Ringing Bells, the phone's manufacturer, said that the manufacturing cost of the phone is around ₹2,500.
