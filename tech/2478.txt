Mobile phones worth ₹90,000 crore were produced in India in the year 2016-17, according to government data. "During 2014-15, mobile handsets worth ₹18,900 crore were produced in India, which increased to ₹54,000 crore in 2015-16," the Telecom Minister Manoj Sinha said. The imports of mobile handsets also fell to 76 million this year from 146 million in 2015-16.
