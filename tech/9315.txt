Homegrown company iBall on Monday launched the Cobalt 5.5F Youva phablet in India at ₹8,999. The 5.5-inch phablet is powered by a 64-bit quad-core processor with 2 GB RAM. Designed exclusively for the younger audience, the phone features ‘Video Eye Control’ functionality, which automatically plays and pauses a video by tracking the user’s eye movements.
