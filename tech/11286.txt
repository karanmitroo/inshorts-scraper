India-based smartphone manufacturer Xolo on Wednesday launched its dual-SIM smartphone Xolo Prime priced at Rs 5,699. The 4.5-inch 854x480 resolution IPS display device runs on Android 5.0 Lollipop and comes with a glass body, 5MP rear and 0.3MP selfie camera. It is powered by a 1.3GHz quad-core processor along with 1GB RAM and 8GB internal memory.
