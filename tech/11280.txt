Audi on Monday launched its latest version of super sports car Audi RS 7 Sportback priced at Rs 1.40 Cr (ex-showroom). The car is powered by a 3,993 cc twin- turbo V8 4.0 engine which produces 560hp and 700 Nm of torque. The car boasts of Audi Matrix LED headlamps and weighs less because of its hybrid aluminium design.
