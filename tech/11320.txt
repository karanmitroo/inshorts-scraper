Yamaha India, Friday launched a new scooter 'Fascino' in the Indian market at Rs. 52,500. The scooter has a curvy body resembling Vespa and is powered by 113cc, 4 stroke air cooled engine which claims to give 66kmpl mileage. The vehicle is claimed to be the most fuel-efficient scooter in its league, because of its light weight (103kg) and Blue-Core-technology.
