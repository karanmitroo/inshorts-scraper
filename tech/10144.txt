With a market capitalisation of $303 billion, Facebook became the eighth US company to cross the $300-billion m-cap mark. Apple, the most valuable company in the world, leads the chart with $674.9 billion m-cap, followed by Alphabet (Google's parent company), Microsoft, Berkshire Hathaway among others. “We had a good quarter and got a lot done,” Mark Zuckerberg, Facebook CEO, said.
