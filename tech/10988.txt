Michael Mandiberg, a New York artist is creating a print edition of Wikipedia that runs to 7,600 volumes and is priced at $500,000. Mandiberg has developed a software which parses the complete English-language Wikipedia database and programmatically lays out 7,600 volumes and uploads them to print-on-demand site Lulu.com. Mandiberg is a Wikipedia contributor with almost 2,000 edits to his name.
