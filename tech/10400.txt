Ford India on Wednesday launched the second generation Figo. The petrol variant is starting with an ex-showroom price of Rs 4.29 lakhs, whereas the diesel version is priced at Rs 5.29 lakhs (ex-showroom) in Delhi. Three engine options are available with the new Figo, the 1.2-litre Ti-VCT petrol, the 1.5-litre Ti-VCT petrol and the 1.5-litre TDCi diesel engine.
