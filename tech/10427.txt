India has slipped in global rankings on broadband penetration, despite an increase in the number of internet users, according to a United Nations’ report. 'The State of Broadband' report evaluating 189 countries saw India’s rank drop to 131 on fixed-broadband subscriptions in 2014, from 125 in 2013. The active mobile-broadband subscriptions ranking fell from 113 in 2013 to 155.
