Google has rolled out its 4G LTE Nexus 9 tablet in India at a price of Rs 43,073. Available at Amazon, the 32 GB tablet features a 8.9-inch screen with an 8-megapixel rear camera and a 1.6-megapixel front camera.  Having the latest Android 5.0 Lollipop OS and a 6700mAh battery, the tablet runs on a 64-bit processor.
