Special emoticons inspired by Hrithik Roshan-starrer sci-fi movie "Krrish 3" have been launched on Facebook making it the first Indian film to do so. The emoticons are available for free download on Facebook's chat window.  "Krrish 3", Directed by Rakesh Roshan and also featuring Priyanka Chopra, Kangana Ranaut and Vivek Oberoi, is due for release on Nov 4.
