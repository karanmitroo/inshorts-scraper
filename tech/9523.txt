Motorola has slashed the prices of its Moto G (Gen 3) and Moto G Turbo Edition smartphones in India by up to 17% and 14% respectively. The 8 GB storage variant of the Moto G (Gen 3) is now available at ₹9,999, down from ₹11,999, and the price of the Moto G Turbo Edition is reduced to ₹12,499 from ₹14,499.
