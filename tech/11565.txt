In a bid to target the low-budget smartphone buyers, Celkon Thursday launched its new smartphone 'Millennia Q450' in India, priced at Rs 4,799. The dual-SIM device runs on Android KitKat and is powered by 1.3GHz quad-core processor coupled with 512MB RAM. It has 4.5-inch display, 5MP camera with LED flash, 2MP selfie camera and 4GB inbuilt storage, expandable upto 32GB.
