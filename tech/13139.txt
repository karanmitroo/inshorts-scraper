Entering into two new segments, Finnish manufacturer Nokia unveiled its first tablet(Nokia Lumia 2520) and phablets(Nokia Lumia 1520 and Lumia 1320) at an event in Abu Dhabi. The Tablet is Windows RT-based with a 10-inch 1080p HD display whereas phablets have 6-inch display and run on Microsoft Windows Phone 8 OS with the recently unveiled GDR3 update.
