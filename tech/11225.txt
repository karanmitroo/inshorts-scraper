Mobile manufacturer Lava on Thursday launched its two entry-level smartphones, Iris 348 and Iris 349 Sleek, in India priced at Rs 3,099 and Rs 2,999 respectively. Both the devices have a 3.5-inch display, a 1GHz single-core processor, 256MB of RAM and 512MB of inbuilt storage. Both have dual-SIM support, 2MP primary camera with LED flash and 0.3MP selfie camera.
