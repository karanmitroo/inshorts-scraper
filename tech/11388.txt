LG unveiled its flagship smartphone G4 at events in New York, London, Paris, Singapore, Istanbul and Seoul. The phone, having a 5.5-inch IPS Quantum Display, sports a Slim Arc design that makes it more durable. It has 32GB internal memory, expandable upto 128GB, 3,000mAh removable battery, 16MP rear and 8MP front camera and a leather back, available in six colors.
