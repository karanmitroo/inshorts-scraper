A parody Twitter account with the handle '@TrumpDraws' photoshops child-like drawings into a GIF of US President Donald Trump holding up signed executive orders. The account's bio reads, "I'm the President and I like to draw" and has gained over 2.4 lakh followers in two days. Some of the GIFs include misspelled drawings of 'Peenut', 'Mickee Mouse' and 'Kat'.
