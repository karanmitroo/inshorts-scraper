Chinese electronics company Xiaomi launched Mi 4i smartphone priced at Rs 12,999 at an event in Delhi on Thursday. The 5-inch full HD display device comes in five colour variants and will go on sale on Flipkart beginning April 30. With 16GB internal storage, the smartphone is powered by 1.7GHz Snapdragon 615 octa-core 64-bit processor paired with 2GB RAM.
