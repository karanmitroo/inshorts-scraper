In the financial year 2014-15, Apple's reported sales in India crossed the $1 billion mark for the first time. The company's sales grew by 44% to ₹6,472.89 crore and net profit doubled to ₹242.85 crore year-on-year. Apple has been reporting double-digit increase in sales since 2012 after it started advertisement campaigns and introduced newer models closer to global launch dates.
