Lava on Thursday launched its low budget smartphone Iris X1 Atom in India priced at Rs 4,444. The 4-inch display device sports a 5 megapixel rear camera with high intensity LED flash and VGA front camera. It runs on Android 4.4 and is powered by 1.2 GHz quad-core processor coupled with 512MB RAM and 4GB internal storage, expandable upto 32GB.
