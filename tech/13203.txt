With a 5.2-inch Full HD display and 13MP camera, LG's new smartphone 'G2' is now available on online store, Saholic.com, for Rs 40,499. Running on Android 4.2.2, the smartphone has Qualcomm's Snapdragon-800 processor, 2GB RAM and 32GB expandable memory. The device, which has all the buttons on the rear, is the first smartphone to be completely devoid of side buttons.
