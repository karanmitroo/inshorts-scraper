Within a fortnight of launching the Galaxy Duos 2, Samsung unveiled the Galaxy Grand 2 phablet in the Indian market on Monday. The android smartphone has 5.25-inch HD screen, 1.2GHz quad-core processor, 1.5GB RAM, 8GB internal storage, 8MP camera. Reportedly, the device which will hit the markets in January will cost between Rs 19,000 and 21,000.
