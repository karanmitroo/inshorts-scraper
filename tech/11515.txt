BHEL, a Maharatna PSU, commissioned a 600MW thermal power plant located in Janjgir Champa district of Chhattisgarh. This is the second unit of the power project by Dainik Bhaskar Power Ltd (DBPL). With a cumulative installed capacity of 11,400 MW, the share of BHEL stands at 68 per cent of the total installed capacity in the state.
