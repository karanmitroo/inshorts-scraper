Samsung has slashed the prices of its first wearable device, the Galaxy Gear smartwatch from Rs. 22,990 to Rs. 19,075 in India. The smartwatch was globally announced alongside the Galaxy Note-3 in September, 2013. According to an earlier report it's sales figures have been disappointing. With a 1.9-megapixel camera, the smartwatch can make calls, display messages and record videos.
