Indian mobile-phone manufacturer Micromax on Tuesday launched a new low-budget smartphone Canvas Spark priced at Rs 4,999. The device will be put for flash sale on online marketplace Snapdeal beginning April 29. The 4.7-inch qHD display smartphone sports 8MP rear camera with LED flash and 2MP front camera. It is powered by 1.3GHz MediaTek MT6582 processor coupled with 1GB RAM.
