Indian smartphone maker Micromax  launched its biggest-ever and most expensive smartphone, Canvas Doodle 2, priced at Rs 19,990 in India. Powered by a 1.2GHz quad-core processor, the dual-sim phone has a 5.7-inch screen (1280 x 720p), 1GB RAM, 12GB internal storage, 12MP rear camera with dual-LED flash, 5MP front camera, 2600mAh battery and runs on Android4.2 (Jelly Bean).
