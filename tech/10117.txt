An Odisha-based innovator's TycheeJuno's Burst Prevention and Puncture Curative technology (BPPC) has won awards at Nasa Tech Brief's 'Create the Future Design Contest' on November 6. BPCC can be manufactured and retrofitted in existing structures, and is environment-friendly, the team's leader said. Tyre bursts reportedly caused 3,371 deaths in India in 2014 and 1.25 million casualties globally.
