Chinese smartphone maker Xiaomi Wednesday launched 3G enabled Xiaomi Mi4 in Indian market priced at Rs 19,999. The smartphone features 2.5-GHz quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon processor, 3 GB of RAM and runs on Android 4.4.3 KitKat operating system. The phone, exclusively available on Flipkart, has a 13-MP rear camera and 8-MP front-facing camera.
