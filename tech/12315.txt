Motorola has launched the new Moto X (2nd generation) smartphone at Rs.31,999 in India, which will go on sale on flipkart starting from Wednesday midnight. The smartphone runs Android 4.4 Kitkat and features a 5.2-inch 1080p display with 2GB RAM and a curved back. It has a 13 megapixel rear camera and a 2 megapixel front-facing camera.
