Intex has launched the Aqua i5 Mini dual-SIM (GSM+WCDMA) smartphone in India for Rs. 6,850. The smartphone runs on Android 4.2.2 Jelly Bean and  is powered by a 1.3 GHz quad-core MediaTek MT6582 processor with 1GB RAM. It includes a 2 megapixel front-facing camera and 4GB of built-in storage with expandable memory of up to 32GB using microSD card.
