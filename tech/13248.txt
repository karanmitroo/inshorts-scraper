Micromax is all set to launch yet another budget tablet, Funbook-P255, with Rs. 4,499 as its pre-order price on online retailers like Snapdeal and Flipkart. The tablet runs on Android 4.0 and comes with a 7-inch capacitive display having a resolution of 800x480 pixels, a 1.2GHz Cortex-A8 processor with 512MB-RAM and 32GB-expandable memory and a 2600mAh battery.
