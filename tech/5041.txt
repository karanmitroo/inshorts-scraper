YouTube has released its annual 'Rewind' video highlighting the biggest memes, songs, challenges and pop culture moments of 2016. The mashup features 200 YouTubers like All India Bakchod, Lilly Singh, PewDiePie and Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson from 18 countries. YouTube also released its list of 2016's top music videos like 'Work' by Rihanna and 'Cheap Thrills' by Sia.
