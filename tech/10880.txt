Xolo on Friday launched 'Xolo Black', the first smartphone under its sub-brand Black, priced at Rs 12,999. The 5.5-inch full HD display device comes with dual rear cameras (13MP + 2MP) with Chroma Flash and 5MP front camera with LED Flash. It runs on a 64-bit octa-core second generation Qualcomm Snapdragon 615 CPU with 2GB RAM and 16GB internal memory.
