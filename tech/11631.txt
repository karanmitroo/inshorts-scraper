US-based tech firm Motorola will launch the second generation Moto E on March-10 in India, expectedly at the same price of Rs 6,999 as that of the previous version. The new version has a VGA camera for selfies, 4.5-inch display, Android 5.0 Lollipop, 1.2GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon processor, 5MP rear camera, 2,390 mAh battery and a 1GB RAM.
