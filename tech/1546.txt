Google's Pixel 2 and Pixel 2 XL will cost ₹61,000 and ₹73,000 respectively for the 64 GB variant. The higher 128 GB variants of Pixel 2 and Pixel 2 XL will cost ₹70,000 and ₹82,000 respectively. While pre-orders begin from October 26, Pixel 2 will be available from November 1 and Pixel 2 XL will be available from November 15.
