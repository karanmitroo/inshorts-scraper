Chinese company LeEco on Wednesday launched its Le Max and Le 1S smartphones in India at ₹32,999 and ₹10,999, respectively. Le Max features a 6.3-inch display, 4 GB of RAM and is powered by Qualcomm’s 64-bit 2 GHz octa-core processor. Le 1S features a 5.5-inch full HD display, 3 GB of RAM and is powered by MediaTek’s Helio X10 processor.
