Xiaomi has launched three new smartphones in India - Mi 3, Redmi 1S and Redmi Note at Rs 13,999, Rs 6,999 and Rs 9,999. While Mi-3 is a 5-inch device running 2.3GHz processor with 2GB RAM, Redmi sports a 4.7-inch display having 1.6GHz processor, and Note has 5.5-inch display. The phones will be exclusively available on ecommerce retailer Flipkart.
